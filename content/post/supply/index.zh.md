## 读音
/səˈplaɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 提供，供给，补给。例如: "The store has a good supply of fresh vegetables."
- **动词**: 供应，提供。例如: "The company supplies electricity to the entire region."
- **名词**: 库存，储备。例如: "We need to check the supply of spare parts before starting the repair work."

## 短语
- "supply and demand" 市场供需关系。
- "in supply" 表示充足供应，例如: "There is plenty of food in supply for the guests."

## 例句
- 名词: "The city's water supply comes from a nearby reservoir."
- 动词: "The restaurant supplies organic produce to several local markets."
- 名词: "Their stockroom had a steady supply of office supplies."
