## 读音
/ʃiːt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 平面的薄片，尤指纸张或金属片。例如: "She put a sheet of paper on the desk."
   2. 床单。例如: "I need to wash these bed sheets this weekend."
   3. (电子表格软件中的)工作表。例如: "In Excel, each spreadsheet is divided into multiple sheets."
- **动词 (v)**: 覆盖以床单或其他大面积的物品。例如: "He sheeted the bed with fresh linens."
   
## 短语
- "Sheet music" 指乐谱。
- "Ironing sheets" 意为熨烫床单。

## 例句
- 名词: "She tore a sheet from the notebook and began writing a letter."
- 动词: "He sheeted the boat's deck to protect it from the sun."
