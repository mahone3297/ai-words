## Pronunciation
/ˈsaʊθərn/

## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Noun**: Referring to something located in the southern part of a region, country, or hemisphere. For example: "The southern states have a warmer climate."
- **Pronoun**: Used to refer to people from the southern region or with southern origins. For example: "Many southerners have a distinct accent."

## Phrases
- "Southern hospitality" refers to the warm and welcoming nature of people in the southern regions.
- "Down south" is a colloquial term for the southern United States or a similar southern area.
- "Southern drawl" describes the distinctive pronunciation pattern found in some southern accents.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The southern border of the park is marked by a tall fence."
- **Pronoun**: "She comes from a family of proud southerners."
- **Adverb**: "He enjoyed the slower pace of life down south compared to the city."
