## 读音
/ˈstændərd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 标准，规范：A level of quality or achievement that is considered acceptable or usual. 例如: "The company aims to maintain high standards in customer service."
   - 常规，常态：Something that is commonly used or followed. 例如: "It's standard practice to wear a suit for job interviews."
   - 尺度，准则：A measure of quantity, size, or value. 例如: "The standard unit of measurement in the metric system is the meter."
   - 军旗，旗帜：A flag carried by a military unit or an organization. 例如: "The regiment marched proudly with their standard flying high."

- **形容词**:
   - 标准的，普通的：Conforming to a recognized or established norm or criterion. 例如: "They offer a standard package of benefits to all employees."
   - 常规的，一般的：Usual or typical. 例如: "This model comes in standard colors like black and white."

## 短语
- "Set a standard" 意为设定标准或典范。
- "Meet the standard" 意为达到标准要求。
- "Industry-standard" 指的是行业内广泛接受的标准。

## 例句
- 名词: "The hotel's customer service did not meet the standards we expected."
- 形容词: "The new car has a standard transmission, not an automatic one."
