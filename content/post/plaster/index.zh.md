## 读音
/ˈplæstər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    1. 布满，涂满：She plastered the walls with posters.
    2. 石膏：The doctor applied a plaster to her broken arm.
    3. (英国)创可贴：He had a plaster on his finger after cutting it.

- **动词**:
    1. 布满，涂满：The rain plastered her hair to her face.
    2. 重重打击，猛烈轰炸：The enemy's artillery plastered the city.

## 短语
- "plaster over" 意为覆盖，掩饰：They tried to plaster over their differences.
- "to be plastered" 在口语中表示喝得很醉：He was so plastered that he couldn't even stand up.

## 例句
- 名词: "She put a plaster on the wound to stop the bleeding."
- 动词: "The rain plastered the leaves to the ground."
- "The soldiers were plastered by enemy fire."

## 同义词
- 名词:
    - bandage
    - dressing
    - adhesive strip

- 动词:
    - cover completely
    - bombard
    - drench (in the context of rain or liquid)
