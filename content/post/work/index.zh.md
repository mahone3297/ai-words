## 读音
/wɜːrk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 工作；职责：例如: "He's looking for a new job because he doesn't like his current work."
  - 成果；产品：例如: "Her artwork is the result of many hours of hard work."

- **动词**:
  - 做工作；劳动：例如: "She works as a teacher during the day."
  - 运行；起作用：例如: "The medicine started to work after an hour."

## 短语
- "Work on something" 意味着致力于或努力完成某事。
- "Work together" 指合作或共同完成任务。
- "Work out" 可以指锻炼，也可以指解决问题或计划成功。

## 例句
- 名词: "I'm proud of the work I did on this project."
- 动词: "She works from 9am to 5pm every weekday."
- 运行： "The new software seems to work smoothly without any bugs."
