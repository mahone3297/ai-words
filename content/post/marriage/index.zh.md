## 读音
/ˈmærɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 结婚，婚姻。例如: "They have been in a happy marriage for 20 years."
  - 婚姻关系。例如: "The couple's marriage was on the rocks."

## 短语
- "Get married" 意为结婚，成家。例如: "They decided to get married in the summer."
- "Marriage counselor" 是婚姻顾问，帮助夫妻解决关系问题的专业人士。
- "Same-sex marriage" 指同性婚姻。

## 例句
- "Their marriage is an example of true love and commitment."
- "After a long courtship, they finally tied the knot and got married."
- "In some cultures, arranged marriages are still common."

## 同义词
- Union
- Matrimony
- Wedlock
- Marriage ceremony
- Partnership (in the context of a committed relationship)
