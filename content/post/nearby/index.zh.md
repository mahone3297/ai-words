## 释义
- **形容词**: 附近的，邻近的。例如: "There's a convenience store nearby."
- **副词**: 在附近，靠近。例如: "I saw a park nearby where we can sit."

## 短语
- "within easy reach nearby" 表示容易到达的附近地方。
- "nearby areas" 指附近的区域或地区。

## 例句
- 形容词: "We found a great restaurant nearby for dinner."
