## Word: Nearby

## Pronunciation
/ˌnɛər ˈbaɪ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Situated close by; not far away. For example: "The nearest grocery store is nearby."
  
  > "They chose a hotel nearby so they could easily walk to the conference center."
  
- **Adverb**: In close proximity; not far off. For example: "The dog barked loudly from nearby."

  > "The sound of children playing nearby filled the air with joy."

## Phrases
- "In the nearby area": Referring to a location that's close to another place. Example: "There are several good restaurants in the nearby area."
- "Within walking distance nearby": Signifying something is close enough to reach on foot. Example: "The park is within walking distance nearby our house."
- "Stay nearby": To remain in close proximity. Example: "She asked her friends to stay nearby while she tried out the new skateboard trick."
