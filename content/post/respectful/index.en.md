## Pronunciation
/rɪˈspektfl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Showing politeness and consideration towards others; showing respect. For example: "She was dressed in a respectful manner for the occasion."
- **Adverb**: In a respectful manner; with respect. For example: "Please answer the question respectfully."
- **Noun**: Respectfulness; the quality of being respectful. For example: "The teacher emphasized the importance of mutual respect in the classroom."

## Phrases
- "Be respectful to your elders" is a common advice.
- "Show respect" means to demonstrate politeness or consideration.
- "To give respect" means to acknowledge or honor someone.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The children were expected to behave respectfully during the ceremony."
- **Adverb**: "She answered the phone respectfully, even though she was in a hurry."
- **Noun**: "His act of kindness demonstrated a deep respect for others."
