## 读音
/rɪˈtʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 到达；抵达。例如: "He finally reached his destination after a long journey."
  2. 能够触及；够到。例如: "I can't reach the book on the top shelf."
  3. 达到（某个水平或标准）。例如: "The company's profits have reached an all-time high."
  4. 影响；触动。例如: "His words really reached her heart."

- **名词**:
  1. 范围；影响力。例如: "The radio station has a wide reach across the country."
  2. 可及的距离。例如: "Her arm's reach is not long enough to grab the toy."

## 短语
- "Within reach" 意为触手可及。
- "Out of reach" 意为无法触及或够不到。

## 例句
- 动词: "She reached for the phone when it rang."
- 名词: "His new invention has a huge commercial reach."
