## 读音
/tɔːl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 高的，身材高的。例如: "He is a tall man, almost six feet four inches."
- **副词**: 高度地，大量地。例如: "The tree stands tall above the others."

## 短语
- "tall in stature" 指身材高大。
- "tall tale" 是一个夸张或难以置信的故事。
- "tall order" 指一个艰巨的任务或要求。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She was tall enough to reach the top shelf without a ladder."
- 副词: "The basketball player jumped tall to dunk the ball."
