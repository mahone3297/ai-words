## 读音
/rɪˈvɛndʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 对伤害、侮辱或失败的报复行为。例如: "He sought revenge against his enemy for the injustice done to him."
- **动词**: 对某人进行报复。例如: "She vowed to revenge her brother's death."

## 短语
- "take revenge on sb." 意为对某人进行报复。
- "get one's revenge" 表示成功地报复了对方。

## 例句
- 名词: "His thirst for revenge consumed him."
- 动词: "She finally had the chance to revenge herself on her former boss."
