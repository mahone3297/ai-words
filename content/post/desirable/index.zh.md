## 读音
/dɪˈzaɪrəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 值得渴望的，可取的，理想的。例如: "A desirable property in this area is always in high demand."
- **名词**: （尤指在寻找伴侣时）理想的人选。例如: "She considered him a highly desirable partner."

## 短语
- "highly desirable" 意味非常理想的或非常想要的。
- "desirable qualities" 指某人或某事物所具有的吸引人的特性。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This new car model has many desirable features, making it popular among consumers."
- 名词: "She possessed all the desirable traits for the job."
