## 读音
/ˈsuːbweɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 地铁，一种在城市地下运行的公共交通系统。例如: "I usually take the subway to work in the morning."
- **名词**: （美国）轻轨，一种在地面或高架上运行的城市轨道交通。例如: "The new subway line connects the downtown area with the suburbs."

## 短语
- "Take the subway" 意味着乘坐地铁出行。
- "Subway station" 是地铁站，乘客上下地铁的地方。

## 例句
- 名词: "The fastest way to get to Times Square is by subway."
- 名词: "She bought a weekly subway pass to save money on transportation."

## 同义词
- Metro
- Underground
- Tube (主要指伦敦的地铁系统)
- Rapid transit
- Light rail (在美国，有时用来指轻轨)
