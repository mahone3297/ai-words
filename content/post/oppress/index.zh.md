## 读音
/əˈpres/

## 释义
- **动词**: 压迫；压制；欺侮。例如: "The government was accused of oppressing the minority groups."
- **名词**: 压迫；压制的行为或状态。例如: "The workers反抗他们的雇主的oppression."

## 短语
- "Oppressor" 是压迫者，指的是实施压迫的人。
- "Oppressed people" 指受到压迫的群体。

## 例句
- 动词: "The ruler's harsh laws oppressed the citizens."
- 名词: "The era was marked by widespread poverty and oppression."
