## 读音
/pəˈnɪʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 处罚，惩罚。例如: "The teacher punished the student for talking in class."
    - 制裁，对…施加严厉措施。例如: "The international community punished the country with economic sanctions."
    - 报复，回击。例如: "If you keep provoking him, he'll punish you."

## 短语
- "punish severely" 严厉地惩罚。
- "punish someone for something" 因某事惩罚某人。

## 例句
- "The judge decided to punish the criminal with a long prison sentence."
- "The company will punish any employee found breaking the rules."
- "The football team punished their opponents with a series of goals."
