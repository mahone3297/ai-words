## 读音
/tæst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 味觉，品尝的感觉；风味，味道。例如: "The dish had a unique taste."
- **动词**: 品尝，试吃。例如: "I only tasted a little of the cake."
- **形容词**: 有品味的，高雅的。例如: "She has good taste in music."

## 短语
- "To have a taste for something" 意味着喜欢或对某事有兴趣。例如: "She has a taste for adventure."
- "A taste of one's own medicine" 指让别人尝到自己曾经施加过的苦头。例如: "After being criticized, he got a taste of his own medicine."

## 例句
- 名词: "The chef added a hint of spice to enhance the taste of the soup."
- 动词: "Can you taste the difference between these two wines?"
- 形容词: "Her new dress shows off her excellent taste in fashion."
