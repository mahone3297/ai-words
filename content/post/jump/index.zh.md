## 读音
/jʌmp/

## 释义
- **动词**: 突然用力使身体离开地面，跃起。例如: "The child was jumping on the trampoline."
- **名词**: 跳跃的动作或行为。例如: "He did a high jump over the fence."
- **形容词**: 突然的，跳跃式的。例如: "There was a jump in the stock market prices."

## 短语
- "Jump start" 启动（汽车等）引擎，通常使用另一辆车的电池。
- "Jump the gun" 过早行动，提前开始。
- "Make a jump for it" 快速逃离，迅速采取行动。

## 例句
- 动词: "She jumped up when she heard her name called."
- 名词: "The athlete's jump cleared the bar easily."
- 形容词: "Sales made a sudden jump after the advertising campaign."
