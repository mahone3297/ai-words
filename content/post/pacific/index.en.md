## Word: Pacific

## Pronunciation
/ˈpæsɪfɪk/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to or characteristic of the Pacific Ocean. For example: "The Pacific coastline is renowned for its beauty."
  - Also used to describe something calm, peaceful, or not warlike. Example: "They sought a pacific solution to the conflict."

- **Noun**: Refers to the Pacific Ocean, which is the largest and deepest of Earth's oceanic divisions. Example: "Many island nations are located in the Pacific."

## Phrases
- "Pacific Rim": Refers to the countries bordering the Pacific Ocean, especially those with strong economic ties.
- "Pacific Northwest": A region in North America that includes parts of Canada and the United States known for its natural beauty and mild climate.
- "Keep the peace / maintain pacific relations": To preserve or promote a state of tranquility or non-aggression.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Their diplomatic efforts led to a more pacific atmosphere between the two countries."
- **Noun**: "The sailor had sailed across the vast expanse of the Pacific during his solo voyage."

## Synonyms
- Calm
- Peaceful
- Tranquil
- Serene
- Quiet
- Still (as an adjective for calmness)
- Oceanic (when referring to the Pacific Ocean specifically)
