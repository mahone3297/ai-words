## 读音
/pəˈsɪfɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 平静的，和平的，宁静的。例如: "The pacific ocean is known for its calm waters."
- **名词**: 太平洋，地球上最大的海洋。例如: "They sailed across the Pacific Ocean to reach Australia."

## 短语
- "Pacific Rim" 指太平洋沿岸地区。
- "Pacific Time" 是美国和加拿大西部采用的时区。

## 例句
- 形容词: "After the storm, the sea became pacific again."
- 名词: "We plan to explore the exotic islands in the Pacific."
