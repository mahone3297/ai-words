## 读音
/inˈdʒʊr/

## 释义
- **动词**: 伤害；损害。例如: "He injured his leg in a skiing accident."
- **名词**: （尤指事故造成的）伤害，损伤。例如: "The player is recovering from a knee injury."

## 短语
- "injure oneself" 意为意外伤害自己。
- "injure severely" 表示严重伤害。

## 例句
- 动词: "She injured her ankle while running and had to take a break from training."
- 名词: "The doctor examined his injury carefully before prescribing treatment."
