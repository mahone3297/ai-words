## 读音
/sevər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 严重的，严厉的，剧烈的。例如: "The patient is suffering from severe pain."
- **动词**: 使严重，加重。例如: "The injury was worsened when he fell, severely twisting his ankle."

## 短语
- "severe weather" 指极端或恶劣的天气条件。
- "severe punishment" 意味着严厉的惩罚或处罚。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The storm caused severe damage to the city's infrastructure."
- 动词: "Her condition was severely exacerbated by stress."

- intense
- harsh
- rigorous
- acute
- stern
