## Definitions
- **Adjective**: 1. Severe refers to something that is very harsh, strict, or intense. 2. Medically, it can describe a condition that is serious or critical. 3. In a social context, it can mean unfriendly or cold.
- **Adverb**: Used to intensify the meaning of an adjective or verb, expressing a high degree of severity. For example, "He spoke severely about the situation."

## Phrases

- "Severe weather conditions" denote extreme climatic events.
