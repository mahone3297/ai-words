## 读音
/ˈmɪnɪməm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 最低限度；最小量；最低点。例如: "The minimum age for this job is 18 years old."
- **形容词**: 最小的；最低的。例如: "We need a minimum number of participants for the event to go ahead."

## 短语
- "minimum wage" 最低工资
- "minimum requirements" 最低要求
- "minimum deposit" 最低存款

## 例句
- 名词: "The airline allows a minimum baggage weight of 20 kilograms per passenger."
- 形容词: "We must maintain a minimum level of water in the tank for the fish to survive."

## 同义词
- least
- lowest
- smallest
- bare minimum
- floor (in the context of a floor price or minimum threshold)
