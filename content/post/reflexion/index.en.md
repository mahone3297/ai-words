## Pronunciation
/refleks(ə)n/

## Definitions
### Noun
A reflexion (also spelled reflection) refers to:
1. The return of light, heat, or sound waves after striking a surface, often creating an image or a reversed impression. For example: "The mirror reflected her image perfectly."
2. A mental process of considering oneself, one's actions, or a situation, often leading to self-awareness or self-analysis. For example: "Her writing often reflects her inner thoughts and emotions."

### Verb
To reflex (not a common verb for this word; usually used as "reflect" or "reflect on"):
1. To consider thoughtfully or contemplate. For example: "He took some time to reflect on his decision."
2. To show or exhibit a particular quality or characteristic. For example: "Her eyes reflected her determination."

### Adjective
In some cases, "reflective" can be used as an adjective, describing something that causes reflection or has a reflective surface. For example: "A reflective surface helps to minimize glare."

## Phrases
- "Reflect on a situation" implies thinking deeply about it.
- "Reflective surfaces" are those that bounce back light.
- "A moment of reflection" suggests a brief pause for contemplation.
