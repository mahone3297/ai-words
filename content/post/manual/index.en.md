## Definitions
- **Noun (n)**: A book or document that provides instructions or information about how to use something, operate machinery, or perform specific tasks. For example: "The user manual explained how to assemble the furniture."
- **Adjective (adj)**: Done or operated by hand rather than automatically or electronically. For example: "A manual transmission requires the driver to shift gears themselves."
- **Noun (n)**: A person who does work that requires physical strength or skill, especially one who uses their hands. For example: "The factory employs many manual laborers."

## Phrases
- "Read the manual" implies referring to a guidebook or instructions before using a product or service.
- "Manual override" refers to a control mechanism that allows a human operator to intervene in an automated system.
- "Manual labor" denotes work that involves physical effort, typically not requiring advanced education or technology.
