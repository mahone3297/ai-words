## 读音
/ˈʃɛpərd/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 牧羊人；负责照看羊群的人。例如: "The shepherd watched over his flock of sheep."
- **名词**: 指引领、指导者，尤指在宗教或精神上的。例如: "The spiritual shepherd guided his congregation through difficult times."
- **动词 (v)**: 照顾，引领，指导。例如: "The experienced guide shepherded the tourists through the wilderness."

## 短语
- "Shepherd's pie" 是一种以羊肉和马铃薯泥为原料的传统英国菜肴。
- "Shepherd the group" 意思是带领或指导一个团体。

## 例句
- 名词: "The shepherd's whistle could be heard across the valley as he called his dogs."
- 动词: "The tour guide shepherded the visitors to their next destination."

## 同义词
- Herdsman
- Guide
- Leader
- Caretaker
- Pastor (在宗教语境中)
