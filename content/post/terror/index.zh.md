## 读音
/ˈtɛrər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 强烈的恐惧感；恐怖，惊恐。例如: "The airplane passengers experienced a moment of terror when the plane hit turbulence."
- **名词**: 恐怖主义；恐怖行为。例如: "Terror has no place in a civilized society."
- **名词**: 恐惧引起的人或事。例如: "The serial killer was known as the terror of the city."
- **动词**: 使感到恐怖，吓唬。例如: "The haunted house terrorized the children."

## 短语
- "Live in terror of something" 表示对某事物极度恐惧。
- "A reign of terror" 指恐怖统治时期。

## 例句
- 名词: "The survivors still recall the terror they felt during the earthquake."
- 名词: "The government has increased security measures to combat terrorism."
- 动词: "The clown's appearance terrified the child."
