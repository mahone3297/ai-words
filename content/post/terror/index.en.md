## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. Intense fear, dread, or horror caused by an immediate threat or danger. 2. A person or thing causing such fear. For example: "The terrorist attack filled the city with a sense of terror."
- **Verb**: To inspire or create a feeling of fear or terror. For example: "The movie's plot terrorized the audience."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by fear or terror; horrifying. For example: "The haunted house was a terrifying experience."
