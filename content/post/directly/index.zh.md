## 读音
/dɪˈrektli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 直接地，不经过中间步骤或过程。例如: "He answered the question directly without hesitation."
- **形容词**: 立即的，直接的。例如: "The boss wants a direct report, not an indirect one."

## 短语
- "Act directly on something" 意为直接影响某事物。
- "Speak directly to the point" 意思是直截了当地说重点。

## 例句
- 副词: "She drove directly to the airport after work."
- 形容词: "We need a directly applicable solution, not a theoretical one."
