## 读音
/ˈθʌndər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大声的雷鸣，通常伴有闪电。例如: "The storm was accompanied by loud thunder and heavy rain."
- **动词**: 形容雷鸣般的声音或力量。例如: "The sound of the waterfall thundered in the valley."

## 短语
- "Thunder and lightning" 指暴风雨中的雷电现象。
- "The thunder rolled across the sky" 描述雷声在天空中回荡。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were afraid of the thunder during the night."
- 动词: "The horse's hooves thundered on the ground as it galloped away."

## 同义词
- Boom
- Roar
- Rumble
- Crash
- Clap (of thunder)
