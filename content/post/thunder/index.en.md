## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A loud, deep rumbling sound produced by lightning, typically accompanied by an electric discharge. For example: "The thunder was so powerful that it shook the windows."
- **Verb**: To make a noise like thunder, often metaphorically used to describe a loud or dramatic event. For example: "Her announcement thundered through the auditorium."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of or resembling thunder, especially in intensity or volume. For example: "The thunderous applause echoed through the hall."

## Phrases
- "A clap of thunder" refers to a single, sharp sound.
- "Thunderstorm" is a weather condition with frequent thunder and lightning.
- "Thunderous laughter" describes loud, hearty laughter.
