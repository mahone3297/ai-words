## 读音
/slaɪd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 幻灯片，尤指在演示文稿中的一页。例如: "He presented his ideas on a series of slides."
   2. 滑动，滑行的动作。例如: "The child had fun sliding down the hill on a sled."
- **动词**: 滑动，滑行。例如: "She slid effortlessly across the ice."

## 短语
- "slide show" 意为幻灯片展示。
- "to slide into something" 表示逐渐进入某种状态或位置。例如: "He slowly slid into a deep sleep."

## 例句
- 名词: "The presenter clicked through the slides, discussing each point in detail."
- 动词: "The thief quietly slid open the window and entered the room."
