## 读音
/swɒmp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 浸没；沼泽地；泥潭。例如: "The house was situated in a swampy area."
- **动词**: 使陷入困境；淹没；使无法处理。例如: "The workload swamped the new employee."

## 短语
- "be swamped with work" 忙得不可开交，工作堆积如山。
- "sink into the swamp" 沉入沼泽，比喻陷入困境。

## 例句
- 名词: "The explorers had to navigate through a dense and treacherous swamp."
- 动词: "The sudden influx of customers swamped the small store's staff."
