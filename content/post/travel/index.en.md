## Pronunciation
/trəˈvɛl/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Travel**: The act of moving from one place to another, often for leisure or business purposes.
- **Tourism**: The activity of visiting places for pleasure, typically involving travel and staying overnight away from home.

### Verb
- **To travel**: Move from one place to another, usually for a purpose, such as vacation, work, or visiting.
- **To travel around**: Explore different locations.

### Adjective
- **Travel-related**: Connected to or involving travel.
- **Wanderlust**: A strong desire to travel and explore new places.

## Phrases
- "Go on a trip" or "take a vacation"
- "Travel itinerary" – a planned route or schedule for a journey
- "Travel light" – to pack only essential items for a trip
- "Bucket list" – a list of places one wants to travel before they die

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I've been planning a travel adventure to Europe this summer."
- **Verb**: "After finishing her studies, she decided to travel the world."
- **Adjective**: "The travel-related industry has seen significant growth in recent years."
