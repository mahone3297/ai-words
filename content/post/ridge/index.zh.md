## 读音
/ˈrɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 山脊；山岭：例如 "The hikers followed the ridge along the mountain range."
   - 屋脊；顶部的棱线：例如 "He climbed onto the roof to repair the damaged ridge tiles."
   - 事物的边缘或最高点：例如 "The ridge of the wave crashed against the shore."

## 短语
- "Ridge line" 指的是山脊线。
- "Ridge vent" 在建筑中是指屋脊通风口。

## 例句
- 名词: "The view from the top of the ridge was breathtaking."
- 动词（短语）: "The climber crested the ridge, revealing the valley below."
