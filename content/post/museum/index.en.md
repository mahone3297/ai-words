## Pronunciation
/maɪˈziəm/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A building or institution where objects of historical, scientific, artistic, or cultural interest are stored and displayed for public viewing. 

### Noun Usage Examples
- The British Museum houses some of the world's most valuable artifacts.
- We spent the afternoon exploring the natural history museum.
- The Louvre Museum in Paris is famous for its extensive art collection, including Leonardo da Vinci's Mona Lisa.

## Phrases
- "Museum exhibit": A display within a museum that showcases specific items or themes.
- "A day at the museum": Refers to spending a day visiting and learning from a museum.
- "Museum curator": A person who manages and oversees collections and exhibitions at a museum.

## Example Sentences
- The children were fascinated by the dinosaur skeletons at the museum.
- She had a dream job working as a conservator in a fine arts museum.
- After the renovation, the local history museum was reopened with improved interactive exhibits.
