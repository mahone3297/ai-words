## Pronunciation
/lɪd/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A removable cover or top that fits over something such as a container or an opening, typically one that can be lifted off or removed by turning. For example: "She took the lid off the boiling pot."
- **Verb**: (informal) To close or put a lid on something. For example: "Make sure you lid the trash can so the dog won't get into it."

### Noun Examples
- "The box had a tight-fitting lid to protect its contents."
- "He removed the lid from the jar with a twist."
- "A well-placed lid keeps food fresh in the pan."

### Verb Examples
- "I always lid my coffee cup when I'm not drinking from it."
- "Mom told her kids to lid the cookie jar so they wouldn't eat too many sweets."

## Phrases
- "Put a lid on it" means to stop talking or to keep something secret.
- "Lift the lid on something" means to reveal or expose information that was previously hidden.

## Synonyms
- Cover
- Top
- Cap
- Lid (as a verb): Close, cover up, seal
- Put the lid on (idiomatic usage)
