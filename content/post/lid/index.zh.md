## 释义
- **名词**: 盖子，尤指容器、箱子或罐子的盖。例如: "Don't forget to put the lid back on the jar."
- **动词**: 盖上，覆盖。例如: "She quickly lidded the pot to let the steam cook the vegetables."

## 短语
- "lid off" 指去掉盖子或揭露真相。例如: "The journalist finally managed to lift the lid off the corruption scandal."
- "keep a lid on something" 意为控制或保密。例如: "They're trying to keep a lid on the details of the negotiations."
