## 读音
/liːf/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 树叶，植物的叶子。例如: "The tree had lost all its leaves in autumn."
2. 书页，纸张的一边。例如: "Please turn to page 56, the leaf with the poem on it."

## 短语
- "leaf through" 快速翻阅，浏览。例如: "She leafed through the magazine looking for an interesting article."
- "turn over a new leaf" 开始新的生活，改过自新。例如: "After his accident, he decided to turn over a new leaf and quit smoking."

## 例句
- 名词: "The garden was filled with colorful leaves of different shapes and sizes."
- 短语: "She leafed through the book, stopping occasionally to read a passage."

## 同义词
- blade (of grass or a plant)
- foliage
- page
- sheet (of paper)
