## Pronunciation
/ˌɪndɪspensəbl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Absolutely necessary; essential; not able to be done without. It describes something that is so important that it cannot be dispensed with or omitted.

### Examples
- _**Adj**_. The internet has become an indispensable tool for communication and research in today's society.
  - _"A first-aid kit is indispensable on any camping trip."_
  - _"Water is an indispensable resource for life."_

## Phrases
- _"Indispensable to"_ emphasizes the necessity of something for a particular purpose. Example: "Good time management skills are indispensable to achieving success in one's career."
  
- _"An indispensable part of"_ highlights how something is an integral component. Example: "Exercise is an indispensable part of maintaining good health."

## Example Sentences
- _**Adj**_. "Her expertise in coding made her an indispensable member of our software development team."
- _"In remote work scenarios, video conferencing technology has become indispensable."_

## Synonyms
- Essential
- Vital
- Necessary
- Crucial
- Imperative
- Compulsory
- Indefensible (in the sense of being unable to justify not having it)
