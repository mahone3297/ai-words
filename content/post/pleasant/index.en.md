## Pronunciation
/pleɪsnt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or producing a sense of enjoyment, satisfaction, or contentment. For example: "The weather was pleasantly warm."
- **Adverb**: In a pleasant manner; agreeably. For example: "She spoke to us pleasantly."
- **Noun**: A pleasing or agreeable quality or experience. For example: "The garden had a pleasant aroma."

## Phrases
- "A pleasant surprise" indicates an enjoyable unexpected event.
- "Pleasant company" refers to enjoyable or delightful companionship.
- "Have a pleasant day" is a common expression wishing someone a good day.

## Example Sentences
