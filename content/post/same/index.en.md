## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Identical in nature, quality, or appearance; not different. For example: "The two paintings are the same."
- **Adverb**: In the same manner or way; similarly. For example: "She sings the song the same way every time."
- **Pronoun**: Used to refer to a previously mentioned person or thing, often emphasizing sameness. For example: "It's the same old story."

## Phrases
- "The same applies to..." indicates that something is true or relevant in a similar situation.
- "Not the same as..." contrasts two things.
- "Do the same thing" gives a command to replicate an action.
