## 读音
/seɪm/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 相同的；一样的。例如: "These two shirts are the same color."
- **副词**: 同样地；同样。例如: "I feel the same way about the situation."

## 短语
- "The same to you" 回应别人的祝福或问候，意为“你也一样”。
- "Same old, same old" 表示事情没有变化，还是老样子。

## 例句
- 形容词: "We have the same interests in music."
- 副词: "She reacted to the news in the same surprised manner."

- Identical
- Similar
- Equivalent
- Alike
