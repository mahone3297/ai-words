## 读音
/speɪs/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**:
  - 空间，指物理或抽象的区域。例如: "There's not enough space in this room for another table."
  - 领空，太空。例如: "The satellite orbits in outer space."
  - 间隙，空白。例如: "Please leave some space between your words."
  - 距离，间隔。例如: "The two cities are several hundred miles of space apart."

- **动词 (v)**:
  - 给予空间，留出空间。例如: "Can you space your books out on the shelf?"
  - 安排，分隔。例如: "The teacher spaced the desks evenly in the classroom."

## 短语
- "in space" 在太空中。例如: "The International Space Station orbits Earth in space."
- "take up space" 占据空间。例如: "This sofa takes up too much space in our living room."

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist used the entire canvas, filling every inch of space with color."
- 动词: "Space your plants so they have room to grow."
- 感叹词（用在句首，表示惊讶）: "Space! Look at that shooting star!"
