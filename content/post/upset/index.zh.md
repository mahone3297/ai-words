## 读音
/ʌpˈset/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 心烦的，沮丧的。例如: "She felt upset after hearing the bad news."
- **动词**: 使心烦，使不安。例如: "The sudden noise upset the baby."
- **名词**: 打翻，混乱。例如: "He knocked over the vase, causing an upset."

## 短语
- "to upset someone's plans" 打乱某人的计划。
- "in a state of upset" 处于心烦意乱的状态。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She looked upset when she came out of the meeting."
- 动词: "The unexpected delay upset his whole schedule."
- 名词: "There was an upset in the stock market today."
