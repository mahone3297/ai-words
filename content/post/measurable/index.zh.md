## 读音
/ˈmeʒərəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 可以测量的，可量度的，有明显数量或程度的。例如: "The effects of the new policy are measurable in terms of increased productivity."
- **名词**: 可测量的事物或量度。例如: "We collected measurable data on the project's progress."

## 短语
- "Measurable results" 指可以量化或明确定义的成果。
- "Measurable goals" 是指可以明确衡量和追踪的目标。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The improvement in her health is now measurable after weeks of treatment."
- 名词: "The researchers aimed to collect measurable data to support their hypothesis."
