## Pronunciation
/ˈneɡətɪv/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Indicating the absence, withholding, or negation of something; not positive. For example: "A negative response to the proposal."
- **Noun**: A negative statement or outcome; the opposite of a positive. In photography, a film or digital image where the colors and tones are reversed from reality (darks appear light, and lights appear dark). For example: "The test result was negative.", "She developed the negatives from her camera."
- **Verb**: (transitive) To deny, reject, or nullify something. For example: "She negatively impacted the project by missing the deadline."

## Phrases
- "Negative feedback" refers to criticism or information that points out flaws or suggests improvement.
- "Test negative" means the result of a test shows no presence of a substance or condition.
- "Negative space" in art or design refers to the area around and between the main subjects of an image.
