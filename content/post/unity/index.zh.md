## 读音
/ˈjuːnɪti/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - **团结**: 指人们在共同目标或信念下的紧密结合。例如: "The team's unity was the key to their success."
    - **统一**: 指事物的一致性和整体性。例如: "There should be unity in design across all marketing materials."
    - **单位**: 在数学中，1 的别称。例如: "The multiplicative identity is unity."

## 短语
- "Unity in diversity" 表示在多样性中保持统一或和谐。
- "Maintain unity" 意味着保持团队或群体的团结一致。

## 例句
- 名词: "The country celebrated its unity during the national holiday."
- 名词: "The concept of unity is central to the philosophy of Buddhism."
- 名词（数学）: "Multiplying any number by unity results in the original number."
