## Pronunciation
/ˈjuː.nɪ.ti/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Unity** (noun): The state or quality of being united or undivided, especially in a group or organization; a sense of oneness or agreement. Example: "The country was striving for political unity after years of conflict."
2. **Unity of purpose** (noun): The shared determination or goal within a group or team. Example: "The team's success was due to their strong unity of purpose."
3. **Mathematical unity** (noun): In mathematics, the number 1, which serves as the multiplicative identity. Example: "In algebra, the product of any number and unity is that number itself."

### Verb
1. To **achieve unity** (verb): To bring together or unite people, ideas, or things. Example: "They worked tirelessly to achieve unity among the different factions."
2. To **uphold unity** (verb): To maintain a sense of harmony or agreement within a group. Example: "The school administration is committed to upholding unity among its students."

## Phrases
- "Maintain unity" means to preserve a sense of cohesion or agreement.
- "Celebrate unity" is used to honor or promote a shared identity or cause.
- "Unity in diversity" refers to the coexistence of various elements with a shared purpose.
