## Pronunciation
/pɪsˈtəl/

## Definitions
### Noun
A pistol is a small, handheld firearm typically designed for personal defense or compact size. It is often semi-automatic or revolver and may be chambered for various calibers.

### Verb (informal)
To use a pistol or imitate its sound, usually for emphasis or playfully. "He fired a pretend pistol during the game."

### Adjective
Referring to a firearm with a compact design or used in close quarters. "A concealed carry pistol for self-defense."

## Phrases
- "Draw your pistol" means to quickly bring a concealed weapon out for protection.
- "Pistol-whip" is a slang term for using a whip or similar object to strike someone forcefully.
- "Pistol-packing" refers to carrying a concealed pistol.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The officer holstered his service pistol after securing the area."
- **Verb**: "In self-defense, he had to pistol-whip the attacker."
- **Adjective**: "She was nervous about carrying a pistol in a crowded city."

## Synonyms

- Compact firearm

## Antonyms
