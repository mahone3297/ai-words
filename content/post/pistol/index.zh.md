## 读音
/pɪˈstɑːl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种小型手枪，通常有单发装填和自动装填两种。例如: "He kept a pistol in his bedside drawer for protection."
- **动词**: 使用手枪射击。例如: "The officer drew his pistol and aimed at the suspect."

## 短语
- "Pistol grip" 手枪握把，指枪械的握持部分。
- "Pistol whip" 用手枪柄击打对方。

## 例句
- 名词: "The security guard was licensed to carry a pistol."
- 动词: "In self-defense, she had no choice but to pistol the attacker."
