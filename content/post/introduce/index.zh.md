## 读音
/ɪntrəˈdjuːs/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    1. 引见；介绍。例如: "I would like to introduce my friend, John, to you all."
    2. 引入；引进。例如: "The new technology was introduced to the market last year."
    3. 使初次了解；使开始接触。例如: "The teacher introduces the students to basic algebra concepts."

## 短语
- "introduce oneself" 自我介绍。例如: "When you meet someone new, it's polite to introduce yourself."
- "introduce a topic" 提出话题。例如: "The presenter began her speech by introducing the main topic of the conference."

## 例句
- "He introduced his girlfriend to his parents at the family dinner."
- "The company is introducing a new product line to expand its offerings."
- "The teacher introduced the students to the history of ancient Greece."
