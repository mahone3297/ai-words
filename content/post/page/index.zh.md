## 读音
/page/

## 释义
- **名词**: 书或文件中的一页；网页；（戏剧、电影的）场；（报纸的）版面。例如: "Please turn to page 32 in the textbook."，"The homepage of the website is the first page you see."
- **动词**: （在比赛中）换人。例如: "The soccer coach paged a substitute player during halftime."

## 短语
- "turn the page" 意味着翻到下一页或开始新的阶段。
- "blank page" 指空白的纸页或新的开始。
- "home page" 在互联网上指的是网站的主页。

## 例句
- 名词: "She marked an important passage on page 15 of her novel."
- 动词: "The basketball coach paged a new player into the game."
