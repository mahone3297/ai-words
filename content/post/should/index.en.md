## Pronunciation
/ˈʃʊd/

## Definitions
### Verb
1. **Modal verb**: Expresses possibility, obligation, advice, or recommendation. For example: "You should study harder for the exam." "I should have called earlier."
2. **Infinitive**: To indicate what one ought to do or would naturally do in a given situation. For example: "We should be grateful for what we have." "It's important that you should apologize."

## Phrases
- "Should have done" implies something was missed or regretted.
- "Could/Should/Would + have + past participle" expresses hypothetical regret.
- "Should be" suggests an expectation or requirement.
- "It's best to do sth." means it's advisable or recommended.

## Example Sentences
- **Recommendation**: "You should try this new restaurant in town."
- **Obligation**: "You should turn off the lights when you leave the room."
- **Hypothetical regret**: "I should have studied more for the test."
- **Expectation**: "The weather should clear up by tomorrow."

## Synonyms
- Ought to
- Need to
- Be supposed to
- Presumably
- Reasonably

## Antonyms
- Shouldn't (negative form)
- Wouldn't (conditional form)
- Could not (impossibility)
