## 读音
/ˈwiːk.ɛnd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一周中的最后两天，通常是星期六和星期日，人们在这两天休息或娱乐。例如: "I have no plans for the weekend."
- **动词**: 休假，度过周末。例如: "They decided to weekend in the countryside."

## 短语
- "Have a great weekend" 用来祝福别人周末愉快。
- "Long weekend" 指包含公共假期在内的超过两天的周末。
- "Weekend getaway" 一次短暂的周末旅行或度假。

## 例句
- 名词: "I'm looking forward to relaxing on the weekend."
- 动词: "They're weekending at their beach house."
  
## 同义词
- Weekend can mainly be used as a noun and doesn't have direct synonyms as a verb. However, similar expressions for spending time off include:
- - "take a break"
- - "relax"
- - "enjoy one's days off"
