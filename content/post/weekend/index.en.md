## Pronunciation
/n ˈwɛkˈend/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **weekend** is a period of time typically occurring at the end of a workweek, usually from Friday evening to Sunday night, during which people have leisure time and often engage in recreational activities.

### Verb
To **spend the weekend** (or **have a weekend**) means to allocate that time off for rest, relaxation, or social activities.

### Adjective
- "Weekend getaway" describes a short vacation taken over the weekend.

## Phrases
- "Enjoy your weekend" is a common expression wishing someone a good time.
- "Weekend routine" refers to a person's typical activities on the weekends.
- "Long weekend" is a weekend that is longer than usual, often extending to Monday.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "We planned a fun-filled weekend at the beach."
- **Verb**: "After a busy week, we decided to spend the weekend hiking in the mountains."
- **Adjective**: "She looked forward to her relaxing weekend getaway."
