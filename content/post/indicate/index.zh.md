## 读音
/ɪnˈdaɪteɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 表明；显示：指出或展示事物的迹象或特征。例如: "The rising temperature indicates spring is coming."
  - 暗示；表明意图：表达或暗示某事可能发生或某人的意图。例如: "His smile indicated he was pleased with the news."
  - 指示；指引：给某人提供信息或指导。例如: "The sign indicates that the museum is just around the corner."

## 短语
- "indicate to someone" 意味着向某人表明或指出。例如: "The teacher indicated to the student that he should pay attention."
- "indicate a direction" 指出方向。例如: "The compass indicates north."

## 例句
- "The smoke from the chimney indicates that someone is at home."
- "Her facial expression indicated she was upset about something."
- "The traffic light indicates we should stop."
