## 读音
/ˈθɜːrdli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 第三，其次，第三点。常用来引出一个观点或论据的第三个方面。例如: "Firstly, we discussed the plan; secondly, we considered the budget; thirdly, we talked about potential challenges."

## 短语
- "Thirdly, but no less important" 用来强调尽管是第三个提出的，但同样重要。

## 例句
"First, we need to gather information; second, we must analyze it; thirdly, we should develop a strategy."
