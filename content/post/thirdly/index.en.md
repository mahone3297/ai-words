## Pronunciation
/thɜːdli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a third place or order; as a third point or item in a series. It is often used to indicate a particular stage or rank in a list or sequence.

## Phrases
- "Thirdly, we should consider..." indicates the third point in a list.
- "In conclusion, thirdly..." precedes a final point in a series of arguments.
- "Lastly, but not least, thirdly..." emphasizes a third important point despite being last in the enumeration.

## Example Sentences
- "Firstly, we discussed the problem. Secondly, we brainstormed solutions. Thirdly, we assigned tasks to each member."
- "There are three main reasons: firstly, it's convenient; secondly, it's cost-effective; thirdly, it's environmentally friendly."
- "The steps are: research, plan, and execute. Thirdly, execute your plan with determination."

## Synonyms
- Thirdly
- Third in the series
- Thirdly, and finally
- Lastly (when emphasizing the final point)
- Third on the list
- Thirdly and importantly

Note: "Thirdly" is primarily used as an adverb in this context and does not have separate noun, verb, or other parts of speech forms.
