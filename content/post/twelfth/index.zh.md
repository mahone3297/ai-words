## 读音
/twelfθ/

## 释义
- **数词**: 12的序数词形式，表示顺序。例如: "December is the twelfth month of the year."

## 短语
- "On the twelfth day of Christmas" 是一首传统圣诞歌曲中的歌词，指的是圣诞节后的第十二天。

## 例句
- "Today is my twelfth birthday, and I'm excited to celebrate it with my friends."
- "There are twelve months in a year, and December is the twelfth one."

## 同义词
- 十二th (in the context of numbers, not commonly used as an ordinal)
- Dozenth (非正式，通常指第十二个，但不如twelfth常见)
