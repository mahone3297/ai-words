## Pronunciation
/spəˈrɑː(r)/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A small, typically brown passerine bird with a long tail and a pointed beak, found in open fields and around human habitation, often associated with springtime and chirping songs. Scientific name: Passer domesticus (house sparrow).
- **Verb**: To catch or observe a sparrow, often implying a casual sighting or activity. "I spied a sparrow perched on a branch."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or similar to a sparrow, such as the small size or agility. "The sparrow-like bird nested in the eaves."
