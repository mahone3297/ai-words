## Pronunciation
/ˈwɜːkˌdeɪ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **weekday** refers to any day of the week that is not a Saturday or Sunday, typically considered part of the working or school week. It is often used in contrast to weekends.

### Verb
To **work on weekdays** means to engage in job-related activities or attend classes during normal business or school days.

## Phrases
- "Weekday routine" refers to the daily schedule on non-holiday weekdays.
- "Enjoy your weekdays" is a way to encourage someone to make the most of their work or study time.
- "Meet up on a weekday" suggests scheduling an appointment during regular work hours.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I usually go for a run on weekdays to stay active."
- **Verb**: "My weekdays are packed with meetings and deadlines."
- **Sentence**: "We decided to catch up over lunch on a weekday when we both had a break."

## Synonyms
- Mon-Fri (Monday through Friday)
- Workdays
- Business days
- Regular days
- School days

## Antonyms
- Weekends (Saturday and Sunday)
