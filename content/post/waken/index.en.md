## Pronunciation
/wəˈkeɪn/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To rouse or awaken someone from sleep or a state of unconsciousness; to stimulate or invigorate. Also used figuratively to bring to mind or recollection. For example: "The morning sun wakened her from her slumber."
- **Noun**: The act of awakening or the state of being awake. For example: "The sound of birds wakened him early."

## Phrases
- "Waken up" means to rouse someone from sleep.
- "He was just wakened by a loud noise."
- "Her voice wakened memories of their childhood."

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The baby was gently wakened by her mother's soothing lullaby."
- **Noun**: "After a long night, he needed a few minutes to fully waken before starting his day."
- **Idiomatic**: "The news of the emergency wakened the entire town."
