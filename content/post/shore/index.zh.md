## 读音
/ʃɔːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 海岸，湖岸，河岸；陆地与水相接的地方。例如: "They walked along the beautiful sandy shore of the lake."
- **动词**: 岸上停靠（船只）。例如: "The ship will shore at midnight."

## 短语
- "on the shore" 指位于海岸或河边。例如: "The seagulls were flying over the shore."
- "shore up" 意为支撑，加固。例如: "They had to shore up the damaged wall to prevent it from collapsing."

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were building sandcastles on the shore."
- 动词: "The boat slowly shored, allowing passengers to disembark."
