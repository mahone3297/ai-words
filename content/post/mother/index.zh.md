## 读音
/ˈmʌðər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 母亲，指一个人的生母或者比喻上的养育者。例如: "She is a devoted mother to her three children."
- **名词**: 指事物的起源或基础，如“发源地”。例如: "The mother company founded several subsidiaries around the world."

## 短语
- "Mother Nature" 指大自然，地球或宇宙的创造力量。
- "Like mother, like daughter" 表示女儿和母亲有相似的性格或行为。

## 例句
- 名词: "She spent hours talking to her mother on the phone."
- 名词: "The mother ship was waiting for the smaller boats to return."
