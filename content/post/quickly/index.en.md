## Pronunciation
/ˈkwɪkli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Characterizing or describing the manner in which an action is performed. It indicates speed or rapidity.
- **Adjective**: Fast or promptly; swift. For example: "She quickly finished her work."

## Phrases
- "Quickly run to catch the bus"
- "He reacted quickly to avoid the accident"
- "She ate her breakfast quickly so she could leave on time"

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "He answered the phone quickly, not wanting to miss the call."
- **Adjective**: "The athlete completed the race in record time, running quickly across the finish line."
- **Comparative**: "She is quicker than her sister at solving math problems."

- Swiftly
- Promptly
