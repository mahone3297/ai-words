## 读音
/təˈpɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 话题，主题，论题。例如: "The discussion centered around the topic of climate change."
   - （报纸、节目等的）版块，栏目。例如: "The news program had a segment on health topics."

## 短语
- "Change the topic" 意味着转移讨论的主题。
- "热门话题" 指人们广泛讨论或关注的问题。例如: "COVID-19 has been a hot topic for the past year."

## 例句
- 名词: "At the meeting, we will discuss several important topics, including budget and marketing strategies."
- "Her research focuses on the topic of artificial intelligence."

- Issue
