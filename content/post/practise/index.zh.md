## 读音
/prɪˈæktɪs/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 练习；实习。例如: "I need to practise my piano skills every day."
  - 实行；执行。例如: "The new law will be practised from next month."

## 短语
- "practise makes perfect" 意为熟能生巧。
- "in practice" 表示在实际操作中，实际上。例如: "In practice, the theory is often more difficult than it sounds."

## 例句
- 动词: "He spends hours practising his free throws before each game."
- "In many countries, it's not actually practised as much as it's preached."
