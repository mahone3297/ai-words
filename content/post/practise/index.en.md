## Pronunciation
/prəˈsɪst/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The act of practicing or the state of being practiced; regular exercise or training in a skill or activity. For example: "She spends hours every day practising her violin."
2. A session of practice or training. For example: "The football team had a two-hour practice session this morning."

### Verb
1. To engage in the act of training or rehearsing in order to improve one's skills or performance. For example: "He practises his guitar every day to prepare for the concert."
2. To perform or carry out (an activity) regularly or habitually. For example: "She practises good time management."

### Adjective
Refers to something that has been done or experienced repeatedly, often to gain proficiency or skill. For example: "Her practised eye spotted the error in the code."
