## 读音
/ten/

## 释义
- **数词**: 10，表示数字十。例如: "There are ten fingers on one hand."

## 短语
- "Ten of something" 表示某物中的十个。例如: "I have ten apples in my basket."
- "Up to ten" 表示从一到十的范围。例如: "Count from one to ten."

## 例句
- 数词: "The number ten is an even digit."
- "She scored ten points in the basketball game."

## 同义词
- Ten can be replaced with "X" in Roman numerals.
- 在某些语境下，"a dozen" 或 "twice five" 可以表示十，但通常不作为直接的同义数词使用。
