## 读音
/pleɪn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 简单的，不复杂的。例如: "She prefers a plain white shirt over a patterned one."
- **形容词**: 平坦的，无起伏的。例如: "The land was so plain that you could see for miles."
- **形容词**: 清楚的，明白的。例如: "The instructions were plain and easy to follow."
- **名词**: 平原，广阔的平坦地区。例如: "The prairie is a vast plain in North America."
- **副词**: 直接地，坦率地。例如: "He spoke to her plain, without any sugarcoating."

## 短语
- "plain and simple" 意为简单明了，不复杂。
- "in plain sight" 意为在显眼的地方，容易看到。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The design of the room was very plain, with no fancy decorations."
- 形容词: "The road ahead was plain, allowing us to drive quickly."
- 副词: "She spoke to him plain, telling him exactly what she thought."
