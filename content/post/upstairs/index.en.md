## Pronunciation
/əˈstɔːrz/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Referring to the location or direction above the current level, typically in a building. For example: "I need to go upstairs to my room."
- **Preposition**: Used to indicate that something is located or situated above the ground floor or a lower level. For example: "The meeting is on the second floor, upstairs from the entrance."

## Phrases
- "Upstairs, down the hall" points to a specific location within a building.
- "Take the stairs to the second floor, it's upstairs." 
- "She lives upstairs, on the top floor."

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "I left my keys upstairs, I'll be right back."
- **Preposition**: "The library is upstairs, where you can find the fiction section."

## Synonyms
- Higher up
- Above
- On the upper level
- In the upper part
- Ascending

## Antonyms
- Downstairs
