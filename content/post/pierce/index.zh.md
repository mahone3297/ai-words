## 读音
/pɪərs/

## 释义
- **动词**: 刺穿，穿透。例如: "The arrow pierced the target."
- **名词**: 穿孔，刺孔。例如: "She has a piercing in her nose."
- **名词**: （码头上的）突堤，栈桥。例如: "The ferry docked at the end of the pier."

## 短语
- "pierce through" 穿透，刺透。例如: "The sword pierced through the armor."
- "ear piercing" 耳洞。例如: "She got her ears pierced last week."

## 例句
- 动词: "The spear pierced the knight's shield."
- 名词: "The child was fascinated by the colorful lights on the pier."
- 名词: "She sat on the pier, enjoying the sea breeze."
