## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a successful manner; effectively. For example: "She completed the task successfully."
- **Adjective**: Accomplished with success; resulting in a favorable outcome. For example: "The project was successfully completed."
- **Verb**: To perform or accomplish something with success. For example: "He successfully defended his thesis."

## Phrases
