## 读音
/səkˈsɛs·fəli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 成功地；有效地。表示某事完成得圆满或者达到了预期目标。

## 短语
- "to carry out successfully" 成功地执行
- "to accomplish something successfully" 成功地完成某事

## 例句
- "She completed her project successfully, meeting all the deadlines." (她成功地完成了项目，赶上了所有的截止日期。)
- "The operation was performed successfully without any complications." (手术成功进行，没有出现任何并发症。)

## 同义词
- Successfully 的同义副词有：
  - Effectively
  - Efficiently
  - Triumphantly
  - fruitfully
  - Accomplished
