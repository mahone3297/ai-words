## 读音
/ˈsʌbstəns/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 物质，实体；具有实际存在或物理特性的任何东西。例如: "Water is a chemical substance."
- **名词**: 基本成分，本质；事物的根本属性或重要部分。例如: "The substance of the speech was about climate change."
- **名词**: 药物，毒品（非法或医用）。例如: "The abuse of certain substances can lead to addiction."

## 短语
- "Substance abuse" 指药物滥用或成瘾行为。
- "Mental substance" 可以指精神或心理的本质。
- "Chemical substance" 特指化学物质。

## 例句
- 名词: "The substance found in the package turned out to be illegal drugs."
- 名词: "The philosopher argued that mind and body are two distinct substances."
