## 读音
/sɪˈmələr/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 相似的，相像的。例如: "These two paintings are very similar in style."
- **副词**: 相似地，类似地。例如: "They performed the task similarly, with slight differences."

## 短语
- "similar to" 表示与...相似。例如: "Her situation is similar to mine."
- "in similarity with" 表示在...方面相似。例如: "Their opinions were in similarity with ours."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The twin brothers have similar features."
- 副词: "Their approaches to problem-solving are similar, but not identical."

## 同义词
- Alike
- Comparable
- Identical (在某些情况下)
- Equivalent
- Near-identical
