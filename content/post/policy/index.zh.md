## 读音
/pəˈlɪsɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 政策：国家、组织或个人遵循的规则或计划。例如: "The company's hiring policy has changed."
  - 保险单：一份合同，其中保险公司同意在特定情况下支付或赔偿损失。例如: "He bought a life insurance policy to protect his family."

## 短语
- "Policy maker" 指制定政策的人。
- "Insurance policy" 指保险合同。
- "Foreign policy" 指一个国家对外关系的方针和策略。

## 例句
- 名词: "The government's new environmental policy aims to reduce carbon emissions."
- "She reviewed her health insurance policy to understand the coverage."
