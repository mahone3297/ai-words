## Pronunciation
/pəˈlɪsi/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A plan or course of action determined by an organization or government**: *The company's new privacy policy.*
2. **A set of rules or principles**: *The school policy on tardiness.*
3. **A position or stance on a particular issue**: *Her foreign policy is focused on diplomacy.*

### Verb
1. **To establish or adopt a course of action**: *The government will soon policy on climate change measures.*
2. **To direct or guide (a course of action)**: *The CEO policies the company's strategic decisions.*

## Phrases
- "Follow the company policy" means to adhere to established rules or procedures.
- "Policy change" indicates a modification or update to a previously implemented rule.
- "Public policy" refers to actions taken by governments to address societal issues.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: *The government's new policy on renewable energy is expected to boost the economy.*
- **Verb**: *The CEO policies that all employees attend mandatory training sessions.*
- **Phrasal Verb**: *The policy shift led to significant improvements in workplace safety.*
