## 读音
/kiːp/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    - 保持；维持：保持某事物的状态不变。例如: "I need to keep my house clean."
    - 保存；保留：不丢弃或使用，以备将来之需。例如: "She keeps old photographs in a box."
    - 遵守；遵循：按照规定或承诺行事。例如: "You must keep your promise."
    - 照顾；看护：照管或监护某人或某物。例如: "Can you keep an eye on my dog while I'm away?"
    - 继续；持续：做某事而不间断。例如: "We'll keep dancing until midnight."

## 短语
- "Keep fit" 保持健康。
- "Keep quiet" 保持安静。
- "Keep up with" 跟上，不落后于。例如: "It's hard to keep up with the latest technology."

## 例句
- 动词: 
    - "Please keep your voice down in the library."
    - "He's trying to keep his job by working extra hours."
    - "She keeps a journal of her daily thoughts."

- Maintain
