## 读音
/pəˈsəbiləti/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 可能性，潜在性。指某事可能发生或成为现实的状态或机会。例如: "There is a possibility of rain tomorrow."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 可能的，有希望的。用来描述某事在逻辑上或实际中有实现的机会。例如: "It's possible that he will accept the job offer."

## 短语
- "The possibility of something happening" 指某事发生的可能性。
- "Consider all possibilities" 意味着要全面考虑所有可能的情况。

## 例句
- 名词: "We need to prepare for every possibility in case of an emergency."
- 形容词: "There is a small possibility that we might change our plans."
