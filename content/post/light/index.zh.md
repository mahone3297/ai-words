## 读音
/laɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 光，光线：The sun provides us with natural light.
2. 灯，照明设备：Please turn on the light in the room.

- **形容词**:
1. 轻的，不重的：This bag is light and easy to carry.
2. 清淡的，不浓的：She prefers light coffee without sugar.
3. 易于理解的：The book has a light style, suitable for beginners.

- **动词**:
1. 点燃，照亮：He lit a candle in the dark room.
2. 变得明亮：The room instantly lighted up when the lights came on.

- **副词**:
1. 轻轻地，轻松地：She moved light on her feet.

## 短语
- "light up" 照亮，使变得明亮：The city really lights up at night.
- "in the light of" 根据，考虑到：In the light of new evidence, they changed their decision.

## 例句
- 名词: "The moonlight provided just enough light for them to see."
- 形容词: "She wore a light blue dress for the summer wedding."
- 动词: "He lit a fire to keep warm during the camping trip."
- 副词: "She danced light and gracefully across the stage."
