## 读音
/ɪmˈplɔɪiː/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 受雇于公司或组织的工作人员，雇员。例如: "The company has a large number of employees working in various departments."
- **动词 (v)**: 通常不作为动词使用，但在某些语境下可表示雇用或使用某人的服务。例如: "The startup is looking to employee more developers to expand their team."

## 短语
- "Employee benefits" 指的是公司为员工提供的福利，如医疗保险和退休计划。
- "Full-time employee" 是指每周工作40小时或更长时间的员工。
- "Part-time employee" 是指工作时间少于全职员工的员工。

## 例句
- 名词: "The manager discussed the new project with all her employees."
- 动词: "The restaurant owner decided to employee a new chef for the busy season."
