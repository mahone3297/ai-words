## 读音
/ˈtɜːrkɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 鸡，火鸡，尤其是指感恩节或圣诞节时烹制的大鸟。例如: "They served a delicious roasted turkey for Thanksgiving dinner."
   2. (非正式) 失败者，笨蛋，蠢货。例如: "He made such a mess of things; he's a real turkey."

## 短语
- "turkey shoot" 意为易得的猎物，也可比喻为轻而易举的任务或比赛。
- "to turkey out" 在紧张或关键时刻退缩，失败。

## 例句
- 名词: "The family gathered around the table to enjoy the centerpiece, a huge stuffed turkey."
- (非正式) 形容词: "That movie was a total turkey; it flopped at the box office."

## 同义词
- 鸡：fowl, chicken
- 笨蛋：fool, idiot, dunce
- 失败的作品：flop, bomb, dud
