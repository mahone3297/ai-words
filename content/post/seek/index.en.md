## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Seek**: A verb that refers to the act of searching for something or someone, often with a purpose or intention. (e.g., "He sought out his old friend.")
- **Search**: An extended effort to find something or someone. (e.g., "The police were searching for the missing child.")

### Verb
- **To seek**: To look for, attempt to find, or pursue with determination. (e.g., "She sought a better job opportunity.")
- **Pursue**: To follow closely in order to catch or obtain something. (e.g., "He pursued his passion for music.")

### Adjective
- **Seeking**: In the process of looking for or desiring something. (e.g., "She was seeking adventure.")

## Phrases
- "Seeking advice" implies asking for guidance.
- "Seeking employment" is a common phrase for job hunting.
- "Seek and ye shall find" is a biblical proverb suggesting that if one actively looks for something, they will discover it.
