## 读音
/siːk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 寻求；寻找。例如: "She is seeking a new job opportunity."  
2. 请求；要求。例如: "They sought help from the local authorities."
3. 努力；力求。例如: "He seeks to improve his skills every day."

## 短语
- "Seek out" 意为找出或找到（某人或某物）。例如: "We sought out the best restaurants in the city."
- "Seek refuge" 意为寻求庇护或避难。例如: "Refugees are seeking refuge in neighboring countries."

## 例句
- 动词: 
1. "He is seeking information on the project."
2. "She sought advice from her mentor."
3. "They seek to understand the root cause of the problem."
