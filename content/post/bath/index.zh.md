## 读音
/bɑːθ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 浴缸或沐浴的地方。例如: "I need to take a bath after a long day."
   2. 洗澡的动作或过程。例如: "She enjoys a relaxing bath every evening."
- **动词**: 给…洗澡或在浴缸里洗身体。例如: "The mother bathed her baby in warm water."

## 短语
- "bathe in the sun" 意为在阳光下晒太阳。
- "have a bath" 或 "take a bath" 都是指洗澡。

## 例句
- 名词: "The bathroom has a large bath for soaking in."
- 动词: "The dog needs to be bathed regularly to keep its coat clean."

- Shower
- Wash
- Bathe oneself
- Cleanse
