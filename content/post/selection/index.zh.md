## 读音
/ˈsɛlekʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 选择；挑选：从一系列可能的人、事物或选项中进行的挑选。例如: "She made her selection from the menu."
  - 部分；选集：某一主题或作者的作品精选。例如: "The museum's latest exhibition is a selection of Impressionist paintings."
  - 招聘；选拔：在面试或竞赛中挑选出最佳候选人。例如: "The team's selection process for new members is rigorous."

## 短语
- "make a selection" 选择或决定一个选项。
- "selection criteria" 选择标准或准则。
- "random selection" 随机选择，无特定规律的选取。

## 例句
- 名词: "The store offers a wide selection of clothing for all ages."
- 名词: "The bookshop has a good selection of second-hand books."
