## 读音
/mɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 1. 磨坊，尤指水磨或面粉厂。例如: "The old mill by the river has been converted into a restaurant."
  - 2. 千（公制单位，常用于表示距离或数量）。例如: "It's about two miles (3.2 kilometers) from my house to the mill."

- **动词**:
  - 磨碎，碾磨。例如: "The miller milled the wheat into flour."

## 短语
- "to grind at the mill" 意味着做繁重的工作。
- "the mill of justice" 常用来比喻法律或司法程序的缓慢进程。

## 例句
- 名词: "The watermill in the countryside is a historic landmark."
- 动词: "They mill grains into different types of flours for baking."

## 同义词
- 磨坊：factory, workshop, processing plant
- 磨碎：crush, grind, powder, reduce to dust

请注意，"mill"作为名词时有磨坊和千这两个意思，作为动词时则表示磨碎。同义词根据具体上下文可能有所不同。
