## 读音
/steɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 股份；投资；权益：An amount of money or other assets invested in a business, venture, or gamble.
  - 利害关系；赌注：Something at risk, especially in a contest or competition.

- **动词**:
  - 把（钱）当作赌注；投资：To risk (money) on an outcome, especially in gambling.
  - 在…上打赌：To put something at stake; to risk something on the outcome of something.

## 短语
- "Have a stake in something" 意味着对某事有利益关系或关心。
- "Stake out" 表示监视、守候或占据某个位置。

## 例句
- 名词: "He bought a large stake in the startup and became one of its major shareholders."
- 动词: "They staked a considerable amount on the horse race."
- 短语: "The detectives staked out the suspect's house for hours."
