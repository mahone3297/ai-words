## 读音
/rеɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 种族，人类的种族群体。例如: "People of different races live together in this city."
   2. 比赛，竞赛。例如: "The marathon is a long-distance running race."
   3. 种类，类别。例如: "There are various car models from different races."

- **动词**: 参加比赛。例如: "He raced his friends to the finish line."

## 短语
- "Race against time" 在限定时间内行动或完成任务。例如: "They were racing against time to complete the project."
- "Races of the world" 指世界上的各种种族。

## 例句
- 名词: "The Olympic Games bring together athletes from all races."
- 动词: "She decided to race her brother to the park."
