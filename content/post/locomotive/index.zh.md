## 读音
/ˈləʊkəməʊtɪv/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种交通工具，通常是火车头，用来牵引列车。例如: "The steam locomotive pulled the passenger cars along the tracks."
- **形容词**: 用于描述与运动或移动有关的，特别是指能自身提供动力的。例如: "The locomotive force of the engine powered the ship through rough seas."

## 短语
- "Locomotive engine" 指火车头的发动机。
- "Locomotive power" 指提供动力以使物体移动的能力。

## 例句

- 形容词: "The new model has a more efficient locomotive system, allowing for faster speeds."
