## 读音
/prəˈmɪnənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 显著的，突出的，重要的。例如: "He played a prominent role in the project."
- **名词**: 有名望的人，杰出人物。例如: "She is a prominent figure in the art world."

## 短语
- "prominent feature" 指的是显著的特征或特点。
- "prominent position" 意味着显眼或重要的位置。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The mountain peak is so prominent that it can be seen from miles away."
- 名词: "He is a prominent scientist whose work has influenced many others."
