## Pronunciation
/prəˈmɪnənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of being easily seen or noticed; prominent; outstanding. For example: "The prominent landmark was visible from miles away."
- **Adverb**: In a prominent manner; conspicuously. For example: "She stood out prominently in the crowd."

## Phrases
- "Be prominently featured" means to be given a noticeable position or attention.
- "Prominently displayed" indicates something is put on show in a clear and noticeable way.
- "Prominently remembered" suggests a person or event is recalled with great clarity.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The prominent leader's speech inspired the crowd."
- **Adverb**: "The award was prominently displayed on the wall."
- "Her voice was prominent in the choir performance."
