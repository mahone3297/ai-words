## 读音
/nail/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 小钉子，通常由金属制成，用于固定物体。例如: "He used a hammer to drive the nails into the wood."
   - 趾甲或指甲。例如: "She painted her nails a bright red color."
   - （美式足球）得分，尤指通过踢球进入对方得分区。例如: "The kicker scored a field goal with a well-placed nail."

- **动词**:
   - 固定或钉住（某物）。例如: "The carpenter nailed the two pieces of wood together."
   - 完成或成功。例如: "She nailed the job interview and got the position."

## 短语
- "Nail down" 意味着确定或固定某事。例如: "We need to nail down the details of the project before starting."
- "On the nail" 表示立即或直接支付。例如: "I paid the full amount on the nail."

## 例句
- 名词: "He picked up a handful of nails from the toolbox."
- 动词: "She nailed the high note during her performance."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但在某些语境下可接受）: "Nailed it! That was the right answer."

## 同义词
