## 读音
/səˈtisfæktəri/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 能令人满意的，足够的，合格的。例如: "The service was satisfactory, but not exceptional."
- **名词**: 满意，满足。例如: "A feeling of satisfactory accomplishment after finishing a project."

## 短语
- "to one's satisfaction" 意为达到某人的满意程度。
- "satisfactory performance" 指的是表现良好或达到了预期的标准。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The hotel room was clean and comfortable, providing a satisfactory stay."
- 名词: "She felt a deep sense of satisfactory when she finally solved the puzzle."
