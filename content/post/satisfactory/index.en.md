## Pronunciation
/səˈtɪsflɪŋ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Meeting or achieving a standard of acceptability or adequacy; fulfilling expectations. For example: "The results were quite satisfactory."
- **Adverb**: To a satisfactory degree; adequately. For example: "The work was done satisfactorily."

## Phrases
- "Satisfactory performance" means meeting or exceeding expectations in terms of quality or accomplishment.
- "A satisfactory answer" indicates a response that is considered adequate or appropriate.
- "He's a satisfactory employee" implies that the person is competent and meets job requirements.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The customer was pleased with the satisfactory service they received."
- **Adverb**: "She completed the project satisfactorily within the given time frame."
- "His grades were satisfactory, indicating he had put in enough effort."
