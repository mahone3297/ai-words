## 读音
/main/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 主要的，重要的。例如: "The main reason for his success was hard work."
- **名词**: 主体，主要部分。例如: "The main course of the meal was a delicious steak."
- **副词**: 主要地，主要。例如: "She spends most of her time working on the main project."

## 短语
- "in the main" 意味着基本上，大体上。例如: "The book is good in the main, but some parts are boring."
- "main event" 指主要事件或重要活动。例如: "The boxing match was the main event of the evening."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The main goal of the company is to increase customer satisfaction."
- 名词: "The river's main tributary flows from the mountains."
- 副词: "Most of the employees work mainly on weekdays."
