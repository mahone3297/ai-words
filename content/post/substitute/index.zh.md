## 读音
/ˈsʌbstɪ tjʊtʃuːt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 替代品，替补者。例如: "We need a substitute for sugar in this recipe."
- **动词**: 代替，替换。例如: "When the lead actor was sick, they substituted a stand-in."
- **形容词**: 代替的，代用的。例如: "The substitute teacher took over the class."

## 短语
- "Substitute one thing for another" 意味着用一件事物替换另一件事物。
- "Substitute player" 在体育比赛中指的是替补队员。

## 例句
- 名词: "The store was out of almond milk, so I bought soy milk as a substitute."
- 动词: "The chef had to substitute red wine with apple cider vinegar in the sauce."
- 形容词: "The substitute teacher did an excellent job managing the class."
