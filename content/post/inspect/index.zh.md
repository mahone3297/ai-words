## 读音
/ɪnˈspekt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 检查；审视：仔细查看以确定质量、状况或遵守规定。例如: "The customs officer inspected our luggage for any forbidden items."
2. 视察；审查：对某事物或情况进行正式检查。例如: "The teacher will inspect the classroom to ensure it is clean and tidy."
3. 监督：监督工作或活动以确保其正确进行。例如: "The construction site is regularly inspected for safety."

## 短语
- "inspect for" 检查...的存在或状态。例如: "Inspect for any damage before signing for the delivery."
- "inspect something thoroughly" 彻底检查某物。例如: "They'll need to inspect the car thoroughly before deciding to buy it."

## 例句
- 她仔细地检查了新买的家具，确保没有瑕疵。 "She inspected the new furniture carefully to make sure there were no defects."
- 警察在事故现场进行了彻底的调查。 "The police inspected the scene of the accident thoroughly."
