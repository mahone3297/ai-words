## 读音
/pərˈtʃɑːs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 购买；购得的东西。例如: "I made a purchase of a new laptop yesterday."
- **动词**: 购买；购置。例如: "She decided to purchase a house in the countryside."

## 短语
- "make a purchase" 意为购买某物。
- "purchase order" 在商业中指的是购买订单。
- "purchase price" 指的是购买物品的价格。

## 例句
- 名词: "The purchase he made turned out to be faulty, so he asked for a refund."
- 动词: "He plans to purchase a car as soon as he saves enough money."

## 同义词
- buy
- acquire
- obtain
- procure
- get (in the context of buying)
