## 读音
/ˈmaɪnəs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 在数学中，减号，表示减法。例如: "In the equation 5 minus 3 equals 2, 'minus' is the operator."
- **介词**: 表示减少或排除。例如: "I have ten apples, but minus three that are rotten, I have seven good ones."

## 短语
- "Minus something" 表示从总数中减去某个数量。
- "Minus point" 在评分或评价中表示负面的方面。

## 例句
- 名词: "The temperature dropped to minus 10 degrees Celsius last night."
- 介词: "My bank balance is minus $20 after paying the bills."

## 同义词
- Subtract
- Less
- Minus sign (-)
