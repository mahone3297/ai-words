## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The act or process of changing or altering something to produce a different form, result, or version. 
2. A variant or different form of a word, phrase, or idea. 
3. A variety or diversity within a group or system. 

### Verb
1. To change or modify (a form, design, or system) to create a new version or adaptation. 
2. To take on a different form or appearance.

### Adjective
1. Characterized by diversity or change. 
2. Offering alternative versions or forms.
