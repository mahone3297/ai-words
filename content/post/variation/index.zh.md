## 读音
/veərɪˈeɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 变化；变种：事物的不同形式或版本。例如: "There are various variations of the story."
  - 变动；变化率：在一定范围内的变化或波动。例如: "The stock market showed significant variation throughout the day."

## 短语
- "Variation on a theme" 意为基于同一主题的变化或不同版本。
- "Statistical variation" 指统计数据中的变化或波动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The chef created several variations of the classic dish using local ingredients."
- 名词: "The experiment showed small variations in temperature over time."
