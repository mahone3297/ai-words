## 读音
/sərˈveɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 对某事物的正式检查或研究；调查。例如: "The company conducted a customer satisfaction survey."
- **动词**: 进行调查或查看。例如: "The archaeologists surveyed the ancient site for clues."
- **形容词**: 用于描述提供全面视图或概览的。例如: "A survey course covers a broad range of topics."

## 短语
- "Survey the land" 指测量或评估一片土地。
- "Customer satisfaction survey" 是用来评估顾客对产品或服务满意程度的问卷。
- "Pre-construction survey" 是在建筑开始前对现场进行的评估。

## 例句
- 名词: "The survey revealed that most people prefer online shopping."
- 动词: "They hired a team to survey the area for potential building sites."
- 形容词: "The survey course on world history provides an overview of major events."
