## 读音
/əˈtæk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 攻击，进攻。例如: "The army launched a surprise attack on the enemy's base."
    - 病发，发作。例如: "She suffered a sudden heart attack."
  
- **动词**:
    - 袭击，攻击。例如: "The pirates attacked the merchant ship without warning."
    - 批评，指责。例如: "The journalist attacked the government's policies in his article."

## 短语
- "attack on" 表示对...的攻击。例如: "There was an attack on the city's main power station."
- "under attack" 表示正受到攻击。例如: "The castle was under attack from the enemy forces."

## 例句
- 名词: "The football team launched a series of attacks on the opponent's goal."
- 动词: "The hacker attacked the company's computer system and stole sensitive data."
