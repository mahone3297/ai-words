## 读音
/plæn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 设计或安排：详细考虑并制定的策略或行动方案。例如: "We need to have a solid plan for the project."
  - 图纸或蓝图：展示建筑、工程或其他设计的图形表示。例如: "The architect presented the building plan to the client."
  - 计划或打算：打算要做的事情。例如: "I have a plan to finish my work early."

- **动词**:
  - 规划或设计：为某个目标或目的制定计划。例如: "She is planning a surprise birthday party for her friend."
  - 打算或意图：决定做某事。例如: "I plan to travel abroad next year."

## 短语
- "make plans" 制定计划
- "follow a plan" 遵循计划
- "change one's plans" 改变计划

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's new marketing plan includes several innovative strategies."
- 动词: "They are planning to meet at the park in the afternoon."
- 没有作为感叹词的常见用法。
