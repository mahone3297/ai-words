## Pronunciation
/plæn/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A plan** is a detailed proposal or arrangement for achieving a particular goal or purpose, often involving a series of steps or actions. [Oxford](https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/plan)
2. A map or diagram showing the layout of a building, area, or route. [Cambridge](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/plan)

### Verb
1. To devise or make a plan for something. *I'm planning a surprise birthday party.*
2. To prepare or organize oneself for a particular activity or event. *She planned her day carefully.*

## Phrases
- "Draw up a plan" means to create a detailed plan.
- "Follow a plan" means to execute a plan according to its outlined steps.
- "Change of plans" indicates a deviation from the originally intended course of action.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The architect presented a detailed plan for the new office building."
- **Verb**: "We need to plan our vacation itinerary before we leave."
- **Action**: "He meticulously planned each step of his project."
