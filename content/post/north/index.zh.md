## 读音
/nɔːθ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 地理上的北方；北方向。例如: "They live in a house facing north."
- **形容词**: 在北方的；朝北的。例如: "The north window gets the least amount of sunlight."

## 短语
- "Go north" 意为向北行进或移动。
- "Northern hemisphere" 指北半球。

## 例句
- 名词: "The compass pointed towards the north."
- 形容词: "The north side of the street is usually colder in winter."

## 同义词
- Northern
- Upward (在某些上下文中)
- Arctic (特指北极地区)
- Antipodean (相对于南半球的)
- Boreal (地理学上指北温带)
