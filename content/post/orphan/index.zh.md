## 读音
/ˈɔːr.fən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 没有父母的孩子；孤儿。例如: "The orphanage took care of many children who had lost their parents."
- **形容词**: 无父无母的；孤儿的。例如: "The orphan boy was very grateful for the support he received."

## 短语
- "orphaned child" 指失去双亲的孩子。
- "orphan drug" 在医药领域，指治疗罕见病的药物。
- "orphan work" 在版权法中，指的是无法找到版权所有者的作品。

## 例句
- 名词: "The charity worked tirelessly to provide for the needs of the orphans."
- 形容词: "She wrote a book about her experiences growing up as an orphan."

## 同义词
- Abandoned child
- Parentless
- Unparented
- Fatherless/motherless
- Waif (通常指流浪的孤儿)
