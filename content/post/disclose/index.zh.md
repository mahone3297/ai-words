## 读音

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 公开透露；揭露：例如: "The company was forced to disclose the details of the deal."
  2. 使显露，揭示：例如: "The therapist helped him disclose his deepest emotions."

## 短语
- "disclose information" 泄露信息
- "disclose one's intentions" 表明意图
