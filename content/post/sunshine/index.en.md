## Pronunciation
/sʌnˈʃaɪn/

## Definitions
### Noun
**Sunshine** refers to the light and warmth from the sun, which is vital for life on Earth. It can also symbolize happiness, positivity, and bright energy.

### Verb (informal)
To "enjoy sunshine" means to bask in the pleasant warmth and brightness of the sun, often metaphorically implying a sense of pleasure or relaxation.

## Phrases
- "Bask in the sunshine"
- "A day filled with sunshine"
- "Seeking the sunshine" (to go outside on a sunny day for leisure or to improve mood)

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children played happily in the sunshine at the park."
- **Verb (informal)**: "After a long, dreary winter, she loved to enjoy the sunshine on her daily walks."
- **Phrases**: "She decided to spend her afternoon basking in the sunshine by the beach."
