## 读音
/sʌnʃaɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 太阳光，晴朗的天气。例如: "The children played outside in the sunshine."
- **形容词**: 充满阳光的，令人愉快的。例如: "She had a sunshine smile that brightened up the room."

## 短语
- "bask in the sunshine" 意为在阳光下取暖或享受。
- "sunshine state" 通常指的是阳光充足的州，如美国的佛罗里达州。

## 例句
- 名词: "The garden was filled with the warmth of the early morning sunshine."
- 形容词: "Her sunny disposition made everyone feel at ease."
