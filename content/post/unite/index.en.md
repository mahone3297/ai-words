## Definitions
### Noun
- **Unite** (noun): A collective or unified group, often referring to a joining together of people, forces, or ideas. For example: "The diverse nations united under a common cause."

### Verb
- **To unite** (verb): To come together as one, merge, or join forces. For example: "Countries around the world united to combat climate change."
- **Combine** (verb): To unite or join two or more things, often in a practical sense. For example: "The companies decided to unite their resources to increase efficiency."

### Adjective
- **United** (adjective): Joined as one, in a collective or harmonious manner. For example: "The United Nations represents a united effort to promote peace worldwide."

### Adverb
- **Unitedly** (adverb): In a united or concerted manner. For example: "The athletes marched unitedly during the opening ceremony."
