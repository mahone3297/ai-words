## 读音

## 释义
- **形容词**: 淘气的，顽皮的，不守规矩的。例如: "The naughty child kept pulling the dog's tail."
- **名词**: (口语)淘气鬼，顽皮的人。例如: "He was such a naughty when he was young."

## 短语
- "naughty behavior" 指淘气的行为。
- "naughty boy/girl" 用来形容淘气的小孩。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The teacher had to scold the naughty children in class."
- 名词: "My little brother is such a naughty, always getting into mischief."
