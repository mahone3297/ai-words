## Word: Naughty

## Pronunciation
/ˈnɔːti/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Misbehaving or disobedient, especially in a playful or mildly disrespectful manner. For example: "The child was being naughty and wouldn't listen to his teacher."
  - *Extended Definition*: Can also refer to something that is slightly wicked or risqué; not proper or socially acceptable. E.g., "The comedian told a few naughty jokes."

### Example Sentences
- **Adjective (misbehaving)**: "The puppy chewed on the couch cushions when he was being naughty."
- **Adjective (risqué)**: "Her sense of humor tends to be a bit naughty, which sometimes catches people off guard."

## Phrases
- "Naughty but nice": Describes something or someone who is mischievous or slightly improper, yet still attractive or appealing.
- "Get into mischief": To engage in naughty behavior or actions.
  
## Synonyms
- Mischievous
- Wayward
- Disobedient
- Wicked
- Rude
- Impish
- Rebellious
- Ill-behaved
- Improper
- Indiscreet (in the sense of risqué)
