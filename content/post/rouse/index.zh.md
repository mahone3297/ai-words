## 读音
/raʊz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 唤醒；使醒来。例如: "The sound of the alarm clock roused her from sleep."
  - 激励；鼓舞。例如: "The speech roused the team's spirits before the game."
  - 引起（兴趣、愤怒等）。例如: "His story roused our curiosity."

## 短语
- "rouse oneself" 意味着自己从睡眠或其他状态中振作起来。
- "rouse someone to action" 表示激发某人采取行动。

## 例句
- "The baby was sound asleep, but the loud noise roused her."
- "The coach's words roused the athletes to give their best performance."
- "The news roused public anger about the injustice."
