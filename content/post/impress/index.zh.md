## 读音
/ɪmˈpres/

## 释义
- **动词**: 给予深刻印象；使铭记。例如: "The performance impressed the audience with its brilliance."
- **名词**: 印象；印记。例如: "The child's face wore an impressed look of awe."

## 短语
- "make an impression on sb." 意为给某人留下深刻印象。
- "first impression" 初步印象或初次见面的印象。

## 例句
- 动词: "Her kindness greatly impressed the new colleagues."
- 名词: "The artist took a mold to create a bronze statue from the clay impression."
