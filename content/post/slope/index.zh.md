## 读音
/slop/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 地面或表面的倾斜度；斜坡。例如: "The house is built on a steep slope."
- **动词 (v)**: 使倾斜；有斜度。例如: "The road slopes gently downwards towards the river."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 倾斜的；有坡度的。例如: "slope-shouldered buildings"

## 短语
- "slope downward" 意为向下倾斜。
- "slope upward" 意为向上倾斜。
- "slope angle" 指斜坡的角度。

## 例句
- 名词: "The skier gracefully descended the snowy slope."
- 动词: "The gardener sloped the soil around the plants to direct water away from their roots."
- 形容词: "The roof has a steep slope to prevent snow accumulation."
