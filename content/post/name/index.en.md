## Pronunciation
/neɪm/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A word or set of words by which a person, animal, place, or thing is known, addressed, or referred to. For example: "Her name is Sarah."
- **Verb**: To give a label or title to something or someone; to identify or refer to by a specific appellation. For example: "They named their daughter Emily."

## Phrases
- "In the name of": On behalf of or representing (someone or something). For example: "She acted in the name of justice."
- "To make a name for oneself": To gain recognition or establish a reputation. For example: "He made a name for himself as a talented musician."
- "Call someone by name": To use someone's name when addressing them. For example: "The teacher called each student by name during roll call."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The author's name is printed on the cover of the book."
- **Verb**: "She named her new puppy Charlie after her favorite uncle."
