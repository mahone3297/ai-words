## 读音
/pərˈfɔrməns/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 表演；演出：The theater group's performance of Hamlet was excellent.
  - 执行；表现：The car's performance on the track was impressive.
  - 成绩；成果：Her performance in the exam was outstanding.
  - 展示；展现：The athlete's performance in the high jump was a spectacle.

- **动词 v**:
  - 表演；执行：The band will perform live tonight.
  - 实施；完成：The project is being performed according to plan.

## 短语
- "Give a performance" 意为进行一次表演。
- "Performance review" 是对员工工作表现的评估。
- "Performance art" 指以行为作为艺术表达形式的表演。

## 例句
- 名词: "The dance troupe's performance was the highlight of the festival."
- 动词: "The magician performed several tricks for the audience."
- 介词短语: "Their team's performance in the last season was disappointing."
