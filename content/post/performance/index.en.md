## Pronunciation
/perfərˈməns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act of performing, especially a skill, act, or show; execution. 2. A display or exhibition of one's abilities or skills. 3. [Music] A composition or piece performed by a musician or group. 4. [Theater] A play or show put on for an audience. 5. [Sports] The result or outcome of a game or event. 6. [Psychology] The manner in which someone behaves or functions under specific conditions.

- **Verb**: To carry out or execute a task, skill, or activity. To perform a role or function.

## Phrases
- "Perform a task" means to complete a job or duty.
- "Performance review" is a formal evaluation of an employee's work.
- "Theatrical performance" refers to a live acting show.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She gave a brilliant performance at the concert."
- **Verb**: "It's important to practice regularly to improve your performance."
- **Performance**: "The team's recent performance has been impressive."
