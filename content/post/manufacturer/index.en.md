## Word: Manufacturer

## Pronunciation
/ˌmænjuˈfæk.tʃərər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person or company that produces goods on a large scale using machines or industrial processes, often for sale and distribution. For example: "The car manufacturer announced a new model release this year."

### Phrases
- "Original equipment manufacturer (OEM)": A company that produces parts or products that are purchased by another company and sold under that purchasing company's brand name.
- "Manufacturer's warranty": A guarantee provided by a manufacturer that a product will work as intended for a specific period.
- "Contract manufacturer": A company that produces goods under contract for another company, which retains ownership of the product design and intellectual property.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The electronics manufacturer plans to expand its production facilities overseas."
- "They approached several manufacturers to find the best deal on raw materials."
