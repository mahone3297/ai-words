## 读音
/ˈmænjuːfæktʃərər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 制造商，生产商。指制造商品的个人、公司或工厂。例如: "The car is made by a well-known German manufacturer."
   - 制造过程。例如: "The manufacturer of this product follows strict quality control guidelines."

## 短语
- "Manufacturing industry" 指制造业整体。
- "Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM)" 指生产原始设备的制造商。

## 例句
- 名词: "The manufacturer guarantees their products for two years."
- "The new smartphone is expected to boost the manufacturer's profits."

## 同义词
- Producer
- Maker
- Builder
- Fabricator
- Manufacturer (in the sense of 'production process') can also be synonymous with 'production' or 'manufacturing'.
