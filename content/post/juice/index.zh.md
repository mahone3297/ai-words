## 读音
/juːs/

## 释义
- **名词 n**: 水果或蔬菜榨出的汁液，通常指饮用的果汁。例如: "I love drinking orange juice for breakfast."
- **名词 n**: 情况，处境，尤指复杂或困难的状态。例如: "They found themselves in a difficult juice."

## 短语
- "Juice up" 意味着给某物增加能量或活力，如"juice up a presentation"。
- "Cold press juice" 指使用冷压技术制作的未经高温处理的果汁。

## 例句
- 名词: "She ordered a glass of fresh carrot juice at the café."
- 名词: "His business is in a tough juice due to the economic downturn."
