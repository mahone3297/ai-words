## 读音
/ˈsiːkrət/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 不公开的事情，秘密；机密。例如: "She kept her plans a secret from everyone."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 秘密的，保密的。例如: "The secret meeting was held in a private room."

## 短语
- "keep something a secret" 保密，不告诉别人。例如: "I promised not to reveal his secret."
- "secret agent" 秘密特工，间谍。例如: "James Bond is a famous secret agent in fiction."

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's new product launch is still a closely guarded secret."
- 形容词: "She wore a secret smile, knowing what was about to happen."
