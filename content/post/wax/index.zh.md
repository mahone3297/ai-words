## 读音
/wæks/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 蜡，一种由蜜蜂或其他昆虫产生的具有可塑性的物质，常用于照明、防腐、上光和制造蜡烛。例如: "She used beeswax to make candles."
  - 蜡状物，类似蜡的物质。例如: "The floor had a shiny wax coating."
- **动词**:
  - 给…打蜡，涂蜡。例如: "I need to wax my car to protect the paintwork."
  - 去除毛发（使用蜡）。例如: "She got a bikini wax before going on vacation."

## 短语
- "Wax lyrical" 意思是热情洋溢地谈论或描述。
- "Wax and wane" 表示事物有增有减，时盛时衰。

## 例句
- 名词: "The museum displayed an ancient candle made of pure beeswax."
- 动词: "I'll have to wax the floors to remove the stains."

## 同义词
- 蜡状物: polish, coating
- 打蜡: polish, treat with wax
- 去毛发: depilate, hair removal
