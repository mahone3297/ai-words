## 读音
/ˈpəʊstɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 邮资：费用，通常由寄件人支付，用于递送信件、包裹或其他邮寄物品。例如: "I need some stamps to pay the postage for these letters."
  - 邮费：特定邮件或包裹的运输费用。例如: "The postage for sending this package internationally will be quite high."

## 短语
- "Postage stamp"：邮票，贴在信件上表示已支付邮资的小型纸质标签。
- "Postage due"：欠邮资，当邮件的邮资不足时，需要收件人支付的额外费用。

## 例句
- 名词: "I bought a book online and had to pay extra for the postage."
- "Please remember to include sufficient postage on your envelope."

## 同义词
- Shipping fee
- Delivery charge
- Handling fee (in certain contexts)
- Postal charges
