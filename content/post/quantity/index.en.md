## Pronunciation
/ˈkwɒnəti/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The amount or number of something, often measured or specified: *The recipe calls for a certain quantity of sugar.*
2. A measure or standard of something, such as size or quality: *The car's fuel efficiency is measured in quantities of miles per gallon.*

### Verb
1. To determine or specify the amount or number of something: *To quantify the data is essential for analysis.*
2. To have or possess a specific amount or number: *We have a limited quantity of stock left.*

## Phrases
- "In sufficient quantity" means enough to meet the need.
- "Measure in quantity" involves determining the amount or number of something.
- "Keep track of quantities" implies managing or recording the amounts of items.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The recipe calls for three quantities of eggs."
- **Verb**: "She quantified the ingredients before starting to cook."
- **Verb**: "We need to order more quantities of flour to finish the project."
