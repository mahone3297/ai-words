## 读音
/ˈkwɑːntɪti/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 事物的数量或多少，通常可以度量或计数。例如: "The quantity of water in the tank is sufficient."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 关于数量的，定量的。例如: "They need to make a quantity purchase to get the discount."

## 短语
- "Quantity over quality" 表示重视数量而非质量。
- "Large quantities" 指大量或众多的数量。
- "In quantities" 用于表示以某种数量购买或存在。例如: "The product is available in quantities."

## 例句
- 名词: "Please measure out the correct quantity of flour for the recipe."
- 形容词: "The company focuses on producing goods in large quantities to meet demand."
