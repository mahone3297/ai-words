## Pronunciation
/wɪˈdaɪn/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: 
  - **Transitive verb**: To make wider in size, extent, or scope. For example: "The river widened after heavy rainfall."
  - **Intransitive verb**: To expand or grow broader in perspective or understanding. For example: "Her knowledge widened with each new experience."

## Phrases
- "Widen the road" refers to the process of expanding a road's width.
- "Widen one's horizons" means to broaden one's range of experiences or knowledge.
- "Widen the gap" is used to describe increasing the difference between two things.

## Example Sentences
- **Transitive**: "The contractor widened the bridge to accommodate larger vehicles."
- **Intransitive**: "Reading extensively widened her appreciation for different cultures."
- **Adjective**: "The garden's path had widened over time."
