## 读音
/waɪd/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 扩大；放宽：使变得更宽或更广。例如: "The road has been widened to accommodate more traffic."
  - 增加（知识、视野等）：扩展或增加了解的范围。例如: "Reading different genres can widen your horizons."

## 短语
- "widen the gap" 增大差距或分歧。例如: "Their policies only seem to widen the gap between the rich and the poor."
- "widen one's perspective" 扩大视野或观点。例如: "Traveling can help to widen one's perspective on life."

## 例句
- 动词: "The government plans to widen the main street to reduce traffic congestion."
- 动词: "Studying abroad widened her understanding of different cultures."
