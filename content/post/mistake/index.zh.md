## 读音
/mɪˈsteɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 错误；误会。例如: "I made a mistake in my calculation."
- **动词**: 做错；误解。例如: "She mistook him for her brother."

## 短语
- "make a mistake" 意思是犯错误。
- "correct one's mistakes" 是改正错误的意思。
- "learn from one's mistakes" 意味着从错误中吸取教训。

## 例句
- 名词: "It's okay to make mistakes; we all do."
- 动词: "He mistook my silence for agreement."
