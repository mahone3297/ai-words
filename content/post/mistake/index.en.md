## Pronunciation
/ˌmɪsˈteɪk/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: An act or instance of making an error or being incorrect; a misunderstanding or misjudgment. For example: "Realizing his mistake, he quickly corrected the report."
  - A fault or defect that occurs due to human error or misinformation. For example: "The typo was an unfortunate mistake in the manuscript."

## Phrases
- "To make a mistake" means to do something incorrectly or inaccurately.
- "Learn from your mistakes" suggests using past errors as a guide for future improvement.
- "An honest mistake" refers to an error made unintentionally or without malice.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I realized too late that forgetting my passport was a costly mistake."
- "After reviewing her calculations, she found several small mistakes that had led to the wrong result."
