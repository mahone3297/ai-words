## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To make changes or adjustments to something, typically in order to improve it, correct it, or adapt it to a new situation or purpose. For example: "The designer modified the dress to fit her client's preferences."
- **Noun**: (n) A change or alteration made to something, often referring to a document, software, or plan. For example: "The latest software update includes several important modifications."

## Phrases

- "To modify a contract": To make changes or amendments to a legal agreement.
- "Modify your approach": To alter the method or strategy being used towards achieving a goal.
