## 读音
/ˈmɒdɪfaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 改变；修改：使变得不同或适应新情况。例如: "She modified her diet to improve her health."
  - 调整；改进：对事物进行微调以改善其性能或功能。例如: "The engineers modified the car's engine for better fuel efficiency."

## 短语
- "modify something to something" 意味着改变某物以适应或符合另一物。例如: "They modified the software to meet the client's requirements."
- "make modifications" 指进行一系列的改变或调整。例如: "The company will make modifications to its production process based on customer feedback."

## 例句
- "The architect modified the building design to incorporate more green spaces."
- "To reduce noise pollution, they had to modify the engine's exhaust system."
- "The developer modified the code to fix the bug."
