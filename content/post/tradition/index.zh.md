## 读音
/trəˈdɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种长期沿袭下来的做法、信仰、习俗或传统。例如: "The Chinese New Year is a rich tradition filled with customs and rituals."
- **形容词**: 传统的，遵循传统的。例如: "She wore a traditional wedding dress."

## 短语
- "Follow a tradition" 意为遵循或保持传统。
- "Break with tradition" 意味着打破或背离传统。
- "A family tradition" 指家族中的一个习惯或活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The Christmas Eve feast is a cherished tradition in our family."
- 形容词: "The restaurant serves traditional Italian cuisine."
