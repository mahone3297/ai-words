## 读音
/pɪnt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种容量单位，通常在英国和美国使用，等于16液体盎司（约473毫升）。例如: "I ordered [a](/post/a/) [pint](/post/pint/) [of](/post/of/) [beer](/post/beer/)."
- **动词**: 在某些地区，指倒或量出一品脱的量。例如: "[The](/post/the/) bartender pints [the](/post/the/) ale [into](/post/into/) [a](/post/a/) [glass](/post/glass/)."

## 短语
- "[a](/post/a/) [pint](/post/pint/) [of](/post/of/) [something](/post/something/)" 指的是某物的一品脱量，如啤酒或冰淇淋。
- "pint-sized" 形容小巧的，与一品脱大小相似的事物或人。

## 例句
- 名词: "[They](/post/they/) [serve](/post/serve/) [traditional](/post/traditional/) ales [in](/post/in/) [half](/post/half/) [or](/post/or/) [full](/post/full/) pints [at](/post/at/) [the](/post/the/) [local](/post/local/) pub."
- 动词: "[The](/post/the/) bartender expertly pints [the](/post/the/) foamy stout."
