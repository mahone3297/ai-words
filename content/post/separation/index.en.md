## Pronunciation
/sepəˈreɪʃn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act of separating or dividing something into parts or groups. 2. The state of being apart from others; isolation. 3. A distance or interval between things. 4. (Computing) The process of dividing data into separate files or sections.
- **Verb**: 1. To cause to be separated or divided. 2. To keep apart; isolate.

## Phrases
- "Physical separation" refers to physical distancing.
- "Separation anxiety" describes the distress felt by individuals when separated from loved ones.
- "Administrative separation" is a military term for the termination of a service member's contract.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children were given separate rooms to ensure they had enough privacy."
- **Verb**: "The authorities decided to separate the suspects to prevent them from coordinating their defense."
- **Intransitive**: "After years of marriage, they experienced a painful separation."
