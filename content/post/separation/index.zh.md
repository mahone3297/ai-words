## 读音
/seprəˈteɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  分离；分开；隔离。例如: "The separation of powers is a fundamental principle in our government."（分权制是我们政府的基本原则。）
  分居；离婚。例如: "They decided to undergo a trial separation."（他们决定试分居一段时间。）

## 短语
- "separation of church and state" 宗教与国家的分离。
- "separation anxiety" 分离焦虑，通常指儿童或动物在与主要依恋者分开时感到的不安。

## 例句
- "The company's policy allows for a six-month separation period before an employee can return to their former position."（公司的政策规定员工离职六个月后才能回到原来的职位。）
- "The parents are going through a bitter separation, which has been hard on their children."（父母正在经历痛苦的分居，这对他们的孩子来说很艰难。）
