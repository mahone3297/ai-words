## Pronunciation
/skɑːf/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A long piece of cloth worn around the neck, often to provide warmth or as an accessory. For example: "She wore a colorful scarf to complete her outfit."
- **Verb**: To wrap or tie a scarf around one's neck. For example: "He tied a silk scarf around his neck before leaving the house."

## Phrases
- "Wrap a scarf around your neck" means to put a scarf on for warmth or style.
- "A scarf can be a fashion statement" highlights its role in clothing.
- "A wool scarf is perfect for cold weather" describes its function.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She draped a lightweight scarf over her shoulders on a chilly day."
- **Verb**: "He rolled up his scarf and tucked it into his backpack."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "I spotted a lady gracefully tying a silk scarf as she entered the café."
