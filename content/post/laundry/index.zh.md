## 读音
/ˈlɔːndri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 洗涤物，待洗的衣服。例如: "I have a lot of laundry to do this weekend."
    - 洗衣店或洗衣服务。例如: "I usually take my laundry to the local laundry."

## 短语
- "Do the laundry" 意味着清洗衣服。
- "Wash and dry laundry" 包括洗涤和烘干衣物的过程。
- "Laundry basket" 是用来装待洗衣物的篮子。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need to fold the clean laundry before it piles up."
- "The laundry service will have your clothes ready by tomorrow."
