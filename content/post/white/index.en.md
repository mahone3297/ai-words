## Pronunciation
/waɪt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having the quality or appearance of being free from color or lightness; bright and pure. For example: "The snow was a pristine white."
- **Noun**: The color of something that appears to reflect all visible light; the absence of color. For example: "The walls were painted a soft white."
- **Adverb**: In a white manner; without any color. For example: "She wore a white dress to the wedding."

## Phrases
- "A whiteout" refers to complete darkness or whiteness due to snow or glare.
- "A blank slate" is something new and unmarked, like a white piece of paper.
- "White noise" is a constant, soothing background sound often used for relaxation or sleep.
