## 读音
/stɪtʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一线缝合；针脚：例如 "She carefully made a row of stitches on the torn dress."
  - 缝纫；刺绣：例如 "The tailor is skilled in various stitches for different fabrics."

- **动词**:
  - 缝合；用针线连接：例如 "The doctor will stitch up the wound after the surgery."
  - 刺绣；做针线活：例如 "She spent the afternoon stitching a beautiful pattern on her sampler."

## 短语
- "stitch in time" 意为“及时行动”，常与 "saves nine" 连用，表示小问题及时处理可以避免更大的麻烦。
- "to stitch something together" 意为“匆忙拼凑；临时凑合”。

## 例句
- 名词: "The tailor added a few decorative stitches to the hem of the skirt."
- 动词: "He had to stitch his own finger after cutting it while woodworking."
