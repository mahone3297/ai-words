## 读音
/ˈlɛŋθ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 物体的尺寸，从一端到另一端的距离。例如: "The length of the table is three meters."
- **名词**: 持续的时间。例如: "The movie has a length of two hours."

## 短语
- "in length" 表示长度是多少。例如: "The river is 500 kilometers in length."
- "at length" 意为详细地，详尽地。例如: "He explained the problem at length."

## 例句
- 名词: "The dress is just the right length for her height."
- 名词: "We need to measure the length of the rope to know if it's enough."

## 同义词
- Duration
- Extent
- Breadth (在某些语境中，如宽度)
- Measurement
- Distance (在指物理距离时）
