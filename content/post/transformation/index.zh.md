## 读音
/ˌtrænsˈfɔːrməʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 彻底的改变；转化。例如: "The transformation of the city skyline is remarkable."
  - 变形；转换。例如: "The caterpillar undergoes a transformation into a butterfly."
- **动词**:
  - 改变；转变。例如: "The company's strategy aims to transform its business model."
- **形容词**:
  - 转变的；变革的。例如: "The transformational leadership style inspires change."

## 短语
- "Transformation process" 指转变的过程。
- "Metamorphic transformation" 指变质作用，地质学中岩石的转变。
- "Social transformation" 指社会变革。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist's paintings reflect a transformation in her style over the years."
- 动词: "His training program transformed him from an amateur to a professional athlete."
- 形容词: "The new policy has brought about a transformation in the education system."
