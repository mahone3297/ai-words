## Pronunciation
/tɹænsfərˈmeɪʃən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act or process of changing from one form, state, or appearance to another. 2. An instance of such a change. 3. A transformational operation in mathematics or computer science.
- **Verb**: To convert or alter something fundamentally, often into a different form or shape. 4. To undergo a change or development.

## Phrases
- "Transformational change" indicates a fundamental shift.
- "Transformation process" refers to the steps involved in a change.
- "Transformation of thought" denotes a change in perception or understanding.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The transformation of a caterpillar into a butterfly is a remarkable natural process."
- **Verb**: "She underwent a transformation in her career after receiving training in a new field."
- **Gerund**: "The transformation from digital to analog media is rapidly occurring."
