## 读音
/əˈkɜːrəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 发生；出现；事件；存在。例如: "There have been several occurrences of theft in the area lately."
- **动词**: 发生；出现。例如: "The occurrence of natural disasters has increased over the past decade."

## 短语
- "Frequency of occurrence" 表示发生的频率或次数。
- "Unexpected occurrence" 指的是意外发生的事情。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police are investigating the occurrence of a break-in at the local store."
- 动词: "An unusual occurrence took place during the experiment."
