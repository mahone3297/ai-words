## Word: Occurrence

## Pronunciation
/əˈkɜːrəns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: An instance of something happening or existing; an event or incident. For example: "The frequent occurrences of wildfires in the region have raised concerns among environmentalists."
  - *In a scientific context*: A recorded instance or observation in data collection or research. For example: "The high number of occurrences of this species in our study area suggests it's thriving here."
- **Verb (intransitive)**: To happen or take place; to come about. For example: "Unexpected occurrences can disrupt even the most carefully planned schedules."

## Phrases
- "A rare occurrence" implies that something does not happen very often.
- "In the event of an occurrence" is used to refer to if something happens or takes place.
- "Frequency of occurrence" refers to how often something happens.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The occurrence of severe storms has increased due to climate change."
- **Verb**: "It was only after her promotion that such discrimination began to occur."
