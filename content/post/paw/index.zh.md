## 读音
/pɔː/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 动物的脚掌，尤指猫或狗的爪子。例如: "The puppy playfully nuzzled my hand with its soft paw."
  - 采矿或挖掘时的挖掘工具，通常为金属制成。例如: "The miner used his paw to dig through the rocky soil."

## 短语
- "paw at" 表示用爪子抓或摸索。例如: "The kitten pawed at the string, trying to catch it."

## 例句
- 名词: "The cat's paw left a gentle scratch on my arm."
- 动词: "The dog pawed the ground nervously before running off."

## 同义词
- claw (名词)
- foot (名词，但更通用，不特指爪子部分)
- digging tool (名词，特指采矿或挖掘工具)
