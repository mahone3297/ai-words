## 读音
/reɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 雨水。例如: "It's raining outside, so take an umbrella." 2. 下雨的状态。例如: "The garden needs some rain to grow."
- **动词**: 下雨。例如: "It often rains in Seattle during the winter."

## 短语
- "Rain cats and dogs" 意味下大雨。
- "Make it rain" 在口语中常指大量金钱或资源的突然出现。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children played in the rain despite getting soaked."
- 动词: "The forecast says it will rain all week."
- 短语: "It was raining so hard that the streets turned into rivers."

## 同义词
- Shower
- Downpour
- Drizzle
- Precipitation
- Rainfall
