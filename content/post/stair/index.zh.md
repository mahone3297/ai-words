## 读音
/stɛr/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. (复数形式 stairs 或 steps) 楼梯，阶梯。例如: "Please be careful on the stairs, they're slippery."
2. 一段楼梯。例如: "The child was climbing up the third stair when he lost his balance."

## 短语
- "Upstairs/downstairs" 指楼层的上层或下层，通常与 stairs 相关。
- "Stairwell" 指楼梯井，即建筑物内楼梯所在的区域。

## 例句
- 名词: "She carefully carried the heavy box up the stairs to her apartment."
- "The baby learned to walk by holding onto the banister of the stairs."

## 同义词
- Steps
- Escalator (电动扶梯，通常在商场或地铁站)
- Rungs (常用于梯子的台阶)
- Landing (楼梯中间的平台)
