## 读音
/stɛr/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. (复数形式 stairs 或 steps) 楼梯，阶梯。例如: "[Please](/zh/post/please/) [be](/zh/post/be/) [careful](/zh/post/careful/) [on](/zh/post/on/) [the](/zh/post/the/) stairs, they're [slippery](/zh/post/slippery/)."
2. 一段楼梯。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) [child](/zh/post/child/) was climbing [up](/zh/post/up/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [third](/zh/post/third/) [stair](/zh/post/stair/) [when](/zh/post/when/) [he](/zh/post/he/) lost [his](/zh/post/his/) [balance](/zh/post/balance/)."

## 短语
- "[Upstairs](/zh/post/upstairs/)/[downstairs](/zh/post/downstairs/)" 指楼层的上层或下层，通常与 stairs 相关。
- "Stairwell" 指楼梯井，即建筑物内楼梯所在的区域。
