## Pronunciation
/stɛər/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Stair**: A set of steps that form a continuous flight for ascending or descending from one level of a building, room, or other space to another.
- **Staircase**: A structure consisting of a series of stairs, typically enclosed or with a railing, leading between different floors of a building.

### Verb (transitive)
- **To ascend the stairs**: To climb up a set of stairs, going from a lower to a higher level.
- **To descend the stairs**: To go down a set of stairs, moving from a higher to a lower level.

### Adjective
- **Stairwell**: A corridor or passage within a building that contains a staircase, often with walls on either side.
- **Staircase-like**: Having the appearance or configuration of a staircase.

## Phrases
- "Climb the stairs"

- **Noun**: "I need to take the stairs to reach the top floor."
- **Verb**: "He carefully descended the spiral staircase to the basement."
- **Adjective**: "The old house had narrow staircases that creaked underfoot."
