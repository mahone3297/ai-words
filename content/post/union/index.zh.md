## 读音
/ˈjuː.nɪən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 联合；联盟；工会。指两个或多个实体或群体结合成一个整体。例如: "The teachers' union is negotiating with the school board."
- **动词 (v)**: 结合；联合。指使事物或人联合在一起。例如: "They unionized to improve working conditions."

## 短语
- "form a union" 意为建立或加入联盟。
- "labor union" 特指工人组织的工会。
- "industrial union" 指涵盖整个行业的工会。

## 例句
- 名词: "The European Union is a political and economic union of 27 member states."
- 动词: "The employees decided to unionize to protect their rights."
