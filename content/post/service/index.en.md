## Pronunciation
/sərˈvɪs/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The act of providing goods or facilities to customers, especially in a restaurant, hotel, or store: *She enjoyed excellent service at the restaurant.*
2. A system or an individual's job of assisting or attending to people's needs: *The hotel staff provided top-notch customer service.*
3. Help or support given to someone: *The community came together to provide medical service during the crisis.*
4. An instance or period of work performed: *He clocked in for his shift at the gas station.*

### Verb
1. To perform the duties or tasks of serving: *The waiter served the customers with efficiency.*
2. To supply or provide (a product or service): *We aim to serve the best coffee in town.*
3. To be useful or beneficial to: *This new software should greatly improve our service to clients.*

### Adjective
1. Given or done to fulfill a need or request: *Our mission is to offer efficient and friendly service.*
2. Performed by a professional: *A skilled hairdresser provides salon-quality service.*
3. Characterizing the quality of assistance or care: *Excellent customer service sets this hotel apart.*

## Phrases
- "Provide excellent service" means to give high-quality assistance or care.
- "Service with a smile" describes a friendly and welcoming attitude.
- "Get your car serviced" refers to having it checked and maintained by a mechanic.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The hotel staff were praised for their attentive service."
- **Verb**: "The chef personally served the special dish to each guest."
- **Adjective**: "The hotel's 24-hour concierge service is unparalleled."
