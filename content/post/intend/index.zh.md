## 读音
/ɪnˈtend/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 计划；打算。例如: "I intend to finish this project by tomorrow."
  - 意欲；想要。例如: "She intends to study abroad next year."
  - 预定；安排。例如: "The concert is intended for music lovers."

## 短语
- "intend to do something" 表示计划或打算做某事。
- "have no intention of doing something" 意味着没有做某事的打算。

## 例句
- "I intend to start my own business in the near future."
- "He has every intention of attending the meeting, despite being ill."
- "The book is not intended for children, as it contains mature themes."
