## 读音
/rɪˈlaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 依靠；依赖：表示信任或依靠某人或某事物。例如: "I can always rely on my sister for support."
2. 信赖；确信：确信某事是真实的或会发生。例如: "We rely on accurate data to make informed decisions."

## 短语
- "Rely on someone/something" 意味着依赖或信赖某人或某物。
- "Rely completely" 表示完全信赖或依赖。

## 例句
- 动词: "She relies on public transport to get to work every day."
- 动词: "Their success relies heavily on teamwork."
