## 读音
/ˈleɪər/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 一层，薄层：A thin or superficial layer of material, such as a coating or stratum.
  - (电子/计算机)层：A level or division in a hierarchical structure, especially in computing or electronics.

- **动词 v**:
  - 堆叠，铺设：To place one thing on top of another in layers; to spread or arrange in layers.
  - 给...涂层：To cover something with a layer of a substance.

## 短语
- "lay a new layer" 意为添加新的一层。
- "layer upon layer" 表示一层又一层。
- "network layer" 在计算机科学中指的是网络协议的层次结构。

## 例句
- 名词: "The cake had a delicious chocolate layer between its sponge layers."
- 动词: "She carefully layered the salad ingredients in a glass bowl."
- 感叹词: "This is not used as an interjection."
