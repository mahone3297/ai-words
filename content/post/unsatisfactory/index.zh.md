## 读音
/ʌnˈsætɪsˈfæktəri/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 令人不满意的，不够的，未达到期望的。例如: "The service was unsatisfactory, so we decided to complain."

## 短语
- "an unsatisfactory result" 表示一个不满意的结果。
- "unsatisfactory performance" 指表现不佳或成绩不理想。

## 例句
- "The food at the restaurant was unsatisfactory, and we won't be going back anytime soon."
- "Despite his hard work, the student received an unsatisfactory grade on the exam."
