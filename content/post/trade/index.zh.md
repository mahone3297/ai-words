## 读音
/trād/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 贸易；商业活动。例如: "International trade is vital for many countries' economies."
  - 交易；买卖。例如: "They made a good trade in antique furniture."
  - 专业；行业。例如: "He comes from a long line of farmers; farming is his trade."

- **动词**:
  - 进行交易。例如: "The company trades with partners in Europe."
  - 交换；以物换物。例如: "We traded our old car for a new one."

## 短语
- "Trade off" 意味着交换或权衡。例如: "They had to trade off quality for quantity."
- "In the trade" 表示在某个行业中工作。例如: "She's been in the fashion trade for over a decade."

## 例句
- 名词: "The trade deficit has been a major concern for the government."
- 动词: "They trade stocks on the New York Stock Exchange."
- "He used to trade goods with neighboring villages."
