## 读音
/ˈsɪŋ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 用嗓音发出和谐的音调，通常为了娱乐、表达感情或宗教仪式。例如: "She loves to sing in the choir."
- **名词**: 歌唱的行为或一首歌。例如: "The children were practicing their Christmas sing."

## 短语
- "Sing along" 意为跟着一起唱。
- "A solo sing" 指独唱表演。
- "To sing a lullaby" 是唱摇篮曲哄小孩睡觉。

## 例句
- 动词: "John has a beautiful voice and enjoys singing opera."
- 名词: "The audience was captivated by her powerful sing during the concert."
