## 读音
/ˈtɑːɡɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 目标；瞄准点。例如: "The archer hit the bullseye on the target."
2. 对象；受攻击的人或事物。例如: "She was the main target of the criticism."
3. （市场营销）目标市场；目标群体。例如: "Their new product is aimed at a younger target audience."

- **动词**:
1. 把…作为目标；瞄准。例如: "The sniper targeted the enemy's command post."
2. 针对；把…作为攻击对象。例如: "The advertising campaign targeted consumers' insecurities."

## 短语
- "set a target for" 设定目标。例如: "We set a target of increasing sales by 20% this quarter."
- "hit the target" 击中目标。例如: "His proposal hit the target perfectly."

## 例句
- 名词: "The athlete's goal was to break the record and hit the target time."
- 动词: "The marketing team targeted social media platforms to promote their product."
