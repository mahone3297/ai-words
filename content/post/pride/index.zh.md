## 读音
/praɪd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 自豪感；自尊心；傲慢。例如: "She felt a deep sense of pride in her accomplishment."
- **动词**: 以...为荣；自豪。例如: "He prides himself on his punctuality."
- **形容词**: 骄傲的；自豪的。例如: "The proud parent watched their child graduate with honors."

## 短语
- "Take pride in" 意味着对某事感到自豪或珍视。例如: "She takes pride in her work and always strives for excellence."
- "Pride goes before a fall" 是一句谚语，意味着过于骄傲常常会导致失败。

## 例句
- 名词: "His family's long history of military service fills him with pride."
- 动词: "She prides herself on never giving up."
- 形容词: "The pride of the lions was evident in their majestic stance."

## 同义词
- Self-esteem
- Esteem
- Honor
- Vanity
- Arrogance
