## 读音
/ˈkɪŋdəm/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 一个由国王或女王统治的国家或领土。例如: "The United Kingdom is a constitutional monarchy."
   - 一个特定领域或事物的领域、范围或王国。例如: "The animal kingdom is divided into several phyla."

## 短语
- "kingdom of heaven" 在基督教中指的是神的国度。
- "animal kingdom" 指所有动物的分类单位。

## 例句
- 名词: "The kingdom of Thailand has a rich cultural heritage."
- 名词: "In biology, we study organisms from all domains and kingdoms."

## 同义词
- Realm
- Empire
- State (in the context of a monarchy)
- Dominion
- Sovereignty
