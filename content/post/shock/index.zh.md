## 读音
/ʃɑːk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 突然的震惊或惊吓；剧烈的震动。例如: "The news of his death was a shock to everyone."
    - （身体上的）休克，昏厥。例如: "She went into shock after the accident."
    - 电击。例如: "Don't touch that wire, it carries a dangerous shock."

- **动词**:
    - 使震惊或惊讶。例如: "The news shocked us all."
    - 给…以电击。例如: "The defibrillator was used to shock his heart back into rhythm."

## 短语
- "electric shock" 意为电击。
- "shock absorber" 在汽车或机械中指的是减震器。

## 例句
- 名词: "The sudden noise gave me a terrible shock."
- 动词: "The revelation about her past shocked everyone present."

## 同义词
- Surprise
- Startle
- Astonish
- Stun
- Jolt
