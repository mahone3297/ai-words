## 读音
/stəˈteɪmənt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 陈述，声明，表达。例如: "He made a statement about his political beliefs."
- **名词**: 会计报表，财务报表。例如: "The company's annual financial statement shows a profit."
- **名词**: 法律上的声明，证词。例如: "She gave a detailed statement to the police."
- **动词**: 声明，宣称。例如: "He stated that he was innocent."

## 短语
- "Financial statement" 财务报告。
- "Make a statement" 做出声明或表达观点。
- "Statement piece" 在时尚中指引人注目的单品。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist's bold use of color was a statement in itself."
- 名词: "The bank sent me a statement showing my account balance."
- 动词: "In his speech, he stated that climate change is a major threat."

## 同义词
- Declaration
- Assertion
- Affirmation
- Claim
- Utterance
