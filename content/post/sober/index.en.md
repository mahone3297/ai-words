## Pronunciation
/ˈsəʊbər/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Free from intoxication; not under the influence of alcohol or drugs; calm and composed after a period of intoxication. For example: "After a night of partying, she woke up feeling quite sober."
- **Verb**: To become or become again free from intoxication; to regain one's senses. For example: "It took him a while to sober up after drinking too much."

## Phrases
- "Come down from the drunk" refers to the process of sobering up.
- "Stay sober" is a commitment to not drink alcohol or use drugs.
- "Sober up quickly" implies rapid recovery from intoxication.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "He was asked to leave the party for being too drunk, but he managed to sober up by the morning."
- **Verb**: "After a long night, she decided to sober up before driving home."
