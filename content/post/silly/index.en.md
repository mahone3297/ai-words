## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Marking lack of sense or seriousness; foolish or absurd. For example: "His jokes were quite silly."
- **Adverb**: In a silly manner; foolishly. For example: "She acted silly after winning the game."

## Phrases
- "Act silly" means to behave in a playful or foolish way.
- "Silly putty" is a popular example of a seemingly silly but entertaining material.
- "Don't be silly" is a phrase used to scold or reassure someone of their irrationality.
