## 读音
/mekənɪkl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 与机械、机器或力学相关的；通过机械力运作的。例如: "The mechanical clock has a intricate gear system."
- **名词**: 机械师；机械装置。例如: "He is an experienced mechanical engineer."
- **名词 (术语)**: 机械学；力学。例如: "She studied mechanical engineering in college."

## 短语
- "Mechanical advantage" 指的是机械装置提高力的能力。
- "Mechanical engineering" 是关于设计和制造机械设备的学科。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The mechanical arm lifted the heavy load effortlessly."
- 名词: "The car's mechanical problems were causing frequent breakdowns."
- 术语名词: "In physics, we learn about the principles of mechanical energy."
