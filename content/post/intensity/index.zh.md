## 读音
/ɪnˈtɛnsəti/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 强度；剧烈：表示事物的程度、力量或浓度。例如: "The heat intensity during summer can be unbearable."
  - 紧张；压力：心理或情感上的紧张程度。例如: "The intensity of her emotions was visible in her facial expressions."
  - 热情；专注：对某事的强烈兴趣或全神贯注。例如: "He showed great intensity while playing the piano."

## 短语
- "High intensity interval training (HIIT)" 是高强度间歇训练，一种健身方法。
- "Intensity of feeling" 指感情的强度或深度。

## 例句
- 名词: "The intensity of the storm increased as it moved closer to the coast."
- 形容词: "She performed with such intensity that the audience was captivated."
