## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈtɛnsəti/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The quality or state of being intense; great strength, force, energy, or concentration. It can refer to emotions, sensations, colors, sounds, or any measurable quantity that has a high degree. Example: "The intensity of her passion was evident in her eyes." 

- **Noun (Physics)**: In physics, it refers to the magnitude of a given quantity per unit area, volume, or time, such as light intensity, sound intensity, or gravitational intensity. Example: "The earthquake had a high intensity, causing severe damage."

### Phrases
- "Intensity level" describes the comparative degree of intensity, often used when discussing sound or light.
- "At full intensity" means performing or operating at maximum power or strength.
- "Exercise intensity" refers to how hard one is working out physically.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (Emotion)**: "The intensity of his feelings towards his work was unmatched."
- **Noun (Physics)**: "Scientists measured the light intensity emanating from the star."
- **Adjective**: "Her workout routine was designed for high-intensity interval training."
