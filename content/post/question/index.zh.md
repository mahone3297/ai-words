## 读音
/ˈkwestʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 问题，疑问。询问或讨论中的一个点或主题。例如: "She asked him a difficult question about his past."
- **动词**: 提问，质询。提出疑问或要求解释。例如: "The teacher questioned the student about her homework."
- **形容词**: 可疑的，不确定的。表示对某事有疑问。例如: "The authenticity of the painting is still very much in question."

## 短语
- "To raise a question" 提出问题。
- "Question mark" 问号，表示疑问的符号。
- "Without question" 毫无疑问，肯定地。

## 例句
- 名词: "The debate focused on several key questions."
- 动词: "The interviewer questioned him about his qualifications."
- 形容词: "The outcome of the election is still very much in question."
