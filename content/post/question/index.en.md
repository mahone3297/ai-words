## Pronunciation
/həˈlɛk/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A word or query used to inquire about something or seek information. *Example*: "The teacher asked a question to check understanding."
- **Verb**: To ask, inquire, or pose a problem for clarification or response. *Example*: "She questioned the validity of the claim."
- **Interjection**: Expressing doubt, surprise, or curiosity. *Example*: "Oh, really? Can you answer this question?"

## Phrases
- "Ask a question" implies seeking knowledge or information.
- "To raise a question" means to bring up an issue for discussion.
- "The burning question" refers to a pressing or important matter.

## Example Sentences
