## 读音
/hæt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一顶帽子；遮阳帽；头饰。例如: "She wore a stylish hat to complete her outfit."
- **动词**: 戴帽子；覆盖（头部）。例如: "He always hats when going outside in cold weather."
- **形容词**: 与帽子相关的；关于帽子的。例如: "The hat store had a wide selection of hat styles."

## 短语
- "Take your hat off" 意味着脱下你的帽子，通常出于礼貌或进入室内。
- "To make a hat out of something" 表示用某种材料制作帽子。

## 例句
- 名词: "He picked up his hat from the table and put it on."
- 动词: "She hats her head with a fashionable beret."
- 形容词: "The hat section in the boutique was filled with hat options for every occasion."
