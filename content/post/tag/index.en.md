## Pronunciation
/həˈləʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A term used to greet or address someone. Often marks the beginning of a conversation or interaction. **Example**: "She waved a friendly hello at the waiter."

### Verb
To express greeting or acknowledge someone's presence, typically with the word "hello." **Example**: "He greeted his friends with a hearty hello."

### Adjective
Characterizing or expressing a friendly or welcoming attitude. **Example**: "Her hello was warm and genuine."

### Adverb
In the manner of saying "hello" or expressing a greeting. **Example**: "She greeted everyone cheerfully."

## Phrases
- "Pay your hello" or "Exchange hellos" means to greet or acknowledge someone.
- "Goodbye, hello" is a phrase that describes the cycle of parting and reuniting.
- "Hello, my love" is a term of endearment used to address a loved one.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children exchanged hellos as they arrived at school."
- **Verb**: "After a long day, it felt good to hear her familiar hello."
- **Adjective**: "Her hello was a ray of sunshine on a gloomy day."
