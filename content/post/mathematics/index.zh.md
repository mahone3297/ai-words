## 读音
/maθəˈmætɪks/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
数学，一门研究数量、结构、变化和空间等概念的学科。例如: "She is studying advanced mathematics at university."

## 短语
- "Pure mathematics" 指的是理论性的数学研究。
- "Applied mathematics" 是将数学原理应用于实际问题的领域。

## 例句
- "Mathematics plays a crucial role in many scientific fields."
- "He solved the complex equation with ease, showcasing his strong grasp of mathematics."
