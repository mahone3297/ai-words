## Word: July

## Pronunciation
/dʒuˈlaɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The seventh month of the Gregorian calendar, usually consisting of 31 days. It's named after Julius Caesar in the Roman calendar reform.

Example Sentence: 
- "We're planning to have our annual family reunion in July this year."

## Phrases
- "Mid-July" refers to the middle of the month of July.
- "July 4th" specifically denotes Independence Day in the United States.
- "Dog days of summer" often refers to the hot, sultry period occurring in late July and early August.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "July is the peak season for tourism in many Mediterranean countries."
- "Her birthday falls on the 27th of July."
