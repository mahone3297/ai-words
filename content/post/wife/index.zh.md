## 读音
/waɪf/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 妻子，已婚男子的合法配偶。例如: "He introduced his wife to the guests."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与妻子有关的，妻子般的。例如: "He took care of her with a wife's devotion."

## 短语
- "the breadwinner of the wife" 指家庭中主要赚钱养家的人，通常是丈夫，但也可以是妻子。
- "to be husband and wife" 意味着两个人已经结婚，成为合法夫妻。

## 例句
- 名词: "John and his wife celebrated their 25th wedding anniversary."
- 形容词: "She showed a wife's understanding and support during tough times."
