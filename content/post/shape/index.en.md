## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A shape** is the form or external appearance of an object, typically defined by its edges and corners. For example: "The shape of a circle is perfectly round."
- **Geometric shape** refers to a specific type of shape with mathematical properties, like triangles, squares, or circles. For example: "A triangle has three sides and three angles."

### Verb
- To **shape** something means to mold or form it into a particular design or structure. For example: "The artist carefully shaped the clay into a vase."
- To **describe the shape of** something is to give details about its form. For example: "She described the shape of the ancient ruin as being rectangular."

### Adjective
- **Shaped** describes an object that has been formed into a particular design or size. For example: "The table was made from a single piece of wood, shaped to fit the room."

## Phrases
- "The shape of a thing" refers to its overall form or appearance.
- "In the shape of" is used to describe the form or appearance of an object, like "the cake was cut in the shape of a heart."
- "A new shape emerges" signifies the appearance of something different or innovative.
