## 读音
/skəʊp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 范围；领域。例如: "The project's scope includes designing and implementing a new software system."
- **名词**: 视野；观察范围。例如: "The telescope offers an extensive scope for stargazing."
- **名词**: (机器或仪器的)瞄准镜；目镜。例如: "The rifle has a high-powered scope for long-distance shooting."
- **动词**: 详查；审视。例如: "She scoped out the area before making her decision."

## 短语
- "Within the scope of something" 意味在...的范围内。
- "Out of scope" 表示超出范围或不相关。
- "Scope out" 意为仔细查看或调查。

## 例句
- 名词: "The research project has a broad scope, covering various aspects of environmental science."
- 名词: "The sniper adjusted the scope to get a clearer view of the target."
- 动词: "Before planning the event, they scoped out several potential venues."
