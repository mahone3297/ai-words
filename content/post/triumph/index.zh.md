## 读音
/trɪˈfɪm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大胜，胜利，成功。例如: "The team celebrated their triumph over their rivals."
- **动词**: 获胜，凯旋。例如: "She triumphed over her fears and completed the marathon."

## 短语
- "Triumph in something" 表示在某事上取得胜利。
- "Triumph over someone/ something" 意味着战胜某人或某事。

## 例句
- 名词: "Their triumph in the championship was a source of pride for the whole city."
- 动词: "Despite all odds, she triumphed and proved everyone wrong."
