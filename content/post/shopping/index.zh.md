## 读音
/ˈʃɒpɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   购物；购买行为。例如: "I did some shopping for groceries this morning."
   商店；购物中心。例如: "There's a new shopping mall in town."

- **动词**:
   购物；买东西。例如: "She enjoys shopping for clothes on weekends."

## 短语
- "Go shopping" 意为去购物。
- "Window shopping" 指只看不买的逛商店。
- "Online shopping" 是指在线购物。

## 例句
- 名词: "We need to go shopping to buy some presents for Christmas."
- 动词: "She spent the whole afternoon shopping for a dress for the wedding."
