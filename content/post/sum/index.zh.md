## 读音
/ˈsʌm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 总数，和，金额。例如: "The sum of these numbers is 20."
- **动词**: 汇总，加起来。例如: "Can you sum up the expenses for this month?"
- **形容词**: 总体的，全部的。例如: "A sum account gives an overall view of your finances."

## 短语
- "Add up to a sum" 意为加起来等于某个总数。
- "In summary" 表示总结或概括。

## 例句
- 名词: "The sum they paid for the house was quite large."
- 动词: "Let's sum the numbers in this column."
- 形容词: "The sum effect of his actions was disastrous."
