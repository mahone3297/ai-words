## 读音
/rɪˈplai/

## 释义
- **动词**: 回答，回应。例如: "I replied to her email promptly."
- **名词**: 回复，回答。例如: "His reply was concise and to the point."

## 短语
- "Make a reply" 意为做出回应或答复。
- "Prompt reply" 指迅速的回复。

## 例句
- 动词: "She replied to his question with a smile."
- 名词: "I'm still waiting for your reply to my message."
