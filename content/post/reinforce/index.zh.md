## 读音
/rɪˈfɔːrs/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 加强，增强。例如: "The addition of steel beams reinforces the building's structure."
  2. 增援，支援。例如: "The army reinforced the troops at the front line."
  3. 巩固，强化（信念、观点等）。例如: "Reading more books reinforces one's knowledge on the subject."

## 短语
- "reinforce one's position" 表示加强自己的立场或观点。
- "reinforce troops" 意味着增派军队或兵力。
- "reinforce learning" 指通过重复或实践来巩固学习。

## 例句
- 动词: "The engineers decided to reinforce the bridge to ensure its safety."
- 动词: "The company reinforced its marketing strategy with a new advertising campaign."
- 动词: "His speech reinforced his commitment to environmental issues."
