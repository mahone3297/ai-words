## Pronunciation
/kɪˈsaɪt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A cigarette is a thin rolled or folded leaf of tobacco, often impregnated with cutting agents, that is smoked as a leisure activity or for its psychoactive effects.

### Verb (informal)
To smoke a cigarette, typically habitually.

## Phrases
- "Light up a cigarette" means to start smoking a cigarette.
- "Take a break and have a cigarette" suggests a moment of rest with a cigarette.
- "Giving up cigarettes" refers to quitting smoking.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "He took a long drag on his cigarette as he sat by the window."
- **Verb**: "She tried to quit smoking, but eventually caved in and lit a cigarette."
- **Informal**: "Let's go outside for a cigarette break."
