## 读音
/ˈdɑːktər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 医生；医师。例如: "She is a respected doctor in her field."
- **名词 (n)**: 博士。例如: "He earned his doctorate in physics last year."
- **名词 (n)**: 在某些国家，对神父或牧师的称呼。例如: "The local doctor will be officiating the wedding."

## 短语
- "Call the doctor" 意味着需要医生的帮助或建议。
- "Doctor's visit" 指的是看医生的预约或过程。
- "Doctor up" 指的是改进或掩饰事物，尤指食物或药物。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need to make an appointment with my doctor for a checkup."
- 名词: "Her doctor recommended a change in diet and exercise routine."
- 名词: "The village doctor was well-known for his kindness and healing skills."
