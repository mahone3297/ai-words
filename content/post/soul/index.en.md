## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
  - 1. The vital principle or essence of a person, often regarded as immortal and separate from the body: "The loss of one's soul is considered a great tragedy."
  - 2. A spiritual or moral quality that gives life and purpose to a person or thing: "The book was a reflection of her soul's longing for adventure."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or relating to the spirit or inner nature: "A soulful melody."

- "Soul mate" refers to a person with whom one has a deep emotional connection.
- "Sell one's soul" is a metaphor for making a deal or compromise that one believes is morally questionable.
