## 读音
/soʊl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 人的精神、灵魂：The essence or spirit of a person.
   - 音乐中的灵魂：A style of music with strong emotional content, especially African American popular music.
   - 心灵：The innermost part of one's being, often regarded as the seat of emotions.

- **形容词**:
   - 灵魂的，精神上的：Relating to the soul or spirit.
   - 具有灵魂音乐特点的：Characteristic of soul music.

## 短语
- "Soul searching" 意味着深入自我反思。
- "Soul mate" 指的是理想的人生伴侣或知心朋友。
- "Soulful performance" 指深情或充满感情的表演。

## 例句
- 名词: "His paintings captured the beauty and pain of the human soul."
- 形容词: "Her voice was soulful, touching every listener's heart."
