## 读音
/west/

## 释义
- **名词**: 地理上的西方，与东方相对的方向。例如: "They live in a house facing west."
- **形容词**: 西方的，来自西方的。例如: "The western culture has a significant influence on global trends."
- **副词**: 向西，朝西。例如: "We drove west for several hours until we reached the mountains."

## 短语
- "Westward expansion" 指的是向西扩展或扩张。
- "The West" 常用来指代欧美发达国家，尤其是西方文化。

## 例句
- 名词: "The sunset was breathtaking as it dipped below the western horizon."
- 形容词: "She loves the traditional music of the American West."
- 副词: "They decided to fly west to explore new opportunities."

## 同义词
- Western
- Occidental
- European (在指代文化时)
- Left (在地理上相对于东的对称位置)
