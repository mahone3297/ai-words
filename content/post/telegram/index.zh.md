## 读音
/ˈtɛləˌɡræm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 电报，一种通过电信系统快速传递的文字信息。例如: "She sent a telegram to congratulate him on his success."
- **动词**: 发送电报。例如: "In the old days, people would telegram their loved ones when they arrived safely."

## 短语
- "Telegram service" 指提供电报发送和接收的服务。
- "Wireless telegram" 指通过无线电波传输的电报。

## 例句
- 名词: "The telegram arrived just in time for them to prepare for the emergency."
- 动词: "In the past, important news was often telegraphed across long distances."

## 同义词
- Message (in the context of rapid communication)
- Wire (as a means of sending a message)
