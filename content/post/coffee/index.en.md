## Pronunciation
/kəˈfi/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Coffee**: A beverage made from roasted coffee beans, usually ground and brewed with hot water. It is a popular drink worldwide and often consumed in the morning or as a pick-me-up.
- **Coffee shop**: A place where coffee and other refreshments are sold, often serving as a social gathering spot.

### Verb
- **To brew coffee**: The process of making coffee by steeping ground coffee beans in hot water.
- **To have a cup of coffee**: To consume a serving of coffee.

### Adjective
- **Coffee-colored**: Referring to a shade of brown similar to that of coffee.
- **Coffee-flavored**: Characterized by the taste or scent of coffee.

## Phrases
- "A cup of Joe" is slang for a cup of coffee.
- "Black coffee" refers to coffee without milk or cream.
- "Coffee break" is a short pause for a cup of coffee.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I need a strong cup of coffee to start my day."
- **Verb**: "She brews her own coffee every morning."
- **Adjective**: "The furniture in his apartment had a coffee-stained look."
- **Adverb**: "He took a long sip of his freshly brewed coffee."
