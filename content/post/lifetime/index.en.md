## Pronunciation
/ˈlaɪftaɪm/

## Definitions
- **Noun (n)**: The duration of a person's existence from birth until death; the total period during which something functions or remains usable. For example: "The car has a lifetime warranty." "She dedicated her whole lifetime to helping others."

### Phrases
- "Lifetime guarantee": A promise that something will be repaired or replaced free of charge for as long as it exists or is owned by the original purchaser.
- "Lifetime achievement": An award or recognition given for the collective body of work accomplished throughout one's career or life.
- "In a lifetime": Referring to the entire span of one's life up to a particular point or event. Example: "She had visited every continent in a lifetime."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "His remarkable discoveries have influenced science throughout his lifetime."
- "The company guarantees its products for their lifetime against defects."
- "She was honored with a lifetime membership to the organization for her dedication."
