## 读音

## 释义
- **形容词**: 足够的，适当的，胜任的。例如: "The resources provided were adequate for the task."
- **副词**: 足够地，适当地。例如: "She performed adequately in the interview."

## 短语
- "adequate supply" 足够的供应
- "adequate preparation" 充分的准备
