## 读音
/ˈeɪʃən/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 亚洲的，属于亚洲的，具有亚洲特征的。例如: "She is an Asian artist with a unique style."
- **名词**: 亚洲人，泛指来自亚洲的人。例如: "There is a diverse population of Asians in this city."

## 短语
- "Asian cuisine" 亚洲菜，指亚洲各地的烹饪风格和食物。
- "Asian continent" 亚洲大陆，指地理上的亚洲区域。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The restaurant serves authentic Asian food from various countries."
- 名词: "Many Asian cultures have a deep respect for their elders."
