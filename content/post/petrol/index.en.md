## Pronunciation
/pəˈtrəl/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Petrol**: A clear, colorless liquid derived from crude oil, used as a fuel for internal combustion engines. Also known as gasoline in some countries.

### Verb (informal)
- To use or consume petrol, often in the context of filling up a vehicle's fuel tank.

## Phrases
- "Fill up with petrol" or "Refuel with petrol"
- "Running on empty, need to top up the petrol"
- "Petrol prices have risen again"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I had to stop at the gas station to fill up my car with petrol."
- **Verb**: "The car ran out of petrol on the highway, so we had to pull over."
- "The old car needed regular petrol refills due to its high consumption."

## Synonyms (Noun)
- Gasoline
- Petrol/gas (informal, UK)
- Fuel
- Motor spirit

## Antonyms
- None directly, but "electricity" could be seen as a contrasting energy source.
