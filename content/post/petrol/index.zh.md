## 读音
/ˈpɛtrəl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 汽油，一种石油提炼出的燃料，主要用于汽车引擎。例如: "I need to fill up my car with petrol."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与汽油相关的或由汽油驱动的。例如: "The petrol engine runs on fossil fuels."

## 短语
- "Petrol pump" 意为加油站。
- "Petrol price" 指汽油的价格。
- "Petrol-powered vehicle" 指汽油驱动的车辆。

## 例句
- 名词: "The cost of petrol has increased significantly over the past year."
- 形容词: "This vintage car has a beautiful petrol-blue paint job."
