## 读音
/ˈpɑːspɔrt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种官方文件，通常包含持有者的个人信息和照片，允许其在国际间旅行，并证明其国籍和身份。例如: "I need to renew my passport before my trip abroad."
- **动词 (v)**: 准许或允许进入或通过（通常用于比喻）。例如: "Her talent passported her straight to the top of her profession."

## 短语
- "Apply for a passport" 申请护照。
- "Check your passport validity" 检查你的护照有效期。
- "Hold a valid passport" 拥有一本有效的护照。

## 例句
- 名词: "Make sure you have your passport with you when you go through customs."
- 动词: "His exceptional skills passported him into a prestigious job."
