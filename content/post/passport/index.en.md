## Pronunciation
/pəˈspɔːrt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **passport** is a government-issued travel document that certifies the identity and nationality of a citizen, allowing them to enter and exit a country's borders. It often contains a photograph, personal information, and visa stamps.

### Verb
To **passport** (a journey) means to travel using a passport, especially when it's an official or required process.

## Phrases
- "Carry a passport" or "be in possession of a passport"
- "Apply for a passport"
- "International travel requires a passport"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After obtaining her passport, she was ready for her first solo trip abroad."
- **Verb**: "He had to passport his documents through customs before entering the country."
- **Passive construction**: "The passport was stamped upon arrival at the airport."
