## 读音
/ˈlɛsən/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  1. 课程，一堂课：A planned period of teaching or learning, typically in a school or college.
    - 例句: "We have a math lesson every morning."
  2. 教训，经验：Something that is learned from an experience or event.
    - 例句: "That mistake was a valuable lesson for all of us."

## 短语
- "Learn a lesson" 意味着从经历或错误中吸取教训。
- "Teach someone a lesson" 是指给某人一个教训，让他们明白错误所在。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher prepared a new lesson on grammar for her students."
- 教训: "After losing the game, they learned the importance of teamwork."
