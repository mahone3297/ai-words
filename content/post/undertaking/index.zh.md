## 读音
/ˌʌndərˈteɪkɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 任务；事业：一项需要努力和承诺的工作或活动。例如: "The construction of a new bridge is a major undertaking."
  - 承诺；保证：正式同意做某事的承诺。例如: "He made an undertaking to complete the project on time."
  - 葬礼：在某些文化中，指处理丧葬事务。例如: "The family entrusted the undertaking to a local funeral director."

## 短语
- "undertake an undertaking" 意为承担一个任务或项目。
- "business undertaking" 指商业企业或公司。
- "undertaking agreement" 指合同或协议，其中一方承诺完成特定工作。

## 例句
- 名词: "Managing the company's expansion was a challenging undertaking."
- 承诺: "The contractor gave an undertaking to finish the repairs by next week."
