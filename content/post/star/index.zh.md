## 读音
/stɑːr/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 天空中明亮的天体，尤其是太阳以外的其他恒星。例如: "The stars shine brightly in the night sky."
2. 影视或音乐界的名人。例如: "She's a big movie star."
3. （体育等领域的）明星。例如: "He's a star basketball player."
4. 图标或徽章上的五角形装饰物。例如: "The badge has a gold star on it."

- **动词 v**:
1. 在电影、电视节目或舞台上担任主角。例如: "She stars in the new Broadway play."
2. 作为突出或主要的角色。例如: "He starred in several hit movies."

## 短语
- "a rising star" 指在某个领域崭露头角的人。
- "follow one's star" 意为追求自己的梦想或目标。

## 例句
- 名词: "The constellation Orion contains several bright stars."
- 动词: "She stars alongside Tom Hanks in the upcoming film."
- 形容词: "The star quarterback led the team to victory."
