## 读音
/ˈprɑːvɪns/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个国家或地区内的行政区域，通常比州或县更大，但小于国家。例如: "[She](/post/she/) comes [from](/post/from/) [the](/post/the/) [beautiful](/post/beautiful/) [province](/post/province/) [of](/post/of/) Zhejiang [in](/post/in/) [China](/post/china/)."

## 短语
- "[province](/post/province/) [of](/post/of/)" 表示某个国家或地区的省份，如 "[the](/post/the/) [province](/post/province/) [of](/post/of/) Ontario"
- "[province](/post/province/) [capital](/post/capital/)" 指省份的首府或主要城市

## 例句
- "[The](/post/the/) [government](/post/government/) has implemented [new](/post/new/) agricultural policies [in](/post/in/) [several](/post/several/) provinces."
- "[Each](/post/each/) [province](/post/province/) has [its](/post/its/) [own](/post/own/) [unique](/post/unique/) [culture](/post/culture/) [and](/post/and/) traditions."
