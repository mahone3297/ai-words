## 读音
/ʃɪld/

## 释义
- **名词**: 保护物，防御物；盾牌。例如: "The knight carried a large metal shield in battle."
- **动词**: 保护，遮蔽。例如: "The umbrella shielded her from the rain."

## 短语
- "raise a shield" 抬起盾牌，防御。
- "shield someone from harm" 保护某人免受伤害。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police officer held up his shield to protect himself from the protesters."
- 动词: "The tree branches shielded the campsite from the wind."
