## 读音
/ˈmætʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 1. 相配的人或物；匹配的事物。例如: "The shoes are a perfect match for the dress."
  - 2. 比赛；对决。例如: "They played a thrilling tennis match yesterday."
  - 3. 火柴。例如: "Can you pass me a match to light the candle?"

- **动词**:
  - 1. 与...相配，匹敌。例如: "Her skills don't match his in this field."
  - 2. 使比赛，安排对决。例如: "We'll match the two teams against each other in the final round."

- **形容词**:
  - 1. 相配的，相符的。例如: "The colors are a perfect match."

## 短语
- "be a match for sb/sth" 意味着能与某人或某事物匹敌。
- "match made in heaven" 形容非常理想或完美的结合。

## 例句
- 名词: "The rug's pattern was a beautiful match for the living room's decor."
- 动词: "His strength doesn't match hers when it comes to weightlifting."
- 形容词: "Their personalities were a match made in heaven."
