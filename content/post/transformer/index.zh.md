## 读音
/ˈtrænsfôrmeər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种电子设备，用于改变交流电的电压或电流，通常由两个或多个电磁线圈组成，它们之间通过磁耦合传递能量。例如: "The transformer stepped up the voltage for transmission over long distances."
- **名词 (n)**: 在文学、艺术等领域，指具有变革性影响的人物或事物。例如: "The artist was considered a transformer of the contemporary art scene."
- **动词 (v)**: 改变或转换某物的形式、性质或功能。例如: "The new technology transformed the way we communicate."

## 短语
- "Transformer circuit" 指包含变压器的电路。
- "Transformer effect" 指由于变压器导致的电气效应。

## 例句
- 名词: "The power station had huge transformers to regulate the voltage."
- 名词: "His innovative ideas were a transformer in the field of education."
- 动词: "The invention transformed the manufacturing process, making it more efficient."

- Transformer (名词时):

- Transform (动词时):
