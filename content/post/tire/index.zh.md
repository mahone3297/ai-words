## 读音
/taɪr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 轮胎，车胎。例如: "The car needs new tires for a safer drive."
- **动词**: 使疲劳，累。例如: "Hours of work really tire me out."

## 短语
- "be tired of something" 意味着对某事感到厌倦或不耐烦。例如: "I'm getting tired of this repetitive job."
- "tire oneself out" 指通过努力工作或活动让自己筋疲力尽。例如: "She tired herself out playing tennis all day."

## 例句
- 名词: "I had to change a flat tire on the side of the road."
- 动词: "After a long day, he was completely tired."
