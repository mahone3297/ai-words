## 读音
/ʌnduː/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 取消：撤销或取消某事。例如: "I accidentally deleted the file, but I managed to undo it."
  - 恢复原状：使恢复到先前的状态或位置。例如: "Can you undo the knot in this string?"
  - 反转：改变或逆转一个动作或过程。例如: "The medicine helped undo the effects of the headache."

## 短语
- "Undo a mistake" 意味着改正错误。
- "Undo the damage" 指的是修复或弥补损失或破坏。

## 例句
- "Click 'undo' if you don't want to save the changes."
- "The surgeon worked carefully to undo the damage caused by the accident."
- "He quickly undid the buttons on his shirt."
