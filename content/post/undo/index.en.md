## Pronunciation
/ʌnˈduː/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To reverse the action or effect of something, usually by pulling or pushing in the opposite direction. For example: "I need to undo the changes I made."
- **Noun**: The act of undoing, or the state of being undone. For example: "The button on this shirt has come undone."
- **Adjective**: Reversible or capable of being undone. For example: "The knot is easily undoable."

## Phrases
- "Undo a mistake" means to correct an error or action.
- "Undo the damage" refers to repairing or reversing the effects of harm.
- "Undo a transaction" is when a financial deal is reversed.
