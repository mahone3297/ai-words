## 读音
/ˈeər.pɔrt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个设施，通常设有跑道和航站楼，供飞机起飞、降落和停靠，供乘客上下和货物装卸。
    - 例: "We'll meet you at the airport tomorrow morning."
- **名词 (n)**: 在某些情况下，也可指航空交通或航空业。
    - 例: "The airport has seen a significant increase in passenger traffic this year."

## 短语
- "International Airport" 指提供国际航班服务的机场。
- "Arrival Terminal" 用于旅客抵达的航站楼。
- "Departure Lounge" 旅客等待登机的区域。

## 例句
- "We have a two-hour layover at Heathrow Airport before our connecting flight."
- "She checked her luggage in at the airport before boarding the plane."
- "The airport shuttle takes passengers directly from the airport to the city center."
