## 读音
/ɔːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 矿石，含有金属或有价值矿物的岩石。例如: "Gold is often found in quartz veins within ore deposits."
- **名词**: 资源，原料。例如: "The country's vast oil and ore reserves make it an important player in the global market."

## 短语
- "ore deposit" 矿床，矿藏，指富含某种矿物的地质构造。
- "ores of iron" 铁矿石，指含铁量较高的岩石。

## 例句
- "The miners were extracting ore from the depths of the mountain."
- "The new discovery of copper ore has boosted the local economy."
