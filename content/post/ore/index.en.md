## Pronunciation
/ɔːr/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A naturally occurring solid material from which a metal or valuable mineral can be extracted profitably, typically by smelting. _Example_: "The mine was rich in copper ore."
- **Noun (rare)**: The raw material from which a particular product is made; a source or basis. _Example_: "Petroleum is the ore of many chemical products."

## Phrases
- "Vein of ore" refers to a deposit of ore in the earth's crust.
- "Extract ore" describes the process of mining and removing the ore from the ground for processing.
- "Ore deposits" are areas where minerals have accumulated and can be economically extracted.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Miners were excavating large quantities of gold ore from the recently discovered mine."
- _Rare usage_ : "Coal is the ore that supplies energy for most power plants around the world."

## Synonyms
- Mineral deposit
- Rock containing metals
- Ore body
- Raw material
- Commodity (in the context of mining)
