## 读音
/drɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 钻头，钻具。例如: "The worker used a power drill to make holes in the metal."  
2. 训练，操练。例如: "The football team had a drill session to practice their passing skills."

- **动词**: 训练，操练。例如: "The teacher drilled the students on multiplication tables."  
- **及物动词**: 钻孔。例如: "I need to drill a hole for the nail."

## 短语
- "drill sergeant" 军事训练教官
- "drill routine" 训练程序或步骤

## 例句
- 名词: "He sharpened the drill before starting the job."
- 动词: "The coach drills the athletes every morning to improve their speed."
- 及物动词: "She drilled a hole in the wall to hang a picture."
