## 读音
/θɔrn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 刺，荆棘，尖刺。指植物上尖锐的突起部分，常用来象征困难或困扰。例如: "The rose has beautiful flowers but be careful of its thorns."
- **形容词**: 刺痛的，令人不悦的。形容人或事物带来痛苦或烦恼。例如: "The thorny issue of taxation caused much debate."

## 短语
- "A bed of thorns" 比喻困苦或艰难的生活境遇。
- "Thorn in one's side" 意指使人烦恼或难以对付的人或事。

## 例句
- 名词: "The gardener wore gloves to protect his hands from the thorns while pruning the roses."
- 形容词: "The thorny problem of climate change requires immediate attention."
