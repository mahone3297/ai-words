## Pronunciation
/θɔːrn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A sharp, pointed projection on the stem of a plant, often causing pain when touched. 2. A person or thing that causes trouble or annoyance, metaphorically. 3. (pl.) Difficulty or hardships in life.

## Phrases
- "A thorn in one's side" refers to a persistent annoyance or problem.
- "Draw a thorn out" is a figurative expression meaning to confront or deal with a difficult situation.
- "Get rid of a thorn" implies removing a troublesome person or issue.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The hiker had to be careful not to touch the bramble, full of thorns."
- **Metaphorical usage**: "His constant criticism was like a thorn in her professional life."
- **Imperative**: "Remove the thorn from your finger before it gets infected."
