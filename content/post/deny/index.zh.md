## 读音
/daɪˈnaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 否认，拒绝承认。例如: "He denied any involvement in the incident."
- **名词**: 否认的动作或声明。例如: "Her denial of the allegations was strong and convincing."

## 短语
- "to deny something" 意为否定或拒绝接受某个事实或说法。
- "deny responsibility" 否认承担责任。

## 例句
- 动词: "She denied knowing anything about the missing money."
- 名词: "His denial of guilt left many people skeptical."

## 同义词
- Disclaim
- Repudiate
- Refute
- Contest
- Deny oneself (意思是克制或拒绝享受某种事物)
