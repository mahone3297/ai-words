## 读音
/stiˈdi/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 稳定的，持续的，不改变的。例如: "He has a steady job with good benefits."
- **动词**: 使稳定，稳固。例如: "The nurse helped the patient steady himself after the operation."
- **名词**: 稳定的关系或状态。例如: "They've been in a steady relationship for two years."

## 短语
- "steady as she goes" 意为保持现状，不要改变方向或速度。
- "on a steady course" 表示在稳定的道路上，按计划进行。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Her grades have been steady throughout the semester."
- 动词: "The sailor steadied the ship during the storm."
- 名词: "After several casual dates, they became a steady couple."

- Stable
