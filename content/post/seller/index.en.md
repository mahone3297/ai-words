## Pronunciation
/sɛlər/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A person who sells goods or services**: "The street vendor was a skilled seller of handmade crafts."
2. **One who engages in transactions**: "The online auction site had many professional sellers from around the world."

### Verb
1. **To offer for sale**: "The estate seller listed several valuable antiques."
2. **To act as an intermediary in a transaction**: "She helped her friend sell their house."

## Phrases
- "Sell something to someone"
- "Sell out" (to sell all available items)
- "Sell well" (to be popular or successful)

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The seller negotiated a good deal with the buyer."
- **Verb**: "The seller showcased their products at the trade fair."
- **Intransitive**: "After months of preparation, the seller finally sold the old house."
