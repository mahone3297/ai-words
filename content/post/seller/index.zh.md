## 读音
/ˈselər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 卖方；销售者；零售商。例如: "The seller presented several options for the product."
- **动词**: 出售；卖。例如: "She is trying to sell her old car."

## 短语
- "seller's market" 指的是买方需求大于供应，卖家有更多议价权的市场状况。
- "seller's remorse" 指卖家在出售物品后感到后悔的情绪。

## 例句
- 名词: "The seller assured us that the product was in excellent condition."
- 动词: "He has been trying to sell his house for months."
