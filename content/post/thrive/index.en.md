## Pronunciation
/θraɪv/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To grow, develop, or succeed in a healthy and vigorous manner, especially in a new environment or challenging situation. For example: "The startup thrived after securing funding."
- **Noun**: The act or state of flourishing; robust growth or progress. For example: "The company's success was a testament to their ability to thrive."

## Phrases
- "Thrive in adversity" means to do well despite difficult circumstances.
- "A thriving community" is one that is healthy, active, and growing.
- "To help something thrive" is to support its growth and development.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The plants thrived under the care of the experienced gardener."
- **Noun**: "Their marriage has thrived over the years, despite the challenges they faced."
- **Adverb**: "She thrived in her new job, quickly adapting to the fast-paced environment."
