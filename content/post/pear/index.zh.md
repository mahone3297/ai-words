## 读音
/peər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种水果，形状类似梨，通常是甜的，可食用。例如: "I love eating pears for dessert."
- **形容词**: 指与梨有关的或类似于梨的。例如: "The pear-shaped diamond was stunning."

## 短语
- "pear tree" 梨树，产梨的树。
- "pear blossom" 梨花，梨树的花朵。

## 例句
- 名词: "The farmer picked some fresh pears from his orchard."
- 形容词: "She wore a pear-shaped necklace that caught the light beautifully."
