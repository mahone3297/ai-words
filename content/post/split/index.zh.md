## 读音
/splɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 将某物分成两部分或更多部分。例如: "I split the apple in half."
  - 分离，分开。例如: "The couple decided to split up after years of marriage."
  - 分配，分摊。例如: "We'll split the cost of the dinner equally."
  - （时间）分配。例如: "She splits her time between work and family."

- **名词**:
  - 分开的部分。例如: "There was a split in the wooden door."
  - 分裂，分歧。例如: "The party suffered a major split over policy differences."

## 短语
- "Split second" 表示极短的时间。
- "Split decision" 在体育比赛或决策中指不一致的裁决。
- "Split personality" 指双重人格或性格分裂。

## 例句
- 动词: "He split the logs into smaller pieces for firewood."
- 名词: "The split in the team caused by the disagreement was significant."
