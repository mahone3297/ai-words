## Pronunciation
/spəʊnˈteɪniəs/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by occurring naturally, without prior planning or conscious effort; occurring or done instinctively or abruptly. For example: "Her laughter was spontaneous."
- **Adverb**: In a spontaneous manner; without prior arrangement or preparation. For example: "He answered the question spontaneously."

## Phrases
- "Spontaneous reaction" refers to an immediate response without thought.
- "Spontaneous combustion" is a chemical reaction that ignites without an external spark.
- "Spontaneous applause" is unexpected cheering or clapping.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The children's performances were full of spontaneous energy."
- **Adverb**: "She decided to go on a trip spontaneously, without telling anyone."
- "His answer came out spontaneously, surprising everyone."
