## 读音
/spəˈnænətɪs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 自发的；自然发生的；无预谋的。例如: "The demonstration was completely spontaneous."
- **名词**: （生物体的）自然生长或发展过程。例如: "The process of cell division is a spontaneous event in biology."

## 短语
- "spontaneous combustion" 自然燃烧
- "spontaneous laughter" 喜不自胜的笑声

## 例句
- 形容词: "Her creativity flows spontaneously, without any need for planning."
- 名词: "The plant's growth is a beautiful example of spontaneous life."
