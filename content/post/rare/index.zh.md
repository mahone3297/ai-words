## 读音
/rɛə(r)/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不常见的，稀有的，珍贵的。例如: "Rare animals are becoming more endangered every year."
- **副词**: 很少地，难得地。例如: "He visits his parents only rarely these days."

## 短语
- "a rare occasion" 意为不常发生的情况或事件。
- "rare metals" 指稀有金属，如铂、铑等。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The white lion is a rare and beautiful creature."
- 副词: "It's rare for her to be late for work."
