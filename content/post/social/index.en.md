## Definitions
### Noun
- **Social**: Referring to the way people interact with each other in society, often involving shared interests or activities. Example: "The social event was well-attended."
- **Social media**: Online platforms where individuals connect and share information, typically for personal or professional purposes. Example: "She updates her social media regularly."

### Verb
- **To socialize**: To engage in conversation, activities, or relationships with others for amusement or to form connections. Example: "They enjoy socializing with friends over dinner parties."
- **Social dynamics**: The way people behave and interact in group settings. Example: "Understanding social dynamics is crucial in a workplace."

### Adjective
- **Socially**: Relating to society or its norms; involving or characterized by interaction with others. Example: "She's a socially skilled diplomat."
- **Sociable**: Enjoying the company of others or easily adapting to social situations. Example: "He's a very sociable person who loves to host gatherings."

### Adverb
- **Socially**: In a social manner or context. Example: "He handled the situation socially, avoiding conflict."
