## Pronunciation
/ˈvɛləsiˌtɪ/

## Definitions
### Noun
**Velocity** is a measure of speed in a particular direction. It combines both the magnitude (speed) and direction of motion.

### Verb
To **move with velocity** or **achieve velocity** means to travel quickly or change position rapidly.

## Phrases
- "Reach a high velocity" signifies achieving a rapid speed.
- "Maintain a constant velocity" refers to moving at a steady pace.
- "Decelerate to a safe velocity" means slowing down gradually.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The car accelerated to a high velocity on the highway."
- **Verb**: "The astronaut maneuvered the spacecraft to maintain a constant velocity during orbit."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "The bullet's velocity upon impact was crucial to its effectiveness."
