## 读音
/ruːd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 粗鲁的，无礼的，不礼貌的。例如: "His behavior was very rude to the waiter."
- **副词**: 粗鲁地，无礼地。例如: "She answered me quite rudely."

## 短语
- "Be rude to someone" 意味着对某人不礼貌。
- "Rude awakening" 指突然而令人不悦的现实发现。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It's not polite to be rude to people when they're trying to help you."
- 副词: "He interrupted her rudely while she was speaking."
