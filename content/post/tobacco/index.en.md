## Pronunciation
/təˈbækəʊʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The dried leaves of the tobacco plant, often used for smoking in cigarettes, cigars, or pipes, and as a component of chewing tobacco and snuff. 2. (informal) The habit or practice of smoking tobacco.
- **Verb**: To consume or use tobacco, typically as a habit or product. 3. (transitive) To introduce or supply with tobacco.

## Phrases
- "Tobacco addiction" refers to the compulsive need to use tobacco.
- "Smoking tobacco" is the act of inhaling smoke from burned tobacco.
- "Quitting tobacco" is the process of discontinuing tobacco use.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The store specializes in a wide variety of imported tobaccos."
- **Verb**: "He tried to quit smoking tobacco after his doctor's warning."
- **Phrase**: "Her grandfather was a lifelong tobacco farmer."
