## 读音
/yaːrd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 院子，庭院。例如: "There is a small garden in the backyard of our house."
  - 美制长度单位，等于3英尺或91.44厘米。例如: "The football field is 100 yards long."

## 短语
- "front yard" 前院
- "backyard" 后院
- "a yardstick" 一杆（测量工具）

## 例句
- 名词: "The kids were playing soccer in the yard."
- 数量单位: "The fabric measures two yards in length."

## 同义词
- Garden
- Courtyard
- Area
- Lot
- Section (在指土地或区域时)
