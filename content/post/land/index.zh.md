## 读音
/land/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 地面；陆地：The plane landed on the land.
2. 国家；地区：He comes from a distant land.
3. 土地（所有权或使用权）：They own a large tract of land.

- **动词**:
1. （飞机、船只等）着陆，降落：The airplane landed safely at the airport.
2. （使）上岸；登陆：The immigrants landed in New York in search of a better life.
3. （非正式）使陷入某种状态或境地：His bad luck landed him in debt.

## 短语
- "land on" 着陆，到达：The spaceship landed on the moon.
- "land a job" 获得一份工作：After many interviews, she finally landed a job at a tech company.
- "land in trouble" 陷入困境：If you keep lying, you'll land in serious trouble.

## 例句
- 名词: "The farmer plowed the fertile land for his crops."
- 动词: "The hot air balloon landed gently in the field."
- 动词（非正式）: "Her careless driving could land her in jail."
