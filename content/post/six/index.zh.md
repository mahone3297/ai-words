## 读音
/sɪks/

## 释义
- **数词**: 表示数字6，六个。例如: "There are six apples on the table."

## 短语
- "six of one, half a dozen of the other" 意为两者并无太大差别。
- "up to six" 表示最多六个人或事物。

## 例句
- "We need six people to form a team."
- "She has six siblings in total."
