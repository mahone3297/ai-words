## Definitions
- **Numeral**: A numeral that represents the quantity six. It comes before a countable noun to indicate the number. For example: "There are six apples on the table."
- **Adjective**: Designating the position or order in a series, being the sixth in a set. For example: "The sixth book in the series is my favorite."
- **Adverb**: Expressing the quantity or degree of something, usually in reference to time or frequency. For example: "I visit my grandparents every six months."
