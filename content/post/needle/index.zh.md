## 读音
/ˈniːdl/

## 释义
- **名词 n**: 1. 缝纫或医疗用途的细长尖头物体，通常由金属制成。例如: "She threaded the needle and began sewing."
   - 2. 针状物；尖锐批评。例如: "His comments were like needles, piercing her ego."

## 短语
- "thread the needle" 意味着穿过针眼，常比喻完成困难的任务。
- "stick someone with a needle" 指注射或刺伤某人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The nurse carefully inserted the needle into his arm."
- 短语: "She managed to thread the needle on the first try."
