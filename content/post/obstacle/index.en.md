## Pronunciation
/ˈɑbstəkəl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: An object, thing, or situation that blocks one's way or hinders progress. For example: "The fallen tree was an obstacle on the hiking trail."
  - _Formal definition_: A hindrance or difficulty impeding accomplishment, achievement, or further progress.

## Phrases
- "Overcome obstacles": To successfully deal with problems or challenges that stand in one's way.
- "Obstacle course": A series of physical challenges designed to test strength, agility, and endurance.
- "Remove obstacles": To eliminate or minimize barriers that prevent success or forward movement.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her fear of public speaking proved to be a significant obstacle in her career advancement."
- "They had to navigate numerous obstacles during their journey across the desert."
