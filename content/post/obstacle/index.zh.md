## 读音
/ˈɑːb.stl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 障碍物；阻碍；困难。例如: "There are several obstacles to overcome before we can finish the project."
- **动词**: 阻碍；阻挡。例如: "The broken bridge obstructed our way and became an obstacle."

## 短语
- "Obstacle course" 指的是障碍赛跑或训练课程。
- "Remove obstacles" 意味着消除障碍或解决问题。

## 例句
- 名词: "The construction site has become a major obstacle for pedestrians."
- 动词: "His lack of experience didn't obstacle his promotion; he was still given the job."

- Hindrance
