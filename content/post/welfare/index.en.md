## Definitions
### Noun
**Welfare** is a noun that refers to the state of being physically, mentally, or socially healthy and prosperous, especially as it relates to public support systems and government programs aimed at improving the well-being of citizens. It can also be used to describe the general quality of life or standard of living.

### Verb
As a verb, "to welfare" is not a common term in the English language. However, one might use the phrase "to promote welfare" or "to advocate for welfare programs."

### Adjective
"Welfare" can also function as an adjective to describe the services or policies designed to benefit people's well-being.

## Phrases
- "Promote welfare" indicates supporting or advancing the well-being of a community or group.
- "Welfare benefits" are the financial assistance provided by governments to those in need.
- "Welfare state" refers to a society where the government plays a significant role in providing social services and support.
