## 读音
/wɛlˈfɛr/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 福利，福利事业；善事。例如: "The company provides good welfare for its employees."
   2. 幸福，安康。例如: "The main goal of the government is to improve the welfare of its citizens."

- **形容词 (adj)**: 有关福利的，福利的。例如: "welfare programs"（福利计划）

## 短语
- "welfare state" 指提供广泛社会福利服务的国家体制。
- "child welfare" 关注儿童福祉的政策和服务。
- "welfare reform" 福利制度改革

## 例句
- 名词: "The new policy aims to enhance social welfare services for low-income families."
- 形容词: "There are various welfare organizations working towards animal protection."
