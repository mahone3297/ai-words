## 读音
/pʌls/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 心跳；脉搏。例如: "He felt his own pulse racing with excitement."
  - 电子信号的脉冲；波形。例如: "The computer sends and receives data through electrical pulses."

- **动词**:
  - 感觉或检测脉搏。例如: "The doctor asked him to pulse his wrist for a moment."
  - 发送或产生脉冲。例如: "The laser gun pulsed a beam of light."

## 短语
- "Pulse rate" 指的是每分钟的心跳次数。
- "Pulse width modulation" (PWM) 是一种电子技术，通过改变脉冲的宽度来调节信号的平均值。

## 例句
- 名词: "The athlete's strong and steady pulse indicated good physical condition."
- 动词: "The nurse pulsed the patient's neck to check their vital signs."
- 感叹词: (不常见作为感叹词使用，通常作为名词或动词)
