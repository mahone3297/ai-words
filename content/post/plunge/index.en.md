## Pronunciation
/plʌnʤ/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (n) To drop or fall suddenly or rapidly into a liquid or a hole; to descend with force or momentum. (v) To make a sudden or drastic change or decision. (n) An act or instance of plunging.

## Phrases
- "Plunge into" means to jump or enter something with great enthusiasm or force.
- "A financial plunge" refers to a sudden decline in the stock market or economy.
- "To take the plunge" is an idiomatic expression for making a bold decision or starting something new without hesitation.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The diver plunged into the icy water without a second thought."
- **Noun**: "Her investment portfolio took a significant plunge after the company's announcement."
- **Idiomatic**: "After much contemplation, they decided to take the plunge and start their own business."
