## 读音
/plʌndʒ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 突然深深地插入或投入。例如: "He plunged his hand into the icy water."
2. 急剧下降或下跌。例如: "The stock market plunged dramatically."
3. 猛烈地跳入水中。例如: "She plunged into the swimming pool."

- **名词**:
1. 猛烈的跳跃或投入。例如: "The diver made a graceful plunge into the pool."
2. 忽然的下落或暴跌。例如: "The plane experienced a terrifying plunge."

## 短语
- "take the plunge" 冒险尝试，决定去做某事。例如: "After months of hesitation, she finally took the plunge and started her own business."
- "plunge into" 深入参与或开始做某事。例如: "He immediately plunged into his work after arriving at the office."

## 例句
- 动词: "She plunged headfirst into the icy river to save the drowning child."
- 名词: "His decision to quit his job was a bold plunge into the unknown."
