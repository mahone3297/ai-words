## 读音
/ˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 解放，释放，赋予自由。例如: "The liberation of Paris marked the end of Nazi occupation."
- **动词**: 解放，使自由。例如: "The troops were tasked with liberating the city from enemy control."

## 短语
- "Political liberation" 指政治上的解放或独立。
- "Liberation movement" 指争取自由或解放的运动。
- "Liberation theology" 是一种结合社会正义和基督教教义的神学理论。

## 例句
- 名词: "The country celebrated its independence day, marking the anniversary of their national liberation."
- 动词: "The prisoners were finally liberated after a long and brutal captivity."
