## 读音
/knɒk/

## 释义
- **动词**: 敲击；撞击。例如: "He knocked on the door three times before anyone answered."
- **名词**: 敲击声；撞击声。例如: "I heard a knock at the window."
- **动词**: 打败；挫败。例如: "She knocked her opponent out in the first round."
- **短语动词**: "knock about/around" 漫游，闲逛。例如: "They spent the summer knocking around Europe."

## 短语
- "Knock on wood" 是一种迷信说法，用于祈求好运或避免厄运。
- "Knock someone out" 使某人失去知觉，打败某人。
- "Knock something over" 打翻某物。

## 例句
- 动词: "He knocked the vase off the table by accident."
- 名词: "There was a loud knock at the door that made us jump."
- 短语动词: "She's been knocking about town all day, looking for a job."
