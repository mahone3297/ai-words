## 读音
/məˈrɪməl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 纪念碑，纪念物。例如: "They unveiled a new war memorial in the city center."
  - 纪念活动，纪念仪式。例如: "The annual memorial for the victims takes place on May 12th."

## 短语
- "Memorial Day" 美国阵亡将士纪念日，通常在五月的最后一个星期一。
- "In memory of" 用来表示对逝者的怀念，例如: "A plaque reads 'In memory of our beloved friend, John'."

## 例句
- "The family gathered around the memorial to pay their respects."
- "The university held a memorial service for the late professor."
