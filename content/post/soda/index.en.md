## Pronunciation
/ˈsəʊdə/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A carbonated beverage, typically flavored with sugar or artificial sweeteners, made from water, syrup, and carbon dioxide gas. 2. A generic term for any soft drink, fizzy drink, or pop. 3. (US) A synonym for "soda pop."
- **Verb**: To consume or serve soda, often as a beverage. 4. To refer to soda as a type of drink.

## Phrases
- "Have a soda" means to offer or request a glass of soda.
- "Soda fountain" refers to a place where soft drinks are served.
- "Regular soda" or "plain soda" usually means a non-flavored carbonated water.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I grabbed a bottle of soda from the fridge for my friend."
- **Verb**: "The kids love to order soda with their meals at the diner."
- **Referring to soda**: "She prefers her soda without ice, just like it was in her childhood."

## Synonyms
- Pop
- Soft drink
- Carbonated beverage
- Fizzy drink
- Sodaphile (a person who enjoys soda)

## Note: The verb form "soda" is not a standard usage, but it can be informally used to describe the act of consuming soda. A more common verb would be "drink" or "have."
