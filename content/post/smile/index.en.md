## Pronunciation
/smɪl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A facial expression showing pleasure, happiness, or friendliness, often accompanied by a lifting of the lips and corners. For example: "Her infectious smile brightened up the room."
- **Verb**: To display a smile, often as a sign of happiness, friendliness, or agreement. For example: "He smiled warmly when he heard the good news."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by a smiling appearance. For example: "A sunny smile greeted the visitors at the entrance."

## Phrases
- "Wear a smile" implies being cheerful or friendly.
- "Return a smile" means to respond with a similar expression.
- "A genuine smile" indicates sincerity in the expression.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She wore a big smile after receiving the birthday present."
- **Verb**: "He smiled politely when introduced to the new colleague."
- **Adjective**: "The teacher's kind smile put the students at ease."
