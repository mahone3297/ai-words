## 读音
/ˈjuːtəlaɪz/

## 释义
- **动词**: 充分利用；使用；应用。例如: "We need to utilize every available resource to complete the project."
- **名词**: 利用；使用。例如: "The company's goal is to maximize the utilization of its technology."

## 短语
- "Utilize something for something" 意味着将某物用于特定目的。例如: "They utilized their skills to build a successful business."
- "Maximize utilization" 指尽可能充分利用。例如: "The manager wants to maximize the utilization of the staff during peak hours."

## 例句
- 动词: "We will utilize this new software to streamline our workflow."
- 名词: "The machine has a high utilization rate, running almost 24/7."
