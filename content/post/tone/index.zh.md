## 读音
/təʊn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 音调，语气：The tone of her voice indicated she was angry.
    - 色调，色彩：The painting had a warm tone.
    - 气氛，氛围：The party had a relaxed and friendly tone.
    - (音乐)音阶中的一个音：C is the first tone in the major scale.

- **形容词**:
    - 音调的，声调的：a tone poem
    - 身体状况良好的：He looks very fit and toned.
    - (皮肤)有光泽的：She has a beautifully toned complexion.

## 短语
- "Set the tone" 意为定下基调或气氛，如：His speech set the tone for the rest of the meeting.
- "In tone with" 表示与…一致，如：The decor was in tone with the period of the house.

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist used different tones of blue to create a sense of depth in the painting."
- 形容词: "Regular exercise helps keep your muscles toned."

## 同义词
- 音调/语气:
    - Inflection
    - Cadence
    - Pitch
- 气氛/氛围:
    - Atmosphere
    - Vibe
    - Mood
