## 读音
/pəˈliːs/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 警察，警力，警方。指维护法律和秩序的人员或组织。例如: "The police arrested the suspect."
- **名词 (n)**: 警察局，警察署。例如: "You can report a crime at the local police station."

## 短语
- "call the police" 意为报警。
- "police action" 指警方采取的行动或执法行为。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police were called to break up the protest."
- 名词: "There's a police station just around the corner."
