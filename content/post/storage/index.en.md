## Pronunciation
/stɔrɪdʒ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
    - 1. The act of keeping or the state of being kept; holding something in reserve or for future use. [Example: "The storage facility holds a large amount of inventory."]
    - 2. A place or container where items are stored. [Example: "The garage serves as additional storage space."]
- **Verb**: To keep or retain something for later use. [Example: "We need to store these documents securely."]
- **Adjective**: Designating or relating to the capacity or process of storing. [Example: "A high-capacity storage drive."]

## Phrases
- "Storage capacity" refers to the maximum amount of data or items that can be held.
- "Cloud storage" is the remote storage of data on servers accessed over the internet.
- "Storage unit" is a rented space for storing personal belongings.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "We're looking into expanding our data storage solutions."
- **Verb**: "The company has been storing old files in an offsite storage facility."
- **Adjective**: "The new server has ample storage for all our projects."
