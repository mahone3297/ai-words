## 读音
/stɔːrɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 存储，存放东西的地方。例如: "We need more storage space for our files."
- **动词 (v)**: 储存，把东西保存起来。例如: "She stored the extra furniture in the attic."

## 短语
- "Data storage" 指数据的储存。
- "Cloud storage" 是指云存储服务。
- "Home storage solutions" 指家庭存储解决方案。

## 例句
- 名词: "The garage is full of boxes, we urgently need more storage."
- 动词: "Before winter, we store firewood near the house for easy access."
