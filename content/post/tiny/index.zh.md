## 释义
- **形容词**: 极小的，微小的，细小的。例如: "The tiny ant was almost invisible on the ground."
- **名词**: 小事物的昵称或爱称。例如: "She called her new kitten 'Tiny'."

## 短语
- "tiny bit" 表示非常少量，如 "I just need a tiny bit of sugar in my coffee."
