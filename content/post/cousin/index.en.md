## Pronunciation
/kəˈzʌn/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **cousin** is a relative who is the child of an aunt or uncle, or the grandchild or great-grandchild of one's parent's sibling. It can also refer to a more distant relative with a shared familial connection.

### Verb
To **cousin** (informally) is to resemble or act like a cousin, often in terms of behavior or appearance.

### Adjective
The term **cousinly** describes qualities or actions that are characteristic of cousins, such as familiarity or shared family traits.
