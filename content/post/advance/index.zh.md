## 读音
/ədˈvɑːns/

## 释义
- **动词**: 向前移动；进步。例如: "The army advanced towards the enemy's position."
- **名词**: 前进；进展。例如: "We've made significant advances in medical research."
- **动词**: 提前支付；预付。例如: "She asked for an advance on her salary."
- **名词**: 预付款；定金。例如: "He received a $1,000 advance for his book."
- **形容词**: 预先的；提前的。例如: "The advance warning saved many lives."

## 短语
- "Make an advance" 意为取得进步或提出提议。
- "Advance payment" 指预付款。
- "Advance notice" 意为提前通知。

## 例句
- 动词: "The team advanced their strategy for the next season."
- 名词: "The company's financial report showed impressive advances in sales."
- 形容词: "They offered an advance booking discount for the concert tickets."
