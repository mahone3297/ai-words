## 读音
/lez/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 较少的，较小的，不及…那么多的。例如: "He wants to eat less sugar to stay healthy."
- **副词**: 更少地，较少。例如: "She exercises less frequently than before."

## 短语
- "Less is more" 意味着简洁或适度往往更好。
- "No less than..." 表示不少于，不亚于。例如: "He scored no less than 90 points in the exam."

## 例句
- 形容词: "To save money, we need to buy less expensive items."
- 副词: "She practices less diligently now compared to when she first started."

## 同义词
- Fewer (用于可数名词)
- Smaller
- Not as much
- Decreased
- Reduced
