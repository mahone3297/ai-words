## Pronunciation
/səˈspɛlɪzeɪz/

## Definitions
### Verb
- **Noun**: A person or thing that specializes, focuses on a particular subject or skill, gaining expertise in it. For example: "She decided to specialize in veterinary medicine."
- **Verb**: To become proficient or expert in a specific area. For example: "He specialized in computer programming after completing his degree."

## Phrases
- "Specialize in something" means to develop expertise in a particular field.
- "To specialize professionally" signifies focusing on a career niche.
- "Outsourcing specialization" refers to delegating work to experts in a specific area.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She specialized in marine biology during her undergraduate studies."
- **Verb**: "After years of practice, he specialized in complex surgeries."
- **Adjective**: "The company has diversified its offerings by specializing in eco-friendly products."

## Synonyms
- Specialize (verb): concentrate, focus, excel, concentrate, develop expertise, master
- Specialization (noun): expertise, proficiency, concentration, specialization, focus

## Antonyms
- Generalize, diversify, broaden one's scope

## Usage notes
- The word "specialize" can be used both as a verb and a noun, referring to the act of becoming specialized or the state of being specialized, respectively. It is often associated with professional development or academic pursuits.
