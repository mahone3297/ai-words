## 读音
/ˈspeʃəlaɪz/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 专攻，专门从事（某一领域的工作或学习）。例如: "She specialized in art history during her studies."
    - 使专业化，使成为专家。例如: "The company specializes in producing high-quality software."

## 短语
- "Specialize in something" 意味着在某个特定领域有深入的知识或技能。
- "To be a specialist" 指的是在某一特定领域有专业知识的人。

## 例句
- "He specialized in quantum physics and became a renowned scientist."
- "The restaurant specializes in Italian cuisine, offering a wide range of pasta dishes."
- "She decided to specialize in environmental law to help protect the planet."
