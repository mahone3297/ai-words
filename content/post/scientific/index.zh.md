## 读音
/ˈsɪkəntɪfɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 关于科学的；遵循科学方法的；基于科学研究的。例如: "The research was highly scientific and well-documented."
- **名词**: 科学家。例如: "She is an accomplished scientific with numerous publications."

## 短语
- "Scientific method" 指科学研究中系统和逻辑的方法。
- "Scientific theory" 指经过验证和广泛接受的科学解释。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The study employed a scientific approach to analyze the data."
- 名词: "Many scientific discoveries have changed our understanding of the world."

## 同义词
- Research-based
- Evidence-based
- Scholarly
- Methodical
- Rational
