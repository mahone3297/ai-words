## Pronunciation
/əˈkeɪʒənəl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Occurring from time to time; not constant or regular but happening now and then. 

Example sentence: _We have occasional meetings to discuss project updates._
- **Adjective**: Intended for use on special or infrequent occasions rather than regularly.

Example sentence: _She brought out her occasional table when guests came over._

## Phrases
- "On occasion": Sometimes, but not regularly.
  Example: _I visit my hometown on occasion._
- "Occasional visitor/guest": Someone who visits or attends somewhere but not frequently.
  Example: _He's just an occasional visitor to our club._
  
## Example Sentences
- **Adjective (irregular occurrence)**: "Despite being busy, he manages to make occasional trips to the gym."
- **Adjective (for special occasions)**: "The occasional chair in the living room was reserved for special guests."
