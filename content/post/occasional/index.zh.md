## 读音
/əˈkeɪʒənl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 偶尔的，不经常的，非惯常的。例如: "He visits his parents on occasional weekends."
- **名词**: 偶然事件，不时之需。例如: "She had to handle some occasional work emergencies."

## 短语
- "on occasion" 意为有时，偶尔。例如: "I go running on occasion when the weather is nice."
- "an occasional visitor" 指偶尔来访的人。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She works from home on occasional Fridays."
- 名词: "The restaurant offers special discounts for occasional customers."

## 同义词
- Infrequent
- Occasional
- Sporadic
- Uncommon
