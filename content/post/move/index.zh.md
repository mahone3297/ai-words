## 读音
/muːv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 移动，改变位置。例如: "I need to move the sofa to the other wall."
  - 起身，行动。例如: "It's time to move; we have a meeting in 10 minutes."
  - （使）感动，打动。例如: "The movie really moved me to tears."
  - 发起，开始。例如: "They decided to move against the enemy's position."

- **名词**:
  - 行动，动作。例如: "His next move was unexpected."
  - 变化，进展。例如: "We're waiting for some positive moves in the negotiations."
  - （棋类游戏中的）走棋。例如: "It's your move now."

## 短语
- "on the move" 表示在移动中，忙碌。例如: "The company is always on the move, expanding its operations."
- "make a move" 意为采取行动。例如: "If you don't make a move, we'll miss the train."

## 例句
- 动词: "He carefully moved the fragile vase to a safer spot."
- 名词: "The chessmaster analyzed his opponent's last move and planned his response."
