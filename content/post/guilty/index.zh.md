## 读音
/ˈɡɪlti/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有罪的，内疚的，感到自己有过错的。例如: "She felt guilty for forgetting her friend's birthday."
- **名词**: 罪恶感，内疚感。例如: "His conscience was plagued by guilt."

## 短语
- "guilty conscience" 表示良心不安，因为做了亏心事。
- "feel guilty about something" 意为对某事感到内疚。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The defendant claimed he was innocent, but the evidence made him look guilty."
- 名词: "After eating the whole cake, she felt a wave of guilt wash over her."
