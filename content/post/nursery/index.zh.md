## 读音
/nərˈsəri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 幼儿园；保育室：A place where young children are cared for and educated, typically before they start primary school.
    - 栽培苗圃：A place where plants are cultivated, especially young ones before they are sold or transplanted.

## 短语
- "nursery rhyme" 儿歌，童谣：Rhymes and songs that are traditional for young children.
- "plant nursery" 苗圃：A business that grows and sells young plants.
