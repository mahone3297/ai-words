## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈvestmənt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The action or process of investing money for profit or material result. For example: "They made a substantial investment in the startup company."
- **Noun**: Something that is invested, especially money put into financial schemes, property, or a commercial venture with the expectation of achieving a profit. For instance: "Real estate can be a good long-term investment."
- **Noun**: A thing that is acquired or purchased for future benefits or advantages; expenditure of resources with the goal of securing future benefits. Example: "Education is often considered as an investment in one's future."

## Phrases
- "Investment portfolio" refers to a collection of investments held by an individual or organization.
- "Return on investment (ROI)" is a performance measure used to evaluate the efficiency of an investment.
- "Investment strategy" describes a plan or method for selecting investments to meet financial goals.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She decided to diversify her investment portfolio by investing in both stocks and bonds."
- "The company plans to make a significant investment in research and development this year."
- "After careful consideration, he chose to invest his savings in a new business opportunity, hoping for a high return on investment."
