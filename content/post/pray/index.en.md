## Pronunciation
/prɛɪ/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To engage in the act of communicating with God or a higher power, typically asking for help, guidance, or forgiveness. *Prayer may involve silent meditation, vocal recitation, or communal worship.*
- **Noun**: A formal or informal request or invocation addressed to a deity or higher power. *She offered a prayer of gratitude before the meal.*
- **Noun**: A religious or spiritual practice that often includes devotion, reflection, and petition. *He spent his mornings in quiet prayer.*

## Phrases
- "Pray for someone" means to ask for their well-being or protection.
- "Prayer time" refers to a designated period for personal or communal prayer.
- "Say a prayer" is a request to join in or perform a prayer.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "After a long day at work, he would pray before going to bed."
- **Noun**: "The priest led the congregation in a collective prayer."
- **Noun**: "She found solace in her daily prayer ritual."
