## 读音
/pray/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 向神明祷告或祈求。例如: "He kneels down to pray before going to bed."
  - 希望，期待。例如: "I pray that everything turns out well."

## 短语
- "Pray for someone" 意为为某人祈祷。
- "Pray together" 指一起祷告。

## 例句
- 动词 (祷告): "The congregation stood up to pray during the service."
- 动词 (希望): "We all pray that the pandemic will end soon."

## 同义词
- Beg
- Implore
- Entreat
- Supplicate
- Ask (in a formal or earnest manner)
