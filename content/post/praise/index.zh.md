## 读音
/prais/

## 释义
- **动词**: 赞扬，表扬。例如: "The teacher praised the student for his hard work."
- **名词**: 赞扬，称赞。例如: "He received high praise from his colleagues."

## 短语
- "to heap praise upon someone" 表示大量赞扬某人。
- "praise be to..." 用于表达感激或敬意，例如 "Praise be to God."

## 例句
- 动词: "Her parents often praised her for her excellent grades."
- 名词: "The actor received lavish praise for his performance."
