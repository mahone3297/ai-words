## 读音
/ɪnˈtenʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 意图，目的。例如: "Her intention was to help him succeed."
- **动词 (v)**: 打算，计划。例如: "I intend to finish this project by tomorrow."

## 短语
- "Good intentions" 好意，善意。例如: "They had good intentions but their actions caused more harm than good."
- "With no ill intention" 没有恶意。例如: "He approached her with no ill intention."

## 例句
- 名词: "His true intention behind the suggestion remained unclear."
- 动词: "She intends to study abroad next year."
