## 读音
/ˈɪntɪmət/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 私人的，亲密的。例如: "They have an intimate relationship."
- **名词**: 密友，知己。例如: "She considers him one of her intimates."

## 短语
- "intimate knowledge" 指深入了解或熟悉。例如: "He has an intimate knowledge of the city's history."
- "intimate conversation" 指私密或深入的交谈。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The couple shared an intimate moment by the fireplace."
- 名词: "She only opens up to her intimates about her personal struggles."
