## 读音
/ˈsɜːvənt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个为他人服务的人，尤指在家庭、机构或政府中的雇员；仆人，佣人。例如: "The butler was the head of all the servants in the mansion."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 服务性的，用于提供服务的。例如: "The servant class has been an essential part of traditional societies."

## 短语
- "at the servant's disposal" 表示某事物可供仆人使用。
- "be in service as a servant" 意味着作为仆人在工作。

## 例句
- 名词: "In the old days, wealthy families had many servants to attend to their every need."
- 形容词: "The room was designed for the servant's convenience."
