## 读音
/prɪˈzɛns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 存在；出席；到场。例如: "His presence filled the room with an air of authority."
- **名词**: (常与the连用) 威严；庄重。例如: "The queen's presence demanded respect."
- **名词**: (常与one's连用) 个人魅力；风采。例如: "She had a captivating presence on stage."

## 短语
- "in the presence of" 表示在...的面前。例如: "They behaved politely in the presence of their elders."
- "a commanding presence" 指有威严或影响力的存在。例如: "The general had a commanding presence that inspired his troops."

## 例句
- 名词: "We felt his presence even though he wasn't physically in the room."
- 名词: "Her mere presence lightened the mood of the gathering."
