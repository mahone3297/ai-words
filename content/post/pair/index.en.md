Word Pair: Goodbye

## Pronunciation
/ˌɡʊdˈbaɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A word or expression used when parting or taking leave, typically expressing courtesy or friendliness. Example: "They exchanged goodbyes before leaving for their separate homes."
  
  - **Verb**: To bid farewell or say goodbye to someone. Example: "She was sad to be saying goodbye to her friends before moving away."
  
  - **Interjection**: Used to express departure or conclusion. Example: "Goodbye! Have a safe trip!"

## Phrases
- "Say goodbye" means to bid farewell or express departure wishes. Example: "It's time for us to say goodbye until next week."
- "Farewell" or "goodbye forever" implies a more permanent or long-term separation.
- "Goodbye and good luck" is a phrase used to wish someone success along with bidding them farewell.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Their last goodbye was filled with tears and hugs."
- **Verb**: "He waved and said goodbye as the train pulled out of the station."
- **Interjection**: "Goodbye, folks! I'll see you all tomorrow."
