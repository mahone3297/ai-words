## 读音
/pɛər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一对，一双，通常指的是两个相同或相配的事物。例如: "They bought a new pair of shoes."
- **动词 (v)**: 匹配，成对。例如: "These socks don't pair well; one is blue and the other is green."

## 短语
- "a pair of" 表示一对或一双。例如: "I need a new pair of gloves."
- "pair up" 意味着将事物或人组合在一起。例如: "The students paired up for the group project."

## 例句
- 名词: "She was wearing a stylish pair of sunglasses."
- 动词: "Can you pair these earrings with something from your collection?"
