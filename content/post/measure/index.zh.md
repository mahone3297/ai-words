## 释义
- **名词**: 测量；计量单位；标准；措施。例如: "The measure of a man's success is not in his wealth but in his character."
- **动词**: 测量；估量；权衡。例如: "I need to measure the dimensions of the room before buying new furniture."
- **及物动词**: 制定（法律、政策等）。例如: "The government will measure a new law to combat climate change."

## 短语
- "take measures" 意为采取行动或措施。例如: "We must take measures to protect the environment."
- "in measure" 意为适度地，有节制地。例如: "Enjoy life in measure, without excess."
