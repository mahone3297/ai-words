## 读音
/grænt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 授予物；赠款：A sum of money or other aid given, especially by a government or other organization, for a specific purpose.
  - 权利；许可：Permission or authority to do something.

- **动词**:
  - 授予；给予：To give (money, permission, etc.) officially or formally.
  - 承认；同意：To admit or concede something, such as a point in an argument.

## 短语
- "grant aid" 意为提供援助或补助。
- "grant permission" 意为给予许可。
- "grant access" 意为允许进入或使用。

## 例句
- 名词: "The government has announced a new grant for small businesses affected by the pandemic."
- 动词: "The university granted her a scholarship to pursue her PhD studies."
- 承认: "After much debate, they finally granted the legitimacy of his argument."
