## Pronunciation
/rɪˈzɪdənt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who lives or works in a particular place, especially on a permanent basis. ["A resident of New York City."](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/resident)
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of a place or period; native or inhabitant. ["The resident artist has a unique style."](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/resident_1)

## Phrases
- "Resident doctor" refers to a physician who is living and working in a specific medical facility.
- "Long-term resident" is a person who has lived in a place for a considerable amount of time.
- "Tenant resident" is a person who occupies a dwelling as a tenant.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The residents of the neighborhood organized a community clean-up event."
- **Adjective**: "The resident plants in the garden attract various birds."
- "The resident expert gave a presentation on local history."
