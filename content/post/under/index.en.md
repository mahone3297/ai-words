## Pronunciation
/həˈloʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to greet or address someone. For example: "She waved a friendly hello at the waiter."
- **Verb**: To express greeting or acknowledgement; to say "hello" to someone. For example: "He greeted the new employee with a hearty hello."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of or expressing friendliness or warmth; amiable. For example: "Her friendly hello brightened up the room."

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to extend a greeting.
- "Hello, dear" is a familiar way to address someone.
- "Hello there, stranger" can be used to acknowledge someone unexpectedly.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children exchanged hellos as they entered the playground."
- **Verb**: "After a long day, it felt good to hear a genuine hello from a colleague."
- **Adjective**: "Her warm and friendly hello was a welcome sight after a cold, rainy day."
