## 读音
/ˈʌndər/

## 释义
- **介词**: 在...下面；低于，少于。例如: "The book is under the table."，"The temperature dropped under zero."
- **形容词**: 下面的；较低的；次级的。例如: "The under layer of soil is rich in nutrients."，"He was the underdog in the race."
- **副词**: 在下面；向下。例如: "She reached under the bed to get her shoes."

## 短语
- "under pressure" 压力之下。
- "under the weather" 身体不适。
- "under construction" 正在建设中。

## 例句
- 介词: "The cat likes to sleep under the couch."
- 形容词: "The underclassmen are still learning their way around campus."
- 副词: "He ducked under the low doorway."

## 同义词
- Below
- Beneath
- Less than
- Down
- In the lower position
