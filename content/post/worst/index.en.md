## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Adjective**: The lowest point or degree of quality, quantity, or value among others; the most unfavorable or unsatisfactory. For example: "This was the worst performance I've ever seen."
- **Adverb**: In the most unfavorable manner or to the greatest extent; extremely. For example: "She felt the worst after the long journey."

### Verb
- To consider or regard something as the worst; to judge negatively. For example: "The critics panned the movie as the worst of the year."
- To experience the least favorable outcome or consequences. For example: "I feared the worst when I saw the storm approaching."

## Phrases
- "Worst-case scenario" refers to the most pessimistic or unfavorable situation.
- "Not the worst thing that could happen" implies a slightly less severe situation.
- "Better than the worst" suggests a comparative improvement.
