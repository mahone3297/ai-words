## Pronunciation
/tɔːr/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **tour** is a journey or visit taken to inspect, admire, or experience a particular place, series of places, or attractions, often as part of a guided group. It can also refer to a circuit of visits or events, such as a concert tour.

### Verb
To **tour** means to travel around a specific area, usually on a guided trip, for the purpose of sightseeing or performing. It can also mean to inspect or review something sequentially.

### Preposition
In the context of sports or entertainment, "on tour" refers to being away from home and performing or traveling for competitions or shows.

## Phrases
- "Go on a tour" - To embark on a planned trip to explore a place.
- "City tour" - A guided visit of a city's landmarks and attractions.
- "Concert tour" - A series of performances by a musical artist across different locations.
- "Museum tour" - A guided visit through an art or historical museum.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The tourists were taking a guided tour of the ancient ruins."
- **Verb**: "The band is embarking on a world tour next month."
- **Preposition**: "The artists are on a promotional tour to promote their new album."
