## 读音
/tʊr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 旅行；观光；巡回演出。例如: "We're planning a tour of Europe next summer."
- **动词**: 做旅行；游览。例如: "She's touring the country to promote her new book."
- **名词**: 巡回比赛或表演。例如: "The band is on a nationwide tour."

## 短语
- "Guided tour" 指有导游的游览。
- "City tour" 指城市观光之旅。
- "World tour" 指环球旅行或巡回演出。

## 例句
- 名词: "They went on a wine-tasting tour in Napa Valley."
- 动词: "The football team is touring South America for a series of friendly matches."
- 名词: "The rock band's latest album was released during their world tour."
