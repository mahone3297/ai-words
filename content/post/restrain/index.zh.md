## 读音
/riːˈstreɪn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 控制或阻止（某人或某事物）的行为或发展。例如: "The police were unable to restrain the angry crowd." 
  2. 使（自己）克制，避免做（某事）。例如: "I had to restrain myself from laughing at his joke."

## 短语
- "restrain oneself" 意为控制自己的行为或情感。
- "restrain from doing something" 表示避免做某事。

## 例句
- "The security guards were instructed to restrain any troublemakers."
- "She had to restrain her excitement when she saw her favorite singer."
- "The doctor advised him to restrain his eating habits to improve his health."

## 同义词
- control
- hold back
- curb
- check
