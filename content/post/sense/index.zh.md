## 读音
/sɛns/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 感官：身体的感觉器官，如视觉、听觉、味觉、触觉和嗅觉。例如: "We have five basic senses: sight, hearing, taste, touch, and smell."
  2. 感知：通过感官接收到的信息或理解。例如: "The child had a strong sense of danger in the dark room."
  3. 理解力：对事物的理解、判断或洞察力。例如: "She has a good sense of humor."
  4. 觉察：对某种情况或变化的察觉。例如: "He had a sense that something was wrong."

- **动词**:
  1. 感觉到：通过感官察觉到某物或某情况。例如: "The dog sensed danger and started barking."
  2. 理解：领悟或理解某事的意义。例如: "Can you sense the irony in his statement?"

## 短语
- "make sense" 意思是有道理，讲得通。例如: "What he said doesn't make any sense."
- "common sense" 指常识。例如: "Using common sense can often prevent accidents."

## 例句
- 名词: "The blind man relies on his other senses to navigate through the world."
- 动词: "She could sense the tension in the room."
