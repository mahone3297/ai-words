## 读音
/ˈaɪlənd/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 海洋或湖泊中的陆地，通常四面环水。例如: "They went on vacation to a beautiful tropical island."
- **名词 (n)**: 某一领域或情况中的孤立部分。例如: "The island of tranquility in the middle of a busy city."

## 短语
- "desert island" 荒岛，通常指的是无人居住的岛屿。
- "island hop" 在多个岛屿之间跳跃旅行或移动。

## 例句
- "We spent our honeymoon on a secluded island in the Maldives."
- "The island in the middle of the river is a popular picnic spot for locals."
