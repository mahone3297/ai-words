## 读音
/hʌnˈflaɪ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不快乐的，不幸福的。表示情绪上的不满或沮丧。例如: "She looked unhappy about the news."

## 短语
- "unhappy with something" 表示对某事不满意。
- "unhappy relationship" 指不愉快或有问题的关系。

## 例句
- "He seemed unhappy about the decision made at the meeting."
- "The child looked unhappy when he didn't get his favorite toy."
