## 读音
/ɪnˈsɜːrt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 插入，放进。例如: "Please insert the CD into the player."
2. 在文章、文本中加入（内容）。例如: "She inserted a new paragraph in her essay."
3. （在数据库或计算机程序中）添加数据。例如: "The program will automatically insert new records into the database."

## 短语
- "Insert after/before" 意为在...之后/之前插入。
- "Insert key" 在计算机术语中指的是用于插入文本的键盘键。

## 例句
- "Insert your credit card into the machine to start the transaction."
- "Please insert the SIM card into your phone correctly."
- "The editor inserted a correction at the end of the sentence."
