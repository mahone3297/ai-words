## 读音
/θroʊ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 投掷，扔。例如: "He threw the ball to his teammate."
- **名词**: 扔的动作或投掷物。例如: "The pitcher made a perfect throw to first base."

## 短语
- "throw away" 丢弃，浪费。例如: "Don't throw away that old book, it might be valuable."
- "throw off" 脱掉，摆脱。例如: "He threw off his coat and sat down."
- "throw oneself into" 猛烈投入，全身心投入。例如: "She threw herself into her work after the breakup."

## 例句
- 动词: "She threw the frisbee with great accuracy."
- 名词: "His powerful throw almost broke the window."
