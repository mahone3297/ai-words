## 读音
/plɪnˈtɛlɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 富裕的；丰富的；充足的。例如: "The garden is plentiful with fruits and vegetables."
- **副词**: 充分地；大量地。例如: "The store was plentifully supplied with goods."

## 短语
- "plentiful supply" 意味着丰富的供应。
- "plentiful resources" 表示丰富的资源。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The harvest this year was plentiful, so we'll have enough food for the winter."
- 副词: "The library is plentifully stocked with books on various subjects."
