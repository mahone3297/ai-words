## Pronunciation
/shəˈrəli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: **1.** With sharpness or keenness; clearly or distinctly. For example: "He spoke sharply to get her attention."
- **Adjective**: **2.** Marked by or characterized by severity or harshness; severe. For example: "Her criticism was sharp."
- **Adjective**: **3.** Accurate or precise in perception or judgment. For example: "His vision was sharp."
- **Verb**: (usually followed by 'with') To cut or slice with a sharp edge or instrument. For example: "She sharply cut the lemon to juice it."

## Phrases
- "Speak sharply" means to address someone in a forceful or direct manner.
- "A sharp turn" describes a sudden change in direction.
- "A sharp intellect" refers to someone with exceptional mental acuity.
