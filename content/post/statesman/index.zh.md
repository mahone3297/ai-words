## 读音
/steɪtzmən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个有经验且在政治上有影响力的人，尤指通过外交手段和政策制定对国家有重大影响的领导者。例如: "He was respected as a wise and experienced statesman."
  
## 短语
- "A statesman-like figure" 指具有政治家风范的人物。
- "To act with statesmanship" 表示以政治家的智慧和远见行事。

## 例句
- "The retired general is considered a potential statesman for his country."
- "In times of crisis, we need leaders who display true statesmanship."

- Stateswoman
