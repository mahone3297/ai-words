## Pronunciation
/ˈsɛntɪmɪtər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A unit of length in the metric system, equal to one hundredth of a meter. Abbreviated cm. For example: "The book is 2.5 centimeters thick."
- **Adjective**: Measuring or relating to one hundredth of a meter. For example: "The ruler is marked in centimeters."

## Phrases
- "A centimeter-long object" describes something measured in centimeters.
- "Measure in centimeters" means to use this unit for measurement.
- "One centimeter difference" indicates a small disparity in length.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The package was sealed with a strip of tape that was exactly one centimeter wide."
- **Adjective**: "The fine print on the label was minuscule, only visible under a magnifying glass, with text that was half a centimeter high."
- "I need to measure the distance between these two points to the nearest centimeter."

## Synonyms
- Millimeter (mm)
- Inch (in, when referring to a similar unit)
- Fraction of a meter (e.g., one-tenth of a meter)
- Metric unit of length
