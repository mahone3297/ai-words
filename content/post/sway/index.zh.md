## 读音
/sweɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 使摇摆，使摆动。例如: "The tree branches swayed in the wind."
- **名词**: 摇摆，摆动的动作。例如: "The ship's deck had a gentle sway."
- **形容词**: 有影响力的，能说服人的。例如: "His sway over the public was considerable."

## 短语
- "Sway back and forth" 意为来回摇摆。
- "Under someone's sway" 意味在某人的影响或控制之下。

## 例句
- 动词: "The dancers' bodies swayed rhythmically to the music."
- 名词: "The baby fell asleep to the soothing sway of the cradle."
- 形容词: "Her persuasive speech held the audience under her sway."
