## 读音
/ˈɑːftəwərdz/

## 释义
- **副词**: 在时间上指“后来”，“随后”。例如: "I'll call you afterward to discuss the details."

## 短语
- "Afterward, we can talk about the plan." 意思是在某个事件或行动之后进行讨论。

## 例句
- "We had a meeting, and afterward, I went for a coffee with a colleague."
