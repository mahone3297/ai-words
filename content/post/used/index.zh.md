## 读音
/ juːzd /

## 释义
- **形容词**: 用过的，旧的，熟悉的。例如: "She wore a used dress for the occasion."
- **动词 (过去式)**: 使用，利用。例如: "I used to drive that car every day."
- **动词 (过去分词)**: 被使用，被消耗。例如: "The book is well used and has many annotations."

## 短语
- "get used to something" 意味着习惯于某事。例如: "It took me a while to get used to living in the city."
- "be used for something" 表示用于某种目的。例如: "Old newspapers can be used for packing fragile items."

## 例句
- 形容词: "He bought a used car to save money."
- 动词（过去式）: "I used to play tennis every weekend."
- 动词（过去分词）: "The tools were so well used that they needed replacing."
