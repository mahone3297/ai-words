## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to express welcome, recognition, or acknowledgement; a salutation. Example: "She waved a friendly hello to her neighbor."
- **Verb**: To make a greeting or vocal expression of recognition or pleasure; to address with "hello." Example: "He greeted the teacher with a hearty hello in the morning."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of or expressing friendliness or familiarity. Example: "The hotel staff had a friendly hello for each guest."

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to initiate a greeting.
- "Hello, sunshine!" is a phrase to express joy upon seeing someone bright and positive.
- "Hello, old friend" is a way to address a long-time acquaintance.
