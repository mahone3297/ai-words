## Pronunciation
/ˈjɛs/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Affirmative or expressing agreement, typically in response to a question or statement. For example: "She nodded and said yes."
- **Verb**: To acknowledge or agree with something by saying "yes." For example: "Did you finish the homework? Yes, I did."
- **Interjection**: Expressing agreement or confirmation, often used as a reply. For example: "That's a great idea! Yes!"

## Phrases
- "To answer yes" means to respond positively.
- "I'm afraid not, but yes, otherwise." A conditional agreement.
- "Yes, and..." is a common way to build on someone else's statement in a conversation.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The project was approved; it's a yes from the manager."
- **Verb**: "She asked if he wanted to join, and he said yes."
- **Interjection**: "Did you want to go to the movies? Yes, I'd love to."
