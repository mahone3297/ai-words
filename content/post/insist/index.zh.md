## 读音
/ɪnˈsɪst/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    - 坚持要求；坚决主张：例如: "She insisted on paying for dinner."
    - 强调，着重指出：例如: "He insisted that he was innocent."

## 短语
- "insist on" 意为坚决要求或坚持认为。
- "insist upon" 含义相同，强调坚持某一观点或行动。

## 例句
- "Despite our objections, he insisted on walking home alone at night."
- "She insisted on the importance of regular exercise for good health."

- persist
