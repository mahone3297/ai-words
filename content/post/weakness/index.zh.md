## 读音
/ˈwēknəs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 薄弱点，缺点；不足之处；疾病；弱点（对某事的爱好或倾向）。例如: "He admitted his main weakness was public speaking." 或 "She has a weakness for chocolate."
- **形容词**: 虚弱的，无力的。例如: "The old man felt a sudden weakness in his legs."

## 短语
- "Weakness for something" 表示对某事物特别喜爱或容易沉迷。
- "Physical weakness" 指身体虚弱。
- "Moral weakness" 指道德上的软弱。

## 例句
- 名词: "His biggest weakness in tennis is his backhand."
- 形容词: "She felt a weakness in her arms after lifting heavy boxes all day."
