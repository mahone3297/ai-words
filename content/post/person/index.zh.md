## 读音
/pərˈsən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个人，人类个体。例如: "Every person in the room had a unique story."
- **名词**: 法律上或统计上的个体。例如: "The policy covers each person individually."
- **代词**: 用于指代某人，尤其是当性别不明或不重要时。例如: "Please have a seat, person."

## 短语
- "In person" 表示亲自到场，不是通过电话或网络。例如: "I prefer to discuss this matter in person."
- "A person of interest" 在法律调查中指可能与案件有关的人。例如: "The police are looking for a person of interest."

## 例句
- 名词: "The person at the counter was very helpful."
- 代词: "There's a person outside who wants to speak with you."

- Someone
