## Pronunciation
/pərˈsən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. An individual human being, characterized by reason, consciousness, and the ability to perceive or act. 2. A member of a group or community. 3. (pl.) People collectively. 4. A character or role in a story, play, or film. 5. A user of a computer or software application.

## Phrases
- "A person of interest" refers to someone who is under investigation.
- "The person in charge" is the one responsible for overseeing a situation.
- "Personality traits" describe the characteristics that make up an individual's behavior.
