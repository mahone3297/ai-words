## 读音
/treɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 路径，小径；如："The hikers followed a narrow trail through the forest."
   2. 痕迹，踪迹；如："The detective found a trail of blood leading to the suspect's hideout."
   3. 尾随，追踪；如："The spy was trailing the ambassador closely."
   4. （比赛、活动等的）路线，赛程；如："The marathon trail covered 26 miles."

- **动词**: 跟踪，尾随；如："The dog was trained to trail missing persons."
   - 追随，模仿；如："Her style has been trailed by many young singers."

## 短语
- "on someone's trail" 意为在追踪某人；如："The police are on the thief's trail."
- "trail off" 指逐渐减弱或结束；如："His story trailed off into silence."

## 例句
- 名词: "They discovered an old trail that led to a hidden waterfall."
- 动词: "The tracker managed to trail the deer for several hours."
- "The speaker's voice trailed off as he realized he had lost the audience's attention."
