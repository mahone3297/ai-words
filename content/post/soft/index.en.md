## Definitions
### Noun
- **Soft** (adj): Refers to something that is physically gentle, not hard, or lacking stiffness. For example: "The pillow was soft and comfortable."
- **Softness** (noun): The quality or state of being soft; gentleness. For example: "She was touched by the softness of his voice."

### Verb
- **To soften** (v): To make something less firm, rigid, or harsh; to become less severe or intense. For example: "The light softened the harsh shadows in the room."
- **To soften a surface** (v): To apply a substance that makes a material more pliable or malleable. For example: "She softened the leather with a special cream."

### Adjective
- **Soft materials** (noun phrase): Referring to substances that are easily deformed or yield to pressure. For example: "Cotton is a soft fabric."

### Adverb
- **Softly** (adv): In a gentle or tender manner. For example: "He spoke softly to comfort the crying child."
