## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A tunnel** is a long, underground passageway, typically dug through earth or rock, designed for the movement of people, vehicles, or utilities.
- In architecture or engineering, it can also refer to a conduit or passage for pipes or cables.
- In figurative usage, it may denote a channel or conduit for something like ideas or information.

### Verb
- To **tunnel** means to excavate a passage, usually underground, usually for transportation or communication purposes.
- It can also refer to the act of creating a path or passage through a medium, such as data tunnels in networking.

## Phrases
- "Tunnel through the mountain" describes the process of digging a passage through solid rock.
- "Traffic tunnels" are infrastructures found in urban areas.
- "Data tunneling" is a method used to securely transmit data over the internet.
