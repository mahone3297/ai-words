## 读音
/tʌnəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种地下或山体中的长廊或通道，通常供行人、车辆或铁路通过。例如: "The tunnel connects the two cities beneath the mountain."
- **动词**: 开凿或建造隧道。例如: "They are tunneling through the rock to build a new subway line."

## 短语
- "Underground tunnel" 指位于地下的隧道。
- "Tunnel vision" 指过于专注某一事物而忽视其他方面的情况。

## 例句
- 名词: "The traffic was slow in the tunnel due to construction work."
- 动词: "The miners have been tunneling for weeks to reach the ore deposit."
