## Pronunciation
/rɪˈkɑːmənd/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A recommendation is a suggestion or advice given by one person to another, typically about a product, service, or course of action. Example: "The restaurant received high recommendations from friends."
- A commendation or praise for an achievement or behavior. Example: "He was awarded a medal for his outstanding recommendations."

### Verb
- To suggest or advise someone to do something, especially because of positive qualities or experiences. Example: "I recommend trying their signature dish."
- To express confidence in someone or something, often with the expectation that they will be successful. Example: "I recommend him for the promotion."

## Phrases
- "Recommend something" suggests a particular choice or option. Example: "She recommended a great book for me to read."
- "I wholeheartedly recommend" expresses strong endorsement. Example: "I wholeheartedly recommend this hotel for a family vacation."
- "Personal recommendation" refers to an individual's own endorsement. Example: "Her personal recommendation was crucial in my decision-making process."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The doctor's recommendation for a healthy diet was crucial to her recovery."
- **Verb**: "I recommend reading this article to learn more about the topic."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "His boss highly recommends him for the job."
