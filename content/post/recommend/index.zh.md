## 读音
/ˌrekəˈmend/

## 释义
- **动词**: 建议；推荐。例如: "I highly recommend this book to all readers."
- **名词**: 推荐；建议。例如: "She gave me a strong recommendation for the job."

## 短语
- "Recommend someone for a job" 意为推荐某人担任某个职位。
- "Recommend something to someone" 表示向某人推荐某物。

## 例句
- 动词: "My friend recommended the Italian restaurant down the street."
- 名词: "The teacher's recommendation carried a lot of weight with the school board."

## 同义词
- Suggest
- Advise
- Propose
- Endorse
- Advocate
