## Pronunciation
/ɪˈvæljˌuˌeɪt/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To assess, judge, or determine the significance, worth, or quality of something by considering all its aspects and characteristics carefully. It can also refer to computing when a program or system checks variables or conditions to produce a result. 

  - Example: "The teacher evaluated each student's progress based on their assignments and tests."
  - Example (Computing): "The software evaluates the user input before executing the command."

## Phrases
- "Evaluate one's performance": Assess how well someone has done in a specific task or job.
- "To evaluate a proposal": To carefully consider and judge the merits and feasibility of a plan or suggestion.
- "Evaluate risks": To judge the likelihood and potential impact of negative events or outcomes.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: 
  - "The hiring manager will evaluate candidates' skills during the interview process."
