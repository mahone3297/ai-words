## 读音
/ˈweðər/

## 释义
- **连词**: 引导从句，表示选择或疑问，意为“是否”或“无论”。例如: "I don't know whether to go or stay."，"It doesn't matter whether you come or not."
- **名词**: 气候或天气状况。例如: "The weather was warm and sunny."

## 短语
- "Whether or not" 用于表示不论是否的意思。例如: "Whether or not you agree, we'll still proceed with the plan."
- "The question of whether" 引导一个关于是否的问题。例如: "The question of whether we should invest in this project is still being debated."

## 例句
- 连词: "She was unsure whether to accept the job offer."
- 名词: "We had to cancel the picnic because of the bad weather."

## 同义词
- If
- Whether or not
- No matter whether
