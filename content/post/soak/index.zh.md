## 读音
/soʊk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 浸泡；使湿透。例如: "I need to soak the shirt in cold water to remove the stain."
  - 轻轻地吸收或渗透。例如: "The bread soaks up the sauce nicely."
  - （时间久）沉浸，沉溺。例如: "He spent the whole afternoon soaking in the bathtub."

## 短语
- "Soak up" 意为吸收（液体、知识等）。例如: "The towel quickly soaked up the spill."
- "Soak in" 指在某种环境或情感中沉浸。例如: "Take some time to soak in the beautiful scenery."

## 例句
- "She's soaking the beans overnight before cooking them."
- "The puppy was happily soaking up the sun on the porch."
- "After a long day, he just wanted to soak in a hot bath."
