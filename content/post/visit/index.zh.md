## 读音
/ˈvɪzɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 拜访，访问。例如: "She paid him a surprise visit yesterday."
- **动词**: 去看（某人），参观（某地）。例如: "We're visiting my grandparents this weekend."

## 短语
- "pay a visit to someone" 意思是去拜访某人。
- "make a visit" 表示进行一次访问或参观。

## 例句
- 名词: "The royal couple is on an official visit to our country."
- 动词: "I'll visit you after work today."
- 感叹词（非正式用法）: "Hey there, nice to visit with you!"
