## 读音
/bathe/
## 释义
- **动词**: 洗澡，沐浴。例如: "I need to bathe before going to bed."
- **动词**: 浸泡，使受水的洗礼。例如: "The baby was bathed in a small tub."
- **名词**: 洗澡的动作或过程。例如: "She enjoyed a relaxing bath after a long day."

## 短语
- "bathe in sunlight" 意为沐浴阳光。
- "bathe someone/something in something" 指将某人或某物浸在液体中，如 "bathe the wound in antiseptic solution"（用消毒液清洗伤口）。

## 例句
- 动词: "The children love to bathe in the ocean during summer vacations."
- 名词: "A warm bath can help you unwind after a stressful day."
