## 读音
/prɪˈsʌst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 压力；重压；负担。例如: "The high workload put too much pressure on the employees."
- **动词**: 对...施加压力；逼迫。例如: "They pressured him into signing the contract."
- **形容词**: 压力的；紧张的。例如: "She felt a lot of pressure before the exam."

## 短语
- "Under pressure" 意为在压力下。
- "Pressure cooker" 指高压锅，也可比喻为充满压力的环境。
- "Blood pressure" 血压，医学术语。

## 例句
- 名词: "The pilot had to deal with extreme pressure at high altitudes."
- 动词: "Her parents pressured her to choose a more stable career path."
- 形容词: "The athlete performed well despite the pressure of the competition."
