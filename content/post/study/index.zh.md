## 读音
/stuːdi/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 学习；研究：例如 "He is doing a study on ancient Chinese history."
  - 书房；学习室：例如 "She spends most of her time in her cozy study."

- **动词**:
  - 学习；研读：例如 "She studies French every morning."
  - 研究；调查：例如 "The scientists are studying the effects of climate change."

## 短语
- "Study hard" 意味着努力学习。
- "To study for an exam" 是为了考试而复习。
- "A case study" 是对特定情况或事件的详细分析。

## 例句
- 名词: "He has set up a small study in the corner of his bedroom."
- 动词: "She will study abroad next semester."
- 介词短语: "He spends hours studying in the library."
