## 读音
/rɪˈækt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 响应，反应：对某事做出回应或改变。例如: "The body reacts to allergens by producing histamine."
2. 发生化学反应：两个或多个物质相互作用产生新物质的过程。例如: "Acids and bases react to form salt and water."

## 短语
- "React to something" 意味着对某事作出回应或反应。例如: "She reacted positively to the news."
- "React violently" 表示激烈或过度的反应。例如: "The crowd reacted violently when their team lost."

## 例句
- 响应： "When he heard the news, he reacted with anger."
- 化学反应： "Iron and oxygen react to create rust."
