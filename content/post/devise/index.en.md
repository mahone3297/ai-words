## Pronunciation
/dɪˈvaɪz/

## Definitions
### Noun
A device or contraption designed for a specific purpose or function. Can also refer to a plan or method devised to achieve a particular goal.

### Verb
1. To create, invent, or plan something with care and ingenuity.
2. To determine or figure out a solution or strategy for a problem.

## Phrases
- "Devise a solution"
- "Come up with a new devise"
- "To devise a plan"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The engineer devised a sleek and efficient new design for the car engine."
- **Verb**: "After several failed attempts, they devised a clever method to fix the leak."
- **Verb**: "She devised a system to organize her workspace more efficiently."
