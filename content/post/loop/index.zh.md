## 读音
/luːp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. (形状像环的东西) 环, 圈: "She wore a silver necklace with a small loop."  
2. (重复的过程或部分) 循环, 圈套: "The program has an infinite loop."
3. (电路中的回路) 回路, 环线: "Close the electrical loop to turn on the light."

- **动词**: 使成环状; 缠绕: "Loop the rope around the tree."
- **形容词**: 形成环状的; 环形的: "a loop of wire"

## 短语
- "jump through loops" 意为经历许多麻烦或困难。
- "in a loop" 表示陷入反复无常或无法摆脱的状态。
- "email loop" 指电子邮件循环，即邮件被不断自动回复。

## 例句
- 名词: "The rollercoaster made a thrilling loop in mid-air."
- 动词: "She looped the scarf around her neck for warmth."
- 形容词: "The track had several loop turns that made the race exciting."
