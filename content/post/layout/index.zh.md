## 读音
/ˈlaʊət/

## 释义
- **名词**: 布局，设计，安排。例如: "The magazine's layout is both elegant and user-friendly."
- **动词**: 设计，布置，规划。例如: "She was responsible for laying out the new office space."

## 短语
- "Page layout" 指的是页面上的内容和图形的排列设计。
- "Design layout" 可以指任何项目的视觉呈现和结构。
- "Layout plan" 是建筑或工程中的平面设计图。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist spent hours perfecting the book's cover layout."
- 动词: "The architect will layout the house's blueprint before construction begins."
