## 读音
/plɛnti/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 大量的，充足的。例如: "We have plenty of food for the party."
- **名词**: 丰富，充足。例如: "Her garden is full of plenty."

## 短语
- "plenty of" 意为大量的，足够的。例如: "There's plenty of time to finish the project."
- "more than plenty" 表示过多，超量。例如: "The cake was delicious, but one slice was more than plenty."

## 例句
- 形容词: "We don't need to buy more bread; we still have plenty left."
- 名词: "The harvest brought in a good plenty this year."
