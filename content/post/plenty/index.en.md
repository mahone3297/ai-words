## Pronunciation
/plenti/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: In large quantity; abundant; copious. For example: "The garden was filled with plenty of flowers."
- **Noun**: An adequate or sufficient amount or number; abundance. For example: "There was plenty of food for everyone."

## Phrases
- "Plenty of" is used to indicate a large quantity or many examples. For example: "Plenty of people came to the event."
- "In plenty" means in sufficient supply or quantity. For example: "The store had everything in plenty."

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "She had plenty of time to finish her work before the deadline."
- **Noun**: "We brought plenty of water for our hike in the mountains."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "The restaurant offers a wide variety of dishes, so there's plenty to choose from."
