## 读音
/startl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 使惊吓；突然吓到。例如: "The sudden noise startled the horse."
- **名词**: 惊吓；受惊。例如: "The child gave a startle when the door slammed shut."

## 短语
- "to give someone a startle" 意味着让某人吃一惊。
- "startled look" 指的是因惊吓而露出的表情。

## 例句
- 动词: "The loud noise startled the baby, making it cry."
- 名词: "She wore a startle on her face after hearing the unexpected news."
