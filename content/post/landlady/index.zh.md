## 读音
/ˈlændleɪdi/

## 释义
- **名词**: 女房东；出租房屋的女性主人。例如: "The landlady came by to collect the rent."
- **名词 (非正式)**: 女经理；女店主。例如: "The landlady of the inn welcomed us warmly."

## 短语
- "landlord and landlady" 指房东夫妇。
- "pay the landlady" 意味着支付租金给房东女士。

## 例句
- "The landlady was strict about keeping the apartment clean."
- "I had a pleasant conversation with the landlady about the lease renewal."

## 同义词
- female landlord
- proprietress
- innkeeper (if referring to a hotel or pub owner)
