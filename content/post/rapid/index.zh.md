## 读音
/ˈræpɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 快速的，迅速的，疾速的。例如: "The river was flowing with a rapid current."
- **名词**: （尤指水流）急流，湍流。例如: "They navigated through the rapids with skill."

## 短语
- "At a rapid pace" 意为以很快的速度。
- "Rapid fire" 通常用来描述快速连续的射击或发言。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new technology has brought about rapid changes in our daily lives."
- 名词: "The kayakers faced challenging rapids on their adventure down the river."

## 同义词
- Swift
- Quick
- Fast
- Hasty
- Immediate
