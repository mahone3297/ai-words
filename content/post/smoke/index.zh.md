## 读音
/sməʊk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 燃烧产生的可见气体和微粒混合物。例如: "There was a lot of smoke coming from the chimney."
- **动词**: 使（烟草、食物等）产生烟，吸烟。例如: "He enjoys smoking a pipe in the evenings."
- **形容词**: 与烟有关的或含有烟的。例如: "The smoke-filled room made it hard to breathe."

## 短语
- "Smokestack" 指工厂或烟囱排放烟的地方。
- "Smoke alarm" 是一种检测火警并发出警报的装置。
- "Smoking ban" 指禁止在特定区域吸烟的法规。

## 例句
- 名词: "The fire produced thick black smoke that could be seen for miles."
- 动词: "She doesn't allow smoking inside her house."
- 形容词: "The bar has a no-smoke policy, so it's fresh and clean inside."
