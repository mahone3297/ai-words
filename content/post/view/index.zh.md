## 读音
/ˈvjuː/

## 释义
- **名词**: 观点，看法，意见。例如: "He expressed his view on the matter."
- **名词**: 景色，视野。例如: "The house has a beautiful view of the mountains."
- **动词**: 查看，观察。例如: "Let's view the video again to understand what happened."
- **名词**: （电脑或网络上的）页面，视图。例如: "The new design improved the user view of the website."

## 短语
- "Change one's view" 意思是改变观点或看法。
- "Take a closer view" 意味着更仔细地查看或考虑。

## 例句
- 名词: "We had a difference of views on the project."
- 动词: "She viewed the painting from different angles."
- 名词（景色）: "From the balcony, we could enjoy a stunning sunset view."
