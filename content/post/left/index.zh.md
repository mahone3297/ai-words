## 读音
/left/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 左边，左侧。例如: "He stood on the left side of the stage."
  - (政治)左翼，左派。例如: "The left wing of the party proposed new policies."
  - (物体的)左侧部分。例如: "The handle is on the left side of the tool."

- **形容词**:
  - 在左边的，位于左侧的。例如: "The left window needs to be cleaned."
  - 左手的，适用于左手的。例如: "He's left-handed and uses a left-handed baseball glove."
  
- **动词**:
  - 留下，遗弃。例如: "She left her keys on the table."
  - 剩余，剩下。例如: "There's only a little food left in the fridge."

## 短语
- "turn left" 向左转。
- "on the left" 在左边。
- "left behind" 被留下，被遗忘。

## 例句
- 名词: "The child was standing on his mother's left."
- 形容词: "She wore a ring on her left hand."
- 动词: "When he left, he forgot his phone."

## 同义词
- 左侧的: 
  - opposite (反面的)
  - to the west (对于北半球的人而言)

- 遗留的:
  - remaining (剩下的)
  - deserted (被遗弃的)
