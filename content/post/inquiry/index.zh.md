## 读音
/inˈkweri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 调查，询问，查询。例如: "The company is conducting an inquiry into the accident."
- **动词**: 进行调查，询问。例如: "The police are inquiring into the missing person's whereabouts."

## 短语
- "Make an inquiry" 意为进行询问或查询。
- "Inquiry-based learning" 是一种基于问题和探索的学习方法。

## 例句
- 名词: "The customer service department is handling numerous inquiries about the product."
- 动词: "The journalist decided to inquire further into the politician's past records."
