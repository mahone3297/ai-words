## Word: Inquiry

## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈkwaɪri/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A seeking for information or knowledge through asking questions; an act of questioning or investigation. For example: "The police conducted a thorough inquiry into the matter."
  - Also, a formal investigation or examination, often by a court or official body. For instance: "A public inquiry was set up to look into the causes of the disaster."
- **Verb**: (inquire) To ask questions in order to gain information or knowledge. Example: "She inquired about the availability of tickets."

## Phrases
- "Make an inquiry" means to ask a question or request information formally.
- "Inquiry process" refers to the steps taken to investigate or research something thoroughly.
- "Customer inquiry" is a question or request for information from a customer, usually related to products or services.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "We received several inquiries regarding the new product launch."
- **Verb**: "The journalist inquired deeply into the company's financial practices."
