## 读音
/wɪl/

## 释义
- **助动词**: 用于构成将来时态，表示将要发生或打算进行的动作。例如: "I will go to the gym tomorrow."
- **名词**: 意为意志、决心或意愿。例如: "Her strong will helped her overcome the challenge."
- **动词**: 用作动词时，表示遗嘱或决定。例如: "He willed his estate to his children."

## 短语
- "Will power" 表示意志力或自制力。
- "In one's will" 指在某人的遗嘱中。

## 例句
- 助动词: "If it rains, we will stay inside."
- 名词: "His strong will enabled him to finish the marathon."
- 动词: "She willed her books to the local library after her death."

## 同义词
- 将来时助动词:
  - Shall (主要用于第一人称)
  - Going to
- 意志/决心:
  - Determination
  - Resolution
  - Purpose
