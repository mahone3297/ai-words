## Pronunciation
/sevərli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Describing the degree or intensity of an action, often indicating severity or harshness. For example: "She spoke severely to her child."
- **Adjective**: Marking a significant or extreme degree of something, often negatively. For example: "The punishment was severely harsh."

## Phrases
- "Severely ill" means extremely unwell.
- "Severe weather conditions" indicates extreme climatic conditions.
- "Severely cut" describes a deep or significant wound.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "The teacher severely criticized the students' lack of effort."
- **Adjective**: "The new policy will have severely affected the company's profits."
- "He was severely injured in the car accident."

## Synonyms
- Severely
- Heavily
- Strikingly
- Rigorously
- Severely (as an adverb, same as the adjective)

## Antonyms
- Mildly
- Lightly
- Gently
- Slightly
- Not at all (when referring to severity)
