## 读音
/sevərli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 极度，严重地，严厉地。表示程度深或影响大。例如: "The patient is recovering severely after the operation."
- **形容词**: 严重的，严厉的。多用于形容病情、批评或天气等。例如: "The storm was severely damaging to the coastal area."

## 短语
- "severely injured" 表示受重伤。
- "severely criticized" 指受到严厉的批评。

## 例句
- 副词: "He was severely punished for his actions."
- 形容词: "The storm caused severely flooded streets."
