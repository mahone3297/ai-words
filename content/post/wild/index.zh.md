## 读音
/waɪld/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 未驯化的，野生的。例如: "The wild animals in the forest live without human intervention."
- **形容词**: 狂野的，不受控制的。例如: "Her wild behavior surprised everyone at the party."
- **副词**: 原始地，自然状态地。例如: "This flower grows wild in the meadows."
- **名词**: 野生动物。例如: "The lion is a majestic creature of the wild."

## 短语
- "Run wild" 意为放纵，不受约束。例如: "The children were allowed to run wild during the summer break."
- "In the wild" 指在自然环境中。例如: "Pandas are rarely seen in the wild."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The wild horses roamed freely across the草原."
- 形容词: "His hair was untamed and looked wildly untidy."
- 副词: "The flowers grow wild along the roadside."
- 名词: "The zookeepers took good care of the injured wild bird."
