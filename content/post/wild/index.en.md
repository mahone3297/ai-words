### Noun
- **1.** adjective: Characterizing or belonging to nature; not cultivated or domesticated. Example: "The wildflowers grew along the riverbank."
- **2.** noun: A person who behaves unpredictably or has an untamed personality. Example: "He's a wild child, always getting into mischief."
- **3.** noun: (plural) An area of land not inhabited by people, often having a natural state. Example: "We went on a hike through the remote wilderness."

- **2.** (informal) To wander or roam freely in the wilderness. Example: "The hikers decided to go wild for a day in the mountains."

### Adjective
- **1.** Describing something that is not tamed, controlled, or predictable. Example: "The wildcat darted through the forest."
- **2.** Expressing strong emotions or intensity. Example: "Her laughter was a wild cackle."
