## 读音
/raid/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 军事或警察的突然袭击，通常为了搜捕、破坏或夺取控制权。例如: "The police conducted a raid on the drug lab."
  - 在电子游戏或团队活动中，大规模的协同攻击或探索。例如: "Our guild is planning a raid on the dungeon tonight."

- **动词**:
  - 进行袭击或突袭。例如: "They raided the enemy's headquarters."
  - 在在线游戏中，组织或参与一次攻击。例如: "We're raiding the boss in World of Warcraft."

## 短语
- "Raid the fridge" 意味着随便吃冰箱里的东西。
- "Police raid" 指的是警方的突袭行动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The SWAT team executed a carefully planned raid on the匪徒's hideout."
- 动词: "The hackers managed to raid the company's database and steal sensitive information."
