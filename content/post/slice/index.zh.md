## 读音
/slaɪs/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一片，薄片，通常指切下的部分。例如: "She cut the cake into slices."
- **动词 (v)**: 切割成片；切开。例如: "He sliced the bread thinly."

## 短语
- "slice of bread" 一片面包。
- "slice off" 切下，割掉。例如: "Be careful not to slice your fingers when cutting the vegetables."
- "slice through" 切穿，快速解决。例如: "The knife sliced through the rope effortlessly."

## 例句
- 名词: "I put two slices of ham on my sandwich."
- 动词: "He expertly sliced the tomatoes for the salad."
