## Pronunciation
/slaɪs/

## Definitions
### Noun
A slice is a thin, usually horizontal, portion or part of something that has been cut, especially from a round or loaf-shaped object, like bread, cake, or a pizza.

### Verb
To slice refers to the action of cutting something, typically with a sharp blade, into thin, even pieces.

### Adjective
Sliced can describe something that has been cut into thin slices, as in "sliced cheese" or "sliced tomatoes."

## Phrases
- "Take a slice of cake"
- "Cut the bread into thin slices"
- "Prepared sliced vegetables"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I grabbed a slice of pizza from the oven."
- **Verb**: "She carefully sliced the ripe melon into portions."
- **Adjective**: "The store sells pre-packaged sliced ham."
