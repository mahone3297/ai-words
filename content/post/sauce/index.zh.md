## 读音
/ˈsɔːs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 浓汁，调味汁，酱料。通常用于增加食物的味道。例如: "She poured a delicious sauce over her pasta."
- **动词**: 制作或提供调味汁。例如: "The chef sauced the chicken with a tangy barbecue sauce."

## 短语
- "Sauce for the goose is sauce for the gander" 意味着对所有人应用同一标准。
- "Red sauce" 常指番茄酱，常用于披萨或意大利面。
- "White sauce" 指的是奶油或基于乳制品的调味汁。

## 例句
- 名词: "The steak was excellent, but it was the pepper sauce that really made the meal."
- 动词: "Can you sauce the vegetables with a little bit of soy sauce?"
