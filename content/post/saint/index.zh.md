## 读音
/seɪnt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 圣人；圣徒：基督教中的圣人，被视为模范人物或有特殊灵性的人。例如: "St. Francis of Assisi was known for his love of nature."
  - （天主教）殉道者：在信仰上受迫害并最终牺牲的人。例如: "Many early Christians were considered saints for their martyrdom."

- **形容词**:
  - 圣洁的；神圣的：具有纯洁或崇高的品质。例如: "The saintly figure inspired awe and reverence in all who met her."

## 短语
- "Saint's Day" 指基督教中纪念特定圣人的节日。
- "Live like a saint" 意为生活得非常纯洁和高尚。

## 例句
- 名词: "The cathedral is dedicated to St. Peter, one of the twelve apostles."
- 形容词: "She led a saintly life, always putting others before herself."

## 同义词
- Holy person
- Mystic
- Spiritual leader
- Venerated figure
- Martyr (仅限名词时的同义词)
