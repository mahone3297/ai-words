## 读音
/ˈveri/

## 释义
- **副词**: 非常，很，十分。用来加强前面形容词或副词的程度。例如: "This cake is very delicious."
- **形容词**: 真正的，十足的。例如: "He's a very talented musician."

## 短语
- "very much" 表示程度很高，例如: "I appreciate your help very much."
- "very well" 在同意或确认时使用，例如: "Very well, let's begin."

## 例句
- 副词: "She was very happy to see her old friend again."
- 形容词: "This is a very unusual situation."

## 同义词
- extremely
- highly
- totally
- utterly
- quite
