## Pronunciation
/wɪəri/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Tired due to physical or mental exhaustion; worn out. For example: "After a long day, she felt very weary."
- **Verb**: To become tired or exhausted; to feel weary. For example: "The children were weary after their hike through the mountains."
- **Noun**: A feeling of weariness or fatigue. For example: "She collapsed onto the couch, overcome by her weariness."

## Phrases
- "Feel weary" describes the state of being tired.
- "Weary traveler" refers to someone who has been on a long journey and is exhausted.
- "Weariness sets in" signifies the onset of tiredness.
