## 读音
/ˈpraɪvət/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 私人的，私有的，不公开的。例如: "He has a private meeting with the CEO."
- **名词**: 私人，个人。例如: "She works as a private tutor."
- **名词**: （军事）私立的，下士。例如: "He was a private in the army."
- **动词**: 私下拥有，私人拥有。例如: "The company is privately owned."

## 短语
- "private property" 意为私有财产。
- "private conversation" 指私下谈话。
- "private education" 指私立教育。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The garden is private and not accessible to the public."
- 名词: "The new recruit is a private fresh out of boot camp."
- 动词: "She prefers to keep her medical information private."
