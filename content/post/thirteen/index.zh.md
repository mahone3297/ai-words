## 读音
/θɜːˈtiːn/

## 释义
- **数词**: 表示数字13。例如: "[There](/post/there/) are [thirteen](/post/thirteen/) students [in](/post/in/) [this](/post/this/) [class](/post/class/)."

## 短语
- "[Thirteen](/post/thirteen/) years [old](/post/old/)" 表示13岁的年龄。
- "[The](/post/the/) thirteenth [floor](/post/floor/)" 在一些文化中可能被视为不吉利的楼层。

## 例句
- "[Today](/post/today/) is [my](/post/my/) thirteenth [birthday](/post/birthday/)."
- "[She](/post/she/) was [the](/post/the/) thirteenth [person](/post/person/) [to](/post/to/) [arrive](/post/arrive/) [at](/post/at/) [the](/post/the/) [meeting](/post/meeting/)."

## 同义词
