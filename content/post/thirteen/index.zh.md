## 读音
/θɜːˈtiːn/

## 释义
- **数词**: 表示数字13。例如: "[There](/zh/post/there/) are [thirteen](/zh/post/thirteen/) students [in](/zh/post/in/) [this](/zh/post/this/) [class](/zh/post/class/)."

## 短语
- "[Thirteen](/zh/post/thirteen/) years [old](/zh/post/old/)" 表示13岁的年龄。
- "[The](/zh/post/the/) thirteenth [floor](/zh/post/floor/)" 在一些文化中可能被视为不吉利的楼层。

## 例句
- "[Today](/zh/post/today/) is [my](/zh/post/my/) thirteenth [birthday](/zh/post/birthday/)."
- "[She](/zh/post/she/) was [the](/zh/post/the/) thirteenth [person](/zh/post/person/) [to](/zh/post/to/) [arrive](/zh/post/arrive/) [at](/zh/post/at/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [meeting](/zh/post/meeting/)."
