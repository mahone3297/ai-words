## Pronunciation
/ˈɜːrdʒənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterizing something that requires immediate attention or action due to its importance or urgency. For example: "The urgent matter needed to be addressed immediately."
- **Noun**: An urgent matter or situation that demands prompt attention. For example: "There was an urgent request for medical assistance."

## Phrases
- "Deal with the urgent matter" means to attend to a pressing issue.
- "Urgent notification" refers to an important message requiring immediate attention.
- "Act on an urgent basis" implies taking swift action due to the severity of the situation.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "She had to make an urgent call to her boss regarding the project deadline."
- **Noun**: "The manager gathered his team to address the urgent crisis at the company."
- **Prepositional phrase**: "In an urgent situation, time is of the essence."
