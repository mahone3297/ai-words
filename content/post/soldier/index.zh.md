## 读音
/ˈsəʊldʒər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 军人，士兵。通常是军队中的初级成员，执行战斗任务。例如: "The soldier was decorated for his bravery in battle."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与军事或士兵有关的。例如: "soldierly discipline"（军人般的纪律）

## 短语
- "soldier on" 继续努力，坚持不懈。例如: "Despite the difficulties, she decided to soldier on with her project."
- "at soldier's pay" 指收入微薄。例如: "He's working at soldier's pay, but he loves his job."

## 例句
- 名词: "The young soldier had just returned from a tour of duty in Afghanistan."
- 形容词: "He wore a soldier's uniform, proud of his service."
