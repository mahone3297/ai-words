## 读音
/rɪdld/

## 释义
- **名词**: 谜语，谜；一个需要解答的难题。例如: "Can you solve this riddle for me?"
- **动词**: 提出谜语；使困惑不解。例如: "The mysterious situation riddled us all."

## 短语
- "Riddle me this" 用于引出一个问题或谜语。例如: "Riddle me this: What has keys but can't open locks?"
- "A riddle wrapped in a mystery inside an enigma" 是一句著名的描述，意为极其复杂难以理解的事物。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children loved to exchange riddles during their playtime."
- 动词: "The movie's plot riddles the audience with its intricate twists and turns."
