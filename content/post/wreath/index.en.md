## Definitions
- **Noun**: A decorative circular arrangement of flowers, leaves, or other materials, often worn as a garland or symbolizing honor or victory. For example: "The bride wore a stunning wreath of lilies on her wedding day."
- **Verb**: To decorate with a wreath, especially as a sign of respect or celebration. For example: "We wreathed the memorial with flowers to honor the fallen soldiers."
- **Noun (figurative)**: A token or symbol of something, often representing an achievement or a state of being. For example: "Her award was a crown of laurel wreaths, signifying her literary success."

## Phrases
- "Wreath of honor" signifies a tribute or recognition.
- "Evergreen wreath" refers to one made from durable plants like pine, symbolizing immortality.
- "Wreath of remembrance" is used to commemorate the dead.
