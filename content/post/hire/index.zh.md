## 读音
/haɪr/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 雇佣，租用。例如: "The company hired a new marketing manager last week."
    - 租车，租船等。例如: "We hired a car for our trip to the countryside."

## 短语
- "to hire someone out" 意味着将某人出租或雇佣给其他人。例如: "They hired their gardener out to a neighbor during the weekends."
- "to hire something for a period" 指租借某物一段时间。例如: "I hired a bike for the day."

## 例句
- "The restaurant is hiring waiters for the busy season."
- "We hired a boat to explore the lake."
