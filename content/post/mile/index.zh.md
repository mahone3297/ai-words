## 读音
/maɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 英里，长度单位，1英里约等于1.60934公里。例如: "The car can travel 300 miles on a full tank."
- **名词**: 距离或路程的抽象表达。例如: "We still have a long mile to go before we reach the destination."

## 短语
- "A mile in someone's shoes" 意为设身处地为他人着想。
- "Miles apart" 表示相隔很远的距离。

## 例句
- 名词: "The marathon covers 26.2 miles."
- 名词: "Despite living in the same city, they felt like they were miles apart emotionally."
