## Definitions
### Noun
A **compass** is a navigational instrument consisting of a magnetized pointer that aligns with Earth's magnetic field, indicating directions relative to the geographic north and south poles. It typically has a graduated dial marked with points of the compass (N, S, E, W, NE, NW, SE, SW) and may also include a sighting mirror or electronic features.

### Verb
To **compass** can mean to survey or encompass an area, as in "The expedition compassed the entire island." It can also refer to having a wide range of knowledge or skills, as in "Her interests compassed art, science, and literature."

### Adjective
In some contexts, "compassionate" describes someone who shows kindness and understanding towards others, as in "A compassionate teacher."
