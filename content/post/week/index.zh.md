## 读音
/wiːk/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 一周的时间，通常包括七天。例如: "There are usually five working days in a week."
  - 时期，阶段，尤指按周计算的。例如: "I'll see you next week."

## 短语
- "Weekdays" 指周一到周五的工作日。
- "Weekend" 是周六和周日，或者一周的末尾。
- "In a week's time" 表示一周后。

## 例句
- 名词: "I have a meeting every Monday morning at the start of the week."
- 名词: "Let's plan to meet again sometime next week."

## 同义词
- Seven days
- A period of seven days
- Fortnight (两星期)
- Monthly cycle的一部分 (如: "a quarter of a month")
