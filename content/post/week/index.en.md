## Pronunciation
/wiːk/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
    - A period of seven days, typically reckoned from Monday to Sunday: *We have a meeting every week.*
    - A week-long period during which something regularly happens: *The sale lasts for one week.*
- **Adjective**: 
    - Relating to or constituting a week: *It's the first week of the new semester.*
- **Verb**: 
    - To go through or complete a week: *We're almost done with this week's project.*

## Phrases
- "Weekend" refers to the last two days of the week (Saturday and Sunday).
- "Weekly" describes something that occurs once a week: *A weekly newsletter.*
- "Weeklong event" is an activity lasting for an entire week.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I'll see you next week, when we start our new project."
- **Adjective**: "This is the final week of the school year."
- **Verb**: "She spends her weekends working on her weekly report."

## Synonyms
- Seven-day period
- Lunar week (in some religious or cultural contexts)
- Weekly cycle
- Duration of a week
- One-week stretch

## Antonyms
- Daily
- Monthly
- Yearly (for a longer time frame)
