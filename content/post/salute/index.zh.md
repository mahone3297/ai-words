## 读音
/ˈsæljuːt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 军礼；敬礼；致敬行为。例如: "The soldiers saluted their commanding officer."
- **动词**: 执行敬礼；向…致敬。例如: "He saluted the flag as it was raised."
- **名词**: 开场白；欢迎词。在某些场合，如表演或演讲时的开场。例如: "The singer's salute to the audience set the tone for the evening."

## 短语
- "Salute the flag" 表示对国旗敬礼。
- "Opening salute" 指活动或演出的开场致意。

## 例句
- 名词: "At the ceremony, all attendees were instructed to salute the national anthem."
- 动词: "The new recruit saluted his superior officer smartly."
- 开场白: "The orchestra's conductor gave a short salute before starting the concert."
