## Pronunciation
/səˈluːt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A salute is a gesture, action, or expression of respect, often involving raising one's hand in a specific manner (such as a military salute), as a form of greeting, or acknowledging someone's presence or achievement.

### Verb
To salute is to perform a gesture of respect, often by raising one's hand, as a sign of honor, greeting, or recognition. This can apply to military personnel, flags, or individuals.

### Adjective
Salutary refers to something that is beneficial or conducive to health.

## Phrases
- "Return the salute" means to respond with the same gesture or show of respect.
- "Give a salute to someone" denotes acknowledging their presence or achievement.
- "Formal salute" signifies a more ceremonial or official manner of greeting.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The soldiers stood at attention, saluting their commander."
- **Verb**: "When he finished his presentation, the audience gave him a thunderous salute."
- **Adjective**: "A salutary lifestyle includes regular exercise and a balanced diet."
