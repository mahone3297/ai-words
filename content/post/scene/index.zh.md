## 读音
/siːn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 场景，景象：具体的情境或视觉画面，如戏剧、电影或现实生活中的一个片段。例如: "The opening scene of the play was set in a busy market."
2. 现场，地点：事件发生的实际位置。例如: "The crime scene was carefully examined by the detectives."
3. (舞台)幕：戏剧、电影或电视节目中的一段连续表演。例如: "In the next scene, the characters are discussing their plans."

## 短语
- "behind the scenes" 指幕后的工作或不为人知的情况。
- "set the scene" 意为设定背景或情境。

## 例句
- 名词: "The romantic scene on the beach was beautifully lit by the moonlight."
- 名词: "The police arrived promptly at the accident scene."
