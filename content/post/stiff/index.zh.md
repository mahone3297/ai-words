## 读音
/stɪf/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 坚硬的，不灵活的。例如: "The chair was so stiff that it was uncomfortable to sit on."
- **形容词**: 严肃的，正式的。例如: "The dinner party had a rather stiff atmosphere."
- **副词**: 僵硬地，不自然地。例如: "He stood stiffly, not knowing what to do with his hands."
- **名词**: 不易弯曲或柔软的东西。例如: "Remove the stiff bristles from the brush."

## 短语
- "stiff competition" 指激烈的竞争。
- "stiff upper lip" 形容在困难面前保持冷静和坚韧的态度。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new suit felt stiff and uncomfortable at first."
- 副词: "He smiled stiffly, trying to hide his nervousness."
- 名词: "The paint had dried into a stiff layer."

- rigid
- inflexible
- formal
