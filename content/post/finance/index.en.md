## Pronunciation
/ˈfaɪˌnæns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The management of large amounts of money, especially by governments, businesses, or organizations. It includes activities such as investing, borrowing, lending, budgeting, saving, and forecasting future income and expenses. Example: "The company's finance department manages all financial transactions."

  - (as a plural noun) Financial resources or arrangements; monies: "They are still seeking the necessary finances for the new project."

- **Verb**: To provide funds for or to manage the monetary aspects of something. Example: "The government will finance the construction of the new highway."

## Phrases
- "Financial planning" refers to the process of managing your money to achieve personal financial goals.
- "To take out a loan for finance" means to borrow money to fund a purchase or project.
- "Corporate finance" deals with the financial activities related to running a corporation, usually involving capital investment decisions and funding sources.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She has a degree in business finance and hopes to work in a bank."
- **Verb**: "The start-up was successfully financed by venture capitalists."
