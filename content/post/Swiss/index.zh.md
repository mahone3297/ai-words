## 读音
/ˈswɪs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 
   - 与瑞士（Switzerland）相关联的，瑞士人的，瑞士制造的。例如: "She wears a Swiss watch."
   - 精密的，高质量的，通常用于描述工艺或产品。例如: "The Swiss cheese has perfect holes."

## 短语
- "Swiss army knife" 瑞士军刀，多功能刀具。
- "Swiss bank account" 瑞士银行账户，常被认为是保密和安全的。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The Swiss chocolate is renowned for its rich flavor."
- 短语: "He carries a trusty Swiss army knife on all his camping trips."

## 同义词
- Swiss-made
- European
- High-quality
- Precision
- Delicate
