## 读音
/ˈwelθi/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 富有的，富裕的，有大量财富的。例如: "She comes from a wealthy family."
- **名词**: 财富，富裕状态。例如: "He has accumulated a great deal of wealth over the years."

## 短语
- "wealthy individual" 指的是有钱的个人。
- "wealthy elite" 指的是富裕阶层或精英人士。
- "live a wealthy life" 意味着过着富裕的生活。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The wealthy businessman donated a large sum to charity."
- 名词: "Their wealth allowed them to travel the world in luxury."
