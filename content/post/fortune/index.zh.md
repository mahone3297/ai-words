## 读音
/ˈfɔːrtʃuːn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 命运；运气；财富；成功的机会。例如: "She made her fortune in the tech industry."
- **动词**: 偶然发生；预测命运。例如: "Fortune favors the brave."

## 短语
- "At fortune's mercy" 意味着受命运摆布。
- "To make one's fortune" 是指发财或取得成功。
- "A change of fortune" 指的是运气的转变。

## 例句
- 名词: "His good fortune seemed endless, as he won the lottery twice."
- 动词: "The astrologer claimed to be able to fortune people's future."
