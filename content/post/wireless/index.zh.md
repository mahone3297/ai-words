## 读音
/waɪr.ləs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 没有电线或电缆连接的；无线的。例如: "A wireless keyboard allows you to type without any physical connection to your computer."
- **名词**: 无线电通信；无线电广播。例如: "In the old days, people listened to news on the wireless."

## 短语
- "Wireless network" 指的是无线上网或无线网络。
- "Wireless charger" 是一种无需物理连接即可为设备充电的装置。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new smartphone has a long-lasting battery and is completely wireless."
- 名词: "She tuned in to the radio station via the wireless."

## 同义词
- Wireless can be used as an adjective:
  - Cable-free
  - Cordless
  - Unwired
- As a noun (referring to radio communication):
  - Radio
  - Broadcast
