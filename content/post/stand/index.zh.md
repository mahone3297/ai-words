## 读音
/stænd/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 站立，处于直立状态。例如: "Please stand up for the national anthem."
2. 坚持，维持不变。例如: "She stands by her decision despite criticism."
3. 履行，承担（责任）。例如: "He will stand as a witness in the trial."
4. 抵抗，忍受。例如: "The old tree stood strong against the storm."

- **名词**:
1. 站台，柜台。例如: "There's a food stand over there."
2. 立场，观点。例如: "They have different political stands on the issue."
3. 比赛中的位置。例如: "The goalkeepers usually take their stands before the match starts."

## 短语
- "Take a stand" 意为采取立场或做出决定。
- "Stand up for oneself" 表示为自己辩护或维护自己的权益。
- "Stand aside" 让开，让别人过去。

## 例句
- 动词: "He stood confidently in front of the audience."
- 名词: "The hotdog stand is popular among the tourists."
