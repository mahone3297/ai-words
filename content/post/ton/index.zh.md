## 读音
/tɒn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 重量单位，通常指2000磅（约907公斤）；大量，许多。例如: "The truck can carry several tons of cargo."，"She received tons of messages after posting the news online."
- **形容词**: 大量的，很多的。例如: "He has a ton of work to do before the deadline."

## 短语
- "tons of" 表示大量的，许多的。例如: "They have tons of experience in this field."
- "a tonne of" 在某些地区，特别是欧洲，用来表示公吨（1000公斤）。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new bridge is designed to hold up to fifty tons."
- 形容词: "She learned a ton about gardening from her grandmother."

- heaps of
