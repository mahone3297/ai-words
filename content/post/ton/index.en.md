## Definitions
### Noun
- **Mass noun**: A unit of weight or volume equal to 2000 pounds (or about 1000 kilograms) in the United States and the UK.
- **Slang**: A person's body weight, often used affectionately or colloquially.
- **Music**: A series of notes forming a chord or scale, typically with a particular pitch or key.

### Verb
- **Transitive verb**: To lift or move something with a considerable weight, often using mechanical assistance.
- **Intransitive verb**: To be transported or moved in large quantities, as in a shipment of goods.

### Adjective
- Describing a large quantity, especially of something heavy or measured in tons.

### Adverb
- (rarely used) In a large or significant amount.
