## 读音
/ˈprɪvəlɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 特殊权利或利益；优待；特权。例如: "He was given the privilege of meeting the president."
- **形容词**: 特权的；有优惠的。例如: "The members enjoyed privileged access to the club facilities."

## 短语
- "Without privilege" 意为没有特别的优待或权利。
- "Privileged information" 指的是只有特定人才能获得的机密信息。
- "Social privilege" 指在社会中某些群体享有的优势地位。

## 例句
- 名词: "She felt grateful for the educational privilege she had received."
- 形容词: "As a VIP, you'll enjoy privileged treatment during your stay."

## 同义词
- Advantage
- Benefit
- Favor
- Exemption
- Precedence
