## Definitions

1. A small, eight-legged invertebrate typically having a body divided into cephalothorax and abdomen, with spinnerets for producing silk, and used for web-making and catching insects: *The spider sat on its web, patiently waiting for prey.*
2. A person who is skilled or expert in spinning or weaving (especially in a figurative sense): *She's a web-design spider, creating stunning sites effortlessly.*

### Verb
- To spin webs or catch prey using a web, as a spider does: *Spiders often spider around the garden at night.*
- (Informal) To navigate or deal with something skillfully or efficiently: *He's a spider at managing his time and workload.*

### Adjective
- Characterizing or pertaining to spiders or their traits: *Spider-like agility*

### Adverb
- In a spider-like manner: *She moved spiderily across the room.*
