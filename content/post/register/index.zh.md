## 读音
/ˈrɛdʒɪstər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 记录或登录册：一份官方或正式的记录，如注册名单、登记册或数据库。例如: "Please sign the visitor's register."
   - 注册，登记：在系统或服务中进行官方登记以获得认可或使用权限。例如: "You need to register for the conference before the deadline."
   - （电子设备的）寄存器：计算机硬件中的一个存储单元，用于临时保存数据。例如: "The CPU contains several registers for processing data."

- **动词**:
   - 报名，登记：在活动中报名或在某个地方进行官方登记。例如: "She registered for the yoga class online."
   - 记录，记载：把信息或事件记录下来。例如: "The temperature gauge registered a high of 38 degrees Celsius."
   - 注意到，察觉：感知或注意到某事物的存在或变化。例如: "I could register a faint smell of smoke in the air."

## 短语
- "Register for something" 意味着报名参加某项活动或服务。
- "Be registered as" 表示被登记为某种身份或状态。例如: "He is registered as a self-employed worker."
- "Come up on the register" 意味着在记录或检测中出现。例如: "The sound didn't come up on the audio register."

## 例句
- 名词: "All guests must check in and sign the hotel register."
- 动词: "She registered her new business with the local authorities."
- 注意到: "I only just registered the fact that he was wearing a uniform."
