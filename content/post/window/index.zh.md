## 读音
/ˈwɪndəʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 透明的装置，通常由玻璃制成，安装在建筑物的墙上，用于采光和通风。例如: "She sat by the window, enjoying the view outside."
- **动词**: 用窗户展示，特指商店橱窗展示。例如: "The store will window its new collection this weekend."
- **名词 (计算机术语)**: 计算机操作系统中的一个应用程序界面，用户通过它与程序交互。例如: "Open a new window to work on another task."

## 短语
- "Window shopping" 指只看不买的逛街行为。
- "To look out the window" 意为看向窗外。
- "Window of opportunity" 表示短暂的有利时机。

## 例句
- 名词: "The cat was perched on the windowsill, watching the birds."
- 动词: "The boutique windowed its latest fashion designs to attract customers."
- 名词（计算机术语）: "Click the minimize button to shrink the program window."
