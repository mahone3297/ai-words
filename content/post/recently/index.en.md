## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Referring to a time or period not long ago, lately, or recently. It can also be used to modify verbs, indicating that an action occurred recently. For example: "I've been working recently on a new project."

## Phrases
- "Recently, there's been a lot of change."
- "She's been traveling recently."
- "The weather has been unusually warm recently."

## Example Sentences

- "He's been working out recently, which has really improved his fitness."
- "The company has recently launched a new product line."
