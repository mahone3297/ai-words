## 读音
/rɪˈsntli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 近来，最近，不久前。例如: "I've been busy recently and haven't had time to visit my parents."
- **形容词**: 最近的，新近的。例如: "The recently released movie has received great reviews."

## 短语
- "recently arrived" 意为最近到达的。
- "in recent times" 表示在最近的时期。

## 例句
- 副词: "She has recently started working at a new company."
- 形容词: "The recently discovered evidence changed the course of the investigation."
