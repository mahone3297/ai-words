## 读音
/laɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 呈现虚假的情况，说谎。例如: "He lied about his age to get the job."
  - 平躺，处于休息状态。例如: "She was lying on the couch watching TV."

- **名词**:
  - 谎言，不实之词。例如: "The story he told was a complete lie."

## 短语
- "Lie down" 意为躺下。
- "Tell a lie" 意为说谎。

## 例句
- 动词: "She lied when she said she had finished her homework."
- 名词: "His explanation was just a cleverly constructed lie."

## 同义词
- Deceive
- Fabricate
- Misrepresent
- Prevaricate
- Falsehood
