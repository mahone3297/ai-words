## Word: Microcomputer

## Pronunciation
/ˌmaɪkrəʊkəmˈpjuːtər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A small, relatively inexpensive computer designed for personal use, often with a keyboard, display screen, and operating system, capable of running applications and processing data. Initially coined in the 1970s and 1980s for computers that were smaller than mainframes or minicomputers.

### Example Sentences
- _In the early days of home computing, the Apple II was one of the most popular microcomputers._
- _The Raspberry Pi has become a favorite among hobbyists for DIY projects due to its compact size and affordability, embodying the spirit of classic microcomputers._

## Phrases
- _Home microcomputer_ - Refers to a microcomputer intended for use in a domestic setting.
- _Educational microcomputer_ - A type of microcomputer designed specifically for educational purposes in schools and colleges.

## Synonyms
- Personal computer (PC)
- Desktop computer
- Home computer
- Mini computer
- Workstation (in some contexts)

Note: While the terms 'microcomputer' isn't as commonly used today as it once was, having largely been replaced by 'personal computer' or 'PC', the original concept refers to any small, standalone computing device that can perform general-purpose tasks compared to larger systems.
