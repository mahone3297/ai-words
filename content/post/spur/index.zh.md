## 读音
/spɜr/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 鼓励，激励；刺激物。例如: "The prospect of a promotion was a strong spur to his efforts."
  - 马蹄铁上的尖刺，用于刺激马匹前进。例如: "He adjusted the spurs on his riding boots."

- **动词**:
  - 刺激，鼓舞。例如: "The coach's inspiring words spurred the team to victory."
  - 用马刺刺（马）。例如: "The jockey spurred his horse to go faster."

## 短语
- "spur on" 意为鼓励或激励某人去做某事。例如: "Her parents' encouragement spurred her to pursue her dreams."
- "a spur of the moment decision" 指的是临时决定。例如: "We decided to go on a road trip on a spur of the moment."

## 例句
- 名词: "The threat of losing their jobs was a spur to increased productivity."
- 动词: "His coach's sharp words spurred him to train harder than ever before."
