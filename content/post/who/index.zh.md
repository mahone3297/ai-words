## 读音
/huː/

## 释义
- **代词**: 谁，哪一个，何人。用于询问身份或指代未知的人。例如: "[Who](/post/who/) is [at](/post/at/) [the](/post/the/) [door](/post/door/)?"，"[Who](/post/who/) is [the](/post/the/) [best](/post/best/) [player](/post/player/) [on](/post/on/) [the](/post/the/) [team](/post/team/)?"
- **引导词**: 引导宾语从句或主语从句，常在疑问句中使用。例如: "I [wonder](/post/wonder/) [who](/post/who/) [will](/post/will/) [win](/post/win/) [the](/post/the/) [race](/post/race/)."

## 短语
- "[Who](/post/who/) knows?" 表示不确定或疑问，意思是“谁知道呢？”
- "[Who](/post/who/) cares?" 表示不在乎或不感兴趣，意思是“谁在乎呢？”

## 例句
- 代词: "[Who](/post/who/) [left](/post/left/) [their](/post/their/) [coat](/post/coat/) [on](/post/on/) [the](/post/the/) [chair](/post/chair/)?"
- 引导词: "[She](/post/she/) asked [who](/post/who/) was [responsible](/post/responsible/) [for](/post/for/) [the](/post/the/) [accident](/post/accident/)."

## 同义词

- [Which](/post/which/) (当指代物时)

请注意，who主要作为代词和引导词使用，没有名词、动词、形容词、副词、介词、连词、数词或感叹词的词性。
