## Pronunciation
/ˈsuːn/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Expressing or indicating a relatively near time in the future or shortly after something else has happened. For example: "I'll be there soon."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing something that will happen in the near future or with little delay. For example: "The project should be completed soon."

## Phrases
- "In a few moments" refers to a short time later.
- "Soon after" indicates a quick succession of time.
- "Not long to come" signifies that something is imminent.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "I'll arrive at the airport soon; don't worry."
- **Adjective**: "The new restaurant opens soon, so we're excited to try it out."
- "The storm is expected to pass soon."
