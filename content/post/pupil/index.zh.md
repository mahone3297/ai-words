## 读音
/ˈpjuːpl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 学生，尤指小学生。例如: "There are 30 pupils in this class."
  - （某些动物的）瞳孔，指眼睛中央可变大的小孔。例如: "The light reflected in his large brown pupils."

## 短语
- "pupil of the eye" 指眼睛的瞳孔。
- "star pupil" 指特别优秀的学生。
- "head pupil" 或 "monitor" 在某些学校中指学生领袖或班长。

## 例句
- "The new teacher greeted each pupil by name on the first day of school."
- "In dim light, her pupils dilate to allow more light in."
