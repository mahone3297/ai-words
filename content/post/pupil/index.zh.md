## 读音
/ˈpjuːpl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 学生，尤指小学生。例如: "[There](/post/there/) are 30 pupils [in](/post/in/) [this](/post/this/) [class](/post/class/)."
  - （某些动物的）瞳孔，指眼睛中央可变大的小孔。例如: "[The](/post/the/) [light](/post/light/) reflected [in](/post/in/) [his](/post/his/) [large](/post/large/) [brown](/post/brown/) pupils."

## 短语
- "[pupil](/post/pupil/) [of](/post/of/) [the](/post/the/) [eye](/post/eye/)" 指眼睛的瞳孔。
- "[star](/post/star/) [pupil](/post/pupil/)" 指特别优秀的学生。
- "[head](/post/head/) [pupil](/post/pupil/)" 或 "[monitor](/post/monitor/)" 在某些学校中指学生领袖或班长。
