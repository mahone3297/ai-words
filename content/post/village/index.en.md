## Pronunciation
/vɪˈlɪdʒ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A small community or cluster of houses, typically in a rural area, inhabited by a small population. [**Etymology**: Old French villag(e) from Latin villa, from villa rustica country estate]

## Phrases
- "Live in a village" means to reside in a small village.
- "A village green" refers to a common or open space in a village.
- "Village life" describes the simple, close-knit lifestyle found in rural communities.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The village was surrounded by rolling hills and a peaceful river."
- **Verb**: "The children helped their grandparents tend to the village garden every weekend."
- **Adjective**: "The quaint village had a traditional charm that attracted tourists."
