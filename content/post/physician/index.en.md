## Pronunciation
/ˈfɪsɪʃən/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **physician** is a medical professional who practices medicine, typically holds a degree in medicine, and is qualified to diagnose and treat patients. They may specialize in various fields such as internal medicine, surgery, pediatrics, or psychiatry.

### Verb
To **practice as a physician** or to **consult with a physician** means to work or seek advice from a medical doctor.

## Phrases
- "See a physician" implies visiting a doctor for a medical examination or treatment.
- "Seek medical advice from a physician" refers to consulting a doctor for health-related guidance.
- "Physician's orders" are the instructions given by a doctor for a patient's care.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The renowned physician was scheduled for a series of lectures on advanced treatments."
- **Verb**: "After the accident, he rushed to the nearest emergency room, where a team of physicians assessed his condition."
- **Sentence using 'Practice':** "He has been practicing medicine for over 20 years, specializing in cardiology."
