## 读音
/ˈfɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 医生；内科医师。通常指经过专业医学训练并具备行医资格的人。例如: "She is a respected physician in her field."
- **形容词**: 与医学或医生相关的。例如: "The physician's office is on the second floor."

## 短语
- " Consulting a physician" 意为咨询医生。
- "Physician-assisted death" 指在医生协助下的死亡，如安乐死。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need to make an appointment with my physician for a check-up."
- 形容词: "The physician's advice should be followed strictly when dealing with a medical condition."

## 同义词
- Doctor
- Medical doctor
- GP (General Practitioner)
- MD (Medical Doctor)
