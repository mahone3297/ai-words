## Word: Medical

## Pronunciation
/ˈmɛdɪkəl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to medicine or the practice of medicine; concerning the treatment or prevention of disease and the maintenance of health. For example: "Medical research has led to many life-saving discoveries."
  - "She's studying for her medical degree."
- **Noun (collective)**: The field of medicine collectively, including its practitioners and institutions. For example: "The medical community has widely endorsed this new vaccine."

## Phrases
- "Medical advice": Professional guidance on matters related to health or treatment.
- "Medical history": A record of a person's past illnesses, treatments, and operations.
- "Medical checkup": A routine examination by a doctor to assess a person's general state of health.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "A team of medical experts was sent to the region to deal with the outbreak."
- **Noun**: "The patient sought help from various medical sources before finding the right treatment."
