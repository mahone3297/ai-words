## 读音
/lɪp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 口唇，嘴唇。例如: "She applied lipstick to her lips."
  - （乐器等的）吹口，吹边。例如: "The trumpet player's lips were sore from practicing."

## 短语
- "Kiss on the lips" 意为亲吻嘴唇。
- "Lip service" 指口头上的尊敬或承诺，而不付诸行动。

## 例句
- 名词: "He smiled, revealing his perfect teeth and full lips."
- "The musician pressed his lips to the trumpet's mouthpiece."
