## 读音
/mʌtʃ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 很多的，大量的。例如: "There was much food left over after the party."
- **副词**: 非常，很。例如: "I like it much better now that it's clean."

## 短语
- "Much of" 表示很多，大部分。例如: "Much of the book is dedicated to this topic."
- "Not much" 意味着不多或者没什么。例如: "I haven't seen her much lately."

## 例句
- 形容词: "There was much excitement when the movie star arrived."
- 副词: "I appreciate your help much, thank you."

## 同义词
- A lot
- Plenty
- Great deal
- Large quantity
- A great amount
