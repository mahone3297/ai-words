## 读音
/məˈbeɪ/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示可能或或许。例如: "Maybe it will rain tomorrow."
- **连词**: 引导从句，表达可能性或不确定性。例如: "Maybe because he was tired, he didn't come."

## 短语
- "Maybe so" 表示同意或承认某种可能性。例如: "Maybe so, but we still need to check."
- "Maybe not" 表示否定或怀疑。例如: "I thought he would come, but maybe not."

## 例句
- 副词: "I'm not sure, maybe we should try a different approach."
- 连词: "Maybe if we talk to her, she'll change her mind."
