## Word: Maybe

## Pronunciation
/ˈmeɪbi/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Perhaps; possibly. It indicates uncertainty or likelihood. 

  > Example Sentence: _Maybe we should wait until tomorrow to make a decision._
  
- **Contraction**: A shortened form of "may be", which is used to express possibility.
  
  > Example Sentence: _It may be raining later today; we'll have to check the forecast._

## Phrases
- "Maybe so": An informal way of agreeing with something that might be true or possible.
  > Example: _"Are we going to the beach today?" "Maybe so, if the weather stays nice."_
  
- "Maybe not": Expresses the possibility that something will not happen or is unlikely.
  > Example: _"Do you think she'll come to the party?" "Maybe not, she's been feeling unwell lately."_

## Example Sentences
- **Adverbial usage**: _I'll try calling her again later; maybe she's busy right now._
- **Contracted usage**: _There's a chance he'll change his mind; it could maybe be a good idea to give him some space._

## Synonyms
- Perhaps
- Possibly
- Perchance
- Could be
- Mayhaps (informal)
