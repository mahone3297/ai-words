## 读音
/leg/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 人体从臀部到脚踝的部分：例如 "He broke his leg in a skiing accident."
  - 动物身体的相应部分：例如 "The dog shook its leg to get rid of the water."
  - 物体的支撑部分或分支：例如 "The table has four legs."
  - 一段时间的一部分（如一天中的一个时段）：例如 "The morning is the first leg of the day."

## 短语
- "On one's last leg" 表示处于极其疲惫或即将崩溃的状态。
- "Break a leg" 在演艺圈中用于祝好运，而非字面意义。

## 例句
- 名词: "She rested her leg on the ottoman after a long day of work."
- 动词（短语）: "When the race started, he was off like a shot, running on all fours for a moment before regaining his balance."
