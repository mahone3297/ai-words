## Definitions
### Noun
1. An expression of regret or歉意 for one's actions, words, or behavior. For example: "She offered a heartfelt apology for her mistake."
2. A formal statement acknowledging a fault or wrongdoing. For example: "He wrote a letter of apology to his friend."

### Verb
1. To acknowledge a fault or wrongdoing with an apology. For example: "She apologized profusely for her rude behavior."
2. To express remorse or regret for something done. For example: "He apologized to his boss for being late."

### Adjective
1. Expressing or involving an apology. For example: "An apologetic tone in her voice."
2. Showing regret or contrition. For example: "She wore an apologetic look on her face."

## Phrases
- "Make an apology" means to express regret or remorse.
- "Apologize in person" suggests giving a verbal apology directly.
- "I'm sorry for the inconvenience" is a common way to apologize for causing trouble.
