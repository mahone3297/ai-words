## 读音
/soʊ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 种植，播种。例如: "Farmers sow seeds in the spring for a summer harvest."
    - 散播，散布（信息、谣言等）。例如: "The news was sown quickly through the town."

- **名词**:
    - 猪，尤其是母猪。例如: "The sow was nursing her litter of piglets."

## 短语
- "Sow one's wild oats" 意指在年轻时放纵享乐，通常与性行为或轻浮行为有关。
- "Sow the wind, reap the whirlwind" 意味着做了坏事的人将承受由此带来的严重后果。

## 例句
- 动词: "He carefully sowed the seeds in rows for optimal growth."
- 名词: "The farmer tended to her prize-winning sow and her piglets."

## 同义词
- 播种 (动词):
    - plant
    - scatter
- 母猪 (名词):
    - female pig
    - gilt (年轻母猪)
