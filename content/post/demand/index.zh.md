## 读音
/dɪˈmænd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 对某事物的要求或需要；需求。例如: "The company needs to meet the growing demand for its products."
- **动词**: 要求，请求。例如: "She demanded an explanation for his behavior."
- **形容词**: 强烈要求的，紧迫的。例如: "There is a demand performance from the artist."

## 短语
- "In demand" 表示某物或某人非常受欢迎或需要。
- "Demand for something" 指对某物的需求量。

## 例句
- 名词: "The demand for renewable energy sources has increased in recent years."
- 动词: "The customer demanded a refund for the faulty product."
- 形容词: "His skills as a programmer are always in demand."
