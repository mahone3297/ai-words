## 读音
/ɪnˈdɪfrənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不关心的，漠不关心的。例如: "She was indifferent to their opinions."
- **副词**: 冷淡地，无所谓地。例如: "He answered my question indifferent."

## 短语
- "be indifferent to" 表示对...不在乎，不感兴趣。例如: "She is indifferent to fame and fortune."
- "with indifference" 以无所谓的态度。例如: "She received the news with indifference."

## 例句
- 形容词: "He seemed indifferent about the outcome of the game."
- 副词: "She treated his apology with indifference."

- Disinterested
- Indifferent
