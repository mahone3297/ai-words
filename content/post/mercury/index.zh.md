## 读音
/ˈmɜːrkjəri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 汞，一种化学元素，符号为Hg，常温下为液态金属。例如: "Mercury is a toxic heavy metal found in some old thermometers."
- **名词**: 瑞吉神，罗马神话中的商业、旅行和艺术之神。例如: "In Roman mythology, Mercury was the messenger of the gods."
- **天文学名词**: 水星，太阳系内离太阳最近的行星。例如: "Mercury has a very short orbital period around the Sun."
- **占星学名词**: 水星，占星术中代表沟通和思维的行星。例如: "According to astrology, Mercury's position can influence one's communication skills."

## 短语
- "Mercury poisoning" 汞中毒，由于摄入或接触汞导致的健康问题。
- "Mercury retrograde" 在占星学中，水星逆行，被认为可能带来通信混乱或延误。

## 例句
- 名词: "The old-fashioned barometer contained a column of mercury to measure atmospheric pressure."
- 天文学名词: "Observing Mercury during its transit across the Sun is a rare celestial event."
- 占星学名词: "When Mercury is retrograde, people often advise against signing contracts or making important decisions."

## 同义词
- Quicksilver (指液态汞)
- Hermes (在希腊神话中，与罗马神话中的瑞吉神相对应)
