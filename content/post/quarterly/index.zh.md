## 读音
/ˈkwɔːrtərli/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 每季度的，每季的。例如: "We have a quarterly financial report."
- **副词**: 每季度地，四季一次地。例如: "The company holds a meeting quarterly to discuss progress."

## 短语
- "quarterly report" 指的是每个季度的财务或业务报告。
- "quarterly review" 是指每三个月进行一次的评估或审查。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The magazine publishes new issues on a quarterly basis."
- 副词: "The rent is due quarterly, every three months."

## 同义词
- Every three months
- Trimesterly
- Semiannually (in some contexts)
- Bimonthly (in certain contexts, referring to every two months)

请注意，"quarterly" 通常不作为名词、动词、代词、数词、介词、连词或感叹词使用。
