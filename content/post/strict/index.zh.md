## 读音
/strɪkt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 严格的，严谨的，不宽容的。例如: "The teacher has a strict policy on late assignments."
- **副词**: 严格地，严密地。例如: "She follows the instructions strictly."

## 短语
- "Be strict with sb." 意味着对某人要求严格。
- "Strict adherence to rules" 表示严格遵守规则。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The company has a strict dress code for its employees."
- 副词: "The coach trains his athletes strictly to ensure peak performance."

## 同义词
- Severe
- Rigorous
- Tight-laced
- Uncompromising
- Stern
