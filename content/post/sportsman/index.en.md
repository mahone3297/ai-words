## Pronunciation
/sportsmən/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **sportsman** is a noun referring to an individual who participates in sports, typically with a focus on sportsmanship, fair play, and adherence to the rules. It can also denote a person known for their skill, dedication, or enthusiasm in a particular athletic activity.

### Verb
The term "sportsman" can be used as a verb in a less common sense, to describe the act of exhibiting sportsmanship or behaving in a dignified and courteous manner while participating in sports.

## Phrases
- "Displaying sportsmanship"
- "Being a true sportsman"
- "Sportsmanlike conduct"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "He was a true sportsman, always congratulating his opponents even when he lost."
- **Verbal use (less common)**: "He showed great sportsmanship by helping a competitor who had fallen during the race."

## Synonyms
- Athlete
- Player
- Sports enthusiast
- Gentleman athlete
- Sports participant
