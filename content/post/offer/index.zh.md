## 读音
/ˈɔːfər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 提议，提供。例如: "They made an offer for my house."
- **动词**: 提供，提议。例如: "The company offered her a job."
- **动词**: 出价，报价。例如: "He offered $50,000 for the car."

## 短语
- "Make an offer" 意思是提出一个建议或价格。
- "Counteroffer" 是对先前的提议提出一个新的提议。

## 例句
- 名词: "The employer received several job offers from different companies."
- 动词: "She decided to accept the university's offer of admission."
- 动词 (出价): "The buyer offered less than the asking price."
