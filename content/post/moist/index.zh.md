## 读音
/maɪɔɪst/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 湿润的，微湿的。例如: "The air was pleasantly moist after the rain."
- **名词**: 指含有水分的状态。例如: "The soil needs to maintain a certain level of moist."

## 短语
- "moisturizer" 是保湿剂，常用于皮肤保养。
- "moist environment" 指湿润的环境。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Her skin felt soft and moist after applying the moisturizer."
- 名词: "The plants thrive in a constantly moist environment."
