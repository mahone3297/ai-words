## 读音
/teər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 眼泪，泪水。例如: "Her eyes filled with tears as she read the sad news."
    - 裂口，破损处。例如: "There's a tear in my shirt."

- **动词**:
    - 撕裂，扯破。例如: "Be careful not to tear the paper when you open it."
    - （情感上）使痛苦，使伤心。例如: "The news of his death tore her apart."

- **形容词**:
    - 撕裂的，破损的。例如: "The old book had a tear along the spine."

## 短语
- "Tear off" 意为迅速撕下。例如: "Tear off a piece of paper and write your name on it."
- "In tears" 指哭泣的状态。例如: "She was in tears after the argument."

## 例句
- 名词: "She wiped away the tears from her cheeks."
- 动词: "He accidentally tore the photo while trying to remove it from the frame."
- 形容词: "The dog had torn the cushion cover, leaving it in tatters."

## 同义词
- 名词:
    - drop (眼泪)
    - rift (裂口)

- 动词:
    - rip (撕裂)
    - shred (撕碎)
