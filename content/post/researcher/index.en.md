## Pronunciation
/reɪˈsɜːrtər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who carries out research, investigates, or studies a particular subject or topic. (adj: research-oriented)
- **Verb**: To engage in research activities, gather information, or conduct inquiries. (syn: study, investigate)

## Phrases
- "A renowned researcher" refers to an expert in their field.
- "Conducting research" involves systematic inquiry.
- "Researcher collaboration" often leads to groundbreaking discoveries.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The renowned researcher published several groundbreaking papers on climate change."
- **Verb**: "She dedicated months to researching the history of the ancient city."
- **As a verb phrase**: "The team is currently researching new methods to improve crop yields."
