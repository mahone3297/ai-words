## 读音
/ˈliːvər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 一种工具，通常有长柄和扁平的宽头，用于撬动或移动物体。例如: "He used a lever to lift the heavy rock."
    - 机械装置中的部件，通过杠杆原理传递力或改变力的方向。例如: "The lever on the machine controls its speed."
    - 权力或影响力。例如: "She holds a significant lever in the company's decision-making process."

## 短语
- "Operating lever" 操纵杆，控制杆。
- "Pivot lever" 转轴杠杆。
- "Financial lever" 财务杠杆，指用债务来增加投资回报的可能性。

## 例句
- 名词: "The engineer showed us how to use the lever to open the heavy door."
- 机械用法: "The crane has a long lever for lifting heavy loads."
- 影响力: "His political connections gave him considerable leverage in negotiations."
