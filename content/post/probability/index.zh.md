## 读音
/prəˈbəbiləti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 表示某事发生的可能性或概率。例如: "The probability of rain tomorrow is high."
- **形容词**: 形容事物的可能性或有无把握。例如: "There's a high probability that he will come."

## 短语
- "Probability theory" 是关于概率的数学理论。
- "Probability distribution" 描述随机变量可能出现的各种结果及其概率。

## 例句
- 名词: "The probability of winning the lottery is very low."
- 形容词: "The probability of success in this experiment is quite high."
