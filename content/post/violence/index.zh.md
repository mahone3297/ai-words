## 读音
/vɪˈlɑːns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 暴力；暴行；凶猛。例如: "The movie contains scenes of extreme violence."
- **形容词**: 暴力的；猛烈的。例如: "The football match was marked by violent clashes between fans."

## 短语
- "Act of violence" 指暴力行为。
- "Domestic violence" 指家庭暴力。
- "Violent crime" 指暴力犯罪。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police were called to intervene in a case of domestic violence."
- 形容词: "The storm was so violent that it uprooted trees and damaged buildings."
