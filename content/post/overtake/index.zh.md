## 读音
/əʊvərˈteɪk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 超过；赶过。例如: "The fast car easily overtook the slow truck on the highway."
2. 接管；取代。例如: "New technology is rapidly overtaking traditional methods."

## 短语
- "Overtake someone/something" 意为超过某人或某物。例如: "He was determined to overtake the leader in the race."
- "Overtake by surprise" 意为出其不意地超过。例如: "The attacker overtook the guard from behind, surprising him."

## 例句
- 动词: "She managed to overtake the leading runner in the final stretch of the marathon."
- 动词: "His new job quickly overtook his old one in terms of importance."
