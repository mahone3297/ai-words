## Pronunciation
/rɪˈkɔːrdər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A device or software used to record sounds, images, or data. For example: "The student used a digital recorder for their project."
- **Verb**: To make a recording or take notes; to document. For example: "She recorded the lecture for those who couldn't attend."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of a person who is efficient or thorough in their work. For example: "He is a very organized and accurate recorder of events."

## Phrases
- "Record a message" means to capture sound or audio as a file.
- "Video recorder" is a specific type of device that captures video footage.
- "Keep a record" implies maintaining a written or digital log of information.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She attached a microphone to the recorder so she could capture every detail."
- **Verb**: "After the meeting, he recorded his notes on the whiteboard."
- **Adjective**: "The recorder kept a meticulous record of the team's progress."
