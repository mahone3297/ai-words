## 读音
/pərˈfaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 使纯净；提纯：例如 "The process of purifying water removes impurities."
  - 净化（心灵、思想等）：例如 "She sought to purify her soul through meditation."
  - 去除杂质；清除：例如 "The scientists are working on a way to purify gold ore."

## 短语
- "Purification process" 指的是净化或提纯的过程。
- "Purify one's thoughts" 意味着净化或澄清一个人的思想。

## 例句

- "In alchemy, the goal was to purify base metals into gold."
