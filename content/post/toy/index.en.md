## Pronunciation
/toʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **toy** is an object designed for children to play with, often imitating real-life objects or characters, providing entertainment and learning opportunities.

### Verb
To **play with toys** or **engage in toy play** refers to the activity of using toys for amusement.

### Adjective
* Toy-like* describes something that resembles or has the appearance of a toy.

### Noun (plural)
Toys can also refer to a collection or assortment of such items.

## Phrases
- "Play with your toys" for children's activities.
- "Collectible toys" for items that are collected as hobbies.
- "Toy store" or "toy section" refers to a retail location or part of a store dedicated to selling toys.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The child was engrossed in playing with her new toy car."
- **Verb**: "After work, he spent hours playing with his collection of action figures."
- **Adjective**: "The toy-like robot fascinated the kids at the science fair."
