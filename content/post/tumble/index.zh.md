## 读音
/tʌmbl/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 倾倒；摔倒：例如 "The vase tumbled off the shelf when the cat jumped."
2. 跌落；滚落：例如 "She tumbled down the stairs."
3. 翻滚；跌跌撞撞地移动：例如 "The children were tumbling around on the grass."
4. （信息、秘密等）意外揭露：例如 "The truth about the scandal tumbled out."

- **名词**:
1. 摔倒；跌倒：例如 "He had a nasty tumble while playing soccer."
2. 翻滚动作；连续翻转：例如 "The acrobat performed several impressive tumbles in the air."

## 短语
- "Tumble down" 意为倒塌或滚下：例如 "The old house finally tumbled down after years of neglect."
- "Tumble dry" 在烘干机中翻滚干燥衣物：例如 "I'll put these towels in the dryer on the tumble dry setting."

## 例句
- 动词: "The stack of books tumbled onto the floor when I accidentally brushed against it."
- 名词: "She got a bruised knee from her recent tumble during dance practice."
