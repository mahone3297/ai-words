## Pronunciation
/tʌmbl/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (n.) To fall or roll down or over, typically in an uncontrolled manner. (v.) To lose one's balance and fall. (n.) A sudden or unexpected event or collapse. (adj.) Disordered or chaotic; confused.

## Phrases
- "Tumbledown house" refers to a dilapidated building.
- "Tumbleweed" is a slang term for a person who rolls with the punches or is carefree.
- "Tumble and roll" is a self-defense maneuver.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The child tumbled off the swing and landed on her feet."
- **Noun**: "After the earthquake, the once-grand building had become a tumbledown ruin."
- **Adjective**: "The messy room was a tangle of clothes and books, a total tumble."
