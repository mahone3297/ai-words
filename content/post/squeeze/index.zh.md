## 读音
/skruːz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 用力挤压或压榨。例如: "He squeezed the orange to get the juice out."
2. 在狭窄空间中勉强通过。例如: "We had to squeeze through the narrow doorway."
3. 紧缩，压缩。例如: "The company had to squeeze its budget to save costs."

- **名词**:
1. 挤压的动作或结果。例如: "There was a gentle squeeze on my hand."
2. 紧迫的情况或压力。例如: "They're feeling the financial squeeze."

## 短语
- "squeeze out" 挤出或榨取。例如: "Squeeze out every last drop of toothpaste."
- "squeeze by" 从旁边挤过去。例如: "The car barely squeezed by the crowd."

## 例句
- 动词: "She squeezed the sponge to remove the excess water."
- 名词: "The economic squeeze has forced many families to cut back on expenses."
