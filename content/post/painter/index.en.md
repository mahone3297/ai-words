## Pronunciation
/ˈpeɪntər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who practices the art or craft of painting, especially as an occupation or profession. For example: "The painter created a stunning landscape on canvas."
- **Noun (figurative)**: A machine or device that applies paint, such as a spray painter or road marking painter.
- **Noun (archaic)**: One who paints or colors something; could refer to someone who applies makeup or cosmetics.

## Phrases
- "Painter's tape" is a type of masking tape used by painters to mask off areas while painting.
- "A house painter" specifically refers to a professional who paints buildings and houses.
- "To put oneself in the picture", a figurative expression often used when referring to a painter, means to imagine oneself as part of a particular situation.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (artist)**: "The famous painter exhibited his latest collection at the gallery."
- **Noun (device)**: "We hired a professional team with advanced painters to quickly finish the exterior wall painting."
- **Noun (archaic)**: "In Elizabethan times, a painter was employed to apply rouge and powder to the queen's face."

## Synonyms
- Artist
- Artisan
- Decorator (in the context of painting)
- Brush-wielder (informal)
- Colorist (referring to an artist specializing in color use)
- Painter-decorator (for those who specialize in decorating homes and buildings)
