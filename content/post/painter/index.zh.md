## 读音
/ˈpeɪntr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 画家，从事绘画工作的人。例如: "The famous painter, Van Gogh, created many masterpieces."
- **名词**: 油漆工，涂漆的人。例如: "The painter was busy repainting the house."

## 短语
- "a master painter" 指的是技艺高超的画家。
- "to paint like a true painter" 意味着画得很有技巧。

## 例句
- 名词: "Monet was a renowned French painter of the Impressionist era."
- 名词: "The painter carefully selected the colors for his next artwork."
