## 读音
/rɪˈfjuːz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 拒绝接受或采纳。例如: "She refused his offer to help."
  - 不愿意做或不同意。例如: "I refuse to believe that's true."
  - 回绝邀请或提议。例如: "They politely refused the invitation to the dinner."

## 短语
- "refuse to do something" 意为决定不做某事。
- "refusal" 是 "refuse" 的名词形式，表示拒绝或不接受的行为。

## 例句
- "He refused to answer any more questions from the reporters."
- "She felt hurt when her friend refused her invitation to the wedding."
- "Their refusal to compromise led to the breakdown of the negotiations."
