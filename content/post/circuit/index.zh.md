## 读音
/ˈsɜːr.kɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 电路，由导线、开关、电源和负载组成的系统，电流在其内部流动。例如: "The electrician is checking the wiring and circuits in the house."
- **名词**: 路线，巡回，尤指在一定路线上的活动或访问。例如: "The band is on a national concert circuit."

## 短语
- "Closed circuit" 表示一个完整的、没有断开点的电路。
- "Electrical circuit" 特指与电相关的电路。
- "Circuit training" 是一种结合多个不同锻炼的健身训练方法。

## 例句
- 名词: "Make sure to turn off the main circuit breaker before working on any electrical installation."
- 名词: "The athlete follows a strict circuit training routine to improve their stamina."
