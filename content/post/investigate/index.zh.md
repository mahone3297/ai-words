## 读音
/ɪnˈvestɪɡeɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 进行调查，研究。例如: "The police are investigating the crime scene."
- **名词**: 调查，探究。例如: "The investigation uncovered new evidence."

## 短语
- "To investigate something" 意为对某事进行调查。
- "Investigate thoroughly" 意味着彻底调查。

## 例句
- 动词: "The journalist is investigating the corruption allegations."
- 名词: "The company launched an internal investigation into the safety issue."
