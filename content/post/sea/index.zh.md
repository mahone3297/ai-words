## 读音
/siː/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 大海，海洋。例如: "The vast expanse of the open sea."
  - 海洋的水。例如: "The smell of salt in the sea air."

## 短语
- "at sea" 意为在海上，或困惑不解。例如: "The ship was at sea for weeks."，"I was completely at sea with the math problem."

## 例句
- 名词: "The whales were swimming in the deep blue sea."
- "The sailor loves the endless expanse of the sea."
