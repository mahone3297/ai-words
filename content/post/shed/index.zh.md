## 读音
/ʃɛd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个小屋，棚子或简易建筑物，通常用来存放工具、车辆或其他物品。例如: "He keeps his gardening tools in a wooden shed at the bottom of the garden."
- **动词**: （动词短语 "shed something"）丢弃、脱掉或流出。例如: "The snake shed its skin last week."
- **动词**: （动词短语 "shed light on something"）使某事变得清楚或揭示真相。例如: "New evidence has shed light on the mystery surrounding the disappearance."

## 短语
- "shed tears" 意为哭泣。
- "shed weight" 指减肥或减轻重量。
- "shed light on a subject" 意为解释或阐明某个主题。

## 例句
- 名词: "The old shed in the backyard needs a new coat of paint."
- 动词: "She shed her coat as soon as she entered the warm house."
- 动词短语: "The detective's findings shed light on the motive behind the crime."
