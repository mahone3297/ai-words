## 读音
/fəɪnt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 薄弱的，微弱的。例如: "The light was too faint to read by."
- **动词**: (使)变得微弱；(感到)晕眩。例如: "She felt faint after running up the stairs."
- **名词**: 晕厥，眩晕。例如: "He had a sudden faint and fell to the ground."

## 短语
- "faint hope" 意为渺茫的希望。
- "faint heart never won fair lady" 是一句谚语，意思是胆小鬼得不到美人。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The sound of the distant music grew increasingly faint."
- 动词: "She nearly fainted when she saw the snake."
- 名词: "He woke up feeling dizzy and with a faint memory of the night before."
