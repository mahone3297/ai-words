## 读音
/məˈstli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 主要地，大部分情况下，通常。例如: "I work from home mostly."

## 短语
- "mostly harmless" 意为基本上无害的，常用于幽默表达。

## 例句
- "We spend our weekends mostly relaxing and catching up on TV shows."
- "The population of the town is mostly composed of young professionals."

## 同义词
- primarily
- mainly
- largely
- predominantly
- for the most part
