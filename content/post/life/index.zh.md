## 读音
/lʌɪf/

## 释义
- **名词**: 生命，生物体存在的状态。例如: "All living creatures need water to sustain life."
- **名词**: 生活，生存的方式或经历。例如: "She leads a simple life in the countryside."
- **名词**: 时期，阶段。例如: "His teenage life was full of challenges."
- **名词**: 生涯，一生的工作或活动。例如: "He dedicated his life to science."
- **动词**: 度过（时间）。例如: "She has lived in Paris for five years."

## 短语
- "The quality of life" 指的是生活的质量或幸福感。
- "Life expectancy" 指平均寿命。
- "New life" 可以指新生或新的开始，如 "The spring brings new life to the garden."

## 例句
- 名词: "The discovery of a single-celled organism proved that life exists on Mars."
- 名词: "She cherished every moment of her life with her family."
- 动词: "He's trying to life through this difficult period."
