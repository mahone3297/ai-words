## 读音
/pərˈspektɪv/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    1. 观点；看法：Each person has their own unique perspective on life.
    2. 方式；角度：The artist painted the scene from a bird's-eye perspective.
    3. 深度感；透视感：The painting has a great sense of perspective.

- **形容词**:
    有远见的；深思熟虑的：It's important to maintain a perspective attitude towards challenges.

## 短语
- "Change your perspective" 意味着改变你的观点或思考方式。
- "In the long perspective" 指从长远的角度看。

## 例句
- 名词: "She gained a new perspective after traveling abroad."
- 形容词: "Keeping a balanced perspective helps in decision-making."
