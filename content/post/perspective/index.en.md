## Pronunciation
/pəˈspektɪv/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Perspective**: The point of view or opinion from which something is seen or judged; a way of looking at a situation or problem. (adj.)
   - Example: "Each person's life experience shapes their unique perspective on the world."
2. **Visual perspective**: The arrangement and depiction of objects in space, often in art and design, to create a sense of depth and three-dimensionality. (n.)
   - Example: "The artist carefully considered the vanishing point to achieve correct linear perspective."

### Verb
1. To perceive or regard something from a particular point of view or with a specific attitude. (v.)
   - Example: "It's important to gain different perspectives on complex issues."
2. To change one's perspective after gaining new information or insights. (v.)
   - Example: "Reading different books broadened her perspective on life."
