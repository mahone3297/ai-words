## 读音
/nəˈtʃrəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 1. 自然的；非人工的。例如: "The park has a lot of natural beauty."
             2. 天生的；固有的。例如: "She has a natural talent for music."
             3. 生理的；正常的。例如: "It's only natural for children to be curious."

## 短语
- "In its natural state" 指事物未受人为改变的状态。
- "Natural resources" 指自然界提供的资源，如水、森林和矿产。
- "Acting naturally" 意为表现得不做作或真实。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The animals live in their natural habitat without human interference."
- 形容词: "She has a natural ability to make people feel at ease."
- 形容词: "Breathing deeply helps to restore the body's natural rhythm."
