## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by having a high degree of proficiency, dexterity, or ability in a particular activity or field. For example: "The chef is skillful in preparing intricate dishes."
- **Noun**: A display of skill or expertise; a proficient or competent performance. For example: "His skillful piano playing impressed the audience."

## Phrases
- "Skillful craftsmanship" refers to excellent workmanship.
- "A skillful surgeon" is a highly competent and experienced doctor.
- "Skillful problem-solving" denotes the ability to find effective solutions.
