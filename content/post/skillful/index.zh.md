## 读音
/skɪlfl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 具有技巧的，熟练的，有才能的。例如: "She is a skillful dancer with years of practice."
- **副词**: 熟练地，巧妙地。例如: "He performed the task skillfully, impressing his colleagues."

## 短语
- "Skillful in something" 指在某方面有技巧或熟练。
- "Skillfully done" 意味着完成得非常熟练。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The surgeon's skillful hands made the operation a success."
- 副词: "She skillfully negotiated a better deal for the company."
