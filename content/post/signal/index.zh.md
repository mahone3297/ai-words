## 读音
/ˈsɪɡnəl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 信号，标志，指示：A visual, audible, or other means of conveying information, such as a traffic signal or a communication in code.
   - 信号（通信）：A coded message sent by telegraph, radio, television, or other means of communication.
   - 生物信号：A chemical or physical cue that triggers a response in a cell or organism.

- **动词**:
   - 发送信号：To communicate with or give a signal to someone, especially through a specific medium.
   - 指示，示意：To indicate or show something, often with gestures or lights.

## 短语
- "Signal for help" 意为发出求救信号。
- "Signal intent" 表示传达意图或目的的信号。
- "Signal a change" 意味着通过某种信号表明变化即将发生。

## 例句
- 名词: "The red light is a signal to stop."
- 动词: "He signaled the waiter for another drink."
- 感叹词: "In the old days, sailors would signal each other using flags."
