## 读音
/ˈtɛknələdʒi/

## 释义
- **名词**: 科学和技术的总称，尤指应用于工业和商业的发明、方法和知识。例如: "Advances in technology have transformed our daily lives."
- **动词**: 使用或应用科学技术。例如: "The company is technologizing its production process to increase efficiency."

## 短语
- "Information technology" (信息技术) 涵盖了处理和通信数据的系统。
- "High technology" (高科技) 指尖端或最新的科技产品或技术。
- "Technology transfer" (技术转移) 指将技术从一个组织转移到另一个组织的过程。

## 例句
- 名词: "Modern technology has made communication faster and more convenient than ever before."
- 动词: "They are technologizing their manufacturing processes to reduce waste."
