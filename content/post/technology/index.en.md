## Pronunciation
/tekˈnɒlədʒi/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The application of scientific knowledge and techniques for practical purposes, especially in industries.
2. The collection of tools, machines, materials, and processes used to solve problems or create goods and services.
3. The state of being advanced in terms of science and equipment.
4. The field that deals with the development, use, and study of tools, systems, and processes for improving efficiency and effectiveness.

### Verb
1. To apply or employ technological methods or advancements.
2. To innovate or develop new technologies.
