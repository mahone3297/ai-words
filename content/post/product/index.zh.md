## 读音
/ˈprɑːdʌkt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 生产的结果，产品，制品。例如: "The company's latest product is a smartphone with advanced features."
- **名词**: 经济学中，指在一定时间内生产的所有商品和服务的总和。例如: "GDP measures the total output of a country's economy, or its gross domestic product."
- **名词**: 数学中，两个或多个数相乘的结果。例如: "The product of 3 and 4 is 12."

## 短语
- "End product" 指最终产生的结果或产品。
- "Consumer product" 指面向消费者的商品。
- "Product launch" 指新产品的发布活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new product received positive reviews from customers."
- 名词: "Their goal is to increase the productivity of their workforce."
- 名词: "The teacher asked students to find the product of 5 and 8."
