## Pronunciation
/həˈloʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to greet or address someone. For example: "She waved a friendly hello at the bus stop."
- **Verb**: To initiate communication with a greeting or expression of recognition. For example: "He greeted the guests with a hearty hello."
- **Interjection**: Expressing a salutation, surprise, or attention. For example: "Oh, hello! You startled me."

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to extend a greeting.
- "Hello, how are you?" is a standard conversational opening.
- "Hello, old friend" is a familiar way to address someone you know well.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children said their goodbyes with a chorus of hellos as they left for school."
- **Verb**: "After a long day at work, he was happy to hear his son's hello from the other end of the phone."
- **Interjection**: "Hello, sunshine! It's such a beautiful day today."
