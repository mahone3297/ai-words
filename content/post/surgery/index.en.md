## Pronunciation
/ˈsɜːrɡəri/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: **1**. The branch of medicine that deals with operations on the body, especially using instruments or other techniques to repair or remove diseased or damaged tissue. **2**. A surgical operation or procedure. For example: "The patient underwent a successful surgery."
- **Verb**: To perform surgery or operate on someone. For example: "The surgeon will be performing surgery tomorrow."
- **Noun (plural)**: Surgical skills or expertise. For example: "She has a talent for surgical precision."

## Phrases
- "Undergo surgery" means to have a medical operation.
- "Pre-surgery preparation" refers to the steps taken before a surgery.
- "Post-surgery recovery" is the period after a surgery when the patient heals.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The patient was discharged from the hospital after a successful knee surgery."
- **Verb**: "The doctor scheduled the surgery for next week."
- **Noun**: "She's been undergoing extensive surgical training."
