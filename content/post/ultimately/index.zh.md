## 读音
/ˈʌltɪmətli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 最终地，最后，根本上。例如: "Ultimately, it was his decision that mattered most."
- **形容词**: 极端的，根本的。例如: "The ultimately goal of the project is to find a cure."

## 短语
- "In the end, ultimately" 表示经过所有过程或考虑后，最终结果。
- "Ultimately responsible" 意味着负有最终责任。

## 例句
- 副词: "After months of negotiations, the companies ultimately agreed on a merger."
- 形容词: "This is an ultimately important matter that requires immediate attention."
