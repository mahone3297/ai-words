## 读音
/scout/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 侦察兵，童子军：A person, especially a child or少年, trained in military-style activities and skills for use in emergencies or for educational purposes.
  - 观察员，探子：A person who searches for information or potential talent, typically in a discreet or informal manner.

- **动词**:
  - 侦查，侦探：To search an area or gather information about it, often secretly or carefully.
  - 挑选（运动员、演员等）：To search for and select talented individuals, such as athletes or actors, for a team or a performance.

## 短语
- "Scout around" 意为四处搜寻或侦察。
- "Scout team" 在体育中指的是模拟对手的预备队。

## 例句
- 名词: "The boy scout helped set up the campsite."
- 动词: "The talent scout discovered the singer at a local gig."
