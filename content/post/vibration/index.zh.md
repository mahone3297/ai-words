## 读音
/vərˈbeɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 物理学中的振动或波动：物体或波的周期性运动，如声波或地震波。例如: "The sound is caused by the vibration of air molecules."
  - 情绪或感觉的波动：情绪上的起伏或激动。例如: "After the concert, the audience was still under the vibration of the music."
  
- **动词 v**:
  - 使震动：使物体产生振动。例如: "The engine started to vibrate violently when we reached high speeds."
  - 引申为激发或触动：引起感情或思想的共鸣。例如: "Her speech vibrated with passion and conviction."

## 短语
- "Vibration test" 是用来检测设备或结构在振动环境下的性能测试。
- "Vibrate with emotion" 形容强烈的情绪反应。

## 例句
- 名词: "The bridge was closed due to excessive vibrations caused by high winds."
- 动词: "The phone was vibrating in my pocket, indicating an incoming call."
