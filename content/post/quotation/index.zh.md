## 读音
/ˈkwəʊtəʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 引用的话；引述：一段从书籍、演讲或其他来源中直接引用的文字，通常用引号括起来。例如: "[She](/post/she/) included [a](/post/a/) [famous](/post/famous/) [quotation](/post/quotation/) [from](/post/from/) Shakespeare [in](/post/in/) [her](/post/her/) [essay](/post/essay/)."
  - 报价；估价：对商品或服务的价格估计或正式报价。例如: "[The](/post/the/) contractor gave [us](/post/us/) [a](/post/a/) detailed [quotation](/post/quotation/) [for](/post/for/) [the](/post/the/) renovation [work](/post/work/)."

## 短语
- "[In](/post/in/) [quotation](/post/quotation/) marks" 表示使用引号括起的内容。
- "[Stock](/post/stock/) [quotation](/post/quotation/)" 指股票市场的报价信息。
- "[Price](/post/price/) [quotation](/post/quotation/)" 指价格的报价。
