## 读音
/ˈkwəʊtəʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 引用的话；引述：一段从书籍、演讲或其他来源中直接引用的文字，通常用引号括起来。例如: "She included a famous quotation from Shakespeare in her essay."
  - 报价；估价：对商品或服务的价格估计或正式报价。例如: "The contractor gave us a detailed quotation for the renovation work."

## 短语
- "In quotation marks" 表示使用引号括起的内容。
- "Stock quotation" 指股票市场的报价信息。
- "Price quotation" 指价格的报价。

## 例句
- 名词: "The article begins with a thought-provoking quotation from Einstein."
- 名词: "The stockbroker provided me with the latest market quotations."
