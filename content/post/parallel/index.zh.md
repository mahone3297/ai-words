## 读音
/ˈpærəlel/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 平行的，成平行线的。例如: "The two roads ran parallel to each other for miles."
- **名词**: 平行线，相似的事物。例如: "They drew two parallel lines on the paper."
- **动词**: 与...平行，与...相似。例如: "Her experiences paralleled mine in many ways."

## 短语
- "run parallel to" 意味着与...并行，与...一致。例如: "The train tracks run parallel to the river."
- "parallel lives" 指两个人或事物的生活轨迹相似或同时发生。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The bookshelves were designed to be parallel to the wall."
- 名词: "In geometry, parallel lines never intersect."
- 动词: "Her career path paralleled his, both rising through the ranks simultaneously."

## 同义词
- corresponding
- equivalent
- similar
- congruent
- coordinated
