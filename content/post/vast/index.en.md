## Pronunciation
/væst/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Vast** as a noun refers to something extremely large, extensive, or immense in size, scope, or extent. 
- It can also be used to describe a sense of grandeur or magnificence.

### Adjective
- As an adjective, it describes something that is enormous, unbounded, or immeasurably great.

## Phrases
- "A vast landscape" denotes a large, expansive scenery.
- "The vast universe" implies the infinite expanse of space.
- "Vast resources" signify an abundant supply.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The library housed a vast collection of books."
- **Adjective**: "The ocean's depths are a vast and mysterious world."
- "The empire spanned vast territories across the continent."
