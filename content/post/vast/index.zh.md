## 读音
/væst/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 极大的，辽阔的，广大的。例如: "The vastness of the desert is overwhelming."
- **名词**: 大量，巨大，浩渺（常用于抽象概念）。例如: "They were faced with a vast amount of work."

## 短语
- "vast majority" 表示绝大多数。
- "vast expanse" 指广阔的区域或空间。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The Amazon rainforest is a vast and biodiverse ecosystem."
- 名词: "The company received a vast number of applications for the job."
