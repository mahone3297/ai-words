## 读音
/ˈækses/

## 释义
- **名词**: 可进入、可使用或可接触的权利或机会。例如: "The hotel guests have access to the swimming pool."
- **动词**: 获得、进入或使用（信息、地方或资源）。例如: "I accessed my emails from the library's computer."
- **形容词**: 可接近的，可到达的。例如: "The new office building has easy access from the highway."

## 短语
- "have access to" 意味着能够使用或得到。例如: "Students have access to a wide range of online resources."
- "deny access to" 意味着不允许进入或使用。例如: "The security system will deny access to unauthorized personnel."

## 例句
- 名词: "The key gives you access to the restricted area."
- 动词: "He accessed the database to retrieve the customer information."
- 形容词: "The clinic provides free and accessible healthcare for the community."
