## 读音
/ˈprɑːspərəs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 富裕的，繁荣的，成功的。例如: "The city has experienced a prosperous economy in recent years."
- **副词**: 繁荣地，成功地。例如: "The business is prosperously expanding its market share."

## 短语
- "Prosperous times" 意味着经济或生活的富裕时期。
- "Prosperous nation" 指的是繁荣昌盛的国家。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The country enjoyed a long period of prosperous growth after the war."
- 副词: "Their company has been prosperously operating for over a decade."
