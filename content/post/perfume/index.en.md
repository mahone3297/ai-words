## Pronunciation
/ˈpɜːrfjuːm/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Perfume**: A scented liquid used to give off a pleasant aroma, typically applied to the skin or clothing.
   - Definition: A mixture of fragrant essential oils and solvents, used to enhance one's personal scent.
   - Example: "She wore a delicate perfume that lingered in the air."
2. **Scent**: The distinctive aroma associated with a particular substance or person.
   - Example: "The room was filled with the rich scent of freshly baked cookies."

### Verb
1. To apply perfume or cologne.
   - Example: "She sprayed her perfume before leaving the house."
2. (figuratively) To have a distinctive aroma or atmosphere.
   - Example: "His work had a distinct perfume of creativity."

## Phrases
- "Spray some perfume on" or "apply perfume"
- "Her perfume was subtle but noticeable"
- "The perfume industry has evolved significantly over the years"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She dabbed some perfume behind her ears before heading out."
- **Verb**: "He could smell the subtle perfume of the flowers in the garden."
- **Idiomatic**: "The library had a familiar scent of old books."
