## 读音
/perfjuːm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种芳香的液体，通常用于个人护理或室内香氛。例如: "She dabbed some perfume behind her ears before going out."
- **动词**: 给某物增添香味。例如: "The room was perfumed with lavender oil."

## 短语
- "Perfume oil" 指的是用于制作香水的精油。
- "Eau de toilette" 或 "Eau de parfum" 是不同浓度的香水产品。

## 例句
- 名词: "She chose a delicate floral perfume for her wedding day."
- 动词: "The store specializes in perfuming clothes with unique scents."

## 同义词
- Fragrance
- Scent
- Aroma
- Essence
- Cologne (通常指的是男士香水)
