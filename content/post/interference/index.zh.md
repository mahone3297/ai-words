## 读音
/ɪnˈfɜːrəns/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 干扰，妨碍：任何对通信、活动或进程的干扰。例如: "There was too much radio interference for a clear signal."
    - 介入，干涉：在他人事务中的不受欢迎的参与。例如: "She resented his constant interference in her work."

## 短语
- "interference with" 表示对某事物的干扰或阻碍。例如: "The construction caused interference with our daily routines."
- "collateral interference" 指间接或附带的干扰。例如: "The new policy had collateral interference on other departments."

## 例句
- 名词: "The astronomers had to find ways to reduce the light interference from nearby cities."
- 动词（非标准用法）: "His constant interference in my decisions made me frustrated."

## 同义词
- Disturbance
- Hindrance
- Obstruction
- Intercession
- Intrusion
