## 读音
/stæf/

## 释义
- **名词**: 工作人员，职员。例如: "The company's staff consists of 50 employees."
- **名词**: 长矛，棍棒，权杖。例如: "The king held his golden staff high during the ceremony."
- **名词**: 军队或组织的指挥系统。例如: "The general oversees the military staff."
- **动词**: 配备工作人员。例如: "The restaurant needs to staff more servers for the busy dinner shift."

## 短语
- "Management staff" 指管理层人员。
- "Support staff" 指辅助或后勤工作人员。
- "On staff" 表示某人是机构或公司的正式员工。

## 例句
- 名词: "The hotel has a friendly and efficient staff."
- 动词: "They are currently staffing the new branch office."
- 名词 (军事用法): "The general's staff planned the operation."
