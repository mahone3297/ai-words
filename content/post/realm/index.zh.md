## 读音
/riːm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 1. 领域，范围：A particular field or area of activity, knowledge, or influence. 例如: "the realm of politics"
    - 2. 王国，领土：A kingdom or territory, especially one ruled by a sovereign. 例如: "the ancient realms of Europe"

## 短语
- "in the realm of" 表示在...的范围内。例如: "The discussion was in the realm of science fiction."
- "royal realm" 指王室统治的领域或王国。例如: "The royal realm spanned across several provinces."

## 例句
- 名词: "Exploring the realm of quantum physics can be mind-bending."
- 名词: "The queen held absolute power over her realm."
