## Pronunciation
/rɪˈlɛm/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A particular sphere or branch of knowledge, activity, or experience: *the realm of science*; *the medieval fantasy realm*.
2. The dominion or territory ruled by a monarch or other authority: *the Roman Empire's realm*.
3. (informal) A situation or condition: *the realm of possibility*; *the realm of dreams*.

### Verb
1. To occupy or exercise authority over: *the gods ruling the realm of the afterlife*.
2. To have control or influence over: *to reign over one's thoughts*.

## Phrases
- "In the realm of art" refers to the field of artistic expression.
- "Beyond this realm" means outside of a particular area or context.
- "To explore the realm of imagination" means to venture into the world of creative thought.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The wizard's realm was filled with magical creatures and ancient artifacts."
- **Verb**: "The king's realm was known for its prosperity and peace."
