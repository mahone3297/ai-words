## Pronunciation
/səˈmɜːkət/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A large retail store that sells a wide variety of goods, typically including food, household items, and groceries. Example: "The supermarket was bustling with shoppers on a Saturday morning."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing a store or market that offers a diverse range of products. Example: "A modern supermarket offers a self-checkout option."

## Phrases
- "Do your grocery shopping at the supermarket" means to buy food and household essentials at a supermarket.
- "Supermarket specials" refer to discounted prices on products.
- "One-stop supermarket" signifies a store where customers can find almost everything they need.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I usually pick up my groceries at the local supermarket on my way home from work."
- **Adjective**: "The supermarket's layout is designed for easy navigation."
- **Verb**: "The cashier greeted me with a friendly smile at the supermarket."

## Synonyms
- Grocery store
- Mart
- Hypermarket
- Big box store
- Retail mall (in some contexts)

## Antonyms
- Convenience store (smaller, limited selection)
- Specialty store (narrow focus on specific products)
