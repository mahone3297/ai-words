## Definitions
### Noun
- **Stony**: A noun referring to a surface or material composed of stones, as in a rocky terrain or a stony road.
- **Stony expression**: An adjective describing a cold, unfriendly, or stern facial expression.

### Verb
- **Stony**: Informal term for refusing to give money or help, often used metaphorically (e.g., "He's stony to her request for assistance.")
- **Be stony faced**: To have a stony expression, appearing unemotional or unfriendly.

### Adjective
- **Stony**: Characterizing something hard or solid, like a stony beach or a stony heart (implying emotional detachment).
- **Stony-hearted**: Having a heart of stone, meaning unfeeling or callous.
