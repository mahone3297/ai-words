## 读音
/ʃuː/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 脚穿的鞋子，通常成对出现。例如: "He bought a new pair of shoes for his job interview."
- **动词 (v)**: 给...穿上鞋。例如: "I need to shoe my horse before the ride."

## 短语
- "Shoes off" 意为要求脱鞋，通常在室内。
- "To have a shoe in the game" 指参与或有影响。

## 例句
- 名词: "The little girl picked out her favorite red shoes for her first day of school."
- 动词: "The cobbler will shoe your horse with care."
