## 读音
/riːˈstrɪkʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 限制；约束：规定或法律等对行动、行为或权利的限制。例如: "There are certain restrictions on smoking in public places."
   - 条件；规定：在做某事时必须遵守的条件或规则。例如: "The contract has some restrictions regarding the use of the software."

## 短语
- "restriction clause" 指合同或法律文件中的限制条款。
- "under restriction" 表示处于某种限制之下。

## 例句
- 名词: "The restaurant has a dress code restriction that doesn't allow casual wear."
- "The government imposed new restrictions on travel to prevent the spread of the virus."

## 同义词
- Limitation
- Constraint
- Regulation
- Control
- Prohibition
