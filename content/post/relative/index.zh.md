## 读音
/ˈrɛlətɪv/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 与某人有亲属关系的人。例如: "My cousin is my closest relative."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 有关联的，相关的。例如: "The relative importance of this issue needs to be considered."

## 短语
- "Close relatives" 指的是直系亲属或关系非常亲近的人。
- "Relative clause" 在语法中指的是修饰名词的从句。

## 例句
- 名词: "We're having a family gathering with all our relatives next weekend."
- 形容词: "The two languages have many relative words due to their shared history."
