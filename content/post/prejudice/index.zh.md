## 读音
/prɪˈdʒuːdɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 预先判断；成见；偏见。例如: "He tried to overcome his prejudice against people from different cultures."
- **动词**: 对…持有成见；对…有偏见。例如: "Don't prejudice the case before hearing all the evidence."

## 短语
- "racial prejudice" 种族偏见
- "prejudice someone's chances" 影响某人的机会

## 例句
- 名词: "It's important to judge people based on their actions, not on our prejudices."
- 动词: "Her opinion was prejudiced by the rumors she had heard."

## 同义词
- Bias
- Stereotyping
- Preconception
- Discrimination
- Bigotry
