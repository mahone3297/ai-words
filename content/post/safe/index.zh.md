## 读音
/ˈseɪf/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 安全；安全状态。例如: "Everyone felt safe in their homes."
   2. 保险箱；储物柜。例如: "She kept her jewels in the hotel safe."

- **形容词**: 1. 安全的；无危险的。例如: "It's safe to cross the road now."
   2. 可靠的；稳妥的。例如: "This method is safe and tested."

## 短语
- "be safe" 表示处于安全的状态。
- "safe bet" 指风险小、有把握的事情。
- "feel safe" 意味着感到安心，没有恐惧。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company installed new security systems to ensure the safety of its employees."
- 形容词: "After the storm passed, we all felt safe again."
