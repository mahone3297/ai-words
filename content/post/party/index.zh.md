## 读音
/ˈpɑːrti/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 聚会，社交活动。例如: "We're having a party this weekend."
- **名词**: 政党，政治团体。例如: "The two main parties in the country are the Democrats and Republicans."
- **动词 (v)**: 参加聚会。例如: "They decided to party all night."
- **名词**: 一方，参与者。例如: "In a contract, there are usually two parties involved."

## 短语
- "Party animal" 指喜欢参加聚会，经常狂欢的人。
- "Host a party" 主办或举办一个聚会。
- "After-party" 派对之后的聚会或续摊。

## 例句
- 名词: "Everyone was excited about the costume party."
- 动词: "They partied until the early hours of the morning."
- 名词: "The two political parties have different views on taxation."
