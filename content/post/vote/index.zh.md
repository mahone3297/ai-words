## 读音
/vəʊt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 投票；选举权；表决。例如: "Everyone above the age of 18 has the right to vote in the election."
- **动词**: 投票；表决支持。例如: "They voted in favor of the new proposal."

## 短语
- "Vote for" 支持并投票给。例如: "Please vote for your favorite candidate."
- "To abstain from voting" 不参与投票。例如: "Some people chose to abstain from voting in the referendum."

## 例句
- 名词: "The final result will depend on the number of votes cast."
- 动词: "She voted early to avoid long lines on election day."

- Cast one's ballot
- Elect
