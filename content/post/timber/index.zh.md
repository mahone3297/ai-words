## 读音
/tɪmˈbər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 木料，木材。由树砍伐后加工成的条状或板状材料，常用于建筑和家具制造。例如: "The house was built with strong timber from local forests."
- **动词**: 伐木，采伐树木以获取木材。例如: "They plan to timber the area next week for new construction projects."

## 短语
- "timber industry" 指的是木材生产和加工的工业。
- "timber frame" 是一种建筑结构，主要由木头制成的框架。
- "falling timber" 指倒下的树木，通常在伐木作业中使用。

## 例句
- 名词: "The carpenter carefully selected high-quality timber for the new table."
- 动词: "The logging company will timber the northern forest this winter."
