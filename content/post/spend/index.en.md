## Pronunciation
/spend/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Spend**: The act of using money, time, or effort on something; expenditure or consumption. For example: "The family spends a lot on vacations each year."
- **Expenditure**: The amount of money that is spent or used. For example: "The government's education expenditure has increased."

### Verb
- **To spend**: To use (money) for a particular purpose; allocate or devote time to an activity. For example: "I plan to spend my savings on a new computer."
- **To spend time**: To devote time to an activity or person. For example: "She spends her weekends reading books."

### Adverb
- **Spending**: In a manner of spending, usually indicating how one spends their time or resources. For example: "His spending habits have changed since he started working."

## Phrases
- "Spent time" or "spent the day": To have passed the time doing something.
- "Money well spent": Describes the usefulness or value of a purchase or investment.
- "Budgeting your spend": Managing and controlling your expenses.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She tracked her monthly spend to stay within her budget."
- **Verb**: "He spent his entire inheritance on a luxurious vacation."
- **Adverb**: "He spends a lot of time spending time with his family."
