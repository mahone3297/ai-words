## 读音
/biˈheɪvɪər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 行为，举动，表现。例如: "Children's behavior can be influenced by their environment."
- **名词**: (尤指不寻常或不可接受的)行为模式，举止。例如: "His aggressive behavior was a cause for concern."

## 短语
- "Good behavior" 指良好的行为或举止。
- "Bad behavior" 指不良的行为或举止。
- "Behavior modification" 是一种改变行为的心理学方法。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher praised the student for her good behavior in class."
- 名词: "The psychologist studied the behavior of animals in their natural habitat."
