## 读音
/rəˈdeɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 发射（光、热、辐射能等）。例如: "The sun radiates heat and light."
  2. 散发出；传播。例如: "Her happiness radiated throughout the room."
  3. （信息、情感等）传递，扩散。例如: "His smile radiated joy."

- **名词**:
  1. 辐射；放射物。例如: "The danger zone around the nuclear power plant is due to high levels of radiation."
  2. （建筑）辐射形结构，如辐射状的楼梯。例如: "The unique design of the building featured a radial staircase."

## 短语
- "Radiant energy" 指辐射能量，如光和热。
- "Radiation sickness" 是由辐射暴露引起的疾病。

## 例句
- 动词: "The flowers in the garden radiate a sweet fragrance."
- 名词: "The radioactive material must be handled with caution."
