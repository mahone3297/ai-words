## Pronunciation
/ˈɪnər/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Located or situated within; further towards the centre or interior. For example: "The inner door led to a small study."
  - *Psychology*: Relating to a person's deeper emotions or thoughts; private or hidden. Example: "Her innermost fears were revealed through her paintings."
- **Noun**: The inner part or parts of something, often referring to a place or thing that is surrounded by something else. Example: "The inner workings of a clock."

## Phrases
- "Inner city": The central area of a town or city, often with high population density.
- "Inner strength": A person's deep-rooted resilience or fortitude.
- "Inner peace": A state of mental or emotional calmness and serenity.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The inner walls of the castle had been reinforced."
  - "Through meditation, he found his inner balance and clarity."
- **Noun**: "The inner courtyard was filled with flowers and greenery."
  - "Exploring one's inner self can lead to personal growth."
