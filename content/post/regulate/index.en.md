## Pronunciation
/rɪˈɡʌleɪt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A regulation or set of rules governing a particular activity or system.

### Verb
1. To control or manage something, usually by setting standards or boundaries.
2. To adjust or bring something into conformity with established rules or norms.
3. To govern or administer (a company, organization, or process).

## Phrases
- "Regulate the flow" means to control the rate of something, like water or electricity.
- "Financial regulations" refers to rules governing financial institutions.
- "Air quality regulations" are measures enforced to limit pollution.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The government implemented new regulations on carbon emissions."
- **Verb**: "The company must regulate their employees' working hours to comply with labor laws."
- **Verb**: "The thermostat regulates the temperature in the room."
