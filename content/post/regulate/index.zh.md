## 读音
/ˈrɛɡjəleɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 控制或调整（机器、系统、组织等）以确保其正常运行或符合规定标准。例如: "The government regulates the financial industry to prevent fraud."
  - 制定规则或法律来管理。例如: "New regulations were introduced to protect the environment."
  - 使（行为、情绪等）有规律。例如: "She needs to regulate her sleep pattern."

## 短语
- "Regulate the temperature" 调节温度。
- "Regulate traffic flow" 管理交通流量。
- "Regulate one's breathing" 控制呼吸。

## 例句
- "The thermostat automatically regulates the room temperature."
- "The police are responsible for regulating public order during demonstrations."
- "He had to regulate his pace to match hers."
