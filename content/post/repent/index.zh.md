## 读音
/rɪˈpɛnt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 意为悔悟，懊悔，对过去的错误或罪行感到后悔并决心改正。例如: "After realizing his mistake, he repented and asked for forgiveness."
- **名词**: 在宗教语境中，指的是悔改的行为或状态。例如: "The sermon spoke of the importance of repentance in seeking redemption."

## 短语
- "Repent one's sins" 意味着承认并为自己的罪行感到懊悔。
- "To turn over a new leaf" 表示决定改过自新，通常伴随着repentance。

## 例句
- 动词: "He repented his dishonest actions and decided to make amends."
- 名词: "In the confessional, he expressed his true repentance for his misdeeds."

- Reproach
- Atone
