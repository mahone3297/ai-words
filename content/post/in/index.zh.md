## 读音
/ɪn/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示在内部，进入，加入等含义。例如: "The book is in the drawer."，"She put her coat on and went out in the rain."
- **副词**: 表示处于某种状态或情况。例如: "He was deeply in debt."，"She is very much in love with him."
- **形容词**: 表示属于或关于某个时期、风格或范围。例如: "in fashion"（流行的），"in power"（执政的）

## 短语
- "be in trouble" 处于困境或麻烦之中。
- "in addition" 除此之外，此外。
- "in time" 及时，适时。

## 例句
- 介词: "The cat is sleeping in the sun."
- 副词: "He is so in love that he can't think of anything else."
- 形容词: "The new design is very much in line with modern trends."

## 同义词
- Inside (介词)
- Within (介词)
- Into (介词)
- During (副词，表示时间)
- Amid (介词)
