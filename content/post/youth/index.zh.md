## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 青春，年轻时期。例如: "In their youth, they were full of energy and dreams."
- **名词 (n)**: 年轻人，青年人。例如: "The youth of today are more tech-savvy than previous generations."

## 短语
- "Youth movement" 指的是年轻人参与的社会或政治运动。
- "Youthful enthusiasm" 意为年轻人特有的热情和活力。

## 例句
- 名词: "The memories of my youth are filled with adventures in the countryside."
