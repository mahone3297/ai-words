## Pronunciation
/ˈjʌθ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
    - 1. The period of life between adolescence and middle age, typically considered to be between the ages of 15 and 30.
    - 2. (pl.) Young people as a group, often considered to be energetic, optimistic, and rebellious.
- **Adjective**: Relating to or characteristic of young people; youthful.

## Phrases
- "The youth of today" refers to the current generation of young people.
- "A youth movement" is a social or political movement involving young people.
- "Keep your youth" means to maintain your vitality or freshness.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She reminisced about her carefree youth spent traveling the world."
- **Adjective**: "The youth on the dance floor were filled with energy and enthusiasm."
- **Adverb**: "He was surprised by the wisdom displayed by the youth leader."

## Antonyms

## Usage Notes
- Youth is often associated with growth, exploration, and learning, and can also imply a sense of rebellion or non-conformity. Its meaning can vary depending on cultural context and historical periods.
