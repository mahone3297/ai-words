## Pronunciation
/sɪl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: (n) The ability to do something well or competently, often with ease and expertise. For example: "She has excellent painting skills."
- **Verb**: (v) To possess or display a particular ability or proficiency. For example: "He skillfully solved the problem."
- **Adjective**: (adj) Characterizing or showing a high level of proficiency or aptitude. For example: "Her skillful craftsmanship is admired."

## Phrases
- "Acquire a new skill" means to learn or develop a new ability.
- "Skillful execution" refers to the competent and precise performance of an action.
- "Skill training" is the process of improving one's abilities through practice or instruction.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She honed her cooking skills over the years."
- **Verb**: "The athlete improved their skill through rigorous practice."
- **Adjective**: "His skillful handling of the situation saved the day."
