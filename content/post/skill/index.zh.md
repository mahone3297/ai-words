## 读音
/ skɪl /

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 技能，才能，技巧。例如: "She has excellent communication skills."
- **动词 (v)**: 学会，掌握。例如: "To succeed in this job, you need to skill in using various software tools."

## 短语
- "Develop skills" 意味着培养或提高技能。
- "Skill-based learning" 强调以技能学习为主导的教学方法。
- "A skilled worker" 指具有专业技能的工人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The job requires a high level of technical skills."
- 动词: "He's skilled himself in carpentry over the years."
