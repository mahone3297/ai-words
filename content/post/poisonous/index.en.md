## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or producing harmful effects; deadly or causing illness or injury if ingested or contacted. For example: "The snake's venom was poisonous."
- **Noun**: A poisonous substance or agent. For example: "The mushroom was toxic due to its poisonous nature."

## Phrases
- "Poisonous plants" refers to those with harmful chemicals.
- "A poisonous bite" indicates a venomous sting or puncture wound.
- "Be cautious of poisonous substances" is a warning to avoid harm.
