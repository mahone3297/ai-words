## 读音
/pəˈzənəs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有毒的，含有毒的，能引起伤害或死亡的。例如: "The plant is highly poisonous and should be handled with care."
- **名词**: 毒素，毒物。例如: "The antidote was administered to counteract the effects of the poisonous substance."

## 短语
- "Poisonous gas" 指有毒气体。
- "Poisonous snake" 指毒蛇。
- "Poisonous mushrooms" 指有毒蘑菇。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Don't touch those berries; they're poisonous and can make you very sick."
- 名词: "The doctor warned us about the potential dangers of exposure to this poisonous chemical."
