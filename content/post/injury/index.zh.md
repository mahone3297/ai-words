## 读音
/ˈɪndʒəri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 身体或情感上的伤害；损害。例如: "He suffered a serious injury in the accident."
- **动词**: 使受伤；损害。例如: "Careless driving can injury not only the driver but also other road users."

## 短语
- "Injury prone" 指容易受伤的。
- "Injure oneself" 意味着不小心伤害到自己。

## 例句
- 名词: "The football player is out for the season due to a knee injury."
- 动词: "Slipping on the ice can easily injury your back."
