## Pronunciation
/ˈɪnʤəri/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: An instance of harm or damage to living tissue caused by external force, accident, infection, disease, or surgery. For example: "She suffered a serious knee injury during the game."
- **Noun (figurative)**: Damage or harm to something non-physical, such as reputation or feelings. For example: "The scandal caused a significant injury to his political career."

### Phrases
- "Personal injury": refers to physical or psychological harm inflicted on a person, often used in legal contexts.
- "Injury time": additional minutes played in a football match to compensate for time lost due to injuries or other stoppages.
- "On the injured list": a sports term referring to athletes who are unable to participate due to injury.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (physical injury)**: "After the fall, he had to receive medical treatment for multiple injuries."
- **Noun (figurative)**: "Her words were like a sharp knife, inflicting deep emotional injury."
