## Pronunciation
/rəˈpæsidli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Describing the speed or manner in which something happens, typically with regard to being fast or quickly. For example: "The car drove rapidly down the highway."
- **Adjective**: (of a process or action) occurring or developing quickly or at high speed. For example: "The infection rapidly spread through the community."

## Phrases
- "Rapidly changing times" refers to a period of significant and swift change.
- "Rapid cooling" describes a situation where something cools down quickly.
- "He recovered rapidly from his surgery."

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "The train was rapidly approaching the station."
- **Adjective**: "Technology has rapidly advanced in recent years."
- "She rapidly scanned the text to find the important information."
