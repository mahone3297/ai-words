## Pronunciation
/tʃiːtʃər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who teaches students, especially in a school or educational institution. **Example**: "The dedicated teacher inspired her students to learn."
- **Verb**: To act as a teacher, imparting knowledge or skills. **Example**: "She decided to teach her younger sibling how to play the piano."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or relating to a teacher or teaching. **Example**: "The teacher-friendly classroom had various resources for learning."

## Phrases
- "Teach a subject" means to impart knowledge on a particular topic.
- "Teacher's pet" refers to a favored student.
- "Teacher training" denotes the process of preparing individuals to become teachers.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The teacher was patient with the struggling students."
- **Verb**: "After retirement, he continued to teach part-time at a local community center."
- **Adjective**: "Her teacher-like demeanor made everyone feel comfortable."
