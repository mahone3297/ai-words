## 读音
/ˈtiːtʃər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 教师，教育工作者。例如: "She is a dedicated teacher with years of experience."
   - 2. 指导者，导师。例如: "He acted as a teacher in guiding me through the project."

## 短语
- "teach someone a lesson" 教训某人，使某人得到教训。例如: "If he keeps being rude, his parents will teach him a lesson."
- "teacher-student relationship" 师生关系。例如: "The teacher-student relationship should be based on mutual respect."

## 例句
- "My favorite teacher inspired me to pursue a career in science."
- "She spent her summer break attending a teacher training workshop."
