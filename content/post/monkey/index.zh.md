## 读音
/ˈmʌŋki/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 哺乳动物，类人猿的一种，通常有长尾巴和灵活的手指，生活在热带地区。例如: "Monkeys swing from tree to tree in the jungle."
   - 俚语或贬义：愚蠢的人或行为。例如: "Don't act like a monkey and cause trouble."

## 短语
- "Monkey around" 意为玩耍、胡闹或浪费时间。例如: "The kids were monkeying around in the playground."
- "Make a monkey out of someone" 使某人显得愚蠢或被愚弄。例如: "He made a monkey out of his opponent with his clever strategy."

## 例句
- 名词: "The baby monkey clung to its mother's back."
- 俚语： "Stop monkeying around and get back to work!"
