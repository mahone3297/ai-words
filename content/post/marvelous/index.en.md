## Pronunciation
/ˈmær.və.ləs/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Extremely good or impressive; causing wonder and admiration. 

  - Example Sentence: _The magician performed a series of marvelous tricks that left the audience astounded._
  
- **Adjective (informal)**: Used to emphasize that something is very good or enjoyable. 
   
   - Example Sentence: _We had a marvelous time at the beach yesterday._

## Phrases
- "A marvelously good idea": This phrase emphasizes that an idea is exceptionally good.
- "In a marvelous state": Refers to something being in an excellent condition or situation.
- "Marvelous achievement": Describes an accomplishment that is truly extraordinary.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: _She wore a marvelous gown that shimmered under the ballroom lights._
