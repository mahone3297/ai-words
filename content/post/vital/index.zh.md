## 读音
/ˈvaɪtəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 极其重要的，生死攸关的，至关重要的。例如: "Adequate nutrition is vital for good health."
- **名词**: 生命力，活力。例如: "The patient's vital signs were stable."

## 短语
- "vital organs" 指的是生命维持的重要器官，如心脏、大脑和肺。
- "vital statistics" 指人口统计资料，如出生率、死亡率等。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Maintaining a balanced diet is vital for athletes to perform at their best."
- 名词: "The doctor monitored her vital during the surgery."
