## Pronunciation
/vɪˈtæl/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Adjective**: Of great importance; essential; critical. For example: "Physical activity is a vital component of a healthy lifestyle."
2. **Adjective**: Indicating life or vitality; vibrant. For example: "The city's vital energy was palpable on a sunny day."

### Verb
1. To be essential or crucial for something's survival or success. For example: "This new technology is vital to the company's growth."
2. To maintain or support life or well-being. For example: "Vital signs like heart rate and blood pressure must be monitored closely."

### Adverb
Used to emphasize the importance or significance of something. For example: "Her role in the project is vitally important."

## Phrases
- "A vital organ" refers to a body part that is essential for life.
- "Vital discussions" are those that address key issues.
- "Vital statistics" include demographic data that provide insights into a population's health.
