## 读音
/spel/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 正确拼写（一个单词）。例如: "Please spell 'receive' for me."
2. 把字母逐一念出（常用于儿童学习）。例如: "Can you spell your name out loud?"
3. 在魔法或咒语中念出字词。例如: "In the story, the wizard spelled the enemies into sleep."

## 短语
- "Spell out" 意味着逐个字母念出或详细解释。例如: "Spell out your full name, please."
- "Under a spell" 指受某种魔力或强烈情绪的影响。例如: "She was under his spell and couldn't resist him."

## 例句
- 动词: "The teacher asked the students to spell 'encyclopedia'."
- "The witch cast a spell that made the prince turn into a frog."
