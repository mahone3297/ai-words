## 读音
/riːˈleɪʃnʃɪp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 人际关系，联系，关系。例如: "They have a close relationship with their neighbors."
- **名词**: 关联，相互作用。例如: "The relationship between exercise and health is well documented."

## 短语
- "Personal relationship" 指私人关系，如朋友或伴侣关系。
- "Business relationship" 指商业或职业上的联系。
- "In a relationship" 指处于恋爱关系中。

## 例句
- "The relationship between the two countries has been strained recently."
- "Building a good working relationship with colleagues is essential for success."
- "They announced that they are no longer in a relationship."
