## 读音
/ˌʌndərˈstændɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 理解，领悟，认识。例如: "She has a deep understanding of the subject."
- **动词**: 理解，明白。例如: "I don't understand what you're trying to say."

## 短语
- "Mutual understanding" 意为互相理解。
- "Lack of understanding" 表示理解不足或误解。

## 例句
- 名词: "His extensive reading has given him a broad understanding of history."
- 动词: "Can you explain it again? I'm afraid I don't fully understand."
