## Pronunciation
/dɔːn/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The period of time just before sunrise, marking the beginning of the day.
   - Definition: The start of daybreak or黎明。
   - Example: "The first light of dawn painted the sky with hues of pink and orange."

2. The appearance or start of something new or hopeful.
   - Definition: The initial stage or inception of a situation, idea, or project.
   - Example: "After a long night, a fresh dawn of opportunity awaited him."

### Verb
1. To begin or come into view at the break of day.
   - Definition: To signify the start of something, often figuratively.
   - Example: "Dawn broke over the mountains, signaling the start of a new day."

2. To indicate the start or the beginning of a process or event.
   - Definition: To usher in or initiate a new phase.
   - Example: "Dawning awareness of climate change led to policy changes."

## Phrases
- "Watch the dawn break" implies witnessing the sunrise.
- "A new dawn of innovation" signifies a promising beginning in a field.
- "Dawn's early light" describes the first light of morning.
