## 读音
/ˈjuː.tɪ.lə.ti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 实用性，功效，效用。例如: "The main utility of this tool is its ability to save time."
- **名词**: 公用事业，公共设施。例如: "The city's utility company provides electricity and water services."
- **形容词**: 实用的，有用的。例如: "This kitchen gadget is quite utility for preparing meals."

## 短语
- "Household utilities" 指家庭的公用设施，如电、气和水。
- "Public utilities" 涵盖所有为公众提供服务的基础设施。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new software has greatly increased the utility of our computer systems."
- 形容词: "The car's utility features make it perfect for camping trips."
