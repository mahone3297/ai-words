## 读音
/niːd/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 需要，必须有。例如: "I need your help with this problem."
    - 想要，渴望。例如: "She needs some time alone to relax."

- **名词**:
    - 需求，要求。例如: "The project has a pressing need for more funding."
    - 必要性，必需品。例如: "Food and shelter are basic human needs."

## 短语
- "in need of" 表示需要或缺乏。例如: "The old building is in need of repair."
- "meet one's needs" 满足某人的需求。例如: "This new plan should meet all our needs."

## 例句
- 动词: "We need to finish this task by tomorrow."
- 名词: "The company identified the need for better communication."
