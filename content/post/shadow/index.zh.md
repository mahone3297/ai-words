## 读音
/shædəʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 阴影，影子。例如: "The tree cast a long shadow on the ground."
- **名词**: 影响，影响的痕迹。例如: "His absence left a shadow over the celebration."
- **动词**: 跟踪，监视。例如: "The detective was shadowing the suspect."
- **形容词**: 阴暗的，不明确的。例如: "A shadowy figure emerged from the darkness."

## 短语
- "in the shadow of" 表示在...的阴影下，比喻受其影响。例如: "The small town lived in the shadow of the big city."
- "cast a shadow" 引起不快或担忧。例如: "Her illness cast a shadow over the family's holiday plans."

## 例句
- 名词: "The moon created eerie shadows on the ground during the full moon night."
- 动词: "The private investigator was hired to shadow the businessman."
- 形容词: "The story has a shadowy ending, leaving readers guessing."
