## 读音
/meɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 肉；肉类：指动物的可食用部分，通常指哺乳动物的肌肉组织。例如: "She is a vegetarian and doesn't eat any meat."
  - 食物的核心或精华：比喻指事物的关键或最有价值的部分。例如: "The meat of the matter is that we need to find a solution quickly."

## 短语
- "Red meat" 指牛肉、羊肉和猪肉等颜色鲜红的肉类。
- "White meat" 主要指鸡肉和火鸡肉等颜色较浅的肉类。
- "Meat and potatoes" 用来形容基本的、重要的事物或饮食习惯。

## 例句
- 名词: "We're having chicken as the main course for dinner tonight, which is considered white meat."
- "In some cultures, red meat is considered a luxury and reserved for special occasions."

## 同义词
- Flesh
- Protein
- Carnivore's food
- Mutton, beef, pork (specific types of meat)
