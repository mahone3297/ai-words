## 读音
/rɪˈsɛnt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 近期的，最近的。例如: "I just read a recent article on climate change."
- **副词**: 最近，不久前。例如: "She recently moved to a new apartment."

## 短语
- "Recent past" 指的是不久之前的时间段。
- "Recent developments" 意味着最新的进展或变化。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The recent increase in temperature has been quite noticeable."
- 副词: "He recently started learning a new language."
