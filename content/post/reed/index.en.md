## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A tall grass plant with long, slender stems, often used to make musical instruments (reed pipes) or as a material in baskets and other woven items. Example: "The wind blew through the reeds by the riverbank."
- **Verb**: To use a reed as a musical instrument, especially to produce sound by blowing air across it. Example: "She learned to play the clarinet by blowing on the reed."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of a reed, having a flexible and resilient nature. Example: "The reed-like texture of the fabric gives it a unique look."

## Phrases
- "Play a reed instrument" refers to playing a musical instrument like a clarinet or saxophone.
- "Cut a reed" means to prepare a new reed for a wind instrument by trimming its ends.
- "Rustle of reeds" describes the sound made by moving or swaying reeds.
