## 读音
/riːd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 芦苇，一种高大的禾本科植物，常用于制作乐器的笛管或建筑材料。例如: "The reeds by the riverbank provided shelter for small animals."
   2. (尤指长形的)管乐器，如芦笛或萨克斯管。例如: "He played a beautiful melody on his reed instrument."

- **动词**: 使用芦苇或类似物制成的工具或产品。例如: "The craftsman reeds the thatched roof with skill."

## 短语
- "to play a reed instrument" 意为吹奏管乐器。
- "reeds swaying in the breeze" 形容芦苇在风中摇曳的景象。

## 例句
- 名词: "The ducks were nesting among the tall reeds."
- 动词: "She learned how to reed a bamboo flute from her grandfather."
