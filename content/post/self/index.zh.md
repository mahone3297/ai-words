## 读音
/self/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  自我，自己，自身。指个人的意识、个性或身份。例如: "She needs time to focus on herself and her own needs."
- **反身代词**:
  用作代词，表示动作返回到主语本身。例如: "I taught myself how to play the guitar."

## 短语
- "be true to oneself" 意为忠于自己。
- "take care of oneself" 意思是照顾自己。
- "one's better self" 通常指一个人更好的或高尚的一面。

## 例句
- 名词: "He discovered a new side of himself during his travels."
- 反身代词: "She managed to finish the project all by herself."
