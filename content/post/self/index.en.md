## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The reflexive form of the pronoun 'I', referring to oneself. For example: "She was proud of her self-improvement."
   2. An essential part of a person's identity, character, or consciousness. For example: "She had a strong sense of self-confidence."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing oneself; self-referential. For example: "The self-driven approach to learning is beneficial."
- **Pronoun**: Referring back to oneself. For example: "He asked himself what he should do next."

## Phrases
- "Know thyself" is a famous philosophical maxim emphasizing self-awareness.
- "Self-care" is the practice of taking care of one's mental and physical well-being.
- "Selflessness" is the quality of being unselfish or disinterested in one's own gain.
