## 读音
/ˈsɪriəsli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 严肃地，认真地，诚实地。例如: "I'm being serious, this is important."
- **形容词**: 严重的，重要的，认真的。例如: "The situation is getting seriously worrying."

## 短语
- "Take something seriously" 意为认真对待某事。
- "In all seriousness" 用于强调即将说的话是认真的。

## 例句
- 副词: "When I talk about climate change, I'm seriously concerned."
- 形容词: "The illness became so serious that he had to be hospitalized."
