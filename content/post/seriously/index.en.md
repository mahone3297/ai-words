## Pronunciation
/səˈriəsli/

## Definitions
### Adverb
- **1** : in a serious manner; especially : with gravity or concern : gravely
  - He spoke seriously about the upcoming election.
- **2** : to a significant extent or degree : notably
  - This matter deserves to be taken seriously.

## Phrases
- "Take something seriously" implies giving it importance or considering it carefully.
- "Be serious" is used to request someone to be more attentive or not joke around.
- "Not to be taken seriously" means something is meant to be humorous or not to be considered literally.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: She listened to his concerns seriously, offering her advice.
- **Adjective**: The situation required that they be treated with seriousness.
- **Adverb**: After the accident, people began to take drunk driving seriously.
