## Pronunciation
/ˈlɔɪər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who is trained and licensed to practice law, advise clients on legal matters, and represent them in court. For example: "She hired a lawyer to handle her divorce proceedings."
- **Noun (Informal)**: A person who argues or debates tenaciously. Example: "In any discussion, he's the lawyer for the opposing side, always ready with counterarguments."

## Phrases
- "Lawyer up" means to hire or consult with a lawyer, especially when facing legal trouble. Example: "When the investigation started, he quickly lawyered up."
- "To act as someone's lawyer" implies representing someone legally or advocating for them. Example: "Throughout the case, she acted as his lawyer, fighting tirelessly for his rights."
  
## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The defendant was advised by his lawyer to accept the plea bargain."
- **Noun (Informal)**: "She can be quite convincing; she's like a lawyer in defending her opinions."

## Synonyms
- Attorney
- Solicitor
- Barrister
- Legal counsel
- Advocate
- Counselor (American English) / Counsellor (British English)
