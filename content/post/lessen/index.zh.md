## 读音
/ˈlɛsən/

## 释义
- **动词**: 减少，减轻。例如: "Taking regular breaks can lessen work-related stress."
- **名词**: (尤指正式的)课程，一节课。例如: "The teacher prepared for her English lesson."

## 短语
- "Lessen the burden" 减轻负担
- "To lessen one's workload" 减轻工作量

## 例句
- 动词: "A good night's sleep will greatly lessen your fatigue."
- 名词: "The students were given a new lesson on grammar."

## 同义词
- Reduce
- Diminish
- Minimize
- Alleviate
- Decrease
