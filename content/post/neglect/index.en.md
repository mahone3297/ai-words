## Pronunciation
/ˈnɛɡlɛkt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The action or state of not giving enough care, attention, or effort to something that needs it; disregard, omission, or lack of care. Example: "The garden was a victim of neglect."
- **Verb**: To fail to give proper care, attention, or consideration to someone or something. Example: "She neglected her health while focusing on her career."

### Phrases
- "Child neglect" refers to the failure of a parent or guardian to provide adequate care for a child's physical, emotional, medical, or educational needs.
- "Neglect of duty" implies the failure to fulfill one's responsibilities or obligations.
- "Willful neglect" suggests intentional disregard or indifference.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Years of neglect had left the old mansion in ruins."
- **Verb**: "He neglected to water the plants, which led to their withering."
