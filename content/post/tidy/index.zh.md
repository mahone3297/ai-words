## 读音
/tɪˈdaɪ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 整齐的，有序的。例如: "She likes to keep her room tidy."
- **动词**: 整理，收拾。例如: "I need to tidy up the living room before guests arrive."
- **名词**: 整理，收拾的过程或结果。例如: "After the party, we had a lot of tidying to do."

## 短语
- "Tidy up" 意味着把东西整理好，使地方变得整洁。
- "Tidy away" 意味着把东西放回原处或藏起来，以保持整洁。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The desk was so tidy that you could find anything easily."
- 动词: "Could you please tidy your toys before bedtime?"
- 名词: "The house looked much better after a good tidy."
