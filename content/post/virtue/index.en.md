## Pronunciation
/ˈvɜːrtjuː/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A moral excellence or a positive quality that is considered to be good and worthy of admiration, often associated with moral principles or character traits. For example: "Honesty is a fundamental virtue."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or showing moral excellence; praiseworthy. For example: "Her virtue was her strength in difficult times."
- **Verb**: To practice or exhibit a particular virtue, or to possess a strong sense of it. For example: "She strives to cultivate patience as a virtue."
