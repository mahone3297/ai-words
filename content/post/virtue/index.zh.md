## 读音
/vərˈtʃuːt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 品德，美德；优点，长处。例如: "She is known for her virtues of honesty and kindness."
- **名词**: (某种)道德规范，德行。例如: "One of the cardinal virtues is courage."
- **名词**: 价值，效用。例如: "The virtue of hard work is often emphasized in success stories."

## 短语
- "Virtue signaling" 指在公开场合表达高尚道德立场以获得赞誉的行为。
- "The seven virtues" 包括谦逊、节制、慈善、耐心、勤勉、宽容和贞洁。

## 例句
- 名词: "His integrity is one of his most admirable virtues."
- 名词: "The virtues of regular exercise are widely recognized."
- 名词: "In ancient Greece, wisdom was considered a virtue."
