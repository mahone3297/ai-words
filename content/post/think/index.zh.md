## 读音
/θɪŋk/

## 释义
- **动词**: 形成想法或意见；思考；认为。例如: "I need some time to think about your proposal." (我需要一些时间来考虑你的提议。)
- **名词**: 想法，思考，观念。例如: "He shared his thoughts on the subject." (他分享了他对这个问题的看法。)

## 短语
- "Think twice" 意为三思，慎重考虑。例如: "Before making a decision, always think twice."
- "Think out of the box" 指创新思考，跳出常规。例如: "To solve this problem, we need to think out of the box."

## 例句
- 动词: "She's thinking about going back to school for her master's degree."
- 名词: "His think was that the project was too risky."
