## 读音
/səˈspekt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 怀疑的人；嫌疑犯。例如: "The police have a suspect in custody."
- **动词**: 怀疑；认为可能。例如: "She suspects he might be lying."
- **形容词**: 可疑的；令人怀疑的。例如: "There were suspect activities going on late at night."

## 短语
- "to suspect someone of something" 意思是怀疑某人做了某事。
- "suspected criminal" 指被怀疑犯罪的人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The detective had a prime suspect in the case."
- 动词: "I suspect that our dog ate the missing cake."
- 形容词: "The package looked suspicious, so I didn't open it."
