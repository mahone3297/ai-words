## 读音
/ɪnˈdɪɡ.nənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 愤慨的，义愤的，愤愤不平的。表示对不公正或令人不满的情况感到愤怒和不满。

## 短语
- "feeling indignant about something" 意为对某事感到愤慨。
- "to show one's indignant anger" 表现出愤慨的愤怒。

## 例句
- "She looked at him with indignant eyes when he accused her of lying."
- "The public expressed their indignant outrage over the unfair treatment."

## 同义词
- Angry
- Furious
- Incensed
- Outraged
- Displeased
