## 读音
/mjuˈzɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 艺术形式，通过声音组织成有美感和表达力的组合。例如: "She loves listening to classical music."
- **名词**: 音乐作品，歌曲或乐曲。例如: "The new album by the band has some great music."

## 短语
- "背景音乐" refers to music played in the background of a film, show, or event.
- "流行音乐" means music that is popular among a wide audience.

## 例句
- 名词: "The soothing music helped him relax after a long day."
- 名词: "He plays music from various genres, including jazz and rock."
