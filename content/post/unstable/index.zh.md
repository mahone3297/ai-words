## 读音
/ʌnˈsteɪbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不稳定；不牢固；易变的。例如: "The economy is in an unstable state."
- **副词**: 不稳定地；摇摆不定地。例如: "The ladder stood unstable on the uneven ground."

## 短语
- "an unstable situation" 指的是不稳定的情况或环境。
- "unstable elements" 可以指不稳定的元素，如化学元素或政治局势中的不稳定因素。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The weather is highly unstable, with frequent thunderstorms."
- 副词: "The building was constructed on such unstable soil that it began to tilt."

## 同义词
- Insecure
- Unreliable
- Volatile
- Shaky
- Unstable can also be synonymous with terms like changeable or unpredictable in certain contexts.
