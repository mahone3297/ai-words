## 读音
/ræŋk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 等级，级别：A position or level in a hierarchical system; a classification.
  - 军衔，军阶：A position in the military hierarchy.
  - 队列，行列：A line of people or things arranged in order.

- **动词**:
  - 排列，分类：To arrange in a row or series; to classify.
  - 给予军衔：To assign a military rank to someone.
  - 在等级中位居…：To occupy a particular position in a ranking or scale.

- **形容词**:
  - 等级的，级别的：Relating to or denoting a position in a hierarchy or classification.
  - 权威的，有威信的：Having authority or influence.

## 短语
- "rank and file" 指普通士兵或组织内的普通成员。
- "rank up" 在游戏或军事中提升等级或军衔。
- "rank with" 把…与…相提并论，视为同等重要。

## 例句
- 名词: "He was promoted to the rank of colonel."
- 动词: "The new recruits will rank according to their performance."
- 形容词: "He held a rank position in the company, but had little real power."
