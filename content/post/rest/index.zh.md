## 读音
/rest/

## 释义
- **名词**: 休息，宁静，安宁。例如: "I need some rest after a long day of work."
- **动词**: 休息，倚靠，搁置。例如: "She rested her head on the pillow."
- **形容词**: 剩余的，其余的。例如: "There is still some rest meat in the fridge."
- **副词**: 其余，剩下的。例如: "The rest of the guests have already left."

## 短语
- "At rest" 表示静止或安息的状态。
- "In the rest" 指的是其他剩余的部分。
- "Take a rest" 意味着暂时停止活动以恢复体力。

## 例句
- 名词: "After a hard day's work, all I want is a good night's rest."
- 动词: "He leaned against the wall and rested his eyes for a moment."
- 形容词: "Could you pass me the rest of the bread?"
- 副词: "She finished her homework, and the rest of the evening was free for leisure."

## 同义词
- Relaxation
- Repose
- Leisure
