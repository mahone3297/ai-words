## Pronunciation
/rest/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
  - 1. The remaining part or quantity after something else has been taken or used; remainder. Example: "After eating dinner, there was still some food left in the plate."
  - 2. A period of rest or relaxation. Example: "We took a rest after hiking for several hours."
  - 3. The state of being free from work or activity. Example: "She enjoyed the peaceful rest of her weekend."
  - 4. (Physics) The state of an object at complete stop or without motion relative to its surroundings. Example: "The ball came to rest on the ground."

- **Verb**: To pause or cease active movement, often for recuperation or relaxation. Example: "The children rested after playing all day."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or relating to rest or repose. Example: "A restful vacation allowed them to recharge."

## Phrases
- "Take a rest" means to pause and relax.
- "Get some rest" is an expression used to advise someone to sleep or recover.
- "Rest and recuperate" implies allowing time for recovery from illness or exertion.
