## 读音
/nuː/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 新的；未被使用过的。例如: "I bought a new car yesterday."
- **副词**: 刚刚；最近。例如: "She just moved into a new apartment."

## 短语
- "Start anew" 意为重新开始。
- "Something brand new" 指的是全新的事物。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The store has a collection of new fashion designs."
- 副词: "We've just received a shipment of new books."

## 同义词
- Fresh
- Recent
- Novel
- Unused
- Up-to-date
