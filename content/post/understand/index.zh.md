## 读音
/ˌʌndərˈstænd/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 理解，明白。例如: "I need you to understand the importance of this task."
  - 接受（想法、观点等）。例如: "She understands and agrees with our proposal."

## 短语
- "to get the wrong end of the stick" 误解了情况。
- "to fully understand something" 完全理解某事。

## 例句
- "Please make sure you understand the instructions before starting the experiment."
- "He seemed to understand my feelings without me saying a word."
- "The teacher explained the concept again until all the students understood."
