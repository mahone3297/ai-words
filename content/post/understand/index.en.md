## Pronunciation
/ˈʌndərˌstænd/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: 
  - **Noun**: The act or ability to comprehend or grasp the meaning of something, often involving mental effort. For example: "She has a good understanding of complex concepts."
  - **Verb**: To perceive or interpret (information, instructions, etc.). For example: "I understand that you need help with the project."
  - **Noun**: A level of familiarity or knowledge about a subject. For example: "He has a deep understanding of history."

## Phrases
- "Understand something" means to comprehend or grasp the meaning of it.
- "Misunderstanding" refers to a wrong or incorrect interpretation.
- "To have an understanding with someone" implies agreement or mutual comprehension.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "Can you please explain it to me so I can understand?"
- **Noun**: "Her understanding of the situation helped her make a wise decision."
- **Adjective**: "He showed a remarkable understanding of the customer's needs."
