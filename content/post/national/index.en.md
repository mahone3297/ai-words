## Word: National

## Pronunciation
/ˈnæʃənəl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to or characteristic of a nation; common to or shared by a whole nation. For example: "The national anthem unites people from all regions of the country."
  - _National identity_ refers to the sense of belonging and attachment individuals have to their country.
  - _National flag_ is a symbol that represents a nation.

- **Noun**: A citizen of a particular nation. For example: "All nationals are entitled to certain rights under the constitution."

## Phrases
- _National pride_ refers to the collective feeling of honor and respect for one's own country.
- _National holiday_ is a day celebrated officially across the entire nation.
- _National security_ involves protecting a nation from internal and external threats.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The national team will be representing our country in the World Cup this year."
- **Noun**: "All nationals must carry a valid passport when traveling abroad."

## Synonyms
- **Adjective**: Patriotic, nationwide, federal, domestic
- **Noun**: Citizen, subject, countryman/woman

### Note on Usage
As a noun, 'national' often implies legal status or citizenship rather than just residency in a country. When used as an adjective, it can describe anything that pertains to or originates from a nation, such as national cuisine, national dress, or national policy.
