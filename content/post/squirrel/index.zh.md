## 读音
/ˈskwɜːrəl/

## 释义
- **名词 n**: 一种小型哺乳动物，属于松鼠科，通常有灰色、棕色或黑色的毛皮，长尾巴，善于攀爬树木，以坚果、种子和果实为食。例如: "The squirrel was busy gathering nuts for the winter."
- **动词 v** (非标准用法，偶尔在口语中使用): 指储存食物，尤指松鼠的行为。例如: "She's squirreling away money for a rainy day."

## 短语
- "squirrel away" 存储或藏起（尤指为以后使用）。例如: "She squirreled away some money in a secret drawer."
- "red squirrel" 红松鼠，一种特别的松鼠品种，毛色通常为红色。例如: "In some parts of Europe, the red squirrel is endangered."

## 例句
- 名词: "The squirrel scampered up the tree, clutching an acorn in its paws."
- 动词: "He started squirreling food away in his cheeks before descending the tree."
