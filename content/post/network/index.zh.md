## 读音
/ˈnɛtwɜːrk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一组相互连接的设备，通过电子方式通信。例如: "The internet is a global network connecting millions of computers."
  - 人际交往的群体或系统。例如: "She has an extensive network of friends and colleagues."

- **动词**:
  - 建立或加入一个网络。例如: "Companies often network to share resources and information."
  - 在社交活动中建立联系。例如: "He was busy networking at the conference."

## 短语
- "Business network" 指商业领域的人际关系网。
- "Social network" 指社交媒体平台，如Facebook或Twitter。
- "Broadcast network" 指电视或广播的频道系统。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new office building has a state-of-the-art computer network."
- 动词: "They networked with other professionals in their field to expand their business."
