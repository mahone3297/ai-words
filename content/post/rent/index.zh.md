## 读音
/rent/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 租金；租费。例如: "She pays $500 in rent each month."
   - 出租；租赁。例如: "The car company offers short-term rent options."

- **动词**:
   - 租用；租。例如: "I'm going to rent a bike for the weekend."
   - 出租（房屋、车辆等）。例如: "He decided to rent out his spare room on Airbnb."

## 短语
- "Rent a movie" 意为租电影观看。
- "Pay rent" 指支付租金。
- "Rent control" 是对租金水平进行政府管制的政策。

## 例句
- 名词: "Their rent includes utilities such as electricity and water."
- 动词: "I'm renting a small apartment near the city center."
- 作为动词的短语: "She rents out her beach house during the summer season."

## 同义词
- Lease
- Hire
- Borrow (for a fee)
- Let
- Sublet
