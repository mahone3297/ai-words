## 读音
/rʌsti/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 
    - (铁锈的)生锈的；覆盖着铁锈的。例如: "The old bike was rusty from years of neglect."
    - (技能、知识等)生疏的，不熟练的。例如: "After a long break, his tennis skills were a bit rusty."

## 短语
- "get rusty" 意味着变得生疏或不熟练。
- "rusty on something" 表示对某个主题或技能不熟悉。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The lock was so rusty that it took me ages to open it."
- "After not driving for months, I felt a bit rusty behind the wheel."

## 同义词
- Dilapidated (破旧的)
- Unpracticed (不熟练的)
- Unfamiliar (不熟悉的)
- Deteriorated (退化的)
- Obsolete (过时的)
