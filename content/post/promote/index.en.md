## Pronunciation
/proməʊt/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: 
  - **Noun**: The act of advancing someone or something to a higher position, level, or rank; promotion. For example: "The manager promoted her assistant to a management role."
  - **Verb**: To bring forward, advertise, or make known; to raise public awareness or interest in. For example: "They promoted their new product through social media."
  - **Verb**: To encourage or stimulate (a person's progress or development). For example: "The teacher promotes critical thinking in her students."

## Phrases
- "Promote a cause" to support or advance a particular idea or issue.
- "Promotion campaign" a series of actions to increase visibility or sales.
- "Promote from within" to select and advance employees from within the company.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After five years of hard work, he finally received a promotion at the company."
- **Verb**: "The company is planning a major promotion for its new line of eco-friendly products."
- **Verb**: "Regular exercise and a balanced diet can promote overall health."
