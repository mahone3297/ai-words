## 读音
/prəˈmōt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 提升；提拔：例如 "He was promoted to the position of manager."
2. 促进；推动：例如 "The event is designed to promote local artists."
3. 推销；促销：例如 "The company is promoting its new product with a big advertising campaign."

- **名词**:
1. 促销活动：例如 "There's a special promotion on laptops this week."
2. 提升；晋级：例如 "She received a promotion after her hard work."

## 短语
- "Promote someone to" 用来表示提升某人的职位或级别。例如 "They decided to promote her to senior designer."
- "Promotion campaign" 指为推广产品或服务所做的宣传活动。

## 例句
- 动词: "The company decided to promote John to the head of the sales department."
- 名词: "The new movie has an aggressive promotion campaign on social media."
