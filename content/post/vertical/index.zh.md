## 读音
/ˈvɜːrtɪkl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 垂直的；与地平线成90度角的；竖直的。例如: "The ladder is positioned vertically against the wall."
- **名词**: 垂直方向；垂直线。例如: "The building has several vertical structures that catch the eye."

## 短语
- "vertical alignment" 意为垂直对齐。
- "vertical integration" 指在产业链中的上下合并或控制。
- "vertical market" 是指特定行业或细分市场的垂直领域。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The plant grows straight up, maintaining a vertical posture."
- 名词: "In architecture, the use of vertical lines can create an illusion of height."

## 同义词
- Upright
- Perpendicular
- Plumb
- Straight up
- Parallel (反义词，表示与地面平行)
