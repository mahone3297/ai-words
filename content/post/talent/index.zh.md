## 读音
/tælənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 才能，天赋。指个人在某方面的特殊能力或技能。例如: "She has a great talent for painting."
- **名词**: 人才，有才能的人。例如: "The company is always on the lookout for new talent."
- **动词**: 把...视为有才能的人。例如: "The director talents him as a leading actor."

## 短语
- "talent show" 才艺展示，一种展示个人才能的比赛或表演。
- "talented in something" 在某方面有天赋。例如: "She's incredibly talented in music."

## 例句
- 名词: "His talent for solving complex problems earned him a promotion."
- 动词: "The scout talents young athletes with potential for professional sports."
