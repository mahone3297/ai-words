## Pronunciation
/truːlɪ/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a truthful manner; genuinely or completely. For example: "She truly believes in her cause."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by truthfulness or sincerity; genuine. For example: "His apology was truly heartfelt."
- **Adverb**: To an extent that is real or actual; fully. For example: "The weather was truly beautiful that day."

## Phrases
- "Speak truly" means to express one's thoughts honestly.
- "Truly remarkable" is used to describe something extraordinary.
- "Truly sorry" expresses genuine regret.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "She truly enjoys spending time with her family."
- **Adjective**: "He told a truly inspiring story about his journey."
