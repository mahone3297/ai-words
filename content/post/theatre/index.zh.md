## 读音
/ˈθɪətər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 电影院或剧院，用于表演戏剧、音乐会或电影的地方。例如: "We're going to the theatre to watch a play."
   - 戏剧艺术或表演艺术。例如: "She's studying drama at a prestigious theatre school."
   - 剧场式的环境或场景，常用于比喻。例如: "The meeting turned into a real theatre when the CEO entered."

## 短语
- "Live theatre" 指现场表演的戏剧。
- "Theatrical performance" 指在舞台上进行的表演。
- "Theatre district" 通常指的是城市中集中了多个剧院的区域。

## 例句
- 名词: "We have tickets for the opening night at the West End theatre."
- "The actors rehearsed their lines in the backstage of the theatre."

- Opera house
