## 读音
/prəˈnaʊn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 代词的一种，用来代替名词或名词短语，以避免重复或保持语言流畅。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) [pronoun](/zh/post/pronoun/) '[they](/zh/post/they/)' refers [to](/zh/post/to/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [group](/zh/post/group/) [of](/zh/post/of/) [people](/zh/post/people/) [we](/zh/post/we/) met earlier."

## 短语
- "[Personal](/zh/post/personal/) pronouns" 包括I, [you](/zh/post/you/), [he](/zh/post/he/), [she](/zh/post/she/), [it](/zh/post/it/), [we](/zh/post/we/), [and](/zh/post/and/) [they](/zh/post/they/)，分别代表不同的人称。
- "[Relative](/zh/post/relative/) pronouns" 如who, [whom](/zh/post/whom/), [whose](/zh/post/whose/), [which](/zh/post/which/), that用于引导定语从句。
