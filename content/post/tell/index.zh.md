## 读音
/tel/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 告诉；讲述：例如: "Please tell me a story."
  - 通知；告知：例如: "I'll tell you when it's time to leave."
  - 计数；识别：例如: "She can tell one coin from another easily."
  - (打赌时)喊出（数目）：例如: "He told the dealer he had a pair of aces."

## 短语
- "Tell the truth" 意味着说实话。
- "Tell someone off" 表示责备或训斥某人。
- "Tell the difference between" 指区分两者。

## 例句
- 动词: "Can you tell me how to get to the nearest library?"
- "Don't tell anyone about our secret plan."
- "He could tell by her face that something was wrong."

- Communicate
- Speak up
