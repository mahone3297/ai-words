## 读音
/səkˈsɛs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 成功；成就；胜利。例如: "She finally achieved her goal and considered it a great success."
- **形容词**: 成功的；有成效的。例如: "The new product launch was a success, with high sales in the first month."

## 短语
- "to have success in something" 在某事上取得成功。
- "to be a success" 成为一个成功的人或事物。
- "to make a success of something" 把某事做得非常成功。

## 例句
- 名词: "His hard work paid off, and he is now considered a success in his field."
- 形容词: "The event was a huge success, attracting thousands of visitors."
