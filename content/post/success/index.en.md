## Definitions
### Noun
- **Success**: 1. The achievement of a desired or planned outcome; favorable result. 2. A person or thing that achieves such results; a winner. 3. The act of being successful or accomplishing something.

### Verb
- **To succeed**: To achieve a goal or objective; attain a level of excellence or fulfillment.
