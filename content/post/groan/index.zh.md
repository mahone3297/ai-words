## 读音
/ɡroʊən/

## 释义
- **动词**: 发出呻吟声，常表示痛苦、不满或劳累。例如: "He groaned in pain when he twisted his ankle."
- **名词**: 呻吟声；抱怨声。例如: "I could hear her soft groans from the other room."

## 短语
- "to groan in protest" 表示以呻吟声表示反对或不满。
- "groans of exhaustion" 指因疲惫而发出的呻吟。

## 例句
- 动词: "The old man groaned as he tried to lift the heavy box."
- 名词: "The children's groans of boredom filled the car during the long journey."

## 同义词
- Moan
- Grumble
- Whine
- Complain
- Sigh
