## 读音
/weɪk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 将某人唤醒或使某人醒来。例如: "I woke up early this morning."
  - 使意识到或认识到。例如: "The news woke us to the seriousness of the problem."
  - 使活跃或振作起来。例如: "The music woke the sleeping city."

- **名词**:
  - 睡醒后的一段时间。例如: "After a good night's sleep, I feel fresh and ready for the day."
  - 船行过后的波纹。例如: "The boat left a long wake behind it in the water."
  - （逝者）葬礼后的聚会。例如: "There will be a wake at the church after the funeral."

## 短语
- "wake up" 唤醒或起床。例如: "Don't forget to set your alarm so you can wake up on time."
- "in someone's wake" 在某人的影响或后果之后。例如: "The new policy was introduced in the wake of the financial crisis."

## 例句
- 动词: "The sound of the alarm woke me from my deep sleep."
- 名词: "We stayed at the family home for the wake, sharing stories and memories."
