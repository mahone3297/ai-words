## 读音
/ɪnfərˈmeɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 数据、事实或知识，通常以沟通或传播的形式存在。例如: "She provided us with detailed information about the project."
- **动词 (v)**: 告知，提供信息。例如: "The officer informed us that the road was closed."

## 短语
- "Information technology" 指的是处理和使用信息的系统和设备。
- "To seek information" 是指寻找或询问信息。
- "Dissemination of information" 意味着广泛传播信息。

## 例句
- 名词: "The website contains a wealth of information on health and wellness."
- 动词: "The librarian informed visitors about the library's opening hours.
