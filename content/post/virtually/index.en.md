## Pronunciation
/vəˈrɪtʃuəli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a virtual manner; nearly or almost, but not quite actual or real; by means of technology or simulation.
- **Adjective**: Essentially; nearly; almost; to a great extent.

## Phrases
- " Virtually all" means almost all.
- "Virtual meeting" refers to a conference or gathering conducted online.
- "Virtually unattainable" describes something that is nearly impossible to achieve.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "Due to the pandemic, we're holding our seminar virtually this year."
- **Adjective**: "The performance was so realistic that it felt virtually like being there."
- "Virtually every student has access to high-speed internet at home."

- Nearly
- Almost
- Essentially
- Effectively
- Mostly

## Antonyms
- Actually
- Physically
- Concretely
