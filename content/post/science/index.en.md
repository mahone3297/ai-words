## Pronunciation
/sciˈnsiː/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The systematic study of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment, aiming to discover underlying laws and principles. 2. Knowledge or facts obtained by studying such phenomena. 3. A branch of knowledge dealing with a particular aspect of the natural world (e.g., physics, biology, chemistry).
- **Verb**: To engage in scientific research, study, or inquiry.
- **Adjective**: Characterized by the use of scientific methods or principles.
