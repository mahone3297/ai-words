## 读音
/rɪˈpɔːrtər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 记者，新闻报道者。例如: "The reporter covered the event for the local newspaper."
   2. 报告员，播报员。例如: "She works as a sports reporter for the TV station."

## 短语
- "news reporter" 新闻记者
- "crime reporter" 刑事记者
- "weather reporter" 天气预报员

## 例句
- 名词: "The experienced reporter was sent to cover the war in Ukraine."
- "The weather reporter predicted heavy rain for the weekend."

## 同义词
- journalist
- correspondent
- writer
- editor
- anchor (in the context of TV news)
