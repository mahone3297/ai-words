## 读音
/wɔːrn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 警告，告诫。例如: "The teacher warned the students not to cheat on the exam."
  - 预告，预报。例如: "The weather forecast warns of possible storms later today."

## 短语
- "Give someone a warning" 意味着提醒某人即将发生的不好的事情。
- "Warn against something" 是警告某人不要做某事。
- "Warn off" 是劝阻某人离开或不做某事。

## 例句
- "The police warned the driver to slow down or face a ticket."
- "The doctor warned him about the potential side effects of the medication."
- "The sign warns swimmers of strong currents in the area."
