## 读音
/ˈspɪfɪˌkaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 具体说明；详细描述：例如: "Please specify your requirements for the project."
  2. 指定；确定：例如: "The contract specifies that work must be completed by December 31st."

## 短语
- "to be specified" 意为待确定或需要进一步说明。
- "specify exactly" 意为精确地指出或明确说明。

## 例句
- "In your proposal, you need to specify the budget and timeline for each task."
- "The doctor specified that I should take two pills daily after meals."
- "The instructions say to use a specific brand of paint for best results."
