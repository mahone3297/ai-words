## Pronunciation
/səˈspɪsəi/

## Definitions
### Noun
A noun form of the verb, referring to:
- The act of giving precise or detailed information about something.
- A particular instance or item specified.
- [Computing] A command-line option that requires a value to be provided.

### Verb
To give a clear and exact definition or description of; state specifically.
- "Please specify your dietary requirements for the meal."

### Adjective
1. Detailed or particular.
- "The instructions were very specific."
2. Demanding exactness or precision.
- "He requested a specification for the project."

## Phrases
- "Provide a detailed specification"
- "I need you to specify your preferences"
- "The design needs to be finalized with all the necessary specifications"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She asked me to specify the color and size of the car I wanted."
- **Verb**: "Before we begin, let's specify our meeting location."
- **Adjective**: "The project manager insisted on a highly specific timeline."
