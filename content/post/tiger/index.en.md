## Definitions

- A large, carnivorous feline with distinctive orange fur, black stripes, and a powerful build. It is often considered the king of the jungle. Example: "The tiger prowled through its enclosure at the zoo."
- A person or thing that is powerful, fierce, or dominant, often in a particular field or area. Example: "She's a tiger on the dance floor."

### Verb
- To attack or display dominance over something or someone. Example: "The team is known for tigering their opponents in every match."
- To emulate the qualities or performance of a tiger, especially in a competitive setting. Example: "He tigers his studies to maintain top grades."
