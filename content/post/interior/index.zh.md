## 读音
/ɪnˈtɪriər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 内部；里面。例如: "The interior of the house was beautifully decorated."  
2. 内部地区；内地。例如: "They explored the interior of the Amazon rainforest."
3. 内部结构；内脏。例如: "The car's interior is well-designed and comfortable."

## 短语
- "the interior design" 室内设计
- "interior minister" 内政部长
- "landlocked country" 一个没有海岸线，全境位于内陆的国家，其全部边界都是其他国家的领土，即其"interior"与海洋隔绝

## 例句
- 名词: "The hotel's lobby had a luxurious interior with plush sofas and chandeliers."
- 名词: "The spacecraft's interior was designed for maximum comfort during long missions."
