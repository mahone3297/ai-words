## Pronunciation
/sɪnˈsɜːr/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by genuine honesty and truthfulness; genuine, heartfelt. For example: "Her sincerity was evident in her eyes."
- **Adverb**: In a sincere manner; genuinely. For example: "He thanked her sincerely for her help."

## Phrases
- "Sincerely yours" is a formal closing used in letters.
- "Expressing one's sincerity" means to convey true feelings or intentions.
- "A sincere apology" indicates a genuine regret for an action.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "He had a sincere desire to learn from his mistakes."
- **Adverb**: "She spoke sincerely about her love for the project."
- "His heartfelt thanks were sincere."
