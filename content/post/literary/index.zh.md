## 读音
/lɪˈrɛrɪ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 与文学有关的，具有文学价值的，书面的。例如: "She has a deep interest in literary works."
- **名词**: 文学，文学作品，文学创作。例如: "The study of literature encompasses poetry, novels, and plays."

## 短语
- "Literary criticism" 指的是对文学作品的分析和评价。
- "Literary festival" 是指庆祝和探讨文学的活动或节日。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The book is considered a masterpiece of literary fiction."
- 名词: "The author won a prestigious award for her literary achievements."

## 同义词
- Literary can also be replaced by:
- Artistic
- Erudite
- Scholarly
- Bookish
- Aesthetic
