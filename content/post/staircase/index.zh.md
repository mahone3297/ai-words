## 读音
/stəˈtreɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一组连接楼层的台阶或梯子。例如: "The staircase led up to the second floor."
- **动词**: 沿着楼梯走。例如: "He staircased his way down to the ground floor."

## 短语
- "Staircase wit" 指事后才想到的机智回答。
- "A flight of stairs" 通常指的是连续的一段楼梯。

## 例句
- 名词: "The staircase in the old mansion was made of elegant mahogany."
- 动词: "She staircased cautiously, holding onto the railing for support."

## 同义词
- Stairs
- Steps
- Escalator (电动扶梯，多用于商场或地铁站)
- Ramp (坡道，供轮椅或自行车使用)
