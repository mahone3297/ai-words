## Pronunciation
/stəˈkeɪs/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **staircase** is a set of steps or a series of platforms with a handrail, typically connecting different levels of a building or structure, allowing people to move between floors.

### Verb (informal)
To ascend or descend a staircase, often used in the sense of climbing or going up or down the steps.

## Phrases
- "Take the staircase to the second floor"
- "Use the back staircase for a quieter entrance"
- "Climb the spiral staircase to reach the rooftop"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She carefully made her way up the winding staircase."
- **Verb**: "After work, he climbed the stairs to his apartment on the fifth floor."
- **Informal**: "He jogged up the staircase three flights to get home faster."

## Synonyms
- Stairwell
- Steps
- Ladder (informal, usually for indoor use)
- Escalator (mechanical alternative)
- Ramp (for accessibility)

## Antonyms
- Elevator
- Lift
- Ramp (for wheelchair access)
