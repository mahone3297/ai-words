## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having worth, importance, or usefulness; estimable. For example: "The antique vase is a valuable possession."
- **Noun**: Something that has significant value or worth. For example: "This painting is a valuable artwork."
- **Adverb**: In a valuable manner or to a high degree. For example: "Your advice was invaluable to me."

## Phrases
- "A valuable lesson" implies a lesson with lasting significance.
- "Valuable real estate" refers to property with high market value.
- "She's a valuable asset to the team" means she is an important and useful member.
