## 读音
/pəˈpər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 1. 纸张: A thin material made from the pulp of wood or other fibrous materials, typically used for writing, drawing, printing, and packaging. 例如: "She wrote a letter on a sheet of paper."
  - 2. 论文, 文章: A written work, especially one that is academic, scholarly, or dealing with a specific topic. 例如: "He presented his research paper at the conference."
  - 3. 报纸: A publication printed on paper and issued daily or weekly, containing news, articles, advertisements, and opinions. 例如: "I read the morning paper over breakfast."
  - 4. 文件, 文件夹: A document or set of documents that contain information or instructions. 例如: "The employee submitted all the necessary papers for the job application."

- **形容词**:
  - 1. 纸质的: Relating to or made of paper. 例如: "A paper bag is less durable than a plastic one."
  - 2. 虚构的, 假的: Existing only in theory or on paper; not practical or real. 例如: "The plan looked good on paper, but it failed in practice."

## 短语
- "Write on paper" 意思是在纸上写字。
- "Wallpaper" 指用于装饰墙壁的纸质材料。
- "Paperwork" 指需要填写或处理的各种文件和表格。

## 例句
- 名词: "The child drew a picture on a piece of paper."
- 形容词: "The company had a paper profit, but actual earnings were lower."

## 同义词
- Document (for written works)
- Sheet (for a single piece of paper)
- Publication (for newspapers)
