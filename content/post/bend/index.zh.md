## 读音
/bend/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 使弯曲；使折弯。例如: "She carefully bent the wire into a heart shape."
2. 屈服；顺从。例如: "The stubborn child finally bent to his mother's wishes."

- **名词**:
1. 弯曲处；拐角。例如: "There's a sharp bend in the road ahead."
2. （常用于复数形式）弯曲的动作。例如: "He demonstrated the proper technique for a golf swing and the follow-through bend."

## 短语
- "Bend over" 意为俯身，弯腰。例如: "Bend over and touch your toes."
- "Bend the rules" 表示违反规则或灵活处理。例如: "Sometimes we have to bend the rules a little to get things done."

## 例句
- 动词: "The gardener used pliers to bend the metal stake into position."
- 名词: "At the bend in the river, there's a beautiful waterfall."
