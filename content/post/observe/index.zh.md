## 读音
/əbˈzɜːrv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 观察；监视：例如，"Scientists observe the behavior of animals in their natural habitat."
2. 遵守；执行：例如，"It's important to observe traffic rules for safety."
3. 注意到；察觉：例如，"She observed a strange detail in the painting."

- **名词**:
1. 观察；观测结果：例如，"The doctor made several observations about the patient's condition."

## 短语
- "Observe carefully" 意为仔细观察。
- "Observe a holiday" 意为庆祝节日。
- "Observe silence" 意为保持安静。

## 例句
- 动词: "We need to observe the stars for several hours to track their movements accurately."
- 名词: "His careful observations helped us understand the problem better."
