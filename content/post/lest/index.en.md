## Pronunciation
/lɛst/

## Definitions
- **Adverb/Conjunction**: Expressing fear that something undesirable might happen; for the purpose of preventing something undesirable. It introduces a clause expressing a consequence to be avoided. 

Example definition usage:
- **Adverb**: He worked diligently on his project lest he fail the class.
- **Conjunction**: She double-checked her notes lest she miss any important details during the presentation.

## Phrases
- "Lest we forget": A phrase often used in remembrance events, urging people not to forget significant events or sacrifices made by others.
  
## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: The students revised thoroughly lest they should fail the exam.
- **Conjunction**: Bring a map with you lest you get lost in the city.

## Synonyms
- In case (that)
- For fear (that)
- So as not (to)
- To avoid (the possibility of)
- To prevent (from)
