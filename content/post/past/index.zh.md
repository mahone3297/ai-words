## 读音
/pɑːst/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 以前的，过去的。例如: "We often reminisce about our past experiences."
- **名词**: 过去，往事。例如: "She keeps thinking about her past mistakes."
- **副词**: 以前，曾经。例如: "He used to be a football player, but those days are in the past now."

## 短语
- "in the past" 指的是在以前的时间。
- "the recent past" 意味着最近的过去。
- "put the past behind" 表示忘记过去，开始新的生活。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She still has feelings for her past love."
- 名词: "The historian studied events from the distant past."
- 副词: "I used to weigh more, but I've lost weight since the past."
