## 读音
/ɪnˈhɛrɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 继承，从父母或其他前辈那里得到（财产、特质、习惯等）。例如: "She inherited her mother's blue eyes."
- **名词**: 继承物，遗产，指继承得来的财产或权利。例如: "The heir will receive a large inheritance."

## 短语
- "Inherit the Earth" 意指子孙后代将继承地球或承担其未来。
- "Inherit a trait" 意味着从祖先那里获得一个特性或特征。

## 例句
- 动词: "John is set to inherit the family business when his father retires."
- 名词: "The children were left with a substantial inheritance after their parents passed away."

## 同义词
- Receive from ancestors
- Come into possession of
- Take over
- Succeed to
- Bequeath (反义词，作为动词时)
