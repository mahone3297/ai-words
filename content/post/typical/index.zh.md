## 读音
/ˈtɪpɪkl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 典型的，平常的，具有代表性的。例如: "The typical American breakfast includes eggs and bacon."
- **名词**: 典型例子，代表人物。例如: "She is the typical high school student, always busy with homework."

## 短语
- "typical behavior" 指的是常见的行为模式。
- "typical example" 意为典型的例子或样本。

## 例句
- 形容词: "His typical day starts with a morning run and ends with reading a book."
- 名词: "She's a typical extrovert, always surrounded by friends."
