## 读音
/hɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个小山丘，比山小但比地面高的地形。例如: "There's a beautiful view from the top of that hill."
- **名词**: 用作姓氏。例如: "John Hill is a famous author."

## 短语
- "On top of the hill" 指的是山丘的顶部。
- "Roll down the hill" 意味着从山坡上滑下来。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children loved to play on the grassy hill in the park."
- 姓氏: "Hill is a common surname in England."
