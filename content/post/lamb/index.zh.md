## 读音
/ læm /

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 小羊，羔羊。例如: "The shepherd watched over his flock of lambs."
  - 指温和、无辜的人。例如: "She has a lamb-like innocence about her."

## 短语
- "lamb to the slaughter" 指无辜受害者或被轻易欺骗的人。
- "like a lamb" 形容人或动物非常温顺。

## 例句
- 名词: "The lambs were frolicking in the field with their mother."
- "He was led to the meeting like a lamb, not knowing what was in store for him."

## 同义词
- Ewe lamb (特指雌性小羊)
- Kid (在某些地区，指小山羊)
- Fawn (在某些情况下，也可指小鹿)
