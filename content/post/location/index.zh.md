## 读音
/ˈloʊkəʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 地点；位置：例如 "The restaurant is in a convenient location."
   - 存放或发生的地方：例如 "The accident occurred at a dangerous location on the highway."
   - 驻地；所在地：例如 "Our company's headquarters has moved to a new location."

## 短语
- "Change of location" 意味着改变位置或迁移。
- "Remote location" 指偏远或远离中心的地方。
- "GPS location" 指全球定位系统确定的位置。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please provide your current location for delivery."
- 动词（通常不作为动词使用，但在某些语境下可理解为动词）: "Let's locate the nearest pharmacy on the map."
