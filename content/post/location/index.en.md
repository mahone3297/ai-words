## Word: Location

## Pronunciation
/ˌləʊkəˈkeɪʃn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A place or position where something can be found. It can refer to a physical spot, an area, or a specific point within a larger space. For example:
  - _The location of the restaurant is perfect, right in the heart of the city._
  - _We're looking for a filming location with beautiful natural scenery._

## Phrases
- **At location**: Being physically present at a particular place. Example: "The film crew was already set up at location by sunrise."
- **Location scouting**: The process of finding suitable places to shoot scenes for films, TV shows, or photographs.
- **Location-based services**: Services that use real-time geodata to provide information or services based on a user's location.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: _They decided to change the meeting location due to construction nearby._
- _Finding a prime real estate location is crucial for a successful business._
- _GPS helps us determine our exact location anywhere on Earth._
