## 读音
/rɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 戒指，如: "She wore a diamond ring on her finger."
2. 圈，环，如: "The children were playing in a circle, holding hands to form a ring."
3. 电话铃声，如: "My phone rang with an urgent call."
4. 铃，钟，如: "The school bell rang, signaling the end of class."

- **动词**:
1. 按铃，打电话，如: "He rang the doorbell but there was no answer."
2. 围成一圈，如: "They danced, ringing around the Maypole."
3. 发出铃声，如: "The phone rang loudly."

## 短语
- "Ring someone up" 意为打电话给某人。
- "Ring a bell" 意味着某事唤起回忆或听起来熟悉。
- "Ring true" 表示某事真实可信。

## 例句
- 名词: "He presented her with a beautiful engagement ring."
- 动词: "She rang the hotel to confirm her reservation."
- 感叹词: "Ring! The alarm clock startled her awake."

## 同义词
- Band (for a ring worn on the finger)
- Ding (for the sound of a bell or phone)
- Call (for making a phone call)
- Chime (for the sound of a bell)
