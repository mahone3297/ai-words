## 读音
/ˈreɪdiəs/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   - 圆的半径，从圆心到圆边的距离。例如: "The radius of the circle is 5 centimeters."
   - 半径的长度。例如: "The radius of the sphere is twice its diameter."
   - 在其他几何形状或结构中的类似概念。例如: "The radius of a cone is the distance from the vertex to the circumference."

- **名词 n** (科学和技术领域):
   - 在无线电通信中，一个信号覆盖的范围或距离。例如: "The radio station has a broadcast radius of 50 miles."
   - 在天文学中，一个星体的半径。例如: "The radius of Jupiter is about 11 times that of Earth."

## 短语
- "radius of a circle" 指的是圆的半径。
- "communication radius" 描述信号传输的有效距离。

## 例句
- 名词: "The tree's branches spread out in a radius of 10 feet around the trunk."
- 名词 (科技): "The satellite has a communication radius of up to 300 kilometers."
