## 读音
/ˌhwɪtsəˈevər/

## 释义
- **代词**: 无论哪个；无论哪一个；任何（用于选择疑问句或条件从句中）。例如: "Choose whichever book you like." 或 "You can have whichever one you prefer."
- **连词**: 表示“无论是…还是…”。例如: "Whichever way you turn, you'll see a beautiful view."

## 短语
- "Whichever comes first" 意为“先发生的那个”。
- "Whichever way" 指的是“无论向哪边，不管怎样”。

## 例句
- 代词: "Take whichever chair is more comfortable."
- 连词: "Whichever team wins, we'll be happy for them."
