## 读音
/pəˈpjuːs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 目的，意图，目标。例如: "The main purpose of the meeting is to discuss the new project."
- **动词**: 有目的地做，旨在。例如: "She purposefully arranged the flowers in a vase."

## 短语
- "With the purpose of" 表示为了某个目的。例如: "He went to the library with the purpose of studying for his exam."
- "Aim or goal" 可以作为 purpose 的同义短语。例如: "The aim of the program is to reduce poverty."

## 例句
- 名词: "The purpose of this software is to simplify data analysis."
- 动词: "She purposefully chose a quiet spot to read her book."

## 同义词
- Aim
- Goal
- Objective
- Intention
- End
