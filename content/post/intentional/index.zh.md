## 读音
/ɪnˈtenʃənl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有意的，故意的，蓄意的。表示行为或结果是出于明确的目的或计划。例如: "The accident was not intentional; it was just a mistake."
- **名词**: (法律用语)故意的行为或犯罪意图。例如: "The act was deemed intentional and thus punishable by law."

## 短语
- "intentional act" 指的是有意识、有目的的行为。
- "with intent" 或 "on purpose" 与 intentional 类似，表示有明确意图的。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She knocked over the vase, but it was not intentional; she simply lost her balance."
- 名词: "In the legal case, the defendant's actions were considered intentional, leading to severe consequences."

## 同义词
- Deliberate
- Purposeful
- Intended
- Calculated
- Malicious (如果指负面意图时)
