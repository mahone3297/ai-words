## 读音
/ kənˈteɪn /

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 包含；容纳: 指事物内部包含其他事物。例如: "The bottle contains water." 
2. 拥有，持有: 指人或物拥有某种特性或品质。例如: "She contains great wisdom."
3. 控制，抑制: 使感情、情绪不外露。例如: "He managed to contain his anger."

## 短语
- "contain oneself" 保持克制，控制自己的情绪。
- "contain within" 在...之内包含。例如: "All the answers can be found contained within the book."

## 例句
- The box contains several small presents for my friends.
- The ocean contains countless species of marine life.
- He tried to contain his excitement about the surprise party.
