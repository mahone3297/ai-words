## 读音
/ˈdɪstɜːrb/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 打扰，使不安。例如: "I'm sorry to disturb you, but I need your help."
2. 干扰，扰乱。例如: "The noise from the construction site disturbed my concentration."
3. 引起混乱或麻烦。例如: "His sudden appearance disturbed the peace of the village."

## 短语
- "disturb the peace" 意味着制造噪音或引起公共秩序的混乱。
- "to disturb someone's sleep" 指打扰别人的休息。

## 例句
- "Please don't disturb me while I'm working."
- "The loud music disturbed the neighbors."
- "His constant interruptions disturbed her train of thought."
