## 释义
- **形容词**: 没有尽头的；无尽的；无穷的。例如: "The movie had an endless supply of action scenes."
- **名词**: 无尽；无穷。例如: "Her love for him was an endless source of inspiration."

## 短语
- "endless loop" 无限循环，通常在计算机编程中使用。
- "endless possibilities" 无穷的可能性。
