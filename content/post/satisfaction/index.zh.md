## 读音
/sətɪsˈfækʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 满意，满足。指达到或超越期望而产生的愉快感觉。例如: "Customer satisfaction is our top priority."
- **动词**: 使满意，使满足。例如: "The new product really satisfies the customers' needs."

## 短语
- "Customer satisfaction survey" 是用来评估客户对产品或服务满意度的调查。
- "To satisfy one's curiosity" 指满足好奇心。
- "Satisfaction guaranteed" 表示如果客户不满意，会提供退款或其他补偿。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company aims to increase customer satisfaction ratings by improving their service."
- 动词: "Eating a delicious meal can satisfy your hunger and make you feel content."

- Gratification
