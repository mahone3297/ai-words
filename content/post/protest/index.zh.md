## 读音
/ˈprɑːtɛst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 公开表达反对或不满的行为或事件。例如: "The peaceful protest was organized against the government's policy."
- **动词**: 表达反对或抗议。例如: "They protested against the rise in taxes."
- **形容词**: 表示异议的或抗议的。例如: "The protest song expressed the people's discontent."

## 短语
- "Protest march" 指的是游行示威，以表达抗议。
- "To protest one's innocence" 意味着坚决声明自己是无罪的。
- "In protest" 用来表示对某事的反对或不满。

## 例句
- 名词: "A large crowd gathered for the climate change protest."
- 动词: "She decided to protest the unfair treatment at work."
- 形容词: "The protest signs carried slogans against the war."
