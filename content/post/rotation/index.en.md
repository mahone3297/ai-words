## Pronunciation
/ˈrəʊtəʃn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act or process of rotating, turning around a central axis. 2. A sequence of people or things taking turns in a particular activity or position. 3. (Computers) The movement of data from one storage location to another in a circular manner. 4. (Mathematics) The act of changing the orientation of an object in a plane or space by a specified angle.
- **Verb**: To move or cause to move in a circular or rotatory motion. 2. To change positions or roles periodically.

## Phrases
- "Rotate the wheel" means to turn the wheel in a circular motion.
- "Shift the rotation" refers to changing the order or sequence.
- "24-hour rotation" describes a system where tasks are done in a continuous cycle throughout the day.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The windmill's rotation generated electricity for the nearby village."
- **Verb**: "In the game, players take turns rotating the board."
- **As a verb phrase**: "The employees work in a six-month rotation, alternating between office and field assignments."
