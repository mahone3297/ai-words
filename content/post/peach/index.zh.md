## 读音
/ˈpiːtʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 一种圆形水果，有柔软的肉质内核和毛茸茸的皮，通常是黄色或红色，味道甜美。例如: "I love eating fresh peaches in the summer."
   - 桃树，属于蔷薇科，产生这种水果。例如: "The peach tree blossoms in early spring."
   
## 短语
- "Peachy keen" 表示非常好，非常满意。例如: "I'm feeling peachy keen today."
- "Peach fuzz" 指人脸或其他部位上的细软毛发。例如: "He had just a bit of peach fuzz on his chin."

## 例句
- 名词: "The farmer's market had the most delicious, juicy peaches I've ever tasted."
- 形容词: "Her skin was as soft as a peach."

## 同义词
- Nectarine (油桃)
- Apricot (杏子)
- Plum (李子)
- Melon (甜瓜)
