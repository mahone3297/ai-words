## Word: During

## Pronunciation
/dərˈɪŋ/

## Definitions
- **Preposition**: At the same time as (an event or period); throughout the duration of something. For example: "During the summer, we often go on vacation."

### Example Sentences
- _Preposition_: "I read several books during my staycation."
- "They renovated the house during their absence."
- "She practiced her singing every day during her lunch break."

## Phrases
- "During the course of": Over the duration or while something happens; as something progresses. Example: "During the course of the project, we encountered several challenges."
- "During that time": Referring to a specific period in the past or future. Example: "During that time, he honed his skills in photography."
