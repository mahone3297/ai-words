## 读音
/ˈledʒənd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 传说，传奇故事：A story or myth that is passed down through generations, often about a heroic figure or an event.
   - 传记，人物传记：A written account of someone's life, especially a famous person.
   - 名人，传奇人物：A person who is famous and greatly admired for their achievements or abilities.

- **形容词**:
   - 传说中的，具有传奇色彩的：Relating to or denoting a legendary person or event.
   - 极好的，卓越的：Exceptional or outstanding in quality or reputation.

## 短语
- ".legendary hero" 传说中的英雄
- "a legend in one's own time" 当世传奇人物
- "live up to one's legend" 履行或符合传说中的声誉

## 例句
- 名词: "The legend of King Arthur has inspired countless books and films."
- 形容词: "Muhammad Ali was a legendary boxer known for his skills and personality."
- 动词（作为形容词使用）: "Her performance on stage was so powerful that it became legendary."
