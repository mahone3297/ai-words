## Pronunciation
/scɪzərz/

## Definitions
### Noun
A pair of sharp-edged tools with handles, used for cutting paper, cloth, or other thin materials. *Example: She picked up a pair of scissors from the drawer.*

### Verb
To cut with scissors or use them as a tool for trimming or separating things. *Example: He carefully scissors the excess fabric from the edges.*

### Adjective
Referring to the tools themselves, typically used to describe their function or presence. *Example: The metal scissors were old but still functional.*

## Phrases
- "Snip with scissors" implies using the tool for cutting.
- "She handled the scissors deftly" describes skillful use.
- "Scissors work wonders on paper" highlights their effectiveness.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The tailor used a pair of fabric scissors to cut the pattern."
- **Verb**: "She snipped the tags off the new shirt with her nail scissors."
- **Adjective**: "The kids were fascinated by the intricate work done with sewing scissors."
