## 读音
/lɪˈsɛns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 特许证，许可证，执照。例如: "You need a driver's license to operate a car."
- **动词**: 许可，授权。例如: "The software requires you to license it for commercial use."

## 短语
- "Business license" 商业许可证
- "Software license" 软件许可证
- "Open-source license" 开源许可证

## 例句
- 名词: "Before you can practice medicine, you must obtain a medical license."
- 动词: "The city licensed new vendors to sell food on the streets."

## 同义词
- Permit
- Authorization
- Consent
- Approval
- Endorsement
