## 读音
/imˈmɛdiətli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 立即，马上，直接。表示动作没有延迟或间隔。例如: "I need this report immediately."
- **形容词**: 立即的，直接的。用于描述动作或情况的即时性。例如: "The doctor provided immediate assistance."

## 短语
- "Do it immediately" 意为立刻去做。
- "Act immediately" 表示要迅速采取行动。

## 例句
- 副词: "As soon as I finish this call, I'll call you back immediately."
- 形容词: "The emergency services arrived on the scene with immediate response."

## 同义词
- Straightaway
- Promptly
- Without delay
- Instantly
- Directly
