## 读音
/vaɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 藤；葡萄藤；常春藤；葡萄酒厂。例如: "The grapes grow on the vine in the sunny vineyard." 
- **名词**: （象征性）根源，基础。例如: "The idea was the vine from which many new projects sprang."
- **动词**: （植物）蔓延，攀爬。例如: "The ivy vines its way up the old brick wall."

## 短语
- "vineyard" 葡萄园，葡萄酒产地。
- "grapevine" 非正式的信息来源或渠道，如 "rumors spread through the grapevine."

## 例句
- 名词: "The winemaker carefully tended to his vines to ensure a good harvest."
- 动词: "The little plant began to vine around the trellis."
