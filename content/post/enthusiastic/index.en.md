## Pronunciation
/ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Showing or characterized by strong excitement and interest; very eager or keen. For example: "She's an enthusiastic supporter of the local sports team."
- **Noun**: A person who is enthusiastic about something. For example: "The club welcomes all new enthusiasts who share our passion for photography."

## Phrases
- "Enthusiastic about": Expresses a strong liking or interest in something. For example: "He's enthusiastic about trying out new recipes."
- "With enthusiasm": Describes doing something with great energy and eagerness. For example: "She tackled her new project with enthusiasm."

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "He was enthusiastic about joining the hiking trip this weekend."
- "His enthusiastic response reassured us that he was fully committed to the project."
- **Noun**: "The science enthusiasts gathered for the annual conference."
