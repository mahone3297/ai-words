## 读音
/rɪˈsiːpt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 收据；发票。例如: "Please keep your receipt for warranty purposes."
- **动词**: 发给收据；确认收到。例如: "The store clerk receipted the customer's payment."

## 短语
- "Cash receipt" 指的是现金收据。
- "Receipt of goods" 表示货物已收到。
- "Make out a receipt" 意味着填写收据。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need to find my receipt to return the faulty product."
- 动词: "The bank will receipt your deposit once it's processed."
