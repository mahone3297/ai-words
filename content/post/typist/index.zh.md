## 读音
/ˈtaɪpɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 专门从事打字工作的人；打字员。例如: "She works as a professional typist in a law firm."
- **动词**: (非正式)指进行打字行为。例如: "I've been typing away at my computer all morning."

## 短语
- "typist pool" 指一个组织或公司中的打字员集合或部门。
- "typing speed" 指打字的速率，通常以字每分钟（WPM）计算。

## 例句
- 名词: "The office employs several experienced typists for their daily paperwork."
- 动词: "She's practicing to improve her typing skills for her new job."

## 同义词
- Keyboarder
- Data entry clerk
- Word processor
- Clerk-typist
- Stenographer (尤指速记员)
