## Pronunciation
/taɪˈpɪst/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **typist** is a person who primarily uses a keyboard to type on a computer or typewriter, usually for the purpose of producing written documents or transcribing text.

### Verb (informal)
To **type** or **key in** text, often implying speed and accuracy, as when referring to someone's typing skills.

## Phrases
- "Typing up a report" means preparing a document using a keyboard.
- "A professional typist" refers to someone skilled in typing.
- "Speed typing" or "touch typing" refers to the efficient and automatic method of typing without looking at the keys.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She was a skilled typist, able to type 100 words per minute."
- **Verb**: "The secretary quickly typed out the client's letter on her computer."
- **Informal**: "I need someone to type up these notes from the meeting."

## Synonyms
- Keyboarder
- Transcriptionist
- Clerk (in a typing context)
- Typographer (less common, but referring to a professional involved in typesetting)
- Encoder (when referring to data entry)
