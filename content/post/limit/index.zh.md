## 读音
/ˈlɪmɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 限制；界限；最高限度。例如: "The speed limit on this road is 60 mph."
- **动词**: 限制；限定。例如: "The government is limiting the number of tourists allowed in the national park."
- **形容词**: 有限的；受限制的。例如: "We have limited resources, so we need to be careful."

## 短语
- "without limit" 意为无限制的。
- "push the limits" 指挑战极限。
- "set a limit" 设置一个限制或规定。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company has reached its credit limit and can't borrow more money."
- 动词: "The new policy will limit the amount of time employees can spend on personal tasks during work hours."
- 形容词: "Our budget for the project is quite limited."
