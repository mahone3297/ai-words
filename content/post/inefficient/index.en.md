## Pronunciation
/ˌɪnəˈfɪʃənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Not achieving maximum productivity, effectiveness, or efficiency; wasteful or lacking practicality. For example: "The old machine was inefficient and needed replacement."

### Phrase
- "Inefficient use of resources": This phrase refers to the failure to utilize resources in the best possible manner, often resulting in waste or reduced productivity.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The company's outdated management system contributed to its overall inefficient operations."
- "Her work process was quite inefficient; it took her twice as long to complete the task compared to her colleagues."
- "The car's fuel consumption was inefficient, which led us to consider purchasing a hybrid model."
