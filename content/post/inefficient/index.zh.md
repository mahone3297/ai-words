## 读音
/ɪnˈfɪʃənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不有效的，效率低下的。例如: "The old machine is inefficient and often breaks down."
- **副词**: 无效率地，不经济地。例如: "They worked inefficiently, wasting a lot of time."

## 短语
- "inefficient system" 指的是运行不佳或生产力低下的系统。
- "inefficient use of resources" 意味着资源使用不当或浪费。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The company decided to replace its inefficient lighting with energy-saving LED bulbs."
- 副词: "The project was completed inefficiently due to poor planning."

- Ineffective
- Unproductive
- Laborious
