## 读音
/plʌk/

## 释义
- **动词**: 用力拉下或摘取（果实、羽毛等）。例如: "He plucked a ripe apple from the tree."
- **名词**: 勇气，胆量。例如: "She showed great pluck in facing her fears."
- **形容词**: 敢于冒险的，勇敢的。例如: "The plucky explorer braved the harsh conditions."

## 短语
- "pluck up one's courage" 意为鼓起勇气。
- "pluck out" 指拔掉或摘掉某个物体。

## 例句
- 动词: "She plucked a flower and presented it to her friend."
- 名词: "His pluck impressed everyone during the crisis."
- 形容词: "The plucky dog refused to give up despite the danger."
