## 读音
/ˈrɛbəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 反叛者；起义者；不顺从的人。例如: "The rebels fought against the government's oppression."
- **动词**: 反抗；违抗。例如: "She rebelled against her parents' strict rules."
- **形容词**: 叛逆的；反抗的。例如: "The teenager had a rebellious streak in him."

## 短语
- "Rebel forces" 指的是反抗政府或权威的武装力量。
- "To rebel against" 意味着对某事物进行反抗或抵制。例如: "They rebelled against the unjust laws."

## 例句
- 名词: "The leader of the rebel group sought peace negotiations with the government."
- 动词: "He rebelled against societal norms and lived a free-spirited life."
- 形容词: "Her rebellious nature often got her into trouble."
