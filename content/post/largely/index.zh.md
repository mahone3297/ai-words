## 读音
/lɑːrɡli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 大部分地，主要地，广泛地。例如: "The project was completed largely due to his efforts."
- **形容词**: 很大程度上的，大规模的。例如: "The company has largely increased its profits this year."

## 短语
- "Largely unaffected" 意为基本上没有受到影响。
- "Largely successful" 表示大部分成功的。

## 例句
- 副词: "The town remains largely unchanged since I last visited."
- 形容词: "The new policy has been largely welcomed by the public."
