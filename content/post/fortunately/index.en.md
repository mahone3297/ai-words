## Word: Fortunately

## Pronunciation
/ˈfɔːtʃənətli/

## Definition
**Adverb**: Happening by good fortune; with luck or good luck. It's often used to introduce a positive statement that counters a negative expectation or situation.

- **Meaning**: Expressing relief or happiness about something unexpected but favorable.
  
## Example Sentences
- _Fortunately_, the storm passed over without causing any damage to our house.
- Despite being lost, _fortunately_ we stumbled upon a helpful local who showed us the way back.
- We were worried about finances, but _fortunately_ my bonus came through just in time.

## Phrases
- _Fortunately enough_, the package arrived just before the deadline.
- _Fortunately for us_, the traffic cleared up quickly and we made it to the airport on time.

## Synonyms
- Luckily
- Happily
- Fortuitously
- Providentially
- Serendipitously
- By good fortune
- With good luck
