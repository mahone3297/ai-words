## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 哺乳动物、鸟类或昆虫的外部硬壳；保护内部身体的坚硬外罩。例如: "The turtle has a hard shell to protect itself."
- **名词**: 贝壳，尤指海螺的壳。例如: "She collected various shells on the beach."
- **名词**: 计算机操作系统外壳程序，提供用户与系统交互的界面。例如: "In computing, the command-line shell is a tool for interacting with the OS."
- **名词**: 地雷或炮弹的金属外壳。例如: "The explosion destroyed several shell casings."

## 短语
- "Shell out" 意为花费大量金钱。例如: "He had to shell out a lot of money for his new car."
- "Beneath the shell" 指在表面之下，真实的本质。例如: "There's a kind heart beneath her tough shell."
