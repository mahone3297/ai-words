## 读音
/fiː/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 费用，小费，服务费。例如: "There's a small fee for using the service."
  - 学费，报名费。例如: "The university has increased its tuition fees."

- **动词**:
  - 收取费用。例如: "They will fee you for late payment."

## 短语
- "Service fee" 指提供服务时收取的费用。
- "Administration fee" 是行政管理费用。
- "No fee required" 表示无需支付任何费用。

## 例句
- 名词: "The hotel charges a daily internet fee."
- 动词: "The lawyer agreed to fee us a flat rate for his services."
