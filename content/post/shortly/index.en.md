## Pronunciation
/ˈʃɔːltli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a short time; shortly after; soon. It can also be used to indicate that something will happen without much delay.

## Phrases
- "He'll be back shortly." (meaning he will return soon)
- "We'll discuss the matter shortly." (meaning they will talk about it soon)
- "Wait a moment, I'll be with you shortly." (meaning they will be there soon)

## Example Sentences
- "I'll finish this task shortly." (meaning they will complete it soon)
- "The train arrives shortly before 5 PM." (meaning it will arrive around 5 PM)
- "Please hold on, I'll call you back shortly." (meaning they will call soon)

## Synonyms
- Soon
- Immediately
- In a jiffy
- Promptly
- Right away

## Antonyms
- Later
- Eventually
- Gradually
- Delayed
- Soonish (a less formal synonym)
