## 读音
/piːl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 去除（水果或蔬菜）的皮或外层。例如: "I need to peel an apple before eating it."
- **名词**: 皮，外层，剥离物。例如: "The peel of an orange is often used in cooking."

## 短语
- "peel off" 意为撕掉或剥落。例如: "The old paint started to peel off the wall."
- "peel an onion" 指剥洋葱。例如: "Carefully peel the onion without cutting yourself."

## 例句
- 动词: "He peeled the potatoes for the dinner."
- 名词: "She put the potato peels in the compost bin."
