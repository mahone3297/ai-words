## 读音
/ʌp/

## 释义
- **副词**: 指向上的方向，增加，提高。例如: "The balloon went up into the sky."
- **形容词**: 表示开启、活跃、积极的状态。例如: "The store is up and running after the renovation."
- **动词**: 表示移动到高处或增加。例如: "Can you help me move this box up the stairs?"
- **名词**: 在某些固定搭配中作为名词，如 "upstairs"（楼上）。

## 短语
- "upward" 向上
- "up and down" 上下，来来回回
- "pick up" 拾起，接载，学会
- "get up" 起床

## 例句
- 副词: "Please raise your hand up so I can see it."
- 形容词: "The restaurant is open and up for business."
- 动词: "She picked up the phone and dialed her friend's number."
- 名词: "There's a bathroom up on the second floor."
