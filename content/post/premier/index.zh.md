## 读音
/prɪˈmɪər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 首相，总理。指一个国家或地区的政府首脑，通常在议会制国家中担任此职务。例如: "The newly elected premier took office last week."
- **形容词**: 最初的，首要的，顶级的。例如: "The premier league is the highest level of professional football in the country."

## 短语
- "Premier League" 英超联赛，英国最高等级的职业足球联赛。
- "Premier Minister" 首相或总理，正式称呼一国的政府首脑。

## 例句
- 名词: "The premier held a press conference to discuss the new economic policies."
- 形容词: "This hotel is considered one of the premier luxury resorts in the world."

## 同义词
- 首相/总理:
  - Prime Minister
  - Chief Minister
- 首要的/顶级的:
  - Primary
  - Principal
  - Top-notch
  - Leading
