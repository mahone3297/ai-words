## 读音
/glaɪd/

## 释义
- **动词**: 滑行；平滑地移动或前进。例如: "The plane glided smoothly over the clouds."
- **名词**: 滑行；滑动的动作。例如: "The ice skater executed a beautiful glide across the rink."

## 短语
- "glide path" 在航空中指飞机降落时应遵循的下滑路径。
- "glide slope" 是与"glide path"相关的无线电导航信号，帮助飞行员保持正确的下降角度。

## 例句
- 动词: "The eagle spread its wings and began to glide effortlessly through the air."
- 名词: "The skateboarder performed an impressive long glide on the empty street."

## 同义词
- coast
- slide
- swoop
- drift
- sail (in certain contexts)
