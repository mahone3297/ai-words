## 读音
/tiːm/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一组共同工作以实现共同目标的人。例如: "The soccer team practiced for hours before the match."
- **名词 (n)**: 在某些游戏或活动中，一方的成员。例如: "Team A defeated Team B in the chess tournament."
- **动词 (v)**: 组成团队或集体努力。例如: "They teamed up to complete the project."

## 短语
- "Work as a team" 意味着协同工作。
- "On the same team" 表示共享相同的目标或立场。
- "Team player" 指善于与他人合作的人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's sales team exceeded their quarterly targets."
- 动词: "The two athletes decided to team up for the doubles tournament."
