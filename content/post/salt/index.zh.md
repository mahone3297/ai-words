## 读音
/sɔːlt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 盐，化学式为NaCl，常用于烹饪和调味。例如: "Add a pinch of salt to the soup."
   2. 某事物的基本成分或精髓。例如: "He is the salt of the earth, always ready to help others."

- **动词**: 1. 在食物中加盐。例如: "Don't forget to salt the potatoes before boiling them."
   2. 用盐处理（如保存食物）。例如: "In ancient times, people salted meat to preserve it."

## 短语
- "Take with a grain of salt" 意思是对待某事持保留态度，不全信。例如: "His advice should be taken with a grain of salt."
- "Salt away" 意味着储存或积累（金钱或物品）。例如: "They decided to salt away some money for their retirement."

## 例句
- 名词: "Please pass the salt, I need to season my steak."
- 动词: "She salts her eggs in the morning for extra flavor."
