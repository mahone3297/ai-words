## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A chemical compound with the formula NaCl, consisting primarily of sodium and chlorine ions, essential for various biological processes and commonly used as a seasoning or preservative. 2. A type of seasoning made from mineral salts, often derived from seawater or mined rock. 3. (informal) A person's temperament or character; their nature.
- **Verb**: To season or preserve food with salt. 4. To add salt to taste.
- **Adjective**: Salty; having a strong taste of salt.
