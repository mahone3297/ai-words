## 读音
/ˈdʒɜːrnəlɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 从事新闻工作的人，记者。例如: "She's a journalist for a national newspaper."
- **形容词**: 与新闻业或新闻报道相关的。例如: "The journalist's article covered the event in detail."

## 短语
- "Journalistic integrity" 指的是记者的职业道德和诚实。
- " Freelance journalist" 是指自由职业的记者，不受雇于特定媒体机构。

## 例句
- 名词: "The journalist conducted an interview with the famous actor."
- 形容词: "He has a background in journalistic writing."
