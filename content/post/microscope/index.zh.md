## 读音
/maɪˈskrəʊsəʊp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种精密仪器，用来放大微小物体，使细节可见。常用于科学实验和医学研究。例如: "The biologist used a microscope to study the cells."
- **动词**: 使用显微镜进行观察。例如: "She microscoped the slide to examine the bacteria."

## 短语
- "under the microscope" 意为仔细检查或严密审视。例如: "Their work is currently under the microscope for quality control."
- "microscopic details" 指极其微小的细节。例如: "The artist captured even the microscopic details of the flower."

## 例句
- 名词: "The student borrowed a high-powered microscope from the lab."
- 动词: "The scientist microscoped the sample to identify the species."
