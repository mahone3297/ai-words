## 读音
/tɪmˈteɪbl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个详细列出事件或活动在特定时间安排的表格或计划。例如: "The school's timetable shows when each class starts and ends."
- **名词**: 公共交通（如火车或公交车）的时刻表。例如: "Check the timetable to find out when the next train departs."

## 短语
- "Follow a timetable" 意味着按照预定的时间表进行。
- "Train timetable" 是指铁路服务的时间安排。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need to refer to my timetable to know when my next meeting is."
- 名词: "The bus company updated their timetable, so now the buses arrive earlier in the morning."
