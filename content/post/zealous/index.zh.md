## 读音
/ˈziːləs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 热情的，热心的，热衷的。表示对某事充满激情和投入。例如: "She is zealous about her work."
- **副词**: 热切地，热心地。例如: "He performed his duties with zealous enthusiasm."

## 短语
- "Zealous supporter" 意味着非常支持或拥护某人或某事物的人。
- "Zealous advocate" 指积极推广或捍卫某个观点或事业的人。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The young activist was known for her zealous dedication to environmental causes."
- 副词: "The team worked zealousy to meet the project deadline."
