## Pronunciation
/ˈziːələs/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Zeal** (countable): Intense enthusiasm or devotion, especially towards a cause or belief. Example: "The zealous dedication of the volunteers was inspiring."
- **Zealousness** (uncountable): The quality or state of being zealous; fervor or passion. Example: "Her zealousness for her work was evident in her long hours."

### Verb
- To be zealous about something: To show great enthusiasm or devotion in pursuing or supporting it. Example: "He was zealous about promoting environmental conservation."

## Phrases
- "Be zealous in your pursuit" encourages strong commitment.
- "A zealous advocate" refers to someone who promotes something with great passion.
- "Displaying zealous enthusiasm" shows lively interest.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The zealous fan cheered loudly for their favorite team."
- **Verb**: "She was zealous in her efforts to improve her skills."
- **Adjective**: "Her zealous approach to her work earned her many accolades."
