## 读音
/hæpən/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 发生；出现。例如: "An accident happened on the highway."
  - 偶然遇到；碰巧。例如: "I happened to meet an old friend in the park."

## 短语
- "Something happens by chance" 意味着某事偶然发生。
- "Happen upon/onto something" 表示偶然发现或遇到某事物。例如: "I happened upon a great book in the bookstore."

## 例句
- 动词: "The meeting happened earlier than we expected."
- "It happened that I had the solution to his problem."

## 同义词
- Occur
- Take place
- Arise
- Emerge
- Fall out
