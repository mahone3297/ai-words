## 读音
/luːz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 失去（拥有物、联系、控制等）。例如: "I lost my keys yesterday." 
  - 输掉（比赛、赌局等）。例如: "They lost the game in the last minute."
  - 忘记。例如: "I lose track of time when I'm reading a good book."
  - 使某人感到困惑或迷茫。例如: "The complicated instructions just made me lose my mind."

## 短语
- "Lose weight" 减轻体重。
- "Lose touch with someone" 与某人失去联系。
- "Lose one's temper" 发脾气。

## 例句
- 动词: "I was so busy that I completely lost the time to call her."
- 动词: "He almost lost control of the car on the icy road."
- 动词: "She was upset after losing the chess match against her brother."
