## Pronunciation
/əˈbɪləti/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The quality or skill that allows someone to do something well; the capacity for effective action or performance. 

## Phrases
- "To the best of my ability" implies doing something as well as one can.
- "Ability to + verb" indicates the capacity for a specific action, e.g., "ability to sing."
- "Develop one's abilities" refers to improving or enhancing skills over time.

## Example Sentences
- She has an extraordinary ability to solve complex mathematical problems with ease.
- Through consistent practice, he improved his public speaking ability significantly.
- Despite her young age, she demonstrated remarkable artistic ability.
