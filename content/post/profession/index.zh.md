## 读音
/prəˈfeʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 职业；行业： "He pursued a career in the medical profession."
   - (尤指需要专门知识和训练的)专业： "She has a degree in computer science and works in the IT profession."

## 短语
- "Profession of faith" 意为信仰的宣示或声明。
- "In the same profession" 指在相同的行业或职业中工作。

## 例句
- "His father and he both followed the legal profession."
- "She is proud to be part of the teaching profession."
