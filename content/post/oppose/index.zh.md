## 读音
/əˈpəʊz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 反对；抵制。例如: "She openly opposes the new tax law."
2. 对立；对抗。例如: "The two political parties oppose each other on many issues."

## 短语
- "oppose to something" 表示反对某个事物或观点。例如: "Many citizens oppose the construction of a new factory in their neighborhood."
- "oppose someone's views" 指反对某人的看法或立场。例如: "I strongly oppose his views on climate change."

## 例句
- "Despite public opinion, the mayor opposes any increase in public transportation fees."
- "In the debate, he opposed the idea of introducing a mandatory vaccine policy."
- "Their team opposes ours in the final match of the season."
