## 读音
/triː/

## 释义
- **名词 n**: 一种多年生植物，通常有木质主干，分枝并长有叶子和（通常）果实。例如: "There's a beautiful oak tree in my backyard."
- **名词 n** (抽象概念): 生命、成长或自然的象征。例如: "The tree of life represents immortality."

## 短语
- "Under the tree" 指在树荫下。
- "Family tree" 指家谱。
- "Up a tree" 意为陷入困境或无法脱身。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children love climbing the apple tree in their garden."
- 名词 (抽象概念): "The tree symbolizes strength and endurance in many cultures."
