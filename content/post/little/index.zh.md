## 读音
/lɪtəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 小的，微小的。例如: "The little cat is playing with a ball."
- **副词**: 很少地，略微。例如: "I know little about the subject."
- **名词**: （与a或the连用）小孩，少年。例如: "She has a little boy and a little girl."

## 短语
- "Not much of a little" 表示“不怎么多”或“不怎么重要”。
- "A little goes a long way" 意味着少量的东西就能产生很大效果。

## 例句
- 形容词: "There's a little dog running around in the park."
- 副词: "He spoke so little that I could hardly understand him."
- 名词: "The teacher asked the little ones to sit down."
