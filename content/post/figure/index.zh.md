## 读音
/ˈfɪɡjər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 形状或图形。例如: "The figure of a tree was silhouetted against the sky."
  2. 数字或数量。例如: "The company's revenue figures were impressive."
  3. 人物或人物形象。例如: "She is a prominent figure in local politics."
  4. (人的)身材或体态。例如: "She has a slender figure."
  5. 某人在某领域的地位或角色。例如: "He is a key figure in the field of physics."

- **动词**:
  1. 计算或估计。例如: "We need to figure out how much paint we'll need."
  2. 出现或扮演角色。例如: "She figured prominently in the documentary."
  3. 包括或考虑在内。例如: "The budget figure includes expenses for travel."

- **形容词**:
  1. 图形的，形象的。例如: "figure-skating"（花样滑冰）

## 短语
- "figure out" 意思是计算出或弄明白。例如: "Can you figure out the cost per item?"
- "play a key figure in" 表示在...中起重要作用。例如: "She plays a key figure in the development of new technologies."

## 例句
- 名词: "The painting features a dancing figure in a flowing dress."
- 动词: "I need to figure the dimensions of the room before buying new furniture."
- 形容词: "The figure skater executed a series of complex jumps."

## 同义词
- 形容词时的同义词:
  - shape
  - form
  - image

- 动词时的同义词:
  - calculate
  - estimate
  - reckon
