## Pronunciation
/ˈrəʊbət/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Robot**: A machine designed to perform tasks automatically, often resembling a human or animal in appearance and behavior, typically controlled by a computer program or electronic signals.
- **Artificial intelligence**: A type of machine that can perform tasks requiring human-like intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

### Verb (transitive)
- To operate or control a robot.
- To create or build a robot.

### Verb (intransitive)
- To function or behave like a robot.

## Phrases
- "Robotics" is the study and application of robots.
- "Robotic process automation" uses software to automate repetitive tasks.
- "Self-driving robot" refers to a robot capable of navigating without human intervention.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The factory floor was filled with robots assembling cars."
- **Verb (transitive)**: "The engineer programmed the robot to sort objects efficiently."
- **Verb (intransitive)**: "The robot vacuum cleaner operated autonomously through the house."
