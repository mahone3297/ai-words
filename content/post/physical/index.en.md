## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Physical** (plural: physicals): Refers to the body, its characteristics, or something related to the material or tangible aspects.
- **Physical examination**: A medical check-up that involves inspecting and testing a person's bodily functions.
- **Physical fitness**: The state of being in good health and having a strong, well-developed body.

### Verb
- To undergo a physical: To have a medical examination.
- To do physical work: Engaging in tasks that require manual labor or exercise.

### Adjective
- Physically: Relating to the body, its abilities, or the material world.
- Physically challenged: Having difficulty with mobility or physical abilities.

### Adverb
- Physically: In terms of the body or physical attributes.

## Phrases
- "Physical activity" refers to exercises or movements that engage the body.
- "Physical education" is the teaching of body-related skills and knowledge.
- "Physically demanding job" describes a position requiring significant physical exertion.
