## 读音
/ˈfɪzɪkl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 物理的，与物质或自然法则相关的。例如: "The teacher explained the physical properties of water."
- **名词**: 体检，身体检查。例如: "I have a physical scheduled with my doctor next week."
- **名词**: （美式英语）体育课，体育活动。例如: "We have physical education every Tuesday and Thursday."

## 短语
- "Physical fitness" 指身体健康状况。
- "Physical therapy" 是物理疗法，用来恢复或改善身体功能。
- "Physical attraction" 指身体上的吸引或魅力。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The athlete's physical strength is crucial for winning the competition."
- 名词: "The doctor recommended regular physical activity to improve his health."
- 名词（美式英语）: "In school, we play different games during physical class."

## 同义词
- Natural
- Bodily
- Material
- Physical properties: attributes related to matter and energy
