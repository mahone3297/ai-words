## 读音
/taɪt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 紧的；牢固的；密集的。例如: "The belt is too tight, please loosen it."
- **副词**: 紧紧地；牢固地。例如: "She held onto the rope tightly."
- **名词**: 紧身衣物或空间。例如: "She wore a tight dress to the party."
- **动词**: 使变紧；收紧。例如: "Tighten the screws with a wrench."

## 短语
- "tight fit" 表示刚好合身或空间狭窄。
- "tight-lipped" 形容口风很紧，不愿意透露信息。
- "on a tight budget" 指预算很紧张。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The shoes were so tight that they caused blisters."
- 副词: "He gripped the steering wheel tightly during the storm."
- 名词: "The closet was so full there wasn't an inch of spare space, it was really tight."
- 动词: "She tightened the knot on the backpack to secure it."

- Close-fitting
- Snug
