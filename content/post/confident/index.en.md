## Pronunciation
/ˈkɒnfɪdənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having a feeling of self-assurance, belief in one's abilities, or confidence in a particular situation. For example: "She was confident in her presentation skills."
- **Noun**: A person who is self-assured and certain. For example: "The confident leader inspired the team."

## Phrases
- "Be confident about" means to have faith or trust in something.
- "Display confidence" refers to showing assurance or self-belief.
- "Lack of confidence" implies a lack of self-assurance or belief.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "She felt confident that her hard work would pay off."
- **Noun**: "The confident candidate won the election by a landslide."
- **Sentence**: "He regained his confidence after receiving positive feedback on his project."
