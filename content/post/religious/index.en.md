## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Religious** (adj): Pertaining to matters of faith, belief, or organized religion; devoted or committed to religious practices or beliefs.
   - Definition: A person who is deeply involved in their spiritual or denominational traditions.
   - Example: "The religious community organizes annual charity events."

2. **Religiousness** (n): The quality or state of being religious; the degree of commitment to one's faith.
   - Example: "Her religiousness was a source of strength for her family."

### Verb
- Not commonly used as a standalone verb, but can be found in the phrase "to engage in religious activities."

### Adjective
- Sometimes used to describe something that has a religious character or significance.
   - Example: "A religious painting depicts神圣的 figures."
