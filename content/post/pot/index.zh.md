## 读音
/pɒt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 陶瓷容器，尤指有柄和盖的小锅：例如 "She boiled water in a small pot."
2. 花盆：例如 "The plant was growing in a terracotta pot."
3. 量词，尤指一定数量的钱：例如 "He won a pot of money in the lottery."

- **动词**:
1. 把…放入锅中烹调：例如 "Pot the vegetables before serving."
2. （非正式）积累（金钱）：例如 "He's been potting away at his savings."

## 短语
- "Pot luck" 意为偶然的运气，尤指聚餐时不确定的食物。
- "Potter around" 表示漫无目的地闲逛或做事。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need a bigger pot to cook soup for the family."
- 动词: "She potted the new plants in decorative ceramic pots."
- 量词: "The winner took home a pot of $10,000."
