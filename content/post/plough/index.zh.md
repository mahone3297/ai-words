## 读音
/plaʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种农具，通常有锐利的金属刀刃，用来翻耕土壤。例如: "The farmer was using a heavy iron plough to prepare the field for planting."
- **动词**: 指使用犁进行耕作或艰难前进。例如: "The horse was ploughing through the muddy field."
- **形容词**: 指与犁有关的或耕过的。例如: "The ploughed field looked ready for sowing seeds."

## 短语
- "Ploughing the land" 意味着耕种土地。
- "To plough one's way through" 指奋力前进或努力突破困难。

## 例句
- 名词: "The old wooden plough in the museum displayed how farming used to be done."
- 动词: "He spent the whole day ploughing the fields in preparation for the spring planting."
- 形容词: "The ploughed field glistened with moisture after the rain."
