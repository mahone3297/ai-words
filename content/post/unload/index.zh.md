## 读音
/unˈlōd/

## 释义
- **动词**: 将货物、物品或信息从运输工具、容器或存储位置移出或卸下。例如: "They started unloading the cargo from the ship."
- **名词**: 卸货；解除负担或压力。例如: "After a long day, he felt the need to unload his worries."

## 短语
- "Unload a truck" 意味着从卡车上卸下货物。
- "Unload your mind" 指的是释放或分享心中的思绪和压力。

## 例句
- 动词: "The dockworkers were busy unloading boxes from the container."
- 名词: "She needed to unload her problems onto a friend."

## 同义词
- Offload
- Discharge
- Empty
- Remove
- Dismantle (in the context of unloading equipment or structures)
