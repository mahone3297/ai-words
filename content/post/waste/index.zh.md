## 读音
/weɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 不必要的或废弃的物质；损失或消耗的东西。例如: "There's a lot of waste in the production process."
- **动词**: 浪费；不加利用。例如: "Don't waste your time on things that don't matter."
- **形容词**: 无用的；废弃的。例如: "The abandoned house was in a state of waste."

## 短语
- "Waste not, want not" 意思是珍惜资源，避免浪费。
- "Food waste" 指的是食物的浪费。

## 例句
- 名词: "We need to reduce industrial waste to protect the environment."
- 动词: "Don't waste your energy arguing with them; it's pointless."
- 形容词: "The land had been left in a wasteland after years of neglect."
