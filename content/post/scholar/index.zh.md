## 读音
/ˈskɑːlər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 有学问的人，学者；尤指在学院或大学接受教育的学生。例如: "He is a renowned scholar in the field of literature."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 学术的，学者的；具有广泛知识的。例如: "The scholar's detailed analysis showed his profound knowledge."

## 短语
- "Scholarship" 指学术奖学金或学者的才智。
- "Full scholar" 指获得全额奖学金的学生。
- "To act like a scholar" 意为表现得很有学识或认真对待学习。

## 例句
- 名词: "She was awarded a prestigious scholarship to study at Oxford University."
- 形容词: "His scholarly articles are widely read in academic circles."
