## 读音
/melt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 融化；变为液体。例如: "The chocolate melted in the sun."
  - （使）消融，（使）熔化。例如: "The ice cube melted in the glass of water."
  - （情感、紧张等）消失，缓和。例如: "Her anger melted away when she saw his smile."
  
- **名词**:
  - 融化的过程。例如: "The metal undergoes a high temperature melt during manufacturing."

## 短语
- "Melt down" 指物质完全融化或崩溃，也可指情绪失控。例如: "The nuclear reactor had a partial melt down."
- "Melt into" 意为融入，消失在...中。例如: "She melted into the crowd and was soon out of sight."

## 例句
- 动词: "The snow on the roof began to melt with the spring sunshine."
- 名词: "The goldsmith monitored the temperature of the gold melt carefully."
