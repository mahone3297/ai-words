## 读音
/ˈvɒkəbələri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 词汇，词汇量，全部的词汇或知识。例如: "She has an extensive vocabulary in French." 
- **名词**: 词汇表，词汇书，特别是教学或学习中的词汇资源。例如: "The students were given a new vocabulary list for the week."

## 短语
- "扩大词汇" means to increase one's vocabulary.
- "词汇学习" refers to the process of learning new words.

## 例句
- "His large vocabulary impressed the interviewer during the job interview."
- "The children are learning new vocabulary through reading stories."
