## Pronunciation
/kənˈfɜːrəns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
    - A meeting where people discuss a particular subject or business, often formally or professionally. Example: "The annual science conference attracted experts from around the world."
    - An event or gathering where ideas, information, or opinions are exchanged. Example: "We had a conference call with our clients this morning."

- **Verb**: To hold or participate in a conference. Example: "They scheduled a conference to finalize the project details."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing a situation or discussion as involving many people or viewpoints. Example: "A conference-style debate encouraged open dialogue."

## Phrases
- "Attend a conference" means to go to a meeting or event for professional purposes.
- "Conference room" is a designated area for meetings and discussions.
- "Conference schedule" refers to the planned events and sessions at a conference.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The educators convened for a conference to discuss new teaching methods."
- **Verb**: "I'll be presenting my research findings at the upcoming academic conference."
- **Adjective**: "The company's annual conference fosters a collaborative atmosphere among employees."
