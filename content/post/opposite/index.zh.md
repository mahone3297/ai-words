## 读音
/ˈɑːpəzɪt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 相反的，对立的。例如: "The opposite of love is not hate, but indifference."
- **名词**: 反面，对立面。例如: "The opposite side of the road."
- **介词**: 表示在...的对面。例如: "The store is located opposite the library."

## 短语
- "on the opposite side" 在另一边
- "in contrast to" 与...对比
- "the opposite effect" 相反的效果

## 例句
- 形容词: "These two colors have opposite tones on the color wheel."
- 名词: "The opposite of hot is cold."
- 介词: "There's a park opposite my house."
