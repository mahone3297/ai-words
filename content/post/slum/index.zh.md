## 读音
/slʌm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 贫民窟；贫民区。例如: "The city government is trying to improve living conditions in the slums."
- **动词**: （非正式）居住在贫民窟。例如: "They had to slum it for a while after losing their jobs."

## 短语
- "slum it" 意味着忍受恶劣的条件或简陋的住所。
- "slum area" 指的是贫困和破败的住宅区。

## 例句
- 名词: "The documentary shed light on life in the slums of Mumbai."
- 动词: "They had to slum it in a small, run-down apartment until they found something better."

## 同义词
- Ghetto
- Shantytown
- Squatter settlement
- Informal settlement
- Poverty-stricken area
