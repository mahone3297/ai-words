## 读音
/liːv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    - 离开；出发：例如 "I'm leaving for work now."（我现在要去上班了。）
    - 遗留；留下：例如 "Please leave your keys on the table."（请把钥匙留在桌子上。）
    - 放弃；抛弃：例如 "She decided to leave her job and travel the world."（她决定放弃工作去环游世界。）
    - 给予；赋予：例如 "The teacher left a lasting impression on the students."（老师给学生留下了深刻的印象。）

- **名词**:
    - 离开；许可：例如 "May I have your permission to leave early?"（我可以早点离开吗？）
    - 休假：例如 "I'm taking a two-week leave next month."（我下个月要休两周假。）

## 短语
- "Take leave of" 意为告别：例如 "It was time to take leave of our friends and continue our journey."（是时候告别我们的朋友继续旅行了。）
- "On leave" 表示正在休假：例如 "He's currently on leave from his job."（他现在正在休假。）

## 例句
- 动词: "After finishing his presentation, he left the room."（做完报告后，他离开了房间。）
- 名词: "She requested a sick leave due to her flu."（因为感冒，她请了病假。）

## 同义词
- Depart
- Exit
- Go away
- Abandon
- Grant permission (for leaving)
