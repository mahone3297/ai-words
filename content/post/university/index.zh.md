## 读音
/ˌjuːnɪˈvɜːsəti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 高等教育机构，通常提供学士、硕士和博士学位课程。例如: "She is studying medicine at Cambridge University."
- **名词**: 全体大学师生员工的集体。例如: "The university community is diverse and vibrant."

## 短语
- "University education" 指高等教育。
- "State university" 指由政府资助的公立大学。
- "Private university" 指由私人或非政府组织运营的大学。

## 例句
- "She was awarded a scholarship to attend Harvard University."
- "After graduating from university, he started working for a tech company."
- "The university's library contains an extensive collection of rare books."

## 同义词
- College
- Academy
- Institution of higher learning
