## 读音
/ˈweðər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 天气；气候。例如: "The weather was sunny and warm."
- **动词**: 经受住，度过（尤指艰难时期）。例如: "We weathered the storm without any damage."

## 短语
- "Weather forecast" 天气预报。
- "Weather conditions" 气象条件。
- "To weather the crisis" 度过危机。

## 例句
- 名词: "Check the weather forecast before you plan your outdoor activities."
- 动词: "Our company has weathered several economic downturns over the years."
