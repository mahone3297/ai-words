## Pronunciation
/weather/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The state of the atmosphere with respect to temperature, humidity, cloudiness, wind, and precipitation, especially as regarded with regard to its effects on human activities and events. ["Weather conditions are quite unpredictable today."](https://www.example.com/weather)
- **Verb**: To describe or talk about the weather. ["Let's discuss the weather over coffee."](https://www.example.com/weather-discussion)
- **Adjective**: Characterizing the atmospheric conditions at a particular time or place. ["Sunny weather made for a perfect picnic day."](https://www.example.com/sunny-weather-picnic)

## Phrases
- "Check the weather forecast" means to look up the expected weather conditions for the future.
- "Good weather for outdoor activities" indicates ideal conditions for being outside.
- "Weather permitting" suggests that something will happen only if the weather is suitable.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The weather has been quite chilly lately, so make sure to wear a jacket."
- **Verb**: "He always checks the weather before deciding whether to go cycling."
- **Adjective**: "We had beautiful weather during our vacation in Hawaii."
