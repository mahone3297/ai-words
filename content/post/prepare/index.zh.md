## 读音
/prɪˈpeər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 预备，准备。例如: "I need to prepare for my exam."
2. 制作，调制。例如: "She is preparing a delicious dinner."
3. 使适应，使适合。例如: "The athletes are being prepared for the competition."

## 短语
- "Prepare for something" 意为为某事做准备。例如: "You should prepare for the possibility of rain on your outdoor event."
- "Prepare oneself" 表示自我准备。例如: "He is preparing himself mentally for the interview."

## 例句
- 我正在准备明天的会议资料。("I am preparing the materials for tomorrow's meeting.")
- 她正在厨房里准备晚餐。("She is preparing dinner in the kitchen.")
- 孩子们必须学会如何为自己的未来做准备。("Children need to learn how to prepare for their future.")
