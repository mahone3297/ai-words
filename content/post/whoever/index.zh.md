## 读音
/hwərˈɛvər/

## 释义
- **代词**: 任何人，无论谁。例如: " Whoever breaks the rule will be punished."
- **连词**: 引导主语从句，意为“无论谁”。例如: "Whoever finishes first gets the prize."

## 短语
- "Whoever said that was wrong." 意为“无论谁说那个都是错的。”

## 例句
- 代词: "Whoever left their bag here should come and claim it."
- 连词: "Whoever wants to join the team must sign up before Friday."
