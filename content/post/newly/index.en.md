## Word: Newly

## Pronunciation
/ˈnjuːli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Recently; just now; very recently. It describes an action that has taken place not long ago or something that has been acquired or come into existence not long before the present time.

### Example Sentences
- _Adverb_: "They have newly moved into the neighborhood."
- "She's wearing a newly bought dress for the event."
- "The company is under newly appointed management."

## Phrases
- "Newly married": Describes a couple who have recently married.
- "Newly arrived": Refers to someone or something that has just reached a destination.
- "Newly released": Indicates something, such as a book, movie, or product, that has just been made available to the public.
