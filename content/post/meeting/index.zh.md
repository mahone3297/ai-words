## 读音
/meˈtɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一次集会，会议，或会面。通常指为了讨论特定议题或进行合作而聚集在一起的场合。例如: "We have a staff meeting every Monday morning."
- **动词**: 遇见，与…会面。例如: "I'll be meeting her at the airport later today."

## 短语
- "Business meeting" 指商业或工作相关的会议。
- "Meeting room" 是举行会议的房间。
- "By chance meeting" 指偶然相遇。

## 例句
- 名词: "The board members are preparing for an important meeting with investors."
- 动词: "I accidentally met an old friend from college on my way to the coffee shop."
