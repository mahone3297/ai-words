## 读音
/kɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 踢的动作或结果；打击。例如: "He gave the ball a powerful kick."
- **动词**: 踢，踹。例如: "The goalkeeper kicked the ball out of the penalty area."
- **形容词**: 引起不快或恼怒的。例如: "His rude behavior was a real kick in the teeth."

## 短语
- "Give someone a kick" 意为刺激某人或使某人振奋。
- "Kick off" 可以表示开始（活动、比赛等）。
- "Get a kick out of something" 意味从某事中获得乐趣。

## 例句
- 名词: "The soccer player scored with a well-placed kick."
- 动词: "She accidentally kicked over the vase while walking."
- 形容词: "It was a kick to hear such negative feedback from the boss."

## 同义词
- Punch
- Strike
- Jab
- Boot
- Tap (in the context of kicking)
