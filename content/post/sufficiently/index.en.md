## Pronunciation
/səˈfɪʃli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: To an adequate or sufficient degree; adequately or enough. It modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. For example, "The work was done sufficiently well."

## Phrases
- "Sufficiently large" means ample or adequate in size.
- "Sufficiently qualified" implies having the necessary skills or credentials.
- "He speaks English sufficiently well to communicate with foreigners."

## Example Sentences
- "The project was completed sufficiently on time."
- "The weather was warm enough, but not sufficiently hot for swimming."
- "She studied hard, and her performance was sufficiently impressive."
