## Pronunciation
/wɒnt/

## Definitions
### Verb
1. **Noun**: The act or desire to have or possess something, often with a sense of need or craving. For example: "I really want that new phone."
2. **Verb**: To feel a strong desire or intention to do something. For example: "She wants to travel the world."
3. **Verb**: To request or ask for something. For example: "Can I please have some water? I'm so thirsty."

## Phrases
- "Want something" expresses a desire for an object or action.
- "Don't want" implies refusal or lack of interest.
- "More than want to" indicates a strong inclination or eagerness.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She wants a healthier lifestyle."
- **Verb**: "I want to quit my job and start my own business."
- **Request**: "I don't want any cake; I'm on a diet."
