## 读音
/wɒnt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 想要；希望得到。例如: "I really want a new computer."
  - 需要；必须。例如: "The baby wants feeding now."
  - 愿意；打算。例如: "I don't want to go out tonight."

## 短语
- "Want something badly" 表示非常想要某物。
- "Don't want for anything" 意为不缺少任何东西，生活富足。

## 例句
- 动词（想要）: "She really wants to improve her English skills."
- 动词（需要）: "The car wants a new set of tires."
- 动词（愿意）: "He doesn't want to discuss the issue further."
