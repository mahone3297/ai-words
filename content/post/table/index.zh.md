## 读音
/ˈteɪbl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一张平的家具，通常有四条腿，用来放东西。例如: "There's a vase on the table."
- **名词**: 数据或议题的展示，如会议上的议程。例如: "The proposal is still on the table for discussion."
- **动词**: 将（议题）提交讨论。例如: "The committee decided to table the budget until next month."
- **动词**: 在游戏中将（牌）面朝下放在桌子上。例如: "He tabled his cards, revealing a winning hand."

## 短语
- "Set the table" 意为摆放餐具准备吃饭。
- "Turn the tables" 表示形势反转或逆袭。例如: "She managed to turn the tables and defeat her opponent."

## 例句
- 名词: "We need to buy a new dining table for our kitchen."
- 动词（议题）: "The council decided to table the new zoning proposal until further research was done."
- 动词（牌类游戏）: "In poker, you can only table your cards when it's your turn."
