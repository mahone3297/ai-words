## Pronunciation
/wɛn/

## Definitions
### Adverb
- **Refers to**: A word that modifies a verb, an adjective, or another adverb, indicating time, frequency, or manner.
- **Meaning**: At the time of; during the period; as to the time; accordingly; thus.

### Preposition
- **Refers to**: A word that introduces a clause, expressing the relationship of one noun or pronoun to another or to a point in space or time.
- **Meaning**: Indicating the time at which an action occurs; during which.

## Phrases
- "When it rains" signifies the time of day or season when rain is expected.
- "When I was young" refers to a past time.
- "When I arrive" indicates the anticipated time of arrival.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "I'll meet you when you finish your work."
- **Preposition**: "Please call me when you get home."
- "The party starts when the clock strikes eight."
