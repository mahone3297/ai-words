## 读音
/wen/

## 释义
- **连词**: 引导时间状语从句，表示“在…时候”或“当…时”。例如: "I'll call you when I arrive home."
- **疑问词**: 询问时间。例如: "When did the meeting start?"

## 短语
- "When in Rome, do as the Romans do" 意为入乡随俗。
- "When it rains, it pours" 表示不好的事情往往会接踵而至。

## 例句
- 连词: "She started studying when she was just five years old."
- 疑问词: "When will we have the next meeting?"

## 同义词
- At what time
- On what occasion
- During which period
- In what circumstances
- Provided that (在某种条件下)
