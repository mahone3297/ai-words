## 读音
/ˈsteɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 车站：公共交通系统中的一个站点，如火车站、地铁站或公交车站。例如: "I need to catch the train at the next station."
  2. 岗位：工作或服务的特定位置。例如: "He is on duty at the police station."
  3. 驻地：军队或政府机构的驻扎地点。例如: "The embassy has a large station in the capital city."
  4. 广播电视台：电视或广播节目的发射地点。例如: "She works for a local radio station."
  5. 星球或卫星上的固定地点：如太空站。例如: "The astronauts live and work on the International Space Station."

## 短语
- "train station" 火车站
- "police station" 警察局
- "TV station" 电视台

## 例句
- 名词: "There's a convenience store right outside the train station."
- 岗位: "He was assigned to a remote weather station in Antarctica."
- 广播电视台: "The news broadcast originates from our main radio station."
