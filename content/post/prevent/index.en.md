## Pronunciation
/preɪˈvent/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To stop something from happening or developing; to keep something from occurring. For example: "The security measures were put in place to prevent theft."
- **Noun**: The act or process of preventing something; a measure taken to avoid a particular outcome. For example: "The prevention of disease is a public health priority."

## Phrases
- "Prevent a disaster" means to try to stop a catastrophic event from happening.
- "Preventive measures" are actions taken to anticipate and guard against potential problems.
- "Preventive healthcare" refers to services that aim to maintain good health and avoid illnesses.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The new security system was designed to prevent unauthorized access."
- **Noun**: "Her daily exercise routine is a key part of her preventive lifestyle."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "He tried to prevent the argument from escalating."
