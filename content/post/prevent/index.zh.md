## 读音
/prɪˈvent/

## 释义
- **动词**: 阻止，防止某事发生。例如: "Wearing a helmet can prevent head injuries."
- **名词**: 预防，防范措施。例如: "Regular exercise is a good prevent for heart disease."

## 短语
- "Prevent something from happening" 阻止某事发生。
- "Preventive measures" 预防措施。

## 例句
- 动词: "The security system is designed to prevent unauthorized access."
- 名词: "Good hygiene habits are an important prevent in controlling the spread of germs."

## 同义词
- Stop
- Halt
- Ward off
- Avoid
- Obstruct
