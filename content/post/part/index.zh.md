## 读音
/pɑːrt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 部分，组件，角色，份额。例如: "This is only a small part of the puzzle."（这只是拼图的一小部分。）
- **动词 (v)**: 分离，分开，扮演角色。例如: "She parted her hair in the middle."（她把头发从中分开了。）

## 短语
- "Play a part" 意为参与，扮演角色。例如: "He played an important part in the project."
- "Do your part" 意为尽你的一份力。例如: "Everyone needs to do their part to keep the environment clean."
