## Pronunciation
/həˈloʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A term used to greet or address someone. For example: "She waved a friendly hello at the waiter."

### Verb
- To say "hello" or greet someone. For example: "He greeted the guests with a hearty hello."

### Adjective
- Expressing friendliness or familiarity. For example: "The hotel staff had a friendly hello ready for each guest."

### Interjection
- Used to draw attention or initiate a conversation. For example: "Oh, hello there, how's it going?"

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to acknowledge or greet them.
- "Goodbye, hello" describes a routine where one leaves and then returns.
- "Hello, my old friend" is a nostalgic greeting.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her greeting was warm and genuine."
- **Verb**: "He said hello to the new neighbors next door."
- **Adjective**: "The children exchanged hellos as they passed by."

## Synonyms
- Hi
- Hey
- Greetings
- Hail
- Salutations
- Yo (informal)
- Howdy (informal, mainly American)

### Note: The use of "Hello" as a verb is often informal, and "greet" is a more standard alternative in formal contexts.
