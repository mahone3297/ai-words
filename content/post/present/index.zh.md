## 读音
/prɪˈzent/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 提供的礼物；赠品。例如: "She received a beautiful necklace as a birthday present."
  - 现在；当前。例如: "Live in the present and make the most of every day."

- **动词**:
  - 呈现；展示。例如: "The speaker presented his ideas clearly to the audience."
  - 介绍；引见。例如: "May I present my colleague, Mr. Smith?"
  - 出席；到场。例如: "All the team members were present at the meeting."

## 短语
- "Present something to someone" 意思是将某物赠予或展示给某人。
- "Be present at an event" 表示参加某个活动或场合。

## 例句
- 名词: "On Christmas morning, children eagerly open their presents."
- 动词: "The model presented her collection on the runway."
- 出席: "All candidates must be present for the job interview."
