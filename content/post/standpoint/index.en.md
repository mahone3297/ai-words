## Pronunciation
/ˈstændpɔɪnt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A position or viewpoint from which something is considered or judged. "His perspective is from a high moral standpoint." 2. An opinion or belief held by a person or group. "Their political standpoint is clear."
- **Verb**: To adopt or assume a particular point of view. "She tried to understand his standpoint on the issue."

## Phrases
- "From their standpoint"
- "Take a different standpoint"
- "Considering someone's standpoint"
- "Standing on one's feet morally"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The debate centered on the different scientific viewpoints, each side presenting their own standpoint."
- **Verb**: "It's important to understand where your colleagues are coming from when discussing their standpoint on the project."
- "She defended her decision, explaining it from her unique standpoint."
