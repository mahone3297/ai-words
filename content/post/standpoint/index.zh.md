## 读音
/ˈstændpɔɪnt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 立场，观点，看法。指个人或团体看待事物的基础或出发点。例如: "From my standpoint, the project should focus more on user experience."
- **名词**: 观察点，观测位置。指观察事物或景色的具体地点。例如: "The mountain climber chose a safe standpoint to admire the view."

## 短语
- "Take someone's standpoint" 意为站在某人的立场考虑问题。
- "In the light of one's standpoint" 表示从某人的观点来看。

## 例句
- 名词: "We need to understand the customer's standpoint to improve our product."
- 名词: "The photographer found a high standpoint to capture the city skyline."
