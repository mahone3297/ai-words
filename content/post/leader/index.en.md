## Word: Leader

## Pronunciation
/ˈliːdər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who leads or commands a group, organization, or country. They typically possess strong influence and decision-making abilities. For example: "The company's new leader has a background in finance."
   - _Example Sentence_: "The team elected John as their leader because of his strategic vision."
   
- **Noun (figurative)**: The foremost or leading part of something. For instance, the leading edge of technology or the leader in the race.
   - _Example Sentence_: "This tech firm is considered a leader in the field of artificial intelligence."

## Phrases
- "Take the lead": To assume control or guide others in a task or situation.
- "Follow the leader": A children's game where one player acts as the leader and others mimic their actions.
- "Thought leader": A person recognized as an authority in a specific area or industry, shaping opinions and trends through innovative ideas.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (person)**: "She was a charismatic leader who inspired her team to achieve remarkable results."
- **Noun (figurative)**: "Their product became the market leader within just six months of its launch."
