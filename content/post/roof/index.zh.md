## 读音
/roʊf/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 建筑物顶部的覆盖部分，通常指防止雨水和保护内部结构的结构。例如: "The roof of the house needs repairs after the storm."
- **动词 (v)**: 在某物上建造或提供屋顶。例如: "They are roofing the new garage this week."

## 短语
- "Under the roof" 指在同一屋檐下，常用来表示住在一起或共同生活。例如: "We all live under the same roof."
- "Roof over one's head" 指有地方住，基本的生活保障。例如: "Having a roof over your head is essential."

## 例句
- 名词: "The roof was made of durable tiles that had lasted for decades."
- 动词: "The workers were busy roofing the extension to the house."
