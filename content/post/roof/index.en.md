## Pronunciation
/rʊf/

## Definitions
### Noun
A roof is a covering or structure that projects over and protects the top or upper part of a building or vehicle, typically consisting of pitched or flat materials like tiles, shingles, or metal.

### Verb
To roof (something) is to construct or install a roof on a building or structure.

### Adjective
Roof-like or having the qualities of a roof, as in "a sloping roof."

### Adverb
In a roofing manner, referring to the act of roofing or construction.

## Phrases
- "Cover the roof with tiles"
- "Repair the roof after the storm"
- "A flat roof is suitable for urban areas"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The old house needed a new roof after years of neglect."
- **Verb**: "The workers were busy roofing the shed."
- **Adjective**: "The modern apartment had a sleek, glass-roofed atrium."
- **Adverb**: "The contractor carefully inspected the roof's condition."
