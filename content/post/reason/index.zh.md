## 读音
/ˈriːzn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 原因，理由。指导致某种结果或行动的因素。例如: "The reason for his absence was illness."
- **动词**: （与for连用）解释，说明（原因）。例如: "She couldn't reason out why he was upset."

## 短语
- "For reasons unknown" 意为由于未知的原因。
- "Give a reason for something" 意为给出某事的理由。

## 例句
- 名词: "There are many reasons why people learn a second language."
- 动词: "Can you reason why she made that decision?"

- Cause
- Motive
- Explanation
- Grounds
- Justification
