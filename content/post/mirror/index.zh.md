## 读音
/mɪˈrɔːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种反射光线，使物体成像的平滑表面。例如: "She checked her appearance in the mirror before leaving."
- **动词**: 反映或模仿（某个事物）。例如: "His actions mirrored his father's values."

## 短语
- "Reflect in the mirror" 意为在镜子里反射。
- "Mirror image" 指的是与原物完全对称的形象。

## 例句
- 名词: "The dressing table had a large, ornate mirror above it."
- 动词: "Her dance moves mirrored those of her partner perfectly."
