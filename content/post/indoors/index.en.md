## Word: Indoors

## Pronunciation
/ˌɪnˈdoʊrz/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Inside a building or house; not outside. It describes a location that is under a roof or within enclosed premises.

Example Sentence: "The children decided to play indoors because of the rain."

## Phrases
- "Stay indoors": To remain inside, typically referring to avoiding going out due to weather conditions, illness, or other reasons.
- "Indoor activities": Activities that take place inside buildings, such as reading, board games, or exercising at home.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Due to the severe snowstorm, we had to cancel our plans and spend the whole weekend indoors."
- "She prefers indoor sports like badminton and table tennis over outdoor ones."

## Synonyms
- Inside
- Within
- In the house
- Under cover
- Inside the building
- Indoor (as a noun, referring to indoor spaces or activities)
