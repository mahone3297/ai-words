## 读音
/mætər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 物质；事情；问题；重要性。例如: "Matter is anything that has mass and takes up space."，"The matter at hand needs immediate attention."
- **形容词**: 实质性的；重要的。例如: "That's a matter of great concern."

## 短语
- "As a matter of fact" 意为"事实上"。
- "It doesn't matter" 意为"没关系"，"无所谓"。
- "A matter of time" 指的是某事肯定会发生，只是时间问题。

## 例句
- 名词: "The universe is made up of various forms of matter."
- 形容词: "Your opinion on this matter is highly valued."
