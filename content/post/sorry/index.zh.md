## 读音
/səˈriː/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 表示歉意或后悔。例如: "I'm sorry for being late."
- **动词**: 向别人道歉或表达遗憾。例如: "He apologized with sincerity, saying 'I'm sorry'."
- **感叹词**: 用于引起注意或表示惊讶。例如: "Sorry, did you say something?"

## 短语
- "Sorry about that" 用于表示对不便或错误的歉意。
- "Sorry not sorry" 表示并不后悔自己所说或所做的事。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She looked sorry when she realized she had broken the vase."
- 动词: "He immediately said sorry after realizing he had offended her."
- 感叹词: "Sorry, I didn't mean to interrupt."
