## Pronunciation
/səˈraɪ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Expressing regret, remorse, or apology for an action or fault. For example: "I'm sorry for being late."
- **Verb**: To feel or express regret, guilt, or歉意 for something one has done or said. For example: "She apologized for her rude behavior."
- **Interjection**: Expressing regret, sorrow, or sympathy. For example: "Oh, I'm so sorry to hear that."

## Phrases
- "I'm truly sorry" is a sincere expression of regret.
- "Sorry, I can't make it" is used to decline an invitation with an apology.
- "Sorry, I didn't mean to hurt your feelings" is a common way to acknowledge unintentional harm.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "I feel terrible; I didn't realize I would cause such a mess."
- **Verb**: "After realizing his mistake, he immediately apologized to his friend."
- **Interjection**: "Sorry, but I have to go now."
