## 读音
/ˈsʌdn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 突然的，出乎意料的。例如: "A sudden storm caught us by surprise."
- **副词**: 突然地，忽然。例如: "She stopped talking suddenly and left the room."

## 短语
- "suddenly" 常用来修饰动词，表示动作发生的突然性。
- "a sudden change" 意为突然的变化。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The sudden noise made us jump."
- 副词: "He stood up suddenly and knocked over the chair."
