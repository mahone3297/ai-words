## Pronunciation
/sʌd(ə)n/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Occurring or coming without warning; unexpected. For example: "A sudden storm hit the city."
- **Adverb**: In a sudden or unexpected manner. For example: "She laughed suddenly."
- **Noun**: An instance of something happening unexpectedly. For example: "The sudden silence made everyone turn around."

## Phrases
- "Sudden change" refers to a rapid transformation or alteration.
- "Out of the blue" is used to describe a sudden occurrence with no prior indication.
- "Caught off guard" means someone was not prepared for a sudden event.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Her sudden departure left us all surprised."
- **Adverb**: "He jumped up from his chair, acting suddenly."
- **Noun**: "A sudden loss of power disrupted the concert."
