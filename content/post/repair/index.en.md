## Pronunciation
/rɪˈper/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The act of fixing, mending, or restoring something that is broken or damaged: *The mechanic specializes in car repairs.*
2. The state of being in good condition or working order: *The house needed significant repairs before we could move in.*

### Verb
1. To restore to a good or usable condition after damage or decay: *They repaired the roof after a storm.*
2. To fix or mend something: *I'll repair your bicycle if you can't figure it out yourself.*

### Adjective
1. Suitable for restoration or improvement: *The antique furniture required minor repair.*
2. Designed to be easily repaired: *A repair-friendly appliance has easy-to-access parts.*
