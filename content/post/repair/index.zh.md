## 读音
/riːˈpeər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 修复，修理（损坏的东西）。例如: "I need to repair my bicycle after the accident."
2. 弥补，改正（错误或损失）。例如: "She tried to repair the damage caused by her mistake."

- **名词**:
1. 修理，修补工作。例如: "The house needs some urgent repairs before we can sell it."
2. 维修服务。例如: "The company offers free repairs for the first year."

## 短语
- "In need of repair" 表示某物需要修理。例如: "The old building is in desperate need of repair."
- "Repair work" 指进行修理或修复的过程。例如: "The repair work on the road will take several weeks."

## 例句
- 动词: "He spent the weekend repairing the leaky faucet."
- 名词: "The mechanic provided an estimate for the car repairs."
