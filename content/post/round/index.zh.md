## 读音
/raʊnd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 形状圆形的物体。例如: "The table has a round surface."
  - 数量接近某个整数但略高或略低的数值。例如: "The bill came to around $50."
  - 轮次，回合。例如: "It's my turn in the next round of the game."
  - 圆形区域或部分。例如: "The town square is surrounded by roundabouts."

- **形容词**:
  - 圆形的，弯曲的。例如: "The moon was full and round."
  - 全面的，周全的。例如: "They discussed the issue from all round angles."

- **动词**:
  - 使成圆形。例如: "She rounded the corners of the paper."
  - 大约估计。例如: "He rounded the number to 100."

## 短语
- "round up" 意为把数量向上舍入到最接近的整数。例如: "They rounded up the sheep into the pen."
- "round off" 指对数字进行四舍五入。例如: "Let's round off the total to $10."

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were playing with a round ball in the park."
- 形容词: "The round moon shone brightly in the night sky."
- 动词: "She rounded the number to make calculations easier."
