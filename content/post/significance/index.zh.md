## 读音
/ˈsɪɡnɪfɪkəns/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 重要性；意义：The significance of this discovery cannot be overstated.
    - （统计学）显著性：The study's results reached statistical significance.
    - （符号、标志等的）含义：The flag has great symbolic significance.

## 短语
- "significance level" 在统计学中指确定结果是否显著的阈值。
- "loss of significance" 指在数学或计算中由于舍入误差导致的重要性降低。

## 例句
- 名词: "The significance of this agreement lies in its potential to resolve long-standing conflicts."
- 动词（常与介词to连用）: "This finding is significant to our understanding of the disease."

## 同义词
- Importance
- Relevance
- Consequence
- Significance itself can also be a self-synonym, depending on the context.
- Noteworthiness
