## 读音
/strɛtʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 伸展；拉长：例如: "He stretched his arms after a long drive."
  2. 延续；绵延：例如: "The beach stretches for miles along the coast."
  3. 尽力；勉强：例如: "She stretched her budget to buy the new laptop."
  
- **名词**:
  1. 一段距离或时间：例如: "There's a long stretch of empty road ahead."
  2. 拉伸动作：例如: "The yoga instructor demonstrated a series of stretches."

## 短语
- "Stretch one's legs" 意为站起来走动，放松一下。
- "Stretch the truth" 指夸大事实或说谎。
- "In a stretch" 可能表示在一段时间内，例如: "I can finish the project in a stretch if needed."

## 例句
- 动词: "After sitting for hours, I need to get up and stretch my legs."
- 名词: "The athlete performed several stretches before the match to warm up."
