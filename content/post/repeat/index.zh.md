## 读音
/rɪˈpit/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 重复；再说一遍。例如: "Can you please repeat the question?" 
2. 复制；再现。例如: "The song is often repeated on the radio."
3. （在时间上）再次发生。例如: "The same mistake repeated itself during the exam."

## 短语
- "Repeat after me" 让别人跟着你说同样的话。
- "Repeat the process" 重复这个过程。

## 例句
- 动词: "I missed what you said, could you repeat it slowly?" 
- "The teacher asked us to repeat the multiplication tables."
- "The pattern repeats every four bars in the music."

- Reiterate
- Redo
