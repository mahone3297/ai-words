## 读音
/ˈpraɪmərəli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 主要地，首要地，基本上。例如: "Her primary concern is the safety of her children."
- **形容词**: 主要的，基本的，最初的。例如: "The primary colors are red, blue, and yellow."

## 短语
- "primary source" 原始来源，第一手资料。
- "primary education" 初等教育，小学教育。

## 例句
- 副词: "His job is primarily focused on research and development."
- 形容词: "The primary function of a heart is to pump blood."
