## 读音
/wɪnd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 自然界的风。例如: "The wind was blowing strongly from the west."
  - 风扇或风力发电机产生的风。例如: "I turned on the electric fan to cool down the room."
  - 转动或缠绕的动作。例如: "He wound the string around the pole."

- **动词**:
  - 吹动或使吹动。例如: "The wind blew the leaves off the trees."
  - 缠绕或卷起。例如: "Wind the thread onto the spool."
  - 引申为逐渐增加或发展。例如: "The project has been winding up for several months."

## 短语
- "Be at the mercy of the wind" 意味受风力摆布，无法控制。
- "Wind up" 可以表示结束或完成，如 "They are winding up the meeting."

## 例句
- 名词: "The strong wind made it difficult to walk along the coast."
- 动词: "She carefully wound the scarf around her neck."
- 引申义: "As the company winds down, employees are looking for new jobs."
