## Pronunciation
/rɪˈep/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To gather (harvest) crops, especially after they have ripened; to gather the fruits or rewards of one's labor, effort, or actions. (n) The act of harvesting or the gathered produce.

## Phrases
- "Reap the benefits" means to enjoy the advantages or rewards of something.
- "Reaping season" refers to the time when crops are harvested.
- "Reap what you sow" is a proverb suggesting that the consequences of one's actions will come back to them.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The farmer spent all day reaping the wheat field."
- **Noun**: "After months of hard work, he enjoyed the bountiful harvest he had reaped."
- "His dedication at work eventually led to a successful reaping of promotions."
