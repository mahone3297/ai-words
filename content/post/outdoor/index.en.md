## Word: Outdoor

## Pronunciation
/ˌaʊtˈdoʊr/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to, taking place in, or suitable for the open air or for use outdoors. For example: "We're planning an outdoor picnic this weekend."
- **Noun**: An activity that takes place outside; also, something designed for use outside. For example: "Hiking is my favorite outdoor activity." "The new furniture set is perfect for our outdoor space."

### Adjective Definitions and Examples
- **Definition**: Describing an event, activity, or item that occurs or is located in the open air, not under a roof or within a building.
  - _Example Sentence_: "They organized an outdoor concert on the lawn."
  
### Noun Definitions and Examples
- **Definition**: Referring to an activity that happens in the open air or the area where such activities occur.
  - _Example Sentence_: "Playing sports like soccer and basketball are great outdoor pursuits."
  - _Example Sentence (as a reference to an object)_ : "The outdoor lighting really enhanced the garden's beauty."

## Phrases
- "Outdoor recreation": Activities done for enjoyment, exercise, or leisure in natural environments or open-air settings.
- "Outdoor enthusiast": A person who loves spending time and engaging in activities outside.
- "Outdoor gear": Equipment or clothing designed for use during outdoor activities.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The school organizes weekly outdoor education programs for its students."
- **Noun**: "After a day spent enjoying various outdoor activities, they sat by the campfire to unwind."
