## 读音
/ruːm/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 房间：一个有墙、门和窗户的室内空间，通常用于特定目的，如卧室、办公室或教室。例如: "I have a small room on the third floor."
2. 空间：某个物体或人所占据的物理空间。例如: "There's enough room for everyone to sit comfortably."

## 短语
- "living room" 客厅
- "bedroom" 卧室
- "study room" 书房
- "storage room" 储藏室

## 例句
- 名词: "The hotel offered us a spacious room with a beautiful view."
- 名词（空间）: "The table takes up too much room in the kitchen."
