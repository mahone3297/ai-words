## Pronunciation
/səˈplɪmənt/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A word or phrase added to a text, especially a newspaper or magazine, to provide additional information or fill gaps:** *The weekly supplement features recipes and health tips.*
2. **A product or service offered in addition to the regular one, typically for extra cost:** *The hotel offers a breakfast supplement.*

### Verb
1. **To add something extra or complementary to (a meal, for example):** *We'll have a salad supplement with our dinner.*
2. **To supply or provide something as an addition or enhancement:** *The package includes a free supplement book.*

## Phrases
- "Take a supplement" for taking vitamins or dietary supplements.
- "Supplement your diet" to improve nutrition with additional nutrients.
- "A supplement to income" for extra earnings.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Readers can find the crossword puzzle supplement on the back page."
- **Verb**: "She took a calcium supplement every day to maintain her bone health."
- **Intransitive verb**: "The new issue comes with a special travel supplement."
