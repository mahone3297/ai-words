## 读音
/ˈsʌplɪmənt/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   - 补充物；增补版：例如 "A supplement to the newspaper contains additional articles."
   - 营养补充剂：例如 "He takes vitamin supplements every day."
   - 额外收入：例如 "Her part-time job is a supplement to her pension."

- **动词 v**:
   - 增补；补充：例如 "The new chapter supplements the existing text."
   - 提供额外收入：例如 "Her freelance work supplements her salary."

## 短语
- "Supplemental income" 意为额外收入。
- "Supplementary material" 指附加的学习或参考资料。

## 例句
- 名词: "The magazine published a supplement on gardening tips."
- 动词: "The teacher's guide supplements the textbook with additional exercises."
- 连接用法: "Her part-time job supplements her full-time salary."
