## 读音
/raɪd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 乘坐；旅程。例如: "I'm going on a long train ride to New York."
  - 骑行（交通工具）。例如: "She loves taking her bike for a ride in the park."

- **动词**:
  - 骑，驾驶。例如: "He rides his motorcycle to work every day."
  - 乘坐（交通工具）。例如: "We're going to ride the roller coaster."

- **形容词**:
  - 免费的。例如: "The company offered a free ride to all employees."

## 短语
- "Take a ride" 意为乘坐或骑行。例如: "Let's take a ride in my new car."
- "Ride it out" 表示忍受或熬过（困难时期）。例如: "We just have to ride out the storm until it passes."

## 例句
- 名词: "The kids were excited for their first Ferris wheel ride at the carnival."
- 动词: "She decided to ride the bus instead of driving in rush hour traffic."
- 形容词: "The new employee got a free ride to the office on the company shuttle."
