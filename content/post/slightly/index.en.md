## Pronunciation
/slaɪtli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: To a moderate degree or extent; not very much. It modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. 

## Phrases
- "Slightly more" indicates a small increase or difference.
- "Slightly damp" means slightly wet or moist.
- "She was slightly tired after the long journey."

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb modifying a verb**: "He smiled slightly when he saw her."
- "The temperature dropped slightly during the night."
- "She felt slightly uncomfortable in the new shoes."

## Synonyms
- Moderately
- Slightly less
- Barely
- Minimally
- Trivially

## Antonyms

- Dramatically
- Significantly
