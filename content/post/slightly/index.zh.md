## 读音
/ˈslaɪtli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 稍微，略微，轻轻地。表示程度不强或数量不多。例如: "The temperature dropped slightly during the night."
- **形容词**: 轻微的，微小的，不明显的。例如: "She felt slightly ill after eating the spicy food."

## 短语
- "slightly better" 意为稍微好一些。
- "slightly annoyed" 表示稍微恼火或不满。

## 例句
- 副词: "I prefer my coffee slightly sweetened."
- 形容词: "There was a slightly unpleasant smell in the room."
