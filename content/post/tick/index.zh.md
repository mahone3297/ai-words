## 读音
/tɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 小标记或勾号，表示已检查或完成。例如: "Please tick the box if you agree with the terms."
  - 蜱虫，一种吸血昆虫。例如: "Watch out for ticks in the grassy areas."
  - 钟声或滴答声。例如: "I could hear the clock ticking in the silence."

- **动词**:
  - 在旁边做标记或打勾以表示选择或确认。例如: "Tick off the items on your list as you complete them."
  - 时间流逝。例如: "Time is ticking away, and we need to make a decision."

## 短语
- "tick all the boxes" 意味着满足所有要求或条件。
- "tick someone off" 可以表示激怒或惹恼某人。

## 例句
- 名词: "There's a small tick on my dog's fur, I need to remove it."
- 动词: "She ticked the 'yes' option after reading the question carefully."
- 感叹词: "Tick-tock, tick-tock – time is running out!"

## 同义词
- Mark
- Check
- Cross off
- Select
- Nod (in the context of agreement or acknowledgement)
