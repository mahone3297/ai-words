A **torrent** is a large, powerful stream or flow of water, especially one that moves rapidly, often over a wide area, during heavy rain or snowmelt. It can also refer to a rapid, continuous flow of data or information, such as from the internet or a file download.

### Verb
To **torrent** can mean to flow powerfully, surge, or overwhelm with a strong current, as in water or data. It can also refer to downloading a large file from a torrent network.

### Adjective
In the context of technology, a **torrential** adjective describes something that is extremely fast, intense, or overwhelming, like a torrential rain.

### Adverb
A **torrentially** can be used to describe something that happens with great force or intensity, like rain falling heavily.
