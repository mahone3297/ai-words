## Pronunciation
/proʊˈfɛʃənl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who is highly skilled or knowledgeable in their profession, typically showing a high level of competence and dedication. Example: "A professional chef knows how to prepare a five-course meal."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or relating to a person's occupation or work, marked by expertise, skill, or adherence to standards. Example: "Her professional demeanor impressed her clients."
- **Adjective**: Done or executed with care and competence; competent. Example: "The professional presentation was well-received."

## Phrases
- "Act professionally" means to conduct oneself in a manner appropriate for one's job or position.
- "Professional development" refers to improving skills and knowledge for career advancement.
- "On a professional level" indicates something done in a work-related context.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The team hired a new professional designer for their marketing campaigns."
- **Adjective**: "She always maintains a professional appearance at the office."
- **Adverb**: "He handled the situation with great professionalism."
