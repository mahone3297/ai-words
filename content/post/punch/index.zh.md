## 读音
/pʌntʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 打击；拳击。例如: "He threw a powerful punch at his opponent."
2. （饮料机上的）冲压按钮；打孔。例如: "She ordered a cold drink and pressed the punch button."

- **动词**: 打；揍；猛击。例如: "The boxer landed a solid punch on his adversary's jaw."

## 短语
- "punch out" 意味着用力打出去，通常指拳击。
- "punch a ticket" 在票上打孔，常用于公共交通。

## 例句
- 名词: "The boxer's punch sent his opponent crashing to the floor."
- 动词: "The security guard punched the intruder in self-defense."
