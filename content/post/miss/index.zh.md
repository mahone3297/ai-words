## 读音
/ˈmɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 1. 错过；未击中：如 "He missed the target by a mile."
  - 2. 失去（机会、人或事物）：如 "She misses her old friends since moving to a new city."
  - 3. 少女；未婚女子：如 "Miss Smith is the headmistress of the school."
- **动词**:
  - 1. 未察觉；未注意到：如 "I missed the obvious solution to the problem."
  - 2. 未能出席；未赶上：如 "She missed the flight due to heavy traffic."
  - 3. 想念；怀念：如 "I miss you when you're not around."

## 短语
- "miss out on something" 意为错过（机会、经历等）：如 "Don't miss out on this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity."
- "miss someone dearly" 深切地想念某人：如 "She misses her family dearly while studying abroad."

## 例句
- 名词: "She was sad to miss the concert."
- 动词: "If you don't hurry up, you'll miss the bus."
- 想念： "He misses his hometown and often talks about it."
