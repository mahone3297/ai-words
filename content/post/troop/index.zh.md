## 读音
/troʊp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 军队，部队：A group of soldiers serving together.
  - （尤指表演者）一行，一队：A group of performers, such as dancers or acrobats, who perform together.

- **动词**:
  - 集结，列队行进：To move in a group, especially in an orderly manner.
  - （尤指演员）演出，表演：To perform as part of a group.

## 短语
- "Troop movement" 指军队的调动或行军。
- "Theater troop" 指戏剧团体或剧团。

## 例句
- 名词: "The troop of soldiers marched through the town square."
- 动词: "The theater troop rehearsed their new play for weeks before premiering it."

## 同义词
- Army
- Battalion
- Company
- Squad
- Ensemble (for non-military groups)
