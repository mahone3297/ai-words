## 读音
/wiːv/

## 释义
- **动词**: 编织；穿梭；交织。例如: "She weaved a beautiful tapestry using natural fibers."
- **名词**: 织法；编织物。例如: "The ancient loom was used for weaving intricate patterns."

## 短语
- "weave in and out" 在...中穿梭或穿行。例如: "The cars weaved in and out of traffic."
- "weave a story" 编织故事。例如: "He wove a fascinating tale about his adventures."

## 例句
- 动词: "She skillfully weaved the wool into a cozy scarf."
- 名词: "The weaver's art lies in the intricate weave of the fabric."
