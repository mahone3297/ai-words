## Definitions
### Noun
- A useless object or thing is one that has no practical purpose or value. For example: "The broken machine was a useless piece of equipment."
- A person who is useless is unproductive or ineffective. For example: "He's been a useless employee, not contributing much to the team."

### Adjective
- Describes something that is of no use or serves no purpose. For example: "A useless attempt will never lead to success."
- Characterizes a person as inefficient or incapable. For example: "She is a useless driver, always getting lost."

### Adverb
- Sometimes used to emphasize that something is completely without value or effectiveness. For example: "He tried to solve the problem, but his efforts were utterly useless."

## Phrases
- "A useless endeavor" refers to a futile or pointless activity.
- "Useless worrying" describes excessive or unnecessary anxiety.
- "She's as useful as a one-legged man in a butt-kicking contest" is an idiomatic expression meaning she is completely ineffectual.
