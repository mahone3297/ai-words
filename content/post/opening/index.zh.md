## 读音
/ˈoʊpənɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 开始；开幕：例如 "The opening of the play was impressive."
  2. 可用的机会；职位空缺：例如 "There are several job openings in the company."
  3. 孔；开口；缝隙：例如 "There was just enough opening for me to squeeze through."

- **动词**:
  1. 打开；开启：例如 "Please open the door."
  2. 开始；启动：例如 "The meeting will open with a speech."

## 短语
- "at the opening of" 在…开始时：例如 "At the opening of the ceremony, a speech was given."
- "open up" 打开心扉；分享内心：例如 "After a while, he started to open up about his past."

## 例句
- 名词: "The opening scene of the movie set the tone for the entire film."
- 动词: "She opened the window to let in some fresh air."
- 动词短语: "He decided to open up a small business in his hometown."
