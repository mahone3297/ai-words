## 读音
/ˈʌp.wərd/

## 释义
- **副词**: 向上；朝上。例如: "The ladder leaned upward against the wall."
- **形容词**: 向上的；上升的。例如: "The upward trend in sales is encouraging."

## 短语
- "upward movement" 指的是向上的移动或趋势。
- "upward bound" 形容有向上发展的势头或潜力。

## 例句
- 副词: "He threw the ball upward into the air."
- 形容词: "The company's upward financial trajectory is impressive."
