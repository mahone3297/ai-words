## 读音
/ˈtʃaɪniːz/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 与中国的文化、语言、人民或事物有关的。例如: "She speaks fluent Chinese."
- **名词**: 中国的官方语言，汉语。例如: "Learning Chinese is challenging but rewarding."
- **名词**: 中国人，华人。例如: "The Chinese community has a rich cultural heritage."
- **名词**: 中国菜。例如: "I love the variety of flavors in Chinese cuisine."

## 短语
- "Chinese characters" 指汉字。
- "Chinese New Year" 是春节，中国最重要的传统节日。
- "Chinese cuisine" 指中国菜系，包括多种地方风味。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This dish is typically Chinese, with its spicy and savory flavors."
- 名词（语言）: "I'm taking Chinese lessons to better understand the culture."
- 名词（人）: "My friend is Chinese and she introduced me to traditional tea ceremonies."
- 名词（食物）: "Let's try that new Chinese restaurant downtown."

## 同义词
- Mandarin (主要指普通话)
- Sinic (更偏向文化的同义词)
- Sino- (前缀，用于表示与中国有关的事物，如Sino-American, Sino-Japanese等)
