## 读音
/slam/

## 释义
- **动词**: 猛烈地关上（门、窗等）；猛击；严厉批评。例如: "He slammed the door in frustration."，"The reviewer slammed the new novel in his critique."
- **名词**: 猛烈的撞击或关闭声；（体育）扣球；（网络）恶意评论或攻击。例如: "The sound of the slam echoed through the hallway."，"She performed an impressive slam in the volleyball match."

## 短语
- "slam the brakes on" 意味着突然猛烈地刹车。
- "slam dunk" 在篮球中指的是轻松扣篮的动作。
- "Internet slam" 指网络上的负面评论或攻击。

## 例句
- 动词: "He slammed the phone down after an argument with his friend."
- 名词: "The crowd cheered as the basketball player executed a perfect slam dunk."
