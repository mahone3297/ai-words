## 读音
/ɪmˈpreʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 印象，感觉。例如: "She had a good impression of the company after the interview." 2. 影响，效果。例如: "The artwork made a strong impression on the audience."
- **动词 (v)**: 给予...印象，影响。例如: "His performance impressed the judges."

## 短语
- "make an impression on sb" 意为对某人留下深刻印象。
- "first impression" 指初次见面的印象。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her friendly smile left a positive impression on me."
- 动词: "The new employee immediately impressed his colleagues with his professionalism."
