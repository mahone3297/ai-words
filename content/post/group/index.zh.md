## 读音
/ɡrʊp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一群有共同特征或目的的人或物。例如: "A group of friends went on a hiking trip together."
- **动词**: 将人或物分组或组织起来。例如: "The teacher asked us to group ourselves according to our interests."

## 短语
- "in a group" 意味着集体或团队中。例如: "They prefer to work in a group rather than individually."
- "group discussion" 指集体讨论。例如: "There will be a group discussion on the project after the lecture."

## 例句
- 名词: "The marketing department is divided into several smaller groups."
- 动词: "Let's group these books by genre for easier organization."
