## Pronunciation
/rɪˈzɪdəns/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **residence** is a place where someone lives, especially their permanent home. It can also refer to a building or dwelling occupied by a particular person or family.

### Verb
To **reside** is to live in a particular place on a regular basis.

## Phrases
- "Live in a residence" or "Reside at a location"
- "Primary residence" or "Secondary residence"
- "Residential address" or "Private residence"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The president resides at the White House."
- **Verb**: "After moving to the city, they found a cozy residence near the park."
- "Many students reside in dormitories during their college years."

## Synonyms
