## 读音
/rɪˈzɪdəns/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 居住地；住所。例如: "He has a large residence in the countryside."
  - （尤指官员或贵族的）府邸，官邸。例如: "The ambassador's residence is in the heart of the city."

## 短语
- "Permanent residence" 永久居住地。
- "Summer residence" 夏季别墅。
- "Residence permit" 居留许可。

## 例句
- 名词: "After years of living abroad, she returned to her family residence."
- "Students are required to live in college residence during their first year."
