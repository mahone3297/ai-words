## Pronunciation
/restəˈrɑːnt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A place where food and drink are served to customers, typically in a seated setting. Example: "We decided to have dinner at that cozy little restaurant downtown."
- **Verb**: To dine at or patronize a restaurant. Example: "They're going to the restaurant for a romantic anniversary dinner."
- **Pronoun**: A collective term for people dining together, often used informally. Example: "The whole family enjoyed a delicious meal at the restaurant."

## Phrases
- "Drop by the restaurant" means to visit without prior arrangement.
- "Make a reservation at a restaurant" is the act of booking a table in advance.
- "Enjoy a meal at the restaurant" implies having a leisurely dining experience.
