## 读音
/rɪˈstərənt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种提供食物和饮料给顾客消费的商业场所，通常有服务员服务。例如: "We had a delicious dinner at the French restaurant last night."

## 短语
- "Fine dining restaurant" 指提供高级美食和服务的餐厅。
- "Fast food restaurant" 指快餐店，如麦当劳或肯德基。
- "Brunch restaurant" 提供早午餐的餐馆。

## 例句
- "Let's meet at that new Italian restaurant on Saturday evening."
- "The restaurant was so crowded, we had to wait for a table."
- "We enjoyed the live music while having dinner at the riverside restaurant."
