## 读音
/strength/ 

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 力量，力气：The weightlifter demonstrated great strength in his arms.
  - 强度，强度大：This metal has a high strength and can withstand heavy loads.
  - 优点，长处：One of her strengths is her ability to communicate effectively.
  - 耐力，韧性：His physical strength allowed him to work long hours without getting tired.

- **形容词 adj**:
  - 强大的，有力的：The medicine had a strong strength and quickly relieved the pain.
  - 坚定的，坚决的：Her political strength made her a formidable opponent.

## 短语
- "Strength training" 指力量训练，如举重或健身。
- "In strength" 表示人数众多或力量强大，如 "They came in strength to support the cause."

## 例句
- 名词: "He built up his strength through regular exercise."
- 形容词: "The team's strength lies in its unity."
