## 读音
/congrəˈtʃuːləʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 庆祝；祝贺。例如: "We sent them a card with our congratulations on their wedding."
- **动词**: 祝贺；道贺。例如: "They congratulated her on her promotion."

## 短语
- "Congratulations on your success" 用来对别人的成就表示祝贺。
- " Extend one's congratulations" 意为转达祝贺之意。

## 例句
- 名词: "The team received congratulations from their fans after winning the championship."
- 动词: "I congratulated him personally when he got the job."

## 同义词
- Best wishes
- Felicitations
- Kudos
- Compliments
- Congratulations (本身既是名词也是动词，所以此处作为同义词列出)
