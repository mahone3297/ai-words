## Pronunciation
/selfɪf/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Referring to a person who is excessively concerned with their own interests or desires, often at the expense of others. Also used to describe behavior characterized by self-centeredness.
- **Noun**: A selfish person, someone who is primarily focused on themselves.

## Phrases
- "Acting selfishly" refers to behavior that is self-focused.
- "Being selfish" describes a personality trait or habit.
- "Selfishness" is the quality or tendency to be selfish.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "She was accused of being too selfish when it came to sharing her belongings."
- **Noun**: "The selfish boss never considered his employees' needs."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "Her selfishness caused tension among the family members."

## Synonyms
- Self-centered
- Self-absorbed
- Self-indulgent
- egocentric
- Inconsiderate

## Antonyms
- Unselfish
- Altruistic
- Selfless
- Considerate
- Generous
