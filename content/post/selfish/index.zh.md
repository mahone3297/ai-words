## 读音
/selfɪʃ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 关心自己的利益超过别人，自私的。例如: "He was so selfish that he didn't share his food with anyone."
- **名词**: 自私的人。例如: "She couldn't stand being around such a selfish person."

## 短语
- "Selfish behavior" 指的是只考虑自己而不顾及他人的行为。
- "Acting in one's own selfish interests" 意味着为了自己的利益而行动。

## 例句
- 形容词: "His constant demand for attention showed his selfish nature."
- 名词: "She was surprised by his selfishness when he refused to help."

## 同义词
- Self-centered
- Egotistical
- Narcissistic
- Greedy
- Inconsiderate
