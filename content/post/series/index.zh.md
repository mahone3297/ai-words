## 读音
/ˈsiəriːz/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一系列相同或相关的事物，通常按照顺序排列。例如: "He wrote a series of books on ancient history."
- **名词**: 电视节目或广播节目的一季。例如: "The new season of the TV series starts next week."
- **名词**: 数列，如数学中的等差或等比数列。例如: "In this arithmetic series, each term is 3 more than the previous one."

## 短语
- "A series of events" 指一连串的事件。
- "Serial number" 在物品上标识的序列号，用于区分不同的个体。
- "Final series" 在体育比赛中，通常是决定冠军的最后几场比赛。

## 例句
- 名词: "The museum is hosting an exhibition featuring a series of paintings by Van Gogh."
- 名词: "This is the final series of the basketball playoffs, and both teams are giving their all."
- 名词: "In mathematics, a geometric series is one in which each term is a constant multiple of the previous one."
