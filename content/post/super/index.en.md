## Definitions
### Noun
1. Superlative form of 'good' or 'excellent', used to emphasize superiority or greatness. Example: "This cake is the most delicious; it's super tasty."
2. A person who has exceptional abilities or skills in a particular field. Example: "She is a super athlete, winning every race she enters."
3. A superhero or a character with extraordinary powers. Example: "The superhero saved the day; he's a true super."

### Verb
1. To be extremely good or excellent at something. Example: "He's super talented at playing the piano."
2. (informal) To assist or help in a remarkable way. Example: "She supercharges the team's productivity."

### Adjective
1. Beyond ordinary or exceptional. Example: "The super fast car reached top speeds in seconds."
2. Extremely good or impressive. Example: "The view from the top was absolutely super."
