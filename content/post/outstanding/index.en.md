## Word: Outstanding

## Pronunciation
/ˌaʊtˈstændɪŋ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Exceptionally good or excellent; superior in quality or performance. For example: "She has an outstanding academic record."
- **Adjective**: Unpaid or unsettled; remaining to be paid, dealt with, or answered; not yet resolved. For example: "There's an outstanding balance on your account."

### Phrase (adjective - excellent)
- **Outstanding achievement**: An especially noteworthy accomplishment.
- **Outstanding debt**: Debt that remains unpaid or unsettled.
- **Outstanding warrant**: A warrant that is still valid and has not been executed by the police.

### Example Sentences
- **Adjective (excellent)**: "His outstanding performance in the play earned him a standing ovation."
- **Adjective (unpaid)**: "The company must settle all its outstanding invoices before the end of the financial year."
