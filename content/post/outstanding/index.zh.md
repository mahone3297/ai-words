## 读音
/ˈaʊtstændɪŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 杰出的，卓越的，显著的。例如: "He has shown outstanding performance in his field."
- **动词**: 突出，显眼。例如: "Her talent stood out, making her outstanding among her peers."

## 短语
- "outstanding achievement" 意为杰出的成就。
- "outstanding debt" 指未偿还的债务。
- "an outstanding student" 是指一个优秀的学生。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The athlete's outstanding speed earned him a gold medal."
- 动词: "Her painting stood out in the exhibition, attracting many admirers."
