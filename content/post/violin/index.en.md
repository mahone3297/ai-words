## Pronunciation
/vɪˈlɪn/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A small stringed musical instrument with a narrow, elongated body and four strings, typically played with a bow.
- In music, a member of the violin family, which also includes violas, cellos, and double basses.

### Verb
- To play or perform on a violin.
- To produce or make a sound using a violin.

## Phrases
- "Play the violin" or "Violin performance"
- "Learn to play the violin"
- "A skilled violinist"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She spent hours practicing her violin every day."
- **Verb**: "The talented violinist mesmerized the audience with his performance."
- **Action**: "He decided to take up violin lessons after years of admiring classical music."

## Synonyms
- Fiddle
- String instrument
- Bow instrument
- Viola d'amore (archaic)
- Acoustic instrument

## Antonyms
- None (as a noun, it's a specific type of instrument; as a verb, it's an action, not a quality)
