## 读音
/əˈdres/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 地址，指具体的邮寄地址或电子邮箱地址。例如: "Please write your full address on the envelope."
   2. 演讲，致辞。例如: "The president delivered an inspiring address to the nation."
   3. 对话，谈话。例如: "He gave her an emotional address after the concert."

- **动词**: 向...说话，称呼。例如: "He addressed the audience with confidence."
   - 2. 解决，处理（问题）。例如: "We need to address the issue of climate change."

## 短语
- "To address someone" 意味着直接和某人交谈。
- "Address the envelope" 指在信封上写上收件人的地址。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please provide your email address for further communication."
- 动词: "She began her speech by addressing the concerns of the students."
- 演讲: "The mayor's address focused on improving public services in the city.
