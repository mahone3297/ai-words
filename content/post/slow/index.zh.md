## 读音
/sləʊ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 缓慢的，不快的。例如: "The turtle moved at a slow pace."
- **副词**: 缓慢地，慢慢地。例如: "Please drive slow on the icy road."
- **动词**: 使减速，放慢。例如: "The old computer started to slow down after running many programs."

## 短语
- "Slow down" 意味着减缓速度或节奏。
- "Take it slow" 建议某人谨慎行事，不要急于求成。

## 例句
- 形容词: "He is a slow learner, but very persistent."
- 副词: "She played the piano slow and melodiously."
- 动词: "The traffic slowed us down, so we arrived late."

## 同义词
- Sluggish
- Leisurely
- Gradual
- Steady
- unhurried
