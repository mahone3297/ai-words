## 读音
/ɪmˈprɛsɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 给人深刻印象的，令人赞叹的，引人注目的。例如: "Her performance was so impressive that it left the audience speechless."
- **动词**: 使印象深刻。例如: "The speaker impressed upon us the importance of teamwork."

## 短语
- "make an impressive impression" 创造深刻的印象
- "an impressive array" 令人印象深刻的阵容

## 例句
- 形容词: "The grandeur of the palace was truly impressive."
- 动词: "The teacher's stories always impressed upon us the value of hard work."
