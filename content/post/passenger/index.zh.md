## 读音
/pæˈsɪndʒər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 乘坐公共交通工具或私人车辆的人，尤指飞机、火车、汽车或轮船上的乘客。例如: "There were many passengers on the bus."
- **名词 (n)**: 在某种情况或经历中的参与者。例如: "She was just a passenger in her own life, letting events unfold around her."

## 短语
- "passenger train" 火车旅客车厢
- "front passenger" 副驾驶座乘客
- "carry a passenger" 运载乘客

## 例句
- 名词: "The airline lost my luggage, and I had to be a passenger without any belongings for a week."
- 名词: "Please ensure all passengers fasten their seat belts before we take off."
