## Definitions
- **Noun**: A sport played with a ball between two teams of six players each on a raised court, usually divided by a net. [TFD](https://www.thefreedictionary.com.volleyball)
- **Verb**: To play the game of volleyball, often informally. [Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/volleyball)
- **Noun**: The act or action of striking the ball in this sport using one's hands or arms. [Cambridge](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/volleyball)
