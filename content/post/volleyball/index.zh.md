## 读音
/ˈvɒlibɔːl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种球类运动，球员用手将球击过网，目标是让球落在对方场地内而得分。

## 短语
- "Volleyball game" 指的是排球比赛。
- "Volleyball player" 指的是排球运动员。
- "Set, spike, and serve" 是排球中的三个基本动作：设定、扣球和发球。

## 例句
- "I'm joining the school volleyball team this year."
- "The volleyball game was intense, with both teams fighting for every point."
- "She practiced her serves for hours to improve her volleyball skills."

## 同义词
- Beach volleyball (沙滩排球)
- Indoor volleyball (室内排球)
