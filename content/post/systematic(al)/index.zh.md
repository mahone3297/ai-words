## 读音
/ˌsɪstəˈmætɪk(ə)l/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有系统的；有条理的；遵循一定规则的。例如: "She has a systematic approach to her work."
- **副词**: 系统地；有条不紊地。例如: "The research was conducted systematically over several years."

## 短语
- "systematically organized" 意味着有秩序地组织。
- "systematic review" 是一种科学方法，用来系统性地评估和综合相关研究的结果。

## 例句
- 形容词: "His study of the subject is very systematic and thorough."
- 副词: "The company trains its employees systematically to ensure consistency."
