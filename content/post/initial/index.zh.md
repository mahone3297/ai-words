## 读音
/ˈɪnɪʃəl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 字母缩写，通常是一个名字、短语或公司名称的首字母。例如: "My initials are J.S."
2. 起始部分，开始阶段。例如: "In the initial stages of the project, we focused on research."

- **形容词**:
最初的，开始的。例如: "We need to analyze the initial data before proceeding."

## 短语
- "Initial reaction" 表示第一反应或初步看法。
- "Initial investment" 指开始时投入的资金或资源。

## 例句
- 名词: "She signed the letter with her initials and a flourish."
- 形容词: "The initial response to the new product was overwhelmingly positive."
