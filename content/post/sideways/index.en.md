## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a direction perpendicular to the forward motion, or at an angle; off to one side. For example: "She turned sideways to avoid the obstacle."
- **Adjective**: Describing movement or position at an angle, not straight on. For example: "The car maneuvered sideways through the narrow alley."
- **Pronoun**: Used to refer to oneself or others when indicating direction or position. For example: "Look at them walking sideways on the balance beam."

## Phrases
- "Sideways glance" refers to a quick look to the side.
- "Sideways thinking" denotes a non-traditional or unconventional approach.
- "Sideways shift" can mean a change in direction or focus.
