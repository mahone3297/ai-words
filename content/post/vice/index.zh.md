## 读音
/vaɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 副职，副手；恶习；替代品；(某些游戏或组织中的)副总裁职位。例如: "He was appointed as the vice president of the company."，"Smoking is considered a vice in many cultures."

- **形容词**: 副的，副职的；邪恶的；有不良嗜好的。例如: "The vice squad investigates criminal activities."，"He has a vice-like grip on the steering wheel."

## 短语
- "vice president" 副总裁或副理事长。
- "in the vice of" 在...的控制或影响下，通常指不好的影响。
- "vice grip" 钳子，一种强力夹持工具。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new vice principal started working at the school this week."
- 形容词: "He struggled to overcome his vice of gambling."

## 同义词
- Deputy
- Assistant
- Bad habit
- Wickedness
- Vice-like (形容词的同义词：极紧的，牢固的)
