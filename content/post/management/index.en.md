## Word: Management

## Pronunciation
/ˈmænɪdʒm(ə)nt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The process of controlling, organizing, and directing resources (human, financial, material, etc.) towards specific goals within an organization or system. It involves planning, decision-making, problem-solving, and coordinating efforts to achieve efficiency and effectiveness. 

Example sentence: _The company's management team decided on a new strategy to improve productivity._
  
Phrases:
- "Upper management" refers to the senior executives or directors who make high-level decisions in an organization.
- "Resource management" involves planning and organizing the use of available resources effectively.
- "Time management" is the act or process of planning and exercising conscious control over the amount of time spent on specific activities, especially to increase effectiveness, efficiency, and productivity.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Effective management is crucial for the success of any business."
- "She pursued a degree in Business Management to enhance her career prospects."
- "The project management team ensured that the project was completed on schedule."
