## 读音
/ˈmænɪdʒmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 组织和指导某事的过程或一组人负责这个过程。例如: "The management team made some tough decisions."
- **动词**: 操纵、控制或处理某事物。例如: "She managed to finish the project on time."

## 短语
- "Business management" 指商业运营和管理。
- "Employee management" 关注员工的领导和指导。
- "Financial management" 是关于财务资源的规划和控制。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's management is considering new strategies for growth."
- 动词: "He manages a team of engineers and ensures their work is efficient."
