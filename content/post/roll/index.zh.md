## 读音
/rol/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 卷，卷状物。例如: "She wrapped the paper around the gift and tied it with a ribbon, making a perfect roll."
   - 音乐中的旋律或节奏。例如: "The drummer played a dynamic roll in the jazz band."
   - 餐桌上的面包卷。例如: "Please pass the butter, my roll is getting cold."

- **动词**:
   - 滚动，转动。例如: "The ball rolled across the field."
   - 把东西卷起来。例如: "She rolled up her sleeves to get to work."
   - （赌博游戏中）掷骰子。例如: "It's your turn to roll the dice."

- **形容词**:
   - 滚动的，波动的。例如: "The waves rolled in from the ocean."
   - 彻底的，全面的。例如: "She did a roll call to make sure everyone was present."

## 短语
- "Roll out" 意为展开或推出（新产品、计划等）。例如: "The company will roll out its new product next month."
- "On a roll" 表示连续成功或好运。例如: "After winning three games in a row, the team was really on a roll."

## 例句
- 名词: "She ordered a hot dog with mustard and a side of potato chips."
- 动词: "The child rolled down the grassy hill, laughing all the way."
- 形容词: "The wind caused the curtains to roll in the open window."
