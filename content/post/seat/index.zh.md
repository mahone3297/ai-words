## 读音
/siːt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 座位，坐的地方。例如: "There are no free seats on the bus."
  - 安置，使就座。例如: "The hostess seated us at a table by the window."
  - 家庭位置，家族中的地位。例如: "He is the heir to the family seat."

- **动词**:
  - 坐下，使坐下。例如: "Please seat yourself."
  - 容纳，能容纳（人或物）。例如: "The theater can seat 1,000 people."

## 短语
- "Take a seat" 意为坐下。
- "Seat belt" 是安全带的意思。
- "Seat of power" 指权力中心或政府所在地。

## 例句
- 名词: "I reserved a seat for you next to the stage."
- 动词: "The teacher asked the students to seat themselves in alphabetical order."
- 介词短语: "The capital is the seat of government."
