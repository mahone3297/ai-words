## 读音
/ˈtrɛtmənt/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 医疗治疗：The medical care or attention given to a patient, as for an illness or injury.
  - 对待方式：The way someone is dealt with or regarded; handling.

- **动词 v**:
  - 给予治疗：To subject to a process or method of treatment, especially medical or therapeutic.

## 短语
- "treatment plan" 治疗计划：A detailed plan outlining the course of therapy or medical care.
- "treat someone with respect" 以尊重对待某人：To deal with someone in a considerate and courteous manner.

## 例句
- 名词: "The cancer patient is undergoing a new chemotherapy treatment."
- 动词: "The therapist will treat your back pain using a combination of massage and acupuncture."
