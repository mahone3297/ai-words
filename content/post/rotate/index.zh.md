## 读音
/ˈrəʊtəte/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 旋转；转动。例如: "The Earth rotates around its axis once every 24 hours."
2. 轮流；替换。例如: "Employees rotate through different departments to gain more experience."

## 短语
- "Rotate around" 表示围绕...旋转。例如: "The planets rotate around the sun."
- "Rotate shifts" 指工作班次的轮换。例如: "The nurses rotate shifts every three days."

## 例句
- 旋转: "The windmill blades rotate slowly in the breeze."
- 轮流: "The team members rotate responsibilities during the project."
