## Pronunciation
/proʊˈbəbl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having a high likelihood of occurring or being true; likely. For example: "The weather forecast predicts a probable rain tomorrow."
- **Adverb**: In a probable manner; likely. For example: "It's probable that he'll arrive on time."

## Phrases
- "Probable cause" refers to sufficient grounds for taking action or making a decision.
- "Probable outcome" denotes the most likely result.
- "A probable explanation" suggests a reasonable assumption.

## Example Sentences
