## 读音
/prəˈbəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 可能的，很可能的，有根据的。例如: "It is probable that it will rain tomorrow."
- **副词**: 可能地，大概。例如: "He probably forgot about the meeting."

## 短语
- "probable cause" 在法律中指有合理依据相信某事已经发生或即将发生。
- "probable outcome" 指可能的结果或预测。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Based on the forecast, it's probable that we'll have a storm tonight."
- 副词: "He's probably still asleep, considering the late night he had."
