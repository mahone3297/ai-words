## 读音
/spelɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 写法，拼写，指字母按照特定顺序组成单词的方式。例如: "The correct spelling of 'receive' is with an 'i' before 'e'."
- **动词**: 给出（单词）的正确拼写。例如: "Can you spell your name for me?"

## 短语
- "Spelling bee" 是一种竞赛，参赛者需要正确拼写单词。
- "Correct spelling" 指的是单词的准确拼写形式。

## 例句
- 名词: "She always pays attention to her spelling in written work."
- 动词: "The teacher asked the students to spell 'restaurant'."

## 同义词
- Orthography (书写学)
- Spelling out (逐一说出字母)
- Writing
- Punctuation (标点符号)
