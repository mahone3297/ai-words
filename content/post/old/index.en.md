## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having lived or existed for a long time; no longer young; aged. For example: "She's an old friend from college."
- **Adjective**: Having been in existence for a long time; not modern or new. For example: "This old house has so much character."
- **Adjective (informal)**: Used to emphasize how long something has been the case or that it should have been dealt with sooner. For example: "I've been telling you this old story for years now."
- **Noun (plural form - olds)**: Informal reference to people of a certain age group, often derogatory. For example: "The marketing team targeted the 18-24 olds."
