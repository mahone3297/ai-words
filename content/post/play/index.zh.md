## 读音
/pleɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 表演；演出：如 "The theater group is putting on a play about ancient Rome."
  - 游戏；玩耍：如 "The children were engaged in a game of play in the park."

- **动词**:
  - 做游戏；玩耍：如 "The dog loves to play with its ball."
  - 参加比赛（尤指体育运动）：如 "He plays for the local football team."
  - 表演（角色）：如 "She will play the lead role in the new movie."

- **形容词**:
  - 开玩笑的；嬉戏的：如 "They were just being playful, not serious."

## 短语
- "Play it safe" 意为采取谨慎行动，避免冒险。
- "Play along" 表示配合别人的游戏或计划。
- "Play hard to get" 指在感情关系中故意表现得不易得到。

## 例句
- 名词: "The school's annual play is always a big event for the students."
- 动词: "He plays the guitar skillfully during the concert."
- 形容词: "Their playful behavior showed they were having fun."

## 同义词
- Act (动词时)
- Compete
- Perform
- Game
- Sport
