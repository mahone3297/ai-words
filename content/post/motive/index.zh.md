## 读音
/məʊˈtiːv/

## 释义
- **名词**: 动机，原因，目的。例如: "The main motive behind his actions was to help others."
- **形容词**: 有动机的，有目的的。例如: "A motive crime is one committed with a specific intention."

## 短语
- "Motive force" 意为动力，推动力。
- "Motive power" 指的是提供运动或工作能力的力量。

## 例句
- 名词: "Detectives were trying to uncover the killer's motive."
- 形容词: "The suspect had a clear motive for stealing the valuable artifact."
