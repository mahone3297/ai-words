## Pronunciation
/səˈrɪnʤə(r)/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **surgeon** is a highly trained medical professional who performs surgical procedures on patients. They are specialists in operating within the body to diagnose and treat various conditions.

### Verb (transitive)
To **perform surgery** on someone, typically by a surgeon, is to carry out a surgical operation to repair, remove, or treat a medical condition.

### Adjective
- **Surgeon-like** describes someone who exhibits qualities or skills similar to a surgeon, such as precision or expertise.

## Phrases
- "Undergo surgery" means to have a surgical procedure done.
- "The surgeon's hands are steady" implies a calm and skilled demeanor during operations.
- "A renowned surgeon" refers to a highly respected professional in their field.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The surgeon carefully performed the delicate operation with great skill."
- **Verb**: "The patient was scheduled to undergo a bypass surgery next week."
- **Adjective**: "The surgeon-like assistant was an invaluable asset to the team."
