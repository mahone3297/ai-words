## 读音
/ˈsɜːr.dʒən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 医生，尤指外科医生。专门进行手术的医学专家。例如: "The surgeon successfully operated on the patient's heart."
- **动词**: 执行手术；动手术。例如: "He was trained to surgically remove tumors."

## 短语
- "surgeon general" 美国公共卫生局局长，或其他国家类似的官方职位。
- "in the hands of a skilled surgeon" 指由技术熟练的外科医生处理。

## 例句
- 名词: "The renowned surgeon has performed thousands of successful operations."
- 动词: "The patient will be going under the knife as the surgeon operates tomorrow."
