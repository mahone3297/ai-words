## 读音
/ˈnjuːtrəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 中立的，不偏不倚的，无倾向性的。例如: "The country maintained a neutral stance in the conflict."
- **名词**: 中立者，不偏向任何一方的人或国家。例如: "Switzerland is known as a neutral nation."
- **副词**: 中立地，不偏不倚地。例如: "The judge handled the case neutrally."

## 短语
- "neutral territory" 指没有归属或冲突的地带。
- "neutral ground" 可以指双方都能接受的妥协点或讨论平台。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The mediator was careful to remain completely neutral during the negotiations."
- 名词: "In soccer, the goalkeeper must wear a shirt of a neutral color if the team's colors are too similar."
- 副词: "The news anchor reported the story without any neutral tone, ensuring balance in their coverage."

- impartial
- unbiased

- detached
