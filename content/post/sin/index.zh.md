## 读音
/sɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 罪恶；罪行。例如: "He repented for his sins."
- **名词**: 过失；错误。例如: "There was no sin in being curious."
- **动词**: 犯罪；做错事。例如: "She knew it would be a sin to steal."

## 短语
- "Sin against God" 意指违背神的意志。
- "Sin no more" 告诫某人不要再犯错误或罪行。

## 例句
- 名词: "In many religions, sin is believed to separate people from their deity."
- 动词: "It would be a sin to waste such a precious resource."
- 名词: "The act of lying is considered a sin in some moral codes."
