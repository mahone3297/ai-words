## 读音
/θruː/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示通过、经过。例如: "The car drove through the town."
- **副词**: 表示从一端到另一端，完全穿过。例如: "She walked through the door."
- **连词**: 连接句子成分，表示原因或结果。例如: "She was so tired that she fell asleep through the movie."

## 短语
- "go through" 经历，完成。例如: "We have to go through a lot of training before the exam."
- "get through" 通过，完成。例如: "I finally got through to her on the phone."
- "see through" 看穿，识破。例如: "He saw through her fake smile."

## 例句
