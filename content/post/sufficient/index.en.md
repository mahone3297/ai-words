## Definitions
### Noun
A **sufficient** is a noun that refers to an adequate amount or quantity, enough to meet a need or requirement. It can also be used to describe a person or thing that is considered adequate or satisfactory.

### Verb
To **suffice** is a verb that means to be enough or to satisfy a need or purpose adequately. It can also mean to serve as a substitute or replacement.

### Adjective
An **adequate** or **sufficient** adjective describes something that is adequate in quantity, quality, or capability to meet a need or expectation.

### Adverb
The adverb form is not commonly used as an independent adverb, but it can modify verbs to indicate adequacy or sufficiency.
