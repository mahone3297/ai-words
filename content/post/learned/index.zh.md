## 读音
/ˈlɜːrnɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 已经学习过的；有学问的；博学的。例如: "He is a learned scholar with extensive knowledge in ancient history."
- **过去分词**: learn 的过去分词，表示动作已经完成。例如: "She has learned to play the piano since childhood."

## 短语
- "a learned person" 指知识渊博的人。
- "to have learned from one's mistakes" 意味着从错误中吸取教训。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The learned professor delivered an insightful lecture on quantum physics."
- 过去分词: "By the end of the course, they had learned a new language."
