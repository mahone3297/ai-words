## 读音
/tɔːk/

## 释义
- **动词**: 谈话；交谈。例如: "They spent hours talking about their shared interests."
- **名词**: 谈话；演讲。例如: "The teacher gave an inspiring talk on the importance of perseverance."

## 短语
- "Small talk" 意为闲聊。
- "Talk show" 是一种电视或广播节目形式，以访谈为主。
- "Give a talk" 指做一次演讲或讲解。

## 例句
- 动词: "Let's talk about your plans for the weekend."
- 名词: "She attended a talk on renewable energy sources."
