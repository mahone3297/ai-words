## 读音
/ˈraɪvəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 竞争者，对手。例如: "He is my main rival in the race for the championship."
- **动词**: 与...竞争，对抗。例如: "Our team rivaled the defending champions in the match."
- **形容词**: 竞争的，敌对的。例如: "The two companies have been rival firms for years."

## 短语
- "rival for something" 指的是在某事物上的竞争对手。例如: "They were both rivals for the job promotion."
- "to rival someone in something" 表示在某方面与某人匹敌。例如: "Her talent easily rivals that of the famous artist."

## 例句
- 名词: "Their rivalry on the court is legendary."
- 动词: "The new startup is already rivaling established companies in the market."
- 形容词: "The two political parties have maintained a bitter rival attitude towards each other."
