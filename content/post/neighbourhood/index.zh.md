## 读音
/nəʊˈbɜːrɪənd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个社区或居住区，通常指的是一个人居住或工作的周边环境。例如: "The children love playing in the park in their neighbourhood."
- **名词**: 附近，邻近的地方。例如: "There's a great coffee shop just around the corner in the neighbourhood."

## 短语
- "In the neighbourhood of" 表示大约的数量或距离。例如: "The population of this city is in the neighbourhood of 500,000."
- "Neighbourhood watch" 是一种社区安全计划，居民们相互监视以防止犯罪。

## 例句
- 名词: "We're having a block party this weekend for all the residents of our neighbourhood."
- 名词: "The local police regularly patrol the neighbourhood to ensure safety."
