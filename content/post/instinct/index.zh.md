## 读音
/ˈɪnskinst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 生来就有的本能在动物和人类中的内在倾向或能力。例如: "Babies have an instinct to cling to their mothers."
- **形容词**: 表示本能的，无意识的，出自内在的。例如: "His instinctive reaction was to run away."

## 短语
- "Follow one's instinct" 意思是按照直觉行事。
- "Instinctive behavior" 指的是天生的行为模式。

## 例句
- 名词: "The survival instinct is a fundamental part of human nature."
- 形容词: "She made the right decision based on her instinct rather than logic."

## 同义词
- Inborn
- Innate
- Natural
- Unconscious
- Intuitive
