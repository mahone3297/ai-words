## 读音
/ˈpæsɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 被动的；不主动的；受动的。例如: "The dog was in a passive state, waiting for its owner's command."
- **名词**: 被动语态，被动形式。例如: "In English, verbs can be in the active or passive voice."

## 短语
- "Passive voice" 指动词以被动形式表达，主语是动作的接受者。
- "Passive resistance" 非暴力抵抗，消极反抗。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She tends to take a passive role in group discussions."
- 名词: "In this sentence, 'The book was read by him,' the verb 'was read' is in the passive voice."
