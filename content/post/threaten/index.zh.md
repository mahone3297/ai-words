## 读音
/ˈθrɛt.nən/

## 释义
- **动词**: 威胁，恐吓；使面临危险。例如: "The storm threatened to damage the houses in the area."
- **名词**: 威胁，潜在的危险。例如: "His words were a direct threat to her safety."

## 短语
- "threaten with" 用...威胁，例如: "The boss threatened the employees with job loss if they didn't meet the deadline."
- "under threat" 处于危险之中，例如: "The wildlife habitat is under threat due to deforestation."

## 例句
- 动词: "The criminal threatened the witness not to testify against him."
- 名词: "The security team assessed the new threat and took necessary precautions."
