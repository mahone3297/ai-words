## 读音
/ˈjɪr/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 年，一年的时间。例如: "It's been five years since we last met."
- **名词 (n)**: 2. 年龄，岁数。例如: "She is in her thirtieth year."

## 短语
- "In the year of..." 用于表示具体年份。例如: "In the year of 2023, many significant events took place."
- "New Year" 指新的一年，常与庆祝和新年决议相关。例如: "We celebrate New Year with fireworks and resolutions."

## 例句
- 名词: "The child is now three years old."
- 名词: "The contract expires at the end of this year."

## 同义词
- Annual
- Calendar year
- Twelve months
- Period of 365 days (or 366 in a leap year)
