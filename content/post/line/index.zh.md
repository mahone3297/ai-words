## 读音
/laɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 1. 直线，如数学中的线。例如: "The line on the graph represents sales over time."
  - 2. 排队，行列。例如: "There's a long line of people waiting for tickets."
  - 3. 电话线路或通话。例如: "Can you hold the line for a moment?"
  - 4. 行业，领域。例如: "She works in the fashion line."
  - 5. 阵线，队伍。例如: "The two teams stood in their respective lines before the match."

- **动词**:
  - 1. 排成行。例如: "The students lined up outside the classroom."
  - 2. 沿着...画线。例如: "He lined the edges of the page with a ruler."
  - 3. 填充，布置。例如: "The shelves were lined with books."

- **形容词**:
  - 1. 系列的，成行的。例如: "The company offers a line of beauty products."
  - 2. 相关的，属于同一类的。例如: "They come from a long line of musicians."

## 短语
- "On the line" 意味着有风险或处于危险之中。例如: "Your job is on the line if you keep missing deadlines."
- "In a straight line" 指沿着直线。例如: "The path goes in a straight line through the forest."
- "Family line" 指家族血统。例如: "He is the eldest in his family line."

## 例句
- 名词: "The dancers formed a straight line across the stage."
- 动词: "She lined the cups up neatly on the shelf."
- 形容词: "The store has a new line of eco-friendly clothing."

## 同义词

- Series (名词)
- Arrange (动词)
- Straight (形容词)
