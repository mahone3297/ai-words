## 读音
/skriːm/

## 释义
- **动词**: 大声尖叫或喊叫。例如: "The child screamed in pain when he fell off his bike."
- **名词**: 尖叫声或喊叫声。例如: "The movie was so scary that it made everyone in the theater let out a collective scream."

## 短语
- "Scream for help" 意为大声呼救。
- "A blood-curdling scream" 形容令人极度恐惧的尖叫声。

## 例句
- 动词: "The baby started to scream when it was time for a diaper change."
- 名词: "The sudden loud scream startled everyone in the library."
