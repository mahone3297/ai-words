## 读音
/stɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 蜇刺，刺痛。例如: "The bee left a sting on my arm."
- **动词**: 蜇，刺痛。例如: "The wasp stung me while I was gardening."
- **名词**: (警察或卧底的)诱捕行动。例如: "The police set up a sting operation to catch the thief."
- **动词**: 使受骗，欺诈。例如: "The con artist stung several people out of their life savings."

## 短语
- "get stung" 意为被蜇或被骗。例如: "Be careful not to get stung by a jellyfish when swimming."
- "sting operation" 是指警方的诱捕行动。例如: "The TV show exposed a high-profile sting operation against a drug ring."

## 例句
- 名词: "The scorpion has a venomous sting at the end of its tail."
- 动词: "The bee stung me, causing a sharp pain."
- 名词（警察行动）: "The sting operation involved an undercover cop posing as a buyer."

## 同义词
- Noun: bite, puncture
- Verb: cheat, deceive, swindle
