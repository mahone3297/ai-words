## 读音
/vəˈkænt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 空的；未被占用的。例如: "The apartment next door is currently vacant."
- **动词**: 使空出；腾空。例如: "She vacated her office when she resigned."
- **名词**: 空缺；职位的空缺。例如: "There's a vacant position in the sales department."

## 短语
- "vacant lot" 意为闲置的土地。
- "vacant expression" 指的是空洞或无神的表情。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The hotel had several vacant rooms available for booking."
- 动词: "They will vacate the property by the end of the month."
- 名词: "The company is looking to fill two vacant management roles."

- empty
- unoccupied
- free
- available
