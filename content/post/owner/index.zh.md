## 读音
/ˈəʊnər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 拥有者，所有者。指某物或某权利的合法占有者。例如: "The car's owner is responsible for its maintenance."
- **动词**: 拥有，具有（通常与of连用）。例如: "She owns a small art gallery."

## 短语
- "owner of" 表示某物的所有者。例如: "The owner of the house has lived there for 20 years."
- "take ownership" 意味着取得所有权或承担责任。例如: "After buying the business, he took ownership and began making changes."

## 例句
- 名词: "The boat was returned to its rightful owner."
- 动词: "She proudly owns several rare books in her collection."
