## Pronunciation
/əˈtæɪn/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The act or achievement of reaching or attaining a particular level, standard, or goal. For example: "He reached his goal and attained academic excellence."
2. The state of being obtained or gained. For example: "She attained the rank of captain in the army."

### Verb
1. To acquire or gain (a particular status, skill, or knowledge). For example: "She attained fluency in Spanish after years of study."
2. To reach or come close to (a desired level or position). For example: "The company hopes to attain market leadership by 2025."
