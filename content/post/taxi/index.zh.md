## 读音
/tæksi/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种小型客车，通常供出租使用，由司机驾驶，乘客可以按需搭乘。例如: "I hailed a taxi to take me to the airport."
- **动词 (v)**: 搭乘出租车。例如: "We decided to taxi to the hotel instead of walking."

## 短语
- "Take a taxi" 意为乘坐出租车前往目的地。
- "Call a taxi" 表示打电话预订出租车。
- "Taxi rank" 是指出租车停靠和乘客等待的地方。

## 例句
- 名词: "The taxi driver helped me with my luggage."
- 动词: "We'll need to taxi to the conference center because it's too far to walk."

## 同义词
- Cab
- Limo
- Ride-hailing service (如 Uber 或 Lyft)
- Minicab
