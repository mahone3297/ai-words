## 读音
/ˈtɛndənsi/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 趋势；倾向；偏向。指事物发展或行为表现的自然或惯常的方向。例如: "There is a tendency for prices to rise in inflationary periods."
- **动词 (v)**: 倾向于；趋于。描述某人或某事可能的行为或状态。例如: "She tends to be shy around new people."

## 短语
- "a natural tendency towards something" 指某人或某物天生就倾向于某个方面。
- "tendency to do something" 表示经常或习惯性的行为趋势。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's tendency to overspend on marketing has caused financial problems."
- 动词: "In his early years, he tended to avoid confrontations."

## 同义词
- inclination
- proclivity
- disposition
- drift
- trend
