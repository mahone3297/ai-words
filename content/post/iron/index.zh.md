## 读音
/ˈaɪrən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 1. 铁，一种化学元素，符号Fe，具有银灰色金属光泽，常用于制造钢铁和其他合金。
       例句: "The wrought iron fence around the garden is very sturdy."
   - 2. (熨斗) 用于平烫衣物的器具。
       例句: "I need to iron my shirt before the meeting."

- **动词**:
   - 1. 熨烫，用熨斗使衣物平整。
       例句: "She spends hours ironing her clothes every week."
   - 2. （非正式）解决，消除。
       例句: "We need to iron out the details of the contract before signing it."

## 短语
- "Iron out" 意味着解决或消除问题或困难。
- "Iron clothes" 是指用熨斗烫平衣物。

## 例句
- 名词: "The blacksmith heated the iron in his forge."
- 动词: "Could you please iron my dress for the wedding?"
- 短语: "Let's iron out any misunderstandings before we continue the discussion."

## 同义词
- 铁: Steel, Metal
- 熨烫: Press, Smooth
- 解决: Resolve, Address
