## 读音
/ɪnˈsaɪd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 里面；内部。例如: "The treasure was hidden inside a small box."
- **副词**: 在里面；向内。例如: "Please put the letter inside the envelope."
- **介词**: 表示位置在某物之内。例如: "She sat inside the car."

## 短语
- "on the inside" 指事物的内侧或内部部分。
- "from inside" 指从内部出发或来自内部。

## 例句
- 名词: "The cat was curled up sleeping inside the basket."
- 副词: "He placed the key inside the lock and turned it."
- 介词: "She could hear the rain tapping on the window from inside the house."
