## Definitions
### Noun
- **1.** An act or result of rendering, typically a performance or interpretation of something: "The artist's rendering of the landscape was stunning."
- **2.** The act of presenting or providing something, especially in a particular form or style: "The software comes with a user-friendly rendering of complex data."
- **3.** A visual representation or reproduction, often in art or design: "A digital rendering of the building's facade."

### Verb
- **1.** To present or create a visual representation, usually by computer software: "The designer rendered the 3D model into a realistic image."
- **2.** To give a particular appearance or effect to (a material or object): "The paint rendered the old wall looking fresh."
- **3.** To execute or carry out: "She rendered her decision without delay."

### Adjective
- **1.** Characteristic or typical: "The book's writing style is very poetic and visually rendered."
- **2.** Achieved or produced by a particular method: "A physically rendered special effect."
