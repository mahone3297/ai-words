## 读音
/suːˈfɪsiəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 表面的，肤浅的，仅限于表面的。例如: "His knowledge of the subject is superficial."
- **名词**: 肤浅的人或事。例如: "She was accused of having a superficial attitude towards life."

## 短语
- "superficially" 副词形式，表示表面上地，肤浅地。例如: "The wound looked superficial, but it was actually quite deep."
- "superficial charm" 表面上的吸引力或魅力。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Their friendship was based on superficial similarities rather than shared values."
- 名词: "He was criticized for his superficial understanding of the issue."

- shallow
- trivial
- surface-level
