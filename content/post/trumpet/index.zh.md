## 读音
/trʌmpɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 小号，一种铜管乐器。例如: "The trumpeter played a beautiful melody on his trumpet."
   2. (体育)喇叭，哨子。例如: "The football referee blew his trumpet to signal the end of the match."

- **动词**: 大声宣扬，吹嘘。例如: "He was always trumpeting his achievements to anyone who would listen."

## 短语
- "blow the trumpet" 吹小号或宣布消息。
- "trumpet fanfare" 小号齐奏的乐章，常用于庆典或正式场合。

## 例句
- 名词: "The orchestra's trumpeter played a solo that left the audience in awe."
- 动词: "She was trumpeting her success in the latest project to her colleagues."
