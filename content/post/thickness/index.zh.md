## 读音
/ˈθɪknəs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 物体或材料的厚度或稠度。例如: "The wall has a thickness of 10 centimeters."
- **形容词**: 形容具有相当深度或密实度。例如: "The soup has a nice thickness to it."

## 短语
- "thickness of the material" 指材料的厚度。
- "at varying thicknesses" 表示在不同的厚度上。

## 例句
- 名词: "The wood plank's thickness allowed it to support heavy weight."
- 形容词: "The painter adjusted the paint to achieve the desired thickness."

## 同义词
- Depth
- Density
- Breadth (when referring to a horizontal dimension)
- Consistency (when describing a substance's thickness)
