## Pronunciation
/θɪknəs/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The quality or state of being thick; the degree of density or fullness. 2. The distance between two surfaces measured through their substance. 3. (Physics) The ratio of the mass per unit area of a material. 4. (Film) The darkness or opacity of a film or emulsion.
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or indicating a high degree of thickness; dense.

## Phrases
- "Increase the thickness of the layer"
- "The book's thickness made it difficult to carry."
- "A thick slice of bread"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The artist applied several coats of paint to achieve the desired thickness."
- **Adjective**: "The soup had a rich, thick consistency."
- "The tree trunk was so thick that it took both hands to encircle it."
