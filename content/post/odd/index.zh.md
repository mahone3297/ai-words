## 读音
/ɒd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 奇怪的；不寻常的。例如: "She has an odd sense of humor."
- **名词**: 单数形式，奇数（非偶数）。例如: "The sum of two odd numbers is always an even number."
- **副词**: 偶尔；时而。例如: "I only eat ice cream on odd occasions."

## 短语
- "Odd one out" 指的是在一组中与众不同的那个。
- "On odd days" 指的是不是常规的、偶尔的日子。

## 例句
- 形容词: "There was something odd about his behavior."
- 名词: "The number 5 is an odd number."
- 副词: "I usually go running, but I only do it on odd days."

## 同义词
- Unusual
- Strange
- Peculiar
- Quirky
- Bizarre
