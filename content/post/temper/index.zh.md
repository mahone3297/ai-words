## 读音
/ˈtempər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 性情，脾气。例如: "He has a quick temper."
             2. 温度，热度。例如: "The temperature dropped suddenly."

- **动词**: 调整，缓和。例如: "She tempered her words to avoid offending him."
- **形容词**: 耐性的，有节制的。例如: "A temper glass won't break easily."

## 短语
- "To lose one's temper" 意为发脾气。
- "To temper justice with mercy" 意为在执行公正时表现出怜悯。

## 例句
- 名词: "His hot temper often leads to arguments."
- 动词: "The chef tempered the chocolate to get the right consistency."
- 形容词: "The athlete was praised for his temper play in the face of adversity."
