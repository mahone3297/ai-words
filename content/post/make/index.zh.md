## 读音
/make/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 制造；制作。例如: "He makes furniture by hand."
2. 做成；达成。例如: "They finally made a deal after weeks of negotiation."
3. 生产；赚得。例如: "She makes a good salary as a lawyer."
4. 引起；导致。例如: "His actions made everyone laugh."
5. 完成。例如: "I have to make the bed before leaving for work."

- **名词**:
1. 制造；生产。例如: "The factory's output has increased since last year's make."
2. 品质；特性。例如: "This car has a great make and model."

## 短语
- "Make something out of nothing" 表示从无到有创造事物。
- "Make up one's mind" 决定，下定决心。
- "Make do with" 用现有资源凑合着用。例如: "We'll have to make do with the old sofa until we can afford a new one."
