## 读音
/ɪmˈprɪzn/

## 释义
- **动词**: 将某人关押在监狱或拘留所中；剥夺自由。例如: "The activist was imprisoned for her political beliefs."
- **名词**: 监禁的状态或行为。例如: "The sentence imposed was life imprisonment."

## 短语
- "imprisonment for life" 意为终身监禁。
- "to be imprisoned without trial" 指未经审判就被关押。

## 例句
- 动词: "The judge sentenced the criminal to 10 years' imprisonment."
- 名词: "He spent many years in the harsh conditions of imprisonment."
