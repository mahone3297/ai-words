## 读音
/skɔːr/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 计分；得分：例如 "The final score of the game was 3-1 in favor of the home team."
  - 评分；评价：例如 "She received a high score on her exam."
  - 划痕；刻痕：例如 "There were deep scores on the wooden table."

- **动词**:
  - 得分；记分：例如 "He scored a goal in the last minute of the match."
  - 划线；刻痕：例如 "The artist scored lines into the clay to create texture."

## 短语
- "Keep score" 意为记录分数或成绩。
- "Score a point" 指在比赛中得一分。
- "To have something scored off" 意为取消或抹去某项记录。

## 例句
- 名词: "The soccer match ended with a score of 2-2."
- 动词: "He scored three times in the game, leading his team to victory."
- 刻痕: "The sculptor used a chisel to score lines into the marble."
