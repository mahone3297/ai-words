## Pronunciation
/stjuːˈɛrɪd/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A female flight attendant on an airplane, responsible for passenger service and safety. [Oxford](https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/stewardess)
- **Adjective**: Referring to a female flight attendant. [Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/stewardess)

## Phrases
- "Meet the flight stewardess" to introduce a female airline attendant.
- "Onboard stewardess" to refer to the staff member during a flight.
- "Stewardess duties" encompass tasks performed by an airline attendant.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The stewardess carefully explained the safety procedures to all passengers."
- **Adjective**: "The young stewardess was calm under pressure."
- **Verb**: "The captain greeted the crew and the stewardesses before takeoff."

## Synonyms
- Air hostess
- Flight attendant (informal, includes both genders)
- Steward (male equivalent, but less common)
- Cabin crew member

## Etymology
The word "stewardess" originated from the male term "steward," which referred to attendants on ships and trains. The -ess ending was added to create a female-specific role. In modern times, "flight attendant" is a more gender-neutral term.
