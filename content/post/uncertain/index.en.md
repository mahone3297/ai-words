## Pronunciation
/ʌnˈʃʊərənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having an unsure or doubtful state; not certain or fixed. For example: "The outcome of the election is still uncertain."
- **Adverb**: In a manner that lacks certainty; doubtfully. For example: "He spoke uncertainly about his plans for the future."

## Phrases
- "Be uncertain about something" implies having doubts or lack of confidence.
- "An uncertain future" refers to a situation with unpredictability.
- "Uncertain times" describe periods of instability or insecurity.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "She felt uncertain about her decision to accept the job offer."
- **Adverb**: "His expression was uncertain as he read the news."
- "The direction of the economy remains uncertain."
