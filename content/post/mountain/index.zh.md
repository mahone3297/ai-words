## 读音
/ˈmaʊntən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 高大的山，通常指自然形成的高山。例如: "The view from the top of the mountain was breathtaking."
- **动词 (v)**: 有时用作不及物动词，表示增加、上升或堆积。例如: "Prices have been mounting steadily over the past year."

## 短语
- "Mount Everest" 是世界最高峰。
- "To climb a mountain" 指的是登山活动。
- "Mountain range" 指一系列相连的山脉。

## 例句
- 名词: "We plan to hike up the mountain next weekend."
- 动词: "Her worries seemed to mount with each passing day."
