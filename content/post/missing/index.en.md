## Pronunciation
/ˈmɪsɪŋ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Not present; absent or not found where expected. For example: "The key was missing from the hook."
  - *Figurative sense*: Lacking or deficient in something essential. For instance: "The plan is missing a crucial detail."
- **Noun**: The state of being absent or not found. Example: "Her disappearance has been a mystery since she went missing."

## Phrases
- "Go missing": To disappear or be unable to be found. Example: "The necklace went missing after the party."
- "Missing person": A person who has disappeared and whose whereabouts are unknown.
- "Missing link": Something that is believed to fill a gap or provide a connection between two related things.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "One page is missing from this book."
- **Noun**: "The authorities have launched a search for the missing hiker."

## Synonyms
- Absent
- Lost
- Gone
- Unaccounted for
- Nonexistent (when referring to something that should be present)
- AWOL (informal, mainly military use) - Absent Without Leave
- Disappeared (for the noun sense)
