## 读音
/ˈmɪsɪŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 缺失的，不在的，找不到的。例如: "The key is missing from the drawer."
- **动词**: 失去，错过。例如: "I missed the bus and had to wait for the next one."
- **名词**: 缺少的人或物。例如: "There's a missing piece in this puzzle."

## 短语
- "Be missing" 表示某物或某人不见了。
- "Missing in action" (MIA) 指战争中失踪的士兵。
- "Missing person" 指失踪的人。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The wallet was empty except for a few coins; the cash was missing."
- 动词: "I accidentally deleted an important file and now I'm trying to recover it – I really missed something!"
- 名词: "The police have launched a search for the missing child."

## 同义词
- Lost
- Absent
- Not there
