## 读音
/ˈɔːrdərli/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有秩序的，整齐的，有条理的。例如: "The room was kept very orderly."
- **名词**: 整齐，秩序。例如: "Maintaining military order is essential in boot camp."
- **副词**: 有序地，有条不紊地。例如: "The staff worked orderly to complete the task."

## 短语
- "in orderly fashion" 表示以有条理的方式。
- "orderly progression" 指有顺序的发展或进展。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The files were arranged in an orderly manner on the shelf."
- 名词: "The hospital assigned me an orderly to assist with my recovery."
- 副词: "The students lined up orderly for their lunch break."
