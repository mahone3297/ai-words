## 读音
/mɛs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一团糟；混乱： "The room was in a terrible mess after the party."
  - 混乱的状态；紊乱： "His paperwork was always in a mess."
  - 食物，尤指随便准备的餐食： "I'll just whip up a quick mess for dinner."

- **动词**:
  - 弄乱；搞得一团糟： "Don't mess up my organized files."
  - 搞砸；破坏： "He really messed up his chances by being late."

## 短语
- "make a mess" 意为制造混乱或弄脏。
- "clean up the mess" 意为清理混乱或收拾残局。

## 例句
- 名词: "It took them hours to clean up the mess after the kids' playtime."
- 动词: "Stop messing around and get to work!"
  
## 同义词
- Disorder
- Chaos
- Confusion
- Disarray
- Shambles
