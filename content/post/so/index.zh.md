## 读音
/soʊ/

## 释义
- **副词**: 
    - 表示程度，意为“如此地，这么样”。例如: "She was so happy that she cried."
    - 表示结果，意为“以至于”。例如: "He worked so hard that he fell asleep at his desk."
    - 强调并列，意为“也”。例如: "Not only is she smart, but she's also so kind."

- **连词**: 
    因此，所以。例如: "It was raining, so we stayed inside."

## 短语
- "so...that..." 结构用来表示结果状语从句，意为“如此...以至于...”。

## 例句
- 副词: "The weather was so beautiful that we decided to have a picnic."
- 连词: "I was tired, so I went to bed early."
