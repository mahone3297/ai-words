## 读音
/səˈrəndər/

## 释义
- **动词**: 自愿交出，投降，放弃。例如: "The army surrendered to the enemy after a long siege."
- **名词**: 投降，缴械，屈服。例如: "The act of surrender is often seen as a sign of weakness."

## 短语
- "Surrender oneself" 意味着自愿交给或屈服于某人或某种情况。
- "Surrender arms" 在军事中指的是交出武器。

## 例句
- 动词: "After weeks of resistance, the rebels finally surrendered to the government forces."
- 名词: "The treaty included provisions for unconditional surrender."

## 同义词
- Yield
- Give up
- Capitulate
- Submit
- Quit
