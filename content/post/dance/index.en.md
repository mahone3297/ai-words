## Definitions
- **Noun**: (v.t.) The act of moving rhythmically to music, often with a series of steps or patterns. "The dance floor was filled with people gracefully moving to the beat."
- **Verb**: To perform or engage in dancing. "She loves to dance at weddings."
- **Noun**: A social event or occasion where dancing takes place. "The annual dance competition drew many participants."

## Phrases
- "Dance the night away" means to enjoy dancing until late into the evening.
- "Learn to dance" refers to acquiring the skill of dancing.
- "Ballroom dancing" specifies styles like waltz, tango, or foxtrot.
