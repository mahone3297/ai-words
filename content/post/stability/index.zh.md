## 读音
/stəˈbɪləti/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 稳定；稳固：指事物保持平衡、不变或可预测的状态。例如: "The government is working on improving economic stability."
    - 安定；平稳：指情感、心理或社会状态的稳定。例如: "She values the stability of her home life."

## 短语
- "Maintain stability" 意为保持稳定。
- "Stability of the system" 指系统的稳定性。
- "Financial stability" 指金融稳定。

## 例句
- "The new policy aims to ensure long-term stability in the region."
- "Her job provides her with the stability she needs for her family."
- "The stock market's stability is crucial for investors' confidence."

## 同义词
- Balance
- Steadiness
- Constancy
- Equilibrium
- Firmness
