## Word: Morning

## Pronunciation
/ˈmɔːnɪŋ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The early part of the day, from sunrise to noon, or until the first meal (usually breakfast). For example: "I love going for a run in the morning."
- **Noun (figurative)**: The beginning or early stage of something; the start of a new period or activity. Example: "This project marks a new morning for our company."

### Phrases
- "Good morning": A polite greeting used when meeting someone for the first time during the morning hours.
- "Morning routine": The series of activities one typically does upon waking up, such as washing, dressing, and having breakfast.
- "In the wee/small hours of the morning": Refers to the very early hours before dawn.

### Example Sentences
- **Noun (time of day)**: "She woke up early every morning to watch the sunrise."
- **Noun (figurative)**: "After a difficult year, we're finally seeing a brighter morning for our economy."
