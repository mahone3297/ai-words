## Pronunciation
/snuː/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A utensil with a bowl-like cup and a handle, used for eating liquid or solid food.
- In a figurative sense, a small quantity or amount.
- In gaming, a point or score awarded, often in card games.

### Verb
- To serve or eat food using a spoon.
- To take or use a spoonful of something.
- To scoop or gather (a substance) with a spoon.

### Adjective
- Relating to or denoting a spoon as a utensil.
- Small or delicate in quantity or size.

## Phrases
- "Take a spoonful of sugar"
- "Eat with a spoon"
- "A spoonful of kindness goes a long way"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She ladled soup into each person's bowl with a large serving spoon."
- **Verb**: "He scooped some ice cream into his bowl using a long-handled spoon."
- **Adjective**: "The children enjoyed their dessert, each holding a brightly colored plastic spoon."
