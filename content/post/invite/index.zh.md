## 读音
/ɪnˈvaɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 邀请，请求参加。例如: "They invited us to their wedding."
- **名词**: 邀请，请柬。例如: "I received an invite to the opening ceremony."

## 短语
- " extend an invitation" 邀请某人。
- "accept/reject an invitation" 接受/拒绝邀请。
- "send out invitations" 发送邀请函。

## 例句
- 动词: "She invited him to her birthday party."
- 名词: "The invitation said 'black tie' dress code."
- 作为名词用于句子时，通常需要加上冠词或物主代词，例如: "I got an invite to the concert."
