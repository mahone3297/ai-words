## 读音
/ˈziːbrə/

## 释义
- **名词**: 哺乳动物，斑马，以其黑白相间的条纹而著名。例如: "Zebras can be found in savannas and grasslands of Africa."
- **形容词**: 指具有类似斑马条纹特征的。例如: "The zebra-striped shirt caught my eye in the store."

## 短语
- "Zebra crossing" 是一种有黑白条纹的人行横道。
- "Zebra print" 指的是模仿斑马条纹的图案设计。

## 例句
- 名词: "The zebras were grazing peacefully in the African plain."
- 形容词: "She was wearing a zebra-print dress to the party."

## 同义词
- Striped horse (在某些语境下)
- Horses with black and white stripes
- Dapple grey (在描述某些马的颜色时)
