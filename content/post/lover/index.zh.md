## 读音
/ˈlʌvər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 1. 热爱者，爱好者：A person who has a strong affection or passion for something or someone.
        - 例句: "She is a true lover of classical music."
    - 2. 情人，爱人：A romantic partner or someone with whom one is in love.
        - 例句: "They have been lovers for years."

## 短语
- "lover boy/girl" 指特别受异性喜欢的人。
- "secret lover" 指秘密的情人或外遇对象。

## 例句
- 作为名词: "She is a lover of art and visits galleries frequently."
- 作为名词（指情人）: "They met in college and have been secret lovers ever since."
