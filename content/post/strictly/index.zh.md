## 读音
/ˈstrɪktli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 严格地，严密地，精确地。表示按照规定、规则或标准不折不扣地执行或解释。例如: "The teacher marked the exam strictly according to the grading rubric."
- **形容词**: 严格的，严谨的。描述某人或某事物要求高，不容许有任何偏差。例如: "He has a strictly disciplined lifestyle."

## 短语
- "Strictly speaking" 意为从技术上讲，严格来说。
- "Strictly confidential" 表示极其保密，严禁泄露。

## 例句
- 副词: "You must follow the instructions strictly to complete this experiment."
- 形容词: "The military has a strictly enforced code of conduct."
