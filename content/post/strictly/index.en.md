## Pronunciation
/ˈstrɪktli/

## Definitions
### Adverb
- **Strictly speaking**: In a strict, literal, or exact manner. For example: "Strictly speaking, you should arrive on time."
- **Strictly**: Without exception or leniency;严格按照规则。 For example: "She followed the rules strictly."
- **Stricter**: Referring to a higher degree of severity or rigidity. For example: "The new policy is stricter than the old one."

## Phrases
- "Be strict with someone" means to enforce rules or standards firmly.
- "Strictly confidential" indicates that information is not to be shared.
- "Strict discipline" refers to a rigorous system of rules and punishments.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "She was strictly against the idea of canceling the event."
- **Comparative**: "He's a stricter teacher than his colleague."
- **Adjective**: "He maintained a strictly professional relationship with his clients."
