## 读音

## 释义
- **副词**: 轻松地，容易地，不费力地。例如: "He solved the problem easily."
- **形容词**: 容易的，简单的。例如: "The task was so easy that anyone could do it easily."

## 短语
- "do something easily" 做某事很轻松
- "learn easily" 学习东西快

## 例句
- 副词: "She can play the piano easily after years of practice."
