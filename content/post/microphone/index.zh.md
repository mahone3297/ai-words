## 读音
/maɪkrəˈfəʊn/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种将声音转化为电波信号的设备，通常用于放大声音或录音。例如: "She held the microphone close to her mouth to speak."
- **动词 (v)**: 使用麦克风进行讲话或录制声音。例如: "The singer was mic'd up for the live performance."

## 短语
- "Hold the microphone" 意为握住麦克风。
- "On microphone" 表示正在使用或被麦克风捕捉到声音。
- "Off microphone" 指麦克风未开启或声音未被收录。

## 例句
- 名词: "The radio host spoke into the microphone to address his audience."
- 动词: "The sound engineer carefully placed the microphone in front of the speaker."
