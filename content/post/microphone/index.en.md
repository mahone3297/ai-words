## Pronunciation
/maɪˈkrəʊfəʊn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: An electronic device with a sensitive transducer that converts sound waves into electrical signals for amplification, recording, transmission, or reproduction of sound. It typically consists of a diaphragm vibrated by sound waves, which produces corresponding variations in an electric current.

Example Sentence:
- "The singer tested the microphone before starting her performance."

Phrases:
- "On the microphone": Refers to someone speaking or singing into a microphone, often in a public setting like a concert or broadcast.
- "Pass the microphone": This phrase can be literal, referring to physically passing the microphone to someone else during a meeting or event, or metaphorical, suggesting giving someone a chance to speak or share their opinion.
  
## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The podcast host spoke clearly into the microphone so listeners could hear every word."
- "In the recording studio, the lead singer stood behind the pop filter attached to the microphone."
