## 读音
/ˈdʒʌstɪfaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    1. 证明（行动、观点等）是正当的；为...辩护：例如, "He tried to justify his decision by explaining the circumstances."
    2. 使版面排得匀称，在文字两侧留出相同的空间：例如, "The printer justified the text to create a clean, symmetrical appearance."

## 短语
- "justify one's actions" 意味着为自己的行为提供合理的理由。
- "justify the means" 指认为目的合理，即使手段可能不道德。

## 例句
- "She had to justify her spending to her strict parents."
- "The designer used a justified alignment for the text in the brochure."
