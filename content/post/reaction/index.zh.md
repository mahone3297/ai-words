## 读音
/riˈækʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 反应；回应：对某种刺激或情况的物理、化学或情绪上的响应。例如: "The chemical reaction produced heat and light."
  - 感受；意见：对某一事件或情况的个人看法或评论。例如: "I was interested in hearing her reaction to the news."

## 短语
- "chemical reaction" 指化学变化。
- "positive/negative reaction" 表示正面或负面的回应。

## 例句
- 名词: "The patient had an allergic reaction to the medicine."
- 名词: "His reaction to the news was one of surprise."
