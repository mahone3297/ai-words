## Word: Funny

## Pronunciation
/ˈfʌni/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Causing laughter or amusement; humorous. For example: "He told a funny joke that made everyone laugh."
  
  > "*The comedian's witty remarks were so funny that the audience couldn't stop laughing.*"
- **Adjective (informal)**: Somewhat strange or odd; peculiar. For example: "There's something funny going on here."
  
  > "*I found it funny how he wore his shoes on the wrong feet, but he seemed unaware of it.*"

## Phrases
- "Funny bone": The part of the elbow that, when struck, produces a tingling sensation rather than pain; medically known as the ulnar nerve.
- "Funny business": Strange or suspicious activity; underhanded dealings.
- "In a funny way": In an unexpected or unusual manner.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective (humorous)**: "Her sense of humor is so funny that she can turn any situation into a comedic one."
- **Adjective (peculiar)**: "It was funny how the old clock still worked even though it had been broken for years."
