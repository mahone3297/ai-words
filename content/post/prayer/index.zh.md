## 读音
/prəˈɛr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 祈祷；祷告。一种向神或超自然力量表达愿望、感谢或悔罪的行为。例如: "She attended church to say her evening prayers."
- **动词**: 做祈祷；祈求。例如: "He always prays for his family's safety before going to sleep."

## 短语
- "Offer a prayer" 意为提出或进行祈祷。
- "Pray for someone" 是为某人的福祉而祈祷。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were taught to say their bedtime prayers every night."
- 动词: "She took a moment to pray for guidance before making an important decision."

## 同义词
- Supplication
- Petition
- Entreaty
- Appeal
- Invocation
