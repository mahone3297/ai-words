## Pronunciation
/prɛə(r)/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. An invocation or request addressed to a deity or higher power, often seeking guidance, comfort, or assistance. 2. A formal or solemn expression of thanks, gratitude, or devotion, typically in religious or spiritual contexts. 3. A silent or inward thought or petition, often directed to God or a divine being.

- **Verb**: To utter or perform a prayer, usually in a religious or spiritual context.
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or expressing prayerful feelings or thoughts.

## Phrases
- "Say a prayer" (to ask for divine help or intervention)
- "Prayer time" (a designated period for prayer, especially in religious traditions)
- "Prayerful silence" (a moment of quiet reflection and meditation)
- "Pray for someone" (to ask for their well-being or protection)

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After finishing her meal, she took a moment to silently pray before leaving the restaurant."
- **Verb**: "The priest led the congregation in reciting the Lord's Prayer."
- **Adjective**: "Her serene face reflected a prayerful calmness during the storm."

## Synonyms
- Supplication
- Invocation
- Devotion
- Fervent request
- Benediction
- Amen (as a response or conclusion to a prayer)
