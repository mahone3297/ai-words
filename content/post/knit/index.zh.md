## 读音
/knɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 编织；针织。例如: "She is knitting a scarf for her grandson."
2. （使）紧密联合，团结。例如: "The community was knitted together by a shared sense of purpose."

- **名词**:
编织物；针织品。例如: "The sweater is made of a soft wool knit."

## 短语
- "knit together" 意为将…编织在一起或紧密结合。例如: "The fibers were knitted together to form a strong fabric."
- "to knit one's brows" 表示皱眉，集中注意力。例如: "He knitted his brows in deep concentration."

## 例句
- 动词: "She enjoys knitting in front of the fireplace on cold evenings."
- 名词: "The baby wore a hand-knit hat, keeping its head warm."
