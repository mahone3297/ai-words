## 读音
/pɑːrk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个供公众休闲和娱乐的绿地，通常有树木、草坪和设施。例如: "We had a picnic in the park on Sunday."
- **名词**: 停车场，供停放车辆的地方。例如: "There's a large parking park behind the mall."
- **动词**: 将（车）停在特定地方。例如: "I parked my car on the street outside my friend's house."
- **动词**: （棒球）接球并阻止其前进。例如: "The outfielder made an impressive park of the fly ball."

## 短语
- "Park your car" 意思是将车停在某个位置。
- "City park" 指城市中的公共绿地。
- "Dog park" 是专为狗狗设立的公园，允许它们自由活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The kids loved playing on the swings in the park."
- 动词: "Please park your car in the designated area."
- 动词（棒球）: "He parked the ball over the fence for a home run."

## 同义词
- Stop (for parking)
- Green space (for public parks)
- Rest area (for certain types of parking)
- Grounds (for larger areas)
