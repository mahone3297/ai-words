## Pronunciation
/pɑːrk/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A place designed for recreational activities, typically with grass, trees, and facilities for sports or relaxation.** For example: "The children enjoyed playing in the local park."
2. **A large area of land set aside for conservation or wildlife preservation.** For example: "The national park is home to various endangered species."
3. **An amusement park or theme park with rides, games, and attractions.** For example: "We had a fantastic day at the amusement park."

### Verb
1. **To go to a park for leisure or recreation.** For example: "We usually take our dog for a walk in the park on weekends."
2. **To leave or abandon (a vehicle) in a public space, often illegally.** For example: "Unfortunately, some people park their cars illegally on the street."
