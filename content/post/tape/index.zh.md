## 读音
/teɪp/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 
    - 1. 磁带，尤指录音或录像带。例如: "I recorded the concert on an old cassette tape."
    - 2. 布基胶带，宽胶带。例如: "Use some duct tape to fix the broken pipe temporarily."
    - 3. 尺带，卷尺。例如: "The surveyor measured the distance with a tape measure."

- **动词 (v)**: 
    - 用胶带粘贴或固定。例如: "She taped the poster onto the wall."

## 短语
- "tape recording" 录音
- "to be on tape" 被录在磁带上
- "tape something down" 用胶带固定某物

## 例句
- 名词: "The music store still sells vinyl records and cassette tapes."
- 动词: "She quickly taped the package shut before sending it off."

- **名词**:
    - recording
    - cassette
    - adhesive strip
- **动词**:
    - stick with tape
    - secure with tape
    - fasten with tape
