## 读音
/tɪl/

## 释义
- **介词**: 直到；直至。例如: "I'll work till midnight." 
- **名词**: 收银机；找零抽屉。例如: "She counted the money in the till."
- **动词**: (常与until连用)耕犁；翻土。例如: "He tilled the soil for the new crop."

## 短语
- "till the end" 意为直到最后。
- "till the cows come home" 是一种表达无限期等待或持续到很晚的习语。

## 例句
- 介词: "I'll wait for you till you finish your work."
- 名词: "The cashier put the cash in the till."
- 动词: "He tilled the field to prepare it for spring planting."
