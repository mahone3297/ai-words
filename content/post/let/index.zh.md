## 读音
/let/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 允许；准许。例如: "I won't let you leave until you finish your homework."
2. 租给；出租。例如: "She lets out her spare room to a student."
3. 让（某事）发生。例如: "Don't let the situation get out of control."

- **名词**:
- 准许；允许。例如: "The teacher gave him the let to go to the restroom."

## 短语
- "Let it be" 意为顺其自然，不要干预。
- "Let someone down" 意为让某人失望。
- "Let off steam" 意为发泄情绪或释放压力。

## 例句
- 动词: "You can't let fear control your decisions."
- 名词: "The rule gives them no let for relaxation."

- Permit
