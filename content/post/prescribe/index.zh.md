## 读音
/prɪˈskraɪb/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 医生开药方。例如: "The doctor prescribed antibiotics for my infection."
  2. 规定；指示。例如: "The teacher prescribed a strict study schedule for the students."
  3. 命令；建议。例如: "The coach prescribed a new training routine for the athletes."

## 短语
- "Prescribe medication" 意为开药。
- "Prescribe a course of action" 指的是建议或规定一系列行动。

## 例句
- "The pediatrician prescribed a daily dose of vitamin D for the child."
- "The boss prescribed a more collaborative approach to team projects."
- "The nutritionist prescribed cutting down on sugar to improve health."

- Order
- Instruct
