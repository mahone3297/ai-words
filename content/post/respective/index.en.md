## Pronunciation
/rɪˈspektɪv/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Referring to each member of a group individually or separately. It implies that each has their own distinct quality or characteristic. For example: "Each student received a respective prize for their achievement."
- **Adverb**: In a particular way that relates to each person or thing in a specific group. For example: "The teacher distributed the books to the students respectively."

## Phrases
- "Respective rights" refers to individual rights granted to each member of a group.
- "To treat each case respectively" means to handle them with care and according to their unique circumstances.
- "Their respective fields" indicates their areas of expertise or work.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The students were asked to submit their respective reports by the end of the week."
- **Adverb**: "In his lecture, he addressed each question from the audience respectively."
