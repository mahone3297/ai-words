## 读音
/rɪˈspektɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 各自的，分别的，各自的（指两个人或事物各自的情况）。例如: "They have respective roles in the project."
- **介词**: 关于，就…而言。例如: "Their opinions on the matter were respective."

## 短语
- "In their respective fields" 意为在各自的领域。
- "Respective responsibilities" 指各自的责任。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Each team member has their respective tasks to complete."
- 介词: "They discussed their respective ideas for the new project."

## 同义词
- Individual
- Separate
- Distinct
- Particular
- Unique
