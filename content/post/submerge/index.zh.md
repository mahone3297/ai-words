## 读音
/ˈsʌbmɜːrdʒ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 使（物体）浸没或潜入水中。例如: "The submarine submerged beneath the ocean's surface."
  2. （情感或记忆）淹没，压抑。例如: "Her sadness was submerged by her determination to move on."

## 短语
- "submerged in water" 指物体完全浸在水中。
- "submerge oneself in work" 意味着全神贯注于工作，忘记周围的事物。

## 例句
- 她轻轻地将花瓶放入水中，直到它完全被水淹没。
- He submerged himself in his studies, ignoring all distractions around him.
