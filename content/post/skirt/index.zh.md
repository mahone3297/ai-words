## 读音
/skɜːrt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 女性的下装，通常指裙子。例如: "She was wearing a beautiful red skirt."
- **动词**: （非正式）指避开或轻触某物边缘。例如: "The horse skirted around the puddle."

## 短语
- "Wear a skirt" 意为穿着裙子。
- "Skirt the issue" 意味着避开问题或不直接回答。
- "The hem of the skirt" 指裙子的下摆。

## 例句
- 名词: "She chose a flowy skirt for the summer picnic."
- 动词: "He skirted the subject when asked about his past."

## 同义词
- Dress
- Skirt-like garment
- Garment for women's lower body
- Tunic
- Wrap-around (for clothing)
