## 读音
/læzi/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 缺乏活力，不愿意做事的；懒散的。例如: "He's too lazy to clean his room."
- **名词**: 懒汉，懒人。例如: "The lazy cat just slept all day."

## 短语
- "lazybones" 用来形容非常懒惰的人。
- "too lazy to do something" 表示懒得去做某事。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She's feeling lazy today and doesn't want to go out."
- 名词: "The lazy dog just lay in the sun all afternoon."

## 同义词
- Indolent
- Slothful
- Unmotivated
-慵懒
- 不勤快的
