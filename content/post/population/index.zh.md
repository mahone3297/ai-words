## 读音
/ˌpɑːpjuˈleɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个地区、国家或特定时间的人口数量；全体居民。例如: "The city's population has doubled in the last decade."
- **名词**: (生物) 种群，某一物种在某一特定区域内的全部个体。例如: "The endangered species has a small and fragile population."

## 短语
- "Population growth" 指人口的增长。
- "Population density" 指人口密度，即单位面积内的人口数量。
- "Age distribution of the population" 描述人口中各年龄段的比例。

## 例句
- "The current world population is estimated to be over 7 billion."
- "The small village has a population of only a few hundred people."
- "The study analyzed the age distribution of the population in the city."

## 同义词
- Inhabitants
- Residents
- Folk

- Demography (更倾向于指人口统计学)
