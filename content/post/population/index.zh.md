## 读音
/ˌpɑːpjuˈleɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个地区、国家或特定时间的人口数量；全体居民。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) city's [population](/zh/post/population/) has doubled [in](/zh/post/in/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [last](/zh/post/last/) [decade](/zh/post/decade/)."
- **名词**: (生物) 种群，某一物种在某一特定区域内的全部个体。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) endangered species has [a](/zh/post/a/) [small](/zh/post/small/) [and](/zh/post/and/) fragile [population](/zh/post/population/)."

## 短语
- "[Population](/zh/post/population/) [growth](/zh/post/growth/)" 指人口的增长。
- "[Population](/zh/post/population/) [density](/zh/post/density/)" 指人口密度，即单位面积内的人口数量。
- "[Age](/zh/post/age/) [distribution](/zh/post/distribution/) [of](/zh/post/of/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [population](/zh/post/population/)" 描述人口中各年龄段的比例。
