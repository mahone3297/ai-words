## 读音
/ˈʌpər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 在上面的，上部的，较高的。例如: "The upper floor of the building has a great view."
- **名词**: 上部分，上层。例如: "Please hand me the upper shelf."
- **名词**: 较高级的，上级的。例如: "The upper management is responsible for major decisions."

## 短语
- "upper class" 高级阶层，上流社会。
- "upper body" 身体的上半部分，通常指胸部以上。
- "upper limit" 上限，最高限制。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The upper part of the mountain is covered in snow."
- 名词: "The upper management decided to implement new policies."
- 名词: "She comes from an upper-class family."
