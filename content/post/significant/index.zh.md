## 读音
/ˈsɪɡnɪfɪkənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 重要的，有意义的，显著的。例如: "The discovery had significant implications for medical research."
- **名词**: 重要人物，有影响力的人。例如: "She is a significant figure in the art world."

## 短语
- "significantly different" 表示显著不同的。
- "significant other" 指重要的人，通常指伴侣或配偶。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The increase in sales was statistically significant."
- 名词: "Her contributions made her a significant other in his life."
