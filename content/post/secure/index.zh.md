## 读音
/ˈsɪkjʊr/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 安全的，可靠的；确保的。例如: "The house has secure locks for added safety."  
- **动词**: 使安全，固定，获得。例如: "She secured the door before going to bed."  
- **名词**: （尤指工作或合同）保障，职位。例如: "He finally got a secure job after years of unemployment."

## 短语
- "secure something against something" 保护某物免受某事侵害。例如: "We need to secure our property against theft."
- "secure a loan" 获得贷款。例如: "She managed to secure a large loan for her business."
- "secure one's position" 确保自己的地位。例如: "His excellent performance secured his position in the team."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The bank's online system is highly secure, protecting customers' information."
- 动词: "He quickly secured the tent with ropes and pegs."
- 名词: "After years of hard work, he finally felt some financial security."
