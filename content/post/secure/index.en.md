## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Adjective**: Firmly held or protected from harm or risk; not liable to be lost, stolen, or damaged. For example: "The company ensures the security of its data."
2. **Noun**: A feeling of being safe or protected; confidence in oneself or a situation. For example: "She felt secure in her relationship."

### Verb
1. To make something or someone safe from harm, danger, or unauthorized access. For example: "We secured the building before leaving."
2. To ensure the confidentiality or integrity of information. For example: "They securely encrypted all sensitive data."

### Adverb
1. In a manner that provides safety or protection. For example: "He locked the door securely."
