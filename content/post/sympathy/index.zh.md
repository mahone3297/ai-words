## 读音
/sɪmpəθi/

## 释义
- **名词**: 对他人的困难、悲伤或痛苦表示理解和关心的情感。例如: "She offered her sympathy to the grieving family."
- **动词**: 表达同情或共鸣。例如: "To sympathize with someone's situation means to share their feelings."
- **形容词**: 富有同情心的，表示理解的。例如: "He was known for his sympathetic nature."

## 短语
- "Express sympathy" 意为表达同情。
- "Sympathy card" 是一种用来表达慰问的卡片。
- "In sympathy with" 表示对某事有共鸣或支持。

## 例句
- 名词: "The family appreciated the outpouring of sympathy from friends and neighbors."
- 动词: "He tried to sympathize with her, but he couldn't understand her pain."
- 形容词: "The doctor was very sympathetic towards the patient's fears."
