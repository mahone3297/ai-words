## 读音
/saʊnd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 声音，声响。例如: "The sound of birds singing woke me up in the morning."
  - (身体或器官)健康状况。例如: "She is in good sound after the operation."
  - (乐器等的)音色。例如: "The violin produced a rich, full sound."

- **动词**:
  - 发出声音。例如: "The bell sounded for the beginning of class."
  - 听起来，显得。例如: "That plan sounds promising."

- **形容词**:
  - 完好的，无损坏的。例如: "The old vase was still in sound condition."
  - 坚实的，可靠的。例如: "His arguments were sound and convincing."

## 短语
- "in sound" 表示处于良好的状态。
- "make a sound" 意为发出声音。
- "sound asleep" 形容睡得很沉。

## 例句
- 名词: "The baby fell asleep to the soothing sound of the ocean waves."
- 动词: "The alarm sounded, signaling an emergency."
- 形容词: "The investment advice seemed sound, so he followed it."
