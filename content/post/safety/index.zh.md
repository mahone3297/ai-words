## 读音
/səˈfeɪti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 安全；平安。指没有危险、伤害或事故的状态。例如: "The primary concern of the company is the safety of its employees."
- **形容词**: 安全的；无害的。形容事物不会造成危险或伤害。例如: "It's important to use safety equipment when working with machinery."

## 短语
- "safety measures" 安全措施
- "safety net" 安全网，保障措施
- "safety first" 安全第一

## 例句
- 名词: "The new car model has been designed with advanced safety features."
- 形容词: "The toy passed all safety tests and is suitable for children under three."
