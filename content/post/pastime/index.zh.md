## 读音
/pæsˈtaɪm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 消遣；娱乐活动；业余爱好。例如: "Reading is my favorite pastime."
- **动词**: （过去式，过去分词）消磨时间；娱乐。例如: "They pastime their evenings with board games."

## 短语
- "A good pastime for rainy days" 意为适合阴雨天的娱乐活动。
- "To pass the time with a pastime" 意为通过某种娱乐活动来打发时间。

## 例句
- 名词: "Gardening is a popular pastime for many retirees."
- 动词: "She pastimes herself by painting during her free hours."

- Recreation
- Leisure activity
