## 读音
/tel/

## 释义
- **名词**: 故事，传说，叙述。例如: "She told an interesting tale about her travels."
- **动词**: 讲述，叙述（故事）。例如: "The old man was known for his ability to tale captivating stories."

## 短语
- "tale of woe" 悲惨的故事或经历。
- "a tall tale" 夸张或难以置信的故事。
- "spin a tale" 创造或编造一个故事。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children sat around the campfire, listening to their grandfather's tales."
- 动词: "He was adept at taling his adventures in such a way that they sounded true."
