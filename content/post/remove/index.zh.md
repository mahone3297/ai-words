## 读音
/rɪˈmuːv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 移除，拿掉。例如: "Please remove your shoes before entering the house."
  2. 解雇，开除。例如: "He was removed from his position due to poor performance."
  3. 删除，去掉（信息、内容等）。例如: "She removed the incorrect information from the report."
  4. 脱掉（衣物）。例如: "He removed his jacket and hung it on the back of the chair."

## 短语
- "Remove oneself" 意为离开或移出某个地方。
- "Remove the obstacle" 意为移除障碍物。
- "Remove stains" 意为去除污渍。

## 例句
- 移除: "The cleaner removed all the dirt from the carpet."
- 解雇: "The company decided to remove several employees to cut costs."
- 删除: "She removed the controversial paragraph from her essay."
- 脱掉: "He removed his tie after a long day at work."

- Take away
