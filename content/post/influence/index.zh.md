## 读音
/ˈɪnflʊəns/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   影响，势力。指对某人或某事产生改变或改变方向的力量。例如: "Her good work had a positive influence on her colleagues."
   作用，效果。指某种因素产生的结果或改变。例如: "The drug had a significant influence on the patient's recovery."

- **动词**:
   影响，左右。指对某人的思想、行为或决定产生作用。例如: "His opinion greatly influenced her decision."

## 短语
- "Under someone's influence" 意为在某人的影响下。
- "Influence peddling" 指的是利用权力或影响力来获取利益的行为。
- "Exert one's influence" 意为运用影响力。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist's work has had a profound influence on contemporary art."
- 动词: "His mentor's advice significantly influenced his career choices."
