## Pronunciation
/ˈhʌləʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: An absence or lack of something; a privation. For example: "Without water, life is impossible."
- **Adverb**: In the absence or without the presence of something; not having. For example: "She can't work without her glasses."
- **Preposition**: Expressing negation or exclusion; not with or by. For example: "The experiment was conducted without any interference."

## Phrases
- "In the absence of" signifies when something is not present.
- "Without warning" implies a sudden, unexpected occurrence.
- "Without hesitation" means without any delay or uncertainty.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The garden flourished without proper care."
- **Adverb**: "He left without saying goodbye."
- **Preposition**: "They decided to go on the trip without checking the weather forecast."

## Synonyms
- None (since "without" is primarily a preposition and adverb)
- Lack
- Withoutness
- Void
- Scarcity

Note: "None" is mentioned as a synonym because "without" doesn't have direct equivalent nouns or synonyms like it does for other parts of speech. However, it's often used to indicate the absence of something in a list.
