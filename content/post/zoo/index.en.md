## Definitions
- **Noun**: A facility where animals are kept for public exhibition and educational purposes, often with the aim of conservation and research. For example: "The kids loved visiting the zoo last weekend."
- **Noun**: (informal) A group of people gathered together, often for a specific purpose or activity. For example: "The after-work zoo was full of colleagues chatting and laughing."
- **Verb**: To house or confine animals, typically in a zoo setting. For example: "The zookeepers are working hard to create a natural habitat for the animals."

## Phrases
- "Take a trip to the zoo" means to visit a zoo for leisure or educational purposes.
- "A zookeeper's job is to maintain the zoo" highlights their responsibilities.
- "Endangered species at the zoo" refers to protected animals in captivity.
