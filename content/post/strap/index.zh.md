## 读音
/stræp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 带子，尤指宽的皮带或布带。例如: "He fastened the backpack with two sturdy straps."
       2. 支持物，固定装置。例如: "The sofa has adjustable straps for extra support."
- **动词**: 固定，绑紧，用带子束住。例如: "Strap the safety belt across your lap."

## 短语
- "strap something on" 意味着将某物系在身上或固定在某处。例如: "Make sure to strap on your helmet before riding the bike."
- "strap something down" 用于确保物品被牢固地固定。例如: "Strap the luggage down tightly on the roof rack."

## 例句
- 名词: "The camera came with a comfortable neck strap."
- 动词: " Strap the skis to your backpack securely before heading up the mountain."
