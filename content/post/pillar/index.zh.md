## 读音
/pɪˈlɑːr/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 建筑中的柱子，支撑结构的主要垂直构件。例如: "The ancient temple still stands thanks to its strong pillars."
2. 支撑物，支柱，常比喻重要的支持者或基础。例如: "She is the pillar of strength for her family."

- **形容词 adj**:
1. 柱状的，像柱子的。例如: "The pillar-shaped rocks in the desert are a natural wonder."

## 短语
- "Pillar of society" 指社会的重要成员或基石。
- "Pillared porch" 是指有柱子的门廊。

## 例句
- 名词: "The grand cathedral has magnificent marble pillars that reach towards the ceiling."
- 形容词: "The tree had grown into a pillar, providing shade for the garden."
