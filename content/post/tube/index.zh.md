## 读音
/tjuːb/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 管，筒，如塑料管或金属管。例如: "The plumber used a long tube to fix the drain."
   2. 电视或无线电的管状部件，尤指阴极射线管（在旧式电视机中）。例如: "The old TV had a big tube inside."
   3. 地铁，尤指伦敦的地铁系统。例如: "I'll take the tube to work today."
- **动词 (v)**: 把…装入管内或通过管道输送。例如: "The paint was tube-d into the can."

## 短语
- "tube station" 指地铁站。
- "London Underground" 或 "the Tube" 特指伦敦的地铁网络。

## 例句
- 名词: "He used a metal tube to transport the powder safely."
- 动词: "The dentist tube-fed the medicine into my tooth."
- 地铁用法: "She takes the Victoria line on the Tube to get to her office."

## 同义词
- Pipe
- Cylinder
- Channel
- Subway (for the transportation system)
- Cathode-ray tube (for the electronic component)
