## 读音
/nap/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 短时间的睡眠，尤指日间的小憩。例如: "I'm going to take a quick nap before dinner."
- **动词**:
  - 睡一会儿。例如: "Don't fall asleep during the meeting; try not to nap."

## 短语
- "power nap" 指短暂的、恢复精力的小憩。
- "cat nap" 常指非常短的、不深的睡眠。

## 例句
- 名词: "After a long day at work, all I want is a nice, long nap."
- 动词: "The baby napped for an hour this afternoon."

- doze
