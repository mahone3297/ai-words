## 读音
/ˈmænɪdʒər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 负责管理、监督或控制一个组织、公司、团队或项目的人员。例如: "The new manager has a lot of experience in the industry."
- **动词**: 某人处理、操纵或控制事务。例如: "She managed to finish the project on time despite the challenges."

## 短语
- "Managerial role" 指的是管理职位或职责。
- "Manage expectations" 意味着调整或处理他人的期望。
- "To manage a team" 是指领导和指导一个团队。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's sales manager is responsible for overseeing the sales team."
- 动词: "He managed to negotiate a better deal with the supplier."
