## 读音
/troʊˈpɪkl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 与热带地区或气候有关的。例如: "The tropical rainforest is home to an incredible variety of species."
- **名词**: 热带地区，热带气候。例如: "They are planning a vacation to a tropical island."

## 短语
- "Tropical storm" 指热带气旋，通常伴有强风和暴雨。
- "Tropical climate" 指一年四季温暖且湿润的气候类型。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The hotel had a beautiful tropical garden with exotic flowers."
- 名词: "The hurricane originated in the tropical waters of the Atlantic Ocean."
