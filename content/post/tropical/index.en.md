## Pronunciation
/tɹəˈpɪkl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of, located in, or found near the tropics; having a hot and humid climate with high annual rainfall. For example: "The island has a tropical rainforest."
- **Noun**: A tropical region or climate. For example: "The hotel is situated on a tropical beach."
- **Adverb**: In a tropical manner or style. For example: "She wore a tropical print dress for the party."

## Phrases
- "Tropical climate" describes a warm and humid environment.
- "Tropical paradise" is a term for a lush, idyllic location.
- "Tropical foliage" refers to plants typical of warm climates.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The tropical birds sang melodiously in the early morning."
- **Noun**: "We decided to visit the tropical conservatory during our vacation."
- **Adverb**: "She decorated her house with a touch of tropical flair."
