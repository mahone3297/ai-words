## 读音
/ˈjestədeɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 昨天，指过去的某一天，通常用于谈论过去发生的事情。例如: "I met an old friend yesterday."
- **副词**: 用于描述发生在前一天的动作或状态。例如: "I finished the project yesterday."

## 短语
- "The day before today" 也是指昨天。
- "Yesterday once more" 表示对过去的怀念或重演。

## 例句
- 名词: "Yesterday, I went to the park and had a picnic."
- 副词: "I realized I left my keys at home yesterday when I was already at work."

## 同义词
- The day before
- The previous day
- Past day
- A day ago
- Yesterday evening (如果指的是工作日结束后的那一晚)
