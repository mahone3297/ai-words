## Pronunciation
/ˈjɛstədeɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A time period that refers to the day immediately preceding the current one, typically considered part of the past. For example: "Yesterday was a beautiful sunny day."
- **Adverb**: Referring to the day before today, used to describe when an event occurred. For example: "I remember yesterday's meeting clearly."

## Phrases
- "What did you do yesterday?" is a common question asking about someone's activities from the previous day.
- "It feels like yesterday" implies that something seems to have happened only recently, despite it being in the past.
- "Yesterday evening" refers to the time late in the day before today.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I still can't believe it was only yesterday that we won the game."
- **Adverb**: "She reminisced about the amazing view she had yesterday while hiking."
- "Yesterday afternoon, I went to the park with friends."

## Synonyms
- The day before
- Formerly
- Antecedently
- Prior
- Yesterday morning / afternoon / night (referring to specific parts of the day)
