## Pronunciation
/wɪɡ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The amount of money paid regularly by an employer to an employee for work done. 2. (U.S.) A fixed sum of money paid weekly or monthly as compensation for labor. 3. Wages or earnings collectively. (UK) *Alternative spelling: wage.*
- **Verb**: To pay someone their wages or salary.

## Phrases
- "Receive a wage increase" means to get a higher salary.
- "Living wage" refers to a minimum income considered adequate for a decent standard of living.
- "Wage gap" denotes the difference in average earnings between men and women.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The workers demanded a raise in their hourly wage."
- **Verb**: "He cashed his paycheck to cover his living expenses."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "The government pledged to raise the national minimum wage."
