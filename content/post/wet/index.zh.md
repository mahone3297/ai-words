## 读音
/wet/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 湿的，湿润的。指物体表面有水分或液体。例如: "The grass was wet after the rain."
- **动词**: 使潮湿，弄湿。例如: "Don't wet your clothes in the rain."

## 短语
- "Stay dry, not wet" 意思是保持干燥，不要弄湿。
- "Wet weather" 指的是下雨的天气。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The puppy's fur felt soft and wet."
- 动词: "I accidentally wet my shoes while walking through the puddle."

## 同义词
- Damp
- Moist
- Humid
- Soaked
- Saturated
