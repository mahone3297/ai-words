## Pronunciation
/wɔɪˈteɪər/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A person who serves food and drinks to customers in a restaurant, hotel, or similar establishment: *The waiter brought us a menu.*
2. (informal) A person who attends to the needs or requests of others: *The waiter was very attentive that night.*

### Verb
1. To serve food or drinks to customers at a table: *The waiter served a hot cup of coffee to the guest.*
2. To wait on or attend to someone's needs: *The waiter waited on us promptly.*

## Phrases
- "Waiter/waitress service"
- "Place an order with the waiter"
- "Request the waiter for assistance"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The waiter took our orders and disappeared into the kitchen."
- **Verb**: "I asked the waiter if he could recommend a wine to pair with our meal."
- **Informal**: "The waiter was so busy, he barely had time to catch his breath."

## Synonyms
- Server
- Attendant
- Maitre d' (in restaurants)
- Bartender (specifically in bars)
- Waitperson
- Waitstaff
