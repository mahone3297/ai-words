## Pronunciation
/səˈpɜːrp/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A meal typically eaten in the evening, after dinner but before bedtime, often lighter than a full dinner. Example: "We had a delicious supper of roasted chicken and vegetables."
- **Verb**: To have or consume a meal in the evening, usually as the main part of the day's meals. Example: "They usually have supper together as a family."

## Phrases
- "Supper time" indicates the time for having dinner.
- "Having a light supper" refers to a meal that is not very substantial.
- "Supper club" is a group of people who meet regularly for an evening meal.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After a long day at work, they looked forward to their quiet Sunday supper."
- **Verb**: "The children were eager to start their supper after playing outside all afternoon."
- "I usually have a bowl of soup before my main supper."

## Synonyms
- Dinner
- Supper meal
- Evening meal
- Light dinner
- Evening repast

## Antonyms
- Lunch
- Breakfast
- Brunch (a combination of breakfast and lunch)
