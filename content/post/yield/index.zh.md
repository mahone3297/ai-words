## 读音
/yield/  

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 放弃或交出（权利、控制等）。例如: "He finally yielded to their demands."
  - 产生或提供（产量、收入、结果等）。例如: "The farm yields a good crop of corn every year."
  - （在压力下）屈服或让步。例如: "The old building yielded to the force of the earthquake."

- **名词**:
  - 产量或生产量。例如: "The yield of this new crop is much higher than last year's."
  - 结果或效果。例如: "The experiment had an unexpected yield."
  - （投资或努力的）回报。例如: "The stock market yield has been quite high this year."

## 短语
- "yield to" 意味着让路或服从。例如: "Drivers must yield to pedestrians at crosswalks."
- "yield something to someone" 指将某物或权利交给他人。例如: "She reluctantly yielded the throne to her son."

## 例句
- 动词: "The factory is expected to yield more products next month."
- 名词: "The yield from the mine has decreased over the past few years."
