## 读音
/ˈlɑːdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 旅馆；寄宿处：A place where travelers can stay, especially for a night or a short time.
  - 小屋；木屋：A small, often rustic house or cabin.
  - 投宿；寄宿：The act of staying in such a place.

- **动词**:
  - 寄宿；投宿：To stay temporarily at an inn or other lodging.
  - 提交（申请、投诉等）：To submit (an application, complaint, etc.) officially.

- **名词（英式法律用语）**:
  - 法庭申诉：In British law, a formal complaint made to a court.

## 短语
- "Lodge a complaint" 表示提出投诉。
- "Lodge with someone" 意味着在某人家中寄宿。
- "Lodge a protest" 意为提出抗议。

## 例句
- 名词: "They decided to lodge at a cozy mountain lodge for their vacation."
- 动词: "I need to lodge my tax return by the end of the month."
- 法律用语: "The defendant has lodged an appeal against the verdict."
