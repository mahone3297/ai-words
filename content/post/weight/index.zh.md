## 读音
/weiɡt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 物体的质量或分量；重力。例如: "The weight of the bag was too much for her to carry."
- **名词**: 影响或压力的程度。例如: "The decision carries a lot of weight in the organization."
- **动词 (v)**: 给...称重；考虑（意见、因素等）的重要性。例如: "We'll weigh the pros and cons before making a decision."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 重的；有分量的。例如: "The weighty issue needed immediate attention."

## 短语
- "lose weight" 减肥
- "carry the weight" 承担重任
- "in weight" 在重量上

## 例句
- 名词: "The box is quite heavy due to its weight."
- 动词: "They weighed the gold bars to ensure their authenticity."
- 形容词: "The athlete needs to maintain a specific weight for his sport."
