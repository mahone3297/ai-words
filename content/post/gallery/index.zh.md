## 读音
/ˈɡæləri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个展示艺术品、照片或其他收藏品的地方，如画廊或走廊。例如: "The new gallery opened with an exhibition of contemporary art."
- **名词**: 电影院或剧院中的包厢或楼座。例如: "They had seats in the gallery for the performance."

## 短语
- "Art gallery" 指专门展示艺术作品的场所。
- "Photo gallery" 指展示照片或图片的集合。
- "Theater gallery" 指剧院或电影院的楼上观众席。

## 例句
- 名词: "We spent hours exploring the various galleries at the museum."
- 名词: "The gallery was filled with admirers admiring the paintings."
