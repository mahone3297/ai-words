## Word: Midday

## Pronunciation
/ˈmɪddeɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The middle of the day, typically around 12 o'clock when the sun is at its highest point in the sky. 

### Examples:
- **Noun**: "At midday, the sun was blazing overhead, making the streets deserted."
- "The workers took a break for lunch at midday."

## Phrases
- "At midday prayers": Referring to a religious observance or prayer time that occurs around noon.
- "High noon": An idiomatic expression for midday, often associated with the hottest part of the day or a dramatic confrontation.
- "Midday meal": A lunchtime meal, usually taken during the middle of the day.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The schoolchildren were let out for their midday recess."
- "It's customary to take a siesta during the hot midday hours in many Mediterranean countries."
