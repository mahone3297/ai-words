## 读音
/prɪˈfɜːrəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 更可取的，更喜欢的。表示在两个或多个选项中更倾向或更满意一个。例如: "A vegetarian diet is generally more preferable for health."
- **副词**: 相对而言更可取地。例如: "It's preferable to study in a quiet environment."

## 短语
- "preferably in advance" 意为最好提前。
- "preferable over" 表示比...更可取。

## 例句
- 形容词: "I would choose a house with a garden, which is more preferable for my children to play in."
- 副词: "It's preferable to book your tickets online to avoid long queues."
