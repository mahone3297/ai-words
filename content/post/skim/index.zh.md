## 读音
/skɪm/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 轻轻掠过表面；快速浏览。例如: "I only had time to skim the newspaper headlines."
  - 从液体表面舀取或去除浮在上面的东西。例如: "She skimmed the cream off the milk."

- **名词**:
  - 轻轻掠过的行为或结果。例如: "He gave the text a quick skim before the meeting."
  - 从液体表面去掉的浮渣或浮沫。例如: "There was a thin layer of skim on top of the soup."

## 短语
- "skim through" 快速浏览，粗略阅读。例如: "I skimmed through the report to find the key points."
- "skim money off" 不正当手段获取，非法挪用。例如: "They were accused of skimming money off the company's accounts."

## 例句
- 动词: "He skimmed the article to get the main idea."
- 名词: "After boiling, she removed the skim from the soup."
