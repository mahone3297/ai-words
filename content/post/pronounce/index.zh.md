## 读音

## 释义
- **动词**: 发音；清楚地读出（某个单词或音节）。例如: "Can you pronounce this word correctly?"
- **名词**: 发音；读音。例如: "There are several possible pronunciations of the word 'schedule'."

## 短语
- "Pronunciation guide" 提供发音指导的指南或工具。
- "Correct pronunciation" 正确的发音。

## 例句
- 动词: "The teacher helped the students with their pronunciation of French words."
- 名词: "The dictionary includes audio recordings for correct pronunciation."
