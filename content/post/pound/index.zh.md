## 读音
/paʊnd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 英镑，英国的货币单位。例如: "He paid for the book in pounds."
   2. 磅，重量单位，等于16盎司或约0.4536千克。例如: "The box weighs 50 pounds."

- **动词**: 重重地敲击或打击。例如: "The hammer pounded the nail into the wood."

## 短语
- "pound sterling" 指英镑货币。
- "pound the pavement" 意为努力找工作或长时间走路。

## 例句
- 名词: "The exchange rate between dollars and pounds is fluctuating."
- 动词: "The construction worker was pounding away at the concrete all day."

## 同义词
- Currency: GBP (Great British Pound)
- Weight unit: kilogram (for equivalent weight measurement)
