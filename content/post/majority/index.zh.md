## 读音
/ˈmæjrəti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大多数，大部分。例如: "The majority of people prefer coffee over tea."
- **形容词**: 多数的，占优势的。例如: "The majority opinion was in favor of the proposal."

## 短语
- "In the majority" 意味着在数量上占多数。
- "The ruling majority" 指执政的大多数群体或政党。

## 例句
- 名词: "A majority of voters supported the new law."
- 形容词: "The majority shareholder has significant control over the company."
