## Pronunciation
/ˈrɒtəri/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **rotary** refers to:
1. An engine or machine with a rotating mechanism, especially one that converts mechanical energy into work through the use of a crankshaft or gears.
2. A device or system using rotating elements, such as a printing press or a telephone exchange.
3. A group or club meeting in rotation, where members take turns hosting events or participating.
4. (Informal) A type of dance, particularly a ballroom dance characterized by turning movements.

### Verb
To **rotate** something is to cause it to turn around a central axis, often repeatedly.

### Adjective
- Describing something that moves in a circular or revolving motion.
- Referring to a system or group that operates on a rotating basis.

## Phrases
- "Rotate machinery" means to make a machine turn.
- "Rotary engine" designates a specific type of internal combustion engine.
- "Participate in a rotary club" implies involvement in a community service organization.
