## 读音
/prɪˈɔːr/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 优先的，较早的，在前的。例如: "The prior experience is required for this job."
- **名词**: 修道院院长（基督教天主教和东正教的僧侣领袖）；在某些组织中的前任或前一任。例如: "The prior of the monastery oversaw all the monks."

## 短语
- "prior to" 意味着在...之前。例如: "We need to finish the report prior to the meeting."
- "in priority order" 指按优先级顺序。例如: "Tasks should be completed in priority order."

## 例句
- 形容词: "He was given the job because of his prior experience in the field."
- 名词: "The new prior had a different approach to managing the abbey."

- Previous
- Former
- Earlier
- Preceding
