## Pronunciation
/sweɪtər/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A garment** designed to cover the upper part of the body, typically with long sleeves and a collar, worn for warmth or style, often made of knitted wool or similar fabric.
- **A sweater vest** is a type of sleeveless sweater worn over a shirt.

### Verb
- To put on or wear a sweater, especially as an act of dressing up.
- To use or refer to a sweater informally, as a term of endearment or affection.

## Phrases
- "Throw on a sweater" for casual dressing.
- "Wearing a cozy sweater" to describe someone's attire.
- "Sweater weather" to describe cool, comfortable temperatures suitable for wearing sweaters.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She knitted a beautiful cable-knit sweater for her son."
- **Verb**: "It's chilly outside, so I decided to throw on my favorite sweater."
- **Informal Usage**: "Call me your little sweater, it makes me feel special."

## Synonyms

- Pullover (US)
- Sweater jacket

- Knitwear
- Sweatshirt (informal, usually without a collar)

## Antonyms
