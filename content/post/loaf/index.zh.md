## 读音
/loʊf/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一块完整的面包，通常比较大且未切片。例如: "I bought a fresh loaf of sourdough bread from the bakery."
- **动词**: 不常用，但在某些方言或口语中可能表示缓慢或懒散地移动。例如: "He was loafing around the house all day."

## 短语
- "A loaf of bread" 指一整条面包。
- "Loaf about/around" 意味着无所事事或闲逛。

## 例句
- 名词: "She sliced a thick piece from the loaf for breakfast."
- 动词（非标准用法）: "He was loafing on the couch, watching TV all afternoon."

## 同义词
- Bread
- Loaf of bread
- Batch of bread
- Sourdough (如果特指某种类型的面包)
