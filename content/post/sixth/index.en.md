## Pronunciation
/sɪksθ/

## Definitions
### Noun
- The number that comes after the fifth and before the eighth in the series of whole numbers.
- In a series or ranking, the position that is the sixth from the beginning or end.
- Abbreviation: 6th

### Verb
- To occupy the sixth place in a series or sequence.
- To be the sixth in a series or order.

## Phrases
- "The sixth sense" refers to an alleged ability beyond the five traditional senses.
- "In the sixth grade" indicates the level of education typically for students aged around 11 or 12.
- "Sixth form" (UK) or "junior year" (US) refers to the final year of secondary education.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She was proud of being the sixth person to climb Mount Everest."
- **Verb**: "After five years of practice, he finally reached the sixth position in the competition."
- **Adjective**: "The sixth book in the series is my favorite."
