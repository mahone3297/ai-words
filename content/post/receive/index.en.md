## Pronunciation
/rɪˈsiːv/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: 
  - Noun: The act of receiving something, typically mail, goods, or information. For example: "She eagerly received the package."
  - Verb (transitive): To accept or get something, often with an object. For example: "The store owner received a large order."
  - Verb (intransitive): To be given or presented something. For example: "The audience received a standing ovation."

## Phrases
- "Receive a gift" means to get a present.
- "Received wisdom" refers to widely accepted knowledge or beliefs.
- "Receive an invitation" means to be invited to an event.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The volunteer center receives donations from all over the city."
- **Verb (transitive)**: "She received a promotion after months of hard work."
- **Verb (intransitive)**: "The students received their report cards at the end of the semester."
