## 读音
/riːˈsiːv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    1. 接收，接到。例如: "I received a letter from my friend yesterday."
    2. 收到（礼物、信息等）。例如: "She was happy to receive a surprise gift."
    3. 承认，接受（想法、观念）。例如: "The public received the new policy with mixed feelings."
    4. 欢迎，接待。例如: "The guests were warmly received by the host."

## 短语
- "Receive with open arms" 意为非常热情地欢迎。
- "Receive a warm welcome" 表示受到热烈的欢迎。

## 例句
- 接收： "We need to receive the package at the front desk."
- 收到： "He was surprised to receive a call from his old teacher."
- 承认： "The company has received criticism for its environmental record."
- 欢迎： "The children received their visitors with excitement."
