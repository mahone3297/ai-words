## 读音
/ˈpəʊstmæn/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 送信或包裹的人，邮差。例如: "The postman delivers mail every morning."
- **名词 (n)**: 在某些上下文中，也可以指邮局工作人员。例如: "She works as a postman at the local post office."

## 短语
- "Meet the postman" 意味着遇到负责递送邮件的人。
- "A postman's rounds" 指邮差的投递路线。

## 例句
- "The postman arrived with a stack of letters and packages."
- "In the village, everyone knows the friendly postman by name."

## 同义词
- Mailman
- Letter carrier
- Postal worker
- Delivery person
