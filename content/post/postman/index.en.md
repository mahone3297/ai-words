## Pronunciation
/postmən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person whose job is to deliver mail or postal items to people's homes or businesses. ["A postman brings the morning mail."](https://www.example.com/postman-definition)
- **Verb**: To perform the duties of a postman, delivering mail. ["The postman delivered the letters on time every day."](https://www.example.com/postman-deliveries)

## Phrases
- "Mailman's rounds" refers to the regular delivery schedule of a postman.
- "Postbox delivery" means the act of placing mail in a designated postbox.
- "Postman's knock" is a colloquial term for the sound of someone delivering mail, often敲门声.

## Example Sentences

- **Verb**: "After finishing his postman duties, he headed home for lunch."
- **Phrasal**: "The postman had already completed his deliveries by noon."
