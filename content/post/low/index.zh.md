## 读音
/loʊ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 
    - 表示低的、矮的、下降的或低于正常水平的。例如: "The low wall surrounds the garden." 或 "Her voice was low and husky."
    - 表示数量小的、价值不高的或质量差的。例如: "The stock market hit a low point yesterday." 或 "The price of oil is currently quite low."

- **副词**: 
    - 指在音调、级别或程度上低。例如: "He sang the song in a low voice."
    - 表示时间上的晚或迟。例如: "I woke up late, so I had a low start to the day."

## 短语
- "at a low point" 指处于最低谷或最困难的时期。
- "low and behold" 用于表达意外发现或惊讶的心情。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The basement has a low ceiling, so watch your head."
- 副词: "She whispered something in a low voice, so I couldn't hear her."
