## Pronunciation
/ˈsɒftli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Describing or characterized by a gentle or quiet manner. For example: "He spoke softly to avoid waking the baby."
- **Adjective**: Gentle, not harsh or loud. For example: "The music was softly played in the background."
- **Pronoun**: (informal) An endearing form of "he" or "she" used to refer to oneself or another in a gentle manner. For example: "She whispered softly to her child."

## Phrases
- "Speak softly" means to talk in a gentle voice.
- "A softly spoken voice" describes a quiet and soothing tone.
- "He kissed her softly on the cheek."

## Example Sentences

- **Adjective**: "The sunlight filtered through the curtains, casting softly colored shadows on the floor."
