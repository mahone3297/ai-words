## Pronunciation
/əˈsəʊsiət/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A person who is connected or affiliated with another, often in a professional or social context.**
- **A colleague or co-worker at a company.**
- **A partner in a business or organization.**
- **A psychological link or connection between two people or things.**

### Verb
- **To form a close relationship or association with someone.**
- **To join forces or collaborate with someone.**
- **To link or relate (ideas, concepts, or events) mentally.**
- **To become friends or friendly with someone.**

### Adjective
- **Connected or related.**
- **Having a shared interest or profession.**
- **Working together or jointly.**

## Phrases
- "Associate with" implies being in frequent contact or partnership.
- "Professional associates" refer to colleagues in a work setting.
- "Team associates" denote members of a group working towards a common goal.
- "Forming an association" means creating a bond or link.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "He has several business associates who share his vision."
- **Verb**: "She decided to associate herself with a local charity event."
- **Adjective**: "Their academic backgrounds made them strong project associates."
- **Phrasal verb**: "The two lawyers had been associates for over a decade."
