## Pronunciation
/rɪˈeɪndʒ/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A range** is a sequence or series of numbers, values, or items that follow a specific order or pattern, often between a minimum and a maximum value. For example: "The temperature ranged from 50°F to 70°F today."
2. **Geographical range** - the area where a species naturally lives or can be found. For example: "The bald eagle has a wide range across North America."
3. **Musical range** - the notes a person or instrument can play comfortably. For example: "She has a vocal range of three octaves."

### Verb
1. To **cover a range** - to include or extend over a variety or spectrum. For example: "This book covers a wide range of topics in history."
2. To **set a price range** - to establish a specific cost bracket for something. For example: "The hotel offers rooms at various price ranges to cater to different budgets."
