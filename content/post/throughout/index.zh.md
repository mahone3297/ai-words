## 读音
/θruːˈaʊt/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示从头到尾，自始至终。例如: "She worked diligently throughout the project."
- **副词**: 一直，始终。例如: "The temperature remained cold throughout the winter."

## 短语
- "Throughout the day" 意为一整天。
- "Throughout their lives" 指在他们的整个生命期间。

## 例句
- 介词: "He remained focused throughout the meeting."
- 副词: "The concert was exciting throughout, with no dull moments."

## 同义词
- All through
- From start to finish
- Throughout the duration
- Consistently
- Continuously
