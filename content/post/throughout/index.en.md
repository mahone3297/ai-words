## Pronunciation
/əˈθruːtˌaʊ/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Throughout the entire duration or extent of something; continuously or everywhere. For example: "The project will be monitored throughout its execution."
- **Preposition**: Indicating a span of time or space that something extends over. For example: "The event took place throughout the day."

## Phrases
- "Throughout history" refers to a period of time from beginning to end.
- "Throughout the country" means across the entire nation.
- "Throughout the year" signifies a complete calendar year.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "She studied diligently throughout the semester."
- **Preposition**: "I remember reading this book throughout my childhood."

## Synonyms
- Across the board
- All the way through
- Everywhere
- At all times
- Continuously
