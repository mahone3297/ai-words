## 读音
/ˈrɛflekʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 反射，映像，倒影。例如: "The reflection of the moon in the water was mesmerizing."
   - 沉思，深思，反思。例如: "After the incident, he spent some time in quiet reflection."
   - （光、声等的）反射，折射。例如: "The room had no windows, so there were no reflections from the light."
   - （思想、情感的）表现，体现。例如: "Her paintings are a reflection of her inner world."

- **动词**:
   - 把…反映出来，映现。例如: "The mirror reflected her image."
   - 思考，反思。例如: "He reflected on his actions and decided to apologize."

## 短语
- "Reflect on something" 意味着思考或对某事进行反思。
- "Reflection in the mirror" 指的是镜中的映像。

## 例句
- 名词: "The lake was calm, providing a perfect reflection of the surrounding mountains."
- 动词: "She looked at herself in the mirror, reflecting on how much she had changed over the years."
