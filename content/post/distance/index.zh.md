## 读音
/ˈdɪstəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 距离，相隔的空间或时间。例如: "The distance between the two cities is 500 miles."
- **动词**: 使疏远，拉开距离。例如: "Their different interests gradually distanced them from each other."

## 短语
- "at a distance" 意为在远处。
- "keep one's distance" 意思是保持距离，不亲近。
- "cover a distance" 表示走过或行驶一段距离。

## 例句
- 名词: "It takes about an hour to drive the full distance to the beach."
- 动词: "Their different opinions distanced them in the debate."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但可理解为表达对遥远或难以触及事物的感叹）: "Distance! How will we ever overcome it?"
