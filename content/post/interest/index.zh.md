## 读音
/ˈɪntrəst/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 兴趣；爱好：例如 "He has a great interest in photography."
   - 利益；权益：例如 "The company's financial interests are at stake."
   - 债务的利息：例如 "She pays 5% interest on her loan."

- **动词**:
   - 使感兴趣；引起关注：例如 "The lecture interested everyone in the audience."
   - 在意；关心：例如 "I really don't interest in politics."

## 短语
- "Take an interest in something" 意为对某事产生兴趣。
- "Loss of interest" 表示失去兴趣或兴趣减退。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her interest in painting started at a young age."
- 动词: "The new project immediately interested the team members."

- Fascination
- Enthusiasm
