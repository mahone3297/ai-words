## 读音
/ briːf/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 简洁的，简短的。例如: "He gave a brief summary of the report."
- **名词**: 简报，摘要。例如: "The lawyer prepared a detailed brief for the case."
- **动词**: 为...作简报，向...简介。例如: "The expert was briefed on the project before starting work."

## 短语
- "in brief" 意为简而言之，简要地。
- "to give someone a brief" 是向某人提供简要信息或指导。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Please give me a brief overview of the project."
- 名词: "The attorney studied his client's legal brief before the trial."
- 动词: "The new employee was briefly about company policies."
