## 读音
/ˈlæbəl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 标签，标记，用以表明事物的身份、性质或特征的文字或符号。例如: "There's a label on the bottle saying 'Danger'."
- **动词 (v)**: 给…贴标签，分类，标记。例如: "The products are labeled with their prices."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 被认为属于某一类别或具有特定特征的。例如: "She's a label-conscious person, only buying designer clothes."

## 短语
- "Product label" 商品标签，指附在产品上的信息标签。
- "Label someone as" 把某人归类为，把某人打上…的标签。例如: "They labeled him as an outsider."

## 例句
- 名词: "Read the label before taking any medication."
- 动词: "The company labels all its organic products."
- 形容词: "She doesn't care about being labeled as a feminist."
