## 读音
/stɔːrm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大风和剧烈降雨或降雪的天气现象，通常伴有雷电或雪暴。例如: "The storm caused widespread power outages."
- **动词**: 剧烈地或愤怒地冲进或离开。例如: "He stormed out of the room after an argument."
- **形容词**: 强烈的，猛烈的。例如: "The stormy weather made it impossible to go out."

## 短语
- "Weather the storm" 意味着度过困难时期。
- "A storm is brewing" 表示即将有麻烦或冲突发生。
- "Storm the castle" 原意为攻占城堡，常比喻坚决地争取目标。

## 例句
- 名词: "We had to stay indoors during the storm."
- 动词: "She was so angry that she stormed into her boss's office."
- 形容词: "The stormy night made the atmosphere even more eerie."

## 同义词
- Tempest
- Hurricane
- Typhoon
- Tornado
- Heavy rain/snowfall
