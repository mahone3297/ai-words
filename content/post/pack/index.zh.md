## 读音
/pæk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一包，一捆，一组：A package or collection of things wrapped or tied together.
  - 打包，行李：The act of putting things into a bag or suitcase for travel.
  - 产品套装：A set of goods or services offered together as a single deal.

- **动词**:
  - 打包，装箱：To put things into a container, especially for transport or storage.
  - 将…做成产品套装：To combine items or features into a single product or offer.

## 短语
- "pack up" 意味着收拾行李或整理东西。
- "pack a punch" 表示有力度，影响大：to have a strong effect or impact.
- "pack it in" 用于表示放弃或停止做某事：to give up or stop doing something.

## 例句
- 名词: "I bought a software pack that included several useful programs."
- 动词: "Before leaving for the trip, she had to pack all her clothes into a suitcase."
- "The new smartphone comes with a pack of accessories."
