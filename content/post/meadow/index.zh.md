## 读音
/ˈmeɪdoʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一片长满草的开阔地，通常位于田野或林边。例如: "The horses grazed in the peaceful meadow."
- **形容词**: 形容具有草地特征的。例如: "A meadow landscape with wildflowers and butterflies."

## 短语
- "Meadow grass" 指生长在草地上的草。
- "Meadowlands" 通常指的是广阔的草地地区。

## 例句
- 名词: "We had a picnic on the lush green meadow."
- 形容词: "The meadow-style garden was filled with blooming daisies."
