## 读音
/dɪp/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 将（某物）短暂地浸入液体中。例如: "She dipped the bread into the olive oil."
2. 略微下降；降低。例如: "The stock market dipped slightly today."
3. （身体）下弯，俯身。例如: "He dipped his head in respect."

- **名词**:
1. 浸泡的动作。例如: "The dip in the pool was refreshing."
2. 涂料或染料的溶液。例如: "The fabric was given a color dip to achieve the desired shade."

## 短语
- "dip into something" 意味着从某物中取出一部分。例如: "I'll just dip into my savings for this emergency."
- "dip your toe in the water" 比喻尝试新事物。例如: "It's time to dip your toe in the world of entrepreneurship."

## 例句
- 动词: "She dipped her finger into the paint and swirled it on the canvas."
- 名词: "The chocolate dip was a delicious addition to the strawberries."

## 同义词
- Immerse
- Soak
- Lower
- Budge (for dipping down)
- Smear (for applying a dip as a coating)
