## 读音
/ʌnˈkaɪnd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不友善的，刻薄的，无情的。例如: "She was unkind to her little brother, criticizing every move he made."
- **副词**: 不友善地，刻薄地。例如: "He spoke unkindly about her in public."

## 短语
- "Be unkind to someone" 意味着对某人不友好或刻薄。
- "Do an unkind deed" 做一件残忍或无情的事情。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It was unkind of you to laugh at his misfortune."
- 副词: "She answered my question unkindly, making me feel embarrassed."
