## 读音
/splæʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 溅起的水花或液体；突然的、短暂的声响或影响。例如: "The child made a big splash in the pool."
- **动词**: 溅出，使溅起；引起注意或轰动。例如: "The new product launch splashed across all major news outlets."
- **形容词**: 引人注目的，有冲击力的。例如: "The artist's splashy style drew crowds to the gallery."

## 短语
- "Make a splash" 意味着引起轰动或吸引注意。
- "Splash water on" 指把水溅到某物或某人身上。

## 例句
- 名词: "There was a big splash when the stone hit the pond."
- 动词: "Her first novel splashed onto the bestseller list."
- 形容词: "The designer's splashy dress was the talk of the evening."
