## Pronunciation
/θrəʊn/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A large chair, typically with armrests and a back, that is placed on a raised platform, used by a king, queen, or other monarch as a symbol of their power and authority.
2. The position or role of a monarch or ruler.
3. (informal) A seat of power or authority, metaphorically.

### Verb
To ascend or occupy a throne, especially as a monarch or ruler.

### Adjective
Related to a throne or the position of a monarch.

## Phrases
- "Assume the throne" means to become a monarch or take over a position of power.
- "On the throne" refers to a monarch who is currently ruling.
- "To sit on the throne" signifies assuming the role of a ruler.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The new king sat solemnly on his ornate throne during the coronation ceremony."
- **Verb**: "After the long battle, the victorious general ascended the throne."
- **Adjective**: "The regal throne was adorned with precious jewels and gold."
