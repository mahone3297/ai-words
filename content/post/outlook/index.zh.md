## 读音
/ˈaʊtklʊk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 对未来的看法或期望；展望。例如: "She has an optimistic outlook on life."
   - 微软的电子邮件和日历应用。例如: "I use Outlook for managing my emails and schedule."

- **动词**: 
   - 预期；展望。例如: "The company outlooks strong growth in the coming year."

## 短语
- "Outlook on life" 指个人对生活的看法或态度。
- "Microsoft Outlook" 特指微软的办公软件套件中的电子邮件客户端。

## 例句
- 名词: "Despite the challenges, he maintains a positive outlook on his career."
- 动词: "The market outlooks a decline in sales due to the pandemic."
