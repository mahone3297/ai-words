## 读音
/mɪdst/

## 释义
- **介词**: 在...中间；在...之中。例如: "She was standing right in the midst of the crowd."
- **名词**: 中间；中心。例如: "The midst of the storm was the most dangerous time."

## 短语
- "in the midst of" 意味在...的中间或期间。例如: "In the midst of all the chaos, she found a moment of peace."

## 例句
- 介词: "We held a meeting in the midst of all the preparations for the event."
- 名词: "The midst of winter is when we usually plan our summer vacation."

## 同义词
- among
- amid
- in the center of
- surrounded by
- in the heart of
