## 读音
/pəˈrɑːdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 粥，一种由谷物（如燕麦、大米）煮成的稠状食物，通常加水或牛奶，并可能配以糖、水果或其它调料。例如: "She likes to have porridge for breakfast."

## 短语
- "Oatmeal porridge" 指的是燕麦粥。
- "Porridge with honey" 是蜂蜜配粥的意思。

## 例句
- "I usually have a bowl of porridge in the morning to start my day."
- "The children were served warm porridge and fresh fruit for their school breakfast."

## 同义词
- Gruel
- Congee
- Oatmeal
- Porridgy dish
- Breakfast cereal (in some contexts)
