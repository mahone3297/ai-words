## Pronunciation
/pəˈrɪdʒ/

## Definitions
### Noun
Porridge is a type of thick, usually hot cereal made from grains like oats, barley, or rice, often cooked with water or milk and seasoned with sweeteners or spices. It is a traditional breakfast food in many cultures.

### Verb (informal)
To eat porridge, especially as a staple or comfort food.

## Phrases
- "Have a bowl of porridge" to consume the dish.
- "Eat some porridge for breakfast" to describe the act of eating it in the morning.
- "Porridge-like consistency" to describe a thick, creamy texture.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She likes to start her day with a steaming bowl of porridge."
- **Verb**: "After a long night, he decided to have a hearty bowl of porridge to warm him up."
- **Informal**: "He's been known to devour a big bowl of porridge on cold winter mornings."

## Synonyms

- Gruel

- Bowl of mush
- Bowl of oats

Porridge can also be a metaphor for bland or uninteresting content, as in "His lecture was as dull as porridge."
