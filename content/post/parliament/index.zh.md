## 读音
/ˈpɑːlɪmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 国会，立法机关。通常指一个国家或地区的最高立法机构，如英国的议会或联合国大会。例如: "The UK's Parliament is located in London."
- **名词**: 议会制度，指实行议会民主制的政体。例如: "They have a parliamentary system of government."

## 短语
- "Parliamentary democracy" 指实行议会民主的国家。
- "House of Commons" 和 "House of Lords" 是英国议会的两个主要部分。
- "Session of Parliament" 指议会的开会期间。

## 例句
- "The newly elected members were sworn in during the opening session of Parliament."
- "In Canada, the Prime Minister is the head of the government, while the Governor General represents the Queen in the federal Parliament."
