## Pronunciation
/pəˈlaɪt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or showing respect for social convention, manners, or decorum; courteous. For example: "The waiter was very polite in taking our order."
- **Verb**: To behave in a courteous and respectful manner towards others. For example: "She politely declined the invitation."
- **Noun**: Politeness or courtesy, often in a specific act or gesture. For example: "His politeness was appreciated."

## Phrases
- "Be polite to others" encourages respectful behavior.
- "Please have a polite conversation" suggests maintaining good manners during a discussion.
- "A polite gesture" refers to an action that shows respect or consideration.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The children were expected to be polite guests at the dinner party."
- **Verb**: "He politely excused himself from the meeting when his phone rang."
- **Noun**: "Her politeness was a hallmark of her personality."
