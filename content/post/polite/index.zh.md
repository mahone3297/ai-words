## 读音
/ˈpɑːlaɪt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有礼貌的，客气的，文雅的。例如: "He was very polite in his response."
- **副词**: 礼貌地，客气地。例如: "She asked politely for the time."

## 短语
- "be polite to sb." 意味着对某人表现得有礼貌。
- "polite society" 指的是讲究礼仪和文明行为的社交圈子。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It's important to be polite when interacting with others."
- 副词: "He opened the door for her politely."

## 同义词
- Well-mannered
- Courteous
- Respectful
- Civilized
- Considerate
