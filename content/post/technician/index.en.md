## Pronunciation
/ˈtɛkniʃən/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **technician** is a skilled worker who specializes in a particular area of technology, often dealing with the repair, maintenance, or operation of equipment, machinery, or systems. They may work in various industries such as healthcare, IT, automotive, or construction.

### Verb
To **technician** something (informal) can mean to perform tasks or duties that require technical expertise, often involving fixing or maintaining devices.

## Phrases
- "Technician's report" refers to a detailed analysis or assessment by a technician.
- "Train a new technician" means to teach someone the skills needed for a technical job.
- "On-site technician" is a professional who provides services at a client's location.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The IT technician fixed the server issue promptly."
- **Verb**: "The technician diagnosed the problem with the car's engine."
- **Informal**: "He's been techincianing his skills since he started working in the lab."
