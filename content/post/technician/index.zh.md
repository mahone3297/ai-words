## 读音
/ˈteknɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 专门的技术人员或工匠，具有特定技术知识和技能的人。例如: "The technician fixed my computer in no time."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与技术相关或涉及技术的。例如: "He has a technician background in electronics."

## 短语
- "Technical support technician" 指提供技术支持的专业人员。
- "Lab technician" 是在实验室里进行实验和技术工作的人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The car dealership sent a skilled technician to diagnose the issue."
- 形容词: "She pursued a technician job in the medical field after completing her studies."
