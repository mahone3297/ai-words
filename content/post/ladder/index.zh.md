## 读音
/lædər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种有横档和立柱构成的长梯，用于攀爬。例如: "He used a ladder to reach the top of the wall."
- **名词**: 比喻指晋升的途径或等级序列。例如: "She climbed the corporate ladder to become the CEO."
- **动词**: 指在股市中采用阶梯式买卖策略。例如: "Traders often ladder their stock purchases to minimize risk."

## 短语
- "A step ladder" 是一种可以折叠的便携式梯子。
- "Ladder up" 指在等级或地位上提升。
- "Ladder down" 指在等级或地位上下降。

## 例句
- 名词: "The fireman carried a tall ladder to rescue the trapped cat."
- 名词: "In the company, there are several rungs on the ladder to reach the management level."
- 动词: "Investors ladder their options to spread out their investment over time."
