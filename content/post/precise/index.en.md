## Pronunciation
/prɪˈsaɪs/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Accurate, exact, or free from error; very careful and detailed. For example: "The instructions were precise in their steps."
- **Adverb**: In a precise manner; exactly. For example: "She speaks French with great precision."

## Phrases
- "A precise measurement" indicates an exact value.
- "Precision engineering" focuses on high accuracy in manufacturing.
- "With precision" means in a highly accurate and deliberate way.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The scientist made precise observations to ensure reliable results."
- **Adverb**: "He executed the task with precision, avoiding any mistakes."
- "She appreciated his precise memory, always recalling details accurately."
