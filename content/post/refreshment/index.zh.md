## 读音
/ˈrɛfɹɛʃmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 提神的东西；饮料，小吃；休息，恢复精力。例如: "They served refreshments during the intermission."，"A cool drink was just the refreshment he needed after a long walk."
- **动词**: 使恢复精力；更新，刷新。例如: "The swim refreshed her on a hot day."，"She refreshed herself with a glass of water."

## 短语
- "Refreshment break" 意为休息时间，通常提供食物和饮料。
- "Refreshment stand" 是售卖饮料和小吃的小摊或摊位。
- "To refresh one's memory" 意为复习以恢复记忆。

## 例句
- 名词: "The guests enjoyed various refreshments at the garden party."
- 动词: "After a long workday, she decided to take a quick shower to refresh before dinner."
