## 读音
/nɪər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 距离不远的，接近的。例如: "The park is near our house."
- **副词**: 接近地，几乎。例如: "She came near to crying when she heard the news."

## 短语
- "be near to" 表示接近（某人或某事物）。例如: "The deadline is near to, so we need to hurry up."
- "in the near future" 意指在不久的将来。例如: "We plan to travel in the near future."

## 例句
- 形容词: "There's a convenience store near the corner."
- 副词: "He lives so near that he can walk to work."

- nearby
