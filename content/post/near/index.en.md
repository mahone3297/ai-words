## Pronunciation
/ˈnɪr/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Located close to; not far away. For example: "The house is near the river."
- **Preposition**: In close proximity to; nearby. For example: "She lives near the city center."
- **Adverb**: Close by; nearly. For example: "We arrived near midnight."

## Phrases
- "Near and dear": Something or someone very close to one's heart or affections.
- "As near as makes no difference": Almost identical or virtually the same.
- "Close but no cigar": Nearly achieving a goal but falling short; almost correct or successful.
- "Near miss": An accident or event that comes very close to happening but does not occur.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The park is near our school, so we often go there during breaks."
- **Preposition**: "There's a good restaurant near your new apartment."
- **Adverb**: "I was near exhausted after finishing the marathon."
