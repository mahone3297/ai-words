## Pronunciation
/kənˈsʌmpʃən/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Consumption**: 1. The act of using or consuming goods or services, especially food and drink: *Her healthy lifestyle is a testament to her low sugar consumption.*
   - Synonyms: intake, usage, expenditure, utilization, ingestion
2. The act of taking in or absorbing information: *He spent hours in the library, voraciously consuming knowledge.*

### Verb
- **To consume**: 1. To eat or drink: *She consumes a balanced diet every day.*
   - Synonyms: ingest, devour, partake, relish, savor
2. To use up or exhaust (resources): *The company's profits have been rapidly consumed by expansion.*

### Adjective
- **Consumer**: Referring to a person who buys and uses goods or services: *The consumer market is highly competitive.*
   - Synonym: user

## Phrases
- "High consumption of alcohol" refers to excessive drinking.
- "Energy consumption" is the amount of energy used by a device or system.
- "Sustainable consumption" promotes environmentally friendly practices.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her low-fat consumption helped her lose weight."
- **Verb**: "He quickly consumed the entire book in a single sitting."
- **Adjective**: "The company is launching a campaign to encourage responsible consumer behavior."
