## 读音
/əbˈzɜːrvər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 观察者；监视者；见证人。例如: "The observer noted every detail of the experiment."
- **动词**: 观察；注视；遵守。例如: "Scientists observer the behavior of animals in their natural habitat."

## 短语
- "observer effect" 指的是观察者对被观察对象的影响。
- "aircraft observer" 在军事或航空领域，指的是飞机上的观测员。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police had an observer on the rooftop watching the protest."
- 动词: "She observed the children playing in the park from a distance."
