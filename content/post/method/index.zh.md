## 读音
/ˈmɛθəd/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种系统性的步骤或程序，用于完成特定任务或解决问题。例如: "She used a scientific method to conduct her research."
- **动词 (v)**: 采用某种方法或方式去做。例如: "He methodically went through each step of the process."

## 短语
- "by method of" 通过...的方法。例如: "The problem was solved by method of elimination."
- "teaching methods" 教学方法。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new management method has improved our productivity."
- 动词: "She methoded her way through the complex task with ease."
