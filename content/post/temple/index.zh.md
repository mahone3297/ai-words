## 读音
/ˈtemp(ə)l/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 宗教的庙宇，通常供奉神明或先知。例如: "The ancient Greeks built temples to honor their gods."
   2. 佛教或印度教中的修道院或圣地。例如: "Buddhist monks live and meditate in temples."
   3. 某事物的中心或圣地，常用于比喻。例如: "The art museum is a temple of creativity."

## 短语
- "temple of learning" 指学术或知识的殿堂，如大学或图书馆。
- "temple of music" 用来形容音乐的重要场所，如音乐厅或歌剧院。

## 例句
- 名词: "The towering temple stood majestically on the hilltop, attracting tourists from all over the world."
- 形容词: "The intricate carvings on the temple walls depicted stories from ancient mythology."
