## Definitions
### Adjective
- **slender** (noun): Referring to something that is thin, narrow, or having a small size or proportion. For example: "The dancer had a slender figure."
- **slender** (adjective): Characterizing a person or thing with a delicate or graceful appearance. For example: "A slender branch reached over the fence."
