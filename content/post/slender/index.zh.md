## 读音
/ˈslɛndər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 苗条的，瘦长的。描述物体或人的身材细长而不粗壮。例如: "She has a slender figure."
- **副词**: 稍微，略微。用于表示程度不强的动作或状态。例如: "The branch moved slenderly in the wind."

## 短语
- "slender build" 指某人瘦削的体型。
- "slender chance" 表示可能性很小。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The model's slender waist caught everyone's attention on the runway."
- 副词: "He answered the question slenderly, avoiding giving too much detail."
