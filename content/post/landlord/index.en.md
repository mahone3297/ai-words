## Word: Landlord

## Pronunciation
/ˈlændlɔːd/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who owns a house, apartment, or other property which they let to tenants for rent. _Example:_ "The landlord promised to fix the leaky faucet this week."
  - _Phrasal Verb_: (landlord over) To exercise authority or control over someone like a landlord does over their property. _Example:_ "She felt he was trying to landlord over her every decision."

### Phrases
- "Landlord-tenant relationship": The legal and practical relationship between the owner of a property and the person who occupies it under a lease.
- "Meet the landlord": To have a meeting with the property owner, often when viewing a rental property or discussing tenancy terms.
- "Pay the landlord": To give payment (rent) to the property owner.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The new landlord has agreed to reduce the rent for the first three months."
