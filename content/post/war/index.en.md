## Pronunciation
/wɔː(r)/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A state of armed conflict between nations or other political groups. 2. A large-scale fight or battle, often resulting in significant loss of life. 3. A period of intense hostility or strife. 4. (informal) A serious disagreement or conflict.
- **Verb**: To initiate or engage in warfare; wage war. 2. To cause or contribute to a state of war.
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or involving war; martial.

## Phrases
- "Declare war on" means to formally announce hostile intentions towards another country or entity.
- "At war with" describes a state of open conflict.
- "War-torn" describes a place severely affected by war.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The two countries were at war for decades, causing immense suffering."
- **Verb**: "The alliance decided to go to war against the oppressors."
- **Adjective**: "The war-ravaged village had little left standing."
