## 读音
/out/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示从内部到外部，离开。例如: "He walked out of the room."
- **副词**: 表示在外部，外面。例如: "It's raining outside, so stay in."
- **动词**: 结束或退出。例如: "The meeting is about to be called out."
- **形容词**: 不在，出局。例如: "The player was out when he missed the catch."

## 短语
- "to be out of something" 意味着没有或用完。例如: "We're out of milk."
- "to go out" 可以指外出或熄灭。例如: "Let's go out for dinner tonight." 或 "The lights went out suddenly."

## 例句
- 介词: "She left the house and walked out into the garden."
- 副词: "There's a bench out by the entrance."
- 动词: "The match ended with one team out."
- 形容词: "The store is closed; they're out for the day."
