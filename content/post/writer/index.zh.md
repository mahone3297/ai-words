## 读音
/wraɪtər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 写作的人；作者。例如: "She is an accomplished writer with several published novels."
   2. 打字机或打印机的一部分，用于在纸上打印字符。例如: "The old typewriter had a worn-out writer that needed replacement."

- **动词 (v)**: 写；创作。例如: "He spends most of his day writing poetry."

## 短语
- "a freelance writer" 自由撰稿人
- "the writer of the article" 文章的作者

## 例句
- 名词: "The famous writer will be giving a lecture on her new book."
- 动词: "She's currently writing a script for a TV series."

## 同义词
- Author
- Composer
- Scribe
- Penman
- Novelist (特指小说家)
