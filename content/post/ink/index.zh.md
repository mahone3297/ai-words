## 读音
/ɪŋk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 墨水，液体颜料，常用于书写、绘画或印刷。例如: "I need more ink to refill my pen."
   2. 文字，墨迹，笔迹。例如: "The letter was written in blue ink."

## 短语
- "ink well" 指墨水瓶，用于装墨水的容器。
- "ink stain" 指墨水污渍。
- "ink cartridge" 墨盒，打印机中装墨水的部件。

## 例句
- 名词: "I accidentally spilled the ink all over my shirt."
- "She signed the contract with a black ink pen."

## 同义词
- dye
- pigment
- liquid color
- writing fluid
- printer's ink
