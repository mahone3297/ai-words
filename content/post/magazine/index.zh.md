## 读音
/mæɡəˈziːn/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种定期出版的印刷品，通常包含文章、图片、故事和广告。例如: "I always read the fashion section in the magazine."
- **名词**: 一种弹匣，用于储存枪械的子弹。例如: "He loaded the magazine with fresh rounds."

## 短语
- "订阅杂志" means to pay for and receive regular issues of a magazine.
- "杂志封面" refers to the front page of a magazine.

## 例句
- 名词: "She subscribes to several health and wellness magazines."
- 名词: "The soldier checked the magazine of his rifle before heading out on patrol."
