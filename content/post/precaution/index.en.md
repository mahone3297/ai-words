## Pronunciation
/prənˈkɔːʃ(ə)n/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A measure taken to avoid harm or danger, typically before it occurs. For example: "The doctor advised taking precautions against infection."
- **Verb**: To take steps to prevent something from happening or to be prepared for a possible risk. For example: "We must take all necessary precautions when handling chemicals."

## Phrases
- "Precautionary measures" are actions taken to prevent potential problems.
- "Under no circumstances should one overlook precautions."
- "A safety precaution" is a protective measure implemented for safety reasons.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The company implemented strict fire precautions after the recent incident."
- **Verb**: "Before traveling to a new country, it's important to research and take necessary precautions."
- **Adjective**: "The new employee was given thorough training in workplace safety precautions."

## Synonyms
- Safeguards
- Caution
- Precautiousness
- Protective measures
- Antidote (informal)

## Antonyms
- Carelessness
- Neglect
- Risk-taking
- Lack of foresight
- Inattention
