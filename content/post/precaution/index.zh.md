## 读音
/prɪˈkɑːʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 预防措施；谨慎行事。例如: "Taking precautions is essential to avoid accidents."
- **动词**: 采取预防措施。例如: "We should precaution ourselves against potential risks."

## 短语
- "Precautionary measures" 意为预防性的措施。
- "Better safe than sorry" 是一句谚语，意味着采取预防措施总比事后后悔要好。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company implemented strict safety precautions to protect its employees."
- 动词: "Before going on a hike, it's wise to precaution yourself by checking the weather forecast.
