## 读音
/ˈrɑːk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 岩石，大石头。例如: "The mountain is composed of solid rock."
   - (音乐)摇滚乐。例如: "She's a big fan of classic rock music."

- **动词**:
   - 使摇晃；震动。例如: "The baby fell asleep while being rocked in the cradle."
   - 在音乐中指演奏摇滚乐。例如: "They're rocking the stage with their energetic performance."

## 短语
- "Rock the boat" 意为制造麻烦，打破现状。例如: "Don't rock the boat; we've got a good thing going here."
- "Rock and roll" 除了指摇滚乐，也可表示一种舞蹈动作。例如: "Let's dance to some rock and roll!"

## 例句
- 名词: "The hiker carefully picked his way over the rocky terrain."
- 动词: "The band was rocking the concert venue with their powerful sound."

## 同义词
- Stone
- Boulder
- Pebble (for smaller rocks)
- Jam (in the context of "rock the boat")
- Swing (for the action of rocking, e.g., a cradle or a baby)
