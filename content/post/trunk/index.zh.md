## 读音
/trʌŋk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大象的长鼻子；汽车后备箱；行李箱；树干。例如: "The elephant used its trunk to pick up the banana."，"I put my suitcase in the trunk of the car."，"The tree's strong trunk supported the weight of the branches."
- **动词**: 剪除树木的枝条，使其成为一根直立的树干。例如: "The gardener decided to trunk the tree to encourage new growth."

## 短语
- "car trunk" 汽车后备箱
- "suitcase trunk" 行李箱
- "tree trunk" 树干

## 例句
- 名词: "I stored my camping gear in the trunk before we hit the road."
- 动词: "The arborist trunks trees to maintain their health and shape."
