## Pronunciation
/pəʊm/

## Definitions
### Noun
A poem is a form of literature that uses aesthetic and rhythmic qualities of language to evoke meanings in addition to, or in place of, the prosaic ostensible meaning. It may use various verse structures and techniques to create a sense of beauty, music, or emotional depth.

### Verb
To compose or write a poem, especially as a creative expression or artistic endeavor.

### Adjective
Poetic, referring to something having qualities or characteristics of poetry, such as imagery, metaphor, or rhythm.
