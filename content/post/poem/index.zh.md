## 读音
/poɪm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种文学形式，通常由有节奏和韵律的行组成，表达情感、想法或描绘画面。例如: "She wrote a beautiful poem about nature."
- **名词**: 诗歌作品。例如: "The poet recited his latest poem at the literary event."

## 短语
- "A love poem" 指的是表达爱情主题的诗。
- "Free verse poem" 指没有固定韵律或格律的诗。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children learned to recite a classic Chinese poem in class."
- 名词: "The book contains a collection of her published poems."
