## 读音
/mɪˈrækl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 不可思议的事件或现象，奇迹。例如: "The survival of the accident victim was considered a miracle."
- **形容词**: 极其罕见的，非凡的。例如: "She has a miracle talent for painting."

## 短语
- "Miracle worker" 指能解决难题或创造奇迹的人。
- "A miracle cure" 指效果显著的治疗方法。

## 例句
- 名词: "Many people believe in miracles performed by saints."
- 形容词: "It's a miracle that the old building still stands after so many years."
