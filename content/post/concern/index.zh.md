## 读音
/kənˈsɜːrn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 关心的事，关注点；关系，牵涉。例如: "Environmental issues are a major concern for many people."
- **动词**: 涉及，关系到。例如: "The project concerns several departments in the company."
- **形容词**: 担忧的，关切的。例如: "She looked concerned when I told her the news."

## 短语
- "of concern to sb." 意味着对某人来说是重要的或相关的。
- "be concerned about sth." 意味着担心或关心某事。
- "concerning" 作为介词，用于引出相关的话题或信息。例如: "There have been several concerning reports about the safety of the product."

## 例句
- 名词: "The main concern for parents is their children's well-being."
- 动词: "His decision directly concerns the future of the company."
- 形容词: "She had a concerned expression on her face after hearing the news."
