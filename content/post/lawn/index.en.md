## Word: Lawn

## Pronunciation
/lɔːn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: An area of land covered with closely mown grass, typically found around a house, park, or other outdoor space. It's often used for recreation or aesthetic purposes. 

Example Sentence: _The homeowner spent hours maintaining his pristine green lawn._

## Phrases
- "Lawn mower": A machine used to cut grass on a lawn.
- "Lawn care": The act of maintaining and tending to a lawn, which includes mowing, watering, fertilizing, and weed control.
- "Roll out the red carpet / roll out the lawn": To welcome someone in a particularly grand or special way.
- "On the front lawn": Refers to the lawn located in front of a house or building.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children played soccer on the back lawn."
- "A striped pattern on the lawn indicates it has been mowed in different directions."
- "They hosted a garden party on their well-manicured lawn."

## Synonyms
- Grass plot
- Turf
- Green
- Garden (informal, especially when referring to a small, maintained area of grass)
- Yard (chiefly American English) 
- Grounds (when referring to larger areas surrounding a property)
