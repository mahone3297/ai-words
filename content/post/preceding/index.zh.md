## 读音
/prɪˈsiːdɪŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 在前的，先于的，早于的。例如: "The preceding paragraph contains important information."
- **名词**: 先前的事物或时期。例如: "The preceding year was the hottest on record."

## 短语
- "preceding year" 指的是上一年。
- "preceding chapter" 指的是前一章。

## 例句
- 形容词: "In the preceding sentence, he mentioned his plans."
- 名词: "The report summarizes events from the preceding month."

## 同义词
- Previous
- Anterior
- Former
- Earlier
- Precedent
