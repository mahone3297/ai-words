## Pronunciation
/paɪˈplain/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A series of connected pipes or tubes, typically carrying fluids or gases from one place to another, often in a pipeline system. 2. (figurative) A sequence or progression of tasks, steps, or events leading to a specific outcome, especially in a business or technological context. 3. An ongoing project or development process.

## Phrases
- "Pipeline management" refers to the systematic handling of tasks in a workflow.
- "Project pipeline" denotes the list of upcoming projects or tasks in a company's plans.
- "Gas pipeline" or "oil pipeline" are specific examples of physical pipelines.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The oil pipeline from the well to the refinery is over 500 miles long."
- **Verb**: "The company is working on diversifying its product pipeline to include new software features."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "The project is currently in the design phase, with implementation scheduled for next quarter."
