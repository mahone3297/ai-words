## 读音
/paɪˈplaɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 一种管道，用于输送液体、气体或浆状物质的长金属管或塑料管。例如: "The oil is transported through a pipeline from the well to the refinery."
   2. (计算机科学) 数据流或信息传递的连续过程。例如: "In software development, the code goes through a pipeline of compilation, testing, and deployment stages."

## 短语
- "Pipeline system" 指整体的管道网络系统。
- "Pipeline project" 可能指的是建设或改进管道系统的工程。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new gas pipeline will connect our city with the main supply network."
- 名词（计算机科学）: "The image processing software uses an optimized pipeline to enhance photos quickly."
