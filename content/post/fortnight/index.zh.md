## 读音
/ˈfɔːrtnaɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 十四天；两星期。例如: "We have two weeks left, or in other words, a fortnight."

## 短语
- "fortnightly" 意为每两周一次的，如 "fortnightly magazine"（半月刊）。

## 例句
- "I'll be on holiday for a fortnight starting next Monday."（从下周一开始我将休假两周。）

## 同义词
- two weeks
- fourteen days
- biweekly
- quaterweek
