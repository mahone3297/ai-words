## 读音
/təˈwaːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 高耸的建筑物，尤指有多个楼层的塔状结构。例如: "The Eiffel Tower is a famous landmark in Paris."
- **动词**: 高高耸立；远远超过。例如: "The skyscraper towers above the surrounding buildings."

## 短语
- "Tower block" 指高层住宅楼。
- "Tower crane" 是一种大型建筑工地用的起重机。
- "To be in seventh heaven (from the tower)" 意味着极其快乐或满足。

## 例句
- 名词: "The ancient tower still stands tall in the heart of the city."
- 动词: "The church tower dominated the skyline, towering over the town."
