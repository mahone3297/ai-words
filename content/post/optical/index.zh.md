## 读音
/ˈɑːp.tɪ.kəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 与光有关的；光学的。例如: "An optical fiber is used for transmitting light signals."
- **名词**: 光学仪器或配件，如眼镜、望远镜。例如: "She needs to get a new pair of optical glasses."

## 短语
- "Optical illusion" 指视觉错觉。
- "Optical microscope" 是光学显微镜。
- "Optical properties" 指物质对光的吸收、反射和折射等特性。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The optical telescope allows us to observe distant galaxies."
- 名词: "He went to the optician for an eye exam and got new optical glasses."

## 同义词
- Light-related
- Photonic
- Visual
