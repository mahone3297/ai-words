## 读音
/lep/

## 释义
- **动词**: 突然用力跳起；跃过。例如: "The athlete leaped over the high bar in a single bound."
- **名词**: 跳跃的动作或过程。例如: "He made a huge leap from the cliff into the water."
- **名词**: 突飞猛进，飞跃。例如: "The company experienced a technological leap forward."

## 短语
- "take a leap of faith" 意为冒险尝试，充满信心地行动。
- "leap forward" 指在发展或进步上取得显著进展。
- "leap year" 是指闰年，每隔四年出现一次，二月有29天。

## 例句
- 动词: "The gazelle leaped across the river with ease."
- 名词: "Her heart was pounding after that final leap in the gymnastics competition."
- 名词: "The invention marked a significant leap in communication technology."
