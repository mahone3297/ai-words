## Pronunciation
/slɪpər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A type of footwear typically worn in the house or while relaxing, often made of soft material with a loose fit. Example: "She wore her favorite slippers to bed."
- **Verb**: To slide or move easily, often unintentionally, on a surface. Example: "He slipped on the wet floor and fell."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of being smooth, unobtrusive, or informal. Example: "A quiet, slipper living room."

## Phrases
- "Slide into your slippers" means to put on comfortable slippers after waking up or coming home.
- "Slippery when wet" is a warning sign indicating a potentially hazardous surface.
- "Slipper socks" are designed to be worn inside shoes for added comfort.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After a long day, she sank into her soft slippers by the fireplace."
- **Verb**: "He slipped the letter under her door without being seen."
