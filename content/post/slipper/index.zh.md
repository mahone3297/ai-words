## 读音
/ˈslɪpər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 轻便的室内鞋或拖鞋，通常无带。例如: "She wore her slippers while relaxing at home."
- **动词**: 滑倒，失足。例如: "He slipped on the icy sidewalk."

## 短语
- "Slipper socks" 是一种有防滑底部的袜子，类似于室内鞋。
- "Put on your slippers" 意味着穿上拖鞋或室内鞋。

## 例句
- 名词: "He took off his shoes and put on his cozy slippers after a long day at work."
- 动词: "She almost slipped on the wet floor but managed to catch herself."

## 同义词
- House shoe
- Mule
- Sandal (在某些情况下)
- Flip-flop (在某些非正式场合)
