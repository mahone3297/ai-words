## Pronunciation
/ziːl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: **Zeal** (noun) refers to intense enthusiasm, passion, or devotion towards a cause, belief, or activity. It can also describe a fervent loyalty or dedication.
- **Adjective**: **Zealous** (adjective) is used to describe someone who is extremely enthusiastic, zealous, or fervently committed to a particular cause or belief.

## Phrases
- "Displaying zeal for a project" means showing great enthusiasm and effort.
- "A zealous advocate" is someone who is strongly supportive and passionate about a cause.
- "With unbridled zeal" expresses complete and unchecked enthusiasm.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She brought a zealous energy to her work, inspiring others to follow her lead."
- **Adjective**: "The zealous teacher motivated her students with their unwavering commitment to learning."
- **Adverb**: "He pursued his hobbies with unyielding zeal, spending countless hours perfecting his craft."
