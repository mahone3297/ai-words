## 读音
/ˈpærəde/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种公开的游行或展示，通常包括队伍、表演者和装饰华丽的车辆。例如: "The city held a grand parade to celebrate the festival."
- **动词**: 组织或参加游行。例如: "They paraded through the streets, waving flags and chanting slogans."

## 短语
- "Military parade" 指的是军队的阅兵式。
- "Fashion parade" 指时装秀，展示新设计的服装。
- "Parade ground" 是阅兵场或训练场地。

## 例句
- 名词: "The annual Thanksgiving Day parade is a beloved tradition in New York City."
- 动词: "The local schoolchildren paraded around the town square, showcasing their costumes."
