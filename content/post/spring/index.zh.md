## 读音
/sprɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 春天，一年中的季节。例如: "Spring is the season of blooming flowers and new beginnings."
   - 弹簧，用于储存和释放能量的装置。例如: "The toy has a coiled spring inside that makes it jump."

- **动词**:
   - 跳跃，突然跳起。例如: "The cat sprang up onto the table."
   - 发生，出现。例如: "News of the event sprung up suddenly."

- **形容词**:
   - 春季的，与春天相关的。例如: "Spring flowers decorate the garden."
   - 活泼的，充满活力的。例如: "She had a spring in her step."

## 短语
- "Spring forward" 在日光节约时间中，意味着将时钟往前调一小时。
- "Spring cleaning" 指春季进行的大扫除。
- "To spring a surprise" 意为给某人一个惊喜。

## 例句
- 名词: "In spring, the countryside is filled with the sounds of birds chirping."
- 动词: "The rabbit sprang away from the predator."
- 形容词: "The colors of the dress were vibrant and spring-like."

## 同义词
- Season of renewal
- Awakening
- Bounce
- Leap
- Energetic
