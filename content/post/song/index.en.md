## Pronunciation
/sɒŋ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A song is a musical composition with lyrics, typically set to music and intended for singing. It can be a piece of art that expresses emotions, tells a story, or serves as entertainment.

### Verb
To sing a song means to perform it vocally, often with melody and rhythm. To compose a song is to create the musical and lyrical content.

### Noun (countable & uncountable)
- Countable: A specific instance of a song, e.g., "She has a beautiful collection of folk songs."
- Uncountable: The act or quality of being a song, e.g., "Music is the soul of a song."

## Phrases
- "Sing a love song" to express affection through music.
- "Lullaby" a soothing song usually sung to help someone fall asleep.
- "Popular song" a widely recognized and enjoyed piece of music.
- "Ballad" a narrative song with simple, melodic tunes.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The concert featured a diverse range of songs from different decades."
- **Verb**: "He sang an old favorite song at the karaoke bar."
- **Noun (uncountable)**: "Listening to music is a great way to unwind after a long day."
