## Pronunciation
/səˈraɪzɪŋ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by causing unexpected or remarkable surprise; astonishing. For example: "The ending of the movie was surprisingly thrilling."
- **Verb**: To cause surprise or astonishment in (someone). For example: "Her performance surprised everyone at the concert."
- **Adverb**: In a surprising manner; unexpectedly. For example: "She finished the marathon surprisingly fast."

## Phrases
- "A surprising discovery" refers to something unforeseen and remarkable.
- "Surprisingly enough" is used to emphasize an unexpected fact.
- "Not surprisingly" implies that something was expected after all.

## Example Sentences
