## 读音
/ˈrɛmɑːk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 评论，意见。例如: "He made an interesting remark about the movie."
  - 注意，观察。例如: "The teacher made a remark on his poor handwriting."

- **动词**:
  - 说，评论。例如: "She remarked that the food was delicious."
  - 注意到，察觉。例如: "He remarked the unusual behavior of his colleague."

## 短语
- "remark on something" 意思是对某事发表意见或评论。
- "make a passing remark" 指随意或不经意的评论。

## 例句
- 名词: "The speaker's opening remarks set the tone for the entire lecture."
- 动词: "She remarked how much he had changed since they last met."
