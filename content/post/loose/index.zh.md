## 读音
/luːs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不紧的，松散的，不牢固的。例如: "The knot is loose, it needs tightening."
- **动词**: 使变得宽松，解开。例如: "Loosen the belt after a big meal."
- **名词**: 松弛的状态或事物。例如: "There's a loose thread on this sweater."

## 短语
- "Loose ends" 指未完成或未解决的事情。
- "Loose change" 指零钱。
- "Loose lips sink ships" 是一句谚语，意思是说话不小心会泄露机密。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The collar of his shirt was loose and untucked."
- 动词: "Please loosen the screws so I can take apart the furniture."
- 名词: "There were some loose stones on the path, so we had to be careful."
