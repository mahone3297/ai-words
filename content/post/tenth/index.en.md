## Definitions
### Noun
A noun referring to the number that comes after nine and before twenty in the series of whole numbers. It is often used as an ordinal number to indicate position. For example: "The tenth item on the list was a pencil."

### Adjective
An adjective used to describe something being the eleventh in a series or ranking. For example: "This is our tenth wedding anniversary."

### Adverb
As an adverb, "tenth" can be used to indicate the order or position in a sequence. For example: "She finished the race in tenth place."
