## Word: Kindness

## Pronunciation
/kɪndnəs/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The quality of being friendly, generous, and considerate towards others. It involves showing compassion, empathy, and a willingness to help others without expecting anything in return. 

Example sentences:
- _Her unwavering kindness helped many people through their tough times._
- _The small act of kindness from a stranger made my day._

Phrases:
- _An act of kindness_ - A single instance where someone shows generosity or consideration towards another person.
- _Kindness to animals_ - Treating animals with care and respect.
- _Pay it forward_ - Repaying a kindness received by doing something kind for someone else.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: _The old man's kindness knew no bounds; he would often give his food to those who were hungry._
- _Through her consistent kindness, she taught us the true meaning of generosity._
