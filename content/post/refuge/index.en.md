## Pronunciation
/rɪˈfjuːdʒ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
    - A place of safety or shelter from danger, threat, or persecution; a haven. Example: "The refugees sought refuge in a nearby village."
    - A person who takes shelter or finds protection, especially from persecution or trouble. Example: "He found refuge with his friends during the storm."

- **Verb**: To seek or provide sanctuary, protection, or relief. Example: "They had to refuge themselves in a nearby cave during the attack."

- **Adjective**: Offering or providing protection or safety. Example: "A refugee camp is a refuge for those in need."

## Phrases
- "Find refuge in" implies seeking solace or shelter in something or somewhere.
- "Take refuge" means to go to a place for safety.
- "Offer refuge" is when someone gives shelter or protection.
