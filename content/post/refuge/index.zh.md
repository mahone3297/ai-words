## 读音
/ˈrɛfjʊdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 提供安全庇护的地方；避难所。例如: "The refugees found refuge in a nearby church."
    - 某人逃避困境或危险时的庇护。例如: "He sought refuge from his troubles in art."

## 短语
- "take refuge in" 意为在...中寻求庇护。例如: "They took refuge in the embassy during the civil unrest."
- "refuge seeker" 指寻求庇护的人，常指难民。例如: "The country has welcomed many refuge seekers fleeing war-torn regions."

## 例句
- "The hurricane victims found refuge in a Red Cross shelter."
- "She sought refuge with her friends after a difficult breakup."
