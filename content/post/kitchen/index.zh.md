## 读音
/ˈkɪtʃɪn/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 家中的房间，通常用来准备食物和烹饪。例如: "She spent most of her afternoon in the kitchen cooking dinner."
- **名词 (n)**: 在餐馆或酒店中，专门用于烹饪的地方。例如: "The head chef works in the busy kitchen of the five-star restaurant."
- **名词 (n)**: 比喻指组织或机构的幕后操作中心。例如: "In politics, the kitchen is where the real decisions are made."

## 短语
- "Kitchen staff" 指在厨房工作的人员。
- "Kitchen appliances" 包括各种用于烹饪和食物准备的电器，如冰箱、炉灶等。
- "Kitchen sink drama" 是指展示日常生活琐碎细节的戏剧或电影。

## 例句
- 名词: "The kitchen was filled with the aroma of freshly baked bread."
- 名词: "The new kitchen design featured modern, energy-efficient appliances."
