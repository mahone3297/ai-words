## 读音
/toʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 脚趾，人或动物脚的最前端部分。例如: "He stubbed his toe on the table leg."
- **动词**: (非标准用法) 踢，用脚尖触碰。例如: "She toed the ball gently towards the goal."

## 短语
- "On your toes" 意为警觉或准备好行动。例如: "Stay on your toes, the boss might walk in anytime."
- "Toe the line" 意为遵守规则或服从命令。例如: "You need to toe the line if you want to keep your job."

## 例句
- 名词: "She carefully painted each of her toes a different color."
- 动词: "He toed the starting line, ready for the race."

## 同义词
- Finger (在某些上下文中，如脚趾与手指的对比)
- Digit (通常在医学或解剖学中使用)
