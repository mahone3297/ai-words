## 读音
/ klaʊd /

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 天空中的水蒸气凝结成的小水滴或冰晶集合体，形成可见的悬浮物，即云。例如: "The sky was filled with white fluffy clouds."
  - 云计算：通过互联网提供计算资源和服务。例如: "Our company relies on cloud storage for all its data."
  - 云状物：形状类似云的物体或现象。例如: "There was a cloud of smoke rising from the chimney."

- **动词**:
  - 使模糊或变暗，常与视线、理解等搭配。例如: "His sadness clouded his judgment."
  - 在网络上存储数据或信息。例如: "We're clouding our backups to ensure safety."

## 短语
- "in the cloud" 指在网络上，云端。例如: "All my files are stored in the cloud."
- "cloud computing" 云计算技术。例如: "Cloud computing has revolutionized the way businesses operate."

## 例句
- 名词: "The sunset was a beautiful display of orange and pink clouds."
- 动词: "Her mood clouded her ability to enjoy the concert."
- 云状物: "A cloud of butterflies swarmed around the flowers."

## 同义词
- Fog
- Mist
- Overcast
- Sky
- Cumulus (特指某种云类型)
