## 读音
/pʌnɪʃmənt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 因犯错或违法行为而给予的责罚或处罚。例如: "He received a punishment for cheating on the exam."
- **动词 (v)**: 对某人施加惩罚或处罚。例如: "The teacher punished the student for being disruptive."

## 短语
- "Punishment fits the crime" 意味着处罚应该与所犯的罪行相匹配。
- "To avoid punishment" 指的是为了避免受到处罚而采取行动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The judge decided on a severe punishment for the convicted criminal."
- 动词: "The coach punished the team by making them run extra laps."
