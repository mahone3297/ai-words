## 读音
/ˌpərˈmɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 允许，许可，授权。例如: "You need permission from the owner to enter the property."
- **动词**: 给予许可，允许。例如: "The teacher permitted the students to leave early."

## 短语
- "Ask for permission" 请求许可。
- "Give permission to" 授权给。
- "Without permission" 未经许可。

## 例句
- 名词: "The event organizer needs to obtain a permit from the city council."
- 动词: "The parents have given their permission for their child to travel alone."

## 同义词
- Consent
- Authorization
- Approval
- Allowance
- License
