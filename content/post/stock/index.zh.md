## 读音
/ˈstɑːk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   1. 股票，股份。例如: "He invests in the stock market."
   2. 库存，存货。例如: "The store has a good stock of winter coats."
   3. 基础，根源。例如: "They come from noble stock."
   4. （家畜的）品种，血统。例如: "The farm breeds high-quality dairy stock."

- **动词**:
   1. 储存，备有。例如: "The supermarket is stocking up for the holiday season."
   2. 充实，补充（库存）。例如: "We need to stock our pantry with more food."

## 短语
- "in stock" 表示有现货，可立即购买。
- "out of stock" 表示暂时无货。
- "keep sth. in stock" 保持某物的库存。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her company's stock price has been rising steadily this year."
- 动词: "The store stocks a variety of organic products."
- 基本用法: "The farmer keeps a large stock of hay for the winter."
