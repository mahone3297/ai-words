## Pronunciation
/prəˈbɪkt/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To forbid or prevent by authority; to make it illegal or impossible. For example: "The government prohibits smoking in public places."
- **Noun**: The act of prohibiting; a rule or order that forbids something. For example: "The prohibition on alcohol during Prohibition era."

## Phrases
- "Prohibit something" means to not allow or ban it.
- "Prohibited items" refers to things that are not allowed.
- "Prohibition era" in the US refers to the time when the production, sale, and transport of alcohol were banned.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The school prohibits chewing gum during class."
- **Noun**: "The law prohibits driving under the influence of drugs."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "The government's decision to prohibit single-use plastics has been met with mixed reactions."
