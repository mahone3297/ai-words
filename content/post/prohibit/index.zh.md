## 读音
/prəˈhɪbɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 禁止；阻止。例如: "The law prohibits smoking in public places."
- **名词**: 禁令；禁止的事物。例如: "There is a prohibit on carrying weapons in this area."

## 短语
- "prohibit from" 意味着不允许某人做某事。例如: "The school prohibits students from using mobile phones during class."
- "under prohibition" 指在禁令之下。例如: "Alcohol was under prohibition in some states during the early 20th century."

## 例句
- 动词: "The city council has prohibited parking on this street during rush hour."
- 名词: "The new policy includes several prohibitions aimed at protecting the environment."

- ban
- restrict
