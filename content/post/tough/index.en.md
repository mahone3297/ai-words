## Definitions
### Noun
- **Tough**: A noun referring to a person or thing that is hard, durable, or difficult to deal with. Can also mean physically strong or resilient.
- Example: "The construction worker had a tough job carrying heavy materials."

### Verb
- **To toughen**: To make (a person or material) harder, stronger, or more resistant. Can also mean to endure or withstand hardship.
- Example: "She toughened up her child by exposing them to different challenges."

### Adjective
- **Tough**: Describing something that is hard, solid, or requiring great effort to overcome. Can also refer to someone who is strong-willed or resilient.
- Example: "The old oak tree was tough enough to withstand the storm."

### Adverb
- **Toughly**: In a manner that is hard, severe, or persistent. Can describe physical strength or a challenging approach.
- Example: "He played the game tirelessly, tackling opponents toughly."
