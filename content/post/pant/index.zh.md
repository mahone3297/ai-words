## 读音
/pænt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 裤子；短裤。例如: "He was wearing a pair of black pants."
  - （动物）喘息声。例如: "The dog was panting heavily after running."

- **动词**:
  - 喘息，急促呼吸。例如: "She was panting from the exertion of climbing the stairs."
  - （尤指兴奋或紧张时）急促呼吸。例如: "He panted with anticipation."

## 短语
- "pant for something" 渴望得到某物。例如: "She pants for a better future."
- "panting heavily" 喘着大气。例如: "After the race, they were all panting heavily."

## 例句
- 名词: "She bought a new pair of pants for the summer."
- 动词: "The child was panting after playing soccer."

## 同义词
- Breathless
- Gasping
- Wheezing
- Heavy breathing
- Exhaling rapidly
