## 读音
/toɡeðər/

## 释义
- **副词**: 意为“一起”，“共同”，“同时”。表示人或事物在空间或时间上的合并或聚集。例如: "They worked together on the project."
- **连词**: 连接两个或多个词或短语，表示“和”或“并且”。例如: "She likes coffee and tea together."

## 短语
- "get together" 意为聚会或相聚。例如: "Let's get together for dinner sometime."
- "work together" 意为合作。例如: "The two companies decided to work together on a new product."

## 例句
- 副词: "The whole family sat together at the dinner table."
- 连词: "She ordered a sandwich together with a salad."
