## Pronunciation
/əˈdɪsəbəl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or regarded as advisable or suitable, especially under given circumstances. For example: "It's advisable to wear sunscreen on sunny days."
- **Adverb**: In a manner that is advisable or suitable. For example: "She handled the situation advisabley, considering the risks involved."

## Phrases
- "It's advisable to…" suggests that something is recommended or necessary.
- "Acting advisable" implies making a wise decision or taking a course of action that is judicious.
- "Be advisable" can be used to imply that something should be done or considered.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "It's advisable to consult a doctor before starting any new exercise routine."
- **Adverb**: "He made an advisable choice by accepting the job offer."
- "The expert recommended it as being advisable for everyone to get vaccinated."
