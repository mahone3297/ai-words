## 读音
/ˈpɛpər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种辣味调料，由干的胡椒果实制成。例如: "She added some black pepper to the dish for flavor."
- **名词**: 胡椒科植物，其果实可用于制作调料。例如: "The pepper plant needs plenty of sunlight to grow well."
- **动词**: 用胡椒粉撒在食物上。例如: "He peppered his steak with generous amounts of salt and pepper."
- **动词**: 大量散布或投掷（如子弹或石子）。例如: "The soldiers peppered the enemy's position with gunfire."

## 短语
- "pepper with questions" 意思是连续不断地提问。
- "pepper sauce" 是一种含有胡椒的辣酱。

## 例句
- 名词: "I prefer white pepper to black pepper in this recipe."
- 动词: "The chef peppered the salad with fresh ground pepper."
- 动词: "The kids started throwing snowballs, peppering each other with icy shots."
