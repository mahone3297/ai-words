## 读音
/swel/

## 释义
- **动词**: 变大；膨胀。例如: "The swelling in his ankle got worse after he walked too much."
- **形容词**: 膨胀的；肿胀的。例如: "She had a slight swell around her eye from the injury."
- **名词**: 膨胀；隆起。例如: "There was a swell on the surface of the water after the storm."
- **名词**: （音乐、情绪等）高涨；激昂。例如: "The music swelled to a crescendo."

## 短语
- "swell up" 表示（身体部位）肿胀。
- "a swell party" 指一个很棒的聚会。
- "the swell crowd" 通常指上流社会或富有的人群。

## 例句
- 动词: "After eating too much, his stomach began to swell."
- 形容词: "She put ice on the swollen ankle to reduce the pain."
- 名词: "The ship rode the swell of the ocean gracefully."
