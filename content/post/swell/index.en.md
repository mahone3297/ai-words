## Pronunciation
/swɛl/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Swell**: (1) An increase in size or volume, often of water or wind: "The ocean swelled with each passing wave." (2) A swelling or bulging part, especially on a person's body: "He had a swelling on his ankle from the injury."
- **(Euphemism)**: (3) A pregnancy: "She's expecting a baby, so her belly is swelled."

### Verb
- **To swell**: (1) To become larger or fuller, often as a result of increased fluid or pressure: "The river swelled after heavy rainfall."
- (2) (Informal) To increase in quantity or intensity: "His popularity swelled during the campaign."

### Adjective
- **Swollen**: Having a larger size than normal due to increased fluid or inflammation: "Her swollen feet from standing all day."

### Adverb
- **Swellingly**: In a swelling or increasing manner: "She sang swellingly as the orchestra played."

## Phrases
