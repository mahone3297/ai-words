## 读音
/stɜːrn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 船的后部或尾部。例如: "The captain stood at the stern of the ship."
- **形容词**: 严厉的，苛刻的。例如: "She has a stern expression on her face."
- **副词**: 严格地，坚定地。例如: "He sternly warned the children not to play with matches."

## 短语
- "At the stern" 指的是在船的尾部。
- "Stern lecture" 意味着严厉的训斥或批评。

## 例句
- 名词: "The lifeboats were stored at the stern."
- 形容词: "His stern demeanor made the new employees nervous."
- 副词: "The teacher looked sternly at the student who was talking during class."

## 同义词
- Harsh
- Severe
- Strict
- Rigid
- Uncompromising
