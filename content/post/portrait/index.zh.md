## 读音
/ˈpɔːrtrət/

## 释义
- **名词**: 人物的画像或肖像，通常描绘脸部和肩膀。例如: "She had a beautiful portrait of her grandfather hanging on the wall."
- **动词**: 绘制或画肖像。例如: "The artist was hired to portrait the queen."

## 短语
- "Portrait painting" 指的是肖像画艺术。
- "Full-length portrait" 指从头到脚描绘的人物肖像。

## 例句
- 名词: "The museum displayed an exquisite portrait of a 17th-century nobleman."
- 动词: "She spent hours in the studio, carefully portrait her friend's expression."
