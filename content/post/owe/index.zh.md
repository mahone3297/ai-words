## 读音
/əʊ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 欠债，负债。例如: "I owe you $10 for lunch."
- **动词**: 应给予感激或回报。例如: "I owe a great debt of gratitude to my mentor."
- **动词**: 归功于，归因于。例如: "My success owes much to my hard work and determination."
- **名词**: 债务，欠款。例如: "He has a large owe to the bank."

## 短语
- "owe someone money" 意味着欠某人钱。
- "owe it to someone" 表示对某人有所亏欠或应该为某人做某事。

## 例句
- 动词: "I still owe you an apology for my behavior yesterday."
- 动词: "She owes her success to her parents' constant support."
- 名词: "The company is struggling with a huge owe to its creditors."

- be indebted to
