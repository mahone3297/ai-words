## 读音
/rɪˈgɑːd(ə)ls/

## 释义
- **副词**: 不顾，不管，不理会。例如: "Regardless of the weather, we went for a walk."
- **介词**: 跟 "of" 连用，表示“不顾，不管”。例如: "Regardless of the risks, he decided to go on with the plan."

## 短语
- "Regardless of" 用来表示不考虑某个因素。例如: "Regardless of your age, you can learn new things."

## 例句
- 副词: "Regardless of the time, she always answers her phone."
- 介词短语: "Regardless of the cost, we will find a way to solve this problem."

## 同义词
- Despite
- In spite of
- No matter
- Whatever
- Be it ever so
