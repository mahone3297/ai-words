## Pronunciation
/proʊˈsiːd/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (n) To begin or continue an action in a planned or organized manner; advance or proceed with something. (v) To carry out a process or step, often after another has been completed. (adj) Characterizing or relating to the act of proceeding or progression.

## Phrases
- "Proceed with caution" means to be careful when continuing.
- "Proceed to the next step" indicates moving on in a sequence.
- "In due course, we will proceed" signifies that something will happen at an appropriate time.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "After finishing her homework, she proceeded to clean her room."
- **Adj**: "The meeting proceeded smoothly, without any interruptions."
- **Adv**: "Please proceed to the registration desk."
