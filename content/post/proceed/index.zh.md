## 读音
/prəˈsiːd/

## 释义
- **动词**: 继续进行；前进。例如: "After a short break, we proceeded with the meeting."
- **名词**: （尤指正式的）开始，进行。例如: "The opening ceremony of the event was followed by the main proceed."

## 短语
- "to proceed with something" 继续做某事。例如: "We decided to proceed with the project despite the difficulties."
- "without further ado, let's proceed" 没有更多耽搁，让我们开始。例如: "Without further ado, let's proceed to the next agenda."

## 例句
- 动词: "Once we have all the necessary information, we can proceed to make a decision."
- 名词: "The auctioneer announced the start of the bidding, and the proceeds would go to charity."

- continue
- advance
- go on
- move forward
