## 读音
/ɔɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种液体，通常是石油、油脂或润滑剂。例如: "Add some oil to the pan before frying the eggs."
- **动词**: 加油或涂抹油。例如: "I need to oil the hinges so they stop squeaking."

## 短语
- "oil the wheels" 比喻促进事情顺利进行。
- "oilskin" 用于防雨或防水的油布。

## 例句
- 名词: "The car requires a regular change of engine oil to maintain its performance."
- 动词: "She oiled her hands to prevent them from getting chapped in the cold weather."
