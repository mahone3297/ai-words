## Pronunciation
/ˈneɪvl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to ships or the sea, especially in terms of war or military operations. For example: "The naval forces were deployed to protect the coast."
- **Noun**: Referring to the navy, which is the branch of a country's armed forces that operates warships and other vessels on water. For example: "He joined the naval academy to become an officer."

### Noun Usage Examples
- "The naval base was strategically located near the harbor."
- "Their nation has one of the most powerful navies in the world."

### Adjective Usage Examples
- **Adjective**: "Naval warfare tactics have evolved significantly over time."
- "A naval architect designs ships and other marine vessels."

## Phrases
- "Naval combat" refers to battles fought at sea between ships.
- "Naval fleet" refers to a group of ships under a single command.
- "Naval intelligence" pertains to information-gathering activities specific to maritime matters.

## Example Sentences
