## 读音
/əs/

## 释义
- **代词**: 第二人称单数的宾格形式，你。例如: "I gave the book to him and her, and to us."
- **形容词**: 美国的，美国人的。例如: "The US is short for the United States of America."
- **名词**: 美国，美利坚合众国。例如: "They moved to the US for better job opportunities."

## 短语
- "in the US" 表示在美国。
- "US citizen" 指美国公民。
- "US dollars" 或 "USD" 代表美元。

## 例句
- 代词: "Do you have any plans for the weekend, us too?"
- 形容词: "She loves exploring different cultures, especially those in the US."
- 名词: "The US economy is one of the largest in the world."
