## 读音
/ˈværɪəti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 多样性，种类。例如: "There is a great variety of birds in the forest."
- **形容词**: 各种各样的，多样的。例如: "The festival offers a variety of cultural events."

## 短语
- "A variety of" 表示多种多样的。例如: "We have a variety of flavors for you to choose from."
- "Variety is the spice of life" 意味着生活因多样性而有趣。

## 例句
- 名词: "The store carries a wide variety of products to suit all tastes."
- 形容词: "The menu features a variety of international dishes."

## 同义词
- Diversity
- Range
- Assortment
- Selection
- Multiplicity
