## 读音
/ɪmˈpruːv/

## 释义
- **动词**: 改进，提高，改善。例如: "We need to improve our customer service."
- **名词**: 改进，改善的地方。例如: "There is still room for improvement."

## 短语
- "to improve on something" 意味着在某方面做得更好。
- "improvement plans" 指的是旨在改进的计划或策略。

## 例句
- 动词: "Regular exercise can significantly improve your health."
- 名词: "The company's profits have shown a steady improvement over the past year."
