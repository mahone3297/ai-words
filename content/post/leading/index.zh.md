## 读音
/ˈliːdɪŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 领先的，主要的，重要的。指在某个领域处于主导地位或具有显著影响。例如: "She is a leading expert in her field."
- **名词**: 领导，指引，主演角色。例如: "The company's new CEO has taken a strong leading role in decision-making."
- **动词**: 领导，指挥，引导。例如: "The experienced guide led us through the forest."

## 短语
- "Leading figure" 指重要人物或领导者。
- "Leading role" 在戏剧、电影或生活中扮演主要角色。
- "Leading indicator" 经济学中指预示未来趋势的关键指标。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This university is known for its leading research in artificial intelligence."
- 名词: "He played the leading role in the Broadway production."
- 动词: "The experienced hiker led the group safely through the challenging trail."
