## 读音
/wiːk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 薄弱的，不强的，缺乏力量或强度的。例如: "He was too weak to lift the heavy box."
- **形容词**: 疲倦的，虚弱的，身体不适的。例如: "She felt weak after a long day of work."
- **形容词**: 不坚定的，意志薄弱的。例如: "His resolve was weak in the face of temptation."
- **副词**: 微弱地，无力地。例如: "The signal was received weakly on the radio."

## 短语
- "Weak in something" 指在某个方面不擅长或不强。
- "Weak points" 指事物的不足之处或弱点。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She was so weak that she needed help to stand up."
- 形容词: "He has a weak immune system, so he gets sick easily."
- 副词: "Her voice sounded weak over the phone."
