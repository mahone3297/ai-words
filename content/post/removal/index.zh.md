## 读音
/rɪˈmuːvəl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 移除；拿开；去掉。例如: "The removal of the furniture from the room took several hours."
    - 解雇；开除。例如: "His removal from the company was unexpected."
    - 搬迁；搬家。例如: "They are planning for their removal to a new city next month."

## 短语
- "Removal company" 是指专门负责搬运和搬家的公司。
- "Removal process" 指的是移除或淘汰某事物的过程。

## 例句
- 名词: "The removal of the old paint revealed the beautiful wooden surface underneath."
- "After several complaints, the management decided on his removal from the position."
