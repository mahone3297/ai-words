## 读音
/fækˈtəri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 工厂，制造厂，生产或加工物品的大型建筑或场所。例如: "The new car models are produced in their state-of-the-art factory."
- **动词**: （非正式用法）制造，大量生产。例如: "This small company factories custom furniture for clients."

## 短语
- "Factory outlet" 是工厂直销店，通常价格较低。
- "Factory settings" 指设备或软件的原始默认设置。
- "Factory automation" 是指工厂自动化，即使用机器和技术自动完成生产过程。

## 例句
- 名词: "The workers went on strike at the textile factory."
- 动词: "They factory high-quality electronics in this facility."
