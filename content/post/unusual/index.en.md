## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by being uncommon, not usual, or atypical. For example: "The event was unusual for its lack of attendees."
- **Noun**: Something that is not common or expected; an exceptional case. For example: "The painting was an unusual find in the attic."

## Phrases
- "An unusual occurrence" refers to something that isn't typical or expected.
- "An unusual talent" highlights an exceptional skill or ability.
- "Unusual behavior" denotes actions that deviate from what is normally expected.
