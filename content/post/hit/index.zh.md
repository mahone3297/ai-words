## 读音
/hɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - (击打) To strike with force; to make contact forcefully. 例如: "The ball was hit by the batter straight to the center field."
  - (碰撞) To collide with something. 例如: "I accidentally hit my head on the door frame."
  - (成功) To achieve or reach a target. 例如: "His new song hit the top of the charts."
  - (影响) To affect or impact. 例如: "The economic crisis hit many families hard."

- **名词**:
  - (打击) An act of striking or hitting. 例如: "He received a heavy hit from his opponent."
  - (成功的事物) A popular or successful product, song, or movie. 例如: "Her latest book is a big hit with readers."

## 短语
- "Take a hit" 意为遭受损失或承受痛苦。例如: "The company had to take a significant hit during the pandemic."
- "Hit the books" 指开始努力学习。例如: "I need to hit the books before the exam."

## 例句
- 动词: "The boxer hit his opponent with a powerful right hook."
- 名词: "The movie was a huge hit and grossed millions at the box office."
