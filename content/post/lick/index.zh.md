## 读音
/lik/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 舔，用舌头触碰。例如: "The cat licked its paws clean."
2. 轻轻地擦过或触碰。例如: "He licked his lips nervously."

- **名词**:
1. 一舔的动作。例如: "She gave the ice cream a quick lick."
2. 轻微的胜利。例如: "They gained a small lick in the debate."

## 短语
- "lick one's chops" 表示对某事期待或贪婪的样子。例如: "He was licking his chops at the thought of the delicious dinner."
- "get a lick in" 意为在短时间内完成一些事情。例如: "I just have time for a quick shower before work; I'll get a lick in."

## 例句
