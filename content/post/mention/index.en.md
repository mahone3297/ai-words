## Pronunciation
/ˈmenʃ(ə)n/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A reference or spoken or written allusion to a person, thing, event, etc. For example: "The book contains several mentions of her work."
  - "He received a special mention in the acknowledgments section."
- **Verb**: To speak or write about (someone or something), typically briefly or incidentally. For example: "The author mentioned his previous travels in the first chapter."
  - "She mentioned that she had seen him earlier today."

## Phrases
- "A passing mention" refers to a brief or incidental reference.
- "Mention something/someone in dispatches" means to commend or praise officially.
- "Don't mention it" is often said in response to an expression of gratitude, meaning "it's no problem" or "you're welcome".

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "His name was mentioned several times during the meeting."
- **Verb**: "I'll make sure to mention your idea in my report."
