## 读音
/ˈmenʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 提到，提及，说起。例如: "She made no mention of the problem in her report."
- **动词**: 提及，说到，提到。例如: "He mentioned his recent trip to Paris in passing."

## 短语
- "make no mention of" 不提，不谈及。例如: "In his speech, he made no mention of the controversy."
- "mention something to someone" 向某人提起某事。例如: "I mentioned the issue to my boss during our meeting."

## 例句
- 名词: "There was no mention of the budget cuts in the news."
- 动词: "She casually mentioned her plans to travel next summer."

## 同义词
- refer to
- bring up
- allude to
- cite
- discuss
