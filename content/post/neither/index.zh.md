## 读音
/ˈnaɪðər/

## 释义
- **代词**: 表示两者都不。例如: "Neither of them likes coffee."
- **连词**: 连接两个否定的选项。例如: "I neither have time nor energy for that."

## 短语
- "Neither...nor..." 用来同时否定两个事物。例如: "Neither Sam nor Sarah will be attending the party."

## 例句
- 代词: "Neither Sam nor I am going to the cinema tonight."
- 连词: "She neither knows how to swim nor wants to learn."

## 同义词
- Not...or...
- None of...
- Neither one nor the other...
