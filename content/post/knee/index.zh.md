## 读音
/niː/

## 释义
- **名词**: 人体腿部的关节，在大腿和小腿之间。例如: "She hurt her knee in the accident."
- **动词**: （常与to连用）膝行，屈膝。例如: "The child kneeled down to pick up the toy."

## 短语
- "knee-high" 表示到膝盖高度的，常用于描述袜子、裤子等。例如: "She wore knee-high boots with her dress."
- "knee-jerk reaction" 指本能的、直觉的反应。例如: "His response was a pure knee-jerk reaction."

## 例句
- 名词: "He put ice on his swollen knee after the match."
- 动词: "The children kneeled in prayer before bedtime."
