## 读音
/spɛr/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 多余的，剩下的：东西或时间等未被使用或消耗的部分。例如: "I have some spare time today."
  - 备用的：额外的或替换用的物品。例如: "Keep a spare tire in your car in case of emergencies."
  
- **动词**:
  - 节省，留出：不使用或保留以备将来之需。例如: "Spare me some money for tomorrow."
  - 宽恕，饶恕：不给予惩罚或伤害。例如: "The judge spared him from a prison sentence."

## 短语
- "Spare no effort" 意味着竭尽全力。
- "Spare a thought" 用来表示对某人的关心或考虑。例如: "Spare a thought for those less fortunate."

## 例句
- 名词: "There's a spare room in the house if you need a place to stay."
- 动词: "Can you spare me a few minutes for a quick chat?"
- 情态动词+动词原形: "Please spare me the details; I just want the summary."
