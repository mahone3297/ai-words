## 读音
/pæst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 粘贴物，胶水，糊状物。例如: "She applied some paste to the paper to stick it on the wall."
- **动词**: 粘贴，复制并粘贴（电子文档中的内容）。例如: "I pasted the text from one document to another."

## 短语
- "cut and paste" 在计算机操作中，指剪切并粘贴文本或图像。
- "paste something together" 意味着将事物组合或拼凑在一起。

## 例句
- 名词: "The child used a brush to apply the paste onto the cardboard."
- 动词: "In Word, you can easily paste copied text by pressing Ctrl+V."
