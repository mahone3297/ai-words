## Pronunciation
/pæst/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A thick, sticky substance made by mixing flour or other dry ingredients with water, used for binding, coating, or filling (e.g., in paper, glue, or food preparation).
   - Example: "I spread some paste on the back of the photograph to attach it to the wall."
2. A preparation of substances, such as herbs or spices, ground together and often mixed with a liquid for a specific purpose (e.g., medicine or food flavoring).
   - Example: "The chef added a paste of garlic and ginger to the curry."

### Verb
1. To apply a paste-like substance to a surface (e.g., wallpaper or paint).
   - Example: "She carefully pasted the wallpaper onto the wall."
2. To use or spread a paste mixture (e.g., for fixing, coating, or filling).
   - Example: "He pasted some glue onto the envelope flap."

## Phrases
- "Apply paste" or "use paste"
- "Make a paste" or "create a paste"
- "A paste consistency" or "a thick, adhesive consistency"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She mixed flour and water to make a paste for the project."
- **Verb**: "He pasted a label on the jar to mark the contents."
- **As a verb**: "I need to paste the letters onto the poster before it's ready to print."
