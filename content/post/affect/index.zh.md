## 读音
/əˈfekt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 影响；对…产生作用。例如: "The weather affected our travel plans."
- **名词**: 情感；假装的行为。例如: "He put on a brave affect despite feeling scared."

## 短语
- "affect positively/negatively" 影响某事的正面或负面
- "to have an affect on something" 对某物产生影响

## 例句
- 动词: "Her illness seriously affected her ability to work."
- 名词: "He wore a cheerful affect to hide his sadness."

## 同义词
- impact
- influence
- alter
- modify
- touch (in the sense of emotional impact)
