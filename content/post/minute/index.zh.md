## 读音
/ˈmɪnət/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 时间单位，一分钟，等于60秒。例如: "There are 60 minutes in an hour."
    - 记录详细信息的小册子或笔记。例如: "I'll write down the details in a meeting minute."
    - 音乐或广播节目中的一小段。例如: "The radio show features a one-minute segment on local news."

- **形容词**:
    - 微小的，细微的。例如: "A minute error can cause big problems."
    - 极其精确的。例如: "The scientist made minute measurements."

## 短语
- "by the minute" 表示每一分钟。例如: "The situation was changing by the minute."
- "minute details" 表示详尽无遗的细节。例如: "She paid attention to every minute detail in her artwork."

## 例句
- 名词: "We have only five minutes left until the presentation starts."
- 形容词: "The surgeon's hands moved with minute precision."
