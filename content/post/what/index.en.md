## Pronunciation
/ˈwɒt/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A word used to inquire about something or ask for information. "What's the time?"
2. A thing; an object, substance, or matter. "I saw a beautiful what flying by."
3. The thing or quality that someone or something is, or that characterizes them. "Her what is her kindness."

### Verb
1. To ask or inquire about something. "She asked what was happening."
2. To be the matter or point; concern. "That's not what I meant."

### Interjection
1. Expressing surprise, curiosity, or uncertainty. "What? You're leaving already?"

## Phrases
- "What's up?" is a colloquial way to ask how someone is doing.
- "What's your favorite color?" is a question to gather personal preferences.
- "What if...?" is a hypothetical phrase used to consider possible outcomes.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "What's the best way to get to the airport?"
- **Verb**: "I don't understand what you're trying to say."
- **Interjection**: "What? You've finished the entire project?"
