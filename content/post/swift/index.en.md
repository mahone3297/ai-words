## Pronunciation
/swɪft/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: 1. Characterized by speed, agility, or rapidity; quick. For example: "The eagle flew swiftly across the sky."
- **Adverb**: In a swift manner; rapidly. For example: "She finished her work swiftly."
- **Noun**: A type of bird known for its speed and agility, especially the common swift (Hirundo rustica). For example: "The swifts nested in the chimney."

## Phrases
- "Swift response" indicates a rapid and efficient answer or action.
- "Swiftly moving waters" describe water that flows quickly.
- "Swift change" refers to a sudden or rapid alteration.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The car drove swiftly down the highway."
- **Adverb**: "She completed the task swiftly, impressing her colleagues."
- **Noun**: "The swifts were seen diving and catching insects in mid-air."
