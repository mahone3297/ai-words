## 读音
/ˈmɑːstər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 主人：如 "He was the master of the house."
  - 高手；专家：如 "She is a master in painting."
  - （尤指男性）硕士：如 "He holds a master's degree in economics."
  - 导师：如 "The students respected their master deeply."
  - （棋类等的）大师称号：如 "He became a chess master at age 15."

- **动词**:
  - 掌握；精通：如 "To master a new language takes time and effort."
  - 控制；指挥：如 "She mastered her fear and continued on."

## 短语
- "master something" 学会或掌握某事：如 "He has mastered the art of public speaking."
- "under the master's guidance" 在大师的指导下：如 "She learned to paint under the master's guidance."

## 例句
- 名词: "The master of the house welcomed us warmly."
- 动词: "With practice, he mastered the difficult piano piece."
