## 读音
/stɜːr/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 搅拌，搅动：使（液体）混合或移动。例如: "Stir the soup gently to prevent it from burning."
  - 激发，引起：引发（感情、兴趣或反应）。例如: "The speech stirred emotions in the audience."
  - 唤醒：使从睡眠状态中醒来。例如: "The noise outside stirred her from her sleep."

## 短语
- "Stir up" 意味着激起或煽动。例如: "Their actions stirred up controversy."
- "Stir something in" 表示将某个成分加入并混合。例如: "Stir the sugar into the coffee."

## 例句
- 动词: "She stirred the batter until it was well combined."
- 激发： "His words stirred a desire for change in many people."

## 同义词
- Mix
- Agitate
- Blend
- Whisk
- Shuffle (when referring to emotions or interest)
