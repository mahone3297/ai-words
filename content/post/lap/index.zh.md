## 读音
/læp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 膝盖上的一片区域或大腿的一部分。例如: "The child sat on her father's lap."
2. （通常指长条形的）表面的一段。例如: "He folded the newspaper over one lap."
3. （竞赛中的）一圈。例如: "She completed three laps around the track."

- **动词**:
1. 把（孩子、宠物等）抱在膝上。例如: "The grandmother gently lapped the baby in her arms."
2. 舀起或舔食液体。例如: "The cat lapped up its milk."

## 短语
- "lap up" 表示迅速或愉快地接受。例如: "The audience lapped up his performance."
- "lap over" 意味着一部分覆盖另一部分。例如: "The fabric lapped over the edge of the chair."
