## 读音
/məʊst/

## 释义
- **副词**: 最多，最，极其。例如: "This is the most delicious cake I've ever tasted."
- **形容词**: 最多的，最大的，最高的。例如: "He has the most books in the library."

## 短语
- "Most likely" 意为很可能，可能性最大。
- "The majority of" 用于表示大多数。例如: "The majority of people prefer coffee over tea."

## 例句
- 副词: "She practices most days of the week."
- 形容词: "The most common cause of the problem is user error."
