## 读音
/outˈwərd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 向外的，外部的，外面的。例如: "The outward appearance of the house was very appealing."
- **副词**: 向外，往外。例如: "He pushed the door outward to let her in."

## 短语
- "outward journey" 指的是外出的旅程。
- "outward bound" 常用来描述即将出发或朝外进行的动作。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The outward design of the car caught everyone's attention."
- 副词: "He leaned outward to catch the falling object."

## 同义词
- External
- Exterior
- Outside
- Towards the outside
- In a direction away from the center
