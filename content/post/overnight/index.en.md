## Pronunciation
/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Happening, done, or delivered during the night or immediately before or after it. For example: "We need to make an overnight stay due to the late flight."
- **Adverb**: During the night or from one day to the next; happening or done quickly or suddenly. Example: "The package will be delivered overnight."

### Adjective (definitions and examples)
- **Definition**: Describing something that lasts or happens throughout the night, or that is arranged to happen within a single night. Example sentence: "They took an overnight trip to the mountains."

- **Definition**: Referring to a financial transaction that takes place between the close of one business day and the start of the next. Example sentence: "The stock market experienced an overnight surge."

### Adverb (definitions and examples)
- **Definition**: Indicating that something occurs or should occur between the end of one day and the beginning of the next. Example sentence: "The guests can check in any time overnight."

- **Definition**: Denoting a transformation or change that happens swiftly. Example sentence: "His attitude towards work changed overnight."

## Phrases
- "Overnight stay": A brief accommodation lasting only one night.
- "Overnight success": Refers to a person or thing that becomes successful very quickly.
- "Overnight bag": A small suitcase or bag containing items needed for a one-night stay away from home.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "She packed an overnight bag for her business trip."
- **Adverb**: "The snowstorm hit, causing roads to become impassable overnight."
