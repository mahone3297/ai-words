## 读音
/treɪn/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 火车：A long series of connected railway cars pulled by a locomotive.
2. 列车：A group of vehicles or individuals moving in a line or procession.
3. 培训：The process of teaching or instructing someone in a particular skill or activity.

- **动词 v**:
1. 训练：To educate, develop, or prepare someone, especially in a specific skill or for a specific job.
2. 训练（动物）：To teach an animal to obey commands or perform certain tasks.

## 短语
- "train ride" 指乘坐火车旅行。
- "train station" 是火车站。
- "train someone/something" 表示训练某人或某物。

## 例句
- 名词: "I'm taking the train to New York tomorrow."
- 动词: "She is training for a marathon."
- 名词（比喻用法）: "Their children are the pride and joy of their life, the apples of their eye, the train they've been pulling for years."
