## 读音
/ˈlɪkwɪd/

## 释义
- **名词 n**: 1. 液体，液态物质。例如: "Water is a liquid at room temperature."
   2. 流动资金，流动资产。例如: "The company has plenty of liquid assets to cover its debts."

- **形容词 adj**: 1. 液体的，流质的。例如: "A liquid substance can flow easily."
   2. 可变现的，易于转换为现金的。例如: "Liquid investments can be quickly turned into cash."

## 短语
- "Liquid assets" 指的是可以迅速转换为现金的资产。
- "Liquid state" 指物质处于液体状态。
- "Liquidated damages" 在合同中指预先约定的损害赔偿金。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please pour me a glass of that red liquid."
- 形容词: "This material is so liquid that it pours smoothly."
