## 读音
/ˈrɪfrɪɡəreɪtər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种电器设备，用于冷却和保持食物和饮料低温的器具。例如: "I need to buy some groceries before I can fill up the refrigerator."
- **动词**: 使冷却或冷藏。例如: "The leftovers need to be refrigerated to prevent spoilage."

## 短语
- "refrigerator compartment" 指冰箱内部的存储区域。
- "to keep something in the fridge" 意思是将某物放入冰箱保存。

## 例句
- 名词: "I just bought a new energy-efficient refrigerator for my kitchen."
- 动词: "After opening the milk, make sure to refrigerate it immediately."

## 同义词
- Fridge
- Cooler
- Chiller
- Icebox
- Air conditioner (在某些特定上下文中，如冷藏大空间)
