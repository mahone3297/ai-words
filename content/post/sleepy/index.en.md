## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by a strong desire to sleep or feeling drowsy. For example: "After a long day, she felt very sleepy."
- **Noun**: A state of being asleep or drowsy. For example: "He fell asleep during the movie, feeling extremely sleepy."
- **Verb**: To make someone feel sleepy; to induce sleepiness. For example: "The warm milk made her feel really sleepy."

## Phrases
- "Feeling sleepy" describes the sensation of tiredness.
- "A sleepy afternoon" refers to a sluggish time of day.
- "Stay awake, don't let sleepiness overcome you" is a common advice.
