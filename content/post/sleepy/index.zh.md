## 读音
/sˈliːpi/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 昏昏欲睡的，困倦的。例如: "I feel so sleepy after a long day at work."
- **动词**: 使困倦，使想睡觉。例如: "The soft music made me sleepy."

## 短语
- "feeling sleepy" 意为感到困倦。
- "to fall asleep sleepy" 表示在困倦的状态下入睡。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It's late, and I'm getting really sleepy."
- 动词: "The baby was sleepy, so we put him to bed early."
