## 读音
/ˈseɪnət/

## 释义
- **名词**: 参议院，立法机构中的上议院。例如: "The Senate is part of the bicameral legislature in the United States."
- **名词**: 某些国家或地区的高级咨询或审议团体。例如: "In ancient Rome, the Senate was a body of advisors to the consuls."

## 短语
- "the upper house of the US Congress" 指美国国会的参议院。
- "senatorial elections" 指参议员的选举。

## 例句
- "The Senator delivered a speech on climate change in the Senate."
- "In Canada, members of the Senate are appointed rather than elected."

## 同义词
- Upper House
- Chamber of Senators
- Legislative Council (在某些国家的名称)
