## 读音
/lend/

## 释义
- **动词**: 借出；提供（钱、物品等）。例如: "I lent my car to my friend for the weekend."
- **名词**: （短暂的）借用行为。例如: "He returned the book after a short lend."

## 短语
- "Lend a hand" 帮忙，协助。例如: "Could you lend me a hand with this heavy box?"
- "Lend someone an ear" 倾听，聆听。例如: "She needed someone to lend her an ear and listen to her problems."

## 例句
- 动词: "She was happy to lend him some money until his next paycheck."
- 名词: "The bank offers low-interest loans as a lend service to its customers."

## 同义词
- Borrow (反义词，意为“借入”)
- Provide
- Grant
- Offer
- Bestow
