## Pronunciation
/rɪzɪˈneɪʃn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act of formally giving up or resigning from a job, position, or duty. 2. A written statement of one's intention to resign. 3. A feeling of quitting or giving up on something. 
- **Verb**: To resign oneself to a situation or accept defeat willingly.

## Phrases
- "Resign from a post" means to officially give up a job or position.
- "Submit a resignation letter" is the formal process of leaving a job.
- "Resignation may be necessary due to personal reasons."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After months of struggling, he finally submitted his resignation letter."
- **Verb**: "She resigned herself to the fact that she couldn't change the outcome."
- "His resignation marked the end of an era in the company."
