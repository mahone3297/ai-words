## 读音
/tend/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 倾向；趋于：表示有朝某个方向发展的倾向。例如: "The flowers tend to grow towards the sunlight."
  - 照料；护理：指照顾、看护某物或某人。例如: "She tends to the sick patients in the hospital."
  - 关注；关心：对某事表示关心或关注。例如: "He tends to the details of his work."

## 短语
- "tend to something" 意为处理或照看某事物。
- "tend towards something" 表示朝着某个方向或结果发展。

## 例句
- 动词: "As temperatures drop, plants tend to go into dormancy."
- 照顾： "She tends her garden every morning."
- 关注： "He tends to neglect his health when he's busy."
