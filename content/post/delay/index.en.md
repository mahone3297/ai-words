## Pronunciation
/dɪˈleɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act of postponing or delaying something; suspension or postponement of an event or action. 2. A period of time spent waiting or delayed. 3. An instance of being late or tardy. 
- **Verb**: 1. To postpone or put off the occurrence of something. 2. To cause a delay or make something happen later than planned.

## Phrases
- "Delay the project" means to postpone the start or completion of a project.
- "No further delay" signifies that there should be no more waiting or postponement.
- "Weather delay" refers to a delay caused by bad weather conditions.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The flight was delayed due to heavy fog."
- **Verb**: "I had to delay my presentation until next week."
- **Adjective**: "His tardiness was causing constant delays in the meeting."
