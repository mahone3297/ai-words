## Pronunciation
/fəʊn/

## Definitions
### Noun
A device that allows communication over a telephone network, typically with the ability to make voice calls and send text messages. Also used as a verb to refer to this action.

### Verb
1. To use a telephone to communicate with someone, often informally.
2. To make a call or contact someone through a phone.

### Adjective
Referring to a device or system that enables telecommunication.

## Phrases
- "Make a phone call"
- "Take a phone interview"
- "Answer the phone"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I grabbed my phone to check the time."
- **Verb**: "I'll give you a call later, just give me your number so I can phone you."
- **Adjective**: "The old-fashioned landline phones have been replaced by smartphones."
