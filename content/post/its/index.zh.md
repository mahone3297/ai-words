## 读音
/ɪts/

## 释义
- **代词**: 形容词性物主代词，表示“它的”，指代无生命的物体的所有权或特征。例如: "[The](/post/the/) [cat](/post/cat/) licked [its](/post/its/) paws."

## 短语
- "[Its](/post/its/) [color](/post/color/) is [blue](/post/blue/)." 意为“它是蓝色的。”
- "[The](/post/the/) [dog](/post/dog/) wagged [its](/post/its/) [tail](/post/tail/) happily." 意为“那只狗高兴地摇着它的尾巴。”

## 例句
- "[The](/post/the/) [tree](/post/tree/) has lost [all](/post/all/) [its](/post/its/) leaves [in](/post/in/) [autumn](/post/autumn/)."（这棵树在秋天失去了所有的叶子。）
- "[The](/post/the/) [baby](/post/baby/) [elephant](/post/elephant/) was playing [with](/post/with/) [its](/post/its/) [trunk](/post/trunk/)."（小象正在用它的鼻子玩耍。）

## 同义词
- 它的 (在中文中没有直接的同义词，[its](/post/its/) 是特定的物主代词，没有其他英文代词能完全替换它，但根据上下文，可以使用"[the](/post/the/) + 名词's"来表达类似的意思)
- [the](/post/the/) + 名词's: "[The](/post/the/) car's [engine](/post/engine/) is [loud](/post/loud/)."（这辆车的发动机声音很大。）
