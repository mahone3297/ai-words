## Pronunciation
/prɪsɪˈdent/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A person who holds the highest office in a country or organization, exercising supreme power or authority.**
   - Example: "The President of the United States is Joe Biden."
2. **A leader or chairperson of a group, committee, or institution.**
   - Example: "The club president organized a charity event."

### Verb
1. **To assume the position of president or take office as the head.**
   - Example: "He was inaugurated as the new president last week."
2. **To act or govern as a president, exercising leadership or decision-making powers.**
   - Example: "The board members discussed the president's proposals."

## Phrases
- "Swearing in the president" marks the official beginning of their term.
- "Meet with the president" implies a meeting with the highest-ranking official.
- "Address the president" refers to speaking directly to the head of state or government.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The president delivered a powerful speech at the summit."
- **Verb**: "As president, he prioritized environmental issues."
- **Interjection**: "Good morning, Mr. President!"
