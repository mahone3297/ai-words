## 读音
/nɒd/

## 释义
- **动词**: 轻轻地点头，以示同意、理解或打招呼。例如: "He nodded in approval when he heard the good news."
- **名词**: 点头的动作或示意。例如: "She gave me a small nod of encouragement."

## 短语
- "nod off" 意思是打盹，小睡一会儿。
- "nod one's head" 表示点头。

## 例句
- 动词: "John nodded his consent to the plan."
- 名词: "A quick nod was her only response."

## 同义词
- acknowledge
- signal
- gesture
- indicate
- approve (仅限动词形式)
