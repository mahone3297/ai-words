## 读音
/ʃɔːrt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 短的，不长的。例如: "The dress is too short for her."
- **名词**: 短裤，短裙，或其他长度较短的衣物。例如: "He wore a pair of denim shorts on a hot day."
- **动词**: 缩短，使变短。例如: "Can you shorten this story for me?"
- **副词**: 简短地，短暂地。例如: "He answered the question short and to the point."

## 短语
- "at short notice" 表示短时间内，突然。例如: "She had to leave at short notice due to an emergency."
- "come up short" 意味未能达到期望或目标。例如: "Their efforts came up short in the final match."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The summer days are short in this part of the world."
- 名词: "She bought a new red short for her vacation."
- 动词: "I need to short this piece of fabric to fit the frame."
- 副词: "He replied to my question short and didn't elaborate further."
