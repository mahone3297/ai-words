## 读音
/ˈkɑːndɛns/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. **压缩；凝缩**：使（物质）体积减小而密度增大，如通过冷却或去除水分。例如: "The gas was condensed into a liquid by cooling it."
2. **简化；精炼**：把（文字、思想等）压缩成更简洁的形式。例如: "The author condensed the novel into a screenplay."

## 短语
- "condense information" 意味着将信息压缩或精简。
- "to condense a long text" 是指将长文本缩短或提炼。

## 例句
- "The water vapor condensed on the cold window pane."
- "The scientist worked to condense the complex theory into an understandable explanation."
- "The editor had to condense the article to fit the newspaper's space limitations."
