## Pronunciation
/portərəbl/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Portable**: An object or device that can be easily moved from one place to another without losing its functionality. For example: "A laptop is a highly portable computer."
- **Portable generator**: A compact, self-contained power source that can be transported and used on the go.

### Verb
- To make something portable: The manufacturer designed the product to be easily portable. 
- To move or carry something portably: She carried her backpack portably across the park.

### Adjective
- Referring to the quality or ability of something to be moved or carried conveniently: The camping stove is a highly portable option.

## Phrases
- "Portability is key" emphasizes the importance of being able to move something easily.
- "A portable charger allows you to stay connected on the go."
- "The portable fridge keeps food and drinks cool during outdoor activities."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The new folding table is very portable and easy to store."
- **Verb**: "I packed my portable keyboard so I could work remotely during the trip."
- **Adjective**: "The company's new smartphone has excellent portability, with a lightweight design."
