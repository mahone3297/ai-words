## 读音
/ˈpɔːrtəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 可以轻易携带或移动的，便于运输的。例如: "The laptop is portable and can be used anywhere."
- **名词**: 可携带的设备或物品。例如: "A portable charger for your phone."

## 短语
- "Portable computer" 指的是笔记本电脑或可携带式电脑。
- "Portable camping stove" 是便携式的露营炉灶。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new speaker is so portable; it fits easily in my backpack."
- 名词: "I bought a portable air conditioner for my summer apartment."
