## 读音
/swɔrm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一大群密集移动的昆虫，如蜜蜂或蚂蚁。例如: "A swarm of bees flew over the garden."
- **动词**: 大量聚集或蜂拥而至。例如: "The fans swarmed around the stadium entrance."

## 短语
- "a swarm of insects" 指大量昆虫群体。
- "to swarm with" 表示某地充满了某物。例如: "The trees were swarming with butterflies."

## 例句
- 名词: "The farmer noticed a swarm of bees settling on a nearby tree."
- 动词: "People swarmed the mall on Black Friday for holiday shopping."
