## 读音
/rɪˈtɜːrn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 回去；返回。例如: "After a long trip, he finally returned home."
  2. 归还；交还。例如: "Please return the book when you finish reading it."
  3. （使）回到原处。例如: "The ball was hit back over the net, returning the play."
  4. 报告；回应。例如: "She didn't answer, but her silence returned a message."
  5. 获得回报；产生利润。例如: "This investment should return a good profit."

- **名词**:
  1. 返回；回归。例如: "He made his triumphant return to the stage."
  2. 收益；回报。例如: "The company saw a significant return on their investment."
  3. （税务等的）申报表。例如: "Don't forget to file your tax return by April 15th."

## 短语
- "return to" 表示回到某个地方或状态。例如: "She plans to return to her hometown after graduation."
- "return something to someone" 归还某物给某人。例如: "I'll return your keys as soon as I find them."

## 例句
- 动词: "He decided to return to college after a break of several years."
- 名词: "The return on her stocks was higher than expected."
