## 读音
/lɪˈsɛn/

## 释义
- **动词**: 倾听，聆听。例如: "Listen carefully to what the teacher says."
- **名词**: 听，听力。例如: "Her ability to listen is remarkable."

## 短语
- "Listen up" 意为集中注意力听，常用于引起大家注意。
- "Listen to music" 是指听音乐。

## 例句
- 动词: "Close your eyes and just listen to the sound of the ocean."
- 名词: "The child needs to improve their listen skills."

- Pay attention
- Give ear
