## Word: Excite

## Pronunciation
/ɪkˈsaɪt/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To stimulate or stir up (a person or their emotions, interest, or enthusiasm). For example: "The upcoming trip excited the children."
  - _To incite to action_: "The news excited the crowd to protest."
- **Noun**: The state of being excited; excitement. For example: "There's a lot of excite around the new product launch."

### Phrases
- "Excite someone about something": To inspire enthusiasm or interest in someone regarding a particular subject or event. Example: "She was able to excite the team about the project goals."
- "Get excited": To experience a feeling of anticipation or eagerness. Example: "I'm really getting excited about my vacation."
- "More than excited": Expressing strong enthusiasm or anticipation. Example: "I'm more than excited to meet the author of my favorite book."
