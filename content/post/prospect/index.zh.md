## 读音
/prəˈspekt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 预期；展望；前景。例如: "The company's financial prospects look promising."
- **动词**: 探查；勘探（矿产等）。例如: "Geologists are prospecting for oil in the new region."
- **形容词**: 有希望的；有望的。例如: "She has a good prospect of getting the job."

## 短语
- "Prospects for the future" 指未来的发展可能性或期望。
- "Prospect mining" 指矿产资源的勘探活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The graduates are eager to learn about their career prospects after college."
- 动词: "They prospect the area to find potential gold deposits."
- 形容词: "With his skills, he is a prospective candidate for the position."
