## 读音
/traɪb/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个传统上由共同祖先、文化和语言联系起来的社会群体，通常有特定的领土和组织结构。例如: "The Masai tribe is known for its vibrant culture."
- **名词**: 在某些组织或社会结构中，具有相似兴趣、目标或背景的团体。例如: "The tech tribe at the company shares a passion for innovation."

## 短语
- "Tribe leader" 指部落的首领或领导人。
- "Tribal society" 指部落社会，以部落为基本单位的社会结构。

## 例句
- "The Amazonian tribe has preserved their ancient customs for centuries."
- "In the startup scene, people often join tribes based on their specific interests or industries."

## 同义词
- Clan
- Community
- Sect
- Group
- Nation (in the context of indigenous peoples)
