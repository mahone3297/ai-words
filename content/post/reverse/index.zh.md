## 读音
/rɪˈvɜːrs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 反面，背面；倒置，颠倒。例如: "Please print on the reverse side of the paper."
- **动词**: 使颠倒，反转，倒转。例如: "He reversed the car and drove back to the house."
- **形容词**: 倒转的，相反的。例如: "The reverse view of the painting showed a different scene."

## 短语
- "reverse gear" 指汽车或其他机器的倒档。
- "on the reverse" 表示在事物的另一面或相反的状态。
- "reverse the process" 意味着把过程反过来或做相反的动作。

## 例句
- 名词: "The label was printed on the reverse of the envelope."
- 动词: "He reversed his decision after hearing public opinion."
- 形容词: "The effect of the medicine is the reverse of what we expected."
