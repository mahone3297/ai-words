## Pronunciation
/rɪst/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A bone located on the lower part of the forearm, connecting the hand to the forearm bones. ["wrist n."](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/wrist)
- **Verb**: To move or rotate the wrist, typically as part of a gesture or to manipulate something. ["wrist v."](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/wrist)
- **Adjective**: Relating to or situated on the wrist. ["wrist adj."](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/wrist)

## Phrases
- "Wrist injury" refers to damage to the wrist area.
- "Wrist strength" measures the ability to exert force with the wrist.
- "Wristwatch" is a timepiece worn on the wrist.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She sprained her wrist while playing tennis."
- **Verb**: "He rotated his wrist to relieve the pain."
- **Adjective**: "The bracelet was delicate and adorned her slender wrist."

## Synonyms

- Carpal tunnel (as a location)
- Hand movement (in a general sense)
- Wrist-flexible (for describing dexterity)

## Antonyms
