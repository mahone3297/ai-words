## 读音
/ˈriːdər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 读者，阅读者。指阅读书籍、文章或任何文字材料的人。例如: "The book has a large number of readers worldwide."
- **名词**: 阅读器，读卡器。电子设备，用于读取数据，如PDF阅读器或条形码读卡器。例如: "You need a QR code reader to access the information."
- **动词 (v)**: 阅读，朗读。指看并理解文字内容。例如: "She reads aloud from her favorite novel every evening."

## 短语
- "digital reader" 电子阅读器，如Kindle或iPad等设备。
- "speed reader" 快速阅读者，能快速阅读和理解大量文字的人。
- "audio reader" 有声读物，通过听的方式阅读。

## 例句
- 名词: "The magazine's target audience is young adult readers."
- 名词: "The new e-reader has a high-resolution display for comfortable reading."
- 动词: "She reads the newspaper every morning over breakfast."
