## 读音
/prəˈpərli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 正确地，恰当地，适当地。例如: "You should use this tool properly to avoid injury."
- **形容词**: 适当的，合适的。例如: "She was dressed properly for the formal event."

## 短语
- "Do something properly" 意味着做某事要做得正确无误。
- "Properly speaking" 用来强调在严格意义上或正确的说法是怎样的。

## 例句
- 副词: "To fix the issue, you must follow the instructions properly."
- 形容词: "This is the proper time to make an important decision."

- Correctly
- Accurately
- Appropriately
- Suitably
- Rightly
