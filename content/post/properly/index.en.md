## Pronunciation
/proʊˈprɪpərli/

## Definitions
### Adverb
- **1.** In a correct or suitable manner; appropriately. Example: "She dressed properly for the formal event."
- **2.** With due regard to rules or requirements. Example: "He completed the task properly after reading the instructions."

## Phrases
- "Properly functioning" means working correctly.
- "Properly trained" refers to someone who has received adequate education or instruction.
- "To do something properly" implies doing it with care and accuracy.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "He always ties his shoes properly before leaving the house."
- "The children should learn to clean their rooms properly."
- "The car was fixed properly by the mechanic."
