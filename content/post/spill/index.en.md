## Pronunciation
/spɪl/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (n) To cause liquid, semiliquid, or powder to flow or leak out of a container or from a broken container; pour. For example: "The milk spilled all over the counter."
- **Noun**: An incident where something is accidentally released or scattered; a mishap. For example: "The car accident was a major spill of oil on the highway."

## Phrases
- "Spill something" means to accidentally release a substance.
- "Clean up a spill" refers to the process of removing a mess caused by a spill.
- "A spill of secrets" is when confidential information is unintentionally revealed.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "I accidentally spilled coffee on my shirt this morning."
- **Noun**: "The company had to hire cleanup crews after a massive chemical spill."
- "After the spill, the environment took years to recover."
