## 读音
/trʌbl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 麻烦，问题，困扰。例如: "I'm having some trouble with my computer."
- **动词**: 使烦恼，困扰。例如: "Don't trouble yourself with these small details."
- **形容词**: 有麻烦的，困难的。例如: "We're in deep trouble now."

## 短语
- "In trouble" 表示处于困境或遇到问题。例如: "He's in trouble with the law."
- "To be no trouble at all" 意为一点也不麻烦。例如: "It was no trouble at all to help you."

## 例句
- 名词: "Can you help me solve this trouble?"
- 动词: "His constant interruptions troubled her concentration."
- 形容词: "The company is facing serious financial troubles."
