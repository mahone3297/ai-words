## Pronunciation
/tæblɪt/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A device with a flat touch-sensitive screen, typically used to browse the internet, read e-books, or take notes.** Example: "She uses her tablet for work, browsing, and watching videos."
2. **A thin, flat piece of stone, wood, or metal, usually marked with writing or symbols, that has been used as a medium for recording information throughout history.** Example: "The ancient tablets found in the dig site held valuable historical records."

### Verb
1. **To interact with a tablet by touching its screen or using input methods.** Example: "He quickly tapped on the tablet to open the app."
2. **To use a digital tablet as a drawing or painting tool.** Example: "She enjoys creating art on her electronic tablet."

## Phrases
- "Using a tablet" refers to the act of operating a tablet device.
- "Tablet computer" is the full name for a portable device with a touch screen.
- "Digital tablet" designates the electronic version for drawing or note-taking.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children were engrossed in their tablets, reading stories and playing games."
- **Verb**: "She drew a portrait on her tablet, amazed by the precision of the digital stylus."
- **Interjection**: "Excuse me, could you pass me my tablet, please?"

- Touchscreen device
