## 读音
/məˈrɪ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 快乐的，愉快的，充满欢乐的。例如: "They had a merry Christmas celebration."
- **名词**: （尤指圣诞时的）欢庆，快乐的气氛。例如: "The sound of merry carols filled the air."

## 短语
- "Merry-go-round" 指旋转木马，一种游乐设施。
- "Have a merry time" 意为祝你玩得开心，过得愉快。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The children were in high spirits, their merry laughter echoing through the house."
- 名词: "The town was decorated with lights and wreaths, creating a festive and merry atmosphere."
