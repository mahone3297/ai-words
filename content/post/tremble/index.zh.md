## 读音
/trembl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 发抖；颤抖。例如: "Her hands trembled with fear."
- **名词**: 颤抖；战栗。例如: "There was a slight tremble in his voice."

## 短语
- "tremble with fear" 意为因害怕而颤抖。
- "tremble at the thought of" 意为一想到就发抖。

## 例句
- 动词: "The old man's knees trembled as he climbed the stairs."
- 名词: "The ground trembled slightly during the earthquake."
