## 读音
/ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一次短途旅行或游览，尤指作为休闲或娱乐活动。例如: "We had an exciting excursion to the mountains."
- **动词**: 进行短途旅行或游览。例如: "They decided to excursion to the nearby castle."

## 短语
- "Day excursion" 指一天内的短途旅行。
- "Group excursion" 指集体组织的旅行活动。
- "Guided excursion" 指有导游带领的游览。

## 例句
- 名词: "The school is organizing a weekend excursion to the zoo for the students."
- 动词: "They regularly excursion to different museums to learn about art and history."
