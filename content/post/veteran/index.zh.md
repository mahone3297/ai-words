- **名词**: 有丰富经验的人，老手；老兵。例如: "The veteran soldier shared his war stories with the young recruits."
- **形容词**: 经验丰富的，资深的。例如: "The veteran journalist knew how to handle a difficult interview."

## 短语
- "veteran of" 表示在某个领域有丰富经验的人。例如: "She is a veteran of the fashion industry."
- "veteran's affairs" 指的是与退伍军人有关的事务或机构。例如: "The Department of Veterans' Affairs provides support for former military personnel."
