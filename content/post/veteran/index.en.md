## Pronunciation
/vərˈtiːən/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **veteran** is a person who has gained extensive experience or long service, especially in a particular field, military, or profession. It can also refer to a former member of a military force.

### Verb
To become or be a veteran (of something) means to gain significant experience or practice in a particular activity or field.

## Phrases
- "Veteran status" refers to the recognition of one's long-term involvement or membership.
- "A veteran of two wars" signifies someone with combat experience in multiple conflicts.
- "Joining the ranks of veterans" implies gaining the same level of experience as those already established.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The veteran teacher had seen it all before and knew how to handle the situation."
- **Verb**: "After 20 years in the industry, he became a veteran graphic designer."
- **Phrasal**: "She was proud to have achieved veteran status in her field."
