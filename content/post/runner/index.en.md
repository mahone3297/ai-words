## Pronunciation
/ˈrʌnər/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A person who runs, especially regularly or competitively: *The marathon runner trained for months to prepare.*
2. A component of a machine, such as a car, that converts energy into motion: *The car's engine has a powerful runner.*
3. A person involved in delivering or distributing something, especially mail: *The postman is a runner for the local newspaper.*

### Verb
1. To run, move quickly on foot: *He likes to go for a morning runner around the park.*
2. To act as a messenger or intermediary: *She asked her friend to be a runner and pick up the package.*

## Phrases
- "Go for a running loop" means to go for a run around a specific route.
- "He's a regular runner" refers to someone who runs frequently.
- "Running errands" means completing small tasks or chores while out and about.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: *The seasoned runner completed the half-marathon in record time.*
