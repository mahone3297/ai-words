## 读音
/ˈrʌnər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 参加跑步比赛的人。例如: "The runner crossed the finish line in first place."
  2. 跑腿的人，传递者。例如: "The restaurant employs runners to deliver food to tables."
  3. （长形的）垫子，布条，如地毯的边饰。例如: "She placed a red runner on the hallway floor."

- **动词**:
  在跑步或赛跑中移动。例如: "He was running along the beach in the morning."

## 短语
- "track runner" 指参加田径比赛的运动员。
- "road runner" 特指在公路上跑步的人，或者《猫和老鼠》中的卡通角色。
- "sprint runner" 指短跑运动员。

## 例句
- 名词: "The experienced runner completed the marathon in under three hours."
- 动词: "She started running to keep fit and now runs marathons regularly."

- Jogger
- Sprinter
