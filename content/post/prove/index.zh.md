## 读音
/pruːv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    - 证明；证实：确保某事真实或正确。例如: "The evidence proves his innocence."
    - 试验；检测：测试某事物的品质或性能。例如: "The new product needs to be proved before it goes on sale."
    - 使显现；展示：展现某人的能力或品质。例如: "Her hard work proved her dedication to the job."

## 短语
- "prove one's point" 证明自己的观点或论据。
- "prove something beyond doubt" 完全无疑地证明某事。
- "prove a theory" 验证一个理论。

## 例句
- 做动词时： "The math homework requires you to prove several theorems."
- "The experiment proved that the new medicine was effective."
