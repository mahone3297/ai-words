## 读音
/inˈkluːd/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    - 包含；包括。例如: "The package includes a manual and software."（该包裹包含一本手册和软件。）
    - 归入；把…算在内。例如: "Everyone in the team should be included in the decision-making process."（团队中的每个人都应参与决策过程。）

## 短语
- "include sth. in sth." 意为将某物包含在某物之中。例如: "We need to include the budget for this project in our report."（我们需要在报告中包括这个项目的预算。）
- "include sb./sth." 意为将某人或某物算进去。例如: "Please make sure to include me in the email distribution list."（请确保把我加入邮件分发列表。）

## 例句
- "The contract includes a non-disclosure clause."（合同包含保密条款。）
- "Don't forget to include your name and address on the application form."（别忘了在申请表上写上你的姓名和地址。）
