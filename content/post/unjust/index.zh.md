## 读音
/ʌnˈdʒʌst/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不公正的，不公平的。例如: "The decision was utterly unjust to the defendant."
- **副词**: 不公正地，不公平地。例如: "He was unjustly accused of the crime."

## 短语
- "Unjust treatment" 意味着不公正的对待。
- "Unjust laws" 指那些不平等或不合理的法律。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The company's policy of favoring certain employees is highly unjust."
- 副词: "She felt unjustly dismissed from her job without any valid reason."

## 同义词
- Unfair
- Inequitable
- Discriminatory
- Partial
- Biased
