## Pronunciation
/unˈdʒuːst/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by unfairness or lack of fairness; not treating someone fairly or according to accepted standards. For example: "The unjust treatment of the workers led to protests."
- **Noun**: An act or instance of being unjust; something unfair or discriminatory. For example: "The court ruled in favor of the plaintiff in this unjust case."

## Phrases
- "Act unjustly" means to treat someone unfairly.
- "An unjust accusation" refers to a false claim.
- "Seeking justice" is the pursuit of fairness and redress.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The company faced public backlash over its unjust wage policies."
- **Noun**: "The unjust verdict shocked the community."
- **Phrase**: "After years of injustice, the victims finally received reparations."
