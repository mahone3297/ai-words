## 读音
/sərˈli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 确实，无疑；肯定地。例如: "Surely he will pass the exam with all his hard work."
- **连词**: 当然，无疑地，用于引出一个显然的事实或推断。例如: "Surely you know that smoking is harmful to your health."

## 短语
- "Surely not" 表示不相信或惊讶，意思是“不会吧”或“不至于吧”。

## 例句
- 副词: "Surely, this is the best solution to the problem."
- 连词: "Surely you realize that time is running out."

## 同义词
- Certainly
- Definitely
- Absolutely
- Indubitably
- Beyond doubt
