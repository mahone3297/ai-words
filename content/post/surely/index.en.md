## Pronunciation
/sɜːˈʃʊəli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Expressing certainty or confidence in a statement or prediction. For example: "I'm sure he'll arrive on time."
- **Adjective**: Certain; without doubt; confidently. For example: "Surely, you remember this from our discussion earlier."

## Phrases
- "Surely you know" implies that something is obviously true.
- "There's no doubt that" emphasizes certainty about a fact.
- "Beyond a shadow of a doubt" indicates complete certainty.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "Don't worry, surely everything will work out."
- **Adjective**: "Surely, the answer must be correct."
- **Interrogative**: "Surely, you can't be serious?"
