## 读音
/ˈnɛrəweɪ/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示在任何地方都不，无处。例如: "There is nowhere to sit in this crowded room."
- **名词**: 表示不存在的地方或无法到达的地方。例如: "The secret location is said to be nowhere to be found."

## 短语
- "Nowhere else" 表示没有其他地方。例如: "There's nowhere else I'd rather be than here."
- "Nowhere man" 指没有固定职业或归属的人。例如: "He was a nowhere man, living a life without purpose."

## 例句
- 副词: "We drove around for hours but found nowhere to park."
- 名词: "She felt like a stranger in a land that seemed like nowhere."
