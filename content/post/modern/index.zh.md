## 读音
/ˈmɒdərn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 代表现代的，新式的，与当前时代同步的。例如: "The modern architecture reflects innovative design."
- **名词**: 用于指代现代时期或现代人。例如: "In the modern era, technology has advanced rapidly."

## 短语
- "Modern art" 指20世纪初以来的各种先锋艺术形式。
- "Modern technology" 涵盖了当代的高科技设备和发明。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This furniture is sleek and modern, perfect for a contemporary home."
- 名词: "Modern artists have challenged traditional art forms."
