## 读音
/inˈklaɪn/

## 释义
- **动词**: 倾斜；偏向。例如: "The hill inclined gently towards the valley."
- **名词**: 倾斜度；倾向。例如: "The incline of the slope is quite steep."
- **形容词**: 倾斜的；有斜度的。例如: "The incline bench helps target specific muscle groups."

## 短语
- "to incline towards something" 意为倾向于某事。
- "an incline plane" 是物理学中的一个概念，指的是带有斜坡的平面。

## 例句
- 动词: "The ladder inclined at a sharp angle against the wall."
- 名词: "The road has a significant incline in this section."
- 形容词: "The chair has an adjustable incline for added comfort."
