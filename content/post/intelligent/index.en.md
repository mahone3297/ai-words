## Word: Intelligent

## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈtelɪdʒənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having or showing the ability to easily learn or understand things or to deal with new or difficult situations. It also describes someone or something that has a high level of mental capacity, reasoning power, or comprehension.

### Example Sentences
- **Adjective (describing a person)**: "She's an intelligent woman who can solve complex problems quickly."
- **Adjective (describing an animal or object)**: "Dolphins are highly intelligent creatures known for their problem-solving skills."

## Phrases
- "Intelligent design": A concept suggesting that certain features of the universe and living things are best explained by an intelligent cause rather than undirected processes such as natural selection.
- "Artificial intelligence": The simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems, especially learning and decision-making.
