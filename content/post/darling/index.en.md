## Pronunciation
/dɑːrliŋ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A term of endearment or affection, often used to address a loved one. "She called him her darling son." 2. A pet name, especially for someone dear or special. "He was her constant darling."
- **Verb**: To refer to someone with great affection or fondness. "She always calls her partner her darling."
- **Adjective**: 1. Adored or beloved; cherished. "He is the apple of her eye, her little darling." 2. Amusingly or playfully charming. "The baby's antics were just darling."

## Phrases
- "Call someone darling" to express affection or admiration.
- "Darling this" or "darling that" to show tenderness or fondness when addressing a task or object.
- "Be a darling" can be a request for cooperation or politeness.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "He was so surprised when his wife greeted him as her darling."
- **Verb**: "She always tells her friends to be darlings and take care."
- **Adjective**: "The little girl's dress was just darling for the wedding."
