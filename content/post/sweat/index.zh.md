## 读音
/sweɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 人体分泌的液体，通常是由于热、紧张或运动产生的。例如: "I was dripping with sweat after the workout."
- **动词**: 出汗，努力工作或焦虑。例如: "He sweated over his exam preparation for weeks."
- **形容词**: 与出汗或辛勤工作相关的。例如: "The athlete was wearing a sweat-soaked T-shirt."

## 短语
- "Break a sweat" 意味着做大量运动或工作到出汗的程度。
- "Sweat it out" 表示忍受痛苦或焦虑，直到事情解决。
- "Sweat equity" 指通过辛勤工作和努力投入而获得的价值或回报。

## 例句
- 名词: "She wiped the sweat from her forehead during the hot summer day."
- 动词: "After hours of gardening, he was sweating profusely."
- 形容词: "The athlete put on his sweatpants and headed for the gym."
