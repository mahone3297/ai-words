## Pronunciation
/sʌnˈlaɪt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: **1**. The light and heat from the sun, considered as a natural resource. "The warmth of sunlight on her face." **2**. (pl.) Daylight, excluding artificial light. "She prefers to work during sunlight hours."
- **Verb**: To be exposed to or receive sunlight. "The plants need sunlight to grow."

## Phrases
- "Bask in the sunlight" means to enjoy the warmth and rays of the sun.
- "Sunlight therapy" refers to exposure to natural light for health benefits.
- "Follow the sunlight" is a metaphor for moving in the direction of light, symbolizing optimism or progress.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She opened the curtains to let in the bright sunlight."
- **Verb**: "After a long winter, he relished the first few days of sunlight."
- **Adjective**: "The garden was filled with the vibrant colors of flowers basking in the golden sunlight."
