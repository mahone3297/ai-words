## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Used to indicate the sequence of events or to show the relationship between actions. It can also be used to emphasize a point or suggest a pause.
- **Interjection**: Expressing a sense of time (then), agreement, or emphasis. Can be used to introduce a statement or express a reaction.

## Phrases
- "Then came the rain" indicates a chronological sequence.
- "I'll do it then" signifies intention after some hesitation or delay.
- "If you're done with that, then let's move on" suggests a logical progression.

## Example Sentences

- **Interjection**: "Yes, I understand. Then, how about we discuss the next steps?"
