## 读音
/ðen/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示时间顺序，意为“然后，接着”。例如: "I woke up, brushed my teeth, and then had breakfast."
- **连词**: 引导结果或推断，意为“因此，所以”。例如: "It was raining heavily, then we decided to stay at home."

## 短语
- "And then some" 表示“还有一些，甚至更多”。例如: "I asked for a cup of coffee, but they brought me a whole pot, and then some."

## 例句
- 副词: "First, we'll discuss the plan, and then we'll start working on it."
- 连词: "She was tired from the journey, then she fell asleep quickly."
