## Pronunciation
/ˈspæسkaft/

## Definitions
### Noun
A spacecraft is a vehicle designed to travel through outer space, typically used for exploration, research, or transportation. It can be either manned or unmanned and may include rockets, shuttles, satellites, or other specialized vehicles.

### Verb
To launch or operate a spacecraft, usually in the context of sending it on a mission or trip through space.

### Adjective
Referring to a vehicle or technology related to space travel.
