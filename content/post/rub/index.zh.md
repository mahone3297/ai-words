## 读音
/rʌb/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    - 摩擦: 用物体的表面接触并移动以产生摩擦力。例如: "He rubbed his hands together to warm them up."
    - 揉搓: 在身体的某个部位施加压力并来回移动。例如: "She rubbed her temples to ease the headache."
    - 擦拭: 用布或其他工具清洁或擦拭物体。例如: "I rubbed the table with a damp cloth to remove the dirt."

## 短语
- "Rub against something" 意味着与某物摩擦。例如: "The cat rubbed against my leg, seeking attention."
- "Rub it in" 表示过分强调或让人感到不舒服的事情。例如: "Don't rub it in that you passed the exam and I didn't."

## 例句
- 动词: "She rubbed the cream gently onto her skin."
- 揉搓: "After a long day, he rubbed his sore muscles."
