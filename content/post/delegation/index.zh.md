## 读音
/ˌdɛlɪˈɡeɪʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 代表团；授权；委派。例如: "The company sent a delegation to negotiate with the union." 或 "He was given the responsibility of leading the delegation."
- **动词 (v)**: 委派；派遣。例如: "The president will delegate some of his duties to his assistants."

## 短语
- "Delegation of authority" 意味着将权力下放给他人。
- "A trade delegation" 指的是进行商务访问的团队。

## 例句
- 名词: "The diplomatic delegation arrived at the embassy for an official meeting."
- 动词: "The manager decided to delegate the task to the most experienced team member."
