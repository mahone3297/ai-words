## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Of high quality; excellent; impressive. For example: "The view from the top was absolutely splendid."
- **Verb**: To make (something) appear splendid or attractive. For example: "The decorator worked wonders, making the old house look splendid."

## Phrases
- "In splendid isolation" describes something that is isolated but still impressive.
- "A splendid occasion" refers to a special and enjoyable event.
- "Splendid job" is used to praise someone for a well-done task.
