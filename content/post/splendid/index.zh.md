## 读音
/ˈsplɛndɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 极好的，壮丽的，灿烂的。例如: "The view from the mountaintop was absolutely splendid."
- **副词**: 极其，非常。例如: "She performed splendidly in the dance competition."

## 短语
- "in all its/someone's splendor" 表示某事物或某人以其全部的辉煌或光彩。
- "a splendid achievement" 指一项杰出的成就。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The palace was decorated in a splendid manner, with golden chandeliers and marble floors."
- 副词: "The orchestra played splendidly, capturing the audience's attention from the first note."

- magnificent
- glorious
