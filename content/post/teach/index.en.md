## Pronunciation
/tiːtʃ/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The act of imparting knowledge, skills, or values to someone, typically by instruction: *She's a skilled teacher who inspires her students.*
2. A person who teaches: *Mr. Johnson is our math teacher.*

### Verb
1. To give instruction or lessons in a particular subject: *She teaches French to high school students.*
2. To guide or mentor someone in a specific skill or field: *The mentor taught him valuable business lessons.*

### Adjective
Referring to a profession or activity involving teaching: *The online learning platform offers a variety of teacher resources.*

## Phrases
- "Teach a lesson" means to impart a moral or educational point through an experience.
- "Teach someone a new skill" refers to imparting a previously unknown ability.
- "Teaching methods" encompasses various approaches to instruction.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The retired teacher still volunteers at the local school."
- **Verb**: "She's teaching her younger sister how to play the piano."
- **Adjective**: "Her teaching style is both engaging and effective."
