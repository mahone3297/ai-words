## 读音
/tiːtʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 教授，教导。例如: "She teaches English at a high school."
- **名词**: 教课，教学过程。例如: "His teaching method is very effective."

## 短语
- "Teach someone a lesson" 意味着给某人一个教训，让他们学到东西。
- "Teach oneself" 表示自学。

## 例句
- 动词: "John teaches mathematics to primary school students."
- 名词: "The teacher's teaching style greatly influences student engagement."
