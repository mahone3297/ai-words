## 读音
/riːˈdʒaɪs/

## 释义
- **动词**: 欢乐，感到欢喜；庆祝。例如: "They rejoiced at the news of their friend's recovery."
- **名词**: 欢乐，喜悦。例如: "Their hearts were filled with rejoice."

## 短语
- "to rejoice in something" 意味着因某事而感到快乐或满足。
- "rejoice together" 指一起分享欢乐。

## 例句
- 动词: "The whole village rejoiced when the war finally ended."
- 名词: "Their faces radiated with rejoice after winning the championship."

- Be joyful
