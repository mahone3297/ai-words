## Pronunciation
/prəˈpəʊzl/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A suggestion or plan put forward for consideration or discussion: *The proposal was met with mixed reactions.*
2. An offer or request to do something: *He made a proposal to marry her.*
3. A formal statement or document outlining a course of action: *The company received multiple proposals for the new project.*

### Verb
1. To present or suggest an idea or plan: *They will propose a new budget next week.*
2. To make a marriage offer: *He proposed to his girlfriend during a romantic dinner.*

## Phrases
- "Submit a proposal" means to send or present a suggestion or plan.
- "Propose a solution" suggests a way to resolve a problem.
- "She accepted his proposal" means she agreed to marry him.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The board members reviewed several proposals for the new building."
- **Verb**: "He carefully prepared a proposal to expand the marketing team."
- **Verb (intransitive)**: "After months of dating, he finally proposed to her."
