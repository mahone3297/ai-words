## 读音
/ˈrɪlɪdʒɪən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 信仰体系，宗教；一套关于超自然或神圣存在的信念、教义、仪式和习俗。例如: "She practices Buddhism, which is her religion."
- **形容词**: 与宗教有关的，神学的。例如: "The religious leaders held a conference to discuss peace."

## 短语
- "Religious beliefs" 指个人或团体的宗教信仰。
- "Religious ceremony" 指宗教仪式或庆典。
- "Religious freedom" 指宗教自由，即信仰任何宗教的权利。

## 例句
- 名词: "People of various religions live peacefully in this diverse city."
- 形容词: "The book explores the religious implications of the story."
