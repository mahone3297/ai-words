## 读音
/strɔŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 强壮的，强健的。例如: "He has strong muscles."
- **副词**: 坚定地，强烈地。例如: "She spoke with a strong conviction."
- **名词**: (常与the连用)力量，强度。例如: "He lifted a weight with great strength."

## 短语
- "strong point" 指某人的长处或优点。
- "strongly believe" 表示坚信不疑。
- "a strong bond" 指牢固的联系或深厚的感情。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She is a strong swimmer and can easily swim across the river."
- 副词: "He feels strongly about the importance of education."
- 名词: "The coffee has a strong flavor."

- Sturdy
