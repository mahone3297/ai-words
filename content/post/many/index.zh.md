## 读音
/ˈmeni/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 许多，大量的，众多的。表示数量大但不精确。例如: "There were many people at the concert."
- **代词**: 许多（事物或人）。例如: "Many thanks for your help."

## 短语
- "Many times" 表示多次，经常。例如: "I've visited this city many times."
- "Many of them" 指的是许多人或事物中的许多。例如: "Many of the students have already finished the assignment."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The library has many books on various topics."
- 代词: "Many people prefer coffee over tea."
