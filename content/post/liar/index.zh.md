## 读音
/ˈlaɪər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 说谎者；骗子。例如: "He proved to be a liar, and nobody believed him anymore."
- **形容词**: 与说谎相关的；不真实的。例如: "That's a liar's tale; it can't be trusted."

## 短语
- "Call someone a liar" 指指控某人在说谎。
- "Liar, liar, pants on fire" 是一种儿童用来指责别人说谎的俏皮话。

## 例句
- 名词: "The liar's story kept changing every time he was confronted with evidence."
- 形容词: "The whole report turned out to be a pack of liar claims."

## 同义词
- Deceiver
- Fabricator
- Prevaricator
- Dishonest person
- Storyteller (in a negative context)
