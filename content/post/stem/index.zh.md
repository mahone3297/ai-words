## 读音
/stɛm/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - （植物的）茎，主干：The stem of the plant holds up the leaves.
  - （花朵的）花梗：The stem of the rose was long and strong.
  - （器物的）柄，把：The stem of the glass is thin and delicate.
  - （动词的）词干，基本形式：In English, many verbs have irregular stems, like "run" and "ran".

- **动词**:
  - 从中心部分伸出或长出：The branches stem from the main trunk of the tree.
  - 源自，起源于：Her interest in music stems from her childhood.

## 短语
- "stem from" 意为源自，由...产生：Our problems seem to stem from poor communication.
- "stem the tide" 意为阻止潮流，制止：We need to work together to stem the spread of misinformation.

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist painted the intricate details of the flower's stem."
- 动词: "His love for nature stems from his childhood spent in the countryside."

## 同义词
- Main part
- Trunk
- Source
- Origin
