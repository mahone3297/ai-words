## Word: Marine

## Pronunciation
/ˈmɛəriːn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A member of a country's naval forces who specializes in combat at sea and amphibious operations on land. Example: "He joined the Marines after graduating from college."
- **Adjective**: Relating to the sea or ocean; maritime. Example: "The marine ecosystem includes a vast array of species."
- **Noun (specific use)**: An organism that lives in the sea or ocean. Example: "Marine life can be found even in the deepest parts of the ocean."
- **Adjective**: Of or relating to military forces that operate in a maritime environment. Example: "The marine corps trains its soldiers for amphibious warfare."

## Phrases
- "In the marines": Refers to being a part of a country's marine corps or naval infantry force.
- "Marine biology": The scientific study of organisms living in the sea and their interactions with the environment.
- "Marine insurance": Insurance coverage for losses related to shipping at sea or goods transported by sea.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (military personnel)**: "The marine stood tall and proud in his dress uniform."
- **Adjective (relating to the sea)**: "The marine biologist studied the effects of pollution on underwater habitats."
- **Noun (sea organism)**: "The coral reef was teeming with diverse marine life."

## Synonyms
- Sailor (for military personnel)
- Naval (adjective for military forces)
- Seafaring (adjective for things related to the sea)
- Oceanic (adjective referring to the ocean) 
- Sea creature (noun for organisms living in the sea)
