## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A feeling or expression of contempt, disrespect, or derision towards someone or something. For example: "Her look of scorn showed her disapproval."
- **Verb**: To express scorn or contempt for; to look down upon. For example: "He scorned their efforts as futile."
- **Adjective**: Showing disdain or contempt. For example: "Her scornful comment stung his pride."

## Phrases
- "Scorned by others" indicates being rejected or ridiculed.
- "To hold in scorn" means to regard something with contempt or disrespect.
- "Scornful laughter" refers to derisive laughter.
