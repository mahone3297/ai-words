## 读音
/ˈstrʌɡ(ə)l/

## 释义
- **动词**: 努力，奋斗，抗争。例如: "She struggled to finish the marathon."
- **名词**: 努力，奋斗，搏斗。例如: "His daily struggle with illness inspired many."

## 短语
- "to struggle against" 表示与...作斗争。例如: "They are struggling against poverty."
- "a fierce struggle" 指激烈的斗争。例如: "There was a fierce struggle for power within the company."

## 例句
- 动词: "Despite the odds, he continued to struggle for his dreams."
- 名词: "The economic crisis has turned into a long and grueling struggle for many families."
