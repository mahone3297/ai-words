## 读音
/ˈlʌv/

## 释义
- **名词**: 深度的感情和喜爱，通常指浪漫或亲情的情感。例如: "They have been in love for years."
- **动词**: 喜爱，钟情于。例如: "She loves playing the piano."
- **形容词**: 表示充满爱意或深受喜爱的。例如: "The love letter expressed his deep feelings."

## 短语
- "fall in love" 意为陷入爱河，开始喜欢上某人。例如: "They fell in love during their summer vacation."
- "love affair" 指恋爱关系或风流韵事。例如: "Their love affair lasted for several months."
- "unconditional love" 指无条件的爱，不带任何条件的关爱。例如: "A parent's love for their child is usually unconditional."

## 例句
- 名词: "Their love for each other is so strong that nothing can tear them apart."
- 动词: "She loves reading books more than anything else in the world."
- 形容词: "This dish is made with love, you can taste it in every bite."
