## 读音
/rɪˈzɜːrv/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 预留；预订：例如 "I've booked a table at the restaurant, so we have a reserve."
  - 储备；储存：例如 "The bank keeps large reserves of cash to handle withdrawals."
  - 保护区；保护区的动物：例如 "The wildlife reserve is home to endangered species."
  - （体育）替补队员：例如 "The reserve player stepped in when the starter got injured."

- **动词**:
  - 预定；预留：例如 "Please reserve a hotel room for two nights."
  - 保存；保留：例如 "We need to reserve some energy for the final sprint."
  - （在金融中）储备：例如 "Banks are required to reserve a certain percentage of deposits."

## 短语
- "reserve a seat" 预定座位
- "reserve funds" 储备金
- "reserve the right to do something" 保有做某事的权利

## 例句
- 名词: "The hotel has a policy of holding a certain number of rooms as reserves for regular guests."
- 动词: "Can you reserve a table for six people at the Italian restaurant?"
- 副词: "The team's star player was injured, so they called up a reserve from the minors."
