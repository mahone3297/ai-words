## 读音
/peɪl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 苍白的，面色无血的。例如: "She looked pale after her long journey."
- **形容词**: 缺乏活力的，暗淡的。例如: "The colors in the painting were pale compared to the original."
- **动词**: （使）变得苍白。例如: "She paled at the sight of the snake."

## 短语
- "pale in comparison" 意为相形见绌，相比之下显得逊色。
- "pale shadow" 比喻指远不如原物的事物。

## 例句
- 形容词: "His face was pale from anxiety."
- 动词: "The flowers paled in the hot sun."

- wan

- dull
