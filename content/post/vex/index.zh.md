## 读音
/vɛks/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    - 使烦恼；困扰：例如: "The constant noise vexes me."
    - 使焦虑；使忧心：例如: "The unresolved problem vexes her mind."

## 短语
- "vex with" 意为因某事而烦恼或困扰某人，如: "She was vexed with endless paperwork."
- "to vex about" 意为为某事担忧或烦恼，如: "He vexes about his future career."

## 例句
- "The difficult customer vexed the salesperson with endless complaints."
- "His indecisiveness vexes his friends, who want him to make up his mind."

## 同义词
- Irritate
- Annoy
- Troubled
- Aggravate
- Nuisance
