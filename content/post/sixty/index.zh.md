## 读音
/sɪˈxti/

## 释义
- **数词**: 表示数字60，六十。例如: "There are sixty minutes in an hour."

## 短语
- "sixty years old" 指的是60岁的年龄。
- "beyond sixty" 意为超过60岁或60以上。

## 例句
- "She turned sixty last month and is still working full-time."
- "The speed limit on this road is sixty miles per hour."

## 同义词
- sixty can also be expressed as "three score" in older or poetic language.
- 没有直接与"六十"同义的简短词汇，但可以表达相似概念的词有 "three dozens"（三打）或 "a score and ten"（二十加十）。在数字表达上，"sixty"本身没有特定的同义词。
