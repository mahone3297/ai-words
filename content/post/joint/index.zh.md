## 读音
/jɔɪnt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 连接两个或多个部分的地方；接头。例如: "The joint between the two pipes needs to be tightened."
- **名词**: 餐馆，尤其是快餐店或售卖街头食品的小店。例如: "We grabbed some burgers at the local joint."
- **名词**: （非法）毒品交易场所或吸毒场所。例如: "The police raided a notorious drug joint."
- **动词 (v)**: 结合，连接。例如: "The carpenter jointed the two pieces of wood together."
- **名词**: 人体中的关节。例如: "She felt a pain in her knee joint."

## 短语
- "Hip joint" 表示髋关节。
- "Shoulder joint" 指肩关节。
- "To join forces" 意为联合力量。

## 例句
- 名词: "The plumber fixed the leaky joint in the pipe."
- 动词: "The carpenter jointed the table legs to the tabletop using dowels."
- 名词（非正式）: "They opened a new pizza joint down the street."
