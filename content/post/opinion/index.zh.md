## 读音
/əˈpɪnjən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 某人的看法、观点、判断或意见。例如: "She expressed her opinion about the new policy."
- **名词**: 专家或权威人士的看法，通常影响公众的观点。例如: "The expert's opinion was highly regarded in the field."

## 短语
- "ask for someone's opinion" 请求某人的意见。
- "in my opinion" 在我看来，据我看来。
- "form an opinion" 形成一个观点。

## 例句
- 名词: "In his opinion, the book was well-written and engaging."
- 名词: "The public opinions on this issue vary widely."
