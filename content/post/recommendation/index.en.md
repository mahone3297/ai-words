## Pronunciation
/rɪˌkɑːmən'deɪʃ(ə)n/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A recommendation** is a suggestion or advice given by one person to another about a product, service, or course of action, often based on personal experience or expertise.
- **A referral** or **endorsement** may also be referred to as a recommendation.

### Verb
- To **recommend** is to suggest or advise someone to use, choose, or try something, often with confidence in its quality or suitability.
- To **endorse** is similar, but typically involves giving official approval or support.

### Adjective
- **Recommended** indicates that something has been suggested or approved by others.

## Phrases
- "I highly recommend this book."
- "His doctor recommended a change in diet."
- "The hotel was recommended by a friend."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She received several job recommendations from her colleagues."
- **Verb**: "The restaurant's signature dish is highly recommended by food critics."
- **Adjective**: "The hotel was conveniently located and well-recommended by travelers."

## Synonyms

## Antonyms
- Disapproval
- Criticism
- Neglect
- Recommendation against (opposite of endorsement)
