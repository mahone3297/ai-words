## 读音
/ˌrekəmˈmen.dəˈkeɪ.ʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个建议、提议或推荐。例如: "I need a recommendation for a good restaurant in the area."
- **动词**: 提供或做出推荐。例如: "She recommended the book to all her friends."

## 短语
- "Personal recommendation" 指的是个人的建议或背书。
- "Professional recommendation" 指的是在工作场合的推荐信或评价。
- "Product recommendation system" 是一种自动推荐用户可能感兴趣商品的系统。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher gave me a strong recommendation for the college application."
- 动词: "The travel agent recommended visiting the local museum."
