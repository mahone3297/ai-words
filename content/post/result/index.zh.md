## 读音
/rɪˈzʌlt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 结果，后果，成绩。例如: "The experiment produced positive results."
- **动词**: 导致，产生。例如: "Their actions will result in serious consequences."

## 短语
- "As a result" 意为因此，所以。
- "To yield a result" 意为得出结果。
- "Final result" 指最终的结果或结论。

## 例句
- 名词: "The medical test showed negative results."
- 动词: "The project resulted in significant cost savings for the company."
