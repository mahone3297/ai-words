## Pronunciation
/həˈloʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to greet or address someone; a friendly expression of recognition. For example: "She offered a friendly hello to the passerby."
- **Verb**: To express greeting or acknowledge someone's presence; to initiate communication. For example: "He greeted his colleagues with a hearty hello."
- **Interjection**: A concise expression of salutation or surprise; an exclamation of recognition. For example: "Oh, hello there! I didn't expect to see you."

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to initiate a friendly interaction.
- "Hello, my friend" is a familiar way to greet a pal.
- "Hello, old chap" is a British informal way to address a male acquaintance.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her smile was a warm hello to her old classmates."
- **Verb**: "He stopped by to hello his neighbor on his way home."
- **Interjection**: "Hello, dear! How's it going?"
