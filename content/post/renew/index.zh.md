## 读音
/riːˈnuː/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    1. 更新，使恢复：使恢复新鲜、活力或有效性。例如: "I need to renew my passport before traveling abroad."
    2. 继续，延长：继续一个已结束或即将结束的过程或状态。例如: "The contract has been renewed for another year."
    3. 修复，翻新：修理或改造以恢复原状。例如: "The old building was beautifully renewed with modern facilities."

## 短语
- "Renewable energy" 指可以持续利用且不会耗尽的能源，如太阳能或风能。
- "Renew one's subscription" 表示更新订阅，例如杂志或在线服务。

## 例句
- 动词（更新）: "I had to renew my library books because I wasn't finished reading them."
- 动词（继续）: "Their friendship renewed after many years apart."
- 动词（修复）: "The city is planning to renew the aging infrastructure over the next decade."
