## 读音
/ˈvɛrɪfaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 验证，确认，证实。例如: "The accountant verifies the financial statements before they're published."
  - 核实（身份、信息等）。例如: "You need to verify your email address to complete the registration process."

## 短语
- "verify information" 意为确认信息的准确性。
- "verify one's identity" 是确认某人的身份。

## 例句
- "The police asked me to verify my identity with my driver's license."
- "The scientist had to verify the results of the experiment multiple times to ensure their validity."
- "Before publishing the news, the journalist verified the source."
