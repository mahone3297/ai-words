## Pronunciation
/ˈlʌki/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having good fortune; receiving or resulting from good luck. For example: "She considers herself lucky to have such supportive friends."

  > Example Sentence: He's one of the lucky few who won the lottery.
  
- **Noun**: (informal) A person or thing that is fortunate or successful. For example: "In this game, the underdog turned out to be the lucky."

## Phrases
- "Feeling lucky": Experiencing a sense of optimism about the likelihood of success or positive outcomes.
- "As lucky as a four-leaf clover": Describing someone who experiences an extraordinary amount of good luck.
- "Lucky charm": An object believed to bring good luck to its possessor.

## Example Sentences

- **Noun**: "Her grandmother's necklace was her lucky charm for interviews."
