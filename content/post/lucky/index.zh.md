## 读音
/lʌkɪ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 幸运的；侥幸的。例如: "He was lucky to escape without injury."
- **名词**: 幸运；好运。例如: "She had all the luck on her side in the game."

## 短语
- "Lucky charm" 意为带来好运的吉祥物。
- "Lucky break" 指意外的好运或机会。
- "Feel lucky" 表示感觉幸运或有信心。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It was lucky that I had my umbrella when it started raining."
- 名词: "She attributed her success to a combination of skill and luck."
