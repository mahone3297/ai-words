## Definitions
- **Numeral**: A cardinal number indicating the quantity 30, used to count or indicate a group of thirty items. For example: "There are thirty students in the class."
- **Adjective**: Designating a quantity or position that is the third in a series of tens. For example: "Thirty percent of the population voted in the election."

## Phrases
- "Thirty years old" refers to the age of someone born 30 years ago.
- "Thirty days hath September" is a mnemonic to remember the number of days in each month with a specific pattern.
- "A score and ten" is an informal way to say approximately thirty.
