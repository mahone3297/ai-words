## Pronunciation
/shɪvər/

## Definitions
### Noun
A shiver is a **noun** referring to an involuntary muscular contraction, typically causing a sensation of cold or fear, often accompanied by a tremor.

### Verb
To **shiver** is a verb that means to experience or exhibit a tremulous, shaking motion, often due to cold, fear, or excitement.

## Phrases
- "Shiver with cold" describes the physical response to being chilled.
- "Shiver uncontrollably" signifies an involuntary shaking.
- "Shiver of anticipation" refers to a nervous excitement before something.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She hugged herself to try to stop the shivers on a cold night."
- **Verb**: "After watching the horror movie, he couldn't stop shivering for hours."
- **Adjective**: "The child's face was pale, and their lips were tinged with a slight shiver of fear."
