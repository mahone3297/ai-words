## 读音
/ˈʃɪvər/

## 释义
- **动词**: 发抖，颤抖。例如: "I was so cold that I couldn't stop shivering."
- **名词**: 颤抖，寒颤。例如: "A shiver ran down my spine when I heard the scary story."
- **形容词**: （指事物）令人发抖的，令人寒心的。例如: "The icy water gave me a shiver."

## 短语
- "to give sb. a shiver" 意为使某人感到恐惧或寒冷。
- "shivers down one's spine" 意味着强烈的情感反应，如恐惧或激动。

## 例句
- 动词: "The cold wind made her shiver uncontrollably."
- 名词: "He felt a shiver go through his body as he entered the dark room."
- 形容词: "The thought of spiders gives me a shiver."
