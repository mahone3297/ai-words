## Pronunciation
/ˈɔːltərənət/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A different possibility or choice, usually offered as an alternative to another. 2. An alternative form or version of something. 3. (music) A substitute or replacement for a main instrument or performer. 4. (medicine) A treatment or remedy that can be used instead of a primary one.
- **Verb**: To offer or choose as a substitute; select an alternative. 2. To be an option or choice.

## Phrases
- "Alternative solutions" refers to several options to solve a problem.
- "Take your pick" implies multiple alternatives to choose from.
- "Go for the alternative" suggests choosing a different course of action.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The restaurant offers a vegetarian alternative to their meat-based dishes."
- **Verb**: "He decided to try an alternative route to avoid the traffic."
- **As a verb**: "She's considering alternative career paths after finishing her degree."
