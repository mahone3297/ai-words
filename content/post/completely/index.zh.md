## 读音
/complɪˈtli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 彻底地，完全地，十足地。表示动作或状态达到了最大程度。例如: "He understood the problem completely." 或 "The house was completely destroyed by the fire."

## 短语
- "completely exhausted" 意为完全筋疲力尽。
- "completely satisfied" 表示非常满意。

## 例句
- "I have completely forgotten his name."
- "The task was completed completely and without any errors."
- "She was completely mesmerized by the performance."
