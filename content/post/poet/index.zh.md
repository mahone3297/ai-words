## 读音
/ˈpoʊɪt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 写诗的人，诗人。例如: "Shakespeare is a renowned poet."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与诗歌或诗人有关的。例如: "Her words were poetic and full of imagery."

## 短语
- "The poet laureate" 是官方的桂冠诗人，通常为国家或重要事件写诗。
- "A poet's pen" 指诗人的写作工具，象征其创作才华。

## 例句
- 名词: "Rumi was a 13th-century Persian poet whose works still inspire many today."
- 形容词: "The poet's language was so vivid it transported me to another world."

## 同义词
- Bard
- Lyricist
- Verse-maker
- Songwriter
- Poetess (特指女性诗人)
