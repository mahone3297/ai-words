## Pronunciation
/pəʊɪt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A poet is a person who writes poetry, often as their profession or as a form of artistic expression. They use language to create imaginative works that evoke emotions, convey ideas, or celebrate cultural themes.

### Verb
To poetize or poeticize (informal) means to express oneself in a poetic manner or to transform something into poetic language.

## Phrases
- "Compose a poem"
- "The poet laureate"
- "A sonnet by the poet"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She was a renowned poet, known for her poignant and thought-provoking verses."
- **Verb**: "After years of practice, he finally found his voice as a poet."
- **Poetic expression**: "The sunset painted the sky like a master poet's canvas."

## Synonyms
- Bard
- Versifier
- Lyricist
- Poetaster
- Quill-wielder

## Antonyms
- Prose writer
- Essayist
- Novelist
- Dramatist (mainly for playwrights)

## Notes
Poets may work with various forms such as sonnets, haikus, free verse, or ballads, and their works can range from romantic love songs to political commentary. The art of poetry has a rich history, spanning many cultures and time periods.
