## 读音
/həˈnəʊw/

## 释义
- **副词**: 以某种方式，不知何故，莫名其妙地。例如: "Somehow, we managed to finish the project on time."
- **连词**: 表示尽管不知道如何，但结果还是发生了。例如: "She was very nervous, but somehow she found the courage to speak."

## 短语
- "Somehow or other" 表示以某种未知或不明确的方式。

## 例句
- 副词: "Despite the difficulties, we got the job done somehow."
- 连词: "I don't know how she did it, but somehow she solved the problem."
