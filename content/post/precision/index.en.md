## Pronunciation
/pəˈriːʃn/

## Definitions
### Noun
**Precision** (noun): The quality or accuracy of being exact or free from error, especially in measurement or manufacturing. It can also refer to careful attention to detail or the ability to perform tasks with great accuracy.

**Example:** "The laser-guided missile demonstrated remarkable precision in hitting its target."

### Verb
**To be precise** (phrase): To give a clear, exact, or specific description or statement, often to avoid ambiguity.

**Example:** "In science, it's essential to be precise when reporting experimental results."

### Adjective
**Precision-oriented** (adjective): Characterized by a strong focus on accuracy and attention to detail.

**Example:** "This new manufacturing process is designed to be highly precision-oriented."

## Phrases
- "Achieving high precision" means reaching a level of accuracy that is exceptional.
- "Precision engineering" refers to the specialized design and manufacture of components with very tight tolerances.
- "A precision instrument" is a device that is extremely accurate.
