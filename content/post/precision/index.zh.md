## 读音
/prɪˈsiʒn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 精确度，准确度；细节的清晰度。例如: "The instrument has a high level of precision."
- **形容词**: 精确的，准确无误的。例如: "Her work is known for its precision and attention to detail."

## 短语
- "Precision engineering" 指的是精确工程，要求极高的精度和准确性。
- "Precision timing" 指精确的时间控制。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new laser technology allows for unprecedented precision in surgical procedures."
- 形容词: "The athlete's movements were precise and well-coordinated."
