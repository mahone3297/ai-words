## 读音
/yɛl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 大声叫喊；大声呼喊。例如: "The fans yelled when their favorite player scored."
- **名词**: 大声的叫喊或尖叫声。例如: "His yell echoed through the valley."

## 短语
- "Yell for help" 意为大声呼救。
- "A loud yell" 描述一次响亮的叫喊。

## 例句
- 动词: "The children were yelling with excitement in the playground."
- 名词: "The sudden yell startled everyone in the library."

- Scream
