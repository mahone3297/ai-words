## 读音
/ˈɪnənsənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 清白的，无罪的；天真的，幼稚的。例如: "The child's innocent smile melted everyone's heart."
- **名词**: 天真的人，无辜者。例如: "Innocent bystanders were caught in the crossfire."

## 短语
- "innocent until proven guilty" 在法律上指被指控者在证明有罪之前被认为是无罪的。
- "innocent-looking" 形容看似无害或不引起怀疑的外表。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The accused claimed to be innocent of any wrongdoing."
- 名词: "The judge showed mercy to the innocent victims of the war."
