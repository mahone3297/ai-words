## 读音
/həˈləʊ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 中空的，空洞的。例如: "The tree had a hollow trunk where birds nested."
- **名词**: 中心或表面有空洞的事物。例如: "The ground was soft and full of hollows."
- **动词**: (使)成为空洞或凹陷。例如: "The wind hollowed out the old tree."

## 短语
- "a hollow laugh" 表示讽刺或无诚意的笑声。
- "hollow cheeks" 指脸颊凹陷，可能是因为瘦弱或饥饿。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The old vase had a beautiful, intricate design on its hollow body."
- 名词: "The owl perched in a hollow of the ancient oak tree."
- 动词: "Years of neglect had hollowed out the once-thriving town."
