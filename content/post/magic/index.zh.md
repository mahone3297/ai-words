## 读音
/ˈmædʒɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 超自然的力量或现象，如巫术、咒语。例如: "She believed in the magic of spells and incantations."
    - 魔法，神秘的魅力。例如: "The magician performed a trick that seemed to defy the laws of physics, filling the audience with wonder and magic."
    - 惊奇，魅力。例如: "The city at night had a certain magic that captivated visitors."

- **形容词**:
    - 具有魔法般的，神奇的。例如: "The magic touch of the artist brought the painting to life."
    - 极好的，美妙的。例如: "It was a magic moment when they first met eyes."

## 短语
- "Magic wand" 指魔法棒，常与童话或魔术表演相关。
- "A touch of magic" 指带来惊喜或神奇元素的事物。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were spellbound by the wizard's magic tricks."
- 形容词: "Her voice had a magic quality that could soothe even the wildest animals."
