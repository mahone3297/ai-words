## 读音
/rеɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 愤怒，狂怒，激烈的情绪爆发。例如: "He was filled with rage after hearing the news."
- **动词**: （使）愤怒，（使）狂怒。例如: "The injustice enraged the whole community."
- **形容词**: 激烈的，狂暴的。例如: "A rage of storms hit the coast."

## 短语
- "In a rage" 表示非常愤怒的状态。
- "Rage against the machine" 是一个表达对体制或权威不满的短语，也可指同名摇滚乐队。

## 例句
- 名词: "She could feel the rage building inside her with each passing moment."
- 动词: "His words enraged the already agitated crowd."
- 形容词: "The athlete performed with a rage that stunned the opponents."
