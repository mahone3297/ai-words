## 读音
/rɪˈspekt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 尊敬，尊重。例如: "They showed great respect for their elders."
- **动词**: 对...表示尊敬。例如: "I always respect other people's opinions."
- **形容词**: 有礼貌的，恭敬的。例如: "He behaved in a very respectful manner."

## 短语
- "show respect for" 表现出对...的尊敬。例如: "Students should show respect for their teachers."
- "pay one's respects" 向某人表示敬意。例如: "We paid our respects at the memorial."

## 例句
- 名词: "His achievements earned him widespread respect."
- 动词: "She always respects her colleagues' work."
- 形容词: "The young man spoke to the old lady in a respectful tone."
