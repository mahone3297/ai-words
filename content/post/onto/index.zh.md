## 读音
/ˈɒntəʊ/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示“在...上面”或“朝向”。例如: "Put the book onto the table."，"She stepped onto the stage."

## 短语
- "onto something" 意味着了解或涉及到某事。例如: "I'm onto your little secret."
- "get onto sb" 表示开始关注或处理某人。例如: "The boss got onto him about the unfinished project."

## 例句
- "She placed the vase gently onto the mantelpiece."
- "Once we're onto this new project, we'll have more opportunities."

## 同义词
- on top of
- upon
- toward
- against (在某些情况下可以表示朝向)
