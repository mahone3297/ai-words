## 读音
/tɪkɪt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种票证，通常用于交通、活动、演出等，作为入场或使用权的证明。例如: "I need to buy a train ticket for my journey."
- **名词**: 用于表示罚款的罚单。例如: "He got a speeding ticket from the police."
- **名词**: 在某些游戏中，代表积分或奖励的物品。例如: "Collect ten tickets to win a prize."

## 短语
- "Ticket office" 是售票处。
- "Ticket holder" 指持有票的人。
- "On the ticket" 通常指作为候选人出现在选举名单上。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please show your ticket before entering the concert hall."
- 名词（罚款）: "I was surprised to find a parking ticket on my car windshield."
- 名词（游戏积分）: "The kids were excited to collect more tickets to play the games."
