## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act or process of sending or receiving information, data, or energy from one place to another through a medium or system. 2. The act of transmitting a disease or infection. 3. The conveyance of a signal, message, or emotion. 4. An electrical or electronic system that carries signals or power.
- **Verb**: To send or pass (information, energy, or a signal) from one place to another. 2. To transmit a disease or infection.

## Phrases
- "Signal transmission" refers to the transfer of signals.
- "Audio transmission" deals with sound signals.
- "Data transmission" is the process of sending data electronically.
