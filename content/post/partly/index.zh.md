## 读音
/ˈpɑːr.tli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 部分地；在一定程度上；不完全。例如: "The task was done partly by hand and partly by machine." 
- **形容词**: 不完全的；部分的。例如: "He was partly responsible for the accident."

## 短语
- "Partly cloudy" 意为部分多云的天气。
- "Partly true" 表示部分正确，不完全真实。

## 例句
- 副词: "The reason for the failure was partly due to lack of preparation."
- 形容词: "The answer is partly correct, but there's more to it."

- Partially
- Somewhat
