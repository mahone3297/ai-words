## 读音
/ɪnˈfɜːr/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 推断；推论：从已知的事实或信息中得出结论。例如: "From her expression, I inferred that she was angry."
2. 断定；推测：根据迹象或暗示判断。例如: "The missing pages inferred that the book had been censored."

## 短语
- "infer from" 意为从...推断出。例如: "We can infer from his silence that he不同意我们的观点."

## 例句
- "By analyzing the data, scientists inferred the age of the Earth to be around 4.5 billion years old."
- "His reluctance to commit implied, and we inferred, that he wasn't ready for a serious relationship."
