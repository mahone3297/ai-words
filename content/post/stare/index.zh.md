## 读音
/stɛər/

## 释义
- **动词**: 直视，凝视。例如: "He stared at the painting in amazement."
- **名词**: 凝视，盯着看的行为。例如: "Her stare was so intense that it made him uncomfortable."

## 短语
- "stare in disbelief" 意为难以置信地盯着看。
- "stare someone down" 表示用眼神威慑或挑战对方。

## 例句
- 动词: "She stared at the stranger for a long moment before speaking."
- 名词: "His stare was so fixed that it seemed he hadn't blinked all day."
