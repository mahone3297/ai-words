## 读音
/ˈsmuːðli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 平稳地，顺利地，流畅地。描述动作或过程没有阻碍或困难。
    - 例如: "The car moved smoothly along the winding road."
    - 例如: "She spoke smoothly without any pauses."

## 短语
- "Everything went smoothly" 意味着事情进展顺利，没有问题。
- "Perform a task smoothly" 指执行任务时效率高，无中断。

## 例句
- "The dance performance was executed smoothly, with no mistakes."
- "The project was completed smoothly within the deadline."
