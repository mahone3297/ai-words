## 读音
/ˈsɜːrk(ə)l/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   圆形的图形；圆环。例如: "The circle has no corners."
   一个群体或社交圈子。例如: "She's part of the inner circle of friends."
   范围或领域。例如: "In the world of art, he is well-known within his circle."

- **动词**:
   绕行；环绕。例如: "The planets circle around the sun."
   把…围成圈。例如: "They circled the fire to keep warm."

## 短语
- "Circle around" 意为环绕或包围。例如: "The children circled around the teacher."
- "Within a circle" 指在某个团体或范围内。例如: "Their discussions often took place within a small circle of intellectuals."

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist drew a perfect circle in the sketchbook."
- 动词: "The birds circled high in the sky before landing."
