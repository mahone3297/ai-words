## 读音
/tæŋk/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个大型容器，通常用于储存液体，如水、油或化学品。例如: "The factory has several large tanks for storing oil."
- **名词**: 在军事中，装甲战斗车辆，装有重型武器和装甲。例如: "The army's tank division played a crucial role in the battle."
- **动词 (v)**: 指股票、经济或个人财务状况大幅下跌。例如: "The company's stock tanked after a series of poor financial reports."

## 短语
- "Fuel tank" 指储油箱。
- "Water tank" 指储水箱。
- "Tank up" 指加满燃料或液体。

## 例句
- 名词: "The farmer needed to clean out his old water tank."
- 动词: "The stock market tanked, causing investors to panic."
- 名词 (军事): "The tanks advanced towards the enemy's position."
