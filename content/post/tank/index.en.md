## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A large, armored fighting vehicle with a crew that carries a main gun and can be used for ground combat, reconnaissance, or support roles.** Example: "The tank was a crucial part of World War II warfare."
2. **A large container, typically metal, used to hold liquid or compressed gas.** Example: "The water tank holds several thousand gallons of water."
3. **An enclosed, often armored structure within a building, providing storage or protection for equipment, supplies, or people.** Example: "The basement was converted into a living area with a small kitchenette and a storage tank."

### Verb
1. **To carry or move something using a tank or armored vehicle.** Example: "The soldiers tanked the enemy position."
2. **To store a liquid or gas in a tank.** Example: "The tanker ship is filling up with crude oil."

### Adjective
1. **Characterized by strength, durability, or resilience, often in a figurative sense.** Example: "His tank-like personality made him hard to crack."
2. **Relating to a type of military vehicle or technology.** Example: "The new tank model has advanced armor systems."
