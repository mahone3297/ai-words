## 读音
/pərˈdɑːn/

## 释义
- **动词**: 原谅，宽恕。例如: "I beg your pardon, I didn't mean to offend you."
- **名词**: 请求原谅，歉意。例如: "He offered his pardon for being late."

## 短语
- "Pardon me" 用于请求原谅或让路。
- "Pardon the interruption" 用于道歉打断了别人的谈话。

## 例句
- 动词: "Please pardon my ignorance, I'm not familiar with the topic."
- 名词: "He accepted her pardon and they continued their conversation."

## 同义词
- Excuse me
- Forgive me
- Sorry
- Apology
