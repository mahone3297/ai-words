## Pronunciation
/pəˈrɑːndən/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To forgive or excuse someone for an error, offense, or misunderstanding. "I pardon your mistake."
- **Noun**: (n) An expression of forgiveness or permission to do something. "She gave him a formal pardon."

## Phrases
- "Pardon me" is used to ask for permission or to apologize.
- "Pardon my French" is a humorous way to excuse oneself for speaking with a perceived accent or making a grammatical error.
- "No hard feelings, I pardon you" is a way to forgive and forget a slight.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The judge granted the prisoner a pardon after serving half his sentence."
- **Noun**: "After realizing her mistake, she offered a sincere pardon to her friend."
- **Phrasal**: "Excuse me, I need to step out for a moment. Pardon me."
