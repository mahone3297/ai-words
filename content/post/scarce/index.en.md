## Pronunciation
/skeər/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: 1. Having very little of something, especially food or money; insufficient. 2. Scarce or rare; not abundant. 3. Difficult to find or obtain. 4. Lacking in quantity or number.

## Phrases
- "Scarce resources" refers to limited supplies.
- "Scarce manpower" means a shortage of workers.
- "A scarce commodity" is something that is difficult to come by.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective (1)**: In times of famine, food was scarce.
- **Adjective (2)**: The antique vase was a rare and valuable piece, rarely seen in auctions.
- **Adjective (3)**: Due to the pandemic, personal protective equipment became a scarce item.
- **Adjective (4)**: There were scarce opportunities for job seekers in the struggling economy.
