## 读音
/ˈskɛərəs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 缺乏的；不足的；稀少的。例如: "Food was scarce during the winter months."
- **副词**: 稀疏地；不常。例如: "The animals were seen scarce in these parts."

## 短语
- "scarce resources" 指稀缺资源。
- "scarce in supply" 意味供应不足。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Water became scarce after the long drought."
- 副词: "The birds were scarce this year due to habitat destruction."
