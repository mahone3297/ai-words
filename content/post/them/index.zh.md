## 读音
/ðəm/

## 释义
- **代词**: 他们（宾格），指代复数的第三人称。例如: "[They](/post/they/) are [happy](/post/happy/), [and](/post/and/) [them](/post/them/) [seem](/post/seem/) excited [too](/post/too/)."

## 短语
- "[See](/post/see/) [them](/post/them/) [later](/post/later/)" 意思是稍后再见。
- "[Take](/post/take/) [care](/post/care/) [of](/post/of/) [them](/post/them/)" 意思是照顾他们。

## 例句
- "I'll [talk](/post/talk/) [to](/post/to/) [them](/post/them/) [later](/post/later/) [about](/post/about/) [the](/post/the/) [project](/post/project/)." (我稍后会和他们讨论这个项目。)
- "[Pass](/post/pass/) [the](/post/the/) [book](/post/book/) [to](/post/to/) [them](/post/them/), [please](/post/please/)." (请把书递给他们。)

## 同义词
- 宾格形式的 "[they](/post/they/)" 在某些情况下可以作为同义词，但在句子中通常用作主语。
- 其他代词如 "[those](/post/those/)" 或 "[these](/post/these/)" 可以在特定上下文中替代 "[them](/post/them/)"，但它们通常指代特定的、已知的人或物。
