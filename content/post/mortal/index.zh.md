## 读音
/mɔːrˈtæl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 1. 人类的，凡人的，会死的。例如: "We, as mortals, will all face death someday."
              2. 致命的，导致死亡的。例如: "The poison was so potent that even a small dose was mortal."

## 短语
- "Mortal sin" 指基督教中严重违反教义的罪行。
- "Mortal combat" 指生死搏斗，殊死战。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The philosopher pondered the mortality of mortals in his writings."
- "The mortal wound proved fatal, and he passed away shortly after."
