## 读音
/ədˈdʒek.tɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 用来描述或修饰名词，表示品质、状态、特征等。例如: "[She](/post/she/) has [a](/post/a/) [beautiful](/post/beautiful/) [voice](/post/voice/)."，"[The](/post/the/) [weather](/post/weather/) is pleasantly [warm](/post/warm/)."

## 短语
- "[Adjective](/post/adjective/) clause" 形容词从句，用来修饰名词的句子。
- "[Adjective](/post/adjective/) [phrase](/post/phrase/)" 形容词短语，由一个或多个词组成的短语，用作形容词来修饰名词。

## 例句
- "[The](/post/the/) [blue](/post/blue/) [sky](/post/sky/) [on](/post/on/) [a](/post/a/) [clear](/post/clear/) [day](/post/day/) is breathtakingly [beautiful](/post/beautiful/)."
- "[The](/post/the/) [book](/post/book/) [with](/post/with/) [a](/post/a/) [red](/post/red/) [cover](/post/cover/) caught [my](/post/my/) [attention](/post/attention/)."
- "[She](/post/she/) is [a](/post/a/) [highly](/post/highly/) [intelligent](/post/intelligent/) [and](/post/and/) ambitious [person](/post/person/)."

## 同义词
- Descriptive
- Qualifying
- [Characteristic](/post/characteristic/)
- Attributive
- Modifying

请注意，adjective本身在英语中主要作为形容词使用，没有常见的动词、名词或感叹词形式。上述解释和例句都是针对形容词词性的。
