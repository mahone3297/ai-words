## 读音
/ˈspɪsɪmən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个事物或生物的样本，标本，通常用于研究或展示。例如: "The scientist studied various insect specimens under the microscope."
- **名词**: 在医学或实验室环境中，指患者的血液、尿液等检查样本。例如: "Please provide a urine specimen for the lab test."

## 短语
- "blood specimen" 指血液样本。
- "pathological specimen" 指病理学中的样本。
- "typical specimen" 指典型的或有代表性的样本。

## 例句
- "The museum has an impressive collection of dinosaur specimens."
- "The doctor requested a stool specimen for further analysis."
- "The botanist compared different tree specimens to identify the species."
