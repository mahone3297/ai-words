## Pronunciation
/ˈpɜːrmənənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by lasting indefinitely; not temporary or changeable. For example: "The permanent solution to the problem was found."
- **Adjective**: Having a continuing or established nature; belonging to a permanent position. For example: "She was offered a permanent job at the company."
- **Noun**: A permanent resident or member; a person with indefinite or lifetime rights to stay in a place. For example: "The family has lived in this house for generations, it's a permanent home."

## Phrases
- "Seek a permanent solution" implies finding a long-lasting fix.
- "On a permanent basis" refers to a permanent or ongoing commitment.
- "A permanent record" is something that remains unchanged and documented.
