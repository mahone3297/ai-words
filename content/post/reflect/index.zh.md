## 读音
/ˈrɛflek/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 反射（光线、声音等）。例如: "The mirror reflects the sunlight."
2. （思想、情感或行为）反映，体现。例如: "Her art reflects her inner emotions."
3. 考虑，深思。例如: "Take some time to reflect on your decision."

- **名词**:
反射，映像。例如: "The surface of the lake offered a perfect reflection of the trees."

## 短语
- "Reflect on something" 意味着对某事进行思考或反思。例如: "After the exam, he spent hours reflecting on his mistakes."
- "Reflect back" 可以表示将某物反射回去。例如: "The glass reflects back most of the ultraviolet light."

## 例句
- 动词: "The moonlight reflected off the calm water."
- 名词: "In still water, you can see your own reflection."
