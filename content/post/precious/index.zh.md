## 释义
- **形容词**: 宝贵的，珍贵的，极重要的。例如: "This necklace is precious to her because it was a gift from her grandmother."
- **名词**: 宝贝，心爱的人或物。例如: "My little girl is my precious."

## 短语
- "precious little" 表示几乎没有，珍贵而少量的。例如: "There's precious little time left before the deadline."
- "precious metals" 指贵重金属，如黄金、白银。例如: "Gold and silver are precious metals."
