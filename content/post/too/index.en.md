## Pronunciation
/to͞o/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: 1. In addition; also. For example: "She likes ice cream too." 2. Excessively; to an excessive degree. For example: "He was too loud at the meeting."
- **Adjective**: 1. Beyond what is necessary or appropriate; excessive. For example: "The noise was too much to bear." 2. Beyond a certain point; to the limit. For example: "She was too tired to continue."

## Phrases
- "Too much of something" implies an excess or overabundance.
- "She's not coming, she's already too busy." means she has no spare time.
- "It's too late to change now" suggests it's impossible or impractical to make a change.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "I love both pizza and pasta, but tonight, I'll have pizza, too."
- **Adjective**: "The cake was so sweet, it was almost too sweet."
- **Adverb**: "He worked too hard and ended up getting sick."
