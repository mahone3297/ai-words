## Pronunciation
/dɪˈskrɪpʃən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A statement that gives information about something or someone, describing its features, qualities, or characteristics. 2. A written or spoken representation of such a statement. For example: "The artist provided a vivid description of the sunset."
- **Verb**: To give a detailed account or explanation of; depict. For example: "She described the scene in great detail."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of or used for description. For example: "A descriptive essay requires careful observation."

## Phrases
- "Provide a description" means to give a clear account or explanation.
- "Visual description" refers to a depiction in words.
- "A description of the event" is a recounting of what happened.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The author's description of the medieval castle was so vivid, it felt like I was there."
- **Verb**: "She described her adventure to her friends over dinner."
- **Adjective**: "The descriptive language in the novel brought the setting to life."
