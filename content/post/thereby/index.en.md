## Pronunciation
/ˈɛθərˌbi/ (eth-uh-bear)

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Used to indicate the manner or result of an action, typically introducing a clause. It can also mean "thus" or "consequently."

## Phrases
- "By that means" or "Thus" is used to introduce a result or explanation.
- "As a result" or "Consequently" implies a consequence of an action.
- "Henceforward" or "Therefore" signals a change or conclusion based on previous events.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "She studied hard, thereby improving her grades significantly."
- "Her diligence in the lab led to groundbreaking discoveries, thereby earning her a Nobel Prize."
- "He was late, thereby causing a delay in the meeting."

## Synonyms
- Consequently
- Hence
- Therefore
- Thus
- As a result
- By this means

## Antonyms
- Incidentally
- Unrelatedly
- Unexpectedly (in the context of a direct connection)
