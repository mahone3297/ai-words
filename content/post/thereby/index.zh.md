## 读音
/ˈðɛrˈbaɪ/

## 释义
- **副词**: 通过这种方式；因此；因而。例如: "By exercising regularly, you'll improve your health thereby living longer."
- **介词**: 引导方式状语，表示结果。例如: "She reduced her stress levels, thereby increasing her productivity."

## 短语
- "Thereby achieving" 表示通过某种方式达到目的。例如: "Thereby achieving success in her career."
- "Thereby causing" 表示因此造成。例如: "His decision led to a conflict, thereby causing tension among team members."

## 例句
- 副词: "The new technology has simplified the process, thereby saving time and effort."
- 介词: "She corrected the mistake promptly, thereby avoiding any further complications."
