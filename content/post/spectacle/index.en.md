## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
    1. An impressive show, display, or event, often involving a large audience. For example: "The fireworks spectacle was a dazzling display of color."
    2. A thing that is looked at with wonder or admiration. For example: "The ancient ruins were a historical spectacle."
- **Verb**: To exhibit or display something as a show, often for public admiration. For example: "The museum will spectacle their latest art collection."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of being grand or impressive; spectacular. For example: "The mountain landscape was a visual spectacle."

## Phrases

- "A sight to behold" describes something that is impressive or remarkable to see.
- "Theatrical spectacle" refers to a dramatic performance with elaborate visuals.
