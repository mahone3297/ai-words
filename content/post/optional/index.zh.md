## 读音
/ˈɑːpʃənl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 可选择的，非强制的，不是必需的。例如: "The extra features on the car are optional."
- **名词**: 可选项，非必需品。例如: "You can choose from several optional courses in this semester."

## 短语
- "Optional extras" 指可选的额外服务或产品。
- "An optional activity" 是一项可以选择参加或不参加的活动。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Attendance at the workshop is optional, so you can decide whether to go or not."
- 名词: "The travel insurance is an optional purchase when booking your trip."
