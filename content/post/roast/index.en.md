## Pronunciation
/rəʊst/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Roast**: 1. A method of cooking food, especially meat, by exposing it to dry heat in an oven or over an open flame. Example: "We're having a beef roast for dinner tonight."
- 2. A piece of meat cooked using this method. Example: "The chef prepared a succulent lamb roast."
- 3. A humorous or mocking public criticism or teasing. Example: "She received a roast during the awards ceremony."

### Verb
- **To roast**: 1. To cook something using the roasting method. Example: "He's expert at roasting vegetables."
- 2. (informal) To subject someone to teasing or mockery, often humorously. Example: "The comedian roasted the host mercilessly."

## Phrases
- "Roast beef" is a classic dish.
- "Grilled roast chicken" is a popular choice.
- "Roast party" can refer to a humorous event where attendees are encouraged to give speeches about others.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The roast was so tender that it fell off the bone."
- **Verb**: "She carefully roasted the potatoes until they were crispy."
- **Informal Verb**: "The stand-up comedian decided to roast the new team member."
