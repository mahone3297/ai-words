## 读音
/roʊst/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 烤；烘烤：将食物置于火或热源上烹调。例如: "I'm roasting a chicken for dinner tonight."
  - 嘲笑；羞辱：以幽默或讽刺的方式公开批评或嘲笑。例如: "He was roasted by his friends at his birthday party."

- **名词**:
  - 烤肉：经过烘烤的食物，如烤肉或烤蔬菜。例如: "We had a delicious beef roast with potatoes and carrots."

## 短语
- "Roast beef" 指烤牛肉。
- "Roast dinner" 在英国指的是包括烤肉、烤土豆和各种蔬菜的正餐。
- "Roast someone" 指的是对某人进行嘲讽或公开批评。

## 例句
- 动词: "She's roasting a turkey in the oven for Thanksgiving."
- 名词: "The aroma of the Sunday roast filled the house."

## 同义词
- Bake
- Grill
- Broil
- Toast
- Cook (in some contexts)
