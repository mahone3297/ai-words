## 读音
/riːˈtrit/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
  - 退却，撤退：The army's retreat in the face of enemy advances was orderly.
  - 避暑别墅，隐居处：He spends his summers at a retreat in the mountains.

- **动词**:
  - 撤退，退却：The troops retreated from the city.
  - 隐退，躲避：She retreated to her room to avoid the argument.

## 短语
- "retreat to somewhere" 意为撤退或逃避到某个地方。
- "retreat from something" 表示从某事物或情况中退出或撤回。

## 例句
- 名词: "The general ordered a retreat when it became clear they couldn't win the battle."
- 动词: "After a long day, she retreated to her bedroom for some peace and quiet."
- 感叹词（非正式用法）: "Well, that's it, time to retreat for the night!"

## 同义词
- withdraw
- fall back
- recess
- withdrawal
- departure
