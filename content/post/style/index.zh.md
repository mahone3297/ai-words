## 读音
/staɪl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 
    - 风格；样式："The artist's unique style sets his work apart from others."
    - 模式；类型："There are various styles of furniture to choose from."
    - 举止；风度："She has a graceful style in her movements."
    - 时尚；流行趋势："This season's style features bold patterns and bright colors."

- **动词 (v)**:
    - 形成风格；赋予特征："His writing style has evolved over the years."
    - 装饰；设计："The designer styled the room with modern furniture."

## 短语
- "In style" 表示流行或时髦，如："That dress is very much in style this season."
- "Write in a particular style" 指以特定的写作风格创作，如："She writes in a humorous and engaging style."

## 例句
- 名词: "The building's architecture reflects a blend of Gothic and Renaissance styles."
- 动词: "The chef styled the dish with a creative arrangement of vegetables."
