## 读音
/ˈnaɪlən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种合成纤维，由尼龙66或尼龙6制成，强度高，耐磨，耐化学腐蚀，常用于制作衣物、绳索、地毯等。例如: "She bought a new pair of nylon stockings."
- **形容词**: 由尼龙材料制成的。例如: "These socks are nylon and very durable."

## 短语
- "nylon fabric" 尼龙面料
- "nylon ropes" 尼龙绳
- "nylon carpet" 尼龙地毯

## 例句
- 名词: "The tent is made of strong nylon material that can withstand harsh weather conditions."
- 形容词: "The jacket has a nylon lining, which makes it water-resistant."
