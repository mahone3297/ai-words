## Word: Nylon

## Pronunciation
/ˈnaɪlən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A synthetic polymer material made from a combination of diacids and diamines, known for its strength, durability, and resistance to wear and tear. It's commonly used in clothing, rope, carpets, and various industrial applications. Example: "The backpack straps were made of strong nylon."

Example Sentence: 
- **Noun**: "She bought a new pair of nylon stockings that wouldn't run easily."

## Phrases
- "Nylon fabric": Refers to textiles made using nylon fibers.
- "Nylon thread": A strong, synthetic thread often used for stitching or sewing.
- "Nylon blend": A fabric that combines nylon with other materials like cotton or polyester.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The parachute was made of lightweight yet sturdy nylon material."

## Synonyms
- Polyamide
- Synthetic fiber
- Artificial silk (archaic)
