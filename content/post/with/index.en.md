## Pronunciation
/həˈləʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A term used to greet or address someone. For example: "She waved a friendly hello at the bus stop."

### Verb
To say "hello" or greet someone. For example: "He greeted the teacher with a hearty hello in the morning."

### Adjective
Adjective form of the noun, not commonly used independently. Can be used to describe a friendly or enthusiastic greeting. For example: "Her cheerful hello filled the room."

### Interjection
Used to greet, attract attention, or express surprise. For example: "Oh, hello there! What a pleasant surprise!"

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to initiate a greeting.
- "Say hello on one's behalf" means to convey a greeting on someone else's behalf.
- "Hello, old friend" is a nostalgic way to greet someone you haven't seen in a while.

## Example Sentences
