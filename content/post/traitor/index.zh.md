## 读音
/trəɪˈɔːtər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 背叛者；叛徒。例如: "He was considered a traitor for collaborating with the enemy."
- **形容词**: 表示背叛或不忠的。例如: "The traitorous act of deserting one's country."

## 短语
- "traitorous behavior" 指的是背叛的行为。
- "a traitor to the cause" 指对某个事业或信念的背叛者。

## 例句
- 名词: "The traitor's capture brought relief to the citizens."
- 形容词: "His traitorous actions led to the downfall of the rebellion."
