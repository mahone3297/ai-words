## Pronunciation
/stjuˈdnt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **student** is a person who is studying, typically at a school, college, or university, to acquire knowledge or develop skills in a particular subject or field. 

### Verb
To **study** can also refer to the act of learning or engaging in academic pursuits, often with the goal of acquiring knowledge or preparing for an exam or assessment.

### Adjective
**Student-like** describes someone who exhibits qualities or behaviors typical of a student, such as being curious or diligent.

## Phrases
- "Study hard" encourages focus on academics.
- "A student's life" often refers to the busy and often challenging period of one's education.
- "Student discounts" are special offers given to students for products or services.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The new student from Japan was welcomed by the class."
- **Verb**: "She spends hours every night studying for her exams."
- **Adjective**: "The student-like enthusiasm for learning is contagious."
