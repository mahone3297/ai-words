## Pronunciation
/%pərˈsentɪdʒ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A fraction or ratio expressed as a number followed by a percent sign (%), indicating the proportion of a whole. For example: "50% of the students passed the exam."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing a proportion or degree, often used to describe a part out of a whole. For example: "The project is 80% complete."

## Phrases
- "Percentage point" refers to a single unit of change in a percentage.
- "Up to X percent" expresses a range of possible percentages.
- "A drop in percentage" signifies a decrease.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The company saw a 15% increase in sales this quarter."
- **Adjective**: "Her savings account has grown by 2% this year."
- "The new policy aims to reduce crime by 30% in the next two years."

## Synonyms
- Rate
- Proportion
- Fraction
- Ratio
- Share
- Per cent
- Decimal equivalent (e.g., 50% = 0.5)

## Antonyms
- None (percentages are not typically compared directly; they represent relative values)
