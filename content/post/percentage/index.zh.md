## 读音
/ˈpɜːr.sɛntɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 表示部分与整体之间的比率或数量占整体的百比率。例如: "The percentage of students who passed the exam was 85%."
- **动词**: 计算或表达以百分比形式的比例。例如: "Can you percentage the increase in sales?"

## 短语
- "Percentage point" 指两个百分比之间的差值。
- "Percentage increase/decrease" 表示增长或减少的百分比。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's profit margin is a healthy percentage of its revenue."
- 动词: "Let's percentage the difference between last year's and this year's budget."
