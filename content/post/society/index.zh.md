## 读音
/ˈsəʊsiəti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 社会；社团；交往；共同体。例如: "We live in a complex and diverse society."，"She is a member of the local gardening society."
- **形容词**: 社交的；与社会有关的。例如: "Society women often attend charity events."，"He has a strong desire to belong to high society."

## 短语
- "Society life" 指的是社交生活或上流社会的生活。
- "In/Out of society" 指在或不在社交圈内，是否受欢迎。

## 例句
- 名词: "The study explores the impact of technology on modern society."
- 形容词: "Her dress was perfect for a society ball."
