## 读音
/ˈθɛrəfɔːr/

## 释义
- **连词**: 因此，所以。用来引出一个结果或结论。例如: "It was raining heavily, therefore, we decided to stay indoors."

## 短语
- "Therefore, in conclusion" 用于引出总结性的陈述。

## 例句
- "We have limited resources, therefore, we need to prioritize our tasks."
