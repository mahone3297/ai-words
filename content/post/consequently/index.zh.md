## 读音
/ˈkɑːnsɪkwəntli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 因此，所以，因而。用来表示一个结果是直接或自然地由前面的情况导致的。例如: "[He](/post/he/) missed [the](/post/the/) [bus](/post/bus/) [and](/post/and/) [consequently](/post/consequently/) arrived [late](/post/late/) [for](/post/for/) [work](/post/work/)."

## 短语
- "[As](/post/as/) [a](/post/a/) [consequence](/post/consequence/)" 或 "[Consequently](/post/consequently/), [therefore](/post/therefore/)" 都表示结果或后果。

## 例句
- "[He](/post/he/) didn't [study](/post/study/) [for](/post/for/) [the](/post/the/) [exam](/post/exam/), [and](/post/and/) [consequently](/post/consequently/), [he](/post/he/) failed."
- "[The](/post/the/) [project](/post/project/) was delayed, [and](/post/and/) [as](/post/as/) [a](/post/a/) [consequence](/post/consequence/), [the](/post/the/) deadline had [to](/post/to/) [be](/post/be/) extended."
