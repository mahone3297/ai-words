## 读音
/seprəˈteɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 分开，隔离。例如: "Please separate the white clothes from the colored ones."
- **形容词**: 单独的，分开的。例如: "The separate rooms provided privacy for each guest."

## 短语
- "separate from" 意为从...中分开。例如: "We need to separate the eggs yolks from the whites."
- "separate ways" 指各自走不同的路。例如: "After graduation, they went their separate ways."

## 例句
- 动词: "The teacher asked the students to separate into groups for the activity."
- 形容词: "The hotel offers separate bedrooms for couples and families."
