## Pronunciation
/seˈpərət/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Distinct or set apart from others; separate. For example: "The rooms were separated by a thin wall."
- **Verb**: To divide or cause to be apart; to isolate. For example: "Separate the whites from the colors when doing laundry."
- **Noun**: An act of separating; a division. For example: "The separation of the children into different classes was difficult."
- **Preposition**: In a way that keeps things apart; away from. For example: "Keep the books separate on the shelf."

## Phrases
- "Separate entities" refers to distinct or independent things.
- "Separate duties" means dividing tasks among different people.
- "Separate accounts" are individual financial records.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Each student sat in a separate seat."

- **Noun**: "Their separation after a long argument was a relief."
