## 读音
/sərˈvaɪv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 继续活着；幸存：在困难或危险之后保持生命。例如: "She survived the plane crash."
2. 比...活得长：在某人去世后继续生活。例如: "He outlived his wife by ten years."
3. （事物）延续存在：在变化或淘汰中持续存在。例如: "The old building still survives in the heart of the city."

## 短语
- "Survive against all odds" 在极其不利的情况下生存下来。
- "Survive the winter" 度过冬天。

## 例句
- 她在飞机失事后奇迹般地活了下来。
- He managed to survive on a deserted island for three months before being rescued.
- The small bookstore has survived the rise of online retailers by offering personalized service.
