## Definitions
### Noun
A **survival** is the act or ability to continue living or existing after a difficult or challenging situation. It can also refer to the remaining part of something after it has been through an event. For example: "The old house still stands as a symbol of survival against the odds."

### Verb
To **survive** means to endure and continue to exist, often in adverse conditions, or to overcome a difficult situation or adversity. For example: "Many people survived the earthquake despite the devastation."

### Adjective
- **Surviving** refers to something that remains after others have perished or ceased to exist. For example: "The surviving members of the expedition told their harrowing tale."

### Adverb
- **Survivingly** describes how something survives, typically with resilience or perseverance. For example: "She managed to live a long, survivingly happy life."
