## 读音
/weld/

## 释义
- **动词**: 将两部分金属或热塑性材料通过加热、压力或两者结合的方式永久连接在一起。例如: "The mechanic welded the broken pipe back together."
- **名词**: 焊接点或焊接处。例如: "The strength of the structure depends on the quality of the weld."

## 短语
- "TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas) welding" 是一种精确的焊接技术。
- "MIG (Metal Inert Gas) welding" 是一种常见的焊接方法，适合各种金属材料。

## 例句
- 动词: "He is skilled in welding different types of metals."
- 名词: "The engineer inspected the welds to ensure they were free from defects."
