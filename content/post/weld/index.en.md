## Pronunciation
/wɛld/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A process of joining or fusing pieces of metal or other materials by heating them and applying pressure, allowing them to adhere together. For example: "The welding process creates strong, durable bonds."
- **Verb**: To join or fasten using the technique of welding. For example: "The workers were welding metal pipes together."
- **Adjective**: Relating to or involving welding. For example: "The welding expert inspected the steel structure."

## Phrases
- "Welding joint" refers to the point where two materials have been joined by welding.
- "Tungsten inert gas welding" (TIG) is a specific welding method.
- "Resistance welding" is another type of welding technique.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The weld on the car's bumper was strong and well-done."
- **Verb**: "The experienced welder skillfully created a seamless joint in the metal sheet."
- **Adjective**: "The building's frame was made entirely from welded steel beams."

## Synonyms
- Joining
- Fusing
- Bonding
- Soldering (for metals)
- Clamping (in some contexts)
- Metalwork connection
