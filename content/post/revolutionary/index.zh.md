## 读音
/ˌrɛvəˈljuːtəri/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 引起根本变革的；革命性的；创新的。例如: "The invention of the internet was a revolutionary change in communication."
- **名词**: 革命者；拥护革命的人。例如: "Many revolutionary leaders fought for their country's independence."

## 短语
- "revolutionary change" 意指重大变革或彻底的改变。
- "revolutionary ideas" 指的是具有开创性和颠覆性的思想或理论。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new technology has brought about a revolutionary improvement in productivity."
- 名词: "He was considered a dangerous revolutionary by the ruling government."
