## 读音
/ˈdʒe.ləs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 表示嫉妒或猜疑的。例如: "She felt jealous of her friend's new job."
- **动词**: 感到嫉妒或羡慕。例如: "He couldn't help but feel jealous when he saw them together."

## 短语
- "jealousy" 是 "jealous" 的名词形式，表示嫉妒的情感或行为。
- "jealous lover" 指的是一个容易产生嫉妒情感的情侣。

## 例句
- 形容词: "His constant praise for her made his coworkers feel jealous."
- 动词: "She couldn't help but jealous of her sister's success."
