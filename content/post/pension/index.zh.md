## 读音
/ˈpenʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 退休金，养老金。定期支付给退休人员的金钱。例如: "He started receiving his pension after retiring from the company."
- **动词**: 提供退休金，使退休。例如: "The government will pension off workers who have reached the age of 65."

## 短语
- "pension fund" 指的是用于支付退休金的基金。
- "public pension" 公共退休金，通常由政府提供。
- "private pension" 私人退休金，通常由雇主或个人储蓄计划提供。

## 例句
- 名词: "She relies on her pension for her monthly income."
- 动词: "The company decided to pension off some of its long-serving employees."

- annuity
