## Pronunciation
/pəˈʃn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A regular income paid to someone, usually after retirement, as compensation for past services or as a benefit. ["The pensioner receives a monthly payment from the government."](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/pension)
- **Verb**: To provide (a pension) to someone as a retirement benefit. ["The company offers a generous pension plan for its employees."](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/pension)

## Phrases
- "Draw a pension" means to start receiving regular pension payments.
- "Pension benefits" refers to the financial support provided by a pension plan.
- "Public pension system" is the network that provides pensions to government employees.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After 30 years of service, he finally qualified for a full pension."
- **Verb**: "The couple has been saving for their future pension to ensure a comfortable retirement."
- **Noun phrase**: "Her father's pension was his only source of income."

## Synonyms
- Retirement allowance
- Retirement pay
- Retirement income
- Benefit (in the context of retirement)
- Pension fund
- Annuity

## Related Terms
- Defined benefit plan: A type of pension scheme where the employer guarantees a specific benefit amount.
- Defined contribution plan: A pension plan where contributions and eventual benefits depend on investment performance.
- State pension: A pension provided by the government to eligible citizens or residents.
- Private pension: A pension offered by employers or purchased by individuals.
- Early retirement: Taking pension before the official retirement age.
