## 读音
/riːˈpiːtɪdlɪ/

## 释义
- **副词**: 反复地，多次地，不断地。描述一个动作或情况是连续且重复发生的。

## 短语
- "repeat something repeatedly" 表示某事被一遍又一遍地重复。
- "do something habitually or frequently" 形容习惯性或频繁地做某事。

## 例句
- "She repeatedly asked him the same question."
- "The child repeated the multiplication tables repeatedly to memorize them."
- "The machine malfunctioned repeatedly, causing delays in production."

- often
- frequently
- constantly
- again and again
