## Pronunciation
/ˈtɛnɪtiər/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **teenager** is a person between the ages of 13 and 19, typically considered to be in the adolescent stage of life. 

### Verb
To **be a teenager** (informal) or **experience teenagehood** refers to going through the adolescence phase.

## Phrases
- "Grow up to be a teenager" means to progress from childhood to adolescence.
- "Act like a teenager" suggests behaving in a typical teenage manner.
- "The teenage years" is a common phrase referring to this age range.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "During her teenage years, she developed a passion for music."
- **Verb**: "My little brother is just starting to become a teenager, and it's hard to believe how fast he's growing up."
- **Adjective**: "The teenager's bedroom was filled with posters of their favorite bands."
