## Pronunciation
/ˈwevrɛə(r)/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Referring to any place, everywhere, or no matter where. It functions to indicate the location or extent of an action. 

## Phrases
- "Wherever you go" implies that the action applies regardless of location.
- "I'll be there wherever you need me" expresses availability or loyalty.
- "She left her keys wherever she went" suggests a habit of carelessness with belongings.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "The storm followed him wherever he traveled."
- "No matter where we are, we'll always find a way to connect."
- "He searched the house, but couldn't locate his wallet - it was wherever."
