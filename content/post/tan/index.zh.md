## 读音
/tæn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 肤色因日晒而变黑的状态。例如: "She has a beautiful tan from her vacation in Spain."
2. 深褐色，棕褐色。例如: "The leather sofa has a rich tan color."

- **动词**:
使皮肤晒成棕褐色。例如: "I'm going to tan by the pool this afternoon."

## 短语
- "get a tan" 意为晒黑。
- "tan line" 指晒太阳后皮肤与未晒部分的分界线。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her tan made her look healthier and more vibrant."
- 动词: "After a day at the beach, he was well on his way to tanning his skin."
