## Pronunciation
/tæn/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Tan** (noun): A suntan or a brownish discoloration of the skin resulting from exposure to sunlight or artificial UV radiation.
- **Tan leather**: A type of leather with a brown, sun-baked appearance.

### Verb
- **To tan** (verb):
  - (1) To darken or brown one's skin through exposure to sunlight or tanning beds.
  - (2) (transitive) To treat leather or fabric with chemicals to give it a brownish color.

### Adjective
- **Tan** (adjective): Having a brownish-yellow color, typically associated with sun-baked skin or leather.

## Phrases
- "Get a tan" means to deliberately expose oneself to sunlight to achieve a suntan.
- "Maintain a tan" refers to keeping a suntan after initial exposure.
- "Tan lines" are the lighter or darker patches on the skin where clothing covers during sun exposure.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After spending a week at the beach, her skin had a beautiful tan."
- **Verb**: "She decided to tan naturally by going for daily walks in the park."
- **Adjective**: "The leather sofa had a rich, tan patina from years of use."

## Synonyms
- Browned (for skin)
- Leathered (for leather)
- Sunburnt (negative connotation)
- Bronzed (for a desired suntan)
- Faded tan (implying loss of color)
