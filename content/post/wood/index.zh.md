## 读音
/ˈwʊd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 木头；木材。例如: "He used wood to build a small shed in his backyard."
  - 树林；森林。例如: "The deer was wandering through the dense wood."

## 短语
- "Hardwood" 指质地坚硬的木材，常用于家具制作。
- "Softwood" 指质地较软的木材，多用于建筑和造纸。

## 例句
- 名词: "The carpenter selected oak wood for its durability."
- "The children played hide-and-seek in the nearby wood."

## 同义词
- Timber
- Lumber
- Forest
- Woods
- Jungle (在指森林时)
