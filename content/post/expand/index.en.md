## Word: Expand

## Pronunciation
/ɪkˈspænd/

## Definitions
- **Verb (v)**: To increase in size, extent, or range; to spread out or open up. It can also refer to making something larger or more comprehensive by adding details or content. 

  > *Example Sentence*: The company plans to expand its operations overseas.
  > *Another Example*: The universe continues to expand since the Big Bang.

- **Noun (n)**: The act or process of expanding, or the state of being expanded. This could be a physical expansion or an abstract one like growth or development.

  > *Example Sentence*: The recent expansion of our product line has led to increased sales.

## Phrases
- **Expand on something**: To give more information about or explain something in greater detail.
  > "*Let me expand on that idea so you can fully understand it.*"

- **Expand into something**: To move into a new area or field, usually in business or territory.
  > "*The startup plans to expand into the European market this year.*"

- **Expand upon something**: To develop or elaborate further upon an idea or topic.
  > "*In his next chapter, the author expands upon the theories presented earlier.*

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "As the dough proofs, it will expand until it doubles in size."
- **Noun**: "The rapid expansion of the city necessitated the construction of new infrastructure."
