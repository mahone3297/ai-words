## 读音
/ɪmˈpruːvmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 改进，改善。指事物变得更好或更有效率的过程或结果。例如: "There has been a significant improvement in his performance."
- **动词**: 改进，改善。使某事或某物变得更好。例如: "We are constantly working to improve our products."

## 短语
- "Continuous improvement" 指持续不断寻求改进的过程。
- "Improvement plan" 是一个旨在提高个人或组织绩效的策略或方案。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new software has brought noticeable improvements in speed and efficiency."
- 动词: "She is taking extra classes to improve her language skills."
