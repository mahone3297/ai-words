## Word: Improvement

## Pronunciation
/ɪmˈpruːvmənt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The act or process of making something better than it was before; enhancement or refinement. It can also refer to the result of this process.

Example Sentences:
- _Noun (the act of improving):_ "The company invested heavily in research and development for product improvement."
- _Noun (result of improvement):_ "The new design represents a significant improvement over the previous model."

### Phrases
- "Continuous improvement" - An ongoing effort to improve products, services, or processes.
- "Room for improvement" - Indicating that there is space or scope to make something better.
- "To work on self-improvement" - Engaging in activities aimed at enhancing personal skills or qualities.

## Example Sentences
- _Noun (the act of improving):_ "She made considerable improvements to her public speaking skills through practice."
- _Noun (result of improvement):_ "The town's infrastructure has seen dramatic improvements since last year."
