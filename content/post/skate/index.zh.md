## 读音
/skate/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种有刀状冰刀的鞋，用于滑冰运动。例如: "She put on her skates and glided across the ice."
- **动词**: 在冰上或特制表面上用冰刀滑行。例如: "He loves to skate on the frozen lake in winter."

## 短语
- "Ice skate" 指在冰上使用的溜冰鞋。
- "Skateboarding" 是一种使用滑板进行的运动。
- "Skate park" 是一个专为滑板和滚轴溜冰者设计的设施。

## 例句
- 名词: "The kids were learning how to tie their new ice skates properly."
- 动词: "He spent the afternoon skating around the park with his friends."

## 同义词
- Slide (on ice)
- Roll (for roller skating)
- Skateboard (for the action of skateboarding)
