## 读音
/straɪp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 条纹，线条：A pattern or mark consisting of alternating bands of different colors or shades.
   - (衣服、旗帜等上的)条纹图案：A distinctive arrangement of parallel lines on clothing, a flag, etc.

- **动词**:
   - 给...加上条纹：To mark with or as if with stripes.
   -  rip off (布料)：To remove a strip of material from something.

## 短语
- "striped shirt" 表示有条纹的衬衫。
- "stripe the bed" 指给床单加上条纹或者整理床铺。
- "to stripe a field" 在田地里划出标记或线条。

## 例句
- 名词: "The zebra has black and white stripes."
- 动词: "The painter striped the wall with blue and yellow."
- "The flag of the United States features red and white stripes."
