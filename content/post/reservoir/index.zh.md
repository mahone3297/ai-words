## 读音
/rɪˈzɜːvər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个储存液体（如水）的大型容器或设施，通常用于供应或控制流动。例如: "The city's water reservoir provides drinking water to thousands of homes."
- **名词**: 大量的储备或储存。例如: "His knowledge of history is a vast reservoir of information."
- **名词**: （动植物的）栖息地或生存环境。例如: "The wetland serves as a natural reservoir for many endangered species."

## 短语
- "Water reservoir" 指储水池或水库。
- "Reservoir of infection" 指病原体的储存库。

## 例句
- 名词: "The engineers are checking the dam to ensure the safety of the reservoir."
- 名词: "Bats are known to be a reservoir for certain viruses."
- 名词: "Maintaining a healthy forest can act as a carbon reservoir, helping to mitigate climate change."
