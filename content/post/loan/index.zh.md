## 读音
/ˈləʊn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 借款；贷款。例如: "She took out a loan to pay for her studies."
- **动词**: 借给；贷款。例如: "The bank loaned him the money to buy a house."

## 短语
- "personal loan" 个人贷款
- "student loan" 学生贷款
- "payday loan" 放薪日贷款

## 例句
- 名词: "He has to repay the loan within a year."
