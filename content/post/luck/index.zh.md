## 读音
/lʌk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 不可预见的有利情况；好运或坏运；偶然的机会。例如: "She was lucky to find a parking space so close to the building."
- **形容词**: 带有好运的；幸运的。例如: "He was a lucky guy who won the lottery."

## 短语
- "Good luck" 表示祝愿某人有好运。
- "Luck out" 指某人运气好，事情结果比预期的好。
- "In luck" 意为处于好运之中，事情进展顺利。

## 例句
- 名词: "His success was largely due to luck rather than skill."
- 形容词: "It's lucky we brought an umbrella; it started raining heavily."
- 情景对话: "I can't believe I found this rare coin – what a stroke of luck!"

## 同义词

- Chance
- Serendipity
- Happenstance
- Stroke of luck
