## 读音
/θrɒŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一大群人；拥挤的人群。例如: "The throng of shoppers filled the street."
- **动词**: 拥挤，聚集。例如: "People thronged to the city center for the festival."

## 短语
- "Throng together" 意味着人群聚集在一起。
- "A throng of people" 描述大量的人聚集。

## 例句
- 名词: "The concert attracted a throng of fans eager to see their favorite band perform."
- 动词: "The fans thronged around the stage, hoping for a glimpse of the musicians."
