## 读音
/plʌɡ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 插头，塞子；堵塞物。例如: "Please unplug the vacuum cleaner before you store it."（请在储存吸尘器前拔掉插头。）
- **动词**: 插入，塞入；宣传，推广。例如: "He plugged the new book on several TV shows."（他在几个电视节目中为新书做宣传。）

## 短语
- "Plug in" 意思是插入电源或连接设备。
- "Plug something up" 意味着堵住或塞住某个孔或开口。

## 例句
- 名词: "The electrician replaced the worn-out plug with a new one."
- 动词: "She plugged her headphones into her phone and started listening to music."
- 插入/宣传: "The band is going to plug their upcoming tour on social media."
