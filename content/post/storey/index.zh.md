## 读音
/stɔːri/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 楼层，层：A level of a building, especially one that is completely enclosed and has a floor and a ceiling.
2. 故事，叙述：A series of events or a plot, especially as presented in a book, movie, or play.

## 短语
- "upper storey" 指较高的楼层。
- "the storey of a building" 指建筑物的层数。
- "a compelling storey" 指引人入胜的故事。

## 例句
- 名词: "The penthouse is located on the top storey of the skyscraper."
- 名词: "She told us an intriguing storey about her travels in Africa."

## 同义词
- Level
- Floor
- Tier
- Story (in the context of a narrative)
- Chapter (in the context of a book or tale)
