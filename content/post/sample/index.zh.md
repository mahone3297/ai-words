## 读音
/ˈsæmpl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个小型的事物或数据，用于展示或测试更大整体的特性或质量。例如: "The company provided a sample of their new product for us to try."
- **名词**: 在音乐、音频或声音设计中，一小段录制的声音片段。例如: "The DJ used a drum sample in his mix."
- **动词**: 取得或提供样本。例如: "Customers can sample the different flavors of ice cream before deciding which one to buy."

## 短语
- "take a sample" 意味着取样或抽取一部分进行检验。
- "sample size" 指的是在统计学中用于分析的样本数量。

## 例句
- 名词: "The lab technician took a blood sample from the patient for testing."
- 名词: "The musician recorded a guitar sample to use in his composition."
- 动词: "The store allows customers to sample the cheese before purchasing."
