## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
  - 1. A general or unspecified object, entity, or matter; an item or piece of equipment. "A thing on the table."
  - 2. A quality or characteristic that defines something. "The beauty of the painting lies in its subtle details."
  - 3. An action or event. "The wedding was a memorable thing."
- **Verb**: To consider, regard, or perceive something. "She's thinking about buying a new thing for her kitchen."
- **Interjection**: Not typically used as an interjection in English, but occasionally it can be found in informal speech or to emphasize something. "Oh, what a thing!"

## Phrases

- "That's one thing" is used to indicate a single point or aspect in a discussion.
- "Nothing is a thing" can mean that something is insignificant or unimportant.
