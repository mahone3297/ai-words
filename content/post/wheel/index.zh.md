## 读音
/wiːl/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   - 轮子：一个圆形的装置，通常安装在轴上，用于转动或移动物体。例如: "The bicycle has two wheels."
   - （比喻）基本组成部分，核心元素：常用于描述系统或过程的关键部分。例如: "She is the driving wheel of the project."

- **动词 v**:
   - 拉动，转动：使物体旋转或移动。例如: "Can you help me wheel the cart into the garage?"

## 短语
- "on wheels" 表示有轮子的，便于移动的。例如: "The new desk is on wheels, so it's easy to move around."
- "wheel and deal" 指进行交易，施展手段。例如: "He's been wheeling and dealing in the stock market for years."

## 例句
- 名词: "The car couldn't move without its four wheels."
- 动词: "He wheeled the suitcase through the airport."
