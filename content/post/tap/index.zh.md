## 读音
/tæp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 小量流出；滴；轻敲；旋塞；龙头；软管接头。例如: "Turn off the tap when brushing your teeth."
- **动词**: 1. 轻敲；轻拍；开发（潜力）；接通（水源）。例如: "He tapped his foot impatiently while waiting."
- **及物动词**: 1. 让（液体）从小孔流出；窃听。例如: "The detective tapped the suspect's phone."
- **不及物动词**: 1. 轻轻地跳或弹跳。例如: "The ball tapped off the wall and rolled away."

## 短语
- "tap into something" 意为利用或开发某资源。例如: "They're tapping into renewable energy sources."
- "tap dance" 是一种舞蹈形式，以脚尖和脚跟在地板上敲击出节奏。例如: "She's taking tap dancing lessons after school."
- "on tap" 通常表示随时可用或供应充足。例如: "There's beer on tap at the pub."

## 例句
- 名词: "Close the tap properly to avoid wasting water."
- 动词: "The bartender tapped a fresh pint of ale for the customer."
- 及物动词: "They tapped her potential as a leader in the company."
- 不及物动词: "The child tapped along to the rhythm of the music."

## 同义词
-敲击：knock, strike, pat
- 开发：exploit, harness, utilize
- 窃听：bug, eavesdrop, wiretap
