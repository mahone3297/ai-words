## 读音
/ˈlɑːftər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 笑声；笑闹；欢笑。例如: "Their laughter filled the room."
- **动词**: 大笑；发出笑声。例如: "She laughed so hard that tears came to her eyes."

## 短语
- "burst into laughter" 突然大笑。
- "laugh off" 以笑应对，轻松化解（问题或尴尬）。
- "at the expense of someone's laughter" 以某人的尴尬为乐。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children's innocent laughter was music to my ears."
- 动词: "They laughed at the clown's silly antics."

## 同义词
- Giggle
- Chuckle
- Titter
- Chortle
- Snicker
