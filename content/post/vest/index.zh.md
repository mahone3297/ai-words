## 读音
/vɛst/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 背心，内衣，通常无袖。例如: "He wore a black vest under his shirt."
  - (尤指英国)学生或职员的马甲，常与学校或工作制服搭配。例如: "The schoolboys wore their grey trousers and navy blue vests."
  - （某些体育活动中的）护胸，如拳击或摔跤。例如: "The boxer put on his gloves and vest before entering the ring."

- **动词**:
  - 穿上背心。例如: "He vested himself in a woolly vest for warmth."

## 短语
- "vested interest" 指某人对某个事物有既得利益。
- "body vest" 可指防弹背心或其他保护身体的装备。

## 例句
- 名词: "She layered a white vest over her blouse for extra warmth."
- 动词: "He always vests a T-shirt under his shirt during winter."
