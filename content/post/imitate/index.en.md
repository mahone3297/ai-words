## Phrases
- "Imitation is the sincerest form of flattery": This phrase suggests that when someone imitates another person, it is a genuine compliment because they admire them so much.
- "Monkey see, monkey do": This idiom implies that people tend to imitate what they see others doing without necessarily understanding why.
