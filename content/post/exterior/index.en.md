## Word: Exterior

## Pronunciation
/ɪkˈstɛriər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The outside part or surface of something; the external aspect. For example: "The house's exterior was painted in a light blue hue."
  - Also used to refer to the outer space or environment: "The spacecraft successfully made it to the Earth's exterior."
  
- **Adjective**: Situated or happening on the outside; pertaining to the outside. For example: "The exterior design of the car is sleek and modern."

## Phrases
- "Exterior walls" refer to the outer walls of a building.
- "Exterior paint" is specifically designed for use on outdoor surfaces.
- "Exterior shot" in filmmaking refers to a scene filmed outside rather than inside a studio or building.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "They renovated the exterior of their home to improve its curb appeal."
- **Adjective**: "The exterior doors were reinforced with steel for added security."

## Synonyms
- Outside
- Outer
- External
- Outdoors
- Surface
- Facade (in context of buildings)
- Appearance (when referring to outward show or look)
