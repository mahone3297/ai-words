## 读音
/steɪb(ə)l/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 稳定的；牢固的：指事物在物理、情感或经济上的稳定状态。例如: "The new government seems to have brought political stability to the region."
  - 马厩：一个用来饲养马匹的地方。例如: "The horses are kept in a large stable at the farm."
  
- **形容词**:
  - 不变的；恒定的：指不改变或持续的状态。例如: "The company's financial situation is quite stable over the past few years."
  - 心理健康的：指精神状态稳定，不易受情绪波动影响。例如: "She has been in a stable mental state since her recovery."

## 短语
- "stable economy" 指经济稳定，没有大的波动。
- "stable relationship" 指关系稳固，不易破裂的恋爱或友情关系。

## 例句
- 名词: "The stable economy has led to increased investments in the country."
- 形容词: "After years of therapy, he has achieved a stable mental state."
