## Word: Frequent

## Pronunciation
/ˈfrek.wənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Occurring often; happening repeatedly or habitually. For example: "She visits the library frequently."
  - _Usage_: "Frequent travelers often have elite status with airlines."
- **Verb**: (frequent something) To visit or attend regularly or often. For example: "He frequents the local coffee shop every morning."
  - _Usage_: "They frequent concerts and music festivals during summer months."

## Phrases
- "Frequent flyer": A person who travels by airplane often, usually a member of an airline's loyalty program.
- "Frequent visitor": A person who visits a place often or regularly.
- "Frequent occurrence": Something that happens regularly or repeatedly.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The park is a popular spot for frequent picnics among the locals."
- **Verb**: "During his college years, he frequently visited the campus library to study."
