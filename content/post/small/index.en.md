## Definitions
- **Adjective**: 1. Having a relatively small size, dimension, or quantity; not large. For example: "The small house was cozy."
- **Adjective**: 2. Characterized by minor importance, insignificance, or triviality. For example: "Her concerns were small matters compared to the global crisis."
- **Adverb**: In a small manner or degree. For example: "She spoke softly, almost in a whisper."

- "Small talk" refers to informal conversation about unimportant topics.
