## Pronunciation
/rɪˈdɪlɪ/

## Definitions
### Adverb
- Refers to the readiness or willingness to do something without hesitation or delay. For example: "She readily agreed to help."
- Describes how easily or quickly a task is performed. For example: "The instructions were so clear that anyone could complete them readily."

## Phrases
- "Readily available" means easily accessible or present.
- "Ready to assist" indicates being prepared to help without delay.
- "With ease" implies a smooth and effortless accomplishment, often with "readily."

## Example Sentences
- "He readily accepted the challenge, showing his determination."
- "The volunteers were readily on hand to help with the event."
- "She speaks French readily, having lived in Paris for several years."
