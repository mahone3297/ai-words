## 读音
/rɪˈdiːdli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 容易地；乐意地；迅速地。表示某事可以迅速、无困难或愉快地完成。

## 短语
- "readily available" 意为容易获取的。
- "readily accept" 意为欣然接受。
- "readily adaptable" 意为易于适应的。

## 例句
- "The information is readily accessible on the internet."
- "She readily agreed to help with the project."
- "He solved the problem readily, showing his expertise."
