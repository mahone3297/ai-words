## 读音
/ˈpʌzəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种需要解决的难题，通常涉及智力和逻辑。例如: "She spent hours working on that crossword puzzle."
- **动词**: 使困惑，使费解。例如: "The complex instructions puzzled me."
- **名词**: 用于拼装的游戏，如拼图。例如: "The children enjoyed putting together the jigsaw puzzle."

## 短语
- "Puzzle out" 意为解决或弄清楚某个问题。
- "Puzzle over something" 表示对某事深感困惑或努力理解。

## 例句
- 名词: "The kids were excited to find a new puzzle in their Christmas stocking."
- 动词: "The riddle puzzled even the smartest student in the class."
- 名词: "He spent his afternoon trying to complete the 1000-piece puzzle."
