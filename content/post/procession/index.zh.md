## 读音
/prəˈsesʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 有组织的队伍行进；列队：例如，"The wedding procession moved slowly down the aisle."
  2. （尤指宗教、官方或节日的）游行，行列：例如，"The annual carnival procession is a colorful spectacle."
  3. 进程，过程；一系列连续的动作或事件：例如，"The healing process can take time."

## 短语
- "Procession of events" 指一系列发生的事件。
- "宗教 procession" 指在宗教仪式中的列队行进。

## 例句
- 名词: "The royal procession was greeted by cheering crowds along the route."
- 名词: "The slow procession of cars caused a traffic jam in the city center."
- 名词: "The scientific research process involves several stages of experimentation and analysis."
