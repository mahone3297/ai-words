## Definitions
- **Noun**: A formal or ceremonial parade, often with a group moving through a particular route, typically consisting of people, vehicles, or floats, often marking an event or occasion. For example: "The Independence Day procession featured floats and marching bands."
- **Verb**: The act of proceeding, especially in a formal or ceremonial manner; to move or go through a series of events or steps. For example: "The bride and groom proceeded down the aisle together."
- **Noun (informal)**: A sequence or progression, often of ideas or actions. For example: "The lesson consisted of a logical procession of concepts."

## Phrases
- "Lead a procession" means to guide or direct a parade.
- "Join the procession" means to participate in a parade or ceremony.
- "Procession of thoughts" refers to a series of related ideas or considerations.
