## 读音
/træm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 有轨电车，一种在导轨上运行的城市交通工具。例如: "The tram was a popular means of transport in the city until it was replaced by buses."
- **动词**: (非标准用法，较少见) 操纵或驾驶有轨电车。例如: "He used to tram along the city's streets as a tram driver."

## 短语
- "tram network" 指一个城市的有轨电车系统。
- "tram tracks" 为有轨电车铺设的铁轨。

## 例句
- 名词: "The old tram depot has been converted into a museum."
- 动词: "He trammed his way through the busy streets, stopping at various stations."

## 同义词
- Streetcar
- Trolley
- Tramcar
- Light rail vehicle (LRV)
