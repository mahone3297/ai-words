## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Tram**: A public transport vehicle, usually powered by electricity, that runs on tracks, especially in urban areas. Example: "The tram line runs through the city center."
2. **Streetcar**: Another term for a tram, particularly in North America. Example: "The historic streetcars in San Francisco are popular with tourists."

### Verb
1. **To tram (a material)**: To move a large piece of stone or metal along a surface using a tram car or a similar device. Example: "The workers trammed the granite slabs across the workshop."
2. **To tram (an eye)**: In眼科, to adjust the position of the eyelid to improve vision. Example: "The doctor trammed her patient's eyelid to realign it properly."
