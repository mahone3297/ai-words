## 读音
/ˈlaɪən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 一种大型猫科动物，雄性有明显的鬃毛，通常生活在非洲草原和丛林中，是力量和勇猛的象征。例如: "The lion is the king of the jungle."
   - 常用来比喻强有力或有威严的人。例如: "He's known as the lion of the Senate."

## 短语
- "Lion's share" 意味着最大的一部分或份额。例如: "They got the lion's share of the profits."
- "Heart of a lion" 形容一个人有极大的勇气或决心。例如: "She faced her fears with the heart of a lion."

## 例句
- 名词: "The lion roared loudly as it spotted its prey in the distance."
- 比喻: "The CEO has the lion's roar when making important decisions."
