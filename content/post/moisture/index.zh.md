## 读音
/ˈmoɪstʃər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 湿气，湿度；微量水分。例如: "The high moisture content in the air made the room feel humid."
- **形容词**: 湿润的，含水分的。例如: "The soil was still moist after the rain."

## 短语
- "Moisturizer" 是保湿剂，常用于护肤产品。
- "Moist air" 指湿润的空气。
- "Keep something moist" 意为保持某物湿润。

## 例句
- 名词: "The plants need regular watering to maintain the necessary moisture in the soil."
- 形容词: "She applied a moisturizing cream to her dry skin."

## 同义词
- Humidity
- Wetness
- Dampness
- Hydration
- Saturation
