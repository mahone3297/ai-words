## 读音
/ˈskætər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 散布；散开：将物体分散到各处。例如: "The wind scattered the leaves all over the yard."
  - 遍及；扩散：遍布某个区域。例如: "News of the accident scattered quickly throughout the town."
  - 使（队伍）解散：在军事或组织中，使人员分散。例如: "At the sound of the alarm, the soldiers scattered."

- **名词**:
  - 散乱的东西：分散的物体或群体。例如: "The table was covered in a scatter of papers and books."
  - （射击游戏）散弹：一次射击中射出的多发子弹。例如: "He used a shotgun with a scatter pattern to hunt birds."

## 短语
- "scatter about" 意为到处散布。
- "scatter in all directions" 指向各个方向散开。
- "scatter seeds" 是指植物传播种子或种植者播种。

## 例句
- 动词: "The children scattered their toys all over the playroom."
- 名词: "The artist used a scatter of small stones to create an interesting texture in the painting."
