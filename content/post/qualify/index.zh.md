## 读音
/ˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 具备必要的条件或标准；符合要求。例如: "She qualified for the Olympics with her impressive performance."
2. （使）取得资格；（通过考试或训练）获得证书或学位。例如: "He qualified as a lawyer after years of study."
3. 限定；修饰（说法）。例如: "The statement needs to be qualified to take into account all possibilities."

## 短语
- "qualify for" 意为达到...的标准或要求。例如: "To qualify for the job, you need at least five years of experience."
- "qualify someone as" 描述某人的身份或特征。例如: "Her achievements qualify her as one of the greatest athletes of all time."

## 例句
- "He had to pass a series of exams to qualify as a pilot."
- "You don't qualify for the discount unless you make a purchase over $100."
- "Their victory qualified them for the regional championships."

## 同义词
- meet the requirements
- satisfy the conditions
- fulfill the criteria
- attain the standards
- reach the benchmark
