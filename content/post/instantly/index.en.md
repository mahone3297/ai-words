## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Happening or done immediately, without any delay; occurring or acting with great speed or rapidity. 

Example usage:
- **Adverb**: "The news spread instantly across social media."
- "She recognized him instantly when he walked into the room."
- "He responded instantly to the emergency call."

## Phrases
- "Instantly recognizable": Describing something that can be identified immediately upon seeing or hearing it.
- "To act instantly": To take action without hesitation or delay.
- "Instantly regretted": Feeling regret immediately after doing something.
