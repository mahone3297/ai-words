## 读音
/ˈleɪtli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 近来；最近。表示时间上的不久之前或当前状态。例如: "I've been working late lately." 或 "Lately, she's been feeling more confident."

## 短语
- "Lately, things have been going well" 意为最近事情进展顺利。

## 例句
- "Lately, I've noticed a change in his behavior." 
- "She's been exercising more lately, which has improved her health."

## 同义词
- Recently
- Recently
- Of late
- In recent times
- In the past few days/weeks/months
