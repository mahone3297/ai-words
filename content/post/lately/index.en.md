## Pronunciation
/ˈleɪtli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Recently; in the recent past; not long ago. It's often used to indicate that something has been happening more frequently or with greater intensity recently.

Example sentence (adverb): 
- "I've been feeling really tired lately."
- "Lately, he's been spending more time reading books."

## Phrases
- "Lately, it seems like..." is often used to introduce a new trend or observation about something that has changed or become more noticeable recently.
- "Not much lately" can be used when responding to questions about one's activities or state recently.

## Example Sentences
- "Lately, I've been waking up earlier than usual."
- "She hasn't been attending the meetings lately due to her busy schedule."
