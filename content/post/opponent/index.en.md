## Word: Opponent

## Pronunciation
/əˈpəʊnənt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person or thing that competes with or opposes another; an adversary or rival. For example: "The team faced a tough opponent in the championship match."

Example sentences:
- **Noun**: "She was the top-ranked tennis player, but her opponent put up a fierce fight."
- "He debated his political opponent on national television last night."
- "Fear is not an opponent to be defeated; it's a signal to be embraced and understood." (Here, 'opponent' metaphorically refers to fear.)

## Phrases
- "Face off against an opponent" means to confront or compete directly with someone or something.
- "Political opponents" refers to individuals or groups with differing political views who are competing for the same position or advocating opposite policies.
- "Team opponent" refers to another team one is competing against in sports or games.
