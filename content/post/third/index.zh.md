## 读音
/θɜːrd/

## 释义
- **数词**: 表示数字3，顺序为第一和第二之后。例如: "There are three apples on the table, the first, the second, and the third."

## 短语
- "Third time's the charm" 意味着第三次尝试通常会成功。
- "In third place" 指排名第三的位置。

## 例句
- "The third person in line will receive a free ticket."
- "She came third in the race, just behind the two frontrunners."

## 同义词
- Thirdly
- Number three
- Tertiary
- Thirdmost
- The next one after second
