## Definitions
### Noun
- **Number**: The third position in a series, coming after the first and second. For example: "The third child in line."
- **Ordinal**: Expressing a position in a sequence (1st, 2nd, 3rd). For example: "The third prize winner."
- **Adjective**: Used to describe something that is the one before the fourth in a series. For example: "The third largest city in the country."

### Adjective (qualifier)
- **Superlative**: Form of "many" or "much", expressing a high degree or ranking. For example: "The third-highest mountain in the world."
- **Relative**: In relation to a specified group or number. For example: "Third from the left."

## Phrases
- "In the third place" refers to being in the third position or rank.
- "The third time" indicates the third occurrence or iteration.
- "Third-degree burn" is a severe type of burn affecting all three layers of the skin.
