## 读音
/ˈlɔːri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种大型的、通常有开放式货箱的机动车辆，用于运输货物。例如: "The lorry carried a load of construction materials to the site."
- **名词 (在某些地区)**: 与卡车同义，用于运输大量物品。例如: "She saw a lorry on the highway loaded with boxes."

## 短语
- "Lorry driver" 指驾驶货车的人。
- "Lorry load" 指货车能承载的一批货物。

## 例句
- "The lorry driver carefully reversed into the loading bay."
- "The company hired extra lorries to cope with the increased demand during Christmas season."
