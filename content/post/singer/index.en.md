## Pronunciation
/sɪŋər/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A person who sings professionally or as a hobby.** A singer typically performs songs with their voice, often accompanied by musical instruments.
- **A vocal performer in various genres such as opera, pop, rock, or folk.**

### Verb
- To produce or perform songs using one's voice.
- To be the vocalist in a musical group or band.

## Phrases
- "Singer-songwriter" refers to an artist who writes and performs their own songs.
- "Up-and-coming singer" describes a new talent with potential for success.
- "Singing contest" is an event where individuals compete to showcase their singing abilities.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The famous singer's concert was sold out within hours."
- **Verb**: "She started her career as a singer after winning a local talent show."
- **Verb**: "The lead singer of the band stepped up to the microphone."

## Synonyms
- Vocalist
- Performer
- Artist
- Songster
- Crooner
- Chanteuse (for female singers)
- Troubadour (folk singer)
