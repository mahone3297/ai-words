## 读音
/ˈsɪŋər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 演唱者，歌手。例如: "She is a famous singer in the pop music industry."
- **名词**: 2. (尤指鸟)鸣禽。例如: "The nightingale is known for its beautiful singing."

## 短语
- "lead singer" 主唱，乐队中的主要歌手。
- "backup singer" 和声歌手，伴唱。
- "singing voice" 唱歌的声音。

## 例句
- 名词: "John has been a professional singer since he was a teenager."
- 名词: "The singer's sweet voice filled the concert hall."
