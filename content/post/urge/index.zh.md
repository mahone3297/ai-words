## 读音
/ɜːrɡ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 强烈地劝告或鼓励（某人）去做某事。例如: "The doctor urged him to quit smoking."
- **名词**: 强烈的欲望或冲动。例如: "She felt an urge to dance."

## 短语
- "urge on sb." 鼓励或催促某人做某事。例如: "Her parents urged her on to finish her homework."
- "an urge to do sth." 对做某事有强烈的欲望。例如: "He had an urge to travel after graduating."

## 例句
- 动词: "The coach urged his team to give their best in the final match."
- 名词: "She couldn't resist the urge to explore the unknown."
