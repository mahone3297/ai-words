## Pronunciation
/twaɪs/

## Definitions
### Adverb
- **1.** In reference to quantity or frequency: occurring or happening twice. For example: "She did the task twice to ensure accuracy."
- **2.** Exactly two times; doubly. For example: "He studied twice as hard this time."

## Phrases
- "Twice a day" refers to doing something once in the morning and once in the evening.
- "Twice over" means to do something twice or in duplicate.
- "Twice removed" indicates a relationship that is two degrees of separation.

## Example Sentences
- "I need to complete this project twice as fast to meet the deadline."
- "She visited her grandparents twice a month when she was a child."
- "The team played twice and won both matches."
