## 读音
/prəˈtektʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 防护；保护：行动或措施，旨在防止伤害、损害或攻击。例如: "The helmet provides protection for the rider's head."
  - 保护权：法律或政策赋予的保护，如版权或专利权。例如: "Copyright law provides protection for authors' original works."
  - 保护者：保护他人的人或事物。例如: "He is considered a protector of the environment."

## 短语
- "Personal protection" 指个人防护装备或措施。
- "Child protection services" 是指为保护儿童免受虐待和忽视的服务。
- "Trade protectionism" 指采取措施保护国内产业免受外国竞争的政策。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company invests in advanced security systems for the protection of its data."
- 名词: "The endangered species need the protection of the government to survive."
- 名词: "He works for an organization that provides protection to victims of domestic violence."
