## Pronunciation
/prəˈtekʃən/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Protection**: The act or state of protecting something or someone from harm, damage, or danger. It can also refer to measures taken for this purpose.
- **Security**: The state of being free from harm, risk, or danger, often in the context of personal safety or data.

### Verb
- **To protect**: To shield or guard someone or something from harm, injury, or potential threats.
- **To safeguard**: To take necessary actions to ensure the safety or well-being of something or someone.

### Adjective
- **Protected**: Sheltered from harm or danger; having security.
- **Defensive**: Aimed at preventing harm or attack.

### Adverb
- **Protectively**: In a manner designed to guard or defend.

## Phrases
- "Provide protection for" means to give safety or defense to.
- "Under protection" indicates that someone or something is being officially guarded or shielded.
- "Proper protection" emphasizes the need for effective safeguards.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The government implemented strict protection measures against cyber attacks."
- **Verb**: "She took her child to a protected area during the storm."
- **Adjective**: "The building's protected windows could withstand strong winds."
- **Adverb**: "He drove defensively, keeping a safe distance from other cars."
