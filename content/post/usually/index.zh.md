## 读音
/ˈjuːʒuəli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 通常，经常，常常，一般情况下。表示行为或情况发生的频率很高。

## 短语
- "usually do something" 指经常做某事，如 "I usually go for a run in the morning."
- "It's usually busy at this time of day" 表示在一天中的某个时段通常很忙。

## 例句
- "I usually wake up at 6 am to start my day."
- "The store is usually closed on Sundays."
- "He arrives at work early, but today he's running late, which is unusual."

- commonly
- often
