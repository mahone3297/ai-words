## 读音
/ˈləʊnli/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 孤独的，寂寞的，无人陪伴的。例如: "She felt lonely after her friends left."
- **副词**: 孤独地，寂寞地。例如: "He sat there lonely, wishing for company."

## 短语
- "Feel lonely" 意味着感到孤独或需要陪伴。
- "A lonely place" 可以指人迹罕至或寂静的地方。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The old man lived in a lonely cottage in the woods."
- 副词: "She walked along the deserted street, feeling very lonely."

- Alone
- Isolated
- Solitary
