## 读音
/træk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 轨道，痕迹：A path or mark left by something that has passed, such as the wheels of a vehicle.
  - 记录，轨迹：A record or trace of something, especially in electronic or digital form.
  - 音轨：A separate recording channel on a piece of music, film, or video.
  - 路线，航线：A course or direction followed.

- **动词**:
  - 追踪，跟踪：To follow or monitor the movements or actions of someone or something.
  - 保持在正确的路径上：To stay on course or on track.

## 短语
- "on track" 意为进展顺利或按计划进行。
- "lost track of" 意为失去了对某事物的追踪或忘记了关于某事的细节。
- "keep track of" 意为记录并了解某事物的最新情况。

## 例句
- 名词: "The train was running on its designated track."
- 动词: "The detective was tracking the suspect's movements through the city."
- 短语: "Our project is still on track for completion by next month."
