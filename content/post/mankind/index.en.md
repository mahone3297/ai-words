## Word: Mankind

## Pronunciation
/ˈmæn.kɪnd/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The human race regarded collectively; all human beings regarded as a group sharing common characteristics and interests. It typically refers to humankind without gender differentiation.

Example Sentences:
- _Mankind has achieved incredible advancements in science and technology._
- _The future of mankind depends on our ability to protect the environment._
- _Mankind's curiosity about space exploration led to numerous missions to the moon._

## Phrases
- "The betterment of mankind": Refers to improving the conditions, quality of life, or moral state of all people.
- "The history of mankind": Refers to the collective story or record of human existence from its earliest beginnings to the present day.
  
## Synonyms
- Humanity
- Humankind
- People
- Human race
- World
- Society
- Civilization
- Men and women (used when referring to both genders)
- Homo sapiens (in a biological context)
