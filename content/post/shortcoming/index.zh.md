## 读音
/ˈʃɔːrtkʌmɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 缺点；不足之处。指某人、事物或情况中存在的问题或缺陷。

## 短语
- "Address one's shortcomings" 意味着承认并改正自己的缺点。
- "To make up for a shortcoming" 是弥补不足的意思。

## 例句
- "His shortcoming in public speaking often holds him back in his career."
- "She recognized her shortcoming in math and started taking extra classes."
- "The software has some performance shortcomings that need to be addressed."

## 同义词
- Flaw
- Weakness
- Defect
- Drawback
- Inadequacy
