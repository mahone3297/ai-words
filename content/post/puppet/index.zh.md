## 读音
/pʌpɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 木偶，傀儡，玩偶。例如: "The child was playing with a puppet."
- **动词**: 控制像傀儡一样，操纵。例如: "He puppeteered the situation to his advantage."
- **形容词**: 像傀儡一样的，受人操纵的。例如: "The politician was seen as a puppet of the ruling party."

## 短语
- "puppet show" 意为木偶戏。
- "puppet government" 指的是受外国控制的政权。
- "puppet master" 是指控制傀儡的人或操纵者。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were mesmerized by the marionette puppet's performance."
- 动词: "She felt like her boss was puppeteering every decision she made."
- 形容词: "He was just a puppet, carrying out the orders of his superiors."
