## Definitions
### Noun
- **Ripe fruit**: A fruit that has fully matured and is ready to be picked or eaten. For example: "The garden was filled with ripe tomatoes."
- **Verb**: To become fully developed or mature, especially in regard to a fruit or vegetable. For example: "The apples on the tree are starting to ripen."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of something that has reached its peak in maturity or quality. For example: "Her writing has ripened over the years."
