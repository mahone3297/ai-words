## 读音
/ˈrɪpən/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 使成熟；使熟：例如 "The sun helps to ripen the tomatoes."
  - 成熟；熟透：例如 "The fruit is just beginning to ripen."

## 短语
- "ripen up" 意味着变得成熟或准备好使用：例如 "The apples will ripen up in a few days."
- "ripe for something" 表示适合或准备好做某事：例如 "The market is ripe for new innovations."

## 例句
- 动词: "The grapes on the vine are slowly ripening with the warm weather."
- 成熟： "When the bananas are fully ripened, they become very sweet."
