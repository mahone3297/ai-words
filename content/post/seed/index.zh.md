## 读音
/siːd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 种子，植物的种子，用于繁殖。例如: "The farmer planted seeds in the spring."
    - 初始的想法或概念，起源。例如: "The seed of the story came from a real-life event."

- **动词**:
    - 播种，种植。例如: "They seeded the field with wheat."
    - 在某种环境中引入或植入（想法、信息等）。例如: "The campaign seeded social media with positive messages."

## 短语
- "seed money" 指初创企业的初期投资。
- "seed round" 在创业投资中指第一轮融资。
- "to seed a tournament" 在体育比赛中，是指将参赛队伍按排名分配到不同的位置。

## 例句
- 名词: "The apple tree produced many seeds that could grow into new trees."
- 动词: "The gardeners seeded the vegetable patch with tomatoes and cucumbers."

## 同义词
- Plant
- Sow
- Germinate
- Propagate
- Spawn (in certain contexts)
