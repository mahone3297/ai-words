## 读音
/θɪn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 瘦的，细长的，薄的。例如: "She is a thin woman with delicate features."
- **动词**: 使变瘦，减少。例如: "The diet has helped him thin down significantly."
- **副词**: 稀疏地，少量地。例如: "The hair on his head thins as he gets older."

## 短语
- "thin out" 意味着减少或稀释。例如: "They decided to thin out their collection of books."
- "thin-skinned" 形容人容易受伤害或敏感。例如: "She's rather thin-skinned and easily offended."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The model has a thin figure that sets fashion trends."
- 动词: "The plants need regular watering or they'll thin out."
- 副词: "The paint was applied so thinly that the wood grain was still visible."
