## 读音
/riːˈstreɪnt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 控制，约束，抑制。例如: "The prisoner was kept under restraint."
- **动词**: 避免冲动或行为，控制。例如: "She restrained herself from shouting at him."
- **形容词**: 克制的，有节制的。例如: "His restraint in the situation was admirable."

## 短语
- "physical restraint" 意为身体上的限制或束缚。
- "self-restraint" 指自我控制或自我约束。

## 例句
- 名词: "The doctor advised him to use restraint when dealing with the patient."
- 动词: "The police had to restrain the angry crowd to prevent a riot."
- 形容词: "Her restrained behavior showed great maturity."
