## 读音
/waɪ/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示疑问，询问原因。例如: "Why are you late?"
- **连词**: 引导原因状语从句。例如: "I'm sorry, I can't come to the party because why I have to work late."

## 短语
- "No reason why" 意为没有特定的原因。
- "There's no need why" 表示没有必要做某事。

## 例句
- 副词: "Why did you decide to change your job?"
- 连词: "I didn't go to the gym yesterday, because why I wasn't feeling well."

## 同义词
- For what reason
- What for
- How come
- In what way
- The reason being
