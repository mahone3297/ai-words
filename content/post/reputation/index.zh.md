## 读音
/rɪˈpreʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个人、团体或事物所获得的普遍看法或声誉。例如: "He built a good reputation for his honesty."
- **动词**: 形成或赢得某种声誉。例如: "The company strives to reputation itself as an environmentally friendly business."

## 短语
- "reputation management" 指维护和塑造公众对个人或组织的看法。
- "to have a bad/good reputation" 意味着有负面或正面的名声。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her reputation as a top chef precedes her wherever she goes."
- 动词: "They have successfully reputationed themselves as experts in their field."
