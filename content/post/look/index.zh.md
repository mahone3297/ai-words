## 读音
/luːk/

## 释义
- **动词**: 看，查看，注视。例如: "He looked at the painting carefully."
- **名词**: 眼神，表情。例如: "She gave him a curious look."
- **不及物动词**: 出现，显得。例如: "The sun began to look through the clouds."

## 短语
- "Take a look" 意为看一看，检查一下。例如: "Take a look at this document and tell me what you think."
- "Give someone a look" 指看向某人。例如: "She gave him a disapproving look."
- "Look out" 用于警告或提醒注意。例如: "Look out! There's a car coming."

## 例句
- 动词: "She looked up from her book when I entered the room."
- 名词: "His worried look made me concerned about the situation."
- 不及物动词: "The sky looks like it might rain later."
