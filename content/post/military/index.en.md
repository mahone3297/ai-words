## Word: Military

## Pronunciation
/ˈmɪlɪtɛri/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to armed forces or war; concerning the profession of arms or service in the army, navy, air force, etc. For example: "Military service is mandatory in some countries."
- **Noun (collective)**: The military refers to the combined armed forces of a country, including its soldiers, sailors, airmen, and marines. For example: "The military is a crucial component of national defense."

### Adjective Definition Examples
- *Military strategy*: Plans and tactics used by the armed forces in warfare.
- *Military discipline*: Strict obedience to rules and orders within the armed forces.

### Noun Definition Examples
- *The military parade showcased new technology.*
- *"Joining the military was his lifelong ambition."*

## Phrases
- *"Military intelligence" refers to information gathered for military purposes.*
- *"Military coup"* describes a sudden and illegal seizure of power, usually by a faction within the military.
- *"Military academy"* is a college that prepares students for service in the armed forces.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "He wore a military uniform with pride."
- **Noun**: "The government increased funding for the military to modernize its weaponry."

## Synonyms
- Armed forces
- Troops
- Service members
- War machine
- Fighting forces
- Defence forces

## Antonyms
- Civilian (as opposed to military personnel)
