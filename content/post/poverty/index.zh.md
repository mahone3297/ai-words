## 读音
/ˈpɑːvəri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 贫困；贫穷。指缺乏基本的生活必需品和资源的状态。例如: "Poverty is a major issue in many developing countries."
- **形容词**: 贫穷的；贫乏的。形容缺乏财富或资源。例如: "The family lived in poverty after losing their jobs."

## 短语
- "Poverty line" 是指划分贫困与非贫困的收入标准线。
- "Deep poverty" 指极度贫困，即收入远低于贫困线的情况。
- "Eradicate poverty" 意为消除贫困。

## 例句
- 名词: "The government has launched a new initiative to alleviate poverty in rural areas."
- 形容词: "Many children in this region suffer from poverty-related malnutrition."

## 同义词
- Destitution
- Indigence
- Hardship
- Want
- Impoverishment
