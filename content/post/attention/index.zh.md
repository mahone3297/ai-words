## 读音
/əˈtenʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 注意，注意力，关注。例如: "Pay attention to the details."
- **动词**: 给予注意，专心于。例如: "She was trying to attract his attention."

## 短语
- "Draw attention to" 意思是使某事受到注意。例如: "The bright colors draw attention to the advertisement."
- "Give one's undivided attention" 意味着全神贯注。例如: "During the lecture, he gave the speaker his undivided attention."

## 例句
- 名词: "Losing focus, he lost touch with the main point of the lecture, and his attention wandered."
- 动词: "The teacher raised her voice to get the students' attention."

- Focus
- Concentration
