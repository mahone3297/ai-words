## Pronunciation
/wɛɪ/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: n. (v) To determine the mass or weight of something by using a scale or balance. (v) To have importance or significance; to carry meaning or influence. (v) To consider or assess the value or impact of something.
- **Noun**: The act of weighing something, or the result of that measurement.

## Phrases
- "Weigh something" means to measure its mass or heaviness.
- "The weight of a person" refers to their body mass.
- "A matter of great/little weight" signifies something important/unimportant.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "I need to weigh the ingredients before mixing them."
- **Noun**: "His words carried a lot of weight in the decision-making process."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "She felt the weight of responsibility on her shoulders."
