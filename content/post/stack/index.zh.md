## 读音
/stæk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 堆；叠；堆积物。例如: "He built a tall stack of books on the desk."
   2. (计算机科学) 栈，一种数据结构，按照后进先出(LIFO)原则存储元素。

- **动词**: 把东西堆起来。例如: "She stacked the plates neatly in the cupboard."

## 短语
- "stack up" 意为积累，大量存在。例如: "The bills have been stacking up all month."
- "stack against" 表示对某人不利。例如: "The odds are stacked against us winning the game."

## 例句
- 名词: "The factory chimney released a huge stack of smoke into the air."
- 动词: "He quickly stacked the boxes in the corner to create more space."
