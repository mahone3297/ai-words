## 读音
/rɪˈmembər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 记住；回忆。例如: "I'll try to remember your name."
  - 提醒；使想起。例如: "The smell of pine trees always reminds me of Christmas."

- **名词**:
  - 回忆；记忆。例如: "Those old photos are precious memories."

## 短语
- "Remember to do something" 表示要记住做某事。例如: "Remember to lock the door when you leave."
- "In memory of" 用于纪念某人或某事。例如: "There's a statue in the park in memory of the war heroes."

## 例句
- 动词: "Can you remember where we parked the car?"
- 名词: "The old song brought back many fond memories."

- Retain
- Bear in mind
