## 读音
/ˌlɪmɪˈteɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 限制；局限；局限性。例如: "The only limitation on his freedom was the law."
- **动词**: 限制；限定。例如: "This policy limits the number of visitors per day."

## 短语
- "Without limitations" 没有限制。
- "Impose limitations on something" 对某事施加限制。
- "The limitations of a study" 一个研究的局限性。

## 例句
- 名词: "The main limitation of this technology is its high cost."
- 动词: "The government has decided to limit the use of plastic bags."
