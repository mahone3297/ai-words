## 读音
/ˈriːdɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 阅读；读物。例如: "I spent the afternoon in the library, enjoying some peaceful reading."
  - 读数；显示值。例如: "The thermometer showed a reading of 37°C."
  - （尤指戏剧、电影的）剧本朗读会。例如: "There will be a reading of the new play next week."

- **动词**:
  - 阅读（文字、书籍等）。例如: "She loves to read books on history."
  - （指人）识字。例如: "Can you read this sign?"

## 短语
- "Reading comprehension" 表示阅读理解能力。
- "Speed reading" 是快速阅读的意思。
- "Reading aloud" 指大声朗读。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher assigned us a challenging reading from Shakespeare's plays."
- 动词: "She reads the newspaper every morning over breakfast."
- 读数: "Check the oil level and make a note of the reading."
