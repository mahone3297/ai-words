## 读音
/ˈdɪfɪkəlti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 难度；复杂性；不易处理的事物。例如: "The project faced numerous technical difficulties."
- **形容词**: 困难的；艰难的。例如: "She had difficulty understanding the advanced math concepts."

## 短语
- "In spite of the difficulties" 尽管有困难。
- "Have difficulty in doing something" 在做某事上有困难。

## 例句
- 名词: "Overcoming these financial difficulties will require careful planning."
- 形容词: "The exam was so difficult that few students passed."
