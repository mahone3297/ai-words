## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The act of repeating something, especially words or sounds. "She practiced her speech for hours to avoid repetition errors."
2. The quality or state of being repeated; recurrence. "The book's theme is marked by its repetitive structure."
3. A copy or duplication of something. "The museum has a fine collection of antique replicas, showcasing repetition in art."

### Verb
1. To repeat something (words, actions, or a sequence). "He repeated the instructions to make sure everyone understood."
2. To do something over and over again. "She repetitively tapped her foot while waiting."

### Adjective
1. Characterized by or involving repetition. "A repetitive pattern can be found in the wallpaper."
2. Done or expressed repeatedly. "The teacher emphasized the importance of practice to avoid repetition."
