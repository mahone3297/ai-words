## 读音
/rɪˈpɛtɪʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 重复的行为或过程；复述；再现。例如: "The child practiced the piano piece for hours without repetition."
- **形容词**: 重复的；重叠的。例如: "The repetition of the same mistake was frustrating."

## 短语
- "in repetition" 意为反复地，连续不断地。
- "repetition of errors" 表示错误的重复发生。
- "mindless repetition" 指无思考的机械重复。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher emphasized the importance of learning through repetition."
- 形容词: "The workout involved a series of repetitive exercises to build muscle memory."
