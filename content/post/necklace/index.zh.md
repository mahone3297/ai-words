## 读音
/ˈneklɛs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 项链，一种戴在脖子上的装饰品，通常由金属、珠宝或其他贵重材料制成。例如: "She wore a beautiful diamond necklace to the party."

## 短语
- "necklace of pearls" 指由珍珠串成的项链。
- "gold necklace" 金项链，由黄金制成的项链。

## 例句
- "The necklace was a family heirloom passed down through generations."
- "She elegantly adorned her neck with a delicate silver necklace."

## 同义词
- Chain (in the context of jewelry)
- Pendant (when referring to a necklace with a hanging ornament)
- Bracelet (for a similar type of jewelry, though worn on the wrist)
- Earrings (another form of jewelry, but for the ears)
