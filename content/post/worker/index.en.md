## Pronunciation
/ˈwɜːkə(r)/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
    - A person who performs work, especially as an employee or tradesperson. Example: "The factory workers were assembling cars."
    - A member of a labor union. Example: "The union representative spoke up on behalf of the workers."
- **Verb**: To perform work or engage in labor. Example: "She works long hours at her office job."
- **Adjective**: Relating to or involving work. Example: "The ergonomic design of the worker's tools."

## Phrases
- "Do a day's work" means to complete a full day's labor.
- "Hard worker" is a compliment for someone who is diligent and committed to their tasks.
- "Team worker" describes someone who collaborates effectively with others.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The construction workers were putting up scaffolding around the building site."
- **Verb**: "He's a dedicated worker, always striving to improve his skills."
- **Adjective**: "The company recognized the outstanding worker of the month for their contributions."
