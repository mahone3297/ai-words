## 读音
/spiːtʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 口头表达；演说，演讲。例如: "He delivered an inspiring speech on climate change."
- **动词**: 发表演讲，说话。例如: "She was too nervous to speech in front of the large audience."

## 短语
- "Public speech" 指公开场合的讲话或演讲。
- "Speech impediment" 指语言障碍，如口吃。
- "Speech therapy" 是指语言治疗，帮助改善语言和发音问题。

## 例句
- 名词: "The president's speech focused on economic reforms."
- 动词: "She practiced her acceptance speech for hours before the ceremony."
