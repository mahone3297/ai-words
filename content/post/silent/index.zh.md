## 读音
/ˈsaɪlənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 没有声音的，寂静的。例如: "The library was completely silent."
- **副词**: 安静地，无声地。例如: "She whispered the answer silently."

## 短语
- "in silence" 表示在没有声音或安静的状态下。
- "to keep silent" 意味着保持沉默，不说话。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The movie theater was so silent that you could hear a pin drop."
- 副词: "He listened to the recording silently, trying to catch every detail."

## 同义词
- Quiet
- Still
- Mute
- Hushed
- Unvoiced
