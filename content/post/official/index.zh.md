## 读音
/əˈfɪʃəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 具有官方身份或地位的；正式的；官方认可的。例如: "He is an official representative of the government."
- **名词**: 官员；公务员；工作人员。例如: "The officials were in charge of organizing the event."

## 短语
- "official language" 指国家或地区官方承认的语言。
- "official statement" 官方声明或公告。
- "official record" 正式记录或官方文件。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The official ceremony will be held at the city hall."
- 名词: "The officials announced the results of the election."
