## 读音
/əˈbændən/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 放弃；抛弃。例如: "They abandoned their plans to travel abroad due to the pandemic."
2. 遗弃；离弃。例如: "The ship's crew was forced to abandon the sinking vessel."
3. 放任；听任。例如: "He abandoned himself to grief after his wife's death."

- **名词**:
被遗弃的状态或行为。例如: "The deserted house had an air of neglect and abandon."

## 短语
- "Abandon hope, all ye who enter here" 是地狱之门上的一句警示语，意为“放弃希望，凡进入者”。
- "Abandon ship" 是紧急情况下指示弃船的命令。

## 例句
- 动词: "Despite his initial reluctance, he eventually abandoned his old career to pursue art full-time."
- 名词: "The orphan felt the full weight of abandon when his parents left him at the shelter."
