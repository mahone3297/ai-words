## 读音
/step/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 脚步；步子：A movement made by lifting one foot off the ground and setting it down again in a new position, as in walking or dancing.
  2. （楼梯的）级：One of a series of risers and treads that form a flight of stairs.

- **动词**:
  1. 踏出，行走：To move by lifting and setting down one's foot.
  2. （舞蹈中）迈步，跳：To perform a step or series of steps in a dance.

- **形容词**:
  1. 步行的，与脚步有关的：Relating to or involving stepping or walking.
  2. 进行中的，逐步的：Done or happening in a series of stages.

## 短语
- "on the step" 指台阶上或门口。
- "step up" 意为增加努力或提高速度。
- "step in" 表示介入或接手某个情况。

## 例句
- 名词: "He climbed up ten steps before reaching the landing."
- 动词: "She stepped lightly across the room to avoid waking the baby."
- 形容词: "The project is in its first step, so we need to be patient."
