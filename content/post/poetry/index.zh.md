## 读音
/pəˈtrɪəri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 文学形式，诗歌，以韵律和形象表达情感和思想的艺术。例如: "She writes beautiful poetry about nature."
    - 诗作，一首诗。例如: "The poet recited his latest poetry at the literary event."

## 短语
- "Poetry reading" 指诗歌朗诵活动。
- "Free verse" 是一种没有固定韵律或格律的现代诗歌形式。
- "Haiku" 是日本传统的三行诗形式。

## 例句
- "The students were asked to write a poem for their poetry class."
- "Robert Frost is known for his evocative poetry about rural life."
- "She performed a powerful poetry reading about love and loss."
