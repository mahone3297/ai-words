## 读音
/oʊk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种硬木树，学名为Quercus，通常指橡树。例如: "The mighty oak tree stood tall in the forest."
- **形容词**: 指由橡木制成的或具有橡木特性的。例如: "The oak furniture was both sturdy and elegant."

## 短语
- "Oak tree" 指橡树本身。
- "Oak wood" 用来指橡木制成的木材。
- "Oak-aged wine" 是指在橡木桶中陈酿的葡萄酒。

## 例句
- 名词: "The park was known for its ancient oak trees."
- 形容词: "The dining table was made of solid oak, giving it a timeless appeal."
