## 读音
/ləˈdi/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 女士；女性；夫人。例如: "The lady at the counter was very helpful."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 1. 女性的；淑女般的。例如: "ladylike manners"

## 短语
- "ladies' room" 指的是女性洗手间。
- "a lady of high society" 指的是贵族或上流社会的女士。
- "lady luck" 用来象征好运或幸运女神。

## 例句
- 名词: "The lady in red caught everyone's attention when she entered the room."
- 形容词: "She always carries herself with ladylike grace."
