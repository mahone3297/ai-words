## 读音
/ˈwɪdθ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 宽度；幅度；广度。物体或空间从一边到另一边的距离。例如: "The width of the river is 50 meters."
- **形容词**: 宽的；宽阔的。描述物体或空间的宽度特征。例如: "This table has a width of one meter."

## 短语
- "width measurement" 表示宽度测量。
- "maximum width" 指最大宽度。
- "width-to-height ratio" 是宽度与高度的比例。

## 例句
- 名词: "The doorframe's width allows only one person to pass through at a time."
- 形容词: "The new TV screen has a wider viewing angle than the old one."
