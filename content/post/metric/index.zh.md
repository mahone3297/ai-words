## 读音
/məˈtrɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 与米制单位或公制系统相关的。例如: "The US uses both imperial and metric systems for measurements."
- **名词**: 公制单位系统。例如: "In the metric system, length is measured in meters."

## 短语
- "Metric system" 是指公制单位系统。
- "Metric units" 包括克、米、升等不同类型的计量单位。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The recipe calls for 250 grams of flour, which is a metric measurement."
- 名词: "In most countries, the metric system is used for everyday measurements."
