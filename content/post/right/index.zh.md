## 读音
/raɪt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 正确的，准确的。例如: "The answer is right in front of you."
- **副词**: 恰恰，正好。例如: "I live right around the corner."
- **名词**: 权利，权益。例如: "Everyone has the right to free speech."
- **动词**: 使正确，修正。例如: "Can you right the ship before it capsizes?"
- **介词**: 在右边。例如: "Sit down on the chair to my right."

## 短语
- "Do it right" 做对，做好。
- "In the right" 指处于正确的一方或有理的一方。
- "All rights reserved" 版权所有。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Your solution is completely right."
- 副词: "I need you here right now."
- 名词: "He fought for workers' rights."
- 动词: "The teacher righted the tilted desk."
- 介词: "The store is located on the right side of the street."
