## 读音
/ˈsəː.bjuəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 可溶解的；能溶于液体的。例如: "This type of sugar is soluble in water."
- **动词**: 使溶解；使可溶。例如: "The chemical solvent dissolves even the toughest substances."

## 短语
- "soluble in": 表示某物能在某液体中溶解。例如: "Salt is soluble in water."
- "insoluble in": 表示某物不能在某液体中溶解。例如: "Silver is insoluble in water."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The medicine comes in a soluble form for easy consumption."
- 动词: "The cleaner can dissolve grease easily."

## 同义词
- Dissolvable
- Solvable
- Liable to dissolve
- Malleable (在某些上下文中，指容易处理或改变)
