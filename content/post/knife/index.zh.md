## 读音
/knaɪf/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种有刀刃和柄的小刀，通常用于切割食物或其他物品。例如: "He used a sharp knife to cut the bread."
- **动词 (v)**: 使用刀切割。例如: "She knife-d through the packaging with ease."

## 短语
- "a butter knife" 用来抹黄油的小刀。
- "a steak knife" 用于切肉的刀。
- "knife edge" 刀刃般尖锐的边缘。

## 例句
- 名词: "He kept a small knife in his pocket for emergencies."
- 动词: "She knife-d through the cake with great precision."

## 同义词
- Blade
- Cutter
- Scalpel
- Dagger
- Sword (在某些语境下，长刀或剑可作为刀的同义词)
