## Pronunciation
/statjuː/

## Definitions
### Noun
A statue is a solid, often lifelike representation of a person, animal, or object, typically made of stone, metal, or other durable materials. It is a sculptural work meant to depict a particular subject or scene.

### Verb
To statue, in a figurative sense, can mean to immortalize or create a lasting image or representation of someone or something through a statue or sculpture.

### Adjective
Statuesque refers to having a tall, dignified, or impressive appearance, reminiscent of a statue.
