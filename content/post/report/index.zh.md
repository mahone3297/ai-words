## 读音
/rɪˈpɔrt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 报告；汇报：一份详细说明情况或结果的文件或演讲。例如: "She presented her final report on the project."
  - 新闻报道：关于事件或情况的新闻或信息。例如: "The evening news included a report on the election results."
  - 学术报告：学生提交的作业，通常是对研究或实验的总结。例如: "The students had to write a 5-page report on their summer research."

- **动词**:
  - 报告；告知：传达信息或情况给他人。例如: "I reported the accident to the police."
  - 记录；记载：将信息或观察结果记录下来。例如: "The journalist reported the events as they unfolded."

## 短语
- "File a report" 提交或填写报告。
- "Give a verbal report" 口头汇报。
- "Report for duty" 到达指定地点开始工作。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's annual report showed significant growth in profits."
- 动词: "He reported the theft to the authorities immediately."
- 作为引述动词： "The newspaper reported that the negotiations had failed."
