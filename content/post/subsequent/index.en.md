## Pronunciation
/subˈsɛkwənt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Occurring or coming after another; following in time or order. For example: "The subsequent events were unexpected."
- **Noun**: A thing that comes after another; a later or following event or occurrence. For example: "The subsequent meeting was scheduled for next week."
- **Verb**: To follow (something) in time or sequence; to come after. For example: "The project was completed, and subsequent tasks were assigned."

## Phrases
- "Subsequent developments" refers to events that happen after something else.
- "As a result of the previous discussion, subsequent changes were made."
- "There were no subsequent complaints about the service."

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The subsequent chapters in the book explore deeper themes."
- **Noun**: "After the initial presentation, we had a subsequent round of questions."
- **Verb**: "The company's profits increased subsequent to the new marketing strategy."
