## 读音
/subˈsɛkwənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 发生在后，随后的，接着的。例如: "The subsequent events were unexpected."
- **名词**: 后续的事物，结果。例如: "The subsequent report detailed the aftermath of the accident."

## 短语
- "subsequent to" 表示“在…之后发生，紧接着”。例如: "Subsequent to the meeting, we received a formal proposal."
- "subsequent developments" 指的是后来的发展或进展。例如: "There have been several significant subsequent developments in the field."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The subsequent meeting was more productive than the previous one."
- 名词: "The subsequent report on the investigation provided new insights."
