## Pronunciation
/send/

## Definitions
### Verb
- **Noun**: The action or act of sending something or transmitting it from one place to another. For example: "I sent a letter to my friend."
- **Verb**: To dispatch or convey (a message, information, or goods) by mail, email, or other means. For example: "She sent me an important document via email."
- **Verb**: To convey a thought, feeling, or message through a medium like a signal or gesture. For example: "He sent his apologies with a bouquet of flowers."

### Adjective
- Refers to the act of sending or the quality of being dispatched or conveyed. For example: "The package was sent express."

## Phrases
- "Send something to someone" means to deliver an item or information to that person.
- "Sent out invitations" is the act of distributing invitations.
- "Send your regards" is a way to express affection or best wishes on behalf of someone.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "I need you to send the report to the manager by 5 PM."
- **Adjective**: "The company's policy is to send all customer inquiries within 24 hours."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "I'll send you a text when I arrive at the airport."
