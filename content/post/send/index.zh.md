## 读音
/send/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 发送；传递：例如 "I just sent an email to my boss."
  - 打发；派遣：例如 "The company sent him on a business trip."
  - （通过无线电、电话等）传达：例如 "She sent a message via radio."

## 短语
- "Send off" 意为送行，例如: "We had a party to send off our friend who was moving abroad."
- "Send for" 表示召唤或派人去取，例如: "If you need a doctor, send for one immediately."

## 例句
- 动词: "Please send the report to me by tomorrow."
- "The astronaut sent a message back to Earth from space."
