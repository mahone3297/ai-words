## 读音
/ˈpɔːr/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 贫穷的，贫乏的，不足的。例如: "The family was too poor to afford medical treatment."
- **副词**: 糟糕地，可怜地。例如: "He played poor in the match."
- **名词**: 贫困的人，穷人。例如: "The charity helps the poor in the community."

## 短语
- "Living in poverty" 指生活在贫困之中。
- "Poor health" 指健康状况不佳。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Despite working hard, they still live in poor conditions."
- 副词: "Her performance was poor compared to her usual standard."
- 名词: "The government launched a program to aid the poor."
