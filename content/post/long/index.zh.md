## 读音
/ læŋ /

## 释义
- **形容词**: 长的，具有相当长度的。例如: "The river is very long."
- **副词**: 长久地，长时间。例如: "We haven't seen each other for a long time."

## 短语
- "For a long time" 意为持续很长时间。
- "As long as" 表示只要...就...。例如: "As long as you're happy, I'm happy too."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The sofa is so long that it covers half of the living room."
- 副词: "She studied for a long time before taking the exam."

## 同义词
- Lengthy
- Extended
- Drawn-out
- Prolonged
- Lengthwise
