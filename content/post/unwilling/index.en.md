## Pronunciation
/unˈwɪlɪŋ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Referring to a person who is not willing or reluctant to do something. For example: "He was unwilling to accept the invitation."
- **Verb**: To be or show a lack of willingness. For example: "She unwillingly agreed to the terms."

## Phrases
- "Unwilling participant" describes someone who is not eager or voluntary in a situation.
- "Reluctant to comply" means hesitant to follow a request or instruction.
- "Unwillingness to change" implies resistance to making adjustments.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Despite her unwillingness to leave, she eventually agreed to go."
- **Verb**: "He unwillingly submitted his resignation letter."
- "Her unwillingness to confront the issue was causing problems."
