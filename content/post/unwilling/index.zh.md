## 读音
/unɪˈwɪlɪŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不愿意的，不情愿的，不愿意做某事的。例如: "She was unwilling to accept the offer."
- **副词**: 不愿意地，勉强地。例如: "He unwillingly agreed to help."

## 短语
- "unwilling participant" 表示不愿意参加的人。
- "be unwilling to do something" 意为不愿意做某事。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Despite his talent, he was unwilling to join the team."
- 副词: "She signed the contract unwillingly, knowing it wasn't in her best interest."

## 同义词
- Reluctant
- Averse
- Disinclined
- Hesitant
- Unwilling to do (in the sense of not wanting to do something)
