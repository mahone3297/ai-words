## 读音
/wɛnˈevər/

## 释义
- **连词**: 表示在任何时候，无论何时。例如: "You can call me whenever you need help."
- **副词**: 相当于 "at any time" 或 "no matter when"。例如: "Whenever she visits, we have a great time."

## 短语
- "Whenever possible" 意为只要有可能或任何时候都可以。
- "Whenever you're ready" 是一种邀请或等待对方准备好的表达。

## 例句
- 连词: "Whenever I feel stressed, I take a walk in the park."
- 副词: "The concert starts at 8, but come whenever."
