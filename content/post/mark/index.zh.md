## 读音
/ˈmɑːrk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 符号，记号，标记：A small sign or symbol used to indicate something.
   - （品质、成绩等的）评分，分数：A grade or point given for quality or performance.
   - 品牌，商标：A distinctive name or symbol identifying a product or company.
   - 个人特征，特色：A characteristic or feature that distinguishes someone.

- **动词**:
   - 在…上做标记：To put a mark on something.
   - 记录，记载：To note down or register an event or fact.
   - 评价，评估：To assign a grade or score to someone's work.

## 短语
- "Mark the spot" 意为标记位置。
- "Make your mark" 指的是在某个领域取得成就。
- "Mark time" 表示原地踏步，保持现状。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher put a checkmark next to the correct answers."
- 动词: "She marked each page with a pencil line for future reference."
- "The brand's mark is recognized worldwide."
