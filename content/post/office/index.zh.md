## 读音
/ˈɒfɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 办公室，指工作或行政管理的房间或建筑物。例如: "He works in a high-rise office building."
  - 职务，职责，官方职位。例如: "She holds the office of the president."
  - 公司，机构，办事处。例如: "They have just opened a new office in New York."

- **动词**:
  - 在办公室工作，执行公务。例如: "I have to office from 9am to 5pm every day."

## 短语
- "work in an office" 意为在办公室里工作。
- "the post office" 特指邮局。
- "city hall office" 指市政厅的办公室。

## 例句
- 名词: "The office manager is responsible for overseeing all daily operations."
- 动词: "She's currently officeing from home due to the pandemic."
