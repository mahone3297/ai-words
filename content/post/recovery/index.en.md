## Pronunciation
/rɪˈkʌvəri/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The act of regaining health, strength, or normal function after an illness, injury, or period of decline. For example: "After the surgery, her recovery was slow but steady."
- **Verb**: The process of getting well again or returning to a normal state after an illness, injury, or setback. For example: "The patient's recovery was monitored closely by the medical team."
- **Adjective**: Restored to good condition or normal functioning; recuperative. For example: "The recovery period required a lot of patience and hard work."

## Phrases
- "Full recovery" indicates a complete return to health or normal functioning.
- "Speedy recovery" is used to wish someone a rapid return to wellness.
- "Post-recovery" refers to the period after a person has recovered from an illness or injury.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "His recovery from the flu was a long and challenging process."
- **Verb**: "She started physical therapy to aid in her recovery from the accident."
- **Adjective**: "The recovery rate for COVID-19 patients is encouraging."
