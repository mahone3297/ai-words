## 读音
/rɪˈkʌvəri/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 恢复，复原：指身体、经济、环境等方面的恢复到正常或健康状态。例如: "After the operation, he made a rapid recovery."
  2. 收回，重新获得：指找回失去的事物。例如: "They were able to recover most of the stolen money."

- **动词**:
  1. 恢复：如身体状况、健康、繁荣等。例如: "She is recovering from a cold."
  2. 重新获得：找回或弥补损失。例如: "The company hopes to recover its losses soon."

## 短语
- "make a full recovery" 意为完全康复。
- "financial recovery" 指经济复苏。
- "recover one's senses" 意思是清醒过来。

## 例句
- 名词: "His recovery from the illness was a great relief for his family."
- 动词: "The team is working hard to recover the lost data."
- "The economy is showing signs of recovery after the recession."
