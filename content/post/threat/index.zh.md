## 读音
/θret/

## 释义
- **名词**: 有害或危险的情况、事物或行为；威胁。例如: "The constant threat of flooding made life difficult for the villagers."
- **动词**: 威胁，恐吓。例如: "He threatened to call the police if they didn't leave."

## 短语
- "under threat" 处于危险之中，受到威胁。
- "to threaten someone with something" 用某物威胁某人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The presence of nuclear weapons poses a serious threat to global peace."
- 动词: "The boss threatened to fire anyone who was late again."
