## Pronunciation
/thret/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **threat** (n) refers to a statement or action that indicates an intention to cause harm, damage, or danger, often used to force someone into a particular action or to create fear or concern.

### Verb
To **threaten** (v) means to make a warning or assertion with the intention of intimidating or coercing someone into doing something or to suggest a possibility of harm.

## Phrases
- "Issue a threat" denotes making a warning or声明 of possible harm.
- "Under threat" signifies being exposed to danger or coercion.
- "Threat level rising" communicates an increase in the severity or likelihood of a harmful event.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The company faced a legal threat from a rival firm."
- **Verb**: "He threatened to quit if they didn't raise his salary."
- **Sentence with threat as a verb**: "She felt threatened by her coworker's aggressive behavior."
