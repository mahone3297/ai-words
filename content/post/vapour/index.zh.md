## 读音
/vəˈpɔːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 蒸汽；水蒸气；烟雾。例如: "The vapour rising from the hot water is water vapour."
- **名词**: 虚无缥缈的事物；幻影。例如: "Their hopes were just a vapour in the air."

## 短语
- "vapour trail" 指飞机留下的蒸汽轨迹。
- "vapour pressure" 指液体在一定温度下蒸发产生的蒸汽压力。

## 例句
- 名词: "The boiler produced a lot of vapour, making it difficult to see inside."
- 名词: "The scent of lavender filled the room as the essential oil vapourised."
