## Pronunciation
/ˈɛərəʊplæn/

## Definitions
### Noun
An aeroplane, also spelled airplane, is a powered aircraft with fixed wings that is capable of sustained flight through the air. It is used primarily for transportation and travel.

### Verb
To aeroplane (informal) can mean to travel by airplane or to describe the action of flying in an aircraft.

## Phrases
- "Board the aeroplane" - To get on an airplane for a flight.
- "Aeroplane journey" - A trip taken by air.
- "Aeroplane enthusiast" - A person who has a strong interest in airplanes.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The aeroplane took off from Heathrow Airport, bound for New York."
- **Verb**: "We decided to aeroplane to Hawaii for our vacation this year."
- **Informal**: "She's been dreaming of owning her own aeroplane since she was a child."
