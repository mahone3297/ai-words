## Pronunciation
/tuː/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A word or expression indicating motion or direction towards something or someone. For example: "The arrow pointed to the door."
- **Verb**: To direct one's course or attention towards; to move or go towards. For example: "She turned to look at him when he called her name."
- **Preposition**: In the case of "to", it is used to indicate direction or destination. For example: "I'm going to the store."

## Phrases
- "To someone's surprise" indicates an unexpected turn of events.
- "To one's heart" expresses deep affection or commitment. 
- "To the rescue" signifies offering help in a critical situation.
