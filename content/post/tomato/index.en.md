## Pronunciation
/təˈmɑːtəʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **tomato** is a round, red fruit with a slightly sweet and tangy taste, commonly used in cooking and salads. It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae).

### Verb
To **tomato** something (informal) means to apply or spread tomato sauce, salsa, or similar condiments on a dish. It can also metaphorically refer to emphasizing or highlighting something.

### Adjective
- **Tomato-red** describes a vivid, deep red color similar to that of a ripe tomato.

## Phrases
