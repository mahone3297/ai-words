## 读音
/prəˈpræktɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 实践，实际操作。例如: "Practice makes perfect."（熟能生巧）
  - 排练，训练。例如: "The band is having a practice session tonight."
  - 专业活动或业务。例如: "She has a law practice in downtown."

- **动词**:
  - 练习，实习。例如: "I need to practice my piano skills every day."
  - 实施，运用。例如: "They plan to practice sustainable farming methods."

## 短语
- "In practice" 意为在实际操作中，实际上。例如: "In practice, the theory doesn't always work out as expected."
- "Put something into practice" 意为将某事付诸实践。例如: "Let's put our new strategy into practice."

## 例句
- 名词: "The doctor started her medical practice after graduating from医学院."
- 动词: "He practices yoga every morning to stay fit."
- 感叹词: "Practice your swing, and you'll hit the ball farther!"
