## Pronunciation
/prəˈsæktɪs/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **The act or process of performing an activity in order to improve one's skills or gain experience**: *She spends hours every day practicing her piano.* 
2. A place where one engages in such activities, often with an instructor or coach: *The gym is a popular practice facility for local athletes.*
3. A drill or exercise, especially in sports: *The football team had a rigorous practice session this morning.*

### Verb
1. To perform or engage in an activity repeatedly to become proficient or better at it: *He practices his guitar every evening.*
2. To rehearse or prepare for a performance or event: *They're going to practice their play before the opening night.*

### Adjective
1. Concerning or relating to practice: *The practice of mindfulness is becoming more popular.*
