## 读音
/trent/

## 释义
- **名词**: 趋势；倾向；潮流。例如: "The trend in fashion is towards more sustainable materials."
- **动词**: 趋向；走向。例如: "Sales have been trending upwards over the past quarter."

## 短语
- "Follow the trend" 意为跟随潮流或趋势。
- "Upward trend" 表示上升趋势。
- "Downward trend" 表示下降趋势。

## 例句
- 名词: "Market analysts predict a positive trend for the stock market next year."
- 动词: "Economic indicators suggest that inflation is trending downwards."
