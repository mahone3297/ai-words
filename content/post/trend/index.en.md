## Pronunciation
/trend/

## Definitions
### Noun
A trend is a **noun** that refers to a general direction or development, especially in social, economic, or cultural matters, that is currently observed or expected to continue. It can also denote a fashionable or popular style, or a sequence of data points showing a particular pattern over time.

### Verb
To trend (as in "trending") is not a standard verb form for this word. However, it is occasionally used informally to mean to become popular or fashionable, or to refer to something that is currently in vogue.

## Phrases
- "On a rising trend" describes something that is increasing in popularity or frequency.
- "Keep up with the latest trends" means to stay informed about current styles or developments.
- "The fashion trend" refers to a current clothing style.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Social media trends change rapidly, with new ones emerging every season."
- **Informal Verb**: "This new song has been trending on the music charts for weeks."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "It's important to understand the current marketing trends to stay competitive."
