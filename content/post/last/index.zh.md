## 读音
/ læst /

## 释义
- **形容词**: 最后的；末尾的；最近的。例如: "This is the last book on the shelf." (这是书架上的最后一本书)
- **名词**: 最后的一段时间或事件。例如: "The last of the sun was setting behind the hills." (太阳的最后一缕余晖正落在山后)
- **副词**: 最后地；终于。例如: "After hours of waiting, he arrived at last." (等待了几个小时后，他终于到了)

## 短语
- "at last" 表示最终，终于。例如: "I finished my project at last."
- "for the last time" 意为最后一次。例如: "I'm telling you for the last time, stop doing that!"

## 例句
- 形容词: "This is the last piece of cake, would you like it?"
- 副词: "We can relax now that our exams are finally over; it's all done and dusted at last."

- final
