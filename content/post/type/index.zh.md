## 读音
/taɪp/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 类型，种类。例如: "There are different types of dogs." 2. 样式，模式。例如: "This is the new type of smartphone." 3. 打字机或打印机上的字符。例如: "She hit the spacebar to move the cursor to the next type."
- **动词 (v)**: 1. 打字，键入。例如: "I need to type up this report before the meeting." 2. 归类，分类。例如: "The animals were typed based on their genetic traits."

## 短语
- "Typical of something" 意味着某事物的典型特征。
- "Typecast" 指因常扮演某一类型角色而被定型。
- "Type error" 在编程中指的是错误的类型使用。

## 例句
- 名词: "The novel has a mix of romance and adventure genres, so it's a type of crossover literature."
- 动词: "She quickly typed out her email and hit send."
- 感叹词（非正式用法）: "Type it up now, or you'll be late!"
