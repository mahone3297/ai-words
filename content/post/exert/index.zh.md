## 读音
/ɪɡˈzɜːrt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 施加；运用（力量、影响或权力）：例如, "He exerted all his strength to lift the heavy box."
2. 尝试；尽力：例如, "She exerted herself to finish the project on time."

## 短语
- "Exert influence on" 意味着对某人或某事施加影响。
- "Exert effort" 指付出努力或尽力去做某事。

## 例句
- 动词: "He exerted great influence over the decision-making process."
- 动词: "She had to exert herself to keep up with the fast pace of the class."
