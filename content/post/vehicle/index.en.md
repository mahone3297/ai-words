## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Vehicle**: A machine or contraption designed for transport, typically with wheels, powered by an engine or motor, and used for personal or commercial purposes.
   - A car, truck, or bus are examples of vehicles.
2. **Means of transportation**: Any mode by which people or goods are conveyed from one place to another.
3. **Abstract concept**: A metaphorical or symbolic representation of a process or idea that moves or facilitates progress.

### Verb
1. To travel or move using a vehicle.
2. To convey or transport someone or something using a vehicle.
