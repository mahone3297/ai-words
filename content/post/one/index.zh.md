## 读音
/ wʌn /

## 释义
- **数词**: 1，表示数量中的第一个。例如: "I have one apple."
- **代词**: 指一个人或物，通常作为不定代词使用。例如: "One should always be honest."
- **形容词**: 单个的，唯一的。例如: "This is the one I want."

## 短语
- "At one" 表示一致或和谐。例如: "They were at one on the decision."
- "Once in a while" 意为偶尔，有时候。例如: "I see him once in a while."

## 例句
- 数词: "There is only one solution to this problem."
- 代词: "One of these days, we'll go on a trip together."
- 形容词: "She wore a one-of-a-kind dress to the event."
