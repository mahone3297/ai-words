- **名词**: 邮件；邮政服务。例如: "I need to buy some stamps at the post office."
- **名词**: 岗位；职位。例如: "He works as a security guard on night post."
- **名词**: 帖子；博客文章。例如: "She just published a new post on her travel blog."
- **动词**: 邮寄；发布。例如: "I'll post your letter later today."
- **形容词**: 邮政的；邮寄的。例如: "The post box is blue and has a white post sign on it."
- **副词**: 在后；随后。例如: "He finished first, with his teammate coming in post."

## 短语
- "By post" 意为通过邮寄的方式。
- "On the post" 可能指在岗位上工作。
- "Post-date" 用于指日期写在未来的支票。
