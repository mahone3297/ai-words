## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈɔːməs/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Extremely large in size, quantity, or extent; immense. 

### Examples:
- _**Adjective**_: The elephant's enormous ears flapped in the hot African sun.
  - _Sentence_: "The new shopping mall was an enormous structure that dominated the city skyline."
  
## Phrases
- "Enormous effort": A great deal of energy or work put into something.
- "An enormous amount/number of something": Refers to a vast quantity or scale.
- "Enormous impact": Describes a significant or far-reaching effect.

## Example Sentences
- _Adjective (size)_ : "The task required an enormous amount of planning and coordination."
- _Adjective (extent)_ : "Her decision had an enormous impact on the future of the company."
