## Pronunciation
/səkˈsesfl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Achieving a high level of proficiency, success, or accomplishment. For example: "She is a successful entrepreneur."
- **Noun**: A person who has achieved a significant level of success or is characterized by success. For example: "Many successful people attribute their achievements to hard work."

## Phrases
- "Be successful in" means to achieve success in a particular endeavor.
- "A successful career" refers to a long-term, prosperous professional life.
- "She's had several successful projects in a row."

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "His innovative marketing strategy led to a successful product launch."
- **Noun**: "The successful CEO shared her insights during the conference."
- **Adverb**: "She studied tirelessly to become a successful doctor."
