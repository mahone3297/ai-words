## 读音
/səkˈsɛsfl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 成功的，达到预期目标的。例如: "She was a successful businesswoman."
- **副词**: 成功地，有效地。例如: "He executed the plan successfully."

## 短语
- "be successful in something" 在某事上成功。例如: "She was successful in her new job."
- "to have a successful career" 拥有成功的事业。

## 例句
- 形容词: "His first novel became an instant bestseller, making him a successful author."
- 副词: "The team successfully completed the project within the deadline."
