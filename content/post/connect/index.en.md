## Pronunciation
/kəˈnekt/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Connect**: A noun referring to a connection or link between people, things, or ideas.
- **Connection**: A relationship or association between individuals or entities.
- **Link**: A means of joining or relating two things.

### Verb
- **Connect**: To join or relate two or more things together, often electronically or physically.
- **Associate**: To form a close or meaningful connection with someone or something.
- **Interconnect**: To establish connections or links among various elements.

### Adjective
- **Connected**: Having a relationship or connection; related or networked.
- **Interconnected**: Linked or joined in such a way that one part affects another.

## Phrases
- "Connect two devices" means to establish a connection between them.
- "Stay connected" implies staying in touch or maintaining a relationship.
- "The internet connects millions of people worldwide."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She formed a strong connection with her mentor during her internship."
- **Verb**: "The new software allows computers to connect seamlessly."
- **Adjective**: "The houses are connected by a series of pathways."
