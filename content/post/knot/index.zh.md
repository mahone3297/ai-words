## 读音
/knɒt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 结，绳结。例如: "He tied a secure knot in the rope."
   2. 困难，复杂情况。例如: "They found themselves in a financial knot."

- **动词**: 打结，系上。例如: "She carefully knotted the scarf around her neck."

## 短语
- "In a knot" 意为紧张或焦虑的状态。例如: "She was so nervous that her stomach was in knots."
- "Untie the knot" 指解开结或解决复杂问题。例如: "They finally untied the knot in the legal case."

## 例句
- 名词: "The sailor knew how to tie various knots for different purposes."
- 动词: "She knotted the laces of her shoes tightly before starting the run."

- Tie
- Fasten
- Bunch
- Complication
- Snarl
