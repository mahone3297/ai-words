## 读音
/ˈpɑːrsəl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 一件包裹；邮包：例如: "She received a parcel containing a new dress."
   - 一部分；份额：例如: "Each child got an equal parcel of the cake."

- **动词**:
   - 包裹；打包：例如: "Please parcel these books carefully for shipping."
   - 分配；分发：例如: "The teacher parcelled out the tasks among the students."

## 短语
- "parcel out" 分配，分摊：例如: "The manager parceled out the workload among the team members."
- "parcel delivery" 快递服务：例如: "The parcel delivery company promised next-day service."

## 例句
- 名词: "I'm expecting a parcel from Amazon today."
- 动词: "She parceled the gifts in colorful wrapping paper."
- 连用短语: "The company will parcel out the project responsibilities among the employees."
