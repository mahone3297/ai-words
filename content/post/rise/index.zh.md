## 读音
/raɪz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 上升；升起：指物体从低处向高处移动。例如: "The sun rises in the east every morning."
2. 增加；提高：指数量、水平、价格等上升。例如: "Her salary has risen by 10% this year."
3. 站起来；起床：从坐、躺或跪的状态变为站立。例如: "I usually rise early in the morning for exercise."

- **名词**:
1. 上升的动作或过程：如日出、物价上涨等。例如: "The rise of the stock market is encouraging investors."
2. 增长或增加的量：例如: "There has been a significant rise in crime rates recently."

## 短语
- "rise up against" 意为反抗或起义。例如: "They rose up against the oppressive government."
- "rise to the occasion" 表示在关键时刻表现出色。例如: "She always rises to the occasion when challenges arise."

## 例句
- 动词: "The temperature is predicted to rise sharply tomorrow."
- 名词: "The rise in unemployment is a cause for concern."
- 短语: "The people rose up against the unjust laws."
