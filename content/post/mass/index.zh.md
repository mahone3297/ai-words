## 读音
/mæs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 大量；众多：例如 "The project required the collection of mass amounts of data."
  - 质量；物质量：例如 "Einstein's theory of relativity deals with the relationship between mass and energy."
  - 集体；群众：例如 "The mass of people in the city square demanded change."
  - 大规模的仪式，尤指天主教弥撒：例如 "He attended mass every Sunday."
  - （物质的）团，块：例如 "A rock has a large mass."

- **形容词**:
  - 大规模的；群众的：例如 "mass production"（大规模生产）
  - 通俗的；大众的：例如 "mass media"（大众媒体）

## 短语
- "mass production" 大规模生产
- "mass consumption" 大众消费
- "mass market" 大众市场

## 例句
- 名词: "The scientists analyzed the mass of ice melting in the Arctic."
- 形容词: "The company focuses on mass-producing affordable clothing."
