## Pronunciation
/ʌmˈbrelə/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A portable canopy with a frame and fabric or other material, designed to provide protection from rain, sun, or snow.
   - Example: "She carried an umbrella to shield herself from the rain."
2. A thing that provides protection or support, especially in times of trouble or adversity.
   - Example: "An umbrella of insurance covers unexpected expenses."

### Verb
1. To use or carry an umbrella, typically as protection from rain or sun.
   - Example: "He quickly opened his umbrella when it started raining."
2. To provide or offer protection or support, often metaphorically.
   - Example: "The company's policy umbrellas their employees with legal assistance."

## Phrases
- "Under an umbrella" implies shelter or protection.
- "Umbrella policy" refers to a comprehensive coverage that combines multiple insurance policies.
- "Keep your head down under the umbrella" is a figurative expression to advise someone to avoid trouble or danger.
