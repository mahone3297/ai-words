## 读音
/ɪmˈplɪkˈeɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 含义1: 暗示，隐含。例如: "There are serious implications for the economy if interest rates rise."
    - 含义2: 结果，影响。例如: "The policy change has far-reaching implications for small businesses."

- **动词**:
    - 引起（某种后果或结果）。例如: "This decision implicates a shift in our company's strategy."

## 短语
- "Implications of something" 指的是某事的含义或影响。
- "To imply something" 意味着暗示或表明某事。

## 例句
- 名词: "The study reveals potential implications for future medical treatments."
- 动词: "His silence implies that he doesn't agree with the proposal."
