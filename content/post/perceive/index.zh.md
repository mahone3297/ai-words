## 读音
/pərˈsiːv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 感知；察觉：意识到某事物的存在、性质或意义。例如: "He perceived a faint smell of smoke in the air."
  - 理解；领会：对某个观点、信息或情况的理解。例如: "She perceives the situation differently from her colleagues."

## 短语
- "perceive something as something else" 意为将某物误认为另一物。例如: "Some people perceive him as arrogant, but he's just confident."
- "perceive oneself as" 描述自我认知。例如: "He perceives himself as a leader rather than a follower."

## 例句
- "She quickly perceived the danger and took cover."
- "The child perceives colors more vividly than adults do."
- "They perceive the new policy as an infringement on their rights."
