## Pronunciation
/prɪˈsi:v/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: 
    - **Transitive verb**: To become aware of through the senses, especially by sight or hearing. For example: "I perceived a faint light in the distance."
    - **Intransitive verb**: To have a realization or understanding about something. For example: "She perceived the truth behind his words."

## Phrases
- "Perceive a difference" means to notice a change or distinction.
- "Perceived value" refers to how something is understood to be valuable.
- "Perceptual bias" is a tendency to interpret information in a certain way influenced by one's beliefs or experiences.

## Example Sentences
- **Transitive**: "After focusing, he perceived the intricate details on the painting."
- **Intransitive**: "Her intuition allowed her to perceive the danger before it was obvious."
- "The new employee was surprised by how quickly they perceived the company culture."
