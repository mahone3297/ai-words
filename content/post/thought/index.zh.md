## 读音
/θɔːt/

## 释义
- **名词** (n): 想法，思考，观念。例如: "She had many deep thoughts about life."
- **动词** (v): 思考，考虑。例如: "He sat quietly, lost in thought."

## 短语
- "In my thought" 表示在思考中。
- "Thought process" 指思考过程。
- "A thought crossed my mind" 意味着某个想法突然出现。

## 例句
- 名词: "After reading the book, he had new thoughts on the subject."
- 动词: "Take some time to thoughtfully consider your decision."
