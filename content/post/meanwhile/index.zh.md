## 读音
/meɪkˈwaɪl/

## 释义
- **副词**: 在此期间；与此同时。表示两个动作或事件同时发生或在另一动作进行时发生。

## 短语
- "meanwhile, on the other side" 表示在另一方面的情况。
- "in the meantime" 意为暂时，作为临时的替代。

## 例句
- "She started cooking dinner; meanwhile, her son was doing his homework."
- "We're waiting for the bus; in the meantime, let's grab a coffee."

## 同义词
- in the meanwhile
- during this time
- at the same time
- concurrently
- parallelly
