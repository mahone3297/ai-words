## 读音
/meɪkˈwaɪl/

## 释义
- **副词**: 在此期间；与此同时。表示两个动作或事件同时发生或在另一动作进行时发生。

## 短语
- "[meanwhile](/post/meanwhile/), [on](/post/on/) [the](/post/the/) [other](/post/other/) [side](/post/side/)" 表示在另一方面的情况。
- "[in](/post/in/) [the](/post/the/) [meantime](/post/meantime/)" 意为暂时，作为临时的替代。

## 例句
- "[She](/post/she/) started cooking [dinner](/post/dinner/); [meanwhile](/post/meanwhile/), [her](/post/her/) [son](/post/son/) was doing [his](/post/his/) homework."
