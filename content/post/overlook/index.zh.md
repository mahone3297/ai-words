## 读音
/ˌoʊvərˈlʊk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 忽视；未注意到：例如, "I overlooked his mistake in the report."
2. 监督；管理：例如, "She was appointed to overlook the construction project."
3. 俯瞰；眺望：例如, "The hotel room overlooked the city skyline."

## 短语
- "overlook something" 忽视或忽略某事。
- "overlook from" 从某个位置俯瞰：例如, "We overlooked the ocean from the hilltop."

## 例句
- "I overlooked the small typo in the document, which led to confusion."
- "The manager overlooked the team's daily operations."
- "From the balcony, we could overlook the bustling street below."
