## Pronunciation
/twentieth/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: The number that comes after the nineteenth and before the twenty-first in the series of whole numbers representing position or order. For example: "The twentieth century was marked by significant technological advancements."

## Phrases
- "In the twentieth year" refers to a particular year within a century.
- "On the twentieth" specifies an ordinal position.
- "Born in the twentieth" relates to a birth year within a century.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The twentieth anniversary of our company will be celebrated next month."
- **Adjective**: "The twentieth century saw rapid urbanization across the globe."
- "She visited her hometown on the twentieth of July."

## Synonyms

- Tenth from the end
- Twelveth from the start (since it's the second decade)
- Two score (informal, British usage)
