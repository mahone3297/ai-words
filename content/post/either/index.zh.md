## 读音
/ˈaɪðər/

## 释义
- **副词**: 用于否定句中，表示两者中的任何一方。例如: "I don't [care](/zh/post/care/) [whether](/zh/post/whether/) [you](/zh/post/you/) [go](/zh/post/go/) [or](/zh/post/or/) [stay](/zh/post/stay/); [either](/zh/post/either/) [way](/zh/post/way/) is [fine](/zh/post/fine/)."
- **连词**: 引导选择关系的从句，表示“或者…或者…”。例如: "[You](/zh/post/you/) [can](/zh/post/can/) [either](/zh/post/either/) [study](/zh/post/study/) [now](/zh/post/now/) [or](/zh/post/or/) [do](/zh/post/do/) [it](/zh/post/it/) [later](/zh/post/later/)."

## 短语
- "[Either](/zh/post/either/) [way](/zh/post/way/)" 意味着无论哪种情况。
- "[Neither](/zh/post/neither/)...[nor](/zh/post/nor/)..." 与 "[either](/zh/post/either/)...[or](/zh/post/or/)..." 相反，表示两者都不。

## 例句
- 副词: "[You](/zh/post/you/) [can](/zh/post/can/) [take](/zh/post/take/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [red](/zh/post/red/) [car](/zh/post/car/) [or](/zh/post/or/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [blue](/zh/post/blue/) [one](/zh/post/one/); it's [either](/zh/post/either/) [one](/zh/post/one/)."
- 连词: "[She](/zh/post/she/) [can](/zh/post/can/) [either](/zh/post/either/) [sing](/zh/post/sing/) [or](/zh/post/or/) [dance](/zh/post/dance/), [but](/zh/post/but/) [not](/zh/post/not/) [both](/zh/post/both/)."
