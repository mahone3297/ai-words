## 读音
/roʊb/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   - 长袍，罩袍，通常指宽松的长袖衣物。例如: "She wore a flowing silk robe to the event."
   - (宗教或正式场合)法衣，僧袍。例如: "The bishop donned his purple robe for the ceremony."

## 短语
- "Robe oneself in" 意为穿上长袍。例如: "She robed herself in a luxurious velvet gown for the ball."
- "Evening robe" 特指晚上穿的休闲长袍。例如: "He enjoyed reading in his comfortable evening robe after a long day."

## 例句
- 名词: "The bride wore a beautiful white satin robe on her wedding day."
- 动词（引申义）: "He robe himself in the dignity of his office."

## 同义词
- Dress
- Garment
- Attire
- Vestment
- Gown (在某些语境下可作为同义词)
