## Pronunciation
/sɪˈsɛndri/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Coming after the main or primary; second in rank or importance. For example: "The secondary school is across the street from the primary school."
- **Adjective (informal)**: Less important or significant; secondary role. For example: "Her secondary interests include painting and reading."
- **Noun**: A person attending a secondary school, or a less important school or college. For example: "Many students attend secondary schools after primary school."
- **Noun (computing)**: A backup or secondary storage device. For example: "The data is stored on both the primary and secondary drives."

## Phrases
- "Secondary education" refers to high school or post-primary schooling.
- "Secondary purpose" describes a lesser goal or function.
- "Secondary character" is a supporting role in a story or film.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The secondary roads are often narrower than the primary highways."
- **Adjective (informal)**: "In his free time, he focuses on his secondary passions like photography."
- **Noun**: "She completed her secondary education at a local college."
- **Noun (computing)**: "The system automatically transfers data to the secondary drive during low usage periods."
