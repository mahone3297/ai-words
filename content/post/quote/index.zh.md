## 读音
/kwəʊt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 引用的话语；引述；语录。例如: "She included a famous quote in her speech."
- **动词**: 引用；援引。例如: "He quoted Shakespeare in his essay."
- **名词**: 报价；估价。例如: "The mechanic gave me a quote for the car repairs."

## 短语
- "to quote someone" 意思是引用某人的原话。
- "at a quote" 在某种报价下，通常指股票或商品的价格。

## 例句
- 名词: "The book is filled with inspiring quotes from great thinkers."
- 动词: "In his speech, he quoted a passage from Martin Luther King Jr.'s 'I Have a Dream' speech."
- 名词（报价）: "The stock was trading at a quote of $50 per share."
