## 读音
/sæd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 悲伤的，不快乐的。例如: "She looked sad after hearing the bad news."
- **动词**: 使悲伤，使不快乐。例如: "The news of her friend's illness saddened her."

## 短语
- "feeling sad" 意味着感到悲伤。
- "sad story" 指的是一个令人伤心的故事。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The boy had a sad expression on his face when he lost the game."
- 动词: "The rain on their wedding day saddened the couple."

- Depressed
- Disheartened
