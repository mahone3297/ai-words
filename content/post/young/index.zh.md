## 读音
/ˈjʌŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 年轻的，未成熟的。指年龄、心态或事物的状态处于早期阶段。例如: "She is still young and has a lot to learn."
- **副词**: 稍微，有点儿（表示程度）。例如: "He's young at heart, even though he's in his sixties."

## 短语
- "Young and innocent" 意为年轻而纯真。
- "In one's young years" 指某人的青壮年时期。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The young couple just bought their first house."
- 副词: "She still feels young despite her age."
