## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Designating someone or something as not old or having qualities typically associated with a younger age. For example: "The young children were excited to start their first day at school."
- **Noun**: A person who is not yet fully grown or mature, typically one considered to be below the age of adulthood. For example: "The young adult decided to pursue higher education."
- **Adverb**: In a youthful manner; energetically. For example: "She danced young and lively at the party."
