## 读音
/pɪti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 对不幸或苦难的同情；怜悯。例如: "She felt deep pity for the homeless child."
- **动词**: 对...表示怜悯或同情。例如: "He pitied her for the loss of her parents."
- **形容词**: 值得怜悯的；可怜的。例如: "The old dog looked so pity that everyone stopped to give it some food."

## 短语
- "have pity on someone" 表示对某人表示同情。
- "feel pity for someone" 意味着对某人的不幸感到同情。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her eyes showed nothing but pity when she saw his broken leg."
- 动词: "He couldn't help but pity the workers in such harsh conditions."
- 形容词: "The poor animal was in a pity state after the accident."
