## 读音
/ʃeɪk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
   - 摇动，使振动：例如 "He shook the tree to make the apples fall."
   - 摇晃（物体）：例如 "She shook the bottle to mix the contents."
   - （与人）握手：例如 "They shook hands on the deal."
   - （疾病或紧张导致的）颤抖：例如 "His hands were shaking from fear."

- **名词**:
   - 摇动的动作：例如 "There was a slight shake in the ground during the earthquake."
   - 颤抖：例如 "He had a feverish shake."
   - 摇滚乐：例如 "They love listening to old-school rock 'n' roll, especially the shakes."

## 短语
- "shake hands" 握手
- "shake it off" 摆脱，甩掉（常用于情绪或事物）
- "a milkshake" 奶昔

## 例句
- 动词: "The baby's rattle shook loudly when he grabbed it."
- 名词: "The earthquake caused a violent shake that toppled over furniture."
- 摇滚乐: "The band's performance ended with an energetic shake that got the crowd dancing."
