## 读音
/ˈpæʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 强烈的情感或感情；热情；激情。例如: "She has a passion for painting."
- **名词**: 强烈的兴趣或爱好。例如: "His passion for music led him to become a professional musician."
- **动词**: 对某事充满激情。例如: "She passions about environmental issues."

## 短语
- "Follow your passion" 意为追求你热爱的事物。
- "A passion for something" 表示对某事的极度喜爱或热衷。

## 例句
- 名词: "His passion for cooking inspired him to open his own restaurant."
- 动词: "She passions about helping others, which is why she became a nurse."
