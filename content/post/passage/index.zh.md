## 读音
/pæsɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一段（文字、音乐、时间等）: 一段文章或故事的一部分。例如: "Please read the first passage of the book."
  - 通道，通路: 可以通过的空间或路径。例如: "There's a narrow passage between the buildings."
  - 过程，经过: 某事物从开始到结束的发展。例如: "The passage of time has changed everything."

## 短语
- "passage of time" 指时间的流逝。
- "passage through" 表示穿过或通过某个地方。例如: "We made our way through the forest with ease, thanks to the clear passage."
- "air passage" 在航空中指的是飞行路线。

## 例句
- 名词: "The essay had several well-written passages that captured the reader's attention."
- 通道: "The ship navigated safely through the treacherous passage in the reef."
- 过程: "The passage from adolescence to adulthood can be challenging for many young people."
