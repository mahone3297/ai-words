## 读音
/ˈʌvn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种烹饪设备，通常用电或燃气加热，用于烘烤食物，如面包、蛋糕或烤肉。例如: "She preheated the oven before baking the cookies."
- **名词**: 有时也比喻为极热的环境或情况。例如: "The heat in the factory was like working in an oven."

## 短语
- "oven mitt" 手套，用于隔热，防止在操作烤箱时烫伤手。
- "to bake in the oven" 在烤箱里烘烤。例如: "I'm going to bake a pizza in the oven for dinner tonight."

## 例句
- 名词: "The turkey needs to cook in the oven for about three hours."
- 名词（比喻）: "The desert sun turned the car into an oven during the day."

- stove
- cooker

- roaster
- furnace (在某些上下文中，特别是比喻使用时)
