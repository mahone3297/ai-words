## Word: Oven

## Pronunciation
/ˈʌvn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A device, typically electric or gas-powered, used for cooking or heating food by means of dry heat. It's a closed chamber with a door and interior space for holding food on racks or shelves. For example: "I preheated the oven before putting the pizza inside to bake."
  
  > *Phrase*: "Turn on the oven" - to start the heating process in an oven.
  > 
  > *Example Sentence*: "She pulled a tray of cookies out of the hot oven, filling the kitchen with a sweet aroma."

- **Noun (Metaphorical)**: A place or situation of intense heat or pressure. For instance, "The kiln was like an oven, baking the pottery to a perfect finish."

## Phrases
- "Bake in the oven" - to cook something using the dry heat of an oven.
- "Clean the oven" - to remove grime and residue from the interior of an oven.
- "From the oven to the table" - describing food that has been freshly cooked and is ready to be served immediately.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (Cooking appliance)**: "The turkey needed to roast in the oven for several hours."
- **Noun (Metaphorical use)**: "Working in the steel mill was like being in an oven during summer days."

## Synonyms
- cooker
- stove
- baking oven
- broiler
- roaster
- furnace (in some contexts)
- toaster oven (for smaller appliances)
