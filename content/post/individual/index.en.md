## Word: Individual

## Pronunciation
/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A single person considered separately from others; a distinct being or entity. Example: "Each individual has their own unique set of talents."
  - _As a countable noun_: "The individuals in the group had different opinions."
  - _As an uncountable noun (rarely used)_: "Individuality is what sets us apart from one another."
- **Adjective**: Separate; distinct; not shared by others. Example: "Each student has their individual learning style."

## Phrases
- "As an individual": Referring to a person in isolation from a group or collective.
- "Individual rights": Rights that belong to each person as a separate entity.
- "In the individual sports category": Referring to sports where athletes compete on their own rather than as part of a team.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The company policy applies to all employees, regardless of their individual roles."
- **Adjective**: "She values her individual freedom to make personal choices."
