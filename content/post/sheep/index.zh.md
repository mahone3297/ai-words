## 读音
/ʃiːp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 哺乳动物，羊，通常被饲养以获取羊毛、肉类和奶制品。例如: "The shepherd tended to his flock of sheep in the field."
- **复数形式**: sheep 与单数形式相同，不加-s。例如: "There are many sheep grazing on the hillside."

## 短语
- "count sheep" 用于描述在尝试入睡时想象数绵羊的行为，帮助缓解失眠。
- "sheepish" 形容词，意思是害羞的或尴尬的，例如: "He looked sheepish when caught in the lie."

## 例句
- 名词: "The shepherd led the sheep through the gate into the pasture."
- 复数形式: "The sheep wandered away from the flock and got lost."

## 同义词
- goats (山羊)
- lambs (小羊)
- ewes (母羊)
- rams (公羊)
