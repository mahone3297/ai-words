## Definitions
### Noun
A **sophisticated** person or thing is highly developed, advanced, or having a high level of refinement, knowledge, or culture. It can also refer to something complex or intricate.

### Verb
To **sophisticate** means to make someone more knowledgeable, experienced, or worldly, often by exposing them to new ideas or sophistication.
