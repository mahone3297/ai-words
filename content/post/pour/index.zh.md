## 读音
/pɔːr/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 倾倒，倒出：将液体从一个容器倒入另一个容器。例如: "He poured water from the jug into the glass."
2. 浇注：在表面上连续地放出液体。例如: "She poured cement onto the ground to build a foundation."
3. 大量涌出，倾泻：（非物质）大量流出或涌出。例如: "The crowd poured out of the stadium after the game."

## 短语
- "pour water" 倒水
- "pour rain" 下大雨
- "pour one's heart out" 倾诉心事

## 例句
- 动词(1): "She carefully poured the milk into the bowl without spilling any."
- 动词(2): "It was pouring rain, so we decided to stay inside."
- 动词(3): "He couldn't contain his excitement and poured his feelings out to his friends."
