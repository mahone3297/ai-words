## 读音
/nɪəli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 差不多，几乎，将近。例如: "I was nearly late for the meeting." 
- **形容词**: 接近的，邻近的。例如: "The nearly empty bottle is on the table."

## 短语
- "nearly always" 几乎总是
- "nearly done" 差不多完成了
- "nearly there" 快到了

## 例句
- 副词: "I finished the project nearly at midnight."
- 形容词: "The nearly forgotten town has a rich history."
