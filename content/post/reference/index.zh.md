## 读音
/reference/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 参考；引用；提及。例如: "Please include a reference for that statistic."
  - 资料来源；文献。例如: "The book has a comprehensive list of references in the back."
  - 证明人；推荐人。例如: "You'll need a reference from your previous employer for this job application."
  - 版本；版次。例如: "I'm using the third edition of the dictionary as my reference."

- **动词**:
  - 查阅；参考。例如: "I'll reference the dictionary to check the spelling."
  - 将…作为参照。例如: "The design was referenced from a famous artist's work."

## 短语
- "Reference book" 指参考书籍，如词典或百科全书。
- "In reference to" 意为关于，涉及。例如: "In reference to your question, the answer is..."
- "Personal reference" 指个人推荐信。

## 例句
- 名词: "The bibliography lists all the references used in the research paper."
- 动词: "She referenced her mentor's advice when making the decision."
