## Pronunciation
/pəʊz/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A temporary stop or interruption in speaking, activity, or a sequence, often for a moment of reflection or to allow others to speak. For example: "She took a brief pause before continuing her story."
- **Verb**: To stop or suspend action momentarily; to interrupt briefly. For example: "Please pause the video when you reach the end of the chapter."
- **Adverb**: In a manner that involves pausing, temporarily. For example: "He spoke slowly and deliberately, pausing occasionally to emphasize his points."

## Phrases
- "Pause and think" suggests taking a moment to reflect.
- "Press pause" is a command to stop a machine or process.
- "Pausing for a breath" implies taking a moment to catch one's breath.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After finishing her presentation, she allowed a brief pause for questions."
- **Verb**: "The conductor paused the orchestra to correct the musician's mistake."
- **Adverb**: "She walked into the room, pausing at the doorway to observe the situation."
