## 读音
/pɔːz/

## 释义
- **名词**: 暂停，间歇，停顿。例如: "There was a brief pause in the conversation."
- **动词**: 停下来，暂时中断。例如: "He paused to catch his breath."

## 短语
- "Take a pause" 意味着稍作休息或中断。
- "Pause for thought" 表示停下来思考一下。

## 例句
- 名词: "The musician inserted a short pause between two phrases for dramatic effect."
- 动词: "She paused the movie to make some popcorn."

- Halt
- Interruption
- Stop
