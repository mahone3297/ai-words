## 读音
/ skɪn /

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 人体或其他生物体的外部表层，通常指覆盖并保护身体的柔软组织。例如: "She has delicate skin that easily burns in the sun."
- **动词 (v)**: 剥去或脱掉（某物）的外皮或表层。例如: "The apple was skinned before being eaten."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 涉及或影响皮肤的。例如: "skin-care products"

## 短语
- "skin deep" 表示仅存在于表面，不深沉或真实的。例如: "Her beauty is skin deep, but her kindness shines through."
- "in the skin of one's teeth" 形容勉强或险胜。例如: "We won the race by the skin of our teeth."

## 例句
- 名词: "He applied sunscreen to protect his skin from the harmful UV rays."
- 动词: "The cook carefully skinned the potatoes before boiling them."
- 形容词: "This new lotion is specifically designed for sensitive skin."
