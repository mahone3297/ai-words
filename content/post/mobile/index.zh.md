## 读音
/ˈməʊbaɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 移动电话，手机: "I just bought a new mobile phone with great camera features."
  - 可移动的，便携的: "The artist created a beautiful mobile that hangs from the ceiling."
- **形容词**:
  - 可移动的，能自由移动的: "The furniture in this room is all mobile, so we can rearrange it easily."
  - 无线的，行动通信的: "The mobile network allows us to stay connected on the go."

## 短语
- "mobile app" 指的是手机应用程序。
- "mobile device" 涵盖所有可移动的电子设备，如智能手机和平板电脑。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need to charge my mobile before the flight."
- 形容词: "The office has a mobile workstation that can be moved around as needed."
