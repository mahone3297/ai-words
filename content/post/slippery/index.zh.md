## 读音
/ˈslɪpəri/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 表示滑的，易滑的，不稳的。例如: "The floor was slippery when wet."
- **副词**: 表示滑溜地，巧妙地。例如: "He slipped away from the scene unnoticed."

## 短语
- "a slippery surface" 意指滑动表面。
- "to get a slippery grip" 意味着得到不稳定的抓握。
- "slippery when wet" 常见于警告标志，表示路面在潮湿时会变得很滑。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Be careful walking on these stairs, they can be quite slippery."
- 副词: "She tried to explain her way out of the situation with a slippery answer."
