## Pronunciation
/sliːpɪ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having a smooth surface that makes it difficult to walk on or grasp; slippery. For example: "The floor was slippery after the rain."
- **Adverb**: In a slippery manner; unsteadily or with difficulty. For example: "She tried to climb the ladder, but her feet were very slippery."

## Phrases
- "Slippery slope" refers to a situation where a seemingly small action leads to a continuous downward decline.
- "Be careful, the floor is slippery" is a warning to take caution.
- "A slippery customer" is a colloquial term for someone who is tricky or untrustworthy.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The bathroom floor was so slippery, I almost fell."
- **Adverb**: "She slipped on the icy sidewalk and had a hard time keeping her balance."

- Sleazy
