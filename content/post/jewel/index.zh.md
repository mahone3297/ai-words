## 读音
/ˈdʒuːəl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 宝石，珠宝。例如: "She wore a necklace adorned with precious jewels."
             2. 珍宝，贵重的东西。例如: "In her eyes, their friendship was a true jewel."
- **动词 (v)**: 装饰以宝石。例如: "The crown was jeweled with diamonds and rubies."

## 短语
- "jewel in the crown" 意味着最珍贵或最重要的部分。
- "jewel-encrusted" 描述被宝石装饰的物品。

## 例句
- 名词: "The queen's tiara was studded with rare jewels."
- 动词: "The designer jeweled the dress with Swarovski crystals."
