## 读音
/prɪˈveɪl/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. **普遍流行；盛行**: 如 "The use of smartphones has prevailed in recent years."
2. **占优势；获胜**: 如 "His argument prevailed over all others."
3. **（疾病）侵袭；弥漫**: 如 "The flu prevailed in the community last winter."

## 短语
- "prevail upon someone" 意为说服或劝说某人做某事。例如: "She prevailed upon him to give up smoking."
- "prevail in the face of adversity" 表示在困难面前取得成功。例如: "Despite all obstacles, he prevailed in the face of adversity."

## 例句
- 动词1: "Social media has prevailed as the primary means of communication for many people."
- 动词2: "In the debate, his opinion ultimately prevailed."
- 动词3: "The flu virus was prevailing in several regions during the winter season."

## 同义词
- dominate
- triumph
- overcome
- prevail over
- gain the upper hand
