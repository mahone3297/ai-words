## 读音
/ˈrɪdʒən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 地区；区域：一个特定的地理、政治或文化区域。例如: "The Amazon rainforest is a vast region in South America."
  - 部门；领域：某个活动或兴趣的特定部分。例如: "The sales region for our company includes five states."
  - 范围；界限：事物的边界或限制。例如: "The study focuses on the economic region of the city."

## 短语
- "Regional differences" 表示地区间的差异。
- "In this region" 指在某个特定的区域内。
- "A region of interest" 指感兴趣或重要的区域。

## 例句
- 名词: "The European Union consists of 27 member regions."
- 名词: "The Arctic region is experiencing rapid climate change."
