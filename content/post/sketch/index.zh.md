## 读音
/skɛtʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 草图；素描：A rough drawing or painting done quickly, often as a preliminary study for a more finished work.
  - 概述；梗概：A brief summary or outline of something.

- **动词**:
  - 画草图；素描：To draw a sketch, especially quickly and roughly.
  - 概括；概述：To give a brief description or outline of something.

## 短语
- "sketch artist" 指的是专门绘制素描或肖像的艺术家。
- "sketch out" 意为大致描绘或规划（计划、想法等）。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist was working on a sketch of the city skyline."
- 动词: "He sketched a portrait of his friend during their lunch break."
- 感叹词（非正式用法）: "Sketch! That was a close call!"
