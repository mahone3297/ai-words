## Pronunciation
/dɪnər/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A main meal, usually eaten in the evening, typically consisting of several courses.
2. An event or occasion when this meal is served, often at home or in a restaurant.
3. Informal: An evening meal, especially one that is substantial.

### Verb
1. To have or partake in a dinner, usually as a social gathering.
2. To provide or arrange a dinner for others.

## Phrases
- "Have a nice dinner"
- "Dinner's on me tonight"
- "Join us for dinner"
- "A light dinner"
- "Dinner party"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "We had a delicious dinner at the fancy restaurant last night."
- **Verb**: "I'm looking forward to hosting a dinner party this weekend."
- **Verb (informal)**: "Let's grab a bite to eat; it's almost dinner time."

## Synonyms
- Supper
- Meal
- Feast
- Banquet
- Luncheon (informal, usually referring to a midday meal)
- Lunch (informal, can also refer to a dinner)
- Tea (informal, typically a light meal served in the afternoon)
