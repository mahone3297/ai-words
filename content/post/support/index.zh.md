## 读音
/səˈpɔːrt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 支持，支撑；援助；拥护者；底部支撑物。例如: "The government needs the support of the public." 
- **动词**: 支持，支撑；帮助；鼓励；赞助。例如: "She supports her local charity by volunteering regularly."
- **形容词**: 支持的，辅助的；可支撑的。例如: "The chair has a support for your back."

## 短语
- "give support to someone" 意为给予某人支持或帮助。
- "financial support" 指经济上的支持。
- "emotional support" 指情感上的支持。

## 例句
- 名词: "The injured athlete was grateful for the emotional support from his teammates."
- 动词: "My parents have always supported my decision to become an artist."
- 形容词: "This new software provides excellent support for video editing."
