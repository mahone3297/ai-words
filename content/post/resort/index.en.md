## Pronunciation
/rɪˈzɔːrt/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A place chosen as a holiday destination, often with leisure facilities: *We decided to go to the beach resort for our vacation.*
2. A resort town or village: *The ski resort was bustling with tourists during the winter season.*
3. A resort to: *In difficult times, people often resort to using their savings.*
4. (informal) A solution or course of action taken when faced with a problem: *Their only resort was to seek legal advice.*

### Verb
1. To go to a resort for vacation or leisure: *They're planning to resort to the Caribbean this summer.*
2. To turn to a particular resource or strategy: *Failing the exam, she resorted to studying harder for the retake.*

## Phrases
- "Take a resort break" or "go on a resort vacation"
- "Resort to" (as in "resort to extreme measures")
- "Have a resort-style wedding"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After a long week at work, they decided to spend the weekend at a mountain resort."
- **Verb**: "When the weather turned bad, they had no choice but to resort to indoor activities."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "Faced with financial troubles, they resorted to selling some of their assets."
