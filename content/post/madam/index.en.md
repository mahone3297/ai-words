## Word: Madam

## Pronunciation
/ˈmædəm/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A polite form of address for a woman, especially an older or respected one. For example: "Madam President, may I present the committee members."
- **Noun (informal)**: The owner or manager of a brothel. For example: "The madam ran a discreet establishment on the outskirts of town."

### Phrases
- "Excuse me, madam" is a polite way to get someone's attention or seek forgiveness.
- "Yes, madam" or "No, madam" are often used by people in service positions when addressing a woman respectfully.
- "At her majesty's pleasure, madam" can be used in a formal or humorous context to imply deference to a woman's authority.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (polite address)**: "Madam, your order will be ready shortly."
- **Noun (informal meaning)**: "The madam ensured that all her clients' needs were attended to with discretion."
