## Pronunciation
/həˈləʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to greet or address someone, often informally. For example: "He gave her a friendly hello when they met."
- **Verb**: To express greeting or salutation towards someone, typically by saying "hello." For example: "He greeted the guests with a hearty hello."
- **Adverb**: Expressing or characterized by a friendly or enthusiastic manner of greeting. For example: "She said hello cheerfully."

## Phrases
- "Pay your hello" or "Give a hello" means to initiate a greeting.
- "Hello and goodbye" is a common phrase used to acknowledge both arrival and departure.
- "Hello, sunshine!" is a way to express warmth and positivity towards someone.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children exchanged hellos as they started their day at school."
- **Verb**: "After a long day, it felt good to hear a familiar hello from my friend."
- **Adverb**: "She said hello brightly, brightening up the room."
