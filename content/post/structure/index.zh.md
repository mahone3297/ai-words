## 读音
/ˈstrʌktʃər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 建筑物或物体的构造、框架或布局。例如: "The bridge's steel structure is an engineering marvel."
  - 事物的组织方式或构成部分。例如: "The company's organizational structure has several departments."
  - （语言、音乐等的）结构或模式。例如: "The poem has a complex structure with multiple stanzas."

- **动词**:
  - 构建或安排事物的结构。例如: "They are structuring a new financial plan for the business."

## 短语
- "Structural integrity" 指的是物体或建筑物的稳定性。
- "Social structure" 涉及社会的阶层、群体和关系。
- "Data structure" 在计算机科学中指的是数据的组织方式。

## 例句
- 名词: "The building's unique structure makes it a local landmark."
- 动词: "Engineers are structuring the new highway to minimize traffic congestion."
