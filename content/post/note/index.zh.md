## 读音
/nəʊt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 笔记，记录。例如: "I took a note of the important points in the lecture."
   2. 提示，便条。例如: "She left him a note reminding him of the meeting."
   3. 音符，调子。例如: "The singer hit each note perfectly."
   4. 注意，关注。例如: "It's worth noting that the sales have increased significantly."

- **动词 (v)**: 记录，做笔记。例如: "Note down the key details for later reference."

## 短语
- "Take a note" 意思是做笔记。
- "Musical note" 指音乐中的音符。
- "A matter of note" 意味着值得注意的事情。

## 例句
- 名词: "She scribbled a quick note on a piece of paper and handed it to him."
- 动词: "He noted every detail of the accident scene for the police report."
