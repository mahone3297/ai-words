## 读音
/səˈnjuːm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 姓氏；家族名。例如: "My surname is Zhang."
- **形容词**: （用作修饰语）指与姓氏相关的。例如: "The surname Li is very common in China."

## 短语
- "Family surname" 指的是家庭的姓氏。
- "Given name and surname" 指的是全名，包括名和姓。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please write down your first name and surname on the form."
- 形容词: "The surname Wang is shared by many people in this community."

## 同义词
- Last name
- Family name
- Patronymic
- Surnames (复数形式，指多个姓氏)
