## 读音
/ˈpeɪʃnt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 在医疗或心理学中指接受治疗或咨询的人；病人。例如: "The doctor saw several patients in his clinic today."
- **形容词**: 能忍耐的，有耐心的。例如: "She was very patient while waiting for her turn."

## 短语
- "be patient with sb." 意为对某人有耐心。例如: "Please be patient with me; I'm still learning."
- "run out of patience" 意为失去耐心。例如: "I'm afraid I'm running out of patience with their constant complaints."

## 例句
- 名词: "The nurse took the patient's temperature before administering the medication."
- 形容词: "You need to be patient when teaching children how to read."

- forbearing
- enduring
- long-suffering
