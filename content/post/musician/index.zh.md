## 读音
/mjuˈzɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 职业演奏音乐或创作音乐的人，音乐家。例如: "She is a talented musician who plays the piano professionally."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与音乐相关的，音乐的。例如: "The musician's fingers danced gracefully over the keyboard."

## 短语
- "Classical musician" 指的是演奏古典音乐的专业人士。
- "Session musician" 是指在录音或演出中作为临时成员参与的音乐家。
- "Musician's musician" 指那些被同行高度尊重和认可的音乐家。

## 例句
- 名词: "The young musician received a standing ovation after her performance."
- 形容词: "The musician's album features a blend of jazz and classical influences."
