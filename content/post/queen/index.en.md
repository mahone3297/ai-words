## Pronunciation
/ˈkwiːn/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A female ruler of a country, especially a monarch: *The British monarchy has had several queens.*
2. A person considered to be the best or most important in a particular field: *She is the chess queen and never loses a game.*
3. (informal) A highly skilled or dominant person in a group or community: *He's the queen of the coding world.*

### Verb
1. To appoint or make someone a queen: *She was crowned queen of the ball.*
2. To rule or reign over: *The queen of the bees oversees the hive.*

## Phrases
