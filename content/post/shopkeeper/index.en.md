## Pronunciation
/shopˈkiːpər/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **shopkeeper** is a person who owns and manages a retail store or shop, typically dealing with goods for sale to customers.

### Verb
To act as a **shopkeeper** can mean to run or operate a store, manage its inventory, and interact with customers.

## Phrases
- "Run a shop" or "manage the store"
- "Be the face of the shop"
- "Serve as a shopkeeper"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The friendly shopkeeper greeted me with a warm smile."
- **Verb**: "My grandfather used to be a skilled shopkeeper in the local market."
- **Phrases**: "She took over her father's shop when he retired."

## Synonyms
- Proprietor
- Owner
- Merchant
- Retailer
- Storekeeper

## Antonyms
- Customer (the opposite role)
- Consumer (someone who buys goods, not someone who sells them)
