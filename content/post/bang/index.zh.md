## 读音
/bæŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 爆炸声，砰的一声。例如: "There was a loud bang from the kitchen."
- **动词**: 猛烈撞击或敲击。例如: "The door banged shut when the wind blew."
- **形容词**: 形容突然或剧烈的动作。例如: "The bang of the gunshot startled everyone."

## 短语
- "Bang the table" 意味着用力敲击桌子。
- "Bang in" 可以表示迅速或有力地插入或安装。例如: "He bangs in the nails with a hammer."
- "Bang on" 有时用来表示非常准确或恰好。例如: "That's bang on what I was thinking!"

## 例句
- 名词: "The fireworks ended with a series of loud bangs."
- 动词: "She banged her head against the wall in frustration."
- 形容词: "The car hit the curb with a bang, causing damage to the tire."
