## 读音
/wrɪˈtɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 写作的行为或过程。例如: "She is practicing her writing skills every day."
   2. 手写体；笔迹。例如: "The detective analyzed the suspect's writing to match it with the note left at the scene."
   3. 文字；书面表达。例如: "The book contains excellent writing on modern politics."

- **动词 (v)**: 书写；记下。例如: "He was writing a letter to his parents when the phone rang."

## 短语
- "Creative writing" 指的是文学创作，如小说、诗歌或剧本。
- "Business writing" 涉及到正式的商业沟通，如报告、邮件或合同。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her writing has been published in several literary magazines."
- 动词: "I need to write down this idea before I forget it."
