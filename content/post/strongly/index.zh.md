## 读音
/ˈstrɒŋli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示强烈地、坚决地或坚定地。例如: "She believes strongly in the power of education."
- **形容词**: 少见用法，表示强烈的，坚固的。例如: "He has a strongly built physique."

## 短语
- "do something strongly" 意味着非常坚定地做某事。
- "feel strongly about something" 表示对某事有强烈的看法或感情。

## 例句
- 副词: "He supports the project strongly and won't back down easily."
- 形容词: "The coffee has a strongly flavored aroma."
