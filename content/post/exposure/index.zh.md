## 读音
/ɪkˈspəʊʒər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 公开露面；展示： "The artist's new work is currently on exposure in a local gallery."
   - 曝光（摄影）： "The camera needs the right exposure time for a perfect shot."
   - 风险；暴露： "Children need to have some exposure to germs to build their immune system."
   - 影响；接触： "Regular exposure to sunlight can improve your mood."

## 短语
- "exposure to something" 指接触或体验某事物： "They had little exposure to foreign cultures growing up."
- "risk exposure" 指面临的风险程度： "The company needs to minimize its financial risk exposure."

## 例句
- 名词: "Her poetry received widespread exposure after winning the literary prize."
- 动词（短语用法）: "The experiment exposed the plants to different temperatures to study their growth patterns."
- 风险： "The building's structural weaknesses increase its exposure to earthquakes."
