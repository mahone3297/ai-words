## 读音
/taɪ/

## 释义
- **名词** (n):
   - 领带，尤指男性在正式场合系在衬衫上的装饰性带子。例如: "He wore a blue suit with a matching tie."
   - 关联，联系。例如: "There is a strong tie between the two companies."

- **动词** (v):
   - 系，打结，如领带。例如: "Can you help me tie this tie?"
   - 平局，不分胜负。例如: "The game ended in a tie."

## 短语
- "tie the knot" 指结婚。
- "in a tie" 表示平局或势均力敌的状态。
- "tie something up" 意为完成或解决某事。

## 例句
- 名词: "He chose a red tie to match his wife's dress."
- 动词: "The soccer match ended in a tie, with both teams scoring two goals each."
