## 读音
/ˈrɪvər/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   - 自然形成的水流，通常较大并流入海洋、湖泊或其他水体。例如: "The river Nile is the longest river in Africa."
   - 比喻为生命的源泉或历史的流程。例如: "Her life was like a river, flowing steadily towards its destiny."

## 短语
- "Upstream/downstream" 指河流的上游和下游方向。
- "Riverbank" 指河流两岸。
- "Follow the river" 意为沿着河流前进。

## 例句
- "We went for a picnic by the river on a sunny afternoon."
- "The salmon swim upstream to spawn in the river's shallow waters."
- "The city was built on the banks of the river, providing easy access to water resources."
