## 读音
/sock/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 袜子，尤指短袜。例如: "I need to buy a new pair of socks for my son."
   - 击打，猛然一击。例如: "He was so surprised that he dropped his phone when I socked him playfully."

## 短语
- "Put on socks" 意为穿上袜子。
- "Socks off" 意为脱下袜子。

## 例句
- 名词: "These cotton socks are very comfortable to wear all day."
- 动词: "The little boy socked the ball hard, sending it over the fence."

## 同义词
- Stockings (for longer socks)
- Footwear (in a broader sense)
- Knitwear (for a category including socks)
- Tap (for the action of hitting, in the context of socking someone)
