## 读音
/mekəˈnɪks/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 机械学，力学，物理学的一个分支，研究物体运动的原理和力的作用。例如: "He is studying mechanics to understand how engines work."
   2. 机械工，技工，从事机械设备维修和操作的人。例如: "The mechanic fixed my car in no time."
- **形容词**: 机械的，力学的，与机器或其工作原理相关的。例如: "Mechanical engineering deals with the design and production of machines."

## 短语
- "Molecular mechanics" 指分子力学，计算化学的一个领域。
- "Automotive mechanics" 指汽车维修技术员，专门修理汽车的人。
- "Theoretical mechanics" 是理论力学，研究物体运动的理论基础。

## 例句
- 名词: "The mechanics of the machine are too complex for me to understand without further study."
- 形容词: "The new car has many mechanical features that improve its fuel efficiency."
