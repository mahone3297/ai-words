## 读音
/workmən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个从事体力劳动或手艺工作的人，通常指男性。例如: "The workman fixed the leaky pipe in my bathroom."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 表示与工人或手工技艺相关的。例如: "He wore his workman's boots to the job site."

## 短语
- "A skilled workman" 指的是有技术的工人。
- "Workman's compensation" 是指为工伤提供赔偿的保险。

## 例句
- 名词: "The workman arrived promptly at 8 am to start the repairs."
- 形容词: "The workman's toolbox was filled with various tools for different tasks."
