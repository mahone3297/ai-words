## Pronunciation
/workmən/

## Definitions
### Noun
A workman is a noun that refers to a person who is skilled in a particular trade or craft, typically one who works with their hands and is involved in manual labor or construction. It can also be used more generally for a worker or employee, especially in a non-managerial role.

### Verb (transitive)
To workman something means to perform tasks or handle materials in a skillful, competent manner, often associated with a specific trade or craft.

## Phrases
- "Workmanlike efficiency" describes a high level of skill and professionalism in completing a task.
- "A workman's job" signifies a task that requires technical expertise and attention to detail.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The workman carefully measured and cut the wood for the new cabinet."
- **Verb**: "The plumber arrived as a workman to fix the leaky faucet."
- "The carpenter showed great workmanship in building the staircase."
