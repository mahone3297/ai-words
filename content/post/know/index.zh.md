## 读音
/naʊ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 知道，了解；认识。例如: "I know the answer to this question."（我知道这个问题的答案）
- **名词**: 知识，了解。例如: "He has a great know of history."（他对历史非常了解）

## 短语
- "to know about something" 知道有关某事的情况。例如: "She knows about the upcoming event."
- "know someone well" 对某人非常了解。例如: "We've been friends for years, so I know him well."

## 例句
- 动词: "I know how to fix this computer problem."
- 名词: "His know of ancient cultures is impressive."
