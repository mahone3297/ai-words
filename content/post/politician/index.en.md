## Pronunciation
/polɪˈtɪkən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who holds a political office or is actively involved in politics, typically as a member of a legislative body or a political party. [Oxford](https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/politician)
- **Verb**: To engage in political activities or hold a political position. [Cambridge](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/politician)

## Phrases

- **Verb**: "After years of campaigning, she finally became a politician, representing her district in the state legislature."
