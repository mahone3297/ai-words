## Pronunciation
/rʌɡ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A thickly woven floor covering, typically made of pile fabric, that is placed on a floor. For example: "The Persian rug added warmth to the living room."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or covered with a rug. For example: "A cozy rug-covered floor made the study inviting."
- **Verb**: To cover (a floor) with a rug. For example: "They decided to rug the dining room with a beautiful oriental carpet."

## Phrases
- "Lay a rug down" means to place a rug on the floor.
- "rug it up" is a colloquial expression to wear warm clothes or accessories, especially during cold weather.
- "Rug out" can refer to setting up or preparing a space, often with a rug, for a particular purpose.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The antique rug was a family heirloom passed down through generations."
- **Adjective**: "She stepped onto the soft, plush rug and felt the warmth beneath her feet."
- **Verb**: "He rugged up his boots before venturing outside into the snow."
