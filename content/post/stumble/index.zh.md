## 读音
/stʌmbl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 不小心绊倒；踉跄。例如: "I stumbled over a rock in the dark."
- **动词**: 偶然发现；偶然碰到。例如: "I stumbled upon an old photo album while cleaning my closet."
- **名词**: 绊倒；跌跤。例如: "He had a stumble but quickly regained his balance."

## 短语
- "stumble upon" 意为偶然发现或遇到。例如: "I stumbled upon a hidden garden in the city."
- "stumble over" 表示在…上绊倒。例如: "Watch out for that cable, you might stumble over it."

## 例句
- 动词(绊倒): "She stumbled while walking up the stairs but managed not to fall."
- 动词(偶然发现): "While browsing online, I stumbled upon a great deal on a new laptop."
- 名词: "He caught himself before the stumble could turn into a fall."
