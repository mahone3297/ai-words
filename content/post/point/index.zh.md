## 读音
/ˈpɔɪnt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 点，尖端：A small, sharp end or tip, as of a pen or needle.
  2. （观点、论点）要点，重点：The main or most important part of an argument or discussion.
  3. 分数：A unit of score in various games and sports.
  4. 地方，位置：A particular place or location.

- **动词**:
  1. 指向，指向某物：To direct one's finger, a tool, or something similar toward something.
  2. （在比赛中）领先：To have a higher score than one's opponent in a game or contest.

## 短语
- "On point" 意为准确无误，非常到位。
- "Point out" 指出，强调。
- "Point in time" 特定的时间点。

## 例句
- 名词: "The pencil has a sharp point for precise writing."
- 动词: "He pointed at the map to show us our destination."
- 比赛中的领先："Our team is currently three points ahead in the match."
