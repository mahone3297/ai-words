## 读音
/ˈsprɪŋkl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 轻轻撒上，散布。例如: "She sprinkled sugar on her cereal in the morning."
- **名词**: 少量，少许。例如: "A sprinkle of rain didn't stop us from going out."

## 短语
- "to sprinkle with" 意味着用少量的东西覆盖。例如: "The baker sprinkled the cake with colorful sugar crystals."
- "a light sprinkle" 指小雨或少量的水。例如: "We had a light sprinkle this morning, but it didn't last long."

## 例句
- 动词: "She sprinkled some glitter on the card to make it more festive."
- 名词: "There was just a sprinkle of snow on the ground, not enough for a snowman."
