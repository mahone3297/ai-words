## 读音
/prɪˈfɜːrəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 喜好，偏爱；优先选择。例如: "She has a strong preference for classical music over pop."
- **动词**: 更喜欢，倾向于。例如: "I prefer tea to coffee in the morning."

## 短语
- "have a preference for" 表示对某物有偏好。例如: "She has a preference for red over blue."
- "show a preference" 意为表现出对某事物的倾向。例如: "The children showed a preference for playing outdoors."

## 例句
- 名词: "His preference for adventure movies led him to choose that film."
- 动词: "When given a choice, I always prefer to work from home."

## 同义词
- liking
- fondness
- inclination
- taste
- predilection
