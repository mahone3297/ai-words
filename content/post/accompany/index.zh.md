## 读音
/əˈkʌmpəni/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 陪伴；陪同。例如: "She accompanied her friend to the doctor's appointment."
  - 伴奏；配乐。例如: "The pianist accompanied the singer during the performance."

## 短语
- "accompany someone" 意为陪伴某人。
- "accompany by" 指由...伴奏。

## 例句
- 动词: "He accompanied his sister to the cinema."
- "The violinist accompanied the ballad with a soothing melody."

## 同义词
- go with
- escort
- chaperone
- attend
- support (in a musical context)
