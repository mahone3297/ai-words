## 读音
/ˈrɪzɪstəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 抵抗，反抗；阻力；（电子等的）电阻；免疫力。例如: "The swimmer felt the resistance of the water."，"This wire has low electrical resistance."
- **动词**: 抵抗，对抗。例如: "She resisted the temptation to eat the cake."
- **形容词**: 抗拒的，有抵抗力的。例如: "The material is highly resistant to fire."

## 短语
- "Physical resistance" 物理阻力，通常指物体对运动的阻碍。
- "Electrical resistance" 电阻，指电流通过材料时遇到的阻碍。
- "Resistance training" 抗阻力训练，一种增强肌肉力量的运动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The airplane's engines must overcome air resistance to take off."
- 动词: "He tried to resist the urge to laugh during the serious speech."
- 形容词: "This type of wood is resistant to termites."

## 同义词
- Opposition
- Objection
- Refusal
- Resistance to
- Counteraction
