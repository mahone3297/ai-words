## 读音
/ˈsɪmplɪsɪti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 简单；单纯；朴素。例如: "The beauty of the design lies in its simplicity."
- **形容词**: 简单的；易懂的。例如: "The instructions were so simple that even a child could follow them."

## 短语
- "In simplicity" 表示简单或朴素的状态。
- "Live life simply" 倡导简单的生活方式。
- "Simplicity is the ultimate sophistication" 这是一句名言，意为“简单是最高级的优雅”。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist's goal was to capture the simplicity of rural life."
- 形容词: "The recipe calls for simple ingredients and minimal preparation."

## 同义词
- Uncomplicated
- Straightforward
- Basic
- Elemental
- Bare
