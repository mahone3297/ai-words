## Pronunciation
/sɔːrs/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The origin or point of derivation of something, especially of a belief, custom, or idea. 2. A supply or source of water, fuel, power, etc. 3. A person or thing from which something is derived or obtained. 4. [Computing] The initial or starting point of a program or data flow.
- **Verb**: To originate or come from; be the source of. To supply or provide (something).

## Phrases
- "Trace back to its source" means to find the original origin or cause.
- "Water source" refers to the natural or man-made supply of water.
- "The source of inspiration" denotes the origin of an idea or motivation.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The theory was traced back to its historical source."
- **Verb**: "The river serves as a reliable water source for the nearby village."
- "The book's popularity can be attributed to its compelling source material."
