## 读音
/ˈsɔːrs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 起源；来源：指事物的出处或产生之处。例如: "The news report cited multiple sources for their information."
  - 出处；提供者：指提供信息、数据或支持的人或物。例如: "She is a reliable source for business insights."
  - 原始材料；素材：在创作中使用的原始或未经加工的材料。例如: "The artist drew inspiration from various sources for her new painting."

## 短语
- "Source of income" 指收入的来源。
- "Primary source" 指第一手资料，直接来自事件或时期的信息。
- "Secondary source" 指第二手资料，基于第一手资料的分析或解释。

## 例句
- 名词: "The water supply for the city comes from several underground sources."
- 名词: "She credited the original source for the idea in her speech."
