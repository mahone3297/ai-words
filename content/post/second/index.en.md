## Pronunciation
/sɛkənd/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The number that comes after the first and before the third in the order of counting: *The second child in line.*
2. The position or rank as the next one in a series: *He finished second in the race.*
3. A second unit or portion of something: *A glass of water, please, just a second.*

### Verb
1. To occupy the place or rank as the second: *She secured second place in the competition.*
2. To come or be after the first: *He will speak second on the agenda.*

## Phrases
- "In second place" denotes not the top spot.
- "The second time" refers to a repetition or subsequent occurrence.
- "Wait a second" is used to request a moment's pause.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The second book on the shelf is the one I want."
- **Verb**: "She was second to cross the finish line."
- **Phrasal**: "I need to think about it for a second."
