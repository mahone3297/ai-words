## 读音
/sʌd'nli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 突然地，出乎意料地。表示行为或事件发生得很迅速，没有预兆。

## 短语
- "suddenly appear" 意为突然出现。
- "suddenly change" 表示突然改变。

## 例句
- "I was walking down the street when, suddenly, a dog ran out in front of me."
- "The sky was clear a moment ago, but suddenly, dark clouds rolled in."

## 同义词
- abruptly
- all of a sudden
- unexpectedly
- without warning
- in a flash
