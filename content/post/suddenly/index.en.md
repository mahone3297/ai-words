## Pronunciation
/səˈdʒenli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Expressing or denoting that something happens without warning or expectation. For example: "The storm suddenly struck in the middle of the day."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or indicating a sudden change or occurrence. For example: "Her mood changed suddenly from happy to sad."

## Phrases
- "Suddenly, the lights went out."
- "The plan was revised suddenly due to unforeseen circumstances."
- "He looked up suddenly as he heard his name."

## Example Sentences

- **Adjective**: "The situation took a sudden turn for the worse."
- **Noun**: "A sudden gust of wind blew through the room."
