## 读音
/ˈlɪnɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 由亚麻纤维制成的布料，通常用于制作衣物、床单、桌布等。例如: "She ironed the linen napkins for the dinner party."
- **名词**: 亚麻制品的总称，包括衣物和家居用品。例如: "The hotel provided high-quality linen for their guests."

## 短语
- "Linen closet" 是存放亚麻布制品如床单、毛巾等的储藏室。
- "Linen fabric" 指的就是亚麻布料。

## 例句
- "The dress was made of soft, breathable linen."
- "They had to restock the linen closet with fresh towels and sheets."

## 同义词
- Linen cloth
- Flax fabric
- Cotton
- Hemp
- Batiste (轻薄亚麻布)
