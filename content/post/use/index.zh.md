## 读音
/juːz/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 使用；利用。例如: "She uses a computer for work."
    - 习惯于做某事。例如: "He's used to getting up early."
    - 对…有某种影响或效果。例如: "The medicine doesn't seem to use on his headache."

- **名词**:
    - 用途；使用。例如: "The primary use of this tool is for gardening."
    - 习惯；惯例。例如: "It's against the rules to smoke in public, but people still do it out of habit or use."

## 短语
- "Put something to use" 意思是将某物投入使用。
- "Get into the habit of using something" 意味着养成使用某物的习惯。

## 例句
- 动词: "We use water to cook and clean."
- 名词: "The new technology has many practical uses."
