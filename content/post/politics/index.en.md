## Pronunciation
/polɪˈtɪks/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The activities, policies, and principles that guide the governance of a country or organization, often involving competition for power and influence. *Example*: "Politics play a significant role in shaping government decisions."
- **Verb**: To engage in the activities and processes of government, especially in making decisions and trying to gain power or support. *Example*: "He's been heavily involved in local politics for years."
- **Adjective**: Relating to the affairs of government or the application of power and authority. *Example*: "Her expertise lies in political analysis."

## Phrases
- "Get involved in politics" means to participate in the political system.
- "Political discourse" refers to discussions about politics.
- "The political spectrum" represents the range of opinions and ideologies on a particular issue.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The debate focused on domestic politics and international relations."
- **Verb**: "He decided to run for office after years of political activism."
- **Adjective**: "The political climate was tense during the election season."
