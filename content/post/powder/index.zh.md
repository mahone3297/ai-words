## 读音
/pəˈdraʊər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 细小的粉末，通常由固体研磨或分解而成。例如: "She applied face powder to her skin."
- **名词**: 雪粉，滑雪场上的新鲜雪。例如: "The skiers were thrilled with the fresh powder on the slopes."
- **名词**: 炸药，火药。例如: "The old cannon was loaded with gunpowder."
- **动词**: 用粉末覆盖或涂抹。例如: "The chef powdered the dish with cinnamon."

## 短语
- "Baking powder" 是烘焙中的发酵剂，由碱性物质和酸性物质组成。
- "Gunpowder tea" 指的是经过轻微发酵的绿茶，形状像火药颗粒。

## 例句
- 名词: "She used baby powder to keep her skin dry and comfortable."
- 名词: "The mountain received a fresh blanket of powder overnight."
- 动词: "The baker dusted the cake with powdered sugar before serving."
