## Definitions
- **Noun**: The act of relaxing or the state of being relaxed; ease or freedom from tension. For example: "After a long day, he enjoyed a peaceful moment of relaxation."
- **Verb**: To become less tense or stressed; to rest or unwind. For example: "She likes to relax by reading a book."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by relaxation or ease; not tense or strained. For example: "A relaxing vacation is just what we need."
