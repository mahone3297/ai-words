## 读音
/ˈrɛlətɪvli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 相对地，比较而言。表示某个事物在某种程度上与其他事物相比。例如: "This task is relatively easy compared to the last one."

## 短语
- "relatively speaking" 意为“相对地说”，用来引入一个比较。例如: "Relatively speaking, we have more time than we thought."

## 例句
- "The weather today is relatively mild for this time of year."
- "Her new job pays relatively well compared to her previous one."

- Fairly
- Somewhat
