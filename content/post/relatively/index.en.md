## Pronunciation
/rɪˈlaɪtli/

## Definitions
### Adverb
- Used to modify verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs, indicating the degree to which something is true or occurs. It suggests a comparison or measurement with respect to some standard.

## Phrases
- "Relatively speaking" introduces a comparison or qualification.
- "He's quite fit, relatively speaking."
- "Her work is good, considering the short time frame, relatively."

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "The project was completed relatively quickly compared to previous ones."
- "She is doing well, relatively, given her circumstances."
- "Their income is above average, relatively speaking."

## Synonyms
- Fairly
- Approximately
- Comparatively
- To some extent
- In a comparative manner

## Antonyms
- Absolutely
- Completely
- Unconditionally
- Exclusively
- Literally (when emphasizing an absolute truth)
