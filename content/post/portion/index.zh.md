## 读音
/ˈpɔːrʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   1. 部分；一份：A part or section of something, especially a specified quantity or share.
   2. 分配物；配额：A quantity of something, especially food or drink, that is or is intended to be one person's share.

- **动词 v**:
   分配；给予一部分：To divide something into parts or shares; to give or assign a part of something.

## 短语
- "a portion of" 表示一部分：例如 "She ate only a small portion of her meal."
- "portion out" 意为分配：例如 "Portion out the soup equally among the guests."

## 例句
- 名词: "The restaurant serves generous portions of food."
- 动词: "He portioned the cake into equal slices for each guest."
