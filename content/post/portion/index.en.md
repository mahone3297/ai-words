## Pronunciation
/ˈpɔːrʃn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A part or division of something, typically considered separately or as a unit: "A portion of the cake was left uneaten." 
- **Verb**: To take or divide something into parts or sections: "She portioned out the food for the guests."
- **Adjective**: Constituting a part or share of a whole; fractional: "He received a small portion of the inheritance."

## Phrases
- "Take a portion" means to have or serve a specific amount of something.
- "Portion out" refers to dividing and distributing evenly.
- "Portion control" is the practice of managing the size of one's servings.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Each child received a portion of their birthday cake."
- **Verb**: "The chef portioned the vegetables according to the recipe."
- **Adjective**: "He saved a portion of his savings for emergencies."
