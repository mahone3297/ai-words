## 读音
/ˈtəʊtəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 总计的，全部的，完全的。例如: "The total cost of the items was $100."
- **名词**: 总数，总额。例如: "The total number of students is 500."
- **动词**: 计算总数，总计。例如: "Let's total up the expenses."

## 短语
- "In total" 意为总计，合计。例如: "There were 20 people in attendance in total."
- "Total amount" 指的是总金额或总数量。例如: "What's the total amount we need to pay?"

## 例句
- 形容词: "The total amount of time spent on the project was 120 hours."
- 名词: "The total included both the price of the product and the shipping fee."
- 动词: "We need to total the scores to determine the winner."
