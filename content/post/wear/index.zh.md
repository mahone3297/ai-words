## 读音
/wer/

## 释义
- **动词**: 穿着，佩戴。例如: "She wears a red dress to the party."
- **名词**: 穿着，服装，损耗。例如: "The wear on his shoes showed he had walked a long way."

## 短语
- "wear something well" 表示某人穿某件衣服好看。
- "wear out" 意味着磨损或疲惫。
- "wear something proudly" 表示自豪地穿戴某物。

## 例句
- 动词: "He always wears a hat when he goes outside."
- 名词: "The new collection features modern and comfortable wear."
- "Her smile never wears out, even after a long day."
