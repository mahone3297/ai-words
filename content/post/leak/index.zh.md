## 读音
/liːk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 渗漏，泄漏；泄露（信息）。例如: "There's a leak in the roof."，"The company suffered from a data leak."
- **动词**: 泄露，使渗漏。例如: "The pipe was leaking water."，"She leaked the confidential information to the press."

## 短语
- "leak out" 意为逐渐透露或泄露出去。例如: "News of the merger leaked out before the official announcement."
- "to have a leak" 表示有泄漏问题。例如: "Our bathroom seems to have a leak in the faucet."

## 例句
- 名词: "The government is investigating the source of the security leak."
- 动词: "The plumber fixed the leak in our bathroom sink."
- 感叹词: (不适用，"leak" 不作为感叹词使用)

## 同义词
- Disclose
- Reveal
- Expose
- Let slip
- Inform
