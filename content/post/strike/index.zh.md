## 读音
/straɪk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 敲击；撞击。例如: "He struck the ball with his golf club."
  2. 罢工；停工。例如: "The workers went on strike to demand better working conditions."
  3. 突然发生；爆发。例如: "A sudden storm struck the area."
  4. 划燃；点燃。例如: "He struck a match to light the candle."

- **名词**:
  1. 敲击；打击。例如: "The boxer landed a powerful strike on his opponent's jaw."
  2. 罢工；停工行动。例如: "The teachers' strike lasted for three weeks."
  3. （地质）断层；裂缝。例如: "The mine was located along a major geological strike."
  4. （棒球）三振出局。例如: "The pitcher struck out the batter with a fastball."

## 短语
- "on strike" 在罢工中。例如: "The nurses are currently on strike for higher pay."
- "strike out" （棒球）三振出局；尝试失败。例如: "The batter struck out in the bottom of the ninth inning."
- "strike while the iron is hot" 趁热打铁，抓住时机。例如: "Now is the time to strike while the iron is hot and invest in the stock market."

## 例句
- 动词: "The hammer struck the anvil with a loud clang."
- 名词: "The baseball player celebrated his game-winning strike."
- 短语: "The union decided to call off the strike after reaching a settlement."
