## 读音
/tuː/

## 释义
- **数词**: 二，基数词，表示数量。例如: "There are two apples on the table."
- **代词**: 用于指代两个人或事物中的第二个。例如: "One for you and one for me, that makes two."

## 短语
- "Twosome" 表示两人一组或一对。
- "Two of a kind" 指两个相似或匹配的事物。
- "Two-bit" 有时用来形容微不足道或便宜的。

## 例句
- 数词: "We need two tickets for the movie."
- 代词: "The first and second runners-up were neck and neck, but in the end, it was a tie between them – two excellent performers."
