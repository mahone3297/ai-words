## Pronunciation
/səˈwʌm/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: To some extent; to a moderate degree. It modifies adjectives or adverbs, indicating a lesser degree or moderation. 

## Phrases
- "Somewhat cold" implies a mild coldness.
- "Somewhat improved" suggests a noticeable but not complete improvement.
- "Somewhat surprised" expresses a moderate level of surprise.

## Example Sentences
- "The weather was somewhat chilly for this time of year."
- "Her mood was somewhat better after receiving the news."
- "He was somewhat knowledgeable about the topic."

## Synonyms
- Moderately
- Fairly
- Rather
- To some extent
- A little
- Bit

## Antonyms
- Completely
- Utterly
- Absolutely
- Very
- Extensively (when modifying a negative quality)
