## 读音
/səmˈðʌt/

## 释义
- **副词**: 稍微，有点儿，某种程度上。例如: "I'm somewhat tired after the long journey."
- **形容词**: 有些，某种程度上的。例如: "The weather is somewhat better today."

## 短语
- "somewhat of" 表示“有些，有一点”。例如: "She has somewhat of a talent for painting."
- "somewhat like" 意为“有点像”。例如: "His new job is somewhat like his previous one."

## 例句
- 副词: "The room was somewhat cold, so I turned up the heating."
- 形容词: "The movie was somewhat disappointing, but still enjoyable."
