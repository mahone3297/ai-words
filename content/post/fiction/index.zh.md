## 读音
/fɪkˈʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 虚构的事物，小说，虚构的故事。例如: "Science fiction is a genre of literature that explores futuristic or imaginative concepts."
- **名词**: (尤指文学作品中的)虚构，编造。例如: "The character in the novel is purely a work of fiction."
- **动词**: 编造，虚构。例如: "She fictioned a story about her past to impress her friends."

## 短语
- "works of fiction" 指的是小说或其他虚构文学作品。
- "fictional characters" 是指故事中虚构的人物。

## 例句
- 名词: "I love losing myself in the fascinating worlds of science fiction."
- 动词: "He fictioned a tale about his travels to distant lands."
- 名词: "Her imagination was so rich that she could create entire universes in her fiction."
