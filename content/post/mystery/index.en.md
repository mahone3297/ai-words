## Pronunciation
/ˈmɪstəri/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: Something that is difficult or impossible to understand or explain, typically because it is based on secret or unknown factors. For example: "The mystery of the missing keys was never solved."
- **Noun**: A novel, movie, or play involving a suspenseful plot with unsolved crimes or secrets, often involving a detective solving the case. Example: "She loves reading murder mysteries set in Victorian England."
- **Noun**: A religious truth that can only be known by revelation and cannot be fully understood through reason alone. Example: "The mystery of the Holy Trinity is central to Christian theology."

## Phrases
- "The mystery of life" refers to the unexplained nature of existence.
- "To unravel a mystery" means to solve or discover the truth behind something mysterious.
- "A mystery shopper" is a person hired to evaluate customer service by anonymously visiting businesses.
- "A mystery guest" is an unexpected or unannounced visitor.

## Example Sentences
