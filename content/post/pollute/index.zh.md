## 读音
/pəˈluːt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 污染，使沾染有害物质。例如: "The factory was polluting the river with toxic waste."
- **名词**: 污染，污染物。例如: "Air pollution is a major environmental issue."

## 短语
- "Pollution control" 污染控制，用于减少或防止污染的措施。
- "Water pollution" 水污染，指水体受到有害物质的污染。
- "Noise pollution" 噪音污染，指过度或不适宜的声音造成的干扰。

## 例句
- 动词: "We need to find ways to reduce carbon emissions that pollute the atmosphere."
- 名词: "The government has introduced new policies to tackle air pollution in cities."
