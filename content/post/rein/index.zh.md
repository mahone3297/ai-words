## 读音
/riːn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 马具的一部分，挂在马笼头两侧，用来控制马匹的方向。例如: "He adjusted the reins before mounting the horse."
   - 控制或指导的权力。例如: "She finally had the reins of the company in her hands."

- **动词**:
   - 控制或指导（某人或某事物）。例如: "The coach reins in his players' enthusiasm during practice."
   - 制止或约束（某人或某事物）。例如: "The mayor promised to rein in spending on municipal projects."

## 短语
- "take the reins" 意味着开始掌管或控制局面。
- "loosen the reins" 表示放松控制或给予更多自由。

## 例句
- 名词: "The jockey gently tugged on the reins to signal the horse to slow down."
- 动词: "The manager decided it was time to rein in the department's excessive overtime."

## 同义词
- control
- guide
- direct
- steer
