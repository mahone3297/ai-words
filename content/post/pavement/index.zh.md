## 读音
/pəˈmeɪmnt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 建筑物外部的硬铺面，通常指街道或人行道。例如: "The workers were repaving the city's worn-out pavement."
- **名词**: 在某些情况下，也指机场跑道或停机坪。例如: "The plane landed smoothly on the concrete pavement."

## 短语
- "pavement cafe" 指设在人行道上的咖啡馆或餐厅。
- "off the pavement" 指离开人行道，进入草地或其他非硬地表面。

## 例句
- "The children were drawing with chalk on the pavement outside their house."
- "Watch out for pedestrians walking on the pavement."
- "After a heavy rain, the water pooled on the pavement."

## 同义词
- sidewalk
- footpath
- walkway
- terrace (在特定上下文中)
- tarmac (尤其指公路或机场跑道)
