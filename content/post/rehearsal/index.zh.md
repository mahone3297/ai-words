## 读音
/ˈrɪˈzɜːʃəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 排练；预演：指在正式演出或表演前的练习和彩排。例如: "The actors are having a final rehearsal before the opening night."
    - 复习；温习：在学习或考试前对材料的回顾和巩固。例如: "She spent the whole weekend in the library, rehearsing for her exams."

## 短语
- "rehearsal session" 指排练或彩排的过程。
- "rehearsal dinner" 在婚礼前一天举办的晚宴，让双方亲友熟悉彼此。

## 例句
- 名词: "The band had a long rehearsal to perfect their new song."
- 动词（非标准用法，较罕见）: "They rehearsed their lines until they could deliver them flawlessly."
