## 读音
/ˈlʌksəri/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 豪华，奢华：享受的最高水平，通常与昂贵和非必需品相关联。例如: "The hotel offered every luxury one could imagine."
  - 奢侈品：非必需但令人愉快的物品或享受。例如: "A luxury car is one that provides comfort and high-end features beyond basic transportation needs."
  
- **形容词 adj**:
  - 豪华的，奢华的：具有高级品质或舒适度的。例如: "They stayed in a luxury suite overlooking the ocean."

## 短语
- "Luxury goods" 指的是那些非必需但人们渴望拥有的高档商品。
- "Luxurious lifestyle" 指过着充满享受和高级品味的生活方式。

## 例句
- 名词: "The cruise ship was known for its luxurious amenities."
- 形容词: "She treated herself to a luxurious spa day after a long week of work."

- Opulence
