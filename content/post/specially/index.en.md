## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a special manner; particularly; especially. It can modify a verb, adjective, or other adverb to emphasize its uniqueness or distinction. For example, "She decorated the room specially for the party."

## Phrases
- "Specially designed" indicates something that has been created or tailored for a specific purpose.
- "With special care" highlights extra attention or effort put into something.
- "Specially arranged" means something has been organized or prepared in a particular way.
