## 读音
/səˈpeʃəli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 特别地，专门地，独特地。例如: "He prepared the dish specially for her."
- **形容词**: 专门的，特别的。例如: "This is a specially designed tool for that job."

## 短语
- "specially made" 意为特制的，定制的。
- "specially trained" 表示特别训练过的。

## 例句
- 副词: "She came to the event specially to see him perform."
- 形容词: "The company offers a specially formulated product for sensitive skin."

- especially
- particularly
- uniquely
- distinctively
- exclusively
