## Pronunciation
/rɪˈspɒnsəbl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: 1. Having the duty or ability to take care of something; accountable. For example: "The team leader is responsible for managing the project."
- **Adjective**: 2. Showing a readiness to accept blame or consequences. For example: "She felt responsible for the mistake."
- **Verb**: To be accountable for one's actions or decisions. For example: "He takes responsibility for his actions."

## Phrases
- "Be responsible for" implies accountability or ownership.
- "Take responsibility" means to acknowledge and act upon a task or situation.
- "Act responsibly" encourages proper conduct and decision-making.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Each employee is responsible for their work area cleanliness."
- **Verb**: "After realizing the mistake, he took responsibility and offered a solution."
- **Adverb**: "She always acts responsibly when making important decisions."
