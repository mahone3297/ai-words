## 读音
/rɪˈspɑːnsəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有责任的；负责的；可靠的。例如: "She is responsible for managing the project."
- **动词**: 承担责任；对...负责。例如: "I take full responsibility for the mistake."

## 短语
- "be responsible for" 意味着对...负有责任。
- "take responsibility" 表示接受并承担后果或义务。

## 例句
- 形容词: "As a manager, he is responsible for his team's performance."
- 动词: "She decided to take responsibility and apologize for the error."

## 同义词
- accountable
- dependable
- liable
- answerable
- trustworthy
