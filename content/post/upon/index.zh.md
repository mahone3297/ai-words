## 读音
/əˈpɒn/

## 释义
- **介词**:
1. 在...上面：指一个物体直接放置在另一个物体的表面。例如: "The book is upon the table."
2. 针对，关于：表示涉及或与某个主题或情况有关。例如: "He spoke upon the importance of education."

## 短语
- "upon arrival" 意为到达时。
- "upon consideration" 表示经过考虑后。
- "depend upon" 意为依靠，依赖。

## 例句
- 介词: "As soon as she arrived, she placed her bag upon the bed."
- 介词: "The discussion will focus upon the new policy proposal."
