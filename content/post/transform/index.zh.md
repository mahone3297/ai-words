## 读音
/ˈtrænsfɔːrm/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 改变形状、形式或性质。例如: "The caterpillar transforms into a butterfly."
  2. 转换；转化。例如: "The software can transform data from different formats."
  3. 彻底改变；改革。例如: "New management transformed the company's fortunes."

## 短语
- "Transform into" 意为转变成。例如: "The superhero transforms into a powerful being when danger arises."
- "Transform something into something else" 指将某物变为另一物。例如: "The artist transformed an old garage into a beautiful studio."

## 例句
- 动词: "The magician transformed the rabbit into a hat."
- 动词: "His ideas have transformed the way we think about technology."
