## Pronunciation
/səˈspɛʃələti/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A particular skill, occupation, or product that someone is especially good at or known for. Also, a dish or specialty meal served in a restaurant. For example: "Her culinary speciality is French cuisine."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of a particular person or place; unique or exceptional. For example: "The town's speciality is its picturesque architecture."

## Phrases
- "A person's area of speciality" refers to their expertise or field they excel in.
- "To showcase one's speciality" means to demonstrate their unique abilities or skills.
- "Ordering a speciality dish" is common when trying something specific at a restaurant.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "As a graphic designer, his speciality is creating stunning visual identities for brands."
- **Adjective**: "This small bakery boasts a wide range of delicious pastries, with their croissants being a speciality."
- **Noun (restaurant context)**: "The restaurant's signature dish, the seafood paella, is a true speciality."
