## 读音
/ˈmætʃər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 成熟的，成年的，发展完全的。例如: "The mature apples on the tree were ready for picking."
- **动词**: 发展成熟，成长。例如: "As they grew, their ideas matured and became more complex."

## 短语
- "matured wine" 指的是已经充分陈酿的葡萄酒。
- "mentally mature" 指的是心智成熟的。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The mature trees provided shade for the picnic area."
- 动词: "By the age of 30, he had matured into a responsible adult."
