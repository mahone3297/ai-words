## Definitions
- **Noun**: A baked dish typically made with a pastry crust and filled with sweet or savory ingredients, often served as a dessert or main course. For example: "She was baking a delicious apple pie for the picnic."
- **Verb**: To serve or eat a slice of pie. For example: "We enjoyed a slice of cherry pie after dinner."
- **Noun (informal)**: A person, especially a woman, considered attractive or desirable. For example: "She was the center of attention at the party, like a tasty pie."

- "Apple pie a day keeps the doctor away" is a proverb suggesting a healthy diet.
- "Key lime pie" is a popular dessert from the southern United States.
