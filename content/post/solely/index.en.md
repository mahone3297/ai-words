## Pronunciation
/səʊˈliː/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Solely means acting or done completely by oneself; without assistance or involvement from others. It can also indicate the exclusive reason or purpose for an action.

## Phrases
- "Solely responsible" implies that someone is the only one accountable for a particular outcome.
- "The task was completed solely by John" highlights John's individual effort.
- "She relies on her own skills, solely," emphasizes self-reliance.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "He solved the problem solely凭借自己的智慧."
- "She decided to take the job solely because of the flexible hours."
- "The company's success was solely due to their innovative marketing strategy."
