## 读音
/ˈsoʊli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 独自地，唯一地，仅仅，只。例如: "He completed the project solely by himself."
- **形容词**: 独一无二的，仅有的。例如: "The responsibility was solely hers."

## 短语
- "solely responsible" 意味着只有某人对某事负责。
- "solely dependent" 表示完全依赖于某事物。

## 例句
- 副词: "The decision was made solely based on financial considerations."
- 形容词: "The award was given solely for his outstanding achievement."
