## 读音
/ɪnˈsted/

## 释义
- **副词**: 代替，而不是。例如: "I'll stay home instead of going out tonight."
- **连词**: 表示选择，常与of连用，意为“而不是”。例如: "She chose to study instead of watching TV."

## 短语
- "instead of" 表示替代或选择，如 "Do this instead of that."

## 例句
- 副词: "I could have driven, but I decided to walk instead."
- 连词: "She wrote a letter instead of making a phone call."

## 同义词
- rather than
- in place of
- as an alternative to
- substituting for
- opting for
