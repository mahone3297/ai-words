## Word: Instead

## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈsted/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: As an alternative; in place of something else. It indicates what one does, has, or chooses when the other option is not taken or possible.

Example usage (adverb):
- _I'll take tea instead of coffee._
  - In this sentence, "instead" shows that tea is being chosen as a substitute for coffee.

## Phrases
- **Instead of**: This phrase is commonly used to introduce the thing being replaced or the alternative action.
  - "She went for a walk instead of watching TV."
    - Here, "instead of" introduces the alternative activity (watching TV) that was not done.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "I decided to stay home instead and read a book."
  - In this example, "instead" implies that the speaker chose reading a book over some other activity.

## Synonyms
- In its place
- As an alternative
- Rather than
- Substituting for
- In lieu of
