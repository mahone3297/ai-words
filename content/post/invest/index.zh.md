## 读音
/inˈvest/

## 释义
- **动词**: 把钱投入（股票、债券、房地产等）以期望获得回报。例如: "He decided to invest his savings in stocks."
- **名词**: 投资；投入的资金。例如: "The company attracted a large amount of invest from venture capitalists."

## 短语
- "Invest in something" 意味着在某物上投入资金。例如: "They're considering investing in renewable energy projects."
- "Long-term invest" 指的是长期的投资计划。例如: "Making long-term invests can be more profitable than short-term ones."
- "Risk invest" 指带有较高风险的投资。例如: "High-risk invests often yield higher returns but come with greater potential losses."

## 例句
- 动词: "She decided to invest $10,000 in a startup company."
