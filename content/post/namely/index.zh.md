## 读音
/ˈneɪmli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 意为“也就是”，“换句话说”，用来强调或解释前面提到的人或事物。例如: "John is a physicist, namely, he studies the behavior of matter and energy."

## 短语
- "namely" 常常用来引入对前文的详细说明或具体列举。

## 例句
- "Our team consists of three members, namely, Tom, Mary, and myself."

## 同义词
- that is
- i.e.
- in other words
- specifically
- put differently
