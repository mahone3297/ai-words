## 读音
/ɪnˈveɪʒən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 军事侵略，入侵，强行进入。例如: "The country was under threat of a foreign invasion."
- **名词**: 大量突然的出现或到来。例如: "An invasion of ants took over the kitchen."

## 短语
- "Invasion force" 指的是进行侵略的军队。
- "Invasion of privacy" 指侵犯个人隐私的行为。

## 例句
- 名词: "The invasion by the enemy army caused widespread panic among the civilians."
- 名词: "The sudden invasion of tourists during peak season put a strain on local resources."
