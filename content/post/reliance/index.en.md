## Pronunciation
/rɪˈlaɪəns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A(n) **noun** (pl. reliances) 1. Dependence on something for support or assistance; trust. 2. Confidence or reliance placed in a person or thing. 3. An object or system relied upon for support, such as a crutch or communication device.
- **Verb**: To depend on or rely upon someone or something; to place confidence in. To depend for support or success.

## Phrases
- "Rely on someone" signifies trusting someone to do or provide something.
- "Place reliance on" means to count on someone or something for help.
- "Lack of reliance" indicates a lack of dependence or trust.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Their reliance on technology for communication has increased over the years."
- **Verb**: "In difficult times, we must rely on our friends for emotional support."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "The company's future success depends on their ability to rely less on external funding."
