## 读音
/rɪˈlaɪəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 信赖，依靠，依赖。例如: "Our success depends on the reliability of this technology."
- **动词**: 信赖，依靠。例如: "She relies on her team to complete the project."

## 短语
- "reliance on something" 意味着对某事物的依赖。例如: "There is too much reliance on fossil fuels in our economy."
- "place reliance in someone" 意思是信任某人。例如: "They place great reliance in their leader's judgment."

## 例句
- 名词: "His friends admired his independence, but he knew his survival still relied on a certain degree of reliance."
- 动词: "She has learned not to rely solely on her own abilities."
