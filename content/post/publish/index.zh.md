## 读音
/pəˈbliːʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    1. 发表；出版：例如: "He published his first novel last year."
    2. 公开宣布；宣告：例如: "The company published their new policy yesterday."
    3. 刊登（信息）：例如: "The newspaper published an article about the event."
    4. （在学术期刊上）发表（研究成果）：例如: "She published several research papers in leading journals."

## 短语
- "publish a book" 出版一本书
- "publish online" 在线发布
- "publish regularly" 定期出版

## 例句
- 动词: "She decided to publish her poetry collection next spring."
- "The magazine publishes articles on current affairs every week."
- "He published a series of articles exposing corruption in the government."
