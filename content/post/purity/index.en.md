## Pronunciation
/pjuːˈrɪti/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The quality or state of being pure; freedom from impurities, contamination, or defects. 2. Moral uprightness, innocence, or integrity. 3. In religious contexts, the concept of spiritual cleanness or perfection. 4. (Chemistry) The absence of impurities in a substance.
- **Adjective**: Free from impurities or imperfections; uncontaminated. 2. Moral or spiritual cleanliness. 3. Of high quality or standard.

## Phrases
- "Purity of heart" refers to a person's inner goodness.
- "Maintain purity in thought, word, and deed" emphasizes the importance of ethical behavior.
- "The purity of a crystal" describes its transparency and clarity.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She cherished the purity of their childhood friendship."
- **Adjective**: "The water from this spring is renowned for its purity."
- "In her pursuit of purity, she rejected all worldly temptations."
