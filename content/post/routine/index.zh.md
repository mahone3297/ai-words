## 读音
/rʊˈtiːn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 日常的、常规的或例行的工作、程序或活动。例如: "I need to break out of my daily routine."
- **动词**: 按照惯例或常规进行。例如: "She routines her workouts every morning."
- **形容词**: 常规的、日常的或习惯性的。例如: "The job involves routine tasks."

## 短语
- "daily routine" 每日的常规活动
- "fall into a routine" 进入或形成习惯
- "break the routine" 打破常规

## 例句
- 名词: "His morning routine includes a jog and meditation."
- 动词: "She routines her day by starting with yoga and ending with reading."
- 形容词: "The office work is mostly routine, but it requires attention to detail."
