## 读音
/saɪd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一边，一侧：例如 "The house has a garden on one side and a garage on the other."
  - 面，方面：例如 "We need to consider all sides of the problem."
  - 支持者，立场：例如 "He's on the side of the government in this debate."
  - 边缘，沿线：例如 "There's a path running along the river side."

- **动词**:
  - 站在（某人）一边：例如 "She always sides with her younger brother in arguments."

## 短语
- "On both sides" 意为在两边，例如 "Traffic was heavy on both sides of the road."
- "Take sides" 意为支持一方，例如 "In the argument, everyone was asked to take sides."
- "Stand by one's side" 意为支持某人，例如 "A true friend will stand by your side through thick and thin."

## 例句
- 名词: "He sat down at the table, facing the window on his right side."
- 动词: "In the debate, most people tended to side with the environmentalists."
