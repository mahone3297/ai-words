## 读音
/jet/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 高速喷气飞机: "He flies a supersonic jet."
  - 喷气流: "The jet of water shot high into the air."
  - (喷气式发动机的)喷嘴: "The engine's jet produced a powerful thrust."

- **动词**:
  - 以快速或突然的方式移动: "She jetted off to Paris for a weekend getaway."
  - (尤指飞机)喷射: "The plane jets across the Atlantic in just a few hours."

## 短语
- "jet set" 指富有的、经常乘喷气飞机旅行的人群。
- "jet black" 形容非常深的黑色，像喷气机的颜色。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new jet liner is equipped with state-of-the-art technology."
- 动词: "They jetted to New York for a business meeting and returned the same day."
