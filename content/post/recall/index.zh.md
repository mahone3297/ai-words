## 读音
/riːˈkɔːl/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 回忆；回想。例如: "I can't recall his name right now."
  - 召回；撤销。例如: "The company recalled the faulty products from the market."
  - 请求（某人）回忆。例如: "The detective asked the witness to recall the events of that night."

## 短语
- "recall something from memory" 意为从记忆中想起某事。
- "recall an event" 指回忆某个事件。
- "recall a product" 指召回产品。

## 例句
- "She tried to recall the details of their conversation but it was fading away."
- "The government ordered a recall of all cars with defective brakes."
- "The therapist asked him to recall his childhood experiences."

## 同义词
- Remember
- Retrieve
- Rely on memory
- Bring to mind
- Think back
