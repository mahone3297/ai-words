## 读音
/ˈæŋ.kjəs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 忧虑的，焦虑的。例如: "She was feeling anxious about the exam results."
- **动词**: 使焦虑，使忧虑。例如: "The uncertainty of the situation made him anxious."

## 短语
- "anxious to do something" 表示急于做某事。例如: "I'm anxious to hear from you."
- "anxiety attack" 指突然的强烈焦虑感。例如: "She experienced an anxiety attack before her presentation."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The child was too anxious to enjoy the amusement park."
- 动词: "Her parents' constant worrying only anxious her more."
