## 读音
/ˈʌðər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 另一个的，其他的，别的。例如: "There is another book on the table." 
- **代词**: 另外的人或事物。例如: "Some people like coffee, others prefer tea."

## 短语
- "The other one" 指的是除了已提及或知道的那一个之外的另一个。
- "On the other hand" 表示对比或转折，意为"另一方面"。

## 例句
- 形容词: "I have two sisters, but I only mentioned one; the other is studying abroad."
- 代词: "We need to consider the interests of others, not just our own."

## 同义词
- Another
- Else
- Different
- Additional
