## 读音
/ˈkɑːlɪdʒən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 相互碰撞；冲突；撞击。例如: "The two cars had a head-on collision on the highway."
- **名词**: 碰撞事件；冲突情况。例如: "There has been an increase in collisions between cyclists and pedestrians."

## 短语
- "Collision course" 指即将发生碰撞的路线或行动。例如: "Their policies are on a collision course, which could lead to a political crisis."
- "Collisions with" 表示与...相撞。例如: "The spacecraft experienced several collisions with space debris."

## 例句
- 名词: "The football players collided during the match, causing one of them to get injured."
- 名词: "The collision between the two ships caused a massive oil spill."
