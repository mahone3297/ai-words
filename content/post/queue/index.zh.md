## 读音
/kuː/

## 释义
- **名词**: 排队；行列。例如: "There's a long queue for the rollercoaster."
- **动词**: 把…排成队；等待。例如: "They had to queue for hours to get tickets."

## 短语
- "Queue up" 意味着排队等待。
- "Join the queue" 是指加入队伍等待。
- "In a queue" 描述的是处于排队的状态。

## 例句
- 名词: "We arrived early to avoid the ticket office queue."
- 动词: "Please queue behind the yellow line."
- 情景对话: "The customers were queuing outside the store before it opened."

## 同义词
- Line
- Waiting list
- Crowd
- Formation
- Train (in a British context)
