## 读音
/wɪˈtɪnəs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 目击者，证人。指看到或听到事件发生的人。例如: "The witness's testimony was crucial to the case."
- **动词**: 见证，目睹。例如: "I witnessed the accident from across the street."

## 短语
- "key witness" 关键证人，指在法律案件中提供重要信息的人。
- "to witness an event" 见证一个事件，指亲自经历并可以描述其经过。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police were interviewing several witnesses to the crime."
- 动词: "He witnessed the signing of the peace treaty."

## 同义词
- Observer
- Spectator
- Beholder
- Seer
- Testifier
