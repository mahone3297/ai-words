## 读音
/bæt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 蝙蝠，一种能飞行的哺乳动物。例如: "There are several species of bats living in this cave."
   2. 球棒，尤指棒球中的击球工具。例如: "He swung the bat with great force."
   3. (板球)球拍。例如: "The cricket player held his bat confidently."

- **动词**: 打击，击打。例如: "She batted the ball over the fence."
- **形容词**: 用来形容与蝙蝠有关的事物。例如: "The bat-shaped kite flew high in the sky."

## 短语
- "hit for six" 在板球中击球出界，得分六分。
- "bat an eye" 不眨眼，表示惊讶或不在意。例如: "She didn't bat an eye when hearing the shocking news."

## 例句
- 名词: "The little brown bat is known for its echolocation abilities."
- 动词: "He batted the ball with incredible speed."
- 形容词: "The bat cave was filled with thousands of sleeping bats."

## 同义词
- 蝙蝠：flying fox, pipistrelle
- 球棒：club, stick
- 击打：swat, hit, strike
