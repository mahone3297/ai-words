## 读音
/ˈskeɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 尺度，比例尺：A graduated measure, especially one used as a standard of measurement.
   - 鱼鳞：The thin, overlapping plates that cover the skin of fish.
   - 大小，规模：The relative size or extent of something.
   - 重量，磅秤：An instrument for weighing.
   - (音乐)音阶：A series of notes in ascending or descending order.

- **动词**:
   - 攀登，翻越：To ascend or descend by means of a ladder or by climbing.
   - 去除鱼鳞：To remove the scales from a fish.
   - 规模调整：To adjust the size or dimensions of something.

## 短语
- "scale a mountain" 攀登山峰。
- "scale down" 缩小规模或尺寸。
- "scale up" 扩大规模或尺寸。

## 例句
- 名词: "The map uses a scale of one inch to ten miles."
- 动词: "He scaled the tall building in record time."
- 音乐: "She's practicing the C major scale on her piano."
