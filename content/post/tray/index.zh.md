## 读音
/treɪ/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个浅而平底的盘子，通常有边，用于盛放食物、饮料或其他物品。例如: "She arranged the sandwiches on a silver tray."
- **动词 (v)**: 将食物或其他物品放在托盘上提供或展示。例如: "The waiter will tray the appetizers for the guests."

## 短语
- "Serve on a tray" 意为在托盘上提供或端上食物。
- "Tray of food" 指装满食物的托盘。

## 例句
- 名词: "He carried in a large tray filled with tea cups and saucers."
- 动词: "The caterer trayed up the canapés for the cocktail party."

- Dish
- Serving platter
