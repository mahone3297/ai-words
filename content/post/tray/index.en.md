## Pronunciation
/tréɪ/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A flat dish, typically with sides, used for serving food or holding small items.
- In a kitchen, a container with compartments for storing utensils or serving dishes.
- [Computing] A data structure that holds multiple items, similar to a stack or a collection.

### Verb
- To place items on a tray or serve food using a tray.
- To transfer objects from one location to another using a tray.

### Adjective
- Characterizing something that is shallow or flat, like a tray.

## Phrases
- "Serve food on a tray"
- "Carry the tray to the table"
- "A tray of hot dishes"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She placed a cup of coffee and a plate of cookies on the breakfast tray."
- **Verb**: "The waiter carried the tray of steaming entrées to the customers' table."
- **Adjective**: "The shallow tray made it easy to reach all the items."

## Synonyms

- Dish

- Serving tray

- Tray-like

## Antonyms
- Deep
- Bowl
- Cup
