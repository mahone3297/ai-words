## 读音
/ˈiːɡər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 表示热切、渴望或急切的心情。例如: "She was eager to start her new job."
- **动词**: 表示非常愿意或渴望做某事。例如: "I'm eager to learn more about this subject."

## 短语
- "eager to do something" 表示非常想做某事。例如: "He was eager to prove himself in the competition."
- "eager anticipation" 指热切的期待。例如: "The fans were filled with eager anticipation for the concert."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The children were eager to open their Christmas presents."
- 动词: "She's eager to hear from him after their first date."

## 同义词
- enthusiastic
- keen
- anxious (in a positive context)
- excited
- zealous
