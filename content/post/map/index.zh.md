## 读音
/mæp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 地图：一种图形，显示地理位置、地形、道路等信息。例如: "I need a map to navigate through the city."
  - 计划；蓝图：用于规划或设计的详细计划。例如: "The project manager presented a new map for increasing productivity."
  - 映射；对应关系：在数学或计算机科学中，表示元素之间的关系。例如: "The function creates a map between input and output values."

- **动词**:
  - 绘制地图：制作地理或抽象概念的图表。例如: "They are mapping the human genome."
  - 把...规划出来：把事物按计划或策略组织。例如: "We'll map out our strategy for the next quarter."

## 短语
- "Map out" 意为详细规划或设计。例如: "Let's map out our travel itinerary before we leave."
- "Mind map" 是一种思维导图，用于可视化思考和组织信息。例如: "Creating a mind map helped me organize my ideas."

## 例句
- 名词: "I found a detailed map of the national park at the visitor center."
- 动词: "The software can automatically map your hard drive for file recovery."
