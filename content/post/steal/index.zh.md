## 读音
/stiːl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 偷窃；非法拿走他人的财物。例如: "He was caught stealing from the store."
- **名词**: 偷窃行为；便宜货（表示某物价格极低或性价比极高）。例如: "Getting this designer dress for such a low price was a real steal."

## 短语
- "steal something from someone" 偷取某人之物。
- "steal the show" 在表演或活动中抢尽风头。

## 例句
- 动词: "The thief managed to steal a valuable painting from the museum."
- 名词: "That car is a steal considering its features and price."
