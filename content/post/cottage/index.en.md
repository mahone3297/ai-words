## Pronunciation
/kɒˈtɪnj/

## Definitions
### Noun
A small, typically rural, one-story house, often used as a vacation home or retreat. 

### Verb
To spend time or stay in a cottage, especially as a holiday. 

### Adjective
Relating to or characteristic of a cottage, simple, or cozy.
