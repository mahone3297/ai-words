## 读音
/məˈteɪn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    - 保持；维持：确保（事物）继续存在或处于良好状态。例如: "It's important to maintain a healthy lifestyle."
    - 维修；保养：定期修理或保养以防止损坏。例如: "Regular maintenance is necessary for your car."
    - 继续；保持不变：使（关系、状态等）持续。例如: "They've managed to maintain their friendship over the years."
    - 坚持主张；声称：坚持自己的观点或立场。例如: "The defendant maintained his innocence throughout the trial."

## 短语
- "Maintain balance" 保持平衡。
- "Maintain discipline" 维持纪律。
- "Maintain contact" 保持联系。

## 例句
- 动词: "The company must maintain high standards of customer service."  
- 动词: "She works out daily to maintain her fitness level."
- 动词: "The old house requires constant maintenance to prevent decay."
