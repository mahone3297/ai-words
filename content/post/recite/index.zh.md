## 读音
/ˈrɪsaɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 背诵；朗诵：例如: "He was asked to recite a poem in front of the class."
2. 重述；复述：例如: "She could recite the events of that day in detail."

## 短语
- "recite from memory" 意为凭记忆背诵。
- "recite a story" 是讲述一个故事的意思。

## 例句
- 动词: "The students were practicing reciting their multiplication tables."
- 动词: "She could recite the whole movie script by heart."
