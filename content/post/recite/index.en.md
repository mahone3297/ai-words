## Pronunciation
/rɪˈsaɪt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A recitation is the act of repeating a passage, poem, or text by memory, often in a formal setting, such as a lesson or performance.

### Verb
To recite means to repeat something verbatim from memory, usually a learned text or speech, often in a public or educational context.

### Adjective
Recital refers to a performance where a piece is recited, especially a musical or dramatic one.

## Phrases
- "Recite a poem" or "Recite from a script"
- "Recital of a famous speech"
- "Reciting the alphabet"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The students were asked to recite their multiplication tables during the quiz."
- **Verb**: "After months of practice, she confidently recited Shakespeare's sonnet at the school assembly."
- **Adjective**: "The piano recital was a highlight of the cultural event."
