## 读音
/ˈprɑːfɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 获利；收益：The company made a substantial profit last year.
   - 利润：Their main goal is to maximize profits.
   - 好处；益处：Regular exercise brings many health profits.

- **动词**:
   - 获得利润；得益：The investment will profit us in the long run.
   - 受益；从...中得到好处：Reading widely can profit one's mind.

## 短语
- "Profit margin" 指利润率，即收入与成本之间的差额。
- "At a profit" 意为以盈利的方式。
- "To one's profit" 意为对某人有利。

## 例句
- 名词: "The business reported an increase in profits despite the economic downturn."
- 动词: "Investing in renewable energy can profit both the environment and the economy."
- 及物动词: "Learning a new language can profit you in your career."
