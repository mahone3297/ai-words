## 读音
/rɪˈstrɪkt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 限制；约束。例如: "The government has restricted the number of visitors to the park."
    - 控制；限定。例如: "The company policy restricts employees from accessing certain websites during work hours."

## 短语
- "restrict to" 限制在...之内。例如: "The new law restricts smoking to designated areas only."
- "restrict oneself" 自我约束。例如: "To lose weight, she had to restrict herself to a low-calorie diet."

## 例句
- "The city council has decided to restrict car traffic in the downtown area during peak hours."
- "The doctor recommended he restrict his alcohol consumption for health reasons."
- "The contract restricts the use of the software to non-commercial purposes only."
