## 读音
/ˈveɪlɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有效的，合法的，有根据的。例如: "Your passport is valid for another six months."
- **名词**: 效力，有效期限。例如: "The offer is only valid until the end of the week."
- **动词**: 使有效，证实。例如: "This certificate validates your identity."

## 短语
- "valid argument" 意为合理的论点。
- "valid contract" 表示有效的合同。
- "validate one's feelings" 意味着确认或认可某人的感受。

## 例句
- 形容词: "All contestants must be over 18 and have a valid ID."
- 名词: "Please check the validity of your insurance policy before driving."
- 动词: "The signature on the document was validated by a notary public."
