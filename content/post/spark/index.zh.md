## 读音
/spɑːrk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 火花，小火星。例如: "A spark flew from the grinding wheel."
          2. 灵感，创意。例如: "The idea for the novel came as a spark of inspiration."

- **动词**: 引起，点燃。例如: "His words sparked a new interest in the subject."
          2. 产生（感情）火花。例如: "Their conversation sparked a romance between them."

## 短语
- "Spark off" 意味着引发或激起。例如: "The news sparked off widespread protests."
- "Spark between" 描述两人之间的情感联系。例如: "There was an instant spark between them when they first met."

## 例句
- 名词: "The campfire produced a shower of sparks into the night sky."
- 动词: "Her speech sparked a heated debate among the audience."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但有时用于表达惊喜）: "Hello! Spark, what a surprise to see you!"

## 同义词
- Flame
- Glint
- Flash
- Idea
- Emotion (for the metaphorical sense)
