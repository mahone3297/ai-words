## 读音
/ˈɡriːtɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 表示问候或欢迎的话语或行为。例如: "She sent him a friendly greeting card."
- **动词**: 向某人表示问候。例如: "He greeted her with a smile and a handshake."

## 短语
- "Send a greeting" 意为发送问候。
- "Greeting card" 是一种用来表达祝福或问候的卡片。
- "Opening greeting" 在会议或活动中指开场的问候。

## 例句
- 名词: "The receptionist gave us a polite greeting as we entered the office."
- 动词: "He greeted his neighbors with a wave as he walked down the street."

## 同义词
- Welcome
- Salutation
- Acknowledgment
- Farewell (在某些情况下，如告别时的问候)
- Hello (有时也可作为问候的同义词)
