## 读音
/pay/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 支付金钱；付款。例如: "I need to pay the rent this week."
  - 给予报酬或报偿。例如: "She was well paid for her work."
  - 承担代价或后果。例如: "If you don't study, you'll pay the price in exams."

- **名词**:
  - 工资，薪水。例如: "He earns a good pay as a software engineer."
  - 付款，支付的金额。例如: "The total pay for the service was $50."

## 短语
- "Pay attention" 意为集中注意力，留意。例如: "Please pay attention when the teacher is explaining the math problem."
- "Pay off" 意为还清债务或取得成功。例如: "After years of hard work, he finally paid off all his loans."
- "Pay someone a visit" 意为拜访某人。例如: "I'm going to pay my friend a visit this weekend."

## 例句
- 动词: "I just paid the electricity bill online."
- 名词: "His monthly pay allows him to live comfortably."
