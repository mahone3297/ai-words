## Pronunciation
/p eɪ/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To give money to someone in exchange for goods or services; to compensate or settle a debt. [IPA: /peɪ/]
- **Noun**: The act of paying or the amount of money given in payment. [IPA: /peɪ/]
- **Adjective**: Showing respect or appreciation, often in the form of a tip or gratuity. [IPA: /peɪ/]

## Phrases
- "Pay for something" means to provide the required funds to acquire or use a product or service.
- "Pay off a loan" refers to repaying the entire amount borrowed plus interest.
- "Pay tribute" is to show respect or admiration by giving something, often symbolic, to someone or something.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "I need to pay the rent before the due date."
- **Noun**: "The total bill came to $100, including tax and tip."
- **Adjective**: "She left a generous tip for the waiter's excellent service."
