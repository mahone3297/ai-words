## Pronunciation
/səˈkɜːr/

## Definitions
### Noun
A saucer is a small, shallow dish, typically made of ceramic or another material, that is used to hold the bottom of a cup or mug, often for catching drips or serving as a coaster. In some contexts, it can also refer to a UFO-shaped object in popular culture.

### Verb (informal)
To put a cup or mug down on its saucer, usually after drinking or to serve tea or coffee.

## Phrases

- "Serve tea with a saucer"
- "Place the saucer under the teapot"
