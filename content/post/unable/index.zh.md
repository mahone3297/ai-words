## 读音
/ʌnˈeɪbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不能的，无能的，没有能力的。指缺乏必要的技能、力量或资源来完成某事。

## 短语
- "be unable to do something" 意味着无法做某事。
- "unable to cope" 表示无法应对或处理某个情况。

## 例句
- "I'm unable to attend the meeting due to illness."
- "The old man was unable to lift the heavy box."
- "She felt unable to express her feelings."

## 同义词
- incapable
- helpless
- incompetent
- powerless
- unqualified
