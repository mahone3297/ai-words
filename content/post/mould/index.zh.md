## 读音
/məʊld/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 形状或形式的模型，模子。例如: "The sculpture was made in a clay mould."
    - 发霉或霉菌。例如: "There was black mould growing on the bread."
- **动词**:
    - 使成形，塑造。例如: "The potter carefully moulded the clay into a vase."
    - 影响或塑造（人的性格或行为）。例如: "Our childhood experiences often mould our personalities."

## 短语
- "mould breaker" 打破常规的人
- "mould someone's character" 塑造某人的性格

## 例句
- 名词: "She carefully removed the sculpture from the plaster mould."
- 动词: "Years of practice have moulded him into a skilled musician."

- Mold (美式拼写)
