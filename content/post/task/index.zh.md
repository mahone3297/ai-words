## 读音
/ˈtɑːsk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 工作，任务：A specific piece of work assigned to be done; a duty or assignment. 例如: "My main task for today is to finish the report."
2. 负担，重担：Something that requires effort or difficulty to accomplish. 例如: "Lifting those boxes was quite a task."

- **动词**:
1. 给予任务：To assign a job or responsibility to someone. 例如: "The teacher tasked each student with writing an essay."

## 短语
- "set oneself a task" 设定目标或任务
- "take on a task" 承担任务
- "complete a task" 完成任务

## 例句
- 名词: "The manager gave us a challenging task to complete by the end of the week."
- 动词: "She tasked him with organizing the company's annual event."
