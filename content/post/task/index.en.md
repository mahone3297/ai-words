## Pronunciation
/tæsk/

## Definitions
### Noun
A task is a piece of work or duty that needs to be done, often with a specific purpose or goal. It can also refer to a particular assignment or responsibility given by a teacher, employer, or supervisor.

### Verb
To task (someone) is to assign them a job or responsibility, often implying that they should complete it. This can be in the context of giving a specific assignment or delegating duties.

### Adjective
Tasks can be used to describe something that is assigned or required, as in "a list of daily tasks" or "a challenging task."

## Phrases
- "To complete a task" means to finish the job assigned to you.
- "Task-oriented" refers to someone who focuses on completing tasks efficiently.
- "On a task" implies being fully engaged in a specific activity.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The manager assigned several tasks to the team for the upcoming project."
- **Verb**: "She tasked me with organizing the event for next week."
- **Adjective**: "The employees were working diligently on their assigned tasks."
