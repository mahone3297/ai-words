## Pronunciation
/shaɪ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Referring to a person who is timid, bashful, or reluctant to interact with others, especially in social situations. Synonyms: bashful, timid, introverted.
- **Verb**: To feel or act shy; to be hesitant or reserved in social situations. Synonyms: be bashful, feel self-conscious, be reticent.

## Phrases
- "Be shy about something" implies feeling hesitant or uncomfortable.
- "She's quite shy around strangers."
- "Don't be shy, come say hello!"

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The shy girl hesitated to speak in front of the class."
- **Verb**: "He was shy to ask for her number, but eventually mustered up the courage."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "She felt shy as she introduced herself to the new colleagues."
