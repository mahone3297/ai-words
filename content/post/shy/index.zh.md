## 读音
/ʃaɪ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 腼腆的，害羞的。例如: "She is a shy child who doesn't like to speak in public."
- **副词**: 害羞地，胆怯地。例如: "He approached her shyly, not knowing what to say."

## 短语
- "shy away from something" 意为避开或不敢面对某事。
- "be shy of an amount" 表示不足某个数量。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The young boy was too shy to ask for help."
- 副词: "She looked at him shyly and smiled."
