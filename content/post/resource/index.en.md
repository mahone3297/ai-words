## Pronunciation
/rɪˈzɔːrs/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **resource** is a material, information, or asset that is used to fulfill a need or achieve a goal. It can be physical, such as natural materials or equipment, or intangible, like knowledge or skills.

### Verb
To **utilize** or **employ** a resource means to make use of it for a specific purpose or advantage.

### Adjective
*Efficient* or *abundant* are adjectives often used to describe the availability or effectiveness of a resource.

## Phrases
- "Utilize resources effectively"
- "Maximize resource utilization"
- "A vital resource for learning"
- "Natural resources like water and land"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The company relies heavily on its human resources to innovate and stay competitive."
- **Verb**: "Farmers must conserve water as a precious resource during droughts."
- **Adjective**: "She has access to abundant resources for her research project."
