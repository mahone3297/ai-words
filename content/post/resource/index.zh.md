## 读音
/rɪˈsɔːrs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 资源；财力；手段。例如: "Natural resources such as oil and water are essential for life."
- **名词**: 信息来源；参考材料。例如: "The internet is a vast resource of information."
- **名词**: (人力资源、技术等)可用的人或物。例如: "Our team's biggest resource is their creativity."

## 短语
- "Human resources" 指的是一个组织的人力资源部门或员工。
- "Financial resources" 指经济上的财富或资金。
- "To tap into a resource" 意味着利用某种资源。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company has ample financial resources to invest in new projects."
- 名词: "The library is a valuable resource for students researching their assignments."
- 名词: "Our team's diverse skillset is a valuable resource when problem-solving."
