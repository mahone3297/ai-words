## 读音
/prəˈpɔːrʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 比例，部分与整体之间的关系。例如: "The proportion of students who passed the exam was high."
    - 定量，适量。例如: "Add the ingredients in the right proportion."
- **动词**:
    - 分配，按比例分配。例如: "The cake ingredients need to be proportioned correctly."
- **形容词**:
    - 成比例的，均衡的。例如: "The furniture is proportioned well in the room."

## 短语
- "In proportion to" 表示成正比，按照...的比例。例如: "Her income is in proportion to her experience."
- "Out of proportion" 意味着不成比例，过大或过小。例如: "His reaction was out of proportion to the problem."

## 例句
- 名词: "The golden ratio is a mathematical proportion found in art and nature."
- 动词: "The chef proportioned the flour and water to make perfect pasta dough."
- 形容词: "The building's design features harmonious proportions."
