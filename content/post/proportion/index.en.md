## Pronunciation
/prəˈpɔːrʃən/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **proportion** is a relationship between two quantities or magnitudes that can be expressed as a ratio or a fraction. It describes how much one thing is relative to another, often in terms of size, quantity, or part-to-whole relationship.

### Verb
To **proportion** something means to determine its size, shape, or arrangement according to a given ratio or standard.

## Phrases
- "In proper proportion" indicates a balanced or harmonious relationship.
- "Out of proportion" suggests an imbalance or disproportion.
- "Proportionate response" refers to an adequate or suitable reaction based on the situation.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The house was designed with large windows in proportion to its size."
- **Verb**: "She proportioned her time between work and leisure activities."
- **Adjective**: "His anger was not in proportion to the issue at hand."
