## 读音
/rɪˈliːs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 公开发行，发布：如 "The new software has just been released to the public."
  - 放开，释放：如 "The bird was released from its cage."
  - 解除，消除：如 "He felt a sense of release after sharing his worries."

- **动词**:
  - 发行，发布：如 "They will release their new album next month."
  - 放开，释放：如 "The prisoner was finally released from jail."
  - 宽慰，放松：如 "The yoga class helped her release tension in her muscles."

## 短语
- "Release the pressure" 意为减轻压力。
- "Release a statement" 指发表声明。
- "Let go and release your fears" 意为放下并释放你的恐惧。

## 例句
- 名词: "The movie studio announced the release date for the summer blockbuster."
- 动词: "The company decided to release the product earlier than planned."
- 放松: "After a long day at work, she just wanted to release all her stress."
