## 读音
/spɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 呼吐（唾液或其他小量液体）。例如: "He spat on the ground in disgust."
2. （用力）吐出。例如: "The child spat out the bitter medicine."

- **名词**:
1. 呕吐或吐出的液体。例如: "There was a small pool of spit on the floor."
2. （口语中）不友好的话或行为。例如: "She let out a string of spit when she was angry."

## 短语
- "spit and polish" 形容精心打理、清洁得一尘不染的状态。
- "spit in the ocean" 比喻做无足轻重的事情。

## 例句
- 动词: "He spat angrily after hearing the bad news."
- 名词: "Be careful not to step on that spit on the sidewalk."
