## Pronunciation
/system/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A collection of interconnected components or elements that work together to form a unified whole, often with a set of rules or procedures. For example: "The school's grading system is quite complex."
- **Verb**: To establish or organize according to a particular method or arrangement. For example: "They are working on reorganizing the office system."
- **Noun**: A person's mental or emotional makeup, especially their attitudes, beliefs, and values. For example: "She has a unique belief system."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of a particular organization or structure. For example: "The system architecture is scalable."

## Phrases
- "Operate a system" means to manage or control a machine or process.
- "User-friendly system" refers to an easy-to-use interface.
- "Hierarchical system" describes a structured arrangement with clear levels of authority.

## Example Sentences
