## 读音
/ɪrˈɡərərəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不规则的，不符合常规的，无规律的。例如: "The verb 'go' has irregular past tense forms (went)."
- **名词**: 不规则的事物，特指不遵循一般规律的行为或形式。例如: "The heartbeat became more irregular."

## 短语
- "irregular verb" 指不遵守常规变化规则的动词。
- "irregular polygon" 指边数不相等或角度不规则的多边形。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The pattern of her sleep was highly irregular."
- 名词: "There are several irregularities in this financial report that need to be investigated."
