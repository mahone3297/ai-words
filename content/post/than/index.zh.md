## 读音
/ðæn/

## 释义
- **连词**: 表示对比，意为“比...更...”。例如: "She is taller than her sister."
- **介词**: 在某些固定搭配中，如"than ever"，表示程度的增加。例如: "He works harder than ever before."

## 短语
- "more than" 表示超过、多于。例如: "I have more than ten books."
- "less than" 表示少于、不足。例如: "There are less than five apples left."

## 例句
- 连词: "I prefer tea than coffee in the morning."
- 介词: "She's happier now than she was last year."

## 同义词
- compared to
- rather than
- in comparison with
- as opposed to
- more so than
