## 读音
/priːˈfæs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 书籍、报告或演讲等前面的引言或序言，通常介绍作者、目的或背景。例如: "The preface of the book explains the author's motivation for writing it."
- **动词**: 写作或提供前言或引言。例如: "He prefaced his speech with a few words about his background."

## 短语
- "Preface to" 指的是某个作品或讨论的前言部分。例如: "The preface to the novel discusses the author's inspiration."
- "Preface a statement" 意味着在陈述之前添加说明或解释。例如: "She prefaced her comment with an apology."

## 例句
- 名词: "In the preface, the author thanks those who supported him during the writing process."
- 动词: "She prefaced her remarks by acknowledging the importance of the issue at hand."

## 同义词
- Introduction
- Foreword
- Prologue
- Opening remarks
- Preliminary comments
