## 读音
/ˈreɪdiəʊækˈtɪvɪti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 物质自发放出射线的性质或状态，通常指放射性。例如: "Radioactivity is the process by which certain elements decay over time."
- **形容词**: 形容具有放射性的。例如: "Radioactive materials must be handled with extreme caution."

## 短语
- "Radioactive decay" 指放射性物质衰变的过程。
- "Radioactive isotopes" 是指不稳定的同位素，会经历放射性衰变。

## 例句
- 名词: "Scientists study radioactivity to understand the origins of the universe."
- 形容词: "The radioactive waste generated by nuclear power plants requires careful disposal."

## 同义词
- Radioactivity can be synonymous with:
  - Radioisotropy
  - Nuclear radiation
  - Radioactive emission
