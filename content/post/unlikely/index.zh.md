## 读音
/ʌnˈlaɪkli/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不可能的，不大可能的。例如: "It's unlikely that he'll arrive on time."
- **副词**: 很不可能地，未必。例如: "They unlikely found a solution so quickly."

## 短语
- "unlikely to happen" 表示某事发生的可能性很小。
- "an unlikely pair" 指看起来不相配或意想不到的一对。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The weather forecast predicts an unlikely snowfall in August."
- 副词: "Despite their differences, they unlikely became close friends."

- Unimaginable
