## 读音
/ˈɪmpækt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 结果，影响；撞击，冲击。例如: "The new policy had a significant impact on sales."
- **动词**: 影响，对...产生作用。例如: "Her speech impacted the audience deeply."
- **形容词**: 有影响力的，冲击力的。例如: "The artist's work has an immediate impact on viewers."

## 短语
- "Have an impact on" 意味着对...产生影响。例如: "Positive feedback can have a great impact on one's confidence."
- "Make an impact" 表示产生显著的影响。例如: "She aims to make a big impact in her field of research."

## 例句
- 名词: "The economic crisis had a profound impact on people's lives."
- 动词: "His decision to quit smoking impacted his health positively."
- 形容词: "The movie was praised for its powerful and impactful storyline."
