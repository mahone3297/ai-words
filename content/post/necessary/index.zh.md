## 读音
/ˈnɛsərəri/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 必要的，必需的，不可或缺的。例如: "It is necessary to wear a helmet while riding a bike."
- **名词**: 必需品，必要条件。例如: "Food and shelter are the necessities of life."

## 短语
- "In the necessary case" 在必要的情况下。
- "The necessary expenses" 必要的开支。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Doing regular exercise is necessary for good health."
- 名词: "The company provided all the necessary tools for the job."

## 同义词
- Essential
- Indispensable
- Vital

- Compulsory
