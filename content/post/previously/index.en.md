## Pronunciation
/preˈviəri/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a previous manner or time; previously. For example: "She had previously worked at that company."
- **Adjective**: Occurring or done before; prior. For example: "The event was previously scheduled for next week."
- **Particle**: Used to indicate a point in time before another action, often in clauses. For example: "I will tell you previously what happened."

## Phrases
- "Previously mentioned" refers to something that has been talked about earlier.
- "Previously unseen" indicates something that hasn't been encountered before.
- "Previously owned" describes something that has been owned by someone else.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "Previously, they had discussed the topic at length."
- **Adjective**: "The report details previously unknown facts."
- **Particle**: "She explained previously how she fixed the issue."

## Synonyms
- Earlier
- Formerly
- Prior to
- Previously stated
- Antecedently

## Note
- The adverb form can also be written as "previously" without the hyphen, while the adverbial particle is usually hyphenated. However, both usages are commonly accepted.
