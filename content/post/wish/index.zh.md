## 读音
/wɪʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 表达希望或愿望。例如: "I wish I could fly like a bird."
- **名词**: 愿望，希望。例如: "She had but one wish, to be reunited with her family."
- **名词**: 在某些固定表达中，如 "birthday wish"（生日愿望）。

## 短语
- "Make a wish" 意为许愿，通常在吹灭生日蜡烛时。
- "Wish upon a star" 指对星星许愿，表达对美好事物的渴望。

## 例句
- 动词: "I really wish we could spend more time together."
- 名词: "The children blew out the candles and made their birthday wishes."
- 固定表达: "He wished upon a shooting star for good luck."
