## 读音
/ˈfɪzɪsɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 物理学家。指研究物理学的人，专门探讨物质、能量、空间、时间及它们之间相互作用的专业人士。例如: "Albert Einstein was a famous physicist."

## 短语
- "a theoretical physicist" 指专注于物理理论研究的专家。
- "an experimental physicist" 指进行物理实验以验证理论的科学家。

## 例句
- "The physicist proposed a new theory about the nature of dark matter."
- "She won the Nobel Prize for her groundbreaking work in physics."

## 同义词
- Scientist
- Physic
- Natural philosopher (archaic)
