## Pronunciation
/ʃɒp/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A retail establishment where goods or services are sold, often in small quantities: *I need to go to the grocery shop for some milk.*
2. The act of shopping: *She spent the afternoon browsing through the shops downtown.*
3. A person's place of work, especially if they sell goods: *He works as a mechanic at the car shop.*

### Verb
1. To buy goods or services from a shop: *I'll be shopping for new clothes this weekend.*
2. To browse or look at items in a shop without necessarily buying: *She just likes to shop for fun.*

### Preposition
To indicate the location of a business or activity: *The shop is located on Main Street.*

## Phrases
- "Do some shopping" means to go to a store to buy things.
- "Shop around" implies comparing prices or options before making a purchase.
- "Open the shop" signifies starting the business for the day.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She usually does her shopping on Saturdays."
- **Verb**: "I'll stop by the bookshop after work to pick up a novel."
- **Preposition**: "The flower shop opens early every morning."

## Synonyms
- Store
- Market
- Retail outlet
- Shopper
- Buying
- Browse

## Antonyms
- Sell (opposite of buying)
- Close (opposite of open)
