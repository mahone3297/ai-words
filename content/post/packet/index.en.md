- **Noun (Computing)**: A unit of data transmitted over a network in a single message or frame. For instance: "The file was broken down into packets and sent across the internet."
- **Verb**: To put something into a packet or pack it up in this manner. For example: "I need to packet all these documents before sending them off."

## Phrases
- "Data packet" refers to a specific amount of data handled as a single entity within a communication network.
- "Packet boat" is a historical term for a ship that travels regularly between ports carrying mail, goods, or passengers.
- "Packet switching" is a method of transmitting data by breaking it down into small units called packets and routing them independently over a network.
