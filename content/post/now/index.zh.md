## 读音
/naʊ/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示现在的时间或强调当前的状态。例如: "I am busy now." 
- **连词**: 引导时间状语从句，表示在某一时刻或时间段。例如: "Now, let's talk about the plan."

## 短语
- "Right now" 意为"此刻"或"马上"，例如: "I need your help right now."
- "As of now" 表示"从现在起"，例如: "As of now, we will be working on this project together."

## 例句
- 副词: "It's raining now, so take an umbrella with you."
- 连词: "Now that everyone is here, we can start the meeting."

## 同义词
- Currently
- At present
- Immediately
- Straight away
- In the present moment
