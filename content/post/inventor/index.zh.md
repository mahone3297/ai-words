## 读音
/ɪnˈvəntər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 发明者，创造者。指发明新事物或改进现有事物的人。例如: "Thomas Edison was an inventor of great repute."
- **动词**: 发明，创造。描述做出创新或原创设计的行为。例如: "She inventoried a new method for producing solar panels."

## 短语
- "The inventor of the telephone" 指的是电话的发明者。
- "To invent something useful" 是指创造出有用的新事物。

## 例句
- 名词: "Nikola Tesla was an inventor known for his contributions to electrical engineering."
- 动词: "She invented a device that made daily tasks easier."
