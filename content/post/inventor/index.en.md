## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈvɛntər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who invents something, typically a new device, process, or technique. They create or design original items or concepts that often lead to patents and practical applications in various fields like science, technology, and engineering.

Example Sentence: 
- _Alexander Graham Bell was the inventor of the telephone._

Synonyms:
- Creator
- Innovator
- Designer
- Discoverer
- Founder
- Developer
- Pioneer

### Phrases
- "The inventor of" - used to attribute the creation of something to a specific individual. For example: "Thomas Edison was the inventor of the light bulb."
- "Inventor's mind" - referring to a creative and innovative thought process. Example: "Only an inventor's mind could devise such a unique solution."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She dreamed of becoming an inventor from a young age, constantly tinkering with machines and creating prototypes."
- "As a prolific inventor, he held over a hundred patents throughout his lifetime."
