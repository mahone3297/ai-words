## Pronunciation
/əˈfeər/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. An event, especially one involving romantic or sexual involvement: *an extramarital affair*
2. A matter of importance or concern: *a political scandal* 
3. A social engagement or meeting: *a business dinner affair*

### Verb
1. To engage in a romantic or secret relationship: *They were rumored to be having an affair.*
2. To involve oneself in a particular activity or project: *She's currently involved in several charitable affairs.*

### Adjective
1. Concerning or involving a relationship between two people: *an affair of the heart*

## Phrases
- "An affair of state" refers to a matter of great importance to government.
- "A love affair" denotes a romantic relationship.
- "In the public eye, their affair ended amicably."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Their affair was exposed by a series of incriminating emails."
