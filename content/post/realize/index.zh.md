## 读音
/ˈriːəlaɪz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 实现（想法、目标等）：将想法或计划变为现实。例如: "She finally realized her dream of becoming a writer."
  - 意识到，认识到：明白或理解到一个事实或情况。例如: "It took him a while to realize the mistake he had made."

## 短语
- "Realize one's potential" 意为发挥某人的潜力。
- "Realize profits/losses" 表示实现利润或亏损。

## 例句
- 动词（实现）: "After years of hard work, he realized his ambition to start his own business."
- 动词（意识到）: "She only realized the importance of saving when it was too late."
