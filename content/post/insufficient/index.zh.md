## 读音
/ɪnˈsʌfɪʃənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不足够的；不足的；不充分的。例如: "The amount of food is insufficient for the number of guests."
- **副词**: 不足地；不够地。例如: "He worked insufficiently, which led to mistakes."

## 短语
- "insufficient funds" 意为存款不足。
- "insufficient evidence" 指证据不足。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Her savings were insufficient to cover the unexpected expenses."
- 副词: "His preparation for the exam was insufficient, so he failed."

- Inadequate
- Insufficiently
- Deficient
