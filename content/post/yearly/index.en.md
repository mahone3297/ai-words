## Pronunciation
/ˈjɪəli/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Occurring, happening, or performed once every year; annual. For example: "The company holds a yearly retreat."
- **Adverb**: In a yearly manner; annually. For example: "She saves money yearly for her vacation."

## Phrases
- "Yearly income" refers to the earnings received once a year.
- "Yearly subscription" is a recurring payment for a service or product on an annual basis.
- "Yearly report" is a summary of activities or financials for a 12-month period.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The company publishes a yearly financial report."
- **Adverb**: "We review our goals yearly to set new targets."
- "She takes a yearly vacation to explore new places."

## Synonyms
- Annual
- Seasonal
- Regularly annual
- Once-a-year
- Year-round (referring to a recurring event throughout the year)
