## 读音
/ˈjɜːrlɪ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 每年的，年度的，按年计算的。例如: "We have a yearly performance review at work."
- **副词**: 每年一次地，一年一次地。例如: "The family reunion happens yearly in the summer."

## 短语
- "Yearly income" 指年收入。
- "Yearly subscription" 指年度订阅。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Her yearly salary is quite satisfactory."
- 副词: "The company holds a yearly conference for its employees."

## 同义词
- Annual
- Yearly can also be synonymous with "annually" when used as an adverb.
