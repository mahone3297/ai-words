## 读音
/læɡ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 延迟；滞后：表示在时间上比预期或应有的慢。例如: "There was a noticeable lag between the signal and the response."
  - 落后状态：在发展或进步上的滞后。例如: "The economy is experiencing a lag in job creation."
  - （尤指长裤的）吊带：固定裤子的带子。例如: "He adjusted the lags on his overalls before starting work."

- **动词**:
  - （使）滞后，延迟：使某事比预期或应有的慢。例如: "The internet connection is lagging during peak hours."
  - （在游戏中）作弊，通过操控网络延迟以获取优势。例如: "Some players were accused of lagging to gain an unfair advantage."

## 短语
- "lag behind" 表示落后于他人或进度。例如: "She was determined not to lag behind her classmates in studies."
- "lag time" 指两个事件之间的时间间隔。例如: "There's usually a lag time between submitting an application and receiving a response."

## 例句
- 名词: "The video streaming was suffering from lag due to poor network connectivity."
- 动词: "His computer started lagging when he opened too many programs at once."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但在某些语境下可能被接受）: "Hello? Can you hear me? There seems to be some lag on the call."

## 同义词
- Delay
- Hesitation
- Sluggishness
