## 读音
/ˈlɪŋk/

## 释义
- **名词** (n): 连接或联系；环节。例如: "There is a direct link between smoking and lung cancer."
- **动词** (v): 把两个事物连接在一起。例如: "The software links all the files in the folder."
- **名词** (n) - 在互联网上：超链接。例如: "Click on the link to access the website."
- **动词** (v) - 在互联网上：创建超链接。例如: "I linked my social media profiles on my blog."

## 短语
- "Create a link" 创建一个连接或超链接。
- "Broken link" 指失效或无法访问的超链接。
- "Follow the link" 按照链接进行操作或访问。

## 例句
- 名词: "The two parks are connected by a footpath link."
- 动词: "The program can link your calendar with your email."
- 名词 (互联网): "Please share the link to the article with your friends."
- 动词 (互联网): "I've linked the video tutorial in the description below."
