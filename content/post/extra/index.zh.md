## 读音
/ˈekstrə/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 额外的，附加的，超出通常的。例如: "We have some extra seats available for the show."
- **名词**: 附加物，额外的东西。例如: "Can I have an extra cookie, please?"
- **副词**: 非常，特别。例如: "She did extra well on her exam."

## 短语
- "Extra charge" 意为额外费用。
- "Extra work" 指的是额外的工作或加班。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The hotel offered us an extra bed for our child."
- 名词: "I ordered an extra large pizza for dinner."
- 副词: "She studied extra hard to prepare for the test."
