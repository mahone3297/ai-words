## Pronunciation
/rɪˈspɒnd/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **noun** in this context refers to a response, either verbal or written, given in reply to a question or request. It can also imply a reaction or answer. Example: "His quick response to the challenge was unexpected."

### Verb
As a **verb**, 'respond' means to reply or give an answer, typically in reaction to something. Example: "She responded to the email promptly."

### Adjective
In some cases, 'responsive' can be used as an adjective to describe someone who is quick to react or attentive to others' needs. Example: "A responsive customer service team is essential."

### Adverb
'Respondingly' acts as an adverb, indicating how one reacts or answers. Example: "He spoke responsively to the crowd's questions."

## Phrases

- "The company's prompt response to the crisis"
- "He didn't respond well to the news"
