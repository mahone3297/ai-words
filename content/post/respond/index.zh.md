## 读音
/rɪˈspɑːnd/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 回答或答复（问题、评论等）。例如: "She responded promptly to my email."
2. 对…作出反应或回应。例如: "The audience responded enthusiastically to the performance."
3. （身体）对刺激或治疗作出反应。例如: "The patient responded well to the new medication."

## 短语
- "Respond to" 意为对…作出回应或回答。例如: "Please respond to the questionnaire by Friday."
- "In response to" 表示作为对…的回应。例如: "They organized a protest in response to the government's decision."

## 例句
- 作为回答: "When asked about his plans, he responded that he was going to travel."
- 对刺激的反应: "The plant responds to light by turning its leaves towards it."
- 对评论的回应: "She responded angrily to his criticism."
