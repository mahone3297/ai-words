## 读音
/stɔːri/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个叙述性的事件或经历的序列，通常具有开始、发展和结局。例如: "She told us an interesting story about her childhood."
- **名词 (n)**: 作品，如小说、剧本或电影中的情节。例如: "The movie's story was based on a true event."
- **名词 (n)**: 新闻报道或消息。例如: "There was a breaking news story on the evening news."

## 短语
- "Once upon a time" 常用来开头讲故事。
- "The story goes that..." 用于介绍传说或传闻。
- "Behind the story" 指的是事情的真实情况或不为人知的一面。

## 例句
- 名词: "He wrote a story about a brave knight and a dragon."
- 名词: "The movie's story kept us on the edge of our seats."
- 名词: "The newspaper published an investigative story on corruption in local politics."
