## 读音
/trɪm/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 装饰；修饰：The curtains have a beautiful trim in gold thread.
  - 修剪；整饰：She gave her hedge a trim to make it look neat.

- **动词**:
  - 修剪；修整：He trimmed the branches to let more light into the garden.
  - 减少；削减：The company had to trim its expenses due to financial difficulties.

- **形容词**:
  - 整齐的；整洁的：The room looked trim and tidy after cleaning.
  - 精致的；讲究的：The model wore a trim black dress for the event.

## 短语
- "trim the sails" 在帆船上调整帆的角度以利用风力。
- "in trim" 表示处于良好状态或准备就绪：The ship was in trim for the voyage.

## 例句
- 名词: "The decorator added some trim around the window to enhance the room's appearance."
- 动词: "She regularly trims her hair to keep it looking healthy."
- 形容词: "The athlete was in excellent physical trim for the competition."
