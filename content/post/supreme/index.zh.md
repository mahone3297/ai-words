## 读音
/suːˈpriːm/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 最高（级）的，至高无上的；极好的。例如: "The Supreme Court is the highest legal authority in the country." 
- **名词**: (某些国家的)最高法院。例如: "The decision was made by the US Supreme Court."

## 短语
- "supreme authority" 指最高权力或权威。
- "supreme ruler" 指至高无上的统治者。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Her cooking skills are supreme, making every meal a delight."
- 名词: "The Supreme Court has the power to interpret the constitution."

## 同义词
- Ultimate
- Topmost
- Greatest
- Utmost
- Apex
