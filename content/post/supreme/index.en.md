## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Of the highest rank, most important, or most excellent; paramount. For example: "The supreme court is the highest judicial authority."
- **Noun**: A person or thing considered to be the best or most important in a particular field. For example: "She was crowned the supreme champion of the competition."
- **Adverb**: Absolutely, without comparison; to the utmost degree. For example: "He worked supreme effort to finish the project on time."

## Phrases
- "Be supreme in something" means to excel or be the best at it.
- "Supreme authority" refers to the highest level of control or command.
- "Supreme comfort" describes the utmost level of ease or relaxation.
