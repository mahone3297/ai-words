## 读音
/ˈmɑːskɪtoʊ/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 一种小型昆虫，属于双翅目，通常以人类或其他动物的血液为食，雌蚊会叮咬宿主。例如: "The mosquito is known for transmitting diseases like malaria."
  
## 短语
- "mosquito repellent" 防蚊剂
- "mosquito net" 蚊帐

## 例句
- 名词: "I put up a mosquito net around my bed to avoid getting bitten at night."

## 同义词
- Bug
- Insect
- Bloodsucker
- Nuisance
- Fly (in some contexts)
