## 读音
/ˈlɑːɡ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 记录或日志，通常是指计算机中的系统记录或航海日志。例如: "I checked the server logs for any errors."
   - 木头块，原木。例如: "The lumberjack cut down the tree and left a large log on the ground."

- **动词**:
   - 记录或登记。例如: "The program automatically logs all user activities."
   - 切成或劈成木头块。例如: "He logged the wood for winter fuel."

## 短语
- "Log in/out" 登录/退出（账户）。
- "Activity log" 活动日志，记录了某段时间内的活动。
- "Cut logs" 切割原木。

## 例句
- 名词: "The forest ranger reviewed the daily log to monitor wildlife sightings."
- 动词: "She logged every book she read during the summer vacation."
- "He stacked the freshly cut logs neatly by the shed."
