## 读音
/nɪnˈti/

## 释义
- **数词**: 九十个；第九十。例如: "There are ninety students in this class." 或 "It's my ninetieth birthday today."

## 短语
- "ninety-nine percent" 表示百分之九十九。
- "in ninety days" 意为在九十天内。

## 例句
- "She is ninety years old but still very active."
- "The company has a ninety percent success rate."
- "We have ninety minutes left until the movie starts."

## 同义词
- ninety = 90 (数字上没有同义词，但可以表达相似概念的词有)
- three-score and ten (旧式表达法，意为90)
- nonagenary (形容词，指90岁的)
