## 读音
/pɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 别针，大头针。例如: "She used a pin to hold the fabric in place."
2. (体育)球杆，如保龄球中的木瓶。例如: "He knocked down all ten pins with one roll."

- **动词**:
1. 囫囵吞枣；把...钉住。例如: "The child pinned the poster on the wall."
2. 在比赛中将对手压在地板上（如摔跤）。例如: "The wrestler managed to pin his opponent to the mat."

## 短语
- "pin something down" 意味着固定或确定某事。例如: "I need to pin down the details of the project."
- "pin someone's hopes on something" 表示寄托希望于某事。例如: "They pinned all their hopes on winning the lottery."

## 例句
- 名词: "She found a safety pin in her purse to fix her broken necklace."
- 动词: "The wrestler pinned his opponent to the mat for three seconds and won the match."

## 同义词
- noun: staple, tack, fastener
- verb: attach, secure, anchor
