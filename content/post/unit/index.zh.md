## 读音
/ˈjuːnɪt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 基本单位；单元；部件。例如: "A unit is the smallest quantity that can exist alone or be counted."  
2. 数学中的一个，常指1的数值。例如: "In arithmetic, 1 is often referred to as a unit."

- **形容词 (adj)**: 整体的；成套的；单个的。例如: "The unit apartment has its own kitchen and bathroom."

## 短语
- "Measurement unit" 测量单位。
- "Teaching unit" 教学单元。
- "One-unit increase/decrease" 表示数量增加或减少一个单位。

## 例句
- 名词: "The chemistry lab is equipped with various measuring units for precise experiments."
- 形容词: "The hotel offers comfortable unit apartments for long-term stays."
