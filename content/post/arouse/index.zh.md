## 读音
/əˈraʊz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 唤醒；激起：例如: "The sound of the alarm clock arouses me from sleep."
  2. 引起；激发：例如: "The movie aroused her curiosity about ancient civilizations."
  3. 使兴奋；唤起兴趣：例如: "The performance aroused the audience's enthusiasm."

## 短语
- "Arouse suspicion" 意为引起怀疑。
- "Arouse one's passion" 表示激发某人的激情或热情。

## 例句
- "The smell of fresh coffee arouses me in the morning."
- "His speech was so inspiring that it aroused a desire for change in many listeners."
- "The unusual behavior aroused the police's suspicion."
