## 读音
/sprɛd/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 扩展；展开：The fire quickly spread through the building.
  - 散布；传播：They spread rumors about her.
  - 涂抹；铺开：She spread jam on her toast.
  - 增加；增长：Sales have spread rapidly in recent months.

- **名词**:
  - 范围；分布：The spread of the disease is worrying.
  - （金融）价差：The bid-ask spread is the difference between buying and selling prices.
  - 摆设的食物：We set out a delicious spread for the guests.

## 短语
- "spread out" 意为展开或摊平：The children spread out their picnic blanket.
- "spread the word" 意为传播消息：Please spread the word that the meeting has been canceled.

## 例句
- 动词: The news of her victory spread like wildfire.
- 名词: The menu offered a wide spread of dishes from around the world.
