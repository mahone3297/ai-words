## 读音
/mæsk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 遮掩面部的东西，如口罩或面具。例如: "Wearing a mask is mandatory in public transport."
  - （戏剧、电影）假面，角色。例如: "The actor played a tragic mask in the play."
  - （化学、工程）面罩，遮罩。例如: "The worker wore a protective mask during the painting job."

- **动词**:
  - 掩饰，隐藏。例如: "Her smile masked her true feelings."
  - 在（照片、图形等）上涂上一层遮蔽物。例如: "He masked out the background in the photo editing software."

## 短语
- "mask one's face" 意为遮住脸部。
- "mask one's emotions" 意味着掩饰情绪。
- "put on a brave mask" 表示假装坚强或不在乎。

## 例句
- 名词: "She put on a surgical mask before entering the hospital."
- 动词: "His laughter masked his sadness."
- 感叹词: (不适用，mask 不作为感叹词使用)
