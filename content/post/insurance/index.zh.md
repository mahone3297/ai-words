## 读音
/ɪnˈʃʊrəns/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种金融产品，为可能发生的损失提供经济补偿。例如: "Car insurance is mandatory in most countries."
- **动词 (v)**: 购买或提供保险。例如: "I need to insurance my new house against fire and theft."

## 短语
- "Life insurance" 指人寿保险，为被保险人的死亡或生存提供经济保障。
- "Health insurance" 提供医疗费用的保障。
- "Auto insurance" 或 "car insurance" 是汽车保险，用于保护车主免受意外损失。

## 例句
- 名词: "The monthly premium for her car insurance was quite high."
- 动词: "Before traveling, he made sure to insurance himself against any unforeseen circumstances."
