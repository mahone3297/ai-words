## 读音
/ʌnˈkʌm.fər.tə.bəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不舒服的，不自在的，不安的。指身体、情绪或环境上的不适。例如: "The chair was uncomfortable to sit on for a long time."
- **形容词**: 令人不悦的，使人为难的。例如: "The awkward silence made everyone feel uncomfortable."

## 短语
- "Feel uncomfortable" 表示感到不舒服或不自在。
- "Uncomfortable truth" 指难以接受但又必须面对的事实。

## 例句
- "After a long flight, my back started to feel very uncomfortable."
- "She felt uncomfortable when asked about her personal life in front of everyone."
- "The temperature in the room was uncomfortably hot."
