## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by uneasiness, discomfort, or awkwardness; causing uneasiness or discomfort. For example: "The chair was so uncomfortable that I couldn't sit on it for long."
- **Adverb**: In an uncomfortable manner; uneasily. For example: "She shifted in her seat, looking extremely uncomfortable."

## Phrases
- "Feel uncomfortable" means to experience physical or emotional discomfort.
- "Uncomfortable silence" refers to an awkward pause or quietness.
- "Don't be uncomfortable" is a way of telling someone not to feel self-conscious or uneasy.

## Example Sentences
