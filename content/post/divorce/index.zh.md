## 读音
/dɪˈvɔːrs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 法律上解除婚姻关系的状态；离婚。例如: "[They](/zh/post/they/) decided [to](/zh/post/to/) [seek](/zh/post/seek/) [a](/zh/post/a/) [divorce](/zh/post/divorce/) [after](/zh/post/after/) years [of](/zh/post/of/) marital problems."
- **动词**: 结束婚姻关系，离婚。例如: "[She](/zh/post/she/) filed [for](/zh/post/for/) [divorce](/zh/post/divorce/) [from](/zh/post/from/) [her](/zh/post/her/) [husband](/zh/post/husband/)."

## 短语
- "[Divorce](/zh/post/divorce/) proceedings" 指离婚诉讼的过程。
- "No-fault [divorce](/zh/post/divorce/)" 是指不需要证明对方有过错的离婚方式。

## 例句
- 名词: "[The](/zh/post/the/) couple's [divorce](/zh/post/divorce/) was finalized [last](/zh/post/last/) [month](/zh/post/month/)."
- 动词: "[After](/zh/post/after/) [many](/zh/post/many/) attempts [to](/zh/post/to/) reconcile, [they](/zh/post/they/) [finally](/zh/post/finally/) decided [to](/zh/post/to/) [divorce](/zh/post/divorce/)."
