## 读音
/prɪˈtend/

## 释义
- **动词**: 假装；佯装。例如: "He pretends to be interested in the topic."
- **名词**: 假装的行为；伪装。例如: "Her pretense of being busy fooled no one."

## 短语
- "Pretend to be" 意味着假装成某人或处于某种状态。例如: "The children were pretending to be astronauts."
- "Put on an act" 或 "put on a show" 也表示假装或表演。例如: "He put on an act of being confident, but inside he was nervous."

## 例句
- 动词: "She pretended not to hear their comments."
- 名词: "His pretence of ignorance was obvious."
