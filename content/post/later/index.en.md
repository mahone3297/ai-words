## Word: Later

## Pronunciation
/ˈleɪ.tər/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: At a subsequent time; afterwards. For example: "I'll call you later."
  - Also used to indicate something happening after the present moment: "Later that day, we went for a swim."
- **Noun**: A future time, often distant but unspecified. For example: "We'll discuss this matter at a later date."
  
## Phrases
- "See you later": An expression used when parting ways, meaning 'until we meet again'.
- "Later on": This phrase indicates something will happen at some point in the future, not immediately.
- "Later, alligator": A playful, informal way of saying goodbye with the response being "After while, crocodile".

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "I can finish the report later tonight."
- **Noun**: "In a later chapter, the author delves deeper into the topic."

## Synonyms
- Afterwards
- Subsequently
- Eventually
- Down the road
- In due course
- At a later time
- In the future
