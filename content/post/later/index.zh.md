## 读音
/læˈtər/

## 释义
- **副词**: 晚些时候，随后，后来。例如: "I'll call you later this afternoon."
- **形容词**: 较晚的，随后的。例如: "The later chapters of the book are more challenging."

## 短语
- "See you later" 意为稍后见，再见。
- "Later on" 表示在某个稍后的时刻。
- "In the later stages" 指在后期或接近结束的阶段。

## 例句
- 副词: "I have a meeting now, but we can discuss it later."
- 形容词: "The later version of the software has more features."
