## Word: Manly

## Pronunciation
/ˈmænli/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having qualities traditionally associated with men, such as strength, courage, and confidence. For example: "He exhibited a manly demeanor during the difficult situation."
- **Relating to Men**: Pertaining to or characteristic of men, especially in contrast to women or children. Example: "The manly clothing section of the store had suits and ties."

### Phrases
- "Manly virtues": Refers to characteristics like bravery, self-reliance, and resilience typically considered desirable in men.
- "Manly tears": Tears shed by men, often in moments of intense emotion or pride, that do not detract from their masculinity.
  
### Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "His manly voice boomed across the room, capturing everyone's attention."
- "The boy was trying to act manly, standing tall and speaking assertively."
