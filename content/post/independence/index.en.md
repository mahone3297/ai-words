## Pronunciation
/ˌɪndɪˈpendəns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The state or quality of being independent; not subject to control by others; self-sufficiency; freedom from external rule or dependence. 

  - **Example Sentence**: "The country gained its independence from colonial rule in the early 20th century."
  - "Personal independence allows individuals to make their own decisions without undue influence from others."

## Phrases
- "Declaration of Independence": A historical document declaring a nation's separation from another power or empire.
- "Financial independence": The state where one has enough income or resources to live without having to work, relying on others, or receiving public assistance.
- "Independence Day": A national holiday celebrated to commemorate the date when a country achieved independence.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The pursuit of happiness and the desire for independence are fundamental human drives."
- "She valued her independence highly and refused any financial support from her parents."
