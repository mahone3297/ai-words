## 读音
/meet/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 遇见；相遇。例如: "I met her at a friend's party last weekend."
  - 会面；会晤。例如: "The two leaders will meet to discuss the trade agreement."
  - 满足；符合。例如: "His expectations were not met by the reality of the situation."
  - 结识。例如: "I met a lot of interesting people during my travels."

- **名词**:
  - 相遇的时刻或地点。例如: "Let's meet at the café at 3 pm."

## 短语
- "Meet someone halfway" 意为做出妥协，各让一步。
- "Meet up with someone" 意为与某人会合或见面。
- "Meet the requirements" 意为满足条件或要求。

## 例句
- 动词: "I accidentally met an old friend from school while shopping yesterday."
- 名词: "It was nice to have a chance meeting with an old colleague."
