## 读音
/mekəˈnɪk/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 机械师，修理技工。指专门修理、维护或组装机器和汽车等的人。例如: "The mechanic fixed my car's engine."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 机械的，力学的，有关机械原理的。例如: "Mechanical engineering deals with the design and operation of machines."

## 短语
- "Car mechanic" 指专门修理汽车的技工。
- "Mechanical advantage" 在物理学中指的是通过机械装置增加力或移动物体的能力。

## 例句
- 名词: "The experienced mechanic diagnosed the problem with my motorcycle quickly."
- 形容词: "The mechanical system in this building is highly efficient."
