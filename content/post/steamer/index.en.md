## Pronunciation
/stiːmər/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A steamer** can refer to:
  - A type of locomotive or train powered by steam, especially one that runs on a railway.
  - A large ship or boat propelled by steam power.
  - A device for cooking food using steam, often found in a kitchen or restaurant.
  - In video games, a character or vehicle that uses steam as a means of propulsion.
  - A term for a sauna or spa where water vapor is used for relaxation.

### Verb (transitive)
- To **steam** (food) means to cook it using steam, often in a steamer basket or with a lid to trap the moisture.
- To **steam clean** refers to the process of using pressurized steam to clean surfaces or fabrics.

### Adjective
- Describing something related to steam, like a steam-powered machine or a steamed environment.

## Phrases
- "Steam engine" or "steam locomotive"
