## 读音
/ˈstiːmər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 蒸汽船，一种以蒸汽为动力的船只。例如: "The old steamer sailed along the river."
- **名词 (n)**: 蒸锅，用于蒸食物的器具。例如: "I used a steamer to cook the vegetables."

## 短语
- "steamship liner" 指定期航行的蒸汽客轮或货轮。
- "vegetable steamer" 专用于蒸蔬菜的炊具。

## 例句
- 名词: "The steamer was a common sight in the 19th-century harbors."
- 名词: "I put the fish in the steamer for a healthy dinner."
