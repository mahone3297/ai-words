## 读音
/ˈveri/

## 释义
- **动词**: 改变，变化。例如: "The weather varies a lot in this region."
- **形容词**: 不同的，多样的。例如: "There is a vary range of opinions on this topic."

## 短语
- "vary from...to..." 表示范围的变化。例如: "Prices vary from $10 to $50."
- "vary in..." 指在某方面有所不同。例如: "Students' abilities vary in math."

## 例句
- 动词: "The temperature can vary greatly from day to night."
- 形容词: "The menu offers a vary selection of dishes to suit all tastes."
