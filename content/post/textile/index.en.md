## Definitions
### Noun
A textile is a flexible material made from natural or synthetic fibers, such as cotton, wool, silk, or polyester, that is often woven, knitted, or otherwise processed into fabrics for clothing, home furnishings, and other uses.

### Verb
To textile (as in "textile production") refers to the process of making textiles, including weaving, knitting, or spinning fibers into fabric.

### Adjective
Textile can also be used as an adjective to describe materials or products related to textiles, such as a textile design or a textile industry.

## Phrases
- "Working with textiles" describes the activity of creating or dealing with fabrics.
- "A textile artist" is someone who specializes in creating art using textiles.
- "Textile industry" encompasses the businesses involved in producing and selling textiles.
