## 读音
/ˈtɪkstaɪl/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 与纺织品相关的产品，如布料、纱线或织物。例如: "The company specializes in high-quality textiles for clothing."
  - 纺织业或纺织工艺。例如: "Textiles have been an important industry in this region for centuries."

## 短语
- "Textile industry" 指的是生产和制造纺织品的工业。
- "Textile design" 涉及到纺织品的图案和颜色设计。

## 例句
- "Her dress was made from a beautiful silk textile."
- "The textile mill employs thousands of workers in the area."
- "She studied textile art at university."
