## 读音
/ˈækjərət/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 精确的，准确的，无误的。表示某事或某物非常接近真实或正确的情况。例如: "The measurements were extremely accurate."
- **副词**: 精确地，准确地。用来修饰动词，强调动作或结果的精确性。例如: "He shot the target accurately from a distance."

## 短语
- "accurate measurement" 指精确的测量。
- "accurately predict" 意为准确预测。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The GPS system is highly accurate for navigating."
- 副词: "She described the event accurately, without leaving out any details."
