## 读音
/pɪtʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 音高：音乐或声音中的高度，如 "She has a beautiful high pitch in her voice."
  - 投掷；投：如 "The pitcher threw a perfect pitch to strike out the batter."
  - 角度；倾斜度：如 "The roof has a steep pitch."
  - 提案；提议：如 "He made a pitch for a new marketing strategy."
  - （球场上的）区：如 "The baseball field has different pitches for infield and outfield."

- **动词**:
  - 投掷；扔：如 "The quarterback pitched the ball to the running back."
  - 提出（建议或计划）：如 "She pitched her idea to the investors."
  - 安营扎寨：如 "They decided to pitch their tent near the river."

## 短语
- "Pitch in" 意为贡献力量，帮忙。例如: "Everyone needs to pitch in to finish the project on time."
- "Pitch perfect" 形容音高准确无误。例如: "Her singing was pitch perfect."

## 例句
- 名词: "The singer hit a high pitch that left the audience amazed."
- 动词: "The pitcher's pitch was so fast that the batter couldn't react."
- 安营扎寨: "They spent the evening pitching their tent before settling down for the night."
