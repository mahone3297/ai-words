## 读音
/sɜːr/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 确定的，无疑的。例如: "I'm sure about my decision." 
- **副词**: 肯定地，确实。例如: "I'm sure she'll be on time."

## 短语
- "Be sure of something" 意为对某事有把握或确信。
- "Make sure" 用来确保某事发生或完成。例如: "Make sure you lock the door before leaving."

## 例句
- 形容词: "He was sure that his answer was correct."
- 副词: "I'm sure we'll have a great time at the party."
