## 读音
/ˌrɪˈstɔːr/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 修复；恢复：将某物恢复到以前的状态或功能。例如: "The old building was beautifully restored to its former glory."
  2. 归还；交还：将某物归还原主或原来的位置。例如: "She restored the borrowed book to the library."
  3. 恢复健康或精力：使某人恢复健康或精神状态。例如: "A good night's sleep restored her energy."

## 短语
- "restore order" 重建秩序
- "restore peace" 重新实现和平
- "restore balance" 恢复平衡

## 例句
- 动词: "The antique furniture needed to be carefully restored by a professional craftsman."
- 动词: "After the storm, the community worked together to restore electricity and repair damages."
- 动词: "The doctor recommended a special diet to restore his patient's health."
