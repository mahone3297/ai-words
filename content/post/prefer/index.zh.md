## 读音
/prɪˈfɜːr/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 喜欢；更喜欢。例如: "I prefer coffee to tea in the morning."
  - 要求；希望。例如: "When ordering, he usually prefers a quiet table by the window."

## 短语
- "Prefer something to something else" 意味着在两者之间更喜欢前者。
- "Would prefer (not) to do something" 用来表达对某个行动的倾向。例如: "I would prefer not to discuss this topic now."

## 例句
- "She prefers reading books to watching TV."
- "When given a choice, he always prefers to work from home."
- "If you could, what time would you prefer for our meeting?"

## 同义词
- Like better
- Favor
- Choose
- Opt for
- Tend towards
