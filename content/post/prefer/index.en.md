## Pronunciation
/preˈfɜːr/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To have a stronger liking or preference for one thing over another; to choose or opt for. For example: "I prefer tea over coffee."
- **Noun**: (n) A tendency or inclination towards a particular choice or option. For example: "Her preference is for spicy food."
- **Adjective**: (adj) Showing a strong liking or inclination; favorite. For example: "That's his preferred method."

## Phrases
- "Have a preference for" indicates a strong liking or inclination.
- "Preferable to" suggests that one thing is better or more suitable.
- "No preference" means having no particular liking or choice.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "I'd prefer to stay at home tonight."
- **Noun**: "Her preference is for reading books over watching TV."
- **Adjective**: "The quiet corner of the library is his preferred study spot."
