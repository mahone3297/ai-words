## Pronunciation
/ˈstrætədʒi/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A plan or method for achieving a particular goal**: *The company's new marketing strategy is to focus on digital platforms.*
2. **An arrangement of forces or actions designed to gain an advantage over an opponent**: *Their strategic partnership helped them gain a competitive edge.*
3. **A calculated plan for managing a situation**: *She developed a diplomatic strategy to resolve the conflict.*

### Verb
1. **To devise or formulate a plan or course of action**: *They're working on a strategy to increase sales.*
2. **To execute a plan in a systematic manner**: *The team is executing their winning strategy for the tournament.*
