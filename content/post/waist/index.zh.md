## 读音
/weɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 身体的腰部，即胸部和臀部之间的部分。例如: "She tied a belt around her waist."
- **动词**: 消耗，浪费（时间、精力等）。例如: "Don't waist your time on unimportant matters."

## 短语
- "At waist level" 指在腰部的高度。
- "To waist something" 意味着将某物束紧在腰间，或者浪费掉。

## 例句
- 名词: "The dress fitted her perfectly at the waist."
- 动词: "He was accused of waisting company resources on unnecessary projects."

## 同义词
- Abdomen
- Midsection
- Hips
- Torso
- Section (when referring to a part of something, like a section of a garment)
