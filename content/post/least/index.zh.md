## 读音
/liːst/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 最小的，最少的。例如: "This is the least amount of money I've ever had."
- **副词**: 最少，最小程度地。例如: "He cares least about what others think."

## 短语
- "at the very least" 意为至少，最低限度。例如: "We should arrive at the meeting at least on time."
- "do something to the least" 表示尽可能少地做某事。例如: "Try to use the least amount of water when washing dishes."

## 例句
- 形容词: "This is the least expensive option we have."
- 副词: "She answered him with the least interest."
