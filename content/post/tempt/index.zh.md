## 读音
/tempt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 引诱；诱惑：尝试以某种方式吸引或诱导某人做某事。例如: "The delicious smell tempted her to eat more."
2. 使感兴趣；激起兴趣：激发某人的欲望或好奇心。例如: "The new book tempted me with its intriguing title."

- **名词**:
- 诱惑物；引诱物：引起欲望或冲动的事物。例如: "Chocolate is a common temptation for many people."

## 短语
- "Resist temptation" 抵抗诱惑
- "Give in to temptation" 屈服于诱惑

## 例句
- 动词: "The offer of a promotion tempted him to stay with the company."
- 名词: "Her biggest temptation was the box of chocolates sitting on the kitchen counter."
