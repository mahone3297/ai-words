## 读音
/ˈlaɪbrəriən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 负责管理图书馆的人，图书管理员。例如: "The librarian helped me find the reference book I needed."
- **形容词**: 与图书馆或图书管理员相关的。例如: "The librarian's desk was piled high with books."

## 短语
- "Ask the librarian for assistance" 意为寻求图书管理员的帮助。
- "Librarian's hours" 指图书馆开放时间，通常由图书管理员在场时提供服务。

## 例句
- 名词: "The local librarian is organizing a book club for children."
- 形容词: "The librarian's office is filled with shelves of old, rare, and librarian-approved books."

## 同义词
- Library worker
- Bookkeeper
- Archivist
- Information specialist
- Bibliothecary
