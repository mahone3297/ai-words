## Word: Librarian

## Pronunciation
/ˌlaɪbrəˈreɪn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who works in a library and is responsible for managing and organizing its resources, helping users find information, and overseeing borrowing activities. For example: "The librarian recommended several books on the subject."

Example sentence: "I asked the librarian to help me locate a book about ancient history."

## Phrases
- "Reference librarian": A librarian with specialized expertise in assisting patrons with research and answering specific questions.
- "School librarian": A librarian working in an educational institution, often assisting students and teachers with learning materials.
- "Digital librarian": A librarian who specializes in managing digital collections and databases.

## Example Sentences
- Noun: "She decided to become a librarian because of her passion for literature and organization."
- "As a child, he spent hours talking to the friendly librarian about his favorite stories."

## Synonyms
- Archivist
- Bibliothecarian
- Information specialist
- Media specialist
- Library manager
- Bookkeeper (in a historical context)
