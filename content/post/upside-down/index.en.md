- **Adjective**: In a position or orientation with the upper part or surface facing downward or upside compared to its usual position. For example: "The table was flipped upside-down."
- **Adverb**: Describing a movement or action that places something in an upside-down position. For example: "She spun the bottle upside-down to choose a partner."

## Phrases
- "Flip something upside-down" means to turn it over so that the top becomes the bottom.
