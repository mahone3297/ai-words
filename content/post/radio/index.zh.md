## 读音
/ˈreɪdiəʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 电子设备，通过无线电波接收和播放声音广播。例如: "I love listening to the radio in the morning while getting ready for work."
- **名词**: 指无线电广播服务或节目。例如: "The radio station plays a variety of music genres throughout the day."
- **动词**: 通过无线电通信联系。例如: "We will radio the base for instructions."

## 短语
- "Turn on the radio" 打开收音机。
- "Listen to the radio" 听广播。
- "Radio silence" 在无线电通讯中指停止一切通信。

## 例句
- 名词: "She keeps a small radio by her bed to wake up to the news every day."
- 动词: "The climbers radioed for help when they got lost in the mountains."
