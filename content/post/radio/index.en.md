## Pronunciation
/rɪˈdɪo/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A device or system that receives and transmits radio waves to receive and play audio broadcasts or for communication purposes, often portable or in a car.
   - Example: "The radio was playing my favorite song this morning."
2. A broadcast or program transmitted through radio waves.
   - Example: "He tuned in to the local news radio station."

### Verb
1. To listen to or operate a radio.
   - Example: "I'll be listening to the radio on my way to work."
2. To use radio waves for communication.
   - Example: "They radioed for help when they got lost in the wilderness."

## Phrases
