## 读音
/ɪkˈsperɪmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一项尝试，尤指为了检验理论、方法或新想法而进行的有控制的操作或观察。例如: "They conducted an experiment to test the effect of light on plant growth."
- **动词**: 进行实验或试验。例如: "The scientists are experimenting with new techniques to cure cancer."

## 短语
- "Scientific experiment" 指的是基于科学方法进行的实验。
- "Experiment with something" 意味着尝试使用或应用某物以观察结果。

## 例句
- 名词: "The biology class performed a fascinating experiment on photosynthesis."
- 动词: "She's experimenting with different cooking methods to perfect her recipe."
