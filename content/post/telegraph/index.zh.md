## 读音
/ˈtelɪɡrɑːf/

## 释义
- **名词**: 电报机，电报；（过去式和过去分词为telegraphed，现在分词telegraphing）电报通信
- **动词**: 发电报；用肢体语言或表情传递信息

## 短语
- "Telegraph message" 电报信息
- "Undersea telegraph cable" 海底电缆
- "Telegraph one's intentions" 显露出自己的意图

## 例句
- 名词: "The invention of the telegraph revolutionized long-distance communication."
- 动词: "She telegraphed her excitement with wide-eyed gestures."
- 感叹词: (不常见作为感叹词使用，但可以表示强调) "Hello! The telegraph was a groundbreaking innovation in its time."
