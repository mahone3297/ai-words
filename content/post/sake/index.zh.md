## 读音
/ˈskeɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 日本清酒，一种由大米、米曲和水酿制的传统日本酒精饮料。例如: "I enjoy sipping on a good sake during a Japanese dinner."
   - 目的，利益，为了…的缘故。例如: "She did it for the sake of her children's future."

- **介词**:
   - 表示为了某个目的或原因。例如: "We're studying hard solely for the sake of passing the exam."

## 短语
- "For the sake of" 意为为了…的缘故，出于…的考虑。例如: "For the sake of our friendship, let's put this argument behind us."

## 例句
- 名词: "The sommelier recommended a premium sake to complement the sushi."
- 介词: "She agreed to stay late at work, not because she wanted to, but for the sake of completing the project on time."
