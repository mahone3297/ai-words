## 读音
/lɔː/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 法律，法规：A set of rules or principles governing a specific area or society. (一套管理特定领域或社会的规则或原则)
  - 法学，法律学：The study of law and its application. (法律及其应用的研究)

- **动词**:
  - 遵守（法律）：To act in accordance with the law. (按照法律行事)

## 短语
- "the rule of law" 意为法治，即法律高于个人或政府机构。
- "law and order" 通常指维护社会治安和稳定。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new law aims to protect consumers from unfair practices."
- 动词: "Drivers must law the speed limit on highways."
