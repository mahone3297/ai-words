## Pronunciation
/scenery/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A noun referring to the view or arrangement of natural features, buildings, or objects that form a picturesque or attractive scene, typically from a particular viewpoint. For example: "The Alpine scenery was breathtaking."
- **Adjective**: Describing the visual appearance of a place, often with an emphasis on its beauty or attractiveness. For example: "The landscape offered stunning scenery."

## Phrases
- "Enjoy the scenery" means to appreciate the view while traveling or during an outing.
- "A postcard-worthy scenery" describes a scene that is particularly picturesque or memorable.
- "Scenic drive" refers to a route with particularly beautiful views.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The tourists marveled at the ever-changing scenery along the coast."
- **Adjective**: "The cabin sat nestled amidst the serene, idyllic scenery."
- "As the train chugged through the countryside, passengers admired the scenery outside."
