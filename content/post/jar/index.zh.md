## 读音
/jɑːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种有盖子的宽口玻璃或陶瓷容器，通常用来装食物或液体，如果酱、腌菜或饮料。例如: "She kept her homemade jam in a large glass jar."
- **动词**: 不常用，偶尔用于表示把东西放入罐头瓶中保存。例如: "The farmer jarred the tomatoes for the winter."

## 短语
- "a jar of jam" 指一罐果酱。
- "to open a jar" 指打开罐头瓶的盖子。

## 例句
- 名词: "There was a small jar of honey on the kitchen counter."
- 动词: "She carefully jarred the pickles, making sure they were fully submerged in vinegar."
