## Word: Normally

## Pronunciation
/ˈnɔːrməli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a usual, expected, or typical manner; customarily or habitually. It indicates what typically happens or is true under regular circumstances.

Example sentence: _**Adverb**_: She normally wakes up at 6 a.m.

## Phrases
- "Normally speaking": As a general rule or in most cases.
   - Example: "Normally speaking, we finish work by 5 p.m."
- "Under normal circumstances": In usual or standard conditions.
   - Example: "Under normal circumstances, the train arrives on time."
- "As normal": In the usual way or returning to a routine.
   - Example: "Classes will resume as normal after the holiday break."

## Example Sentences
- _**Adverb**_: He doesn't normally eat dessert after dinner, but today he made an exception.
- The store is open from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m., but during the holidays, it stays open later than *normally*.

- Usually
- Generally

- Habitually
- Ordinarily
- Routinely
