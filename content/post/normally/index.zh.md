## 读音
/ˈnɔrməli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 通常；正常地；按照惯例。例如: "Children normally sleep for 10-12 hours a night."
- **形容词**: 正常的；一般的。例如: "It's normally warm at this time of year."

## 短语
- "In normal circumstances" 意为在正常情况下。
- "Acting normally" 意为表现得正常。

## 例句
- 副词: "She normally arrives at work by 9 AM."
- 形容词: "The temperature is normally around 25°C in summer."
