## Pronunciation
/ˈmæstəpiːs/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A work of outstanding artistry, skill, or creativity; typically referring to a painting, literary work, musical composition, film, or any other form of creative endeavor that represents the pinnacle of its creator's achievements. 

  > For example: 
  > - *The Mona Lisa is considered Leonardo da Vinci's masterpiece.*
  > - *"Pride and Prejudice" is often cited as Jane Austen's masterpiece.*

## Phrases
- "*A true masterpiece*" implies something is unparalleled in quality or brilliance.
- "*Masterpiece of literature/art/music*" refers to a work that is considered the best or most significant within its field.
  
## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The Sistine Chapel ceiling is Michelangelo's undisputed masterpiece, admired by millions around the world."
- "Her latest novel is being hailed as a literary masterpiece."
