## 读音
/məˈstɛrpiːs/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   - 代表作，杰作：一件展示出最高技艺或才能的作品，如艺术、文学或音乐作品。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) Mona Lisa is considered Leonardo da Vinci's [masterpiece](/zh/post/masterpiece/)."
   - 绝妙的东西：用来形容任何表现出非凡技能或创新的事物。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) chef's [new](/zh/post/new/) [dish](/zh/post/dish/) was [a](/zh/post/a/) [masterpiece](/zh/post/masterpiece/) [of](/zh/post/of/) culinary [art](/zh/post/art/)."

## 短语
- "[a](/zh/post/a/) [masterpiece](/zh/post/masterpiece/) [of](/zh/post/of/)..." 描述在某个领域的杰出作品。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) [film](/zh/post/film/) is [a](/zh/post/a/) [masterpiece](/zh/post/masterpiece/) [of](/zh/post/of/) suspense."
- "[to](/zh/post/to/) [work](/zh/post/work/) [on](/zh/post/on/) one's [masterpiece](/zh/post/masterpiece/)" 指致力于创作自己的代表作或重要项目。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) composer spent years working [on](/zh/post/on/) [his](/zh/post/his/) [masterpiece](/zh/post/masterpiece/) [opera](/zh/post/opera/)."
