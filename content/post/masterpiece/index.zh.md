## 读音
/məˈstɛrpiːs/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   - 代表作，杰作：一件展示出最高技艺或才能的作品，如艺术、文学或音乐作品。例如: "The Mona Lisa is considered Leonardo da Vinci's masterpiece."
   - 绝妙的东西：用来形容任何表现出非凡技能或创新的事物。例如: "The chef's new dish was a masterpiece of culinary art."

## 短语
- "a masterpiece of..." 描述在某个领域的杰出作品。例如: "The film is a masterpiece of suspense."
- "to work on one's masterpiece" 指致力于创作自己的代表作或重要项目。例如: "The composer spent years working on his masterpiece opera."

## 例句
- 名词: "Van Gogh's Starry Night is considered one of the greatest masterpieces in modern art."
- 形容词（用作形容词时，常与不定冠词"a"连用）: "This building is a masterpiece of Gothic architecture."

## 同义词
- magnum opus
- crowning achievement
- tour de force
- supreme accomplishment
- paragon of excellence
