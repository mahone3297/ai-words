## 读音
/əˈkjuːt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 
    - 急性的，剧烈的：指疾病突然而严重，或者疼痛、感觉敏锐。例如: "He suffered from acute appendicitis."
    - 敏锐的，机智的：指智力、洞察力或判断力非常出色。例如: "She has an acute sense of humor."
    - 尖锐的，刺耳的：形容声音高而尖。例如: "The sound of the alarm was acute."

## 短语
- "acute angle" 表示锐角，小于90度的角。
- "acute pain" 指剧烈的疼痛。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The patient's condition became acute, requiring immediate medical attention."
- "Her acute observations often revealed the hidden truths behind people's actions."
