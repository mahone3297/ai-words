## 读音
/saɪz/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 大小，尺寸：The dress comes in different sizes.
  - 容量，量度：A large cup has a bigger size than a small one.
  - 规模，数量：The company has grown in size over the years.

- **动词 v**:
  - 测量大小：To size a piece of cloth means to determine its dimensions.
  - 调整到合适的尺寸：The tailor sized the jacket to fit her perfectly.

## 短语
- "Size up" 意味着估计或评估某人的能力或情况。
- "Size down" 指的是减小尺寸或规模。
- "Size up against" 用来比较两个事物的大小或重要性。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please check the size chart before ordering clothes online."
- 动词: "The carpenter sized the wooden plank to fit the frame."
- 感叹词（非正式用法）: "Size, look who's here! It's been ages!"
