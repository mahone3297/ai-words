## 读音
/mɔːrn/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 表示哀悼，为失去某人而悲痛。例如: "They are mourning the loss of their loved one."
    - 也指怀旧，对过去时光的思念。例如: "She was mourning the passing of her youth."

- **名词**: 哀悼的行为或时期。例如: "The family observed a week of mourning."

## 短语
- "Mourn for someone" 意味着为某人的去世感到悲痛。
- "In mourning" 指处于哀悼状态或穿着黑色等表示哀悼的服饰。

## 例句
- 动词: "The whole community was mourning the death of the local hero."
- 名词: "She wore black during the mourning period."

## 同义词
- Grieve
- Lament
- Bemoan
- Sorrow
- Condole
