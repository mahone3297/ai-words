## 读音
/rʌbɪʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 无用的东西，垃圾；废物。例如: "There is a lot of rubbish in the garden that needs to be cleared out."
- **动词**: 把...视为废物，认为...不重要。例如: "Don't rubbish my ideas just because you disagree with them."

## 短语
- "Rubbish bin" 或 "trash can" 指垃圾桶。
- "Rubbish talk" 指胡言乱语，无意义的谈话。

## 例句
- 名词: "The council provides weekly rubbish collection services for households."
- 动词: "She rubbished his suggestion without even considering it."

## 同义词
- Trash
- Garbage
- Junk
- Waste
- Detritus
