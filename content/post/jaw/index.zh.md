## 读音
/jɔː/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 人类或动物的上下颌骨，尤指其中能开合的部分。例如: "He broke his jaw in a skiing accident."
- **名词**: 口腔的前部，由下颌骨和上颌骨构成的结构。例如: "She felt a sharp pain in her jaw while eating an apple."
- **动词 (v)**: 用力咬紧或张开下巴。例如: "The dog was jawing on a large bone."

## 短语
- "Lock jaws" 表示上下颌紧闭，无法开口。
- "Drop one's jaw" 意味着惊讶到嘴巴张大。

## 例句
- 名词: "The dentist examined his patient's jaw for signs of infection."
- 动词: "The baby was jawing on its teething ring."
- 惊讶时用法: "When she saw the surprise party, she literally dropped her jaw."
