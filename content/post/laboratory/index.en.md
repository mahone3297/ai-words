## Word: Laboratory

## Pronunciation
/ˈlæbrətəri/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A room or building equipped for scientific research, experimentation, or analysis. It typically contains specialized equipment and facilities, such as workbenches, fume hoods, and safety gear. For instance: "The chemist spent most of her day in the laboratory conducting experiments."

Example Sentence: 
- "The students were fascinated by the array of microscopes in the biology laboratory."

## Phrases
- "Laboratory experiment": A scientific procedure carried out in a lab to test a hypothesis or demonstrate a known fact.
- "Medical laboratory": A facility where clinical tests are performed on biological specimens to obtain information about a patient's health.
- "Research laboratory": A space dedicated to conducting original research in various fields, like physics, chemistry, or biology.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The team conducted their groundbreaking research in the state-of-the-art laboratory."
- "After donning protective gear, they entered the microbiology laboratory to culture bacteria samples."

## Synonyms
- Lab
- Research facility
- Testing center
- Experiment station
- Workshop (in certain contexts)
