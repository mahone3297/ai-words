## 读音
/ˈlæbrətɔːri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个进行科学实验、研究或教学的房间或建筑物。例如: "The students conducted experiments in the biology laboratory."
- **名词**: 指从事某种专业工作或研究的场所。例如: "The software development laboratory is where they create new applications."

## 短语
- "Research laboratory" 专门用于科学研究的实验室。
- "Clinical laboratory" 医学临床检验的实验室。
- "Chemistry laboratory" 进行化学实验的场所。

## 例句
- "The chemistry student was carefully measuring substances in the laboratory."
- "The laboratory has state-of-the-art equipment for genetic analysis."
- "She started her first day at the medical laboratory, excited to learn more about pathology."
