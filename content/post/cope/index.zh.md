## 读音
/kəʊp/

## 释义
- **动词**: 应对，处理（困难或压力）。例如: "She copes well with stress."
- **名词**: 手段，方法（应对问题）。例如: "Finding new coping mechanisms is crucial."

## 短语
- "cope with" 意味着成功地应对或处理。例如: "I need to find a way to cope with the workload."
- "cope up" 有时非正式地用来表示应对挑战。例如: "He's coping up with the loss of his job."

## 例句
- 动词: "Despite the challenges, she copes admirably."
- 名词: "They developed new coping strategies for dealing with anxiety."

- Deal with
- Manage
- Handle
- Tackle
- Overcome
