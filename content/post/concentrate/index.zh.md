## 读音
/ˈkɑːnsntreɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 集中（注意力、精力等）；浓缩。例如: "I need to concentrate on my studies." 
- **名词**: 浓缩物；精华。例如: "The juice has been concentrated for longer storage."

## 短语
- "Concentrate on something" 意为专注于某事。例如: "You should concentrate on your task at hand."
- "Concentration camp" 是指集中营，二战期间用来关押囚犯的地方。

## 例句
- 动词: "To pass the exam, you must concentrate while studying."
- 名词: "The juice concentrate is more potent than the original drink."
