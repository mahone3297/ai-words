## 读音
/triːt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 对待，处理。例如: "He received a fair treatment from his employer." 2. 治疗，疗法。例如: "The doctor recommended a new treatment for her illness."
- **动词**: 1. 对待，处置。例如: "She treats her employees with respect." 2. 款待，招待。例如: "They were treated to a delicious dinner at a fancy restaurant."

## 短语
- "treat someone well" 意为善待某人。
- "treat someone badly" 意为虐待或不善待某人。
- "treat something as something else" 意为把某事物视为另一事物。

## 例句
- 名词: "The patient was happy with the results of the new cancer treatment."
- 动词: "He always treats his guests with kindness and hospitality."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但可理解为对待或款待）: "Let's treat ourselves to some ice cream!"
