## 读音
/ʌnˈjuːzənli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 不寻常地，异常地，特别地。用来强调某事与常规或期望不同。

## 短语
- "unusually high" 表示异常高的。
- "unusually low" 意味着异常低的。
- "unusually quiet" 指的是异常安静。

## 例句
- "The weather was unusually warm for this time of year."
- "She was unusually reserved during the meeting."
- "His performance was unusually poor compared to his usual standards."

## 同义词
- extraordinarily
- remarkably
- exceptionally
- surprisingly
- unusually itself can also be a synonym when used in comparisons (e.g., "more unusually than usual")
