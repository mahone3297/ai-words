## Pronunciation
/unˈdjʊənli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In an atypically or unexpectedly manner; not according to the usual pattern or degree. For example: "Her reaction was unusually calm given the situation."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by being uncommon or out of the ordinary. For example: "The weather was unusually cold for May."

## Phrases
- "Unusually high" refers to something that is exceptionally high compared to the norm.
- "Unusually quiet" describes a situation that is unusually free from noise.
- "Unusually talented" is used to describe someone who possesses exceptional skills.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "She finished the marathon unusually fast, breaking the record."
- **Adjective**: "The unusually large crowd surprised the organizers."
- "Due to an unusually heavy rainfall, the streets were flooded."
