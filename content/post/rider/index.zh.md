## 读音
/ˈraɪdər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 骑行者；骑手；乘坐者。例如: "The biker was a skilled rider."
- **名词**: (马术)骑师。例如: "The jockey, a young rider, won the race."
- **名词**: (自行车、摩托车等)乘客。例如: "There's no room for another rider on this motorcycle."

## 短语
- "Ride with a rider" 意为与某人一同骑行。
- "The backseat rider" 可指坐在后座的人或爱指手画脚的人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The experienced rider effortlessly controlled the horse."
- 名词: "The child was too small to be a rider on the rollercoaster."

## 同义词
- Cyclist
- Motorcyclist
- Passenger
- Equestrian
- Driver (在某些上下文中)
