## 读音
/ˈneɪbər/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 邻居，住在附近的人。例如: "We have friendly neighbours who often share their home-grown vegetables."
  - 相邻的事物或位置。例如: "The shop is located in the same building, just a few doors down from my office - it's practically a neighbour."

- **形容词 adj**:
  - 邻近的，附近的。例如: "There's a park in the neighbourhood where we often take our dog for walks."

## 短语
- "Good neighbour policy" 指的是与邻国友好相处的外交政策。
- "Neighbourhood watch" 是一种社区居民自发组织的治安巡逻活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The neighbour's cat often comes into our garden to play."
- 形容词: "We love living in this quiet, neighbourly community."
