## 读音
/paɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一堆；堆积物；叠。例如: "There is a pile of books on the desk."
- **动词**: 堆积；堆叠。例如: "She piled the dishes in the sink."
- **量词**: 大量；许多。例如: "I have a pile of work to do."

## 短语
- "pile up" 意为堆积起来。例如: "The snow was piling up quickly during the storm."
- "a huge pile of" 表示大量的。例如: "There was a huge pile of laundry waiting to be washed."

## 例句
- 名词: "The children built a snowman with a large pile of snow."
- 动词: "He piled his clothes on the chair before taking a shower."
- 量词: "She had a pile of unanswered emails in her inbox."

- stack
