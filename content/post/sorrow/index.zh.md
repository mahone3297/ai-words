## 读音
/ˈsɔːrəʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 悲伤，悲痛，哀痛。例如: "She felt deep sorrow after her mother's death."
- **动词**: 为…感到悲伤，哀悼。例如: "They sorrowed over the loss of their friend."

## 短语
- "In sorrow" 表示处于悲痛之中。
- "Sorrowful tears" 指因悲伤而流的眼泪。

## 例句
- 名词: "The family was overcome with sorrow at the funeral."
- 动词: "She sorrowed for many days after the breakup."

## 同义词
- Sadness
- Grief
- Distress
- Melancholy
- Bereavement
