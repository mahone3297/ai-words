## Pronunciation
/sɔːˈroʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A deep emotional pain or sadness over loss, disappointment, or regret. **(adj)** causing sorrow; mournful. **(v)** to feel or express sorrow or grief.
- **Adjective**: Characterized by sorrow; mournful. **(pron)** (slang) An expression of sympathy or grief.

## Phrases
- "Feeling/expressing deep sorrow" to show profound sadness.
- "A pang of sorrow" a sudden, intense feeling of sadness.
- "Sorrowful memories" recollections that evoke sadness.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: She was overcome with sorrow after hearing the news of her grandfather's passing.
- **Adjective**: The sorrowful atmosphere filled the room after the funeral.
- **Verb**: He couldn't help but sorrow over the loss of his childhood home.
