## 读音
/stɑːp/

## 释义
- **动词**: 停止，中断某个动作或过程。例如: "Please stop talking when the teacher starts the lesson."
- **名词**: 停留点，车站。例如: "This is our next bus stop."
- **名词**: 阻止，制止。例如: "The traffic lights are there to stop drivers from running the crossing."

## 短语
- "Make a stop" 意为在某处停留或停车。
- "Stop by" 表示短暂访问或顺道拜访。
- "Come to a stop" 指完全停下来。

## 例句
- 动词: "The train will stop at several small stations before reaching the final destination."
- 名词: "There's a stop sign ahead, so slow down and prepare to stop."
- 名词: "The police set up a roadblock to stop speeding cars."
