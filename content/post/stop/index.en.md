## Pronunciation
/stɒp/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A pause or interruption in action; the act of ceasing movement or activity. For example: "The train came to a stop at the station."
- **Verb**: To cease moving, come to a halt, or bring something to an end. For example: "I stopped walking when I saw a beautiful sunset."
- **Verb**: To prevent or hinder the progress of something. For example: "The heavy rain stopped the construction work."
- **Verb**: To request or require someone to stop doing something. For example: "Please stop talking during the movie."

## Phrases
- "Stop and think" means to pause and consider.
- "Stopgap measure" refers to a temporary solution to a problem.
- "Stop motion animation" is a filmmaking technique where objects are made to appear in motion by photographing them in a series of still frames.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The car came to a sudden stop after hitting a rock."
- **Verb**: "She stopped her bike at the traffic light."
- **Verb**: "The teacher stopped the students from cheating on the exam."
- **Verb**: "I asked him to stop smoking for his health."
