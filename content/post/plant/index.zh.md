## 读音
/plɑːnt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 生物体，通常指有根、茎、叶、花和种子的绿色植物。例如: "There is a variety of plants in the garden."
- **动词 (v)**: 种植，播种。例如: "We planted some tomatoes in our vegetable patch last week."
- **名词 (n)**: 工厂，尤指制造工厂。例如: "The new car plant will create hundreds of jobs."

## 短语
- "Plant seeds" 意为播种种子。
- "Indoor plants" 指在室内生长的植物。
- "Factory plant" 指工业生产设施。

## 例句
- 名词: "The gardener carefully tended to his collection of exotic plants."
- 动词: "They decided to plant a tree in their front yard for shade."
- 名词（工厂）: "The new plant will increase the company's production capacity."
