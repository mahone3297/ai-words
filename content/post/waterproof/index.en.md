## Pronunciation
/wɜːˈtʃuːfl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by being impervious to water; not allowing water to pass through or penetrate. For example: "The backpack is waterproof, so it's perfect for hiking."
- **Verb**: To make (a material) resistant to water; to impregnate with a water-repellent substance. For example: "The company treated the fabric to make it waterproof."

## Phrases
- "Waterproof material" refers to a substance that resists water penetration.
- "Waterproof coating" is a layer applied to protect something from water damage.
- "Waterproof seal" denotes a barrier that prevents water from entering.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The tent is waterproof, so we won't have to worry about rain."
- **Verb**: "They waterproofed the roof to prevent leaks during heavy rainfall."

- Impervious

## Antonyms
