## 释义
- **形容词**: 能防水的；防潮的。例如: "This coat is waterproof, so you won't get wet in the rain."
- **动词**: 使...防水；使...防潮。例如: "I need to waterproof my tent before the camping trip."

## 短语
- "waterproof clothing" 防水衣物
- "waterproofing a surface" 为表面做防水处理
