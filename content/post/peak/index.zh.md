## 读音
/piːk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 山峰；最高点；顶点。例如: "The mountain climber reached the peak of Everest."
- **动词**: 达到最高点；达到顶峰。例如: "Her career peaked in the late 90s."
- **形容词**: 最高的；尖峰的。例如: "peak season for tourism"

## 短语
- "at the peak of" 在…的顶峰或最高水平。例如: "He was at the peak of his physical fitness."
- "peak experience" 极好的经历或体验。例如: "A mountaineer might call reaching the peak of a challenging mountain a peak experience."

## 例句
- 名词: "The view from the peak was breathtaking."
- 动词: "His sales performance peaked in the fourth quarter."
- 形容词: "Traffic is usually heaviest during peak hours."
