## Pronunciation
/piːs/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The state or quality of being free from war, conflict, or violence; tranquility. 2. Agreement, harmony, or concord between individuals or groups. 3. [U] A period characterized by calm and order after a disturbance or conflict. 4. [C] An absence of noise or disturbance. 5. [C] Peace treaty or agreement, a formal document ending a war.

- **Verb**: To maintain or bring about a peaceful state or condition; to reconcile or settle differences peacefully.
