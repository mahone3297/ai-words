## 读音
/ˈpiːs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 和平，宁静，和睦。例如: "They signed a peace treaty to end the war."
- **形容词**: 安静的，和平的。例如: "The peaceful countryside was a welcome change from the city."

## 短语
- "Keep the peace" 意味着维持和平或避免冲突。
- "Peace talks" 指为解决争端而进行的和平谈判。

## 例句
- 名词: "The Nobel Peace Prize is awarded to individuals or organizations working for peace."
- 形容词: "The peaceful atmosphere allowed her to concentrate on her work."
