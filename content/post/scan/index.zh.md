## 读音
/skæn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 仔细查看；扫描：例如 "I scanned the document for any errors."
  - 用扫描仪或其他设备把图像或文字转化为电子格式：例如 "She scanned the photo and sent it by email."
  - （无线电）搜索信号或频道：例如 "The radio scanner picked up an emergency signal."

## 短语
- "Scan through" 意为快速浏览：例如 "Scan through the text quickly to find the main points."
- "Scanner" 可指扫描设备：例如 "The security guard used a scanner to check our bags."

## 例句
- 动词: "Before printing, he carefully scanned the document to ensure no mistakes."
- 名词: "The airport uses advanced body scanners for security checks."
