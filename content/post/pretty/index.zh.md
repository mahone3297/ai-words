## 读音
/ˈprɪti/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 漂亮的，可爱的，吸引人的。例如: "She looked pretty in her new dress."
- **副词**: 相当地，颇。例如: "He did pretty well on the exam."

## 短语
- "Pretty please" 用来加重请求的语气，表示非常希望得到对方的同意。
- "Not bad, pretty good" 表示还不错，相当好。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The garden was filled with pretty flowers of various colors."
- 副词: "She plays the piano pretty skillfully for someone so young."
