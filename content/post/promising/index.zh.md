## 读音
/prɪˈmɪs.ɪŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 富有希望的，有前途的，显示出潜力的。例如: "The new project seems very promising."
- **名词**: 承诺，许诺，诺言。例如: "He made several promises but failed to deliver."

## 短语
- "promising future" 意味着有光明的未来。
- "promising talent" 指有潜力的人才。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The young athlete has shown great potential and is considered a promising star."
- 名词: "He made a promising to finish the work on time, but didn't keep his word."
