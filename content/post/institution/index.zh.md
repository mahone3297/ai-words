## 读音
/ɪnstɪˈtuʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 组织，机构；制度，规定；学院，学校
    - 例如: "The new museum is an important cultural institution."
    - 例如: "Marriage is a traditional institution in many societies."

## 短语
- "Institutionalize" 意味着使某事成为正式制度或惯例。
- "Educational institutions" 指的是各种教育机构，如学校和大学。

## 例句
- 名词: "The World Health Organization is a leading international institution in public health."
- 名词: "He graduated from a prestigious military institution."
