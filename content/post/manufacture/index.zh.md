## 读音
/ˈmænəˌfʌktʃər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 制造；工业生产；制品；产品。例如: "The company specializes in the manufacture of high-quality furniture."
- **动词**: 制造；生产；加工。例如: "They manufacture cars in their state-of-the-art factory."

## 短语
- "Mass manufacture" 意为大规模生产。
- "To manufacture goods" 是指制造商品。
- "Domestic manufacture" 指国内生产。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new smartphone is a remarkable manufacture with advanced features."
- 动词: "The company plans to manufacture more electric vehicles to meet increasing demand."
