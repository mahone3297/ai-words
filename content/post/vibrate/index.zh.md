## 读音
/vaɪˈbreɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - **振动，颤动**：物体快速来回移动或摇摆。例如: "The strings of the guitar vibrate when plucked."
  - **共鸣，共振**：与另一物体产生共鸣。例如: "The sound made the windows vibrate."
  - **感到震动**：身体或情绪上的震动感受。例如: "I could feel the ground vibrate during the earthquake."

## 短语
- "vibrate with emotion" 表示因强烈情绪而颤抖。
- "vibrate at a certain frequency" 指物体以特定频率振动。

## 例句
- 动词: "The phone was vibrating on the table."
- "When the truck passed by, the house vibrated from its engine's power."
- "She could feel her heart vibrate with excitement."
