## 读音
/juːˈnɪər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 年纪较小的；初级的；下级的。例如: "My junior brother is two years younger than me." 
- **名词**: 高中或大学的低年级学生；下级职员。例如: "She is a junior in high school."
- **名词**: （在公司或组织中）地位较低的人。例如: "He was promoted from junior associate to senior partner."

## 短语
- "junior high school" 初中
- "junior staff" 初级员工
- "senior-junior relationship" 上下级关系

## 例句
- 形容词: "The junior team members were given tasks to complete under the guidance of seniors."
- 名词: "She was chosen to represent the junior class in the student council."
- 名词: "In many companies, junior employees often work longer hours."

## 同义词
- Younger
- Less experienced
- Novice
- Inexperienced
- Lower-ranking
