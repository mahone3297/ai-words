## 读音
/meɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 邮件，通常指通过邮政系统发送的信件。例如: "I received a letter in the mail yesterday."
   - 电子邮件，电子通信的一种形式。例如: "Please check your email for the meeting details."
   - 邮箱，存放邮件的地方。例如: "The postman dropped the package into the mail box."

- **动词**:
   - 发送邮件。例如: "I'll mail the invitation to your address later today."
   - 通过电子邮件发送。例如: "She mailed the report to her boss before leaving the office."

## 短语
- "Send mail" 意为邮寄或发送电子邮件。
- "Check the mail" 检查邮件，通常指检查邮箱或电子邮件收件箱。
- "Mail server" 是处理电子邮件发送和接收的服务器。

## 例句
- 名词: "I'm expecting an important document by mail."
- 动词: "She mailed the contract to the client using express delivery."
