## 读音
/ˈɔːrɡənɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 1. 有机的，与生命体相关的，尤其是指来源于植物或动物的产品。例如: "Organic farming avoids using synthetic pesticides."
            2. 自然形成的，非人为的。例如: "The organic structure of the stone has been preserved for millions of years."
            3. 遵循自然法则的，系统的。例如: "The company's growth has been organic rather than through acquisitions."

- **名词**: 有机物，特别是指在生物体内发现的化合物。例如: "Carbon is the basis of all organic compounds."

## 短语
- "Organic food" 指的是未使用化学肥料和农药的农产品。
- "Organic chemistry" 是研究有机化合物的化学分支。

## 例句
- 形容词: "I prefer to buy organic vegetables to avoid chemical residues."
- 名词: "Scientists have discovered new organic molecules with potential medical applications."
