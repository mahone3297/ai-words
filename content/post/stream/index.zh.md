## 读音
/strɪm/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 流，溪流；连续流动的东西；广播或互联网上的媒体流。例如: "The river flows into a calm stream."，"We watched a live stream of the concert."
- **动词 (v)**: 流动，像水流一样传播；连续不断地涌现。例如: "Water streamed down the windows during the heavy rain."，"News about the event streamed across social media."

## 短语
- "stream of consciousness" 意识流，指连续不断的思绪或想法。
- "live stream" 直播，实况转播。
- "data stream" 数据流，指连续传输的数据。

## 例句
- 名词: "The mountain stream was crystal clear and inviting for a refreshing swim."
- 动词: "Her thoughts streamed through her mind as she wrote the story."
- 感叹词（非正式使用）: "Hey, stream this new song on Spotify!"

## 同义词
- Flow
- Current
- Rush
- Cascade
- Broadcast (for media streaming)
