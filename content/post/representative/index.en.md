## Pronunciation
/ˈreprɪzentətɪv/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A person who represents another person or group**: *The senator is the representative of his district.*
   - Synonyms: delegate, envoy, proxy, spokesperson, agent

2. **A characteristic or quality that is typical or typical of something**: *This painting is a good representative of the artist's style.*

### Verb
1. **To act as a delegate or agent for**: *She will represent her company at the conference.*
   - Synonym: advocate, espouse, stand in for

2. **To exhibit or display the qualities or features of**: *The new product is a representative example of the brand's innovation.*

### Adjective
1. **Characterizing or showing what is typical or typical of a person, thing, or group**: *Her design was a representative of the Art Deco movement.*
   - Synonym: typical, typical, distinctive

### Adverb
1. **In a representative manner**: *The survey results are meant to be representative of the entire population.*

## Phrases
- "Be a good representative of your team."
- "His artwork is a strong representative of the era."
- "Choose a diverse group of representatives to ensure all voices are heard."
