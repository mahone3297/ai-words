## 读音
/laɪm/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 肢体，尤指四肢的一部分，如手臂或腿。例如: "The child was climbing a tree using both her strong limbs."
  - 动物或植物的分支。例如: "The tree had many limbs spreading outwards."

## 短语
- "limb of a tree" 树枝
- "upper/lower limb" 上肢/下肢

## 例句
- 名词: "After the accident, he had to learn to walk again with an artificial limb."
- "The bird perched on a limb high above us."
