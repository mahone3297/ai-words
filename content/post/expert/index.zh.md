## 读音
/ˈekspɜːrt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 有特殊技能或知识的人；专家。例如: "She is an expert in her field of archaeology."
- **形容词**: 熟练的，精通的。例如: "He demonstrated expert cooking skills."

## 短语
- "Expert advice" 意为专业的建议。
- "Expert knowledge" 指在某一领域的专业知识。

## 例句
- 名词: "We hired an expert to analyze the data."
- 形容词: "The athlete performed with expert precision."
