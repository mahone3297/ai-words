## 读音
/ˌrɛzəˈluːʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 决议，决定：正式或官方就特定问题作出的决定，如 "The council passed a resolution to ban plastic bags."
    - 解析度：图像或声音的清晰度，如 "This TV has a high resolution, so the picture is very clear."
    - 解决，解决方法：找到或达成问题的解决方案，如 "He finally found a resolution to his financial troubles."
    - 确定，坚定：决心或坚定的态度，如 "At the beginning of the year, people often make resolutions to exercise more."

## 短语
- "New Year's resolution" 新年决心，指一年开始时设定的目标或决定。
- "Make a resolution" 决定做某事或改变某种行为。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's board of directors approved a resolution to increase employee benefits."
- 解析度: "The camera captures images in 4K resolution, providing stunning detail."
- 解决: "After hours of discussion, they reached a resolution to the conflict."
- 确定: "She made a personal resolution to quit smoking."
