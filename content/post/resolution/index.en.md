## Pronunciation
/rɪˌzəʊlˈʃn/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **1.** A formal plan or decision to do something, often outlining a course of action for the future: *New Year's resolutions for personal improvement.*
- **2.** The act of resolving a problem or conflict: *After much discussion, they reached a resolution.*
- **3.** (Computing) A setting or configuration that determines how a device or software operates: *The display resolution on my monitor is set to 1920x1080.*

### Verb
- **To resolve** (resolve a problem): *The team worked tirelessly to resolve the technical issue.*
- **To resolve into** (break down into simpler parts or components): *The fog resolved into separate buildings as the sun rose.*

## Phrases
- "Resolution day" or "New Year's resolutions"
- "Resolution of a dispute"
- "High-definition resolution"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her New Year's resolution was to exercise every day."
- **Verb**: "The company resolved to increase its investment in renewable energy."
- **Phrasal**: "After several weeks of negotiations, the two parties finally resolved their differences."
