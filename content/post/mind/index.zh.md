## 读音
/mɪnd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 思维，智力：The human mind is capable of great things.
  - 想法，意见：I have a few ideas in mind for our next project.
  - 注意力，集中精神：Keep your mind on the task at hand.

- **动词**:
  - 记住，留意：Don't forget to mind your manners.
  - 在意，介意：Would you mind if I join you?

## 短语
- "Have a change of mind" 表示改变想法。
- "Out of my mind" 意为疯狂或无法思考。
- "Keep something in mind" 意为记住某事。

## 例句
- 名词: "She has a sharp mind and can solve complex problems easily."
- 动词: "He minds the store when his parents are away."
- 注意事项: "Mind the gap between the train and the platform."

## 同义词
- Brain
- Thoughts
- Intellect
- Consciousness
- Attitude (in the sense of opinion or view)
