## 读音
/skiːm/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 计划，方案: 详细规划或设计，通常用于实现特定目标或目的。例如: "The company has launched a new marketing scheme."
  - 阴谋，诡计: 不正当或秘密的计划，通常带有负面含义。例如: "They suspected that someone was behind a fraudulent scheme."
  - 结构，体系: 事物的组织或安排方式。例如: "The color scheme of the room was soothing."

## 短语
- "color scheme" 颜色搭配或色彩方案
- "conspiracy theory" 阴谋论
- "social security scheme" 社会保障计划

## 例句
- 名词: "The architect presented a detailed scheme for renovating the old building."
- 名词: "The government's new education scheme aims to improve literacy rates."
- 名词: "She suspected a scheme to undermine her position in the company."
