## 读音
/pjʊər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 纯的，纯净的，无杂质的。例如: "The water from this spring is pure."
- **名词**: 纯度，纯洁，无污染的状态。例如: "The purity of gold is measured in karats."
- **副词**: 完全地，纯粹地。例如: "She plays the piano purely for pleasure."

## 短语
- "Pure heart" 意为纯洁的心灵。
- "In its purest form" 表示事物最原始、最纯净的状态。
- "Purely by chance" 意为完全偶然，纯属巧合。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This organic milk is pure and free from hormones."
- 名词: "The purity of his intentions was admirable."
- 副词: "She sang purely for the love of music, not for fame."

- Untainted
