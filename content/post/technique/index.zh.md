## 读音
/ˈtɛknɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 技术，方法，技巧。例如: "She improved her painting technique over time."
- **形容词**: 技术的，专门的。例如: "The technique section of the book covers various cooking methods."

## 短语
- "Surgical technique" 指外科手术的技术。
- "Learning techniques" 指学习方法。
- "New techniques in marketing" 指市场营销的新方法。

## 例句
- 名词: "The athlete spent hours practicing his free throw technique."
- 形容词: "The course focuses on developing a strong technical foundation for programmers."
