## 读音
/wɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 聪明才智，智慧；机智。例如: "He was admired for his wit and humor."
- **形容词**: 机智的，聪明的。例如: "She displayed great wit in her response."

## 短语
- "Keen wit" 指非常敏锐的智慧。
- "Wit and humor" 指幽默和机智并存的品质。

## 例句
- 名词: "The playwright's works are celebrated for their wit and clever dialogue."
- 形容词: "The comedian was known for his witty jokes that left the audience laughing."
