## 读音
/ˈʃedjuːl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个详细的时间表或计划，列出要做的事情和它们预计完成的时间。例如: "I need to check my schedule for today's appointments."
- **动词**: 安排或计划（某事）在特定时间进行。例如: "The meeting has been scheduled for next Monday."

## 短语
- "On schedule" 指按计划进行，没有延迟。例如: "The project is right on schedule."
- "Behind schedule" 表示落后于预定计划。例如: "We're behind schedule due to unexpected delays."

## 例句
- 名词: "Please send me your daily work schedule so we can find a time to meet."
- 动词: "The event was scheduled to start at 3 pm but got postponed."
