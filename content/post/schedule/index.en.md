## Pronunciation
/səˈskɛl/ (US) /ˈsɛkjəl/ (UK)

## Definitions
### Noun
A **schedule** is a plan or arrangement of events, activities, or tasks, usually displayed in a list or calendar format, detailing when and where they will take place.

### Verb
To **schedule** means to arrange or plan out a series of activities or appointments, specifying their times and order.

### Adjective
- **Scheduled** refers to something that has been arranged or planned in advance.

## Phrases
- "Keep to a schedule" means to adhere to a planned timeline.
- "Reschedule an event" indicates changing the time or date of an activity.
- "On a tight schedule" describes someone who has a busy or limited time frame.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I have a packed schedule for the week with meetings and classes."
- **Verb**: "I need to reschedule our appointment because of an unexpected conflict."
- **Adjective**: "The flight was scheduled to depart at 7 AM."
