## Definitions
### Noun
- **A relationship between people or things**: A connection or association between individuals or concepts. For example: "The book relates the two characters' experiences."
- **To understand or connect with**: The ability to comprehend and make connections. For example: "She has a strong ability to relate to others' emotions."

### Verb
- **To connect or associate**: Establish a link or correspondence between. For example: "These events are related chronologically."
- **To convey or express**: Share information, feelings, or ideas. For example: "He related the story from his childhood."
- **To have a relationship with**: Be connected or involved with someone or something. For example: "She relates well with her students."

### Adjective
- **Connected or associated**: Showing a relationship or connection. For example: "Their ideas are closely related."
- **Relevant or pertinent**: Having a bearing on or connection to a particular subject. For example: "The findings are highly relevant to our research."
