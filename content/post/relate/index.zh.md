## 读音
/riːˈleɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 关联；联系：指事物之间存在某种关系。例如: "The two events are closely related."
  2. 讲述；叙述：讲述故事或信息。例如: "He related his adventures during the trip."
  3. 与...有关：涉及或关联到某人或某事。例如: "This problem relates directly to your work."
  
- **名词**:
  关系：指人与人之间的关系或事物之间的相互作用。例如: "We need to improve our working relationship."

## 短语
- "relate to something" 意味着与某事有联系或能理解某事。例如: "I can really relate to the challenges she faced."
- "related by blood" 表示血缘关系。例如: "They are related by blood, being first cousins."

## 例句
- 动词: "She was able to relate her experiences to the audience effectively."
- 名词: "Their relationship had been strained for months."
