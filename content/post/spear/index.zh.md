## 读音
/speər/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 一种长矛，尖头武器。例如: "The warrior carried a spear in his hand."
  - (体育)标枪，投掷用的长杆。例如: "She excels in the javelin throw, which is also called spear throwing."

- **动词 v**:
  - 用矛刺，投掷矛。例如: "The hunter speared the fish with a long spear."

## 短语
- "spearhead" 意为领导或发起（行动）。例如: "He was chosen to spearhead the new project."
- "at spear's length" 表示保持安全距离。例如: "They kept their enemies at spear's length."

## 例句
- 名词: "The ancient warriors used spears as their primary weapons."
- 动词: "The gladiator skillfully speared his opponent in the arena."
