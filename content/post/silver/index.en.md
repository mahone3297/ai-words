## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Metallic element**: A chemical element with the symbol Ag and atomic number 47, characterized by its silvery-white appearance and high conductivity. It is a precious metal often used in jewelry, coins, and tableware.
   - Example: "The silver spoon was a family heirloom passed down through generations."
2. **Currency**: In some countries, silver is used as a material for coins.
   - Example: "The value of the silver dollar increased over time."
3. **Color**: A pale metallic shade resembling that of silver.
   - Example: "The moon shone brightly with a silver glow."

### Verb
1. To decorate or plate with silver.
   - Example: "The antique vase was silver-plated for a special event."
2. (informal) To refer to something made of silver or having a silver color.
   - Example: "She bought a silver phone case for her new phone."
