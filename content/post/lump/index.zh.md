## 读音
/lʌmp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一团，块：指物质聚集形成的不规则团块。例如: "There was a small lump of sugar in my tea."
  - 肿块：医学上指身体上的肿起或肿瘤。例如: "She felt a strange lump on her neck and went to see a doctor."

- **动词**:
  - 把…归为一类：将多个项目或人视为一个整体处理。例如: "The manager lumps all the new employees together for training."
  - 忽视，一笔勾销：对某事或某人不予考虑或忽略。例如: "He just lumps all criticism aside and continues with his plan."

## 短语
- "lump sum" 指一次性支付的总额，通常与金钱相关。例如: "She received a lump sum payment when she retired."
- "lump together" 意味着将不同的事物组合在一起。例如: "They lumped all the expenses under one category."

## 例句
- 名词: "She removed the lump of clay from the potter's wheel."
- 动词: "The company decided to lump all overtime hours together and pay them out monthly."
