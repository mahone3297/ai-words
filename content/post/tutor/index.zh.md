## 读音
/tuːtər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 教导者，家庭教师。例如: "She works as a math tutor for high school students."
- **动词**: 辅导，教。例如: "He tutors his younger brother in English every evening."

## 短语
- "Private tutor" 指私人教师，一对一教学。
- "Tutoring session" 是辅导课程的意思。
- "Subject tutor" 特指某一科目的教师或辅导员。

## 例句
- 名词: "My son has a chemistry tutor who helps him with difficult concepts."
- 动词: "She tutors children in reading and writing during weekends."

- Instructor
- Teacher
- Coach
- Mentor
