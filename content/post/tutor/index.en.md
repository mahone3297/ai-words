## Pronunciation
/tuːˈtər/

## Definitions
### Noun
A tutor is a person who provides instruction or guidance, especially in a specialized subject, to an individual or group, typically on a regular basis. This can include academic tutoring, career guidance, or private lessons.

### Verb
To tutor someone is to act as their teacher or mentor, guiding them in learning or mastering a particular subject or skill.

## Phrases
- "Tutor a student" refers to teaching or helping a student with their studies.
- "Tutoring sessions" are planned periods of instruction.
- "Online tutoring" is the provision of educational support remotely.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The math tutor helped the struggling student improve their understanding of algebra."
- **Verb**: "After school, she tutored her younger sister in piano."
- **Phrasal verb**: "He hired a tutor to prepare for his upcoming exams."
