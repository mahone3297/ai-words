## 读音
/ˈrɪfaɪn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 提纯，精炼：将物质去除杂质，使其更纯净。例如: "The oil is refined to remove impurities."
  - 改进，完善：使事物在质量、形式或风格上变得更好。例如: "She is refining her writing skills through daily practice."
  - 修饰，打磨：对艺术、语言或其他表达形式进行细致的改进。例如: "The musician refined his performance after weeks of rehearsal."

## 短语
- "refine one's skills" 意味着提高某项技能。
- "refine a process" 表示优化或改进一个过程。

## 例句
- 动词: "The gold needs to be refined before it can be sold."
- "He is constantly refining his painting techniques to create more realistic landscapes."
