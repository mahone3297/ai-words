## Definitions
### Noun
1. The part that remains after something else has been consumed, used, or removed; leftovers.
   - Example: "The dinner table was cleared, leaving only a few remnants on the plate."
2. Something that persists or continues to be present, often after a change or event.
   - Example: "After the storm, the community remained resilient and united."

### Verb
1. To remain in a particular state or condition; persist.
   - Example: "The old house still remains standing after all these years."
2. To continue to exist or be applicable.
   - Example: "Her advice continues to remain relevant even today."

### Adjective
1. Not changed or altered, as in appearance or character.
   - Example: "The ruins of the castle still bear witness to its original remains."
2. Remaining; leftover.
   - Example: "There were some bread crumbs and fruit remains in the fridge."
