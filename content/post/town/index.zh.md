## 读音
/taʊn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一个有组织的居民区，通常比村庄大但比城市小。例如: "The town has a population of about 10,000 people."
  - 市镇，城镇。例如: "They live in a historic town with well-preserved buildings."
  - 在某些国家（如美国），特指县治所在地。例如: "The county seat is a small town with a courthouse."

## 短语
- "Small town" 指人口不多、规模较小的城镇。
- "Market town" 指定期有集市的城镇。
- "Townspeople" 指城镇的居民。

## 例句
- 名词: "We're planning a trip to explore different towns in the countryside."
- 形容词: "The town's annual festival attracts visitors from all around."

## 同义词
- Village
- City
- Borough
- Settlement
- Community
