## Pronunciation
/taʊn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
    - A relatively large and permanent settlement, smaller than a city, often with a central area, public buildings, and houses or apartments arranged in rows.
    - A human settlement characterized by a compact group of buildings, often surrounded by residential areas.
- **Adjective**: Relating to or denoting a town or its inhabitants.

## Phrases
- "Live in a small town" means to reside in a smaller community.
- "Visit a quaint town" implies exploring a picturesque or charming village.
- "City vs. town living" refers to the differences between urban and suburban lifestyles.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The mayor gave a speech at the annual town hall meeting."
- **Adjective**: "She loves the peaceful life in her rural town."
- "They decided to move from the big city to a tranquil coastal town."
