## 读音
/wəlɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个小型扁平的皮质或其他材料制成的袋子，通常用来装钱、信用卡和身份证等物品。例如: "I left my wallet in the taxi."
- **名词**: 指数字货币钱包，用于存储和管理数字资产。例如: "She downloaded a cryptocurrency wallet to buy bitcoins."

## 短语
- "front pocket wallet" 指放在裤子或衣服前面口袋的钱包。
- "digital wallet" 指电子钱包，用于在线支付。

## 例句
- "He reached into his back pocket for his wallet to pay the bill."
- "She keeps her e-wallet linked to her smartphone for easy mobile payments."

## 同义词
- Purse
- Billfold
- Coin purse (for smaller ones)
- Digital wallet (for electronic versions)
