## 读音
/pəˈteɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 能够容忍等待、延迟或不愉快情况的能力；忍耐，毅力。例如: "She showed great patience while waiting in the long queue."
- **形容词**: 有耐心的，忍耐的。例如: "He is a patient person, never getting angry easily."

## 短语
- "have patience with someone" 耐心对待某人。
- "run out of patience" 没有耐心了，失去忍耐力。

## 例句
- 名词: "His patience was tested when the computer froze for the third time."
- 形容词: "The teacher is very patient with her young students."

## 同义词
- Endurance
- Forbearance
- Tolerance
- Long-suffering
- Perseverance
