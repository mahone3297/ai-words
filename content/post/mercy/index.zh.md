## 读音
/ˈmɜːrsi/

## 释义
- **名词**: 宽恕，怜悯，仁慈。例如: "The prisoner begged for mercy from the judge."
- **动词**: 对…表示怜悯或宽恕。例如: "The captors showed no mercy to their prisoners."
- **形容词**: 仁慈的，宽容的。例如: "She was known for her mercy towards those in need."

## 短语
- "Have mercy on someone" 意为对某人表示怜悯或宽恕。
- "Mercy rule" 在体育比赛中，指一方领先过多而提前结束比赛的规则。

## 例句
- 名词: "The king granted mercy to the defeated soldiers."
- 动词: "The judge decided to show mercy and reduce the sentence."
- 形容词: "Her mercy towards the homeless earned her many admirers.
