## Pronunciation
/dɪˈzaɪər/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by variety, showing a wide range of differences or qualities. For example: "The team has a diverse set of skills."
- **Noun**: A diversity; a mixture of different types or qualities. For example: "The company values cultural diversity in the workplace."

## Phrases
- "Diverse backgrounds" refers to people from different origins or experiences.
- "Diverse perspectives" mean viewpoints that encompass a wide range of opinions.
- "Diversity and inclusion" is a focus on embracing differences and equal opportunities.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The class was diverse, with students from various countries and cultures."
- **Noun**: "The organization strives to promote diversity in its hiring practices."
- **Verb**: "To foster a diverse environment means encouraging different ideas and contributions."
