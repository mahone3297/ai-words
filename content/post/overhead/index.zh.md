## 读音
/ˌoʊvərˈhed/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 费用：指企业运营、管理或生产过程中产生的间接成本。例如: "The company needs to reduce its overhead expenses."
  - 天花板：物理空间中的上方或顶部。例如: "The light fixture is attached to the overhead."

- **形容词**:
  - 在头顶上的：指位于头部正上方的。例如: "The airplane flew overhead."
  - 额外的或附加的：指超出基本需求的。例如: "The overhead costs included insurance and utilities."

## 短语
- "Overhead costs" 指企业的非直接生产成本。
- "Under the overhead lights" 指在头顶的灯光下。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's overhead includes rent, salaries, and utilities."
- 形容词: "The kids played catch in the park, watching the planes fly overhead."
