## 读音
/ˈloʊkeɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 找到或确定…的位置：找出或确知（某物）的位置。例如: "Can you locate the nearest library on the map?"
  2. 安置；设在：将（某人或某事物）放在某一特定位置。例如: "The company decided to locate its new headquarters in downtown."

## 短语
- "locate someone/something" 意为找到某人或某物的位置。
- "locate near" 表示在…附近安置或找到。例如: "They located their new store near a busy shopping mall."

## 例句
- 作为动词： "Using GPS, we were able to locate the lost hiker within minutes."
- 作为动词： "The company is currently locating a new factory in an industrial zone."
