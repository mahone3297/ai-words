## 读音
/ˈmaɪnər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 年纪较小的人；未成年人。例如: "He's only a minor, so he can't vote yet."
  - 较小的事物或次要的部分。例如: "The minor details in the painting were exquisite."
  - 学科副修。例如: "She is majoring in biology and minoring in psychology."

- **形容词**:
  - 较小的；次要的。例如: "The minor injuries were treated on site." 
  - 不重要的；不显著的。例如: "There were some minor issues with the software update."

## 短语
- "Minor in something" 意味着在主修之外选择一个辅修专业。
- "Minor league" 在体育中指的是较低级别的联赛。

## 例句
- 名词: "Minors under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult."
- 形容词: "The minor adjustments made a big difference in the overall performance."
