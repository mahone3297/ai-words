## 读音
/priːst/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 宗教中的神职人员，通常指基督教中的牧师或神父。例如: "The priest led the Sunday service."
- **名词 (n)**: 在某些宗教或信仰体系中，男性或女性的祭司。例如: "In ancient Egypt, priests were responsible for temple rituals."

## 短语
- "Priesthood" 指的是牧师或祭司的团体或状态。
- "Confession to a priest" 指向神父忏悔罪行。

## 例句
- 名词: "Father Thomas is a well-respected priest in our community."
- 名词: "The young girl went to the priest for spiritual guidance."

## 同义词
- Minister
- Clergyman
- Pastor
- Vicar
- Imam (在伊斯兰教中)
