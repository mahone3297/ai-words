## Pronunciation
/prɪs't/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A male religious leader, typically one who is ordained, who performs religious duties in a Christian church, especially a Catholic or Anglican one.
- **Verb**: To perform the duties of a priest, usually in a religious context.
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of or relating to a priest.

## Phrases
- "Priestly duties" refers to the responsibilities of a priest.
- "A priest's robes" are the traditional clothing worn by a priest during religious ceremonies.
- "Take holy orders" is the ceremony by which a man becomes a priest.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The priest offered a prayer before the start of the service."
- **Verb**: "Father John has been a priest for over 20 years."
- **Adjective**: "The priestly calmness he displayed during the crisis was inspiring."
