## Pronunciation
/rɪˈwɔrd/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A payment or gift given as recognition for services, efforts, or achievements: *She was rewarded for her hard work with a promotion.*
2. An incentive to motivate someone to perform a particular action: *The promise of a bonus was a big reward for finishing the project early.*
3. A feeling of satisfaction or pleasure derived from an accomplishment: *The sense of reward after completing a challenging task.*

### Verb
1. To give or bestow something as a reward: *The teacher rewarded the students for their good behavior.*
2. To receive or accept something as a reward: *He was rewarded with a standing ovation for his performance.*
