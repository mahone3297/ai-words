- **名词**: 计量器具，尤指测量长度、体积、速度或功率的设备。例如: "The speedometer on the car is a type of meter."
- **名词**: 公尺，米（长度单位）。例如: "The room is about ten meters wide."
- **名词**: 音乐中的节拍或韵律。例如: "The poem has a strong iambic meter."
- **名词**: 诗歌中的行或诗节。例如: "Shakespeare's sonnets typically consist of fourteen lines with an ABAB CDCD EFEF GG meter."
- **名词**: （燃气、电、水等的）计费器。例如: "The utility company reads the electric meter every month."
