## 读音
/ˈdɛfəns/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 防御；保护。例如: "The country has built strong defences along its borders."
  - 辩护；辩解。例如: "He presented a strong defence against the allegations."

- **动词**:
  - 防卫；保卫。例如: "They were determined to defend their home from invaders."
  - 为…辩护。例如: "She defended her thesis in front of a panel of experts."

## 短语
- "Defence mechanism" 指心理防御机制，人们用来应对压力或困扰的心理策略。
- "On the defensive" 意味着处于守势或试图保护自己免受批评或攻击。

## 例句
- 名词: "The football team's defence was weak, allowing the opponents to score easily."
- 动词: "He immediately started defending his actions when questioned."
