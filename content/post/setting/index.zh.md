## 读音
/ˈsetɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 1. 设定，设置：the arrangement or placement of objects or elements in a particular environment or space, such as a table setting for a meal.
    - 2. 情境，背景：the context or circumstances in which something occurs, like the setting of a story or a scene in a play.
    - 3. (时间或地点)环境：the time and place in which a story, event, or situation takes place.
    - 4. (电子设备的)设定选项：the options or adjustments made on a device or software.

- **动词**:
    - 1. 安置，摆放：to put or arrange something in a particular position or location.
    - 2. 设置（时间、日期等）：to establish or fix a time, date, or other parameter.

## 短语
- "Set the table" 意为摆放餐桌。
- "Setting sun" 指落日。
- "Software settings" 指软件的配置或设置。

## 例句
- 名词: "The romantic setting by the candlelight made the dinner even more special."
- 动词: "She set the alarm clock for 6 am."
- 环境: "The story is set in a small village in the 1950s."
