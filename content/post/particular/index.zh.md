## 读音
/pərˈtɪkjələr/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 特定的，特别的，详细的。例如: "She wants to know the particular details of the event."
- **名词**: 细节，特点。例如: "There are several particular aspects to consider in this project."

## 短语
- "in particular" 意为尤其，特别是。例如: "I like all kinds of music, but jazz in particular."
- "the particular circumstances" 指特定的情况或环境。

## 例句
- 形容词: "He was interested in the particular shade of blue used in the painting."
- 名词: "Can you provide more information about that particular issue?"
