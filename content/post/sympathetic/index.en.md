## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Showing or feeling sympathy; compassionate or understanding. For example: "She was deeply sympathetic towards the homeless man's plight."
- **Noun**: A person who is sympathetic or showing sympathy. For example: "The sympathetic audience clapped after the performance."
- **Verb**: To feel or express sympathy. For example: "He sympathized with her when she told him about her troubles."

## Phrases
- "Displaying sympathetic understanding" means to show empathy or comprehension.
- "A sympathetic listener" is someone who pays close attention and feels for others' emotions.
- "Sympathetic donation" refers to a charitable contribution made out of compassion.
