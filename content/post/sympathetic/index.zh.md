## 读音
/sɪmpəˈθetɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 表示同情的，有共鸣的。例如: "She was very sympathetic towards his situation."
- **名词**: (常与to连用) 同情，共鸣。例如: "There was a lot of sympathy for the victims."

## 短语
- "Be sympathetic to someone" 表示对某人表示同情或理解。
- "Show sympathy" 意为表现出同情心。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The nurse was very sympathetic when I told her about my pain."
- 名词: "They showed great sympathy after hearing the news of her loss."
