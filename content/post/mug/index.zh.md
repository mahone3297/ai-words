## 读音
/mʌɡ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 大而有柄的杯子，通常用来喝热饮，如咖啡或茶。例如: "She sipped her coffee from a large ceramic mug."
2. 俚语：面部，尤指有特征的脸。例如: "He had a distinctive mug that made him easily recognizable."

- **动词**:
1. (俚语)粗暴地打，抢劫。例如: "The mugger hit him and stole his wallet."
2. (非正式)照相，尤其是拍摄面部照片。例如: "The police asked him to mug for their records."

## 短语
- "mug shot" 指的是警方在逮捕或拘留时所拍摄的犯罪嫌疑人的面部照片。
- "to mug up" 意为快速学习或死记硬背，通常用于考试前。例如: "I need to mug up all the formulas before the exam."

## 例句
- 名词: "He held his steaming hot mug of cocoa in both hands."
- 动词（抢劫）: "The thief mugged an elderly lady on the street."
- 动词（拍照）: "The detective took a mug shot of the suspect."

## 同义词
- 杯子: tumbler, cup
- 打劫: assault, rob, hijack
- 照相（面部）: photograph, snap, shoot
