## 读音
/inˈwɔrd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 指向内心、内部的；内心的。例如: "She kept her feelings inward and never shared them with others."
- **副词**: 向内，朝里面。例如: "He turned inward, reflecting on his past mistakes."
- **名词**: 在心理学中，有时用来描述一个人的内向性格。例如: "She's quite an inward person, preferring solitary activities."

## 短语
- "Turn inward" 意味着转向内心，思考或反思。
- "Inward journey" 可以指个人的内心探索或精神旅程。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The child was shy and had a very inward nature."
- 副词: "He looked inward, avoiding eye contact with the crowd."
- 名词: "Through meditation, she embarked on an inward journey to find inner peace."
