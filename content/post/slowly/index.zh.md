## 读音
/ˈsləʊli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示动作或过程进行得缓慢或不慌不忙。例如: "He walked slowly across the street."
- **形容词**: 形容某事或某物速度慢或进展迟缓。例如: "The old computer ran programs very slowly."

## 短语
- "Do something slowly" 意为慢慢地做某事。
- "Time passed slowly" 表示时间过得缓慢。

## 例句
- 副词: "She spoke slowly so that everyone could understand her."
- 形容词: "The movie's slow pace made some viewers lose interest."
