## 读音
/maʊs/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 小型哺乳动物，通常指家鼠或田鼠。例如: "The cat caught a mouse in the garden."
   2. 计算机硬件设备，指鼠标。例如: "She moved the mouse to click on the icon."

## 短语
- "computer mouse" 指电脑上的鼠标设备。

## 例句
- 名词: "The mouse ran across the kitchen floor."
- 名词(计算机): "His fingers danced over the keyboard while he moved the mouse."

## 同义词
- **名词 (小鼠)**:
  - rat
  - rodent

- **名词 (鼠标)**:
  - trackball
  - pointer device
