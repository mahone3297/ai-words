## Pronunciation
/ˈrɛg(ə)rli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a regular or fixed manner; consistently. For example: "She exercises regularly."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or occurring at regular intervals; periodic. For example: "Their meetings are held regularly."
- **Noun**: A regular practice or schedule; routine. For example: "He has a regular coffee break every afternoon."

## Phrases
- "Regularly scheduled" indicates something that happens on a set schedule.
- "To do something regularly" means to perform an action frequently or habitually.
- "Regular attendance" refers to consistent presence at a particular place or event.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "He arrives at work regularly at 9 am."
- **Adjective**: "The train runs regularly between cities."
- **Noun**: "She has a regular yoga practice to maintain her flexibility."
