## 读音
/ˈrɛgjələli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 有规律地，定期地，常常。例如: "I exercise regularly to stay fit."
- **形容词**: 有规律的，定期的。例如: "She follows a regular sleep schedule."

## 短语
- "regular basis" 表示“定期地”，如 "We have meetings on a regular basis."
- "regular customer" 指常客，如 "He's a regular at the local coffee shop."

## 例句
- 副词: "She goes to the gym regularly to maintain her fitness routine."
- 形容词: "The store has a regular supply of fresh produce."
