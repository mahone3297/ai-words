## 读音
/məˈdʒɔr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大学或学院的主要学科；主修课程。例如: "Her major is Psychology at university."
- **形容词**: 主要的，重要的。例如: "The major cities in the country have experienced rapid growth."
- **动词**: （在大学）主修（某个科目）。例如: "He majors in Computer Science."

## 短语
- "major in something" 意味着在学术上专注于某一领域。
- "major league" 在体育中，通常指最高级别的联赛或组织。

## 例句
- 名词: "After graduating, she hopes to find a job related to her major."
- 形容词: "The major challenge they faced was lack of funding."
- 动词: "He decided to major in Philosophy after discovering his passion for it."
