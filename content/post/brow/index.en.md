## Pronunciation
/braʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A facial feature consisting of the upper part of the forehead, typically between the eyes and the hairline, especially when furrowed or raised in thought, anger, or surprise.
- (informal) The area between the eyes and the eyebrows; the forehead.
- (figurative) An expression that shows one's feelings or thoughts, often without words.

### Verb
- To raise the eyebrows in surprise, curiosity, or displeasure.
- To form a line of furrows between the eyebrows in concern or thought.
- To express one's opinion or judgment about something by raising the eyebrows.

### Adjective
- Characteristic of the brow, especially as regards its shape or appearance.
- Marking a person's face with wrinkles or creases, especially between the eyes.

### Adverb
- (informal) With raised eyebrows, indicating skepticism, disbelief, or surprise.

## Phrases
- "He frowned with a deep brow."
- "She arched her brow in curiosity."
- "She wiped the sweat from her brow."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She noticed the lines on his weathered brow."
- **Verb**: "He raised his brow in disbelief when he heard the news."
- **Adjective**: "The athlete had a broad, muscular brow."
- **Adverb**: "Her brow furrowed in concentration as she solved the puzzle."
