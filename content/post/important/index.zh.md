## 读音
/ɪmˈpɔːrtənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 重要的，具有重大意义的。例如: "This is an important decision you have to make."
- **名词**: （常与the连用）重要性，重大。例如: "The importance of education cannot be overstated."

## 短语
- "of great importance" 表示非常重要的。
- "be important to sb." 意味着对某人来说很重要。
- "make something important" 强调某事的重要性。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It's important to keep a balanced diet for good health."
- 名词: "The importance of maintaining good relationships cannot be ignored."

- Vital
- Crucial
- Significant
- Essential
- Momentous
