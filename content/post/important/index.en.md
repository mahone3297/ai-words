## Word: Important

## Pronunciation
/ɪmˈpɔːrtnt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having great significance or value; crucial. It can describe something that is essential, weighty, or influential.

Example Sentence: 
- _The meeting tomorrow is very important; please make sure you attend._
- _Good communication skills are important in any job._

Phrases:
- _Be important to someone/something_: To hold significant meaning or value for a person or thing. Example: "Family is important to her."
- _Important decision/issue/matter_: A critical choice, topic, or problem that requires serious consideration. Example: "We need to discuss this important issue during the board meeting."

## Example Sentences
- _Education is an important factor in shaping one's future._
- _Your vote could make an important difference in the election results._
- _It's important not to underestimate the power of teamwork._
