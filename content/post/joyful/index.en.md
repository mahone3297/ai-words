## Word: Joyful

## Pronunciation
/ˈdʒɔɪfl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Filled with joy; expressing great happiness or pleasure. 

### Example Usage
- *Joyful laughter* filled the air during the celebration.
- The children were all *joyful* after hearing about the surprise trip to the amusement park.

## Phrases
- **Be joyful**: To feel or express joy and happiness. Example: She decided to be joyful despite the difficult circumstances.
- **Joyful occasion**: An event that brings about happiness and delight. Example: The wedding was a truly joyful occasion.
- **A joyful noise**: A loud expression of happiness or praise, often used in religious contexts. Example: They sang a joyful noise unto the Lord.

## Example Sentences
- The news brought *joyful tears* to her eyes.
- It's a *joyful experience* watching the sunrise from the mountaintop.
- The community organized a *joyful parade* to celebrate their victory.
