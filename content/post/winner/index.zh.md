## 读音
/ˈwɪnər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 获胜者；赢家。指在比赛中获得胜利的人或事物。例如: "The winner of the race received a gold medal."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 胜利的；成功的。形容取得显著成就或具有优势的状态。例如: "She felt like a winner after acing her exam."

## 短语
- "The big winner" 指主要或最重要的获胜者。
- "Come out on top" 表示在竞争中成为赢家。
- "Winning strategy" 指成功或获胜的策略。

## 例句
- 名词: "In the end, John was declared the winner of the chess tournament."
- 形容词: "The winner mindset is all about believing in your ability to succeed."
