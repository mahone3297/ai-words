## 读音
/ˈaɪsəleɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 隔离；使孤立。例如: "The patient was isolated in a quarantine ward."
  2. 分离；独立出来。例如: "The scientist isolated the active ingredient in the plant extract."

## 短语
- "isolate oneself" 意为自我隔离或与世隔绝。
- "isolate a substance" 表示从混合物中提取或分离出某种物质。

## 例句
- 动词: "To prevent the spread of the virus, infected individuals must be isolated."
- 动词: "The chemist managed to isolate the pure metal from its ore."
