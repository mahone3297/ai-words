## 释义
- **数词**: 14，代表从1到15中的数字。例如: "There are fourteen students in the class."

## 短语
- "Fourteen days" 表示两周的时间。
- "Fourteen years old" 表示一个人或事物的年龄或存在时间。

## 例句
- "My birthday is on the 14th, so I'm turning fourteen this month."
- "It takes fourteen days to form a habit, they say."
