## Pronunciation
/səˈdɛlm/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Used to indicate frequency or rarity, meaning "not often" or "rarely". It modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs. 

## Phrases
- "Seldom do we have this weather." (indicating rarity)
- "Seldom does he visit his hometown."
- "She seldom speaks up in class."

## Example Sentences
- "He comes to the gym seldom, if ever."
- "I seldom have time to cook on busy days."
- "They seldom agree on political matters."
