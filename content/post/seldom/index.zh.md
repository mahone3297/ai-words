## 读音
/səˈlʌdli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 很少，不常，罕有。表示频率较低的情况。例如: "I seldom go out on weekdays."
- **形容词**: 不常有的，罕见的。例如: "It's a seldom-seen species of bird."

## 短语
- "seldom or never" 表示几乎从不。
- "seldom without" 表示几乎总是伴随着。

## 例句
- 副词: "I seldom have time to read books during the workweek."
- 形容词: "This is a seldom-discussed topic in our field."

## 同义词
- rarely
- infrequently
- occasionally
- from time to time
- now and then
