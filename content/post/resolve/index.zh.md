## 读音
/rɪˈzɑːlv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    1. 决定（问题、冲突等）：例如: "She resolved the conflict between her friends with a compromise."
    2. 下定决心；决定做某事：例如: "I've resolved to exercise every day this month."
    3. 解决，消除：例如: "The company resolved the customer's complaint promptly."
- **名词**:
    1. 决议，决定：例如: "The committee passed a resolution to reduce waste."
    2. 解决心态，坚定的决心：例如: "His strong resolve helped him through tough times."

## 短语
- "to resolve into" 表示分解成或归结为：例如: "The problem resolved into several smaller tasks."
- "resolve to do something" 表示决定做某事：例如: "I resolve to change my habits for the better."

## 例句
- 动词: "He resolved the complex issue by working late into the night."
- 名词: "The team discussed their new year's resolutions in the meeting."
