## 读音
/ˈmɛdɪsɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 药物；医学。例如: "Take this medicine three times a day for the fever." (这种药一天吃三次，治发烧。)
- **名词**: 治疗；疗法。例如: "Alternative medicines include acupuncture and herbal remedies." (替代医疗包括针灸和草药疗法。)

## 短语
- "Home medicine" 指家庭常备药品。
- "Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM)" 是中医的英文表达。
- "Medicine cabinet" 指卫生间的药柜，通常存放药品。

## 例句
- 名词: "She's studying medicine at university, hoping to become a doctor."
- 名词: "In case of headache, there's some aspirin in the medicine cabinet."
