## 读音
/frɔst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 冰霜，指低温下在物体表面形成的白色冰晶。例如: "The grass was covered in a thick layer of frost in the morning."
- **形容词**: 寒冷的，结霜的。例如: "It was a frosty winter morning with temperatures below freezing."

## 短语
- "Frost on the windows" 指窗户上的冰霜。
- "Frost bite" 是冻伤，指皮肤和身体组织因寒冷而受损。

## 例句
- 名词: "The frost on the car windshield made it difficult to see."
- 形容词: "The frosty air bit at my cheeks as I stepped outside."
