## Pronunciation
/tjuːˈɪʃən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The payment of money or other consideration for instruction or education, especially at a school or college. 2. The fees charged for teaching or tutoring services. 3. (informal) The act of teaching or giving lessons.

## Phrases
- "Tuition fees" or "tuition costs" refer to the financial charges for attending school or college.
- "Tuition assistance" or "scholarship" may denote financial aid to help cover tuition.
- "Private tuition" indicates extra paid lessons outside of a regular educational institution.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The family struggled to afford their child's college tuition."
- **Noun**: "Many students work part-time jobs to offset their tuition expenses."
- **Verb**: "She started offering private tuition to supplement her income."
