## 读音
/ˈtuːʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 学费；教育费用。指支付给学校或学院以获得教育的资金。例如: "The tuition for the university is quite high."
- **动词**: 教课；辅导。例如: "She tuitions several students in mathematics after school."

## 短语
- "Tuition fees" 指的是学费总额。
- "Private tuition" 指私人或一对一的教学辅导。

## 例句
- 名词: "The annual tuition for the art school has increased significantly this year."
- 动词: "She is tutoring her neighbor's children in English during the summer break."

## 同义词
- Education cost
- School fees
- College charges
- Teaching
- Instructional fee
