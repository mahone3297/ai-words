## 读音
/ˌʌndərˈɡrædʒuət/

## 释义
- **名词**: 在大学里尚未取得学士学位的学生。例如: "He is an undergraduate studying physics at Harvard."
- **形容词**: 大学本科的；属于大学本科生的。例如: "She is in her second year as an undergraduate student."

## 短语
- "undergraduate degree" 指的是学士学位。
- "undergraduate program" 指大学本科课程或项目。
- "undergraduate studies" 泛指大学本科的学习。

## 例句
- 名词: "John is an undergraduate majoring in computer science."
- 形容词: "The university offers many undergraduate scholarships for talented students."

## 同义词
- college student
- bachelor's student
- uni student (informal)
