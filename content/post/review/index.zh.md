## 读音
/rɪˈvjuː/

## 释义
- **名词**: 对某事物的检查、回顾或评论，通常涉及分析和评估。例如: "The movie received positive reviews from critics."
- **动词**: 查看、回顾或检查以提供意见或反馈。例如: "I need to review the report before submitting it."
- **名词**: 排练或彩排。例如: "The actors had a dress review before the opening night."

## 短语
- "Film review" 指电影的评论或评价。
- "Performance review" 指对员工工作表现的评估。
- "Preparation for a review" 指为审查或评估做准备。

## 例句
- 名词: "She read several book reviews before choosing her next read."
- 动词: "The manager will review our work progress next week."
- 名词: "The play had its final dress review before the première."
