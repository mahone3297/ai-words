## Pronunciation
/ˈpeɪsənt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **peasant** is a member of the lowest social class in a traditional rural society, typically engaged in farming or agriculture. They often live in villages and have limited education and economic opportunities.

### Verb (archaic)
To work as a peasant or engage in peasant-like labor.

## Phrases
- "Working the land as a peasant"
- "A peasant's hard life"
- "Peasant uprising"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The village was home to a community of simple peasants who relied on farming for their livelihood."
- **Verb (archaic)**: "She remembered her childhood, spending long days peasanting in the fields with her family."
