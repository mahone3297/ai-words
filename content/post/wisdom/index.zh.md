## 读音
/ˈwɪzdəm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 高度的智慧，明智或有洞察力的知识。例如: "She is known for her great wisdom in solving complex problems."
- **形容词**: 具有智慧的，明智的。例如: "The wise decision was to seek professional help."

## 短语
- "Ancient wisdom" 指流传已久的古老智慧或知识。
- "Wisdom of the crowds" 指集体智慧，认为大众的综合判断往往比单个人更准确。

## 例句
- 名词: "His words were filled with the wisdom of a life well-lived."
- 形容词: "It was a wise choice to invest in renewable energy."
