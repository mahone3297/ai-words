## 读音
/meɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 主要的；重要的：例如: "The main reason for his success is hard work."
  - 要点；本质：例如: "Let's focus on the main point of the discussion."
  - 平均值；中值：例如: "The mean of these numbers is 10."

- **形容词**:
  - 刻薄的；卑鄙的：例如: "She can be really mean sometimes."
  - 意味着；打算：例如: "I didn't mean to hurt your feelings."

- **动词**:
  - 意思；意味着：例如: "What does this symbol mean in the equation?"
  - 旨在；打算：例如: "I mean to finish the project by tomorrow."

## 短语
- "Mean something to someone" 表示对某人有重要意义。
- "Mean well" 意向良好，本意是好的。

## 例句
- 名词: "The main issue we need to address is climate change."
- 形容词: "He's so mean that he never shares his toys with others."
- 动词: "When she said 'it's nothing', she really meant it."
