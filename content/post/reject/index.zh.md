## 读音
/ˈrɛdʒɛkt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 拒绝接受；不采纳。例如: "She rejected his proposal for a partnership."
  - 拒绝考虑；否定。例如: "The committee rejected the idea outright."
  - 拒绝或驱逐某人。例如: "The team rejected him from their ranks."
  - 不接受（某人）的感情。例如: "He was heartbroken when she rejected his love."

## 短语
- "reject something as" 意为认为某事不可接受。例如: "The publisher rejected the manuscript as poorly written."
- "to be rejected by someone" 指被某人拒绝。例如: "She felt devastated after being rejected by her long-time crush."

## 例句
- 动词: "The company rejected my application for the job."
- "The proposal was rejected due to lack of funding."
- "When he asked her out, she rejected him politely."
