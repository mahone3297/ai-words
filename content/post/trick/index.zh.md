## 读音
/trɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 诡计，花招，技巧。例如: "He used a clever trick to solve the problem."
- **动词**: 欺骗，哄骗。例如: "Don't try to trick me into giving you my password."
- **形容词**: 机灵的，狡猾的。例如: "The trick dog performed amazing stunts."

## 短语
- "Trick or treat" 在万圣节期间孩子们用来要糖果的表达。
- "Up to tricks" 表示在捣乱或恶作剧。
- "Trick question" 是一个故意设计来误导人的问题。

## 例句
- 名词: "The magician's disappearing act was just a trick of the trade."
- 动词: "She tricked him into believing she was a professional singer."
- 形容词: "That cat is so trick, it can open doors by itself."
