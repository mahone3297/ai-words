## Definitions
### Adverb
- Refers to a word that modifies a verb, an adjective, or another adverb, often expressing contrast or reservation. It can be used to indicate despite something else, as a concession, or to emphasize a point.

## Phrases
- "Although he's tired, he still worked through the night."
- "She did well, though not as well as expected."
- "I like the movie, though the ending was disappointing."

## Example Sentences
