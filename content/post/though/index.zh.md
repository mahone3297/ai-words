## 读音
/ðəʊ/

## 释义
- **连词**: 引导让步状语从句，表示虽然、尽管。例如: "Though he was tired, he continued working."
- **副词**: 表示轻微的转折，相当于“不过”、“然而”。例如: "It was a good movie, though not great."

## 短语
- "Though not" 用来表达转折，意思是“虽然不...”。例如: "The book is informative, though not very entertaining."

## 例句
- 连词: "Though it was raining, they decided to go for a walk."
- 副词: "I enjoyed the film, though it had a few slow parts."

## 同义词
- Although
- Even though
- Despite
- In spite of
