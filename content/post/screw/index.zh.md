## 读音
/skruː/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种螺纹工具，如螺丝或螺栓，通常有螺纹以便固定或连接物体。例如: "Use the screw to tighten the loose chair leg."
- **动词**: 用螺丝或类似工具固定或装配。例如: "He screwed the shelf to the wall."
- **名词 (俚语)**: 指麻烦、困境或混乱的情况。例如: "We're in a real screw now, with no way out."
- **动词 (俚语)**: 欺骗或利用某人。例如: "The dealer tried to screw us by charging extra for the car."

## 短语
- "Screwdriver" 是用来转动螺丝的工具，通常有一个扁平或十字形的头部。
- "Screw up" 意为犯错误或搞得一团糟。例如: "I really screwed up this project."

## 例句
- 名词: "The carpenter needed a variety of screws for his woodworking project."
- 动词: "She carefully screwed the cap back onto the bottle to prevent spillage."
- 俚语名词: "Getting fired was the last thing he expected; now he's really in a screw."
- 俚语动词: "Don't let him screw you over; he's known for being dishonest."

## 同义词
- Noun: bolt, fastener, nail
- Verb (in context of fixing): fasten, secure, attach
- Verb (in slang context): cheat, deceive, rip off
