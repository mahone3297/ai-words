## 读音
/tɒs/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 投掷；扔：例如 "He tossed the ball to his teammate."
  - 翻来覆去；摇摆：例如 "The baby was tossing and turning in his sleep."
  - 舍弃；抛弃：例如 "She decided to toss her old clothes and buy new ones."

- **名词**:
  - 投掷动作或过程：例如 "The game was won by a lucky toss of the coin."
  - （尤指赌博中的）掷骰子：例如 "They settled their dispute with a toss of a dice."

## 短语
- "Toss a coin" 用于决定事情，意为抛硬币。
- "Toss up" 可以表示随机选择或难以决定的情况。

## 例句
- 动词: "He effortlessly tossed the apple into the basket."
- 名词: "The winner was determined by a simple toss of a coin."
