## Pronunciation
/tɒs/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: 
  - **Noun**: A light, upward movement of something, typically a small object, with one's hand or a throwing motion. For example: "He tossed the coin into the air."
  - **Transitive verb**: To throw or propel (an object) with a light, casual motion. For example: "She tossed the ball to her friend."
  - **Intransitive verb**: To move or jolt suddenly or roughly. For example: "The car was tossed around on the rough road."

## Phrases
- "Toss a coin" means to make a decision by flipping a coin.
- "Toss and turn" refers to the restless movement of turning over due to sleeplessness.
- "Toss salad" is a culinary term for mixing salad ingredients together.
