## 读音
/ˈfənəmənən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 现象；显像；非凡的事物。例如: "Global warming is a worrying environmental phenomenon."
- **名词**: （科学上的）现象，可观测的事实。例如: "The phenomenon of gravity has been studied for centuries."

## 短语
- "Natural phenomena" 指自然现象。
- "A rare phenomenon" 指罕见的现象。

## 例句
- 名词: "The Aurora Borealis is a beautiful natural phenomenon."
- 名词: "Einstein's theory successfully explained many previously unexplained phenomena."

## 同义词
- Event
- Manifestation
- Occurrence
- Sight
- Display
