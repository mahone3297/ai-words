## Pronunciation
/ˈfænəˌnəmən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A remarkable or unusual event, occurrence, or natural phenomenon. It can also refer to a general characteristic or feature of something. For example: "The aurora borealis is a stunning natural phenomenon."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic or typical of a particular place, time, or situation. For example: "The archaeological findings provided a unique cultural phenomenon."

## Phrases
- "A scientific phenomenon" refers to a natural or observed behavior that can be studied scientifically.
- "Cultural phenomenon" describes a widespread and influential trend or idea within a society.
- "Social phenomenon" denotes a widely observed pattern of behavior or practice in society.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The rapid spread of technology has become a global phenomenon."
- **Adjective**: "The Renaissance was a significant artistic phenomenon in European history."
- **Verb**: "Explorers marveled at the strange and fascinating phenomena they encountered on their expeditions."
