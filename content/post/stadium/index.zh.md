## 读音
/stəˈdiəm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大型露天体育场馆，通常用于足球、田径或其他大型体育赛事。例如: "The match will be held at the famous Maracanã stadium."

## 短语
- "Capacity of the stadium" 指体育场的容纳人数。
- "Stadium seating" 指体育场内阶梯式的座位安排。

## 例句
- "The fans filled the stadium to watch the championship game."
- "We had great views from our seats in the stadium."
