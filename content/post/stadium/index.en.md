## Pronunciation
/stəˈdeɪm/

## Definitions
### Noun
A large, enclosed outdoor structure for sports, concerts, or other public gatherings. 
### Verb
1. To hold or use (a sports event) in a stadium.
2. To assemble or gather in a stadium.
3. To be the venue for (a particular event).
