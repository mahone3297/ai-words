## 读音
/ˈdaʊt-ləs/

## 释义
- **副词**: 肯定地，无疑地，毫无疑问地。表示对某事的坚信或确信。例如: "Doubtless, he will succeed in his endeavor."
- **形容词**: 无疑的，确定的。用来形容某事是毫无疑问的事实。例如: "It is doubtless true that she is the best candidate for the job."

## 短语
- "Doubtless to say" 表示肯定地说，无疑地。
- "There is no doubtless about it" 意味着对某事非常确定。

## 例句
- 副词: "Doubtless, she has prepared thoroughly for the exam."
- 形容词: "The ancient artifact's authenticity is doubtless."
