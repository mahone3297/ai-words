## 读音
/ˈpɑːsəb(ə)l/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 可能的；可行的；有希望的。例如: "It's possible to finish the project on time."
- **名词**: 可能性；潜在的机会。例如: "There are several possible solutions to this problem."

## 短语
- "in the possible" 表示在可能的范围内。
- "possible outcome" 意为可能的结果。
- "possible candidate" 指可能的候选人。

## 例句
- 形容词: "With hard work, it's possible to achieve your dreams."
- 名词: "We need to consider all the possible consequences before making a decision."
