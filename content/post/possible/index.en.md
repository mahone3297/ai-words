## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Adjective**: Capable of being done; feasible; doable. Example: "It's possible to finish the project on time."
2. **Adjective**: Probable or likely to happen; feasible. Example: "The weather forecast predicts a possible storm later today."

### Verb
1. To be capable or able to occur; have the potential. Example: "It is possible that he will arrive soon."
2. To think or consider something as feasible or likely. Example: "We must consider all possible solutions."

### Adverb
1. In a manner that could be accomplished or realized. Example: "She acted as if it were completely possible."

## Phrases

- "A remote possibility" indicates a very low likelihood.
