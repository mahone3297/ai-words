## Pronunciation
/thˈauzənd/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: (countable) A large number representing a multiple of 1,000, usually denoted with the abbreviation 'k'. For example: "There are thousands of stars in the galaxy."
- **Adjective**: Designating a quantity that is a multiple of 1,000. For example: "The project will require several thousand dollars."

## Phrases
- "A thousand times over" expresses emphasis or repetition.
- "In the thousands" indicates a very large quantity.
- "Ten thousand miles" is a common idiomatic expression for a long distance.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She saved up ten thousand dollars for her dream vacation."
- **Adjective**: "The auditorium can seat thousands of people."
- "There were thousands of spectators at the sports event."
