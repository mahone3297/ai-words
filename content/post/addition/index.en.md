## Pronunciation
/əˈdɪʃən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act or process of adding; the addition of something to something else. 2. An additional quantity or item added to something. 3. A sum or total resulting from the addition of numbers. 4. (Computing) The process of inserting data into a database or file.
- **Verb**: To add; to place or insert something together with another thing, usually to increase the quantity or value. 5. To include or incorporate something as part of a whole.

## Phrases
- "Addition problem" refers to a mathematical exercise involving the addition of numbers.
- "Add to" means to increase the amount or number of something.
- "Add up" implies to calculate the total by adding individual amounts.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: She wrote down the sum of the two numbers, which was a significant addition to their total.
- **Verb**: Please add milk to my coffee, it's too plain.
- **In a sentence using 'add'**: He added some spices to the recipe to give it more flavor.

## Synonyms
- Addition: Summation, augmentation, increment, extra, supplement
- Verb: Include, integrate, combine, enhance, augment

## Antonyms
- Subtraction: Remove, deduct, decrease, lessen
- Subtract: Minus, take away
