## 读音
/ˈkiːpər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    1. 看守员，管理员：如 "The zookeeper feeds the animals every day."
    2. 保管人，收藏家：如 "She is a bookkeeper with an extensive collection of first editions."
    3. 守门员（足球、曲棍球等运动）：如 "The goalkeeper made an incredible save in the match."

## 短语
- "keeper of the keys" 指掌管钥匙的人。
- "goalkeeper" 在足球或曲棍球比赛中指守门员。

## 例句
- 名词: "The castle's ancient keepers were responsible for its defense."
- 名词: "The hockey team's new goalkeeper has shown great potential."
- 名词: "My grandfather was a bookkeeper, and he had a keen eye for detail."
