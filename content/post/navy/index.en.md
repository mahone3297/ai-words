## Definitions
- **Noun**: (n) The naval forces of a country, consisting of warships, submarines, aircraft, and personnel trained for service at sea. Example: "The United States Navy is one of the largest navies in the world."
- **Noun**: (adj) A dark blue color, often associated with the uniforms or ships of a navy. Example: "She wore a navy dress for the formal occasion."

## Phrases
- "Join the navy": To enlist in or become a member of a country's naval forces.
- "In the navy": Refers to being a part of or serving in a navy.
- "Naval operations": Military activities carried out by a navy, such as patrols, defense, and strategic missions.
