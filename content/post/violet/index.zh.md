## 读音
/ˈvaɪələt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种紫色的花，紫罗兰，也指这种颜色。例如: "She wore a dress the color of violets."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 紫色的，紫罗兰色的。例如: "The violet sky at dusk was breathtaking."

## 短语
- "Violet flowers" 指紫罗兰花。
- "Violet hue" 指紫色调。

## 例句
- 名词: "The garden was filled with fragrant violet flowers."
- 形容词: "The artist used a violet shade for the background of her painting."
