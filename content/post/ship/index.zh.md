## 读音
/ʃɪp/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 大型水上交通工具，通常指轮船或船舶。例如: "The cruise ship sailed through the Mediterranean Sea."
- **名词 (n)**: 在计算机或网络中，可以指代软件或硬件的版本。例如: "The new version of the software is called 'ship'."

## 短语
- "aboard ship" 在船上。例如: "He was excited to be aboard the ship for his first voyage."
- "launch a ship" 发射或下水一艘船。例如: "The new warship was launched with great ceremony."
- "ship out" 运输或发送货物。例如: "They will ship out the packages tomorrow."

## 例句
- 名词: "The majestic ship sailed past the lighthouse."
- 名词（计算机用语）: "The developers have just shipped the latest update to the app."
