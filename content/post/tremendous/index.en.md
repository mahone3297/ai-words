## Pronunciation
/tremə'diəs/

## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Meaning**: Extremely great or impressive; enormous; vast. 
- **Example**: "The view from the mountain was tremendous."
- **Antonyms**: Small, insignificant, negligible

### Noun (informal)
- **Meaning**: An extremely good or impressive thing or experience. 
- **Example**: "That performance was a real tremendous show."
- **Synonyms**: Amazing, spectacular, impressive

## Phrases
- "A tremendous achievement"
- "Tremendous growth"
- "Tremendous support from the crowd"
