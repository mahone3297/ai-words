## 读音
/ˈtrɛməndəs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 极大的，巨大的，非常强烈的。例如: "He felt a tremendous sense of relief after finishing the project."
- **名词**: (非正式) 极好的事物。例如: "That was a tremendous performance by the orchestra."

## 短语
- "tremendous pressure" 巨大的压力
- "tremendous success" 巨大的成功
- "make a tremendous effort" 做出极大的努力

## 例句
- 形容词: "The company experienced tremendous growth in the last quarter."
- 名词: "The audience gave the speaker a tremendous round of applause."
