## 读音
/ˈspeʃəlɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 某一特定领域或学科的专家，擅长某一特定工作的人。例如: "She is a renowned cancer specialist."
- **动词**: 专门从事或致力于（某领域）。例如: "He specializes in ancient Greek history."

## 短语
- "medical specialist" 医学专家
- "sports specialist" 体育专家
- "market specialist" 市场专家

## 例句
- 名词: "The company hired a digital marketing specialist to improve their online presence."
- 动词: "As a specialist, he spends most of his time researching and analyzing trends in the finance sector."
