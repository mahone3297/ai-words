## 读音
/təˈlɔːrəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 能够接受、忍受不同观点、行为或情况的程度；宽容。例如: "The country is known for its religious tolerance."
- **动词**: 容忍，忍受。例如: "She tolerates his annoying habits because she loves him."

## 短语
- "Tolerance of diversity" 意味着对多样性的接纳和尊重。
- "Zero tolerance" 表示对某种行为绝对不容忍的政策。

## 例句
- 名词: "Our society values and promotes tolerance towards all cultures."
- 动词: "Despite their differences, they learned to tolerate each other for the sake of peace."

## 同义词
- Endurance
- Forbearance
- Patience
- Long-suffering
- Open-mindedness
