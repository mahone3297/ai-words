## 读音
/ˈtɜːrɪtəri/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个国家或政治实体所控制的地理区域；领土，领地。例如: "The country expanded its territory through conquest."
- **名词**: 特定个人、群体或动物的领域或范围。例如: "The lion marked out its territory with urine."

## 短语
- "Within someone's territory" 意为在某人的领域内。
- "Colonial territories" 指的是殖民地领土。
- "Personal territory" 可以指个人的空间或领地感。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new treaty defined the boundaries of both countries' territories."
- 名词: "The penguins fiercely defend their nesting territories from intruders."
