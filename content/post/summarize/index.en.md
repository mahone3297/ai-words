## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To condense or give a brief account of (a text, story, or set of information) in a concise form. For example: "She summarized the main points of the lecture."
- **Noun**: A condensed version or summary of a larger piece of work. For example: "The article provided a brief summary of the book."
