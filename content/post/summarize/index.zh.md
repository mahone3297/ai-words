## 读音
/ˈsʌmˌmaɪze/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 概括；总结：将复杂的信息压缩成简洁的形式。例如: "Could you summarize the main points of the article?"
  - 提要：提取关键点或要点。例如: "She summarized her findings in a brief report."

## 短语
- "summarize a text" 意味着提炼文本的主要思想。
- "to summarize briefly" 表示简要地总结。

## 例句
- "In his speech, he summarized the company's achievements over the past year."
- "Before the meeting, we need to summarize the key data from the research project."
