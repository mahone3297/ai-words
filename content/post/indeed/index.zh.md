## 读音
/ɪnˈdiːd/

## 释义
- **副词**: 确实，的确，无疑地。用来强调事实或同意某人的观点。例如: "It was indeed a beautiful day."，"She works hard, indeed."
- **连词**: 实际上，事实上，用于引入一个与前文对比的事实。例如: "He seemed calm, but indeed he was nervous."

## 短语
- "indeed and" 用于加强语气，表示确实如此。例如: "Indeed and it was the best meal I've ever had."

## 例句
- 副词: "The movie was indeed as good as everyone said."
- 连词: "He appeared to be happy, but indeed, he was deeply troubled."
