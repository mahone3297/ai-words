## Pronunciation
/unˈfɔːrtʃuənət/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by misfortune or bad luck; unfortunate. For example: "The unfortunate incident happened during the trip."
- **Noun**: An unlucky or sad event or person. For example: "She had an unfortunate accident."

## Phrases
- "Be unfortunate to do sth." implies that something is likely to go wrong.
- "On the unfortunate side" refers to the unlucky or unfavorable aspect.
- "To suffer an unfortunate fate" means to experience a negative outcome.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "His unfortunate decision led to significant financial losses."
- **Noun**: "She was the unfortunate victim of a pickpocket."
- "Despite his best efforts, he had an unfortunate string of failures."
