## 读音
/ʌnˈfɔːrtʃənət/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不幸的，倒霉的，令人遗憾的。例如: "He had an unfortunate accident."
- **名词**: 不幸的事，灾祸。例如: "They experienced several unfortunate events during their trip."

## 短语
- "An unfortunate situation" 意为一个不幸的情况。
- "Unfortunately" 通常用作副词，表示遗憾或失望。例如: "Unfortunately, the weather turned bad."

## 例句
- 形容词: "It was unfortunate that she missed the train."
- 名词: "The storm caused widespread damage, an unfortunate consequence of living near the coast."
