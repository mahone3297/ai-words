## 读音
/ˈrɑːtən/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 腐烂的；腐朽的；变质的。例如: "The apples were rotten and had to be thrown away."
- **动词**: （使）腐烂；（使）腐败。例如: "Left outside, the wood will soon rot."

## 短语
- "rotten to the core" 意味着极其坏或腐败透顶。
- "a rotten deal" 指的是不公平或有害的交易。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The old wooden bridge was rotten and needed immediate repair."
- 动词: "If you leave the vegetables in the sun, they'll rot quickly."
