## 读音
/ˈsetlmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. **定居点**: 一个人们为了居住或工作而建立的社区，如村庄或城镇。例如: "The first European settlement in this area was established in the 17th century."
  2. **和解**: 在法律或纠纷中双方达成的协议，以结束争议。例如: "They finally reached a financial settlement after the divorce."
  3. **支付结算**: 满足债务或交易的支付过程。例如: "The company made a settlement for the outstanding invoices."

## 短语
- "Legal settlement" 法律上的和解或解决。
- "Final settlement" 最终的解决或清算。
- "Land settlement" 土地的分配或定居。

## 例句
- 名词: "The old settlement still has some original log cabins intact."
- 名词: "They reached a settlement with the insurance company for the car accident damages."
- 名词: "The land settlement policy aimed to distribute land to small farmers."
