## Word: Ours

## Pronunciation
/ˈaʊərz/

## Definitions
- **Pronoun**: Belonging to us (used without a following noun). It indicates possession or ownership. 

  > Example Sentence: "This house is ours; we bought it last year."
  
- **Adjective**: Used to emphasize that something belongs to a group that includes the speaker but not necessarily the listener.
  
  > Example Sentence: "Ours is the house with the red door."

## Phrases
- "It's ours": This phrase implies a sense of ownership or possession, often with pride or assertion.
  
  > Example: "After years of hard work, the championship title is finally ours."
  
- "Theirs vs. Ours": This comparison is often used when contrasting two items or situations belonging to different groups.

## Example Sentences
- **Pronoun**: "The responsibility for the project's success is ours."
- **Adjective**: "We decided to compare their strategy with ours to identify potential improvements."

## Synonyms
- Our own
- Belonging to us
- Possessed by us
- Under our ownership
- In our possession
