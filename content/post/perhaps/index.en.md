## Pronunciation
/pəˈrɛpəs/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Used to suggest uncertainty or possibility, expressing that something may be true or might happen. It can also indicate hesitation or moderation. For example: "I think we should go, but perhaps it's too late."

## Phrases
- "Perhaps he will come." (Suggesting uncertainty about his arrival)
- "It's possible, perhaps." (Indicating a likelihood)
- "She wondered if she should go, but perhaps staying would be better." (Hesitation)

## Example Sentences
- "Perhaps I've misunderstood the instructions."
- "The weather forecast says it might rain, perhaps we should bring an umbrella."
- "Perhaps it was just a misunderstanding; let's talk about it."

- Maybe
- Maybe so
- Potentially

- Uncertainly
