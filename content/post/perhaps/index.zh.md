## 读音
/pərˈhæps/

## 释义
- **副词**: 可能，也许，大概。表示不确定或可能性。例如: "Perhaps she'll come to the party tonight."
- **形容词**: （古语）可能的，或许的。例如: "It was perhaps the best decision he ever made."

## 短语
- "Perhaps not" 意味着可能不是这样或可能不正确。
- "Perhaps we should try again" 表示建议再试一次，因为可能会有不同的结果。

## 例句
- 副词: "Perhaps we can find a solution if we work together."
- 形容词: "The old book contained perhaps valuable information."
