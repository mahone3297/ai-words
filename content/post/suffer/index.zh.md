## 读音
/ˈsʌfər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 经受（痛苦、苦难或困难）：例如: "She had to suffer through a long illness."
  2. 遭受（损失、失败或惩罚）：例如: "The company suffered huge financial losses last year."
  3. 忍受（不愉快的事物或人）：例如: "I can't stand this noise; I have to suffer it every day."

## 短语
- "Suffer from" 意为遭受…之苦，患病：例如: "Many people suffer from allergies during spring."
- "Suffer the consequences" 意味承受后果：例如: "If you don't study, you'll suffer the consequences in the exam."

## 例句
- 动词: "He was forced to suffer the embarrassment of public criticism."
- "She is suffering from severe headaches recently."
- "Those who break the law must suffer the consequences."

## 同义词
- Endure
- Bear
- Tolerate
- Put up with
- Experience (in the context of hardship or pain)
