## 读音
/ bɪˈlʌvɪd /

## 释义
- **形容词**: 极度喜爱的，深爱的。例如: "She is his beloved wife."
- **名词**: （常用于宗教或文学）心爱的人，至爱。例如: "The poet addressed his beloved in the sonnet."

## 短语
- "beloved one" 指非常爱的人，通常用于指伴侣或亲人。
- "beloved memory" 指对某人的美好回忆。

## 例句
- 形容词: "His beloved dog accompanied him on every adventure."
- 名词: "In her letters, she often spoke of her beloved who was far away."
