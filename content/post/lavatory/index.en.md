## Word: Lavatory

## Pronunciation
/ˈlævətɔːri/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A room with facilities for washing and usually also for toilet purposes, especially one provided for public use. For example: "The airport has several restrooms/lavatories available for travelers."

### Phrases
- "To visit the lavatory": To go to the bathroom/toilet.
- "Public lavatory": A restroom or toilet that is open for use by the general public.
- "Airplane lavatory": The small bathroom on an airplane.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Excuse me, could you tell me where the nearest lavatory is?"
- "Passengers are asked to remain seated while the aircraft is moving unless they need to use the lavatory."
