## 读音
/wɜːrs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 比较级，表示更坏、更糟糕。例如: "The weather is worse today than yesterday."
- **副词**: 用于强调程度，意为更坏地、更糟糕地。例如: "His condition worsened overnight."

## 短语
- "For the worse" 表示情况变得更糟。例如: "Their relationship has gone from bad to worse."
- "Worse still" 用来进一步强调负面的情况。例如: "Worse still, they lost their home in the fire."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new policy has made the situation worse, not better."
- 副词: "Her health has gotten worse with each passing day."

- More terrible
