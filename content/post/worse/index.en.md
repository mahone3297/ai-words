## Pronunciation
/wɜːs/

## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Worse** is an adjective that describes a state or quality that is inferior, less favorable, or more negative than before or compared to something else. It is often used in the context of comparison.

### Adverb
- As an adverb, "worse" modifies verbs, describing a degree of severity or intensity of an action, and can be used to indicate a worsening condition.

## Phrases
- "It's getting worse" implies that a situation is deteriorating.
- "Worse comes to worst" suggests that if the current situation is bad, there is still a possibility of things becoming even worse.
- "No worse off" indicates that a situation hasn't become any more unfavorable.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The weather has gotten worse since this morning."
- **Adverb**: "She felt herself getting worse after the long flight."
- "His health has taken a turn for the worse."
