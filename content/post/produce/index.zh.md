## 读音
/prəˈdjuːs/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 制造；生产：例如 "The factory produces cars every day."
2. 出产；产生：例如 "This farm produces high-quality vegetables."
3. 创作；制作：例如 "She produces documentaries for a living."

- **名词**:
1. 产品；产物：例如 "The company's latest produce is selling well."
2. 农产品；果蔬：例如 "The market has a wide variety of fresh produce."

## 短语
- "Produce goods" 意为制造商品。
- "Local produce" 指的是本地种植或养殖的产品。

## 例句
- 动词: "The factory will increase its production to meet the growing demand."
- 名词: "The farmers' market offers a great selection of organic produce."
