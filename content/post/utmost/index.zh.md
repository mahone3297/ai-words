## 读音
/ˈʌt.məst/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 极端的，最大的，最高的。例如: "He exerted his utmost effort to complete the task."
- **名词**: 极限，最大程度。例如: "The utmost importance lies in maintaining safety."

## 短语
- "to the utmost" 意味着到最大程度或极限。例如: "She pushed herself to the utmost to achieve her goals."

## 例句
- 形容词: "We must show utmost respect to our elders."
- 名词: "The company values customer satisfaction to the utmost."
