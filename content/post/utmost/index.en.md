## Pronunciation
/ˈʌtmaʊst/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Of the highest degree or quality; most extreme or complete. For example: "He showed the utmost respect for his elders."
- **Adverb**: To the greatest extent or degree; completely. For example: "She was grateful for their utmost assistance."

## Phrases
- "Do something with utmost care" means to handle something very carefully.
- "Display utmost patience" implies showing a great deal of tolerance.
- "His utmost efforts were made to solve the problem" signifies maximum exertion.

## Example Sentences
