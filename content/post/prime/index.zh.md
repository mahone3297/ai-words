## 读音
/praɪm/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 第一的，首要的，主要的。例如: "The prime minister is the most important person in the government."
- **名词**: 质数，素数。例如: "2, 3, 5, and 7 are all prime numbers."
- **动词**: 使准备好，预热。例如: "The athlete was primed for the race after weeks of training."

## 短语
- "prime mover" 主要动力源，原动机。
- "prime candidate" 最佳人选，首选对象。
- "prime number" 质数，素数。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The prime objective of the mission is to explore Mars."
- 名词: "Is 19 a prime number? Yes, it is."
- 动词: "The engine needs to be primed before starting."
