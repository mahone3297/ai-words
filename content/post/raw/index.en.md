## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Uncooked; not processed or prepared. For example: "The steak was served raw."
- **Adjective**: In its natural state; crude or unrefined. For example: "The artist prefers to work with raw materials."
- **Verb**: To leave (food) uncooked or partially cooked. For example: "I like my pizza raw on the edges."
- **Verb**: In its simplest or most basic form;未经加工的。 For example: "The recipe calls for raw talent."
