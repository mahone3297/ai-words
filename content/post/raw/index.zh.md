## 读音
/ˈrɔː/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 未经加工的，生的，原始的。例如: "The raw materials are yet to be processed."
- **形容词**: 冷酷的，无情的。例如: "The raw pain of losing a loved one."
- **名词**: 生肉或未煮熟的食物。例如: "I prefer my steak medium-rare, not completely raw."

## 短语
- "raw data" 指未经过处理或分析的原始数据。
- "in the raw" 指事物最自然、未加修饰的状态。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She prefers to eat her vegetables raw for maximum nutrition."
- 形容词: "The winter wind was raw and biting."
- 名词: "The chef served us sashimi, which is raw fish."
