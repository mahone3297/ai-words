## 读音
/mʌd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 黏土，泥浆。例如: "The children were playing in the mud after the rain."
  - 混乱，一团糟。例如: "The project is in a complete mud; we need to sort it out."

## 短语
- "Muddy water" 指浑浊的水。
- "Mud fight" 指用泥巴打斗的游戏。

## 例句
- 名词: "The farmer's boots were covered in thick mud after working in the fields."
- 形容词: "The road was too muddy for the car to pass."
