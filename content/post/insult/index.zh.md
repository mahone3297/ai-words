## 读音
/ˈɪnsʌlt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 侮辱，挑衅，不敬的行为或言语。例如: "He received an insult from a passerby."
- **动词**: 侮辱，挑衅，对某人无礼。例如: "She insulted him with her harsh words."

## 短语
- "An insult to one's dignity" 意为对某人的尊严的侮辱。
- "Return the insult" 意为回应侮辱，反击。

## 例句
- 名词: "His comment was considered an insult to all the hardworking employees."
- 动词: "Don't let his insults get to you; he's just trying to provoke you."
