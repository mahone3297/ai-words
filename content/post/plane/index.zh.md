## 读音
/plen/

## 释义
- **名词** (n): 1. 飞机，航空器。例如: "He saw a plane flying overhead."
2. 平面，平展的部分。例如: "The painter worked on a large canvas, stretching it across a wooden plane."

- **动词** (v): 使平滑，刨平。例如: "The carpenter planed the wood to make it smooth."

## 短语
- "on a plane" 意为在飞机上。例如: "I'm traveling to New York on a plane."
- "be level with the plane" 表示与平面齐平。例如: "The picture needs to be hung so that it's level with the plane of the wall."

## 例句
- 名词: "She was excited to board her first plane for an international trip."
- 动词: "He used a hand plane to smooth out the rough edges of the wood."
