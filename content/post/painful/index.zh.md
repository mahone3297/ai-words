## 读音
/pəˈflaɪn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 引起痛苦的，令人不快的，令人难受的。例如: "She had a painful recovery after the operation."
- **名词**: 痛苦，疼痛。例如: "The wound was still causing him a lot of pain."

## 短语
- "a painful experience" 指一次让人感到痛苦的经历。
- "painful memories" 指令人回忆起来就感到痛苦的记忆。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The dentist warned me that the injection might be a little painful."
- 名词: "She tried to hide her tears, but the pain in her eyes was obvious."
