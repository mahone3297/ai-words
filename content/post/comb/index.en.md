## Pronunciation
/kəm/

## Definitions
### Noun
A comb is a tool with teeth or bristles used for grooming, especially to smooth and detangle hair. It can also refer to a comb-like device, such as a comb-shaped part in a pattern or design.

### Verb
To comb (through) means to run a comb or brush through one's hair to straighten, smooth, or arrange it. It can also be used metaphorically to describe sorting or arranging something systematically.

### Adjective
In some contexts, "comb-like" can be used to describe something having a similar shape or structure to a comb.

## Phrases
- "Comb your hair" is a common instruction to tidy up one's appearance.
- "Comb through the options" implies examining or reviewing choices carefully.
- "A comb-shaped coastline" refers to a geographical feature resembling a comb.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: She was brushing her long hair with a fine-toothed comb.
- **Verb**: He combed through the documents to find the relevant information.
