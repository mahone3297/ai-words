## 读音
/hel·p·ful/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有帮助的，有益的，愿意帮忙的。例如: "She was very helpful in organizing the event."
- **副词**: 有用地，以帮助的方式。例如: "The instructions were helpful in completing the task."

## 短语
- "be of help" 表示对某人有帮助。例如: "I hope my advice will be of help to you."
- "offer help" 提供帮助。例如: "Don't hesitate to offer help when someone needs it."

## 例句
- 形容词: "This tool is really helpful for fixing furniture."
- 副词: "The tutor explained the concept in a way that was helpful to me."

- Supportive
- Assisting
