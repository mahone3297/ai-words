## 读音
/ɪnˈtɜːprɪ.tər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 翻译者：将一种语言翻译成另一种语言的人。例如: "[The](/zh/post/the/) [interpreter](/zh/post/interpreter/) translated [the](/zh/post/the/) [speech](/zh/post/speech/) [from](/zh/post/from/) French [to](/zh/post/to/) English."
    - 解释器：计算机程序，用于解释和执行代码。例如: "Python is an interpreted [language](/zh/post/language/)."

- **动词**:
    - 口译：现场将一种语言转换成另一种语言。例如: "[She](/zh/post/she/) interpretered [for](/zh/post/for/) [the](/zh/post/the/) diplomats [during](/zh/post/during/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [meeting](/zh/post/meeting/)."

## 短语
- "[Professional](/zh/post/professional/) [interpreter](/zh/post/interpreter/)" 指的是专门从事翻译工作的人员。
- "Real-time [interpretation](/zh/post/interpretation/)" 指的是即时翻译，如同声传译。

## 例句
- 名词: "[The](/zh/post/the/) [interpreter](/zh/post/interpreter/) was [essential](/zh/post/essential/) [for](/zh/post/for/) [communication](/zh/post/communication/) [between](/zh/post/between/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [two](/zh/post/two/) delegations."
- 动词: "[She](/zh/post/she/) interpretered [the](/zh/post/the/) opera's libretto [from](/zh/post/from/) Italian [to](/zh/post/to/) English [for](/zh/post/for/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [audience](/zh/post/audience/)."
