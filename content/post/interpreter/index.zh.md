## 读音
/ɪnˈtɜːprɪ.tər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 翻译者：将一种语言翻译成另一种语言的人。例如: "[The](/post/the/) [interpreter](/post/interpreter/) translated [the](/post/the/) [speech](/post/speech/) [from](/post/from/) French [to](/post/to/) English."
    - 解释器：计算机程序，用于解释和执行代码。例如: "Python is an interpreted [language](/post/language/)."

- **动词**:
    - 口译：现场将一种语言转换成另一种语言。例如: "[She](/post/she/) interpretered [for](/post/for/) [the](/post/the/) diplomats [during](/post/during/) [the](/post/the/) [meeting](/post/meeting/)."

## 短语
- "[Professional](/post/professional/) [interpreter](/post/interpreter/)" 指的是专门从事翻译工作的人员。
- "Real-time [interpretation](/post/interpretation/)" 指的是即时翻译，如同声传译。
