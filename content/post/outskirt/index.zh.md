## 读音

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 城市或城镇边缘的郊区；外围地区。例如: "The city's outskirt is known for its scenic countryside."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 指位于城市或城镇边缘的。例如: "There are several outskirt villages with rich agricultural land."

## 短语
- "live on the outskirts of a town" 意味着住在城镇的边缘。
- "the outskirt areas" 泛指城市的外围区域。

## 例句

- 形容词: "The outskirt farm had a breathtaking view of the city skyline."
