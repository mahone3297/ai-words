## Word: Outskirt

## Pronunciation
/ˈaʊtskɜːrt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The outer part or edge of a town or city, away from the main built-up area. It often refers to areas that are less developed or less populated than the center. 

  - Example: "They decided to move to the outskirt of the city for a quieter life."
  - Example: "The new housing development is located on the outskirts of town."

## Phrases
- "On the outskirts": This phrase is used to describe something that's situated at the margins or border of a town or city. 
  - Example: "Their house was nestled on the outskirts of the bustling metropolis."
  
- "Beyond the outskirts": This implies a location even further removed from the main urban area.
  - Example: "Once we crossed beyond the outskirts, the landscape turned rural."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The old factory, now abandoned, once stood at the northern outskirt of the city."
- "As they ventured further into the countryside, they left behind the neon lights of the city's outskirts."

## Synonyms
- Periphery
- Fringe
- Border
- Edge
- Margin
- Suburb
- Rural area (depending on context)
