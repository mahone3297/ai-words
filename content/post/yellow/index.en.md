## Pronunciation
/ˈjeləʊ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Of a color having a mixture of red and green, resembling fresh grass or sunlight. (RGB values: 255, 255, 0)
- **Noun**: A color, typically associated with sunshine or ripe bananas. (Synonyms: golden, lemon, buttery)

## Phrases
- "A yellow dress" describes a garment of that color.
- "The sky was a beautiful yellow during sunrise."
- "Yellow caution signs warn of danger."

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The sun cast a warm yellow light through the window."
- **Noun**: "The school bus has a bright yellow exterior."
- "The autumn leaves turned a vibrant yellow before falling."
