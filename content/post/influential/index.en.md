## Word: Influential

## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈflʊənʃənl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having the power or ability to affect or change someone or something; having a strong effect on someone's behavior, opinions, or actions. 

### Examples
- **Adjective**: "She has become one of the most influential figures in the fashion industry."
  - "His work was highly influential in shaping modern literature."
  
## Phrases
- "An influential person": Refers to someone who holds sway over others or whose ideas have a significant impact on their field or society.
- "Influential factors": These are elements that play a decisive role or exert considerable impact on a particular outcome or decision.
  
## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The influential speaker captivated the audience with his inspiring words."
- "Her latest book became an influential text among students studying environmental science."
