## 读音
/nəˈsesərɪli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 必要地，不可避免地，必然地。表示某事是必须的或不可避免的情况。例如: "To succeed, you must work hard necessarily."

## 短语
- "It is necessarily true" 意味着这一定是真的。
- "Not all changes are necessarily good" 表示并非所有的变化都是好的。

## 例句
- "In order to pass the exam, you must study necessarily." (为了通过考试，你必须学习。)
- "His success was necessarily linked to his determination and hard work." (他的成功必然与他的决心和努力工作有关。)

## 同义词
- Inevitably
- Unavoidably
- Compulsorily
- Definitely
- Absolutely
