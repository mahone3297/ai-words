## 读音
/rɪˈkwɪrɪmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 必要条件；要求；规定。例如: "The job requires a bachelor's degree as a minimum requirement."
- **动词**: 需要；要求。例如: "The project requirements have changed, so we need to revise our plan."

## 短语
- "Meet the requirements" 意味着满足特定条件。
- "Technical requirements" 指技术上的需求或规格。
- "Software requirements specification" (SRS) 是软件开发中的需求文档。

## 例句
- 名词: "The university's admission requirement includes a high school diploma and a good GPA."
- 动词: "The new policy requires all employees to complete a safety training course."
