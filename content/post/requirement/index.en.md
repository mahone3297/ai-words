## Pronunciation
/ˈrɪkwaɪərmənt/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A demand or necessity for something**: A requirement is a necessary condition or standard that must be fulfilled. For example: "The project has strict requirements for code quality."
2. **A needed skill or ability**: A person's requirements may include certain qualifications. For example: "Employers often look for candidates with specific requirements."

### Verb
1. To specify or impose a need or condition: The company requires all employees to attend training. 
2. To ask or request something: She required a meeting with the manager.

## Phrases
- "Meet the requirements" means to fulfill or satisfy the conditions set forth.
- "To have a requirement" signifies needing something for a particular purpose.
- "Basic requirements" refer to the most fundamental necessities.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The job posting listed a variety of technical requirements."
- **Verb**: "The student must meet all academic requirements to graduate."
- **Verbal**: "They required all applicants to submit a resume and cover letter."
