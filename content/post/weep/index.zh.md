## 读音
/wiːp/

## 释义
- **动词**: 哭泣；流出眼泪。例如: "She wept when she heard the sad news."
- **名词**: 哭泣；啜泣。例如: "There were sobs and weeps from the grieving family."

## 短语
- "to weep tears" 意味着流下眼泪。
- "to weep for someone/something" 指为某人或某事哭泣。

## 例句
- 动词: "The child wept uncontrollably after falling off the bike."
- 名词: "His eyes were red from all the weeping he had done."
