## 读音
/rɪˈfres/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 使恢复精力；使清新：例如 "A cold drink really refreshes on a hot day."
  - 刷新；更新：例如 "I refreshed the webpage to see if there were any updates."

## 短语
- "refresh oneself" 意为恢复体力或精神，例如 "After a nap, he felt refreshed."
- "refresh one's memory" 指复习以唤起记忆，例如 "Let me refresh my memory by looking at the notes again."

## 例句
- 动词: "The cool breeze refreshed us after a long hike."
- "Refreshing the page often helps to ensure you have the latest information."
