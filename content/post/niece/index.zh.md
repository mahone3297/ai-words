## 读音
/nɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 女性的侄子；姐妹的女儿。例如: "She adores her niece and often takes her on trips."
  
## 短语
- "My niece is visiting us this weekend." 意思是我的侄女这周末会来拜访我们。

## 例句
- "My niece is such a talented artist, her paintings are amazing."
- "She bought a beautiful dress for her niece's graduation ceremony."

## 同义词
- Nephew (男性侄子)
- Cousin (堂兄弟/堂姐妹，表兄弟/表姐妹)
