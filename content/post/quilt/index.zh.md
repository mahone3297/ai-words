## 读音
/kwɪlt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种由多层布料缝合在一起的被子，通常有装饰性的设计。例如: "She made a beautiful patchwork quilt for her daughter."
- **动词**: 制作被子，特别是通过将多层布料缝在一起。例如: "My grandmother enjoys quilting in her spare time."

## 短语
- "Quilted jacket" 指的是有绗缝设计的夹克。
- "Hand-quilted" 表示被子是手工缝制的。

## 例句
- 名词: "The antique quilt on display was made by my great-grandmother."
- 动词: "She spent hours quilting a comforter for their bed."
