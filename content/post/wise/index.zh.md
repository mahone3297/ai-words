## 读音
/waɪz/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 明智的，聪明的，有智慧的。例如: "She made a wise decision in that situation."
- **名词**: 智者，贤人。例如: "The old man was respected as a wise elder in the village."

## 短语
- "wise beyond one's years" 表示某人年纪虽小但非常成熟。
- "in the fullness of one's wisdom" 指在某人最明智的时候。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It was wise of you to save money for a rainy day."
- 名词: "The wise king listened to the advice of his councilors."

## 同义词
- sagacious
- intelligent
- astute
- perceptive
- judicious
