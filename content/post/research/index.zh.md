## 读音
/ˈrɪsɜːrtʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 研究，调查，探究。例如: "She is conducting research on climate change."
- **动词**: 进行研究，调查。例如: "They are researching new methods of renewable energy production."

## 短语
- "Quantitative research" 指的是定量研究，侧重于数据和统计分析。
- "Qualitative research" 指的是定性研究，关注于理解和解释现象的本质。
- "Primary research" 指的是原始或第一手的研究，通常涉及收集新数据。
- "Secondary research" 指的是使用已存在的资料进行的研究。

## 例句
- 名词: "His latest paper focuses on groundbreaking research in neuroscience."
- 动词: "The archaeologists are researching the ancient ruins for clues about past civilizations."
