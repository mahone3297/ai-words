## 读音
/ˈrɛkəpʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 接待处，接待区：A place where guests or visitors are received, such as in a hotel, office, or event.
   - 欢迎，接待：The act of welcoming or receiving someone, especially in an official or formal manner.
   - 接待员：The person who works at a reception desk, greeting and assisting visitors.

- **动词**:
   - 接待，招待：To receive or greet people formally, especially at an event or in a professional setting.

## 短语
- "Front desk reception" 指酒店或办公室的前台接待。
- "Business reception" 指商业场合的接待活动。
- "Wedding reception" 是婚礼后的招待会。

## 例句
- 名词: "The hotel's reception is open 24 hours a day."
- 动词: "The staff were busy receiving guests at the reception."
- 接待员: "The receptionist helped me book a room."
