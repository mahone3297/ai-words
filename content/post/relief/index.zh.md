## 读音
/rɪˈliːf/

## 释义
- **名词**: 减轻，解除（痛苦、忧虑或负担）。例如: "The medicine brought instant relief from her headache."
- **名词**: 宽慰，安慰。例如: "Knowing that help was on the way was a great relief to her."
- **名词**: （艺术作品中的）浮雕，凸起部分。例如: "The ancient sculpture had intricate relief carvings."
- **动词**: 减轻，解除（痛苦、压力等）。例如: "The charity aims to relieve poverty in developing countries."
- **动词**: 替换，接替（某人的职位或工作）。例如: "He came on as a substitute to relieve the tired striker."

## 短语
- "in relief" 表示如释重负。例如: "She sighed in relief when she found her keys."
- "financial relief" 指经济上的援助。例如: "The government offered financial relief to small businesses affected by the pandemic."

## 例句
- 名词: "The sound of the rain provided relief from the heat."
- 动词: "The volunteer work relieves the burden on the local community."
- 动词: "She was relieved of her duties due to illness."
