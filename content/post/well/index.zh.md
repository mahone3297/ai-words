## 读音
/wɛl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 好的，健康的，顺利的。例如: "I'm feeling well today."
- **副词**: 好，很好地，充分地。例如: "She did well on her exam."
- **名词**: 井，源泉。例如: "There is a deep well in the backyard."
- **动词**: 做得好（主要用于美式口语）。例如: "You're well advised to take his advice."

## 短语
- "As well" 也，而且。例如: "She speaks French as well as English."
- "Do well in" 在...做得好。例如: "He does well in mathematics."

## 例句
- 形容词: "She looked well after her illness."
- 副词: "He plays the guitar very well."
- 名词: "The village relies on the water from that old well."
- 动词: "You're doing well in your new job."

## 同义词
- Good (形容词)
- Properly (副词)
- Successfully (副词)
- Healthily (形容词，指身体状况)
- Adequately (副词)
