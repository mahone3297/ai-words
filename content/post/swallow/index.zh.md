## 读音
/ˈswɔːləʊ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 吞下（食物或液体），不咀嚼或只略微咀嚼。例如: "She swallowed the pill with a glass of water."
- **名词**: 鸟类中的燕子科动物，如燕子。例如: "The swallow is a small, migratory bird."
- **短语动词**: "to swallow something whole" 意为整个或不加咀嚼地吞下。例如: "The child swallowed the candy whole."

## 短语
- "swallow one's pride" 忍受羞辱或屈尊俯就。例如: "In order to solve the problem, he had to swallow his pride and apologize."
- "swallow the bitter pill" 接受不愉快的事实或结果。例如: "She knew she had to swallow the bitter pill and accept the job offer."

## 例句
- 动词: "He swallowed the pill without any difficulty."
- 名词: "The swallows returned to the barn for the summer nesting season."
