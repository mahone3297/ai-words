## 读音
/peɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 疼痛，苦楚。例如: "She was in great pain after the accident."
- **动词**: 使感到疼痛。例如: "The headache was beginning to pain him."
- **形容词**: 痛苦的，令人不快的。例如: "It was a painful decision to let go of her job."

## 短语
- "in pain" 意味着处于疼痛之中。
- "a pain in the neck/back/..." 用来形容某人或某事很烦人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The doctor prescribed painkillers for his chronic back pain."
- 动词: "The splinter pained her whenever she moved her finger."
- 形容词: "It was a painful memory he tried to forget."

## 同义词
- Suffering
- Agony
- Discomfort
- Ache
- Torment
