## 读音
/rɪˈɡɑːrd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 尊重，关注，看待。例如: "She has a high regard for his opinion."
- **动词**: 对待，视为，考虑。例如: "He regarded her with suspicion."
- **介词**: 关于，就…而言。例如: "I have some remarks regarding your proposal."

## 短语
- "regard for" 意为对…的尊重或考虑。例如: "He showed great regard for her feelings."
- "in regard to" 表示关于或就…来说。例如: "I have a question in regard to the contract."

## 例句
- 名词: "His colleagues held him in high regard for his dedication to the job."
- 动词: "She regarded the painting carefully, admiring its details."
- 介词: "There were several issues to be addressed in regard to the new policy."
