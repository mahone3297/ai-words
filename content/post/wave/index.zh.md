## 读音
/weɪv/

## 释义
- **名词**: 波浪，振动，波状运动。例如: "The ocean was calm except for a few gentle waves."
- **动词**: 摇动，挥舞。例如: "She waved her hand in greeting."
- **名词**: 打招呼，致意。例如: "He gave a friendly wave from across the street."
- **动词**: 流行，风靡。例如: "That song really waved last summer."

## 短语
- "Wave goodbye" 意为挥手告别。
- "Wave hello" 指挥手打招呼。
- "A wave of emotion" 指一阵强烈的情感。

## 例句
- 名词: "The surfer rode the powerful wave with ease."
- 动词: "He waved his arms to get the waiter's attention."
- 名词: "She gave us a wave as she got on the bus."
- 动词: "The fans waved their team's flags enthusiastically."
