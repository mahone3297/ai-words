## 读音
/pʌblɪˈkeɪʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 公开发表；出版： "The book is scheduled for publication next month."
   - 出版物；刊物： "She works for a scientific publication."
   - 发行；发布： "The company's annual publication reports their financial status."

## 短语
- "in publication" 意味着正在发行或出版中。
- "publication date" 是指出版物的发行日期。
- "peer-reviewed publication" 指经过同行评审的学术出版物。

## 例句
- 名词: "The magazine's latest publication features articles on global warming."
- 动词（短语）: "Her novel will be published by a major publishing house."
- 发行： "The government published new guidelines on health and safety."
