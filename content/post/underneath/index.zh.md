## 读音
/ˌʌnərˈniθ/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示在...之下，通常指物理位置。例如: "The book is underneath the table."
- **副词**: 指在下方，或者表示秘密地、不为人知地。例如: "She whispered underneath her breath."

## 短语
- "underneath it all" 指在所有表面现象之下，实质上或根本上。例如: "Underneath it all, he's a very kind person."
- "hidden underneath" 指隐藏在下面。例如: "There was a message written underneath the paint."

## 例句
- 介词: "The cat was sleeping peacefully underneath the tree."
- 副词: "He kept his true feelings hidden underneath a calm exterior."
