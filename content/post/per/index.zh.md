## 读音
/per/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示关于、就…而言、按照。例如: "According to the law, it is illegal per se."
- **副词**: 通常用来强调频率或程度，意为“每次”，“每一”。例如: "I need to exercise per day."

## 短语
- "per capita" 意为每人、人均，例如: "The average income per capita in this country is quite high."
- "per diem" 指每日的费用或津贴，例如: "The company pays a per diem allowance for travel expenses."

## 例句
- 介词: "The contract specifies a payment of $500 per week."
- 副词: "I drink two cups of coffee per day."
