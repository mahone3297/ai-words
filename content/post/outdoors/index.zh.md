## 读音
/ˈaʊtdɔːrz/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 在户外的，室外的。例如: "We love to have picnics in the outdoors."
- **副词**: 在外面，露天。例如: "The children were playing outdoors."

## 短语
- "Outdoors activities" 指在户外进行的各种活动，如徒步、露营等。
- "Living outdoors" 意味着在自然环境中居住，而不是在建筑物内。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The festival takes place in the beautiful outdoors."
- 副词: "Let's play football outdoors instead of in the gym."
