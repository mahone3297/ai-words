## Word: Outdoors

## Pronunciation
/ˌaʊtˈdoʊrz/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Located, happening, or used outside a building; not indoors. For example: "We're going for a hike _outdoors_ this weekend."
- **Noun**: The open air; the area outside buildings, especially when considered as a place for spending leisure time. For example: "The children love playing _outdoors_."

### Adjective Examples
- "Let's have a picnic outdoors where we can enjoy the sunshine."
- "They installed an outdoor swimming pool in their backyard."

### Noun Examples
- "After being cooped up inside all day, it felt great to be out in the outdoors."
- "She's passionate about nature photography and spends most of her free time in the outdoors."

## Phrases
- "Outdoors activities" refers to recreational pursuits that take place outside, like hiking, camping, or sports.
- "Go outdoors" means to leave the interior of a building to spend time outside.
- "Bring the outdoors in" is an expression used when decorating to describe incorporating natural elements into indoor spaces.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The festival organizers set up large screens outdoors for people who couldn't fit inside the venue."
- **Noun**: "One of the benefits of living near the countryside is easy access to the great outdoors."
