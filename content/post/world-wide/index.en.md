## Pronunciation
/worldˈwaɪd/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterizing something that is global or spanning the entire planet. For example: "The company's reach is world-wide."
- **Adverb**: Throughout the world; on a global scale. For example: "The event attracted world-wide attention."

## Phrases
- "World-wide impact" signifies influence across the entire globe.
- "A world-wide audience" indicates people from all corners of the Earth.
- "World-wide climate change" emphasizes the global nature of an environmental issue.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The new technology has had a world-wide adoption rate."
- **Adverb**: "The outbreak quickly became a world-wide health crisis."
- "The organization's mission is to improve education world-wide."
