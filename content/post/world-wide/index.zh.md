## 读音
/ˈwɜːrld.waɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 全球范围的；世界各地的。例如: "The internet has made communication world-wide."
- **副词**: 在全球范围内；遍及全世界。例如: "The company operates world-wide."

## 短语
- "world-wide network" 指全球性的网络。
- "world-wide fame" 指全球范围的声誉或知名度。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The event attracted participants from all over the world, making it world-wide in scope."
- 副词: "Their products are available in many countries, sold world-wide."
