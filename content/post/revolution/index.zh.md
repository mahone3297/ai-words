## 读音
/ˈrɛvəluːʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 大规模的变革或彻底改变，通常指政治、社会或科技领域。例如: "The Industrial Revolution marked a significant change in manufacturing processes."
    - 圆周运动，旋转。例如: "The Earth's rotation around its axis is a daily revolution."

## 短语
- "Social revolution" 社会革命，指社会结构或制度的根本变革。
- "Political revolution" 政治革命，指政权或政治体系的变革。
- "Industrial revolution" 工业革命，指从手工生产向机械化生产的转变。

## 例句
- 名词: "The French Revolution was a period of radical social and political upheaval in France."
- 名词: "The invention of the steam engine sparked the beginning of the Industrial Revolution."
