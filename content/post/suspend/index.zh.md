## 读音
/səˈspend/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 暂停，中止。例如: "The school has suspended classes due to the storm."
  2. 悬挂，吊起。例如: "The painting is suspended from the ceiling by wires."
  3. (法律、组织中)停职，停止权利或资格。例如: "He was suspended from his job for misconduct."

## 短语
- "suspend disbelief" 意思是在观看戏剧、电影或阅读时暂时放下对某些不真实或难以置信的情节的怀疑。
- "suspend operations" 指的是暂停业务活动。

## 例句
- 动词(暂停): "The company decided to suspend production until further notice."
- 动词(悬挂): "The hammock was suspended between two trees by strong ropes."
- 动词(停职): "He was suspended from the team for breaking the rules."
