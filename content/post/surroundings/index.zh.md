## 读音
/səˈraʊndɪŋz/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 周围的事物，环境，情境。例如: "The park was peaceful, with beautiful surroundings."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 周围的，附近的。例如: "The restaurant has a pleasant surrounding atmosphere."

## 短语
- "surroundings of a place" 指一个地方的环境或周边环境。
- "improve one's surroundings" 意味着改善一个人的生活或工作环境。

## 例句
- 名词: "The patient felt better after being moved to more comfortable surroundings."
- 形容词: "She loved to sit in her garden, surrounded by blooming flowers."
