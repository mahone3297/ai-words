## Pronunciation
/səˈraʊndɪŋz/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The things that surround or are situated near a particular person, place, or object, often referring to the environment or conditions. For example: "The house was surrounded by a beautiful garden."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing the surrounding area or atmosphere. For example: "The peaceful surroundings helped him relax."

## Phrases
- "Surrounding areas" refers to the regions immediately adjacent to a particular location.
- "Create a comfortable surrounding" means to make an environment that feels welcoming or soothing.
- "In the surrounding countryside" denotes the nearby rural landscape.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children played amidst the surrounding trees and flowers."
- **Adjective**: "The hotel's luxurious surroundings offered a tranquil escape from the city."
- "She loved the view from her apartment, with the mountains forming a stunning surrounding."
