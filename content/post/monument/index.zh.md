## 读音
/ˈmɒnjʊmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 巨大的建筑物或纪念物，通常为了纪念重要事件、人物或历史而建。例如: "The Statue of Liberty is an iconic monument in New York City."
- **名词**: 重要的事件或成就，尤指在历史上的。例如: "His victory in the battle was considered a monument to his bravery."

## 短语
- "Monument to" 用于描述为纪念某人或某事而建立的建筑。例如: "There's a monument to the war heroes in the city square."
- "Monumental task" 表示极其艰巨的任务。例如: "Building a new highway across the mountains was a monumental task."

## 例句
- 名词: "The ancient monument still stands tall after thousands of years."
- 名词: "The dedication ceremony for the new monument took place last week."
