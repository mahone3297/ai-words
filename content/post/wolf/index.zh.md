## 读音
/wʊlf/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种哺乳动物，属于犬科，通常体型大，毛色灰色，以狩猎其他动物为生。例如: "The wolf is a skilled predator in the wilderness."
- **名词 (比喻)**: 比喻贪婪或凶猛的人。例如: "He's a wolf in sheep's clothing, pretending to be kind but actually ruthless."

## 短语
- "wolf down" 意思是迅速地吃掉（食物）。例如: "He wolfed down his dinner in just a few minutes."
- "the lone wolf" 指独自行动的人。例如: "She prefers to work as a lone wolf rather than in a team."

## 例句
- 名词: "The howl of a distant wolf echoed through the forest."
- 比喻名词: "Watch out for those wolves in the business world; they'll take advantage of you if you're not careful."
