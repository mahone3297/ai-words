## 读音
/ˌrɪ prəˈdjuːs/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 复制，再生产：产生与原物相似或相同的事物。例如: "The printer can reproduce high-quality images."
2. 生殖，繁殖：生物体产生后代。例如: "Many animals reproduce by laying eggs."
3. 重现，复制声音或图像：在媒体上再现声音或图像。例如: "The concert was recorded so it could be reproduced on radio and TV."

## 短语
- "reproduce accurately" 准确复制
- "reproduce a sound" 重现一个声音
- "reproduce oneself" 自我繁殖

## 例句
- 动词: "The artist was able to reproduce the painting exactly from memory."
- 动词: "The bees reproduce by building new hives and filling them with honey."
