## 读音
/ˈrɪməʊt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 距离远的，偏远的。例如: "The village is located in a remote area."
- **形容词**: 遥控的，远程的。例如: "We can control the TV with a remote."
- **名词**: 遥控器。例如: "Where's the remote? I want to change the channel."
- **副词**: 遥远地，远远地。例如: "He lives remote from the city."

## 短语
- "remote control" 指遥控器或远程控制设备。
- "remote access" 指远程访问，如远程登录电脑系统。
- "remote possibility" 意为微乎其微的可能性。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The research station is situated in a remote part of Antarctica."
- 形容词: "The server can be accessed via remote connection."
- 名词: "I lost the TV remote, so I had to get up to change the channel."
- 副词: "The mountain peak seems remote even from this distance."

- Isolated
