## Pronunciation
/rɪˈməʊt/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A remote control or device that operates something from a distance, often via radio waves or an internet connection.
- A distant or isolated location or area.
- A person who lives or works far away from others.
- (Computing) A software application or program that allows users to access and interact with a computer system remotely.

### Verb
- To operate or control something from a distance using a remote control.
- To be situated or located at a great distance.
- To engage in a remote communication or interaction.

## Phrases
- "Control something remotely"
- "Be in remote areas"
- "Work remotely"
- "Keep in touch remotely"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I love being able to control my TV with my smartphone app, it's so convenient."
- **Verb**: "Due to the pandemic, many employees have been forced to work remotely."
- "The archaeologist studied ancient artifacts found in remote regions of the Amazon jungle."
