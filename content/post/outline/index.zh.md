## 读音
/ˈaʊtlaɪn/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 大纲，提纲；轮廓；概要。例如: "She prepared an outline of her presentation before the meeting."
- **动词 (v)**: 为…画出轮廓；概述。例如: "He outlined the main points of the project in his email."

## 短语
- "Outline a plan" 意味着制定计划的大致框架。
- "Give an outline of something" 是指给出某事物的基本描述或主要内容。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher provided an outline of the course topics for the semester."
- 动词: "In his speech, he outlined the government's new economic policy."
