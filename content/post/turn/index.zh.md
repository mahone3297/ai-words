## 读音
/ˈtɜːrn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 转动，旋转。例如: "The wheel turned smoothly."
  - 改变方向。例如: "She turned left at the corner."
  - 变换，转变。例如: "His mood turned sour after the argument."
  - 切换或调到（频道、歌曲等）。例如: "He turned up the radio and tuned to a different station."
  - 轮流，依次。例如: "It's your turn to clean the kitchen."

- **名词**:
  - 机会，轮次。例如: "Everyone gets a turn to speak."
  - 旋转，翻转。例如: "She did a complete turn in mid-air."

## 短语
- "Take a turn" 意味着轮流做某事或改变方向。
- "Turn the page" 指翻过书页或开始新的篇章。
- "On the turn" 用于描述事物即将发生变化的时刻。

## 例句
- 动词: "The child happily turned the colorful pages of the picture book."
- 名词: "It was my turn to present my ideas during the meeting."
