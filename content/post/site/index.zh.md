## 读音
/saɪt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 地点，位置。例如: "The construction site is located downtown."
          2. 网站，网址。例如: "I visit that news site every day."
          3. 遗址，遗迹。例如: "Archaeologists discovered an ancient site near the river."
- **动词 (v)**: 建设，设立。例如: "They are planning to site a new school in the suburbs."

## 短语
- "building site" 建筑工地
- "website" 网页，网站
- "historic site" 历史遗迹

## 例句
