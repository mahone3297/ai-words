## 读音
/ˈræɡ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 旧衣物碎片，常用来擦拭或清洁。例如: "She used an old rag to clean the furniture."
   - 报纸的贬称，尤指低质量的出版物。例如: "Don't believe everything you read in that rag; it's full of gossip and lies."
   - 破布，破烂的衣服。例如: "The beggar was wrapped in rags in the cold winter."

- **动词**:
   - 糟蹋，戏弄（某人）。例如: "They were ragging on him for his silly joke."
   - 粗糙地擦洗。例如: "I ragged the floor with a wet cloth to remove the dirt."

## 短语
- "rag about" 意为戏弄或嘲笑某人。
- "rag on someone" 意味着持续不断地批评或取笑某人。
- "in rags" 用来描述衣衫褴褛的状态。

## 例句
- 名词: "She found a piece of rag in the corner to clean the spills."
- 动词: "The boys were ragging each other during their friendly match."
- "The homeless man was seen wandering around in rags."
