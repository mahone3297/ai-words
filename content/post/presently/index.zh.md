## 读音
/prɪˈzentli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 目前，现在；不久，立刻。例如: "I'm busy at present, but I'll call you back presently." 
- **形容词**: 现在进行时态的标志，表示动作正在进行。例如: "He is presently studying for his exams."

## 短语
- "At present" 意为目前，现在。例如: "At present, we have no plans for a vacation."
- "Presently speaking" 指就当前情况而言。例如: "Presently speaking, the situation seems stable."

## 例句
- 副词: "The issue is being dealt with presently, so please be patient."
- 形容词: "She is presently employed as a software engineer."
