## Pronunciation
/pɪŋk/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A color** characterized by a mixture of red and white light, often associated with flowers, sunsets, or femininity.
- **adj**: Of a light, soft red color; rosy.

### Verb
- **To have a pink hue or appearance**: The sky was pink during sunrise.
- **figurative**: To be affected by a particular emotion or feeling (usually love or admiration): Her cheeks pinked up when she heard his compliment.

### Adjective
- **Describing a color**: She wore a pink dress to the party.
- **Referring to a feeling or complexion**: He had a healthy pink glow.

### Adverb
- Not used as an independent adverb in standard English.

## Phrases
- "A pink ribbon" symbolizes breast cancer awareness.
- "A pink sky" indicates a beautiful sunset.
- "Pink blush on her cheeks" describes a shy or embarrassed expression.
