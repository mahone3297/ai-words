## Word: Optimistic

## Pronunciation
/ˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪk/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having or showing hopefulness and confidence about the future or the success of something; inclined to take a hopeful or positive view. For example:
  - _**Adjective Usage**_: "She maintained an optimistic outlook despite the challenges."
  - "The investors remained optimistic about the company's potential growth."

## Phrases
- "Optimistic outlook": A hopeful or confident perspective on future events or outcomes.
- "Optimistic prediction": A forecast that suggests a favorable or desirable outcome.
- "Optimistic tone": A style or mood in writing or speech that reflects hopefulness or positivity.

## Example Sentences
- _**Adjective Usage**_: "He has an optimistic attitude towards his recovery from the illness."
- "Despite the economic downturn, the CEO projected an optimistic forecast for next quarter."
- "Her optimistic nature helped her overcome many obstacles in life."

## Synonyms
- Hopeful
- Positive
- Upbeat
- Confident
- Sanguine
- Pessimism's antonym
- Expectant of good things
- Bright-eyed and bushy-tailed (informal)
- Rosy (as in having a 'rosy outlook')
