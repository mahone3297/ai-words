## 读音
/ˌɑːp.tɪˈmɪ.sɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 态度乐观的，抱有希望的。指某人倾向于看到事物的积极面，对未来持正面预期。

## 短语
- "to be optimistic about something" 对某事持乐观态度。
- "an optimistic outlook" 乐观的看法或前景。

## 例句
- "Despite the challenges, she remained optimistic about the future of the project."
- "His optimistic personality lifted everyone's spirits during tough times."
