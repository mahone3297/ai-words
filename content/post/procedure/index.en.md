## Pronunciation
/proʊˈsiːdʒər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A set of actions or steps followed in a particular order to accomplish a task or reach a goal. 2. A formal or established method or process. 3. (Computing) A routine or algorithm that performs a specific task.
- **Verb**: To carry out or follow a specific procedure; to perform a set of steps.

## Phrases
- "Procedure for" indicates a series of steps to be taken.
- "Standard operating procedure" refers to a well-defined set of actions.
- "Follow the procedure" means to adhere to the prescribed steps.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The surgery went smoothly according to the medical procedure."
- **Verb**: "Employees must follow the company's security procedure when entering the building."
- "We need to review the standard procedure for handling customer complaints."
