## 读音
/prəˈsiːdʒər/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 一个有计划的、系统的步骤或过程，尤指完成特定任务或达到某一目标的一系列行动。例如: "The laboratory procedure took several hours to complete."
  - 法律或组织中的正式程序或规程。例如: "The company's hiring procedure involves multiple interviews and background checks."

## 短语
- "Standard operating procedure" (SOP) 指的是常规操作程序或标准流程。
- "Medical procedure" 通常指的是医疗或手术过程。
- "Legal procedure" 涉及法律诉讼或处理的程序。

## 例句
- 名词: "Follow the safety procedure when handling chemicals in the lab."
- 名词: "The judge explained the legal procedure to the defendant."
