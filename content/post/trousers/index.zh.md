## 读音
/trəʊzəz/

## 释义
- **名词**: 裤子；长裤。通常指上下分开的两条裤管，与裙子相对。例如: "She was wearing smart black trousers for the interview."
- **复数形式**: 单数形式为trouser，但通常作为复数名词使用。

## 短语
- "Pair of trousers" 指一条裤子。
- "Cargo trousers" 是一种有多个口袋的宽松工作或休闲裤。
- "Smart trousers" 指正式或时尚的裤子。

## 例句
- "I need to buy a new pair of trousers for the office."
- "The little boy couldn't find his left trouser after playing in the park."
- "She paired her red blouse with black trousers for a sophisticated look."

## 同义词
- Pants
- Trousers
- Jeans
- Slacks
- Bottoms (在某些语境下)
