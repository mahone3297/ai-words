## 读音
/ɪnˈvɑːlv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 包含；使参与。例如: "The project involves working with a diverse team." 
2. 卷入；牵涉。例如: "She was involved in a car accident yesterday."
3. 需要（某种努力、技能等）。例如: "Learning a new language can involve a lot of time and effort."

## 短语
- "involve someone in something" 意为让某人参与某事。例如: "The teacher decided to involve all students in the debate."
- "to be involved with" 表示与某人或某事有关系。例如: "She's deeply involved with the local charity organization."

## 例句
- 动词: "The cooking class involves learning new recipes and practicing knife skills."
- 卷入: "His business problems began to involve his entire family."
- 需要: "The experiment involves observing the behavior of animals in their natural habitat."
