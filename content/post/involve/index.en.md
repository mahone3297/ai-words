## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (in-volve) To include or contain something as a necessary part or consequence; to have within its scope or makeup. For example: "The project involves working with multiple teams."
  - To engage or occupy someone's time, effort, or interest; to commit oneself to something. Example: "She decided to involve herself in local politics."
  - To connect or link one thing with another, often implying a complex relationship. Example: "His decision will inevitably involve legal complications."

## Phrases
- "Get involved": To participate or become engaged in an activity or situation.
- "Involved in": Indicating participation or having a connection with something. Example: "She was deeply involved in the planning process."
- "Involve oneself in": To actively participate or become committed to something. Example: "He decided to involve himself in charity work."

## Example Sentences
- **Verb (include)**: "The recipe involves several spices that bring out the unique flavor."
- **Verb (engage)**: "They want to involve more young people in community service projects."
