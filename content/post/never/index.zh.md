## 读音
/ˈnevər/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示从不，从未，决不。例如: "I have never been to Australia." 
- **连词**: 连接两个句子，表示如果…决不…，相当于“if…not”。例如: "I'll never leave you unless you ask me to."

## 短语
- "Not ever" 用来强调从未发生过的情况。
- "Never mind" 用于告诉别人不必在意或忘记某个问题。
- "Never say never" 意味着事情总有可能性，不要轻易断言不可能。

## 例句
- 副词: "She has never missed a day of work in her entire career."
- 连词: "I will never leave you, not even in the toughest times."

## 同义词
- Not ever
- At no time
- In no way
- On no occasion
- Under no circumstances
