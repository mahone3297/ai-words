## 读音
/ˈleɪʒər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 自由支配的时间，闲暇；悠闲。例如: "He has plenty of leisure to pursue his hobbies."
- **形容词**: 闲适的，消遣的，非正式的。例如: "They spent their leisure hours reading and gardening."

## 短语
- "At leisure" 表示有充分的时间。例如: "Take your time, we're not in a hurry, do it at leisure."
- "Leisure activities" 指休闲娱乐活动。例如: "Swimming and hiking are popular leisure activities."

## 例句
- 名词: "During weekends, I enjoy spending my leisure time with family and friends."
- 形容词: "The leisure dress code allows for comfortable and casual clothing."

## 同义词
- Free time
- Spare time
- Relaxation
- Recreation
- Down time
