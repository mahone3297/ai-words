## 读音
/stiːp/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 
    - (坡度)陡峭的；险峻的：The mountain has a steep climb.
    - (价格、增长等)急剧的；骤然的：The price increase was very steep.
    - (程度)极大的；过分的：The penalty for the minor offense seemed steep.

## 短语
- "steep slope" 指陡峭的山坡。
- "steep price" 指高昂的价格。
- "steep learning curve" 指学习曲线陡峭，意味着需要快速掌握大量知识或技能。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The road to the castle is so steep that it's challenging to walk up."
- "The company's profits had a steep rise in the last quarter."
- "The penalty for littering is quite steep, with fines up to $500."
