## Pronunciation
/stɪl/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: 1. In a state of not moving or changing; stationary. For example: "The car stopped, and the engine was still."
   - **Adjective**: a. Remaining in the same position or state; unchanging. For example: "The room was still and quiet after the party."
   - **Verb**: To remain in a particular condition or state; persist. For example: "The pain was still there despite the medication."

## Phrases
- "Be still" means to remain motionless.
- "He's still working on it" implies that the task is not yet completed.
- "The sound was still audible" indicates that the noise could still be heard.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "After the storm, the wind had finally died down, and everything was still."
- **Adjective**: "She sat still on the bench, lost in thought."
- **Verb**: "Despite his injury, he tried to still his racing heart."
