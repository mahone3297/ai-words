## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act or process of preparing something, often in advance or with care. 2. An organized state or condition; readiness. 3. (Cookery) A mixture of ingredients, especially one that is to be cooked. 4. (Psychology) The mental or emotional state that precedes a particular behavior or action.

- **Verb**: To make ready or prepare for a specific purpose or situation. 2. To set aside or arrange time or resources for something.
