## 读音
/prɪˈpeər.eɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词** (n):
    - 准备；预备：意为为某个活动或事件做好必要的安排和准备工作。例如: "We need ample time for preparation before the exam."
    - 预制品；制剂：特指化学或医学上的混合物，如药剂或溶液。例如: "The doctor prescribed a special preparation for his sore throat."

## 短语
- "Make preparations for something" 意味着为某事做准备。
- "Preparation H" 是一种治疗痔疮的非处方药。

## 例句
- 名词: "The chef spent hours in the kitchen on food preparation for the dinner party."
- 名词: "She applied the preparation to the affected area as directed by the pharmacist."
