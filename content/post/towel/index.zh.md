## 读音
/ˈtaʊəl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种通常由吸水性材料制成的布巾，用于擦干身体、手或清洁表面。例如: "I need a towel to dry my hands after washing them."
- **动词 (v)**: 使用毛巾擦拭。例如: "She towel-dried her hair after taking a shower."

## 短语
- "towel off" 意味着用毛巾擦干，如 "After swimming, he towel off his wet body."
- "throw in the towel" 比喻放弃或认输，如 "When things got tough, he threw in the towel."

## 例句
- 名词: "There's a clean towel on the rack for you to use."
- 动词: "She quickly towel-dried herself before getting dressed."

## 同义词

- Cloth
- Sponge
- Duster (用于清洁的)
- Rags (常指旧布料)
