## Pronunciation
/juːˈniːk/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having no equal or match; one-of-a-kind. For example: "The painting was a unique piece of art."
- **Adjective (sometimes used as noun)**: A person or thing that is the only one of its kind. For example: "She was the only unique survivor of the accident."

## Phrases
- "A unique combination" refers to something that is distinct and not found elsewhere.
- "Unique qualities" highlight distinctive attributes.
- "Be unique" encourages individuals to be original and different.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Each snowflake has a unique pattern, making them all special."
- **Adjective (as noun)**: "In the animal kingdom, the platypus is a unique creature with its egg-laying trait."
- **Adverb**: "She had a unique perspective on the situation."
