## 读音
/ˈlætər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 较后的，较晚的，指两个中的第二个。例如: "The latter option seemed more suitable."
- **名词**: 后者，指两个事物或时期中后面的那个。例如: "Of the two choices, I prefer the latter."

## 短语
- "the former and the latter" 用来同时提及两个事物，前者和后者。
- "latter days" 指后期，晚期。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She considered both options but eventually chose the latter."
- 名词: "In his latter years, he became more reflective."

## 同义词
- later
- subsequent
- latterly
- second
- final (在某些上下文中)
