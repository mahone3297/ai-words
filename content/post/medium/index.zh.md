## 读音
/ˈmiːdiəm/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 中间物，媒介：Something that serves as a means of communication or expression, such as television, radio, or print.
  - 平均值，中等：A value that is midway between the largest and smallest values in a set.
  - 介质，媒介物：The substance through which something passes or acts, like air or water.

- **形容词**:
  - 中等的，适中的：Not too large or too small; moderate.
  - 平均的，中等水平的：Of average quality or ability.

## 短语
- "the medium of television" 指电视这种传播媒介。
- "medium-sized company" 指中等规模的公司。
- "medium rare" 在烹饪中用来描述牛排等食物的熟度，意为半熟。

## 例句
- 名词: "News can be conveyed through various media, such as newspapers and the internet."
- 形容词: "She likes her steak cooked medium so it's still slightly pink inside."
