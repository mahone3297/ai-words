## 读音
/ˈspeʃəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 特别的，不寻常的，有专门用途的。例如: "She received a special gift for her birthday."
- **名词**: 专门的活动，节目或服务。例如: "The restaurant offers a special dinner menu tonight."
- **副词**: 特别地，专门地。例如: "He was treated specially due to his VIP status."

## 短语
- "Special offer" 指特别优惠或促销。
- "Special guest" 指特邀嘉宾或特殊访客。
- "Special delivery" 指快递或速递服务。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This dress is made of a special fabric that doesn't wrinkle easily."
- 名词: "There's a special on roses at the florist's today."
- 副词: "The chef prepared the dish specially for our anniversary."
