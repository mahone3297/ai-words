## 读音
/waɪr/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 细长而有弹性的金属丝，通常用于导电、编织或构造物品。例如: "He used a wire to hang the picture on the wall."
- **名词 (n)**: 电线，电缆。例如: "The electrician is checking the wires in the house for any faults."
- **动词 (v)**: 通过电线传输，如电子或信息。例如: "They wired the money to his bank account."
- **名词 (n)**: 密谋，阴谋。例如: "There was a suspicion of a wire behind the sudden resignation."

## 短语
- "lay a wire" 意为铺设电线。
- "on wire" 可能指的是在钢丝上或被钢丝固定。
- "wiretap" 指的是非法监听电话或通信。

## 例句
- 名词: "She carefully untangled the wires from the headphones."
- 动词: "The company wired the funds instantly after receiving the confirmation."
- 名词: "The police suspected a political wire behind the attack."
