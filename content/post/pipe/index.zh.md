## 读音
/paɪp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 管道，通常指输送液体或气体的长管。例如: "The plumber fixed a leak in the water pipe."
   2. 吸烟用具，尤指抽烟斗。例如: "He enjoyed smoking his old wooden pipe after dinner."
   3. 音乐中的长笛或风笛。例如: "The piper played a haunting melody on the bagpipe."

- **动词**: 通过管道输送。例如: "The oil is piped from the field to the refinery."

## 短语
- "Pipe down" 意味着安静下来或停止说话。
- "Pipe up" 意味着发表意见或开始说话。
- "Pipe dream" 指不切实际的幻想。

## 例句
- 名词: "The workers dug a trench to lay a new gas pipe."
- 动词: "The oil will be piped to the coast for export."
- 音乐用具: "The sound of the pipes echoed through the valley."

## 同义词
- Tube (for pipes)
- Channel
- Flute (for music pipes)
- Smoking instrument (for smoking pipes)
- Duct (for air or liquid conveyance)
