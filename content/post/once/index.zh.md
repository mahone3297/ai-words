## 读音
/ wʌns /

## 释义
- **副词**: 一次，曾经。例如: "I saw him once in New York."
- **连词**: 一旦，如果…的话。例如: "Once you start, you won't be able to stop."

## 短语
- "once upon a time" 意为“从前”，常用于故事开头。
- "once in a while" 意为“偶尔，有时”。

## 例句
- 副词: "I visited that museum once a few years ago."
- 连词: "Once you finish your homework, you can play video games."

- one time
- at one time
- ever
