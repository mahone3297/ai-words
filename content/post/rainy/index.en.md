## Pronunciation
/rɪˈneɪ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by the presence of rain or having frequent rainfall. For example: "The rainy season brings lush greenery to the region."
- **Noun**: Rainfall or wet weather. For example: "The children enjoyed playing in the puddles after a rainy day."
- **Adverb**: Describing the manner in which it rains. For example: "The rain fell heavily, drenching everything in its path."

## Phrases
- "It's been raining all day" describes continuous rain.
- "A rainy day" refers to a day with precipitation.
- "Under a rainy sky" implies a cloudy or wet atmosphere.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The garden looks vibrant after a recent rainy spell."
- **Noun**: "We decided to stay inside on such a rainy afternoon."
- **Adverb**: "The rain was coming down in torrents, making it hard to see."
