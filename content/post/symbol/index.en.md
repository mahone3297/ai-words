## Pronunciation
/səˈmɪb(ə)l/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A **symbol** is a mark, sign, or representation that stands for, represents, or expresses an idea, concept, or thing, often using a conventional or arbitrary association.
   - Example: The heart symbol (❤️) is a common representation of love.
2. In mathematics or logic, a **mathematical symbol** is a standardized notation used to represent operations, relations, or concepts.
   - Example: The plus (+) and minus (-) symbols for arithmetic operations.

### Verb
1. To **symbolize** or **represent** something through a symbol or gesture.
   - Example: The rainbow is a symbol of hope after a storm.

### Adjective
1. Characteristic of or using symbols.
   - Example: A symbolic painting.

## Phrases
- "Symbolic meaning" refers to the deeper significance of a symbol.
- "Alphabet symbols" denote letters in written languages.
- "Logo symbolism" refers to the meaning behind a company's logo.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: The ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs were a complex system of symbols.
- **Verb**: The Olympic rings symbolize unity among nations.
- **Adjective**: The intricate symbol on her necklace held great symbolic value to her.
