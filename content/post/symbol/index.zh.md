## 读音
/sɪmˈbɑːl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 象征；标志。例如: "The dove is a symbol of peace."
             2. 符号；记号。例如: "In mathematics, the plus sign (+) is a symbol for addition."

## 短语
- "Symbolic representation" 指的是用符号来表示或象征事物。
- "Alchemical symbols" 是炼金术中使用的各种符号。

## 例句
- 名词: "The red cross is a universal symbol of medical aid."
- 名词: "In chemistry, H2O is a symbol for water."
