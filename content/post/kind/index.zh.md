## 读音
/kɪnd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有好意的，仁慈的，宽容的。例如: "She is always kind to the new students."
- **名词**: 种类，类别。例如: "There are many different kinds of dogs."

## 短语
- "Kind-hearted" 意为心地善良的。
- "Of a kind" 表示同类的，某种类型的。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The teacher was kind enough to give us extra time for the assignment."
- 名词: "We can classify animals into several different kinds based on their characteristics."
