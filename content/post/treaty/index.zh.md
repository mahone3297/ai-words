## 读音
/ˈtriːti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 国际间正式签订的协议或条约，通常涉及政治、经济、军事等领域。例如: "The peace treaty ended the war between the two countries."
- **动词**: 结成条约，签订协议。例如: "They are treaty negotiations for a new trade deal."

## 短语
- "Treaty of Paris" 指的是1783年结束美国独立战争的条约。
- "International Treaty" 指国际间的条约或公约。
- "Treaty signing" 指签署条约的仪式。

## 例句
- 名词: "The Treaty of Tordesillas divided the New World between Spain and Portugal."
- 动词: "After months of talks, they finally treaty on a climate change agreement.
