## 读音
/streɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 压力，张力，拉力；紧张，疲劳。例如: "The bridge is under a lot of strain during heavy traffic."
- **名词**: 疲劳，劳累。例如: "I'm feeling quite a bit of physical strain after the marathon."
- **名词**: 压力，负担。例如: "Financial strains can cause a lot of stress in a family."
- **动词**: 使受力，拉紧。例如: "The rope strained when he tried to lift the heavy box."
- **动词**: 努力，竭力。例如: "She strained to hear what they were saying in the next room."

## 短语
- "under strain" 表示处于压力或紧张状态。
- "muscle strain" 指肌肉拉伤。
- "financial strain" 指经济压力。

## 例句
- 名词: "The bridge's structure must be able to withstand considerable strain."
- 动词: "She strained her back while lifting the sofa."
- 动词: "The athlete strained every muscle to reach the finish line first."
