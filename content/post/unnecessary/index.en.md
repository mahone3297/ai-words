## Pronunciation
/unˈnɛsərəri/

## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Meaning**: Not necessary; superfluous; lacking in need or purpose. For example: "The extra furniture was unnecessary in the small apartment."
- **Connotation**: Implies that something is dispensable or not required.

### Noun
- **Meaning**: An unnecessary thing or action. For example: "Removing that old rule was an unnecessary simplification."
- **Connotation**: Refers to something that is surplus or not needed.

## Phrases
- "An unnecessary expense" denotes a cost that isn't required.
- "There's no need for that" suggests something is unnecessary or redundant.
- "It's an unnecessary complication" implies that a situation has been made more complex than necessary.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The project was completed successfully without any unnecessary delays."
- **Noun**: "The team decided to remove all unnecessary tasks from their to-do list."
- **Adverb**: "She handled the situation with unnecessary caution."
