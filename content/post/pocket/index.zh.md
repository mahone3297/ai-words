## 读音
/pəˈkɑːt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 裤子或夹克上的小袋子，通常用来装小物品。例如: "I keep my keys in the pocket of my jeans."
    - 可以放入钱包的小型容器，如信封或小袋。例如: "She had a coin purse in her pocket."
    - 钱或财富的抽象概念。例如: "He has plenty of money in his pocket."

- **动词**:
    - 把东西放在口袋里。例如: "He pocketed the coins after finding them on the street."
    - 私吞或秘密保留（应归他人的钱或物品）。例如: "The dishonest cashier pocketed the cash from the till."
    - 击球运动中使球进入袋中，如台球。例如: "He pocketed the eight ball to win the game."

## 短语
- "In someone's pocket" 意味着某人受到另一个人的控制或影响。例如: "The politician was said to be in the tycoon's pocket."
- "Out of pocket" 指花费了金钱。例如: "After the trip, they were quite out of pocket."

## 例句
- 名词: "There's a hole in my pocket, and my phone fell out."
- 动词: "He pocketed the pen he found on the desk."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但可以表达惊讶或突然理解）: "Oh, hello there! I just realized you were talking to me!"
