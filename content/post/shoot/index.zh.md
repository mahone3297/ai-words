## 读音
/ʃuːt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 射击；发射：例如 "The hunter shot an arrow at the deer."
2. 拍摄（照片或影片）：例如 "They're shooting a new movie downtown."
3. 快速移动：例如 "The runner shot past the finish line."
4. 发送（信息等）：例如 "I'll shoot you an email later."

- **名词**:
1. 射击；发射：例如 "He took a long-range shot and scored a goal."
2. （篮球、足球等运动中的）投篮、射门：例如 "The striker made a powerful shot at the goal."
3. 照片或影片的拍摄：例如 "The director is happy with today's shoot."

## 短语
- "Take a shot" 意为尝试或试图做某事。例如: "I decided to take a shot at solving the problem."
- "On target" 表示射击准确或目标达成。例如: "His shot was right on target."

## 例句
- 动词: "The police officer shot the attacker in self-defense."
- 名词: "The golfer hit a perfect shot that landed in the hole."
