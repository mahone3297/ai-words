## 读音
/seɪz/

## 释义
- **动词**: 抓住；夺取；逮捕。例如: "The police seized the suspect."
- **名词**: 抓住的机会或行动。例如: "Seize the day and live life to the fullest."

## 短语
- "seize control" 意味着取得控制权。
- "seize the opportunity" 表示抓住机会。

## 例句
- 动词: "The soldiers were ordered to seize the enemy's headquarters."
- 名词: "This is your chance, seize it!"
