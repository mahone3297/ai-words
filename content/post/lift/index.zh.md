## 读音
/lɪft/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 电梯：一种垂直运输设备，通常用于建筑物内上下楼层之间。例如: "Take the lift to the tenth floor."
  - 提升，举起：将物体抬高或提升的动作。例如: "I need a lift to carry these heavy boxes."

- **动词**:
  - 抬起，举起：使物体离地面或某一位置。例如: "Lift your feet off the ground."
  - 乘电梯：使用电梯移动。例如: "Let's lift up to the penthouse."

- **短语**:
  - "Give someone a lift" 意为搭载某人一程，例如在汽车中。例如: "Can you give me a lift to the station?"
  - "Lift one's spirits" 意为鼓舞士气，提高情绪。例如: "Listening to music can lift one's spirits."

## 例句
- 名词: "The lift broke down, and we had to use the stairs."
- 动词: "He lifted the box with ease."
- 短语: "She offered to give me a lift to the airport."
