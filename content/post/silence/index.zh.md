## 读音
/ˈsaɪləns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 缺乏声音或安静的状态；无声。例如: "The library was filled with silence."
- **动词**: 使沉默，使安静。例如: "The teacher silenced the noisy students with a single gesture."
- **形容词**: 沉默的，无声的。例如: "The silent room allowed for deep concentration."

## 短语
- "Keep silence" 意为保持安静。
- "In silence" 表示在没有声音或寂静的环境中。
- "Silent treatment" 指故意不与某人交谈以表达不满或惩罚。

## 例句
- 名词: "The movie's opening scene was marked by complete silence."
- 动词: "The conductor silenced his orchestra before starting the performance."
- 形容词: "The silent night was only interrupted by the sound of crickets."

- Quiet

- Hush
