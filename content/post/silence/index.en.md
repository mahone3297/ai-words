## Pronunciation
/səˈlaɪəns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The absence of sound or noise; quietude. 2. A period of complete or respectful quiet; stillness. 3. [U] (uncountable) The act of remaining silent or not speaking, often as a sign of respect or disagreement. 4. [C] (countable) A moment of silence, usually observed in memory or respect.

- **Verb**: To remain quiet or not speak; to hold one's peace. 2. To make someone else stop talking by not responding.

## Phrases
- "Keep silence" implies not making any sound or talking.
- "Silence is golden" suggests that it's better to be quiet than to say something unwise.
- "Speak in silence" refers to communicating non-verbally, often through gestures or written means.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The library was filled with an eerie silence as everyone studied quietly."
- **Verb**: "After the teacher asked a question, there was a moment of silence before anyone answered."
- **Silent treatment**: "She gave her brother the silent treatment after he broke her favorite vase."
