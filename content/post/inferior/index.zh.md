## 读音
/ɪnˈfɪriər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 较差的，次等的，低于标准的。例如: "The inferior quality of the product led to customer complaints."
- **名词**: 下级，下属，较次的人或物。例如: "He was promoted and left his former position as an inferior."

## 短语
- "Inferior to" 比...低劣，不如...。例如: "Her work is inferior to his in terms of creativity."
- "Inferior products" 指的是质量较差的产品。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Don't settle for inferior materials when building your house."
- 名词: "The general treated his inferiors with respect."

## 同义词
- Lower
- Poorer
- Substandard
- Second-rate
- Less capable
