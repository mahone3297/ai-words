## Word: Inferior

## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈfɪəriər/

## Definitions
- **Adjective (adj)**: Lower in rank, quality, or status; less advanced, developed, or capable. For example: "The inferior product couldn't compete with its superior rivals."
  - "The economy class seats on this airline are inferior to those in business class."
- **Noun (n)**: A person or thing that is lower in rank, quality, or status than another. For example: "The company's new model is considered an inferior to its predecessor."
  - "In the hierarchy, he viewed his subordinates as inferiors."

## Phrases
- "Be inferior to": To be of lesser quality or importance compared to something else. Example: "Their technology is vastly inferior to ours."
- "Feel inferior": To have a sense of inadequacy or low self-worth due to perceived shortcomings. Example: "She often felt inferior to her colleagues because of her lack of experience."

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The counterfeit goods were clearly inferior in craftsmanship."
- **Noun**: "The management was accused of treating their inferiors disrespectfully."
