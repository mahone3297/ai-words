## 读音
/mɑːrˈdʒɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 边缘；边界：The book has a wide margin for notes.
   - 余地；空间：There's a small margin of error in the experiment.
   - （金融）保证金：He had to put up a margin to open a trading account.
   - （选举）优势；差距：She won the election with a comfortable margin.

- **动词**:
   - 在…周围留出边缘：Please margin the document before printing.
   - 给…留有余地：We should margin some time for unexpected delays.

## 短语
- "Safety margin" 意为安全余地或允许的误差范围。
- "Margin of error" 指统计调查中的误差范围。
- "Within margins" 表示在可接受的范围内。

## 例句
- 名词: "The designer left a generous margin around the text on the page."
- 动词: "She margins her notebook pages to make room for doodles."
- 金融用语: "The stock market closed with a narrow margin of gain."
