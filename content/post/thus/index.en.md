## Pronunciation
/ðʌs/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: 1. In that way; in such a manner. For example: "The project was completed thus."
- **Adverb**: 2. Therefore; accordingly. For example: "He studied hard, and thus did well on the exam."

## Phrases
- "Thus far" indicates up to this point in time or progress.
- "Thus spoken" is used to emphasize what someone said.
- "Thus it goes" is a colloquial expression meaning things generally proceed in a similar manner.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "He worked diligently, and thus, the task was finished ahead of schedule."
- **Adverb**: "The weather forecast predicted rain, and thus, we decided to stay indoors."

## Synonyms
- Consequently
- Therefore
- Hence
- Accordingly
- So

## Antonyms
- Contrarily
- Oppositely
- Otherwise
- Unconsequently (not a common synonym but implies not as a result)
