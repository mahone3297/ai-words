## 读音
/ðʌs/

## 释义
- **副词**: 这样，如此。表示结果或方式。例如: "He acted thus, showing his disapproval clearly."
- **连词**: 因此，所以。用于引出结果或推论。例如: "It was raining heavily; thus, we decided to stay home."

## 短语
- "Thus far" 意为到目前为止。
- "Thus said" 意思是这样说，如是说。

## 例句
- 副词: "The experiment was conducted, and thus, the results were obtained."
- 连词: "She was tired; thus, she went to bed early."

- Therefore
- Hence
