## 读音
/piːp/

## 释义
- **动词**: 轻轻地看一眼或听一下，通常表示秘密或窥探。例如: "She peeped through the keyhole to see what was happening inside."
- **名词**: 一小瞥或一短暂的声音。例如: "He caught a quick peep of the wildlife in the forest."
- **名词**: 小孩或小鸟的叫声。例如: "The baby bird let out a soft peep."

## 短语
- "give a peep" 意为快速看一眼或听一下。
- "at first peep of dawn" 表示天刚亮的时候。

## 例句
- 动词: "He tried to peep into the room without being noticed."
- 名词: "She heard a tiny peep from the nursery."
- 名词: "As soon as the sun made its first peep, the farmers started their day."
