## 读音
/pɑːrtnər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 伙伴，搭档；配偶，伴侣。例如: "We work closely with our business partners."，"She is my dance partner."
- **动词 (v)**: 与...合作，成为...的伙伴。例如: "He partnered her in starting the new company."

## 短语
- "Business partner" 商业伙伴，合作伙伴。
- "Life partner" 生活伴侣，通常指配偶或长期伴侣。
- "Law firm partner" 律师事务所合伙人。

## 例句
- 名词: "Our new project requires a reliable partner with expertise in marketing."
- 动词: "She decided to partner with a local artist for her upcoming exhibition."

## 同义词
- Companion
- Colleague
- Teammate
- Spouse
- Associate
