## Pronunciation
/partnər/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A **partner** is a person with whom one collaborates, co-owns, or works closely in a relationship, often in a professional or business context. For example: "They're running a successful startup as business partners."
- In a romantic relationship: "She found her soulmate and became his life partner."

### Verb
- To **partner** with someone means to form a partnership or team with them, often in a specific activity or project. For example: "The architects decided to partner on a new design project."
- To be a **partner** in a relationship: "They have been partners for five years."

### Adjective
- **Partnered** describes a relationship or association between individuals or organizations. For example: "The company is partnered with several local schools for community outreach."

## Phrases
- "Form a partnership" means to create a formal agreement or collaboration.
- "Act as a partner" implies taking on a supportive or cooperative role.
- "Partner up" is a colloquial way of saying to pair with someone for a task or activity.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The law firm has several long-standing partnerships with international clients."
- **Verb**: "The students were partnered in pairs for their group project."
- **Adjective**: "The university is partnered with leading tech companies for research initiatives."
