## Pronunciation
/sprɪŋˈtaɪm/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A season between winter and summer, characterized by warmer weather, blooming plants, and increased daylight. For example: "Springtime is a time of renewal and growth."
- **Adjective**: Relating to or denoting the season of spring. For example: "The springtime flowers were in full bloom."
- **Verb**: To bring about or usher in the spring season. For example: "Springtime arrives after a long, cold winter."

## Phrases
- "Enjoy the springtime" means to appreciate the pleasant weather and natural beauty during this season.
- "Spring cleaning" refers to the tradition of tidying up homes before the arrival of spring.
- "Spring forward" (verb) means to adjust clocks ahead to start Daylight Saving Time.
