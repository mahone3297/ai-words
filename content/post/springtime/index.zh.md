## 读音
/sprɪŋtaɪm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 春季，指一年中的早春时节，万物复苏的时期。例如: "Springtime is the season of new beginnings and blooming flowers."
- **形容词**: 春天的，与春天有关的。例如: "The springtime air was filled with the sweet fragrance of blooming lilacs."

## 短语
- "In the springtime" 指在春季期间。
- "Springtime weather" 指春天的气候。

## 例句
- 名词: "We love going for walks in the park during springtime to enjoy the beautiful scenery."
- 形容词: "Her springtime dress was adorned with delicate floral patterns."
