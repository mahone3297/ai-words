## 读音
/ˈreɪdi.eɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 辐射，指能量以电磁波或粒子的形式向外发射或传播。例如: "The sun emits harmful ultraviolet radiation."
   - 放射线，特指如X射线、伽马射线等高能电磁波。例如: "Radiation therapy uses controlled doses of radiation to treat cancer."

- **动词**:
   - 使辐射，发出辐射能量。例如: "The radioactive material radiates heat."

## 短语
- "Radiation therapy" 指使用放射线治疗疾病，特别是癌症。
- "Ionizing radiation" 是能够使原子或分子电离的高能辐射。

## 例句
- 名词: "Protective clothing is worn to shield workers from nuclear radiation."
- 动词: "The radioactive source radiated energy into the surrounding area."
