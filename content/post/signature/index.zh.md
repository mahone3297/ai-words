## 读音
/ˈsɪɡnətʃər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 签名，亲笔签名。例如: "Please sign your name in the space provided."  
   2. 特色，特征。例如: "Her paintings have a distinctive signature style."
   3. （音乐、艺术作品等的）标志，风格。例如: "That melody is the composer's signature tune."

- **动词**: 签名，签署。例如: "I signed the contract with my signature."

## 短语
- "Electronic signature" 指电子形式的签名，常用于电子邮件或文档。
- "Personal signature" 指个人独特的签名。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist put his signature in the corner of the painting."
- 动词: "Before sending the email, don't forget to add your electronic signature."

## 同义词
- Autograph
- Mark
- Handwriting
- Stamp (in the context of signing documents)
