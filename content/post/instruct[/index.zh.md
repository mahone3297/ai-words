## 读音
/inˈstrʌkt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 教导；指导。例如: "The teacher instructed the students on how to write an essay."
- **名词**: 指示；命令。例如: "Follow these instructions carefully."

## 短语
- "Give instructions" 意思是提供或给出指示。
- "Instruct someone in something" 指教某人如何做某事。

## 例句
- 动词: "The coach instructed the team on their defensive strategy."
- 名词: "The recipe included detailed instructions for baking the cake."
