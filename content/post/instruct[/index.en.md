## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈstrʌkt/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To give orders, directions, or information to someone on how to do something; to teach or train by giving lessons or orders. For example: "The teacher will instruct the students on how to solve complex math problems."
  - "She instructed her team on the proper use of the new software."
  
- **Noun**: (n, often in the form "instruction") The act of teaching or directing; guidance or commands given. For example: "Following the instructions carefully, he assembled the furniture."
  - "The employee manual contains all necessary instructions for new hires."

## Phrases
- "To take instructions": To receive and follow directions or orders.
- "Under instruction": Acting under the guidance or direction of someone else.
- "Instructions manual": A document that provides detailed information on how to operate or assemble a product.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The yoga instructor instructed us on the correct posture for each pose."
- **Noun**: "After reading the instructions, they were able to set up the tent without difficulty."
