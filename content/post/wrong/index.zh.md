## 读音
/wrɒŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不正确的，错误的。例如: "You've made a wrong turn here."
- **副词**: 错误地，不正确地。例如: "He answered the question wrongly."

## 短语
- "Do something wrong" 做错事，犯错误。
- "Something is wrong with..." 表示某事物有问题或不正常。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The instructions were wrong, so I couldn't assemble the furniture properly."
- 副词: "She evaluated the situation wrongly and made a bad decision."

- Incorrect
- Mistaken
- Faulty
- Erroneous
