## Pronunciation
/həˈləʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to greet or address someone, often informally. For example: "He gave me a friendly greeting."
- **Verb**: To express greeting or acknowledge someone's presence, typically with the word "hello". For example: "She greeted the guests warmly."
- **Adverb**: Informally, to express surprise or draw attention. For example: "Suddenly, hello, it's you!"

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to extend a greeting to someone.
- "Good morning, hello" is a common morning greeting.
- "Hello there, stranger" is a way to address someone you don't know.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her hello was so warm that it made me feel at home."
- **Verb**: "As he entered the office, he greeted his colleagues with a hello."
- **Adverb**: "Hello, what a pleasant surprise to see you here!"
