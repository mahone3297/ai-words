## 读音
/pəˈtɜːrn/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种模式、样式或布局，通常作为其他事物的模板或范例。例如: "The dress had a beautiful floral pattern."
- **动词 (v)**: 遵循或模仿某种模式或形式。例如: "She patterned her writing style after her favorite author."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 具有特定模式或结构的。例如: "The patterned rug added color and texture to the room."

## 短语
- "follow a pattern" 意为按照某种模式行事。
- "break the pattern" 指打破常规或既定模式。

## 例句
- 名词: "The wallpaper had a repeating geometric pattern."
- 动词: "The child was taught to pattern his behavior after his parents."
- 形容词: "The patterned curtains complemented the plain walls."
