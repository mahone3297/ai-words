## 读音
/pen/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种用于写字或绘画的工具，通常有可伸缩的尖端，里面装有墨水或颜料。例如: "I forgot my pen at home, so I had to borrow one from a friend."
- **动词 (v)**: 使用钢笔或其他类似的书写工具写字。例如: "She pens her thoughts in a journal every evening."

## 短语
- "Ballpoint pen" 指的是圆珠笔。
- "Sign your name with a pen" 意味着用笔签名。

## 例句
- 名词: "In the old days, people used quills as pens."
- 动词: "He pens a letter to his mother every week."

## 同义词
- Pencil
- Marker
- Crayon
- Writing instrument
- Quill (古代的羽毛笔)
