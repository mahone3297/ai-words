## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Characterizing or describing the likelihood or ability to do something, often with a sense of possibility or uncertainty. For example: "She might finish the project possibly today."
- **Adjective**: Expressing the quality of being possible or capable, but not necessarily certain. For example: "The weather forecast indicates a possibly rainy day tomorrow."

- "It's possibly a misunderstanding"
