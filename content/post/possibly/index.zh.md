## 读音
/ˈpɒsəbli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 可能地，也许，或许。表示不确定或可能性。例如: "It's possible that he'll be late." 
- **形容词**: 可能的，有希望的。常用于法律或正式语境。例如: "There is a possibly dangerous situation ahead."

## 短语
- "possibly not" 意味着可能不是或很可能不。
- "possibly even" 用来强调可能性，表示甚至可能会发生更进一步的事情。

## 例句
- 副词: "The weather forecast suggests it will rain tomorrow, but it's possibly just a chance of showers."
- 形容词: "The new treatment has shown possibly significant improvements in patients' health."
