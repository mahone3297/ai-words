## 读音
/ʌnˈbɜːrəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 无法忍受的；难以承受的。例如: "The noise was so unbearable that I had to leave the room."
- **副词**: 无法忍受地；非常讨厌地。例如: "She sang unbearable loudly, disturbing all the neighbors."

## 短语
- "unbearable pain" 极度痛苦
- "unbearable heat/cold" 难以忍受的热/冷

## 例句
- 形容词: "The smell from the garbage was unbearable, and we had to clean it up immediately."
- 副词: "The baby was crying unbearable, keeping everyone awake at night."
