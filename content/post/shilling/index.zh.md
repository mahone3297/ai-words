## 读音
/ˈʃɪlɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 英镑的旧辅币单位，等于1/20英镑。例如: "[In](/zh/post/in/) [the](/zh/post/the/) [old](/zh/post/old/) days, [goods](/zh/post/goods/) were [often](/zh/post/often/) priced [in](/zh/post/in/) shillings."

## 短语
- "[A](/zh/post/a/) [shilling](/zh/post/shilling/) [for](/zh/post/for/) [your](/zh/post/your/) thoughts" 意味着询问对方的想法或意见。
- "[Shilling](/zh/post/shilling/) [for](/zh/post/for/) [someone](/zh/post/someone/)" 指为某人做宣传或支持，有时带有贬义，暗示是有偿的。

## 例句
