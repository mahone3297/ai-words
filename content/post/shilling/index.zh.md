- "[A](/post/a/) [shilling](/post/shilling/) [for](/post/for/) [your](/post/your/) thoughts" 意味着询问对方的想法或意见。
- "[Shilling](/post/shilling/) [for](/post/for/) [someone](/post/someone/)" 指为某人做宣传或支持，有时带有贬义，暗示是有偿的。
