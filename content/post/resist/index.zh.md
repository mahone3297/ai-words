## 读音
/ˈrɪzɪst/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 抵抗；对抗。例如: "The soldiers resisted the enemy's attack."
  - 坚持不从；抵制。例如: "She resisted the temptation to eat the cake."
  - 对…有抵抗力；耐受。例如: "These materials resist heat and water."

## 短语
- "Resist the urge" 表示克制冲动或欲望。
- "Resist something with all one's might" 意味着竭尽全力地抵抗。

## 例句
- "Despite the pain, he refused to resist the police's efforts to arrest him."
- "She managed to resist buying another pair of shoes."
- "The old building's walls still resist the test of time."

## 同义词
- Oppose
- Withstand
- Defy
- Counteract
- Fight back
