## 读音

## 释义
- **动词**: 快速旋转；疾旋。例如: "The child was whirling around in circles."
- **名词**: 旋转的动作；漩涡。例如: "A whirl of leaves swirled around my feet."
- **名词**: 疯狂的活动或社交场合。例如: "They spent the evening in the whirl of the city's nightlife."

## 短语
- "in a whirl" 表示忙得团团转或思绪混乱的状态。例如: "I'm in a whirl trying to finish this project."
- "whirlpool" 指水或其他液体形成的漩涡。例如: "The bathtub drain created a small whirlpool."
