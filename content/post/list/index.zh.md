## 读音
/lɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一个项目或条目的清单。例如: "I made a list of things to buy at the store."
  - 列表，如数据结构中的列表。例如: "In Python, you can create a list by using square brackets."

- **动词**:
  - 把...列成单。例如: "List all the ingredients for the recipe."
  - 记录或列入。例如: "The movie was listed among the top 10 of the year."

## 短语
- "To be on the list" 意味着在某个名单或列表上。
- "Make a list and check it twice" 常用来提醒仔细核对所列事项。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please read the list and tick off each item as you complete it."
- 动词: "She listed her favorite books in her blog post."
