## 读音
/ˈkɔːrəkt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 正确的，准确的。例如: "Please check your answers and make sure they are all correct."
- **动词**: 修正，改正。例如: "I corrected the spelling mistakes in the text."
- **名词**: 改正，校正。例如: "The teacher gave him a correction for his wrong answer."

## 短语
- "Correct me if I'm wrong" 表示请求他人指出自己的错误。
- "To be on the correct side" 意为站在正确的一方。
