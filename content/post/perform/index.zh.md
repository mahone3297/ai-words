## 读音
/ˈpɜːrfɔrm/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 执行；实施。例如: "He performed his duties diligently."
2. 演出；展示。例如: "The dancers performed a breathtaking routine."
3. 实现；达成。例如: "The team performed above expectations and won the championship."

## 短语
- "perform a task" 完成任务。
- "perform on stage" 在舞台上表演。
- "perform under pressure" 在压力下表现。

## 例句
- 动词1: "She performs her daily tasks with great efficiency."
- 动词2: "The magician performed incredible tricks for the audience."
- 动词3: "The company performed well in the financial quarter, exceeding revenue targets."
