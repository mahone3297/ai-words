## 读音
/ˈmɜːrɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 值得赞扬的品质或优点；功绩，功劳。例如: "He was rewarded for his merits."
- **动词**: 应得，值得。例如: "She merits a promotion for her hard work."

## 短语
- "On its own merits" 意为根据其本身的价值来评判。
- "Meritocracy" 是一个名词，指的是基于个人才能和努力的社会或制度。

## 例句
- 名词: "The candidate's merits shone through in his outstanding performance."
- 动词: "Her dedication to the project merits recognition."

## 同义词
- Virtue
- Excellence
- Deservingness
- Worth
