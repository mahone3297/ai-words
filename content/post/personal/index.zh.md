## 读音
/pərˈsənl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 个人的，私人的，涉及个人特性的。例如: "This is a personal matter and should not be discussed publicly."
- **名词**: 个人助手，秘书。例如: "She works as his personal assistant."
- **名词**: 个性，人格。例如: "Each person has their own unique personal style."

## 短语
- "Personal belongings" 意为个人物品。
- "Personal space" 指的是个人的私人空间或领域。
- "Personal relationship" 指的是个人关系，如友谊或家庭关系。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Please keep your personal opinions out of the discussion."
- 名词: "She left her personal items in the hotel room."
- 名词: "Their personal connection made them great partners in business."
