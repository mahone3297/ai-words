## Pronunciation
/pɜːrˈsənl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. Characteristic of an individual; relating to one's private life, feelings, or habits. Example: "Her personal style is very unique."
   - 2. Belonging to a particular person; not shared or public. Example: "Keep your personal belongings organized."
   - 3. Addressed to or concerning a specific person. Example: "Send a personal message to your friend."

- **Adjective**: 1. Relating to a person's own; individual. Example: "Personal development is important for self-improvement."
   - 2. Confidential or private. Example: "Please keep this information personal."
   - 3. Characteristic of a person; distinctive. Example: "Her personal strengths include creativity and empathy."

## Phrases
- "Personal space" refers to the area around a person they consider private.
- "Personal life" encompasses one's non-professional activities and relationships.
- "Personal details" are intimate or private information about a person.

## Example Sentences
