## 读音
/ˈrɛləvənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 有关的，切题的，有意义的。例如: "Your previous work experience is highly relevant to this job position."
- **名词**: 相关性，关联。例如: "The study aimed to measure the relevance of the new marketing strategy."

## 短语
- "Relevance theory" 是关于语言理解和交际的一种理论。
- "Relevant information" 指与主题相关的重要信息。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The information provided was not only accurate but also highly relevant to our discussion."
- 名词: "We need to evaluate the relevance of these statistics before making a decision."
