## Pronunciation
/speɪd/

## Definitions
### Noun
A spade is a hand tool with a blade attached to a long handle, typically used for digging, moving soil, or gardening. It is one of the most common tools in a set of basic gardening equipment.

### Verb
To spade (often as 'spade in' or 'spade out') can mean to dig with a spade, usually for turning over soil, preparing the ground, or moving it from one place to another.

## Phrases
- "Spade through the dirt" means to dig deeply or thoroughly.
- "Use a spade to level the ground" is a common gardening instruction.
- "Get your hands dirty spading the garden" implies manual labor.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "He grabbed a spade to help with the construction project."
- **Verb**: "She spent the afternoon spading the flower bed to prepare for planting."
- **Imperative**: "Remember to spade in the compost before planting the tomatoes."
