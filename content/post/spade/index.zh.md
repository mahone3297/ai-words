## 读音
/speɪd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种园艺或挖掘工具，通常有长柄和金属的平头。例如: "He used a spade to dig a hole for the new tree."
- **牌名**: 在纸牌游戏中，黑色的梅花花色。例如: "He held three spades in his hand."

## 短语
- "Shovel or spade" 通常用来指代铲子或挖掘工具。
- "The suit of spades" 在扑克牌中指的是黑桃。

## 例句
- 名词: "She picked up the spade and started digging in the garden."
- 牌名: "In this deck, the ace of spades is the highest card."

## 同义词
- Shovel
- Trowel (小铲)
- Spade suit (在纸牌游戏中的黑桃花色)
- Gardening tool
- Digging tool
