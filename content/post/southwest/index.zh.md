## 读音
/səʊθˈwest/

## 释义
- **名词**: 地理方位，指正南和正西之间的方向。例如: "The house faces southwest."
- **形容词**: 指位于或朝向西南的。例如: "The southwest corner of the yard is sunny in the afternoon."

## 短语
- "Southwest Airlines" 是一家知名的美国航空公司。
- "southwesterly winds" 指从西南方向吹来的风。

## 例句
- 名词: "The sun sets in the southwest during summer evenings."
- 形容词: "The southwest region of the country has a desert climate."

## 同义词
- West-southwest (W-SW)
- South by west (SbW)
