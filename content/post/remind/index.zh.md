## 读音
/rɪˈmaɪnd/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 提醒，使想起。例如: "I need to remind myself to call the doctor tomorrow."
  - 通知，告诫。例如: "The teacher reminded the students about the upcoming test."

## 短语
- "Remind someone of something" 意为提醒某人某事。例如: "Can you remind me about the meeting later?"
- "Be a reminder of" 表示作为...的提醒。例如: "The old photo is a reminder of our childhood."

## 例句
- "Please remind me to buy milk on the way home." 
- "His message reminded me about the deadline."
- "The sound of the bell reminded us that it was time for lunch."

## 同义词
- Prompt
- Recall
- jog (one's memory)
- bring back
- remind oneself
