## Pronunciation
/rɪˈmɪnd/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A reminder** is something that helps you recall or remember something important. For example: "The calendar serves as a daily reminder of my appointments."
- **Remind (of)** is the act of bringing something back to someone's mind or memory. For example: "The sound of the piano reminded me of my childhood."

### Verb
- **To remind (sb.)** is to cause someone to remember or recall something. For example: "Please remind me to call the doctor tomorrow."
- **Reminded (sb.)** is the past tense of the verb, indicating that someone was reminded. For example: "She reminded me that I had left my keys at home."

## Phrases
- "Remind someone of something" means to jog their memory about a particular event or fact.
- "Remind me to do..." is a request for someone to help you remember a task.
- "Remind me when..." is used to ask for a reminder of a specific time or event.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The post-it note on my desk is a constant reminder to stay focused."
- **Verb**: "I'll remind you of the meeting time tomorrow morning."
- **Interjection**: "Oh, that reminds me, I need to pick up groceries after work."
