## Pronunciation
/rəˈθəl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or situated in a rural area; not in or belonging to an urban or town environment. For example: "The village has a largely rural population."
- **Noun**: A rural area or community. For example: "She grew up in a rustic, rural setting."
- **Adverb**: In a rural manner or style. For example: "He prefers a rural lifestyle away from the city noise."

## Phrases
- "Rural living" describes a lifestyle in the countryside.
- "Rural development" refers to initiatives aimed at improving conditions in rural areas.
- "From rural roots" indicates having origins in a rural background.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The rural landscape was dotted with small farms and forests."
- **Noun**: "Many people commute daily from the rural outskirts to the city center."
- **Adverb**: "She enjoyed her peaceful rural retreat after the hustle and bustle of the city."
