## 读音
/ˈrʊrəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 与乡村、农村或非城市地区相关联的。例如: "They live in a quiet rural area."
- **名词**: 在某些语境下，指乡村地区或农村生活。例如: "Rural life often involves farming and agriculture."

## 短语
- "Rural community" 指乡村社区。
- "Rural development" 涉及乡村地区的经济发展和改善。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The scenery was breathtakingly beautiful in the rural parts of the country."
- 名词: "The government has launched a program for rural development."
