## 读音
/ˈsæk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 大袋子，通常用来装物品。例如: "She put the potatoes in a burlap sack."
             2. 解雇，开除（尤指工人）。例如: "He was sacked from his job for being consistently late."

- **动词**: 1. 把...装入袋中。例如: "The children sack their toys into the storage box."
             2. 破坏，洗劫（如村庄、商店等）。例如: "The invaders sacked the city during the war."

## 短语
- "Sack out" 意味着睡觉或躺下休息。
- "Sack up" 可以表示鼓起勇气或采取行动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The farmer used a sack to carry his harvested wheat."
- 动词: "The company decided to sack several employees due to financial difficulties."
- 感叹词（非正式用法）: "Sack it, I've had enough of your complaints!"
