## 读音

## 释义
- **形容词**: 正确的，恰当的，适当的。例如: "Please use proper grammar in your essays."
- **名词**: 名字，专用名，特有的事物。例如: "She has a proper name for her business."
- **副词**: 完全地，十足地。例如: "The dog behaved properly at the dinner table."

## 短语
- "Do something properly" 意为正确地做某事。
- "In its proper context" 指在合适的情境下。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It's important to wear proper clothing for a job interview."
