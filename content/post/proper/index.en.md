## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Proper noun**: A specific name given to a person, place, or thing, usually capitalized, to distinguish it from others of the same type. For example: "New York is a proper noun."
2. **Proper adjective**: An adjective that modifies a noun, usually denoting a unique or distinctive quality, and is capitalized. For example: "The White House is a proper adjective referring to the official residence of the President of the United States."

### Adjective
1. Appropriate or fitting for a particular person, situation, or context. For example: "Wearing formal attire was proper for the wedding ceremony."
