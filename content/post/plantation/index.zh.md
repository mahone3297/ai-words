## 读音
/plənˈteɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大规模种植作物或树木的地方，尤指为商业目的而经营的农场或庄园。例如: "The plantation was known for its vast fields of cotton."
- **名词**: 特指历史上的美洲奴隶制种植园。例如: "During the 19th century, many slaves worked on Southern plantations."

## 短语
- "Cane plantation" 指专门种植甘蔗的种植园。
- "Coffee plantation" 指种植咖啡树的地方。
- "Tea plantation" 指种植茶叶的茶园。

## 例句
- "The old plantation house still stands at the heart of the sugar plantation."
- "Workers on the rubber plantation had to endure long hours in the hot sun."
