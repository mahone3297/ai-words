## 读音
/pleɪt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 盘子，通常用于盛放食物。例如: "She placed the roast beef on a large plate."
- **名词**: 金属板，尤指硬币或奖牌。例如: "The winner received a gold plate for their achievement."
- **名词**: 打印版，印刷版。例如: "The printer made a new plate for the revised edition."
- **名词**: 部分，板块。例如: "The continent is divided into several geological plates."
- **动词 (v)**: 在盘子上盛装（食物）。例如: "Can you plate these appetizers for the guests?"
- **俚语 (n)**: 账单，尤指餐馆的账单。例如: "Let me get the plate, please."

## 短语
- "on the plate" 意味着即将发生或面临的事情。例如: "I have a lot on my plate this week with work and family commitments."
- "plate up" 指将食物装盘准备上桌。例如: "The chef will plate up the dishes before serving them."

## 例句
- 名词: "The colorful salad was artfully arranged on the plate."
- 动词: "The waiter expertly plated the dessert with a dollop of whipped cream."
