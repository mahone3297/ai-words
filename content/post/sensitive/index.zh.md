## 读音
/ˈsɛnsətɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 敏感的；易受伤害的；有感觉的；灵敏的；对…有反应的。例如: "She is sensitive to other people's feelings." 
- **名词**: 敏感的人或事物。例如: "He has a sensitive nature."

## 短语
- "be sensitive about" 意为对…敏感或在意，例如: "She is very sensitive about her weight."
- "sensitive information" 指的是需要保密的信息。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The baby's skin is so sensitive that it easily gets irritated."
- 名词: "The artist is known for his sensitive portrayal of human emotions."
