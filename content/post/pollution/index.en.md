## Pronunciation
/pəˈluːʃn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: **pollution** (noun n) The presence or introduction of harmful substances or pollutants into the environment, causing harm to living organisms or ecosystems. For example: "Air pollution has led to severe health issues in the city."
- **Verb**: To contaminate or contaminate an area with pollutants. For example: "The factory is polluting the river with its waste."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by the presence of pollution; contaminated. For example: "The polluted water made it unsuitable for drinking."

## Phrases
- "Reduce pollution levels" refers to the effort to decrease the amount of pollutants.
- "Combatting air pollution" is a strategy to fight against atmospheric pollution.
- "Living in a polluted area" describes a situation where the surroundings have high levels of pollution.
