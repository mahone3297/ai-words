## 读音
/pəˈluːʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 污染；污染物。指对环境、空气、水、土壤等的有害物质或能量的引入，导致质量下降或健康风险增加。例如: "Air pollution is a major problem in many cities."
- **动词**: 使污染；弄脏。例如: "The factory was polluting the nearby river with toxic waste."

## 短语
- "Environmental pollution" 指的是对自然环境的污染。
- "Water pollution" 特指水体受到的污染。
- "Noise pollution" 是指噪音污染。

## 例句
- 名词: "The government has implemented new policies to combat air pollution."
- 动词: "Disposing of chemicals improperly can pollute the soil and groundwater."

- Poisoning
- Defilement
