## 读音
/ˈkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 集中，专注。例如: "He needs complete concentration to solve the puzzle."
- **动词**: 集中（注意力、精力等）。例如: "She concentrated on her studies and ignored all distractions."

## 短语
- "Concentration camp" 是指二战时期纳粹用来关押囚犯的地方。
- "High concentration" 指的是浓度高，如在溶液中。

## 例句
- 名词: "The athlete's success depends on his ability to maintain concentration during the game."
- 动词: "Before the exam, he closed his eyes and concentrated deeply to calm his nerves."
