## Pronunciation
/ˈmɪkstʃər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A combination or blend of different elements; especially a homogeneous combination of substances that do not react chemically with one another. For example: "The salad was a delicious mixture of fresh vegetables and fruits."
- **Noun (figurative)**: A blend or combination of diverse things, ideas, or people. For instance: "Their relationship was a complex mixture of love and conflict."

## Phrases
- "A mixture of emotions" implies a variety of feelings experienced simultaneously.
- "In a mixture of excitement and fear" describes a blend of two contrasting emotional states.
- "Oil and water don't mix" refers to substances that cannot form a homogeneous mixture.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (physical mixture)**: "The chemist carefully measured out the ingredients to create the perfect chemical mixture."
- **Noun (figurative)**: "The city is a cultural mixture of people from all over the world."
