## 读音
/ˈliːfənt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 军衔，少校，通常是军队中的中级军官。例如: "The lieutenant commanded the platoon."
- **名词**: 有时也指副手或助理。例如: "She was his right-hand lieutenant in the project."

## 短语
- "lieutenant general" 是将军军衔，高于少校。
- "lieutenant governor" 在某些州或国家中是副州长或副总督的职位。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new lieutenant took charge of the unit after the captain's departure."
- 名词: "In some states, the lieutenant governor assumes the role of the governor if the latter is unable to serve."

## 同义词
- Captain (上尉)
- Commander
- Second-in-command
- Assistant
- Deputy
