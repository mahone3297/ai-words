## 读音
/stroʊk/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 在艺术或设计中，一笔或一划的动作，尤指绘画或书法中的。例如: "The artist's deft brushstrokes captured the essence of the scene."
  - 心脏病发作或中风，指血液流动受阻导致的突发疾病。例如: "She survived a severe stroke last year."
  - 轻轻抚摸的动作，常表示爱抚或安慰。例如: "He gently stroked her hair as she cried."

- **动词**:
  - 轻轻地抚摸或拍打。例如: "She stroked the cat's fur."
  - （在比赛中）划动，如划船或游泳。例如: "The rower stroked rhythmically through the water."
  
## 短语
- "Give someone a stroke" 意为抚摸某人以示安慰。
- "Stroke of luck" 指意外的好运或幸运的机会。

## 例句
- 名词: "The painter used long, sweeping strokes to create the landscape."
- 动词: "He stroked the dog's head, making it feel comfortable."
- 感叹词（在某些固定表达中，如"stroke of genius"）: "That was a stroke of genius, solving the problem so quickly!"

## 同义词
- Brushstroke (名词时)
- Pat (动词时)
- Touch (动词时，有相似含义)
