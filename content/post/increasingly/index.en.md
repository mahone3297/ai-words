## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈkriːsɪŋlɪ/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a growing or progressive manner; by increasing degrees. It describes verbs, typically indicating that something is becoming more frequent, intense, or larger over time.

Example usage:

- **Describing a Verb**: "The demand for electric cars is increasingly high due to environmental concerns."
  - _In this sentence, "increasingly" modifies the adjective "high," emphasizing that the demand is rising more and more._

- **Modifying Adjectives**: "She found herself feeling increasingly anxious before exams."
  - _Here, "increasingly" intensifies the degree to which the person feels anxious._

## Phrases
- "Increasingly popular": Refers to something that gains popularity over time.
  - Example: "Electric bikes have become increasingly popular among urban commuters."

- "Increasingly important": Signifies that something is gaining significance with time.
  - Example: "Environmental protection has become increasingly important in global policy-making."

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb modifying a Verb**: "The need for renewable energy sources is increasingly pressing as fossil fuel reserves dwindle."
- **Adverb modifying an Adjective**: "His confidence grew increasingly throughout the season."

## Synonyms
- More and more
- Gradually
- Steadily
- Progressively
- Growingly
