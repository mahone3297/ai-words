## 读音
/inˈkriːsɪŋli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示逐渐地、日益地、不断增强地。用来强调一个动作或状态在逐步增加或改善。

## 短语
- "increasingly important" 越来越重要
- "increasingly popular" 日益受欢迎

## 例句
- "As technology advances, the need for cybersecurity is becoming increasingly crucial."
- "The company's profits have been increasing steadily, and they are now increasingly optimistic about their future prospects."
- "Young people are increasingly turning to plant-based diets for environmental reasons."

- progressively
- more and more
