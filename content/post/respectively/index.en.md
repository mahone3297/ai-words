## Pronunciation
/ˈrɛspɛktɪvli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Expressing the manner in which something is done or arranged; consecutively or separately. It is often used to indicate the order or sequence of items. 

## Phrases
- "Respectively speaking" introduces a list where each item is to be considered separately.
- "Each person did their task respectively" implies that each individual completed their job in their own designated manner.
- "Their ages are 25, 30, and 35 respectively" shows the ages in a specific order.

## Example Sentences
- "The winners were announced in alphabetical order, with the first place recipient, second place, and third place going respectively."
- "In our team, John is responsible for sales, Jane for marketing, and Peter for product development."
- "The doors will open at 9 AM, 10 AM, and 11 AM respectively for the conference."
