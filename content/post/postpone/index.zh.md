## 读音
/postpone/

## 释义
- **动词**: 推迟；延后。例如: "I've decided to postpone the meeting until next week."
- **名词**: 推迟的行为或决定。例如: "His decision to postpone the trip caused some inconvenience."

## 短语
- "Postpone indefinitely" 意味着无限期地推迟。
- "Postpone until a later date" 是指推迟到未来的某个日期。

## 例句
- 动词: "Due to bad weather, we had to postpone the picnic."
- 名词: "The announcement of the winner was a matter of postpone."

## 同义词
- Delay
- Adjourn
- Reschedule
- Put off
- Halt temporarily
