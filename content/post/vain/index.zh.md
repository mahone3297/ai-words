## 读音
/veɪn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 自负的，徒劳的，空虚的。例如: "His vain attempts to impress her only made him look foolish."
- **名词**: 自负，虚荣心。例如: "She was filled with a deep sense of vanity about her appearance."

## 短语
- "in vain" 意为徒劳无功，白费力气。例如: "They tried in vain to start the car."
- "vain glory" 指的是虚荣的荣耀或自豪感。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Despite his vain efforts, he couldn't solve the problem."
- 名词: "Her excessive concern with her appearance was a sign of her vain nature."

## 同义词
- Arrogant
- Pretentious
- Self-important
- Elitist
- Conceited
