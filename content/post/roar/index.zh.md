## 读音
/roʊr/

## 释义
- **动词**: 发出大声的吼叫或咆哮，通常指动物或机器的声音。例如: "The lion roared in the savannah."
- **名词**: 动物的吼声或类似的声音。例如: "I could hear the distant roar of the ocean."

## 短语
- "Roar of approval" 表示赞同或兴奋的欢呼声。
- "The engine let out a mighty roar" 指引擎发出巨大的声音。

## 例句
- 动词: "The tiger roared loudly when it saw its prey."
- 名词: "The crowd's roar drowned out the speaker's voice."

## 同义词
- Growl
- Scream
- Shout
- Bark (for dogs)
- Holler
