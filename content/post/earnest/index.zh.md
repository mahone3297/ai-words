## 读音
/ˈɜːrnɪst/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 真诚的，认真的，坚定的。例如: "He made an earnest request for help."
- **名词**: 认真，诚意，保证金。例如: "She put down an earnest payment for the house."

## 短语
- "in earnest" 表示认真地，严肃地开始。例如: "They started working in earnest on the project."
- "earnest money" 指购房或租赁时的保证金。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She spoke with an earnest expression, showing how much she cared about the issue."
- 名词: "The buyer paid a substantial earnest to secure the property."
