## 读音
/ˈpɛni/

## 释义
- **名词**: 英镑中的最小货币单位，1便士等于0.01英镑。例如: "I have a few pennies in my pocket."
- **名词**: 美元中的最小货币单位，1美分等于0.01美元（主要在美国使用）。例如: "The price is 59 cents, not 60."

## 短语
- "A penny for your thoughts" 意思是询问对方的想法或意见。
- "Not worth a penny" 表示毫无价值。

## 例句
- 名词: "She counted out the exact change, including two pennies."
- 美元表述: "The vending machine only accepts coins, so I had to find some pennies."

## 同义词
- Cent (美国)
- Penny (英国)
- Coin
- Fractional currency
- Small change
