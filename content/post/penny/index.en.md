## Pronunciation
/pəˈni/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A small, flat, circular coin with a raised edge, once widely used as the standard unit of currency in many countries, especially the United States. [**Etymology**: from Old French penic, from Latin penna, feather, referring to the coin's resemblance to a bird's feather](https://www.etymonline.com/word/penny).
- **Verb**: To pay a small amount, typically one penny, as a symbolic gesture or in jest. For example: "I'll just penny the fare for the taxi ride."

## Phrases
- "Pay a penny" or "Penny for your thoughts" are idiomatic expressions, respectively meaning to ask for a small sum or to inquire about someone's opinion.
- "Throwing in a penny" means making a small donation or contribution.
- "Penny-pinching" refers to being very frugal or吝啬 with money.
