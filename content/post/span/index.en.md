## Pronunciation
/spæn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 
  - The length or extent of something from end to end; a stretch or interval: "The book's span is 500 pages." (Measure)
  - The ability to cover a wide range or variety: "Her musical talents span multiple genres." (Ability)
- **Verb**: To extend or reach over a certain distance or period: "The bridge spans the river." (Action)

## Phrases
- "span of life" refers to the duration of a person's existence.
- "span of control" in management refers to the number of people who can be directly supervised by one person.
- "time span" denotes a duration or period of time.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The artist carefully measured the wall's span before deciding where to hang the painting."
- **Verb**: "The old oak tree's roots spanned the entire yard, anchoring it firmly in place."
- "Her career has spanned three decades, during which she's won multiple awards."
