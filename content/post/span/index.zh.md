## 读音
/span/

## 释义
- **名词**: 跨度；时间或空间的延伸部分。例如: "The bridge has a span of 500 meters."
- **动词**: 跨越；覆盖（一段距离或时间）。例如: "The eagle spanned the valley with its wings."
- **形容词**: 跨越特定范围的；广泛的。例如: "A span of generations in the family."

## 短语
- "the time span" 指一段时间跨度。
- "span an interval" 意为跨越一段间隔。
- "a wide span of topics" 表示涵盖广泛的主题。

## 例句
- 名词: "The old oak tree had a span of over 300 years."
- 动词: "The road construction will span three months."
- 形容词: "His knowledge covers a broad span of subjects."
