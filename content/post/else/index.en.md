## Word: Else

## Pronunciation
/els/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Different; other than what has been mentioned or specified. For example: "There's no one else in the room."
- **Pronoun**: Someone or something different; another person or thing. For example: "If you don't want to go, who else will?"
- **Adverb**: In addition; otherwise; under other circumstances. For example: "I have no choice; I must do this, or else suffer the consequences."

## Phrases
- "What else?" implies asking if there is anything additional or different.
- "Elsewhere" means in or to a different place; somewhere else.
- "If not... then what else?" is a question often posed when exploring alternative possibilities.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Do you have any other plans for the weekend, or is that it?"
- **Pronoun**: "She couldn't think of anyone else who could help her with the problem."
- **Adverb**: "Finish your homework now; you won't get to play video games otherwise."

## Synonyms
- Alternative (adjective)
- Another (adjective and pronoun)
- Otherwise (adverb)
- Different (adjective)
- Additional (adjective)
