## 读音
/əˈnælɪsɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 对事物的详细检查、研究或分解，以了解其结构、性质或功能。例如: "The scientist conducted a detailed analysis of the chemical compound."
   - 逻辑上的分解或推理过程。例如: "His analysis of the problem was both thorough and insightful."

## 短语
- "Data analysis" 意指对数据进行检查和解释，以提取有用信息。
- "Critical analysis" 指对某事物进行深入而批判性的评估。

## 例句
- "The company hired an expert to perform a financial analysis of their investment portfolio."
- "In her thesis, she provided a meticulous analysis of Shakespeare's use of symbolism in his plays."
