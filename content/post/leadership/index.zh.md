## 读音
/ˈliːdərʃɪp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 指导和管理一个团队、组织或国家的能力或角色。例如: "She demonstrated excellent leadership during the crisis."
- **动词**: 指领导、指挥或指导。例如: "He was chosen to lead the new project."

## 短语
- "Leadership skills" 领导能力
- "In the leadership of" 在...的领导下
- "Effective leadership" 有效的领导

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's success is largely due to its strong leadership."
- 动词: "He has been leading the team for over a decade."
