## 读音
/nekst/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 下一个的，紧接着的。例如: "The next person in line, please."
- **名词**: 接下来的事物，接班人。例如: "What's your plan for the next project?"
- **副词**: 紧接着，随后。例如: "I'll be with you in just a minute, next."

## 短语
- "the next day" 次日，第二天。
- "next in line" 排在下一个的，下一位。
- "next door" 隔壁，邻近的。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The next chapter will explore new ideas."
- 名词: "Who is the next speaker at the conference?"
- 副词: "We're meeting at the café, and then we'll go to the movie next."

## 同义词
- following
- succeeding
- upcoming
- coming
- subsequent
