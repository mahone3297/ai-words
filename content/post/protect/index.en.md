## Pronunciation
/prəˈtɛkt/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The act of protecting or the state of being protected from harm, damage, or injury.
   - Example: "The government implemented measures to protect the citizens from terrorism."
2. A person or thing that serves as a guardian or defender.
   - Example: "The shield was the protect of the knight in battle."

### Verb
1. To keep safe from harm, danger, or injury.
   - Example: "The parents worked tirelessly to protect their children's well-being."
2. To defend or support something or someone against attack or criticism.
   - Example: "The company is working to protect its intellectual property rights."

## Phrases
- "Protect oneself" refers to taking actions to safeguard one's own interests or safety.
- "Protect the environment" is a call to conserve natural resources and reduce pollution.
- "Protecting national security" is a government responsibility to safeguard a country from threats.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The wildlife sanctuary provides a protected habitat for endangered species."
- **Verb**: "The soldiers were trained to protect the border from illegal crossings."
- **Intransitive**: "The new technology aims to protect data from cyberattacks."
