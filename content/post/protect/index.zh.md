## 读音
/prəˈtekt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 保护；防御。例如: "The helmet protects your head during a fall."
- **名词**: （尤指法律、政策等的）保护；保护措施。例如: "The new law provides protect for wildlife habitats."

## 短语
- "protect against" 防止，保护免受。例如: "This antivirus software protects against malware."
- "in protection of" 为了保护。例如: "The soldiers stood in protection of the civilians."

## 例句
- 动词: "The sunscreen protects your skin from harmful UV rays."
- 名词: "The government has introduced new measures for consumer protect."
