## 读音
/riːd/

## 释义
- **动词**: 阅读，看（文字）。例如: "I spend my free time reading books."
- **名词**: 读物，阅读材料。例如: "The teacher assigned us a new read for next week."

## 短语
- "Read between the lines" 意味着理解未明说的意思。
- "Read aloud" 表示大声朗读。
- "A good read" 指一本有趣的或引人入胜的书。

## 例句
- 动词: "She reads the newspaper every morning over breakfast."
- 名词: "The novel is a fascinating read with unexpected twists."
