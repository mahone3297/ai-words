## 读音
/rɪˈzəmbl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 类似，像，与...相像。例如: "Their personalities resemble each other in many ways."
- **名词**: 相似之处，类似物。例如: "There are remarkable resemblances between their works."

## 短语
- "resemble someone/something closely" 表示非常相似。
- "to resemble someone in something" 指在某个方面像某人。

## 例句
- 动词: "The twins resemble each other so much that people often mistake them for one another."
- 名词: "The painting showed a remarkable resemblance to the actual scene."

## 同义词
- look like
- bear a resemblance to
- be similar to
- have a likeness to
- correspond to
