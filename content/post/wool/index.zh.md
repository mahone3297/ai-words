## 读音
/woʊl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 织物或衣物的一种材料，由羊毛或其他动物毛发制成。例如: "She was wearing a thick wool sweater on a cold day."
- **名词 (n)**: 羊毛，羊的细毛。例如: "The sheep's wool is harvested annually for textile production."

## 短语
- "Woolen garments" 指的是羊毛制成的衣服。
- "Wool spinning" 是指将羊毛纤维纺成纱线的过程。

## 例句
- 名词: "The scarf is made of pure wool and provides excellent insulation."
- 名词: "The farmer sheared the sheep to get the wool for making blankets."
