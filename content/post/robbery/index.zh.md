## 读音
/ˈrɑːbəri/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 公然抢夺或盗窃的行为，通常涉及暴力或威胁使用暴力。例如: "There was a robbery at the bank last night."
- **动词 (v)**: 抢劫，非法占有他人的财物。例如: "The thief robbed the store at gunpoint."

## 短语
- "armed robbery" 指持械抢劫。
- "bank robbery" 银行抢劫。
- "robbery attempt" 抢劫未遂。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police are investigating a series of robberies in the area."
- 动词: "He was arrested for robbing a convenience store."
