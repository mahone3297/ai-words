## 读音
/rʊlər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 尺子，直尺。例如: "Use the ruler to draw a straight line."
   2. 统治者，领导者。例如: "The queen is the ruler of the kingdom."

## 短语
- "ruler of the land" 指土地的统治者。
- "a measuring ruler" 指用于测量的尺子。

## 例句
- 名词: "The students used plastic rulers to measure the length of the desk."
- 名词: "The ruler of the small country maintained peace for many years."
