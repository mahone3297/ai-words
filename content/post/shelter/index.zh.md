## 读音
/ˈʃɛltər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 提供遮蔽或保护的地方。例如: "They found shelter from the rain under a large tree."
- **动词 (v)**: 为...提供庇护或保护。例如: "The charity helps to shelter homeless people during winter."

## 短语
- "to take shelter" 意为寻求避难或躲避。
- "sheltered accommodation" 指的是提供安全和保护的住所。

## 例句
- 名词: "The refugees sought shelter in a nearby church."
- 动词: "The community center is sheltering families affected by the storm."

- refuge
- protection
- haven
- sanctuary
