## 读音
/strɪp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 裸露的状态或部分；条纹：例如 "He removed his shirt, leaving him in just a pair of shorts and a strip of skin."
  - 一排或一行（物体）：例如 "The houses were built in a neat row along the street, forming a long strip."

- **动词**:
  - 去除（衣物、涂层等）：例如 "She stripped the wallpaper off the walls."
  - 剥夺（权利、身份等）：例如 "The scandal led to the politician being stripped of his title."

- **形容词**:
  - 有条纹的：例如 "The zebra has distinctive black and white striped fur."

## 短语
- "strip down" 意味着脱掉衣服或拆解物品。
- "strip search" 是指在法律允许的情况下对个人进行彻底搜身。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist painted a beautiful sunset with orange and pink strips across the sky."
- 动词: "He carefully stripped the wires before connecting them to the power source."
- 形容词: "The zebra's stripes make it difficult for predators to single it out in a herd."
