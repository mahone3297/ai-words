## 读音
/spaɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 秘密探子，间谍。例如: "The government arrested a spy who had been working for the enemy."
- **动词**: 刺探，暗中监视。例如: "They were accused of spying on their neighbor's activities."

## 短语
- "spy on someone" 意味着秘密监视某人。
- "be a spy for" 为某个组织或国家充当间谍。

## 例句
- 名词: "The movie is about a top-secret agent who works as a spy for the CIA."
- 动词: "The police suspected him of spying for a rival company."
