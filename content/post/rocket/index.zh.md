## 读音
/ˈrɑːkɪt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种能够高速飞行的火箭，通常用于太空旅行、军事或科研目的。例如: "The satellite was launched into orbit by a powerful rocket."
- **名词 (n)**: 在某些语境下，也可以指快速上升或突然成功的事物。例如: "Her career took off like a rocket after winning the award."

## 短语
- "Rocket science" 指非常复杂或高深的学科，尤其是航天工程。
- "Rocket fuel" 用于推动火箭的燃料，比喻极度的热情或能量。

## 例句
- 名词: "The space agency is developing a new generation of rockets for deep space exploration."
- 名词: "His startup became a rocket, experiencing exponential growth in just a few years."
