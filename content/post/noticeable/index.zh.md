## 读音

## 释义
- **形容词**: 易于察觉的，显著的，明显的。例如: "The change in his behavior was noticeable to everyone around him."
- **副词**: 显著地，明显地。例如: "Her improvement was noticeable from one game to the next."

## 短语
- "noticeable difference" 表示有显著的差别或变化。
- "a noticeable improvement" 意味着明显的进步或提升。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The difference in quality between the two products is noticeable."
