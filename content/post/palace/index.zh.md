## 释义
- **名词**: 大型宏伟的住宅，通常是王宫或贵族的府邸。例如: "The Buckingham Palace is the official residence of the British monarch."
- **名词**: 常比喻为权力或权威的中心。例如: "The company's headquarters is often referred to as the 'palace' by its employees."

## 短语

- "palace gardens" 指宫殿周围的花园。
