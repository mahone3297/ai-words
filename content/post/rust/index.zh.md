## 读音
/rʌst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 铁锈，氧化物，通常指金属表面因氧化而形成的红褐色物质。例如: "The old metal gate had a thick layer of rust."
- **动词**: （使）生锈，腐蚀。例如: "Untreated iron will rust quickly in humid conditions."
- **形容词**: 靠近或处于自然状态，未经使用的。例如: "The rustic cabin was surrounded by dense forest."

## 短语
- "Rust-proofing" 指防止金属生锈的处理。
- "Rust-colored" 形容具有铁锈色的。

## 例句
- 名词: "The antique sword had some rust spots that needed to be removed."
- 动词: "The exposed metal parts of the car started to rust after a few months."
- 形容词: "The furniture had a beautiful, rustic charm."
