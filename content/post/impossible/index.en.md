## Pronunciation
/ɪmˈpɑːsəbl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Not able to occur, exist, or be done; not capable of being accomplished, achieved, or fulfilled under any circumstances. For example: "It's impossible to travel faster than light."

### Example Sentences
- "The task seems impossible without additional resources."
- "They thought it was impossible to climb that mountain until Sir Edmund Hillary proved them wrong."
- "It's impossible for her to attend two meetings at the same time."

## Phrases
- "An impossible dream": A goal or aspiration that is considered unattainable.
- "To do the impossible": Accomplishing something that others have deemed unachievable.
- "Impossible odds": Describes a situation where the chances of success are extremely low.
