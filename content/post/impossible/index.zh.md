## 读音
/ɪmˈpɑːsəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不可能的，无法做到的，难以实现的。例如: "It's impossible to finish this task in one day."
- **名词**: 不可实现的事物或无法解决的问题。例如: "The problem seems impossible to solve."

## 短语
- "Something is next to impossible" 表示几乎不可能。
- "Impossible standards" 指的是过高或不切实际的要求。

## 例句
- 形容词: "With this workload, finishing the project on time is practically impossible."
- 名词: "The impossible task of predicting the future accurately has always intrigued scientists."
