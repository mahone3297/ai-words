## Pronunciation
/ˈɪnstənt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A very short period of time; an instant moment. For example: "The accident happened in the blink of an instant."
- **Adjective**: Occurring or acting without any delay; immediate. For example: "Instant coffee dissolves quickly in hot water."
- **Noun (informal)**: A single serving or packet of a product that can be prepared immediately, such as instant noodles or soup. For example: "I just need to boil some water for the instant ramen."

## Phrases
- "In an instant": This phrase means something happens very quickly or suddenly. For example: "The room filled with laughter in an instant."
- "At your instant command": Meaning something is ready or available to be used immediately upon request. For example: "With this smart device, all your favorite songs are at your instant command."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The camera captured the exact instant the lightning struck the tree."
- **Adjective**: "She had an instant dislike for the new neighbor."
- **Noun (informal)**: "After a long day, he opted for an easy dinner of instant mashed potatoes."
