## 读音
/ˈɪnstənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 瞬间；片刻；立即；速溶（咖啡等）。例如: "He took an instant liking to the painting." 
- **形容词**: 立即的；速成的；即时的。例如: "The instant coffee tastes almost like the real thing."
- **副词**: 立即地；马上。例如: "She responded instantly when I called her name."

## 短语
- "in an instant" 意为立刻，瞬息间。例如: "The situation changed in an instant."
- "instant messaging" 即时通讯，常用于描述在线聊天软件。

## 例句
- 名词: "She ordered an instant coffee to save time."
- 形容词: "The new app provides instant access to news updates."
- 副词: "As soon as he saw the danger, he acted instantly."
