## 读音
/kwɪz/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一次小测验或知识竞赛，通常在学校或娱乐活动中进行。例如: "We have a history quiz tomorrow."
- **动词**: 进行小测验或询问以检验某人的知识。例如: "The teacher quizzed us on last week's lesson."
- **形容词**: 有关测验的或与测验相关的。例如: "Quiz night at the local pub is always popular."

## 短语
- "Take a quiz" 意味着参与或完成一个测验。
- "Quiz show" 是一种电视或广播节目，其中参赛者回答问题来赢得奖品。

## 例句
- 名词: "The weekly trivia quiz at the pub challenges our general knowledge."
- 动词: "The professor quizzed the students on their understanding of the complex concept."
- 形容词: "The quiz questions covered a wide range of topics."
