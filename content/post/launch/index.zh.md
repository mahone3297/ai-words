## 读音
/ˈlɔːntʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 发射；启动（如火箭、飞船或新产品）。例如: "The spacecraft was successfully launched into orbit."
  2. 开始；发起（活动、计划或事业）。例如: "They launched a new marketing campaign."
  3. 使船下水。例如: "The ship was launched in a grand ceremony."

- **名词**:
  1. 发射；启航。例如: "The satellite launch was scheduled for dawn."
  2. 新产品的发布或推出。例如: "The company's latest product launch received great media attention."

## 短语
- "launch into" 开始讲述或讨论。例如: "He launched into a passionate speech about climate change."
- "launch a career" 开始职业生涯。例如: "After graduating, she launched her career in finance."

## 例句
- 动词: "The company is set to launch its new smartphone next month."
- 名词: "The rocket launch was visible from miles away."
