## 读音
/ˈpɛbəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 微小的圆形石头，通常光滑，有时用于装饰或作为珠宝的一部分。例如: "The child was playing with a pile of pebbles by the riverbank."
- **动词**: 轻轻投掷小石子。例如: "The children were pebbling at the ducks in the pond."

## 短语
- "pebble beach" 指布满小石子的海滩。
- "pebble watch" 指一种智能手表品牌。

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist used various shades of pebbles to create a beautiful mosaic."
- 动词: "The mischievous boy was pebbling at the window, causing a disturbance."

## 同义词
- gravel (名词)
- stone (名词)
- toss (动词，指投掷行为)
- fling (动词，指快速投掷)
