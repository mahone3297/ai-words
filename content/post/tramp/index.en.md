## Pronunciation
/træmp/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A vagrant or itinerant person, often living on the streets and traveling from place to place. 2. A person with a carefree, bohemian lifestyle. 3. (informal) A person who behaves in a disorderly or irresponsible manner. 4. (US, informal) A military train carrying supplies or troops.

## Verb**: To travel irregularly, often without a fixed home, as a tramp. To wander aimlessly. 

## Adjective**: Characterized by or indicative of a tramp's lifestyle; hobo.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The tramp's belongings consisted of a small backpack and a worn-out guitar."
- **Verb**: "After his divorce, he decided to hit the road as a tramp, seeking adventure."
- **Adjective**: "The tramp-like lifestyle was both appealing and challenging to him."

## Phrases
- "Living like a tramp" implies a life without stability or material possessions.
- "Tramp around" means to walk aimlessly or wander.
- "Tramp through the woods" describes walking over rough terrain.
