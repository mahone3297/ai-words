## 读音
/ɪˈlɪ斯特.eɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    1. 举例说明；图解；阐明。例如: "The book illustrates various techniques for painting."
    2. 绘制插图；为…作插图。例如: "She was hired to illustrate the children's book."

## 短语
- "illustrate a point" 解释或证明一个观点。
- "illustrated edition" 有插图的版本。

## 例句
- "The diagrams in the textbook effectively illustrate the process of photosynthesis."
- "The artist was asked to illustrate the novel with her unique style."
- "His speech was enriched by numerous examples that illustrated his argument."

## 同义词

- explain
