## 读音
/ˈtɔːrtʃər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 极度痛苦；折磨；拷问；摧残。例如: "The prisoner was subjected to torture to extract information."
- **动词**: 使受痛苦；折磨；拷问。例如: "They were accused of torturing the suspects to get a confession."

## 短语
- "Torture someone into doing something" 意味着通过折磨迫使某人做某事。
- "Under torture" 表示在遭受折磨的状态下。

## 例句
- 名词: "The use of torture is condemned by international law."
- 动词: "The interrogators tortured him for hours, but he didn't reveal anything."
