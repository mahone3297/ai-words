## 读音
/riːɡn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 统治，执政。例如: "During his reign, the country experienced prosperity."
    - 时代，时期。例如: "The reign of Queen Elizabeth II has been long and prosperous."
    - 王权，君主政体。例如: "The idea of absolute reign has diminished over time."

- **动词**:
    - 统治，当政。例如: "She reigned over the kingdom for 50 years."
    - 在某领域占主导地位。例如: "His music reigns supreme in the charts."

## 短语
- "reign of terror" 恐怖统治时期，如法国大革命时期的"Reign of Terror".
- "reign supreme" 占据支配地位，处于最高位置。例如: "Her talent reigns supreme in the field of art."

## 例句
- 名词: "The reign of King Henry VIII was marked by religious turmoil."
- 动词: "After winning the election, he began to reign as the new president."
- 副词: "In the world of fashion, her designs have been reigning for decades."
