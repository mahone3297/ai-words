## Pronunciation
/rɪˈɡret/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A feeling of sorrow, disappointment, or remorse for a mistake, loss, or missed opportunity: *I regret not studying harder for the exam.*
2. A sense of regret or unhappiness about something that has happened or been done: *She regrets her decision to quit her job.*

### Verb
1. To feel or express remorse, sorrow, or disappointment over something one has done or failed to do: *I deeply regret my actions in that situation.*
2. To wish or desire something had been different or hadn't occurred: *He regrets not taking that chance.*

### Adjective
1. Expressing regret or remorse: *Her regretful expression showed how sorry she was.*

## Phrases
- "Feel no regret" means to have no remorse or guilt.
- "Regretting the past" is when you look back with disappointment.
- "Live without regret" is a goal to lead a life free from remorse.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She held back tears, filled with regret for her past mistakes."
- **Verb**: "He deeply regrets not telling her the truth sooner."
- **Adjective**: "The regretful look on his face conveyed his sorrow."
