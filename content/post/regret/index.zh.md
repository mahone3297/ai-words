## 读音
/ˈrɛɡrɛt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 对过去的行为或决定感到遗憾或懊悔。例如: "I regret not studying harder for the exam."
- **名词**: 遗憾，懊悔，对过去行为的后悔之情。例如: "She had many regrets about her decision to quit her job."

## 短语
- "have no regrets" 意味着对所做的决定或行动没有遗憾。
- "live with regret" 指的是带着遗憾生活，无法改变过去的事情。

## 例句
- 动词: "I deeply regret my actions that hurt you."
- 名词: "He carried the regret of never telling his father he loved him."

- Repent
- Regretfully
- Bemoan

- Mourn over
