## 读音
/ˈʌpwərdz/

## 释义
- **副词**: 向上，朝上；在上面，向上方。例如: "The bird flew upwards towards the sky."
- **形容词**: 向上的，上升的；朝着上方的。例如: "The upwards trend in sales is encouraging."

## 短语
- "upwards of" 表示“多于，超过”。例如: "There were upwards of 100 people at the event."

## 例句
- 副词: "The ladder leaned upwards against the wall."
- 形容词: "The upwards movement in the stock market indicates growth."
