## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Referring to movement or direction in an upward manner. For example: "The arrow pointed upwards."
- **Preposition**: Expressing direction or location relative to up. For example: "Climb upwards the ladder."
- **Adjective**: Describing something moving or situated above or higher than another point. For example: "The sun sets in the west, while the light shines upwards."

## Phrases
- "Move upwards" indicates a direction of rising or ascending.
- "Upwards and onwards" signifies progress or advancement.
- "Look upwards" can mean to gaze skyward or reflect on something higher.
