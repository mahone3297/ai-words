## 读音
/ˈnɛsəsəti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 必要性，必需品，需要的东西。例如: "Food and shelter are necessities for survival."
- **形容词**: 必要的，必需的，必不可少的。例如: "It is a necessity that you learn to swim if you live near the ocean."

## 短语
- "A matter of necessity" 表示“必要的情况”。
- "Necessity is the mother of invention" 这是一句谚语，意思是“需求是发明之母”。

## 例句
- 名词: "In tough times, people often have to distinguish between wants and necessities."
- 形容词: "Learning a second language has become a necessity in today's globalized world."

## 同义词
- Requirement
- Need
- Essential
- Indispensable
- Compulsory
