## 读音
/riːəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 真实的，实际的，非虚构的。例如: "This is a real-life story."
- **副词**: 确实地，真正地。例如: "She works really hard to achieve her goals."

## 短语
- "For real" 表示确信或强调某事是真的。例如: "Are you serious? For real?"
- "The real deal" 指事物的本质或真正有价值的部分。例如: "He's the real deal when it comes to playing guitar."

## 例句
- 形容词: "We need to face the real problems in our society."
- 副词: "I'm really looking forward to our vacation."
