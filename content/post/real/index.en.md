## Definitions
### Noun
- **Real:** 1. Existing or actual; genuine, not imaginary or pretended. Example: "The real value of a house depends on its location."
- 2. Of high quality or standard; authentic. Example: "She bought a real diamond necklace for her wedding."
- 3. Concerning or involving reality; realistic. Example: "The movie portrayed a real-life story."

### Adjective
- **Real:** 1. True or accurate; genuine. Example: "Her description was a real account of the event."
- 2. Actual or factual. Example: "The real reason for his absence was a family emergency."
- 3. Not artificial or synthetic. Example: "Real flowers last longer than silk ones."

### Adverb
- **Really:** 1. To an extent; truly. Example: "I really enjoyed the concert."
- 2. In fact; actually. Example: "She's really good at playing the piano."

## Phrases

- "Not real" can mean fake or illusory.
