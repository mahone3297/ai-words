## 读音
/prɒmpt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 提示；催促。例如: "The actor needed a prompt from the stage manager."
2. （计算机）提示；指令。例如: "The computer asked for a user prompt before proceeding."

- **动词**: 1. 促使；激励。例如: "Her curiosity prompted her to investigate further."
2. 在演出中给演员提词。例如: "The prompter stood by to prompt the actors if they forgot their lines."

## 短语
- "Prompt reply" 意为迅速回复。
- "On prompt" 表示立即或准时。

## 例句
- 名词: "The software displays a prompt asking for your username and password."
- 动词: "His concern prompted him to call the doctor immediately."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但在某些语境下可理解为引起注意）: "Hello? Can you hear me?"
