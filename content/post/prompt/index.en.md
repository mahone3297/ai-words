## Pronunciation
/həˈləʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A term used to greet or address someone. Often used informally to initiate communication or acknowledge someone's presence. For example: "He gave me a friendly wave and a hearty hello."

### Verb
To express greeting or acknowledgement by saying "hello" or using other similar expressions. For example: "She greeted the new employee with a cheerful hello."

### Adjective
Characterizing something as being friendly or cordial, typically used to describe a greeting. For example: "A warm hello from across the street."

### Adverb
In the form of "hello," used to introduce a statement or question, often to get someone's attention. For example: "Hello, could you pass the salt, please?"

## Phrases
- "Hello, my friend" - A formal or informal way to address a friend.
- "Hello, and welcome" - Greeting someone who has just arrived.
- "Hello, old pal" - Informal way of addressing an acquaintance or friend.
