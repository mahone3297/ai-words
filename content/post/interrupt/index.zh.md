## 读音
/ɪnˈtɜːrˌprɛt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 解释，说明（言语、行为、艺术作品等）的意思。例如: "Could you interpret this poem for me?"
  - 翻译（从一种语言到另一种语言）。例如: "She works as an interpreter at the United Nations."

## 短语
- "Interpret something literally" 指按照字面意思理解。
- "Interpretive dance" 是一种表达情感或故事的舞蹈形式。
- "Legal interpretation" 指法律条文的解释。

## 例句
- "The expert interpreted the ancient hieroglyphs on the wall."
- "She interpreted the song's lyrics in a very emotional way during her performance."
- "The interpreter translated the speech from French to English in real-time."
