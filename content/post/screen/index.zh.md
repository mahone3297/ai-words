## 读音
/skriːn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 显示器，屏幕：如 "He's working on his computer screen."
  - 银幕：如 "The movie will be shown on a giant outdoor screen."
  - （电话或广播的）通话界面：如 "I need to put you on hold for a moment while I screen the incoming calls."
  - （防护或遮蔽物）：如 "The plants are behind a screen of bamboo."

- **动词**:
  - 过滤，筛选：如 "We screened the applicants to find the most qualified candidates."
  - 在（电影、节目等）公映前预览：如 "The film was screened at a private event before its official release."
  - （在电话中）接听并决定是否接通：如 "She screens her calls during meetings."

## 短语
- "on the screen" 指在显示器或银幕上显示。
- "screen off" 用于隔绝或隐藏：如 "Please screen off the area so we can have some privacy."

## 例句
- 名词: "The presentation will be projected onto a large screen."
- 动词: "The HR department is screening resumes for the job opening."
- 感叹词: "In old movies, they would often say 'Hello, Screen!' when addressing the camera."

## 同义词
- Monitor (名词时)
- Filter (动词时)
- Projection (名词时，尤其指银幕)
- Shield (名词时，作遮蔽物之意)
