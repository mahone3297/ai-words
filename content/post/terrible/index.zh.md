## 读音
/ˈtɛrəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 极端的，非常糟糕的，可怕的。例如: "The weather is terrible today."
- **副词**: 非常，极其。例如: "She did terribly on her exam."

## 短语
- "in terrible condition" 指状态非常差。
- "terrible mistake" 指严重的错误。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The traffic was terrible this morning, I was late for work."
- 副词: "He played terribly in the match, missing several easy shots."
