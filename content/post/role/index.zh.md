## 读音
/roʊl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 
    - 在戏剧、电影或生活中扮演的角色或身份。例如: "She played the role of a queen in the play."
    - 任务或职责。例如: "His role in the project was to manage the budget."
    - 角色、功能或地位。例如: "The teacher's role is to educate and guide students."

- **动词 (v)**: 
    - 扮演角色。例如: "She will be roleplaying a detective in the workshop."

## 短语
- "play a role in something" 意为在某事中起作用或扮演角色。例如: "Climate change plays a significant role in global warming."
- "take on a role" 意为接受或承担一个角色。例如: "He has decided to take on the role of team leader."

## 例句
- 名词: "He prepared thoroughly for his new role as CEO."
- 动词: "She eagerly roleplayed different scenarios to practice her negotiation skills."
