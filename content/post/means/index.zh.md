## 读音
/meɪnz/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 方法，手段；工具。例如: "She used different means to achieve her goal."
  - 意义，含义。例如: "The word 'love' has many different means in various contexts."

- **动词**:
  - 意味着，表示。例如: "His silence means he is upset."
  - 能够，足以。例如: "I mean to say that we can solve the problem."

## 短语
- "By all means" 表示同意或允许。例如: "By all means, go ahead with your plan."
- "Means of transportation" 交通工具。例如: " Cars are a common means of transportation in cities."

## 例句
- 名词: "The company invested in new technology as a means to increase efficiency."
- 动词: "His smile means he's happy to see you."
