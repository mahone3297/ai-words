## 读音
/ˈdʒʌŋɡl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 茂密的丛林，尤指热带雨林。例如: "The Amazon rainforest is a vast jungle filled with diverse wildlife."
  - 混乱无序的状态，比喻复杂的环境或情况。例如: "The office was in a jungle of paperwork."

## 短语
- "Lost in the jungle" 意为在丛林中迷路。
- "Corporate jungle" 比喻竞争激烈的商业环境。

## 例句
- 名词: "We hired a guide to help us navigate through the dense jungle."
- 比喻: "The startup world can be a ruthless jungle, where only the strongest survive."
