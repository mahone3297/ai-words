## Pronunciation
/ʌnˈiːzɪ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Feeling nervous, anxious, or uncomfortable; uneasy about something or someone. For example: "She felt uneasy about the upcoming presentation."
- **Adverb**: In a manner that causes uneasiness; uneasily. For example: "He shifted uneasily in his seat during the tense conversation."

## Phrases
- "Feel uneasy" describes the emotional state of being uneasy.
- "With an uneasy heart" expresses concern or apprehension.
- "Uneasy silence" refers to a quietness filled with tension.

## Example Sentences

- **Adverb**: "He swallowed hard and answered uneasily."
