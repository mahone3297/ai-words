## 读音
/ˈlevəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 平坦的状态；水平；标准。例如: "The surface of the water was completely level."
- **动词**: 使平坦；调整至水平；对准。例如: "He leveled the picture on the wall."
- **形容词**: 平坦的；水平的；相等的。例如: "The playing field is level, giving both teams an equal chance."
- **副词**: 完全；彻底；平等地。例如: "She spoke to him levelly, without any hint of anger."

## 短语
- "on the same level" 意味着在相同的层次或地位上。
- "level playing field" 比喻公平竞争的环境。
- "level up" 在游戏或个人发展中的意思是升级或提高能力。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new road was built to maintain a level surface for safe driving."
- 动词: "She used a spirit level to ensure the bookshelf was perfectly leveled."
- 形容词: "The two athletes were so evenly matched that it was a level competition."
- 副词: "He answered her questions levelly, not wanting to show his anxiety.
