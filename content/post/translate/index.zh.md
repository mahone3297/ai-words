## 读音
/ˈtrænzˌleɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 把（一种语言）翻译成另一种语言。例如: "I need to translate this French letter into English."
- **名词**: 翻译的结果，译文。例如: "The book has been translated into multiple languages, and the latest translate is very accurate."

## 短语
- "translate sth. from A to B" 意味着将某事物从A语言翻译成B语言。
- "word-for-word translate" 指逐字逐句的翻译，通常不太注重语境。

## 例句
- 动词: "She's a professional translator who can translate ancient texts fluently."
- 名词: "The movie's title in the Chinese translate is '浪漫星球'."
