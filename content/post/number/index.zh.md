## 读音
/ˈnʌmbər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 表示数字或数目。例如: "The number of students in the class is 30."
- **动词 (v)**: 给...编号或计数。例如: "Number the pages before binding the book."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 关于数量的，有数字的。例如: "We need a number of volunteers for the event."

## 短语
- "Phone number" 指电话号码。
- "Serial number" 是序列号或编号，常用于标识物品的独特性。
- "Number one" 可以表示最重要的或第一。

## 例句
- 名词: "What's your favorite number between 1 and 100?"
- 动词: "Let's number the chairs so we know where to sit."
- 形容词: "There are a number of reasons for this decision."
