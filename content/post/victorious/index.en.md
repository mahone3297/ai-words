- **Adjective**: Conquering or having gained victory; triumphant. For example: "The victorious army entered the city."
- **Noun**: A person who has won or achieved success in a contest or battle. For example: "The victorious athlete was celebrated by fans."

## Phrases
- "Victorious return" signifies a triumphant return after a successful endeavor.
- "Victorious campaign" refers to a successful military or political campaign.
- "Victorious laurels" are the rewards or glory given to a winner.
