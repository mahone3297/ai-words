## Pronunciation
/həˈləʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A term used to greet or address someone. Often serves as an expression of friendliness or recognition. For example: "He waved a friendly hello at the waiter."
### Verb
To say "hello" or greet someone, marking the beginning of a conversation. For example: "She greeted her colleagues with a cheerful hello each morning."
### Adjective
Characterizing or denoting something that expresses or signifies greeting or welcome. For example: "The friendly neighborhood store had a hello sign displayed outside."
### Adverb
In the form of "hello," used to attract attention or express surprise. For example: "Suddenly, he heard a loud hello from behind."

## Phrases
- "Hello, dear" is a formal or affectionate greeting.
- "Hello, folks" is a general way to address a group.
- "Hello, again" is used when reuniting with someone after a while.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her warm hello made him feel instantly at ease."
- **Verb**: "After a long day, it was nice to hear his partner's familiar hello."
- **Adjective**: "The house was decorated with colorful hello balloons for the birthday party."
