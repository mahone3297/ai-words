## 读音
/ˈlɪbrəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 宽容的，自由主义的，倾向于给予个人自由的。例如: "She has liberal views on social issues."
- **名词**: 自由主义者，支持自由主义政策的人。例如: "He is a leading figure in the liberal party."

## 短语
- "Liberal arts" 指人文科学或自由教育。
- "Liberal democracy" 指具有广泛公民自由和选举权的民主制度。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new policy promotes a more liberal approach to immigration."
- 名词: "Many young people identify as liberals due to their stance on environmental issues."

## 同义词
- Progressive
- Open-minded
- Permissive
- Libertarian
