## Pronunciation
/stjuːˈɛə(r)/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who supervises passengers on an aircraft, train, or ship; or a person in charge of a section or department, especially in a hotel or restaurant. [1]
- **Verb**: To act as a steward or supervisor, ensuring the comfort and well-being of passengers or guests. [2]

## Phrases
- "Stewardess" (female steward)
- "Steward aboard" to indicate a steward's role on a vehicle or vessel
- "Steward service" refers to the duties provided by stewards

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The flight attendant, also known as the steward, helped passengers with their luggage."
- **Verb**: "The steward made sure all passengers were seated comfortably before takeoff."
- **Phrasal**: "The hotel staff, including the front desk clerk and the restaurant steward, went above and beyond to make our stay enjoyable."

## Synonyms
- Air hostess (female steward on an airplane)
- Cabin crew member
- Hotel porter
- Waiter/waitress (in a restaurant context)
- Supervisor (in a general supervision role)
