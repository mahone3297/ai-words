## 读音
/stuːərd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 航空公司的乘务员，负责照顾乘客和维护机舱安全。例如: "The steward showed me to my seat and explained the safety procedures."
- **名词**: 体育赛事或活动中的管家或管理员，负责监督和管理。例如: "The steward made sure all the spectators followed the rules during the match."
- **名词**: (过去)庄园的负责人，管理主人的财产和事务。例如: "In medieval times, a steward managed the lord's estate."

## 短语
- "stewardess" 指女性的航空乘务员。
- "stewardship" 指管理和照顾的责任或行为。

## 例句
- 名词: "The steward offered me a drink before takeoff."
- 名词: "The steward was responsible for maintaining order in the stadium."
