## 读音
/nuːn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一天中的正午，十二点。例如: "We usually have lunch at noon."
- **形容词**: 中午的，正午的。例如: "The sun was at its highest at noon."

## 短语
- "at noon" 表示在中午十二点。

## 例句
- 名词: "The town clock chimed precisely at noon."
- 形容词: "It was a hot, noonday sun that beat down on the street."

## 同义词
- midday
- high noon
- noontime
- twelve o'clock
