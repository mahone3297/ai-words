## Pronunciation
/nuːn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The middle of the day, usually defined as 12 o'clock in the afternoon when the sun is at its highest point in the sky.

Example Sentence: "We arranged to meet at noon so we could have lunch together."

## Phrases
- "High noon" refers to the exact moment when the sun reaches its zenith, often associated with a sense of drama or importance, particularly in Western movies.
- "At the stroke of noon" means precisely at 12 o'clock noon.
- "Noon hour" refers to the period around midday, typically from 11:30 AM to 1 PM, which includes the lunch break.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The wedding ceremony was scheduled for high noon under the old oak tree."
- "She took her lunch break during the noon hour to avoid the crowds at the cafeteria."

## Synonyms
- Midday
- Twelve o'clock
- Lunchtime
- Solar noon
- Noonday
