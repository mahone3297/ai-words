## 读音
/truː/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 真实的，符合事实的。例如: "That's the true story of what happened."
- **副词**: 确实地，确实。例如: "She truly loves painting."

## 短语
- "True to one's word" 意为遵守诺言。
- "True blue" 形容忠诚不变的人或事物。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The witness gave a true account of the accident."
- 副词: "He plays the guitar truly beautifully."
