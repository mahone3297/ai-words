## Pronunciation
/səʊl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The bottom or sole part of a shoe or boot, usually made of leather or rubber. 2. The flat, central part of a fish's body, below the head and above the fins. 3. (figurative) The main or only part; essence. 4. (mathematics) The square root of a number, especially the principal root (the non-negative one).
- **Adjective**: Solely, uniquely; exclusively.

## Phrases
- "Wear a sole" means to wear a sole on the bottom of a shoe.
- "The sole survivor" refers to the last person remaining after others have perished.
- "Sole responsibility" indicates that someone is in charge of a particular task or decision.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She carefully polished the leather soles of her shoes before going out."
- **Adjective**: "The company's success was solely due to his innovative ideas."
- **Adjective (used as an adjective)**: "He had the sole authority to make that decision."
