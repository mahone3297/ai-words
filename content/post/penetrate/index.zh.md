## 读音
/pəˈnɛtrəte/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 穿透；刺穿：使（如尖锐物）穿透或穿过物体。例如: "The arrow penetrated his armor."
2. 渗透；深入：进入或透过表面到达内部。例如: "The oil spill penetrated the soil."
3. （思想、信息等）深入理解，领悟：深入理解或领会复杂的事物。例如: "He tried to penetrate the meaning of the poem."

## 短语
- "Penetrate the market" 意为打入市场。
- "Penetrate the enemy's defenses" 指突破敌人的防线。

## 例句
- 动词: "The bullet penetrated the wall and lodged in the adjacent room."
- 动词: "Her ideas penetrated the minds of the audience, leaving a lasting impact."
