## 读音
/ˈjuːnɪfɔːrm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种统一的服装，通常在学校、军队或工作中穿着，以表明身份或遵守规定。例如: "All students must wear a school uniform."
- **形容词**: 一致的，相同的，没有变化的。例如: "The weather has been uniformly warm this week."
- **动词**: 使...统一；使...穿制服。例如: "The new policy will uniform all company branches."

## 短语
- "be in uniform" 表示穿着制服。
- "uniform standards" 指的是统一的标准或准则。

## 例句
- 名词: "The football team wore their red and white uniforms for the game."
- 形容词: "The service at this hotel is uniformly excellent."
- 动词: "The new employees were uniformed before starting work."

## 同义词
- 根据不同词性，同义词有所不同：
  - 名词: attire, dress, outfit
  - 形容词: consistent, uniformed, standardized
