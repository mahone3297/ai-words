## 读音
/stɑːrv/

## 释义
- **动词**: 饿死；挨饿。例如: "The survivors were starving after days without food."
- **动词**: 使挨饿；使饥饿。例如: "The prisoners were being starved as a form of punishment."
- **名词**: 极度饥饿的状态。例如: "They were in a state of starvation."

## 短语
- "starve to death" 意为饿死。
- "starve for something" 指极度渴望或需要某物。例如: "She was starving for attention."

## 例句
- 动词: "The refugees were on the brink of starving to death due to the lack of aid."
- 名词: "Their bodies showed the signs of starvation."
