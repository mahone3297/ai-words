## Pronunciation
/stɑːrv/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To lack sufficient food or nourishment to maintain health or normal bodily functions; to suffer from hunger. *Example*: "The refugees were starving due to the war."
- **Noun**: The state of being extremely hungry; the act of starving oneself. *Example*: "She endured months of starvation during her captivity."

## Phrases
- "Starve to death" refers to dying from lack of food.
- "Starvation diet" is a severe restriction of food intake for weight loss or other purposes.
- "To starve one's children" implies neglecting their basic needs for nourishment.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The elderly man had to starve his pet dog when food became scarce."
- **Noun**: "The prisoners faced the harsh reality of constant starvation."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "They couldn't afford to starve themselves any longer, so they sought help."
