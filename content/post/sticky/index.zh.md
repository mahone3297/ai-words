## 读音
/stɪˈki/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 粘性的；有粘附力的；黏糊糊的。例如: "The glue is very sticky and hard to remove."
- **形容词**: （问题或情况）棘手的，难处理的。例如: "We've run into a sticky problem with the project."

## 短语
- "sticky situation" 指困难或复杂的情况。
- "sticky fingers" 指手很粘或喜欢偷窃的人。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The weather is so hot that the ice cream melts and gets all over my hands, making them really sticky."
- 形容词: "The company is facing a sticky situation due to unexpected financial losses."
