## Pronunciation
/həˈwli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Completely or entirely; without reservation or exception. For example: "She accepted the responsibility wholly."
- **Adjective**: Entirely; completely; full; complete. For example: "The project was executed with a wholly different approach."
- **Pronoun**: (rarely used) A pronoun that can be used to refer to oneself or something, often in a religious context. For example: "I was wholly devoted to my studies."

## Phrases
- "Wholly devoted" expresses complete dedication or commitment.
- "Wholly responsible" means being entirely accountable for something.
- "Wholly satisfied" indicates complete satisfaction.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "She was wholly absorbed in her work, not noticing the time passing."
- **Adjective**: "His honesty was acknowledged, as he was seen as a wholly trustworthy individual."
- **Pronoun**: "He felt wholly cleansed after completing his spiritual retreat."

## Synonyms

- Fully

- Unreservedly
