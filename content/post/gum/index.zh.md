## 读音
/ɡʌm/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 口香糖，一种可以咀嚼但不易吞咽的软糖，通常由橡胶基质和其他添加剂制成。例如: "I like to chew gum when I'm studying."
- **动词 (v)**: 咀嚼口香糖。例如: "The kids were gumming away during the movie."

## 短语
- "Chew gum" 意味着咀嚼口香糖。
- "Gum up" 表示使粘住或阻塞，如 "The old machine gummed up and stopped working."

## 例句
- 名词: "She had a piece of mint gum in her mouth, making her breath fresh."
- 动词: "Don't forget to spit out your gum before entering the classroom."

## 同义词
- Chewing gum
- Bubble gum
- Masticate
- Nicotine gum (if referring to a specific type for quitting smoking)
