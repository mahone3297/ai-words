## 读音
/njuːz/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 新闻，信息，报道。指通过各种媒体传播的最新事件或情况。例如: "The evening news covered the latest developments in the election."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 新的，最近的，未公开的。用来描述事物的状态或时间上的新近。例如: "We have some exciting news to share with you."

## 短语
- "Breaking news" 表示突发新闻或最新的报道。
- "Newsflash" 通常用来强调一个重要的或突发的消息。
- "Good news" 和 "Bad news" 分别指的是好消息和坏消息。

## 例句
- 名词: "I try to stay up-to-date with current news by reading newspapers and watching TV news programs."
- 形容词: "The company announced a new policy that will affect all employees."
