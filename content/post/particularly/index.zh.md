## 读音
/pərˈtɪkjələrli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 特别地，尤其，格外。例如: "I like this book particularly because of its unique style."
- **形容词**: 特定的，具体的。例如: "The particularly cold winter made it difficult for farmers."

## 短语
- "in particular" 意为特别地，尤其是。例如: "She likes classical music in particular."
- "not particularly" 表示不特别，不十分。例如: "I'm not particularly fond of spicy food."

## 例句
- 副词: "He was particularly interested in the chapter on ancient history."
- 形容词: "The boss asked for a report on the particularly successful marketing campaign."
