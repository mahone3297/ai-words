## 读音
/moʊn/

## 释义
- **动词**: 发出呻吟声，尤指因痛苦、不满或性兴奋而发出的声音。例如: "She moaned in pain when she hurt her ankle."
- **名词**: 呻吟声；抱怨。例如: "His moans filled the room as he suffered from a headache."

## 短语
- "to moan in one's sleep" 表示在睡梦中呻吟。
- "a litany of moans" 指一连串的抱怨或不满。

## 例句
- 动词: "The baby moaned as it woke up from a deep sleep."
- 名词: "Her moans echoed through the empty hallway."

## 同义词
- Groan
- Whimper
- Sigh
- Complain
- Grumble
