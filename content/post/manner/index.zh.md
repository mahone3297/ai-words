## 读音
/ˈmænər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 方式，方法，举止，态度。例如: "He has a polite manner when speaking to elders."
- **名词**: 礼貌，规矩，习俗。例如: "It's good manners to say 'please' and 'thank you'."
- **名词**: 风格，特色，典型特征。例如: "Her paintings have a distinct manner."

## 短语
- "in a certain manner" 意为以某种方式。
- "manners maketh man" 表示礼貌使人成为文明人。
- "a particular manner of doing something" 指做某事的特定方式。

## 例句
- 名词: "She greeted her guests with a friendly manner."
- 名词: "The children were taught good manners at an early age."
- 名词: "His writing has a humorous and lighthearted manner."
