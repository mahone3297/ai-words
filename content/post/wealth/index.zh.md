## 读音
/welθ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 财富，富裕：表示个人或国家拥有的大量金钱、财产或有价值的东西。例如: "He inherited a great wealth from his grandfather."
   - 知识，智慧：指知识、经验或技能的积累。例如: "She possesses a wealth of knowledge about ancient civilizations."

## 短语
- "Wealth of nations" 指一个国家的经济实力和资源。
- "Material wealth" 物质财富，指实物形式的财产。
- "Intellectual wealth" 智力财富，指知识、创意和智慧的积累。

## 例句
- 名词: "His wealth allowed him to live a life of luxury."
- "She believes in using her wealth for philanthropic causes."
