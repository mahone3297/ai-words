## 读音
/plɑːt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 故事情节，剧情。例如: "The plot of the movie was full of twists and turns."
- **名词**: 地图上的图形，图表。例如: "He drew a plot on the map to show us the way."
- **名词**: （土地的）小块，地段。例如: "They bought a small plot of land to build their dream house."
- **动词**: 阴谋，策划。例如: "The conspirators plotted to overthrow the government."
- **动词**: 制定计划，规划。例如: "We plotted our route for the hiking trip."

## 短语
- "plot a course" 计划行程或行动路线。
- "plot against someone" 图谋对某人不利。
- "plot a graph" 在图表上标出数据点。

## 例句
- 名词: "The book's plot kept me on the edge of my seat."
- 动词: "They plotted to escape from the prison."
- 名词: "She marked the plot of her garden with stakes and string."
