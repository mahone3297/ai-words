## 读音
/leɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 细绳、线或带状物，常用于装饰或系紧物品。例如: "She tied her shoes with a delicate lace."
  - 一种有孔眼的纺织品，通常由棉、丝或尼龙制成，用于装饰。例如: "The tablecloth was adorned with beautiful lace edges."
  - （运动鞋或衣物上的）系带。例如: "He carefully laced up his new running shoes."

- **动词**:
  - 用细绳、线或带状物打结或穿孔装饰。例如: "The dress was laced with golden thread."
  - 将（鞋、衣服等）系紧。例如: "She laced her corset tightly."

## 短语
- "lace up" 意味着将鞋带或其他带子系紧。例如: "Make sure to lace up your boots securely before hiking."
- "lace something with something else" 表示在某物中混入或添加其他物质。例如: "The coffee was laced with a hint of cinnamon."

## 例句
- 名词: "The curtains were made from exquisite handmade lace."
- 动词: "She carefully laced the ribbon around the gift box."
- "Lace your fingers together and stretch your arms above your head."
