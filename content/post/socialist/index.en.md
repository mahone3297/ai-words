## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who supports or advocates for the principles of socialism, which is an economic and political theory advocating for public ownership and control of major industries, resources, and means of production. For example: "The socialist government nationalized many industries."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of or adhering to socialism. For example: "A socialist ideology emphasizes equality and social welfare."
- **Noun**: (informal) A member of a socialist political party or movement. For example: "He joined the local socialist group."
