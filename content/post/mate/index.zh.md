## 读音
/mɑːt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 伙伴，朋友；同事。例如: "He considers his workmates as his second family."
             2. 配偶，伴侣（常用于动物）。例如: "The mare and her stallion are mates for life."

- **动词 (v)**: 结成伙伴，成为朋友。例如: "They quickly mated over their shared interests."

## 短语
- "workmate" 意为工作伙伴。
- "shipmate" 在船上一起工作的伙伴。
- "mate up" 指配对，结伴。

## 例句
- 名词: "My best mate has known me since primary school."
- 动词: "They mate for life, forming strong bonds."
