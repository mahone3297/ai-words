## 读音
/ˈmæri/

## 释义
- **动词**: 结婚；嫁；娶。例如: "They decided to marry next summer."
- **名词**: 婚姻；结婚的状态。例如: "They've been enjoying a happy marriage for years."

## 短语
- "Get married" 意为结婚。
- "Marry someone off" 表示促成某人的婚姻。
- "Marry well" 指嫁得好或娶得好（通常指经济条件）。

## 例句
- 动词: "After dating for two years, they finally decided to marry."
- 名词: "Their marriage has been an inspiration to many."

## 同义词
- Wed
- Unite
- Tie the knot
- Take as a spouse
- Enter into matrimony
