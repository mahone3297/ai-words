## 读音
/vəˈnaɪti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 自负；虚荣；无价值的东西；虚无
    - 例句: "His vanity prevented him from accepting criticism."
- **形容词**: 虚荣的；自负的；徒劳的
    - 例句: "She spent hours in front of the mirror, preening in her vanity."

## 短语
- "vanity fair" 指充满虚荣和浮华的场所或情况。
- "vanity case" 或 "vanity box" 指化妆箱或梳妆盒。
- "vanity project" 指出于个人虚荣心而进行的项目。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her excessive love for fashion was often seen as mere vanity."
- 形容词: "The actor's vanity made it difficult for him to work with others."

## 同义词
- Arrogance
- Self-importance
- Conceit
- Pretentiousness
- Narcissism
