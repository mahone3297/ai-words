## 读音
/ˈsɜːrv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 服务；为…提供服务。例如: "The waiter served us drinks promptly."
  - 担任；充当。例如: "She serves as the president of the local charity organization."
  - 上菜；端上（食物）。例如: "The chef will now serve the main course."
  - 发球；在网球等运动中发球。例如: "He served an ace to win the match."

- **名词**:
  - 服务；服侍。例如: "The hotel offers excellent customer service."
  - 一份服务；一盘食物。例如: "I'll have the daily special with a side serve of vegetables."

## 短语
- "Serve someone right" 意为某人应得的，适合某人。例如: "If you don't prepare for the exam, it will serve you right if you fail."
- "Serve up" 意为提供，端上。例如: "The chef served up a delicious meal."

## 例句
- 动词: "He serves customers with a friendly smile."
- 名词: "The restaurant's signature dish is their seafood serve."
