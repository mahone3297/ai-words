## 读音
/joy/

## 释义
- **名词**: 快乐，愉快，欢乐。例如: "She felt a surge of joy when she saw her old friend."
- **形容词**: 令人快乐的，使人愉悦的。例如: "The joyous occasion made everyone smile."

## 短语
- "joys of life" 生活的乐趣。
- "find joy in something" 在某事中找到乐趣。
- "joyful celebration" 欢庆的庆典。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children's laughter was a source of pure joy for their parents."
- 形容词: "It was a joyous moment when they announced the good news."
