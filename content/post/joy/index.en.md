## Pronunciation
/dʒɔɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A feeling of great pleasure and happiness; a source or cause of delight. For example: "The birth of their child brought them immense joy."
- **Verb (archaic)**: To experience joy or take pleasure in something. For example: "She joys in her children's accomplishments."

## Phrases
- "In the joy of the moment": Refers to experiencing happiness during a particular event or time.
- "Pure joy": Expresses complete and utter happiness without any negativity.
- "Find joy in the little things": Encourages one to appreciate small pleasures in life.
- "Jump for joy": To express extreme happiness and excitement by jumping up and down.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her face lit up with joy when she saw the surprise birthday party."
- **Verb (archaic)**: "He doth joy in his newfound freedom."
