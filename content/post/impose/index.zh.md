## 读音
/ɪmˈpoʊz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 强加；施加：强制实施（法律、规则等）。例如: "The government imposed new taxes on imported goods."
  2. 征收：对…课税或罚款。例如: "They were imposed a fine for breaking the law."
  3. 要求（某事）：迫使接受或遵守。例如: "The teacher imposed silence on the class."
  4. 把（想法、观点等）强加于人：强迫别人接受。例如: "He tried to impose his beliefs on others."

## 短语
- "Impose a fine" 意为罚款。
- "Impose one's will" 表示强迫自己的意志。
- "Impose restrictions" 意为实施限制。

## 例句
- 动词: "The new government imposed stricter regulations on pollution control."
- "The parents imposed a bedtime curfew on their teenagers."
- "The boss imposed extra work on the employees without notice."
