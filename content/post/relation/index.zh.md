## 读音
/rɪˈleɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 关系，联系。指两个或多个事物之间的相互作用、影响或关联。例如: "There is a close relation between exercise and good health."
- **动词**: 与...有关，涉及。例如: "This problem relates directly to our project."

## 短语
- "Family relations" 家庭关系
- "Social relations" 社会关系
- "Business relations" 商业关系

## 例句
- 名词: "Maintaining good relations with neighbors is important for a peaceful community."
- 动词: "The new policy relates to all employees in the company."
