## 读音
/laːf/

## 释义
- **动词**: 大笑，发出笑声。例如: "The joke made everyone laugh."
- **名词**: 笑声，大笑。例如: "His laugh was contagious."
- **形容词**: 可笑的，令人发笑的。例如: "The situation was so absurd that it was laughable."

## 短语
- "laugh out loud" (LOL) 表示大声笑或讽刺地笑。
- "in fits of laughter" 形容笑得无法自已。
- "to laugh off something" 意为用笑来化解尴尬或不愉快的事情。

## 例句
- 动词: "The comedian's jokes had the audience laughing all night."
- 名词: "I could hear her infectious laugh from down the hall."
- 形容词: "The clown's antics were so ridiculous they were laughable."
