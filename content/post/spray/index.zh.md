## 读音
/spreɪ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 喷雾，喷射物；2. 喷洒器，喷雾器。例如: "She used a can of insecticide spray to kill the flies."
- **动词**: 1. 喷洒，喷射；2. （使）四散飞溅。例如: "The gardener sprayed water on the flowers."
- **形容词**: 喷射状的，散布的。例如: "The spray from the waterfall covered us in mist."

## 短语
- "spray paint" 指的是用喷雾罐进行喷涂的颜料。
- "spray-on tan" 是一种通过喷雾来实现的晒黑效果。

## 例句
- 名词: "The perfume comes in a fine misty spray."
- 动词: "He sprayed the wall with graffiti."
- 形容词: "The beach was covered in a spray of seagulls."

## 同义词
- sprinkle (动词时)
- distribute
- scatter
- atomize
- disperse
