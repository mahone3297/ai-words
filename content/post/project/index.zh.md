## 读音
/ˈprɑːdʒekt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 计划；方案。例如: "We need to present our project proposal to the board." 2. 项目；工程。例如: "The construction of the new bridge is a massive project."
- **动词 (v)**: 1. 投射；发射。例如: "The light from the lamp projects onto the wall." 2. 预测；预计。例如: "Economists project a growth rate of 3% for next year."
- **名词 (n)**: 在电影或戏剧中，放映或演出。例如: "The movie will be projected on a big screen."

## 短语
- "project management" 项目管理
- "project budget" 项目预算
- "project oneself" 展现自我；使自己显得重要

## 例句
- 名词: "The team is working on a new software development project."
- 动词: "The professor projected slides during his lecture."
- 名词 (在电影或戏剧中的使用): "The film was poorly projected, making it hard to see."
