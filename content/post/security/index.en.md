## Pronunciation
/seˈkjʊrəti/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. The state of being protected from harm, danger, or unauthorized access: *The security of the building was enhanced after the recent break-in.*
2. The practice or profession of protecting people, property, or information from threats: *Security guards ensure the safety of the event.*
3. Confidence or trust in a person, system, or organization: *Public confidence in the government's security measures has been declining.*

### Verb
1. To protect or ensure the safety of: *We must take steps to secure the data.*
2. To feel or maintain a sense of safety or security: *Employees felt secure in their jobs after the company's restructuring.*

### Adjective
1. Characterizing or relating to safety or protection: *A high-security zone.*
2. Having a low risk of danger or vulnerability: *The new software has top-notch security features.*

## Phrases
- "Enhance security" means to improve safety measures.
- "Maintain security" is about keeping a protected state.
- "Cybersecurity" refers to the protection of digital assets.
- "Physical security" deals with guarding against physical threats.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The company's IT department is responsible for ensuring data security."
- **Verb**: "She felt secure knowing her home was alarmed."
- **Adjective**: "The high-security clearance allowed him access to classified information."
