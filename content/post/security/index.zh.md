## 读音
/ˈsɪkjʊrəti/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 安全，保障：保护不受伤害或危险的状态。例如: "The main concern of the event organizers was the security of the attendees."
   - 保卫，警卫：确保安全的措施或人员。例如: "There's a tight security in place for the president's visit."
   - 证券：金融资产，尤其是债券，提供固定的收益。例如: "Investing in corporate securities can be risky."
   - 保密，机密：保守信息的秘密状态。例如: "The company's trade secrets are a matter of security."

- **形容词**:
   - 安全的，可靠的：提供或感觉安全的。例如: "She felt a sense of security in her own home."

## 短语
- "Internet security" 指网络安全性。
- "Financial security" 指财务安全。
- "National security" 指国家安全。

## 例句
- 名词: "The airport has increased its security measures after a recent threat."
- 形容词: "The new locks provide better security for our house."
