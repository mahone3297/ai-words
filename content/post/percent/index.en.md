## Pronunciation
/pərˈsent/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A word used to express a part of a whole as a fraction or decimal, typically out of 100.** Example: "The sales increased by 20 percent this quarter."
2. **A measure of proportion or ratio, often expressed as a percentage.** Example: "She scored 85 percent on her math exam."

### Verb
1. **To express a quantity or proportion as a percentage.** Example: "I need to calculate the percent of students who passed the test."
2. **To represent or account for (a certain amount) in terms of a percentage.** Example: "The new policy will cover 75 percent of healthcare costs."

## Phrases
- "What's the percentage?" asks for the proportion or ratio.
- "Up by 10 percent" indicates an increase.
- "Percent of...": Expressing a portion of something as a percentage.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The company aims to increase its market share by 5 percent next year."
- **Verb**: "They converted the weight loss into a percentage for better understanding."
- **Interrogative**: "How many people voted in the election, as a percent?"
