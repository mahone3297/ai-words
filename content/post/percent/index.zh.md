## 读音
/ˈpɜːr.sɛnt/

## 释义
- **名词 n**: 百分比，表示一个数是另一个数的百分之几。例如: "The tax rate is 10 percent of your income."
- **形容词 adj**: 百分之...的，表示比例关系。例如: "I've completed 50 percent of the project."

## 短语
- "percent off" 表示打折的百分比。例如: "There's a 20 percent discount on all items."
- "percent increase/decrease" 用来描述增长或减少的百分比。例如: "The population increased by 7 percent last year."

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's profit margin is only about five percent."
- 形容词: "In this region, 80 percent of the population speaks Spanish."
