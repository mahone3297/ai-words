## 读音
/pet/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 宠物，尤指家庭饲养的小动物，如狗、猫等。例如: "She has a lovely little cat as a pet."
  - 喜爱的人或物，常用于昵称。例如: "Her baby is her little pet."

## 短语
- "pet dog" 指家养的狗。
- "pet cat" 指家养的猫。
- "treat someone like a pet" 表示对某人过分宠爱或溺爱。

## 例句
- 名词: "The pet store sells a variety of animals, from birds to reptiles."
- "She treats her cat like it's her own child, spoiling it with toys and treats."

## 同义词
- Companion animal
- Domestic animal
- Favourite (British English)
- Beloved (in the context of a person)
