## Pronunciation
/pɛt/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Pet**: A domesticated animal kept for companionship, often a dog, cat, bird, or fish. For example: "The family has a pet dog named Max."
- **Term of endearment**: A person's affectionate name or nickname. For example: "Her pet name was 'Sugary' because she had a sweet personality."

### Verb
- **To pet**: To stroke or caress (an animal) affectionately. For example: "The child pets the cat gently."
- **To treat as a pet**: To care for and show affection towards someone like a pet. For example: "She pets her friend's hair while they talk."

### Adjective
- **Adorable pet**: A very cute or lovable animal. For example: "Their new puppy was an adorable pet."
- **Furry pet**: A pet with fur, such as a cat or dog. For example: "She loved playing with her furry pet rabbit."

## Phrases
- "Take good care of your pet" means to ensure proper care and attention for an animal.
- "A house pet" refers to an animal that lives in a home with its owner.
- "A wild pet" or "a stray pet" is an animal that has been taken in as a pet without being originally owned.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children love playing with their family's two pets, a cat and a hamster."
- **Verb**: "After a long day, she likes to come home and pet her cat on the head."
- **Adjective**: "The little girl's furry pet bunny was a source of constant joy."
