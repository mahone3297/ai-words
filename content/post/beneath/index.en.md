## Definitions
- **Noun**: A greeting or expression of welcome, often informal. For example: "She waved a friendly hello to her neighbor."
- **Verb**: To express greetings or acknowledge someone's presence. For example: "He greeted the guests with a hearty hello."
- **Interjection**: A colloquial expression used to start a conversation or call attention. For example: "Hello, sunshine! How's your day?"
