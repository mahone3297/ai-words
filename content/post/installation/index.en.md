## Pronunciation
/ɪnstəˈleɪʃən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The act of installing something, the process of setting up or putting equipment, systems, or software in place so that they are ready for use. For example: "The installation of the new air conditioning unit took several hours."
- **Noun**: A thing that has been installed, especially a work of art or exhibit that is designed to be displayed in a particular location or environment. For instance: "The museum's latest installation explores themes of light and space."
- **Noun**: In military terms, it refers to a group of buildings or facilities established for a specific purpose such as a military base or camp. Example: "The soldiers were assigned to a new installation overseas."

## Phrases
- "Installation process": The series of steps taken to install or set up something.
- "Installation art": A form of contemporary art where the artist creates an environment or an experience often using three-dimensional objects in a particular space.
- "System installation": The setup or configuration of a computer system or software application.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (general)**: "The plumber will arrive tomorrow to complete the installation of the new sink."
- **Noun (artistic)**: "Visitors marveled at the intricate installation which combined light, sound, and sculpture."
- **Noun (military)**: "After completing their training, the recruits were deployed to a key strategic installation."
