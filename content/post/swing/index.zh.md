## 读音
/swɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 摇摆；摆动：The child was sitting on a swing in the park.
  - 摇摆的动作或状态：Her mood swung from joy to sadness.
  - （棒球、拳击等的）挥击动作：He missed the ball with his swing.

- **动词**:
  - 摆动，摇摆：The pendulum swings back and forth.
  - 彻底改变方向或立场：After the debate, public opinion swung in favor of the opposition.

- **形容词**:
  - 有节奏感的；摇摆的：The band played a swing jazz number.
  - 可以摆动的；可移动的：The door has a swing handle.

## 短语
- "in the swing of things" 表示投入工作或活动的状态：Once we got into the swing of things, the project progressed smoothly.
- "on a swing" 在摇摆或旋转中：The kids were on the swing set, laughing and having fun.

## 例句
- 名词: "The baby enjoyed the gentle motion of the swing."
- 动词: "The gymnast swung effortlessly from one bar to another."
- 形容词: "The dance had a lively swing rhythm."
