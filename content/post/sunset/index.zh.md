## 读音
/sʌnˈsɛt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 太阳落山的时候，黄昏，日落。例如: "The sunset was a beautiful display of colors in the sky."
- **动词**: (非正式)指太阳落山的过程。例如: "The sun is setting early these days, sunsetted around 6 pm."

## 短语
- "Sunset view" 指的是日落的景色。
- "Sunset hour" 是指黄昏时刻。
- "Sundown" 也是指日落，常在口语中使用。

## 例句
- 名词: "We enjoyed a picnic while watching the magnificent sunset over the ocean."
- 动词: "The village falls asleep soon after sunset, when the day's activities wind down."

## 同义词
- Dusk
- Twilight
- Sundown
- Eve
- Nightfall
