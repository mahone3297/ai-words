## Pronunciation
/səˈnɛt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: (noun) The setting of the sun, typically producing a beautiful display of colors in the sky during the transition from day to night. [1]
- **Verb**: (verb) To observe or experience the sunset. [2]
- **Adjective**: (adjective) Relating to or describing the appearance of the sky during a sunset. [3]
