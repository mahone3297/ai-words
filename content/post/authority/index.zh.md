## 读音
/əˈθɔːrəti/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 权威；权力：The government is the ultimate authority on law in the country.
  - 机构；官方：He works at a local authority responsible for housing.
  - 专家；权威人士：She is an authority on ancient Greek history.
  - 许可；授权：You need written authority to access this area.

- **动词**:
  - 给予权威或批准：The manager authorized the purchase of new equipment.

## 短语
- "Exercise authority over" 表示对某人行使权力或控制。
- "Have authority for" 表示有根据或理由支持某个说法或行动。
- "Be the authority on" 意味着在某一领域是公认的专家。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police have the authority to arrest anyone breaking the law."
- 动词: "The principal authorized the field trip to the museum."
- 专家: "He is considered the leading authority on quantum physics."
