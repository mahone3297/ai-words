## 读音
/ˈɛfərt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 尽力，努力，尝试。例如: "She put in a great effort to finish the project on time."
- **动词**: 试图，尽力。例如: "He is efforting to improve his communication skills."

## 短语
- "make an effort" 表示做出努力，尝试。例如: "You need to make an effort to learn a new language."
- "without effort" 意为毫不费力地。例如: "She solved the problem without effort."

## 例句
- 名词: "His effort to help others was recognized and appreciated."
- 动词: "She efforted to understand the complex concept by reading several books."
