## 读音
/læk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 缺乏；不足；短缺。例如: "There is a lack of food in the region."
- **动词**: 不具备；没有。例如: "They lack experience in dealing with such situations."
- **形容词**: 缺乏的；不足的。例如: "The project suffered from a lack of funding."

## 短语
- "Lack of something" 指某物的缺乏。例如: "The team's main problem was a lack of communication."
- "To make up for a lack" 补偿不足。例如: "She took extra classes to make up for her lack of knowledge in math."

## 例句
- 名词: "The company is trying to address the lack of diversity in its workforce."
- 动词: "Their success was hindered by their lack of preparation."
- 形容词: "His performance was criticized due to his lack of enthusiasm."
