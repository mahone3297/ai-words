## Pronunciation
/səmˈtaɪm/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Referring to a point in time, usually indicating an unspecified future moment. It can also be used to mean "at some point" or "occasionally." 

## Phrases
- "Sometime soon" indicates a future point in time.
- "I'll see you sometime" means there's no fixed plan but you expect to meet at some point.
- "Sometime during the day" refers to an arbitrary period within the day.

## Example Sentences
- "I'll meet you sometime next week."
- "The event will take place sometime in May."
- "I remember him saying he'd come back someday, sometime."

## Synonyms
- At some point
- Eventually
- Occasionally
- Someday
- Somewhere between

## Antonyms
- Now
- Immediately
- Always
- Regularly
