## 读音
/θrɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 刺激，激动，兴奋。例如: "The rollercoaster ride gave her a thrill."
- **动词**: 使感到兴奋或激动。例如: "The news thrilled the whole nation."
- **形容词**: 令人兴奋的，令人激动的。例如: "The thriller movie was absolutely thrilling."

## 短语
- "to give sb. a thrill" 意味着给某人带来刺激或兴奋。
- "thrill-seeker" 指寻求刺激和冒险的人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The sound of live music always gives me a thrill."
- 动词: "The thought of skydiving thrilled him."
- 形容词: "The new adventure park promises a thrilling experience for all visitors."

## 同义词
- Excitement
- Ecstasy
- Euphoria
- Intense pleasure
- Sensation
