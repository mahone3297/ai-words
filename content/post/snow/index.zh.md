## 读音
/snəʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 冬季时天空中落下的白色结晶体，通常为六角形，堆积在地面上形成覆盖层。例如: "The city was blanketed with a thick layer of snow."
- **动词**: 形容下雪的行为或过程。例如: "It snowed heavily all day yesterday."
- **形容词**: 与雪相关或白色的，像雪一样的。例如: "The snow-covered mountains were breathtaking."

## 短语
- "Snowing cats and dogs" 意味着雪下得非常大。
- "Under a snow blanket" 描述被雪覆盖的景象。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were building a snowman in the yard."
- 动词: "The town often snows during December, making it a winter wonderland."
- 形容词: "She wore a snow-white dress to the party."
