## 读音
/help/

## 释义
- **动词**: 帮助，援助。例如: "Can you help me carry these boxes?"
- **名词**: 援助，帮助。例如: "She offered him some help with his homework."

## 短语
- "ask for help" 请求帮助。
- "help out" 帮忙，协助。
- "be of help" 有帮助的。

## 例句
- 动词: "Please help me set up the table for dinner."
- 名词: "Her advice was a great help in solving the problem."
