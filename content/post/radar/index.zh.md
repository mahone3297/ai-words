## 读音
/ræˈdɑːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 雷达，一种利用无线电波探测目标位置、速度和方向的电子设备。例如: "The airplane relies on radar for navigation in bad weather."
- **动词**: 使用雷达进行探测或追踪。例如: "The air traffic control radar can detect planes from miles away."

## 短语
- "radar system" 指整个雷达设备和其操作的系统。
- "radar detection" 指通过雷达进行的探测过程。

## 例句
- 名词: "The military uses advanced radar technology to monitor enemy movements."
- 动词: "The ship's radar is constantly scanning the ocean for potential threats."

## 同义词
- surveillance equipment
- tracking device
- monitoring system
- radio detection and ranging (缩写即为radar)
