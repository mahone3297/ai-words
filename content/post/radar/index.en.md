## Pronunciation
/rəˈdɑːr/

## Definitions
### Noun
**Radar** (abbreviation of 'Radio Detection and Ranging'):  
1. A system that uses radio waves to detect objects, especially to locate their distance, direction, or speed, especially in aviation and navigation.
2. [countable] An instance or device using this technology.
   - Example: "The ship's radar detected a nearby iceberg."

### Verb
- To scan or track using radar.
   - Example: "The pilot radared the area for potential weather hazards."

## Phrases
- "Radar imagery" refers to images produced by radar systems.
- "Radar signature" is the unique pattern of reflected radar signals that identifies an object.
- "Radar lock" means maintaining a constant radar signal on a target.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The air traffic controller relied on the radar to guide incoming planes."
- **Verb**: "The Coast Guard radar tracked the lost hiker through the dense forest."
- **Passive**: "The storm was picked up by radar before it reached the city."
