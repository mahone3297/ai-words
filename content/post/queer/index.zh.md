## 读音
/ˈkwer/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 
    - 怪异的；奇特的：例如: "Her fashion sense is quite queer, but it suits her personality."
    - LGBTQ+社群中的术语，指同性恋、双性恋、跨性别者或其他非二元性别身份的人：例如: "The queer community has been advocating for equal rights for decades."

## 短语
- "Queer theory" 是一种研究性别和性取向的学术理论。
- "Queer studies" 涉及对LGBTQ+文化和历史的研究。

## 例句
- 形容词: "In the art exhibit, there were several queer interpretations of traditional themes."
- LGBTQ+身份："They use 'queer' as an umbrella term to identify with the broader LGBTQ+ community."
