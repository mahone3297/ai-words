## 读音
/əˈbeɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 遵守（命令、法律、规则等）。例如: "Students must obey their teachers' instructions." 
- **动词**: 屈从，服从（某人的意志或权威）。例如: "She obeyed her father's wishes and took up law as a career."

## 短语
- "Obey the law" 遵守法律。
- "Obey orders" 遵守命令。
- "Obey one's conscience" 跟随良心行事。

## 例句
- "Children should always obey their parents for their own safety."
- "He refused to obey the unjust command, risking his job."
- "In the army, soldiers must blindly obey their superiors."
