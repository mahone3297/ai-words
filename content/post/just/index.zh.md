## 读音
/ˈdʒʌst/

## 释义
- **副词**: 恰好，正好。例如: "The dress fits me just right."
- **形容词**: 公正的，公平的。例如: "The judge made a just decision."
- **动词**: 只不过，仅仅。例如: "I just wanted to ask you a quick question."
- **介词**: 仅仅，刚刚。例如: "She has just arrived."

## 短语
- "just about" 表示几乎，将近。例如: "I'm just about finished with my project."
- "just in time" 指及时，恰好在需要的时候。例如: "We arrived just in time for the movie."

## 例句
- 副词: "I need to leave now, I'll be back just in a minute."
- 形容词: "The company strives to provide just and equal opportunities to all employees."
- 动词: "I didn't mean to upset you, I was just being honest."
- 介词: "She woke up just as the alarm went off."
