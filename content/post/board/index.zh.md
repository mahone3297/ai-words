## 读音
/bɔːrd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 木板；平板：A flat piece of wood, often used as a surface for writing or painting.
   - 船板；甲板：The deck of a ship or boat.
   - 委员会；董事会：A group of people who direct or manage an organization.
   - 乘车（船、飞机）：To get on a vehicle such as a bus, train, or plane.
   - 伙食；膳宿：Food and lodging provided, especially in a school or college.

- **动词**:
   - 加入董事会：To become a member of a board, typically a governing body.
   - 登上（交通工具）：To get onto a vehicle for transportation.

## 短语
- "Board a plane/train/bus" 意为登上飞机/火车/公共汽车。
- "Board of directors" 指公司的董事会。
- "Be on the board" 表示成为某个组织的成员。

## 例句
- 名词: "He nailed the picture frame to the wooden board."
- 动词: "She had to board the train early in the morning for her business trip."
- 名词 (膳食): "The students are provided with full board during their stay at the college."
