## 读音
/wʌtˈevər/

## 释义
- **代词**: 任何事物；无论什么。例如: "You can choose whatever book you like."
- **连词**: 不论…是否；无论…怎样。例如: "I'll go wherever you go."

## 短语
- "Whatever happens" 意为不论发生什么事情。
- "Whatever you say" 可以表示同意或不介意对方的意见。

## 例句
- 代词: "Use whatever tool is most convenient for you."
- 连词: "Do whatever it takes to make your dreams come true."
