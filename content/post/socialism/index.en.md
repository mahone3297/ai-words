## Pronunciation
/səʊʃəˈlɪzəm/

## Definitions
### Noun
**Socialism** is a political and economic system characterized by collective ownership of means of production, distribution, and exchange, with the goal of achieving social equality and often communal control over resources. It contrasts with capitalism, where private property and profit play a central role.

### Verb (Transitive)
To **implement socialism** means to establish or introduce socialist policies and principles in a society or organization.
To **advocate socialism** is to support or promote the ideas and principles of socialism.

### Adjective
**Socialist** describes something related to or based on socialism, emphasizing collective ownership and equitable distribution.

### Adjective (Characterization)
**Communist** (synonym) refers to a specific form of socialism where the state owns everything and controls the economy.
