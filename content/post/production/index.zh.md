## 读音
/prəˈdʌkʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    1. 生产；制造：指将原材料转化为产品的过程。例如: "The factory increased its production of cars this year."
    2. 产量；产物：一个时期内生产的产品总量。例如: "Their crop yield was a record high production."
    3. 创作；制作：如电影、戏剧或音乐作品的创作和呈现。例如: "The new Broadway production received rave reviews."
    4. 生殖；繁殖：生物体产生后代的行为。例如: "The pandas' successful production of offspring was celebrated worldwide."

## 短语
- "Film production" 指电影的制作过程。
- "Theatrical production" 指舞台剧的制作。
- "Productive capacity" 指生产能力或产能。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's production line has been modernized to increase efficiency."
- 制作: "The director oversaw every aspect of the stage production."
- 生殖: "The endangered species' low production rate is a concern for conservationists."
