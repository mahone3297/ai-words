## 读音
/lʌm.bər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 原木或木材，尤指经过切割但未加工成特定形状的木料。例如: "The construction site had a large pile of lumber waiting to be used."
- **动词**: 慢慢笨拙地走动或移动，尤指因负重而显得吃力。例如: "The old man lumbered along the street with his heavy bag."

## 短语
- "Lumber industry" 指的是木材工业或伐木业。
- "Lumber yard" 是存放和销售木材的地方。

## 例句
- 名词: "The carpenter needed more lumber to finish building the deck."
- 动词: "The elephant lumbered through the jungle, carrying a heavy load on its back."
