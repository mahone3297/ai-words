## 读音
/ˈmʌnθli/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 发生或出现每月一次的；按月的。例如: "I receive a monthly newsletter from the company."
- **副词**: 每月一次地；按月。例如: "The payment needs to be made monthly."

## 短语
- "monthly report" 意为月度报告。
- "monthly subscription" 表示月度订阅服务。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She pays a monthly fee for her gym membership."
- 副词: "The meetings are held monthly on the first Friday of every month."

## 同义词
- Biweekly
- Fortnightly
- Periodically
- Quarterly
- Yearly (但这些词可能表示不同的频率，如每两周、每两星期、定期、每季度、每年)
