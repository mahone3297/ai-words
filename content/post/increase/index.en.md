## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈkriːs/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A growth in size, amount, degree, or value; augmentation or rise. For example: "The company reported a significant increase in profits this quarter."
- **Verb**: To become greater in size, amount, degree, or value; to make something greater. For example: "They plan to increase production by 20% next year." 

### Noun usage examples:
- "There has been a noticeable increase in traffic since the new shopping mall opened."
- "Regular exercise can lead to an increase in muscle mass."

### Verb usage examples:
- "Management decided to increase employee salaries to improve morale."
- "By saving money each month, she increased her savings account balance."

## Phrases
- "Increase in": Refers to the growth or addition within a specific area. Example: "There has been an increase in demand for sustainable products."
- "Increase by": Indicates the magnitude of the growth. Example: "Sales have increased by 50% compared to last year."
- "On the increase": This means that something is growing or becoming more frequent. Example: "Crime rates in the city are on the increase."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The population increase in our town necessitates the construction of more schools."
- **Verb**: "We need to increase our marketing efforts if we want to boost sales."
