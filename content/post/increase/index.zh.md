## 读音
/inˈkriːs/

## 释义
- **动词**: 增加，提高。例如: "The company's profits have increased by 20% this year."
- **名词**: 增加量，增长。例如: "There has been a significant increase in the number of visitors."

## 短语
- "increase in" 表示在某方面的增加。例如: "We need to find ways to increase in efficiency."
- "increase by" 表示增长了多少。例如: "The price has increased by 5 dollars."

## 例句
- 动词: "Our sales have increased due to the new marketing strategy."
- 名词: "The doctor recommended increasing the dosage for better results."
