## 读音
/steɪl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不新鲜的，陈旧的，失去原有风味的。例如: "The bread had gone stale and needed to be thrown away."
- **副词**: 逐渐变得不新鲜地，陈旧地。例如: "The idea had become stale over time."

## 短语
- "stale air" 指的是长时间未流通而变得不新鲜的空气。
- "stalemate" 在棋类游戏中指僵局，也可比喻现实中的僵持状态。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The cookies were still good, but the milk had turned stale."
- 副词: "Their relationship had started to feel stale, so they decided to try something new."

## 同义词
- old
- outdated
- worn out
- tired
- dull
