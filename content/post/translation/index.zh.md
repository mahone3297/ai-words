## 读音
/ˈtræns.lə.ʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 文字、语言或信息的转换或解释过程。例如: "The translation of the ancient text required a scholar with expertise in hieroglyphics."
- **动词**: 将一种语言的文字或内容转换成另一种语言。例如: "She's currently translating a novel from French into English."

## 短语
- "Word-for-word translation" 指逐字翻译，可能不考虑语境。
- "Literal translation" 直译，尽量按照原文表达，可能失去原文的风格和意义。
- "Free translation" 自由翻译，注重传达原文的精神，而不拘泥于字面。

## 例句
- 名词: "The translation of Beowulf into modern English allows more people to appreciate the epic poem."
- 动词: "He spent hours translating the contract from Chinese to English to ensure accuracy."

## 同义词
- Interpretation
- Rendering
- Versioning
- Adaptation
- Transliteration (for sounds or characters, not meaning)
