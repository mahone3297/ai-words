## Pronunciation
/spækspaɪs/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A large vehicle designed for space travel, typically having a crew capsule and rocket engines, used for manned or unmanned missions to explore outer space. [**Oxford**]
- **Verb**: To transport or travel in a spaceship, especially as part of a space mission. [**Merriam-Webster**]

## Phrases
- "Board the spaceship" means to enter the spacecraft for a journey.
- "Spacecraft rendezvous" refers to two spaceships meeting in orbit.
- "A spaceship landing" denotes the arrival of a spaceship on a planet's surface or another celestial body.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The astronauts prepared for their descent from the International Space Station in their spaceship."
- **Verb**: "The spaceship launched successfully into Earth's orbit, marking the start of the long-duration mission."
- **Imperative**: "Everyone, take your seats! The spaceship is about to depart."
