## 读音
/ˈspækspleɪn/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种能够太空旅行的飞行器，通常指载人航天器。例如: "The spaceship carried astronauts to the International Space Station."

## 短语
- "Spaceship Earth" 用来描述地球作为一个在宇宙中的封闭系统。
- "rocket spaceship" 指由火箭推进的宇宙飞船。

## 例句
- "The spaceship launched into orbit around the moon."
- "In science fiction, spaceships often travel faster than the speed of light."
