## 读音
/unˈfɔːrtʃənətli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示不幸地，遗憾地。用来引入一个不愉快的事实或情况。例如: "Unfortunately, it started raining just as we were about to have a picnic."

## 短语
- "Unfortunately, this is not possible" 意味着这种情况无法实现。
- "Unfortunately for them, they lost the game." 表示对他们来说很不幸。

## 例句
- "Unfortunately, the flight was delayed due to bad weather."
- "It's unfortunate that we couldn't attend the wedding."
