## 读音
/riːˈtel/

## 释义
- **动词**: 再述；复述；重讲。例如: "He was asked to retell the story from memory."
- **名词**: 再述；复述的内容。例如: "Her retelling of the event brought tears to our eyes."

## 短语
- "retell a story" 意味着讲述一个已听过或读过的故事。
- "retell events" 指重新叙述一系列发生的事件。

## 例句
- 动词: "The teacher asked the students to retell the fable in their own words."
- 名词: "His retelling of the accident was quite different from the witness's account."
