## 读音
/ɪnˈspekʃən/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 对事物的仔细查看或检查，以确定其状态、质量或合规性。例如: "The building inspector carried out a thorough inspection before issuing a safety certificate."
  - 审视，检阅。例如: "The general inspected the troops before the parade."

## 短语
- "Under inspection" 意为正在接受检查。
- "Carry out an inspection" 意为进行检查。
- "Inspection report" 是指检查报告或检验报告。

## 例句
- 名词: "The customs officials performed a routine inspection on the imported goods."
- 动词（作为动词时通常用作被动语态）: "The car had to undergo a safety inspection before it could be registered."

## 同义词
- Examination
- Check
- Review
- Audit
- Scrutiny
