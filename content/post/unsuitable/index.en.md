## Pronunciation
/ʌnˈsuːtəbl/

## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Meaning**: Not suitable or appropriate for a particular purpose or situation; inappropriate or not fitting.
- **Antonyms**: Suitable, appropriate, appropriate

### Noun (rarely used)
- **Meaning**: An unsuitable person or thing; something that is not suitable.
- **Synonyms**: Inappropriate, unfit, unsuited person/thing

## Phrases
- "Unsuitable for the job"
- "She wore an unsuitable outfit for the formal event"
- "The apartment was found to be unsuitable for a family with young children"

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Her new hairstyle was considered unsuitable for the conservative workplace."
- **Noun**: "The candidate's lack of experience made them an unsuitable choice for the position."

## Synonyms
- Inappropriate
- Incongruous
- Unfitting
- Improper
- Irrelevant

## Note
While the noun form of "unsuitable" is less commonly used, it can still be found in some contexts to refer to a person or thing that is not suitable. However, the primary usage of "unsuitable" is as an adjective.
