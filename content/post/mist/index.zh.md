## 读音
/mɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 薄雾；轻雾。例如: "A light mist covered the lake early in the morning."
2. 错误；误解。例如: "There was a mist of confusion about the new policy."

- **形容词**:
模糊的；不清楚的。例如: "The misty memory of their childhood friendship remained."

## 短语
- "in the mist" 意为在雾中，难以看清。例如: "The old castle seemed to emerge from the mist."
- "mist over" 表示（眼睛）变得模糊。例如: "Her eyes misted over as she recalled her late husband."

## 例句
- 名词: "The early morning mist slowly lifted, revealing the beauty of the valley."
- 形容词: "The misty mountain landscape looked enchanting in the soft morning light."
