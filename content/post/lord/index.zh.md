## 读音
/lɔːrd/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 
    - 贵族，勋爵；英国贵族院议员。例如: "He was addressed as Lord Smith by his peers."
    - （某些宗教中的）主，神。例如: "They sang hymns to the Lord."

- **动词 (v)**: 
    - 统治，主宰。例如: "He lorded over his subjects with an iron fist."

## 短语
- "lord it over someone" 意味着以权威或优越感对待别人。
- "at the lord's mercy" 表示在某人的完全掌控之下。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new Lord of the estate was well-respected among the locals."
- 动词: "He lorded his wealth and power over those less fortunate."
