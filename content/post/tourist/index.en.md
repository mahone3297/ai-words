## Pronunciation
/tʊəˈrɪst/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who travels to visit or enjoy the sights, especially in a foreign country. ["A tourist admiring the Eiffel Tower."](https://www.example.com/eiffel-tower-tourist)
- **Verb**: To travel for pleasure or leisure, often as a tourist. ["The family decided to go on a tourist trip to Europe this summer."](https://www.example.com/europe-tourism)

## Phrases
- "Go on a tourist tour" means to visit various tourist attractions as part of a planned itinerary.
- "Be a tourist in your own city" suggests exploring one's hometown like a visitor.
- "Tourist season" refers to the peak travel period when many tourists visit a destination.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The city was bustling with tourists during the festival."
- **Verb**: "As a tourist, she tried local cuisine and visited historical sites."
- **Adjective**: "The tourist-friendly hotel offered guided tours."

## Synonyms
- Traveler
- Sightseer
- Holidaymaker
- Visitor
- Explorer

## Antonyms
- Local resident
- Resident
- Native (in the context of tourism)
