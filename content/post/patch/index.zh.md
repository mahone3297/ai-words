## 读音
/ˈpætʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一块小地方，一小片：A small area or section, such as a piece of land.
  - 补丁，修补程序：A piece of cloth or other material used to repair a torn or worn part; in computing, a software update to fix errors.

- **动词**:
  - 修补，缝补：To mend or repair by applying a patch.
  - 给软件打补丁：To apply a software patch to fix issues.

- **形容词**:
  - 补丁状的，拼凑的：Consisting of patches or made up of various elements; pieced together.

## 短语
- "patch up" 意为修复关系或物品。
- "patch something together" 表示仓促地组合或修理。

## 例句
- 名词: "He sewed a small patch on his jeans to cover the hole."
- 动词: "The programmer applied a patch to the software to resolve the bug."
- 形容词: "Her dress was a patchwork of different fabrics."
