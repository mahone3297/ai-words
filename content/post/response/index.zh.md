## 读音
/rɪˈspɑːns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 对某种行动、问题或请求的答复或反应。例如: "She gave a positive response to his suggestion."
- **动词**: 做出回答或反应。例如: "The audience responded enthusiastically to the speaker's speech."

## 短语
- "Immediate response" 表示立即的回应。
- "Positive/negative response" 分别指正面或负面的回答。
- "In response to" 意为作为对...的回应。例如: "They took action in response to the customer's complaint."

## 例句
- 名词: "We're waiting for the company's response to our proposal."
- 动词: "He responded to my email within an hour."
- 介词短语: "She sang in response to his request."
