## 读音
/rɪˈzuːm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 个人简历，详述工作经历和教育背景的文件。例如: "Please send me your resume when applying for the job."
- **动词**: 继续，重新开始（之前中断的活动）。例如: "After the break, we will resume our discussion."

## 短语
- "to resume work" 意味着重新开始工作。
- "resume a conversation" 表示继续之前的对话。

## 例句
- 名词: "She spent hours perfecting her resume before sending it to potential employers."
- 动词: "The meeting was interrupted, but we resumed after lunch."

## 同义词
- Curriculum Vitae (CV)
- biodata
- vita
- personal history
