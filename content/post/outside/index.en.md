## Pronunciation
/ˌaʊtˈsaɪd/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Located or happening beyond the confines of a building, room, or other defined space; not inside. For example: "The children are playing outside."
  - Weather-related usage: Referring to weather conditions that are not within a sheltered area. "It's raining heavily outside."
- **Noun**: The external part or area of something, often referring to the outdoors. For example: "We're planning a barbecue in the outside this weekend."
  
## Phrases
- "Go outside": Leave an indoor space to be in the open air or outdoor environment.
- "Outside of": Beyond or except for something. For instance: "Outside of work, he enjoys hiking and painting."
- "Outside influences": External factors that affect something, often negatively. "The project failed due to too many outside influences."

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Make sure to take your umbrella because it's still wet outside."
- **Noun**: "They set up a table and chairs on the outside deck."
