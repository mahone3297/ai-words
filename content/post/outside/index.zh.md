## 读音
/ˌaʊtˈsaɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 在外面的；外部的。例如: "There's a beautiful garden outside the house."
- **副词**: 在外面；在外部。例如: "She likes to sit outside on sunny days."
- **名词**: 外部；外表。例如: "The outside of the building is very modern."
- **介词**: 表示在…之外。例如: "Put the trash outside the door."

## 短语
- "on the outside" 意味着表面上或外部。例如: "She looked calm on the outside but was nervous inside."
- "outside chance" 指可能性很小的机会。例如: "There's only an outside chance that we'll win the lottery."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The temperature drops significantly when you go outside during winter."
- 副词: "The children were playing outside in the park."
- 名词: "He painted a lovely scene of the outside world on the canvas."
