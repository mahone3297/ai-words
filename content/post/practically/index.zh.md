## 读音
/præktɪˈkli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 几乎，实际上，事实上。例如: "The car is practically new; it's only been driven a few times." 
- **形容词**: 实际的，实用的。例如: "She has practically no experience in this field."

## 短语
- "Practically speaking" 意为从实际角度来看。
- "Practically everyone" 意为几乎所有人。

## 例句
- 副词: "I've eaten so much that I'm practically full."
- 形容词: "This tool is practically useless without proper instructions."

- Almost
- Nearly
- Virtually
- In effect
- For all intents and purposes
