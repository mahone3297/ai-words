## 读音
/rul/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 规则，规定：A principle or regulation that governs behavior or procedure. 例如: "The school has strict rules about uniform."
  - 标准，准则：A principle or standard by which something is judged or assessed. 例如: "Her work is measured against industry rules."
  - 裁判（尤指体育比赛）：An official in charge of supervising a game or match. 例如: "The referee blew the whistle to start the game."
  - 条纹，斑纹：A line or stripe, especially one of a different color or texture from the surrounding surface. 例如: "The zebra's distinctive black and white stripes."

- **动词**:
  - 统治，管理：To exercise authority or control over; govern. 例如: "The queen ruled the kingdom with wisdom and fairness."
  - 划线，标出：To draw a line or lines on, especially as a guide for cutting or marking out. 例如: "He ruled the paper before writing."

## 短语
- "Follow the rules" 遵守规则。
- "Break the rule" 违反规则。
- "Set a rule" 制定规则。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's dress code states that employees must follow the dress rules."
- 动词: "She ruled the page with a ruler to make straight lines."
