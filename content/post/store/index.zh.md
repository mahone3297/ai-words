## 读音
/stɔːr/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 商店，超市。例如: "I need to go to the grocery store to buy some milk."
            2. 存储空间，仓库。例如: "There's not enough space in the store for all these boxes."
            3. 货源，库存。例如: "The store of wood will last us through the winter."
            4. （计算机）存储，储存。例如: "The computer stores data on its hard drive."

- **动词 (v)**: 储存，存放。例如: "I'm going to store these books on the top shelf."
            5. 准备以备将来使用。例如: "We should store some food and water in case of an emergency."

## 短语
- "store away" 储存，收起。例如: "Store away your winter clothes when summer comes."
- "in store for sb" 将要发生在某人身上的事情。例如: "She had no idea what adventures were in store for her."

## 例句
- 名词: "Let's go to the department store to buy some new clothes."
- 动词: "I'll store these old photos in a safe place."
- 介词短语: "What surprises do you have in store for me today?"
