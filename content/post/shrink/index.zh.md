## 读音
/ʃrɪŋk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 缩小，收缩：减少尺寸、体积或数量。例如: "The sweater shrank in the wash."
  - 脱水：使失去水分而变小。例如: "Don't leave the flowers in direct sunlight or they'll shrink."
  - 感到恐惧或不自在：感到害怕或不安。例如: "He shrank back when he saw the snake."

## 短语
- "to shrink away" 从…退缩，避开。例如: "She shrank away from the angry dog."
- "to shrink in size" 缩小尺寸。例如: "The fabric shrank in size after being washed."

## 例句
- 动词: "The shirt shrank after I washed it in hot water."
- 感到恐惧或不自在: "He shrank at the thought of public speaking."
