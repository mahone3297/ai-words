## 读音
/mərˈoʊvər/

## 释义
- **连词**: 表示添加进一步的信息或论点，通常用于引出一个递进的观点。例如: "She is not only intelligent, but moreover, she is very hardworking."

## 短语
- "Moreover,..." 用于引出额外的、支持性的信息。

## 例句
- "He has experience in the field, and moreover, he possesses excellent communication skills."

## 同义词
- Furthermore
- Additionally
- Besides
- In addition
- Also
