## 读音
/θriː/

## 释义
- **数词**: 表示数字3，指三个。例如: "There are three apples on the table."

## 短语
- "Third time's the charm" 意为第三次尝试往往能成功。
- "One, two, three..." 常用来计数或开始某种活动。

## 例句
- 作为数词使用: "We need three more volunteers for the project."
- 在短语中: "On the third attempt, he finally managed to solve the puzzle."

## 同义词
- Two plus one
- A trio
- A couple and one more
- Ternary (数学术语，表示基数为3的系统)
