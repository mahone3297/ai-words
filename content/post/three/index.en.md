## Definitions
- **Numeral**: A word or symbol representing the number three. For example: "There are three apples on the table."
- **Adjective**: Indicating a quantity or position as the third in a series. For example: "The third book from the left is mine."
- **Determiner**: Referring to a specific, identified item or group as the one out of several. For example: "This is the three-bedroom apartment we're looking for."

## Phrases
- "In three steps" means after three actions or movements.
- "A set of three" refers to a group consisting of three items.
- "The three of us" signifies a collective involving three individuals.
