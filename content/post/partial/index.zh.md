## 读音
/pərˈʃənl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 部分的，不完全的；偏爱的，有偏见的；私人的，个人的。例如: "He has some partial solutions to the problem."，"She is very partial to chocolate ice cream."，"This is my personal opinion."

## 短语
- "partial to" 意味对某事有偏好或特别喜欢。例如: "She's partial to classical music."
- "partial view" 指部分视野或不完整的观点。
- "personal belongings" 指个人财物。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The report only provides a partial analysis of the situation."
- "She is so partial to her youngest child that it upsets the others."
- "Please keep your personal information confidential."

## 同义词
- Fractional
- Incomplete
- Biased
- Subjective
- Individual
