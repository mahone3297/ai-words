## 读音
/skweər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 正方形；四方形。例如: "The park has a beautiful green square in the center."
   2. 广场；市中心的开阔地。例如: "They met at the town square for the annual festival."
   3. (数学)平方；乘积。例如: "The square of 5 is 25."

- **动词**: 使成正方形；调整一致。例如: "She squared her shoulders and walked confidently."
- **形容词**: 正方形的；方正的。例如: "The table has a square shape."

## 短语
- "Square off" 意为对抗或准备比赛。例如: "The boxers squared off in the ring."
- "In a square" 表示在正方形的形状内或正直的状态下。例如: "The building stands firmly in a square."
- "To square something with someone" 意为与某人达成一致或解决分歧。例如: "I need to square my plans with my boss before making a decision."

## 例句
- 名词: "The child drew a simple square in his coloring book."
- 动词: "The architect squared the corners of the building to ensure precision."
- 形容词: "The quilt had squares of different colored fabrics sewn together."
