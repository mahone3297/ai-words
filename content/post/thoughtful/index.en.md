## Pronunciation
/thəʊˈful/

## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Thoughtful**: Displaying or characterized by careful thoughtfulness; considerate and attentive. For example: "A thoughtful gift shows that you really understand their needs."
- **Thought-provoking**: Causing one to think deeply or carefully about something. For example: "The book's themes were both thoughtful and thought-provoking."

## Phrases
- "Be thoughtful of others" means to be considerate and empathetic towards others.
- "A thoughtful gesture" refers to a kind action that demonstrates care.
- "Thoughtful silence" implies reflective quietness.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "She was a thoughtful friend who always remembered important dates."
- **Adjective (as a complement)**: "His writing was filled with thoughtful insights on life."
- **Noun (as a subject)**: "Her thoughtful letter conveyed her heartfelt appreciation."
