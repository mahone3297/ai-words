## 读音
/ˈθɔːtfəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 表示深思熟虑的，体贴入微的，考虑周到的。例如: "She was thoughtful enough to bring me a cup of tea when I was feeling ill."
- **名词**: 有时作名词使用，指深思的人或行为。例如: "His thoughtful nature made him a great problem solver."

## 短语
- "be thoughtful of sb." 意为关心某人，为某人着想。
- "a thoughtful gesture" 指的是体贴的举动。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Tom is always so thoughtful; he remembered my birthday and sent me a card."
- 名词: "Her thoughtful approach to the project impressed the whole team."
