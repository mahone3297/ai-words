## 读音
/pæd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 便携式电脑或平板电脑。例如: "I'm typing this on my new tablet pad."
  - 写字板，画板，尤指有衬垫的。例如: "The kids used a drawing pad to create their artwork."
  - 鞋垫，垫子。例如: "She put a new pad in her running shoes for more comfort."

- **动词**:
  - 融入额外的内容以增加量或填充。例如: "The writer padded the story with unnecessary details."
  - 在鞋里添加衬垫。例如: "He padded his shoes to protect his injured foot."

## 短语
- "padding" 指在文本、衣物或其他物品中增加的填充物。
- "pad out" 意味着扩展或加厚，通常用于文字或内容。

## 例句
- 名词: "I use a digital pad to take notes during meetings."
- 动词: "The athlete padded his knee to prevent further injury."
