## Pronunciation
/tʌm/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A burial place, typically underground, for a deceased person, often with a monument above it. 2. A memorial structure, monument, or vault where the remains of a person are interred. 3. (figurative) An end, conclusion, or final resting place. 4. A type of building or room used for storage or display of objects.

## Phrases
- "Be buried in a tomb" refers to being interred after death.
- "Tombstone" or "headstone" is a marker placed above a tomb.
- "Eternal rest in peace" is a phrase often said at a tomb.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The ancient tomb was filled with treasures from a bygone era."
- **Verb**: "After the funeral, the family laid the ashes to rest in a simple tomb."
- **Figurative**: "His career came to an abrupt tomb when he was fired."
