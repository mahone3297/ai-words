## 读音
/tuːm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 墓穴，坟墓。通常是一个建造来安放死者的结构。例如: "The ancient tomb was discovered by archaeologists."
- **名词**: （比喻）死亡的象征或终结。例如: "His career seemed to be in its tomb after the scandal."

## 短语
- "tombstone" 指墓碑，立在墓上的标记。
- "lost in the tombs" 指被遗忘在历史的尘埃中。

## 例句
- "The explorers entered the dark and mysterious tomb, hoping to find lost treasures."
- "Her name was engraved on a beautiful tombstone in the peaceful cemetery."
