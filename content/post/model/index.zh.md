## 读音
/ˈmɑːdl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 模型，模范：A representation or copy of something, often used as a pattern or example. 例如: "The toy car was a model of a real sports car."
  2. 女模，模特儿：A person, typically a woman, who displays clothing or other products. 例如: "She works as a fashion model for top designers."
  3. 概念模型：A theoretical structure used to explain a concept or system. 例如: "The economic model predicts market trends."
  
- **动词**:
  1. 建立模型，模仿：To create a model of something or imitate someone's behavior. 例如: "The students were asked to model their projects after successful businesses."
  2. 担任模特：To serve as a model for artists, photographers, or designers. 例如: "She modeled for a local art class."

## 短语
- "role model" 指榜样或楷模。
- "machine learning model" 在计算机科学中指的是训练后的算法模型。

## 例句
- 名词: "The architect presented a detailed model of the new skyscraper."
- 动词: "He modeled his business strategy after successful entrepreneurs."
- 概念模型: "The climate change model predicts rising sea levels."
