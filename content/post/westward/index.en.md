## Pronunciation
/westˈwɜːrd/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Directed towards the west; moving in a westerly direction. For example: "The travelers journeyed westward towards the sunset."
- **Adjective**: Relating to or in the direction of the west. For example: "The wind blew westward, carrying seeds across the landscape."

## Phrases
- "Travel westward" implies moving from east to west.
- "Set sail westward" is a maritime expression to head west.
- "Grow up westward" can mean someone's upbringing in a western location.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "The expedition camped westward of the river to avoid flooding."
- **Adjective**: "The sun sets in the westward horizon every evening."
- "She remembered her childhood spent exploring the vast westward prairies."
