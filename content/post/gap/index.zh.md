## 读音
/ɡæp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 空隙，间隙：A space or opening between things or parts of something. 例如: "There's a gap in the fence."
  - 差距，鸿沟：A difference or disparity, especially between two ideas, concepts, or groups. 例如: "The generation gap between young and old."
  - 缺口，空白：An interruption in continuity or a missing part. 例如: "There was a gap in his employment history."

- **动词**:
  - 形成空隙：To create or leave a gap. 例如: "The falling rock gapped the road."
  - 跳过或错过（某段时间）：To skip or miss (a period of time). 例如: "She decided to gap a year before starting college."

## 短语
- "bridge the gap" 意为弥合差距或连接两个事物。
- "gap year" 指在学业之间休学一年的时间。

## 例句
- 名词: "The gap in the wall allowed us to see the view beyond."
- 动词: "The train gapped the station, causing passengers to miss their boarding."
- 感叹词: "Gap! I almost forgot my keys!"
