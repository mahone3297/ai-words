## 读音
/ˈspɪrɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 精神，灵魂：The spirit of teamwork is essential for success.
    - 酒精饮料，烈酒：She enjoys a glass of brandy, a type of spirit.
    - 情绪，态度：He was in high spirits after winning the game.

- **形容词**:
    - 精神上的，无形的：We talked about spiritual matters.
    - 勇敢的，有决心的：She showed great spirit in facing her challenges.

## 短语
- "In high spirits" 意为情绪高涨，心情愉快。
- "The spirit of the law" 指法律的精神或意图，而不仅仅是字面意义。
- "Lift one's spirits" 表示振奋精神或鼓舞士气。

## 例句
- 名词: "The athletes were inspired by the spirit of competition."
- 形容词: "She demonstrated great spirit when she stood up to her opponent."
