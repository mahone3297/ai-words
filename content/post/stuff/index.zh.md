## 读音
/stʌf/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 物品，材料：指的是物品、布料、填充物等。例如: "She bought some new stuff for her wardrobe."
  - 内容，东西：指事物的内容或组成部分。例如: "There's some interesting stuff in this book."
  - 人员，家伙：非正式地指一群人或个人。例如: "The whole staff at the restaurant is great stuff."

- **动词**:
  - 填充，塞满：将东西填入或塞进某个容器或空间。例如: "Stuff the turkey with herbs before roasting it."
  - 拿，抓：迅速拿起或抓住。例如: "Just stuff your things and let's go."

## 短语
- "Stuff and nonsense" 意为无稽之谈，胡说八道。
- "Stuff something into something" 表示快速放入或塞进。例如: "Stuff your coat into the bag."

## 例句
- 名词: "The store had a sale on all kinds of fabric stuff."
- 动词: "He stuffed the cushions with soft feathers."
- 感叹词（非标准用法）: "Oh, stuff it! I've had enough of this argument."

## 同义词
- Material

- Nonsense (贬义，指荒谬的言论)
