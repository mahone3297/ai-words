## 读音
/ɪntərˈækʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 相互作用；互动。例如: "There was a positive interaction between the teacher and students."
- **动词**: 互相影响或交流。例如: "The two chemicals interact to produce a new substance."

## 短语
- "Social interaction" 指人际交往。
- "User interaction" 在技术领域指的是用户与设备的交互。
- "Chemical interaction" 指化学反应中的相互作用。

## 例句
- 名词: "The success of the project depends on effective team interaction."
- 动词: "The software allows for seamless interaction with the database."
