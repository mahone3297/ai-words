## 读音
/ˈpɪɡ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 1. 猪，通常指家猪。例如: "The farmer raised a dozen pigs on his farm."
   - 2. (贬义) 指贪吃的人或行为粗鲁的人。例如: "He's such a pig, always leaving the mess for others to clean up."

## 短语
- "fat as a pig" 形容人非常胖。
- "hogwash" 指无稽之谈或废话。

## 例句
- 名词: "The little piglet followed its mother around the pen."
- 贬义: "Stop being so pig-headed and listen to what I'm saying!"
