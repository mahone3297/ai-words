## 读音
/ˈnɑːnsəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 无意义的话，废话，胡言乱语。例如: "Don't talk nonsense, we need a practical solution."
- **形容词**: 没有意义的，荒谬的。例如: "That's complete nonsense; it doesn't make any sense."

## 短语
- "Nonsense talk" 指毫无根据或不切实际的谈话。
- "Make nonsense of" 意为使…显得荒谬，嘲笑。

## 例句
- 名词: "The child kept repeating the same nonsense rhyme over and over again."
- 形容词: "The idea that the earth is flat is pure nonsense."

- Rubbish
