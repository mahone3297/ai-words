## Word: Joke

## Pronunciation
/dʒoʊk/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A humorous story, statement, or act intended to make people laugh; a jest. For example: "He told a funny joke that made everyone burst out laughing."
- **Verb**: To speak or tell something in a playful or humorous manner with the intention of amusing others. For example: "He joked about his terrible cooking skills."
- **Noun (informal)**: Something that is not serious or genuine, often meant to deceive or tease. For example: "Don't take it seriously, it was just a joke."

## Phrases
- "Pull someone's leg" means to jokingly deceive or tease someone.
- "A practical joke" or "prank" refers to a mischievous trick played on someone for amusement.
- "Make a joke of" means to treat something unseriously or mockingly.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her repertoire of jokes never fails to lighten the mood at parties."
- **Verb**: "When things got tense, he joked around to relieve the stress."
- **Noun (informal)**: "At first, we thought he was serious, but then we realized he was just making a joke."
