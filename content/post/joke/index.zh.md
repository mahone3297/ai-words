## 读音
/joʊk/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种幽默或讽刺的话语，通常为了引人发笑。例如: "He told a funny joke to lighten the mood."
- **动词 (v)**: 开玩笑，讲笑话。例如: "Don't joke about serious matters."

## 短语
- "Tell a joke" 意为讲一个笑话。
- "It's no joke" 用来强调某事不是闹着玩的，很严重。

## 例句
- 名词: "The comedian's opening joke had the audience in stitches."
- 动词: "She was just joking when she said she'd run for president."
