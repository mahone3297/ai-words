## 读音
/ˈnʌn/

## 释义
- **代词**: 表示“没有一个人”或“没有任何事物”。例如: "There was none left in the fridge." 
- **名词**: 指数量为零的人或物。例如: "I have none of that book." 
- **否定强化词**: 用来强调完全没有。例如: "I saw none of my friends at the party."

## 短语
- "None of your business" 意为“不关你的事”。
- "None other than" 用来强调特定的人或事物。例如: "The speaker was none other than the president himself."

## 例句
- 代词: "None of the students answered the question correctly."
- 名词: "There were none available when I went to buy them."
- 否定强化词: "I expected some guests, but none showed up."
