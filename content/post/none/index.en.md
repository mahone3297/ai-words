## Word: None

## Pronunciation
/naʊn/

## Definitions
- **Determiner**: Used to indicate the absence of something or to refer to no person or thing in particular. For example: "None of the books were interesting."
- **Pronoun**: A word that takes the place of a noun or noun phrase, often used to indicate that zero or not any members of a group or category apply. For example: "None of them came to the party."

### Phrases
- "None but the brave deserve the fair" implies only the bravest people deserve the best things in life.
- "None other than" is used to emphasize who or what someone or something is. For example: "It was none other than the famous author herself who called me."
  
## Example Sentences
- **Determiner**: "There were many candidates, but none had the required qualifications."
- **Pronoun**: "I invited several friends over, but none showed up."

- No one
- Not any
- Nobody
- Nothing (in certain contexts)
