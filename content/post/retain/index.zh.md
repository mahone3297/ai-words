## 读音
/riːˈteɪn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 保持；保留。例如: "They retained their original culture over the years."
2. 记住；回忆。例如: "She could retain information easily."
3. 雇佣；聘请。例如: "The company retained a lawyer for legal advice."
4. 保持不变；维持。例如: "The building has retained its historic charm."

## 短语
- "Retain one's composure" 保持镇静。
- "Retain a lawyer" 雇佣律师。
- "Retain water" 保水，如在减肥时体内储存水分。

## 例句
- 保持："Despite the challenges, they managed to retain their market share."
- 记忆："After reading the text once, he was able to retain most of the key points."
- 雇佣："They decided to retain an experienced consultant for the project."
- 保持不变："The old house has retained its original wooden floors."
