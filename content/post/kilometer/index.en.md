## Word: Kilometer

## Pronunciation
/ˈkɪləˌmiːtər/

## Definitions
- **Noun (n)**: A unit of length equal to 1000 meters or approximately 0.621 miles, commonly used to measure distances traveled on land, especially for longer distances.

Example Sentences:
- "The marathon race was 42 kilometers long."
- "We drove 500 kilometers to reach our holiday destination."

## Phrases
- "Per kilometer" - used when referring to something measured or calculated for each kilometer, such as fuel consumption or cost per kilometer driven.
- "Within a radius of X kilometers" - expressing a distance around a specific point.
- "Kilometer zero" - the central point from which distances are traditionally measured in a city or country.

## Example Sentences
- "The cost per kilometer for this rental car is quite reasonable."
- "All locations within a 10-kilometer radius of the city center were searched."

## Synonyms
- KM (abbreviation)
- K (abbreviation)
- kilometre (British English spelling)
- mile (alternative unit of length, used primarily in the US) 
- klick (informal, especially military usage) 

Note that while synonyms like "mile" denote a different unit of length, they can be used contextually to express similar concepts of measuring distances.
