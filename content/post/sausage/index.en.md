## Pronunciation
/sɔːs/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **sausage** is a cylindrical food product made from ground meat (usually pork, beef, or a mixture), often combined with spices, herbs, and fillings, then enclosed in a casing and cooked. It can be served as a standalone dish or as part of a meal, such as in sandwiches or on a platter.

### Verb
To **make sausages** is the process of preparing and shaping the mixture into the desired form, while **grilling, frying, or baking** a sausage refers to the cooking method.

### Adjective
The term **sausagey** can be used to describe something having a strong or distinct sausage flavor or texture.

## Phrases
- "Sausage roll" is a popular dish consisting of a sausage-shaped pastry.
- "Bratwurst" or "hot dog" are specific types of sausages.
- "Sausage fest" is a humorous term for a gathering where many men are present, often used lightheartedly.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I bought some fresh sausages from the butcher shop."
- **Verb**: "He decided to make his own sausages using family recipes."
- **Adjective**: "The air smelled delicious with the aroma of sizzling sausage."
