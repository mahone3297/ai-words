## 读音
/hʌni/

## 释义
- **名词**: 蜂蜜，一种由蜜蜂从花蜜中制成的甜味物质。例如: "I love to drizzle honey on my toast in the morning."
- **名词**: 宠爱的人，宝贝（用于亲昵称呼）。例如: "My honey, will you pass me the salt?"
- **形容词**: 亲爱的，甜心（作为爱称或表示亲切）。例如: "Honey, can you help me with this?"

## 短语
- "sweet as honey" 形容极其甜美或可爱。
- "honey bunny" 或 "honey pie" 是对亲密伴侣或孩子的爱称。

## 例句
- 名词: "The bees produce honey by fanning their wings to evaporate the water from nectar."
- 形容词: "You're such a honey, thank you for helping me."
