## 读音
/rɪˈɡɑːrdɪŋ/

## 释义
- **介词**: 关于，就...而言。例如: "Regarding your question, I'll provide an answer later."

## 短语
- "regarding the matter" 意指关于这个事情。
- "with regard to" 与 regarding 类似，表示对某个主题的关注。

## 例句
- "Regarding the project, we need to discuss the timeline in more detail."
- "Please send me all the information regarding the upcoming conference."

## 同义词
- Concerning
- About
- In relation to
- As to
- Relative to
