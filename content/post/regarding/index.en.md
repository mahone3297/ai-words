## Pronunciation
/rɪˈgɑːrdɪŋ/

## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Referring to** or **pertaining to**: Expressing concern or relevance to something or someone. For example: "The discussion focused on issues regarding climate change."
- **Involving** or **concerning**: Describing a matter that relates to a particular subject. For example: "There were several questions regarding the new policy."

### Preposition
- **With respect to**: Expressing connection or comparison to a particular topic or matter. For example: "We need to discuss the project regarding the budget allocation."
- **About or with regard to**: Introducing a subject or matter for consideration. For example: "Please provide feedback regarding your experience."

## Phrases
- "Regarding your question..."
- "A matter regarding personnel decisions"
- "She has some concerns regarding the safety measures"

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The report delved into various aspects regarding the company's financial performance."
- **Preposition**: "I wanted to speak to you regarding the issue we discussed earlier."
- "They consulted experts regarding the best course of action."
