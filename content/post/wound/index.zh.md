## 读音
/waʊnd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 创伤，伤口；伤害。例如: "He received a deep wound on his arm during the accident."
- **动词**: 使受伤，伤害。例如: "The bullet wounded the soldier in the leg."
- **形容词**: 受伤的，有伤的。例如: "The wounded soldiers were immediately taken to the hospital."

## 短语
- "Close the wound" 意为封闭或包扎伤口。
- "Wound up" 在此作为动词短语，表示紧张或激动的状态，例如: "She was completely wound up after the stressful day."

## 例句
- 名词: "The doctor carefully dressed the wound to prevent infection."
- 动词: "The arrow wounded the deer, causing it to flee."
- 形容词: "The nurse was attending to the wounded soldiers on the battlefield."
