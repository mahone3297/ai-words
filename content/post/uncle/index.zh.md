## 读音
/ˈʌŋkl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 成年男子的兄弟，通常是叔叔或伯父。例如: "My uncle John is visiting from Australia."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与叔叔有关的。例如: "I have some old photos of my uncle's farm."

## 短语
- "Uncle Bob" 指具体的叔叔鲍勃。
- "My uncle's house" 指我叔叔的房子。

## 例句
- 名词: "The kids were excited to see their favorite uncle at the family gathering."
- 形容词: "The uncle's stories about his travels were fascinating."
