## Word: Jacket

## Pronunciation
/ˈdʒækɪt/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A type of clothing worn on the upper part of the body, typically extending below the waistline and often having sleeves and a collar. It's usually worn over other clothes for warmth, protection from the weather, or as a fashion accessory. For example: "She wore her favorite denim jacket to the concert."

Example Sentence (as a Noun):
- "He put on his waterproof jacket before heading out into the rain."

## Phrases
- "A suit jacket" refers to the tailored outer garment that forms part of a man's suit.
- "Wearing one's heart on one's sleeve" can metaphorically refer to revealing one's emotions openly, but literally may mean wearing a jacket with an expressive design or patch on the sleeve.
- "The cherry on top" could be a metaphorical reference to a jacket being the final touch to complete an outfit.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The school uniform requires students to wear a blazer-style jacket."
- "After the intense workout, he removed his sweat-soaked running jacket."
