## Pronunciation
/vəˈkeɪʃən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A period of time when people take a break from work or regular routine to relax, travel, or engage in leisure activities. ["A vacation to the beach is on our agenda this summer."]
- **Verb**: To take a vacation or go on holiday. ["They're planning to vacation in Europe next year."]
- **Adjective**: Characterizing a break or time off, typically for leisure. ["The company offers generous vacation time to its employees."]

## Phrases
- "Take a vacation" or "go on vacation"
- "Use up your vacation days"
- "Go on a winter/vacation retreat"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After working hard all year, they were looking forward to their much-needed vacation."
- **Verb**: "We decided to vacation at a remote cabin in the mountains."
- **Adjective**: "Her job allows for flexible working hours, which she appreciates, especially during her family's annual vacation."

## Synonyms
- Holiday
- Break
- Getaway
- Leisure time
- Time off

## Antonyms
- Work
- Duty
