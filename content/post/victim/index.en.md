## Pronunciation
/vɪktɪm/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A person who has suffered harm, injury, or loss as a result of an accident, crime, or other unfortunate event.
   - "The victim of the car crash was rushed to the hospital."
2. A person or thing that is subjected or affected by something: 
   - "The victim of discrimination often feels powerless."

### Verb
- (Rarely used) To be the target or suffer harm: "The company became a victim of fraud."

## Phrases
