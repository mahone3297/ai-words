## 读音
/vɪktɪm/

## 释义
- **名词 n**: 受害者；受骗者；罹难者。指在犯罪、事故或其他不幸事件中受到伤害或损失的人。
    - 例如: "The victim of the robbery was left shaken and injured."
    - 例如: "She felt like a victim of circumstance rather than a participant in her own life."

## 短语
- "crime victim" 指犯罪行为的受害者。
- "fall victim to something" 意味着遭受某事的影响或欺骗。
- "helpless victim" 指无力抵抗或逃避不幸情况的人。

## 例句
- "The police were searching for the suspect who had fled after attacking the victim."
- "She was a victim of online fraud and lost all her savings."
- "Many innocent people fell victim to the economic crisis."

## 同义词
- Target
- Sufferer
- Casualty
- Survivor (在负面情境中的幸存者)
- prey (尤指被掠食的对象)
