## 读音
/ˈdʒʌdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 法官；裁判员；评判者。例如: "The judge presided over the court proceedings."
- **动词**: 判断；评价；裁决。例如: "She judged the competition's winners based on their performances."
- **及物动词**: 审判；断定。例如: "The judge ruled that the defendant was guilty."

## 短语
- "to judge by something" 根据...判断。例如: "By his expression, I judged he was angry."
- "to judge from something" 从...判断。例如: "Judging from her tone, she wasn't happy about it."

## 例句
- 名词: "The judge's decision was final and binding."
- 动词: "He judged the situation incorrectly and made a wrong call."
- 及物动词: "The judge sentenced the criminal to ten years in prison."
