## Pronunciation
/stɪˈmjuːleɪt/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To incite or encourage activity, growth, or response; to arouse or激发. 
- **Noun**: An act or process that stimulates; a stimulus.

## Phrases
- "Stimulate interest" means to pique someone's curiosity or enthusiasm.
- "Physiological stimulation" refers to the activation of bodily functions.
- "Cognitive stimulation" is the process of engaging the mind to improve mental abilities.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "The teacher stimulated her students' creativity with a project-based approach."
- **Noun**: "The new workout routine provided a strong physical stimulus for his muscles."
- "The sound of music stimulated his memory, bringing back nostalgic memories."
