## 读音
/stɪˈmjuːleɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 刺激；激发：促进（生长、活动等）；使活跃。例如: "Physical exercise can stimulate the immune system."
  2. 促进；鼓舞：激励（兴趣、思考等）。例如: "The discussion stimulated new ideas among the participants."

## 短语
- "stimulate growth" 促进增长。
- "stimulate the economy" 刺激经济。
- "stimulate creativity" 激发创造力。

## 例句
- "The teacher's engaging lessons always stimulate the students' curiosity."
- "The government plans to stimulate the economy by investing in infrastructure projects."
- "The medication was designed to stimulate his appetite."
