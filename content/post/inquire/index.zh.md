## 读音
/inˈkwaɪr/

## 释义
- **动词**: 查问；询问。例如: "I need to inquire about the hotel's cancellation policy."
- **名词**: 查询；询问。例如: "They received several inquiries about the product."

## 短语
- "Make an inquiry" 意为进行询问或调查。
- "Inquire within" 常见于商店招牌，意思是店内有更多信息可询问。

## 例句
- 动词: "Please inquire at the front desk for directions to your room."
- 名词: "The customer service department handles all inquiries during business hours."
