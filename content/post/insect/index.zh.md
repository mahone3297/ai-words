## 读音
/ˈɪnsekt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 小型无脊椎动物，通常有六条腿，分为头、胸和腹三部分，如昆虫纲中的蝴蝶、蚂蚁和蜜蜂。例如: "The bee is an important insect for pollination."
   
## 短语
- "insect repellent" 防虫剂，用来驱赶昆虫的化学物质。
- "insecticide" 杀虫剂，用于杀死昆虫的化学药品。

## 例句
- "The gardener applied insecticide to protect his plants from insects."
- "Children were fascinated by the colorful insects they found in the forest."

## 同义词
- Bug
- Arthropod
- Creepy-crawly
- Critter
- Worm (在某些语境下)
