## 读音
/bʌd/

## 释义
- **名词**: 植物的嫩芽或花蕾，通常指未展开的部分。例如: "The rose bush has several buds ready to bloom."
- **名词**: 朋友或伙伴的非正式称呼，尤其是年轻或新的朋友。例如: "My new bud and I are going to the movies."
- **动词**: 形成或发展成芽。例如: "The tree is just beginning to bud in the spring."

## 短语
- "bud off" 指植物从主干上长出新芽。
- "budding friendship" 指正在形成中的友谊。

## 例句
- 名词: "The apple tree was covered with small green buds."
- 动词: "The vine began to bud with tiny purple flowers."
- 名词（非正式用法）: "Tom and Jerry became buds after their first day of work together."
