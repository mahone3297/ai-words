# obviously

## Pronunciation
/ˈɑːbviəsli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a way that is clear or evident; clearly, plainly, or unmistakably. It's often used to indicate something that should be easily perceived or understood.

Example sentence: _**Adverb**: She was obviously upset about the news._

## Phrases
- **Obviously enough**: Sufficiently clear or evident.
  - Example: "The solution to the problem was obvious enough once he explained it."
- **It goes without saying / It's obvious**: Something is self-evident and doesn't need to be explicitly mentioned.
  - Example: "It goes without saying that punctuality is important for this job."
  
## Example Sentences
- **Adverb (modifying a verb)**: "It's obvious that he has been working out regularly."
- **Adverb (modifying an adjective)**: "Her happiness was obviously genuine."
- **Adverb (modifying a clause)**: "He was late again, obviously not taking his responsibilities seriously."
