## Pronunciation
/saɪtsiːɪŋ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The activity of visiting places of interest or scenic beauty, typically for pleasure or tourism. [Oxford](https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/sightseeing)
- **Verb**: To engage in sightseeing, explore places of interest while traveling. [Merriam-Webster](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/sightseeing)

## Phrases
- "Go on a sightseeing tour" refers to a planned visit to various attractions.
- "Do some casual sightseeing" implies a leisurely exploration.
- "Sightseeing is on our itinerary" means it's part of the travel plan.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The tourists were enjoying a guided sightseeing tour of the city."
- **Verb**: "After settling in, we decided to go sightseeing around the historic district."
- **Sentence**: "She spends her weekends doing weekend sightseeing trips to nearby towns."

## Synonyms
- Touring
- Exploring

- Traveling to see sights

## Antonyms

- Avoiding tourist spots
- Staying indoors
