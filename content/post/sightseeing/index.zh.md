## 读音
/ˈsaɪtsiːɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 游览名胜古迹的行为或活动。例如: "We went on a guided sightseeing tour of the city."
- **动词**: 游览景点。例如: "They spent the day sightseeing in Paris."

## 短语
- "sightseeing tour" 指的是游览城市的观光行程。
- "day trip for sightseeing" 意为一日游，以参观景点为主。
- "sightseeing spots" 指的是旅游景点或观光点。

## 例句
- 名词: "The main reason for our trip was sightseeing."
- 动词: "They decided to sightsee around the old town after lunch."
