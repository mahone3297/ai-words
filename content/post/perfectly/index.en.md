## Pronunciation
/perfɪktli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a perfect manner; without flaw or error. For example: "She sang the song perfectly."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by perfection or excellence. For example: "The performance was perfectly executed."
- **Adjective (informal)**: Absolutely or completely. For example: "I'm perfectly fine, thanks."

## Phrases
- "Perfectly done" is used to praise someone for a job well done.
- "In a perfect world" suggests an ideal situation that doesn't exist.
- "Not a single mistake - it was perfect" highlights flawless execution.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "He completed the task perfectly on time."
- **Adjective**: "Her handwriting is so precise, it's almost perfectly illegible."
- **Informal Adjective**: "I'm perfectly content with my new apartment."
