## 读音
/waɪp/

## 释义
- **动词**: 用布、纸或其他物体擦去（液体、尘土等）。例如: "I wiped the sweat from my forehead with a towel."
- **名词**: 擦拭的动作或结果。例如: "The spill left a big wipe on the floor."
- **名词**: 录像带或数字媒体上的剪辑片段。例如: "They used a special effect called a wipe to transition between scenes."

## 短语
- "Wipe off" 意思是擦掉表面的污渍或水分。例如: "Please wipe off the table before we eat."
- "Wipe out" 可以指完全消除或消灭。例如: "The storm wiped out our entire crop."

## 例句
- 动词: "He wiped his shoes on the doormat before entering the house."
- 名词: "The spill was so big that it required several wipes to clean up."
