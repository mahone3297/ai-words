## 读音
/ˈprɑːspərəti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 成功，繁荣，富饶。例如: "The country enjoyed a period of great prosperity after the war."
- **形容词**: 富裕的，繁荣昌盛的。例如: "Their business is experiencing prosperity and expanding rapidly."

## 短语
- "Prosperous economy" 指的是经济繁荣。
- "Prosperity mindset" 意味着积极向上的成功心态。

## 例句
- 名词: "The government's policies aim to bring prosperity to all citizens."
- 形容词: "The city has seen prosperity in recent years, with new businesses opening up."
