## Pronunciation
/proʊˈsperəti/

## Definitions
### Noun
Prosperity refers to a state of wealth, success, or well-being, often in economic terms. It denotes a flourishing condition characterized by growth, abundance, and general contentment.

### Verb
To prosper means to thrive, succeed, or experience growth in a favorable manner, both financially and personally.
