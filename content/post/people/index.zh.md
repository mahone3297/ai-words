## 读音
/ˈpiːpl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 人类，人们；群体，民众。例如: "The people in this city are very friendly."
- **复数形式**: "people" 通常不被视为单数形式，但作为整体概念时可视为单数。例如: "The people of the world have diverse cultures."

## 短语
- "the whole people" 全体人民
- "the majority of people" 大多数人
- "people person" 善于与人交往的人

## 例句
- 名词: "There were so many people at the concert that we couldn't find a place to stand."
- 复数形式: "Different people have different opinions on the matter."
