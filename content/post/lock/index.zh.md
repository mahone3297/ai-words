## 读音
/ˈlɑːk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种锁定装置，用于防止未经授权的访问或操作。例如: "She made sure to lock the door before leaving."
- **动词**: 将（门、箱子等）关紧并上锁。例如: "Please lock the car when you leave it unattended."
- **名词**: （计算机或网络系统中的）安全防护措施。例如: "The website uses a secure lock to protect user data."
- **动词**: （在比赛中）与对手的身体对抗并固定位置。例如: "The football player locked his arms around the opponent's waist."

## 短语
- "Lock away" 意为将某物安全地储存或隐藏。例如: "He locked away the valuable jewels in a safe."
- "Lock horns" 比喻意见不合而争斗。例如: "They locked horns over the budget proposal."

## 例句
- 名词: "He picked the lock on the old chest and found a treasure inside."
- 动词: "Remember to lock your phone with a strong password for security."
- 名词（比喻用法）: "Their disagreement turned into a battle of wills, each trying to break the other's lock on the situation."
