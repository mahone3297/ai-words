## 读音
/ˈpændə/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 一种哺乳动物，属于熊科，以黑白相间的毛色和圆圆的脸著称，主要生活在中国。例如: "Pandas are known for their cute appearance and bamboo diet."
   - 也用来昵称喜欢或看起来可爱的人或事物。例如: "She's such a panda, always cuddly and sweet."

## 短语
- "Red panda" 指另一种熊科动物，又称小熊猫或火狐，与大熊猫不同。
- "Panda bear" 通常指的是大熊猫。

## 例句
- "The baby panda was a big hit at the zoo, drawing crowds of visitors."
- "I just love watching pandas play in their enclosure; they're so entertaining."

## 同义词
- Giant bear
- Bamboo bear
- Black-and-white bear
- Red panda (for a different species)
