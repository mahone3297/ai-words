## 读音
/ˈsɜːtʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 查找，寻找。例如: "I need to search for my lost keys."
  - 搜索（信息）。例如: "She searched the internet for information on the topic."
  - 对…进行搜查。例如: "The police searched the suspect's house."

## 短语
- "Search engine" 搜索引擎，用于在网上查找信息。
- "Search and rescue" 搜救行动，寻找并救援失踪或被困的人。

## 例句
- 动词: "We spent hours searching the park for our missing dog."
- "He quickly searched through the drawers for his passport."
- "The team conducted a thorough search of the building."
