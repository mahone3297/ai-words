## 读音
/toʊst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 烤面包片，尤指涂有黄油或其他调料的。例如: "She enjoyed her breakfast with a slice of toast and jam."
   - 2. 干杯，祝酒。例如: "Let's raise a toast to the bride and groom."

- **动词**: 为...祝酒，敬酒。例如: "The best man toasted the newlyweds."

## 短语
- "toast someone" 表示向某人祝酒或致敬。例如: "We toasted our friendship with champagne."
- "toasted bread" 意味着烤过的面包。例如: "She prefers her toast slightly burnt."

## 例句
- 名词: "I love having avocado on toast for brunch."
- 动词: "The guests toasted the success of the project."

## 同义词
- 祝酒:
  - Cheers
  - Salute
  - Raise a glass
- 烤面包片:
  - Grilled bread
  - Toasted slice
  - Bruschetta (if topped)
