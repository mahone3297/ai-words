## 读音
/ʌnˈkʌvər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    1. 揭开；发现：指揭示隐藏的事物或信息。例如: "The archaeologists uncovered ancient artifacts."
    2. 拆除覆盖物：指移开覆盖在物体上的东西。例如: "He uncovered the painting after removing the protective cloth."

## 短语
- "Uncover the truth" 意为揭露真相。
- "Uncover one's past" 指挖掘某人的过去历史。

## 例句
- "The investigation uncovered a widespread fraud scheme."
- "She carefully uncovered the antique vase, revealing its intricate design."
