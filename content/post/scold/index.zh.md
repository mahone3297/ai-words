## 读音
/skəʊld/

## 释义
- **动词**: 责骂；斥责。例如: "The teacher scolded the student for being late."
- **名词**: 责骂；斥责。例如: "He received a scolding for his careless behavior."

## 短语
- "scold sb. for sth." 意为因某事责备某人。例如: "She scolded him for forgetting their anniversary."
- "get a scolding" 意为受到责备。例如: "If you're late again, you'll get a scolding from your boss."

## 例句
- 动词: "My mother scolded me when she found out I hadn't done my homework."
- 名词: "He knew he'd receive a scolding if he came home too late."
