## 读音
/ˌɪntrəˈdʌkʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 引导；介绍：如 "The book has a brief introduction explaining its purpose."
    - 介绍文；序言：如 "Read the introduction before starting the chapter."
    - 初步了解；入门：如 "This course provides an introduction to computer programming."

## 短语
- "Introduction to something" 指对某事物的初步介绍。
- "Make an introduction" 指将一个人介绍给另一个人认识。

## 例句
- 名词: "The movie's introduction sets the scene for the entire story."
- 动词: "Let me introduce you to our new team member, John."

## 同义词
- Beginning
- Preliminary
- Start
- Opening
- Foreword
