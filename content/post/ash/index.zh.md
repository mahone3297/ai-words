## 读音
/æʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 灰烬，物体燃烧后的残留物。例如: "After the fire, all that was left was a pile of ash."
- **名词**: (火山)灰，火山爆发时喷出的细小岩石颗粒。例如: "The eruption of Mount St. Helens sent ash across the surrounding area."
- **名词**: (烟草)烟灰，抽烟后留下的灰。例如: "He tapped the ash from his cigarette onto the ashtray."

## 短语
- "ashes to ashes" 意味着死亡和回归尘土，常用于葬礼或引用圣经中的表达。
- "ashes of love" 可以象征爱情的遗迹或消逝的爱情。

## 例句
- 名词: "The fireplace was surrounded by a layer of ash."
- 名词: "The volcanic ash made it difficult for airplanes to fly."
