## 读音
/ɡlɛr/

## 释义
- **动词**: 发出强烈的光，眩目地照射。例如: "The sun glared brightly in her eyes."
- **名词**: 强烈刺眼的光线。例如: "The glare from the headlights made it hard to see."

## 短语
- "glare at someone" 直勾勾地看着某人，通常带有愤怒或不满的情绪。
- "under the glare of the spotlight" 在聚光灯下，处于众人的关注之下。

## 例句
- 动词: "The camera flash glared so much that she couldn't open her eyes."
- 名词: "He squinted against the glare of the afternoon sun."

## 同义词
- shine intensely
- dazzle
- scowl
- flash
- beam (in the context of intense light)
