## 读音
/wɛlˈkɑːm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 表示欢迎的态度或行为。例如: "We received a warm welcome at the hotel."
- **动词**: 欢迎（某人）；对（某人）表示欢迎。例如: "The host welcomed the guests with open arms."
- **形容词**: 用于表示友好的或表示欢迎的。例如: "The restaurant had a welcoming atmosphere."

## 短语
- "Welcome someone warmly" 表示热情地欢迎某人。
- "Make someone feel welcome" 意味着使某人感到被接纳和受欢迎。
- "A welcome change" 指的是一个受欢迎的新情况或变化。

## 例句
- 名词: "Their friendly smiles were a genuine welcome."
- 动词: "The staff welcomed us with drinks and appetizers."
- 形容词: "The new employee felt welcome from the first day."
