## 读音
/lɪˈtrətʃʊr/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 文学，指各种书面创作，包括诗歌、小说、散文、戏剧等。例如: "She has a deep appreciation for classical literature."
  - 文献，指学术研究中的资料。例如: "The scholar referenced several important literature sources in her thesis."

## 短语
- "World literature" 指全球各地的文学作品。
- "Literary analysis" 是对文学作品进行深入解读和批判性思考的过程。

## 例句
- 名词: "The Nobel Prize in Literature is awarded annually to outstanding writers."
- 名词: "Shakespeare's plays are considered masterpieces of English literature."
