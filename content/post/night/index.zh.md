## 读音
/naɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 黑暗的时间，夜晚。例如: "I love walking in the quiet of the night."
   - 夜晚的时刻，尤指与白天相对的时间段。例如: "We usually go to bed early on school nights."
   - 某个特定的晚上。例如: "They had a memorable night at the concert."

## 短语
- "night time" 指夜晚时刻。
- "good night" 用于睡前告别或祝福他人有个好梦。
- "all night" 表示整夜，如 "party all night" 意为彻夜狂欢。

## 例句
- 名词: "The stars shine brightly in the clear night sky."
- 动词 (常作为名词使用，不常见作动词): "She worked through the night to finish the project."

## 同义词
- Evening
- Darkness
- Twilight
- Nighttime
- Nighthour
