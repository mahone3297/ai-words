## 读音
/mend/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 修复；修补：指修复破损或不完整的事物。例如: "He mended his broken watch with great care."
2. 改进；改正：指改善或纠正错误或问题。例如: "She's working on mending her relationship with her sister."

## 短语
- "to mend one's ways" 意味着改正行为或习惯。
- "mend fences" 指修复与他人的关系。

## 例句
- 动词（修复）: "The tailor skillfully mended the torn dress."
- 动词（改进）: "After a heart-to-heart talk, they started to mend their friendship."
