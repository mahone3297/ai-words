## 读音
/drʌm/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 打击乐器，通常为圆柱形，有一面或两面鼓皮。例如: "He played the drums in the school band."
- **动词 (v)**: 敲击，尤指用手指快速敲打物体表面以产生节奏。例如: "The children were drumming on the desks during class."

## 短语
- "drumming lessons" 鼓教学课程
- "drum kit" 一套打击乐器，包括各种鼓和钹
- "drum up business" 创造或增加生意或活动

## 例句
- 名词: "She loved playing the bass drum in the marching band."
- 动词: "The kids were drumming their fingers impatiently on the table."

## 同义词
- Percussion instrument
- Cymbal
- Bass
- Tambourine
- Bongo
