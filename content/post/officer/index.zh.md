## 读音
/ˈəfɪsər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 职员，官员；警官，军官。指在政府、公司、军队等组织中担任一定职务的人。例如: "The police officer arrested the suspect."
- **名词**: （尤指学校的）教官，指导员。例如: "The cadet's drill officer was strict about discipline."

## 短语
- "Officer in charge" 指负责人或主管官员。
- "Police officer" 特指警察。
- "Company officer" 在商业环境中指的是公司管理层的成员。

## 例句
- 名词: "The officer read the suspect his rights before questioning him."
- 名词: "She aspired to become a high-ranking army officer."
