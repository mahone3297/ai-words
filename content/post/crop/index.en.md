## Pronunciation
/krɒp/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Agricultural produce harvested from a field**: A farmer's main crop is wheat.
   - Example sentence: "The harvest of this year's crop was larger than anticipated."
2. **A part of a plant that grows above ground, typically after being sown**: The crops in the garden are growing well.
   - Example sentence: "The farmer rotates between planting different crops to maintain soil fertility."

### Verb
1. **To cultivate or gather (a plant):** Farmers often crop the land to prepare for the next season.
   - Example sentence: "The farmers are busy cropping the corn fields."
2. **To cut off or remove (a section of hair or vegetation)**: She cropped her hair short for the summer.
   - Example sentence: "She decided to crop her bangs into a trendy style."

### Adjective
1. **Relating to a single harvest or farming season**: Winter wheat is a short-season crop.
   - Example sentence: "The spring crop is usually planted later than the fall crop."
