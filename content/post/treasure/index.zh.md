## 读音
/treʒər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 财富，珍宝，贵重的东西。例如: "They found a treasure trove of ancient artifacts."
- **动词**: 珍惜，珍视。例如: "She treasures every moment spent with her family."
- **形容词**: 宝贵的，珍贵的。例如: "treasure memories"

## 短语
- "埋藏的宝藏" (buried treasure) 指隐藏或埋在地下的财富。
- "家庭宝藏" (family treasure) 指具有家族价值或历史意义的物品。
- "宝藏猎人" (treasure hunter) 是寻找和挖掘宝藏的人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The pirates were searching for a map leading to a sunken treasure."
- 动词: "She treasures the old love letters from her late husband."
- 形容词: "Their friendship is a treasure to behold."
