## 读音
/ɪnfərm/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 通知；告诉。例如: "I informed him about the meeting." 
  - 使了解；提供信息。例如: "The news report informed us of the current situation."
  - 告知（法律、规定等）。例如: "They were informed about the company's dress code."

## 短语
- "inform sb. of sth." 意味着告知某人某事。例如: "I need to inform my manager about the project progress."
- "inform against sb." 表示告发或揭发某人。例如: "He was informed against by his former business partner."

## 例句
- 作为动词: "She immediately informed the police when she saw the accident."
- 在被动语态中: "The public will be informed of any changes in the schedule."

## 同义词
