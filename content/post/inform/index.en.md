## Word: Inform

## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈfɔːrm/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To give information or communicate knowledge to someone about something, typically in a formal or official manner. For example: "The police informed him that his car had been stolen."
  - _Inform someone of something_: To tell someone about a particular fact or situation. Example: "I will inform you of any updates on the project."
- **Noun**: The act of giving information or communication of knowledge; information provided. For example: "We received no prior inform about the change in schedule."

## Phrases
- _Inform against someone_: To give information to authorities, usually with the intention of incriminating someone. Example: "He decided to inform against his accomplices to reduce his sentence."
- _Keep someone informed_: To regularly update someone with new information. Example: "Please keep us informed of your progress."
