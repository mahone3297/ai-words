## Pronunciation
/kəmˈpleɪn/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A statement expressing dissatisfaction, unhappiness, or grievance about something: *I've been complaining about the long commute all week.*
2. An action or habit of making such statements: *His constant complaining was wearing on her nerves.*

### Verb
1. To express discontent or dissatisfaction with something: *She complained about the poor service at the restaurant.*
2. To make a formal accusation or charge: *The customer complained to the manager about the defective product.*

## Phrases
- "Complain about" (to express dissatisfaction)
- "Bring a complaint" (to make a formal grievance)
- "No complaints" (meaning no issues or everything is satisfactory)

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her constant complaints about the food were driving the others crazy."
- **Verb**: "If you have a problem, please come forward and complain to the staff."
- **Verb**: "The customer decided to complain to the higher authorities after several attempts to resolve the issue."
