## 读音
/ˈtenənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 租客；承租人。指租赁房产或其他财产的人。例如: "The tenant agreed to pay the rent on time."
- **动词**: （不常用）租用；承租。例如: "They decided to tenant the apartment for a year."

## 短语
- "landlord-tenant relationship" 房东与租户的关系。
- "tenant rights" 租户的权利。
- "good tenant" 好租客，指按时支付租金并维护房屋的租户。

## 例句
- 名词: "As a tenant, it's important to understand your lease agreement."
- 动词: "The couple decided to tenant the house until they could buy their own."
