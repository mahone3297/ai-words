## Pronunciation
/prəˈsɛnəl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: (plural: personnel) 1. The group of people who work for an organization, especially in a particular department or role. "Our company's personnel are highly skilled and dedicated." 2. Staff members collectively. "The entire personnel attended the training session."
- **Adjective**: Relating to employees or human resources. "The personnel policy outlines hiring and promotion procedures."

## Phrases
- "Personnel management" refers to the administration of a company's employees.
- "Personnel turnover" is the rate at which employees leave or are replaced.
- "Recruit new personnel" is a task for HR to find and hire new workers.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The HR department is responsible for managing the personnel records."
- **Adjective**: "The personnel department is working on improving employee morale."
- **Verb**: "The company is hiring additional personnel to handle the increased workload."
