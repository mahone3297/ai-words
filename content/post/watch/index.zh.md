## 读音
/watch/

## 释义
- **名词**: 手表，时钟。例如: "He checked his watch to see the time."
- **动词**: 观看，注视。例如: "I watched the movie with great interest."
- **及物动词**: 监视，守护。例如: "The security guard was watching over the entrance."

## 短语
- "Keep an eye on (watch)" 意思是照看，留意。
- "Watch out for" 警惕，小心。例如: "Watch out for that car turning!"
- "Watch something unfold" 观察事情的发展。

## 例句
- 名词: "She wears a stylish silver watch on her wrist."
- 动词: "We watched the sunset together on the beach."
- 及物动词: "The parents watched their child playing in the park."
