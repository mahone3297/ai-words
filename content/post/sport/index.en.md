## Pronunciation
/sprɔːt/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Sport**: 1. An activity involving physical exertion and skill typically in a competitive context, often played outdoors: "Football is one of the most popular sports in the world."
- 2. A particular game or contest within a sport: "She's an expert in tennis and golf."
- 3. A hobby or recreational pursuit: "Collecting stamps is a popular sport for many people."

### Verb
- **To engage in sport**: To participate in a sport or physical activity for enjoyment or competition: "He enjoys playing basketball every Saturday."

### Adjective
- **Sporty**: Characterized by or suitable for sports or physical activity: "The car had a sporty design."

### Adverb
- **Sportingly**: In a manner befitting a sport or its spirit: "They agreed to let the losing team have a second chance, sportingly."

## Phrases
- "Play sports": Engage in physical games or activities.
- "Athletic sports": Competitive sports that require physical fitness.
- "Participate in a sporting event": Join in a organized game or competition.
