## 释义
- **名词**: 导航；领航；航行方法。例如: "The art of navigation was well developed in ancient times."
- **名词**: 导航系统；导向设备。例如: "The plane's navigation system guided it safely through the storm."

## 短语
- "Navigation system" 指导航系统，如汽车或飞机中的GPS导航。
- "Maritime navigation" 指海上航行技术。
- "Astronomical navigation" 利用天体进行定位的导航方式。
