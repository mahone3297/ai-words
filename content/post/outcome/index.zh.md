## 读音
/ˈaʊtkʌm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 结果；后果。例如: "The outcome of the election was unexpected."
- **名词**: 成果；成绩。例如: "Their hard work led to a positive outcome."

## 短语
- "positive outcome" 意为积极的结果。
- "outcome measure" 在医学或研究中指的是用来评估结果的指标。
- "unforeseen outcome" 指的是未预见的结果。

## 例句
- "We're hoping for a good outcome from the negotiations."
- "The patient's recovery was an excellent outcome for the medical team."
- "The experiment had a disappointing outcome, but we learned valuable lessons."
