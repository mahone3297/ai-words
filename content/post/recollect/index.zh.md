## 读音
/rɪˈkəlɛkt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 回忆，回想。例如: "I'm trying to recollect what he said earlier."
- **动词**: 重新集合，收集。例如: "She recollected all the scattered pieces of information."

## 短语
- "recollect one's thoughts" 意思是整理思绪，回忆起思绪。
- "recollect from memory" 意味着凭记忆回想起。

## 例句
- "As I looked at the old photo, I started to recollect our childhood adventures."
- "Can you recollect where we parked the car?"
- "After a long day, it takes me a while to recollect my thoughts."
