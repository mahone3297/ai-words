## Pronunciation
/rɪˈkɒlɪkt/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A verb meaning to remember or recall something from one's memory. "Recollecting old memories brought a smile to her face."
- Sometimes used as a noun to refer to the act of remembering or the ability to do so. "His recollection of the event was vivid."

### Verb
- To remember or recall something after some time has passed. "I tried to recollect the name of the song I had heard earlier."
- To gather one's thoughts before speaking. "Take a moment to recollect yourself before answering."

## Phrases
- "Recall something" means to bring back to one's mind.
- "Recollecting details" implies the process of remembering specific facts or events.
- "Lost in thought, he was trying to recollect his train schedule."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her recollection of her childhood home was filled with nostalgia."
- **Verb**: "After a long pause, he finally recollected the correct answer."
- **As a verb**: "I need you to recollect the instructions for assembling the furniture."
