## Pronunciation
/səˈleɪmən/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **salesman** is a person whose job is to sell goods or services to customers, often visiting their homes or businesses. They are typically commission-based and responsible for promoting products and closing deals.

### Verb (informal)
To act as a **salesman** can mean to persuade or convince someone to buy something, even if not officially employed as one.

## Phrases
- "Close a deal with the salesman"
- "Be a pushy salesman"
- "The salesman's pitch was very convincing"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The salesman showed us the latest model of the car, highlighting its features."
- **Verb**: "He tried to be a salesman for his new startup, but no one seemed interested."
- **Informal**: "She acted like a salesman when negotiating the price of the house."

## Synonyms
- Salesperson
- Sales representative
- Pusher
- Closer
- Persuader

## Antonyms
- Customer
- Buyer
- Advocate (in a non-sales context)
