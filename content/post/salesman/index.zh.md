## 读音
/ˈsæləmən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 销售员，推销员；指从事销售工作的人。例如: "The salesman demonstrated the features of the new product."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与销售相关的，关于销售的。例如: "He has a lot of experience as a salesman."

## 短语
- "Salesman pitch" 意为销售员的推销介绍。
- "To be on the road as a salesman" 指销售员在外进行销售活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The experienced salesman was able to close the deal easily."
- 形容词: "She has excellent salesman skills, which helped her excel in her job."
