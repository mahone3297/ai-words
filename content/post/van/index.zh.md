## 读音
/væn/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 小货车，厢式货车。例如: "He drives a white van for his delivery job."
  - 车厢，拖车。例如: "The band's equipment was loaded onto the tour van."
  - (荷兰姓氏)范。例如: "Van Gogh is a famous Dutch painter."

- **形容词**:
  - 来自或属于荷兰的。例如: "Van cookies are a traditional Dutch treat."
  - 姓氏前缀，如"van der"或"van den"。例如: "John van der Waals was a Nobel Prize-winning physicist."

## 短语
- "van driver" 指驾驶小货车的人。
- "van life" 指人们在改装的小货车中生活或旅行的生活方式。

## 例句
- 名词: "The moving company sent a large van to transport our furniture."
- 形容词: "She comes from a long line of Van ancestors who settled in America."
