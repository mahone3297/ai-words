## Pronunciation
/væn/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **A vehicle with a boxlike body, typically one with large rear doors, used for carrying goods or passengers.** Example: "The delivery van arrived at the store this morning."
2. **A small enclosed vehicle, often used by artists or photographers, with a removable top.** Example: "She drove her converted van to the countryside for a photoshoot."
3. **A small, compact car, especially one with sliding doors.** Example: "The family chose a compact van to fit their growing needs."

### Verb
1. **To transport people or goods in a van.** Example: "The construction workers loaded the van with tools and materials."
2. **To use a van for personal or commercial purposes.** Example: "She uses her van as a mobile office for her business."

### Adjective
1. **Relating to or characterized by a van or vans.** Example: "The company specializes in van-based delivery services."

## Phrases
- "Hire a van" means to rent a van for transportation purposes.
- "Van life" refers to the lifestyle of living in a van, often as a form of travel or minimalism.
- "Drive a van" is a common action when operating such a vehicle.

## Example Sentences

- **Adjective**: "The van dealership had a wide range of models to choose from."
