## 读音
/prɪnt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 印刷品；打印物。例如: "I need to make a few copies of this print for the meeting."
2. （艺术作品的）版画，印刷复制品。例如: "She collects vintage prints by famous artists."

- **动词**:
1. 打印；印刷。例如: "Please print out the document before the meeting."
2. 显示在纸上或其他介质上。例如: "The computer will print the results on the screen."

## 短语
- "Print run" 指一次印刷的数量。
- "Print quality" 指打印的清晰度和效果。

## 例句
- 名词: "The store sells a variety of framed prints for home decoration."
- 动词: "Can you print the report double-sided to save paper?"
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但偶尔可见）: "Hello, print! It's great to see physical books still in demand."
