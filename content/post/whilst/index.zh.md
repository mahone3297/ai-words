## 读音
/ˈwɪlɪst/

## 释义
- **连词**: 相当于“[while](/zh/post/while/)”，表示在某动作进行的同时。例如: "I was cooking [dinner](/zh/post/dinner/) [whilst](/zh/post/whilst/) listening [to](/zh/post/to/) [music](/zh/post/music/)."
- **副词**: 也作“[while](/zh/post/while/)”，强调两个动作同时发生。例如: "[She](/zh/post/she/) [read](/zh/post/read/) [a](/zh/post/a/) [book](/zh/post/book/) [whilst](/zh/post/whilst/) waiting [for](/zh/post/for/) [her](/zh/post/her/) [appointment](/zh/post/appointment/)."

## 短语
- "[whilst](/zh/post/whilst/) doing [something](/zh/post/something/)" 在做某事的时候。例如: "[She](/zh/post/she/) [often](/zh/post/often/) daydreams [whilst](/zh/post/whilst/) doing [household](/zh/post/household/) chores."

## 例句
- 连词: "[He](/zh/post/he/) improved [his](/zh/post/his/) skills [whilst](/zh/post/whilst/) working [on](/zh/post/on/) [various](/zh/post/various/) projects."
- 副词: "[The](/zh/post/the/) [baby](/zh/post/baby/) fell [asleep](/zh/post/asleep/) [whilst](/zh/post/whilst/) [being](/zh/post/being/) rocked."
