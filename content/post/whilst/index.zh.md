## 读音
/ˈwɪlɪst/

## 释义
- **连词**: 相当于“while”，表示在某动作进行的同时。例如: "I was cooking dinner whilst listening to music."
- **副词**: 也作“while”，强调两个动作同时发生。例如: "She read a book whilst waiting for her appointment."

## 短语
- "whilst doing something" 在做某事的时候。例如: "She often daydreams whilst doing household chores."

## 例句
- 连词: "He improved his skills whilst working on various projects."
- 副词: "The baby fell asleep whilst being rocked."

## 同义词
- while
- during
- at the same time
- in the meantime
- concurrently
