## 读音
/ˈwɪlɪst/

## 释义
- **连词**: 相当于“[while](/post/while/)”，表示在某动作进行的同时。例如: "I was cooking [dinner](/post/dinner/) [whilst](/post/whilst/) listening [to](/post/to/) [music](/post/music/)."
- **副词**: 也作“[while](/post/while/)”，强调两个动作同时发生。例如: "[She](/post/she/) [read](/post/read/) [a](/post/a/) [book](/post/book/) [whilst](/post/whilst/) waiting [for](/post/for/) [her](/post/her/) [appointment](/post/appointment/)."

## 短语
- "[whilst](/post/whilst/) doing [something](/post/something/)" 在做某事的时候。例如: "[She](/post/she/) [often](/post/often/) daydreams [whilst](/post/whilst/) doing [household](/post/household/) chores."

## 例句
- 连词: "[He](/post/he/) improved [his](/post/his/) skills [whilst](/post/whilst/) working [on](/post/on/) [various](/post/various/) projects."
- 副词: "[The](/post/the/) [baby](/post/baby/) fell [asleep](/post/asleep/) [whilst](/post/whilst/) [being](/post/being/) rocked."
