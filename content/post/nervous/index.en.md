## Pronunciation
/ˈnɜːrvəs/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: (of a person) experiencing feelings of anxiety, apprehension, or unease; easily agitated or alarmed. For example: "She was feeling nervous before her big presentation."
- **Noun**: (informal) A nervous person. For example: "The nervousness he displayed before the interview was quite palpable."

## Phrases
- "To be/get nervous": To experience or develop nervousness. Example: "I tend to get nervous before public speaking engagements."
- "Make sb nervous": To cause someone to feel anxious or uneasy. Example: "Her constant fidgeting made me nervous."
- "A case of nerves": A period of intense nervousness or anxiety about something. Example: "His pre-performance jitters were just a case of nerves."

## Example Sentences
