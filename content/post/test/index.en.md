## Pronunciation
/test/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A process or examination designed to assess a person's knowledge, skill, or ability, often in an educational or professional setting. ["A test is a way to measure one's understanding."](https://www.merriam-webster.com/dictionary/test)
- **Verb**: To subject to a test or evaluation. ["The students were tested on their grammar skills."](https://www.oxfordlearnersdictionaries.com/definition/english/test)
- **Noun**: A challenge or trial, especially in terms of resilience or perseverance. ["She passed the toughest test of her life with flying colors."](https://idioms.thefreedictionary.com/test+of+one%27s+mettle)

## Phrases
- "Take a test" means to complete an examination or assessment.
- "Pre-test" refers to an initial evaluation before the main test.
- "Post-test analysis" involves examining results after a test has been administered.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The students were nervous about the final exam, which was the most challenging test of the semester."
- **Verb**: "After months of training, he tested his endurance by running a marathon."
- **Noun**: "She faced a tough mental test when deciding whether to quit her job or stick it out."
