## Pronunciation
/rəˈkeɪʃəl/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Racial**: Referring to or based on race; specifically, belonging to a particular racial group or involving matters of race. For example: "The book explores racial tensions in the workplace."
- **Racism**: Prejudice or discrimination based on race; the belief in the inherent superiority or inferiority of one race over another. For example: "She fought against racial discrimination in housing policies."

### Adjective
- Characterizing or related to a specific race or ethnicity. For example: "The curriculum includes diverse racial perspectives."

### Adjective (archaic)
- Having a complexion or appearance typical of a particular race. For example: "His facial features were of a racial type distinct from his parents'."

## Phrases
- "Racial diversity" denotes the variety of racial backgrounds in a group or environment.
- "Addressing racial disparities" focuses on reducing unequal treatment based on race.
- "Racial profiling" refers to the practice of targeting individuals based on their perceived race.
