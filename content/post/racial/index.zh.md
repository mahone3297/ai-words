## 读音
/ˈreɪʃəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 关乎种族的；基于种族的；种族歧视的。例如: "The policy is criticized for being racial in nature."
- **名词**: 种族特性，种族特征。例如: "There is a wide range of racial diversity in the city."

## 短语
- "Racial discrimination" 意为种族歧视。
- "Racial identity" 指的是种族认同感。
- "Racial profiling" 是指在执法中基于种族特征进行判断的行为。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The company has a strict policy against racial discrimination."
- 名词: "Her study explores the complexities of racial identity in a multicultural society."

## 同义词
- Ethnic
- Racially-based
- Discriminatory (when referring to discrimination)
- Genotypic (when referring to genetic aspects of race)
- Ethnical (less common, but can be used to mean related to ethnicity)
