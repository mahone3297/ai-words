## Definitions
### Noun
- **A noun referring to photography**: The art or practice of creating images using a camera, light, and film or digital sensors.
- **A noun referring to the process**: The act of taking photographs or pictures.
- **A noun referring to equipment**: A camera or other device used for capturing images.

### Verb
- To take photographs or make pictures using a camera.
- To describe or depict something in a visual manner, typically with a high level of detail.

### Adjective
- Characterizing or relating to photography: "A photographic memory."
- Having the appearance or quality of a photograph: "The print had a very photographic quality."

### Adverb
