## Pronunciation
/ˈɑːpəreɪtər/

## Definitions
- **Noun (n)**: A person who operates a machine, system, or apparatus, especially one that requires technical skill or knowledge. For example: "The crane operator carefully lifted the heavy load onto the truck."
- **Noun (n)**: In computing, it refers to a symbol or keyword used to perform specific operations in a programming language. For instance: "The 'for' loop is a control structure used by the programmer as an operator to iterate through a sequence."
- **Verb (v)**: To manage, control, or work with a machine, system, or process. Example: "She has been operating the assembly line machinery for over a decade."
  
## Phrases
- "Operator error": Refers to mistakes made by a human operator rather than mechanical failure.
- "Phone operator": A person who connects calls and provides assistance in a telephone exchange.
- "Operator in a chatbot": The artificial intelligence or script responsible for understanding and responding to user inputs.
