## 读音
/mʌθfʊl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一口所能容纳的量；满满一口。例如: "She took a mouthful of soup and savoured it."
- **形容词**: 饱满的，满口的。例如: "The child had a mouthful of candy."

## 短语
- "a mouthful of food" 指满口的食物。
- "too much for a mouthful" 指东西太多，一口吃不下。

## 例句
- 名词: "The baby managed to fit a mouthful of mashed potatoes in his mouth."
- 形容词: "That sandwich is a real mouthful; I can hardly manage it."

## 同义词
- bite-sized
- ample
- generous
- heaping
- sizable
