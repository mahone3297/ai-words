## 读音
/ˈsʌndeɪ/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一周中的第七天，通常被认为是休息日或礼拜日。例如: "We usually have a family gathering on Sundays."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 与星期日相关的。例如: "Sunday brunch is a popular weekend activity."

## 短语
- "Sunday best" 指人们在周日教堂礼拜或特殊场合穿的最好的衣服。
- "Sunday driver" 形容开车缓慢、悠闲的人。

## 例句
- 名词: "I love to spend lazy Sundays reading books."
- 形容词: "She was wearing her Sunday best for the church service."

## 同义词
- Weekend day
- Day of rest
- Sabbath
- Seventh day
