## 读音
/mɪˈtrɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
1. 女主人，女房东。例如: "The mistress of the house welcomed us warmly."
2. 女情人，情妇。例如: "He had a secret mistress for many years."

## 短语
- "mistress of ceremonies" 指宴会、晚会等活动的女主持人。
- "the mistress of the house" 指家中的女主人。

## 例句
- 名词: "As the mistress of the manor, she oversaw all the estate's affairs."
- 名词: "He was deeply in love with his mistress, despite being married."
