## 读音
/frent/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 前面；正面。例如: "The building has a large front entrance."
  - 阵营；方面。例如: "They belong to opposite political fronts."
  - 前部；前面部分。例如: "The car suffered damage to its front."
  - (表演者)台前；舞台前部。例如: "She stepped into the spotlight on stage front."

- **动词**:
  - 面对；正视。例如: "She decided to front the problem and find a solution."
  - 在…前面。例如: "The house fronts the river."

## 短语
- "In front of" 表示在…的前面。例如: "There's a park right in front of my house."
- "Front line" 指前线或最前线。例如: "Soldiers on the front line face great danger."

## 例句
- 名词: "The store has a big sign on its front."
- 动词: "He decided to front the issue in the meeting."
- 情景: "The soldiers were standing at the front line, ready for battle.

## 同义词
- 前面：front side, anterior, fore
- 面对：confront, meet, address
- 阵营：side, team, faction
