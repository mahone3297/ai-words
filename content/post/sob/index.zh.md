## 读音
/soʊb/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - (尤指因悲伤或感动而发出的)抽泣，啜泣。例如: "She couldn't hold back her sobs during the funeral."
    - (口语)一个爱哭的人，感情脆弱的人。例如: "He's such a sob when it comes to sad movies."

- **动词**: 
    - 抽泣，哭泣。例如: "The child sobbed uncontrollably after falling off the bike."
    - (非正式)表现出情绪化的行为。例如: "He was sobbing about his failure in the exam."

## 短语
- "Sob story" 指一个令人同情或悲惨的故事。
- "To sob one's heart out" 意味着尽情地哭泣。

## 例句
- 名词: "Her sobs echoed through the empty hallway."
- 动词: "He was too upset to speak, only able to sob quietly."
