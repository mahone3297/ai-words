## 读音
/ˈɔːlməst/

## 释义
- **副词**: 几乎，差不多。表示接近某个数量、程度或情况，但不完全达到。例如: "I finished the project almost on time."
- **形容词**: 差不多的，接近的。例如: "The weather is almost perfect for a picnic."

## 短语
- "almost all" 几乎所有
- "almost none" 几乎没有
- "almost there" 就要到了

## 例句
- 副词: "I've read the book almost cover to cover."
- 形容词: "The answer is almost correct, just a small mistake."
