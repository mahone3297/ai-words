## 读音
/təˈnaɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 今晚，当前这个夜晚。例如: "I have a meeting tonight."
- **副词**: 在今晚，今天晚上。例如: "We're going out tonight."

## 短语
- "Have a good night" 是一种在夜晚分别时的祝福语。
- "See you tonight" 表示晚上再见，约好晚上见面。

## 例句
- 名词: "I've booked a table for two at the restaurant tonight."
- 副词: "The concert starts at 8 o'clock tonight, don't be late."

## 同义词
- This evening
- This night
- Tonight itself
- In the evening
