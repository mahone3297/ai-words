## 读音
/ˈsesʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一段连续的时间，通常用于特定的目的或活动。例如: "They had a two-hour meeting session."
- **名词**: 在学校或大学中的一组课程或讲座。例如: "The fall semester has three math sessions."
- **名词**: 在计算机领域，指用户与系统交互的一系列操作。例如: "Please save your work before ending the session."
- **名词**: 音乐录制或演奏的专门时段。例如: "The band had a recording session in the studio."

## 短语
- "Session musician" 是指在录音室为唱片或电影等项目临时工作的音乐家。
- "Group therapy session" 指集体心理治疗的会话。

## 例句
- 名词: "The training session lasted for three hours."
- 名词: "The students attend language classes in separate morning and afternoon sessions."
- 名词: "The software developer ended his coding session to take a break."
