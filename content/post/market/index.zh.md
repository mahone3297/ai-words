## 读音
/mɑːrkɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个公开的场所，人们在那里买卖商品或服务。例如: "The farmer's market sells fresh produce every Saturday."
- **名词**: 市场营销或销售的领域。例如: "The company needs to analyze the target market for their new product."
- **动词**: 销售或推广产品或服务。例如: "They are marketing their new app to college students."

## 短语
- "Stock market" 股票市场，投资者买卖股票的地方。
- "Consumer market" 消费者市场，面向普通消费者的市场。
- "Global market" 全球市场，跨越国界的贸易环境。

## 例句
- 名词: "There was a lively atmosphere at the local market where vendors sold handmade crafts."
- 名词: "The company aims to dominate the smartphone market."
- 动词: "They're marketing their new book through social media campaigns."
