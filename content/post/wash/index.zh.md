## 读音
/ˈwɒʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
   - 清洗，冲洗。例如: "I need to wash my car."
   - 使（衣物等）清洁。例如: "She washes her clothes every weekend."
   - 洗澡。例如: "The baby is taking a bath; I'll wash him later."

- **名词**:
   - 清洗的动作或过程。例如: "Don't forget to do the dishes after the wash."
   - 洗涤剂。例如: "I need more laundry detergent for the wash."
   - 洪水，大浪。例如: "The heavy rain caused a destructive wash in the valley."

## 短语
- "Give something a wash" 意为清洗某物。
- "Wash your hands" 告诉别人要洗手。
- "Wash up" 指洗澡或洗手。

## 例句
- 动词: "I'm going to wash the dog before we take him to the park."
- 名词: "There's still a load of towels in the wash that need to dry."
