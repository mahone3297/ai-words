## 读音
/taɪm/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 时间：不可见的、持续的量，通常以秒、分钟、小时等单位衡量。例如: "We have limited time to finish the project."
  - 时刻：特定的时间点。例如: "The meeting is scheduled for 3 o'clock sharp."
  - 时期：一段时期或阶段。例如: "That was a difficult time in my life."

- **动词**:
  - 计时：记录或测量时间。例如: "I timed my run to see how fast I could go."
  - 安排：确定事件或活动发生的时间。例如: "Let's time the dinner for 7 PM."

- **形容词**:
  - 适时的：在正确或合适的时间发生的。例如: "His arrival was right on time."
  - 耗时的：需要大量时间的。例如: "This task is very time-consuming."

## 短语
- "Time flies" 表示时间过得飞快。
- "Time heals all wounds" 意味着随着时间流逝，痛苦会减轻或消失。
- "Time and tide wait for no man" 意思是时间不等人。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need more time to prepare for the exam."
- 动词: "She timed her speech to fit within the allotted 10 minutes."
- 形容词: "The project is running behind schedule, so we need to work more efficiently to make up the lost time."
