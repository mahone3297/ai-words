## 释义
- **感叹词**: 用于打招呼、引起注意或表示惊讶。例如: "[Hello](/post/hello/)! [How](/post/how/) are [you](/post/you/)?", "[Hello](/post/hello/)! [What](/post/what/) [a](/post/a/) [surprise](/post/surprise/)!"

## 短语
- "[Hello](/post/hello/), [dear](/post/dear/)" 用于亲昵地问候他人。
- "[Hello](/post/hello/), [operator](/post/operator/)?" 用于打电话时找接线员。

## 例句
- 感叹词: "[Hello](/post/hello/), it's [nice](/post/nice/) [to](/post/to/) [see](/post/see/) [you](/post/you/) [again](/post/again/)."
