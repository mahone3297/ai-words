## 读音
/ˈdʒiː.niəs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 极其聪明的人；天才。例如: "Einstein was considered a genius in the field of physics."
- **形容词**: 极其聪明的；天才般的。例如: "The child displayed genius-level intelligence from an early age."

## 短语
- "a genius for something" 表示在某方面有天赋。例如: "She has a genius for solving complex problems."
- "a work of genius" 指的是天才之作。例如: "The painting is widely regarded as a work of genius."

## 例句
- 名词: "Many people believe that Mozart was a musical genius."
- 形容词: "His innovative ideas showed a genius approach to business."
