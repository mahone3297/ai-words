## 读音
/lake/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一个较大的天然淡水水体，通常被陆地环绕。例如: "The view of the calm lake was breathtaking."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 形容具有湖泊特征的。例如: "The lake house had a serene atmosphere."

## 短语
- "Lake house" 湖边的房子或湖畔小屋。
- "To freeze over" 当湖面结冰时，可以说 "The lake froze over in winter."

## 例句
- 名词: "We spent our summer vacation at a beautiful lake in the mountains."
- 形容词: "The lake effect snow is common in regions near large bodies of water."
