## Pronunciation
/skaɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The atmosphere or outer space surrounding the Earth, characterized by air and clouds; 2. The region of the atmosphere visible from the ground, typically with its blue color during the day and stars at night; 3. The vault of heaven, considered as the celestial expanse; 4. A general or indefinite area or direction; 5. (slang) A person's mood or disposition; 6. (figurative) A state or condition.

- **Adjective**: 1. Characteristic of the sky, such as blue or clear.

## Phrases
- "Under a clear blue sky" describes a beautiful day with no clouds.
- "To gaze at the endless sky" means to look up at the sky for a long time in wonder.
- "Skydiving" is the sport of jumping from an aircraft and free-falling before deploying a parachute.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children watched the colorful balloons float higher and higher into the sky."
- **Adjective**: "The sky was painted with hues of pink and orange during the sunset."
