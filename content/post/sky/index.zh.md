## 读音
/skaɪ/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
天空，大气层的上部，通常指可见的蓝色部分。例如: "The sun was shining brightly in the clear blue sky."
- **形容词 adj**:
天空的，与天空相关的。例如: "sky-high prices" (天价)

## 短语
- "under the open sky" 在户外，露天
- "skydiving" 跳伞运动
- "skyrocket" 急速上升，猛增

## 例句
- 名词: "We watched the stars twinkle in the night sky."
- 形容词: "The apartment has a sky view, which is breathtaking."
