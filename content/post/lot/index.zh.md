## 读音
/lot/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 一批，一堆：A quantity of things grouped or collected together; a number of items considered as a unit.
   - （赌博、抽签中的）一份，一注：One of the shares or portions into which something is divided for distribution by chance or lot.
   - 地皮，地段：A piece of land, especially one set aside for a particular purpose.

- **动词**:
   - 分配（由抽签决定）：To distribute (something) by lot; assign by chance.

## 短语
- "a large lot" 指一大片土地或大量物品。
- "lot out" 分配，分给：Divide or distribute by lot.
- "be cast in the same lot" 彼此命运相同：To be considered or treated together as if sharing a common fate.

## 例句
- 名词: "The house came with a large lot that backed onto a forest."
- 动词: "The items were lot out among the bidders."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但有时可见）: "Lot it be so!" (表示希望事情如此)
