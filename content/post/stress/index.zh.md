## 读音
/stres/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 压力，紧张：精神或身体上的负担，常指由工作、生活情况或关系引起的。例如: "She's been under a lot of stress lately."
  - 重音：在发音时强调的某个音节或单词。例如: "The stress in this sentence falls on the second syllable."

- **动词**:
  - 强调，着重：在说话或写作中特别注意或突出某个观点。例如: "I want to stress the importance of teamwork."

## 短语
- "Stress management" 管理压力的方法。
- "Emotional stress" 情绪压力。
- "Work-related stress" 工作导致的压力。

## 例句
- 名词: "Exercise can help reduce stress levels."
- 动词: "The teacher stressed the need for preparation before the exam."
- 感叹词: "Stress not, everything will work out."
