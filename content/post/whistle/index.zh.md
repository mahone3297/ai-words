## 读音
/ˈwɪsl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种能发出尖锐声音的小器具，通常用嘴吹奏。例如: "The referee blew his whistle to start the game."
- **动词**: 发出口哨声或用口哨吹奏。例如: "He was whistling a happy tune while walking down the street."
- **名词**: 指火车、汽笛等发出的声音。例如: "The sound of the steam whistle announced the arrival of the train."

## 短语
- "Blow the whistle" 意味着发出哨声，常用于指示开始或结束。
- "Whistle a tune" 指用口哨吹出旋律。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were playing with toy whistles in the park."
- 动词: "She whistled a popular song as she worked in the garden."
- 名词（火车汽笛声）: "The evening train's whistle echoed through the valley."

## 同义词
- Toot (for a horn or vehicle)
- Blow (in the context of making a sound)
- Sound (as in sounding a horn or whistle)
