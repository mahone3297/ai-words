## 读音
/ˈsædəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 骑马或自行车时放在马背或车座上的器具，通常由皮革制成，供骑手坐。例如: "She adjusted the saddle on her horse before setting off."
- **动词**: 给（动物）装上鞍子。例如: "The cowboy saddled up his horse for the long ride."

## 短语
- "Saddle up" 意味着准备出发，常用于指骑马。例如: "It's time to saddle up and head out on the trail."
- "Take the saddle off" 意味着卸下鞍子，通常用于马匹。例如: "After the ride, remember to take the saddle off your horse."

## 例句
- 名词: "The old leather saddle had seen many years of use."
- 动词: "He carefully saddled the horse, making sure the girth was tight enough."

## 同义词
- Mount (名词时)
- Bridle
- Harness
- Gear (在某些上下文中指骑行装备)
