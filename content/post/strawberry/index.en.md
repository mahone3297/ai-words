## Pronunciation
/ˈstræb(ə)rɪ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **strawberry** is a small, sweet, red fruit with a fleshy exterior and many seeds on its interior. It is often used in desserts, salads, or eaten fresh.

### Verb
To **strawberry** something (informal) means to cover it with strawberries or imitate their appearance, typically in food preparations or art projects.

## Phrases
- "Top a cake with strawberries" for a dessert topping.
- "Make a strawberry smoothie" for a popular drink recipe.
- "Strawberry fields forever" is a metaphor for an idyllic, endless landscape.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The farmer picked a basket full of juicy strawberries for the market."
- **Verb**: "She decided to strawberry her vanilla ice cream with fresh berries."
- **Informal**: "The little girl decorated her cupcakes with a layer of strawberry frosting."
