## 读音
/nɛst/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一个鸟窝，由鸟类建造以养育雏鸟。例如: "The robin built its nest in the tree."
  - 比喻意为一个安全舒适的环境或位置。例如: "The baby fell asleep in the cozy nest of blankets."
  - 一系列相似事物的排列或堆叠。例如: "He found a nest of Russian dolls."

- **动词**:
  - 鸟类筑巢。例如: "The swallows are nesting on the porch."
  - 放置或安放成堆叠状。例如: "She nested the bowls one inside the other for storage."

## 短语
- "Bird's nest" 指鸟窝。
- "Nest eggs" 指储蓄或为未来准备的资金。
- "Nestle" 动词形式，意为舒适地安顿或嵌入。例如: "The cat nestled into the crook of my arm."

## 例句
- 名词: "The eagle returned to its nest with a fish for its young."
- 动词: "The kittens nested together in the warmth of the sun."
- 短语: "They had saved a substantial nest egg for retirement."

## 同义词
- Dwelling (for birds)
- Habitat
- Shelter
- Cozy spot
- Burrow (for animals)
