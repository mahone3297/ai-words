## Definitions
- **Numeral**: A number indicating the quantity twenty, which is between nineteen and twenty-one. It is the cardinal form of the number.
- **Adjective**: Designating or referring to a quantity of twenty. For example: "There are twenty books on the shelf."
- **Adjective (plural)**: In the ordinal form, it describes the position or rank as the second in a series of twenty. For example: "The twentieth place was awarded to the winner."
