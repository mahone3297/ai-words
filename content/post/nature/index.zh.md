## 读音
/nəˈtʃər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 天然状态；自然；本质：例如 "The beauty of nature is breathtaking."
  - 生物学上的种，种类：例如 "The nature of the virus made it difficult to contain."
  - 性格；本性：例如 "She has a kind nature."

## 短语
- "In the nature of" 表示本质上，固有的：例如 "It's in the nature of humans to explore."
- "Laws of nature" 指自然界的基本法则：例如 "Scientists strive to understand the laws of nature."

## 例句
- 名词: "Let's go for a walk and enjoy the peacefulness of nature."
- 动词（在某些特定表达中可作动词）: "His work natured his creativity."
