## 读音
/ˈʃeɪdi/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 阴凉的；不光明正大的；可疑的。例如: "There were plenty of shady spots in the park to escape the sun." 
- **名词**: 与阴暗、非法或不可靠有关的人或事物。例如: "He has some shady connections in the underworld."

## 短语
- "shady dealings" 意味着不正当的交易。
- "a shady character" 指的是行为可疑或不可信的人。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The tree provided a shady spot for us to have a picnic."
- 名词: "She sensed something shady about his business proposal."
