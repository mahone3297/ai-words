## Pronunciation
/kənˈvɪniəns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The state or quality of being convenient; ease of use or access. 2. Something that is convenient or suitable for a particular purpose. 3. An accommodation or service provided for the sake of convenience. 
- **Verb**: To make something convenient or easy; to provide with convenience.

## Phrases
- "Convenience store" refers to a small retail store that caters to everyday needs.
- "Convenience factor" is the ease with which something can be done or accessed.
- "Convenience pricing" is the strategy of setting prices to maximize customer convenience.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The new apartment complex offers excellent convenience with its proximity to public transportation."
- **Verb**: "They designed the app to provide users with the convenience of managing their schedules on-the-go."
- **Adjective**: "The location was chosen for its high level of convenience."
