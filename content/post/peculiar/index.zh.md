## 释义
- **形容词**: 特有的，独特的，特别的。例如: "She has a peculiar way of speaking."
- **名词**: 奇特的事物。例如: "There is something peculiar about that house."

## 短语
- "peculiar to" 意味着独有的或特有的。例如: "This phenomenon is peculiar to our region."
- "peculiar habits" 指特殊或怪异的习惯。例如: "He had some peculiar habits that set him apart from others."
