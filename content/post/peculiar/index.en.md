## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characteristic or unusual; not common or typical. For example: "Her peculiar behavior caught everyone's attention."
- **Adverb**: In a peculiar manner; unusually. For example: "He spoke in a peculiar tone."

## Phrases
- "A peculiar individual" refers to someone who is unusual or unique.
- "His peculiar habits" describes unusual or idiosyncratic routines.
- "That's a peculiar way to solve the problem" implies an unconventional approach.
