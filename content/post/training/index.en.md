## Pronunciation
/træɪnɪŋ/

## Definitions
### Noun
**Training**: 
1. The act or process of teaching someone to acquire a particular skill or knowledge through practice and guidance. 
2. Regular exercise or exercises designed to improve physical fitness or performance. 
3. The period of preparation for a specific role or event, often in sports or military contexts.

### Verb
**Train (someone)**:
1. To teach or educate someone in a particular subject or skill.
2. To prepare or develop (a person) physically for a sport or activity.

## Phrases
- "Undergo training" refers to the process of learning or developing through practice.
- "Basic training" is the initial education given to new recruits.
- "Advanced training" is specialized instruction for experienced individuals.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She completed a six-month training program to become a certified yoga instructor."
- **Verb**: "The athletes are undergoing rigorous training to prepare for the Olympics."
- **Verb**: "The new employees will go through a comprehensive training session next week."
