## 读音
/rʌʃ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 匆忙，迅速行动。例如: "We need to rush to catch the train."
- **名词**: 匆忙，匆促。例如: "There was a rush to get everything done on time."
- **名词**: 潮流，高峰期。例如: "The morning rush hour is always busy on the roads."
- **名词**: 大量，蜂拥而至。例如: "The store had a rush of customers after the sale started."

## 短语
- "rush hour" 指交通或其他地方的高峰时段。
- "in a rush" 表示很匆忙，没有时间。例如: "I'm in a rush, so I can't talk long."

## 例句
- 动词: "He rushed out of the door when he heard the phone ringing."
- 名词: "During the Christmas season, the post office experiences a rush of packages to deliver."
- 名词: "The restaurant gets a rush of diners around lunchtime."
