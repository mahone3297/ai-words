## 读音
/tɪˈtl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 标题；名称。例如: "The book's title is 'To Kill a Mockingbird'."
- **名词**: 2. 头衔；称号。例如: "She received the title of Queen."
- **名词**: 3. 角色；职位。例如: "He played the title role in the movie."
- **名词**: 4. （音乐、电影等作品的）主题曲。例如: "The title track of the album is very popular."
- **动词**: 给…命名；加标题于。例如: "They titled their new song 'Forever Young'."

## 短语
- "Title track" 指的是专辑或电影中的同名歌曲。
- "Title sequence" 指电影或电视节目的开场标题画面。
- "Title deed" 是证明土地或财产所有权的法律文件。

## 例句
- 名词: "The painting's title inspired its creation."
- 名词: "He was given the title Duke after his father's death."
- 动词: "She titled her latest poem 'A Summer's Day'."
