## 读音
/rɪsk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 可能导致损失或不利结果的机会或不确定性。例如: "Investing in stocks carries a certain level of risk."
- **动词**: 冒险做某事。例如: "He risked his life to save the drowning child."
- **形容词**: 涉及风险的；冒险的。例如: "risk-taking behavior"

## 短语
- "at risk" 表示处于危险之中。例如: "Children living in poverty are often at risk for malnutrition."
- "to take a risk" 冒险去做某事。例如: "Starting a new business is always a big risk."
- "risk assessment" 风险评估。例如: "Companies conduct risk assessments before launching new products."

## 例句
- 名词: "The company needs to manage its financial risks effectively."
- 动词: "She risked losing her job by speaking out against the company's policies."
- 形容词: "He's a risk-taker, always willing to try something new."
