## Pronunciation
/ˈsfer/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **sphere** is a geometric shape that is perfectly round and has no edges or corners. It is the three-dimensional counterpart of a circle and is defined by all points equidistant from a central point, called the center.

### Verb
To **sphere** can be used metaphorically to describe something that has a rounded or globular form, or to refer to the process of forming or becoming spherical.

### Adjective
- **Spherical** describes something that is shaped like a sphere, having perfect circular cross-sections.

### Adverb
Not applicable for this word.

## Phrases
- "A sphere of influence" refers to an area where someone's power or control has a significant impact.
- "The Earth is a sphere" is a basic scientific fact.
- "A spherical cow" is a thought experiment used to simplify physics problems by assuming cows have no depth.
