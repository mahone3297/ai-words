- **名词**:

- **形容词**:
    - 球面的，与球体相关的。例如: "A sphere mirror reflects light evenly."

## 短语
- "in the same sphere" 指在同一领域或行业。例如: "Both scientists work in the same sphere of research."
- "sphere of influence" 一个国家或实体的势力范围。例如: "The country's sphere of influence extends over its neighboring territories."
