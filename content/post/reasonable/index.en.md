## Pronunciation
/rɪˈznəbl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Describing something that is logically or reasonably acceptable, fair, or within limits. For example: "The price was reasonable for the quality of the product."
- **Adverb**: In a reasonable manner; sensibly or judiciously. For example: "She handled the situation in a very reasonable way."

## Phrases
- "Act reasonably" means to behave or make decisions with good judgment.
- "Be reasonable" is a request for someone to be logical or considerate.
- "It's not unreasonable to expect..." implies a justified expectation.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "He made a reasonable decision after thorough research."
- **Adverb**: "She asked him to be reasonable about his demands."
- "The child's bedtime was reasonable, around 8 o'clock."
