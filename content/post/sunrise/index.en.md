## Pronunciation
/səˈraɪzn/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: **1** The period of time when the sun rises above the horizon, marking the beginning of the day. **2** A beautiful and spectacular display of colors in the sky at dawn. **3** (figurative) The start or the beginning of something, often symbolizing hope or a new chapter. 

- **Verb**: To observe or experience the sunrise; to witness the start of the day's light.

## Phrases
- "Watch the sunrise" means to enjoy the spectacle of the sun rising.
- "The first light of the sunrise" describes the initial glow of dawn.
- "To greet the sunrise" symbolically signifies a fresh start or positive outlook.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She loved to wake up early to watch the sunrise over the ocean."
- **Verb**: "After a long night, he stumbled out to catch the sunrise."
- **Phrasal Usage**: "Starting her day with a morning jog by the beach, she was grateful to be able to greet the sunrise each day."
