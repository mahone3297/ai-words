## 读音
/rɪˈmɑːrkəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 异常的，显著的，非凡的。例如: "He achieved remarkable success in his career."
- **名词**: 令人惊叹的事物。例如: "Her art collection is a remarkable display of talent."

## 短语
- "remarkable for something" 表示在某方面引人注目。例如: "The city is remarkable for its architecture."
- "remarkable person" 指非常出众或有才华的人。例如: "She is a remarkable person, both in intelligence and kindness."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new technology has brought about remarkable changes in our daily lives."
- 名词: "His recovery from the illness was nothing short of remarkable."
