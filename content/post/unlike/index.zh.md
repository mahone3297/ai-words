## 读音
/ʌnˈlaɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不同的，不相似的，相异的。例如: "Their personalities are unlike in many ways."
- **介词**: 表示对比，与...不同。例如: "Unlike her sister, she enjoys public speaking."

## 短语
- "be unlike sb/sth" 表示与某人或某物不相同。
- "unlike in sth" 用来强调在某个方面的不同。例如: "They are alike in appearance but unlike in temperament."

## 例句
- 形容词: "The two brothers have very different interests; they are unlike in every way."
- 介词: "Unlike her brother, she is a talented musician."
