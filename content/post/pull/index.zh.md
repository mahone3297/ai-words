## 读音
/pʊl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 拉，拖，牵引。例如: "He pulled the door open with all his strength."
- **名词**: 拉力，牵引力。例如: "The gravitational pull of the moon affects the tides."
- **名词**: 影响力，说服力。例如: "She has a lot of pull in the company, so her opinion matters."

## 短语
- "to pull someone/something towards oneself" 意为将某人或某物拉向自己。例如: "The child was pulling his mother closer."
- "to pull in" 可以指吸引或召集。例如: "The concert pulled in a large crowd."
- "to pull one's weight" 意为尽职尽责，做出应有的贡献。例如: "Every team member needs to pull their weight for us to succeed."

## 例句
- 动词: "She pulled the lever to start the machine."
- 名词: "The horse felt the full pull of the cart on its back."
- 名词: "His political pull helped get the project approved."

## 同义词
- Drag
- Tug
- Draw
- Pull off (在某些上下文中)
- Maneuver (在某些情境下)
