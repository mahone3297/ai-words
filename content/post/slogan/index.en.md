## Pronunciation
/səˈlɑːɡən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A short, memorable phrase or slogan used to convey a message, often for advertising, marketing, or promoting a product, idea, or cause. It aims to be catchy and easily remembered. Example: "Just Do It" for Nike.
- **Verb**: To create or use a slogan as part of a campaign or branding strategy. Example: "The company decided to rebrand with a new slogan."

## Phrases
- "A catchy slogan" implies a slogan that is easy to remember and attracts attention.
- "Slogans resonate with consumers" highlights their effectiveness in connecting with people.
- "Slogans are often used in taglines" to summarize a brand's essence.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The environmental group's slogan, 'Reduce, Reuse, Recycle,' has become a household name."
- **Verb**: "The marketing team brainstormed a new slogan to revitalize the brand's image."
- **Sentence**: "The company's slogan 'Think Different' inspired a generation of Apple customers."
