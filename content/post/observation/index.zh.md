## 读音
/ˌɑːbˈzɜːrvəʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 观察；监视：The act of watching or paying attention to someone or something carefully.
    - 观察结果；观测：The information gained through such an act of watching.

- **动词**:
    - 观察；监视：To watch, notice, or study someone or something carefully over a period of time.

## 短语
- "make an observation" 意为做出观察或发表看法。
- "under observation" 表示处于被观察的状态，如在医学或科学实验中。

## 例句
- 名词: "The scientist recorded his observations in a notebook."
- 动词: "The patient is currently under observation in the hospital."
