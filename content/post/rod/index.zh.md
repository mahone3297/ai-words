## 读音
/rd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 一根细长的棍子或棒，通常用于钓鱼、支撑或惩罚。例如: "He used a rod to steady himself while climbing the ladder."
   - 在度量衡中，rod 是一个长度单位，等于16.5英尺或5.0292米。例如: "The field was measured in rods."

- **动词**:
   - 用棍子或其他类似物打、敲击。例如: "The teacher threatened to rod the misbehaving students."
   - 钓鱼时，用竿钓鱼。例如: "He rods for fish every weekend."

## 短语
- "rod of correction" 指用来惩罚的棍子。
- "casting a rod" 指在钓鱼时投掷钓竿。

## 例句
- 名词: "The gardener used a long rod to reach the high branches."
- 动词: "She expertly rods the water, waiting for a bite."
