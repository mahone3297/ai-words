## 读音
/ˈsekʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 一部分；节，段：例如 "The book is divided into several sections."
   - 部门；科：例如 "She works in the sales section of the company."
   - 区域，地段：例如 "There's a new restaurant in the shopping section of town."
   - 法律条款；章节：例如 "Article 3, Section 2 of the Constitution deals with trials."

## 短语
- "Section leader" 指团队或部门的负责人。
- "Section 8" 在美国常指住房补贴计划。
- "Sectional furniture" 是指可拆分组合的家具。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please turn to section 4 for more information on tax laws."
- 动词（作动词时通常与介词搭配）: "The orchestra was divided into different sections for practice."
