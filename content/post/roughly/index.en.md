## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: 1. With roughness or approximate accuracy; not precisely or exactly. For example: "She estimated the distance roughly."
   - **Adjective**: 1. Having a general or approximate quality; not exact or precise. For example: "The measurements were roughly correct."
   - **Adverb**: 2. Coarsely; roughly handled or treated. For example: "The children were roughly handled by their parents."

## Phrases
- "Roughly speaking" means approximately or generally.
- "Roughly estimated" indicates a calculation or approximation.
- "Roughly dressed" describes someone's attire being untidy or casual.
