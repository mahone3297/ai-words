## 读音
/ˈrʌfli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 大致地，粗略地，不精确地。例如: "He estimated the cost roughly."
- **形容词**: 粗糙的，不平滑的。例如: "The surface of the wood was roughly hewn."

## 短语
- "roughly speaking" 意为大约，大致上。
- "roughly the same" 表示差不多，相差无几。

## 例句
- 副词: "The journey took roughly three hours."
- 形容词: "The fabric was so roughly woven that it caused discomfort."
