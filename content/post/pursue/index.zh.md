## 读音
/pərˈsuːv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 追赶，追踪：例如 "The police pursued the suspect through the city."（警察追踪嫌疑犯穿过城市）
2. 坚持，从事：例如 "He pursued his dream of becoming a musician."（他坚持追求成为音乐家的梦想）

## 短语
- "pursue a career" 追求职业生涯
- "pursue one's interests" 追求个人的兴趣
- "pursue an academic degree" 追求学术学位

## 例句
- 动词: "She pursued her studies in art history at the university."（她在大学里继续学习艺术史）
- 动词: "The athlete pursued the ball relentlessly during the match."（运动员在比赛中不停地追赶球）
