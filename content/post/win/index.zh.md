## 读音
/wn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 赢得（比赛、战斗、奖项等）。例如: "He won the gold medal in the marathon."
  - 得到，获得（某物或某人的支持）。例如: "They won the contract after a tough negotiation."
  - （赌博、游戏等）赢钱。例如: "She won a large sum of money playing poker."

- **名词**:
  - 胜利，成功。例如: "Their hard work resulted in a convincing win."
  - 获胜者，优胜者。例如: "The winner of the competition will receive a cash prize."

## 短语
- "Win over" 意为赢得某人的支持或喜爱。例如: "She managed to win over the skeptical audience with her performance."
- "Win against" 表示在竞争中打败某人。例如: "Our team needs to win against their rivals to advance to the next round."

## 例句
- 动词: "They celebrated their win with a big party."
- 名词: "The win gave the team a much-needed confidence boost."
