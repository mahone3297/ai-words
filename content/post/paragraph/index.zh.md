## 读音
/ˈpærəɡrɑːf/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一段文章或写作中的一个自然段落，通常围绕一个主题展开，由一个或多个句子组成。例如: "The first paragraph introduces the main idea of the essay."
- **动词**: 将文字分为段落。例如: "She carefully paragraphed her essay for better readability."

## 短语
- "A new paragraph" 指开始一个新的思想或话题的段落。
- "Indent the first line of each paragraph" 意味着在每个段落的第一行缩进以区分。

## 例句
- 名词: "In the letter, there were three well-structured paragraphs discussing different aspects of the topic."
- 动词: "Please paragraph your text to make it more organized."

## 同义词
- Section
- Part
- Segment
- Division
- Clause (在某些语境下)
