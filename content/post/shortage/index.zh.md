## 读音
/ˈʃɔːrtɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 缺乏，不足；短缺。例如: "There is a shortage of skilled workers in this industry."
- **形容词**: 不足的，短缺的。例如: "The company is experiencing shortage of raw materials."

## 短语
- "shortage of" 表示某种东西的缺乏或不足。例如: "There's a shortage of food in the disaster-stricken area."
- "make up for a shortage" 意味着弥补不足。例如: "They're working overtime to make up for the shortage."

## 例句
- 名词: "The drought has caused a severe water shortage in the region."
- 形容词: "The store had a shortage of toilet paper during the pandemic."
