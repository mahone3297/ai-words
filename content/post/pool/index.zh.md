## 读音
/puːl/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 池塘：A body of water, especially a small one, such as a pond or a swimming pool.
2. 资金池：A collective fund or pool of money contributed by various sources.

- **动词 v**:
1. 集结；汇集：To gather or collect in a pool, as of resources or people.

- **形容词 adj**:
1. 池边的；游泳池的：Relating to a pool, especially a swimming pool.

## 短语
- "swimming pool" 游泳池
- "pool party" 池畔派对
- "pool resources" 共享资源

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were splashing in the pool on a hot summer day."
- 动词: "Several companies decided to pool their resources for the project."
- 形容词: "The poolside chairs were arranged for sunbathing."
