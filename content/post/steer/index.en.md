## Pronunciation
/stɪr/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A device or lever used to control the direction of a vehicle, such as a car or ship.
- The act of guiding or directing something or someone's course, especially by means of a tiller or wheel.
- A person who steers, especially in a group or organization.

### Verb
- To control the course or direction of (a vehicle, ship, or other object).
- To guide or direct the actions or decisions of (a group, project, or process).
- To take charge or lead, particularly in a conversation or debate.

## Phrases
- "Steer clear of" means to avoid something or someone.
- "Steer through the traffic" refers to navigating through busy roads.
- "To steer a company" denotes guiding its direction and strategy.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The captain skillfully steered the ship through the stormy waters."
- **Verb**: "The driver had to quickly steer the car to avoid a collision."
- **Guiding someone**: "He steered the team towards their goals with determination."
