## 读音
/stiːr/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 控制（车辆、船等）的方向；驾驶。例如: "He steered the car through the busy streets."
2. 引导；指导。例如: "The teacher steered the students towards better study habits."

## 短语
- "steer clear of" 避开，避免。例如: "You should steer clear of that topic during the meeting."
- "steer someone in the right direction" 帮助某人找到正确的方法或方向。例如: "Her mentor steered her in the right direction for career advancement."

## 例句
- 动词: "She expertly steered the boat through the narrow canal."
- 动词: "The coach steered the team towards victory with his strategic decisions."
