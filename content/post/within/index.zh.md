## 读音
/ˈwɪðɪn/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示在内部，里面，或在某段时间或范围之内。例如: "The key is within the house." 或 "We should finish the project within two weeks."

## 短语
- "within reach" 意为触手可及，容易得到。例如: "The book is within reach on the desk."
- "within sight" 意为在视线之内，看得见。例如: "The finish line is now within sight."
- "within reason" 意为合理的范围内，适度的。例如: "You can ask for anything, but it has to be within reason."

## 例句
- "The restaurant is just within walking distance from our hotel."
- "The deadline for submission is within the next 48 hours."
- "The child must stay within his mother's sight at all times."
