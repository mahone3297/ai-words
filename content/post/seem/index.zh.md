## 读音
/seem/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 表现出；显得：看起来，似乎。例如: "She seems happy today."
2. 给人以...的印象：好像，仿佛。例如: "It seems that he has a lot of experience."

## 短语
- "Seemingly" 作为副词，意思是表面上，看似。例如: "Seemingly, everything was going well."

## 例句
- "The weather seems to be improving." (天气似乎正在好转。)
- "It seems unlikely that he will come." (他似乎不太可能来。)

- Appear
