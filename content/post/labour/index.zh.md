## 读音
/ˈleɪbər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 工作；劳动；劳动力。例如: "He's been putting in long hours of manual labour."
- **动词**: 做苦工；努力工作。例如: "The construction workers laboured through the hot summer days."
- **形容词**: 与工作或工人有关的。例如: "The labour movement fought for better working conditions."

## 短语
- "Hard labour" 指繁重或艰难的工作。
- "labour market" 指劳动力市场，即求职者和雇主相互作用的地方。
- "labour laws" 是指保护工人权益的法规。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company is investing in new technology to increase productivity and reduce labour costs."
- 动词: "She laboured for months on her thesis, finally completing it just in time for the deadline."
- 形容词: "The labour-intensive process required many workers to complete."
