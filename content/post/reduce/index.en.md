## Pronunciation
/rɪ'duːs/

## Definitions
### Noun
A reduction in quantity, amount, or intensity; a decrease. *Example*: "The company aims to reduce its carbon footprint."

### Verb
1. To lower or decrease the size, amount, or level of something. *Example*: "They reduced the price of their products."
2. To simplify or lessen the complexity of a task or process. *Example*: "The teacher showed the students how to reduce fractions."
3. To cut back on consumption or usage. *Example*: "People are reducing their plastic waste."

### Adjective
Characterized by reduction or decreased. *Example*: "The diet is designed to be low-calorie and nutrient-reducing."

## Phrases
- "Reduce it to the basics"
- "Reduce, reuse, recycle"
- "Cut down on/to reduce"
- "Bring something under control by reducing it"

## Example Sentences
