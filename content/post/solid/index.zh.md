## 读音
/ˈsɑːlɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 固体的，有形的，实体的。例如: "This object is solid, not liquid or gas."
- **名词**: 固体，实体。例如: "The solid state of matter has a defined shape and volume."
- **动词**: (使)坚固，巩固。例如: "Their friendship solidified after they went through a tough time together."

## 短语
- "in solid form" 表示以固体形式存在。
- "solid ground" 指坚实的地面或可靠的依据。
- "solid support" 意为坚定的支持或支援。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The block of ice is solid at this temperature."
- 名词: "The elements in the periodic table can exist as solids, liquids, or gases."
- 动词: "Their teamwork solidified their chances of winning the tournament."
