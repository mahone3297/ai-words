## 读音
/ˈmɪʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
    - 任务，使命：A specific task or purpose that one is sent to fulfill, often by an organization or higher authority.
    - 传教，布道：The act of spreading religious beliefs, especially as part of a religious organization's efforts.

- **形容词**:
    - 有使命感的：Relating to or characterized by a strong sense of purpose or duty.

## 短语
- "Mission statement" 一个组织或个人的目标和宗旨声明。
- "On a mission" 专注于完成特定任务或目标。

## 例句
- 名词: "The astronaut's main mission was to explore the surface of Mars."
- 形容词: "She is a mission-driven person who always strives to achieve her goals."
