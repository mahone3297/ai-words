## 读音
/wənˈdər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 漫步，闲逛。例如: "I love to wander through the park on a sunny day."
2. 走神，游离。例如: "His mind wandered during the long lecture."
3. （思想）飘忽不定。例如: "She wandered in her thoughts, lost in memories."

- **名词**:
1. 游荡，徘徊。例如: "The children were allowed to wander freely in the garden."
2. 迷路，迷失。例如: "He got lost and wandered for hours."

## 短语
- "to wander off" 意为走开或离开原地。例如: "The dog wandered off after chasing a rabbit."
- "wander around" 表示在某处随意走动。例如: "We spent the afternoon wandering around the old town."

## 例句
- 动词: "After dinner, they decided to wander along the beach."
- 名词: "Their wander through the forest led them to a hidden waterfall."
