## 读音
/ˈteknɪkl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 技术的，专业的；涉及技术细节的。例如: "The technical aspects of the project require specialized knowledge."
- **名词**: 技术人员，技工。例如: "The technical team is responsible for fixing the issue."

## 短语
- "Technical support" 指技术支持或帮助。
- "Technical difficulty" 指技术上的问题或故障。
- "Technical jargon" 指行业术语或专业词汇。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The job requires strong technical skills in programming."
- 名词: "The company's technical department handles all software issues."
