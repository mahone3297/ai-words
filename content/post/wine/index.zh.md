## 读音
/waɪn/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 酒，尤指葡萄酒。例如: "She enjoys a glass of red wine with dinner."
  - 葡萄酒的种类。例如: "Chardonnay is a popular white wine."

## 短语
- "a bottle of wine" 一瓶葡萄酒。
- "wine tasting" 葡萄酒品鉴活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The sommelier recommended a crisp Sauvignon Blanc to pair with the seafood."
- 短语: "They organized a wine tasting event to explore different flavors."

## 同义词
- Beer
- Spirits
- Liquor
- Alcohol
- Cider (苹果酒，特定情况下可作为酒精饮料的同义词)
