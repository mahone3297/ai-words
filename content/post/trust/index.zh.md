## 读音
/trʌst/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 信任；信赖：例如 "She has a deep trust in her friends."
  - 信心；信念：例如 "He acted with great trust in his abilities."
  - 可信赖的人或事物：例如 "Her parents are her rock of trust."
  - （法律上的）信托；受托管理的财产：例如 "A trust fund was set up for the child's education."

- **动词**:
  - 信赖；相信：例如 "I trust that he will do the right thing."
  - 委托；托付：例如 "She trusted him with her secret."

## 短语
- "In trust" 表示某物被托管或以信托方式持有。
- "Trust someone with something" 意味着将某事交给某人处理或保管。

## 例句
- 名词: "Building trust takes time in any relationship."
- 动词: "She trusts him completely, knowing he'll always be honest."

- Believe
- Rely on
- Have faith in
- Depend on
