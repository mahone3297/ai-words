## 读音
/ˈrɪv.aɪz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 修改，修订。例如: "I need to revise my essay before submitting it."
  - 复习，温习（功课）。例如: "She spends hours revising for her exams."

## 短语
- "revise for exams" 意为为考试复习。
- "revise the manuscript" 意为修改手稿或稿件。

## 例句
- 动词: "He is revising his novel to include new characters."
- 动词: "We'll revise our lesson plan based on student feedback."
