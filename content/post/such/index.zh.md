## 读音
/sʌtʃ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 如此的，这样的，那样的。表示程度或强调。例如: "Such a beautiful day."
- **代词**: 指示或强调前面提到的人或事物。例如: "I've never seen such talent before."

## 短语
- "such as" 用来列举相似的事物。例如: "There are many fruits, such as apples and oranges."
- "such that" 引导结果状语从句，表示以至。例如: "The problem was so difficult that no one could solve it."

## 例句
- 形容词: "She had such grace and poise on stage."
- 代词: "I've never encountered such an interesting book."

## 同义词
- So (形容词时)
- This kind of
- That (代词时)
