## 读音
/ˈpaɪənɪər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 开拓者，先驱者。指在某一领域或地区首先进行探索或开创工作的人。例如: "He was a pioneer in the field of computer science."
- **动词**: 开创，引领新潮流。例如: "The company is pioneering new technologies in renewable energy."

## 短语
- "Pioneer settlement" 指早期移民建立的定居点。
- "Pioneer work" 指开创性的工作或研究。

## 例句
- 名词: "The early settlers of the American West were true pioneers."
- 动词: "Her innovative teaching methods have pioneered a new approach to education."
