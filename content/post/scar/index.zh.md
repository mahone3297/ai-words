## 读音
/skɑːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 因伤口愈合而留下的痕迹或标记。例如: "She still had a scar on her arm from the accident."
- **名词**: 心灵上的创伤或痛苦的记忆。例如: "The emotional scars of the divorce were still fresh."

## 短语
- "scar tissue" 意为伤疤组织。
- "to bear the scars" 意味着承受过去的痛苦经历。

## 例句
- "The surgery left a small scar on his abdomen."
- "The war veterans carried the scars of their experiences for the rest of their lives."
