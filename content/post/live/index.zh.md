## 读音
/lɪv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 居住：居住在某个地方。例如: "She lives in New York City."
2. 生活，生存：维持生命，过日子。例如: "They live off the land."
3. 实况转播：指电视或广播节目现场直播。例如: "The concert will be broadcast live."

- **形容词**:
1. 活的，有生命的：不是死的或人造的。例如: "We watched a live performance."
2. 进行中的，实况的：如现场音乐或体育赛事。例如: "The show is live tonight."

## 短语
- "live up to" 遵守（诺言），达到（期望）。例如: "She always tries to live up to her promises."
- "live off" 依靠...生活。例如: "Many students live off part-time jobs."

## 例句
- 动词: "He has lived in Paris for five years."
- 形容词: "The band is known for their high-energy live shows."
