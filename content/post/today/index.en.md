## Pronunciation
/təˈdeɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A specific day in the current week or month, usually referring to the present time. For example: "Today is a busy day for me."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of the present time or day. For example: "The weather today is beautiful."
- **Interjection**: Expressing the idea of the current moment or the near future. For example: "Let's meet today!"

## Phrases
- "What's on your agenda for today?" is a question about someone's plans for the day.
- "It's just another day" implies that the day is like any other.
- "Today is the first day of the rest of your life" is a proverb suggesting the importance of making a fresh start.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I have a lot of work to do today, so I can't join you for lunch."
- **Adjective**: "The weather today is perfect for a picnic in the park."
- **Interjection**: "Let's make today a memorable day by trying something new."
