## Pronunciation
/plʌs/

## Definitions
### Noun
A plus sign (+) represents addition or a positive quantity in mathematics. It can also be used metaphorically to indicate something additional or favorable. In computing, it often refers to a feature or option.

### Verb
To "plus" something means to include it, add it, or consider it as an extra benefit or component. It can be used informally to express increment or enhancement.

### Adjective
In the context of numbers, "plus" means greater than zero or positive. It can also denote superiority or advantage.

### Adverb
Sometimes used informally to mean "in addition" or "moreover," expressing that something is added or enhanced.

## Phrases
- "Add a plus one" to indicate an additional person or unit.
- "Plus side" refers to the positive aspects or benefits of a situation.
- "Plus size" is a clothing measurement indicating larger sizes.
- "Plus-minus" (±) indicates a range of values, both above and below a stated value.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The equation shows 3 plus 5 equals 8."
- **Verb**: "She decided to take the course with the plus features included."
- **Adjective**: "The new model has a plus screen size compared to the previous one."
- **Adverb**: "Her project had several plus points, making it stand out among others."
