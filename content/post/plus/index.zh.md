## 读音
/plʌs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 加号，表示增加或正数。例如: "In mathematics, plus (+) means addition."  
2. 优势，加分项。例如: "Her experience was a big plus for the company."

- **形容词**: 额外的，附加的。例如: "The hotel offered plus services such as spa treatments."

- **副词**: 除此之外，另外。例如: "I need more time, plus I have other commitments."

## 短语
- "Plus size" 指加大号的衣服或尺寸。
- "Plus or minus" 表示大约，误差范围。例如: "The estimate is plus or minus five percent."

## 例句
- 名词: "In the equation 5 + 3 = 8, the plus sign indicates addition."
- 形容词: "This phone comes with a plus battery that lasts all day."
- 副词: "We will meet at 7 PM, and plus, we can have dinner together."

- Additional
- Extra
