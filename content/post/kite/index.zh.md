## 读音
/kaɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种玩具，通常由布或塑料制成，有骨架，用一根长线控制，可以在空中飞行。例如: "The children were flying their kites in the park."
- **名词**: 某些鸟类的名称，如红隼（kite bird）。例如: "The red kite is a beautiful bird of prey."
- **名词**: 在某些地区，指一种轻便的飞行器，用于娱乐或体育竞赛。例如: "The international kite festival showcased various types of kites."

## 短语
- "Fly a kite" 意味着放风筝。
- "Kite surfing" 是一种水上运动，结合了风筝和冲浪。

## 例句
- 名词: "The kids spent the afternoon running with their colorful kites in the wind."
- 名词 (bird): "The kites soared gracefully above the fields."
- 名词 (flying device): "He's an expert in kiteboarding and can perform amazing tricks."

## 同义词
- 风筝 (for the toy)
