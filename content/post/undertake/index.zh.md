## 读音
/ˌʌndərˈteɪk/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 承担，着手：开始一项任务或工作。例如: "I have undertaken a new project at work."
2. 承诺，保证：同意做某事或保证完成某事。例如: "The company undertook to deliver the goods within a week."
3. 承担责任：承担某种义务或责任。例如: "He undertook the responsibility of caring for his elderly parents."

## 短语
- "Undertake a task" 意为开始执行一个任务。
- "Undertake extensive research" 指进行广泛的研究。

## 例句
- 动词: "She undertook the challenge of learning a new language in just six months."
- 动词: "They undertook to fix the problem and ensure it wouldn't happen again."
