## Word: Lovely

## Pronunciation
/ˈlʌvli/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Extremely attractive, beautiful, or delightful; charming. For example: "She looked absolutely lovely in her new dress."
- **Adjective (informal)**: Excellent or very good. For example: "We had a lovely time at the beach yesterday."

### Example Sentences
- **Adjective (attractive)**: "The garden was filled with lovely flowers that attracted butterflies."
- **Adjective (informal)**: "The food at the restaurant was simply lovely – especially the pasta dish."

## Phrases
- "Lovely day": Refers to a particularly beautiful or enjoyable day weather-wise or otherwise.
- "Have a lovely day": A common expression used to wish someone a pleasant day ahead.
- "A sight for sore eyes": An idiomatic expression meaning something or someone very pleasing or lovely to look at after a period of difficulty or strain.
