## 读音
/prɪˈpliːz/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示客气或请求的方式，意为“请”。例如: "Please pass the salt."
- **动词**: 请求或恳求某人做某事。例如: "I would please you to lend me your book."
- **形容词**: 用于某些固定表达，如"please sb."，表示使某人满意或愉快。例如: "The meal pleased all the guests."

## 短语
- "Would you please..." 用于礼貌地提出请求。例如: "Would you please turn down the music?"
- "If it pleases you" 用于表示尊重对方的意见或决定。例如: "If it pleases you, we can discuss this later."

## 例句
- 副词: "Please wait outside until I'm finished."
- 动词: "I'm pleasing you to attend the meeting tomorrow."
- 形容词: "Your performance really pleased the audience."
