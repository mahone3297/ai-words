## Pronunciation
/pliːz/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Expressing politeness or request for permission or agreement. It can be used to introduce a statement or to ask for something. For example: "Please pass the salt." or "Could you please close the door?"
- **Verb**: To make a polite request or entreaty. For example: "I would like to please everyone with my performance."
- **Interjection**: Used to express a polite request or expression of desire. For example: "Please don't forget my umbrella."

## Phrases
- "With pleasure" indicates willingness to do something happily.
- "I'll do it, please" is a way to confirm agreement or acceptance.
- "Thank you for your help, please" combines gratitude and politeness.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "Please remember to bring your homework tomorrow."
- **Verb**: "She politely asked if they could have a few more minutes."
- **Interjection**: "Please don't hesitate to ask if you need assistance."

## Synonyms
- Kindly
- Requestingly
- Courteously
- Beguilingly
- Graciously
