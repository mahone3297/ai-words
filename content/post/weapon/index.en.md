## Definitions
- **Noun**: A tool, device, or instrument designed for causing harm, damage, or defense, typically involving the use of force. Examples include guns, swords, or bows.
- **Verb**: To arm oneself or use a weapon in combat or self-defense. For example: "The soldier quickly armed himself with his rifle."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing something that is used as a weapon or has a weapon-like quality. For example: "The robot was equipped with advanced weaponry."

## Phrases
- "Armed with a weapon" signifies having a weapon in one's possession or ready for use.
- "Weaponry" refers to the collection or use of weapons.
- "Non-lethal weapon" describes a weapon that is intended to cause minimal harm.
