## 读音
/lɔːd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 大声的，响亮的。例如: "She sang in a loud voice."
- **副词**: 大声地，响亮地。例如: "Speak louder so everyone can hear you."

## 短语
- "Loud music" 指声音大的音乐。
- "A loud noise" 表示噪音或大声的声音。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The children were playing with loud toys, which bothered the neighbors."
- 副词: "He shouted so loud that the whole neighborhood could hear him."
