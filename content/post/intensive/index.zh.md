## 读音
/ɪnˈtɛnsɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 密集的；集中的；深入的。例如: "She underwent an intensive language course to improve her skills."
- **副词**: 详尽地；集中精力地。例如: "The athlete trained intensively for the upcoming competition."

## 短语
- "intensive care unit (ICU)" 指重症监护室。
- "intensive farming" 意为集约农业，高强度耕作。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The training program was so intensive that participants had no time for leisure activities."
- 副词: "They studied the subject intensively to prepare for the exam."
