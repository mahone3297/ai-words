## 读音
/pʌŋktʃuəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 准时的，守时的。例如: "He is always punctual for his appointments."
- **副词**: 准时地，按时地。例如: "The train arrived punctually at 8 o'clock."

## 短语
- "be punctual for something" 意味着在约定的时间到达或做某事。
- "punctual habits" 指的是准时的习惯。

## 例句
- 形容词: "She is known for her punctual nature, never being late to work."
- 副词: "The delivery service is very punctual, ensuring packages arrive on time."
