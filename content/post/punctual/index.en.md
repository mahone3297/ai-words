## Pronunciation
/pənˈkjuːlt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by being on time or keeping to a schedule; dependable. For example: "The punctual employee arrives at work every day without fail."
- **Noun**: A person who is punctual; one who arrives or does something at the appointed time. For example: "She is known for her punctual nature."
- **Verb**: To be or arrive at a place on time. For example: "He punctually showed up for the meeting."

## Phrases
- "Be punctual" means to be on time.
- "Punctual as a Swiss watch" is a metaphor for someone who is consistently reliable.
- "To run late or be unpunctual" implies being consistently late.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The punctual train always departs precisely at 6:00 AM."
- **Noun**: "Her punctual attendance at meetings was appreciated by the team."
- **Verb**: "I need you to punctually submit your assignments by Friday."
