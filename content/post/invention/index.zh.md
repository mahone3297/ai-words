## 读音
/ɪnˈvenʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 创新；发明：新方法或设备的创造，如 "The invention of the wheel revolutionized transportation."
   - 发明物：一项创新或独特的创造，如 "His latest invention is a machine that can clean windows automatically."

## 短语
- "Patent invention" 指已申请专利的发明。
- "Industrial invention" 指适用于工业生产或应用的发明。

## 例句
- 名词: "The invention of the printing press was a milestone in human history."
- "Her brilliant invention has made her a millionaire."
