## 读音
/ʃeɪm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 感到羞愧或耻辱的状态；惭愧。例如: "He felt deep shame for his actions."
- **动词**: 使感到羞愧或丢脸。例如: "Her behavior shamed her entire family."
- **形容词**: 引起羞愧的；可耻的。例如: "It's a shame to waste so much food."

## 短语
- "a sense of shame" 意为羞耻感。
- "to be ashamed of something" 意为对某事感到羞愧。
- "shame on you" 用于责备或批评某人的行为。

## 例句
- 名词: "The child had no shame about stealing candy from the store."
- 动词: "His dishonesty should shame him, but he seems unbothered."
- 形容词: "It's a shame that such a talented person ended up in jail."

- Embarrassment
- Dishonor
- Disgrace
- Guilt
- Mortification
