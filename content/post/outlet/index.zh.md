## 读音
/ˈaʊtliːt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  1. 出口；排出口：例如 "The river flows through a series of outlets before reaching the sea."
  2. 发泄途径；释放渠道：例如 "Exercise is a great stress outlet for many people."
  3. 销售点；分店：例如 "They have several clothing outlets across the city."
  4. （电器）插座：例如 "Plug the device into the electrical outlet."

## 短语
- "Power outlet" 指的是电源插座。
- "Retail outlet" 指的是零售商店或专卖店。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please locate the nearest power outlet to charge your phone."
- 名词: "The new mall has an outlet for every major brand."
- 名词: "Writing serves as an emotional outlet for her."
