## Pronunciation
/streɪt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: 1. Directly in a line or direction; not bent or curved. For example: "The arrow shot straight through the target."
   - **Noun**: A straight line or path. For example: "Drive straight on until you reach the next intersection."
   - **Adverb**: In a straight manner; without deviation. For example: "She walked straight home."

## Phrases
- "Keep it straight" means to be honest or accurate.
- "Straight talk" refers to straightforward and honest communication.
- "She drove straight into my life" is a figure of speech for unexpectedly meeting someone.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The ruler is straight and precise."
- **Noun**: "The fence runs straight across the yard."
- **Adverb**: "He told me the story straight, without any embellishments."
