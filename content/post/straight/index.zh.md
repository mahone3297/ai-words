## 读音
/streɪt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 直的，不弯曲的。例如: "The road is straight for miles."
- **副词**: 直接地，径直地。例如: "Go straight down this street."
- **名词**: 直线，正直的人或行为。例如: "He drew a straight line on the paper."
- **动词**: 使变直，纠正。例如: "Can you straighten the picture on the wall?"

## 短语
- "Go straight ahead" 意为一直往前走。
- "Be straight with someone" 意味对某人坦诚相待。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The ruler has a perfectly straight edge."
- 副词: "Drive straight through the intersection."
- 名词: "She admires his honesty and straight character."
- 动词: "He straightened the crooked picture frame."
