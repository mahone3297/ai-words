## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Thoroughly done; complete; meticulous. For example: "The investigator conducted a thorough search of the crime scene."
- **Verb**: To perform or examine something in every detail, completely. For example: "She thoroughly cleaned the kitchen before starting a new project."
- **Adverb**: In a thorough manner; completely. For example: "He studied the subject thoroughly."

## Phrases
- "Thorough examination" implies a careful and detailed inspection.
- "A through-and-through professional" refers to someone who is consistently diligent and competent.
- "Thoroughly enjoyed" means to have experienced something with complete satisfaction.
