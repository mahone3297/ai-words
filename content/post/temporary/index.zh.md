## 读音
/ˈtɛmpərəri/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 暂时的，非永久的。例如: "We need a temporary solution to this problem."
- **名词**: 临时工，临时雇员。例如: "She's working as a temporary secretary while she looks for a permanent job."

## 短语
- "Temporary residence" 指临时居住的地方。
- "On a temporary basis" 意味着暂时地，不是长期的。
- "Temporary employment" 指短期的工作机会。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The company is hiring temporary workers to help with the busy season."
- 名词: "He's a temporary worker, so he doesn't have any benefits yet."

## 同义词
- Temporary can be replaced by:
  - Provisional
  - Interim
  - Short-term
  - Transitory
  - fleeting
