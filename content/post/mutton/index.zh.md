## 读音
/ˈmʌtn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 羊肉，尤指绵羊肉。例如: "We had mutton chops for dinner last night."
- **形容词**: 指与绵羊有关的或像绵羊的。在某些地区或语境中，也可指幼稚或缺乏经验的。例如: "The mutton dressed as lamb phrase refers to an older woman trying to look young."

## 短语
- "mutton dressed as lamb" 指年纪较大的女性试图打扮得年轻时尚。

## 例句
- 名词: "The butcher had fresh mutton available in his shop."
- 形容词: "Some people criticized her for acting mutton when she wore that short dress."

## 同义词
- Lamb (尤其是指作为食物的羊肉)
- Sheep meat
- Goat meat (在某些文化中，mutton也可能指山羊肉)
