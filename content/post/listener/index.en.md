## Word: Listener

## Pronunciation
/ˈlɪs.nər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A person who listens attentively to something, especially to spoken words, music, or other sounds. For example: "The radio show had millions of listeners tuning in every week."

Example sentence: 
- "As a good listener, she always provided a comforting presence for her friends to share their problems with."

## Phrases
- "Audience member" or "listener" can refer to a person attending a talk, lecture, or performance to listen.
- "Active listener" describes a person who not only hears but also engages with and comprehends what they're listening to.
- "Listener feedback" refers to comments or opinions from those who have listened to a broadcast or presentation.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The podcast has gained a loyal community of listeners over the years."
- "He was such a dedicated listener that he never missed a single episode of his favorite podcast."

## Synonyms
- Audience
- Hearers
- Listenership
- Auditor
- Spectator (in certain contexts)
- Follower (in the context of podcasts, broadcasts, etc.)
- Attendee (at a live event)
