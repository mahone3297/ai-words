## 释义
- **形容词**: 符合法律的，合法的。例如: "It's legal to drive a car at 18 years old in this state."
- **名词**: 法律事务，法律行业的人或事物。例如: "She works in a law firm as a legal consultant."
- **动词**: 使合法化，正式承认。例如: "The new law will legalise cannabis for medical use."

## 短语
