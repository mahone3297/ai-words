## Pronunciation
/ˈliːɡəl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Pertaining to law or the administration of justice; authorized by law; according to the law. For example: "The legal drinking age in this country is 21."
- **Noun**: A person qualified to practice law; a lawyer. For instance: "She hired a top legal expert to represent her case."

### Phrases
- "Legal advice" refers to guidance provided by a qualified lawyer about legal rights and responsibilities.
- "Legal action" denotes taking legal steps, such as filing a lawsuit, to resolve a dispute.
- "Legal document" is any written instrument that has legal significance, like contracts, deeds, or wills.
- "Within the legal framework" means operating within the bounds of the law.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "This contract is legally binding on both parties."
- **Noun**: "He decided to consult with his legal team before making a decision."
