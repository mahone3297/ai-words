## Pronunciation
/rɪˈlaɪ.əti/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The relationship between physical quantities that are compared relative to a standard system of units; 2. The theory that space, time, and physical laws are relative to the observer's frame of reference; 3. The degree to which something is related or relevant. (Physics) 4. The state or quality of being relative.

- **Verb**: To relate or compare with respect to some standard or condition; to view or consider in terms of relativity.

## Phrases
- "Special relativity" refers to Einstein's theory.
- "General relativity" describes gravity as a curvature of spacetime.
- "Relative motion" describes how objects move with respect to each other.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The concept of relativity has revolutionized our understanding of the universe."
- **Verb**: "To understand relativity, one must grasp its basic principles."
- **Adjective**: "His ideas were considered highly relative to the prevailing scientific consensus."
