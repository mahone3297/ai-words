## 读音
/ˈrɛlətɪvɪti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 相对论，指阿尔伯特·爱因斯坦提出的理论，它描述了物理定律和事件如何在不同参考系中相对存在。例如: "Einstein's theory of relativity revolutionized our understanding of space and time."
- **形容词**: 相对的，关系的，表示事物之间的相互联系或依赖。例如: "The concept of beauty is largely relative to cultural norms."

## 短语
- "Relativity theory" 特指爱因斯坦的相对论理论。
- "Relative to something" 表示相对于某事物而言。例如: "Her success is relative to her starting point."

## 例句
- 名词: "Einstein's groundbreaking work in relativity changed the course of physics."
- 形容词: "What is considered fashionable is often relative to the current trends."
