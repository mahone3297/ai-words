## 读音
/əbˈsɛktɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 客观的；不带个人偏见的。例如: "The judge must remain objective in making his decision."
- **名词**: 目标；目的。例如: "The main objective of the project is to increase efficiency."
- (较少用) **名词**: 对象；目标物。例如: "The telescope's objective lens captures light from distant stars."

## 短语
- "Objective criteria" 指的是基于事实和逻辑的评价标准。
- "Achieve one's objectives" 意味着达成目标或目的。

## 例句
- 形容词: "As a scientist, it's important to maintain an objective perspective when conducting experiments."
- 名词: "Our company's primary objective is to provide excellent customer service."
- (较少用) 名词: "The camera's high-quality objective allows for clear photographs even in low light."
