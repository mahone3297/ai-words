## 读音
/trɪp/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 旅行：一次出行或游览，如 "We're planning a trip to Europe next summer."
  - 摔倒；绊倒：意外失去平衡，如 "Watch out for that rock, you might trip over it."

- **动词**:
  - 旅行：去某个地方，如 "She tripped to the beach for a weekend getaway."
  - 绊倒；使摔倒：使某人失去平衡，如 "The loose carpet tripped him up."

- **形容词**:
  - 临时的；非正式的：短暂的访问或活动，如 "We had a quick trip to the city for a concert."

## 短语
- "trip advisor" 旅行顾问，提供旅行建议和信息的平台。
- "trip over" 绊倒，如 "I almost tripped over my own feet."
- "day trip" 一日游，当天往返的旅行。

## 例句
- 名词: "This trip to the Amazon rainforest will be an adventure of a lifetime."
- 动词: "He tripped while running and sprained his ankle."
- 形容词: "They decided on a last-minute trip to the mountains."
