## 读音
/laɪk/

## 释义
- **动词**: 喜欢，享受，对…有好感。例如: "I like playing tennis on weekends."
- **介词**: 表示相似或相像。例如: "She looks like her mother."
- **名词**: 喜好，爱好。例如: "What are your likes and dislikes?"

## 短语
- "be like" 表示与…相似。例如: "My sister is like me in many ways."
- "like-minded" 意味着思想或兴趣相同的人。例如: "They are like-minded people who enjoy hiking together."
- "likes and dislikes" 指个人的喜好。例如: "Can you tell me about your likes and dislikes when it comes to food?"

## 例句
- 动词: "I really like the color blue."
- 介词: "The dog is like its father, very energetic."
- 名词: "Reading is one of my main likes."
