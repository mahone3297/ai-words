## 读音
/ˈrɛskjuː/

## 释义
- **名词**: 救援；营救行动。例如: "The rescue team arrived just in time to save the stranded climbers."
- **动词**: 营救；解救。例如: "He bravely rescued the child from the burning building."

## 短语
- "rescue operation" 指的是实施救援的行动。
- "to come to the rescue" 意味着提供帮助以解决困难。

## 例句
- 名词: "The coastguard launched a major rescue mission after the shipwreck."
- 动词: "The fireman rescued the cat from the tree."
