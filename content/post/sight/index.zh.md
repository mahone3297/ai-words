## 读音
/saɪt/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 视力；视觉。例如: "She has perfect sight."
  - 景色；风光。例如: "The mountain scenery was breathtaking."
  - 场景；情景。例如: "The battle scene was chaotic."
  - 观察；目睹。例如: "They caught sight of a deer in the woods."
  - (常与the连用)视野；眼界。例如: "拓宽你的知识视野"

- **动词 v**:
  - 看见；看到。例如: "I sighted the plane in the distance."
  - 观察；注视。例如: "The scientist sighted the stars through a telescope."

## 短语
- "catch sight of" 发现，看见。例如: "I caught sight of my friend in the crowd."
- "at first sight" 乍看之下；初见时。例如: "I fell in love with the painting at first sight."

## 例句
- 名词: "The ancient city was a sight to behold."
- 动词: "He sighted a shooting star while stargazing."
