## 读音
/ɪnˈfekt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 传染；感染（疾病）。例如: "The virus can infect computers through email attachments."
2. 影响；毒害（思想、行为）。例如: "His negative attitude infected the whole team."

## 短语
- "infect with" 意为使...受到感染或影响。例如: "The children were infected with a love for music."
- "catch an infection" 意为染上疾病。例如: "Be careful not to catch an infection in the hospital."

## 例句
- "The patient was warned that he could infect others if he didn't wear a mask."
- "Her optimism didn't infect her coworkers, who remained skeptical about the project."
- "He caught a cold and now he's infecting everyone around him."

## 同义词
- contaminate
- transmit
- spread
- communicate (a disease)
- taint (in a figurative sense)
