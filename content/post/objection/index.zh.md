## 读音
/əbˈdʒekʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 反对；异议；抗议。例如: "He raised no objection to the proposal."
- **动词**: 提出反对意见；表示异议。例如: "She objectioned to the terms of the contract."

## 短语
- "objection to" 意为对某事表示反对，如: "I have no objection to your plan."
- "no objection" 表示没有反对意见，如: "There were no objections to the new policy."

## 例句
- 名词: "Despite his initial objections, he eventually agreed to the idea."
- 动词: "She objectioned strongly to being left out of the decision-making process."

## 同义词
- Disapproval
- Protest
- Complaint
- Dissent
- Reservation
