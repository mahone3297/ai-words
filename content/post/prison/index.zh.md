## 读音
/prɪˈzɑːn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 监狱，关押罪犯的地方。例如: "He was sentenced to ten years in prison."
- **动词**: 将某人关进监狱。例如: "The police arrested him and prisoned him for theft."

## 短语
- "break out of prison" 从监狱逃出。
- "go to prison" 被送进监狱。
- "serve time in prison" 在监狱服刑。

## 例句
- 名词: "The new prison is designed to be escape-proof."
- 动词: "She was prisoned for embezzlement."
- 感叹词（非标准用法，但可引申为表达惊讶）: "Prison? I had no idea he'd been convicted!"
