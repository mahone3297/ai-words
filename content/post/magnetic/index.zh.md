## 读音
/mæɡˈnetɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 具有磁性的，能吸引铁、镍或钴等物质的。例如: "Iron is a magnetic material."
- **名词**: 磁场，磁力。例如: "The Earth has a magnetic field."
- **动词**: 吸引，对…有强烈吸引力。例如: "Her personality magnetic many people to her."

## 短语
- "Magnetic north" 指地球磁场的北极。
- "Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI)" 是一种医学成像技术。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This toy has a magnetic back that sticks to the fridge."
- 名词: "The compass needle always points to the magnetic north."
