## 读音
/veɪg/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 模糊的；不清楚的；不明确的。例如: "He gave a vague answer, avoiding the real issue."
- **副词**: 模糊地；含糊地。例如: "She spoke so vaguely that no one could understand her intentions."

## 短语
- "vague idea" 意思是对某事有一个大致但不具体的概念。
- "vaguely remembered" 表示对某事的记忆模糊不清。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The instructions were so vague that I had no idea how to start the project."
- 副词: "She described the event vaguely, leaving us with more questions than answers."

## 同义词
- Unclear
- Indistinct
- Foggy
- Hazy
- Uncertain
