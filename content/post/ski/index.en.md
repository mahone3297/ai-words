## Pronunciation
/skaɪ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A recreational activity involving gliding down snow-covered slopes on skis, typically for sport or leisure. [1]
- **Verb**: To engage in skiing as a pastime or sport. [2]
- **Noun**: A device with two narrow runners or blades attached to boots, used for skiing. [3]

## Phrases
- "Hit the slopes" means to go skiing.
- "Learn to ski" is a phrase for starting to acquire skiing skills.
- "Off-piste skiing" refers to skiing outside designated trails.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She spent her winter vacation skiing in the Alps."
- **Verb**: "After mastering basic techniques, he decided to ski down the challenging slope."
- **Noun**: "The skis were carefully stored in the garage during the summer months."

## Synonyms
- Skiing
- Snowboarding (informal)
- Alpine skiing (specific type)
- Cross-country skiing (another type)
