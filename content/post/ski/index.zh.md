## 读音
/skiː/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 滑雪板，一种冬季运动器材。例如: "She put on her skis and headed for the slopes."
  - 滑雪运动。例如: "Skiing is a popular winter sport in many countries."

- **动词**:
  - 进行滑雪活动。例如: "He loves to ski down the steep mountains."

## 短语
- "ski resort" 滑雪度假胜地。
- "downhill skiing" 高山滑雪。
- "cross-country skiing" 越野滑雪。

## 例句
- 名词: "The children were learning how to ski at the local resort."
- 动词: "She spends her winters skiing in the Swiss Alps."

## 同义词
- Snowboarding
- Sledding
- Tobogganing
- Winter sports
- Alpine skiing (特指高山滑雪)
