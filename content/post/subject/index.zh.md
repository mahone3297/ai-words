## 读音
/ˈsʌbdʒɪkt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 主题，话题。例如: "The subject of the meeting was climate change."
       2. 学科，科目。例如: "Her favorite subject in school is history."
       3. 主体，主语。例如: "In the sentence 'She is singing', 'she' is the subject."
       4. 受试者，实验对象。例如: "The study involved 50 human subjects."

- **形容词 (adj)**: 1. 主要的，首要的。例如: "The subject matter of the book is quite complex."
       2. 受支配的，从属的。例如: "She felt like a subject in her own life."

## 短语
- "Change the subject" 意思是转移话题。
- "Under the subject of..." 意为在某个主题下讨论。

## 例句
- 名词: "Our next art exhibition will feature works on the subject of nature."
- 形容词: "He's a subject expert in quantum physics."
