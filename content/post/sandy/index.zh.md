## 读音
/ˈsændi/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 带有沙的，多沙的。例如: "The beach was sandy and warm underfoot."
- **名词**: 沙，沙地。例如: "Sandy enjoyed playing in the sand at the beach."
- **人名**: (姓氏)桑迪，一个英文名字。例如: "My friend Sandy has a great sense of humor."

## 短语
- "sandy soil" 指含沙量高的土壤。
- "sandy beaches" 指沙质的海滩。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The desert had a vast expanse of sandy terrain."
- 名词: "The kids built a sandcastle on the sandy shore."
- 人名: "Sandy is always up for a game of beach volleyball."

## 同义词
- sandy可以作为形容词时的同义词:
  - gravelly (多石的)
  - stony (多石的)
  - dusty (多尘的)

请注意，"sandy"作为人名时没有直接的同义词，因为它是一个特定的名字。
