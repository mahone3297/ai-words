## 读音
/ˈnəʊbədi/

## 释义
- **代词**: 没有人，没有任何人。表示否定或强调无人。例如: "There's nobody in the room."
- **名词**: 不重要的人，无足轻重的人物。例如: "She thought he was just a nobody, but he turned out to be a famous author."

## 短语
- "Not a soul/nobody else" 意为除了自己之外没有任何人。
- "Don't worry, nobody's perfect." 表示每个人都有缺点。

## 例句
- 代词: "I shouted, but there was nobody around to hear me."
- 名词: "In the beginning, he felt like a nobody, but his hard work changed that."
