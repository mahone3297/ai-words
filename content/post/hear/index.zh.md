## 读音
/hɪər/

## 释义
- **动词**: 听到，听见。例如: "I heard a strange noise coming from the attic."（我听到从阁楼传来一个奇怪的声音。）
- **动词**: 注意到，察觉。例如: "Did you hear what she just said?"（你听到她刚才说什么了吗？）
- **名词**: 听力，听觉。例如: "The baby's hearing is improving day by day."（婴儿的听力一天天在提高。）

## 短语
- "Hear someone out" 意为倾听某人的意见或故事。例如: "Please hear me out before you make a decision."
- "Not hear a word" 表示一点声音都没听到。例如: "I was so focused on my book that I didn't hear a word she said."

## 例句
- 动词: "I heard a bird singing outside my window early this morning."
- 动词: "She finally heard his apologies and forgave him."
- 名词: "His hearing has been impaired since the accident."
