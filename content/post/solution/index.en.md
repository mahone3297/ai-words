## Pronunciation
/səˈluːʃ(ə)n/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **solution** is a homogeneous mixture of two or more substances, usually a liquid, in which the components are evenly distributed and can be separated by physical methods. It can also refer to:
1. A method or plan to resolve a problem or issue.
2. [Chemistry] A state where a solute is dissolved in a solvent, forming a stable mixture.
3. [Business] An agreement or arrangement between parties.

### Verb
To **solve** means to find a way to deal with or answer a problem, equation, or situation.
- "He quickly solved the math problem."

### Adjective
In some cases, "solutions" can be used as an adjective to describe something that provides a satisfactory answer or outcome.
- "The company implemented innovative solutions to improve efficiency."
