## 读音
/səˈluːʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 解决方法；解答；方案。例如: "They came up with a creative solution to the problem."
- **名词**: 溶液；溶剂。例如: "The chemical was completely dissolved in the solution."

## 短语
- "A solution to the problem" 指的是问题的解决办法。
- "In a solution" 表示某物在溶液中。

## 例句
- 名词: "The engineer provided a detailed solution to fix the malfunctioning software."
- 名词: "The chemist mixed salt and water to create a saline solution."
