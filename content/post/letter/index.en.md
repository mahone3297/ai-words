## Word: Letter

## Pronunciation
/ˈlet.ər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A written or printed communication, typically on paper, sent by post or electronically, consisting of one or more sheets containing information, opinions, requests, or other content. Example: "She received a long-awaited letter from her friend abroad."
  - Also, any symbol or character in an alphabet or writing system; a written form of a speech sound. Example: "The letter 'A' is the first letter of the English alphabet."
- **Verb**: (letter something) To inscribe or mark with letters or text. Example: "The jeweler promised to letter my bracelet with my initials."

## Phrases
- "In a letter addressed to..." refers to sending a written message to someone specific.
- "Letter of the law" means following the exact wording of a law rather than its spirit.
- "The lost art of letter-writing" suggests the declining practice of writing personal messages by hand.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "I wrote a heartfelt letter expressing my gratitude to him."
- **Verb**: "She decided to letter her new journal with beautiful calligraphy."
