## 读音
/meɪl/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一顿饭，尤指正餐。例如: "We have three meals a day: breakfast, lunch, and dinner."
  - 食物，尤指定量分配的食品。例如: "The prisoners were given two meals a day."

- **动词**:
  - 给某人提供或供应一餐。例如: "She mealied them with a delicious homemade pasta."

## 短语
- "Three square meals a day" 意为每天三顿正餐。
- "Meal time" 是指吃饭的时间。

## 例句
- 名词: "We usually have a big meal together on Sundays."
- 动词: "The cafeteria meals are served from 12:00 to 2:00 PM."
