## 读音
/ˈspiːkər/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 说话者；发言人。例如: "The speaker at the conference was very inspiring."
               2. 扬声器；音响设备。例如: "I need to buy a new speaker for my computer."

## 短语
- "Loudspeaker" 大声公，公共广播系统中的扬声器。
- "Keynote speaker" 主题演讲者，在会议或活动中发表重要演讲的人。

## 例句
- 名词: "The speaker shared his thoughts on global warming with the audience."
- 名词: "I connected my phone to the Bluetooth speaker for better sound quality."
