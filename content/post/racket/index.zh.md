## 读音
/ˈrækɪt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 球拍，尤指网球、羽毛球或乒乓球拍。例如: "She needs a new tennis racket for the tournament."
   2. 欺诈行为，骗局。例如: "The politician was involved in a corrupt racket."
- **动词**: (非标准用法) 用球拍打球。例如: "He racketed the ball across the net."

## 短语
- "Racket around" 意味着快速而嘈杂地移动或做事。
- "Racket sport" 指的是使用球拍的运动，如网球、羽毛球等。

## 例句
- 名词: "The professional player chose a lightweight racket for better control."
- 动词: "The kids were racketing the ball in the backyard, making a lot of noise."
