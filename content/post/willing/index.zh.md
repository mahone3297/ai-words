## 读音
/wɪlɪŋ/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 自愿的，乐意的，愿意的。表示某人愿意做某事或对某事持开放态度。例如: "She is willing to help with the project."
- **动词**: 用于构成短语 "be willing to do sth."，表示愿意做某事。例如: "They are willing to compromise on the issue."

## 短语
- "be willing to do" 意为愿意做某事。
- "willing participant" 指的是自愿参与的人。
- "unwilling" 是 "willing" 的反义词，表示不愿意或不情愿。

## 例句
- 形容词: "John is so willing to share his knowledge with others."
- 动词: "She was willing to work extra hours to finish the task."

- Willing and able
- Eager
