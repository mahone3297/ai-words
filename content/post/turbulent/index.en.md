## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterized by disturbances, fluctuations, or chaos; marked by agitation or turmoil. For example: "The ocean was turbulent during the storm."
- **Noun**: A turbulent person or situation, often implying unpredictability or difficulty. For example: "She faced a career full of turbulent changes."

## Phrases
- "Turbulent waters" describes rough or chaotic seas or rivers.
- "A turbulent relationship" signifies a volatile and unstable partnership.
- "Weather turbulence" refers to sudden, violent fluctuations in the atmosphere while flying.
