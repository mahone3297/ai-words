## 读音
/tɜːrəˈbjuːlənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 
    - (水流、气流等)汹涌的；动荡的： "The turbulent river made it difficult for boats to navigate."
    - (局势、情况)动荡不安的；混乱的： "The political situation in the region remained turbulent."
    - (人)易怒的；暴躁的： "He had a turbulent temper and was prone to outbursts of anger."

## 短语
- "turbulent flow" 湍流，指的是流体流动时出现的不规则、不稳定的运动状态。
- "turbulent times" 动荡时期，指社会或个人经历的不稳定阶段。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The airplane experienced turbulent air during the flight."
- 形容词: "The economy is going through a period of turbulent change."
- 形容词: "She had to deal with her turbulent emotions after the breakup."
