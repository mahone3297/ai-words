## 读音
/ˈspɑːnsər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 赞助者；发起人；担保人。例如: "The event was sponsored by a local business."
- **动词**: 赞助；支持；发起。例如: "The charity relies on donations and sponsors to continue its work."

## 短语
- "Corporate sponsor" 指的是公司赞助商。
- "Sponsorship deal" 指赞助协议或交易。
- "To sponsor an event" 意为赞助一个活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "The TV show has several high-profile sponsors who advertise during breaks."
- 动词: "She is sponsoring a child from a developing country through a charity organization."
