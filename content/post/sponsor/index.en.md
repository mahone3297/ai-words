## Pronunciation
/səˈspɒnsər/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **sponsor** is a person, organization, or company that provides financial or promotional support for an event, project, or individual, often in exchange for recognition or benefits.

### Verb
To **sponsor** is to officially back or financially support a project, event, or individual, typically with the expectation of visibility or benefits.

## Phrases
- "Sponsor an event" means to fund or endorse a particular activity.
- "Lead sponsor" refers to the primary supporter of a project or initiative.
- "Brand sponsorship" is when a company supports a program to promote its products or services.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The local business was the main sponsor of the charity marathon."
- **Verb**: "The university sponsored several students to attend the international conference."
- **Phrasal verb**: "The sports team's success was largely due to the generous sponsorship from a major corporation."
