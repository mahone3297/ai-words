## 读音
/ɪnˈdɪrɛkt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不直接的；绕道的；不坦率的。例如: "The message was delivered indirectly through a friend." 
- **副词**: 间接地；不直接地。例如: "The heat from the stove was transferred indirectly to the pot."

## 短语
- "indirect speech" 指转述别人的话，而不是直接引用原话。
- "indirect object" 在句子中，接在及物动词后面，但不是动作的直接承受者。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The effect of the policy on the economy is often indirect and hard to predict."
- 副词: "She learned about the news indirectly from a colleague."

## 同义词
- Roundabout
- Circuitous
- Indirectly
- Through intermediaries
- Oblique
