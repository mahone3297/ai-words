## 读音
/ˈfrɪkʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 相互接触的物体间因相对运动或试图相对运动而产生的阻力或能量损失。例如: "Friction causes heat due to the rubbing of surfaces together."
- **动词**: 形容由于冲突或不和谐而产生的紧张状态。例如: "Their different opinions created friction in the team."

## 短语
- "Reduce friction" 意味着减少阻力或使过程更顺畅。
- "Frictional force" 是指由摩擦引起的力。

## 例句
- 名词: "The lubricant was used to decrease the friction between the machine parts."
- 动词: "The manager tried to friction-proof the workflow by simplifying procedures.
