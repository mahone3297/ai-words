## Pronunciation
/wɪˈdɪli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Extensively; broadly; on a large scale. For example: "The news spread widely across the city."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by a wide extent or scope; extensive. For example: "Their knowledge was widely recognized."
- **Pronoun**: In a wide sense or range; generally. For example: "The problem affects people worldwide."

## Phrases
- "Spread widely" means to reach or affect a large area or group.
- "Widely accepted" indicates that something is agreed upon or recognized by many.
- "Widely regarded" suggests that something is considered so by a large number of people.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "The new policy was implemented widely, improving efficiency."
- **Adjective**: "Her views were expressed in a widely-read column."
- **Pronoun**: "The solution was proposed, and it was widely embraced."
