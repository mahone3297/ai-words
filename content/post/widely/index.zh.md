## 读音
/waɪdli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 广泛地，普遍地，大量地。例如: "The new technology is used widely in many industries."
- **形容词**: 广阔的，宽广的。例如: "The park has widely spaced trees."

## 短语
- "Widely accepted" 意味着被广泛接受或认可。
- "A widely known fact" 指一个众所周知的事实。

## 例句
- 副词: "Her opinions are widely respected in the academic community."
- 形容词: "The view from the mountain top was widely expansive."
