## 读音
/ˈslavery/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 制服，奴役；强迫劳动的状态。例如: "Slavery was abolished in the United States in the 19th century."
   2. 奴隶制度。例如: "The transatlantic slave trade was a dark chapter in history."

## 短语
- "Abolition of slavery" 意为废除奴隶制。
- "Slave trade" 指的是买卖奴隶的行为。
- "Life under slavery" 描述在奴隶制度下的生活状况。

## 例句
- "The fight against slavery was a long and hard struggle for human rights."
- "Many historical figures were involved in the Underground Railroad, helping slaves escape from slavery."
- "Slavery was a fundamental aspect of ancient societies like Greece and Rome."
