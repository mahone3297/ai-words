## 读音
/kuːmjuːˈneɪʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 交流；沟通；信息传递。例如: "Effective communication is key in any relationship."
- **动词**: 与…交流；传达信息给…。例如: "She communicated her ideas clearly during the meeting."

## 短语
- "Communication breakdown" 指的是沟通不畅或失败。
- "Nonverbal communication" 指的是通过身体语言、面部表情等方式进行的非言语交流。
- "Open communication" 意味着坦诚和透明的对话。

## 例句
- 名词: "Improving communication between departments will enhance overall productivity."
- 动词: "The teacher communicated the lesson's objectives at the beginning of class."
