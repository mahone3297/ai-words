## 读音
/ˈdʒæz/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 爵士乐，一种起源于19世纪末美国南部的音乐风格，以其即兴演奏和强烈的节奏感为特点。例如: "She loves listening to jazz music on weekends."
   - 活力，兴奋，精神。例如: "The atmosphere at the concert was filled with jazz."

- **动词**:
   - 使具有爵士风格；加入爵士元素。例如: "They jazzed up the old tune with some new improvisations."
   - 即兴演奏爵士乐。例如: "The musicians started jazzing when they felt the crowd's energy."

## 短语
- "Jazz up" 意味着增添趣味或活力，使其更具吸引力。
- "Jazz band" 是指演奏爵士乐的乐队。

## 例句
- 名词: "The New Orleans Jazz Festival celebrates the best of traditional and modern jazz."
- 动词: "They decided to jazz their performance with some spontaneous solos."
