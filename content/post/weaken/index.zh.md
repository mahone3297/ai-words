## 读音
/ˈwiːkən/

## 释义
- **动词**: 使变弱；减弱；削弱。例如: "Regular exercise can help to weaken the effects of stress."
- **形容词**: 虚弱的；无力的。例如: "He was so weak that he could hardly stand up."

## 短语
- "Weaken one's position" 意味着削弱自己的立场或实力。
- "The illness had weakened her immune system" 指疾病使她的免疫系统变得脆弱。

## 例句
- 动词: "The constant arguments between them began to weaken their relationship."
- 形容词: "After a long illness, she felt weakened and needed time to recover."

- Dilute
- Reduce
- Diminish
- Lessen
- Enfeeble
