## 读音
/ˌrɪˈzaɪn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. **辞职**：决定或正式提出不再担任某个职位或工作。例如: "She decided to resign from her position as CEO."
2. **接受（不愉快的事）**：平静地接受或忍受不愉快的情况，而不做抵抗。例如: "They had to resign themselves to the fact that their team lost the game."

## 短语
- "Resign from" 意味着从某个职位上离职。例如: "He announced his intention to resign from the board of directors."
- "Resign oneself to" 表示接受不可避免的事情。例如: "She finally resigned herself to the idea of moving abroad."

## 例句
- 作为动词的辞职: "After a disagreement with management, he submitted his resignation."
- 接受不愉快的事: "They had to resign themselves to living in a noisy neighborhood."
