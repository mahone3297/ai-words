## 读音
/pеɪv/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 铺设（道路、地面等），用材料覆盖。例如: "The workers are paving the new road with asphalt."
2. 准备，为…铺平道路。例如: "His hard work paved the way for his success."

## 短语
- "pave the way for" 意味着为某事创造条件或打下基础。例如: "The agreement paved the way for further negotiations."

## 例句
- 动词: "The city is planning to pave the old dirt road to improve traffic flow."
- 动词: "Her experience paved the path for her to become the CEO of the company."
