## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
1. Beyond refers to a point or extent that is farther than what is currently known, seen, or considered. It can also mean a situation or level that is not within reach or comprehension. Example: "The view from the mountain was breathtaking, stretching beyond the horizon."
2. In terms of time or space, it signifies after or past a certain point or limit. Example: "Beyond tomorrow, we'll discuss the next steps."

### Verb
1. To go or extend beyond a particular boundary or limitation. Example: "Her imagination often reaches beyond the boundaries of reality."
2. To surpass or exceed in some aspect. Example: "His skills surpassed what was expected, going beyond the norm."

### Adverb
1. Describing action that occurs after or outside a specified area or period. Example: "The event will take place beyond the city limits."
2. Expressing a higher degree or intensity than something else. Example: "Her kindness extended far beyond just a simple gesture."

## Phrases
- "Beyond compare" means excellent or superior to all others.
- "Beyond this point" indicates an area or situation that follows.
- "Beyond the pale" refers to something that is unacceptable or inappropriate.
