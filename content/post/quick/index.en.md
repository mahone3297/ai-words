## Definitions
### Noun
- **Quick** (adj): Relating to speed, rapidity, or swiftness. For example: "The quick fox jumps over the lazy dog."
- **Quickness** (n): The quality or state of being quick; agility. For example: "She demonstrated remarkable quickness on the basketball court."

### Verb
- To act or move quickly; to react promptly. For example: "He quickly closed the door before anyone could enter."
- To solve or understand something rapidly. For example: "She was able to answer the math problem quickly."

### Adverb
- Describing action with speed or agility. For example: "He ran quickly to catch the bus."
