## Pronunciation
/tiː/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. A beverage typically made from fermented leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant, served hot or iced. 2. A social gathering or event centered around tea-drinking. 3. A type of steeped herbal or fruit infusion not derived from the Camellia sinensis plant. 
- **Verb**: To serve or consume tea. To partake in a tea ceremony or event.

## Phrases
- "Have a cup of tea" invites someone to share a beverage.
- "Tea time" refers to a scheduled period for tea consumption.
- "Tea party" is an informal gathering for socializing over tea.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She invited her friends over for a cozy afternoon tea."
- **Verb**: "I usually have a cup of green tea before breakfast."
- **Prepositional Phrase**: "The tea ceremony in Japan is a cultural ritual."
