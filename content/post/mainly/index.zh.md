## 读音
/ˈmeɪnli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 主要地，主要，大部分情况下。表示某事是最重要的因素或最常见的状态。例如: "She works mainly on weekends."

## 短语
- "mainly due to" 意为主要是由于...
- "mainly concerned with" 表示主要关注...

## 例句
- "Our company's profits mainly come from online sales."
- "The population in this area is mainly composed of young adults."
- "He focuses on painting, mainly landscapes and portraits."

## 同义词
- primarily
- chiefly
- predominantly
- mostly
- largely
