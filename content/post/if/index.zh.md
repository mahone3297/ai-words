## 读音
/ɪf/

## 释义
- **连词**: 引导条件状语从句，表示假设。例如: "[If](/post/if/) [it](/post/it/) rains, we'll [stay](/post/stay/) [inside](/post/inside/)."
- **名词**: 在某些固定表达中，如 "[if](/post/if/) [and](/post/and/) [only](/post/only/) [if](/post/if/)"（如果且仅如果），表示必要和充分条件。例如: "[A](/post/a/) is [true](/post/true/) [if](/post/if/) [and](/post/and/) [only](/post/only/) [if](/post/if/) B is [true](/post/true/)."

## 短语
- "[If](/post/if/)...[then](/post/then/)..." 结构用于表达因果关系或条件。例如: "[If](/post/if/) [you](/post/you/) [study](/post/study/) [hard](/post/hard/), [then](/post/then/) you'll [pass](/post/pass/) [the](/post/the/) [exam](/post/exam/)."
- "[If](/post/if/) [not](/post/not/)" 用于表示否定的假设。例如: "I'll [go](/post/go/) [with](/post/with/) [you](/post/you/), [if](/post/if/) [not](/post/not/) [alone](/post/alone/)."
