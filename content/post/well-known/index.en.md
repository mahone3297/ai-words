## Pronunciation
/welˈknaʊn/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Widely known or recognized by many people. For example: "The city is well-known for its beautiful architecture."
- **Adverb**: In a well-known manner; famously. For example: "He sang the song well-known to everyone."

## Phrases
- "A well-known fact" refers to something that is generally accepted as true.
- "Well-known for" indicates a particular reason or attribute that a person, place, or thing is famous for.
- "She became well-known after her first book's success."

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The well-known artist's painting was displayed at the gallery."
- **Adverb**: "The well-known chef prepares delicious meals every night."
- "He's well-known in the scientific community for his groundbreaking research."
