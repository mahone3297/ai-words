## 读音
/wellˈnəʊn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 广为人知的，知名的，著名的。例如: "He is a well-known author in the science fiction genre."
- **副词**: 很好地，充分地。例如: "She knows the city well-known."

## 短语
- "Well-known fact" 意为众所周知的事实。
- "A well-known personality" 指一个公众熟知的人物。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The Eiffel Tower is a well-known landmark in Paris."
- 副词: "She sings the song well-known."
