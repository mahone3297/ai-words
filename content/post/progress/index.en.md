## Pronunciation
/prəˈɡres/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Progress**: The act or process of moving forward or advancing, typically towards a goal or improvement. [noun: progress]
- **Development**: The growth or improvement of something over time, especially in a particular field or area. [noun: development]

### Verb
- **To progress**: To move forward or advance in a project, task, or learning. [verb: to progress]
- **To make progress**: To achieve advancement or improvement in a situation. [verb: to make progress]

### Adjective
- **Progressive**: Characterized by continuous improvement or advancement. [adjective: progressive]
- **Advancing**: Increasing in degree or nearing completion. [adjective: advancing]

### Adverb
- **Progressively**: In a way that advances or improves over time. [adverb: progressively]

## Phrases
- "Track progress": Monitor the advancement of a task or project.
- "Undergoing progress": Something is currently being developed or improved.
- "In the face of progress": Despite or alongside advancements.
- "Slow progress": Little or no advancement in a given period.
- "Great strides in progress": Significant improvements have been made.

## Example Sentences
