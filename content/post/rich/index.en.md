## Pronunciation
/rɪtʃ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: 1. Possessing a large amount of wealth, assets, or resources. For example: "The rich man owned several luxury properties."
- **Adjective**: 2. (of soil) Fertile and productive. For example: "The rich soil yielded a bountiful harvest."
- **Noun**: A wealthy person. For example: "The rich philanthropist donated millions to charity."
- **Adverb**: To a great extent; abundantly. For example: "She is rich with knowledge."

## Phrases
- "Be rich in" means to have a plentiful supply of something, often intangible like qualities or experiences.
- "Born rich" refers to someone who was born into a wealthy family.
- "Get rich quick" is a colloquial expression for achieving sudden financial success.
