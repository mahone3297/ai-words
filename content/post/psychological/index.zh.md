## 读音
/ˌsaɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 关于心理的，心理学的，涉及思想、感情或行为的。例如: "She has been studying some psychological effects of stress."
- **名词**: 心理学（研究心理过程和行为的科学）。例如: "He is pursuing a degree in psychological studies."

## 短语
- "Psychological warfare" 指的是通过心理手段影响敌人。
- "Psychological trauma" 指由极端压力或创伤事件引起的精神伤害。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The movie had a profound psychological impact on the viewers."
- 名词: "The psychologist specializes in treating childhood psychological disorders."
