## Pronunciation
/ˈtræktər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A large farm machine with powerful engines and strong wheels, used for plowing, digging, or moving heavy loads. [1]
- **Verb**: To move or pull using a tractor, often in agriculture. [2]

## Phrases
- "Drive a tractor" means operating a tractor for work.
- "tractor power" refers to the mechanical advantage provided by a tractor.
- "Tractor pull" is a competition where modified tractors compete to see who can pull the most weight.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The farmer used his trusty tractor to till the fields."
- **Verb**: "The construction workers hitched the trailer to the tractor and started the day's work."
- "The tractor was essential to the harvest, making short work of the crop."
