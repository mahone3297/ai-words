## 读音
/ˈɑːpəreɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 操作；运行: 操纵或控制机器、设备、系统等。例如: "He operates the complex machinery with ease."
2. 执行；实施: 完成某项任务或计划。例如: "The company is operating a new marketing strategy."
3. 做手术: 医生对病人进行手术。例如: "The surgeon will operate on the patient tomorrow."

- **名词**:
1. 操作员: 负责操作机器或系统的人。例如: "The power station needs skilled operators."
2. 手术: 医学上的外科手术。例如: "The operation was successful and the patient is recovering well."

## 短语
- "Operate on something" 意为对某物进行操作或手术。
- "Operate within a budget" 表示在预算范围内运作。

## 例句
- 动词: "She operates the crane with great precision."
- 名词: "He is an experienced operator in the control room."
