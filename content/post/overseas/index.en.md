## Word: Overseas

## Pronunciation
/ˌoʊvərˈsiːz/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Situated, conducted, or originating in a foreign country or countries across the sea; abroad. For example: "She moved overseas for her job assignment."
- **Adverb**: Beyond or across the sea; to or in a foreign country or countries. For example: "They plan to travel overseas next year."

## Phrases
- "Overseas experience": Refers to gaining work or life experience in a foreign country.
- "Overseas market": An international market in a foreign country.
- "Overseas aid": Financial or material assistance given by one country to another for development or relief purposes.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "Many students choose to pursue their education overseas."
- **Adverb**: "The company exports its products overseas to increase global presence."

## Synonyms
- Abroad
- Foreign
- International
- Transoceanic
- Transatlantic (specifically referring to travel between Europe and America)
- Cross-border
- Global (in some contexts)
