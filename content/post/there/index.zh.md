## 读音
/ðeər/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示存在或位置。指在那儿、那里。例如: "The book is right there on the table."
- **代词**: 指示某个特定的人或事物。例如: "There's your coat, don't forget it."
- **连词**: 引导一个从句，表示结果或情况。例如: "It was raining, so there was a rainbow in the sky."

## 短语
- "There you go" 表示完成某事或给某人东西。例如: "Here's your coffee; there you go."
- "There's no need" 用来表达没有必要做某事。例如: "There's no need to worry; everything will be fine."

## 例句
- 副词: "I left my keys over there by the door."
- 代词: "There's a solution to every problem."
- 连词: "I thought you were busy; there, you're playing video games again."
