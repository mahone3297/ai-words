## 读音
/həˈloʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 用于打招呼的用语。例如: "He gave me a friendly hello."
- **动词**: 说出“你好”来问候。例如: "She helloed her neighbor when she saw him in the garden."
- **感叹词**: 用于打招呼或引起注意。例如: "Hello! Can you hear me?"，"Hello! Where have you been?"

## 短语
- "Hello, my name is..." 用于自我介绍。
- "Hello, goodbye" 是一首著名的披头士乐队歌曲。
- "Hello, old friend" 表示对老朋友的问候。

## 例句
- 名词: "The phone rang, and a familiar voice said, 'Hello?'"
- 动词: "Don't forget to hello your teacher when you enter the classroom."
- 感叹词: "Hello, stranger! It's been a long time."
