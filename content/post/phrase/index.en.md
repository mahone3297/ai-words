## Pronunciation
/heləʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A greeting or expression of welcome, often used to initiate a conversation. Example: "He offered a friendly hello to the stranger."
- **Verb**: To express greeting or salutation, typically by saying "hello." Example: "She habitually hellos her coworkers every morning."
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or relating to a greeting or expression of welcome. Example: "A cheery hello greeted us as we entered the café."

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" = Greet someone.
- "Say hello to someone" = Extend a greeting towards someone.
- "A wave hello" = A non-verbal greeting.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children exchanged hellos as they crossed paths on the playground."
- **Verb**: "After a long day, it felt good to hear a hearty hello from my friend."
- **Adjective**: "Her bright hello lit up the room."
