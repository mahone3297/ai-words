## 读音
/ˈlʌɡɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 旅行时携带的行李，通常包括箱子、包等。例如: "Don't forget your luggage when you leave the hotel."
- **动词 (v)**: 携带或处理行李。例如: "I'll help you luggage to the train station."

## 短语
- "Check your luggage" 意为在机场或火车站托运行李。
- "Luggage compartment" 是指交通工具上的行李存放处。
- "Carry-on luggage" 指可以带上飞机的随身行李。

## 例句
- 名词: "There's a limit of two pieces of luggage per passenger on this flight."
- 动词: "The porter will luggage your bags from the taxi to your hotel room."
