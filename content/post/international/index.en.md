## Pronunciation
/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to two or more nations; involving two or more countries: "international trade agreements."
  - Example Sentence: "The United Nations is an international organization that promotes peace and cooperation among nations."
  
- **Adjective (Law)**: Concerning the relationships between nations, their governments, and peoples: "international law."
  - Example Sentence: "International courts settle disputes between countries under international law."

## Phrases
- "International community": Refers to a collective group of nations considered as a whole, often regarding political, economic, or cultural interactions.
- "International affairs": Matters concerning relations between different countries, such as diplomacy, foreign policy, and global issues.
- "International travel": Traveling from one country to another.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The Olympic Games are a symbol of international unity and friendly competition."
- "Many universities have an international student body, fostering diversity and cultural exchange."

## Synonyms
- Global
- Worldwide
- Cross-border
- Transnational
- Multinational

Antonyms:
- Domestic (when referring to matters within a single country)
- Local (in terms of scope or influence)
