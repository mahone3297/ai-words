## 读音
/prəˈpɔːrʃənl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 成比例的；相称的；相应的。例如: "The size of the picture is proportional to its resolution."
- **名词**: 比例；比率。例如: "They discussed the proportional representation in the parliament."

## 短语
- "proportional to" 表示与...成比例。例如: "The cost is directly proportional to the quantity ordered."
- "proportional reasoning" 比例推理，用于数学和逻辑思考。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The amount of sugar needed for the recipe is proportional to the number of servings."
- 名词: "The population growth in different countries is often studied in terms of proportional demographics."

- comparative
- equivalent
- corresponding
- commensurate
