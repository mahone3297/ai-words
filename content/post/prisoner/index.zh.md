## 读音
/prɪˈzɜːnər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 被监禁的人；囚犯。例如: "The prisoner was awaiting trial for theft."
- **动词**: 俘虏，监禁。例如: "They were prisoners of war for several months."

## 短语
- "hold someone prisoner" 意为扣留某人为人质或囚禁某人。
- "prisoner exchange" 是指交换囚犯的行为。

## 例句
- 名词: "The guards escorted the new prisoner to his cell."
- 动词: "The army captured several enemy soldiers and prisonered them."
