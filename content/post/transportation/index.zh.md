## 读音
/ˌtrænspɔːˈteɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 运输；运送；交通系统。例如: "Public transportation includes buses and trains."
- **动词**: 运送；传输；迁移。例如: "The company transports goods across the country."

## 短语
- "Transportation system" 指的是整个交通网络。
- "Road transportation" 特指通过公路的运输。
- "Intermodal transportation" 涉及多种运输方式的联运。

## 例句
- 名词: "The city is investing in improving its public transportation infrastructure."
- 动词: "The package will be transported by air to ensure timely delivery."

- Carriage
- Conveyance
