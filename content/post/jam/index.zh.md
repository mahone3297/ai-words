## 读音
/jæm/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 堵塞；拥挤：A situation in which vehicles or people are unable to move easily because of too much traffic or too many people in a small space. 例如: "There was a terrible traffic jam on the highway this morning."
  - 果酱：A sweet preserve made by cooking fruit with sugar. 例如: "I love to spread jam on my toast for breakfast."

- **动词**:
  - 使堵塞；卡住：To obstruct or block something so that it cannot move freely. 例如: "The door jammed and we couldn't get out."
  - 在音乐中即兴演奏：To improvise musically with others, especially in jazz. 例如: "They often have jam sessions at the local blues club."

## 短语
- "be stuck in a jam" 意味陷入困境。
- "jam session" 是音乐家们即兴演奏的聚会。

## 例句
- 名词: "The supermarket was so crowded that we got caught in a jam at the checkout."
- 动词: "The key jammed in the lock and we had to call a locksmith."
- "Let's jam together!" 表示邀请一起即兴演奏。
