## Pronunciation
/ˈnætʃrəli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a way that occurs or develops without being forced or planned; spontaneously or by nature. For example: "The plants grew naturally in the wild."
- **Adjective**: Existing in or produced by nature; not made or caused by humankind. For example: "Organic foods are naturally healthier than processed ones."

### Adverbial Usage Examples
- "She speaks French fluently and naturally, having grown up in Paris."
- "The child learned to walk naturally around 12 months old."

### Adjectival Usage Examples
- "His naturally curly hair was the envy of his friends."
- "They use only naturally sourced materials in their products."

## Phrases
- "Act naturally" means to behave in an unaffected or spontaneous manner.
- "In a natural manner" implies doing something as one would expect based on instinct or habit.
- "Naturally occurring" refers to things that happen or exist without human intervention.

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "She approached the problem with a naturally analytical mindset."
- **Adjective**: "This cream is made from naturally derived ingredients."
