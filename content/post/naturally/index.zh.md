## 读音
/nætʃrəli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 
    1. 自然地；自然而然地。例如: "She speaks English naturally without any accent."
    2. 本来地；按本性。例如: "He is a naturally curious person."
    3. 天生地；与生俱来地。例如: "She has a naturally beautiful singing voice."

## 短语
- "In a natural way" 意为以自然的方式。
- "Naturally occurring" 表示自然发生的。

## 例句
- "The child learns to walk naturally without needing much guidance."
- "He was naturally good at sports, excelling in every game he played."
- "The river flows naturally through the valley, creating a picturesque scene."
