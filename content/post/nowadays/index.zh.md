## 读音
/ˈnaʊədeɪz/

## 释义
- **副词**: 现在，当今，目前。表示时间上的现在或当前的状态或情况。例如: "Nowadays, most people carry smartphones with them."
- **形容词**: 时下流行的，现代的。例如: "Nowadays fashion trends change rapidly."

## 短语
- "In nowadays times" 表示在当前的时代。
- "Nowadays technology" 指的是现代科技。

## 例句
- 副词: "Nowadays, it's common for children to learn how to use a computer at a young age."
- 形容词: "Nowadays fashion is more focused on sustainability and eco-friendly materials."
