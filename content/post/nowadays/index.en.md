## Pronunciation
/naʊˈdeɪz/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: At the present time; in these times. It is often used to refer to something that has changed or become more common recently. 

  > Adverbial Usage: "Nowadays, people rely heavily on technology for communication."
  
## Example Sentences
- **Adverb**: "Nowadays, it's much easier to travel long distances than it was in the past."
  
## Phrases
- "In modern times" or "These days" can be used interchangeably with "nowadays" to convey the same meaning.
  
  > Phrase Example: "Nowadays, we have access to information at our fingertips, whereas in the past, research required hours in the library."

## Synonyms
- Currently
- Nowadays
- Presently
- These days
- In modern times
- At present
- In contemporary times
