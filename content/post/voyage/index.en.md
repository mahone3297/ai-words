## Pronunciation
/vɔɪˈɑːʒ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A **voyage** is a journey by sea, air, or land, typically one that involves travel across a considerable distance for a specific purpose, such as trade, exploration, or leisure.

### Verb
To go on a **voyage** can mean to undertake a journey or travel by water, air, or land, especially when it's a significant trip.

### Adjective
Voyage-related adjectives might describe a journey or expedition, like *transoceanic* (crossing an ocean) or *epic* (impressive or adventurous).

## Phrases
- "Embarking on a voyage" to start a journey.
- "A long sea voyage" refers to a prolonged journey at sea.
- "The voyage of discovery" symbolizes exploration and learning.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The crew set sail on a long voyage to discover new lands."
- **Verb**: "After months at sea, they finally completed their transatlantic voyage."
- **Adjective**: "The epic voyage through the Amazon rainforest was filled with adventure."
