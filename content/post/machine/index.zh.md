## 读音
/məˈʃiːn/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一种由机械或电子部件组成的装置，用于执行特定任务。例如: "The machine in the factory is used for packaging products."
- **名词**: 机械设备或工具。例如: "She used a sewing machine to mend her dress."
- **名词**: 机构、系统或过程的比喻。例如: "The political machine worked efficiently to get their candidate elected."
- **名词**: 人类的代名词，常用于描述高效运作的团队。例如: "They function like a well-oiled machine."

## 短语
- "Heavy machinery" 指大型或重型设备。
- "Machine learning" 是人工智能的一个分支，涉及让机器从数据中学习。
- "The human machine" 常用来描述人的身体或大脑的工作方式。

## 例句
- 名词: "The engineers were testing a new machine designed to clean oceans."
- 名词: "His mind was a complex machine, constantly processing ideas and information."
- 名词: "In the metaphor, the company was referred to as a money-making machine."
