## 读音
/flɛr/

## 释义
- **动词**: 突然发出强烈的光或火焰；闪耀。例如: "The sun flared brightly in the sky."
- **名词**: 突然发出的强光或火焰；闪耀。例如: "A sudden flare lit up the dark sky."
- **名词**: (衣服、裙子等的)摆，褶皱。例如: "Her dress had a beautiful flare at the bottom."

## 短语
- "flare up" 指突然强烈地爆发或发炎。例如: "The pain flared up after he lifted weights."
- "flare of light" 指突然出现的亮光。例如: "There was a flare of light from the lighthouse."

## 例句
- 动词: "The campfire flared when more wood was added."
- 名词: "The astronaut saw a flare from a distant planet."
- 名词: "The jeans had a wide flare in the leg."
