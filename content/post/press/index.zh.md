## 读音
/ˈpres/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 印刷，出版业：The newspaper industry is part of the press.
  - 新闻界，媒体：She works for the foreign press.
  - 压力，重压：The athlete felt a lot of press from fans and coaches.
  - 按，压：Please press the button to start the machine.

- **动词**:
  - 按，压：He pressed the button firmly.
  - 印刷，出版：The book will be pressed next month.
  - 督促，催促：The manager pressed the team to finish the project on time.

- **形容词**:
  - 紧急的，紧迫的：There's a pressing need for more doctors in rural areas.

## 短语
- "Press conference" 是新闻发布会。
- "Under pressure" 意为在压力之下。
- "Press the flesh" 用来描述与很多人握手或社交应酬。

## 例句
- 名词: "The press covered the event extensively."
- 动词: "She pressed her ear against the door to listen."
- 形容词: "The company is facing pressing financial problems."
