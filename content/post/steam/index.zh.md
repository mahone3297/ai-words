## 读音
/steam/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 蒸汽，水蒸气。例如: "The kettle was producing steam from the boiling water."
  - 蒸汽动力。例如: "The old steam engine is a relic of the Industrial Revolution."
  
- **动词**:
  - 通过蒸汽加热或处理。例如: "I steamed the wrinkles out of my dress."
  - 以蒸汽驱动。例如: "The ship was powered by a steam engine."

## 短语
- "Steam up" 意味着产生蒸汽或变得充满蒸汽。例如: "The windows steamed up during the hot shower."
- "Steam clean" 指使用蒸汽进行清洁。例如: "I decided to steam clean the carpets for a deeper clean."

## 例句
- 名词: "The steam rising from the pot made the kitchen foggy."
- 动词: "She steamed the fish to preserve its nutrients."
