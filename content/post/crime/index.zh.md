## 读音
/ kraɪm /

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 违法行为，犯罪；罪行。例如: "He was accused of committing a serious crime."
- **动词 (v)**: 犯罪，违法。例如: "She decided not to crime again after serving her sentence."

## 短语
- "Crimes against humanity" 指对全人类的严重犯罪行为。
- "Cybercrime" 指网络犯罪，如黑客攻击和网络欺诈。

## 例句
- 名词: "The police are investigating a series of crimes in the neighborhood."
- 动词: "He was tempted to crime but his conscience held him back."
