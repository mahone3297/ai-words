## 读音
/suːt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一套衣服，尤指包括外套和配套裤子的正装。例如: "He wore a black suit to the interview."
- **名词**: 法律诉讼；诉状。例如: "She filed a suit against the company for discrimination."
- **动词 (v)**: 适合，适宜。例如: "The dress doesn't suit her personality."
- **动词**: 满足（需求、要求）。例如: "These options don't suit my needs."

## 短语
- "A business suit" 指正式场合穿的西装。
- "Suit up" 意味着穿上正装或准备行动。
- "To suit one's fancy" 意为符合某人的喜好。

## 例句
- 名词: "He chose a navy blue suit for his friend's wedding."
- 动词: "This color doesn't suit you; you should try something brighter."
- 动词: "The new job suits his skills and interests perfectly."

## 同义词
- Outfit
- Garment
- Ensemble
- Attire
- Clothing

请注意，"suit" 作为动词时，其同义词可能根据具体含义有所不同，如 "fit" 或 "be appropriate for"。
