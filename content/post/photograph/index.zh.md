## 读音
/ˈfəʊtəɡrɑːf/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 一张照片，通过相机拍摄的图像。例如: "She has a collection of old family photographs."
- **动词 (v)**: 拍照，摄影。例如: "I'll photograph the wedding ceremony for you."

## 短语
- "Take a photograph" 意为拍照。
- "Photograph someone/something" 指给某人或某物拍照。
- "Develop photographs" 指冲洗胶卷或处理数码照片。

## 例句
- 名词: "The photographer captured a beautiful photograph of the sunset."
- 动词: "Let's photograph the children playing in the park."

## 同义词
- Picture
- Image
- Snap
- Shot
