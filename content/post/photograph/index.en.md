## Pronunciation
/ˈfəʊtəgraʊp/

## Definitions
### Noun
A photograph is a **noun** that refers to a still image captured using a camera, typically consisting of light-sensitive chemicals on film or digitally recorded on a storage device. It represents a visual record of a scene, person, or object at a specific moment in time.

### Verb
To photograph is the **verb** meaning to take a photograph, capture a picture using a camera or other imaging device.

### Adjective
Photographic is an **adjective** describing something related to photography, having the appearance or characteristics of a photograph.

## Phrases
- "Take a photograph" means to capture an image with a camera.
- "Develop a photograph" refers to the process of turning a negative or digital image into a print.
- "Old photographs" evoke nostalgia for past times.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She displayed her collection of vintage photographs in the gallery."
- **Verb**: "I'm going to photograph the sunset tonight."
- **Adjective**: "The room had a photographic memory of their childhood vacation."
