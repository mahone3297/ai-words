## 读音
/stæb/

## 释义
- **动词**: 用尖锐物品刺入；攻击。例如: "The attacker stabbed the victim with a knife."
- **名词**: 刺击；刀伤。例如: "The victim suffered multiple stab wounds."

## 短语
- "stab in the back" 意为背叛，背后捅刀子。例如: "I never expected such a stab in the back from my best friend."
- "stab at something" 尝试，努力（做某事）。例如: "She made a stab at solving the problem but failed."

## 例句
- 动词: "The mugger suddenly stabbed him during the robbery."
- 名词: "The doctor was trying to save the patient from the fatal stab."
