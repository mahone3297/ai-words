## 读音
/ˈlɔɪəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 忠诚的，忠心的，尽职的。表示对某人、某组织或原则坚定不移的支持和忠诚。例如: "He is a loyal friend who will always stand by you."

## 短语
- "Loyal follower" 意为忠实的追随者。
- "Loyal customer" 指的是经常光顾并支持某个企业的客户。
- "Loyal to the cause" 意味着对某个事业或目标保持坚定的忠诚。

## 例句
- "Despite all challenges, she remained loyal to her company for over a decade."
- "The fans showed their loyalty by attending every game."
- "A true friend is one who remains loyal through thick and thin."

- Faithful
