## Word: Local

## Pronunciation
/ˈloʊkəl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to or belonging to a particular area or neighborhood; not widespread or distant. For example: "The local library is just down the street."
- **Noun**: A person who lives in or belongs to a specific area or neighborhood. For example: "The locals know the best places to eat around here."
- **Adjective (in reference to a product)**: Produced or available in a particular area; not imported from elsewhere. For example: "We serve only local produce in our restaurant."

## Phrases
- "Local community": The people living in a particular area and their social interactions.
- "Local government": The administrative body responsible for a small, designated area such as a town or district.
- "Go local": To support or use products, services, or businesses that are native or based in one's immediate area.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "This local park has stunning views of the city."
- **Noun**: "The locals organized a festival to celebrate their cultural heritage."
- **Adjective (product)**: "The farmer's market sells fresh fruits and vegetables from local farms."

## Synonyms
- Regional
- Neighborhood
- Community-based
- Area-specific
- Homegrown (when referring to products)
- Native (for people or products)
