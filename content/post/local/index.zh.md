## 读音
/ˈləʊkəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 本地的，当地的；地方性的；局部的。例如: "We visited some local restaurants during our trip." 
- **名词**: 本地人，当地人。例如: "The locals were very friendly and welcoming."
- **副词**: 在本地，当地。例如: "He works locally, never having to travel far for work."

## 短语
- "local community" 指的是当地的社区或居民群体。
- "local government" 指的是地方政府或地区行政机构。
- "local store" 指的是附近的商店或便利店。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The local artists displayed their artwork at the town square."
- 名词: "The tourists enjoyed interacting with the friendly locals."
- 副词: "The festival is celebrated locally with great enthusiasm."
