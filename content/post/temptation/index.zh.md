## 读音
/ˈtɛm.pə.ʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 引诱，诱惑；吸引人的事物。例如: "She resisted the temptation of eating another chocolate cake."
- **动词**: 引诱，诱惑。例如: "The devil tempted him with promises of wealth and power."

## 短语
- "resist temptation" 抵制诱惑。
- "yield to temptation" 向诱惑屈服。

## 例句
- 名词: "The smell of freshly baked cookies was a great temptation for the children."
- 动词: "The devil tempted Eve with the forbidden fruit in the Garden of Eden."
