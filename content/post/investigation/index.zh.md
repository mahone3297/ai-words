## 释义
- **名词**: 调查，探究，研究。例如: "The police launched an investigation into the theft."
- **动词**: 进行调查，研究。例如: "They are investigating the cause of the accident."

## 短语
- "criminal investigation" 刑事调查
- "scientific investigation" 科学研究
- "to investigate a problem" 研究问题

## 例句
- 名词: "The company is conducting an internal investigation into the accounting irregularities."
