## 读音
/ʌndəˈbaɪdlɪ/

## 释义
- **副词**: 毫无疑问地，肯定地，确信无疑。例如: "Undoubtedly, he is the best candidate for the job."
- **形容词**: 无疑的，确定的。例如: "This undoubtedly will be a challenging task."

## 短语
- "There is no doubt about it" 意味着对某事非常确定。
- "Undoubtedly true" 表示某事是毫无疑问的事实。

## 例句
- 副词: "Undoubtedly, his experience played a crucial role in his success."
- 形容词: "The painting is undoubtedly a masterpiece of the Renaissance period."
