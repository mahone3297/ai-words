## 读音
/ˈʃəʊldər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 肩部，身体上连接手臂和脖子的部分。例如: "He has broad shoulders."
- **动词**: 承担，承受（责任、重担等）。例如: "She shouldered the responsibility of caring for her elderly parents."

## 短语
- "Carry the weight on one's shoulders" 意为承受压力或责任。
- "Take the blame on your shoulders" 意为承担责任或过错。

## 例句
- 名词: "The athlete had a strong and well-defined shoulder muscle."
- 动词: "He shouldered the load of work without complaint."
