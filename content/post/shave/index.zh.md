## 读音
/shave/

## 释义
- **动词**: 用剃刀或其他工具刮去（如胡须或头发）。例如: "He shaved his beard before the interview."
- **名词**: 刮胡子的动作或过程。例如: "He takes a shave every morning."

## 短语
- "give oneself a shave" 意为给自己刮胡子。
- "shave off" 用于去除表面的一层，如“剃掉多余的毛发”。

## 例句
- 动词: "I need to shave before the meeting so I look more professional."
- 名词: "She gave him a close shave with her new razor."
