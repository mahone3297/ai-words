## 读音
/θrəʊt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 人体的咽喉部分，连接口腔和食道及气管的管道。例如: "She felt a soreness in her throat."
- **动词**: (用力)吞下（尤指大口或困难地）。例如: "The child had trouble swallowing the medicine, so they had to throat it down."

## 短语
- "Clear one's throat" 表示清嗓子，通常是因为要说话或咳嗽。
- "Get something stuck in one's throat" 指有东西卡在喉咙里。

## 例句
- 名词: "The singer had to take a break because of a sore throat."
- 动词: "The baby managed to throat the whole piece of bread without choking."

## 同义词
- Neck
- Gullet
- Esophagus
- Windpipe (侧重指气管部分)
