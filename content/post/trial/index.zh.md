## 读音
/ˈtraɪəl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 
   - 试验；尝试：例如 "They are conducting a trial of a new cancer treatment."
   - 审讯；法庭审理：例如 "The high-profile murder case went to trial last week."
   - 试用期：例如 "The company offers a free trial of their software for 30 days."

- **动词**:
   - 试验；尝试：例如 "We'll trial the new method and see if it works."

## 短语
- "on trial" 表示在受审或试验中：例如 "The suspect is currently on trial for theft."
- "trial and error" 指通过反复试验找到正确方法的过程：例如 "They solved the problem using trial and error."

## 例句
- 名词: "The new drug has shown promising results in early trials."
- 动词: "They decided to trial the new sales strategy in a few selected markets."
