## 读音
/ɪnˈfɪriər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 干预；妨碍。例如: "I wish they wouldn't interfere in my personal life."
2. 介入；插手。例如: "The government decided to interfere in the economic crisis."
3. 互相干扰。例如: "Radio signals can interfere with each other."

## 短语
- "interfere with" 意味着打扰或阻碍。例如: "Don't let anything interfere with your studies."
- "interfere in" 表示在某事中插入自己的影响。例如: "She doesn't like it when people interfere in her decisions."

## 例句
- 动词: "He refused to interfere unless absolutely necessary."
- "Their noise was interfering with my concentration."
- "The new technology could potentially interfere with existing systems."
