## 读音
/əˈkjuːz/

## 释义
- **动词**: 指控，指责，归咎于。例如: "The witness accused him of theft."
- **名词**: 控诉，指控。例如: "The defense lawyer refuted the plaintiff's accusations."

## 短语
- "accuse someone of something" 指责某人做了某事。例如: "She accused him of lying about the incident."
- "to be accused of" 被指控。例如: "He was accused of embezzlement."

## 例句
- 动词: "The police accused the suspect of involvement in the robbery."
- 名词: "The politician faced numerous accusations of corruption."
