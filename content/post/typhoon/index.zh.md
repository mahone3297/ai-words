## 读音
/taɪˈfoun/

## 释义
- **名词**: 特指热带气旋，一种在热带海洋上形成的强烈风暴，尤指台风或飓风。例如: "The typhoon caused widespread destruction along the coast."
- **动词**: (非正式)指台风或类似风暴的袭击。例如: "The region was typhooned by a powerful storm."

## 短语
- "Typhoon season" 指台风频繁发生的季节，通常在夏季。
- "Typhoon warning" 提醒人们台风即将来临的警告。

## 例句
- 名词: "The typhoon's intensity increased as it approached the island."
- 动词: "The village was typhooned, leaving many homes destroyed."
