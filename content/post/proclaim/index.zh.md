## 读音
/prəˈkleɪm/

## 释义
- **动词**: 公开宣布，声明；宣告。例如: "The government proclaimed a new law."
- **名词**: 宣告，公告。例如: "The king's proclaim was read out in the town square."

## 短语
- "to proclaim something loudly" 大声宣布某事。
- "proclaim independence" 宣布独立。

## 例句
- 动词: "The president proclaimed a state of emergency due to the pandemic."
- 名词: "The city council issued a proclaim against littering."
