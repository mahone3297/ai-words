## 读音
/ˈfərən/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 来自外国的，不属于本国的，外来的。例如: "She speaks several foreign languages."
- **名词**: 外国人，外来事物。例如: "The foreign minister discussed trade with their counterparts from other countries."

## 短语
- "foreign policy" 指一个国家对外关系的政策。
- "foreign exchange" 指外汇，货币兑换。
- "foreign investment" 指来自其他国家的投资。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The restaurant offers a variety of foreign dishes from around the world."
- 名词: "He had to apply for a visa to visit the foreign country."
