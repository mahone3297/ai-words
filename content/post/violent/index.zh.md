## 释义
- **形容词**: 粗暴的，激烈的，猛烈的。例如: "The storm was violent, with strong winds and heavy rain."
- **名词**: 暴力行为，攻击。例如: "There have been several acts of violence in the city recently."

## 短语
- "violent behavior" 指的是暴力行为或举止。
- "violent reaction" 指强烈的反应或反抗。
