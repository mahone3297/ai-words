## 读音
/ˈprəʊɡræm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 计划，安排；程序（尤指计算机程序）。例如: "The computer runs a program to calculate the sales tax."
- **动词**: 编写或制定计划；为计算机编写程序。例如: "She is programming a new app for her business."

## 短语
- "Computer program" 指的是为特定任务而编写的计算机代码。
- "TV program" 指电视上的一个节目或系列节目。
- "Fitness program" 是指一套健身计划或锻炼方案。

## 例句
- 名词: "The software company released an updated version of their flagship program."
- 动词: "He's currently programming a game with advanced artificial intelligence."
