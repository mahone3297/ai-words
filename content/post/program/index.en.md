## Pronunciation
/prəˈgræm/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A sequence of instructions designed to be executed by a computer, often for performing a specific task or operation.
   - "The computer runs various programs to manage its functions."
2. A set of organized steps or tasks that make up a project or activity.
   - "The school's after-school program includes sports and arts activities."
3. An instance of a software application.
   - "I need to update my antivirus program."

### Verb
1. To initiate or start (a computer process or software).
   - "To run the program, double-click on the icon."
2. To carry out or execute a plan or series of actions.
   - "The team programmed a new marketing campaign for the product launch."
