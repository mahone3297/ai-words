## Word: Million

## Pronunciation
/ˈmɪl.jən/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A number equal to 1,000,000, which is one thousand thousands, commonly used to represent a large quantity of something. For example: "The company has sold millions of copies of the book."
  
  Phrase: 
  - "A million dollars/pounds/euros" is often used to denote a substantial amount of money. Example: "He won a million dollars in the lottery."
  - "One in a million" is used to describe something or someone that is extremely rare or unique. Example: "She's a one-in-a-million talent."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "There are approximately a million stars in our galaxy."
- "She dreams of becoming a millionaire by starting her own business."

## Synonyms
- (in terms of quantity) Thousands of thousands
- A great many
- A vast number
- Countless
- Myriad

Please note that 'million' does not typically have verb or adjective uses like the example provided with "hello." It is primarily a numerical noun referring to a specific quantity. However, it can be used idiomatically in various phrases as shown above.
