## 读音
/mɪljən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一百万，数量单位。例如: "He has a fortune of millions."
- **数词**: 用来表示一百万个，常与of连用。例如: "There were millions of stars in the sky."

## 短语
- "Millionaire" 意为百万富翁。
- "In the millions" 用于描述数量极多。例如: "The company's profits were in the millions."

## 例句
- 名词: "She won a million dollars in the lottery."
- 数词: "The population of China is in the millions."

## 同义词
- Hundred thousand (100,000)
- Ten million (10,000,000)
- Multimillionaire (拥有数百万的人)
