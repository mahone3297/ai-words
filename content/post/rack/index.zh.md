## 读音
/ˈræk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. (通常为金属或木制的)架子，格架，通常用来悬挂、展示或存放物品。例如: "She hung her coats on the rack in the closet."
   2. （尤指刑讯时用的）绞架，刑具。例如: "The prisoner was tortured on the rack."
- **动词**: 把…放在架子上；折磨；使痛苦。例如: "He racked his brain trying to solve the puzzle."
- **名词**（台球术语）: 台球比赛中，将球整齐地排成一堆。例如: "The balls were racked at the start of the game."

## 短语
- "on the rack" 表示在压力下，处于困境。
- "rack one's brains" 意味着苦思冥想，竭尽全力思考。

## 例句
- 名词: "I need to clean the spice rack in the kitchen."
- 动词: "The detective racked the suspect for information."
- 名词（台球术语）: "The player broke the rack and sank three balls."

## 同义词
- Shelf
- Stand
- Bracket
- Holder
- Torture device (针对"绞架"的含义)
