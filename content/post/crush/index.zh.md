## 读音
/krʌʃ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 压碎，粉碎；迷恋，强烈爱慕的人。例如: "I accidentally dropped the glass, causing it to turn into a crush of pieces." 或 "She has a crush on her teacher."
- **动词**: 压碎，破碎；使受挫，击败。例如: "The car was crushed under the weight of the truck." 或 "His hopes were crushed when he didn't get the job."
- **形容词**: 拥挤的，人潮汹涌的。例如: "The concert was so crowded, it was a crush of people."

## 短语
- "have a crush on someone" 意味着对某人有强烈的爱慕之情。
- "crush the opposition" 表示击败对手或压制反对者。

## 例句
- 名词: "After the accident, the street was filled with a crush of broken glass."
- 动词: "The ice crusher efficiently crushed the ice for our cocktails."
- 形容词: "The train station was a crush during rush hour, making it difficult to move."

## 同义词
- 压碎：smash, batter, pulverize
- 迷恋：infatuation, fancy, admiration
- 拥挤的：jammed, packed, crowded
