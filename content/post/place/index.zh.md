## 读音
/pleɪs/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 处所，地点。例如: "Please take me to that place over there."  
   2. 位置，方位。例如: "She found her place in the queue."
   3. 职位，角色。例如: "He holds an important place in the company."
   4. 空间。例如: "There's enough place for everyone to sit."
   5. 竞赛名次。例如: "He finished in first place."

- **动词 (v)**: 1. 安置，放。例如: "Can you place the vase on the table?"
   2. 预定。例如: "I've placed a reservation at the restaurant."
   3. 使就职。例如: "She was placed in charge of the project."
   4. 投资（资金）。例如: "He decided to place his savings in stocks."

## 短语
- "in place" 表示在适当的位置或准备就绪。例如: "Everything is in place for the event."
- "take one's place" 意味着占据应有的位置。例如: "It's time for you to take your place on stage."

## 例句
- 名词: "We need to find a quiet place to have our conversation."
- 动词: "She placed the book exactly where it belonged."
- 感叹词: "Place your bets, ladies and gentlemen!"
