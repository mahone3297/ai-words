## 读音
/user/

## 释义
- **名词**: 指使用某种产品、服务或技术的人。例如: "The new software has received positive feedback from its users."
- **动词**: 使用或利用（某物）。例如: "She users her smartphone for both work and entertainment."

## 短语
- "New user" 指初次使用某个系统或应用的人。
- "Heavy user" 指频繁或大量使用某物的人。
- "Regular user" 指经常使用某产品或服务的用户。

## 例句
- 名词: "The app has millions of daily active users worldwide."
- 动词: "He user the library's online resources to research his project."
