## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In a lasting or permanent manner; without end or change. For example: "The decision was made permanently."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by permanence; lasting indefinitely. For example: "A permanently scarred face."

## Phrases
- "Permanently settle" implies a long-term, irrevocable arrangement.
- "A permanently fixed smile" suggests a constant expression.
- "Permanently banned" means a prohibition that cannot be lifted.
