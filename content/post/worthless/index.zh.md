## 读音
/ˈwɜːrθləs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 没有价值的，无用的，不值钱的。例如: "This old car is worthless; it can't even start."
- **名词**: 无价值的东西。例如: "He considered the broken toy to be just another worthless item."

## 短语
- "worthless trinket" 指没有实际价值的小饰品。
- "a worthless piece of advice" 指无用的建议。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The company declared the stock worthless after the bankruptcy."
- 名词: "He threw away all the worthless junk in his garage."
