## 读音
/əˈpærəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 外表，外貌，出现。指事物呈现给人们的视觉印象或人的长相和打扮。例如: "She was impressed by his smart appearance."
- **名词**: 出现，露面。指某人或某物在某一场合首次或再次出现。例如: "The singer made a surprise appearance at the concert."
- **名词**: （事物的）外观，表现形式。例如: "The appearance of the product is crucial for its success."

## 短语
- "make an appearance" 意为出现或露面。
- "put on a good appearance" 指装扮得体或表现得体。

## 例句
- 名词: "The model's appearance on the runway caused a sensation."
- 名词: "The house looked charming with its well-maintained appearance."
- 名词: "His sudden appearance in the room startled everyone."
