## 读音
/spɑːrkl/

## 释义
- **动词**: 发出闪烁的光；闪耀。例如: "The diamonds sparkled in the light."
- **名词**: 闪烁；闪光。例如: "There was a sparkle in her eyes when she talked about her passion."

## 短语
- "sparkle with joy" 意为充满喜悦地闪烁。
- "sparkle like stars" 形容像星星一样闪烁。

## 例句
- 动词: "The fireworks sparkled brightly against the night sky."
- 名词: "Her dress was adorned with sequins that sparkled as she moved."
