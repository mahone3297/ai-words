## 读音
/ˈlɔːs/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - **损失**：指失去某物，尤指金钱、生命或财产的损失。例如: "The company suffered huge losses in the financial crisis."
   - **失败**：在比赛、竞争中的失利。例如: "They were disappointed at their loss in the final match."
   - **悲伤**：失去亲人或朋友时的情感。例如: "She was still grieving over the loss of her mother."

- **动词**:
   - **失去**：不复拥有或不再存在。例如: "He loss his job due to the economic downturn."
   - **减少**：数量或程度上的下降。例如: "Her weight loss was noticeable after a month of dieting."

## 短语
- "At a loss" 表示困惑或不知所措。例如: "I was at a loss for words when she told me the news."
- "Loss leader" 在商业中指低价销售以吸引顾客的商品。例如: "The store uses a loss leader strategy to attract more shoppers."

## 例句
- 名词: "The storm caused extensive damage and heavy losses to local farmers."
- 动词: "She was devastated by the loss of her pet cat."
- 感叹词: "Loss can be a catalyst for personal growth."
