## 读音
/skreɪp/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 用刀或其他工具刮擦表面以去除物质。例如: "I had to scrape the ice off my car's windshield."
  2. 拼命获取或节约。例如: "They were scraping together enough money for the rent."
  3. （在音乐中）用刮擦的方式演奏。例如: "The violinist scraped the bow across the strings."
  
- **名词**:
  1. 刮痕或擦伤。例如: "She got a scrape on her knee while playing soccer."
  2. （互联网上）收集和复制信息的行为。例如: "The website scraped data from various sources for analysis."

## 短语
- "Scrape by" 意味勉强维持生计或仅能过关。例如: "They're barely scraping by on their low salaries."
- "Scrape together" 表示凑齐或拼凑。例如: "We managed to scrape together enough ingredients for dinner."

## 例句
- 动词: "He used a credit card to scrape the ice off his car's windows."
- 名词: "She put a bandage on the scrape on her arm."
