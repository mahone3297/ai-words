## 读音
/rot/

## 释义
- **动词**: 使腐烂，腐败；分解。例如: "The apples left outside began to rot."
- **名词**: 腐败，腐朽；败坏的状态。例如: "The wood was in an advanced state of rot."

## 短语
- "Rot away" 指某物因腐烂而逐渐消失。
- "At the root of the problem" 表示问题的根本原因。

## 例句
- 动词: "If you don't use your vegetables, they'll just rot in the fridge."
- 名词: "The old wooden bridge was replaced due to extensive rot."

- Deteriorate
