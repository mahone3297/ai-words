## Pronunciation
/tɛmˈpərətʃər/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Temperature**: The degree of hotness or coldness of a substance, measured on a scale, such as Celsius (°C) or Fahrenheit (°F).
- **Ambient temperature**: The general air temperature in a particular environment.
- **Thermal comfort**: The condition of being neither too hot nor too cold, considered to be a pleasant level of temperature for human comfort.

### Verb
- **To measure temperature**: The act of determining the heat or coldness of something.
- **To regulate temperature**: Adjusting a system to maintain a desired level of warmth or coolness.

## Phrases
- "Check the room temperature" to monitor the ambient temperature.
- "Keep the temperature steady" for maintaining a constant level.
- "The thermometer hit 100°F" to indicate a high temperature reading.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The temperature outside dropped significantly overnight."
- **Verb**: "The thermostat regulates the temperature inside the house."
- **Phrasal**: "The plants require a consistent temperature to thrive."
