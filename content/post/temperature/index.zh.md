## 读音
/ˈtemprətʃər/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 温度：物体或环境的冷热程度，通常以摄氏度（Celsius）或华氏度（Fahrenheit）为单位测量。例如: "The temperature outside is 25 degrees Celsius."
  - 气氛：特定情境下的情绪或感觉。例如: "There was a tense temperature in the room during the argument."

## 短语
- "temperature gauge" 温度计，用来测量温度的工具。
- "body temperature" 体温，人体内部的温度。
- "room temperature" 室温，室内未受调节的空气温度。

## 例句
- 名词: "The temperature dropped suddenly, and we had to put on more clothes."
- 气氛: "After the speech, the temperature in the room seemed to rise with excitement."
