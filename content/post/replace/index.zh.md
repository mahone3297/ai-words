## 读音
/rɪˈpleɪs/

## 释义
- **动词**:
    1. 取代；替换：将某物或某人换成另一个。例如: "The old computer was replaced by a new model."
    2. 顶替；接替：在职位或职责上接替某人。例如: "She will replace him on the committee next month."
    3. 修补；修复：修复或替换损坏或磨损的部分。例如: "I need to replace the worn-out tires on my car."

## 短语
- "replace something with something else" 意为用某物替换另一物。
- "replace someone in a position" 指在某个位置或角色上接替某人。

## 例句
- "We're going to replace the outdated software with the latest version."
- "The injured player was promptly replaced by a substitute."
- "The company decided to replace the wooden windows with energy-efficient ones."
