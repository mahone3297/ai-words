## Pronunciation
/rɪˈpleɪs/

## Definitions
### Noun
A noun referring to the act of substituting one thing with another, usually a word or item. **Example:** The software has a feature that allows you to replace text easily.

### Verb
1. To substitute one word, item, or person for another, often by intention or necessity. **Example:** He replaced the old sofa with a new one.
2. To take the place of someone or something else in a particular role or function. **Example:** She replaced her boss as team leader during his vacation.

### Adjective
Referring to something that is substitutable or capable of being replaced. **Example:** The company offers replaceable parts for their appliances.

## Phrases
- "Replaceable parts" indicates items that can be easily swapped out.
- "Replace this with that" suggests substitution.
- "Replace worn-out items" implies the need for new ones due to wear and tear.
