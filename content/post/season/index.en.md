## Pronunciation
/ˈsiːzn/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Season**: A period of time characterized by distinct weather conditions and typically associated with a particular activity or event. For example: "Spring is the season when flowers bloom."
2. **Seasons of the year**: The four divisions of the year based on climate (e.g., winter, summer, autumn, and spring).
3. **Sports season**: A set period during which a sport is played competitively, such as football season or baseball season.
4. **Game or show season**: The period when a specific game or television show is regularly aired.

### Verb
- To experience a season: "We're looking forward to enjoying the summer season."
- To go through the different seasons: "The trees change colors as we go through the seasonal cycle."

## Phrases
