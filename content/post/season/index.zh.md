## 读音
/ˈsiːzn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一年中的一个特定时期，通常与气候、天气或特定活动相关。例如: "Spring is the season for planting flowers."
- **名词**: 某种活动或事件的时期。例如: "The football season starts in September."

## 短语
- "In season" 表示某种食物或活动在最佳或最流行的时候。例如: "Blueberries are in season right now."
- "Out of season" 表示不在最佳或最流行的时候。例如: "It's out of season to go skiing now."

## 例句
- "My favorite season is autumn because of the beautiful foliage."
- "The ski resort is bustling during the winter season."
