## 读音
/ˈlaɪkli/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 可能的，大概的，似有可能的。例如: "It's likely that it will rain tomorrow."
- **副词**: 很可能，大概。例如: "She is likely to accept the offer."

## 短语
- "likely candidate" 意为很可能的人选。
- "be likely to do something" 意为很可能做某事。

## 例句
- 形容词: "Based on the forecast, it's likely we'll need an umbrella tomorrow."
- 副词: "Given her skills, she is likely to succeed in the new job."
