## 读音
/leɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一条狭窄的道路或路径，通常在公路、运动场或游泳池中。例如: "The cars were driving in separate lanes on the highway."
- **名词**: (体育)跑道的一条分道。例如: "Each athlete had their own lane for the 100-meter dash."

## 短语
- "lane change" 指车辆在道路上变换车道。
- "swimming lane" 是游泳池中的分道线，供游泳者保持直线。

## 例句
- "The cyclists rode safely in their designated lanes."
- "She was in lane three during the swimming competition."
