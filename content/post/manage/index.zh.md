## 读音
/ˈmænɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 控制和组织（事情）：例如: "She manages the day-to-day running of the company."
  - 成功处理（困难情况）：例如: "He managed to finish the project on time despite the setbacks."
  - 操纵或影响（人或情况）：例如: "The politician manages public opinion skillfully."
  - 维持或保持（健康、情绪等）：例如: "I'm managing my stress levels with yoga and meditation."

- **名词**:
  - 经营或管理的行为：例如: "He has a lot of experience in hotel management."
  - 经理或管理者：例如: "The team manager is responsible for selecting the players."

## 短语
- "manage something well" 表示某事处理得当。
- "manage to do something" 表示成功做某事。
- "manage without something" 意为在没有某物的情况下设法应对。

## 例句
- 动词: "She manages a team of salespeople and oversees all their deals."
- 名词: "The new restaurant hired an experienced manager to handle customer complaints."
