## 读音
/ˈlaɪtli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 轻轻地，轻松地，不费力地。例如: "He handled the fragile vase lightly."
- **形容词**: 轻浮的，不严肃的。例如: "She took the matter too lightly."

## 短语
- "lightly touch" 轻轻触摸
- "lightly cooked" 轻度烹饪
- "treat something lightly" 对待某事不认真

## 例句
- 副词: "She danced lightly on her toes."
- 形容词: "His attitude towards the problem was too lightly."
