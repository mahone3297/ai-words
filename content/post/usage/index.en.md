## Pronunciation
/həˈloʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to greet or address someone, expressing friendliness or recognition. For example: "She offered a friendly hello to the passerby."
- **Verb**: To express greeting or acknowledge someone's presence with the word "hello." For example: "He greeted the teacher with a hearty hello in the morning."
- **Interjection**: An exclamation used to initiate conversation or draw attention, often informally. For example: "Hello, sunshine! It's a beautiful day."

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to extend a greeting to them.
- "Hello, how are you?" is a standard way to ask for someone's well-being.
- "Hello, old sport!" is a British expression used to address a familiar acquaintance.
