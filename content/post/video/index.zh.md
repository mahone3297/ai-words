## 读音
/ˈviːdiəʊ/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 电子视频信号，尤指通过电视、录像或互联网观看的视听记录。例如: "She recorded a video message for her fans."
- **名词**: 视频设备，如摄像机或录像机。例如: "He bought a new video camera for his trip."
- **动词 (v)**: 录制或拍摄视频。例如: "They videoed the entire concert."

## 短语
- "Watch a video" 意为观看视频。
- "Record a video" 表示录制视频。
- "Video call" 指通过网络进行的视频通话。

## 例句
- 名词: "The viral video has been viewed millions of times online."
- 动词: "She videos her daily routine and shares it on social media."
- 名词 (as a verb): "I videoed my son's first steps so I could show it to the family."

## 同义词
- Film
- Recording
- Clip
- Footage
- Movie (in the context of short or informal videos)
