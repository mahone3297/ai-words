## Word: Inexpensive

## Pronunciation
/ˌɪnɪkˈspensɪv/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Costing little; not expensive. It describes something that can be bought or obtained at a low price or with relatively little money spent.

### Example Sentences
- _The thrift store had many inexpensive clothing options._
- _This restaurant offers delicious yet inexpensive meals._
- _We found an inexpensive flight deal to our vacation destination._

## Phrases
- "Inexpensive solution": Refers to a method or product that solves a problem without requiring a large amount of money.
- "Affordable and inexpensive": Describes items or services that are within most people's financial reach.
- "Inexpensive alternative": Suggests a cheaper option compared to another more costly one.

## Synonyms
- Cheap
- Budget-friendly
- Economical
- Affordable
- Low-cost
- Reasonably priced
- Modestly priced
- Cost-effective
- Pocket-friendly
- Bargain-basement (informal)
  
Please note that while "inexpensive" generally carries a positive connotation, suggesting value for money, "cheap" can sometimes imply lower quality. However, in certain contexts, both words can be used interchangeably to mean the same thing – a low price point.
