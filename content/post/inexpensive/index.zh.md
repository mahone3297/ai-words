## 读音
/ɪnˈkənsətɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不昂贵的，价格合理的。例如: "The restaurant offers inexpensive meals for students."
- **副词**: 便宜地，不花大钱地。例如: "You can buy these books inexpensive from online retailers."

## 短语
- "inexpensive option" 意为经济实惠的选择。
- "an inexpensive night out" 指花费不高的夜晚娱乐活动。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This laptop is an excellent choice for those looking for an inexpensive but reliable device."
- 副词: "We managed to travel around Europe inexpensive by staying in hostels."
