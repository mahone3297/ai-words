## 读音
/sərˈfɪs/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 表面；外表：The surface of the table is smooth.
  - (地球等的)表面：The exploration team reached the surface of the ocean.
  - (物体的)面积；表面积：Calculate the surface area of the box.

- **动词 v**:
  - 出现；显露：A new problem surfaced during the meeting.
  - 探索表面：The divers surfaced after an hour underwater.

## 短语
- "on the surface" 表面上；看上去：At first glance, everything seemed fine on the surface.
- "surface level" 表面层次的；浅显的：Let's discuss the topic at a surface level before delving deeper.

## 例句
- 名词: "The artist painted a beautiful landscape on the surface of the canvas."
- 动词: "His fears surfaced when he was alone in the dark."
- 感叹词（不常见，但可作俚语使用）: "Surface, buddy, we've been diving for too long!"
