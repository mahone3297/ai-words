## Pronunciation

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Firmly set or immovable, especially in position or attitude; inflexible. For example: "The rules were rigidly enforced."
- **Noun**: A person who is inflexible or strict in their behavior or opinions. For example: "He's a very rigid authoritarian."
- **Verb**: To make something hard or unyielding, often in a particular direction or orientation. For example: "The carpenter made the frame rigid with braces."

## Phrases
- "Maintain a rigid posture" means to hold one's body in a straight and stiff manner.
- "Rigid discipline" refers to strict adherence to rules or routines.
- "A rigid belief system" denotes a fixed and unyielding set of convictions.
