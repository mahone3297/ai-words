## 读音
/əˈkrɒs/

## 释义
- **介词**: 表示从一边到另一边，穿过或横过。例如: "The bridge goes across the river."
- **副词**: 指在空间上横过或从一边到另一边。例如: "She walked across the street."

## 短语
- "Jump across" 跳过去。
- "Look across" 向对面看。
- "Travel across" 穿越。

## 例句
- 介词: "The train will take you across the country."
- 副词: "The cat dashed across the room."

- Through
