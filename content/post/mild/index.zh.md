## 读音
/maɪld/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 温和的，不严厉的。例如: "She has a mild temper and never loses her cool."
- **名词**: 温和的气候或天气。例如: "The region is known for its mild climate."
- **副词**: 轻微地，稍微。例如: "He was only mildly interested in the topic."

## 短语
- "mild mannered" 表示举止温和。
- "mild weather" 指舒适的天气。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The teacher had a mild tone when addressing the students."
- 名词: "The island enjoys a mild winter with little rainfall."
- 副词: "His headache was only mild, so he didn't take any medicine."
