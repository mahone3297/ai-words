## Pronunciation
/unˈkɒnʃəs/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Unconsciousness**: The state of being unaware of one's surroundings or condition, typically due to loss of awareness or medication.
- **Unconscious mind**: The part of the psyche that contains thoughts, feelings, memories, and desires that a person is not aware of or cannot easily access.

### Verb
- To become unconscious: To lose consciousness, either temporarily or permanently.
- To make unconscious: To render someone unconscious, usually through a medical procedure or substance.

### Adjective
- Unconscious: Lacking awareness or consciousness; not aware of one's actions or surroundings.
- Unconsciously: Without knowing it or being aware of it.

## Phrases
- "In a state of unconsciousness"
- "To awaken from unconsciousness"
- "He hit his head and lost consciousness"
- "Her unconscious actions revealed her true feelings"

## Example Sentences
