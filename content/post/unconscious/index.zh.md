## 读音
/ʌnˈkɑːnʃəs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不知觉的；无意识的；失去知觉的。例如: "He fell down and was unconscious for a few minutes."
- **名词**: 失去知觉的状态。例如: "She came to after a brief period of unconsciousness."

## 短语
- "lose consciousness" 意为失去知觉。
- "fall unconscious" 表示突然失去意识。
- "in an unconscious state" 指处于无意识状态。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The patient remained unconscious throughout the operation."
- 名词: "After the accident, he was in an unconscious state for two days."
