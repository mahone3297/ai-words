## 读音
/ˈlɔɪəlti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 忠诚，忠心；坚定的支持或服从。例如: "His loyalty to his country is unwavering."
- **形容词**: 忠诚的，忠实的。例如: "She demonstrated great loyalty to her employer."

## 短语
- "Loyalty test" 检验或评估某人忠诚度的过程。
- "Customer loyalty program" 顾客忠诚度计划，商家用来奖励常客的策略。

## 例句
- 名词: "The king was known for rewarding the loyalty of his subjects."
- 形容词: "Their employees are fiercely loyal to the company's mission."

- Devotion
- Allegiance
