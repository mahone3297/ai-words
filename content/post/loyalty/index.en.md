## Word: Loyalty

## Pronunciation
/ˈlɔɪəlti/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The quality of being loyal; faithfulness or allegiance to a person, country, organization, or cause. It can also refer to the consistent support or devotion shown over time. 

Example Sentence (as a Noun): 
- "His unwavering loyalty to his friends was admirable."
- "Customers have shown great loyalty to the brand over the years."

Phrases:
- "Loyalty to the crown": Refers to faithful allegiance to one's monarch or nation.
- "Loyalty card": A marketing tool that rewards customers for their repeat business.
- "Loyalty above all else": Emphasizes the importance of remaining faithful even when faced with adversity.

## Example Sentences
- "Their loyalty was tested during the difficult times, but they stood by each other."
- "She earned a promotion due to her long-standing loyalty to the company."

## Synonyms
- Faithfulness
- Devotion
- Allegiance
- Fidelity
- Commitment
- Dedication
- Obedience
- Trustworthiness
- Dependability
- Piety (in religious contexts)
