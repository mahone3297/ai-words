## 读音
/wɔːk/

## 释义
- **动词**: 走路，步行。例如: "I usually walk to work."
- **名词**: 行走的动作或过程。例如: "He went for a long walk in the park."
- **名词**: (演出、活动等的)一段进行。例如: "Her performance included a stunning dance walk."

## 短语
- "Take a walk" 意为散步或外出走走。
- "Go for a walk" 同样表示散步。
- "On foot" 用来表达走路的方式，即不是乘坐交通工具。

## 例句
- 动词: "She enjoys walking along the beach at sunset."
- 名词: "The baby's first walk was unsteady but adorable."
- 名词: "The actor's walk on stage was confident and commanding."
