## 读音
/ˈnɑːvəl/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 长篇小说，尤其是具有复杂情节和深入主题的文学作品。例如: "She's writing a novel about time travel."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 新奇的，新颖的，不寻常的。例如: "The company introduced a novel approach to customer service."

## 短语
- "a work of novel" 指一部小说作品。
- "novel ideas" 指创新或独特的想法。

## 例句
- 名词: "I spent the weekend reading a fascinating novel by my favorite author."
- 形容词: "The designer's novel use of color made the collection stand out."
