## 读音
/ɪnɪˈvɛtəbl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 不可避免的；必然发生的。例如: "The aging process is an inevitable part of life."
- **副词**: 必然地；不可避免地。例如: "The consequences will inevitably lead to disaster."

## 短语
- "Inevitable outcome" 指不可避免的结果。
- "Inevitable change" 指不可避免的变化。

## 例句
- 形容词: "It's inevitable that we all face challenges at some point in our lives."
- 副词: "If we continue like this, failure is inevitably waiting for us."
