## 读音
/ˈvɛntʃər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 冒险；大胆尝试：An undertaking involving uncertainty and risk. 例如: "They decided to start a new business as a venture into the unknown."
   - 投资；企业：A company or project in which money is invested with the hope of profit. 例如: "The investors backed a new venture in renewable energy."

- **动词**:
   - 冒险做；敢于尝试：To take a risk or engage in a risky activity. 例如: "She ventured into the dark forest, not knowing what lay ahead."
   - 提出（意见）：To express an opinion or suggestion, especially when it might be controversial. 例如: "He ventured that the plan was too ambitious."

## 短语
- "Venture capital" 指风险投资，用于资助初创公司或高风险项目。
- "Venture out" 意为冒险外出或离开安全的地方。例如: "Despite the storm, they ventured out to attend the meeting."

## 例句
- 名词: "Their latest venture into the fashion industry has been very successful."
- 动词: "He ventured a guess about the outcome of the game, but he wasn't very confident."
- 感叹词 (非正式): "Well, hello there! Haven't seen you in ages!"
