## Pronunciation
/seɪmˈpjuːtər/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A type of electronic material with electrical conductivity between that of a conductor and an insulator, used in digital devices for processing information and controlling their operation. [**Tech**]
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of, or involving, a semiconductor material or device. [**Tech**]

## Phrases
- "Utilize a semiconductor" refers to using a semiconductor component in a device.
- "Semiconductor technology" encompasses the science and manufacturing of semiconductors.
- "Transistor made from a semiconductor" highlights the role of semiconductors in electronic components.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The development of microprocessors relies heavily on advancements in semiconductor materials."
- **Adjective**: "The new smartphone features a powerful semiconductor processor for faster performance."
- "The invention of the integrated circuit was a major breakthrough in semiconductor engineering."
