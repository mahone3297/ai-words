## 读音
/ˈsɛmɪkɑːndʌktər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 半导体是一种材料，其导电性能介于导体和绝缘体之间，可以通过掺杂剂或电场来改变其导电性。在电子设备和集成电路中广泛应用。例如: "Silicon is a commonly used semiconductor material in computer chips."

## 短语
- "semiconductor device" 指使用半导体材料制成的电子元件，如二极管、晶体管或集成电路。
- "semiconductor industry" 指制造和开发半导体相关产品的行业。

## 例句
- "The development of semiconductor technology has revolutionized the world of computing."
- "The company specializes in manufacturing high-performance semiconductor devices."

## 同义词
- Solid-state material
- Conductor-insulator material
- Non-metallic conductor
