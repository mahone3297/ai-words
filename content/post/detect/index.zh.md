## 读音
/dɪˈtɛkt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 发现；察觉：例如: "The detective detected signs of a break-in."
2. 检测；探测：例如: "The device can detect even the smallest amounts of radiation."

## 短语
- "Detect something/someone" 意味着发现或察觉到某事物或某人。
- "Detect a problem" 表示找出或识别一个问题。

## 例句
- 动词: "The security system was able to detect the intruder immediately."
- 动词: "The doctor used a special tool to detect any potential health issues."
