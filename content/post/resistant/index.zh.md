## 读音
/ˈrɪzən.tənt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 能抵抗的，有抵抗力的，不易受影响的。例如: "The material is highly resistant to water damage."
- **动词**: 抵制，反抗。例如: "The patient was resistant to the treatment."

## 短语
- "resistant to something" 表示对某物有抵抗力或不易受其影响。
- "be resistant against" 意味着能够对抗或防止。

## 例句
- 形容词: "This plant species has developed a natural resistance to pesticides."
- 动词: "Some bacteria have become resistant to commonly used antibiotics."

## 同义词
- immune
- tolerant
- unyielding
- robust
- sturdy
