## 读音
/ɪmˈplaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 暗示，意味着。例如: "His silence implies that he agrees with us."
- **动词**: 推测，推断。例如: "From her expression, we can imply that she's angry."

## 短语
- "To imply something" 意味着表明或暗示一个观点或情况。
- "Implied meaning" 是指话中暗含的意思。

## 例句
- "Her smile implies that she is happy to see us."
- "The统计数据 imply that sales have increased this quarter."
