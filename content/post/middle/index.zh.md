## 读音
/mɪdˈdl/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 中间；中心。例如: "The middle of the road is dangerous to cross."
       2. (时间、年龄、顺序等的)中间阶段。例如: "She's in the middle of her teenage years."
- **形容词**: 1. 在中间的；中央的。例如: "The middle seat is free."
       2. 居中的；中级的。例如: "He was ranked middle in his class."

## 短语
- "in the middle of something" 意思是在某事进行中。例如: "I can't talk now, I'm in the middle of cooking dinner."
- "middle-aged" 用来描述中年的。例如: "Many middle-aged people start to consider their retirement plans."

## 例句
- 名词: "The child sat down on the bench, right in the middle."
- 形容词: "She wore a middle-aged man's suit for the costume party."
