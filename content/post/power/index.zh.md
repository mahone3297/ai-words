## 读音
/ˈpaʊər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
    - 能力或力量，可以是物理的、精神的或政治的。例如: "She has the power to change the situation."
    - 控制权或影响力。例如: "They hold significant power in the company."
    - 电能或动力。例如: "The generator supplies electricity, providing power to the whole house."
    - （尤指体育或音乐中的）强度或威力。例如: "The singer displayed great vocal power."

- **动词**:
    - 给…提供动力。例如: "This car is powered by a hybrid engine."
    - 掌握或控制。例如: "He powers through difficult decisions."

## 短语
- "In power" 指某人处于掌权或统治地位。
- "Power outage" 指电力中断或停电。
- "Power up" 意味着启动或增强能量。

## 例句
- 名词: "The country's economic power is based on its rich natural resources."
- 动词: "The solar panels power the remote cabin off the grid."
