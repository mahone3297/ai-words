## 读音
/sliːp/

## 释义
- **动词**: 睡觉，处于睡眠状态。例如: "I need to get a good night's sleep."
- **名词**: 睡眠的状态或行为。例如: "He had a deep sleep after a long day."

## 短语
- "Sleep well" 祝愿某人睡得好。
- "Sleep in" 指早晨起床晚，多睡一会儿。
- "Sleep over" 在别人家过夜。

## 例句
- 动词: "The baby finally fell asleep after hours of crying."
- 名词: "Getting enough sleep is crucial for your health."

## 同义词
- Rest
- Nap
- Doze
- Slumber
- Repose
