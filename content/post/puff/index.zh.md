## 读音
/pʌf/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 一团烟雾或气流。例如: "A puff of smoke rose from the chimney."
  - 轻轻一吹。例如: "He gave the dandelion a gentle puff and the seeds flew away."
  - 鼓起的部分或膨胀物。例如: "The pillow had a fluffy puff of feathers inside."

- **动词**:
  - 吹一口气。例如: "He puffed on his cigar to light it."
  - 快速而费力地呼吸。例如: "After running up the stairs, he was puffing heavily."

- **形容词**:
  - 自负的，自大的。例如: "She walked in with a pompous puff of self-importance."

## 短语
- "In a puff" 意为迅速，突然。例如: "The balloon disappeared in a puff of smoke."
- "Puff pastry" 是一种酥皮糕点。例如: "I made a delicious apple tart with puff pastry."

## 例句
- 名词: "The magician produced a puff of smoke and then vanished."
- 动词: "The child puffed out his cheeks and blew a big bubble."
- 形容词: "He was so full of himself, always talking in a puff manner."

## 同义词
- Breathe hard
- Exhale
- Inflate
- Blow
- Brag (只在形容词“自负的”意义下适用)
