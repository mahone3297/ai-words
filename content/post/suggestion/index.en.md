## Pronunciation
/həˈlɒʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
A term used to greet or address someone, often expressing friendliness or recognition. For example: "She waved a friendly hello at her neighbor."
### Verb
To express greeting or acknowledge someone's presence, typically by saying "hello." For example: "Please hello the guests as they arrive."
### Interjection
An expression of salutation or attention, typically used to start a conversation. For example: "Oh, hello! Long time no see."

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" = Greet someone
- "Say hello to your family" = Send a greeting to your family
- "Good morning, hello!" = A combination greeting for the day

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After a long day at work, the sight of her children's smiling hellos always lifted her spirits."
- **Verb**: "He greeted the postman with a hearty hello every morning."
- **Interjection**: "Hello, stranger! Can I help you find something?"
