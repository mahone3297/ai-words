## 读音
/ˈsʌdʒɛstʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**: 提议，建议，意见。例如: "I have a suggestion for improving our productivity."
- **动词**: 建议，提议。例如: "She suggested we try a new restaurant for dinner."

## 短语
- "Make a suggestion" 意思是提出一个想法或建议。
- "Take/accept a suggestion" 是接受某人的提议。

## 例句
- 名词: "The boss appreciated the employee's constructive suggestions."
- 动词: "The consultant suggested implementing a new software system."
