## 读音
/riˈkwaɪər/

## 释义
- **动词**: 请求；需要；要求。例如: "The job requires excellent communication skills."
- **名词**: 必需品；要求条件。例如: "The airline has specific requirements for checked luggage."

## 短语
- "require from sb." 意为从某人那里需要。例如: "The project requires input from all team members."
- "require of sb." 表示对某人的要求。例如: "The teacher requires high standards of behavior from her students."

## 例句
- 动词: "This software requires a minimum of 8 GB of RAM to run smoothly."
- 名词: "The immigration process has several requirements that need to be fulfilled."
