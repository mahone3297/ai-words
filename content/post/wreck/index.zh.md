## 读音
/wrɛk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 沉船；残骸；损坏的东西。例如: "The wreck of the old ship was discovered by divers."
- **动词**: 使沉没；毁坏；破坏。例如: "The storm wrecked several boats along the coast."
- **形容词**: 残破的；被毁的。例如: "The house was a wreck after the party."

## 短语
- "wreck havoc" 意为造成严重破坏。
- "survive a wreck" 表示在事故中幸存下来。

## 例句
- 名词: "The divers explored the ancient wreck at the bottom of the ocean."
- 动词: "The reckless driver nearly wrecked his car on the icy road."
- 形容词: "The once-beautiful building now stood as a wreck, abandoned and forgotten."
