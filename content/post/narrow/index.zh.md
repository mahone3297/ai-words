## 读音
/nəˈrɔː/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 狭窄的，不宽的。例如: "The road was so narrow that only one car could pass at a time."
- **动词**: 使变狭窄；限制。例如: "The government's policies have narrowed the gap between rich and poor."
- **名词**: 狭长的部分或区域。例如: "There is a narrow strip of land between the river and the forest."

## 短语
- "narrow escape" 表示勉强逃脱危险或不幸的情况。
- "narrow margin" 指微小的差距或优势。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The alley was so narrow that we had to walk single file."
- 动词: "The debate narrowed down to two main issues."
- 名词: "The ship sailed through the narrow channel safely."
