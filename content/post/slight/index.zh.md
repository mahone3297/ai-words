## 读音
/slaɪt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 轻微的，微小的，不明显的。例如: "She had only a slight headache."
- **副词**: 稍微，略微。例如: "He moved the picture slightly to the left."

## 短语
- "slight of hand" 意为巧妙的手法或魔术技巧。
- "a slight chance" 表示很小的可能性。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The damage to the car was only slight, so we didn't need to claim insurance."
- 副词: "He was slightly injured in the accident."

## 同义词
- Minor
- Tiny
- Small
- Barely
- Slightly (as an adverb)
