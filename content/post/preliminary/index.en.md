## Definitions
- **Adjective**: 1. Occurring before something else; preparatory. For example: "The preliminary meetings were held last week."
- **Noun**: 1. A preliminary stage or event. For example: "The preliminary exams are scheduled for next month."
- **Verb**: To prepare or make ready (usually in a formal sense). For example: "They preliminary discussed the agenda for the conference."

## Phrases
