## 读音
/ˈprɛlɪmɪnəri/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 预先的；初步的；预备的。例如: "We had a preliminary discussion before the main meeting."
- **名词**: 初步的事物；预备步骤。例如: "The preliminary exam is a test to determine if students are ready for the final."

## 短语
- "preliminary studies" 指预备性的研究或学习。
- "preliminary talks" 指开始阶段的谈判或讨论。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The preliminary results showed a positive trend."
- 名词: "They held preliminary meetings to plan the event."
