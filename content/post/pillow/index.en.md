## Pronunciation
/pɪˈloʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A soft, usually rectangular object, typically stuffed with feathers, foam, or other materials, used for supporting the head on a bed or chair. [Oxford](https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/pillow)
- **Adjective**: Relating to or providing comfort or support, especially for the head. [Cambridge](https://dictionary.cambridge.org/dictionary/english/pillow)

## Phrases
- "Use a pillow for support" indicates placing a pillow for added comfort or elevation.
- "A pillow case" is the cover for a pillow.
- "Fluffy pillow" refers to a pillow that is light and soft.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She adjusted the pillows on the couch for a more comfortable seating position."
- **Adjective**: "The hotel provided each guest with a luxurious feather pillow."
- "I sleep with two pillows, one under my head and one for extra support."

## Synonyms
- Cushion
- Pillowcase
- Supporter
- Nesting item
- Soft filler

## Antonyms
- Hard
- Uncomfortable
- Flat (in terms of providing support)
