## Pronunciation
/wɪˈdeɪɪŋ/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A ceremony where two people get married, usually followed by a celebration. Example: "The wedding was a beautiful event, filled with joy and love."
2. The act of exchanging vows and becoming legally married. Example: "They tied the knot in a small, intimate wedding ceremony."

### Verb
1. To have a wedding, or to celebrate a marriage. Example: "They are planning their wedding for next summer."
2. (informal) To unite in marriage, as in "They're getting married." 

### Adjective
Referring to the event or period surrounding a marriage ceremony. Example: "A wedding dress is typically white and elaborate for the wedding day."
