## 读音
/sɛɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 说出，表达，宣告。例如: "He said he would be back soon."
- **名词**: 说话的权利，发言权。例如: "Everyone should have a say in this decision."

## 短语
- "Say something" 意思是说出一些内容。
- "Have your say" 意味着让某人表达自己的意见。

## 例句
- 动词: "She said goodbye before leaving the room."
- 名词: "In a democracy, every citizen has a say in the government."

## 同义词
- Speak
- Tell
- Express
- Utter
- Announce
