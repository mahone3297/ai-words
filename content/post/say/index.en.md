## Pronunciation
/sə/ (US) /seɪ/ (UK)

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A statement or expression conveying information or thought**: The teacher asked the class to say their names.
- **A particular expression or manner of speaking**: He said something that surprised everyone.

### Verb
- **To express or communicate a message or idea**: She said goodbye before leaving the office.
- **To repeat after someone**: The children were asked to say the alphabet.

### Interjection
- **An exclamation to greet, call attention, or express emotion**: Hello, it's nice to meet you!
- **Used to introduce a statement or comment**: Say what you mean, please.

## Phrases
- "Say something" or "Say it again"
- "Say my name"
- "I don't understand, can you say that differently?"
- "I'll be back in a moment, say hello to the kids"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: The teacher asked each student to say their favorite color.
- **Verb**: He said he had forgotten his keys.
- **Interjection**: "Hello, how are you? It's been a while."
