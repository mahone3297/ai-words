## 读音
/sɜːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 尊称，用于对男士的正式称呼，尤其指有社会地位或职业地位的人。例如: "[Sir](/post/sir/), [may](/post/may/) I [help](/post/help/) [you](/post/you/) [with](/post/with/) [something](/post/something/)?"
- **敬辞**: 用于信件或对话中对不熟悉的人的尊称。例如: "[Dear](/post/dear/) [Sir](/post/sir/) [or](/post/or/) [Madam](/post/madam/),"

## 短语
- "[Sir](/post/sir/) John" 用来特指名为John的人，表示尊敬。

## 例句
- 名词: "[Sir](/post/sir/), [your](/post/your/) [table](/post/table/) is [ready](/post/ready/) [in](/post/in/) [the](/post/the/) [corner](/post/corner/)."
- 敬辞: "[Sir](/post/sir/), I [have](/post/have/) [a](/post/a/) [question](/post/question/) [about](/post/about/) [the](/post/the/) [contract](/post/contract/)."
