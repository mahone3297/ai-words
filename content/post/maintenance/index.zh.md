## 读音
/məˈtenəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 维护，保养，维修。指定期检查、修理或保持事物正常运行的状态。例如: "Regular car maintenance is essential for safety."
- **动词**: 维持，保持。例如: "The company maintains strict quality standards."

## 短语
- "Maintenance work" 指进行的保养或维修工作。
- "Building maintenance" 是指建筑物的保养和维修。
- "routine maintenance" 指常规的保养维护。

## 例句
- 名词: "The apartment complex provides free pool maintenance for its residents."
- 动词: "The old house requires constant maintenance to keep it in good condition."
