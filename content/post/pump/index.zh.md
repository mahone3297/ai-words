## 读音
/pʌmp/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种用来输送液体的机械装置，通常通过增加压力来工作。例如: "He installed a water pump in his garden."
- **动词**: 用泵抽送液体或气体。例如: "We need to pump the fuel into the car."
- **名词**: 运动鞋的高帮部分，有时也指鞋的整体。例如: "She was wearing a pair of stylish pumps with her dress."

## 短语
- "pump action" 指泵动式枪械的操作方式。
- "to pump up" 意为充气或增强某事物的能量或活力。例如: "I need to pump up the tires on my bike."
- "pump iron" 常用于指健身房里举重锻炼。

## 例句
- 名词: "The old-fashioned hand pump provided water for the village."
- 动词: "He started the car by pumping the accelerator a few times."
- 名词（关于鞋子）: "She wore elegant black pumps to the office."
