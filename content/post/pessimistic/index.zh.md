## 读音
/ˈpɛsɪmɪstɪk/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 表示悲观的，倾向于看到事物的不利方面。例如: "He has a pessimistic outlook on life."
- **副词**: 悲观地，消极地。例如: "She viewed the situation pessimistically."

## 短语
- "pessimistic about" 表示对某事持悲观态度。例如: "She was pessimistic about the chances of finding a job."
- "a pessimistic view" 指的是悲观的观点或看法。例如: "He held a pessimistic view of the future economy."

## 例句
- 形容词: "Despite his talents, he remained pessimistic about his career prospects."
- 副词: "They discussed the issue pessimistically, focusing on potential problems rather than solutions."
