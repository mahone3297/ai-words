## Pronunciation
/pɛˈsɪmɪstɪk/

## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Pessimistic** refers to a person who tends to have a gloomy or negative outlook on life, expecting the worst-case scenario to occur. It describes someone who believes that things are unlikely to turn out well.

## Phrases
- "A pessimistic attitude" denotes a consistently pessimistic viewpoint.
- "Displaying pessimism" shows a tendency to focus on the negative aspects.
- "Be pessimistic about" expresses doubt or concern about a particular outcome.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: She's known for her pessimistic view on the project's chances of success.
- **Adverb**: He always approaches life with a somewhat pessimistic demeanor.
- **Noun**: The coach's pessimistic assessment dampened the team's morale.
