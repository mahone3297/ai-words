## 读音
/taɪrɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 疲倦的，累的。例如: "I feel so tired after a long day at work."
- **动词**: 使（某人）感到疲倦。例如: "The long hike tired us out."

## 短语
- "Feel tired" 意味着感到疲劳。
- "Tired out" 表示极度疲劳。
- "Get tired of" 意为对某事感到厌倦。

## 例句
- 形容词: "I'm too tired to go out tonight, let's stay in."
- 动词: "The babies tired their mother by crying all night."

## 同义词
- Exhausted
- Fatigued
- Weary
- Drained
- Run-down
