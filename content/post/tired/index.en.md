## Definitions
### Adjective
- **Meaning**: Feeling physically or mentally exhausted due to lack of rest or effort.
- **Usage**: Describes a state of being weary or fatigued after exertion or continuous activity.

### Verb
- **Meaning**: To become tired or feel weary, usually as a result of physical or mental exertion.
- **Usage**: Refers to the act of experiencing fatigue or a decline in energy levels.
