## 读音
/pərˈsweɪd/

## 释义
- **动词**: 说服，劝服；使相信。例如: "He tried to persuade her to go with him."
- **名词**: 说服力，劝诱。例如: "Her argument had great persuade."

## 短语
- "persuade sb. to do sth." 意为说服某人做某事。
- "be persuaded of" 表示被说服相信某事。

## 例句
- 动词: "I managed to persuade my friend to join the gym with me."
- 名词: "His convincing arguments were full of persuade."
