## Pronunciation
/un/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A celestial body, the star at the center of the solar system, providing light and heat. **[countable]** For example: "The sun was shining brightly today."
- **Noun**: A source of warmth, energy, or optimism. **[mass noun]** For example: "The sun is a symbol of hope."
- **Verb**: To emit or reflect light and heat. **[transitive verb]** For example: "The sun rises in the east."
- **Adjective**: Bright and radiant. **[attributive adjective]** For example: "The sunlit fields looked inviting."

## Phrases
- "Bask in the sun" means to enjoy warm sunlight.
- "Under the sun" implies no limit or everywhere. For example: "There's no limit to what can happen under the sun."
- "As the sun sets" signifies the end of the day. For example: "As the sun set, we packed up our picnic."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The sunflowers turned their faces towards the sun."
- **Verb**: "The plants need sunlight to grow."
- **Adjective**: "The sun-drenched beach was a popular spot for vacations."
