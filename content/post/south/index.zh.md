## 读音
/saʊθ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 地理上的南方；南方地区。例如: "They moved from the north to the south for warmer weather."
- **形容词**: 南方的；朝南的。例如: "The south-facing window gets plenty of sunlight."

## 短语
- "Southern states" 指美国的南部各州。
- "South Pole" 南极点。
- "Go south" 有时表示事情变得糟糕或计划失败。例如: "Our plans really went south when it started raining."

## 例句
- 名词: "The southern part of the country has a tropical climate."
- 形容词: "The house has a south-facing garden where we love to sit in the summer."

## 同义词
- Southern
- Southeast
- Southward
- Southwest
- Down south (主要指美国)
