## Pronunciation
/saʊθ/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Designating the direction or position on the Earth's surface lying south of the equator or of a particular reference point. For example: "We traveled south to escape the cold weather."
- **Noun**: The southern direction or region; the part of a country or continent that lies south of a particular line or latitude. For example: "The Amazon rainforest is located in the southern hemisphere."
- **Preposition**: In or toward the south. For example: "The house is situated south of the city center."
- **Adverb**: In a southern direction or manner. For example: "She moved southward, following the sun."

## Phrases
- "Head south" means to travel in the southern direction.
- "South of the border" refers to an area beyond a country's southern boundary.
- "A journey south" denotes a trip towards the southern part of a location.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The house faces south, allowing plenty of sunlight."
