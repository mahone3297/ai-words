## 读音
/ˈwɜːrʃɪp/

## 释义
- **动词**: 尊敬；崇拜；敬拜。例如: "They worship the gods in their temples."
- **名词**: 敬拜；礼拜；崇敬的行为。例如: "The act of worship includes singing and prayers."

## 短语
- "Worship service" 指宗教礼拜仪式。
- "Worship leader" 在宗教活动中带领敬拜的人。

## 例句
- 动词: "The villagers gather every Sunday to worship at the local church."
- 名词: "The cathedral was filled with people for the morning worship."
