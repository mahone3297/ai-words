## 读音
/ˈnjuːzpeɪpər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种定期出版的印刷品，通常包含新闻、文章、广告和评论，以纸质形式分发。例如: "I like to read the newspaper over breakfast."
- **名词**: 电子版的新闻报道或在线新闻网站。例如: "Many people now get their news from online newspapers."

## 短语
- "Headline in the newspaper" 报纸上的标题。
- "Front-page news" 封面新闻，重大事件。
- "The Sunday newspaper" 周日版报纸，通常比平日版更厚，包含更多特辑和增刊。

## 例句
- 名词: "She subscribes to the daily newspaper to stay informed about current events."
- 名词: "There was an interesting article on renewable energy in today's newspaper."
