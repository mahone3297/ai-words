## 读音
/ˌsʌðˈeast/

## 释义
- **名词**: 地理方向之一，指东南方。例如: "They live in a house located in the southeast corner of the city."
- **形容词**: 指东南方的或来自东南方的。例如: "The southeast wind brought warm air from the tropics."

## 短语
- "Southeast Asia" 指东南亚地区，包括多个国家。
- "Southeastern United States" 指美国的东南部地区。

## 例句
- 名词: "The sun rises in the east and sets in the west, but in summer it's higher in the sky when it reaches the southeast."
- 形容词: "The southeast coast of Australia is known for its beautiful beaches."
