## Pronunciation
/rəp/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A type of packaging material, typically plastic or paper, used to cover or secure items, often with an adhesive strip.
- The act of completely covering or enclosing something, usually by folding or bending.
- A manner or style of presenting information or ideas.
- (informal) A person's outer appearance or style.

### Verb
- To cover or fasten something using a wrapping material.
- To prepare or package something, typically food, for transport or storage.
- To present or express something in a particular way, as in a story or performance.

## Phrases
- "Wrap a gift" means to cover a present with a decorative wrapper.
- "Food wrap" refers to eco-friendly alternatives to plastic wrap, like beeswax wraps.
- "Wrap up" can mean to finish a task or discussion, or to bundle something securely.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She carefully wrapped the fragile vase in bubble wrap before sending it."
- **Verb**: "I'll wrap the sandwiches in foil to keep them fresh until lunchtime."
- **Verb**: "The presentation wrapped up with a lively Q&A session."
