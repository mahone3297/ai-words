## 读音
/wræp/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 包裹，缠绕。例如: "She wrapped the baby in a soft blanket."
2. 将…包裹在（纸、布等）中。例如: "I wrapped the present in festive paper."
3. （电影、电视）杀青，完成拍摄。例如: "We've wrapped the shooting for today."

- **名词**:
1. 包裹，包装纸。例如: "He put the gift in a box and added a fancy wrap."
2. 外套，外衣。例如: "She wore a light wrap against the evening chill."

## 短语
- "wrap up" 意为完成，结束。例如: "We need to wrap up this meeting soon."
- "wrap around" 描述某物环绕另一物。例如: "The vine had wrapped around the tree trunk."

## 例句
- 动词: "She carefully wrapped the fragile vase in bubble wrap."
- 名词: "He used a colorful wrap to make the gift look more attractive."
