## 读音
/nərˈθərn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 指位于北方的，与北有关的。例如: "The northern hemisphere experiences winter in December."
- **名词**: 有时指来自北方的人。例如: "People from the northern regions have adapted to colder climates."

## 短语
- "Northern lights" 指北极光，一种在高纬度地区出现的自然现象。
- "Northern region" 可以指一个地方的北部区域。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The northern part of the country has a colder climate than the south."
- 名词: "He's a northern native, so he's used to snowy winters."
