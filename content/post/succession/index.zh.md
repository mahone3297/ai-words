## 读音
/səkˈsɛʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 连续发生的一系列事件或状态；接替；继承。例如: "The succession of power in the kingdom was well planned."
- **名词**: 一系列同类事物的相继出现。例如: "A succession of storms caused flooding in the region."
- **名词**: （家族、公司等的）继承人顺序。例如: "The succession to the throne is determined by birth order."

## 短语
- "succession planning" 在企业管理中指制定接班人计划。
- "the royal succession" 指王位的继承顺序。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company's succession plan ensures a smooth transition of leadership."
- 名词: "The succession of droughts has severely affected agriculture in the area."
