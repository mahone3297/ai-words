## 读音
/bæɡɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   - 行李，包裹，通常指旅行者携带的物品。例如: "Please check your baggage before boarding the plane."
   - 额外负担，心理负担，指精神或情感上的问题。例如: "She had to deal with a lot of emotional baggage from her past."

## 短语
- "Check baggage" 意为托运行李。
- "Excess baggage" 指超出航空公司规定的免费行李额度。
- "Baggage claim" 是机场中领取行李的地方。

## 例句
- 名词: "Make sure you have all your baggage before leaving the airport."
- "His fear of commitment was a significant baggage in their relationship."

## 同义词
- Luggage
- Suitcase
- Bag
- Trunk
- Cargo (在某些上下文中)
