## 读音
/huːz/

## 释义
- **代词**: 谁的，属于谁的。用来询问或提及所有者。例如: "[Whose](/zh/post/whose/) [book](/zh/post/book/) is [this](/zh/post/this/)?"，"I [found](/zh/post/found/) [a](/zh/post/a/) [wallet](/zh/post/wallet/); [whose](/zh/post/whose/) [could](/zh/post/could/) [it](/zh/post/it/) [be](/zh/post/be/)?"
- **关系代词**: 在定语从句中引导从句，指代人或物的所有关系。例如: "[This](/zh/post/this/) is [the](/zh/post/the/) [house](/zh/post/house/) [whose](/zh/post/whose/) windows are painted [blue](/zh/post/blue/)."

## 短语
- "[Whose](/zh/post/whose/) [responsibility](/zh/post/responsibility/)" 指的是谁的责任或归属。
- "[Whose](/zh/post/whose/) [turn](/zh/post/turn/) is [it](/zh/post/it/)?" 用来询问轮到谁了。
