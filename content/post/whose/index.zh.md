## 读音
/huːz/

## 释义
- **代词**: 谁的，属于谁的。用来询问或提及所有者。例如: "Whose book is this?"，"I found a wallet; whose could it be?"
- **关系代词**: 在定语从句中引导从句，指代人或物的所有关系。例如: "This is the house whose windows are painted blue."

## 短语
- "Whose responsibility" 指的是谁的责任或归属。
- "Whose turn is it?" 用来询问轮到谁了。

## 例句
- 代词: "Whose car is parked outside? It's blocking my driveway."
- 关系代词: "She's the woman whose paintings are on display in the gallery."

## 同义词
- Who's (informal, contraction of "who is")
- Of whom (formal)
