## 读音
/ˈnɑːlɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
知识，了解，认识。指通过学习、经验或理解获得的信息和理解力。
    - 例句: "He has a vast knowledge of history."
  
- **形容词 adj**:
知识渊博的，有见识的。形容人对某一主题有深入的理解或丰富的经验。
    - 例句: "She is a knowledgeable guide about local wildlife."

## 短语
- "acquire knowledge" 获得知识
- "apply knowledge" 运用知识
- "the knowledge gap" 知识差距

## 例句
- 名词: "The book imparts valuable knowledge on gardening techniques."
- 形容词: "A knowledgeable teacher can explain complex concepts clearly."
