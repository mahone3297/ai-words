## 读音
/ˈɔːrɡənaɪz/

## 释义
- **动词**: 把…组织起来；安排；规划。例如: "We need to organize a meeting for next week."
- **名词**: 组织；团体；机构。例如: "The company has several departments, each with its own organize."

## 短语
- "organize a event" 意为组织一个活动。
- "get organized" 意味着变得有条理或准备就绪。
- "in (the) organization" 通常指在某个机构或团体中。

## 例句
- 动词: "She's in charge of organizing the annual charity fundraiser."
- 名词: "The sports club is a well-organized group with clear goals and objectives."
