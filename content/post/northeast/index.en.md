## Word: Northeast

## Pronunciation
/ˌnɔːθ ˈiːst/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Situated in, moving toward, or facing the direction between north and east; the compass point that is one quarter of the way round from due north. Example: "The sun rises in the northeast."

### Noun
- A point, direction, or area lying towards the northeast. Example: "The storm was moving in a northeast direction."
- A region or area located in the northeastern part of a country or continent. Example: "Northeast China has a rich cultural heritage."

## Phrases
- "Northeast by east": A specific compass point slightly closer to east than northeast.
- "Drive northeast on the highway": To move in the direction between north and east.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The wind was blowing from the northeast, bringing colder air with it."
- **Noun**: "We took a trip to the beautiful northeast coast last summer."

## Synonyms
- NNE (North-Northeast)
- Northeastern
- Northeasterly (as an adjective for direction)
- In geographical contexts, may be synonymous with regions such as "upper east," "northeastern part," etc., depending on the location being referred to.
