## 读音

## 释义
- **形容词**: 东北的，指方向或地理位置。例如: "They live in a house located in the northeast corner of the city."
- **名词**: 东北部，指一个地区的东北部分。例如: "The northeastern states of the US are known for their cold winters."

## 短语
- "Northeasterner" 指来自东北地区的人。
- "Northeast region" 指某个国家或地区的东北部区域。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The wind was blowing from the northeast, bringing cool air with it."
- 名词: "The company has offices in both the southeast and northeast."
