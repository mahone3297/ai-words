## 读音
/twɪŋk(ə)l/

## 释义
- **动词**: 闪烁；闪耀。例如: "The stars twinkled in the clear night sky."
- **名词**: (尤指眼睛的)闪烁，闪耀。例如: "Her eyes twinkled with amusement."

## 短语
- "twinkle in one's eye" 指某人心中的计划或秘密喜悦。例如: "There was a mischievous twinkle in his eye when he suggested the prank."
- "twinkle, twinkle, little star" 是一首著名的儿童歌曲。

## 例句
- 动词: "The Christmas lights on the tree twinkled merrily."
- 名词: "There was a twinkle in her eye as she told the joke."
