## Pronunciation
/smɒg/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A noun referring to a mixture of smoke and fog, particularly from industrial pollution, that hovers over urban areas. 
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of or caused by smog, having a dark, hazy appearance due to air pollution. 

## Phrases
- "Suffering from smog" denotes poor air quality due to pollution.
- "Clearing the smog" means removing or reducing the haze caused by pollutants.
- "A day with heavy smog" refers to a day with poor visibility due to atmospheric pollution.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The city was blanketed in a thick smog, making it difficult to see the skyline."
- **Adjective**: "The smoggy weather made it hard to enjoy the view from the rooftop."
- "The government implemented measures to combat the persistent smog problem."
