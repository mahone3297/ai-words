## 读音
/ˈpeɪmənt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 付款；支付。指支付金钱或其他形式的偿付。例如: "I made the final payment on my car yesterday."
- **动词**: 支付；结算。表示付清款项的行为。例如: "She will payment the bill when it arrives."

## 短语
- "Payment plan" 指分期付款计划。
- "Upfront payment" 指预付款或首付。
- "Cash payment" 指现金支付。

## 例句
- 名词: "Please confirm your payment details before submitting the order."
- 动词: "He promised to payment the debt as soon as possible."
