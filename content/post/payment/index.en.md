## Pronunciation
/pəyˈmənt/

## Definitions
### Noun
A payment is a **noun** that refers to the act of giving or transferring money, goods, or services in exchange for a product, service, or settlement of a debt. It can also be a sum of money paid regularly, such as rent or salary.

### Verb
To make a payment is a **verb** that involves the act of settling a financial obligation, whether it's a one-time transaction or a recurring installment.

### Adjective
In some cases, "payment" can function as an **adjective** to describe a specific type of transaction, like a "payment plan" or a "payment method."

## Phrases
- "Make a payment on a loan"
- "Payment in full"
- "Late payment fees"
- "Cash payment" or "Credit card payment"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The customer made a payment of $500 to cover the damages."
- **Verb**: "I need to submit my payment by Friday to avoid any late fees."
- **Adjective**: "Many businesses now accept various payment methods, including digital payments."
