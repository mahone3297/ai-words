## 读音
/top/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 最高点，顶端：The top of the mountain offers a breathtaking view.
   - 最重要的位置：She's climbed to the top of her career.
   - 最佳状态：The team is playing at the top of their game.

- **形容词**:
   - 最好的，顶级的：This is the top restaurant in town.
   - 最高的：The top floor has a panoramic view.

- **动词**:
   - 超过，超越：His new score topped his previous record.
   - 放在顶部：She topped the cake with whipped cream.

## 短语
- "at the top of" 表示在...的顶部：The printer is at the top of the shelf.
- "on top of" 表示在...之上，除了...之外：She had to work overtime on top of her regular hours.
- "top something off" 填满，加满：Can you top off my coffee, please?

## 例句
- 名词: "We reached the top of the Eiffel Tower."
- 形容词: "The top chef will be demonstrating his cooking skills."
- 动词: "Her performance topped all expectations."
