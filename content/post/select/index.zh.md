## 读音
/ˈsɛlɪkt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 选择，挑选。例如: "Please select your favorite color from the options provided."
  - 指定，选定。例如: "The manager selected him for the project because of his expertise."
  - (计算机)在屏幕上选取或激活。例如: "Click on the file and select 'Open' to view its contents."

## 短语
- "select from" 从...中选择。例如: "I need to select a book from the library's extensive collection."
- "selected group" 精选群体。例如: "She was part of a highly selected group of scientists working on the project."

## 例句
- "She carefully selected each ingredient for her homemade recipe."
- "The customer selected the red car as his preferred option."
- "In the software, you can select the text and copy it to the clipboard."
