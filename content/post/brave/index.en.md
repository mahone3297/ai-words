## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Displaying courage and fearlessness in the face of danger or difficulty. For example: "The soldier was brave during the battle."
- **Noun**: A brave person; someone who is courageous. For example: "She's known as a real-life hero, a true bravado."

## Phrases
- "Be brave" encourages someone to show courage.
- "She acted with bravery in the emergency."
- "His bravery saved many lives."
