## Pronunciation
/səˈdʒest/

## Definitions
### Noun
A suggestion is an idea or proposal put forward for consideration or action. It may be based on experience, expertise, or intuition. For example: "The manager asked for suggestions on how to improve efficiency."

### Verb
To suggest means to propose, recommend, or indicate an idea or course of action. For example: "He suggested we take a break after the long meeting."

### Adjective
Suggestive implies having a hint or implication that something else might be true or desirable without being directly stated. For example: "The lighting in the room was quite suggestive."

## Phrases
- "Make a suggestion" means to propose an idea or recommendation.
- "I second your suggestion" indicates agreement with a previous proposal.
- "Suggestion box" is a container where people can submit their ideas anonymously.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her suggestion for a new project was well-received by the team."
- **Verb**: "He suggested we meet at the park instead of the office."
- **Adjective**: "The music was so soothing, it had a highly suggestive quality."
