## 读音
/səˈdʒest/

## 释义
- **动词**: 提出，建议。例如: "He suggested going for a walk after dinner."
- **名词**: 建议，提议。例如: "I have a suggestion that might help with the problem."

## 短语
- "make a suggestion" 提出一个建议。
- "take someone's suggestion" 采纳某人的建议。
- "reject a suggestion" 拒绝一个建议。

## 例句
- 动词: "She suggested we should start our project earlier."
- 名词: "The manager listened to all the suggestions before making a decision."
