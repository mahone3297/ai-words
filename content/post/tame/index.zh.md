## 读音
/təm/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 温顺的，驯服的。例如: "The tiger had been tamed after years of training."
- **动词**: 驯服，使温顺。例如: "It took a lot of effort to tame the wild horse."
- **名词**: (常用于复数形式tames) 驯养的动物，尤指马戏团中的动物。例如: "The circus had a group of tames performing tricks."

## 短语
- "Tame an animal" 意味着将野生动物训练成温顺的宠物或工作动物。
- "Tame one's temper" 指的是控制或抑制自己的脾气。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The once fierce lion was now completely tame in the presence of its trainer."
- 动词: "With patience and kindness, he managed to tame the aggressive dog."
- 名词: "The circus featured tames such as elephants and lions performing incredible stunts."

- Subdued
