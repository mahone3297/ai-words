## 读音
/əˈkɜːr/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 发生；出现。例如: "An interesting idea occurred to her."
  - 经历；遭遇。例如: "He never expected to occur such an event in his life."

## 短语
- "occur to someone" 意味着某人想到或意识到某事。例如: "It didn't occur to him that he had forgotten his keys."
- "occurring at regular intervals" 表示定期发生。例如: "The meetings occur every Monday."

## 例句
- "The accident occurred at a busy intersection."
- "It suddenly occurred to me that we hadn't seen John all day."
- "The festival occurs annually in the summer."
