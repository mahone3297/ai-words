## 读音
/ˈlaɪtn/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  - 使变轻；减轻：例如 "Exercising regularly can lighten your load and improve your mood."
  - 减轻（颜色）；提亮：例如 "Adding a touch of white paint can lighten the shade of blue."
  - 加快；轻松处理：例如 "Let me help you with that; it will lighten your workload."

## 短语
- "lighten up" 意为放松，别那么严肃：例如 "You need to lighten up – it's just a joke!"
- "to lighten one's burden" 意为缓解负担：例如 "Her friends helped her lighten her burden during a difficult time."

## 例句
- 动词: "The new diet has helped me lighten my physical weight."
- 动词: "A few jokes helped to lighten the atmosphere during the meeting."
- 动词: "She asked him to lighten her load by taking on some of her responsibilities."
