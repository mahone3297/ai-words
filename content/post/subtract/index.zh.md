## 读音
/ˈsʌbtrækt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 减去，从一个数中减掉另一个数。例如: "Subtract 5 from 10, and you get 5."
- **名词**: 减法运算。例如: "In this math problem, we need to perform a subtract."

## 短语
- "subtract A from B" 意味着从B中减去A。
- "The difference after subtracting" 指的是减法运算后的结果。

## 例句
- 动词: "If you subtract 3 from 7, you're left with 4."
- 名词: "The subtract operation is fundamental in arithmetic."
