## 读音
/prəˈbləm/

## 释义
- **名词**: 难题，问题。指需要解决或思考的困难情况或疑问。例如: "The math problem took her hours to solve."
- **名词**: 问题，困扰。指生活中的烦恼或挑战。例如: "His health has been a constant problem."
- **名词**: 引起争论或讨论的议题。例如: "The problem of climate change is discussed worldwide."

## 短语
- "solve a problem" 解决问题。
- "problem-solving" 问题解决能力或过程。
- "problem child" 行为问题或难管教的孩子。

## 例句
- 名词: "We need to find a solution to this problem before it gets worse."
- 名词: "His laziness became a problem for his parents."
- 名词: "The issue of poverty is a major problem in many countries."
