## 读音
/θiːf/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个偷窃他人财物的人；小偷。例如: "The thief was caught by the police."
- **动词**: 偷窃；盗取。例如: "He thiefed a valuable painting from the museum."

## 短语
- "Thief in the night" 指在夜晚行窃的小偷，也可比喻突然而来的灾难。
- "Thief-proof" 意为防贼的，防盗的。

## 例句
- 名词: "The security camera caught the thief in the act."
- 动词: "She accused him of thiefing her wallet."
