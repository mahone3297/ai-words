## 读音
/ˈpleʒər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 愉快的感觉，快乐，享受。例如: "She derived great pleasure from painting."
- **动词**: 使感到愉快，给...带来快乐。例如: "The concert pleased the audience with its wonderful performance."

## 短语
- "take pleasure in" 喜欢做某事，乐在其中。例如: "She takes pleasure in reading books."
- "at your pleasure" 随你的便，按你的意愿。例如: "Do as you please; it's your decision."

## 例句
- 名词: "The pleasure of spending time with friends is incomparable."
- 动词: "His jokes always please the crowd."
- 连词: "It's a pleasure to meet you."

- Delight
- Satisfaction
