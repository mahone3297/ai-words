## 读音
/feɪz/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 阶段，时期：A distinct period or stage in a process, development, or series of events. 例如: "The project is in its planning phase."
2. 物理学：相位，波的相位：A particular point in the cycle of a periodic phenomenon, such as a wave. 例如: "The two sound waves were out of phase."

- **动词 v**:
1. 进行分期，逐步实施：To introduce or implement gradually or in stages. 例如: "The new policy will be phased in over the next six months."

## 短语
- "in phase" 表示同步，相位相同。
- "out of phase" 表示不同步，相位相反。

## 例句
- 名词: "The project has entered a new phase, requiring more detailed analysis."
- 动词: "The company is phasing out the old product line to make way for the new one."
