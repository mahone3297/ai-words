## 读音
/ˈsaɪlər/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   船员；水手。指在船上工作的人，尤其是海军或商业航海中的人员。
   例如: "The sailor was busy tying ropes on the deck."

## 短语
- "at sea with a sailor" 指在海上与船员一起，可能暗示经历冒险或不稳定的情况。
- "sailor's knot" 指水手结，一种结实的绳结，常用于航海。

## 例句
- "My grandfather was a sailor and traveled the world on merchant ships."
- "The young sailor was learning the ropes from the experienced crew members."

## 同义词
- Seaman
- Mariner
- Nautical worker
- Deckhand
- Boatswain
