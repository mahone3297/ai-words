## 读音
/ˈvɜːrb/

## 释义
- **名词**: 表示动作或状态的词，通常用在句子中表示行为或发生的事情。例如: "[The](/post/the/) [verb](/post/verb/) '[run](/post/run/)' is [in](/post/in/) [the](/post/the/) [present](/post/present/) [tense](/post/tense/)."
- **动词**: 执行动作或表示状态的行为。例如: "[She](/post/she/) verbs [her](/post/her/) ideas [into](/post/into/) [reality](/post/reality/)."

## 短语
- "[Verb](/post/verb/) [tense](/post/tense/)" 表示动词的时间，如现在时、过去时或将来时。
- "Transitive [verb](/post/verb/)" 是需要直接宾语的动词，如 "I [saw](/post/saw/) [the](/post/the/) [movie](/post/movie/)."
- "Intransitive [verb](/post/verb/)" 不需要直接宾语的动词，如 "[She](/post/she/) slept."

## 例句
- 名词: "[The](/post/the/) [verb](/post/verb/) '[eat](/post/eat/)' is [a](/post/a/) [regular](/post/regular/) [verb](/post/verb/) [in](/post/in/) English."
