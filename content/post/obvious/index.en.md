## Word: Obvious

## Pronunciation
/ˈɑːbviəs/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Evident to the eye or mind; clearly apparent or understood without any need for special effort or explanation. For example: "The solution to the problem was obvious once he explained it."
  
  > *It's obvious that she's unhappy.*
  > *The house stood out like an obvious landmark.*

- **Adjective (Law)**: Open to view or knowledge; not concealed or hidden. For instance: "The evidence was obvious and could not be disputed."

  > *The obvious intent of the law was to prevent such activities.*

## Phrases
- "As obvious as the nose on your face": An idiomatic expression used to describe something that is very clear or easily noticeable.
- "It goes without saying / It's obvious that...": Expressions indicating something is so self-evident it doesn't need to be explicitly stated.
- "The obvious choice": Referring to the most logical, clear, or suitable option.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "His dislike for spicy food was obvious from his reaction after taking a bite."
- **Adjective (Law)**: "The suspect's guilt was made obvious by the security camera footage."
