## 读音
/ʃeɪd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 阴影；阴凉处。例如: "She sat in the shade of the tree."
  - 色调；色度。例如: "The painting has many subtle shades of blue."
  - 类型；种类。例如: "There are various shades of opinion on this issue."

- **动词**:
  - 给...投下阴影。例如: "The sun shaded her eyes from the bright light."
  - 变暗；减弱。例如: "The lights dimmed, shading the room into darkness."

## 短语
- "throw (cast) a shadow" 意为投射阴影。
- "shade one's eyes" 指遮挡眼睛以防止强光刺眼。

## 例句
- 名词: "The building cast a long shade across the square as the sun set."
- 动词: "The curtains were drawn to shade the room from the afternoon sun."

## 同义词
- Shadow
- Darkness
- Dimness
- Tone
- Coloration
