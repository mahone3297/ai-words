## Pronunciation
/həˈloʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to greet or address someone; a friendly expression of recognition. For example: "He offered a friendly hello to his neighbor."
- **Verb**: To express greeting or acknowledge someone's presence; to initiate communication. For example: "She greeted the guests with a hearty hello."
- **Adverb**: Expressing a sense of familiarity or friendliness when greeting. For example: "She said hello warmly."

## Phrases
- "Hello, sunshine!" is a cheerful greeting for the day.
- "Hello, how are you?" is a common way to inquire about someone's well-being.
- "Hello, and welcome!" expresses hospitality to newcomers.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The children waved hello to their teacher as they arrived at school."
- **Verb**: "After a long day, it felt good to hear a familiar hello from my partner."
- **Adverb**: "She said hello softly, not wanting to disturb the sleeping baby."
