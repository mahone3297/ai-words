## Pronunciation
/subˈɜːrb/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A residential area that is located on the outskirts of a city or town, typically consisting of houses and smaller communities.
2. The suburban lifestyle, characterized by detached houses, lower population density, and a quieter atmosphere compared to urban centers.

### Verb
1. To live or be situated in a suburb.
2. To refer to or describe an area as suburban.

## Phrases
- "Move to the suburbs" means to relocate to a residential area outside the city center.
- "Suburban sprawl" describes uncontrolled expansion of suburban development.
- "Suburbanite" is a term for someone who lives in the suburbs.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Many families prefer the quiet suburbs over the noisy city life."
- **Verb**: "After years in the city, they decided to move to the suburbs for more space."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "The government is trying to combat suburban sprawl by promoting public transportation."
