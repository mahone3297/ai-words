## 读音
/ˈsʌbɜːrb/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   市郊；郊区。例如: "The city's suburbs sprawl for miles."
   居民区；住宅区。例如: "They moved to a quiet suburb with good schools."

## 短语
- "suburban life" 市郊生活
- "suburban area" 郊区地带
- "suburban sprawl" 过度蔓延的郊区发展

## 例句
- 名词: "Many people prefer the peacefulness of suburban living over the hustle and bustle of the city."
- "The new shopping mall is located in the heart of the suburb."
