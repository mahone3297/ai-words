## Pronunciation
/world/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. A planet Earth and all its inhabitants, flora, and fauna, considered as a single entity. *The world is a diverse place with many cultures.*
2. The global community, collectively. *The world economy is interconnected.*
3. A particular region or sphere of activity, often used to refer to a field of interest or expertise. *He's an expert in the world of finance.*

### Verb
1. To perceive or consider (something) as a whole. *To marvel at the wonders of the world.*
2. To be aware or cognizant of something on a global scale. *She wants to make the world a better place.*

### Adjective
1. Concerning or involving the entire globe or all people. *Global warming is a world issue.*
2. Characteristic of a particular place or culture. *A world-famous landmark.*

### Adverb
1. Throughout the world; universally. *The news spread worldwide.*
