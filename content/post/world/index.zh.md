## 读音
/wɜːrld/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
  - 全球；地球： "We live on a small planet called Earth, also known as the world."
  - 人类社会；世间： "The world of politics is full of surprises."
  - 领域；范围： "She's made a name for herself in the fashion world."

- **形容词 adj**:
  - 世间的；世俗的： "Worldly possessions often lead people astray."
  - 全球性的；影响全世界的： "Climate change is a world problem that requires global cooperation."

## 短语
- "around the world" 意为环游世界。
- "world famous" 表示全球知名。
- "a world of difference" 意为有很大差别。

## 例句
- 名词: "Explorers have traveled to every corner of the world."
- 形容词: "He had a world-weary look after years of traveling."
