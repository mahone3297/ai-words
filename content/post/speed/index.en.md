## Pronunciation
/speɪd/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Speed**: 1. The rate of movement, typically measured in units like miles per hour (mph) or kilometers per hour (km/h). For example: "The car reached a top speed of 200 mph."
- 2. The velocity or swiftness of something, often implying efficiency or performance. For example: "The laptop has a fast boot-up speed."

### Verb
- **To speed**: 1. To increase the rate of movement or travel. For example: "The driver sped up to catch up with the traffic."
- 2. (informal) To administer a drug or substance that increases one's energy or alertness. For example: "She took a caffeine boost to speed through her work."

### Adjective
- **Fast**: Describing high rates of movement or action. For example: "The cheetah is the fastest land animal."

### Adverb
- **Swiftly**: Expressing rapid movement or action. For example: "The storm passed swiftly."

## Phrases
- "Speed limit" refers to the maximum allowed speed on a road or highway.
- "Speed dating" is a structured event where people meet multiple potential partners in a short time.
- "Speed reading" is a technique to quickly scan and comprehend written material.
