## 读音
/ɪnˈvent/

## 释义
- **动词**: 创造，发明。例如: "He invented a new type of engine."
- **名词**: 发明物，创新。例如: "Her latest invention has revolutionized the industry."

## 短语
- "invent something from scratch" 从零开始创造或发明。例如: "They had to invent the whole system from scratch."
- "to be the inventor of something" 意为某物的发明者。例如: "Alexander Graham Bell is credited as the inventor of the telephone."

## 例句
- 动词: "Thomas Edison invented the light bulb after many years of experimentation."
- 名词: "The invention of the printing press changed the course of history."
