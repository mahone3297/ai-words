## Word: Occasionally

## Pronunciation
/ˌɑːkənˈsɛrəli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: From time to time; now and then; not often or regularly. It describes the frequency or occurrence of an action.

### Examples
- **Adverb**: "Occasionally, he enjoys going for long walks in the park."
- "I like to treat myself to a fancy dinner occasionally."
- "She checks her emails only occasionally on the weekends."

## Phrases
- "Occasionally used" refers to something that is not frequently or habitually used.
- "From time to time" is synonymous with "occasionally"; it means something happens irregularly but not never.
- "On occasion" also means the same thing, referring to something happening at certain times but not regularly.

## Example Sentences
- "He occasionally visits his hometown to see his parents."
- "The restaurant offers live music performances occasionally."
- "We should all take breaks from work occasionally to refresh our minds."
