## 读音
/əˈkeɪʒənəli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 偶尔，有时候，不时地。表示动作或情况不是经常发生，而是间歇性的。

## 短语
- "occasionally do something" 意为偶尔做某事。
- "something occurs occasionally" 意为某事偶尔发生。

## 例句
- "I occasionally visit my grandparents during weekends."
- "The weather is occasionally sunny in this region, despite its reputation for rain."
- "She occasionally works from home when she needs to take care of her child."
