## 读音
/comˈpoʊz/

## 释义
- **动词**: 组成，构成。创作（音乐、诗歌、文章等）。例如: "He composed a beautiful symphony."
- **名词**: 作品，作曲。例如: "The composer's latest work is a piano composition."

## 短语
- "compose oneself" 平静下来，镇定自若。例如: "Take a deep breath and compose yourself before going on stage."
- "to be composed of" 由...组成。例如: "The atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen and oxygen."

## 例句
- 动词: "She spent hours composing a heartfelt letter to her friend."
- 名词: "The musician's performance included several original compositions."
