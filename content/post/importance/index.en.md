## Pronunciation
/ɪmˈpɔːrtns/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The quality or state of being important, significance, or having great value or consequence. For example: "The importance of education cannot be overstated."
  - _noun (countable)_ : Something that is important or has great significance. Example: "Conservation is one of the key importances in maintaining biodiversity."

## Phrases
- "Attach importance to something": To consider something as significant or give it priority. Example: "She attaches great importance to maintaining good health."
- "Of utmost importance": Signifying something is extremely important or critical. Example: "Your presence at the meeting is of utmost importance."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Understanding the importance of teamwork is crucial in a professional setting."
- "The teacher emphasized the importance of regular practice for mastering a language."
- "The historical monument holds both cultural and national importance."
