## 读音
/ɪmˈpɔːrtəns/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 重要性，重大：表示事物的价值、影响或必要性。例如: "The importance of education cannot be overstated."
  - 重要意义的事物：指具有重大意义或影响的事。例如: "He is a person of great importance in the company."

## 短语
- "attach importance to something" 意为认为某事重要。
- "in importance" 表示在重要性上，用来比较。例如: "Health is more important than wealth in importance."

## 例句
- 名词: "We discussed the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle."
- "Her opinion carries a lot of importance in decision-making processes."
