## 读音
/ˌɔːrɡəˈnɪzəˈʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 组织；团体；机构。指一个有结构和目标的集体，如公司、社团或政府部门。例如: "She works for a non-profit organization."
- **动词**: 组织；安排。指规划、设置或管理活动或事物的过程。例如: "We need to organize a meeting for next week."

## 短语
- "Business organization" 商业组织
- "Voluntary organization" 志愿者组织
- "Organize an event" 组织一个活动

## 例句
- 名词: "The United Nations is an international organization that promotes peace and cooperation."
- 动词: "He's in charge of organizing the annual charity event."
