## 读音
/ˈmærɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 已婚的，有配偶的。例如: "She is a married woman with two children."
- **动词**: 结婚（过去式和过去分词）。例如: "They got married last summer in a beautiful ceremony."

## 短语
- "to get married" 意为结婚，成为夫妻。
- "a married couple" 指已婚夫妇。

## 例句
- 形容词: "John and Mary are a happily married couple."
- 动词: "My parents were married in their early twenties."
