## 读音
/ˈdɛkəreɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 装饰；布置。例如: "She spent hours decorating the Christmas tree."
- **名词**: 装饰者；设计师。例如: "The decorator suggested a new color scheme for the living room."

## 短语
- "decorate a room" 意味着为房间增添美观或风格。
- "decorate for a special occasion" 指为了某个特殊事件如派对或节日装饰。
- "decorative elements" 指具有装饰性的元素或物品。

## 例句
- 动词: "We're going to decorate our house for the holiday season."
- 名词: "The decorator chose a modern style for the kitchen renovation."
