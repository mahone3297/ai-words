## 读音
/ˈsɜːrtɪnəti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 确定；确信；必然性。例如: "There is no certainty about what will happen tomorrow."
- **形容词**: 确定无疑的；肯定的。例如: "She spoke with a certainty that impressed everyone."

## 短语
- "beyond a shadow of a certainty" 意味着极其确定，没有一丝怀疑。
- "certainty of outcome" 指结果的确定性。

## 例句
- 名词: "The scientist needs more evidence before reaching a certainty about the theory."
- 形容词: "He was so certain of his success that he didn't bother making a backup plan."

- confidence
