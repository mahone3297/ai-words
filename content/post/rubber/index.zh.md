## 读音
/ˈrʌbər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 软橡胶，橡胶制品：A flexible material made from the sap of certain trees, used for various products.
  - 防滑鞋底：The grippy material on the bottom of shoes that provides traction.
  - 橡皮擦，橡皮：An object made of rubber used for erasing marks, typically on paper.

- **动词**:
  - 擦拭，擦去：To remove something using a rubber or similar material, such as erasing pencil marks.

## 短语
- "Rubber band" 指橡皮筋，一种可伸缩的橡胶环。
- "Rubber sole" 指有橡胶底的鞋。
- "Rubber stamp" 可以指橡皮图章，也可比喻无异议地批准或接受。

## 例句
- 名词: "She used a rubber to erase her mistakes on the paper."
- 动词: "He rubbed the dirt off his hands with a rubber glove."
- "The new sneakers have a durable rubber sole for better grip."

## 同义词
- Eraser (for the noun meaning '橡皮擦')
- Tread (for the noun meaning '防滑鞋底')
- Elastic (for the noun meaning '软橡胶')
