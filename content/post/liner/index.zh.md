## 读音
/ˈlaɪnər/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 1. 船或飞机上的乘客：A passenger on a ship or an airplane.
   - 2. 班轮，客轮：A large ocean-going vessel that regularly travels between fixed ports.
   - 3. 直线；线条：A straight line or a stroke made with a pen, pencil, or brush.

- **形容词**:
   - 1. 线状的；沿直线的：Relating to or forming a line.
   - 2. (体育)内线球员：In sports, referring to a player who plays in the inner part of the field.

## 短语
- "Ocean liner" 指定期航行于各大洋之间的豪华客轮。
- "liner notes" 专辑或书籍中的文字说明，通常由作者或艺术家撰写。

## 例句
