## 读音
/month/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个月的时间；月份。例如: "There are twelve months in a year."
- **名词**: 月刊，每月一期的出版物。例如: "She subscribes to a monthly fashion magazine."

## 短语
- "monthly payment" 每月付款。
- "monthly report" 月度报告。

## 例句
- 名词: "We celebrate our wedding anniversary every May, which is the fifth month of the year."
- 名词: "The company's financial statements include a breakdown of sales by month."
