## 读音
/pɑːrˈtɪsɪpeɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 参加；参与。例如: "She participates in various sports activities after school."
2. 共享；分担。例如: "They participate equally in the profits of the company."

## 短语
- "Participate in something" 意味着加入或成为某活动的一部分。例如: "I will participate in the annual charity run."
- "Participate actively" 表示积极参与。例如: "All students are encouraged to participate actively in class discussions."

## 例句
- "He participates in weekly chess tournaments to improve his skills."
- "Everyone is welcome to participate in the community service project."
- "To learn, you must participate and engage with the material."
