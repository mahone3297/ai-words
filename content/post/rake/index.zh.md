## 读音
/rake/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种有长柄和一组平行金属齿的工具，通常用来耙集树叶、草屑等。例如: "He used a rake to gather the leaves in the garden."
- **动词**: 用耙子耙平、收集或整理。例如: "She raked the soil before planting the seeds."
- **名词**: 指一类人，尤指行为不端或名声不佳的人。例如: "He's known as a rake among his peers."

## 短语
- "Rake over the coals" 意味着重新审视或深入讨论过去的事情。
- "Rake it in" 表示赚取大量金钱或成功获得很多东西。

## 例句
- 名词: "He picked up the old rake from the shed and started cleaning the yard."
- 动词: "She raked the sand to create a smooth surface for the kids to play on."
- 名词（指人）: "The notorious rake caused quite a stir when he entered the ballroom."
