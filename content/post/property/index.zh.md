## 读音
/ˈprɑːpərti/

## 释义
- **名词**: 财产，所有物；特性，属性；房地产；（法律）权利；性质
    - *财产/所有物*: "He inherited a large property from his father."
    - *特性/属性*: "Water has the property of expanding when it freezes."
    - *房地产*: "The company owns several properties in the city center."
    - *权利*: "They disputed the property rights over the land."
    - *性质*: "The chemical has unique magnetic properties."

## 短语
- "Real property" 指土地及其附着物，如建筑物。
- "Personal property" 指动产，如家具、汽车等。
- "Intellectual property" 指知识产品，如专利、版权、商标等。

## 例句
- 名词: "The property on the hillside has a stunning view of the ocean."
- 名词: "The company's intellectual property is its most valuable asset."
