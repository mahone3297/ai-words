## Pronunciation
/pəˈlɪtɪkəl/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Relating to politics, government, or policies; involving the exercise of power or influence in a group or society. For example: "The political debate focused on healthcare reform."
- **Noun**: A politician, someone who participates in or is involved in politics. For example: "She aspires to be a prominent political figure."
- **Verb**: To engage in political activities or discussions. For example: "He's actively political, attending rallies and voting regularly."

## Phrases
- "Political climate" refers to the prevailing attitudes and conditions in politics.
- "Political spectrum" describes the range of opinions from left to right.
- "Get involved in politics" means to participate in the political process.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The political unrest led to protests in the city."
- **Noun**: "John decided to run for the local council as a political candidate."
- **Verb**: "She enjoys discussing politics with her friends over coffee."
