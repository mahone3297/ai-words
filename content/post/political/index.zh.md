## 读音
/ˈpɑːlɪtɪkl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 关乎政治的；涉及国家治理、政策或政治立场的。例如: "He gave a political speech about healthcare reform."
- **名词**: 政治家，政客。例如: "Many political figures attended the summit."

## 短语
- "Political party" 指的是参与国家政治活动的政党。
- "Political views" 指个人或团体的政治立场和看法。
- "Political climate" 指当前的社会或国际政治氛围。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The debate focused on several political issues, including taxation and immigration."
- 名词: "She was a respected political who had served in parliament for many years."
