## Pronunciation
/kiŋ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: The male ruler of an independent state, especially one who inherits the position by right of birth. Example: "The young prince was next in line to become king."
- **Noun (figurative)**: The most important or prominent person in a particular field or group. Example: "In the world of chess, he is the undisputed king."
- **Noun (in games)**: The highest-ranking playing card in a suit, often depicted with a picture of a king. Example: "He played the king to win the trick."

## Phrases
- "King size" refers to something that is exceptionally large or of superior quality, often used to describe beds or portions of food. Example: "They upgraded to a king-size bed."
- "King of the hill" describes a person or thing that holds a dominant or leading position. Example: "After years of hard work, they became the king of the tech industry hill."
- "Kingdom come" is an expression meaning indefinitely far into the future or until the end of time. Example: "I'll wait for him till kingdom come."
- "Off with his head!" is a famous phrase associated with kings, particularly from the story of 'Alice in Wonderland', signifying the arbitrary exercise of royal power.
