## 读音
/kaɪŋ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 国王，一个国家或王国的男性统治者。例如: "The king ruled his kingdom with wisdom and justice."
- **形容词**: 最重要的，最高级别的。例如: "He is the king of rock and roll."
- 在某些牌戏或棋类游戏中，指最高级别的牌或棋子。例如: "In chess, the king is the most important piece."

## 短语
- "Kingdom" 指王国或领域，如 "Animal Kingdom"（动物界）。
- "King-sized" 通常用来形容超大号的，如 "king-sized bed"（特大号床）。

## 例句
- 名词: "The king's decision would greatly affect the lives of his subjects."
- 形容词: "She ordered a king-sized pizza for the whole family."

## 同义词
- Monarch
- Ruler
- Sovereign
- Emperor (在某些情况下)
- Prince (当指一个国家的最高统治者时)
