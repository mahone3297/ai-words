## Word: Exceedingly

## Pronunciation
/ɪkˈsiːdɪŋli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: To an exceptionally high degree; extremely or very much so. It modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs.

### Example Uses
- **Modifying Adjective**: "She was exceedingly happy with her exam results."
- **Modifying Verb**: "The project exceeded expectations exceedingly well."
- **Modifying Adverb**: "He worked exceedingly hard on his presentation."

## Phrases
- "Exceedingly well": Describes something done to a notably superior level. Example: "The team performed exceedingly well in the competition."
- "Exceedingly rare": Indicates that something is extremely uncommon or unique. Example: "This type of gemstone is exceedingly rare."

## Example Sentences
- **Adverb modifying an Adjective**: "The cake was exceedingly delicious."
- **Adverb modifying a Verb**: "He spoke exceedingly softly, afraid to disturb anyone."
- **Adverb modifying another Adverb**: "She ran exceedingly quickly to catch the bus."
