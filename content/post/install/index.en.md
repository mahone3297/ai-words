## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈstɔːl/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To place or fit (a piece of equipment, machinery, or software) in position ready for use. For example: "They installed a new security system in the building."
  - _Transitive_ usage: "I will install the latest version of the application on my computer."
  - _Intransitive_ usage: "The program installs quickly and easily."

- **Noun**: The act of installing something or the state of being installed. For example: "The installation process took only a few minutes."

## Phrases
- "Installment plan" refers to a payment plan where one pays for something over time rather than all at once.
- "To install someone in a position" means to put them in a job or role officially. For instance: "She was installed as the company's new CEO."

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "We're installing solar panels on our roof to generate electricity."
  - "The technician is installing a new router to improve our internet connection."
- **Noun**: "The installation of the air conditioning unit required professional assistance."
  - "After the installation, the application needed to be configured properly."
