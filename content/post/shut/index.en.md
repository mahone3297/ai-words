## Pronunciation
/shʌt/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: 
  - **Noun**: A mechanical device that closes by applying pressure, as in a door or window. For example: "The child shut the door quietly."
  - **Verb**: To close with force or firmly, as a door or window. For example: "She shut the book and put it down."
  - **Verb**: To stop or cease functioning, as a process or activity. For example: "The factory shut down due to financial difficulties."
  - **Verb**: To bring to an end or terminate (a relationship, conversation, etc.). For example: "He shut down the discussion after everyone agreed on the plan."

## Phrases
- "Shut up" is an informal way to tell someone to be quiet.
- "Shut off" means to turn something off, like a faucet or electricity.
- "Shut down" can refer to closing a business or system, or simply stopping an activity.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "He had to shut the oven before the cake was fully cooked."
- **Verb**: "She shut the curtains to block out the sunlight."
- **Verb**: "The company shut down temporarily for maintenance."
- **Verb**: "After the argument, they decided to shut the conversation down."
