## 读音
/sərˈpraɪz/

## 释义
- **名词**: 出乎意料的事物或消息，惊喜。例如: "She was surprised by his unexpected visit."
- **动词**: 使感到意外，使吃惊。例如: "The news surprised everyone in the room."

## 短语
- "a pleasant surprise" 意为令人愉快的惊喜。
- "to one's surprise" 表示出乎某人的意料。

## 例句
- 名词: "The birthday party was a total surprise for her."
- 动词: "His calm reaction surprised us all."

- Astonish
- Startle
