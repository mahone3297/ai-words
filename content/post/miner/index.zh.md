## 读音
/ˈmaɪnər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 采矿工人，采石工。例如: "The miner worked deep in the coal mine."
             2. 少数，小规模的。例如: "A minor league baseball team."

- **形容词**: 1. 矿业的，与采矿相关的。例如: "The company specializes in mining equipment for the mineral industry."
             2. 较小的，次要的。例如: "He has a minor role in the play."

## 短语
- "miner's lamp" 矿工灯，指矿工在地下作业时使用的照明设备。
- "mineral deposit" 矿床，地质中富含矿物的区域。

## 例句
- 名词: "The miner returned home after a long day in the mines."
