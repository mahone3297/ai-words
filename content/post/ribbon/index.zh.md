## 读音
/ˈrɪbən/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
   1. 细长的带状物，通常由丝绸、绸缎或其他材料制成，用于装饰、包装等。例如: "She tied a red ribbon around the gift."
   2. 奖章或奖品上的丝带。例如: "The winner received a gold medal with a blue ribbon."
   3. （电脑）标签，尤指Microsoft Office软件界面中的功能区。例如: "Click on the 'File' ribbon to access the save options."

## 短语
- "Tie a ribbon around something" 意为用丝带把东西系上，常用于装饰。例如: "She tied a yellow ribbon around the old oak tree."
- "Ribbon-cutting ceremony" 是指剪彩仪式，通常在新项目或开业庆典上进行。例如: "The mayor will perform the ribbon-cutting ceremony for the new library."

## 例句
- 名词: "The little girl wore a bow made of a pink ribbon in her hair."
- 动词（用作动词时通常用作系动词 be 的表语）: "The package was adorned with a beautiful green ribbon."
