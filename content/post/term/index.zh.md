## 读音
/ˈtɜːrm/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 术语，专业词汇：A specific word or phrase used in a particular field or subject, such as "annual term" in finance.
   - 学期：A period of time during which a school or college holds classes, usually divided into fall, winter/spring, and summer terms.
   - 条款，条件：One of the conditions or provisions in an agreement or contract.
   - 期限：The length of time for which something, like a loan or insurance policy, is valid.

- **动词**:
   - 规定（条款）：To establish or stipulate a term or condition in an agreement.

## 短语
- "On good terms with someone" 表示与某人关系融洽。
- "Under the terms of the contract" 指按照合同的条款。
- "End of term" 指学期末或任期结束。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher explained the scientific term 'photosynthesis' in class."
- 动词: "The lease agreement terms that the tenant must adhere to."
- 条件: "The contract has a non-compete term lasting for one year after termination."
