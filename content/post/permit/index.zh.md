## 读音
/ˈpɜːrmɪt/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 许可，许可证。例如: "You need a permit to camp in this area."
- **动词 (v)**: 允许，准许。例如: "The law doesn't permit smoking in public places."

## 短语
- "Permission to enter" 意为进入的许可。
- "Building permit" 是建筑许可证，允许进行建设工作。
- "Permitting conditions" 指的是允许发生的条件。

## 例句
- 名词: "I had to obtain a fishing permit before going to the lake."
- 动词: "The school permits students to use the library after hours."

## 同义词
- Allowance
- Authorization
- Consent
- License
- Approval
