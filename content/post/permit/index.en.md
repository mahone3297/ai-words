## Pronunciation
/prɪˈmɪt/

## Definitions
### Noun
1. **Permission granted by a higher authority or to do something:** The city council granted a permit for the construction project.
2. **A document authorizing a specific activity or action:** He had to apply for a permit to drive a commercial vehicle.

### Verb
1. **To allow or give permission for (an action or event):** The manager permitted her employees to work from home.
2. **To authorize officially:** The government permits hunting during certain seasons.

## Phrases
- "With a permit in hand"
- "Obtain a permit for"
- "Permitting such behavior"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The construction company needed a permit before they could start digging."
- **Verb**: "After obtaining the permit, he was allowed to operate the heavy machinery."
- **Verb**: "The museum permits photography without flash inside the exhibits."
