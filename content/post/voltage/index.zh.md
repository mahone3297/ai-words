## 读音
/vəˈlɑːtɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词 n**: 电压，电势差，是指电路中两点之间的电位差，单位为伏特（V）。例如: "The voltage across the resistor is 12 volts."

## 短语
- "High voltage" 高电压
- "Low voltage" 低电压
- "Voltage drop" 电压降，指电流通过电阻时电压的下降

## 例句
- "The battery provides a stable voltage to power the electronic device."
- "Be careful when handling high voltage wires; it can be dangerous."
- "The voltage drop across the wire reduces the efficiency of the circuit."

## 同义词
- Electric potential difference
- Electromotive force (EMF)
- Potential difference
- Volt-level
