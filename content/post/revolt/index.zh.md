## 读音
/riːˈvɔlt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 公开反抗或反对权威、传统或既定的权力，通常伴随着暴力或不顺从。例如: "The workers revolted against the unfair working conditions."
- **名词**: 一次反抗或起义。例如: "The 1789 French Revolution marked the end of the monarchy."

## 短语
- "Revolt against" 表示反抗或起义反对。例如: "They organized a revolt against the oppressive government."
- "A spontaneous revolt" 指突然爆发的反抗行动。

## 例句
- 动词: "The people decided to revolt after their demands for democracy were ignored."
- 名词: "The colonial revolt led to the formation of a new nation."
