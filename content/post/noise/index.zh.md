## 读音
/naɪz/

## 释义
- **名词**: 响声，噪音，嘈杂声。指不悦耳或无目的的声音。例如: "The noise from the construction site kept me awake all night."
- **动词**: 制造噪音。例如: "The children were running around, noises filling the house."

## 短语
- "背景噪音" (background noise) 指在某一环境中的持续噪声。
- "降低噪音" (reduce noise) 指减少或消除不需要的声音。

## 例句
- 名词: "The noise level in the stadium was deafening during the match."
- 动词: "The machines in the factory were noiselessly operating after the maintenance."
