## 读音

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 
    - 变化，转换。例如: "There was a noticeable shift in her attitude."
    - 轮班，换班。例如: "She works the night shift at the hospital."
    - 移动，位移。例如: "The rocks shifted slightly during the earthquake."
    - (衣服的)无袖连衣裙。例如: "She wore a simple shift to the summer party."

- **动词 (v)**:
    - 改变，变动。例如: "The company needs to shift its focus to online sales."
    - 转动，移动位置。例如: "Shift your weight to your left foot."
    - 换挡，调档。例如: "He shifted gears as he accelerated on the highway."

## 短语
- "Shift gears" 意味着改变策略或方向。
- "Shift work" 指轮班工作制度。
- "Shift blame" 指把责任推给别人。
