## 读音
/ˈsɪriəs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 严重的，认真的，重要的。例如: "He had a serious injury and needed immediate medical attention." 
- **副词**: 认真地，严肃地。例如: "She spoke seriously about the situation."

## 短语
- "in all seriousness" 表示非常认真地说，不开玩笑。
- "serious about something" 表示对某事非常认真或投入。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The doctor said it was a serious illness that required surgery."
- 副词: "When discussing the issue, he looked at her seriously."
