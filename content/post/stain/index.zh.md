## 读音
/steɪn/

## 释义
- **名词**: 污渍，斑点；痕迹。例如: "There's a red wine stain on the carpet."
- **动词**: 弄污，染色。例如: "Don't spill the ink, it will stain your shirt."
- **名词**: 染料，颜料。例如: "The artist applied a layer of blue stain to the wood."

## 短语
- "Remove the stain" 意为去除污渍。
- "Stain glass" 指的是彩色玻璃，常用于教堂窗户。

## 例句
- 名词: "The rain left stains on the concrete."
- 动词: "The oil stain won't come out of the fabric easily."
- 名词: "The artist was famous for his intricate stain glass designs."
