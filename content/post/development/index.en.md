## Definitions
### Noun
1. The process of growing or changing, especially in a way that is planned or intended: *The country's economic development has been impressive.*
2. The state or condition of being advanced in progress or organization: *The project is still in its early stages of development.*
3. The act or process of forming or creating something new: *The company's recent technological developments are groundbreaking.*

### Verb
1. To bring about the growth, improvement, or maturation of: *We need to develop new strategies for marketing our product.*
2. To cause (a person) to progress or advance in skill or understanding: *She developed a passion for music at a young age.*
