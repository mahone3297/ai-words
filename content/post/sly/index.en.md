## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Characterizing or marked by cunning, cleverness, or stealthiness in one's actions or behavior. Often used with a negative connotation to imply deceitfulness.
- **Adverb**: In a sly manner; cunningly or sneakily.

## Phrases
- "Sly smile" refers to a secret or mischievous grin.
- "He pulled off a sly trick" means he did something cunning or deceptive.
- "She gave him a sly wink" implies a subtle, secretive gesture.

## Example Sentences
