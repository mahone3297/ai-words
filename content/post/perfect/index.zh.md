## 读音
/perˈfɛkt/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 完美的，无瑕疵的。例如: "The painting was so perfect that it looked real."
- **动词**: 使完美，完善。例如: "She has been perfecting her cooking skills for years."
- **名词**: （尤指理想中的）完美状态，极致。例如: "He strives for perfection in all aspects of his life."

## 短语
- "to be perfect for someone/something" 意为对某人或某事来说非常合适。
- "perfectly" 作为副词，意为完全地，极其。例如: "Their plan worked perfectly."

## 例句
- 形容词: "His performance was so perfect that the audience gave him a standing ovation."
- 动词: "She spent hours perfecting her presentation before the big meeting."
- 名词: "In their pursuit of perfection, they left no stone unturned."
