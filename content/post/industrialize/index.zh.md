## 读音
/ɪndəˈstriːəlʌɪz/

## 释义
- **动词**: 
    - 将（国家、地区或行业）工业化；发展（经济）以依赖制造业和机器生产。例如: "The government plans to industrialize the rural areas."
    - 使（工厂、生产过程等）现代化，采用工业生产方法。例如: "The company decided to industrialize its manufacturing process to increase efficiency."

## 短语
- "Industrialization of agriculture" 农业工业化
- "To industrialize a country" 使一个国家工业化

## 例句
- "The rapid industrialization of China has transformed its economy over the past few decades."
- "The new policy aims to industrialize the region by attracting foreign investment."
