## 读音
/ˈsænwɪdʒ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种由两片面包之间夹着肉类、蔬菜、奶酪等食物的食品。例如: "I had a delicious turkey sandwich for lunch."
- **动词**: 把东西夹在两层之间。例如: "She sandwiched a piece of cheese between two slices of bread."

## 短语
- "Ham sandwich" 意为火腿三明治。
- "Club sandwich" 是一种包含多层食材的三明治。
- "Make a sandwich" 意为制作一个三明治。

## 例句
- 名词: "The cafe offered a variety of sandwiches, from classic BLTs to vegetarian options."
- 动词: "She decided to sandwich the lettuce between two slices of whole-grain bread."
