## Pronunciation
/thæŋk/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: (v) To express gratitude or appreciation; to convey thanks. For example: "I want to thank you for your help."
- **Noun**: An expression of gratitude; an act of showing appreciation. For example: "A simple 'thank you' can go a long way."
- **Adverb**: In a thankful manner; gratefully. For example: "She thanked him profusely."

## Phrases
- "Thank you very much" is a formal way to express gratitude.
- "Thanks a lot" is a more casual expression of appreciation.
- "No problem, it's my pleasure" is a response to someone thanking you.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "I'd like to thank the speaker for their inspiring presentation."
- **Noun**: "After the concert, the audience stood up to thank the musicians."
- **Adverb**: "She thanked her friends for coming to her birthday party."

## Synonyms
- Gratitude
- Appreciation
- Acknowledge
- Recognize
- Express thanks
