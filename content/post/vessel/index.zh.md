## 读音
/vəˈsel/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 船；舰艇；容器；血管；器官。例如: "The ship was a large vessel on the open sea."，"The scientist used a small vessel to hold the chemical solution."
- **名词 (n)**: 在法律和商业中，指运输货物的船只。例如: "The vessel is registered under the Panamanian flag."

## 短语
- "ocean-going vessel" 指能够远航的大船。
- "blood vessels" 指人体或动物体内的血管。
- "cargo vessel" 运输货物的船只。

## 例句
- 名词: "The oil tanker was one of the largest vessels in the harbor."
- 名词: "The surgeon carefully operated on the damaged blood vessel."

## 同义词

- Boat
- Craft
- Vehicule (在某些上下文中)
- Container (在指容器时)
