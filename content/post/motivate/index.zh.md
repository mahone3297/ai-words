## 读音
/ˈmoʊtɪveɪt/

## 释义
- **动词**: 激发（某人的）积极性，激励，鼓励。例如: "The boss's words motivated the team to work harder."
- **名词**: 动机，动力，原因。例如: "Her desire for success was her main motivation."

## 短语
- "Motivate someone to do something" 意为激发某人去做某事。
- "Internal motivation" 指内在的动力或个人意愿。
- "External motivation" 指外在的激励，如奖励或惩罚。

## 例句
- 动词: "The coach's inspiring speeches always motivate the athletes before the game."
- 名词: "Understanding your employees' motivations can help improve their job satisfaction."

## 同义词
- Encourage
- Inspire
- Stimulate
