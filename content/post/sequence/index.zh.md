## 读音
/ˈsiːkwəns/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一系列按照特定顺序排列的事物或动作。例如: "The sequence of events led to the accident."
- **名词**: 在数学和计算机科学中，有序的元素集合。例如: "In this arithmetic sequence, each term is 3 more than the previous one."

## 短语
- "Sequence of operations" 指执行任务的步骤顺序。
- "Genetic sequence" 指DNA或RNA分子中的核苷酸顺序。

## 例句
- 名词: "The film's plot follows a precise sequence of events."
- 名词: "Scientists analyzed the genetic sequence to understand the organism's traits."
