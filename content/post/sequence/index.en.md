## Pronunciation
/həˈlɒʊ/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A term used to express welcome, greet, or acknowledge someone. For example: "She waved a friendly hello at the bus stop."
- **Verb**: To make a greeting or say "hello" to someone. For example: "He greeted his friends with a hearty hello."
- **Adverb**: Expressing or denoting a manner of greeting. For example: "He said hello cheerfully."

## Phrases
- "Give someone a hello" means to initiate a greeting.
- "Hello, old friend" is a familiar way to address someone you know well.
- "Hello there, stranger" is a casual way to acknowledge an unfamiliar person.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "Her warm hello always brightened up my day."
- **Verb**: "After a long day, it was nice to hear a simple hello from a passerby."
- **Adverb**: "He entered the room with a jovial hello."
