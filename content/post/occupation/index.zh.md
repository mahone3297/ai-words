## 读音
/ˌɑːkˈskəpʃən/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - **工作，职业**: 一个人所从事的常规或全职工作，通常是为了谋生。例如: "Her occupation is teaching."
  - **占用，占据**: 物理上或概念上占据某处或某事物。例如: "The building was occupied by a law firm."
  - **占领，殖民**: 一个国家或团体对另一地区的控制或统治。例如: "The region was under foreign occupation for many years."

## 短语
- "Change of occupation" 意味着更换工作。
- "Occupational hazard" 指与特定工作相关的职业风险或危险。
- "Under occupation" 表示某物被占用中。

## 例句
- 名词: "He listed his occupation as a software engineer on his resume."
- 占据: "The protesters' main demand was the withdrawal of occupying forces."
- 占领: "After the war, the city was placed under military occupation."
