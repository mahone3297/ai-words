## Pronunciation
/pərˈθli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: To a limited extent; not completely or fully. It modifies verbs, adjectives, or other adverbs, expressing a degree of completeness.

## Phrases
- "Partially cooked" means only partly done.
- "Partially agreed" indicates that both parties have agreed on some aspects but not all.
- "Partially cloudy" describes weather with intermittent cloud cover.

## Example Sentences
- "I can finish the project partially by tomorrow."
- "She understood the concept partially."
- "The room was partially renovated, with new paint but the old furniture still in place."
