## 读音
/pɑːrˈtɪəli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 部分地，不完全地，局部地。表示程度或范围的限制。例如: "The building is partially destroyed by the fire."
- **形容词**: 部分的，不完全的。用于描述某事物只涉及一部分。例如: "A partially completed project."

## 短语
- "Partially due to" 意为部分原因是。
- "Partially responsible" 意为部分责任。

## 例句
- 副词: "I can only attend the meeting partially, as I have another appointment later."
- 形容词: "The book is partially damaged, but still readable."

- Somewhat
- To some extent
- In part
- To a certain degree
- Partly
