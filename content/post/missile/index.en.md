## Pronunciation
/ˈmɪs.əl/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A weapon that is self-propelled, typically having a guidance system, designed to hit a predetermined target. For example: "The country developed a new type of long-range missile capable of carrying nuclear warheads."

  > Phrase: "ballistic missile" - A missile that travels along a ballistic trajectory, which is a curved path determined by the initial velocity and angle of launch, often referring to those used to deliver nuclear weapons.
  
- **Noun (figurative)**: Something thrown or propelled with force towards a target. For example: "The footballer launched a powerful header towards the goal like a missile."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun (weapon)**: "The military deployed several missiles in response to the perceived threat."
- **Noun (figurative)**: "Her words were like missiles, piercing through his defenses."

## Synonyms
- Rocket
- Projectile
- Warhead
- Guided weapon
- Aerodynamic projectile

## Phrases
- "Missile defense system" - A network of radars, command centers, and interceptors designed to destroy incoming missiles.
- "Cruise missile" - A guided missile that flies at relatively low altitudes and at subsonic speeds, often used for precision strikes.
- "Intercontinental ballistic missile (ICBM)" - A long-range missile designed to deliver a payload from one continent to another.
