## 读音
/ˈtræfɪk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 路上的车辆流动；交通（量）。例如: "There was heavy traffic on the highway during rush hour."
- **名词**: 交易；商业活动。例如: "The city's port handles a large volume of international trade traffic."
- **动词**: 在网络上访问或浏览（网站）。例如: "The new website has been getting a lot of traffic since its launch."

## 短语
- "Traffic jam" 表示交通堵塞。
- "Heavy traffic" 指交通繁忙或拥挤。
- "Traffic flow" 指交通的流动情况。

## 例句
- 名词: "The accident caused a traffic jam that lasted for hours."
- 名词: "The company relies heavily on internet traffic to generate sales."
- 动词: "The influencer's latest post attracted a lot of traffic to their blog."
