## Pronunciation
/pəˈtenʃl/

## Definitions
### Noun
**Potential** refers to the capacity or possibility that something has to develop or become actualized, often when under certain conditions or circumstances. It can also denote a person's innate abilities or capabilities.

### Verb
To **potentially** means to have the possibility or likelihood of something happening, without certainty.

### Adjective
**Potent** is an adjective used to describe something that is powerful, effective, or capable of producing a strong effect.

## Phrases
- "Potential energy" in physics refers to stored energy that can be converted into work.
- "Maximizing potential" means to make the best use of one's abilities or resources.
- "Underestimated potential" describes someone or something with more capability than initially perceived.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She has a lot of untapped potential in her artistic abilities."
- **Verb**: "The new technology has the potential to revolutionize the industry."
- **Adjective**: "His potent argument convinced the jury."
