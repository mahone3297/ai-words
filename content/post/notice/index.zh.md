## 读音
/nəʊˈtɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 通知，布告，消息。例如: "There's a notice on the board about tomorrow's meeting."
- **动词**: 注意到，看到，察觉。例如: "I noticed a strange noise coming from the engine."

## 短语
- "Give notice" 提前告知离职或结束租约。
- "Take notice of" 注意到，关注。例如: "It's important to take notice of changes in your body."

## 例句
- 名词: "Please read the notice before entering the museum."
- 动词: "She noticed a stranger following her."

- Notification
- Announcement
- Information
