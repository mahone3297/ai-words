## 读音
/ˈlɪbərti/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   自由；自主权；解放。例如: "The concept of liberty is central to democracy."
   人身自由；言论自由。例如: "They fought for the liberty of speech."
   （美国）自由女神像（Statue of Liberty）。例如: "The Statue of Liberty symbolizes freedom to immigrants."

## 短语
- "Personal liberty" 指个人自由。
- "Civil liberties" 指公民自由权利。
- "Liberty and justice for all" 是美国《效忠誓词》的一部分，意为“为所有人争取自由和公正”。

## 例句
- "Our constitution guarantees every citizen the right to liberty."
- "The prisoners were granted temporary liberty during the holiday season."
- "The statue of Liberty stands tall in New York Harbor, welcoming newcomers."
