## 读音
/ˈpɪri.əd/

## 释义
- **名词**:
   - 时间的一段；一段时间。例如: "We'll discuss this matter again in a later period."
   - 月经周期；月份。例如: "Her period is usually very regular."
   - 句号；点。在书面语中表示句子的结束。例如: "The end of the sentence is marked by a period."
   - （历史上的）时期；时代。例如: "The Renaissance was a period of great artistic achievement."

## 短语
- "Full stop" 在英式英语中，period 也被称为 full stop，表示句子结束的标点符号。
- "Periodical" 指定期出版物，如杂志或期刊。

## 例句
- 名词: "The ancient Greek period was characterized by great philosophers."
- 名词（关于月经）: "She keeps track of her period to know when to expect it."
- 名词（关于句号）: "The email ended with a period, indicating the message was complete."
