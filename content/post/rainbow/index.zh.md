## 读音
/rɪˈbeɪəʊ/

## 释义
- **名词**: 彩虹，天空中出现的一种由太阳光照射雨滴形成的彩色弧形光谱。例如: "A beautiful rainbow appeared after the rain."
- **形容词**: 彩色的，如彩虹般多姿多彩的。例如: "The rainbow-colored flag symbolizes diversity."

## 短语
- "Pot of gold at the end of the rainbow" 指的是传说中彩虹尽头的财宝。
- "Rainbow coalition" 指由不同政治派别或种族团体组成的联盟。

## 例句
- 名词: "We were lucky enough to catch a glimpse of a double rainbow in the sky."
- 形容词: "Her dress was adorned with a rainbow of sequins, shimmering in the light."
