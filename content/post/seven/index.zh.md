## 读音
/ˈsɛvən/

## 释义
- **数词**: 表示数字7，基数词。例如: "There are seven days in a week."
- **名词**: 在某些上下文中，可以指第七个事物或顺序。例如: "The seventh player on the team."

## 短语
- "Seven seas" 通常指世界各地的所有海洋。
- "Seven wonders of the world" 指古代世界的七大奇迹。

## 例句
- 数词: "The number seven is considered lucky in many cultures."
- 名词: "This is my seventh book this year."

## 同义词
- Six and one
- A week has seven days
- The number following six and preceding eight
