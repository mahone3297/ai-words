## 读音

## 释义
- **动词**: 使变得更深；加深。例如: "Regular practice can deepen your understanding of a subject."
- **形容词**: 深入的，深厚的。例如: "Their friendship had deepened over the years."

## 短语
- "deepen one's knowledge" 增进知识。
- "deepen the relationship" 加深关系。
