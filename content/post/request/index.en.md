## Pronunciation
/rɪˈkwest/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **Request**: A polite expression or formal requirement for something, often accompanied by specific details or expectations. For example: "The customer made a request for a refund."
- **Desire**: A strong wish or longing for something. For example: "She had a strong request for a promotion at work."

### Verb
- **To request**: To ask for something politely or formally. For example: "He requested a meeting with the manager."
- **To demand**: To require something forcefully, often with a stronger sense of entitlement. For example: "The employees demanded better working conditions."

## Phrases
- "Make a request" means to ask for something.
- "I'll pass on your request" implies forwarding someone else's request.
- "No request is too small" suggests that any request should be considered, regardless of its size.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The client submitted a request for a project extension."
- **Verb**: "Please submit your request in writing by the end of the day."
- **Phrasal Verb**: "She reluctantly granted his request for a raise."
