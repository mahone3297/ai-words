## 读音
/ˈprəɡrɛsɪv/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 进步的，先进的；倾向于改进和发展的。例如: "The new policy is considered progressive by many."
- **名词**: 支持渐进式改革的人或政治派别。例如: "The progressive movement aims for social change."

## 短语
- "progressive taxation" 渐进税制，税收随着收入增加而增加。
- "progressive education" 进步教育，强调学生主动学习和全面发展。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The government's approach to healthcare is quite progressive."
- 名词: "She is a well-known progressive politician advocating for social justice."
