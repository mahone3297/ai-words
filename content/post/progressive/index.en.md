## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Referring to something that progresses, moves forward, or develops gradually. For example: "The car's engine is running smoothly on the progressive fuel system."
- **Adverb**: In a progressive manner; continuously or ongoing. For example: "She was learning to play the piano progressively, mastering one piece after another."
- **Noun**: A progressive movement or ideology advocating social, political, or economic change. For example: "The progressive agenda aims to reduce income inequality."

## Phrases

- "Make progress" means to advance or improve in a particular area.
- "Progressive taxation" refers to a tax system where higher earners pay a larger percentage.
