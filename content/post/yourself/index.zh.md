## 读音
/ˈjɛrslf/

## 释义
- **反身代词**: 指示自己或自身，强调动作回指主语。例如: "Take care of yourself." 
- **相互代词**: 在某些情况下，表示相互的动作或状态。例如: "You two should help each other and support yourselves."

## 短语
- "by oneself" 表示独自一人。例如: "I can do it by myself."
- "for oneself" 意为为了自己的利益。例如: "She made the decision for herself."

## 例句
- 反身代词: "He prepared his own breakfast in the morning."
- 相互代词: "They teach themselves programming online."
