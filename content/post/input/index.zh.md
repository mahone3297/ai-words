## 读音
/hɪnt/

## 释义
- **名词**:
  - 提示，线索。例如: "The answer to the puzzle is in the hint."
  - 细微的迹象，暗示。例如: "There were hints that he might resign soon."

- **动词**:
  - 给予提示。例如: "Can you give me a hint about how to solve this problem?"
  - 轻轻地触碰。例如: "She hinted at his arm to get his attention."

## 短语
- "Give a hint" 意为提供线索或提示。
- "Take a hint" 意为领会别人的暗示或提醒。

## 例句
- 名词: "The teacher gave us a hint about what would be on the test."
- 动词: "It's time for you to take a hint and leave the party."
