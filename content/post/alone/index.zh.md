## 读音
/əˈləʊn/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 单独的，独自的，孤身一人的。例如: "He enjoyed being alone with his thoughts."
- **副词**: 独自地，一个人。例如: "She stayed at home alone while her parents went out."

## 短语
- "all alone" 意为完全独自一人，没有别人的陪伴。
- "feel alone" 表示感到孤独，即使在人群中也可能有这种感觉。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The child was left alone in the room."
- 副词: "She often works alone on her projects."

## 同义词
- solitary
- single-handed
- unaccompanied
- by oneself
- on one's own
