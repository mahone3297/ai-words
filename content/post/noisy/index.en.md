## Word: Noisy

## Pronunciation
/ˈnɔɪzi/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Producing or characterized by a lot of noise; loud and disruptive. 

### Definition Examples
- **As a descriptor**: "The noisy traffic outside kept me up all night."
- **In a sentence**: "Her neighbors were having a noisy party that disturbed the entire block."

## Phrases
- "Make a noisy entrance": To arrive somewhere with considerable noise or fanfare.
- "Noisy background": Refers to a setting with loud and distracting sounds, often used in the context of recordings or conversations.
- "Noisy neighbor": A colloquialism for someone who creates excessive noise in a residential area.

## Example Sentences
- **As an adjective**: "The construction work next door is incredibly noisy during the day."
- "The children were playing noisily in the park, their laughter echoing throughout the neighborhood."
