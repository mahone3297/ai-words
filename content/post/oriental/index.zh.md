## 读音
/əˈrɪəntl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 东方的，亚洲的，特别是指东亚和南亚的文化、艺术或人的。例如: "She has an oriental rug in her living room."
- **名词**: 东方人，常指东亚或南亚地区的人。例如: "The oriental cuisine is known for its spicy flavors."

## 短语
- "Oriental art" 指的是东方的艺术作品。
- "Oriental culture" 泛指东方国家的文化。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The oriental garden is filled with exotic plants and flowers."
- 名词: "He comes from an oriental background, and his family practices traditional customs."
