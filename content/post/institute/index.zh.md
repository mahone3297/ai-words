## 读音
/ɪnˈstɪtjuːt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一个组织或机构，通常用于教育、研究或专业发展。例如: "She works at a prestigious medical institute."
- **动词**: 建立或设立一个机构或制度。例如: "The government decided to institute new environmental policies."

## 短语
- "Institute of Technology" 通常指的是提供工程和技术教育的高等教育机构。
- "Institute for Advanced Study" 是一种专门从事高级学术研究的机构。

## 例句
- 名词: "The Institute of Physics conducts groundbreaking research in various fields."
- 动词: "They are planning to institute a new program to promote gender equality."
