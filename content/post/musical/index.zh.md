## 读音

## 释义
- **名词**: 1. 音乐剧，一种结合歌唱、对话和表演的舞台艺术。例如: "She's performing in a Broadway musical this season."
   2. 音乐的，与音乐相关的。例如: "The musical instrument store had a wide range of pianos and guitars."

- **形容词**: 有音乐性的，悦耳的。例如: "The singer has a beautiful, musical voice."

## 短语
- "musical theater" 指的是专业的音乐剧制作。
- "musical talent" 指的是音乐方面的才能或天赋。

## 例句

- 形容词: "Her voice is so musical that it gives me goosebumps."
