## 读音
/pərˈsuːt/

## 释义
- **名词**: 追求；追踪；努力；奋斗。例如: "The pursuit of happiness is a fundamental human right." (追求幸福是基本人权)
- **动词**: 追赶；追踪；从事；致力于。例如: "He pursued his dream of becoming an artist." (他追求成为艺术家的梦想)

## 短语
- "in pursuit of" 意为追求或寻找。例如: "She was in pursuit of a better life for her children."
- "pursue one's interests" 意味着追求个人的兴趣或爱好。例如: "He decided to pursue his interests in music."

## 例句
- 名词: "Her life's work was the pursuit of knowledge."
- 动词: "He immediately pursued the thief who had stolen his wallet."
