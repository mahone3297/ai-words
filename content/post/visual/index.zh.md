## 读音
/ˈvɪʒuəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 与视觉有关的；看得见的。例如: "The visual effects in the movie were stunning."
- **名词**: 视觉；视力。例如: "She has perfect visual acuity."
- **名词**: 图像；图表；可视化表示。例如: "The presentation included several visual aids."

## 短语
- "Visual impairment" 意味视力障碍。
- "Visual aid" 指的是帮助人们理解信息的图像或图表。
- "Visual learning" 指通过观察和图像来学习的方式。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The artist used vibrant visual elements in his painting."
- 名词: "The optometrist tested her visual abilities."
- 名词: "The teacher created a visual timeline for the history lesson."
