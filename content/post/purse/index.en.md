## Pronunciation
/pɜːrs/

## Definitions
### Noun
- **A small bag or pouch** typically carried by women or men to hold money, cards, and personal items.
- **A woman's handbag or wallet.**
- **An amount of money, especially cash.**

## Phrases
- "Carry a purse" means to have a purse with one's belongings.
- "Purse strings" symbolically refers to the control over money.
- "Empty your purse" is an expression to give away all one's money.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "She placed her keys and phone inside her leather purse."
- **Noun**: "He took out his wallet, which was essentially just a large purse, to pay for the meal."
- **Noun**: "She kept a small amount of change in her purse for emergencies."

## Verb
- **To take or use something from one's purse.**
- **To refer to a purse when discussing finances.**

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "She reached into her purse for her credit card."
- **Verb**: "The company's financial reports showed a significant decrease in purse revenue."

## Synonyms
- Wallet (for a man)
- Clutch (a smaller, often handheld purse)
- Purse-like bag (a more general term)
- Coin purse
- Handbag (larger and more formal)

## Antonyms
- Wallet (for a man)
- Backpack (a larger bag)
- Briefcase (usually for work or documents)
