## Pronunciation
/sent/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: A distinctive odor or smell, often pleasant. For example: "The garden had a lovely scent of roses."
- **Verb**: To perceive or detect a smell; experience by olfaction. For example: "She could scent the fresh baked bread from the bakery."
- **Adjective**: Characteristic of a particular odor; fragrant. For example: "The air had a subtle, floral scent."

## Phrases
- "Carry a pleasant scent" means to have a pleasing aroma.
- "The scent of lavender is known for its calming properties."
- "Scented candles add a cozy atmosphere to a room."

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The perfume left a lingering scent on her skin."
- **Verb**: "I could scent the damp earth after the rain."
- **Adjective**: "The flowers in the garden were sweet-scented."
