## 读音
/trʌk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 大型机动车辆，通常用于运输货物。例如: "He drives a big red truck for his job."
- **动词**: 用卡车运输（货物）。例如: "They'll truck the goods to the warehouse later today."

## 短语
- "Truck driver" 指驾驶卡车的人。
- "Pickup truck" 指小型货车，通常有开放式的货箱。
- "Trucking industry" 指运输业，特别是使用卡车的部分。

## 例句
- 名词: "The truck arrived with a load of construction materials."
- 动词: "The company trucks their products across the country."

- Van
- Cargo vehicle
