## 读音
/swɪm/

## 释义
- **动词**: 在水中或其他液体中通过手脚或身体的摆动前进。例如: "He loves to swim in the ocean every morning."
- **名词**: 游泳作为一种运动或活动。例如: "She's taking swimming lessons to improve her technique."

## 短语
- "Go for a swim" 意为去游泳。
- "Swim against the current" 比喻逆流而上，克服困难。
- "Swimming pool" 是游泳池。

## 例句
- 动词: "The children are swimming in the lake."
- 名词: "I joined the local gym for their excellent swimming facilities."

## 同义词
- Float
- Dive
- Bathe
- Paddle
- Tread water
