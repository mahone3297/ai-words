## Pronunciation

## Definitions
### Noun
An adjective used to describe someone or something standing or positioned with their top or base vertical, straight, or correct. It can also refer to a person with good moral character or integrity. Example: "The statue was an impressive upright figure."

### Verb
To maintain a vertical position, especially when standing; to stand or sit straight. Can also mean to hold oneself with dignity or propriety. Example: "After the break, the students stood upright at attention."

### Adjective
1. Directly facing upwards or perpendicular to the ground. Example: "The plant grew upright towards the sunlight."
2. Moral or ethical correctness. Example: "An upright citizen always tells the truth."
