## Pronunciation
/ˌʌndərˈɡoʊ/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A noun referring to the process of experiencing or undergoing a change, transformation, or treatment, often with some level of discomfort or difficulty. For example: "The plant underwent a significant transformation over the summer."

### Verb
- A verb meaning to go through a change, experience a process, or suffer something, typically as a result of an external influence. For example: "The patient will undergo surgery next week."

### Adjective
- Occasionally used as an adjective to describe something that has undergone a change or process, e.g., "an undergone renovation."

## Phrases
- "Undergo treatment" implies receiving medical care or therapy.
- "Undergo a transformation" signifies a significant change in appearance or character.
- "Undergoing trials" refers to being in a challenging situation.

## Example Sentences

- **Verb**: "The cancer patients must undergo rigorous chemotherapy."
