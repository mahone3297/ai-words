## 读音
/wʌndər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 惊奇；奇迹；疑惑。例如: "The Grand Canyon is a true wonder of the natural world."
- **动词**: 想知道；对…感到好奇。例如: "I wonder what time the movie starts."
- **形容词**: 令人惊叹的；极好的。例如: "That was a wonderful performance!"

## 短语
- "To wonder about something" 意为对某事感到好奇或思考。
- "A childlike sense of wonder" 指孩子般的惊奇和好奇心。

## 例句
- 名词: "The child stared in wonder at the fireworks display."
- 动词: "I wonder if it will rain tomorrow."
- 形容词: "She had a wonderful talent for painting."
