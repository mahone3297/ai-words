## 读音
/oʊˈklɑːk/

## 释义
- **名词**: 表示钟点的时间表达方式，常与数字搭配使用。例如: "It's [five](/post/five/) o'clock."

## 短语
- "[at](/post/at/) o'clock" 用于表示整点时间，如 "[at](/post/at/) [six](/post/six/) o'clock" 表示六点钟。

## 例句
- "[The](/post/the/) [meeting](/post/meeting/) starts [at](/post/at/) [nine](/post/nine/) o'clock [sharp](/post/sharp/)."
- "[She](/post/she/) [usually](/post/usually/) wakes [up](/post/up/) [at](/post/at/) [six](/post/six/) o'clock [in](/post/in/) [the](/post/the/) [morning](/post/morning/)."

- 在表示时间这一点上，"o'clock" 没有特定的同义词，但可以使用 "[on](/post/on/) [the](/post/the/) [hour](/post/hour/)" 或 "[at](/post/at/) [the](/post/the/) [hour](/post/hour/)" 来表达相同的意思。例如: "[The](/post/the/) [train](/post/train/) arrives [at](/post/at/) [the](/post/the/) [station](/post/station/) [on](/post/on/) [the](/post/the/) [hour](/post/hour/)."
