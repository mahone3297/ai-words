## 读音
/ˈmɒnɪtər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 监视器，显示器，尤指电脑屏幕。例如: "I need to buy a new computer monitor with a higher resolution."
- **名词**: 监控器，检查器，用于监督或监测的设备或系统。例如: "The air quality monitor showed an increase in pollution levels."
- **名词**: （学校）辅导员，助教，负责监督和指导学生的人。例如: "In my high school, monitors helped teachers keep discipline."
- **动词**: 监视，监控。例如: "The teacher asked a student to monitor the class while she was out."

## 短语
- "Monitor one's health" 意味着定期检查健康状况。
- "Monitor the progress" 表示跟踪并评估进展。

## 例句
- 名词: "She adjusted the position of her computer monitor for better viewing."
- 动词: "The security team monitors the cameras 24/7."
- 名词（学校用语）: "John was appointed as the class monitor."
