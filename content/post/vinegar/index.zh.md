## 读音
/ˈvɪnɪɡər/

## 释义
- **名词**: 一种酸味液体，通常由发酵的酒精制成，常用于烹饪和调味。例如: "I need to buy some apple cider vinegar for the salad dressing."
- **动词**: 用醋处理或增加酸味。例如: "The cook vinegar the cucumbers to make pickles."

## 短语
- "a dash of vinegar" 一小撮醋，用于提味。
- "mother of vinegar" 醋中的活性酵母或细菌，是醋发酵过程的一部分。

## 例句
- 名词: "The recipe calls for a tablespoon of red wine vinegar."
- 动词: "She vinegar the onions to give them a tangy flavor."

## 同义词
- Acid
- Sour liquid
- Pickling liquid
- Lemon juice (in some contexts)
- Citric acid (for tartness in cooking)
