## Pronunciation
/əˈləʊtəʊl/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: In its entirety; completely; entirely. For example: "She cleaned the house altogether."
- **Adjective**: Characterized by or indicating unity or totality; comprehensive. For example: "The plan was thought out altogether."
- **Adjective (informal)**: Intense or excessive; completely. For example: "She was altogether too excited about the trip."

## Phrases
- "Altogether different" means entirely different.
- "Altogether too much" signifies an excess or overabundance.
- "Altogether now" is used to encourage unity or agreement.
