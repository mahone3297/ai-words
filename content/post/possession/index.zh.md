## 读音
/ˈpəʊzɪʃ(ə)n/

## 释义
- **名词**: 所有物，财产；拥有，占有；控制，支配；（精神上的）着迷，痴迷。

## 短语
- "in possession of something" 拥有某物，掌握某物。
- "take possession of" 占领，接管，取得控制。
- "out of one's possession" 不在某人的控制或拥有之下。

## 例句
- 名词: "He is in possession of a valuable antique."
- 名词: "The company took possession of the building after the contract was signed."
- 名词: "She felt a strange possession when she started collecting rare books."
