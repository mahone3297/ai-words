## 读音
/ˈsaɪəntɪst/

## 释义
- **名词**: 研究科学，特别是自然现象和规律的专业人士。例如: "The scientist published a groundbreaking paper on quantum physics."
- **形容词**: 与科学研究或方法有关的。例如: "Scientist methods involve systematic observation and experimentation."

## 短语
- "A renowned scientist" 指的是有名望的科学家。
- "Scientific research" 指的是科学研究活动。

## 例句
- 名词: "Marie Curie was a famous scientist who made significant contributions to the field of radioactivity."
- 形容词: "The scientist used a highly scientific approach to solve the problem."
