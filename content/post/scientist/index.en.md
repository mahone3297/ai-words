## Pronunciation
/scɪəntɪst/

## Definitions
### Noun
A scientist is a person who studies or practices science, typically by conducting experiments, analyzing data, and making observations to advance knowledge in a particular field or branch of science. They often engage in research and seek to understand natural phenomena or develop new technologies.

### Verb
To act as a scientist, meaning to employ scientific methods or knowledge to investigate, discover, or innovate.

## Phrases
- "Conduct scientific research"
- "A renowned biologist as a scientist"
- "Scientific inquiry"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The renowned scientist presented her groundbreaking findings at the conference."
- **Verb**: "After years of scientific study, they developed a new method to purify water."
- "As a scientist, he spends most of his time in the laboratory."
