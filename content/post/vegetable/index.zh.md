## 读音
/ˈvedʒɪtəb(ə)l/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 植物性食物，蔬菜。指可食用的植物部分，如叶子、根、果实等。例如: "I had a salad with various vegetables for lunch."
- **形容词 (adj)**: 植物性的，蔬菜类的。用来描述与植物有关或由植物制成的事物。例如: "The restaurant offers a wide range of vegetarian options."

## 短语
- "Leafy vegetables" 指叶状蔬菜，如菠菜和生菜。
- "Starchy vegetables" 包括高淀粉的蔬菜，如马铃薯和玉米。
- "Mixed vegetables" 指混合多种蔬菜的菜肴。

## 例句
- 名词: "The farmer's market had a great selection of fresh, organic vegetables."
- 形容词: "She follows a strict vegetarian diet, avoiding all meat and fish products."

## 同义词
- Veggie
- Plant-based food
- Greens
- Herbs
- Fruits (in some contexts, when referring to edible plant parts)
