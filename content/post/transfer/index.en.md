## Pronunciation
/təˈfrɜːr/

## Definitions
- **Noun**: 1. The act of moving something or someone from one place, position, or account to another. 2. (Finance) The act of transferring funds from one bank account to another. 3. (Technology) The process of moving data or software from one device or system to another.
- **Verb**: To move (money, data, etc.) from one account, location, or owner to another; to change ownership or custody.

## Phrases
- "Transfer of funds"
- "Bank transfer"
- "Data transfer"
- "Employee transfer"
- "Title transfer"

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The company arranged for the transfer of all employees to the new office."
- **Verb**: "I need to transfer the money from my savings account to my checking account."
- "After the upgrade, he had to transfer all his settings to the new software."
