## 读音
/ˈtrænsfɜːr/

## 释义
- **名词**: 转移；传送；转让；换乘；转账。例如: "I need to do a bank transfer to pay my bill."
- **动词**: 转移；转送；调动；迁移；转变。例如: "She transferred from one university to another."
- **介词**: 用于表示从一个地方、位置、人或事物转移到另一个。例如: "The package was transferred to the delivery company."

## 短语
- "Transfer of ownership" 意味着所有权的转移。
- "Transfer learning" 在机器学习中是指将已学习的知识应用到新任务上。
- "Transfer station" 可以指公共交通的换乘站或垃圾处理站。

## 例句
- 名词: "The company needs to process the transfer of funds before the deadline."
- 动词: "He will transfer to a different department next month."
- 介词: "We need to transfer the files from the old computer to the new one."
