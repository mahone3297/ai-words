## 读音
/sædli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示悲伤或不快乐的方式。例如: "He said goodbye to his friend sadly."
- **形容词**: 形容某事令人悲哀或令人感到遗憾。例如: "It was a sadly disappointing performance."

## 短语
- "sadly enough" 意为“不幸的是”。
- "in a sad manner" 指以悲伤的方式。

## 例句
- 副词: "Sadly, they had to cancel their trip due to unforeseen circumstances."
- 形容词: "The movie had a sadly predictable ending."
