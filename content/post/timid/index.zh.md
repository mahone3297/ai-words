## 读音
/tɪˈmɪd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 胆小的，羞怯的，怯懦的。例如: "She was too timid to speak in public."
- **名词**: 胆小鬼，羞涩的人。例如: "The timid rabbit froze at the sight of the fox."

## 短语
- "timid nature" 指胆小或害羞的性格。
- "come out of one's shell" 当某人从胆小的状态变得勇敢时，可以说他/她走出了自己的壳。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The timid child hid behind her mother when meeting new people."
- 名词: "He was once seen as a timid boy, but he grew up to be a confident man."

## 同义词
- Shy
- Fearful
- Timorous
- Cowardly
- Hesitant
