## Pronunciation
/səˈplaɪfaɪ/

## Definitions
- **Verb**: To make something less complex or difficult to understand, use, or deal with. To reduce the number of steps or components involved.
- **Adjective**: Characterized by simplicity; easy to understand or use.

## Phrases
- "Simplify a process" means to make it more straightforward.
- "Simplified instructions" are clear and concise explanations.
- "Simplifying tax laws" is a proposal to reduce their complexity.

## Example Sentences
- **Verb**: "She decided to simplify her daily routine to manage her time better."
- **Adjective**: "The new software design is designed to be highly simplified for users."
- **Noun**: "The teacher explained the concept in a way that would simplify the understanding for students."
