## 读音
/sɪmˈplɪfaɪ/

## 释义
- **动词**: 使简单；简化。例如: "The new software simplifies the task management process."
- **名词**: 简化的事物；简化的过程。例如: "We need a simplification of these complex rules."

## 短语
- "Simplify a problem" 意味着将问题变得更易理解和处理。
- "To simplify things" 是指使事情变得更容易理解或操作。

## 例句
- 动词: "The teacher's explanation simplified the difficult concept for us."
- 名词: "The company's goal was to introduce a product that would be a simplification of existing technology."
