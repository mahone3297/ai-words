## 读音
/ˈpɑːpjələr/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 受大众欢迎的，流行的，普遍的。例如: "This song is very popular among young people."
- **名词**: 大众喜欢的事物或人物。例如: "He became a popular figure after his successful performance."

## 短语
- "popular culture" 指大众文化，如流行音乐、电影等。
- "fall in/out of popularity" 意为变得受欢迎或不再受欢迎。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The new smartphone model quickly gained popularity due to its innovative features."
- 名词: "She was a popular actress during the golden age of cinema."
