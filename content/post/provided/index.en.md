## Definitions
- **Noun**: A greeting or expression of acknowledgement, often informal. Example: "She waved a friendly hello to her neighbor."
- **Verb**: To initiate a conversation or greet someone with "hello." Example: "He greeted the teacher with a hearty hello in the morning."
- **Adjective**: Expressing or characterized by friendliness or warmth. Example: "A cheery hello filled the room."
- **Adverb**: In a friendly or welcoming manner. Example: "He said hello warmly to his colleagues."

## Phrases
- "Say hello on one's behalf" to convey greetings on someone else's behalf.
- "Goodbye, hello" refers to a cycle of parting and reuniting.
- "Hello, stranger" is a way to address someone you don't know.
