## 读音
/prəˈvaidɪd/

## 释义
- **连词 conj**: 假设，假如。例如: "Provided it doesn't rain, we'll have a picnic."
- **介词 prep**: 提供，给予。例如: "The company provides health insurance for its employees."

## 短语
- "Provided that" 意味着“在…条件下，假设”。例如: "I'll go to the concert provided that my friend gets the tickets."
- "Provided with" 指“配备了，供应了”。例如: "The apartment is provided with all modern conveniences."

## 例句
- 连词: "You can come along provided you finish your homework first."
- 介词: "The hotel is provided with a gym and a swimming pool."

## 同义词
- Given
- Supposing
- Assuming
- On condition that
- In case
