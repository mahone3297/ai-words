## 读音
/səmˈweər/

## 释义
- **副词**: 在某处；在某个地方。例如: "I left my keys somewhere in the house."
- **名词**: 某个地方；任何地方。例如: "There must be a solution somewhere."

## 短语
- "Somewhere along the line" 意为在某个阶段或过程中。
- "Somewhere in between" 表示在两者之间或某种程度上。

## 例句
- 副词: "I know I put my book down somewhere, but I can't remember where."
- 名词: "There's a hidden treasure somewhere in this old house."

## 同义词
- Anywhere
- Certain places
- In some place
- Here and there
- Nowhere (反义词，表示不在任何地方)
