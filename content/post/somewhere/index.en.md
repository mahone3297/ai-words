## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Referring to an unspecified place or location, often used to indicate uncertainty or vagueness. For example: "I'll meet you somewhere near the park."
- **Adjective**: Designating a place or position without specifying exactly where. For example: "The package was delivered to a random somewhere in the city."

## Phrases
- "Somewhere over the rainbow" refers to a distant, idealized place.
- "Find your way somewhere" means to locate or reach a destination.
- "Somewhere in between" indicates a middle ground or compromise.
