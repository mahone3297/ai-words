## 读音
/roʊd/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 1. 道路，街道。例如: "The new road connects the city to the highway."
   2. 路线，行程。例如: "We need to plan our road trip carefully."
   3. （体育）赛道。例如: "The cyclists raced on a challenging road course."

## 短语
- "On the road" 意为在旅行或在途中。例如: "They've been on the road for weeks, playing concerts across the country."
- "Road map" 比喻指计划或策略。例如: "The company's road map for growth includes expanding into new markets."

## 例句
- 名词: "There was heavy traffic on the main road during rush hour."
- 短语: "We followed the road map to find our way through the forest."
