## 读音
/əˈkeɪʒən/

## 释义
- **名词 (n)**: 特定的时间或场合，事件或庆祝活动。例如: "The wedding was a grand occasion."
- **名词**: 适当或合适的机会。例如: "This is an occasion to express our gratitude."
- **动词**: 造成或产生（某种情况）。例如: "His absence occasions much speculation."

## 短语
- "on this occasion" 在这个特定的情况下
- "by occasion of" 因为，由于
- "an informal occasion" 非正式场合

## 例句
- 名词: "We dress up for formal occasions like weddings and galas."
- 动词: "Her unexpected visit occasions a change in our plans.
