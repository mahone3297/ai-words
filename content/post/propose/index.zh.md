## 读音
/prəˈpoʊz/

## 释义
- **动词**:
1. 提议，建议。例如: "He proposed a new plan for increasing sales."
2. (正式)求婚。例如: "John proposed to Mary on Valentine's Day."
3. (立法机构)提出(议案)。例如: "The senator proposed an amendment to the bill."

## 短语
- "Propose a toast" 意为提议为某人或某事干杯。
- "Propose a solution" 意为提出一个解决方案。

## 例句
- "At the meeting, she proposed that we work together on the project."
- "After dating for a year, he got down on one knee and proposed to her."
- "The council proposed a law to reduce plastic waste."
