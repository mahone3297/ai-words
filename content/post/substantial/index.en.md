## Definitions
### Noun
1. Of considerable importance, quality, or size; significant.
   - "The new project has substantial financial backing."
2. Sufficient in amount or substance; substantial.
   - "A substantial meal at the restaurant"
3. Consisting of or containing a substantial part or quantity.
   - "Substantial portions of the book are devoted to history."

### Verb
1. To become substantial; take on substance or reality.
   - "The idea slowly became substantial after months of discussion."
2. To support or satisfy in a significant way.
   - "This evidence provides substantial proof of their innocence."

### Adjective
1. Mainly or largely made up of something; solid or reliable.
   - "A substantial wall of stone"
2. Characterized by being substantial or significant.
   - "A substantial increase in sales"
