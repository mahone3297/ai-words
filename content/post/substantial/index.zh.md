## 读音
/subˈstænʃəl/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 实质的，重要的；大量的。例如: "The company has substantial profits this year." 
- **名词**: （常与the连用）实质，本质。例如: "The substantial part of the agreement remains unchanged."

## 短语
- "substantial amount" 表示相当数量。
- "substantial evidence" 指有力的证据。
- "a substantial meal" 意为丰盛的一餐。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The house needs substantial repairs due to years of neglect."
- 名词: "The substantial of the problem lies in communication breakdown."
