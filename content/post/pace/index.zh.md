## 读音
/peɪs/

## 释义
- **名词**: 步伐，步速；节奏。例如: "He set a steady pace for the hike."
- **动词**: 以一定的步伐行走。例如: "She paced back and forth in the room, deep in thought."
- **名词**: 量度单位，常用于测量速度或距离。例如: "The car was traveling at a pace of 60 miles per hour."
- **动词**: 调整（音乐、演讲等）的节奏。例如: "The conductor paced the orchestra to start the piece slowly."

## 短语
- "at a steady pace" 指以稳定的速度进行。
- "pace oneself" 意为根据个人能力调整步伐或速度。
- "set the pace" 指设定标准或引领方向。

## 例句
- 名词: "He kept a quick pace to reach the destination on time."
- 动词: "She paced the room, anxious about the upcoming meeting."
- 名词: "Their team's pace in the project was much faster than ours."
