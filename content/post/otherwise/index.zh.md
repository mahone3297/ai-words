## 读音
/ˈʌðəwaɪz/

## 释义
- **副词**: 表示“否则”，“不然”的意思，通常用来提出一个相反的情况或假设。例如: "Finish your homework now, otherwise you won't have time later."
- **形容词**: 用作形容词时，意为“其他的”，“另外的”。例如: "There are no other options; we have to take this one otherwise."

## 短语
- "In any other case" 意为在任何其他情况下。
- "Otherwise known as" 简称为，也被称为。

## 例句
- 副词: "If it rains, we'll stay inside; otherwise, we'll go for a picnic."
- 形容词: "She had no other choice but to accept the offer; otherwise, she would lose her job."
