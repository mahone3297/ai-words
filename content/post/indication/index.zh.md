## 读音
/ɪndɪˈkeɪʃn/

## 释义
- **名词 n**:
1. 显示，表明。例如: "The rising temperature is an indication of spring's arrival."
2. 指示，象征。例如: "The red light is an indication that you should stop."
3. 医学上的迹象或征兆。例如: "The doctor will look for specific indications of the disease."

- **动词 v**:
1. 显示，指出。例如: "The test results indicate a high level of stress."
2. 告知，提出。例如: "The teacher indicated her approval with a nod."

## 短语
- "Give an indication of" 意思是给出...的迹象。例如: "Can you give any indication of when the project will be completed?"
- "Indicate to" 指出对某人来说。例如: "The map indicates the nearest exit to you."

## 例句
- 名词: "The patient's rapid heartbeat was a clear indication of anxiety."
- 动词: "The study indicates that exercise can improve mental health."
