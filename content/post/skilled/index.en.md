## Pronunciation
/skɪlɪd/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Having a high degree of proficiency, ability, or expertise in a particular skill or activity. For example: "The skilled surgeon performed the operation with precision."
- **Noun**: A person who is skilled or expert in a particular field. For example: "The skilled carpenter crafted a beautiful piece of furniture."

## Phrases
- "Be skilled in" implies having a strong aptitude or proficiency in something.
- "Skilled professionals" refers to experts in their respective fields.
- "Acquire a skill" means to learn and become proficient in a particular ability.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The skilled painter's brushstrokes were impeccable."
- **Noun**: "She hired a team of skilled developers for the project."
- **Phrase**: "To master a new skill takes time and dedication."
