## 读音
/praʊd/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 自豪的，得意的，自尊心强的。例如: "She was proud of her son's achievements."
- **副词**: 自豪地，得意洋洋地。例如: "He played his guitar proudly on stage."

## 短语
- "be proud of" 意为对...感到自豪。例如: "I am proud of my country's rich history."
- "take pride in" 表示在...方面感到自豪。例如: "They take pride in their workmanship."

## 例句
- 形容词: "He was too proud to ask for help."
- 副词: "She sang proudly, knowing all eyes were on her."
