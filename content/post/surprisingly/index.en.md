## Pronunciation
/sərˈprɪzɪŋli/

## Definitions
- **Adverb**: Expressing that something happened in an unexpected or remarkable manner. It modifies a verb, adjective, or other adverbs. For example, "He arrived surprisingly early."

## Phrases
- "Surprisingly well" indicates better than expected.
- "Surprisingly few" suggests a smaller number than anticipated.
- "Not surprisingly" implies that the outcome was expected due to prior circumstances.
