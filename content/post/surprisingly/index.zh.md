## 读音
/səˈpraɪzɪŋli/

## 释义
- **副词**: 意为“惊人地”，“出乎意料地”，用来强调某个结果或情况与预期相反或不同。例如: "The plant grew surprisingly fast in the shade."

## 短语
- "surprisingly enough" 表示惊讶地发现某事是真的或发生了。例如: "Surprisingly enough, he actually enjoyed the lecture."

## 例句
- "She scored surprisingly high on the test, considering she didn't study much."
- "Despite his age, he moves surprisingly agilely."
