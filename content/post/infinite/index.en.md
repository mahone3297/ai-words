## Word: Infinite

## Pronunciation
/ɪnˈfɪnɪt/

## Definitions
- **Adjective**: Without limits; endless or boundless, especially in time, space, or magnitude. It can also mean immeasurably great or immense. 

  > Example sentence (adjective): The universe is believed to be infinite, stretching out beyond our comprehension.

- **Noun**: Infinity, the concept of something being without bounds or limit; it can also refer to an infinite number, quantity, or extent.

  > Example sentence (noun): Mathematicians often discuss the concept of the infinite in their theories.

## Phrases
- "Infinite possibilities": This phrase suggests that there are countless options or outcomes.
- "To infinity and beyond": A popular phrase from the movie 'Toy Story', symbolizing limitless ambition or potential.

## Example Sentences
- **Adjective**: "The human quest for knowledge knows no finite boundaries, as we seek answers in the infinite expanse of the cosmos."
- **Noun**: "Mathematical equations can sometimes lead to infinities, challenging our understanding of numbers."
