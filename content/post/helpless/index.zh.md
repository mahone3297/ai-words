## 读音
/helpləs/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 无法自助的，无能为力的，无助的。例如: "She felt helpless when her car broke down in the middle of nowhere."
- **副词**: 无助地，无法自救地。例如: "He stood there helplessly, not knowing what to do."

## 短语
- "In a helpless situation" 指处于无法自我解决的困境。
- "Feel helpless" 表示感到无能为力或束手无策。

## 例句
- 形容词: "The injured bird lay on the ground, looking completely helpless."
- 副词: "She watched her house burn down, feeling helpless and devastated."

## 同义词
- Incapable
- Powerless
- Impotent
- Unaided
- At a loss
