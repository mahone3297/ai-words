## 读音
/rɪˈkʌvər/

## 释义
- **动词**:
  1. 恢复健康：如 "He is recovering from a cold."
  2. 恢复原状：如 "The market has recovered after the economic downturn."
  3. 找回失去的事物：如 "I managed to recover my lost wallet."
  4. 收回（如说过的话）：如 "He quickly recovered his words after realizing his mistake."

## 短语
- "recover fully" 完全康复
- "recover one's senses" 恢复理智
- "recover costs" 回收成本

## 例句
- 动词: 
  - "After a week of rest, she was finally able to recover her strength."
  - "The company hopes to recover its losses by the end of the year."
  - "He managed to recover the stolen painting."
