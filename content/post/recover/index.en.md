## Pronunciation
/rɪˈkʌvər/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A recovery from an illness, injury, or difficult situation.
- The act of regaining or restoring something that was lost or stolen.
- The process of returning to normal after a period of illness or absence.

### Verb
- To regain health, strength, or well-being after being sick or injured.
- To regain possession or control of something that was lost or taken.
- To come back to normal functioning or performance after a setback or decline.

## Phrases
- "recover from" implies getting better or overcoming an issue.
- "full recovery" means being completely healed or restored.
- "recovering slowly" describes a gradual return to health or normalcy.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "After the surgery, she made a full recovery in just a few months."
- **Verb**: "He's been working hard to recover his strength after the accident."
- **Intransitive**: "The economy is expected to recover gradually over the next year."
