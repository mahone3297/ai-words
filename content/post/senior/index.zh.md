## 读音
/ˈsiːniər/

## 释义
- **形容词**: 年长的，资深的，上级的。例如: "He is a senior member of the team."
- **名词**: 高年级学生，年长者，前辈。例如: "Senior students often mentor the freshmen."
- **名词 (在公司或组织中)**: 资深员工，高级职员。例如: "She was promoted to a senior management position."

## 短语
- "Senior year" 指高中或大学的最后一年。
- "Senior citizen" 指老年人。
- "Senior staff" 指公司的高级员工。

## 例句
- 形容词: "My brother is senior to me in both age and experience."
- 名词: "She just started her senior year in college."
- 名词 (公司): "The senior executive made an important decision."
