## Pronunciation
/sɪˈnɪər/

## Definitions
### Noun
- A **senior** is typically a person who has achieved a high rank, status, or age within a group, organization, or academic setting. In education, it refers to a final year student or a person in a higher position. In a company, it may denote a long-serving or experienced employee.
- In retirement, seniors often have a title like "senior citizen" or "senior advisor."

### Adjective
- **Senior** can also be an adjective describing someone who is advanced in age or holds a superior position in a hierarchy.

## Phrases
- "A senior position" signifies a leadership role.
- "Senior citizens" are often associated with retirement communities.
- "Senior year" refers to the final year of secondary school or college.

## Example Sentences
- **Noun**: "The senior management team decided on the new strategy."
- **Adjective**: "The senior officer was praised for his leadership."
- "In her senior year, she won a scholarship for academic excellence."
