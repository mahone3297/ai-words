## 读音
/myself/

## 释义
- **代词**: 第一人称单数的反身代词，表示我自己、我本人。例如: "I did it myself."（我自己做的。）
- **强调词**: 强调主语的动作是由主语自己完成的。例如: "I myself will go."（我自己去。）

## 短语
- "by myself" 表示独自一人。例如: "I'm staying at home by myself tonight."
- "do something for myself" 表示为自己做事。例如: "It's time I started doing something for myself."

## 例句
- 代词: "I need to do this task myself to ensure it's done correctly."
- 强调词: "She herself confirmed the news."

## 同义词
- myself: 
  - 反身代词：my own, myself, oneself
  - 强调词：personally, individually, actually (in some contexts)
